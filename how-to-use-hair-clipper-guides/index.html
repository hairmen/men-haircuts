How To Use Hair Clipper Guides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barbershop or salon just to get a simple haircut? With the right tools and a little bit of know-how, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. One of the most essential tools in your arsenal is the hair clipper guide. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to use hair clipper guides to achieve the perfect cut every time.

Description: What are Hair Clipper Guides?

A hair clipper guide is a plastic attachment that fits onto the blade of a hair clipper. These attachments come in different sizes and are designed to help you achieve a specific length of hair. Hair clipper guides are an essential tool for anyone who wants to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

How to Use Hair Clipper Guides

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper Guide

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to choose the right clipper guide. Clipper guides come in different sizes, which correspond to different lengths of hair. The smaller the guide, the shorter the hair will be. Make sure you choose the right guide for the length of hair you want.

Step 2: Attach the Clipper Guide

Once you have chosen the right clipper guide, you need to attach it to your hair clipper. Most hair clippers come with a set of clipper guides, but if yours didn't, you can purchase them separately. To attach the clipper guide, simply snap it onto the blade of your clipper. Make sure it is securely attached before you start cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use the clipper guide to guide the clippers through your hair. Make sure you move the clipper in a smooth, even motion to ensure an even cut. If you want a shorter cut, you can use a smaller clipper guide. If you want a longer cut, you can use a larger clipper guide.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut all the hair to the desired length using the clipper guide, it's time to blend the hair. This means you need to use a shorter clipper guide or no guide at all to blend the hair from the bottom to the top. This will create a smooth, seamless transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 5: Clean up the Edges

After you have blended the hair, it's time to clean up the edges. Use a pair of scissors or trimmer to clean up the hair around the ears and neckline. This will give your haircut a polished, professional look.

Tips for Using Hair Clipper Guides

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hair clipper guides:

  • Always start with a longer clipper guide and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure it is even before you start cutting.
  • Make sure the clipper guide is securely attached to the clipper before you start cutting.
  • Move the clipper in a smooth, even motion to ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the hair from the bottom to the top to create a seamless transition between different lengths of hair.
  • Clean up the edges with a pair of scissors or trimmer for a polished, professional look.

FAQs

Do hair clipper guides work with all types of hair clippers?

Most hair clipper guides are designed to work with a specific brand or model of hair clippers. Make sure you choose a clipper guide that is compatible with your clippers.

Can hair clipper guides be used for all hair types?

Hair clipper guides can be used for all hair types, but you may need to adjust the length of the guide depending on the thickness and texture of your hair.

Do I need to oil my hair clippers?

Yes, it is important to oil your hair clippers regularly to keep them running smoothly and prevent rusting. Check the manufacturer's instructions for how often to oil your clippers.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and polished look. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to use hair clipper guides, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. With a little bit of practice and the right tools, you can save money and time on haircuts and still look your best.
