How To Regrow Hair After Scalp Psoriasis A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Dealing with scalp psoriasis can be a frustrating and uncomfortable experience. Not only can it cause redness, itching, and flaking, but it can also lead to hair loss. However, there are steps you can take to regrow hair after scalp psoriasis. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to regrow hair after scalp psoriasis.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Treat Your Scalp Psoriasis
  • Step 2: Practice Good Hair Care
  • Step 3: Consider Hair Growth Products
  • Tips for Regrowing Hair After Scalp Psoriasis
  • FAQs

Step 1: Treat Your Scalp Psoriasis

The first step in regrowing hair after scalp psoriasis is to treat the underlying condition. There are various treatments available, including medicated shampoos, topical creams, and oral medications. Consult a dermatologist to determine the best treatment plan for your scalp psoriasis.

It is important to follow your treatment plan consistently and avoid picking at the affected areas. Picking can lead to further irritation and increase the risk of infection.

Step 2: Practice Good Hair Care

Along with treating your scalp psoriasis, it is crucial to practice good hair care habits. This includes using a mild shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and minimizing the use of hair products.

You should also avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, as they can pull on the hair and cause damage. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that do not put stress on the hair.

Step 3: Consider Hair Growth Products

If you are looking to speed up the hair regrowth process, there are various hair growth products available. These include minoxidil, biotin supplements, and essential oils.

Minoxidil is a topical medication that has been proven to promote hair growth. Biotin supplements can also help improve hair health and growth. Essential oils, such as peppermint and lavender, can help stimulate the hair follicles and promote hair growth.

Tips for Regrowing Hair After Scalp Psoriasis

  • Be patient - hair regrowth after scalp psoriasis can take time
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments, such as perms or relaxers
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage hair health
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair and minimize breakage

FAQs

Can scalp psoriasis cause permanent hair loss?

While scalp psoriasis can cause temporary hair loss, it is usually reversible with proper treatment and hair care.

Can stress trigger scalp psoriasis?

Stress can be a trigger for scalp psoriasis, but it is not the only cause. Other factors, such as genetics and immune system dysfunction, can also contribute to the condition.

Is it safe to use hair growth products while treating scalp psoriasis?

Consult with your dermatologist before using any hair growth products while treating scalp psoriasis. Some products may interact with your medication or worsen your condition.

Overall, regrowing hair after scalp psoriasis requires patience, consistency, and proper care. Follow these steps and tips to promote hair regrowth and maintain healthy hair.
