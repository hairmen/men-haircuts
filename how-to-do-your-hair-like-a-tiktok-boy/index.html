How To Do Your Hair Like A Tiktok Boy

Written by Frank Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a fan of TikTok, you've probably noticed that many of the boys on the platform have some seriously cool hairstyles. And if you're wondering how to achieve those looks for yourself, you're in luck! In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve some of the most popular TikTok boy hairstyles.

Description

TikTok has become a hub for creative expression, and that extends to hairstyles as well. Boys on the app are pushing boundaries and experimenting with new styles, from buzz cuts to long hair and everything in between. One of the most notable things about TikTok boy hairstyles is that they often involve intricate cuts and styling techniques that require some skill to pull off.

How to

The first step in achieving a TikTok boy hairstyle is to have the right haircut. Many of these styles involve fades, undercuts, or other short cuts that require a skilled barber or stylist. If you're not sure what cut to ask for, try bringing in a picture of the TikTok boy whose style you want to emulate.

Once you have the right cut, it's time to start styling. Most TikTok boy hairstyles involve some sort of product, whether that's pomade, gel, or wax. Start with a small amount and work it through your hair, focusing on the areas that need the most hold or definition.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a small amount of product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way outwards.

3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.

4. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product, if desired.

Tips

- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

- Don't be afraid to ask your stylist or barber for advice on how to style your new cut.

- Practice makes perfect - it may take some trial and error to get your TikTok boy hairstyle just right.

FAQ

Q: Do I need to have short hair to achieve a TikTok boy hairstyle?

A: Not necessarily! While many TikTok boy hairstyles do involve shorter cuts, there are also plenty of longer styles to choose from.

Q: Can I achieve these styles on my own, or do I need to go to a professional?

A: It's certainly possible to style your hair on your own, but for the more intricate cuts and styles, it's often best to go to a professional stylist or barber.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair in order to keep it looking healthy and strong. This means using high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular trims to keep split ends at bay. Additionally, if you're using a lot of product to style your hair, it's important to wash it out thoroughly at the end of the day to avoid buildup and potential damage.

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be well on your way to achieving a trendy TikTok boy hairstyle that's sure to turn heads.
