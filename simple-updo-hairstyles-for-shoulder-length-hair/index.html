Simple Updo Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is easy to do but still looks elegant and sophisticated? Look no further than simple updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, from weddings to work events. In this article, we will show you how to create three different updo hairstyles that are perfect for shoulder length hair.

Description

Shoulder length hair can be a bit tricky to style, but with the right techniques, you can create beautiful updo hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. These hairstyles are easy to do and require minimal tools and products. They are perfect for those who are always on the go but still want to look put together.

How To

To create these simple updo hairstyles, you will need a few basic tools and products:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastic
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray (optional)

Low Bun

Start by gathering your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a soft, romantic look. Finish with hairspray.

Messy Updo

Start by adding some texture to your hair with texturizing spray. Pull your hair back into a loose ponytail and twist it into a messy bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a more relaxed look. Finish with hairspray.

Half-Up Twist

Start by pulling the top half of your hair back into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a soft, romantic look. Finish with hairspray.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to create the perfect simple updo:

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail or half-up style.
  2. Twist the ponytail or the top half of your hair.
  3. Wrap the twisted hair around the base to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a soft, romantic look.
  6. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you create the perfect simple updo:

  • Use texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't worry if your updo isn't perfect - a messy, undone look can be just as beautiful.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about simple updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

What is the best way to secure an updo?

The best way to secure an updo is with bobby pins. Make sure to use enough pins to keep your hair in place.

Can I create an updo with short hair?

It can be more difficult to create an updo with short hair, but it is still possible. Try a half-up style or a messy bun.

How do I keep my updo from falling out?

Make sure to secure your updo with enough bobby pins. You can also use hairspray to help hold your hair in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to create simple updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you can try them out for your next event. These hairstyles are easy to do and will have you looking chic and stylish in no time.
