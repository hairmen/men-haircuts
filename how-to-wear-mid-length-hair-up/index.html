How To Wear Mid Length Hair Up The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Mid length hair can be the perfect length for those who want versatility in their hairstyles. One of the most popular ways to wear mid length hair is by pulling it up into an updo. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to wear mid length hair up in a relaxed manner. We'll also provide tips on how to care for your hair to ensure it looks its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Mid Length Hair Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Mid Length Hair Up
  • FAQs

How to Wear Mid Length Hair Up

The first step in wearing your mid length hair up is to decide what type of updo you want. There are several options to choose from, including a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo.

Once you've decided on the type of updo you want, gather all the necessary tools and products. This includes a brush, comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear your mid length hair up in a relaxed manner:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide on the placement of your updo, whether you want it high or low on your head.
  3. Pull your hair back into a ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it.
  5. Secure the wrapped hair with a bobby pin.
  6. If you want a messy bun, use your fingers to loosen the hair around the hair tie.
  7. If you want a sleek ponytail, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.
  8. If you want a braided updo, braid the ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  9. Use hairspray to hold the updo in place.

Tips for Wearing Mid Length Hair Up

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing your mid length hair up:

  • Use hair ties that won't damage your hair, such as ones without metal clasps.
  • Don't pull your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Experiment with different types of updos to find what works best for your face shape and hair texture.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my mid length hair up every day?

A: Yes, you can wear your mid length hair up every day, but it's important to give your hair a break from time to time to prevent damage.

Q: Will wearing my hair up too often cause damage?

A: Wearing your hair up too often can cause damage, especially if you pull your hair too tightly. It's important to give your hair a break and let it down to prevent breakage.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

To keep your mid length hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can wear your mid length hair up in a relaxed manner and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
