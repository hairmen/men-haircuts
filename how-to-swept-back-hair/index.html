How To Swept Back Hair A Relaxed Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to create a sleek and stylish look, swept back hair is a great option. It's a classic style that works for men and women of all ages, and it's perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect swept back hairstyle in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure your style lasts all day.

Description

Swept back hair is a hairstyle that involves combing the hair back away from the face and towards the back of the head. The hair is typically slicked back using a comb or brush, and held in place with some form of hair product, such as gel, wax, or hairspray. This style is popular for its simplicity and versatility, and it can be adapted to suit a range of different hair types and lengths.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect swept back hairstyle:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair, and then towel dry it until it's damp. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as gel or mousse, to your hair, and comb it through to distribute evenly. This will help to give your hair the hold it needs to stay in place.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a hair dryer, blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. Use a brush or comb to help guide your hair back as you dry it. This will help to give your hair the volume and shape you need to create the perfect swept back look.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair product to your hands and run it through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb or brush to shape your hair into the style you want, and then use your hands to smooth out any bumps or kinks.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your swept back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to ensure your hair stays in place all day.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and textures to find the style that works best for you.
  • Try adding some volume to your hair by blow drying it in the opposite direction before combing it back.
  • Consider adding some layers to your hair to give it more movement and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about swept back hair:

What hair types are best suited to a swept back hairstyle?

Swept back hair works well on most hair types, but it's particularly suited to medium to long hair that's straight or wavy.

How do I keep my swept back hairstyle in place?

Use a high-quality hair product, such as gel, wax, or hairspray, to help keep your hair in place. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips.

Can I wear a swept back hairstyle to a formal event?

Yes, swept back hair is a versatile and stylish look that can be adapted to suit any occasion, including formal events.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you care for your hair and keep it looking healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect swept back hairstyle in no time. Whether you're headed to the office, a formal event, or a night out with friends, this classic and versatile look is sure to turn heads.
