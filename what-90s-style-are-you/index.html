What 90S Style Are You

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our fashion blog where we'll be discussing fashion trends from the 90s. In this blog post, we'll be exploring what 90s style suits you the most. The 90s was an era where fashion and music were at its peak, and it's no surprise that many of the fashion trends from the 90s are coming back into style. From grunge to hip hop, there's a 90s style for everyone. So, if you're looking to add some 90s flair to your wardrobe, keep reading to find out what 90s style suits you best.

Description

The 90s was a decade of experimentation and individuality in fashion. The era saw a mix of grunge, hip hop, and minimalistic styles. The grunge style was characterized by oversized flannel shirts, ripped denim, and combat boots. The hip hop style was known for its baggy pants, graphic tees, and gold chains. The minimalistic style was all about simplicity, with neutral colors and clean lines.

How to

When trying to determine what 90s style suits you best, start by taking a look at your current wardrobe. Do you tend to gravitate towards oversized clothing or more form-fitting pieces? Are you drawn to bold prints and bright colors or do you prefer neutral tones? Answering these questions can help you narrow down which 90s style you should try.

Step by Step

Step 1: Take a look at your current wardrobe.

Step 2: Determine if you prefer oversized or form-fitting clothing.

Step 3: Decide if you like bold prints and bright colors or neutral tones.

Step 4: Research 90s fashion trends and decide which style suits you best.

Step 5: Start incorporating pieces from your chosen 90s style into your wardrobe.

Tips

When incorporating 90s fashion into your wardrobe, it's important to keep in mind that less is more. You don't want to overdo it and end up looking like you're wearing a costume. Try to incorporate one or two 90s-inspired pieces into your outfit, such as a graphic tee or a pair of combat boots.

FAQ

Q: What 90s style is best for someone who likes bold colors?

A: The 90s hip hop style is perfect for someone who likes bold colors. This style often features bright and bold graphic tees and oversized pants in vibrant hues.

Q: What 90s style is best for someone who likes neutral tones?

A: The 90s minimalistic style is perfect for someone who likes neutral tones. This style often features clean lines and neutral colors such as black, white, and beige.

Q: Can I mix and match different 90s styles?

A: Absolutely! The 90s was a decade of experimentation, so feel free to mix and match different styles to create a unique look that suits you.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair, the 90s was all about experimenting with different styles. From crimped hair to pixie cuts, there was no shortage of hairstyles to try. If you're looking to incorporate some 90s flair into your hair, consider trying out a messy bun or a half-up half-down style with some scrunchies. You could also try out some bold hair colors, such as bright pink or blue.

In conclusion, the 90s was a decade of individuality and experimentation in fashion. Whether you prefer grunge, hip hop, or minimalistic styles, there's a 90s fashion trend for everyone. By following the steps outlined above, you can determine which 90s style suits you best and start incorporating pieces into your wardrobe. Remember, less is more when it comes to incorporating 90s fashion into your wardrobe, so start small and experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.
