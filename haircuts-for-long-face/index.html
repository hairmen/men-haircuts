Haircuts For Long Face Flatter Your Features With The Right Hairstyle

Written by Christine Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have a long face and struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features? Look no further because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to choose the best haircuts for long face shape. Read on to find out more.

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a high forehead, narrow chin, and elongated features. It’s often considered an angular face shape that requires a bit of softening with the right haircut. The goal is to create balance by adding width to the face and minimizing the length. By choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best.

How to Choose the Best Haircuts for Long Face

Before choosing a haircut, it’s important to determine your face shape. If you’re not sure, you can measure the length and width of your face and compare the two. A long face shape is usually longer than it is wide, with a length-to-width ratio of more than 1.5. Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that flatter your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when choosing the best haircuts for long face:

  1. Look for haircuts that add width to the face, such as layered cuts, waves, and curls.
  2. Avoid haircuts that are too long or straight, as they can elongate the face even more.
  3. Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs to shorten the forehead and balance the face.
  4. If you prefer a shorter haircut, go for a bob or a pixie cut that adds volume to the sides.
  5. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that flatters your features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut for your long face shape, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for long face shape?

The best haircuts for long face shape are layered cuts, waves, and curls that add width to the face. Side-swept fringe or bangs can also help balance the face.

Can I have long hair if I have a long face shape?

Yes, you can have long hair if you have a long face shape, but it’s important to add layers to create volume and avoid elongating the face too much.

Do bangs look good on long face shape?

Yes, bangs can look good on long face shape as they can shorten the forehead and balance the face. Side-swept fringe or curtain bangs are especially flattering.

Hair Care for Long Face Shape

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the natural oils and dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight when styling, as it can cause breakage and damage.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can flatter your long face shape and look your best. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your face shape and lifestyle. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can rock any look with confidence.
