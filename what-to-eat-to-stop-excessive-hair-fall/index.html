What To Eat To Stop Excessive Hair Fall

Written by Frank Sep 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Excessive hair fall can be a frustrating and distressing problem. It can lead to thinning hair, bald patches, and even complete baldness. Many people turn to expensive hair treatments and products to try and combat this issue, but did you know that what you eat can also play a significant role in preventing hair fall?

How to Stop Excessive Hair Fall with Diet

There are certain foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth. Incorporating these foods into your diet can help prevent excessive hair fall. Here are some of the best foods to eat:

1. Eggs

Eggs are an excellent source of protein, which is essential for strong and healthy hair. They are also rich in biotin, which helps prevent hair fall and promotes hair growth.

2. Spinach

Spinach is packed with iron, which helps oxygen reach your hair follicles, promoting healthy hair growth. It also contains vitamins A and C, which help produce sebum, a natural oil that keeps hair healthy and shiny.

3. Salmon

Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which help keep your scalp healthy and prevent hair fall. It also contains vitamin D, which is essential for healthy hair growth.

4. Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are a great source of beta-carotene, which is converted into vitamin A in the body. Vitamin A is essential for healthy hair growth as it helps produce sebum.

5. Nuts

Nuts are a great source of biotin, which helps prevent hair fall and promotes healthy hair growth. They are also packed with vitamin E, which helps improve blood flow to the scalp, promoting healthy hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide to Eating for Healthy Hair

If you want to prevent excessive hair fall by eating a healthy diet, here are some steps you can follow:

Step 1: Incorporate protein into every meal

Protein is essential for healthy hair growth, so try to include it in every meal. Good sources of protein include eggs, chicken, fish, beans, and nuts.

Step 2: Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables

Fruits and vegetables are packed with vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth. Try to eat a rainbow of fruits and veggies to ensure you're getting a variety of nutrients.

Step 3: Choose healthy fats

Healthy fats, such as those found in avocados, nuts, and fatty fish, are essential for healthy hair growth. They help keep your scalp hydrated and prevent hair from becoming dry and brittle.

Step 4: Avoid processed foods

Processed foods are often high in sugar and unhealthy fats, which can lead to hair fall. Try to avoid these foods and opt for whole, nutrient-dense foods instead.

Tips for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair fall:

1. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, can cause hair breakage and lead to hair fall. Try to wear your hair down or opt for looser hairstyles.

2. Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair

Brushing your hair too vigorously or using heat styling tools can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause your hair to become dry and brittle, leading to hair fall. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

FAQs About Eating for Healthy Hair

Q: Can eating a healthy diet really prevent hair fall?

A: Yes, a healthy diet can help prevent hair fall by providing your body with the nutrients it needs for healthy hair growth.

Q: How long does it take to see results from eating a healthy diet?

A: It can take several weeks to several months to see results from eating a healthy diet, as hair growth is a slow process.

Q: Can I take supplements instead of eating these foods?

A: While supplements can be helpful, it's always best to get your nutrients from whole foods whenever possible.

Other Hair Care Tips

In addition to eating a healthy diet, here are some other hair care tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps keep your scalp clean and healthy, which is essential for healthy hair growth.

2. Get regular haircuts

Getting regular haircuts helps prevent split ends, which can lead to hair breakage and hair fall.

3. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner helps keep your hair healthy and prevents damage.

By following these tips and incorporating hair-healthy foods into your diet, you can prevent excessive hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.
