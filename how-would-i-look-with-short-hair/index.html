How Would I Look With Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're thinking about cutting your hair short, you may be wondering how you'll look with the new do. Short hair can be liberating and empowering, but it can also be daunting. Fortunately, there are ways to determine how you'll look with short hair before you make the cut. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how you'll look with short hair.

Description

Short hair can be defined as hair that is cut above the shoulders. It can come in many different styles, from a pixie cut to a bob. Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, depending on your personal preference. Some people find that short hair is easier to manage and style than longer hair, while others prefer the look of long hair.

How to Determine How You'll Look with Short Hair

There are several ways to determine how you'll look with short hair:

1. Use a Virtual Makeover Tool

There are many online virtual makeover tools that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles. This is a great way to see how you'll look with short hair without actually cutting your hair.

2. Look at Photos of People with Similar Face Shapes and Hair Textures

If you have a similar face shape and hair texture as someone with short hair, you may be able to get a good idea of how you'll look with short hair by looking at photos of them.

3. Try on a Wig

If you're not ready to commit to cutting your hair, you can try on a wig to see how you'll look with short hair. This is a great option if you're unsure about how you'll look with short hair.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair Short

If you've decided to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's a step by step guide:

1. Choose a Style

Before you cut your hair, you'll need to choose a style. Look at photos of different short hairstyles and choose one that you like and that will work well with your face shape and hair texture.

2. Find a Good Hair Stylist

Find a hair stylist who has experience cutting short hair. You'll want someone who can help you achieve the look you want.

3. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair before you go to the salon. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any products or buildup.

4. Talk to Your Stylist

Talk to your stylist about the style you've chosen and any concerns you may have. They can help you determine if the style will work well with your face shape and hair texture.

5. Cut Your Hair

Once you've discussed the style with your stylist, it's time to cut your hair. Your stylist will use scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Once you've cut your hair short, here are some tips for maintaining it:

1. Get Regular Trims

Short hair needs to be trimmed more frequently than long hair. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair. This will help you achieve the look you want and keep your hair healthy.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What face shapes look best with short hair?

Short hair can look great on any face shape, but some of the most flattering styles for different face shapes include:

  • Round faces: A layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on top can help elongate the face.
  • Square faces: A textured bob or a side-swept pixie cut can soften angular features.
  • Heart-shaped faces: A chin-length bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can balance out a wider forehead.
  • Oval faces: Almost any short hairstyle can work well with an oval face shape.

What hair textures work best with short hair?

Short hair can work well with any hair texture, but some textures may require different styling techniques. Here are some tips for different hair textures:

  • Straight hair: A blunt bob or a sleek pixie cut can enhance straight hair.
  • Wavy hair: A layered bob or a textured pixie cut can complement wavy hair.
  • Curly hair: A layered bob or a curly pixie cut can show off curly hair.
  • Thick hair: A layered bob or a textured pixie cut can help thin out thick hair.
  • Thin hair: A blunt bob or a pixie cut with volume on top can add the appearance of thickness to thin hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires different hair care than long hair. Here are some tips for taking care of short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays, styling gels, and volumizing mousses.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, to prevent heat damage.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get your hair trimmed every four to six weeks to keep it looking its best.

Overall, cutting your hair short can be a great way to change up your look and feel empowered. By using the tips and advice in this article, you can determine how you'll look with short hair, choose the right style, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
