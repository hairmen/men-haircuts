Description

Point hair cutting is a hair cutting technique that involves cutting hair at various angles to create texture, movement, and volume. It is a modern and versatile cutting technique that can be used on any hair type and length. Point cutting is used to create layers, add volume to hair, and soften blunt ends. This technique is ideal for people who want to add texture and movement to their hair without losing length.

How to

The point cutting technique involves taking small sections of hair between your fingers and cutting into the ends with scissors at an angle. The angle at which you cut determines the amount of texture and movement in the hair. For example, cutting at a steep angle will create more texture, while cutting at a shallow angle will create less texture.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow for point cutting:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair into manageable parts
  3. Take a small section of hair between your fingers
  4. Hold the scissors at an angle and cut into the ends of the hair
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have cut all the sections of hair
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when point cutting:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair
  • Start with a shallow angle and gradually increase the angle to create more texture
  • Point cut sparingly, as it can thin out the hair if overdone
  • Comb the hair between cuts to ensure evenness
  • Point cut on dry hair to see the results immediately

FAQ

Q: Can point cutting be done on any hair type?

A: Yes, point cutting can be done on any hair type and length.

Q: How often should I get a point cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and growth rate. Generally, every 6-8 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape and texture of the hair.

Q: Is point cutting the same as layering?

A: No, point cutting is a technique used to add texture and movement to hair, while layering is a technique used to remove bulk and add shape.

Hair Care

After getting a point cut, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage the hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of the hair
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy

Overall, point cutting is a versatile cutting technique that can add texture, movement, and volume to any hair type and length. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can achieve a point cut that enhances your natural beauty and style.
