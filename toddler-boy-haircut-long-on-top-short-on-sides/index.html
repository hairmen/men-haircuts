Toddler Boy Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides - The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for your little boy, then the long on top short on sides haircut is the perfect choice. This haircut has been popular for a long time and is still going strong. In this ultimate guide, we'll tell you everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to do it, the different styles you can try, and how to take care of your little one's hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

The long on top short on sides haircut is a versatile and stylish haircut that works well for toddlers. It involves keeping the hair on top of the head longer than the hair on the sides and back. This creates a bold and modern look that is perfect for any occasion. There are many variations of this haircut, including the side part, the slick back, and the messy look.

How to

If you're wondering how to do the long on top short on sides haircut, here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by washing your little one's hair and towel drying it.
  2. Use a comb to separate the top section of hair from the sides and back.
  3. Clip the sides and back hair to keep them out of the way.
  4. Trim the hair on top of the head to the desired length using scissors or clippers.
  5. Blend the top section of hair with the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style the hair on top using a hair gel, pomade, or wax.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect long on top short on sides haircut:

Step 1: Preparation

Start by washing your little one's hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. This will make the hair easier to work with and ensure that it's clean and healthy. Towel dry the hair gently.

Step 2: Sectioning

Use a comb to separate the top section of hair from the sides and back. Clip the sides and back hair to keep them out of the way.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

Trim the hair on top of the head to the desired length using scissors or clippers. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it straight across. You can also create layers or texture for a more modern look.

Step 4: Blending

Blend the top section of hair with the sides and back using scissors or clippers. Use a comb to blend the hair smoothly and gradually from the top to the sides and back.

Step 5: Styling

Style the hair on top using a hair gel, pomade, or wax. Use your fingers or a comb to create the desired look, whether it's a side part, a slick back, or a messy style.

Tips

  • Choose the right length for the top section of hair based on your little one's face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair before cutting or styling it.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors and clippers to make the process easier and safer.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your little one.

FAQ

Q: Is the long on top short on sides haircut easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this haircut is relatively easy to maintain. You'll need to trim the hair on top every few weeks to keep it looking neat, but the sides and back can be left longer to grow out naturally.

Q: Can this haircut work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work for all hair types, from straight to curly to thick. You'll just need to adjust the length and style accordingly.

Hair Care

To keep your little one's hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash the hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner every few days.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or detangler to make combing easier.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to give your little one a stylish and trendy long on top short on sides haircut that will make him the envy of all his friends. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products, and don't forget to take care of his precious hair!
