How To Pick Your Next Hair Color A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Christine Jun 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Picking a new hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what shade will suit you best. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of choosing your next hair color. From determining your skin tone to considering your lifestyle, we'll cover everything you need to know to find your perfect shade.

Determining Your Skin Tone

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new hair color is your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and certain hair colors will complement each tone differently. To determine your skin tone, examine the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If it's difficult to tell or a mix of both, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, colors that complement your skin include golden blondes, caramel browns, and rich reds. These colors will bring out the warmth in your skin and make your complexion glow. Avoid colors with blue or green undertones, as they can clash with your skin tone.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, colors that complement your skin include ash blondes, cool browns, and jewel tones like sapphire and ruby. These colors will bring out the coolness in your skin and make your complexion appear bright. Avoid colors with warm undertones, as they can clash with your skin tone.

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you have the best of both worlds! You can pull off a variety of hair colors, from warm to cool. Experiment with different shades to find what looks best on you.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Another factor to consider when choosing your next hair color is your natural hair color. This can impact how easily your hair will take to the new color and how much maintenance it will require. If you're going more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural color, it's best to visit a professional colorist to prevent any mishaps.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new hair color, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Are you someone who loves to spend time in the sun? If so, a lighter shade may be a better choice for you. Are you someone who likes to keep things low maintenance? If so, a natural-looking color may be a better fit. Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling and maintaining your hair before making a decision.

Reflect Your Personality

Your hair color can say a lot about your personality. Are you someone who likes to stand out in a crowd? If so, a bold and vibrant color like pink or purple may be the perfect choice for you. Are you someone who prefers a classic and timeless look? If so, a natural-looking color like a rich brown or golden blonde may be more your style. Think about what message you want your hair color to convey and choose accordingly.

Consider Current Hair Color Trends

If you're someone who likes to stay on top of the latest trends, it's worth considering what shades are currently popular. Balayage, rose gold, and platinum blonde are all popular choices at the moment. However, keep in mind that trends come and go quickly, and you want to choose a color that will still look good in a few months' time.

Factor in Your Budget

When choosing a new hair color, it's important to consider your budget. Some colors require more upkeep than others, which can mean more trips to the salon and more money spent. If you're on a tight budget, it may be better to choose a color that is low maintenance and won't require as much upkeep.

Consider Maintenance Requirements

As mentioned, some hair colors require more maintenance than others. If you're someone who likes to keep things simple, a natural-looking color that doesn't require frequent touch-ups may be a better choice for you. If you're willing to put in the time and effort, a bold and vibrant color can be a great way to switch things up.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip away color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs About Choosing Your Next Hair Color

What hair color is best for me?

The best hair color for you depends on your skin tone, natural hair color, lifestyle, personality, and budget. Consider all of these factors before making a decision.

Can I dye my hair at home?

You can dye your hair at home, but it's always best to visit a professional colorist if you're making a big change. They can help you choose the right color and ensure that the dye is applied correctly.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on the color you choose and how fast your hair grows. Some colors require touch-ups every four to six weeks, while others can last up to three months.

Final Thoughts

Choosing your next hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color, lifestyle, personality, and budget, you can find the perfect shade that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair with regular maintenance and use color-safe products to keep your locks looking vibrant and healthy.
