Best Short Hairstyles For Long Thin Faces

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long and thin face, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be challenging. You want a haircut that will accentuate your best features while making your face appear more balanced. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best short hairstyles for long thin faces, as well as tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great.

Description

A long and thin face can be challenging to style because you want to create the illusion of a wider face without making your hair look too bulky. The right short hairstyle can help balance out your features and create a more harmonious look.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a long and thin face, it's essential to consider the shape of your face. You want a cut that will add volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for long and thin faces. This cut features short layers that add volume and texture to your hair. It's a low-maintenance style that's perfect for those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for long and thin faces. This style features short to medium-length hair that's cut at an angle to create volume and movement. It's a versatile style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a trendy short hairstyle that works well for long and thin faces. This style features layers of different lengths that create volume and texture. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or curled depending on your mood.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect short hairstyle for a long and thin face:

Step 1: Consult with a stylist

Before getting a new haircut, it's essential to consult with a stylist. They can help you choose a cut that works well for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose a style

Once you've consulted with your stylist, it's time to choose a style. Consider your lifestyle, hair texture, and personal preferences when selecting a cut.

Step 3: Make an appointment

Once you've chosen a style, it's time to make an appointment with your stylist. They'll cut and style your hair to create the perfect look for your face shape.

Step 4: Maintain your style

To keep your short hairstyle looking great, it's essential to maintain it. Regular trims and styling will help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your short hairstyle looking great:

1. Use the right products

Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and looking great. Consult with your stylist for product recommendations.

2. Don't over-style your hair

Over-styling your hair can damage it and make it look unhealthy. Use styling products in moderation and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

3. Protect your hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors that can damage it. Wear a hat or scarf when you're outside to protect your hair from the elements.

FAQ

Q: Can short hairstyles work for long and thin faces?

A: Yes, short hairstyles can work well for long and thin faces. Choose a cut that adds volume to the sides of your face to create a more balanced look.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Q: Can I color my short hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short hair. Consult with your stylist for the best color options for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking great, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.

2. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to minimize damage.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Consult with your stylist for the best trimming schedule for your hair type.

In conclusion, a short hairstyle can work well for long and thin faces. Choose a cut that adds volume to the sides of your face to create a more balanced look. Take care of your hair by using the right products, avoiding over-styling, and getting regular trims.
