What Does A No Guard Haircut Look Like

Description

A no guard haircut is exactly what it sounds like - a haircut that is done without any guard attached to the clippers. This means that the clippers are cutting your hair down to the scalp, resulting in a very short haircut that almost looks shaved. It’s a popular style for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks clean and polished.

How to Get a No Guard Haircut

Getting a no guard haircut is fairly simple, but it does require some skill and experience on the part of the barber or stylist. If you’re interested in getting this type of haircut, it’s best to find a barber who specializes in short haircuts and has experience with clippers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a basic step-by-step guide to getting a no guard haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the length you want your hair to be cut (i.e. how close to the scalp).
  3. Use clippers without a guard attached to cut your hair down to the desired length.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back with the clippers or scissors.
  5. Finish with trimming any stray hairs with scissors.

Tips for Maintaining a No Guard Haircut

Once you have your no guard haircut, there are some tips you can follow to keep it looking its best:

  • Get regular touch-ups every 2-4 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for short hair to keep your scalp and hair healthy.
  • Consider using a styling product, like a matte pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a no guard haircut work for all hair types?

A: A no guard haircut can work for most hair types, but it may not be the best choice for those with very curly or wavy hair, as it can result in a “fuzzy” look.

Q: Is a no guard haircut appropriate for the workplace?

A: It depends on the workplace and the industry. In general, more conservative industries may not be as accepting of very short haircuts, but more creative or casual industries may allow it.

Hair Care for No Guard Haircut

Keeping your scalp and hair healthy is important for maintaining any haircut, including a no guard haircut. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for short hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a scalp oil or serum to keep your scalp moisturized and healthy.
  • If you’re going to be spending time in the sun, protect your scalp and hair with a hat or sunscreen.

Overall, a no guard haircut is a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance, clean-cut look. With the right barber, regular touch-ups, and good hair care habits, you can keep your hair looking sharp and stylish.
