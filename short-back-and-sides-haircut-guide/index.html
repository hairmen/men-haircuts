Short Back And Sides Haircut Guide Everything You Need To Know

Are you tired of your long locks or are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you look stylish and trendy? Then, a short back and sides haircut might be the perfect choice for you! In this guide, we will explore everything you need to know about this classic hairstyle, including how to get it, the different styles available, and some tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Get a Short Back and Sides Haircut

A short back and sides haircut involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head shorter than the hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it is usually longer than the sides and back.

To get a short back and sides haircut, you should visit a professional barber or hairdresser. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape and give you the best haircut to suit your features. You can also bring in a picture of the style you want to achieve to help communicate your desired look.

The barber or hairdresser will start by using clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head. They will then use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. Depending on the style you want, they may also use a razor to create texture or add layers to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before going to the salon.
  2. Communicate the style you want to achieve with your barber or hairdresser.
  3. Sit still and relax while your hair is being cut.
  4. After the haircut, ask your barber or hairdresser for styling tips and product recommendations.

Different Styles of Short Back and Sides Haircut

There are many variations of the short back and sides haircut, and the style you choose will depend on your personal preference and face shape. Some popular styles include:

  • The Classic Short Back and Sides - This style features short hair on the sides and back and longer hair on top, with a side part.
  • The Undercut - This style is similar to the classic short back and sides, but with a more dramatic difference in length between the hair on top and the sides and back. The hair on top is usually longer and styled in a slicked-back or quiff style.
  • The Textured Crop - This style features short hair on the sides and back and a choppy, textured look on top.
  • The High and Tight - This military-inspired style features very short hair on the sides and back and a buzz cut on top.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Back and Sides Haircut

Once you have your new short back and sides haircut, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking sharp and stylish. Here are some tips for keeping your haircut looking great:

  • Visit your barber or hairdresser regularly for trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as pomade or hair wax, to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.

FAQs

Q: Will a short back and sides haircut suit my face shape?

A: Yes, a short back and sides haircut can suit any face shape, but the style you choose may differ depending on your features. It's best to consult with a professional barber or hairdresser to find the right style for you.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my short back and sides haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and maintained.

Q: Can I style my short back and sides haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, there are many ways you can style your short back and sides haircut, depending on the look you want to achieve. You can experiment with different hair products and styling techniques to find the right style for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Back and Sides Haircut

Keeping your hair healthy and hydrated is essential for maintaining your short back and sides haircut. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish and trendy short back and sides haircut with confidence. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and hair products to find the perfect look for you!
