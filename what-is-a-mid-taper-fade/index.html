What Is A Mid Taper Fade

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut? Then, you might want to consider getting a mid taper fade haircut. This haircut is a combination of a taper and a fade, which creates a smooth and clean look. In this article, we will discuss what is a mid taper fade, how to get it, and tips for maintaining your haircut.

Description

A mid taper fade is a haircut that gradually fades from a longer length at the top to a shorter length at the sides and back of the head. The fade can be adjusted to your desired length, from a low fade to a high fade. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways, from a messy and textured look to a sleek and polished style. It is suitable for all hair types and face shapes.

How to

If you want to get a mid taper fade, you should visit a professional barber or hairstylist. Here are the steps to get a mid taper fade:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Ask your barber or hairstylist for a mid taper fade haircut.
  3. The barber will use clippers to gradually fade the hair from the top to the sides and back of the head.
  4. The fade can be adjusted to your desired length.
  5. The top of the hair can be styled in various ways, such as a quiff, pompadour, or slicked back.
  6. The barber will finish the haircut with a trim around the hairline and neck.

After the haircut, you can style your hair with hair products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to create your desired look.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a mid taper fade:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.
  3. Visit a professional barber or hairstylist.
  4. Show them a picture of the mid taper fade haircut you want.
  5. The barber or hairstylist will start by cutting your hair on top to your desired length.
  6. Then, they will use clippers to fade the hair from the sides and back of the head.
  7. The fade can be adjusted to your desired length, from a low fade to a high fade.
  8. The barber or hairstylist will finish the haircut with a trim around the hairline and neck.
  9. You can style your hair with hair products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your mid taper fade haircut:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 2-4 weeks for a touch-up.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to style your hair.
  • Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid taper fade:

  • Is a mid taper fade suitable for all hair types?
  • Yes, a mid taper fade is suitable for all hair types, from thick and curly to thin and straight.

  • What face shape is best for a mid taper fade?
  • A mid taper fade is suitable for all face shapes, but it can accentuate a square or angular face shape.

  • How long does a mid taper fade last?
  • A mid taper fade can last for 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

  • Can I style my hair differently with a mid taper fade?
  • Yes, a mid taper fade is versatile and can be styled in various ways, from a messy and textured look to a sleek and polished style.

Hair Care

Maintaining your mid taper fade haircut requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to style your hair.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 2-4 weeks for a touch-up.

Overall, a mid taper fade is a stylish and trendy haircut that is suitable for all hair types and face shapes. By following the steps, tips, and hair care advice in this article, you can maintain your mid taper fade haircut and look your best.
