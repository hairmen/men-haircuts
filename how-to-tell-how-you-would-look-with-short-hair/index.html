How To Tell How You Would Look With Short Hair

Written by Christine Nov 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Have you ever been curious about how you would look with short hair? Perhaps you've been considering a big chop, but you're not sure if it would suit you. In this article, we'll show you how to tell how you would look with short hair using simple techniques and tools. Read on to find out!

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell How You Would Look With Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Trying Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Tell How You Would Look With Short Hair

There are a few ways you can get an idea of how you would look with short hair. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a Virtual Makeover Tool

One of the easiest ways to see how you would look with short hair is to use a virtual makeover tool. There are many apps and websites that offer this service, and all you need to do is upload a photo of yourself and select a short hairstyle to see how it looks on you. Some popular virtual makeover tools include:

  • ModiFace
  • TAAZ
  • Makeover Studio

Using a virtual makeover tool is a quick and easy way to see how you would look with short hair, and you can try out different styles to see which ones suit you best.

2. Try on a Wig

If you're not ready to commit to cutting your hair, you can try on a wig to see how you would look with short hair. Wigs come in all different styles and colors, so you can experiment with different looks until you find one that works for you. You can buy wigs online or at a wig store, and they can be a fun and affordable way to try out a new hairstyle.

3. Use Photoshop

If you're handy with Photoshop or another photo editing software, you can use it to create a mockup of yourself with short hair. Simply upload a photo of yourself and use the editing tools to crop your hair and add a short hairstyle. This method takes a bit more time and skill, but it can be a great way to get an accurate representation of how you would look with short hair.

Step by Step Guide

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

1. Research Short Hairstyles

Before you head to the salon, take some time to research short hairstyles that you like. Look for styles that are similar to your hair type and face shape, and consider how much maintenance each style will require.

2. Find a Good Stylist

Once you have a few styles in mind, it's time to find a good stylist. Look for someone who has experience cutting short hair, and check out their portfolio to see if they have any styles that you like.

3. Communicate with Your Stylist

When you go to the salon, be sure to communicate with your stylist about the style you want. Bring in photos for reference, and be clear about how much hair you want to cut off. Your stylist can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type.

4. Take Care of Your New Haircut

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner for your hair type, and consider using styling products to enhance your new style. If you're not sure how to care for your new haircut, ask your stylist for advice.

Tips for Trying Short Hair

Trying short hair can be a big change, so here are some tips to help you make the transition:

  • Start with a slightly longer style, like a bob or lob, before going for a shorter length.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a short hairstyle.
  • Invest in good styling tools, like a hair dryer and flat iron, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find ones that work well with your hair type.
  • Be patient – it may take some time to get used to your new hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can work for many different face shapes and hair types, so it's worth trying if you're curious. Talk to your stylist about which styles would work best for you.

Will a short hairstyle be high maintenance?

The maintenance required for a short hairstyle varies depending on the style and your hair type. Talk to your stylist about how much time you're willing to spend on styling and they can recommend a style that works for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires different care than longer hair, so here are some tips to keep your new hairstyle looking great:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Use heat protectant products when using styling tools.
  • Try out different styles to keep your look fresh and fun.

Now that you know how to tell how you would look with short hair, it's time to take the plunge and try it out for yourself. Whether you opt for a virtual makeover, a wig, or a real haircut, short hair can be a fun and refreshing change. Happy styling!
