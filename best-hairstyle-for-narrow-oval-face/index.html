Best Hairstyle For Narrow Oval Face

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a narrow oval face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that complements your face shape and enhances your features. In this article, we’ll explore the best hairstyles for narrow oval faces, from short to long and everything in between.

Description

A narrow oval face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded chin and high cheekbones. This face shape is considered one of the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, some styles will flatter your face shape more than others.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a narrow oval face, you want to create balance and proportion. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face
  • Avoid styles that are too long or too short
  • Avoid styles that are too flat or too voluminous
  • Consider layering to add texture and dimension
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your hair texture and type

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle for your narrow oval face:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face
  2. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which styles will work best for your hair texture and type
  4. Consider trying out different hairstyles with virtual makeover tools or trying on wigs before committing to a new cut
  5. Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and styling products

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a narrow oval face:

  • Use volumizing products to add width to your hair
  • Avoid center parts, which can elongate your face
  • Try side-swept bangs to add balance and dimension
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add volume and texture
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat

FAQs

What is a narrow oval face?

A narrow oval face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded chin and high cheekbones.

What hairstyles are best for a narrow oval face?

The best hairstyles for a narrow oval face are those that add width and balance to your face, such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and voluminous styles.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a narrow oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, too short, too flat, or too voluminous. Also, avoid center parts and styles that are too sleek or flat.

Hair Care Tips

Keep your hair healthy and looking its best with these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid over-styling with heat tools, which can damage your hair
  • Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health of your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

Now that you know the best hairstyle for narrow oval face, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember to keep in mind your face shape and hair texture when choosing a style and maintain your hair with regular trims and care. With the right hairstyle, you’ll look and feel your best.
