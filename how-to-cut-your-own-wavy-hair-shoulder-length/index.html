How To Cut Your Own Wavy Hair Shoulder Length

Written by Eveline Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on salon visits? Or maybe you just don't have the time to go to a hairdresser every few weeks. Whatever your reason is, cutting your own wavy hair shoulder length is easier than you think! In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step.

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for those who want to maintain some length while still having the freedom to experiment with different styles. If you have wavy hair, this length can be particularly flattering as it allows your waves to flow freely.

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. It's also essential to cut your hair when it's clean and dry, as wet hair can appear longer and lead to uneven cutting.

How to Cut Your Own Wavy Hair Shoulder Length

It's important to remember that cutting your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you've never done it before. However, with a little patience and practice, you'll soon be a pro. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair in the middle and clip each side to keep it out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair at the back, comb it down, and hold it between your index and middle fingers.
  4. Trim the ends of the hair with your scissors, cutting in a straight line. Remember to make small cuts and avoid cutting large chunks of hair.
  5. Repeat this process with each section, working your way up to the crown of your head.
  6. Once you have finished the back, move to the sides. Take a small section of hair from the side, comb it down, and trim the ends.
  7. Continue this process on the other side.
  8. Check the length of the hair in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Once you're happy with the length, use your scissors to add some texture to the ends of your hair. This will give it a more natural look.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your wavy hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair than to fix a mistake.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. Remember that your hair will appear shorter when it's dry, so be cautious when cutting.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so make sure you're comfortable and take breaks when necessary.

FAQs

Can I cut my wavy hair when it's wet?

We recommend cutting your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can appear longer, and you may end up cutting more than you intended.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and health.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! Mistakes happen, and hair grows back. If you make a mistake, take a break, and come back to it later. You can also try to fix the mistake by cutting the hair around it to make it blend in.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've cut your hair, it's essential to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain its shape and health.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

Cutting your own wavy hair shoulder length can be a fun and empowering experience. With these tips and step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
