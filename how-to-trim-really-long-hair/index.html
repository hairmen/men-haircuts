How To Trim Really Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a real pain to maintain. Whether your hair is naturally long or you've been growing it out for years, trimming your hair is an essential part of keeping it healthy and looking great. But if you've never trimmed your own hair before, you may be wondering where to start.

Description

Trimming long hair may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it's actually quite straightforward. By following the steps below, you can trim your hair at home and save time and money on salon visits.

How to Trim Really Long Hair

Before you begin, you'll need a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Clips or hair ties

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles
  3. Decide on the length you want to trim your hair
  4. Divide your hair into sections, using clips or hair ties to hold them in place
  5. Start with one section and comb it straight down
  6. Hold the hair between your fingers at the length you want to cut it
  7. Using the scissors, make a straight cut across the hair
  8. Repeat steps 5-7 on all sections of your hair
  9. Check your hair in a mirror to make sure it's even
  10. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Trimming Long Hair

Trimming your own hair can be tricky, but these tips can help:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting off too much hair
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Don't cut wet hair, as it can be harder to see the length and may result in uneven cuts
  • Take breaks if your arms get tired or if you need to step back and assess your progress

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming long hair:

How often should I trim my long hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Should I trim my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be harder to cut and may result in uneven cuts.

How much hair should I cut off?

It's up to you how much you want to cut off, but it's recommended to start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting off too much hair.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your long hair healthy and looking great requires more than just trimming it regularly. Here are some tips to help you care for your long hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid damaging your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can dry out your hair
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can damage it

By following these tips and using the right techniques, you can keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
