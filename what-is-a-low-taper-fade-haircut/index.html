What Is A Low Taper Fade Haircut

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old haircut and looking for something new? A low taper fade haircut might just be the perfect choice for you! This style has become increasingly popular in recent years and can be seen on many celebrities and athletes. In this article, we will be discussing what a low taper fade haircut is, how to get it, and some tips on how to maintain it.

Description

A low taper fade haircut is a style in which the hair gradually tapers from the top to the sides and back of the head. The fade can start from very short hair at the bottom and gradually get longer as it goes up. This style is perfect for those who want a clean and sleek look without sacrificing too much length.

How to

If you are interested in getting a low taper fade haircut, it is best to go to a professional barber or hairstylist. They will be able to give you the best results and ensure that the fade is done properly. Here are the steps to getting a low taper fade:

Step 1: Decide on the length

You will first need to decide on the length of your hair. The top can be left longer or cut short depending on your preference. The sides and back will be tapered gradually.

Step 2: Choose the starting point

You will then need to decide where you want the fade to start. It can start high up on the sides and back or be a low taper fade that starts just above the ears.

Step 3: Trim the sides and back

The barber will then use clippers to trim the sides and back of the head. They will start with a longer guard and gradually switch to a shorter guard as they move up.

Step 4: Blend the fade

The barber will then use scissors or clippers to blend the fade into the top of the hair. This will create a smooth and seamless transition from the shorter hair to the longer hair.

Step 5: Style the hair

Finally, the barber will style the hair to your liking. They can use products such as pomade, gel, or wax to create a clean and polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your low taper fade:

  • Visit your barber every 2-3 weeks for touch-ups
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Consider getting a beard trim to complement your new haircut

FAQ

How long does a low taper fade last?

A low taper fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows. It is recommended to visit your barber every 2-3 weeks for touch-ups.

Can anyone get a low taper fade?

A low taper fade can work on most hair types and lengths. However, it is best to consult with your barber to see if this style will work for you.

Is a low taper fade easy to maintain?

Yes, a low taper fade is relatively easy to maintain. Regular visits to your barber and using quality hair products will help keep your hair looking great.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it is important to use quality hair care products. Here are some products that we recommend for maintaining a low taper fade:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for your hair type
  • Hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture
  • Pomade, gel, or wax for styling
  • Hair spray to hold the style in place

A low taper fade haircut is a stylish and modern look that is perfect for those who want a clean and sleek appearance. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a low taper fade that will turn heads and make you feel confident.
