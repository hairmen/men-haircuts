How To Cut Men s Long Hair Short At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to switch to a shorter look? Going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming, so why not try cutting your hair at home? With the right tools and techniques, cutting men's long hair short can be easy and stress-free. Follow this step-by-step guide for a successful haircut.

How to:

1. Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clippers, and a mirror. If you have thick hair, consider using thinning shears to thin out your hair.

2. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely before you start cutting. Wet hair can be difficult to cut as it tends to stretch, making it challenging to determine the length accurately.

3. Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut. You can use a comb to measure the length you want to cut. For example, if you want to cut your hair to two inches, place the comb at the top of your head and mark where the comb meets your hair.

4. Cut the Sides and Back

Use the hair clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach the desired length. Use the comb to blend the hair between the different guards for a seamless look.

5. Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way back, taking small sections of hair at a time. Use the comb to hold the hair straight and cut it to your desired length.

6. Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, use the scissors to trim them. Comb the bangs straight down and cut them at an angle, starting from the center and working your way outwards.

7. Check for Evenness

Check your hair for evenness. Use the mirror to view the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

8. Clean Up

After you finish cutting, clean up any loose hair around your neck and ears. Take a shower to wash off any remaining hair.

Tips:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much hair
  • Go slow and be patient, rushing can lead to mistakes
  • Use a towel around your shoulders to catch loose hair and make cleaning up easier

FAQ:

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

It is not recommended to cut your hair with regular scissors as they can be dull and uneven. Using sharp scissors designed for hair cutting will ensure a clean cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair, use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. It is also essential to use a hair product such as gel or wax to style your hair and keep it in place.

Now that you know how to cut men's long hair short at home, you can save time and money while achieving the desired look. Follow these steps and tips for a successful haircut every time.
