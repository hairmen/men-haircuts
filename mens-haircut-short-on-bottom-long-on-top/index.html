Mens Haircut Short On Bottom Long On Top A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Frank Nov 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish, modern, and trendy haircut, then the mens haircut short on bottom long on top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity among men of all ages due to its versatility and low maintenance. Whether you have straight or curly hair, this haircut will suit you perfectly. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the mens haircut short on bottom long on top.

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

The mens haircut short on bottom long on top is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back is cut short, while the hair on the top is left longer. This haircut is also called the undercut, and it's perfect for men who want to keep the length of their hair but still maintain a clean and polished look. The length on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length hair, depending on your personal preference. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd and make a bold statement.

How to

The mens haircut short on bottom long on top can be achieved easily by following these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving the mens haircut short on bottom long on top is to decide on the length of your hair. The length on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length hair, depending on your personal preference. If you're not sure what length to choose, consult with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

The next step is to cut the sides and back of your hair. Your hairstylist will use clippers or scissors to cut the hair short, leaving a sharp contrast between the top and bottom. The length on the sides and back can vary, but it's usually around 1-2 inches.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The final step is to cut the hair on top. Your hairstylist will use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. The length on top can vary, but it's usually longer than the sides and back. Your hairstylist will also style the hair on top to create the desired look.

Step by Step

If you're planning to cut your hair at home, follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

To achieve the mens haircut short on bottom long on top, you will need the following tools: clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

Decide on the length of your hair on top. You can use a comb to section off the top of your hair, leaving the sides and back exposed.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair, leaving a sharp contrast between the top and bottom. The length on the sides and back should be shorter than the length on top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure even length. Style the hair on top to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens haircut short on bottom long on top:

  • Choose the right length for your hair.
  • Consult with your hairstylist if you're not sure what length to choose.
  • Maintain the style by trimming the sides and back regularly.
  • Use hair products like wax or pomade to style the hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Is the mens haircut short on bottom long on top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all hair types, whether you have straight or curly hair.

How often do I need to trim my hair?

You should trim the sides and back every 2-3 weeks to maintain the style. The length on top can be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Can I style the hair on top differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. You can use hair products like wax or pomade to create different looks.

Hair Care

To maintain the mens haircut short on bottom long on top, you should follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair serum to keep the hair shiny and smooth.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons.
  • Trim the sides and back regularly to maintain the style.

In conclusion, the mens haircut short on bottom long on top is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for men of all ages. It's easy to maintain and can be achieved with a few simple steps. Whether you're looking to make a bold statement or just want to keep your hair looking clean and polished, this hairstyle is the perfect choice for you.
