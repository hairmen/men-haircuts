How To Cut Thick Hair Into A Bob

Written by Petter Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair weighing you down? Do you want a new look that's easy to maintain? A bob haircut might be just what you need. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to cut thick hair into a bob. From choosing the right length to styling your new 'do, we've got you covered.

How to Cut Thick Hair into a Bob

Cutting thick hair into a bob can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a beautiful result. Here's how:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your bob to be. A classic bob usually falls just below the chin, but you can also opt for a longer or shorter length depending on your preference. Keep in mind that shorter bobs require more maintenance and styling.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section at the nape of your neck. Then, divide the top section into two parts. Clip one part to the side and work on the other part first.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Using sharp hair cutting scissors, start by cutting the bottom section of your hair straight across. Then, take a small section of hair from the top section and use it as a guide to cut the rest of the hair to the same length. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair in the top section.

Once you've finished cutting the top section, unclip the other side and repeat the process.

Step 4: Add Layers (Optional)

If you want to add layers to your bob, you can do so by cutting your hair at an angle. Take a small section of hair and cut it at a diagonal angle towards your face. Repeat this process throughout your hair, making sure to blend the layers so they look natural.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair into a bob, it's time to style it. You can use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume and shape, or you can let your hair air dry for a more natural look. You can also add texture and definition with a styling product like hair wax or pomade.

Tips for Cutting Thick Hair into a Bob

Cutting thick hair into a bob can be tricky, but these tips can help:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure evenness.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or professional stylist.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting thick hair into a bob:

How often should I get my bob trimmed?

It's recommended to get your bob trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair into a bob?

If you have experience cutting hair, you can certainly try cutting your own bob. However, it's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist to ensure the best results.

Hair Care for Your Bob

Once you've cut your hair into a bob, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your bob and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut thick hair into a bob, it's time to give it a try. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and easy-to-maintain look that's perfect for any occasion.
