Best Beard Length For Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face and want to grow a beard, you may be wondering what the best beard length is for your face shape. The truth is, there isn't a one-size-fits-all answer, as the best beard length depends on several factors, including your facial features, hair growth pattern, and personal style preference. In this article, we'll explore how to find the best beard length for your round face, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your facial hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best beard length for your round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, compare them to the following face shape categories:

  • Oval: Forehead is wider than the jawline, and cheekbones are the widest part of the face.
  • Round: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, with a more angular jawline.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, with a narrower forehead and jawline.

Best Beard Length for Round Face

If you have a round face, the best beard length is typically longer on the chin and shorter on the sides. This creates the illusion of a more angular jawline, which can help balance out the roundness of your face. A goatee, soul patch, or chinstrap beard can also work well for round faces, as they draw attention to the chin area.

When it comes to beard length, aim for a length that is slightly longer than your desired final length. This allows for some trimming and shaping without going too short. If you're still unsure about the best beard length for your face shape, consult with a trusted barber or stylist for personalized advice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Once you've found the best beard length for your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your beard looking its best:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain your desired length and shape.
  • Comb your beard regularly to prevent tangles and improve its appearance.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products on your beard, as they can damage the hair and skin.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a beard if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! With the right beard length and style, anyone can rock a beard, regardless of their face shape.

Q: Can a beard make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, a well-groomed beard can create the illusion of a slimmer, more angular face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when it comes to growing and maintaining a beard. Here are some tips for keeping your facial hair healthy and strong:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to support hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your skin and hair moisturized.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair and skin.
  • Use a high-quality beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair nourished and healthy.

In conclusion, finding the best beard length for your round face is all about experimenting and finding what works best for you. Use the tips and tricks in this article to help guide you on your beard-growing journey, and remember to take care of your facial hair to keep it looking and feeling its best.
