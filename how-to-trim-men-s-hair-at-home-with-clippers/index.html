How To Trim Men s Hair At Home With Clippers

Written by Oliver Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Trimming men's hair at home with clippers can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and effectively. If you're looking to save some money and time by cutting your own hair, this guide will teach you how to do it right.

Description

Trimming men's hair at home with clippers is a great way to save money and time, especially during the pandemic when going to the barber might not be an option. It can seem intimidating at first, but with some practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. All you need are some clippers, a comb, and some basic knowledge of hair cutting techniques.

How to

To get started, you'll need to gather your tools. Invest in a good pair of clippers with adjustable blades, a comb, and a pair of scissors for any touch-ups. Next, choose a well-lit area with a mirror, and make sure you have a towel or cape to catch any loose hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the clippers don't get clogged with dirt or oil.

2. Decide on the length you want your hair to be, and choose the appropriate guard for your clippers. Attach the guard to the clippers and start with a higher setting, as you can always go shorter if needed.

3. Begin on the sides of your head, working from the bottom up. Use the clippers to cut against the direction of hair growth, starting at the base of your neck and moving upwards. Use the comb to lift the hair and guide the clippers in a straight line.

4. Move to the back of your head and repeat the same process, working from the bottom up. Make sure to trim around your ears and the nape of your neck.

5. Finally, move to the top of your head and use the scissors to trim any longer hairs that weren't caught by the clippers. Blend the sides and top together by using the clippers without a guard, or by using a shorter guard on the sides.

Tips

- Always start with a higher guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

- Use the comb to guide the clippers and create a straight line.

- Take breaks and step back from the mirror to ensure an even cut.

- If you're unsure about a certain area, err on the side of caution and go shorter slowly.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular scissors instead of clippers?

A: While it is possible to use regular scissors to trim hair, clippers are recommended for a more precise and even cut.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always go shorter if needed. If you're unsure about a certain area, it's better to go slowly and take breaks to assess the situation.

Hair Care

After trimming your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy scalp and prevent damage. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like a blow dryer or straightener too often. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks will also help keep your hair looking its best.

Trimming men's hair at home with clippers may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and effectively. With practice, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
