What Number Cuts Less Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who regularly cuts their own hair or someone else's, you might be wondering what number cuts less hair. The truth is, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. However, there are a few things you can keep in mind to help you choose the right number for your needs. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about cutting hair and what number to use.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Cut Hair
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Tips for Cutting Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

When it comes to cutting hair, the number you choose will determine the length of the hair that is left behind. The lower the number, the shorter the hair will be. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. Generally, numbers 1-4 are used for buzz cuts or very short hair, while numbers 5-8 are used for longer hair. However, the length of the hair that is left behind also depends on the type of clipper you are using and the angle at which you hold it.

How To Cut Hair

Cutting hair can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some basic steps to follow:

  1. Wash and comb the hair.
  2. Choose the right number for the desired length.
  3. Start at the back of the head and work your way up.
  4. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it with the clippers.
  5. Work your way around the head until all the hair is trimmed.
  6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to add texture to the hair.
  7. Style the hair as desired.

Step by Step Instructions

Here is a more detailed guide to cutting hair:

  1. Start by washing and combing the hair. Wet hair is easier to work with and will give you a more even cut.
  2. Choose the right number for the desired length. Remember, the lower the number, the shorter the hair will be.
  3. Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Use the clippers to trim the hair in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth.
  4. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it with the clippers. Work in sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  5. Work your way around the head until all the hair is trimmed. Be sure to check for any uneven spots or missed areas.
  6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to add texture to the hair. Use your fingers to pull the hair up and trim it with the scissors.
  7. Style the hair as desired using a hair product like gel or mousse.

Tips for Cutting Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting hair:

  • Start with a longer number and work your way down if needed.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.
  • Don't press too hard with the clippers, as this can cause uneven cutting.
  • Trim slowly and carefully, checking for any missed spots as you go.
  • Use scissors to add texture or to trim any stray hairs.

FAQs

What number should I use for a buzz cut?

For a traditional buzz cut, you will want to use a number 1 or 2. However, the length of the hair can be adjusted based on personal preference.

What number should I use for a fade?

For a fade, you will want to use a combination of numbers, starting with a higher number at the top of the head and gradually working your way down to a lower number at the bottom.

Can I cut hair without clippers?

Yes, you can cut hair with scissors, but it may be more difficult to achieve an even cut. Clippers are recommended for beginners.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and to maintain the shape of the haircut.

By following these tips and using the right number when cutting hair, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
