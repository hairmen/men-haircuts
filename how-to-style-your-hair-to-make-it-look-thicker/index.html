How To Style Your Hair To Make It Look Thicker

Written by Bardi Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick and voluminous hair is a dream for many. But not everyone is blessed with naturally thick hair. If you have thin or fine hair, there are ways to make it look thicker and fuller. In this article, we will guide you on how to style your hair to make it look thicker.

Description

Thick hair not only looks beautiful but also adds a certain level of confidence to your overall appearance. If you have thin hair, don't worry! With the right haircuts, styling techniques, and products, you can create an illusion of thicker hair. You don't have to spend a fortune on hair extensions or hair implants. Follow these simple steps to make your hair look fuller and thicker.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a huge difference in how thick your hair appears. Avoid long haircuts as they make your hair appear flat and lifeless. Instead, opt for shorter haircuts like a bob or a lob. These haircuts create an illusion of thickness and volume. Layers are also a great option for thin hair, as they add movement and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is crucial for making your hair look thicker. Invest in volumizing shampoos and conditioners that add volume and texture to your hair. Use a root-lifting spray or mousse to add volume to the roots of your hair. Avoid heavy products like oils and serums as they weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a simple yet effective technique to add volume to your hair. Flip your hair upside down and blow dry it using a round brush to create volume at the roots. Once your hair is dry, flip it back and use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume to the ends of your hair.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is a classic technique to create volume and texture. Use a teasing comb to backcomb the roots of your hair, and then gently smooth the top layer. This creates a cushion of hair at the roots, making your hair appear thicker and fuller.

Tips

- Avoid using a brush on wet hair as it can cause breakage and damage.
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
- Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons on a daily basis as they can damage your hair.
- Apply a volumizing mousse before blow-drying your hair to add volume and texture.
- Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my hair look thicker without using products?

A: Yes, you can make your hair look thicker without using products. Blow drying your hair upside down and teasing your hair are simple yet effective techniques to create volume and texture.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair extensions are a great option to make your hair look thicker. However, they can be expensive and require regular maintenance.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, if you have dry hair, washing it every other day or twice a week may be sufficient.

Hair Care

Healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as they can damage your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Follow these simple hair care tips to maintain healthy and thick hair.
