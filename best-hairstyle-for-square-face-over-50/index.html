Description

As we age, our faces change and so do our hairstyles. If you have a square face and are over 50, it can be tough to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your age. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin. The key to finding the best hairstyle is to soften the jawline and balance out the wide forehead. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyle for square face over 50.

How to

The best hairstyle for square face over 50 is a layered pixie cut. This hairstyle is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the head with layers on top. The layers add volume to the hair and soften the jawline, making it a great option for square faces. The pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for any occasion.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product.
3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting at the roots to add volume.
4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, if desired.
5. Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to your hair to add texture and definition.
6. Use your fingers to style your hair, creating a messy and tousled look.
7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

1. If you have thin hair, ask your hairstylist to add layers to create the illusion of volume.
2. If you have thick hair, ask your hairstylist to thin out your hair to prevent it from looking too bulky.
3. Add highlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
4. Consider adding bangs to your pixie cut to further soften your face.
5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
6. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q. Is a pixie cut suitable for all ages?
A. Yes, a pixie cut can be tailored to suit any age group, including women over 50.

Q. How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?
A. You should get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Q. Can I style my pixie cut differently for different occasions?
A. Yes, a pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways to suit any occasion.

Hair Care

1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
2. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat-styling tools.
3. Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down.
4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
5. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
6. Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
