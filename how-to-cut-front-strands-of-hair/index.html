How To Cut Front Strands Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of always having to go to the salon to trim your front strands of hair? With the right tools and techniques, you can easily do it yourself at home. In this article, we'll show you how to cut front strands of hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How-To
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The front strands of hair, also known as the bangs or fringe, can frame the face and give it a more polished look. However, these strands can become unruly and overgrown, making it necessary to trim them regularly. Cutting front strands of hair can be tricky, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a salon-worthy look at home.

How-To

To cut your front strands of hair, you'll need a few basic tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or bobby pins
  • Spray bottle
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to cut your front strands of hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair, or dampen it with a spray bottle if it's already dry.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or to one side, depending on your preference.
  3. Comb your front strands of hair forward and clip the rest of your hair back to keep it out of the way.
  4. Hold the ends of your front strands of hair with your fingers and cut them straight across, just below your eyebrows.
  5. Release your hair and check the length. If it's too long, trim a little more off. If it's too short, you can wait for it to grow out or try to blend it in with the rest of your hair.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side.
  7. Blend the two sides together by combing your front strands of hair down and cutting any stray hairs with your scissors.
  8. Check the length again and make any final adjustments.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make your front strands of hair cutting experience even better:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Don't cut your front strands of hair too short, as they will appear shorter when dried and styled.
  • Trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, start with small trims and work your way up.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my front strands of hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the length. On average, every 6-8 weeks is a good time frame for a trim.

Q: Can I cut my front strands of hair myself?

A: Yes! With the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your front strands of hair at home.

Q: Should I cut my front strands of hair when they're wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care

To keep your front strands of hair looking their best, be sure to nourish them with a healthy diet and regular hair care routine. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Cutting your front strands of hair can be a simple and cost-effective way to maintain your hairstyle. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look without ever having to leave your home!
