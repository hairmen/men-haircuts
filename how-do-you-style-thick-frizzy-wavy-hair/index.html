How To Style Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Christine Nov 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of struggling to style your thick frizzy wavy hair? Do you feel like your hair is unmanageable and hard to control? Fear not, because we have some tips, tricks, and hair care methods that will help you achieve the perfect look for your hair type.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we jump into styling, it's important to understand that taking care of your hair is essential for achieving the perfect look. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy thick frizzy wavy hair:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is crucial for keeping your hair healthy. Look for products that are specifically designed for thick, frizzy, and wavy hair. These products will help to tame your hair and keep it looking silky and smooth.

2. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to limit washing your hair to 2-3 times a week and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

3. Use a Deep Conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week can help to nourish and hydrate your hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, and shea butter.

How to Style

Now that you know how to take care of your hair, let's dive into how to style it. Here are some tips for styling thick frizzy wavy hair:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Instead of fighting your hair's natural texture, embrace it! Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your waves and add volume.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

When using heat tools like flat irons or curling wands, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair before using any heat tools.

3. Opt for Low-Maintenance Styles

Low-maintenance styles like messy buns, braids, and ponytails are perfect for thick frizzy wavy hair. These styles are quick and easy to do and can be done on both clean and second-day hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling your thick frizzy wavy hair:

1. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help detangle and soften your hair.

2. Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Scrunch your hair as you dry to enhance your waves.

3. Use a Curling Wand

Use a curling wand to define any loose curls or waves. Hold the wand vertically and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel. Hold for 5-10 seconds and release.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick frizzy wavy hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. Brushes can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz and disrupt your natural wave pattern. Try to avoid running your fingers through your hair too often.

3. Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my thick frizzy wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid straightening your hair too often to prevent damage.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my hair?

A: Using a deep conditioner, avoiding over-washing, and sleeping on a satin pillowcase can all help to reduce frizz in your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, tricks, and hair care methods, you can finally achieve the perfect look for your thick frizzy wavy hair. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
