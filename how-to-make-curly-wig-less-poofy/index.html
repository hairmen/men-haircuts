How To Make Curly Wig Less Poofy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you've ever worn a curly wig, you know that they can sometimes have a mind of their own. One of the most common issues with curly wigs is that they can become poofy and frizzy, making them difficult to style and manage. Fortunately, there are some simple steps you can take to tame your curly wig and make it look more natural and polished. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for making your curly wig less poofy, so you can enjoy a more natural and stylish look.

How to Make Curly Wig Less Poofy

To make your curly wig less poofy, you'll need to take a few key steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Wig

The first step in taming your curly wig is to wash and condition it properly. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to cleanse the wig, and then apply a lightweight conditioner to help detangle and smooth the hair. Rinse thoroughly and gently pat the wig dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing and conditioning your wig, apply a leave-in conditioner to help hydrate and soften the hair. Look for products that are designed specifically for curly hair, as they will help define and enhance the natural texture of your wig.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

To prevent your wig from becoming poofy, it's important to use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently separate the hair. Comb through the wig in small sections, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Avoid using a regular brush, as this can cause the hair to become frizzy and poofy.

Step 4: Apply a Curl-Defining Product

To help define and enhance the natural curls in your wig, apply a curl-defining product to the hair. Look for products that are formulated specifically for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, or mousses. Apply the product evenly throughout the wig, scrunching the hair as you go to help define the curls.

Step 5: Air Dry or Diffuse

Once you've applied your curl-defining product, you can either air dry your wig or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry the hair. If you choose to air dry, avoid touching or scrunching the hair until it's completely dry, as this can cause the hair to become poofy. If you use a diffuser, use a low heat setting and gently scrunch the hair as you dry it to help enhance the curls.

Tips for Taming a Poofy Wig

In addition to following the steps above, there are some additional tips you can use to help tame a poofy wig:

  • Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, on your wig, as these can damage the hair and cause it to become poofy.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase when sleeping in your wig, as this will help prevent frizz and poofiness.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape and prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down the hair and make it look poofy.

FAQ

How often should I wash my curly wig?

It's recommended that you wash your curly wig every 2-4 weeks, depending on how often you wear it and how much product you use.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly wig?

It's best to use products that are designed specifically for wigs, as these are formulated to be gentle and won't damage the hair. However, if you don't have wig-specific products, you can use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and a lightweight conditioner.

How can I prevent my wig from becoming tangled?

To prevent tangling, store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when you're not wearing it, and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down the hair. You can also gently comb through the wig with a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush before and after wearing it to help prevent tangles.

Hair Care for Curly Wigs

To keep your curly wig looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when you're not wearing it to help maintain its shape and prevent tangles.
  • Wash and condition your wig regularly to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Use products that are designed specifically for curly hair to help enhance and define the natural texture of your wig.
  • Avoid using heat tools or heavy styling products, as these can damage the hair and cause it to become poofy.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your wig, and use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to prevent tangles.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your curly wig less poofy and enjoy a more natural and stylish look. With a little bit of care and attention, your wig can look beautiful and vibrant for years to come.
