How To Cut Your Hair At Home Youtube Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your hair at home can be a daunting task, but with the help of a good YouTube video and some patience, it can be done. Not only can it save you money, but it can also be a fun and creative way to experiment with different styles. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your hair at home using a YouTube video.

Description

Before we dive into the guide, it is important to note that cutting your hair at home can be risky. It is always best to consult with a professional hairstylist if you are unsure about anything. However, if you are confident and have the right tools, cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and try out new styles.

How to

The first step in cutting your hair at home is to find a YouTube video that you trust. It is important to choose a video that is clear, concise, and easy to follow. Make sure to watch the video a few times before you start cutting your hair to ensure that you understand the process.

Once you have found a video that you like, gather all of the necessary tools. This includes a pair of sharp scissors, hair clippers (if needed), a comb, and hair ties. It is also important to have a mirror and good lighting.

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure to wash and dry it thoroughly. This will help to ensure that your hair is clean and easy to work with. It is also a good idea to wear old clothes or a cape to protect your clothes from any loose hair.

Step-by-Step

Now that you have everything you need, it is time to start cutting your hair. Follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Section your hair

Use a comb to separate your hair into sections. This will help you to cut your hair evenly and prevent any mistakes. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up.

Step 2: Cut the length

Using your scissors, cut the length of your hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut small sections at a time and check the length after each cut.

Step 3: Blend the layers

Once you have cut the length, use your scissors to blend the layers. This will help to create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 4: Trim the bangs

If you have bangs, use your scissors to trim them to the desired length. Make sure to cut them slowly and check the length after each cut.

Step 5: Use clippers (optional)

If you have clippers, you can use them to create a more even cut. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest guard. Make sure to blend the cut with your scissors to create a natural look.

Step 6: Final touches

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a comb to check for any uneven sections. Make any necessary adjustments until you are happy with the final result.
