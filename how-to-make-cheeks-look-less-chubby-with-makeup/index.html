How To Make Cheeks Look Less Chubby With Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you have chubby cheeks that you want to slim down? You're not alone! Many people struggle with this issue, but luckily, there are makeup techniques you can use to make your cheeks look less chubby. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to achieve a more sculpted look using just makeup.

How to Make Cheeks Look Less Chubby with Makeup

When it comes to making your cheeks look less chubby, there are a few makeup techniques you can use. These include:

  • Contouring
  • Blush Placement
  • Highlighting

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using a darker shade of foundation or contouring powder to create the illusion of shadows on your face. This technique can help to make your cheeks look less chubby by creating the appearance of more defined cheekbones.

To contour your cheeks, start by applying foundation or tinted moisturizer to your entire face. Then, use a contouring brush to apply a darker shade of foundation or contouring powder just below your cheekbones. Blend the product well to ensure a natural-looking finish. You can also apply contouring powder to the temples and jawline for a more sculpted look.

Blush Placement

Blush placement is another makeup technique that can help to make your cheeks look less chubby. Instead of applying blush to the apples of your cheeks, apply it slightly higher up, just below your cheekbones. This will help to create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and a slimmer face.

When choosing a blush color, opt for a shade that complements your skin tone. If you have fair skin, go for a light pink or peach shade. If you have medium or dark skin, opt for a deeper shade of pink or coral.

Highlighting

Highlighting is a makeup technique that involves using a light-reflecting product to draw attention to certain areas of your face. By highlighting the tops of your cheekbones, you can make your cheeks look less chubby and more defined.

To highlight your cheekbones, use a highlighter brush to apply a light-reflecting powder or cream just above your contouring powder. Be sure to blend the product well to ensure a natural-looking finish.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Make Cheeks Look Less Chubby with Makeup

Follow these steps to achieve a more sculpted look using makeup:

  1. Apply foundation or tinted moisturizer to your entire face.
  2. Use a contouring brush to apply a darker shade of foundation or contouring powder just below your cheekbones.
  3. Blend the product well to ensure a natural-looking finish.
  4. Apply blush slightly higher up, just below your cheekbones.
  5. Opt for a shade that complements your skin tone.
  6. Use a highlighter brush to apply a light-reflecting powder or cream just above your contouring powder.
  7. Blend the product well to ensure a natural-looking finish.
  8. Apply mascara and lipstick to complete your makeup look.

Tips for Making Cheeks Look Less Chubby with Makeup

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a more sculpted look using makeup:

  • Choose a contouring powder or foundation that is only one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone to avoid creating an unnatural-looking finish.
  • Be sure to blend your contouring powder or foundation well to avoid any harsh lines or patches.
  • Use a highlighter that complements your skin tone to avoid looking too shiny or glittery.
  • Experiment with different blush colors and textures to find the perfect shade for your skin tone and type.

FAQ: How to Make Cheeks Look Less Chubby with Makeup

Q: Can I use bronzer instead of contouring powder?

A: Yes, bronzer can be used to achieve a similar effect as contouring powder. Just be sure to choose a shade that is only one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone to avoid looking too orange or unnatural.

Q: How do I know where to apply blush?

A: To find the perfect spot for blush, smile and apply the product to the apples of your cheeks. Then, blend the product upwards towards your temples for a natural-looking finish.

Q: Do I need to use all three makeup techniques to make my cheeks look less chubby?

A: No, you can use one or all of the techniques depending on your personal preference and the look you're trying to achieve. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect makeup routine for your face shape and skin tone.

Hair Care Tips for a Slimmer Looking Face

In addition to using makeup techniques to make your cheeks look less chubby, there are also hair care tips you can follow to achieve a slimmer-looking face. These include:

  • Opting for a hairstyle with layers and volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.
  • Avoiding hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as these can make your face look wider and more rounded.
  • Keeping your hair off your face with a sleek ponytail or updo to draw attention to your cheekbones and jawline.

By following these tips, you can achieve a more sculpted look and feel more confident in your appearance. Remember to experiment with different makeup techniques and hairstyles to find what works best for you.
