Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces With Small Foreheads

Heart shaped faces with small foreheads can be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces with small foreheads. We'll also be providing tips on how to style your hair and take care of it to ensure it looks healthy and shiny.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces with small foreheads, let's first determine if you have a heart shaped face. Here's how:

  1. Take a measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  2. Compare the measurements to determine which part of your face is the widest.
  3. If your forehead is the widest part of your face and your jawline is pointed, then you have a heart shaped face.

Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces with Small Foreheads

Now that you know you have a heart shaped face with a small forehead, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for you:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a great option for heart shaped faces with small foreheads. It's short and sweet, and it draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Plus, it's easy to maintain and style.

2. Side Swept Bangs

If you want to keep your hair long, then side swept bangs are the way to go. They'll help balance out your forehead and draw attention away from it. Plus, they're versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for heart shaped faces with small foreheads. It's a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Plus, it's easy to maintain and style.

4. Layered Cut

If you want to add some volume and texture to your hair, then a layered cut is the way to go. It'll help balance out your forehead and draw attention away from it. Plus, it's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways.

Tips on Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces with small foreheads, here are some tips on how to style your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a round brush to add curls and texture to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face with a small forehead?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or too voluminous. Stick to hairstyles that are balanced and draw attention away from your forehead.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a heart shaped face with a small forehead?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to leave some hair out to frame your face and balance out your forehead.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces with Small Foreheads

Lastly, here are some tips on how to take care of your hair to ensure it looks healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you'll be able to rock your heart shaped face with a small forehead confidently. Remember to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best!
