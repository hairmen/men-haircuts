Does Pixie Cut Make You Look Younger The Truth Revealed

Written by Bardi Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a new hairstyle, you may have wondered, "Does a pixie cut make you look younger?" Pixie cuts have been popular for decades, and many women love the edgy, low-maintenance look. But does it actually make you look younger? In this article, we'll explore the truth behind this common myth and provide tips for pulling off a pixie cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Pulling Off a Pixie Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Pixie Cuts
  • Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

How to Get a Pixie Cut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a pixie cut, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts. Look for someone with plenty of experience and a portfolio of pixie cuts they've done in the past.

When you're at the salon, be prepared to discuss your desired length, texture, and styling options. Your stylist will likely ask you questions about your lifestyle and hair care routine to help determine the best cut and style for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is relatively simple, but it does require a bit of practice and patience. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your new cut:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add texture and hold.
  3. Using a blow dryer and a round brush, style your hair in the direction you want it to go. For a tousled look, use your fingers to add texture and volume.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Pulling Off a Pixie Cut

If you want to look younger with a pixie cut, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a cut that flatters your face shape and features. A skilled stylist can help you find the perfect cut for you.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Accessorize with bold earrings or statement necklaces to draw attention to your face.
  • Embrace your natural texture and don't be afraid to play with different styles and looks.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pixie Cuts

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look younger?

A: While a pixie cut can be youthful and edgy, it's not a guarantee that you'll look younger. The key is to find a cut and style that flatters your features and complements your personal style.

Q: Will a pixie cut work for my face shape?

A: Pixie cuts can work for a variety of face shapes, but it's important to work with a skilled stylist to find the right cut for you. A good stylist will take into account your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences.

Q: How often will I need to get a trim with a pixie cut?

A: Pixie cuts require frequent trims to maintain their shape and style. Depending on how quickly your hair grows, you may need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Short haircuts like pixie cuts require a bit of extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help detangle and style your hair.

In conclusion, while a pixie cut can be a fun and edgy hairstyle, it's not a guarantee that you'll look younger. The key is to find a cut and style that flatters your features and complements your personal style. With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock a pixie cut at any age!
