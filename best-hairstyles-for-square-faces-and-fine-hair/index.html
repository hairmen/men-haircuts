Best Hairstyles For Square Faces And Fine Hair

Written by Bardi Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your square face and fine hair? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for square faces and fine hair, as well as hair care tips and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Square faces have a strong jawline and angular features. The key to finding the best hairstyle for a square face is to soften the jawline and add volume to the crown. Fine hair, on the other hand, lacks volume and can be difficult to style. It is important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to fine hair.

How to

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for square faces and fine hair is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine the shape of your face. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face.

Next, consider the texture of your hair. Fine hair can be thin and lack volume, making it difficult to hold a hairstyle. It is important to choose a hairstyle that adds texture and volume to fine hair.

Step by step

1. Layers: Adding layers to fine hair can add volume and texture. Ask your hairstylist to add long layers to your hair to create movement and volume.

2. Pixie Cut: A pixie cut is a great option for square faces with fine hair. The short length creates the illusion of volume, and the angled layers soften the jawline.

3. Bob: A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well on square faces with fine hair. Ask your hairstylist for a chin-length bob with layers to add volume and texture.

4. Side Swept Bangs: Side swept bangs can soften the angular features of a square face and add volume to fine hair. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs at an angle to create a flattering shape.

Tips

- Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lift spray, to add volume to fine hair.

- Avoid heavy products, such as oils or creams, that can weigh down fine hair.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and texture in fine hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a square face and fine hair?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a square face and fine hair. However, it is recommended to add layers or texture to create volume and movement.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a square face and fine hair?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a square face and fine hair. However, it is recommended to add volume and texture to the crown of your head to balance out the angular features of your face.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and voluminous hair, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for fine hair. Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, on a daily basis. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Lastly, get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that flatter square faces with fine hair. Adding layers, opting for a pixie cut or a bob, and adding side swept bangs are great options. Remember to follow a proper hair care routine to maintain healthy and voluminous hair.
