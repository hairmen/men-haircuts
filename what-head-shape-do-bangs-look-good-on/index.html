What Head Shape Do Bangs Look Good On

Written by Petter Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Adding bangs to your hairstyle can give you a fresh and youthful look. However, not everyone can pull off bangs. The key to rocking bangs is to choose the right style that suits your face shape. In this article, we'll discuss what head shape do bangs look good on to help you make the right decision for your next haircut.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Description

Bangs are a section of hair that falls over the forehead. They can be styled in different ways and lengths, depending on your preference. However, not all bangs styles are suitable for every face shape. The right bangs style can enhance your facial features and complement your head shape, while the wrong style can make your face look wider or longer than it actually is.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right bangs style is to identify your face shape. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a specific bangs style.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer. Avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead, as they can make your face look wider.

Oval Face Shape

Oval face shapes are considered the most versatile, as they can pull off almost any bangs style. If you have an oval face shape, you can choose from blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or wispy bangs.

Square Face Shape

Square face shapes have a strong jawline and forehead. To soften your features, choose bangs that are side-swept or wispy. Avoid blunt bangs or straight-across bangs, as they can make your face look boxy.

Heart Face Shape

Heart face shapes have a wider forehead and a narrow chin. To balance out your features, choose bangs that are side-swept or wispy. Avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond face shapes have a narrow forehead and chin, with wider cheekbones. To soften your features, choose bangs that are side-swept or wispy. Avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead, as they can make your forehead appear even narrower.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs

If you're considering cutting your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Use a comb to separate the section into the desired length.
  4. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across.
  5. Take small sections and cut them at an angle to create a wispy effect.
  6. Blend the bangs with the rest of your hair using scissors or a razor.
  7. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some tips to help you maintain your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as the oils from your hands can make them greasy.

FAQs

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help prevent your bangs from curling up too much. Avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead, as they can be difficult to style with curly hair.

What if I have a small forehead?

If you have a small forehead, choose bangs that are side-swept or wispy. Avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead, as they can make your forehead appear even smaller.

What if I have a large forehead?

If you have a large forehead, choose bangs that are side-swept or wispy. Avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead, as they can make your forehead appear even larger.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful bangs:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, choosing the right bangs style for your head shape can make a big difference in your overall appearance. By following these tips and guidelines, you'll be able to find the perfect bangs for your face shape and style them with confidence.
