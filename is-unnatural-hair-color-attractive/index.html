Is Unnatural Hair Color Attractive

Written by Luffy Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to experiment with their hair, then you've probably considered dyeing your hair an unnatural color at some point. Whether it's a neon pink, electric blue or a fiery red, unnatural hair colors are becoming more and more popular by the day. But the question remains, is unnatural hair color attractive?

Description

Unnatural hair colors have been around for a long time, but they have gained immense popularity in recent years. With celebrities like Kylie Jenner, Billie Eilish, and Halsey sporting vibrant hair colors, it's no surprise that more and more people are opting for these bold hair colors.

While some people might shy away from unnatural hair colors, others embrace them with open arms. The idea of standing out in a crowd and expressing oneself through hair color is a trend that has taken the world by storm. But is it attractive?

How to Dye Your Hair an Unnatural Color

If you're planning on dyeing your hair an unnatural color, there are a few things that you need to keep in mind. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to dye your hair an unnatural color:

Step 1: Choose Your Color

The first step in dyeing your hair an unnatural color is to choose the color you want. There are plenty of shades to choose from, so take your time and pick the one that speaks to you the most.

Step 2: Bleach Your Hair

Most unnatural hair colors require bleached hair for the color to show up. If you have darker hair, you'll need to bleach it before dyeing it. If you're not confident in bleaching your own hair, it's best to visit a salon.

Step 3: Apply the Dye

Once your hair is bleached, it's time to apply the dye. Follow the instructions on the dye to ensure that you get the best results.

Step 4: Wait and Rinse

After applying the dye, you'll need to wait for a certain amount of time before rinsing it out. Follow the instructions on the dye to know how long you need to wait. Once the time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly.

Tips for Maintaining Unnatural Hair Color

Once you've dyed your hair an unnatural color, it's important to take care of it to ensure that the color lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip the color from your hair

FAQs

Q. Will Unnatural Hair Colors Damage My Hair?

A. Bleaching your hair can cause some damage, but if you take care of your hair properly, the damage can be minimized.

Q. How Long Will My Unnatural Hair Color Last?

A. The duration of the color depends on several factors such as the color you choose, the condition of your hair, and how well you take care of it. Generally, unnatural colors last for 4-6 weeks before they start to fade.

Q. Do I Need to Bleach My Hair Before Dyeing It?

A. If you have dark hair, you'll need to bleach it before dyeing it an unnatural color. If you have light hair, you might be able to skip the bleaching step.

Hair Care for Unnatural Hair Color

Unnatural hair colors require extra care to maintain their vibrancy. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair
  • Use a color-safe hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and vibrant

So, is unnatural hair color attractive? The answer is subjective. Some people find it attractive, while others might not. At the end of the day, what matters most is how you feel about it. If you love the way you look with an unnatural hair color, then go for it!
