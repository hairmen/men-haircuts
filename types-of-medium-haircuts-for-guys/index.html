Types Of Medium Haircuts For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Medium haircuts for guys have been trending for a while now, and for a good reason. Not only do they offer versatility, but they can also work for any hair type or face shape. In this article, we'll go through the different types of medium haircuts for guys, how to achieve them, and some tips and tricks to maintain them. So, let's get started!

The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that has been around since the 1950s. It's a great option for medium hair because it adds volume and height to the hair. To achieve this look, you'll need to have longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides. Start by applying some pomade or wax to your hair, then blow-dry your hair upwards and backwards using a round brush. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a pompadour shape by pushing your hair forward and up. Finish off with some hairspray to keep your hair in place.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for guys who don't want to spend too much time styling their hair. It's also a great option for guys with fine or thinning hair because it adds texture and volume. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short crop on top and a fade on the sides. Use some texturizing spray or salt spray to add some grit to your hair, then use your fingers to style your hair into a messy, textured look.

The Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works for any occasion, from the office to a night out. It's a great option for guys with thicker hair because it adds structure and definition to the hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a medium-length cut on top and a taper on the sides. Use a comb to create a deep side part, then apply some pomade or wax to your hair to keep it in place.

The Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a popular haircut that's perfect for guys who want a relaxed, effortless look. It's a great option for guys with wavy or curly hair because it adds texture and movement. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a longer cut on top and a taper on the sides. Use some sea salt spray or mousse to add some texture to your hair, then use your fingers to style your hair into a messy, tousled look.

The Fade

The fade is a versatile haircut that works for any hair type or face shape. It's a great option for guys who want a clean, modern look. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a high, mid, or low fade on the sides and back, and a longer cut on top. You can style your hair in any way you like, from a pompadour to a messy fringe.

The Quiff

The quiff is a stylish haircut that's perfect for guys who want a retro-inspired look. It's a great option for guys with thick hair because it adds height and volume to the hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a longer cut on top and a taper on the sides. Use some pomade or wax to create a quiff shape, then use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into place.

The Slick Back

The slick back is a classic haircut that's perfect for guys who want a sophisticated, polished look. It's a great option for guys with straight or wavy hair because it adds shine and definition to the hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a longer cut on top and a taper on the sides. Apply some pomade or gel to your hair, then use a comb to slick your hair back and create a smooth, sleek look.

The Layered Cut

The layered cut is a great option for guys who want a textured, layered look. It's a great option for guys with thick or curly hair because it adds movement and dimension to the hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a layered cut on top and a taper on the sides. Use some texturizing spray or mousse to add some texture to your hair, then use your fingers to style your hair into a messy, tousled look.

The Shag

The shag is a retro-inspired haircut that's perfect for guys who want a laid-back, effortless look. It's a great option for guys with longer hair because it adds texture and movement to the hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a shag cut, which involves layers and choppy ends. Use some texturizing spray or mousse to add some texture to your hair, then use your fingers to style your hair into a messy, shaggy look.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Haircuts for Guys

Now that you know the different types of medium haircuts for guys, it's important to maintain your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage

FAQ

What face shapes work best with medium haircuts for guys?

Medium haircuts for guys work well for any face shape, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features. For example, a pompadour or quiff can add height to a round face, while a side part can add structure to a square face.

What hair types work best with medium haircuts for guys?

Medium haircuts for guys can work for any hair type, from straight to curly. However, certain styles may work better for certain hair types. For example, a messy fringe can add movement to wavy or curly hair, while a slick back can add shine to straight hair.

How do I choose the right medium haircut for me?

The best way to choose the right medium haircut for you is to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. You can also consult with your barber or hairstylist for recommendations on which styles would work best for you.

That wraps up our guide to the different types of medium haircuts for guys. Whether you prefer a classic, polished look or a relaxed, messy style, there's a medium haircut out there for you. Just remember to take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly and using heat styling tools sparingly. Happy styling!
