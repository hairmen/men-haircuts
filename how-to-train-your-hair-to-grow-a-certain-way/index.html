How To Train Your Hair To Grow A Certain Way Tips And Tricks

If you're looking to change your hairstyle and want to train your hair to grow in a certain way, it's important to know the right techniques. In this blog post, we'll share tips and tricks on how to train your hair to grow in a certain way. We'll also cover common questions about hair training and hair care. Let's get started.

How to Train Your Hair to Grow a Certain Way

Whether you want to grow out your bangs, create a side part, or switch up your hair parting, there are a few key steps to follow when training your hair to grow in a certain way. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start training your hair, make sure to wash it thoroughly. This will help remove any product buildup or dirt that could interfere with the hair training process. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.

Step 2: Choose Your Parting

Decide where you want your hair to part. Use a comb to create a clean, straight part down the middle or on the side of your head.

Step 3: Train Your Hair

Now it's time to train your hair. Use a comb or brush to gently guide your hair in the direction you want it to grow. If you're trying to grow out bangs, for example, use a small clip to hold them to the side while they grow out. Repeat this process every day until your hair starts to naturally fall in the desired direction.


Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Training

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to training your hair to grow in a certain way:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Damp Hair

Wash and condition your hair as usual. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Choose Your Parting

Decide where you want your hair to part. Use a comb to create a clean, straight part down the middle or on the side of your head.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold it in place. You can use a light gel or mousse, or a hair spray that provides a flexible hold.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while brushing it in the direction you want it to grow. For example, if you want to create a side part, brush your hair to one side while blow-drying. Use a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 5: Set Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a clip or bobby pin to hold it in place. Leave the clip or pin in for a few minutes to set your hair in the desired direction. Repeat this process every day until your hair starts to naturally fall in the desired direction.


Tips for Successful Hair Training

Here are some additional tips for successful hair training:

  • Be patient. It can take several weeks or even months for your hair to start growing in the desired direction.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to train.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.


Frequently Asked Questions

Can you train your hair to grow in a certain direction?

Yes, with the right techniques, you can train your hair to grow in a certain direction. It takes time and patience, but it is possible.

How long does it take to train your hair?

It can take several weeks or even months for your hair to start growing in the desired direction. Be patient and consistent with your hair training routine.

Can hair training damage your hair?

When done correctly, hair training should not cause damage to your hair. However, using excessive heat or pulling your hair too tightly can cause breakage and damage.


Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair Growth

Finally, here are some hair care tips to support healthy hair growth:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can train your hair to grow in a certain way and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent, and always take good care of your hair.
