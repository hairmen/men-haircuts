10 Trendy Hairstyles For Long Hair In 2020

Written by Christine Oct 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Long hair is a timeless style that continues to evolve with each passing year. If you have long hair, you know how versatile it can be, allowing you to experiment with different styles and looks. In this article, we’ll explore the top 10 hairstyles for long hair in 2020. From braids to buns, we’ve got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Hair

Before we dive into the specific hairstyles, it’s important to understand how to choose the right one for your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Consider Your Face Shape

Long hair looks great on most face shapes, but there are certain styles that can accentuate or balance out certain features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid styles that add volume on the sides, while those with a square face may want to add softness with layers or waves.

Tip #2: Think About Your Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair can also influence which hairstyles work best. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy or bulky styles that can weigh your hair down. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to consider layered styles that can add movement and dimension.

Tip #3: Consider Your Lifestyle

The amount of time and effort you’re willing to put into your hair can also impact your hairstyle choice. If you have a busy schedule, you may want to opt for low-maintenance styles, while those with more time may want to experiment with intricate braids or updos.

10 Trendy Hairstyles for Long Hair in 2020

1. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that’s perfect for any occasion. To achieve this look, simply pull your hair back into a loose bun and let a few strands fall around your face for a relaxed, effortless vibe.

2. The Braided Ponytail

The braided ponytail is a fun and playful style that’s great for summer. Start by braiding a small section of hair at the crown of your head, then gather the rest into a high ponytail. The braid adds texture and interest to the traditional ponytail.

3. The Half-Up, Half-Down Style

The half-up, half-down style is a versatile look that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or elastic band, leaving the rest down.

4. The Sleek High Ponytail

The sleek high ponytail is a chic and sophisticated look that’s perfect for formal events. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, then gather it into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a small section of hair to wrap around the base of the ponytail and conceal the elastic band.

5. The Beachy Waves

The beachy waves look is a popular style that’s perfect for summer. To achieve this look, use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair, then use your fingers to gently tousle and separate the waves for a relaxed, beachy vibe.

6. The Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is a trendy and intricate style that’s perfect for special occasions. Divide your hair into two sections, then take a small piece from the outside of one section and cross it over to the other section. Repeat this process, alternating sides, until you reach the end of your hair.

7. The Layered Cut

The layered cut is a classic style that adds movement and dimension to long hair. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at the chin or collarbone, depending on your face shape and hair type.

8. The Side Braid

The side braid is a simple and versatile style that’s perfect for everyday wear. Simply gather your hair to one side and braid it down to the ends, securing with a small elastic band.

9. The Twisted Bun

The twisted bun is a sleek and polished look that’s great for formal events. Start by twisting two sections of hair together, then wrap them around each other to create a bun at the nape of your neck.

10. The Loose Curls

The loose curls look is a romantic and feminine style that’s perfect for date night. Use a curling iron to create loose, soft curls throughout your hair, then use your fingers to gently separate and tousle for a relaxed, voluminous look.

FAQ About Long Hair

Q: How can I prevent my long hair from getting tangled?

A: Regular brushing and conditioning can help prevent tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently work through any knots, and apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to make your hair more manageable.

Q: How often should I trim my long hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Q: How can I add volume to my long hair?

A: Try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots. You can also use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and fullness to your hair.

Final Thoughts

Long hair is a versatile and timeless style that can be customized to suit any personality and lifestyle. Whether you prefer braids, buns, or loose waves, there’s a long hairstyle out there for you. By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can find the perfect look for your long hair in 2020 and beyond.
