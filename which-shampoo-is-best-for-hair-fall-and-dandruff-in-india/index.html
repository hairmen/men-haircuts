Step-by-Step Guide for Using Anti-Hair Fall and Anti-Dandruff Shampoo

Follow these steps for best results:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with water.
  2. Apply shampoo to your scalp and massage gently for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Rinse off the shampoo with water.
  4. Apply conditioner to the lengths of your hair, avoiding the scalp.
  5. Leave the conditioner on for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Rinse off the conditioner with water.
  7. Pat your hair dry with a towel.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall and Dandruff

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall and dandruff:

  • Wash your hair regularly with an anti-hair fall and anti-dandruff shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.
  • Avoid using hair styling tools that use heat, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your scalp hydrated.
