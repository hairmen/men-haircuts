How To Tame Short Frizzy Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have short frizzy wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it. The frizz and waviness can make your hair look messy and unkempt, which is not the look you want. Fortunately, there are ways to tame your short frizzy wavy hair and make it look sleek and stylish. In this article, we will discuss how to tame short frizzy wavy hair and provide you with some tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Tame Short Frizzy Wavy Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Short Frizzy Wavy Hair

How to Tame Short Frizzy Wavy Hair

The key to taming short frizzy wavy hair is to use products that will help smooth and define your curls. Here are some products that you should consider using:

  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Anti-frizz serum
  • Gel or mousse

These products will help keep your hair moisturized, reduce frizz, and define your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to tame short frizzy wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to keep it moisturized.
  3. Use an anti-frizz serum to reduce frizz.
  4. Apply a gel or mousse to define your curls.
  5. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  6. Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks that will help you tame your short frizzy wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz.
  • Don't touch your hair too much as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about taming short frizzy wavy hair:

  • What causes frizzy hair?
  • How often should I wash my hair?
  • Can I use a hair mask to tame my frizzy hair?
  • What is the best way to blow-dry my hair?

Hair Care for Short Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short frizzy wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Taming short frizzy wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look. Follow the tips and tricks in this article, and you'll be on your way to taming your short frizzy wavy hair in no time.
