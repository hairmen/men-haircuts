Are you a plus-size woman with an oval face shape and wondering what the best haircut is for you? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for plus size oval face, how to achieve it, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a tapered chin. Plus-size women with an oval face shape can experiment with a variety of haircuts that complement their face shape and body size. The best haircut for plus size oval face is one that adds volume to the crown, elongates the face, and creates an illusion of a smaller face.

How To

To achieve the best haircut for plus size oval face, you need to follow the following steps:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before settling on a haircut, you need to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and observing the shape of your face in the mirror. If you have a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a tapered chin, you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Research

Research the best haircut for plus size oval face. You can do this by browsing the internet or consulting with a stylist. Look for haircuts that elongate the face, add volume to the crown, and create an illusion of a smaller face.

Step 3: Consult with a stylist

Consult with a stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and body size. The stylist will recommend a haircut that complements your face shape, body size, and personal style.

Step 4: Maintenance

Maintain your haircut by trimming it regularly. Regular trims will prevent split ends, maintain the shape of the haircut, and keep your hair healthy.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best haircut for plus size oval face:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape and body size
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you
  • Use hair products that add volume to the crown
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to create an illusion of a smaller face

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for plus size oval face:

Q: Can plus-size women with oval face shape have short haircuts?

A: Yes, plus-size women with oval face shape can have short haircuts. However, the haircut should elongate the face, add volume to the crown, and create an illusion of a smaller face.

Q: What is the best haircut for plus size oval face with curly hair?

A: The best haircut for plus size oval face with curly hair is a layered haircut that adds volume to the crown and elongates the face.

Q: How often should I trim my haircut?

A: You should trim your haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools that damage your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth

By following these tips, you can achieve the best haircut for plus size oval face and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
