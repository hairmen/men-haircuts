Description

Long hair can be a beautiful and versatile style, but it can also be challenging to cut and maintain. If you're looking to cut your long hair, you may be wondering how to do it properly to avoid any hair disasters. Luckily, Brad Mondo, a popular hairstylist and YouTube personality, has shared his tips and tricks for cutting long hair. In this article, we'll go over Brad Mondo's advice and give you a step-by-step guide to cutting long hair.

How to Cut Long Hair: Brad Mondo's Tips and Tricks

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of Brad Mondo's tips for cutting long hair:

  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting it, as wet hair can be deceiving and appear longer than it actually is
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up, as it's easier to cut more hair later than to fix a mistake
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Section your hair and work in layers to ensure an even cut
  • Don't cut straight across, as this can create a blunt and unflattering look
  • Consider the shape of your face and the length of your neck when deciding on the length of your hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that we've gone over Brad Mondo's tips, let's dive into the step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back
  2. Take a small section of hair from the back and comb it straight down
  3. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut in a diagonal line, starting from the middle of the hair and working your way down to the ends
  4. Repeat this process on the other side of the back section
  5. Move on to the front sections and repeat the same process, cutting in a diagonal line to create layers
  6. Once you've finished cutting all sections, comb through your hair to ensure an even cut
  7. If you want to add more layers, repeat the process on the top layer of your hair
  8. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Cutting Long Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when cutting long hair:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting hair
  • Don't attempt to cut your hair if you're not confident or experienced, as it can be easy to make mistakes
  • If you're unsure about how much to cut, start with a smaller amount and work your way up
  • If you're cutting someone else's hair, make sure they're comfortable with the length and style before making any cuts

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions people have about cutting long hair:

  • How often should I cut my long hair?

    • It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.

  • Can I cut my own hair?

    • While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's generally recommended to have a professional do it to avoid any mistakes.

  • Can I cut my hair while it's wet?

    • No, it's recommended to cut your hair when it's dry to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Hair

Once you've cut your long hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and appearance. Here are a few tips for caring for your newly-cut long hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair

With these tips and tricks from Brad Mondo, cutting long hair can be an easy and rewarding experience. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and section your hair properly to ensure an even and flattering cut.
