What Hair Suits A Round Face

Written by Luffy Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your round face? With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this blog post, we will discuss what hair suits a round face, how to achieve it, and some tips on hair care to maintain your new look.

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines with a wider forehead and rounded chin. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to balance out the proportions and create an illusion of length. This means avoiding hairstyles that make the face look rounder, such as blunt bangs and chin-length cuts.

How To

The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides and back of the hair shorter. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers that start at the chin and create movement are perfect for those with round faces. This hairstyle adds length to the face and creates a slimming effect. You can also add some highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs that graze the cheekbones are a great option for round faces. They create an asymmetrical look that elongates the face and draws attention to the eyes. Just make sure to keep the rest of your hair long and layered to balance the proportions.

Middle Part

A middle part can work well for round faces if the hair is long and layered. This style creates a vertical line that lengthens the face and draws attention away from the width. You can also add some waves or curls to create more texture and movement.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to achieve the perfect hairstyle for your round face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in cutting hair for round faces.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides and back of the hair shorter.
  3. Avoid blunt bangs and chin-length cuts that make the face look rounder.
  4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.
  5. Use styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume and hold to your hair.
  6. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.
  7. Consider using a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls for more texture.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.
  • Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on the sides.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too sleek.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and texture.
  • Consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and volume to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hair suits a round face:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt bangs, chin-length cuts, and hairstyles that create too much volume on the sides of the face.

What hairstyles are best for round faces?

Long layers, side-swept bangs, and middle parts are great options for round faces.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can use styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, and use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement. You can also consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and texture.

Hair Care

Maintaining your new hairstyle is important to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips on hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using too many products on your hair as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that is perfect for your round face.
