Face Frame Haircuts For Long Straight Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long straight hair looking flat and lifeless? A face frame haircut may be just what you need to add some dimension and movement to your locks. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about face frame haircuts for long straight hair, including how to get the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Face Frame Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice for Long Straight Hair

Description

A face frame haircut is a type of haircut that focuses on framing the face with layers of hair. This style is perfect for long straight hair because it adds texture and dimension to hair that may otherwise look flat and boring. The layers of hair around the face help to draw attention to your features and can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

How to Get the Look

To get a face frame haircut for long straight hair, you'll want to start by finding a hairstylist who is experienced with this type of cut. When you meet with your stylist, be sure to discuss the look you're going for and bring pictures to help illustrate your vision. Your stylist will likely start by cutting layers around the face and then working their way back. They may also add some longer layers throughout the rest of your hair to add volume and movement.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you're looking to cut your own face frame haircut, here are some step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair at the front of your face, and comb it forward.
  3. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut vertically into the hair to create a soft, feathered effect.
  4. Continue this process, working your way around your face, until you've created layers that frame your face.
  5. If desired, add some longer layers throughout the rest of your hair to add volume and movement.
  6. Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Face Frame Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your face frame haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape to your layers.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about face frame haircuts:

What face shape is best for a face frame haircut?

A face frame haircut can work for any face shape, but it's particularly flattering for those with round or square faces. The layers around the face help to soften angular features and add dimension to the hair.

How often should I get a face frame haircut?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your face frame haircut and keep your layers looking fresh.

Can I still wear my hair up with a face frame haircut?

Yes! A face frame haircut looks great both up and down. You can even experiment with different updo styles to show off your layers.

Hair Care Advice for Long Straight Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for long straight hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and prevent it from looking flat.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stunning face frame haircut for long straight hair that's full of volume and movement. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a dramatic transformation, a face frame haircut is a versatile and flattering choice.
