How To Wear Boyfriend Jeans Over 60

Written by Daniel Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Boyfriend jeans can be a stylish and comfortable addition to any wardrobe, regardless of age. However, as we get older, it can be challenging to figure out how to wear them without looking too casual or sloppy. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to wear boyfriend jeans over 60, along with some useful tips, FAQ, and hair care advice to help you look your best.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to wearing boyfriend jeans, it's essential to know how to style them correctly. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Fit

When buying boyfriend jeans, it's crucial to find the right fit. Look for a pair that's comfortable around your waist, hips, and thighs without being too tight or too loose. Also, make sure the length is appropriate for your height.

Step 2: Pick the Right Top

When it comes to tops, you can wear anything from a plain white t-shirt to a blouse, sweater, or jacket. However, it's important to choose a top that's complementary to your figure and doesn't make you look too bulky. Consider tucking your top into your jeans to create a more polished look.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shoes

Boyfriend jeans can look great with many different types of shoes, including sneakers, flats, heels, and ankle boots. However, it's important to choose the right shoes for the occasion. For example, if you're going for a casual look, sneakers or flats are ideal, while heels or ankle boots can dress up the outfit.

Step 4: Accessorize

Accessories can make or break an outfit, so it's essential to choose the right ones. Consider adding a statement necklace, scarf, or belt to your outfit to add some interest and personality. Just make sure not to overdo it and keep it simple.

Tips for Wearing Boyfriend Jeans Over 60

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing boyfriend jeans:

  • Choose a high-waisted or mid-rise style to flatter your figure
  • Avoid baggy or oversized tops that can make you look shapeless
  • Experiment with different styles of shoes to find what works best for you
  • Consider adding a blazer or jacket to dress up the outfit
  • Accessorize with simple jewelry and a classic handbag

FAQ

Can I wear boyfriend jeans to a formal event?

Boyfriend jeans are typically considered casual wear, so it's not appropriate to wear them to a formal event. However, you can dress them up with a blazer, heels, and some accessories for a semi-formal occasion.

What shoes should I wear with boyfriend jeans?

You can wear many different types of shoes with boyfriend jeans, including sneakers, flats, heels, ankle boots, and sandals. Choose the right shoes based on the occasion and your personal preference.

How do I style boyfriend jeans for winter?

You can style boyfriend jeans for winter by adding a warm sweater, coat, scarf, and boots. Consider a pair of ankle boots with a chunky heel for a stylish yet practical look.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing boyfriend jeans, it's essential to keep your hair looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personal style
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Consider using a styling product, such as a mousse or hairspray, to keep your hair in place
  • Visit your hairdresser regularly to keep your hair looking its best

In conclusion, wearing boyfriend jeans over 60 can be a stylish and comfortable way to express your personality. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can create a look that's both chic and age-appropriate. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
