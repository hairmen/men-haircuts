Best Box Braids For Big Forehead A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Box braids are a popular hairstyle that can be worn by anyone, regardless of their hair type or texture. However, if you have a big forehead, you may be wondering which type of box braids will look best on you. In this article, we'll discuss the best box braids for big forehead, how to achieve them, and how to care for them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Best Box Braids for Big Forehead
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Box Braids
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

Description

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves dividing the hair into sections and braiding the hair from the root to the tip. This hairstyle is popular among people with natural hair and those with relaxed hair. Box braids can be worn for several weeks, making them a low-maintenance hairstyle.

If you have a big forehead, you may be self-conscious about wearing box braids. However, there are several styles of box braids that can help to minimize the appearance of a large forehead.

How to Achieve the Best Box Braids for Big Forehead

The best box braids for big forehead are those that create a face-framing effect. This means that the braids should be styled in a way that draws attention away from the forehead and towards the face. Here are some tips for achieving the best box braids for big forehead:

Choose the Right Size of Braids

The size of your box braids can have a big impact on how they look on your face. If you have a big forehead, it's best to choose medium to large-sized braids. Small braids can make your forehead look even bigger, while large braids can overwhelm your face.

Opt for a Side Part

A side part can help to create a face-framing effect by drawing attention away from the forehead. You can choose to part your hair on the left or right side, depending on your preference.

Add Some Layers

If you have long hair, adding some layers to your box braids can help to create a more flattering look. Layers can help to frame your face and draw attention away from your forehead.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your box braids can also have an impact on how they look on your face. If you have a big forehead, it's best to choose braids that fall at or below your shoulders. Braids that are too short can make your forehead look bigger, while braids that are too long can make your face look overwhelmed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the best box braids for big forehead:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using a rat-tail comb.
  4. Start braiding your hair from the root to the tip.
  5. Repeat the process until all of your hair is braided.
  6. Style your braids in a way that creates a face-framing effect.

Tips for Maintaining Your Box Braids

Here are some tips for maintaining your box braids:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

It's important to wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.

Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your box braids, as this can cause damage to your hair. If you want to add some curls or waves to your hair, use perm rods or flexi rods instead.

FAQ

Can I Wear Box Braids if I Have Relaxed Hair?

Yes, you can wear box braids if you have relaxed hair. However, it's important to make sure that your hair is healthy and strong enough to withstand the braiding process.

How Long Can I Wear Box Braids?

You can wear box braids for several weeks, depending on how well you take care of them. It's important to wash your hair regularly and moisturize it to prevent breakage.

Do Box Braids Damage Your Hair?

Box braids can cause damage to your hair if they are installed incorrectly or if you don't take care of them properly. It's important to choose a skilled braider and to follow a hair care routine to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

Here are some hair care tips for box braids:

Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair in a satin scarf or bonnet at night to prevent your braids from rubbing against your pillowcase. This can cause your braids to become frizzy and damaged.

Avoid Tight Styles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your braids, as this can cause breakage. Instead, opt for loose styles that are gentle on your hair.

Take Breaks Between Styles

Take breaks between box braid styles to give your hair a chance to rest and recover. This can help to prevent damage and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the best box braids for big forehead and keep your hair healthy and strong. With the right care and maintenance, box braids can be a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle for anyone.
