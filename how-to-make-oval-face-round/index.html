How To Make Oval Face Round A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having an oval face shape and want to make it round? Well, you are not alone! Many people want to change their face shape for different reasons. In this article, we will guide you on how to make oval face round. We will cover everything from description, how-to, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by having a longer face with a narrower chin and forehead. On the other hand, a round face shape has a wider and shorter face with a rounder chin and forehead. If you have an oval face shape and want to make it round, you can do so by using makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises.

How to

To make your oval face round, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start, it is important to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking at yourself in the mirror and analyzing the length of your face, the width of your forehead, and the shape of your chin. You can also take a picture of yourself and use an online face shape detector tool.

Step 2: Use Makeup

Makeup is a great way to make your oval face round. You can use contouring to create shadows and highlights on your face to give it a rounder appearance. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer under your cheekbones and jawline to create a shadow effect. Then, apply a lighter shade of foundation or highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your forehead, nose, and cheekbones.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also make your oval face round. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or a curly hairstyle. Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as a straight and long hairstyle.

Step 4: Do Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and strengthen the muscles on your face, which can make your oval face round. Some facial exercises that you can do include cheek lifts, lip pulls, and jaw releases. You can do these exercises in the morning and before going to bed.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make your oval face round:

  • Wear earrings that add width to your face
  • Choose a round pair of glasses
  • Avoid wearing hats that make your face look longer

FAQ

Q: Can I change my face shape permanently?

A: No, you cannot change your face shape permanently. However, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises to alter the appearance of your face.

Q: How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: It may take several weeks or months to see results from facial exercises. Consistency is key, so make sure to do the exercises regularly.

Hair Care

To maintain your new hairstyle and make your oval face round, you should take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use hair masks and oils to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

In conclusion, making your oval face round is possible with the right makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises. Remember to determine your face shape, use makeup, choose the right hairstyle, do facial exercises, and follow our tips. With these steps, you can achieve the round face shape that you desire.
