How To Get Rid Of Oval Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

If you have an oval face shape, you may feel that your features are not as defined as you would like them to be. However, there are several ways to enhance your facial structure and get rid of an oval face shape. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of oval face shape, tips, FAQs, and hair care solutions.

How to Get Rid of Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is characterized by a balanced oval shape, which is longer than it is wide. The goal is to create angles and definition to balance out the softer curves of the face. Below are some ways to get rid of oval face shape:

1. Contouring

Contouring is the art of using makeup to create shadows and highlights on your face. It can help to add definition and create angles to your face. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows in the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and on the sides of your forehead.

2. Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can help to balance out your face shape. For an oval face shape, consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved with layers, waves, or a side-swept fringe. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as they can elongate your face shape.

3. Eyebrows

Your eyebrows can help to frame your face and create angles. For an oval face shape, consider shaping your eyebrows with a high arch to add definition. Avoid shaping your eyebrows too thin, as this can make your face appear longer.

4. Accessories

Accessories can help to draw attention away from the oval shape of your face. Consider wearing statement earrings or a bold necklace to add interest to your outfit. Avoid wearing accessories that are too small, as they can make your face appear larger.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get rid of oval face shape:

Step 1: Identify your face shape

Before you can enhance your facial structure, you need to identify your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Contour your face

Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows and highlights on your face. Apply the contouring powder or cream to the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and on the sides of your forehead.

Step 3: Shape your eyebrows

Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to shape your eyebrows with a high arch. Avoid shaping your eyebrows too thin, as this can make your face appear longer.

Step 4: Choose the right hairstyle

Consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, or a side-swept fringe. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight.

Step 5: Wear the right accessories

Wear statement accessories to draw attention away from the oval shape of your face. Avoid wearing accessories that are too small.

Tips for Enhancing Your Facial Structure

Here are some additional tips for enhancing your facial structure:

1. Use a brightening concealer

Use a brightening concealer under your eyes to brighten up your face and make your eyes appear larger.

2. Wear bold lipstick

Wear a bold lipstick to draw attention to your lips and create interest on your face.

3. Use a highlighter

Use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and the bridge of your nose, to add a subtle glow.

4. Experiment with different hairstyles

Experiment with different hairstyles to find one that works best for your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a balanced oval shape, which is longer than it is wide.

What hairstyles are best for an oval face shape?

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, or a side-swept fringe, are best for an oval face shape.

What makeup should I use to enhance my facial structure?

Use a contouring powder or cream, a brightening concealer, and a highlighter to enhance your facial structure.

Hair Care Solutions for Oval Face Shape

Here are some additional hair care solutions for oval face shape:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo

Use a volumizing shampoo to add volume to your hair and create the illusion of a wider face shape.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.

3. Experiment with different haircuts

Experiment with different haircuts to find one that works best for your face shape.


By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of oval face shape and enhance your facial structure. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles, makeup, and accessories to find what works best for you. With a little bit of practice, you can achieve a more defined and balanced facial structure.
