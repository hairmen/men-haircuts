How To Curl A Bob Under A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 18, 2023 · 4 min read

A bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that can be styled in many ways. One popular way is to curl it under for a more polished and elegant look. In this article, we’ll show you how to curl a bob under in relaxed English language. We’ll cover everything you need to know, from getting the right tools to caring for your hair after styling. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You’ll Need
  2. Prepping Your Hair
  3. Curling Your Bob Under
  4. Tips for Perfect Curls
  5. FAQs
  6. Caring for Your Hair After Styling

Tools You’ll Need

Before you start curling your bob under, make sure you have the right tools on hand:

  • A curling iron with a 1-inch barrel
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Sectioning clips
  • Comb or brush

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start curling, you need to prepare your hair by washing and drying it. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Then, use a comb or brush to detangle your hair and create a center part.

Curling Your Bob Under

Now it’s time to start curling. Follow these steps:

  1. Section your hair into two parts by clipping the top half away from the bottom half.
  2. Take a 1-inch section of hair from the bottom half and clamp the curling iron at the ends of your hair.
  3. Roll the curling iron up towards your roots, making sure not to burn yourself.
  4. Hold the curling iron in place for 5-10 seconds, then release the curl.
  5. Continue curling the rest of your hair in small sections, making sure to curl the ends under.
  6. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through your curls to loosen them up.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Perfect Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel to create tight curls.
  • Make sure to curl the ends of your hair under for a polished look.
  • Don’t hold the curling iron in your hair for too long, as this can damage your hair and create uneven curls.
  • Use sectioning clips to keep your hair organized and make it easier to curl.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about curling a bob under:

How long do I need to hold the curling iron in my hair?

You should hold the curling iron in your hair for 5-10 seconds, depending on your hair type and the heat of your curling iron.

What type of hairspray should I use?

You should use a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place without making your hair stiff or crunchy.

Can I curl my bob under without a curling iron?

You can use hot rollers or a straightening iron to curl your bob under, but a curling iron will give you the most control and precision.

Caring for Your Hair After Styling

After curling your bob under, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after curling to let the curls set.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Now that you know how to curl a bob under, you can create a polished and elegant look in no time. Just remember to use the right tools and take care of your hair after styling. Happy curling!
