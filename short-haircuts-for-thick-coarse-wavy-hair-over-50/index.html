Short Haircuts For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50 A Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Getting a haircut can be a daunting experience, especially if you have thick, coarse, and wavy hair. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered with this guide on short haircuts for thick coarse wavy hair over 50. Whether you want to embrace your natural texture or go for a sleeker look, there’s a haircut that will suit your needs. Read on for tips, tricks, and inspiration.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50

Before you head to the salon, it’s important to know what type of haircut will work best for your hair type and face shape. Here are some things to consider:

Face Shape

The first step in choosing a short haircut is to determine your face shape. This will help you find a haircut that flatters your features and brings out your best qualities. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any short haircut. If you have a round face, try a layered bob that hits at the chin or a short pixie cut with longer layers on top. For a square face, go for a short, textured bob or a layered pixie cut that’s longer on top. And if you have a heart-shaped face, try a short bob with layers that frame your face.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a short haircut. If you have thick coarse wavy hair, you may want to go for a layered bob or a pixie cut with longer layers on top to add volume and movement. If you want to embrace your natural texture, try a curly bob or a short shag haircut. And if you prefer a sleeker look, go for a blunt bob or a short layered cut that’s straightened with a flat iron.

Lifestyle

Finally, it’s important to consider your lifestyle when choosing a short haircut. If you’re an active person who likes to work out or swim, you may want a low-maintenance haircut that doesn’t require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you’re someone who likes to experiment with different styles and looks, you may want a haircut that’s more versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Short Haircuts for Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a short haircut, here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Start by looking for inspiration online or in magazines. Save pictures of haircuts that you like and bring them with you to the salon. This will give your stylist a better idea of what you’re looking for.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about the best short haircut for your hair type and face shape. They may also have suggestions based on your lifestyle and personal style.

Step 3: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before the haircut, your stylist will wash and condition your hair to remove any product buildup and make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your stylist will then cut your hair according to the style you’ve chosen. They may use scissors, clippers, or a combination of both.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron. They may also add product to help hold the style in place.

Step 6: Maintain Your Haircut

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it’s important to maintain it with regular trims and styling. Your stylist can recommend the best products and techniques for your hair type.

Tips for Short Haircuts for Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair over 50:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture
  • Bring pictures of haircuts you like to the salon
  • Consult with your stylist before the haircut
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50

Q: What is the best short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair over 50?

A: The best short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair over 50 depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular options include a layered bob, a short shag haircut, and a pixie cut with longer layers on top.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my short haircut?

A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.

Q: What products should I use to style my short haircut?

A: The best products for styling short haircuts for thick coarse wavy hair over 50 include mousse, texturizing spray, and pomade. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and use them sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a short haircut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking its best

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair over 50 with confidence and style.
