How To Manage Hair Extensions In Lockdown Tips And Tricks

Are you struggling to manage your hair extensions during the lockdown? With salons closed and social distancing measures in place, it can be challenging to maintain your extensions without professional help. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you manage your hair extensions at home.

  • How to Manage Hair Extensions in Lockdown
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Manage Hair Extensions in Lockdown

Managing hair extensions during the lockdown requires some extra effort and care. Here are some things you can do to keep your extensions looking great:

  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangling and matting
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use hair products that are specifically formulated for extensions
  • Wash your hair less frequently to avoid drying out the extensions
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair to prevent damage

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to managing your hair extensions at home:

  1. Brush your hair gently from the ends to the roots using a wide-tooth comb or a soft bristle brush
  2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, but if you must, use a heat protectant spray
  3. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo, focusing on the roots and avoiding the extension bonds
  4. Apply a conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out
  5. Gently towel-dry your hair, avoiding rubbing it too hard or pulling on the extensions
  6. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized

Tips for Maintaining Hair Extensions

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your hair extensions:

  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the extensions
  • Don't pull or tug on the extensions, as this can cause them to loosen or fall out
  • Avoid swimming or getting your hair wet in the rain, as this can damage the extensions
  • Don't sleep with wet hair, as this can cause tangling and matting
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing hair extensions in lockdown:

Q: How often should I wash my hair extensions?

A: It's best to wash your extensions no more than once or twice a week to avoid drying them out.

Q: Can I use regular hair products on my extensions?

A: No, it's best to use products that are specifically formulated for extensions to avoid damaging them.

Q: How do I prevent tangling and matting?

A: Brush your hair regularly using a wide-tooth comb or soft bristle brush, and avoid sleeping with wet hair.

Hair Care for Extensions

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your extensions looking great. Here are some additional tips:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair and extensions moisturized
  • Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as they can damage the extensions

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair extensions looking great during the lockdown. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid anything that could cause damage or breakage. With a little extra care and attention, you can enjoy beautiful, healthy hair all year round!
