Best Mousse For Wavy Hair Drugstore Your Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Petter Oct 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Wavy hair can be tricky to manage, but with the right products, you can achieve voluminous, defined waves that will make heads turn. Mousse is a popular styling product for wavy hair, and there are many drugstore options available that can give you salon-worthy results without breaking the bank. In this article, we’ll take a deep dive into the best mousse for wavy hair drugstore options, how to use them, and other tips for achieving gorgeous waves.

  • How to Use Mousse for Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

  • FAQs

Description

Mousse is a lightweight, foamy styling product that can add volume and hold to wavy hair. It’s a popular choice among those with fine or thin hair, as it doesn’t weigh the hair down like other styling products can. Mousse can also help define waves and control frizz, making it a versatile styling product for wavy hair.

When it comes to choosing the best mousse for wavy hair drugstore options, there are a few things to consider. Look for a mousse that is specifically formulated for wavy or curly hair, as these will have ingredients that can help enhance and define curls. You’ll also want to look for a mousse that provides a strong hold without being stiff or crunchy, as this can make your waves look unnatural.

How to Use Mousse for Wavy Hair

Using mousse for wavy hair is a relatively simple process. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and gently towel dry.
  2. Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your palms and distribute it evenly through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the natural wave pattern. If you have particularly stubborn waves, you can also twist small sections of hair around your fingers to help define them.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair gently. Avoid using a regular hair dryer without a diffuser, as this can cause frizz and damage to your waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed step-by-step guide to using mousse for wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and gently towel dry. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause frizz.
  2. Dispense a golf ball-sized amount of mousse onto your palms. If you have particularly long or thick hair, you may need to use more product.
  3. Flip your head upside down and distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use your fingers to comb through your hair and ensure that the mousse is evenly distributed.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the natural wave pattern. You can also twist small sections of hair around your fingers to help define them.
  5. If you have particularly stubborn waves, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to gently dry your hair. This will help enhance your waves and reduce frizz. If you don’t have a diffuser attachment, you can also air dry your hair.
  6. Once your hair is dry, you can add additional product if needed. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Tips for Using Mousse for Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for using mousse for wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before applying mousse. This will help ensure that the product is evenly distributed and will prevent tangles.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different amounts of mousse. Start with a small amount and add more as needed to achieve your desired look.
  • If you have particularly frizzy hair, you may want to use a hair serum or oil in addition to mousse. Apply the serum or oil to the ends of your hair to help control frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much once you’ve applied mousse. This can disturb the natural wave pattern and cause frizz.
  • Consider using a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using mousse for wavy hair:

Q: Can I use mousse on dry hair?

A: While it’s possible to use mousse on dry hair, it’s generally more effective on damp hair. The dampness helps activate the product and encourages your natural wave pattern.

Q: Will mousse make my hair crunchy?

A: Some mousses can make your hair crunchy if you use too much or apply it incorrectly. Look for a mousse that provides a flexible hold and distribute it evenly through your hair to avoid crunchy, stiff hair.

Q: How often should I use mousse on my wavy hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people may find that they only need to use mousse once or twice a week, while others may use it every day. Experiment with different frequencies to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Using the best mousse for wavy hair drugstore options is just one part of a comprehensive hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for taking care of your wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair and make your waves look limp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness. Instead, try washing every other day or every few days.
  • Consider using a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products and hard water.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair when you brush it.

With the right mousse and hair care routine, you can achieve gorgeous, defined waves that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair type.
