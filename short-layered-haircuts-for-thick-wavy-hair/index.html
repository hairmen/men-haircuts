Short Layered Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair A Guide To Relaxed And Chic Hair

Written by Robby Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair? Look no further than short layered haircuts! Not only are they stylish and chic, but they also help to manage the volume of your hair. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect short layered haircut for your thick wavy hair, including how to style and care for it. Let's get started!

Description

Short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair are a great way to add texture and dimension to your hair. By cutting layers into your hair, you can create movement and shape, which can help to manage the volume of your hair. Additionally, shorter haircuts are easier to manage and style, which is perfect for those with busy lifestyles.

How To

When it comes to finding the perfect short layered haircut for your thick wavy hair, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, think about the length you want your hair to be. Shorter haircuts will be easier to manage, but longer haircuts can still work if they are layered correctly. Secondly, consider the type of layers you want. Do you want shorter layers for more volume, or longer layers for a more relaxed look?

Step 1: Consult with a Hair Stylist

The first step to finding the perfect short layered haircut for your thick wavy hair is to consult with a hair stylist. They will be able to give you recommendations based on your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences. Bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to help guide the discussion.

Step 2: Choose Your Haircut

Once you've consulted with a hair stylist, it's time to choose your haircut. Popular short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair include the bob, pixie cut, and shag haircut. Each of these haircuts can be customized to suit your personal preferences and hair type.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Styling short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair is easy and fun. To enhance your waves, use a curling iron or wand to create loose curls. Alternatively, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves. Finish off with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for a relaxed and beachy look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when it comes to short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair:

  • Regular trims will help to keep your haircut looking fresh and maintained.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect one for your hair type.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.

FAQ

What is the best short layered haircut for thick wavy hair?

The best short layered haircut for thick wavy hair will depend on your personal preferences and hair type. Popular options include the bob, pixie cut, and shag haircut.

How do I style short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair?

To style short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair, use a curling iron or wand to create loose curls, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves. Finish off with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered haircut for thick wavy hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet, as this is when it is most fragile.

Conclusion

Short layered haircuts for thick wavy hair are a great way to add texture and dimension to your hair, while also making it easier to manage and style. By following our guide to finding the perfect short layered haircut for your hair type, and following our hair care tips, you can achieve relaxed and chic hair that looks and feels great.
