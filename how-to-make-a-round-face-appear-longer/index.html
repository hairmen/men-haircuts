How to Make a Round Face Appear Longer: Tips and Tricks | Hair Care

Are you tired of having a round face and want to make it appear longer? There are a few simple tips and tricks you can do to elongate your face shape. In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to make a round face appear longer. Plus, we'll answer some FAQs and provide some hair care tips to enhance the overall look.

How to Make a Round Face Appear Longer

There are a few things you can do to make a round face appear longer:

  • Contouring
  • Hairstyles
  • Jewelry
  • Glasses

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of depth and shadows, and lighter shades to highlight certain areas. To make your face appear longer, contour your cheekbones by applying a darker shade just below them and blend it out. This will create the illusion of a more defined cheekbone and will elongate the face shape.

Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can also make a big difference in how your face appears. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only emphasize the roundness. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or top knot. You can also try a side part or layered haircut to add dimension to your face shape.

Jewelry

Wearing long, dangling earrings can also elongate your face shape. Avoid studs or small earrings that sit close to your face, as these will only emphasize the roundness. Instead, opt for long, thin earrings that hang below your jawline.

Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose frames that are rectangular or square in shape. These shapes will add angles to your face and create the illusion of a longer face shape. Avoid round or oval frames, as these will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to make a round face appear longer:

  1. Apply foundation and concealer as normal.
  2. Use a darker shade of contour powder or cream to contour your cheekbones. Blend it out with a brush or sponge.
  3. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add color.
  4. Choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or top knot.
  5. Wear long, dangling earrings to elongate your face shape.
  6. If you wear glasses, choose frames that are rectangular or square in shape.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make your face appear longer:

  • Wear clothing with vertical stripes to create the illusion of a longer body.
  • Avoid turtlenecks or high-necked clothing, as this will make your face appear shorter.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to keep your skin looking its best.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my face appear longer without makeup?

A: Yes! Choosing the right hairstyle and accessories, such as long earrings or rectangular glasses, can also make a big difference in how your face appears.

Q: Can I wear round glasses if I have a round face?

A: It's best to avoid round glasses if you have a round face, as this will only emphasize the roundness. Instead, choose rectangular or square frames to add angles to your face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make a round face appear longer and enhance your overall look. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for you. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
