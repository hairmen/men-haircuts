How To Get Super Curly Hair As A Black Male

Written by Eveline Jul 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair as a black male? Do you want to have the super curly hair that you see some of your friends rocking? If so, you’re in luck! In this article, we’ll go over how to get super curly hair as a black male, step by step. From tips to frequently asked questions, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Super Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Super Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Super Curly Hair

How to Get Super Curly Hair

The first thing you should know is that getting super curly hair as a black male is possible, but it requires a bit of work. You will need to use certain products and follow specific steps to achieve the desired result.

One of the most popular ways to get super curly hair is by using a perm or a texturizer. A perm is a chemical process that permanently changes the hair’s structure, making it curly. A texturizer, on the other hand, is a milder version of a perm that loosens the hair’s curl pattern.

Another way to get super curly hair is by using a curl sponge. A curl sponge is a sponge with small holes that you use to twist your hair, creating small curls. This method is easy and doesn’t require any chemicals.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to get super curly hair as a black male:

  1. Decide which method you want to use – perm, texturizer, or curl sponge.
  2. If you’re using a perm or a texturizer, make sure to read the instructions carefully and follow them precisely.
  3. If you’re using a curl sponge, make sure your hair is clean and damp. Apply a curling cream or gel to your hair.
  4. Take the curl sponge and rub it in a circular motion on your hair. Make sure to apply enough pressure so that the sponge can twist your hair.
  5. Repeat the process until your hair is fully twisted.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it.
  7. If you used a perm or a texturizer, make sure to follow the instructions for aftercare. This may include using a specific shampoo or conditioner.

Tips for Maintaining Super Curly Hair

Once you have achieved super curly hair, it’s essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly. Super curly hair tends to be dry, so you need to keep it moisturized to prevent breakage.
  • Don’t wash your hair too often. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, as it can cause breakage.
  • Sleep with a satin or silk bonnet or pillowcase to prevent your hair from drying out and breaking while you sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get super curly hair without using chemicals?

Yes, you can use a curl sponge to get super curly hair without using any chemicals.

How long does a perm or texturizer last?

A perm or texturizer can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can I dye my hair after getting a perm or texturizer?

You should wait at least two weeks after getting a perm or texturizer before dyeing your hair.

Hair Care for Super Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your super curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get super curly hair as a black male, it’s time to give it a try! Remember to follow the instructions carefully and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
