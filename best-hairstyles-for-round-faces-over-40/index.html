Best Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 40

Written by Aprilia Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes just like the rest of our body. We may experience thinning or greying hair, but that doesn't mean we can't still look fabulous! If you have a round face and are over 40, you may be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. Don't worry, we have got you covered! Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces over 40.

Description

Round faces are characterized by fullness and a lack of sharp angles. As we age, our face tends to lose volume, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and dimension to our face. The right hairstyle can make all the difference in the world!

How to

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for your round face:

Tip 1: Consider Your Face Shape

There are different types of round faces, so it's important to determine which one you have. The most common types of round faces are:

  • The classic round face
  • The square round face
  • The long round face

Once you determine your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Tip 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Choosing a hairstyle that works with your hair texture is important. If you have thin hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that adds volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that thins out your hair.

Tip 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be considered when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want a hairstyle that is low maintenance and easy to style.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces over 40:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add volume and dimension to your hair. They also help to elongate your face, which is perfect for round faces. If you have thin hair, long layers will add volume and make your hair look fuller.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a great option for round faces. The side-swept bangs help to elongate your face, while the bob adds volume and dimension to your hair.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. It's also a great option for those with thick hair. The short length of the pixie cut helps to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

4. Shaggy Layers

Shaggy layers add volume and texture to your hair. They also help to elongate your face, which is perfect for round faces. Shaggy layers are a great option for those with thick hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a blunt cut.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face, such as a layered cut or a pixie cut.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a blunt cut. These hairstyles will only make your face look wider.

What hairstyles are best for round faces?

Hairstyles that add height and dimension to your face are best for round faces. Long layers, a bob with side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, and shaggy layers are all great options.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by choosing a layered cut, using volumizing products, or using a round brush when blow drying your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best with a round face over 40!
