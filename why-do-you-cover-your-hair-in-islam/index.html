Why Do You Cover Your Hair in Islam? | Hair Care Tips and FAQ 2023

As a Muslim woman, it is common to see us wearing a headscarf or hijab. But why do we cover our hair in Islam? In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this practice and provide hair care tips and FAQs.

Why Do You Cover Your Hair in Islam?

In Islam, covering the hair is a part of modesty and piety. It is a commandment from Allah (SWT) in the Holy Quran, where it is stated, "And tell the believing women to lower their gaze and be modest, and to display of their adornment only that which is apparent, and to draw their veils over their bosoms, and not to reveal their adornment save to their own husbands or fathers or husbands' fathers, or their sons or their husbands' sons, or their brothers or their brothers' sons or sisters' sons, or their women, or their slaves, or male attendants who lack vigor, or children who know naught of women's nakedness. And let them not stamp their feet so as to reveal what they hide of their adornment. And turn unto Allah together, O believers, in order that ye may succeed." (Quran 24:31)

Therefore, Muslim women cover their hair as an act of obedience to Allah (SWT) and as a way to show their devotion to their faith. It also serves as a way to protect their beauty from unwanted attention and to maintain their dignity and respect in society.

How to Wear a Hijab

Wearing a hijab can seem daunting at first, but with practice, it becomes second nature. There are different styles of hijabs, including the traditional square scarf, the rectangular scarf, and the shawl-style hijab. Here are some tips on how to wear a hijab:

Step-by-Step Guide to Wearing a Hijab

1. Start by placing the hijab on your head, making sure that one end is longer than the other.

2. Take the longer end and bring it across your forehead, making sure that it covers your hairline.

3. Bring the longer end to the other side of your head and drape it over your shoulder.

4. Take the shorter end and tuck it under your chin.

5. Adjust the hijab so that it covers your neck and chest.

6. Pin the hijab in place with a safety pin, if necessary.

Hijab Hair Care Tips

Wearing a hijab can cause hair to become dry and brittle. Here are some tips to care for your hair while wearing a hijab:

- Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.

- Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your hairline.

- Use a satin or silk scarf under your hijab to prevent friction and breakage.

- Massage your scalp regularly to increase blood flow and promote hair growth.

Hijab FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a hijab:

Q: Do I have to wear a hijab?

A: Hijab is mandatory for Muslim women who have reached puberty. However, it is a personal choice, and some women choose not to wear it.

Q: Can I wear makeup with my hijab?

A: Yes, you can wear makeup with your hijab. However, it is important to keep it modest and not draw unnecessary attention.

Q: Can I wear different styles of hijabs?

A: Yes, you can wear different styles of hijabs. It is a matter of personal preference and cultural tradition.

Q: What if I feel uncomfortable wearing a hijab?

A: It is important to discuss any concerns with a trusted religious authority or counselor. They can provide guidance and support.

Covering the hair in Islam is a way to show devotion to Allah (SWT) and to embody modesty and piety. By following proper hair care tips and seeking guidance for any concerns, Muslim women can confidently wear their hijabs and showcase their faith.
