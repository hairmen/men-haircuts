Does Inverted Bob Suit Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog about hairstyles! Today, we will be discussing the age-old question: does inverted bob suit round face? We know that finding the perfect haircut can be a daunting task, especially when you have a round face shape. But don't worry, we are here to help you! In this article, we will be giving you tips, tricks, and everything you need to know about inverted bob haircuts and round face shapes. So, let's get started!

What Is an Inverted Bob Haircut?

An inverted bob haircut is a hairstyle that is shorter at the back and longer at the front. This type of haircut is also known as a graduated bob because the hair is cut in layers, with the shortest layer at the back and the longest layer at the front. This style is popular among women of all ages because it is versatile and easy to maintain.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we discuss whether an inverted bob suits a round face, let's first determine what a round face shape is. A round face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. To determine your face shape, you can follow these simple steps:

Step 1:

Pull your hair back and away from your face. This will make it easier to see the shape of your face.

Step 2:

Take a measuring tape and measure the widest part of your face. This is usually across your cheekbones.

Step 3:

Measure the distance from your hairline to the bottom of your chin.

Step 4:

Measure the distance across your jawline.

If the width of your face is approximately the same as the length of your face, then you have a round face shape.

Does Inverted Bob Suit Round Face?

The answer to this question is yes! An inverted bob is a great haircut for round face shapes. The layers in the haircut help to create a slimmer and more elongated look, which is perfect for round faces. The longer layers at the front also help to draw the eye down and create the illusion of a longer face.

Tips for Getting an Inverted Bob Haircut for Round Faces

If you have a round face shape and you want to get an inverted bob haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Opt for longer layers at the front:

Longer layers at the front will help to create the illusion of a longer face. This is especially important if you have a round face shape.

2. Keep the back of your hair short:

The back of your hair should be shorter than the front. This will help to create a more graduated look and add volume to the crown of your head.

3. Add some texture:

Adding some texture to your hair will help to create a more dynamic look. You can do this by adding some layers or by using a texturizing spray.

4. Avoid blunt cuts:

Blunt cuts can make a round face shape appear even rounder. Instead, opt for a more layered look.

FAQs

1. Can I get an inverted bob if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! An inverted bob is a great haircut for curly hair as it helps to add volume and shape to your curls.

2. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your inverted bob.

3. Can I style my inverted bob in different ways?

Yes, you can! An inverted bob is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, or wavy.

Hair Care Tips for Inverted Bob Haircuts

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have an inverted bob haircut:

1. Use a heat protectant:

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Use a volumizing shampoo:

A volumizing shampoo will help to add volume to your hair and create a more dynamic look.

3. Avoid using too much product:

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat. Use only a small amount of product and apply it to the roots of your hair.

4. Don't wash your hair every day:

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and make it appear dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

Conclusion

So, there you have it! An inverted bob is a great haircut for round face shapes. Just make sure to follow our tips and tricks to get the best possible look for your face shape. Remember, confidence is key, so wear your new haircut with pride!
