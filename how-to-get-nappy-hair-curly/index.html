How To Get Nappy Hair Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 12, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you have nappy hair, you may be wondering how to get it curly. The good news is that there are several ways to achieve curly hair, whether you have natural or relaxed hair. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to get nappy hair curly. We will cover everything from how to prepare your hair to different styling techniques to maintain your curls. So, let's get started!

  1. How to Get Nappy Hair Curly
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

How to Get Nappy Hair Curly

Before we dive into the different techniques for getting curly hair, it's important to understand that there are different types of curls. Some people have loose curls, while others have tight coils. The type of curls you can achieve will depend on your hair type and the technique you use.

There are several ways to get curly hair, including:

  • Flexi rods
  • Roller sets
  • Twist outs
  • Bantu knots
  • Perm rod sets

Each of these techniques has its own pros and cons, and the results will vary depending on your hair type. In the next section, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve curly hair using flexi rods.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to get nappy hair curly using flexi rods:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product, such as a curl defining cream or mousse.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl.
  3. Take a flexi rod and wrap the end of your hair around the rod. Roll the rod up to your scalp, tucking the ends under the rod to secure them.
  4. Repeat this process on all of your hair.
  5. Once all of your hair is rolled, allow it to dry completely. This can take several hours or overnight.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the flexi rods and separate the curls with your fingers.
  7. Use a light oil or serum to smooth down any frizz or flyaways.

That's it! You should now have beautiful, bouncy curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Refresh your curls in the morning with a water-based spray or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can damage your hair and cause your curls to fall flat.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting nappy hair curly:

Can I get curly hair if I have relaxed hair?

Yes, you can still achieve curly hair if you have relaxed hair. However, you may need to use different techniques, such as roller sets or twist outs, to avoid damaging your hair.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls can last anywhere from a few days to a week or more, depending on how well you maintain them. You can refresh your curls by using a water-based spray or leave-in conditioner.

Can I swim with curly hair?

Yes, you can swim with curly hair. However, you should protect your hair from chlorine and saltwater by wearing a swim cap or applying a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

Hair Care Tips for Nappy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for nappy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls with your nappy hair. The key is to find the technique that works best for your hair type and to maintain your curls with proper care and styling.
