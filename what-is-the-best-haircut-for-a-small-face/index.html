What Is The Best Haircut For A Small Face

Written by William Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different haircuts that do not work on your small face? Do you want to know what is the best haircut for a small face? If yes, then this article is for you! We will discuss everything you need to know about the best haircut for a small face, including how to, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description

A small face is a beautiful feature that needs the right haircut to enhance its beauty. The best haircut for a small face should focus on creating an illusion of volume and width to balance the face's proportions. It should also highlight the face's natural features and complement the person's style and personality.

How to?

The first step in getting the best haircut for a small face is to determine your face shape. There are four primary face shapes: oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right haircut that will flatter your features.

Oval Face

An oval face is the most versatile face shape and can pull off various haircuts. The best haircut for an oval face is a layered cut that adds volume and texture to the hair. You can also opt for a bob, pixie cut, or a long hairstyle with side-swept bangs.

Round Face

If you have a round face, the best haircut is one that adds length and angles to the face. A layered cut with side-swept bangs or a long bob is perfect for a round face. Avoid blunt bangs and center parts as they can make your face appear rounder.

Square Face

A square face has a defined jawline and forehead. The best haircut for a square face is one that softens the angles and adds volume to the hair. A layered cut with side-swept bangs or a long bob is perfect for a square face. Avoid blunt bobs and straight cuts that accentuate the jawline.

Heart-Shaped Face

A heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and a narrow chin. The best haircut for a heart-shaped face is one that balances the proportions and adds volume to the chin area. A layered cut with side-swept bangs or a long bob is perfect for a heart-shaped face. Avoid short hairstyles that make the forehead appear wider.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to get the best haircut for a small face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Choose a haircut that flatters your features.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  4. Show your hairstylist pictures of the haircut you want.
  5. Discuss the maintenance and styling requirements of the haircut.
  6. Book an appointment and get your new haircut!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the best haircut for a small face:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your style and personality.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts until you find the one that works best for you.
  • Invest in hair products that enhance the volume and texture of your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a small face with fine hair?

The best haircut for a small face with fine hair is a layered cut that adds volume and texture to the hair. You can also opt for a bob or a long hairstyle with side-swept bangs.

Can I pull off a short haircut with a small face?

Yes, you can pull off a short haircut with a small face. However, make sure to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and features.

How often should I get my haircut?

You should get your haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

To maintain the health and beauty of your hair, here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know what is the best haircut for a small face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get a new haircut that flatters your features and enhances your beauty!
