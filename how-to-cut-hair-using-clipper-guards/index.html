How To Cut Hair Using Clipper Guards

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money on haircuts? Do you want to learn how to cut your hair at home using clipper guards? Then you are in the right place. In this post, we will show you how to cut hair using clipper guards. This guide is perfect for beginners who want to learn how to cut hair using clipper guards. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs to help you get started.

How to Cut Hair Using Clipper Guards

Cutting hair using clipper guards is easy and simple. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots. If your hair is too long, you can use scissors to trim it to a shorter length. If you are cutting someone else's hair, make sure they are comfortable and have a towel around their neck to catch any loose hair.

Step 2: Attach the Clipper Guard

Choose the right clipper guard size depending on the length of hair you want. Attach the clipper guard securely to the clippers. Make sure the clippers are clean and lubricated before you start.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the bottom and work your way up. Use the clippers to move against the growth of the hair. Use short and smooth strokes to ensure an even cut. Pay attention to the corners and edges of your hairline to ensure a clean and neat finish.

Step 4: Blend and Fade

If you want a blended or faded look, you can use different clipper guard sizes to achieve this. Use a longer clipper guard size to blend the top with the sides and use a shorter clipper guard size to fade the sides to the skin.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for any uneven spots or missed areas. Use scissors or clippers to fix any mistakes. Use a clean towel to wipe away any loose hair.

Tips for Cutting Hair Using Clipper Guards

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting hair using clipper guards:

  • Choose the right clipper guard size for the length of hair you want.
  • Make sure the clippers are clean and lubricated before you start.
  • Use short and smooth strokes to ensure an even cut.
  • Pay attention to the corners and edges of your hairline to ensure a clean and neat finish.
  • Use different clipper guard sizes to achieve a blended or faded look.

FAQs

Can I use clipper guards on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use clipper guards on wet hair. Wet hair can clog the clippers and make it difficult to achieve an even cut. Make sure your hair is dry before you start cutting.

How often should I clean my clippers?

You should clean your clippers after every use. Use a brush to remove any hair or debris and wipe the blades with a clean towel. You should also lubricate the blades regularly to ensure they function smoothly.

Can I cut my own hair using clipper guards?

Yes, you can cut your own hair using clipper guards. Just make sure you have the right clipper guard size and follow the steps we have provided.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Cutting hair using clipper guards is a great way to save money and learn a new skill. With our step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Just remember to choose the right clipper guard size, use short and smooth strokes, and pay attention to the corners and edges of your hairline. Happy cutting!
