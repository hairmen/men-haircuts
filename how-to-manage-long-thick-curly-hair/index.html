How To Manage Long Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long, thick, curly hair, then you know how challenging it can be to manage. It can take hours to dry, and even longer to style. However, with the right tools, techniques, and products, you can keep your curls looking healthy, bouncy, and beautiful. In this article, we'll share our top tips and tricks for managing long thick curly hair.

How to Manage Long Thick Curly Hair

The first step in managing long thick curly hair is to find the right products. You'll need a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair, as well as a leave-in conditioner, detangler, and styling products like gel or mousse. Once you have your products, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all of the product.

Step 2: Detangle

After washing, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, being gentle to avoid breakage.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Style

Apply your preferred styling products to your hair, then use a diffuser to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. If you don't have a diffuser, you can use a t-shirt to scrunch your hair as it dries.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to managing long thick curly hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Apply styling products like gel or mousse.
  5. Use a diffuser to dry your hair, or use a t-shirt to scrunch your hair as it dries.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a curl-defining cream to enhance your curls.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for managing long thick curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to reduce frizz.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing long thick curly hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Some people with curly hair can go a week or more between washes, while others may need to wash their hair every 2-3 days. Experiment to find a routine that works for you.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, but it's important to use heat protectant products and to avoid using heat styling tools too often. Straightening your hair too often can cause damage and breakage.

How can I prevent my curls from falling flat?

Use a curl-defining cream or mousse to enhance your curls, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to pin up sections of your hair to create more volume.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

To keep your long thick curly hair healthy and beautiful, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can manage your long thick curly hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
