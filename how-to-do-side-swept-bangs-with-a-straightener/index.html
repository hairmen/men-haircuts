How To Do Side Swept Bangs With A Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, side swept bangs might be the perfect option for you. Adding a little bit of fringe to your look can give you a whole new vibe. And the best part? You can create this look at home with just a straightener! In this guide, we'll show you how to do side swept bangs with a straightener in just a few easy steps.

Description

Side swept bangs are a versatile and flattering hairstyle that can suit almost any face shape. They're a great way to add some dimension and interest to your hair without committing to a full fringe. Side swept bangs are also relatively low maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways, making them a popular choice for many women.

How to Do Side Swept Bangs with a Straightener: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to start with clean, dry hair so that you can get the best results. Use a hair dryer to blow dry your hair, making sure that it's completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair so that you can focus on your bangs. Use a comb to create a straight line from the crown of your head to your temples, and clip the rest of your hair out of the way. This will help you to focus on your bangs without getting tangled up in the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Prep Your Bangs

Before you start straightening your bangs, it's important to prep them with a heat protectant spray. This will help to protect your hair from damage and ensure that your bangs stay smooth and sleek throughout the day.

Step 4: Straighten Your Bangs

Using a straightener, start at the roots of your bangs and slowly work your way down to the ends. Make sure to keep the straightener moving smoothly through your hair, and avoid clamping down too hard. This can cause damage and lead to split ends.

Step 5: Create the Side Sweep

Once your bangs are straightened, it's time to create the side sweep. Using your straightener, gently curve your bangs towards the side of your face. You can choose to sweep them all the way to one side, or create a more subtle sweep by just angling them slightly.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray. This will help to hold your bangs in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips for Perfect Side Swept Bangs

Here are a few additional tips to help you get the perfect side swept bangs:

  • Use a high-quality straightener with ceramic plates to ensure that your hair stays smooth and shiny.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Practice makes perfect â it might take a few tries to get your side sweep just right.
  • Experiment with different angles and styles to find what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

How often should I straighten my bangs?

You should avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage and lead to breakage. Aim to straighten your bangs no more than once or twice a week, and always use a heat protectant spray.

Can I create side swept bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can create side swept bangs with curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to straighten your hair and create a smooth side sweep. You may need to use a higher heat setting on your straightener and take extra care to protect your hair from damage.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

To keep your side swept bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and lead to breakage.

By following these simple tips, you can keep your side swept bangs looking great and enjoy your new hairstyle to the fullest.
