How To Straighten Medium Length Hair Without Heat

Written by Christine Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Straightening hair can be a tedious and time-consuming process, especially if you have medium length hair. Using heat to straighten your hair can be damaging in the long run, and it's always best to avoid it if possible. Fortunately, there are several ways to straighten medium length hair without heat. In this article, we'll go through the steps on how to straighten medium length hair without heat, tips and tricks to make the process easier, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Medium Length Hair Without Heat
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Straighten Medium Length Hair Without Heat

There are several ways to straighten medium length hair without heat. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Blow Drying

Blow drying is one of the easiest ways to straighten hair without heat. It's important to use a heat protectant spray before blow drying, as the high heat from the blow dryer can still cause damage to your hair.

2. Roller Set

A roller set is another way to straighten hair without heat. You will need to use rollers that are large enough to create a straight look. After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a straightening cream or serum, and then roll your hair with the rollers. Leave the rollers in for a few hours, or overnight, and then remove them.

3. Hair Bands

Using hair bands is a simple way to straighten your hair without heat. Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around a hair band. Leave the hair bands in for a few hours or overnight, and then remove them.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to straighten medium length hair without heat:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a straightening shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Straightening Product

Apply a straightening product to your hair, such as a straightening cream or serum. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair. Use a round brush to help straighten your hair as you blow dry.

Step 4: Roller Set or Use Hair Bands

Depending on the method you choose, either roller set or use hair bands to straighten your hair. Leave the rollers or hair bands in for a few hours, or overnight.

Step 5: Remove Rollers or Hair Bands

Carefully remove the rollers or hair bands from your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you straighten your hair without heat:

1. Use a Straightening Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a straightening shampoo and conditioner can help to make the straightening process easier. These products are formulated to help straighten hair and reduce frizz.

2. Use a Heat Protectant Spray

If you are blow drying your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

3. Don't Brush Wet Hair

Brushing wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I straighten my hair without heat if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat if you have curly hair. However, it may take longer and require more effort.

Q: Can I straighten my hair without heat if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat if you have thick hair. Using a roller set or hair bands may be more effective than blow drying.

Hair Care After Straightening

After straightening your hair without heat, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the straight look. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and maintain the straight look.

2. Avoid Moisture

Avoid getting your hair wet or exposing it to moisture, as this can cause it to revert back to its natural state.

3. Use a Dry Shampoo

Use a dry shampoo to help absorb any excess oil and maintain the straight look.

Straightening medium length hair without heat is possible with the right techniques and products. By following these steps and taking care of your hair afterwards, you can achieve a straight and sleek look without damaging your hair.
