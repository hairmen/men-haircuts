Best Hairstyles for Round Fat Face Male in 2023

If you have a round fat face and looking for a hairstyle that can flatter your features, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best hairstyles for round fat face male, how to achieve them and tips to maintain them. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

A round fat face can be a little challenging when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. The goal should be to create an illusion of a longer face and balance the proportion of the face. The right hairstyle can also help in enhancing your features and drawing attention away from the roundness of the face.


How to

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the widest point of your face, which is usually around the cheekbones. If the measurement is almost the same as the length of your face, then you have a round face.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your features. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, which can make it look even rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that have volume on the top and shorter sides.


Step by Step

One of the best hairstyles for round fat face male is the undercut. This hairstyle involves keeping the top hair long and styled back while the sides and back are trimmed short. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle:

  • Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it to add volume.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel to the top of your hair and comb it back.
  • Trim the sides and back of your hair using clippers, leaving a little length on top.
  • Blend the top and sides using scissors and a comb.
  • Style the top using your fingers to create a messy, textured look.
  • Finish with a hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that have volume on top and shorter sides.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a buzz cut with a round fat face?

A: Yes, you can wear a buzz cut with a round fat face. However, it's important to keep the sides slightly longer than the top to create balance.

Q: Can I wear a fringe with a round fat face?

A: Yes, you can wear a fringe with a round fat face. Opt for side-swept fringes that add length to the face.


Hair Care

Maintaining your hairstyle is just as important as choosing the right one. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as it can strip the natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair styling products as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape.

We hope this article has helped you find the perfect hairstyle for your round fat face. Remember, the key is to experiment and find the one that suits you best. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best!
