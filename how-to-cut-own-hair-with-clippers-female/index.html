How To Cut Your Own Hair With Clippers Female A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you are a woman who wants to use clippers. However, with the right tools and guidance, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will walk you through step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own hair with clippers, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Tools You Will Need

Before you start cutting your own hair with clippers, you will need to gather the following tools:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip
  • Handheld mirror
  • Hair ties or clips

Make sure that your clippers are fully charged, and the guards are properly attached before you begin.

How to Cut Your Own Hair with Clippers Female

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but with some patience and practice, anyone can do it. Here are some tips on how to cut your own hair with clippers:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry completely.
  2. Section your hair and tie or clip it away from the area you will be cutting.
  3. Attach the desired guard to your clippers and turn them on.
  4. Starting from the bottom, work your way up, using the clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. When you reach the top of your head, switch to scissors and begin cutting your hair in small sections.
  6. Use a comb to comb through each section before cutting it.
  7. Cut your hair in a straight line, trimming the ends as needed.
  8. Once you have finished cutting all of your hair, use the clippers to clean up the nape of your neck and around your ears.
  9. Check your hair in a handheld mirror to make sure that it is even and the desired length.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Perfect Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect cut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one as needed.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • If you are unsure, cut less hair than you think you need to.
  • Use scissors to blend the top and sides of your hair for a more natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair with clippers:

Can I cut my own hair with clippers if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, you may want to use scissors to cut your curls, as clippers can sometimes cause frizz.

What guard should I use to cut my hair?

It depends on the length you want. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one as needed.

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it with clippers?

No. However, wetting your hair can make it easier to work with.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it properly:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut your own hair with clippers and achieve a professional-looking cut. Whether you're looking to save money or just want to try something new, cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience.
