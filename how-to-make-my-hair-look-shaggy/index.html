How To Make Your Hair Look Shaggy

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to make your hair look shaggy! If you're looking for a relaxed and effortless hairstyle, then the shaggy look is perfect for you. In this article, we'll be providing you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve that messy, textured look that's so popular right now. We'll also be answering some frequently asked questions and giving you some tips on hair care. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Look Shaggy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Shaggy Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you begin, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. You shouldn't attempt this style if your hair is wet or damp, as it won't hold the shape properly. Here's how to make your hair look shaggy:

Step One: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four equal parts: two at the front and two at the back. Tie each section with a hair tie to keep it separate.

Step Two: Cut Your Hair

Using hair scissors or shears, cut your hair at an angle starting from the bottom. Cut each section to your desired length, making sure that the layers are uneven and choppy. You can also use a razor or thinning shears to create texture.

Step Three: Add Texture

Use a texturizing spray or hair wax to add texture to your hair. Apply the product to your fingertips and run them through your hair, focusing on the ends. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.

Step Four: Tousle Your Hair

Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create that messy, shaggy look. Scrunch and twist your hair to create more texture and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shaggy Look

Here are some tips to help you maintain your shaggy look:

  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oils and add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your shaggy style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair look shaggy without cutting it?

Yes, you can use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls, and then tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, shaggy look.

What products should I use to make my hair look shaggy?

You can use a texturizing spray, hair wax, or pomade to create texture and hold. Just be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

Can I make my hair look shaggy if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a shaggy look with short hair. Just make sure to cut your hair into layers and add texture with a texturizing spray or hair wax.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

We hope that you found our guide on how to make your hair look shaggy helpful! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
