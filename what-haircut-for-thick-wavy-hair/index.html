What Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Nov 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. The right haircut can make all the difference. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for thick wavy hair, how to maintain them, and some tips for styling.

Description

Thick wavy hair is characterized by its fullness, body, and natural texture. It can range from loose waves to tight curls and is often prone to frizz. It requires special care to maintain its shape and volume.

How to

The key to a great haircut for thick wavy hair is finding the right balance between weight and texture. You want to remove enough bulk to prevent your hair from looking weighed down, but not so much that it loses its natural body and bounce.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

2. Consider layers to add movement and texture to your hair.

3. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your hair look too heavy and flat.

4. Opt for a side-swept fringe to frame your face and soften your features.

5. Embrace your natural texture and avoid excessive heat styling.

Tips

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from damage and reduce frizz.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your natural waves.

4. Invest in a high-quality hair serum to add shine and control flyaways.

FAQ

Q: Can I get bangs with thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right style. Side-swept bangs are a great option, as they add softness and dimension to your hair without weighing it down.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. On average, you should aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a wavy haircut?

A: Yes, but it may require more effort and heat styling. To avoid damaging your hair, use a heat protectant spray and a low-heat setting on your straightener.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy, voluminous hair requires a consistent hair care routine. Here are some tips for keeping your thick wavy hair in top shape:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.

2. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and enhance its natural texture.

3. Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when necessary.

4. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

5. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut.

In conclusion, the best haircut for thick wavy hair is one that enhances your natural texture and adds movement and volume. By following these tips and maintaining a healthy hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking its best.
