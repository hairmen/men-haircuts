What Gel Is Good For Wavy Hair In 2023

Written by Bardi Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know that finding the right gel can make a huge difference in how your hair looks and feels. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your hair type. In this article, we will discuss what gel is good for wavy hair, how to use it, and some helpful tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks.

What is Gel?

Gel is a styling product that can help add volume, control frizz, and define curls in wavy hair. Most gels are water-based and contain ingredients like polymers, humectants, and emollients. These ingredients work together to create a flexible, long-lasting hold without leaving hair feeling sticky or crunchy.

How to Use Gel on Wavy Hair

Using gel on wavy hair is easy and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Wet Hair

Before applying gel, make sure your hair is clean and damp. You can either wash your hair with a gentle shampoo or wet it in the shower. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy.

Step 2: Apply Gel

Squeeze a dime-sized amount of gel into your palm and rub your hands together. Then, apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair to avoid clumping or weighing down your curls.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Once you've applied the gel, use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. This will help enhance your natural curls and create volume. Continue scrunching until you've achieved your desired level of definition and hold.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

After scrunching, let your hair air dry. Avoid using a hairdryer as it can cause frizz and damage your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can use your fingers to gently scrunch out any crunchiness that may have formed.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Beautiful Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to use gel on wavy hair, here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Wavy hair is prone to dryness and frizz, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, or argan oil.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, leading to split ends and breakage. Instead of using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, embrace your natural curls and waves by using gel to enhance them.

Tip 3: Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help restore moisture to your hair, leaving it soft, shiny, and healthy. Look for deep conditioners that contain ingredients like keratin, collagen, or avocado oil.

FAQ

Q: Can gel make my hair look greasy?

A: Gel can make your hair look greasy if you use too much or apply it to dry hair. To avoid this, use a dime-sized amount of gel on damp hair and distribute it evenly throughout your locks.

Q: How often should I use gel on my wavy hair?

A: It's best to use gel on wavy hair every other day or as needed. Overuse can lead to buildup and make your hair look dull and weighed down.

Hair Care for Beautiful Wavy Hair

By using the right gel and following these tips, you can maintain healthy, beautiful wavy hair all year round. Experiment with different gels to find the one that works best for your hair type and styling needs. With a little patience and care, you can achieve the perfect balance of definition, volume, and hold.
