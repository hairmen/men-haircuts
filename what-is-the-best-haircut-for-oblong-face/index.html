What Is The Best Haircut For Oblong Face

Written by Aprilia Oct 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face, you may wonder what haircut will flatter your features the best. An oblong face shape typically has a longer length than width, and it may have a pointy chin and high forehead. Fortunately, there are many haircuts that can complement your oblong face shape and enhance your natural beauty.

Description

The oblong face shape is sometimes called a rectangular face shape, and it can be a challenging shape to style. The goal of a great haircut for an oblong face shape is to create balance and harmony by adding width to the face and softening the angles. Some of the best haircuts for an oblong face shape include:

Long Layers

Long layers can frame the face and soften the angles of an oblong face shape. Layers that start at the chin or cheekbones can add width to the face and create a more balanced look.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob that falls just below the chin can be a great choice for an oblong face shape. This haircut can add width to the face and create a more balanced look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can break up the length of an oblong face shape and add balance to the face. Bangs that fall just below the eyebrows can create a more flattering look.

Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut can be a great choice for an oblong face shape. This haircut can add width to the face and create a more balanced look. A pixie cut with choppy layers or side-swept bangs can add even more dimension to the hair.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oblong Face

When choosing a haircut for an oblong face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create balance and harmony by adding width to the face and softening the angles. Second, you want to choose a haircut that complements your hair texture and personal style.

Here are some tips for choosing the best haircut for an oblong face shape:

Consider Your Hair Texture

When choosing a haircut, you want to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter haircut that adds volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a longer haircut that can support the weight of your hair.

Think About Your Personal Style

You want to choose a haircut that complements your personal style. If you prefer a classic look, a blunt bob or long layers may be a good choice. If you like to experiment with your hair, a short pixie cut or asymmetrical bob may be a good choice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for getting the best haircut for an oblong face shape:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

The first step in getting the best haircut for an oblong face shape is to consult with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist can help you choose the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Choose Your Haircut

Once you have consulted with your hairstylist, you can choose your haircut. You may want to bring in pictures of haircuts that you like to show your hairstylist.

Step 3: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before your haircut, you should wash and condition your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

You should dry your hair before your haircut. Your hairstylist may dampen your hair slightly, but it should not be soaking wet.

Step 5: Cut and Style Your Hair

Your hairstylist will cut and style your hair according to the haircut that you have chosen. They may also use styling products to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair to complement your oblong face shape:

Use a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. This can add width to the face and create a more balanced look.

Style Your Bangs

If you have side-swept bangs, consider styling them to frame your face. You can use a flat iron to create a sleek look or a curling iron to add some texture.

Experiment with Products

Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair texture and style. You may want to try volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, or smoothing serum.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for an oblong face with a high forehead?

A long, side-swept bang can be a great choice for an oblong face shape with a high forehead. The bangs can break up the length of the face and create a more balanced look.

Can an oblong face shape wear a bob?

Yes, an oblong face shape can wear a bob. A blunt bob that falls just below the chin can be a great choice for an oblong face shape.

Can an oblong face shape wear short hair?

Yes, an oblong face shape can wear short hair. A short pixie cut or asymmetrical bob can be a great choice for an oblong face shape.

Hair Care for Your Oblong Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your oblong face shape:

Get Regular Haircuts

You should get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your haircut. This can also prevent split ends and breakage.

Protect Your Hair

You should protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. You should also protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Condition Your Hair

You should condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated. You can use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are formulated for your hair type and style. You may want to use a volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, or smoothing serum.

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for an oblong face shape can be a challenge, but with the right haircut and styling tips, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a more balanced look. Consider your hair texture, personal style, and face shape when choosing a haircut, and experiment with different styling techniques to find the ones that work best for you.
