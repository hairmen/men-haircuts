Would I Look Good With Auburn Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Auburn hair is a beautiful shade that can suit a wide range of skin tones and eye colors. If you're considering switching up your hair color, you may be wondering if auburn is the right choice for you. In this guide, we'll answer the question, "Would I look good with auburn hair?" and provide tips for achieving and maintaining this stunning shade.

Description

Auburn hair is a reddish-brown color that falls somewhere between brown and red on the color spectrum. It's a warm, rich hue that can have different undertones depending on the specific shade. Some auburn shades have more of a reddish tone, while others have more brown or copper undertones.

How to Achieve Auburn Hair

If you're interested in trying auburn hair, there are a few different options for achieving the shade. Here are some of the most common methods:

Dyeing Your Hair at Home

If you're comfortable with dyeing your hair at home, you can purchase an auburn hair dye kit from your local drugstore or beauty supply store. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test to ensure you're not allergic to the dye. Keep in mind that the color may not turn out exactly as it appears on the box, especially if you're starting with a different hair color.

Visiting a Salon

If you're not confident in your ability to dye your hair at home, or if you want a more customized color, you can visit a salon for professional hair color services. A stylist can work with you to choose the perfect shade of auburn for your skin tone and apply the color evenly for a natural-looking result.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair Auburn

If you decide to dye your hair at home, follow these steps to achieve an auburn shade:

  1. Choose your shade of auburn hair dye and purchase a kit that includes gloves, developer, and instructions.
  2. Do a patch test to ensure you're not allergic to the dye.
  3. Wash and dry your hair, but do not condition it. This will help the dye adhere better to your hair.
  4. Put on the gloves and mix the dye according to the instructions.
  5. Apply the dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to saturate your hair evenly and avoid getting dye on your skin or clothing.
  6. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time, usually around 30 minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear.
  8. Apply the conditioner included in the kit and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.
  9. Style your hair as usual.

Tips for Maintaining Auburn Hair

Once you've achieved your desired shade of auburn, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it looking vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray when spending time in the sun, as UV rays can cause the color to fade.
  • Consider using a color-depositing shampoo or treatment to refresh your color between dye jobs.

FAQ

What skin tones look best with auburn hair?

Auburn hair can look great on a variety of skin tones, but it tends to be particularly flattering on those with warm undertones. If you have cool undertones, you may want to opt for a cooler shade of red or brown instead.

Is auburn hair high-maintenance?

Like any hair color, auburn hair requires some maintenance to keep it looking its best. However, it's not necessarily more high-maintenance than other shades. By using the right products and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your auburn color looking vibrant for weeks or even months.

Can I achieve auburn hair if I have dark hair?

If you have dark hair, achieving auburn hair may require more effort than if you have lighter hair. You may need to bleach your hair first to lighten it enough for the auburn dye to take. A professional stylist can help you achieve the look you want without damaging your hair.

Hair Care with Auburn Hair

Auburn hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your auburn hair:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Overall, auburn hair can be a stunning and flattering shade for many people. By following the tips and advice in this guide, you can determine whether auburn hair is a good choice for you and achieve and maintain the color with ease.
