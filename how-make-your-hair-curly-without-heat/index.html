How To Make Your Hair Curly Without Heat

Written by Aprilia Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat on your hair to get those perfect curls? Well, you don't have to anymore! There are plenty of ways to make your hair curly without heat. In this article, we will explore some of the best methods for achieving curly hair without using any heat. Let's dive in!

How to Make Your Hair Curly Without Heat

Here are some of the best methods for creating curly hair without using any heat:

Method 1: Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is a simple and easy way to get curly hair without using any heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it is about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Leave the braids in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. Unbraid your hair in the morning and use your fingers to separate the curls.

This method works best on damp hair, so make sure to apply some leave-in conditioner before you braid your hair. You can also add some hair gel to your hair before braiding to help hold the curls.

Method 2: Twist Your Hair

Twisting your hair is another great way to get curly hair without using heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it is about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  3. Leave the twists in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. Untwist your hair in the morning and use your fingers to separate the curls.

Like with braiding, this method works best on damp hair. You can also add some hair gel to your hair before twisting to help hold the curls.

Method 3: Use Foam Rollers

Foam rollers are a great tool for creating curly hair without using heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it is about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and roll each section onto a foam roller.
  3. Leave the rollers in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. Remove the rollers in the morning and use your fingers to separate the curls.

You can also add some hair gel to your hair before rolling to help hold the curls.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curly Without Heat

Here are some tips for getting the best results when making your hair curly without using heat:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair and prevent frizz.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Be patient! It may take some time to get the perfect curls, but with practice, you'll get there.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

Can I use these methods on any hair type?

Yes, these methods can be used on any hair type, but the results may vary depending on your hair texture.

How often can I use these methods?

You can use these methods as often as you like, but it's best to give your hair a break in between to avoid damage.

How long will the curls last?

The curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain the curls.

Do I need to use any products to maintain the curls?

You can use a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to help hold the curls in place, but it's not necessary.

And there you have it! With these methods and tips, you can achieve beautiful, curly hair without using any heat. Give them a try and see which one works best for you!
