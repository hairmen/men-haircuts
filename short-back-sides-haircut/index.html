Short Back Sides Haircut A Classic Style That Never Goes Out Of Fashion

Written by Eveline Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a timeless and stylish haircut that is both classic and modern? Look no further than the short back sides haircut. This style has been around for decades and is still one of the most popular choices for men of all ages. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting a short back sides haircut, provide tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description: What is a Short Back Sides Haircut?

The short back sides haircut is a classic style that involves cutting the hair on the back and sides of the head short, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a clean and sharp look that is easy to maintain and style. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back or textured, depending on your preference.

How to: Getting a Short Back Sides Haircut

Getting a short back sides haircut is relatively easy and can be done at any barbershop or hair salon. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber or Stylist

The first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting short back sides haircuts. Look for someone who has a good reputation and plenty of positive reviews from previous clients.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the hair on the back and sides of your head. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. A good rule of thumb is to keep the hair between 1/8 inch and 1 inch in length.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Barber or Stylist

Communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the length and style you want. Show them pictures if you have a specific look in mind. It's important to be clear and concise so that you get the haircut you want.

Step 4: Sit Back and Relax

Once you've communicated your wishes to your barber or stylist, sit back and relax while they work their magic. The short back sides haircut is relatively quick and easy, so you won't be in the chair for too long.

Tips: Maintaining Your Short Back Sides Haircut

Now that you have your new short back sides haircut, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Use the Right Styling Products

Use the right styling products to keep your hair looking its best. Use a light hold pomade or wax to style the hair on top, and a matte finish product to keep the back and sides looking neat and clean.

Tip 3: Visit Your Barber or Stylist Regularly

Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your short back sides haircut looking sharp. This will help prevent the hair from becoming too long and unkempt.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the short back sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the short back sides haircut is suitable for all face shapes. It can be tailored to suit your individual features and enhance your natural appearance.

Q: Will the short back sides haircut make my hair look thinner?

A: No, the short back sides haircut will not make your hair look thinner. In fact, it can create the illusion of thicker hair by adding texture and volume to the hair on top.

Q: Can I style my short back sides haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your short back sides haircut in various ways to suit your mood and occasion. You can slick it back for a formal look, or add texture for a more casual vibe.

Hair Care: Taking Care of Your Hair

Taking care of your hair is important to maintain a healthy and attractive appearance. Here are some general tips:

Tip 1: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins in your diet.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and blow dryers too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains UV protection. This will prevent your hair from becoming dry and damaged.

With these tips and tricks, you're now ready to rock your short back sides haircut with confidence and style!
