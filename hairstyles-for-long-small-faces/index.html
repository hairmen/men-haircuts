Hairstyles For Long Small Faces Flattering Haircuts For Your Face Shape

Written by William Aug 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long small face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a haircut that will flatter your facial features and balance out your proportions. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for long small faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Small Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Long Small Faces
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Small Faces

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Small Face

The key to finding the right hairstyle for your long small face is to create balance. You want to add volume to the sides of your face to make it appear wider and shorter, while keeping the length to a minimum to prevent it from looking even longer. Here are some hairstyles that can achieve this:

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic style that works well for long small faces. It's a short haircut that ends just below the chin, which helps to create the illusion of a wider face. You can add layers to the haircut to give it more texture and movement.

2. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a shorter haircut, a pixie cut can be a great option. It's a low-maintenance style that can be customized to suit your face shape. You can add layers to the top of the hair to create volume, while keeping the sides and back short.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Another way to add volume to the sides of your face is with side-swept bangs. They help to create the illusion of a wider face while framing your facial features. You can pair them with a bob haircut or a long layered haircut.

4. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help to balance out your facial features. You can add layers to the top of the hair to create volume, while keeping the length to a minimum to prevent it from looking even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your long small face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

1. Start with clean hair.

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, and make it easier to style.

2. Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the style in place. You can use a mousse, gel, or wax depending on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.

3. Blow-dry your hair.

Use a blow-dryer to dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and texture. If you're using a straightening iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Style your hair.

Once your hair is dry, style it using your fingers or a brush. You can add curls or waves to your hair, or leave it straight depending on the style you're trying to achieve.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair and keep it looking healthy:

1. Get regular trims.

Regular trims will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a deep conditioner.

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

3. Protect your hair from heat.

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Long Small Faces

1. Can I wear my hair long if I have a long small face?

Yes, you can wear your hair long if you have a long small face, but it's important to keep the length to a minimum to prevent it from looking even longer. You can add layers to your hair to create volume and movement.

2. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long small face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. These can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too straight, as they can make your face appear narrower.

Hair Care Tips for Long Small Faces

Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo.

Sulfate-free shampoos are gentler on your hair and won't strip it of its natural oils.

2. Brush your hair gently.

Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to brush your hair gently. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Protect your hair from the sun.

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can enhance your facial features and create a balanced look. Try out some of the hairstyles we've shared in this article to find the right one for you.
