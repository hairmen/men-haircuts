How To Choose The Right Hair Color For Your Skin Tone

Written by Christine Oct 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. Whether you’re looking to update your current look or try something new, it’s important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. In this article, we’ll take you through the steps of how to choose the right hair color for your skin tone and answer some frequently asked questions about hair color.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone

When choosing the right hair color for your skin tone, it’s important to consider a few key factors:

  • Your skin tone
  • Your eye color
  • Your natural hair color

Once you have these factors in mind, you can start to consider different hair colors that will complement your skin tone. Here are some general guidelines:

  • For warm skin tones (yellow or golden undertones), choose hair colors that are warm, like golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, or auburn.
  • For cool skin tones (pink or blue undertones), choose hair colors that are cool, like ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, or black.
  • For neutral skin tones (a mix of warm and cool undertones), you have more flexibility to choose a wider range of hair colors.

Keep in mind that these are general guidelines, and there are always exceptions. It’s also important to consider your eye color and natural hair color when choosing a hair color. For example, if you have naturally dark hair and brown eyes, you may want to consider a warm brown or auburn hair color to complement your features.

Step by Step Guide

If you’re still not sure how to choose the right hair color for your skin tone, here’s a step by step guide:

  1. Identify your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear a mix of both, you have neutral undertones.
  2. Consider your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colors like ash blonde or platinum blonde will complement your features. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde or auburn will complement your features.
  3. Think about your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, you may want to consider a warm brown or auburn hair color. If you have naturally light hair, you may want to consider a cool blonde or ash blonde hair color.
  4. Consult with a professional. If you’re still not sure which hair color to choose, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a hair color that will complement your skin tone and features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you don’t have a lot of time to spend on maintenance, choose a hair color that is low maintenance, like a natural shade that is close to your natural hair color.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment. If you’re feeling adventurous, try a hair color that is outside of your comfort zone. Just make sure it complements your skin tone.
  • Take care of your hair. No matter what hair color you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a different color if I have a warm skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a different color if you have a warm skin tone. Just make sure you choose a hair color that is warm, like golden blonde or auburn.

Q: What hair color is best for cool skin tones?

A: Cool hair colors like ash blonde or platinum blonde are best for cool skin tones.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hair color you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be a daunting task, but with these tips and guidelines, you’ll be well on your way to finding the perfect hair color for you. Remember to take care of your hair and enjoy your new look!
