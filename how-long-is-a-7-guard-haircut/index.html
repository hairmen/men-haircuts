Description

A 7 guard haircut is a style that is achieved using a clipper guard labeled as the number 7. The guard is attached to the clippers and cuts hair to a length of 7/8 inch or 22mm. This is a medium length style that is popular among men and women alike.

How to

Getting a 7 guard haircut is a simple process that can be done at home or in a salon. You will need a pair of clippers with a number 7 guard attached, a comb, and scissors.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it.

2. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

3. Attach the number 7 guard to your clippers.

4. Turn on the clippers and start at the back of your head, moving upwards towards the crown.

5. Work your way around the sides and back of your head, using the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to cut it evenly.

6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to create layers.

7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

1. Make sure your clippers are well maintained and oiled before use.

2. Always start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter length.

3. Use a comb to lift the hair and create an even cut.

4. Be careful around the ears and neckline, using a smaller guard or no guard at all in these areas.

5. Take your time and go slowly to get the best results.

FAQ

Q: How long will a 7 guard haircut last?

A: A 7 guard haircut can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

Q: Can I use a 7 guard on all hair types?

A: Yes, a 7 guard can be used on all hair types, but it may not look the same on everyone. It is best to consult with a stylist for advice on the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain a 7 guard haircut, it is important to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Trim any split ends as needed and visit a stylist regularly for touch-ups.

Overall, a 7 guard haircut is a versatile and easy-to-maintain style that can work for anyone. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
