How to Blow Dry Thick Curly Hair Straight Fast

Blow drying your hair straight can be a tricky process, especially if you have thick curly hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve straight locks in no time. Here are some steps you can follow to blow dry your hair straight fast:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start blow drying your hair, you need to prep it first. Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water, but don't rub your hair too hard as this can cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage. This is especially important if you're blow drying your hair straight on a regular basis. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair straight and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Choose the Right Blow Dryer

Choose a blow dryer that's powerful enough to handle your thick curly hair. Look for a dryer with a high wattage and multiple heat and speed settings. A diffuser attachment can also help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair from the roots to the ends, using a round brush to help smooth out your hair. Move the dryer around continuously to avoid overheating your hair. Make sure to dry each section completely before moving on to the next one.

Step 6: Finish with a Cold Shot

Finish blow drying your hair with a cold shot of air from your blow dryer. This will help to set your hair and reduce frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to blow dry thick curly hair straight fast:

  1. Prep your hair
  2. Apply heat protectant
  3. Divide your hair into sections
  4. Choose the right blow dryer
  5. Start blow drying
  6. Finish with a cold shot

Useful Tips

Here are some useful tips to help you blow dry your hair straight fast:

  • Invest in a good quality blow dryer with a high wattage and multiple heat and speed settings.
  • Use a diffuser attachment to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that you don't miss any areas.
  • Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you blow dry it.
  • Finish with a cold shot of air to set your hair and reduce frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about blowing drying thick curly hair straight:

How often should I blow dry my hair straight?

You should avoid blow drying your hair straight on a daily basis as this can cause heat damage and dry out your hair. Try to limit blow drying to once or twice a week, and use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair.

What kind of brush should I use to blow dry my hair straight?

A round brush can help to smooth out your hair and create a straight, sleek look. Look for a brush with natural bristles as these are gentler on your hair.

How can I prevent frizz when blow drying my hair straight?

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. You can also apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair before blow drying to help tame frizz.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your thick curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage and dryness.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know how to blow dry thick curly hair straight fast, you can rock a sleek and straight hairstyle anytime you want. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
