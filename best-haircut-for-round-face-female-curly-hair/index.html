The Best Haircut For Round Face Female Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face and curly hair, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want to accentuate your best features while also finding a style that complements your natural curls. In this guide, we will discuss the best haircut for round face female curly hair, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have curly hair, you want to find a haircut that elongates your face and complements your curls. The best haircut for round face female curly hair is one that adds height to the crown of your head while also accentuating your cheeks and jawline.

How to

When selecting a haircut for round face female curly hair, it is important to consider the shape of your face and the texture of your hair. A layered cut with long, face-framing layers is ideal for round faces, as it adds height to the crown of your head and elongates your face. For curly hair, a layered cut also helps to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Step by step

1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They will be able to assess your hair texture and face shape to recommend the best haircut for you.

2. Consider a layered cut with long, face-framing layers. This will add height to the crown of your head and elongate your face.

3. Opt for a cut that is shoulder-length or longer, as this will help to weigh down your curls and prevent frizz.

4. Use styling products specifically designed for curly hair to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Tips

1. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your face appear rounder.

2. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and accentuate your curls.

3. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut work for round face female curly hair?

A: While a short haircut can work for some round face shapes, it is generally not recommended for curly hair. Short hair can be difficult to maintain and style with curly hair, and it can also make your face appear rounder.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care

To keep your round face female curly hair looking its best, it is important to practice proper hair care. This includes:

1. Using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.

2. Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your curls.

3. Avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your curls.

4. Using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can disrupt your curls.

5. Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and selecting the right haircut for your round face female curly hair, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering hairstyle that accentuates your natural beauty.
