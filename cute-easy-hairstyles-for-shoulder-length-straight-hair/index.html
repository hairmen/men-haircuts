Cute Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Straight Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old ponytail or leave your hair down every day? Well, you're in luck because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing some cute easy hairstyles for shoulder length straight hair that you can do in just a few minutes. Whether you're running late for work or school or just want to switch up your look, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion.

How To

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Invest in quality hair accessories such as bobby pins, hair ties, and clips

Now let's get started!

Step By Step

Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still want it to look cute.

  1. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie
  2. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume
  3. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist them towards the back
  4. Secure the twists with bobby pins under the ponytail

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style.

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look

Side Braid

The side braid is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.

  1. Part your hair to one side
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front and divide it into three sections
  3. Start braiding the hair and add more hair to each section as you go
  4. Secure the braid with a hair tie

Tips

  • Use dry shampoo to add volume to your hair
  • Add a hair accessory such as a headband or hair clip to enhance your hairstyle
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I still do these hairstyles if my hair is layered?

A: Yes, these hairstyles can still work with layered hair. Just make sure to use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Q: How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

A: Each hairstyle should take no more than 10 minutes to do.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Now that you know some cute easy hairstyles for shoulder length straight hair, you can switch up your look and feel confident every day. Just remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!
