Long Curtain Fringe Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 21, 2023 · 3 min read
Long Curtain Fringe Round Face: The Ultimate Guide | Hair Care Tips

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that complements your round face? Long curtain fringe might be the perfect choice for you. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the long curtain fringe look, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Long Curtain Fringe
  • Tips for Long Curtain Fringe
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care and Maintenance

How to Achieve Long Curtain Fringe

Long curtain fringe is a versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to suit your individual face shape and style. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head on one side of the part.
  4. Hold the section of hair between two fingers.
  5. Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle of the section and moving towards the outer edge.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Blend the fringe into the rest of your hair by trimming the ends.
  8. Style your fringe by blow-drying it with a round brush or using a straightener for a sleeker look.
  9. Finish with hairspray or a styling product of your choice.

Remember, it's always a good idea to have a professional stylist cut your hair, especially if you're unsure about the technique.

Tips for Long Curtain Fringe

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long curtain fringe:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage from styling tools.
  • Avoid cutting your fringe too short, as it can make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers to your hairstyle to add dimension and movement.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about long curtain fringe:

What face shape is best suited for long curtain fringe?

Long curtain fringe works well for round and oval face shapes, as it helps to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

How often do I need to trim my fringe?

It's recommended to trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Can I still wear my hair up with long curtain fringe?

Absolutely! Long curtain fringe can be styled in various ways, including updos and ponytails.

Hair Care and Maintenance

To keep your long curtain fringe looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your long curtain fringe with confidence and style. Happy styling!
