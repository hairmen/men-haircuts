Black Male High Top Fade Haircuts The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the black male high top fade haircut. This classic style has been worn by some of the biggest names in music and entertainment and is still popular today. In this guide, we’ll show you how to get the perfect high top fade, along with tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a High Top Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your High Top Fade
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for High Top Fades

Description

The black male high top fade is a hairstyle that features short sides and a longer, flat top. The hair on top is usually cut into a square shape, giving it a boxy look. This style was popularized in the 1980s and 1990s, but it still remains popular today. Many celebrities, including Will Smith and Kendrick Lamar, have rocked the high top fade throughout the years.

How to Get a High Top Fade

Getting a high top fade is easy, but it does require some skill from your barber. You’ll need to find a barber who is experienced in cutting this style. When you go in for your appointment, make sure to bring a picture of the style you want. This will help your barber to understand exactly what you’re looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a high top fade:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Section off the top of your hair with a comb.
  3. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving it short.
  4. Blend the hair on top into the sides using scissors or clippers.
  5. Cut the hair on top into a square shape, leaving it longer than the sides.
  6. Style the top of your hair using a comb and hair gel.

Tips for Maintaining Your High Top Fade

Once you’ve got your high top fade, you’ll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back short.
  • Use a good quality hair gel to style the top of your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about the black male high top fade:

How long does a high top fade last?

A high top fade can last anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Is a high top fade suitable for all hair types?

A high top fade is best suited for hair that is thick and straight. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten it first to achieve the desired look.

Can I style my high top fade differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles for your high top fade. You can try slicking it back or adding a part, for example.

Hair Care for High Top Fades

Keeping your hair healthy is important for maintaining your high top fade. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect black male high top fade haircut. Whether you’re going for a retro look or a modern twist, this style is sure to turn heads.
