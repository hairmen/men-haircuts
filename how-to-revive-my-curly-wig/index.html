How To Revive My Curly Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wigs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without making any permanent changes. But if you have a curly wig, you may notice that it loses its curl pattern over time. This can be frustrating, especially if you love the look of your curly wig. The good news is that there are ways to revive your curly wig and bring it back to life. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to revive your curly wig.

Table of Contents

  • How to Revive My Curly Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Reviving Your Curly Wig
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Revive My Curly Wig

Reviving a curly wig requires a few simple steps. You'll need some basic supplies, including a spray bottle filled with water, a wide-tooth comb, and a curl-defining product. Follow these steps to revive your curly wig:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Fill a spray bottle with water and spritz your wig until it's damp but not soaking wet.

  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle any knots or tangles gently. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots.

  3. Apply a curl-defining product to your wig. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Start by applying a small amount to the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots, scrunching the hair as you go.

  4. Once you've applied the product, use your hands to scrunch the hair and encourage the curls to form.

  5. Let your wig air dry. You can place it on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it keep its shape.

  6. If your wig still looks a bit frizzy or dry, you can use a little bit of oil to tame any flyaways or add some shine.

Tips for Reviving Your Curly Wig

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you revive your curly wig:

  • Be gentle. Curly hair is delicate, so you don't want to be too rough when combing or styling your wig.

  • Use a wide-tooth comb. This will help prevent breakage and keep your wig looking its best.

  • Don't use too much product. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Too much product can weigh down your curls and make your wig look greasy.

  • Let your wig air dry. Using a hairdryer can cause frizz and damage your wig's curl pattern.

  • Store your wig properly. When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it keep its shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about reviving curly wigs:

  • How often should I revive my curly wig?

    • You should only need to revive your wig every few weeks or as needed. If you notice that your wig is losing its curl pattern, it's time to give it a little TLC.


  • What's the best way to store my curly wig?

    • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it keep its shape. You can also cover it with a wig cap to keep it dust-free.


  • Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly wig?

    • It's best to use products specifically designed for wigs. Regular shampoo and conditioner can be too harsh and strip your wig of its natural oils.


  • How can I prevent my curly wig from tangling?

    • Detangle your wig regularly and avoid sleeping in it or wearing it in windy conditions. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help prevent tangles.


Hair Care Tips for Your Curly Wig

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your curly wig looking its best:

  • Detangle your wig regularly using a wide-tooth comb.

  • Wash your wig every 6-8 wears or as needed using a wig shampoo and conditioner.

  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig, including curling irons, flat irons, and hairdryers.

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it keep its shape.

  • Avoid sleeping in your wig or wearing it in windy conditions.

Reviving your curly wig is easy with the right tools and techniques. Follow these tips and tricks to bring your wig back to life and keep it looking its best.
