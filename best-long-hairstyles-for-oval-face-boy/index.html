Best Long Hairstyles For Oval Face Boy

Written by Eveline Jun 22, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are a boy with an oval face shape, then you’re in luck! You have a versatile face shape that can pull off a variety of long hairstyles. Long hairstyles for oval face boys can help to accentuate your features, create a balanced look, and add dimension to your hair. In this article, we’ll explore the best long hairstyles for oval face boys, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile of all face shapes. It features balanced proportions, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being of similar width. This face shape can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, including long hairstyles. The best long hairstyles for oval face boys are those that add volume and dimension to the hair, and balance out the face shape.

How To

The best way to choose a long hairstyle that works for your oval face shape is to consider your hair type, texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect long hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair type: If you have thick hair, then you may want to opt for layered hairstyles that can add movement and volume to your hair. If you have fine hair, then you may want to consider straight hairstyles that can create the illusion of thickness.
  • Think about your face shape: While oval face shapes can pull off a variety of hairstyles, it’s still important to consider your specific features. If you have a long face shape, then you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much height, as this can elongate your face even more.
  • Look for inspiration: Browse through magazines, websites, and social media to find hairstyles that you like. Bring pictures to your hairstylist to help them understand the look you are going for.

Step by Step

Once you’ve chosen a long hairstyle for your oval face shape, here are some steps to help you style it:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Towel dry your hair, but don’t rub it too vigorously as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage while styling.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  • If you have curly hair, then you may want to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create your desired hairstyle. If you’re not sure how to use these tools, then consider watching online tutorials or consulting with your hairstylist.
  • Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray or styling product to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your long hairstyle:

  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hairstyles for oval face boys:

What are the best long hairstyles for oval face boys?

The best long hairstyles for oval face boys are those that add volume and dimension to the hair, and balance out the face shape. Some popular styles include layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and textured hairstyles.

How do I know if I have an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being of similar width. The length of the face is slightly longer than the width, and the jawline is gently curved rather than sharply angled.

Can I still wear a long hairstyle if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can still wear a long hairstyle if you have thin hair. Consider opting for straight hairstyles that can create the illusion of thickness, or layered hairstyles that can add movement and volume to your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is key to pulling off any hairstyle. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and ideas for long hairstyles for oval face boys, you are sure to find a style that suits your unique features and personal style. Remember to consult with your hairstylist for advice and tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
