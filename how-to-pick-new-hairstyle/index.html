How To Pick A New Hairstyle Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and looking to switch things up? Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a new look that suits you perfectly. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to pick a new hairstyle, from considering your face shape to finding inspiration for your new 'do. Let's get started!

How to Pick a New Hairstyle

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important things to consider when choosing a new hairstyle is your face shape. Certain styles will accentuate your features and flatter your face, while others may not be as complementary. Here are some general guidelines to follow based on your face shape:

  • Round Face: Choose styles with height at the crown and length below the chin to elongate the face.
  • Square Face: Soften strong jawlines with styles that have layers and wispy bangs.
  • Heart-Shaped Face: Styles that are chin-length or longer with layers around the jawline are most flattering.
  • Oval Face: Lucky you! Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle.

Remember, these are just general guidelines. Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful, regardless of your face shape.

Step 2: Find Inspiration

Once you have a general idea of what styles will flatter your face shape, it's time to find inspiration for your new 'do. Look through magazines, Pinterest boards, and Instagram profiles of hairstylists to find styles that catch your eye. Be sure to save pictures of styles you like so you can show them to your hairstylist.

Step 3: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is your best resource when it comes to choosing a new hairstyle. They can give you personalized recommendations based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Be sure to bring in pictures of styles you like and be open to their suggestions. Remember, they're the experts!

Tips for Choosing a New Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Are you low-maintenance or do you enjoy spending time styling your hair?
  • Think about the upkeep. Will this style require frequent trims or coloring?
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. You never know, you might love a style you never thought you could pull off!

FAQ About Choosing a New Hairstyle

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new hairstyle:

  • How often should I get my hair cut?
  • It depends on the style and your hair's growth rate. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.
  • What if I don't like my new hairstyle?
  • Don't panic! Hair grows back. Talk to your hairstylist about making adjustments or growing your hair out.
  • Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a new style?
  • Most likely, yes. Your hairstylist can recommend styles that will work with your desired look.

Hair Care for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Depending on your hair type, you may only need to wash it every other day or even less frequently.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to choosing a new hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to have fun with it and don't be afraid to try something new!
