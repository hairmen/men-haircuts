How To Give Super Straight Hair Volume In 2023

Description

Having super straight hair can be a blessing or a curse. While it can be easy to manage and style, it can also be difficult to add volume to. But fear not, because there are ways to give super straight hair volume without using too much heat or damaging your locks. In this blog post, we will be sharing some tips and tricks on how to give super straight hair volume in 2023.

How to

There are several ways to give super straight hair volume, including using volumizing products, blow drying your hair in a certain way, and adding texture to your locks. Below, we will outline some methods you can try to achieve the volume you desire.

Method 1: Use Volumizing Products

The first step to adding volume to your super straight hair is to use the right products. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that are specially formulated to add lift and body to your hair. You can also use volumizing mousses or sprays to give your hair extra oomph.

Method 2: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a simple yet effective way to add volume to your super straight locks. After washing and conditioning your hair, flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in that position. This will help lift your roots and create more volume.

Method 3: Add Texture to Your Hair

Adding texture to your hair can also help give it more volume. You can do this by using a texturizing spray or by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand. This will create waves or curls that will add body to your hair.

Step by Step

Now that you know some methods to give your super straight hair volume, here is a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using volumizing products.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  3. Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in that position.
  4. If desired, add texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or curling iron.
  5. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to add volume to your super straight hair:

  • Avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can make it look greasy and flat.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add more movement and bounce to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving super straight hair volume:

Q: Can I use a straightener to add volume to my hair?

A: While a straightener can be used to add some volume to your hair, it can also make it look flat and lifeless if not used properly. It's best to stick to the methods outlined above for the best results.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain volume?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how oily it gets. Generally, washing your hair every other day or every two days should help maintain volume without stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you should be well on your way to achieving voluminous, super straight hair in 2023. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using too much heat or damaging products, and your locks will thank you for it.
