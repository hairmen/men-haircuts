How To Make Long Hair Look Shorter A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Oct 28, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re tired of dealing with long hair, you might be wondering how to make it look shorter without reaching for the scissors. Fortunately, there are several ways to achieve this look without sacrificing your length. In this article, we’ll show you how to make long hair look shorter through a variety of styling techniques and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How To Make Long Hair Look Shorter
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Make Long Hair Look Shorter

There are several ways to make long hair look shorter, including:

  • Tucking hair behind the ears
  • Creating layers
  • Using braids
  • Trying different hair partings
  • Using hair accessories
  • Creating updos

Each of these techniques can help you achieve the appearance of shorter hair without having to actually cut it.

Tucking Hair Behind the Ears

One of the easiest ways to make long hair look shorter is to tuck it behind your ears. This technique works best if you have long, straight hair that’s not too thick. All you need to do is brush your hair back and tuck it behind your ears. You can use bobby pins to keep it in place if you need to.

Creating Layers

Another way to make long hair look shorter is to create layers. Layers can add dimension and movement to your hair, making it look shorter than it actually is. To create layers, you’ll need to visit a hair stylist who can cut your hair in a way that will help you achieve the desired look.

Using Braids

Braids are another great way to make long hair look shorter. You can create braids in a variety of styles, including French braids, fishtail braids, and Dutch braids. Braids can make your hair look shorter by pulling it away from your face and creating a more compact appearance.

Trying Different Hair Partings

Another way to make long hair look shorter is to experiment with different hair partings. Changing your parting can alter the way your hair falls and make it look shorter. For example, if you usually part your hair in the middle, try parting it on the side instead.

Using Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can also help you achieve the appearance of shorter hair. For example, you can use headbands or hair clips to pull your hair back and create a more compact look. You can also experiment with different types of accessories, such as scrunchies or hair ties.

Creating Updos

Updos are another great way to make long hair look shorter. By pulling your hair up and away from your face, you can create a more compact appearance. There are many different types of updos to choose from, including buns, chignons, and ponytails.

Step by Step Guide

To make long hair look shorter, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Choose a technique that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  3. Follow the instructions for that technique (e.g., tuck hair behind ears, create layers, etc.).
  4. Use hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to create a more compact appearance.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you make long hair look shorter:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to create depth and dimension.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.
  • Try a new haircut, such as a bob or lob, to achieve a shorter look without sacrificing too much length.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make long hair look shorter without cutting it?

Yes! There are many techniques you can use to make long hair look shorter without cutting it. Some of these techniques include tucking hair behind the ears, creating layers, and using braids.

Will using hair accessories really make my hair look shorter?

Yes! Using hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, can help you create a more compact appearance and make your hair look shorter.

Hair Care for Long Hair

If you have long hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these tips, you can help keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful.

In Conclusion

There are many ways to make long hair look shorter, from tucking it behind your ears to creating layers and using braids. By experimenting with different techniques and hair care tips, you can achieve the look you want without having to sacrifice your length.
