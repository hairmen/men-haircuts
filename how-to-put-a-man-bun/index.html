How To Put A Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy way to style your hair, a man bun might be the perfect solution. This versatile hairstyle can be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to the gym to a night out on the town. In this guide, we'll show you how to put a man bun in relaxed English language, so you can achieve the look you want without any confusion.

How to Put a Man Bun

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand:

  • Hair elastics
  • Comb
  • Hairpins

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or styling product to help keep your hair in place.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your fingers, gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Make sure the ponytail is centered and at the height you want your bun to be.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail until it starts to coil into a bun shape. Keep twisting until you reach the ends of your hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Bun

Use a hair elastic to secure the bun in place. Make sure the elastic is tight enough to hold the bun, but not so tight that it hurts your scalp.

Step 5: Tuck in Loose Strands

If you have any loose strands of hair, tuck them into the bun using hairpins. Make sure the pins are hidden and secure.

Tips for Putting in a Man Bun

Here are a few tips to help you perfect your man bun:

  • Experiment with different heights and styles to find the look that works best for you.
  • Use hair products like pomade or hairspray to help keep your bun in place.
  • If you have long hair, consider trimming the ends to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • If you're having trouble getting your bun to stay in place, try using two hair elastics instead of one.

FAQ About Man Buns

What if my hair is too short for a man bun?

If your hair is too short to gather into a ponytail, you may need to wait a few weeks or months until it grows longer. In the meantime, you can try other hairstyles like a slicked-back look or a messy fringe.

How can I keep my man bun looking neat and tidy?

Regular trims and grooming can help keep your man bun looking its best. You can also use hair products like gel or wax to help tame flyaways and keep your hair in place.

Hair Care for Man Buns

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, which can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to put a man bun, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a more casual vibe, a man bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of settings. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong.
