How To Style Loose Waves Short Hair

Written by Christine Jul 20, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have short hair and want to achieve a relaxed, beachy look, loose waves are the perfect style for you. Not only do they look effortless, but they also add volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will show you how to style loose waves short hair in a few easy steps.

How to

To achieve loose waves in short hair, you'll need a few tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Hairspray

Once you have your tools and products ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, take small sections of hair and curl them away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Continue curling all sections of your hair.

Step 3: Add Texture

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. Spray the product onto your hair, and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step 4: Set Your Style

To ensure your waves stay in place, use a light hold hairspray to set your style. Spray the product onto your hair from a distance, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect loose waves:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel size for looser waves.
  • Don't curl all the way to the ends of your hair, as this can create a more polished look.
  • Use your fingers to separate and tousle your curls for a more natural look.

FAQ

How long will loose waves last in short hair?

Loose waves can last up to 2-3 days in short hair, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain your style.

Can I achieve loose waves in short hair without heat styling?

Yes, you can achieve loose waves in short hair without heat styling by braiding your hair overnight and using a texturizing spray in the morning.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before heat styling, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair. Additionally, make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these easy steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect loose waves short hair look in no time. Whether you're going for a casual day look or a night out, loose waves are always a great option.
