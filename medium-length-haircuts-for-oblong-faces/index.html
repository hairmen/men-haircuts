Medium Length Haircuts For Oblong Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut to flatter your oblong face shape? Look no further, as we have compiled a complete guide to medium length haircuts that will enhance your natural features and bring out your best self. Keep reading to discover the best haircuts, how to achieve them, and tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Oblong faces are characterized by their long and narrow shape, with a high forehead and a pointy chin. The goal of a haircut for oblong faces is to create balance and soften the angles of the face. Medium length haircuts are a great option for oblong faces, as they provide versatility and allow for different styling options.

How to

Before getting a medium length haircut, it is important to consider the texture and thickness of your hair, as well as your personal style and preferences. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which haircut will best suit your face shape and features.

The most flattering medium length haircuts for oblong faces include layered cuts, shoulder-length bobs, and blunt cuts with textured ends. Layered cuts add volume and movement to the hair, while bobs bring attention to the jawline and create a more defined shape. Blunt cuts with textured ends soften the angles of the face and create a more feminine look.

Step-by-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a layered medium length haircut for oblong faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and divide it into sections.
  3. Begin cutting the hair at the back, creating long layers that gradually get shorter towards the front.
  4. Move on to the sides, cutting the hair in a diagonal direction to create a soft, feathery effect.
  5. Finish by trimming the ends and adding texture with thinning shears or a razor.
  6. Style your hair as desired, using a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or sleek straight locks.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a medium length haircut for oblong faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can elongate the face even further.
  • Consider adding bangs or fringe to your haircut, as they can shorten the forehead and balance out the face.
  • Use volumizing products to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as braids or updos, to change up your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts for oblong faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oblong face. However, it is recommended to add some texture or volume to your hair to create a more balanced look.

Q: Should I get a side part or a middle part with an oblong face?

A: Either part can work with an oblong face, but a middle part can help to create the illusion of width and balance out the face shape.

Q: What are some hair care tips for medium length haircuts?

A: Some hair care tips for medium length haircuts include using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, avoiding over-washing your hair to maintain natural oils, and regularly trimming the ends to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length haircut looking healthy and vibrant, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes using sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, applying a weekly hair mask, and protecting your hair from heat and environmental damage.

By following these tips and getting a medium length haircut that flatters your oblong face shape, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
