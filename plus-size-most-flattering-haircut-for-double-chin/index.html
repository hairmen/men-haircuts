Plus Size Most Flattering Haircut For Double Chin A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your plus-size figure and double chin? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the most flattering haircut for double chin that will make you look and feel like a million bucks. Whether you have long, short, or medium hair, we have got a hairstyle that will suit your face shape and body type.

Description

A double chin can be a source of insecurity for many women, but it doesn't have to be. With the right haircut, you can accentuate your best features and downplay your flaws. The key is to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and draws attention away from your chin. This means avoiding hairstyles that add volume to your jawline and opting for ones that create vertical lines.

How-To

Before we dive into the most flattering haircuts for double chin, let's first talk about how to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape is crucial when choosing a haircut that flatters your features. Here's how to do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a lipstick or a dry erase marker and trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you have drawn.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a haircut that complements it. Here are some of the most flattering haircuts for double chin based on face shape:

Round Face

If you have a round face, the most flattering haircut for your double chin is one that adds height to your crown and length to your face. This means choosing a hairstyle with layers that start at your cheekbones and end at your collarbone. You can also opt for a side-swept bang to create a diagonal line that elongates your face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your jawline, such as bobs that end at your chin.

Square Face

If you have a square face, the most flattering haircut for your double chin is one that softens your jawline. This means choosing a hairstyle with layers that start at your cheekbones and end at your collarbone. You can also opt for a side-swept bang to create a diagonal line that lengthens your face. Avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline, such as blunt bobs.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, the most flattering haircut for your double chin is one that balances your features. This means choosing a hairstyle with layers that start at your chin and end at your collarbone. You can also opt for a side-swept bang to create a diagonal line that softens your forehead. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your crown, such as short pixie cuts.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you are lucky because you can pull off almost any haircut. The most flattering haircut for your double chin is one that creates volume at your crown and lengthens your face. This means choosing a hairstyle with layers that start at your cheekbones and end at your collarbone. You can also opt for a middle part to create a vertical line that elongates your face.

Step-by-Step

Now that you know which haircut will flatter your double chin, let's talk about how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect hairstyle for your face shape:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to create volume at your crown.
  4. Curl your hair with a curling iron, focusing on the ends.
  5. Tease your hair at the crown to create more volume.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember to choose the right products for your hair type and to use heat protectant before styling with hot tools.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for your double chin:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your jawline.
  • Choose a hairstyle with layers that start at your cheekbones and end at your collarbone.
  • Opt for a side-swept bang to create a diagonal line that elongates your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline.
  • Use volumizing products to create more height at your crown.

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut for a double chin?

The most flattering haircut for a double chin is one that adds height to your crown and lengthens your face. This means choosing a hairstyle with layers that start at your cheekbones and end at your collarbone. You can also opt for a side-swept bang to create a diagonal line that elongates your face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a double chin?

You should avoid hairstyles that add volume to your jawline, such as bobs that end at your chin. Instead, choose a hairstyle with layers that start at your cheekbones and end at your collarbone.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for your double chin can be a daunting task, but with the right information, it doesn't have to be. By knowing your face shape and choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features, you can feel confident and beautiful in your own skin. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
