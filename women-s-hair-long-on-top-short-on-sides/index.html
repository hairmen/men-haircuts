Women s Hair Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, then the women's hair long on top short on sides might just be what you're looking for. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. It's a versatile look that can be styled in many different ways, and it works well with various hair types and face shapes.

Description

The women's hair long on top short on sides is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast in length that adds texture and dimension to the hairstyle. The longer hair on top can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, textured, or curly, while the shorter sides and back add a touch of edge and modernity to the look.

How to Get the Look

Getting the women's hair long on top short on sides look is not difficult, but it does require some skill and patience. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in getting the women's hair long on top short on sides look is to choose the right haircut. You can either go to a hair salon or do it yourself if you're confident with your hair-cutting skills. The key is to keep the hair on top long enough to style it in different ways, while keeping the sides and back short enough to create a contrast.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash and condition it first. This will remove any dirt and oil buildup, making it easier to style your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume and texture on the top of your hair. You can also use a styling product, such as hair wax or pomade, to add shine and hold to your hair. Don't forget to style the sides and back as well, using a clipper or a razor to create a clean and sharp look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect women's hair long on top short on sides look:

  • Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use a comb or a brush to style your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a professional hairstylist.
  • Maintain your hair by washing it regularly and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

FAQ

Is the women's hair long on top short on sides look suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle works well with various hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

Can I style my hair differently every day with this look?

Yes, one of the benefits of the women's hair long on top short on sides look is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can experiment with different hairstyles every day.

Do I need to use a lot of styling products to achieve this look?

Not necessarily. The amount of styling products you use depends on how much hold and shine you want to add to your hair. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Hair Care

Maintaining your hair is important to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and flat irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect women's hair long on top short on sides look and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. Experiment with different styles and products until you find the one that works best for you.
