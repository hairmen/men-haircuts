What Hair Colour Will Suit Me Best Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair colour, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. With so many shades and tones to choose from, it can be difficult to know what will suit you best. That's where our hair colour quiz comes in! By answering a few simple questions, you can find your perfect shade that will compliment your skin tone and features. Let's get started!

Description

Our hair colour quiz is a quick and easy way to find the perfect shade for you. It takes into account your skin tone, eye colour, and other features to determine what will suit you best. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just a subtle update, our quiz will help you make the right decision.

How to

To take the quiz, simply answer the questions below. Be honest and choose the answers that best describe you. At the end, we'll reveal your perfect hair colour match!

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the perfect hair colour for you is to determine your skin tone. Are you cool, warm, or neutral?

If you have cool undertones, your skin will have pink, red, or blue undertones. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool tones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining what hair colour will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, cool tones like ash blonde or platinum may be the way to go. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warmer tones like golden blonde or honey may be more flattering.

Step 3: Think About Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also provide some guidance on what shades will suit you best. If you have naturally light hair, going too dark may be too drastic of a change. If you have naturally dark hair, going too light may not look as natural.

Step 4: Choose Your Style

Are you looking for a dramatic change or just a subtle update? Do you want to go all-over colour or just add some highlights? Consider your personal style and preferences when choosing your perfect shade.

Tips

When choosing a new hair colour, it's important to consider maintenance. Are you willing to commit to regular touch-ups or are you looking for something low-maintenance? Talk to your stylist about what will work best for your lifestyle and budget.

FAQ

Q: How often should I touch up my hair colour?

A: This will depend on the type of colour and your natural hair growth rate. Generally, all-over colour should be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while highlights can go 8-12 weeks between touch-ups.

Q: Will a dramatic hair colour change damage my hair?

A: Any hair colour change can be damaging to some degree, but working with a professional stylist and using quality products can minimize damage.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend working with a professional stylist to ensure the best results.

Hair Care

Once you've found your perfect shade, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for coloured hair, and avoid over-washing and heat styling. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Congratulations on finding your perfect hair colour match! Whether you're going for a subtle update or a dramatic change, your new shade is sure to turn heads. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
