How To Style Long Messy Hair Guys

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with long, messy hair, you probably already know that it can be a challenge to keep it looking stylish and put-together. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a cool, casual look that suits your personality and style. In this article, we'll show you how to style long messy hair guys with some simple steps and tips.

How to Style Long Messy Hair Guys

The first step to styling long, messy hair is to make sure it's clean and healthy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft, shiny, and free from tangles. Once your hair is clean, you can start to style it.

One of the easiest ways to style long, messy hair is to use a styling product like pomade or wax. These products help to create texture and hold, so your hair stays in place throughout the day. To use a styling product, simply rub a small amount between your palms and apply it evenly throughout your hair. You can then use your fingers or a comb to style your hair in any direction you like.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style long, messy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your palms and rub them together.
  4. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that need extra hold.
  5. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair in any direction you like.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Messy Hair Guys

Here are some tips for styling long, messy hair:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and techniques. Messy hair is all about being creative and having fun.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling it. This will help prevent breakage and damage.
  • If you have curly hair, try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Don't overuse styling products. A little goes a long way, and too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long, messy hair:

Q: How often should I wash my long, messy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair and sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair daily or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I style my long, messy hair without using any products?

A: Yes, you can. However, using a styling product can help create texture and hold, so your hairstyle lasts longer.

Hair Care for Long Messy Hair Guys

Here are some tips for keeping your long, messy hair healthy:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your hair and scalp.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your long, messy hair to perfection. Remember to be creative, experiment with different styles, and have fun with your hair.
