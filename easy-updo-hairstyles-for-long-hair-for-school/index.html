Easy Updo Hairstyles For Long Hair For School

Written by Robby Jun 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle for school every day? Spice things up with these easy updo hairstyles for long hair. These hairstyles are perfect for school because they are quick to do and will keep your hair out of your face all day long. Here are some of our favorite updos:

Description

Updo hairstyles are perfect for long hair because they keep your hair out of your face and off your neck. They are also great for school because they are quick and easy to do. There are many different types of updos, so you can choose the one that works best for you.

How to

To create an updo hairstyle, start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, decide where you want to place your updo. You can choose to have it high on your head or lower down.

Next, gather your hair into a ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Then, twist your ponytail until it begins to coil around itself. Once it has coiled, secure it with bobby pins.

If you want to add some extra flair to your updo, try braiding your hair before you twist it. You can also add in some accessories like hair clips or bows.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide for creating a simple updo:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Twist your ponytail until it begins to coil around itself.
  4. Secure the coiled hair with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating the perfect updo:

  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to make them less noticeable.
  • Don't make your updo too tight, as it can cause headaches and discomfort.
  • Experiment with different types of updos to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use accessories like hair clips or bows to add some extra flair to your updo.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles:

Q: How long does it take to create an updo?

A: It depends on the type of updo you want to create, but most updos can be done in less than 10 minutes.

Q: Do I need to use hairspray to keep my updo in place?

A: It's not necessary, but hairspray can help keep your updo in place all day.

Q: Can I create an updo with short hair?

A: It's more difficult to create an updo with short hair, but it's not impossible. Try using bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy while wearing updos, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. If you use hair accessories like clips or bows, make sure they are not too tight and don't pull on your hair.

With these easy updo hairstyles for long hair, you'll be able to switch up your look for school every day. These hairstyles are quick, easy, and will keep your hair out of your face all day long.
