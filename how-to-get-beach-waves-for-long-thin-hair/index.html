How To Get Beach Waves For Long Thin Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your long thin hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to achieve those effortless beach waves that look so effortlessly chic? Look no further! This guide will show you how to get beach waves for long thin hair in just a few easy steps.

Description

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that gives an effortless, tousled look. This hairstyle is perfect for those with long thin hair who want to add volume and texture to their hair. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve beach waves for long thin hair.

How To

To achieve beach waves for long thin hair, you will need a few tools and products:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Sea salt spray
  • Hairspray
  • Wide-tooth comb

Now that you have your tools and products, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, holding it for a few seconds until it curls. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 3: Add Texture

Once you have curled your hair, use a sea salt spray to add texture and create that beachy look. Spray the sea salt spray onto your hair, scrunching it with your fingers to create waves.

Step 4: Set Your Hair

Finally, use a hairspray to set your hair in place. Hold the hairspray about 6 inches away from your hair and spray it evenly over your hair.

Tips

  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • Use a smaller curling iron or wand for tighter curls.
  • Adjust the heat setting on your curling iron or wand depending on your hair type.
  • Use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair to enhance your natural waves.

FAQ

How long does it take to get beach waves?

The time it takes to get beach waves depends on your hair length and thickness. On average, it takes about 20-30 minutes to curl your hair and add texture.

How can I make my beach waves last longer?

To make your beach waves last longer, use a hairspray to set your hair and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Can I achieve beach waves without heat?

Yes, you can achieve beach waves without heat by braiding your hair before bed and leaving the braids in overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you will have natural waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.

With these simple steps, you can achieve beautiful beach waves for your long thin hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Happy styling!
