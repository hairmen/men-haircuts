Shaved Hair Sides Long Top Female A Bold And Edgy Hairstyle

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, then the shaved hair sides long top female is definitely worth considering. This hairstyle is characterized by having a shaved or buzzed section on the sides of the head, while the top part of the hair is left long and styled in different ways. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personality and style. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle.

Description

The shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle is a popular choice among women who want to make a statement. The sides of the head are shaved or buzzed, while the top part of the hair is left long and can be styled in different ways. The length of the top part of the hair can vary, depending on personal preference. Some women prefer to keep it short, while others like to have long locks that can be styled in different ways. The shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle is a bold and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

How to

The shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the top part of the hair. You can choose to keep it short or long, depending on your personal preference.

Step 2: Shave the Sides

The next step is to shave or buzz the sides of the head. This can be done by a professional hair stylist or at home using clippers. It's important to use the right guard size to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once the sides are shaved, you can style the top part of the hair in different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, or wavy, depending on your preference. You can also experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle:

  • Use a good quality hair product to keep the top part of the hair in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Keep the sides of the head shaved or buzzed regularly to maintain the look.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to achieve the desired look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different face shapes. A professional hair stylist can help you choose the right length and style for your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with this hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hat with this hairstyle. However, it's important to choose a hat that complements the style of the shaved hair sides long top female.

Q: Is this hairstyle difficult to maintain?

A: The shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle is relatively easy to maintain. You just need to keep the sides shaved or buzzed regularly and use a good quality hair product to keep the top part of the hair in place.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling the hair to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of the hairstyle.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

The shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle is a bold and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. With the right length, style, and maintenance, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different personalities and styles. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a messy and playful vibe, the shaved hair sides long top female hairstyle is definitely worth considering.
