How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers Curly

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to note that cutting curly hair with clippers can be a bit more challenging than cutting straight hair. Curly hair tends to have a mind of its own, and it can be difficult to get an even cut. That said, with a bit of practice and patience, you can achieve great results.

The first step is to gather the right tools. You'll need a set of clippers with adjustable guards, a comb, and a pair of scissors. You may also want to have a mirror handy so you can check your progress as you go.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to cut men's hair with clippers curly:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so you want to make sure you're working with the hair at its natural length.
  2. Next, comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Choose the guard size you want to use based on the length of the hair you want to leave. If you're unsure, start with a larger guard and work your way down.
  4. Starting at the back of the head, use the clippers to trim the hair. Work in small sections, moving the clippers against the direction of hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any longer strands.
  5. Move to the sides of the head, using the clippers to trim the hair in the same manner.
  6. For the top of the head, use the scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Start by combing the hair straight up, then trim any longer strands. Use the scissors to create texture and movement, as needed.
  7. Check your work in the mirror, making any necessary adjustments.
  8. Use the clippers to clean up the hairline around the ears and neckline.
  9. Finish with a styling product to add definition and hold.

Tips for Cutting Men's Curly Hair with Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting men's curly hair with clippers:

  • Start with a larger guard size, then work your way down as needed. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Work in small sections, moving the clippers against the direction of hair growth. This will help you achieve a more even cut.
  • Use the scissors to add texture and movement to the hair. This will prevent the hair from looking too flat or lifeless.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting curly hair can be more time-consuming than cutting straight hair, but the results are worth it.

FAQ

Here are answers to some common questions about cutting men's curly hair with clippers:

Can you cut men's curly hair with clippers?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut men's curly hair. However, it can be more challenging than cutting straight hair, so it's important to use the right techniques and tools.

What guard size should I use for men's curly hair?

The guard size you use will depend on the length of the hair you want to leave. If you're unsure, start with a larger guard and work your way down.

Can I use scissors to cut men's curly hair?

Yes, you can use scissors to cut men's curly hair. In fact, using scissors can help you achieve a more natural-looking cut with texture and movement.

Hair Care for Men's Curly Hair

Once you've cut your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for caring for men's curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair and cause it to become frizzy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to define the curls and add hold.
  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking cut for men's curly hair with clippers. With practice, you'll become more confident and skilled at cutting curly hair, and you'll be able to create a variety of styles for yourself or your clients.
