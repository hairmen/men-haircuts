What is Finasteride?

Finasteride is a medication that is used to treat hair loss in men. It is a type of medication called a 5-alpha-reductase inhibitor. This means that it works by blocking the production of a hormone called DHT, which is responsible for causing hair loss in men.

How Does Finasteride Work?

Finasteride works by blocking the production of DHT, which is a hormone that is responsible for causing hair loss in men. By reducing the levels of DHT in the body, Finasteride can help to slow down or even reverse hair loss. It is important to note that Finasteride is not a cure for hair loss, but rather a treatment that can help to manage the symptoms.

Does Finasteride Reverse Hair Loss?

Yes, Finasteride can help to reverse hair loss in men. In clinical trials, Finasteride was found to be effective at reducing hair loss and promoting hair growth. In fact, it was found that 83% of men who took Finasteride experienced an increase in hair growth, compared to just 28% of men who took a placebo.

It is important to note, however, that the results of Finasteride can vary from person to person. Some men may experience more significant hair growth than others, while others may not see any improvement at all. Additionally, Finasteride only works for as long as it is taken. If you stop taking the medication, any hair that has been regrown will likely fall out.

How to Use Finasteride

Finasteride is available in the form of a tablet that is taken orally. The recommended dosage is 1mg per day, taken with or without food. It is important to take Finasteride exactly as prescribed by your doctor, and not to take more or less than the recommended dosage.

Tips for Using Finasteride

If you are considering using Finasteride to treat your hair loss, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Speak with your doctor if you have any concerns about using Finasteride.
