Which Hairstyle Suits For Big Forehead Indian Female

Are you an Indian female with a big forehead? Do you struggle to find the right hairstyle that compliments your facial features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss which hairstyle suits for big forehead Indian female. We will cover everything from how to pick the right hairstyle, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care. Let's dive in!

How to Pick the Right Hairstyle

The key to finding the right hairstyle for a big forehead is to balance it out with the rest of your facial features. Here are some tips on how to pick the right hairstyle:

  • Choose hairstyles that have bangs or fringes. This will help to cover up the forehead and create an illusion of a smaller forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or pulled tightly. This will only accentuate the size of the forehead.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head. This will shift the focus away from the forehead.
  • Consider getting layers. This will add dimension to your hair and create a balanced look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know how to pick the right hairstyle, here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some popular hairstyles:

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Part your hair to one side.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it forward.
  3. Cut the hair to your desired length.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have achieved your desired look.

Layered Hair

  1. Start with dry, straightened hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, right, left, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and cut layers into the hair.
  4. Move on to the side sections and cut layers into the hair.
  5. Finish with the top section and cut layers into the hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a big forehead:

  • Use hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short. Longer hair will help to balance out the forehead.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions regarding hairstyles for big forehead Indian females:

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, but make sure to leave some hair in the front to create a balanced look.

Q: Should I get bangs if I have a big forehead?

A: Bangs can be a great option for covering up a big forehead. However, make sure to choose a style that flatters your facial features.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight?

A: Yes, but consider adding layers or volume to create a balanced look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use hair masks or oil treatments to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know which hairstyle suits for big forehead Indian female, it's time to experiment and find the one that works best for you. Remember, confidence is key!
