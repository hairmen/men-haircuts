Short Sides Haircut Boy A Trendy And Low Maintenance Hairstyle

Short sides haircut for boys has been a popular hairstyle for several years now, and it is not going out of trend anytime soon. This hairstyle is perfect for boys who want to look stylish and trendy with minimal effort. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short sides haircut boy, from how to get it to tips for styling and hair care.

Description

Short sides haircut boy is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head short while leaving the hair on the top longer. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair on top can vary, depending on personal preference and face shape. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in different ways, making it a popular choice among boys of all ages.

How to Get a Short Sides Haircut for Boys

Getting a short sides haircut for boys is relatively easy, and you can either do it at home or go to a hair salon. If you are confident enough to do it yourself, you will need a pair of clippers, a comb, and scissors. Start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head with the clippers, using a lower guard size. Then, blend the hair on top with the sides and back, using scissors. If you are not comfortable doing it yourself, you can always go to a hair salon and ask for a short sides haircut.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get a short sides haircut for boys:

  1. Start by washing the hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a comb to part the hair where you want it to be.
  3. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head, using a lower guard size.
  4. Blend the hair on top with the sides and back, using scissors.
  5. Style the hair on top as desired, using a hair product.

Tips for Styling a Short Sides Haircut

Styling a short sides haircut boy is easy, and you can do it in different ways, depending on your preference. Here are some tips for styling a short sides haircut:

  • Use a hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style the hair on top.
  • Try different hairstyles, such as a slick back or a messy look.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short sides haircut boy:

How often should I get a short sides haircut for my boy?

The frequency of getting a short sides haircut depends on how fast your boy's hair grows. On average, you should get a haircut every four to six weeks.

Can my boy with curly hair get a short sides haircut?

Yes, boys with curly hair can get a short sides haircut. However, you may need to leave the hair on top longer to avoid the curls looking too short.

Is a short sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a short sides haircut is suitable for all face shapes. However, you may need to adjust the length of the hair on top to suit the face shape.

Maintenance and Hair Care

Maintaining a short sides haircut boy is easy, and you can do it at home. Here are some tips for maintaining and caring for a short sides haircut:

  • Keep the hair clean by washing it regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Trim the hair on the sides and back of the head every two to three weeks to maintain the length.
  • Use a hair product to style the hair on top, and avoid using too much product as it can weigh down the hair.

Final Thoughts

Short sides haircut boy is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for boys of all ages. It is easy to style and maintain, and you can do it at home or go to a hair salon. Whether you prefer a slick back or a messy look, a short sides haircut can be styled in different ways to suit your preference. With proper maintenance and care, a short sides haircut can last for several weeks, making it a convenient choice for busy parents and boys.
