What Face Shape Do Guys Find Most Attractive

Written by Petter Nov 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're curious about what face shape guys find most attractive, you're not alone. While it's important to remember that everyone has their own tastes and preferences, there are certain facial features that tend to be universally appealing. In this article, we'll explore what those features are and how you can accentuate them to enhance your natural beauty.

Description

When it comes to facial attractiveness, studies have shown that men tend to prefer women with symmetrical features and a "heart-shaped" face. This means a face with a narrow jawline, high cheekbones, and a broad forehead. However, it's important to remember that beauty is subjective and what one person finds attractive may not be the same for another.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can enhance your natural beauty, you need to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face. Then, use a tape measure or ruler to measure the distance between the following points:

  • Forehead - Widest point of your forehead
  • Cheeks - Widest point of your cheekbones
  • Jawline - From the tip of your chin to the base of your earlobe

Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

  • Oval - Forehead and cheeks are roughly the same width, with a gently curved jawline
  • Round - Forehead and cheeks are roughly the same width, with a rounded jawline
  • Square - Forehead, cheeks, and jawline are roughly the same width, with a sharp jawline
  • Heart - Forehead is wider than cheeks, with a narrow jawline
  • Diamond - Cheekbones are the widest point, with a narrow forehead and jawline
  • Triangle - Jawline is wider than forehead and cheeks, with a pointed chin

Step-By-Step Guide To Enhancing Your Face Shape

Once you know your face shape, you can use makeup and hairstyles to enhance your natural beauty. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  • Oval: You're lucky! An oval face is considered the most balanced and symmetrical, so you don't need to do much to enhance your features. Just focus on maintaining clear skin and keeping your face hydrated.
  • Round: To elongate your face, try contouring with a bronzer or darker foundation along your jawline and temples. You can also play with hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of your head.
  • Square: Soften your features by contouring along your jawline with a lighter foundation or highlighter. You can also try side-swept bangs or loose waves to add some softness around your face.
  • Heart: Balance out your face by adding volume to your chin with a darker foundation or contour powder. You can also try hairstyles that add volume to the bottom half of your face, such as a bob or layered cut.
  • Diamond: To highlight your cheekbones, use a highlighter along your cheekbones and temples. You can also try hairstyles that pull your hair away from your face, such as a high ponytail or updo.
  • Triangle: Balance out your face by adding volume to the top half. You can do this with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, or by trying hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head.

Tips For Accentuating Your Best Features

Regardless of your face shape, there are certain facial features that tend to be universally attractive. Here are some tips for accentuating your best features:

  • Eyes: Use mascara and eyeliner to enhance your eyes. You can also use eyeshadow to bring out the color of your eyes.
  • Lips: Use a lip liner to define your lips, and choose a lipstick shade that complements your skin tone.
  • Cheekbones: Use a blush or contour powder to highlight your cheekbones.
  • Skin: Keep your skin hydrated by drinking plenty of water and using moisturizer daily. You can also use a facial oil to add extra hydration.

FAQs About Face Shape and Attraction

Here are some frequently asked questions about face shape and attraction:

  • What is the most attractive face shape? While beauty is subjective, studies have shown that men tend to prefer women with a symmetrical face and a "heart-shaped" face.
  • Can you change your face shape? While you can't change your bone structure, you can use makeup and hairstyles to enhance your natural features.
  • Do men prefer women with a certain nose shape? Again, beauty is subjective, but studies have shown that men tend to prefer noses that are slightly upturned.

Hair Care Tips For Your Face Shape

Your hairstyle can have a big impact on the way your face looks. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle will look good on you, but consider a lob or layered cut to add some volume.
  • Round: Try a long, layered cut or a side-swept fringe to elongate your face.
  • Square: Soften your features with a layered cut or loose waves.
  • Heart: Try a bob or a layered cut that adds volume to the bottom half of your face.
  • Diamond: Pull your hair away from your face with a high ponytail or try a layered cut that adds volume to the top half of your face.
  • Triangle: Add volume to the top half of your face with a voluminous updo or a layered cut.

Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin. Use these tips as a guide, but don't be afraid to experiment and try new things. After all, beauty is all about self-expression!
