Does Curly Hair Make You Look Older

Written by William Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Description | How to | Step by Step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

Curly hair is often associated with youthfulness and playfulness. However, as we age, our hair naturally loses its elasticity and bounce, which can make curly hair look frizzy and unkempt. Additionally, curly hair has a tendency to shrink up, which can make the face look more round and less defined. These factors, combined with the fact that curly hair is often more difficult to maintain and style, can make curly hair make you look older.

How to

If you have naturally curly hair, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking youthful and healthy:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils, which can make curly hair look dry and frizzy. Instead, opt for a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that will gently cleanse the hair without stripping it of its natural moisture.

2. Use a diffuser when blow-drying

A diffuser attachment on your blow dryer can help to distribute the heat evenly throughout your hair, which can help to reduce frizz and define your curls.

3. Avoid using heat tools too often

Excessive heat styling can damage the hair and cause it to lose its elasticity. Instead of using heat tools every day, try to air dry your hair as often as possible, and use heat tools sparingly.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Shampoo and condition your hair with a sulfate-free product, and then gently towel dry your hair until it is damp.

2. Apply a curl-defining product

Apply a curl-defining product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

3. Use a diffuser to dry your hair

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer, and use it to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Gently scrunch your hair as you dry it to help define your curls.

4. Finish with a hair oil or serum

Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your curly hair looking youthful:

1. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make curly hair look frizzy and unkempt.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as braids, buns, and updos. These styles can help to keep your hair looking fresh and youthful.

3. Use a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce friction and prevent frizz, which can help to keep your curls looking defined and bouncy.

FAQ

Does cutting my hair short make it look younger?

Shorter hair can make the face look more defined and youthful, but it ultimately depends on your face shape and personal style preferences. If you have naturally curly hair, a shorter haircut can help to reduce the appearance of shrinkage and make your curls look more defined.

Can I still color my curly hair?

Yes, you can still color your curly hair! However, it's important to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color and prevent it from fading.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for keeping your curly hair healthy:

1. Deep condition once a week

Deep conditioning can help to restore moisture to your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, can pull on the hair and cause it to break. Instead, opt for looser hairstyles that won't put too much stress on your hair.

In conclusion, while curly hair can be a beautiful and playful hairstyle, it can also make you look older if not cared for properly. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your curls looking defined and youthful for years to come.
