How To Trim Your Own Hair Straight Across The Back A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? Or maybe you’re just looking to save some time by cutting your own hair at home? Whatever the reason, learning how to trim your own hair straight across the back can be a game-changer. In this article, we’ll provide you with a complete guide on how to do just that. Description

Trimming your own hair straight across the back can seem daunting at first, but with a few simple steps, it’s actually quite easy. Not only will you save money and time, but you’ll also have the satisfaction of knowing you did it yourself. It’s important to note that this method is best suited for those with long hair, as it can be difficult to reach the back of your head if you have short hair.

How to Trim Your Own Hair Straight Across the Back

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to have the right tools on hand. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A handheld mirror
  • A large mirror
  • Clips or hair ties to section off your hair

Now that you have the tools, let’s get started!

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to trim your own hair straight across the back:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will give you a better idea of how much hair you need to trim.
  2. Section off your hair. Use clips or hair ties to separate your hair into two sections: the top section and the bottom section. The bottom section should be the larger of the two.
  3. Take the bottom section of your hair and gather it at the nape of your neck. Use the comb to comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to trim the ends straight across. Be sure to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  5. Release the bottom section of your hair and comb it out.
  6. Take the top section of your hair and gather it at the crown of your head. Use the comb to comb it straight down.
  7. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to trim the ends straight across. Be sure to match the length with the bottom section.
  8. Release the top section of your hair and comb it out.
  9. Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.
  10. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any final adjustments.

And there you have it! You’ve successfully trimmed your own hair straight across the back.

Tips for Trimming Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trimming your own hair:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. This will ensure a clean cut and prevent split ends.
  • Don’t trim too much at once. It’s better to take small sections and work your way up.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair straight and tangle-free while trimming.
  • Take breaks if needed. Trimming your own hair can be tiring, so don’t be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming your own hair:

  • How often should I trim my hair? It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair? No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can result in split ends or an uneven cut.
  • Can I trim my own hair if I have short hair? It can be difficult to reach the back of your head if you have short hair, so it’s best to seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care After Trimming

After trimming your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain healthy growth. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

That’s it! You now have all the information you need to trim your own hair straight across the back. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be a pro in no time.
