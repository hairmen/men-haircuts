How to Give Yourself a Pixie Cut for Curly Hair

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features short hair on the sides and back of the head, and longer hair on the top. While it may seem like a difficult haircut to achieve with curly hair, it's actually quite simple if you follow the right steps.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here are the steps to give yourself a pixie cut for curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Make sure your hair is free of tangles and knots.
  2. Section your hair. Use clips to separate your hair into four sections: two on the top and two on the bottom.
  3. Start cutting. Begin with the bottom sections, and use a pair of sharp scissors to cut off the desired length. Cut in small sections, about 1/4 inch at a time, to ensure an even cut.
  4. Move to the top sections. Use the same technique as before, but be careful not to cut off too much length. Leave the top sections longer than the bottom sections, as this will create volume and texture.
  5. Trim the sides and back. Use a mirror to see the back of your head, and trim the sides and back to the desired length.
  6. Blend the layers. Use a razor comb or thinning scissors to blend the layers and create texture.
  7. Style your new pixie cut. Use a curling iron or styling product to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Pixie Cut for Curly Hair:

Here are some tips to help you give yourself a successful pixie cut for curly hair:

  • Invest in a pair of sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create uneven cuts.
  • Start with small cuts. It's better to cut off too little than too much.
  • Follow the natural shape of your curls. This will help create a more natural and flattering pixie cut.
  • Use a razor comb or thinning scissors to create texture and blend the layers.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, ask a friend or family member for assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Here are some common questions about giving yourself a pixie cut for curly hair:

  • Will a pixie cut work with curly hair? Yes, a pixie cut can look great with curly hair. It's important to choose a style that works with your curl pattern and face shape.
  • Can I cut my own hair? Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a pixie cut for curly hair at home.
  • How often should I trim my pixie cut? It's recommended to trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.
  • What products should I use to style my pixie cut? Use a curling iron, styling cream, or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Your New Pixie Cut:

Here are some tips for maintaining your new pixie cut for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help define your curls and control frizz.
  • Visit a stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Giving yourself a pixie cut for curly hair can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a stylish and modern pixie cut that complements your natural curls and flatters your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair with the right products and techniques to maintain your new look and keep your curls healthy and happy.
