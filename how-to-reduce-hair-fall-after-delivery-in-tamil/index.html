How To Reduce Hair Fall After Delivery In Tamil Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Aug 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall after delivery and wondering how to manage it? You're not alone. Many new moms face this issue due to hormonal changes, stress, and lack of sleep. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to reduce hair fall after delivery in Tamil.

Table of Contents

  • How to Reduce Hair Fall After Delivery in Tamil
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

After delivery, many women experience hair fall due to hormonal changes. This usually happens around 3-6 months postpartum. Hair fall can be stressful and affect a woman's confidence. However, there are ways to manage and reduce hair fall after delivery.

How to Reduce Hair Fall After Delivery in Tamil

Here are some ways to reduce hair fall after delivery:

Diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential for hair growth. Include foods rich in protein, iron, and vitamins in your diet. Some examples include eggs, spinach, nuts, and fruits.

Stress Management

Stress can trigger hair fall. Practice stress management techniques such as meditation and yoga.

Hair Care

Take care of your hair by using gentle hair products and avoiding heat styling. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to reduce hair fall after delivery:

  1. Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, iron, and vitamins.
  2. Practice stress management techniques such as meditation and yoga.
  3. Take care of your hair by using gentle hair products and avoiding heat styling.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  5. Consider taking supplements such as biotin and iron after consulting with your doctor.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to reduce hair fall after delivery:

  • Massage your scalp with warm oil to improve blood circulation.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Get enough rest and sleep.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and relaxers.

FAQs

Q. How long does hair fall last after delivery?

A. Hair fall usually lasts around 3-6 months postpartum.

Q. Is it normal to experience hair fall after delivery?

A. Yes, it is normal to experience hair fall after delivery due to hormonal changes.

Q. Will my hair grow back after delivery?

A. Yes, your hair will grow back after delivery. However, it may take some time.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid towel-drying your hair vigorously as it can cause hair breakage.

Reducing hair fall after delivery can be challenging, but with the right diet, stress management, and hair care, it is possible. Remember, be patient, and your hair will grow back.
