How To Thin Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally wavy hair, you probably know how difficult it can be to manage. While it's true that wavy hair can look amazing, it can also be thick and unruly, making it difficult to style. One solution to this problem is to thin your hair. Thinning your wavy hair can help reduce its volume, making it easier to style and more manageable. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to thin wavy hair, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining your new hairstyle.

How to Thin Wavy Hair

Thinning your wavy hair is a simple process that you can do at home with the right tools. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in thinning your wavy hair is to wash it thoroughly. This will help remove any dirt or oil that may be weighing your hair down. Be sure to use a shampoo that is designed for wavy hair, as this will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, gently pat your hair dry with the towel.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, use a comb to section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to thin your hair evenly.

Step 4: Thin Your Hair

Using a pair of thinning shears, carefully cut small sections of your hair. Be sure to cut only a small amount of hair at a time, as cutting too much can result in uneven hair. Work your way through each section of your hair, thinning it as you go.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have thinned your hair, you can style it as desired. Wavy hair looks great when it's left natural, but you can also use a curling iron or straightener to create different looks.

Tips for Thinning Wavy Hair

Thinning your wavy hair can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to do it correctly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use thinning shears specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Do not thin your hair too much, as this can result in uneven hair.
  • Be patient and take your time when thinning your hair.
  • If you're unsure about how to thin your hair, consider consulting a professional stylist.

FAQ About Thinning Wavy Hair

Can you thin wavy hair without cutting it?

While cutting your hair is the most effective way to thin it, you can also use a razor comb or thinning brush to thin your hair without cutting it.

How often should you thin wavy hair?

How often you should thin your wavy hair depends on how fast it grows and how thick it is. As a general rule, you should aim to thin your hair every 2-3 months.

Can you thin wavy hair with regular scissors?

No, regular scissors are not designed for thinning hair. You should use thinning shears specifically designed for wavy hair.

How to Care for Thinned Wavy Hair

After thinning your wavy hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

Thinning your wavy hair can be a great way to make it more manageable and easier to style. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, natural look that's perfect for any occasion.
