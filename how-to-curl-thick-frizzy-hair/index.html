How To Curl Thick Frizzy Hair

Written by Frank Jul 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curling thick frizzy hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be easily achieved. In this article, we will discuss the steps to curl thick frizzy hair, tips to make the curls last longer, and how to take care of your hair after curling.

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl Thick Frizzy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Make Curls Last Longer
  • FAQ

How to Curl Thick Frizzy Hair

Before starting the curling process, it is important to have the right tools. You will need a curling iron, heat protectant spray, hair clips, and a hairbrush.

The first step is to apply heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage. Then, brush your hair to remove any tangles.

Next, divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls will be.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to curl your thick frizzy hair:

  1. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  2. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds.
  3. Release the curling iron and let the curl cool for a few seconds.
  4. Repeat the process with the remaining sections of hair.
  5. Once you have curled all of your hair, run your fingers through your curls to loosen them up and create a more natural look.

Tips to Make Curls Last Longer

Here are some tips to make your curls last longer:

  • Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.
  • Do not brush your hair after curling as it can cause the curls to fall out.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun to maintain the curls overnight.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener or hot rollers to curl your hair.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It is not recommended to curl your hair every day as it can cause damage. Curl your hair once or twice a week.

Hair Care After Curling

After curling your hair, it is important to take care of it to prevent damage. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Now that you know how to curl thick frizzy hair, you can experiment with different hairstyles and looks. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
