What Lip Color Is Best For Medium Skin Tone

Written by Eveline Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right lip color can be a daunting task, especially if you have a medium skin tone. Medium skin tone is a versatile skin tone that can rock various lip colors. However, you must choose a lip color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we will discuss what lip color is best for medium skin tone, and we will provide you with some tips on how to choose the right lip color for your skin tone.

Description

Medium skin tone is a warm, golden, or olive skin tone that is not too light or too dark. This skin tone is versatile and can look great in a variety of lip colors. However, not all lip colors are created equal, and some lip colors may not look flattering on medium skin tone. Choosing the right lip color for your skin tone can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to Choose the Right Lip Color for Medium Skin Tone

Choosing the right lip color for medium skin tone can be a little tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips on how to choose the right lip color for your skin tone:

Step by Step

  • Determine your undertone: Before choosing a lip color, determine your undertone. Medium skin tone can have warm or cool undertones. If you have a warm undertone, choose lip colors with yellow or orange undertones. If you have a cool undertone, choose lip colors with blue or purple undertones.
  • Choose the right shade: Choose lip colors that are one to two shades darker or lighter than your natural lip color. Avoid lip colors that are too light or too dark, as they can wash you out or make you look too harsh.
  • Try before you buy: Always try the lip color before you buy it. Test the lip color on your lips, not your hand, as the skin on your hand is not the same as the skin on your lips.
  • Consider your outfit: Consider your outfit when choosing a lip color. If you're wearing a bold outfit, choose a neutral lip color. If you're wearing a neutral outfit, choose a bold lip color.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to choose the right lip color for your medium skin tone:

  • Avoid pastels: Pastel lip colors may not look flattering on medium skin tone. Instead, choose bold and bright colors that will make your lips pop.
  • Go for red: Red is a classic lip color that looks great on medium skin tone. Choose a red lip color with warm undertones, such as brick red or cherry red.
  • Consider your lip shape: Consider your lip shape when choosing a lip color. If you have thin lips, avoid dark colors that can make your lips look smaller. Instead, choose light and bright colors that will make your lips look fuller.
  • Choose a long-lasting lip color: Choose a long-lasting lip color, especially if you're going to be out all day. Long-lasting lip colors will stay on your lips for longer and won't smudge or fade throughout the day.

FAQs

What lip color looks best on medium skin tone?

There are several lip colors that look great on medium skin tone, including red, berry, coral, and nude. Choose a lip color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty.

Can medium skin tone wear dark lip colors?

Yes, medium skin tone can wear dark lip colors, but it's important to choose the right shade. Choose dark lip colors that have warm undertones, such as burgundy or chocolate brown.

Can medium skin tone wear bright lip colors?

Yes, medium skin tone can wear bright lip colors, and it's a great way to make your lips pop. Choose bright lip colors with warm undertones, such as bright pink or coral.

Hair Care

When choosing a lip color for medium skin tone, it's essential to consider your hair color. If you have warm-toned hair, such as red or golden blonde, choose lip colors with warm undertones. If you have cool-toned hair, such as ash blonde or black, choose lip colors with cool undertones.

Choosing the right lip color for medium skin tone can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful. Follow these tips and try different lip colors to find the perfect shade for your skin tone.
