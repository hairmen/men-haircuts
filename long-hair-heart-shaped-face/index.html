Long Hair For Heart Shaped Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a narrow jawline and a wide forehead. Long hair can help balance out these features and create a more symmetrical look. In this article, we will discuss how to style long hair for heart shaped faces, step by step instructions, tips, and hair care.

Description

Heart shaped faces are commonly associated with celebrities like Reese Witherspoon and Scarlett Johansson. These faces have a wide forehead and a narrow, pointed chin. Long hair can soften the sharp angles of a heart shaped face and create a more balanced appearance. However, there are certain styles and cuts that work better than others.

How to Style Long Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before styling your hair, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose the most flattering cut and style. To determine your face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If your forehead is wider than your cheeks and your chin comes to a point, you have a heart shaped face.

Step 2: Choose the Right Cut

The right cut can make all the difference for heart shaped faces. Long layers are ideal for creating volume and movement that balance out the narrow chin. Avoid blunt cuts that can make the chin appear even narrower. Consider adding a side-swept fringe to soften the forehead.

Step 3: Embrace Waves and Curls

Waves and curls can help create volume and balance for heart shaped faces. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves or curls. Avoid tight curls that can make the face appear even narrower.

Step 4: Part Your Hair to the Side

A side part can help balance out a wide forehead and create a more symmetrical look. Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering option for your face shape.

Tips for Styling Long Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some additional tips for styling long hair for heart shaped faces:

  • Use volumizing products to create lift at the crown of the head
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull the hair away from the face, such as tight ponytails and buns
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to create a soft, rounded shape

FAQ

What hairstyles should heart shaped faces avoid?

Heart shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that accentuate the narrow chin, such as blunt bobs or pixie cuts. They should also avoid hairstyles that pull the hair away from the face, such as tight ponytails and buns.

Can heart shaped faces wear bangs?

Yes, heart shaped faces can wear bangs. However, they should opt for side-swept bangs that help balance out the forehead.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Maintaining healthy, long hair is essential for creating a flattering style for heart shaped faces. Here are some tips for maintaining long, healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage and breakage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated and nourished
  • Protect hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

In conclusion, long hair can be a great option for heart shaped faces. By choosing the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can create a flattering and symmetrical look that highlights your best features.
