Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides - A Complete Guide

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain, the men's haircut longer on top short on sides is a great option. This popular style has been around for a while and remains a top choice for men of all ages. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this style, from how to get it to how to care for it.

Description

The men's haircut longer on top short on sides is a classic style that has been around for decades. It's characterized by longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The length on top can vary, from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference. This style is popular because it's easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How To Get The Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides

If you're interested in getting the men's haircut longer on top short on sides, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in getting this style is to choose the length you want on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Your barber or stylist can help you decide on the best length for your hair.

Step 2: Determine Your Fade

The next step is to determine the type of fade you want on the sides and back of your head. The most common types of fades are low fade, mid fade, and high fade. Your barber or stylist can help you choose the best fade for your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair has been cut, it's time to style it. There are many ways to style the men's haircut longer on top short on sides, from slicking it back to leaving it messy and tousled. Experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.

Tips For Maintaining The Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides

Now that you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use The Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. This will help you maintain your look and keep your hair healthy.

Tip 2: Visit Your Barber Regularly

To keep your haircut looking fresh, visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim. This will also help prevent split ends and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other harsh elements by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Face Shapes Are Best For The Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides?

This style works well for most face shapes, but it's especially flattering for men with square or oval faces.

Can I Style The Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides In Different Ways?

Yes! This style is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Is The Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides Easy To Maintain?

Yes! This style is easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of styling time. It's a great option for men who want a stylish look without a lot of fuss.

Hair Care For Men's Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the men's haircut longer on top short on sides. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use The Right Shampoo And Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. This will help keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and damaged. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Tip 3: Use Heat Protectant

If you use a blow dryer, straightener, or other heat styling tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your men's haircut longer on top short on sides looking great for weeks to come!
