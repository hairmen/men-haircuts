Best Hairstyle For Round Shaped Face Female

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a woman with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. However, with the right haircut, it is possible to accentuate your best features and make your face look slimmer. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for round shaped face female.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The best hairstyle for this face shape is one that creates angles and lengthens the face. A good haircut for a round face should balance the width of the face and create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

How to

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for a round face is to determine the length of the hair. Long hair can be flattering on a round face if it is cut in layers and falls below the chin. Short hair can also work well if it is styled to create volume at the crown and the sides are tapered.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, avoid straight, one-length cuts that can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for layers that start at chin level or below. The layers will add movement and texture to your hair, and the length will elongate your face.

Short Hair

If you prefer short hair, go for a layered bob or a pixie cut. A layered bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front will create angles and draw attention away from the fullness of your cheeks. A pixie cut with volume at the crown will also elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Step by step

To achieve the best hairstyle for a round shaped face female, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in cutting hair for round faces.
  2. Determine the length of your hair.
  3. If you have long hair, ask for layers that start at chin level or below.
  4. If you prefer short hair, ask for a layered bob or a pixie cut with volume at the crown.
  5. Style your hair to create volume at the crown and texture throughout.
  6. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair and create volume.
  7. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs that cut across the forehead, as they can make the face look wider.
  • Side-swept bangs can be flattering on a round face, as they create angles and draw attention to the eyes.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that creates angles and elongates the face. This can be achieved with layers, a layered bob, or a pixie cut with volume at the crown.

What kind of bangs are best for a round face?

Side-swept bangs are the best for a round face, as they create angles and draw attention to the eyes.

Can short hair be flattering on a round face?

Yes, short hair can be flattering on a round face if it is styled to create volume at the crown and the sides are tapered.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip the natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for a round shaped face female is one that creates angles and lengthens the face. Whether you have long hair or short hair, there are many options that can be flattering on a round face. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you and always remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
