What Does A Taper Fade Look Like

Written by Bardi Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on what a taper fade looks like. This haircut has been gaining popularity over the years and it's easy to see why. It's a versatile and stylish haircut that can be worn by anyone. In this blog post, we'll break down what a taper fade looks like, how to get it, some tips, and hair care advice for maintaining your new 'do.

Description

A taper fade is a haircut where the hair gradually gets shorter towards the bottom of the head. It's a popular choice for men because it looks clean and modern. The hair is cut shorter on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. The fade can be gradual or abrupt depending on the person's preference.

How to Get a Taper Fade

If you're interested in getting a taper fade, you should go to a professional barber or hairstylist. They'll be able to give you the exact look you're going for. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your hair. This will vary depending on the look you're going for. If you want a more dramatic fade, you'll need to start with shorter hair. If you want a more subtle fade, you can start with longer hair.

Step 2: Choose the Fade

The next step is to choose the type of fade you want. There are several types of fades, including low, medium, and high. A low fade will start near the ears, while a high fade will start near the temples.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

The barber will start cutting your hair, starting with the sides and back. They'll use clippers to create the fade, gradually cutting the hair shorter as they move down. The hair on top will be left longer.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once the hair is cut, the barber will blend the hair to create a seamless transition between the different lengths. This is done using scissors and clippers.

Tips for Maintaining Your Taper Fade

Now that you have your new taper fade haircut, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Invest in a good pair of clippers if you want to maintain your fade at home.
  • Style your hair with a good quality pomade or wax to keep it looking polished.

FAQ

How long does a taper fade last?

A taper fade can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months depending on how fast your hair grows. It's important to visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Can anyone get a taper fade?

Yes! A taper fade is a versatile haircut that can be worn by anyone. It's a popular choice for men, but women can also rock this look. Talk to your barber or hairstylist to see if a taper fade would work for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know what a taper fade looks like, how to get it, and how to take care of it, you're ready to rock this stylish haircut. Remember to visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut. With the right care and styling, your taper fade will look great for weeks to come.
