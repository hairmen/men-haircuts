Toner To Get Rid Of Red Tones In Blonde Hair Say Goodbye To Brassy Hair

Written by William Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a blonde, you know the struggle of keeping your hair looking bright and fresh. Unfortunately, blonde hair can easily turn brassy and develop red or orange tones. This is where toner comes in. In this article, we'll discuss how to use toner to get rid of red tones in blonde hair.

Table of Contents

  • What is Toner?
  • How to Use Toner
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Toner
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After Toning

What is Toner?

Toner is a hair product that helps neutralize unwanted tones in blonde hair. It's typically used after bleaching or coloring to achieve the desired shade of blonde. Toner can come in a variety of shades, including silver, violet, and blue. Each shade targets specific tones in the hair.

How to Use Toner

Before using toner, it's important to understand what shade of toner you need. If your hair has red or orange tones, you'll want to use a toner with blue or violet undertones. If your hair has yellow tones, you'll want to use a toner with silver undertones. It's also important to choose the right developer for your toner.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Mix your toner with the appropriate developer in a bowl.
  4. Apply the toner mixture to your hair, working from the roots to the ends.
  5. Leave the toner on for the recommended amount of time (usually 10-20 minutes).
  6. Rinse the toner out with cool water.
  7. Condition your hair and rinse with cool water.

Tips for Using Toner

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using toner:

  • Always wear gloves when applying toner to avoid staining your hands.
  • Don't leave toner on for too long, as it can damage your hair.
  • Start with a lower volume developer and work your way up if needed.
  • Use a toning shampoo and conditioner to maintain your desired shade.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can toner damage your hair?

While toner is generally safe to use, leaving it on for too long or using it too frequently can damage your hair. Follow the instructions carefully and don't leave toner on for longer than recommended.

How often should I use toner?

This depends on how often you wash your hair and how quickly your hair develops unwanted tones. Generally, toner can be used every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired shade.

Can I use toner on natural blonde hair?

Yes, toner can be used on natural blonde hair to enhance the color or neutralize unwanted tones.

Hair Care After Toning

After toning your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain your desired shade. Use a toning shampoo and conditioner to prevent unwanted tones from developing. Avoid using hot tools and protect your hair from the sun to prevent fading.

Using toner to get rid of red tones in blonde hair is an effective way to keep your hair looking fresh and vibrant. Follow these tips and instructions to achieve the perfect shade of blonde.
