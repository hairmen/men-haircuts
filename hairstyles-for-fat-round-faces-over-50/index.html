Hairstyles For Fat Round Faces Over 50 Flattering Looks For Mature Women

Written by Luffy Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with a round face and fuller cheeks? If so, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that suits your features and makes you feel confident and stylish. However, there are many hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll share some tips, tricks, and hairstyle ideas for women with fat round faces over 50.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Description

Women over 50 with round faces often have fuller cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wider forehead. When choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to create balance and proportion by adding height, volume, and length to your hair. The right hairstyle can create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face and emphasize your best features.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Some hairstyles that flatter round faces include layered cuts, asymmetrical bobs, and side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face or make your face look wider, such as blunt bobs or full fringes.

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider face-framing layers that add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Choose a style that flatters your hair texture, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly.
  • Think about your lifestyle and choose a low-maintenance hairstyle if you're always on the go.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, you'll need to learn how to style it to achieve the desired look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls without adding frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair, depending on the desired look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or hair serum to add shine and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for fat round faces over 50:

  • Q: Can women with round faces wear short hairstyles?
  • A: Yes, women with round faces can wear short hairstyles, such as pixie cuts or layered bobs, as long as the hairstyle adds height and volume to the crown of the head.
  • Q: What kind of bangs suit round faces?
  • A: Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs that graze the eyebrows can flatter round faces by creating a diagonal line across the forehead.
  • Q: Can women with round faces wear their hair in a middle part?
  • A: Yes, women with round faces can wear a middle part as long as the hairstyle adds height and volume to the crown of the head.

Hair Care Tips for Women with Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for women with round faces:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suit your hair type.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle can flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. With the right haircut and styling techniques, women with round faces over 50 can look chic, stylish, and confident. Whether you prefer short or long hair, straight or curly, there's a hairstyle that will suit your face shape and make you feel fabulous.
