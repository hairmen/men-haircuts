Which Hair Colour Suits On Normal Complexion

Written by William Nov 27, 2023 · 3 min read

Choosing the right hair color is a daunting task, especially when you are not sure about your skin tone. Hair color can either enhance your look or make you look washed out. With a normal complexion, you have a vast range of colors to choose from. In this blog post, we will discuss which hair color suits normal complexion and how to maintain it.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How To Choose the Right Hair Color?
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks

Description

Normal complexion is a neutral skin tone that is neither too light nor too dark. It is the most common skin tone that ranges from light beige to medium brown. With a normal complexion, you can experiment with various hair colors without worrying about looking too harsh or dull. However, you need to choose the right shade that complements your skin undertones.

How To Choose the Right Hair Color?

The first step to choose the right hair color is to determine your skin undertones. Normal complexion can have cool, warm, or neutral undertones. To determine your skin undertones, look at your veins. If your veins appear blue, you have cool undertones, if they appear green, you have warm undertones, and if you cannot determine the color, you have neutral undertones.

Once you determine your skin undertones, you can choose the hair color that complements it. For cool undertones, go for cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown. For warm undertones, choose warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and warm brown. For neutral undertones, you can experiment with both cool and warm hair colors.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to choosing the right hair color for normal complexion:

  1. Determine your skin undertones.
  2. Choose the hair color that complements your undertones.
  3. Consider your natural hair color.
  4. Choose a shade that is not too far from your natural color.
  5. Consult a professional stylist.
  6. Do a patch test before coloring your hair.
  7. Follow the instructions on the hair color box.
  8. Maintain the hair color with proper hair care.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you maintain your hair color:

  1. Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  3. Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  4. Protect your hair from sun damage.
  5. Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  6. Avoid chlorine and saltwater.
  7. Use a hair mask once a week.

FAQs

Q. Can I dye my hair at home?

A. Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it is recommended to consult a professional stylist for best results.

Q. Will hair color damage my hair?

A. Hair color can damage your hair if not done properly or if you do not maintain it properly. Use a hair mask and avoid heat styling tools to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for normal complexion:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  3. Use a hair mask once a week.
  4. Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  5. Avoid brushing wet hair.
  6. Protect your hair from sun damage.

Choosing the right hair color for normal complexion can enhance your look and boost your confidence. Follow the above tips and tricks to maintain your hair color and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
