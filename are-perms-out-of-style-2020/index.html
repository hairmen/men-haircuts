Are Perms Out Of Style 2020

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Perms have been a popular hairstyle for decades, but with changing fashion trends, it's natural to wonder if they're still in style in 2020. In this blog post, we'll explore the current popularity of perms and provide tips for upkeep and care.

Description

A perm is a hairstyle that involves treating the hair with chemicals to create permanent curls or waves. Perms were popular in the 1980s and 1990s, but in recent years, we've seen a resurgence of the trend.

Many people are attracted to perms because they offer a low-maintenance hairstyle that can last for several months. Perms work on most hair types and can be customized to create different curl patterns and styles.

How To

If you're considering getting a perm, it's important to choose a reputable hairstylist who has experience with the process. Here are the general steps involved in getting a perm:

  1. The hairstylist will wash your hair and wrap it around perm rods.
  2. The hairstylist will apply a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the protein bonds and makes the hair more malleable.
  3. The hairstylist will rinse the hair and apply a neutralizer to reform the protein bonds and set the curls.
  4. The hairstylist will remove the perm rods and style the hair as desired.

Step-by-Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for getting a perm:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if a perm is right for you.
  2. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  3. Towel dry your hair and section it into small, even sections.
  4. Wrap each section of hair around a perm rod, starting at the ends and rolling upwards.
  5. Apply the perm solution to each section of hair, making sure to saturate the hair evenly.
  6. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and let it process for the recommended amount of time.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water and remove the perm rods.
  8. Apply the neutralizer to your hair and let it process for the recommended amount of time.
  9. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water and towel dry it.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your perm:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to the curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause the curls to loosen or fall out.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair to keep the curls defined and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

1. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it. Generally, perms last longer on coarse or thick hair and shorter on fine or thin hair.

2. Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

You can color your hair after getting a perm, but it's best to wait at least a week to allow the curls to set. It's also important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye to prevent damage to the curls.

3. Can you swim with a perm?

You can swim with a perm, but it's important to rinse your hair thoroughly with fresh water after swimming to remove any chlorine or saltwater. You may also want to wear a swim cap to protect your curls.

Hair Care

To keep your perm looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

In conclusion, perms are still in style in 2020 and can be a great choice for those looking for a low-maintenance, versatile hairstyle. With proper care and maintenance, your perm can last for several months and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
