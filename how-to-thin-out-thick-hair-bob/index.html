How To Thin Out Thick Hair Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair bob being too bulky and heavy? Do you wish to have a more manageable and sleek hairstyle? Thinning out your hair can be a great solution. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to thin out thick hair bob.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Thin Out Thick Hair Bob
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thinning Out Thick Hair Bob
  • FAQs

Description

Thinning out thick hair bob involves removing some of the hair’s bulk and weight, which can make it more manageable and easier to style. This process can be done using various techniques such as razor cutting, point cutting, or texturizing.

While it is possible to thin out your hair at home, we recommend seeking the assistance of a professional hairstylist to avoid damaging your hair or creating an uneven cut.

How to Thin Out Thick Hair Bob

Before thinning out your hair, it is essential to have a clear idea of the hairstyle you want to achieve. Communicate your desired look to your hairstylist, and they will help you determine the best technique to use.

Some popular techniques for thinning out thick hair bob include:

Razor Cutting

Razor cutting involves using a razor to slice into small sections of hair, creating a feathered effect. This technique is best for those with straight hair or loose waves.

Point Cutting

Point cutting involves using scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, creating texture and movement. This technique is best for those with curly or wavy hair.

Texturizing

Texturizing involves using thinning shears to remove some of the hair’s bulk and weight. This technique is ideal for those with thick, coarse hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to thin out thick hair bob:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts, using hair clips.
  3. Start with one section of your hair, and hold it up at a 45-degree angle.
  4. Using your chosen technique, thin out the hair by cutting into it at an angle.
  5. Repeat the process on each section of your hair.
  6. Blend the hair together by running your fingers through it.
  7. Check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Thinning Out Thick Hair Bob

Thinning out your hair can be a great way to achieve a more manageable and sleek hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best technique for your hair type.
  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up to avoid over-thinning your hair.
  • Be gentle when using your chosen technique to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the desired thickness and shape.

FAQs

Can I thin out my hair at home?

While it is possible to thin out your hair at home, we recommend seeking the assistance of a professional hairstylist to avoid damaging your hair or creating an uneven cut.

How often should I thin out my hair?

The frequency of thinning out your hair depends on your desired hairstyle and hair growth rate. It is recommended to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to maintain the desired thickness and shape.

Hair Care After Thinning Out Thick Hair Bob

After thinning out your hair, it is essential to take care of it properly to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly using products suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools frequently, and always use a heat protectant spray when using them.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet to prevent breakage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the desired thickness and shape.

Thinning out thick hair bob can be a great way to achieve a more manageable and sleek hairstyle. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can achieve the desired look while keeping your hair healthy and shiny.
