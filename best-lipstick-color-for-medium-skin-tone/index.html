Best Lipstick Color For Medium Skin Tone

Written by Oliver Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a medium skin tone, finding the right lipstick color can be challenging. With so many options available, it can be difficult to determine which shades will flatter your skin tone the most. In this article, we will explore the best lipstick colors for medium skin tones and provide tips on how to choose the perfect shade for you.

Description

Medium skin tones are those that are not too light or too dark. They fall in the middle of the spectrum and have a warm undertone. Some common features of medium skin tones include olive or golden undertones, a warm complexion, and a balance of cool and warm tones in the skin.

When it comes to lipstick, medium skin tones can wear a wide range of shades. However, there are certain colors that will look more flattering than others. By choosing the right shade, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a polished, put-together look.

How to Choose the Best Lipstick Color for Medium Skin Tone

When selecting a lipstick color for medium skin tones, consider the following factors:

Undertones

Look for lipsticks that have warm, yellow, or golden undertones. These shades will complement the warmth of your skin tone and bring out your natural radiance. Avoid cool-toned lipsticks that have blue or purple undertones, as they can make your skin appear dull or ashy.

Intensity

The intensity of the lipstick color is also important. Medium skin tones can handle both bold and subtle shades, depending on the occasion. For a natural look, go for sheer or nude lipsticks. For a more dramatic look, opt for brighter or deeper hues.

Texture

The texture of the lipstick can also affect how it looks on your skin. Matte lipsticks can be drying and may accentuate any dryness or flakiness on your lips. Creamy or glossy lipsticks are more forgiving and can help to hydrate your lips while providing a smooth, even application.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when choosing the best lipstick color for medium skin tones:

  1. Determine your undertone. Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.
  2. Choose a lipstick color that complements your undertone. If you have warm undertones, go for shades with yellow or golden undertones. If you have cool undertones, go for shades with blue or purple undertones.
  3. Consider the intensity of the lipstick. For a natural look, choose a sheer or nude shade. For a bold look, choose a brighter or deeper color.
  4. Try on the lipstick before buying. Test the color on your lips to see how it looks against your skin tone.
  5. Experiment with different textures. Matte, creamy, and glossy lipsticks can all look great on medium skin tones. Try different formulas to see which one works best for you.

Tips for Wearing Lipstick with Medium Skin Tone

Here are some tips for wearing lipstick with medium skin tones:

  • Pair bold lipsticks with neutral eye makeup to avoid overwhelming your face.
  • Use a lip liner to define your lips and prevent the color from bleeding.
  • Apply a lip balm before lipstick to hydrate your lips and create a smooth base.
  • Avoid wearing lipsticks that are too light, as they can wash out your complexion.
  • Consider your outfit when choosing a lipstick color. Certain shades may complement or clash with your clothing.

FAQ

What lipstick shades should I avoid with medium skin tone?

Avoid lipsticks that are too light or too cool-toned. Shades like pastel pink or blue-based reds can wash out your complexion and make you look ashy.

Can medium skin tone wear red lipstick?

Yes, medium skin tones look great with red lipstick. Look for shades with warm undertones like brick red, tomato red, or coral red.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Skin Tone

When it comes to hair care, medium skin tones look great with warm, rich shades like caramel, honey, or chestnut. Avoid cool-toned shades like platinum blonde or ashy brown, as they can clash with your skin tone.

Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair. Warm tones like gold or copper can complement your skin tone and bring out your natural radiance.

In conclusion, choosing the best lipstick color for medium skin tone requires a bit of experimentation and understanding of your undertones. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect shade that enhances your natural beauty and creates a polished, put-together look.
