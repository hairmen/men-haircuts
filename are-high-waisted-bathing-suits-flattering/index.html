Are High Waisted Bathing Suits Flattering

Written by Frank Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to swimwear, there are a plethora of options available in the market. From bikinis to one-pieces, it can be overwhelming to choose the right style that flatters your body type. One of the most popular styles of swimwear in recent years is the high waisted bathing suit. But the question that arises is, are high waisted bathing suits flattering? In this blog post, we will answer this question and provide tips on how to wear them.

Description

High waisted bathing suits are a retro-inspired style that is making a comeback in recent years. They feature a waistband that sits above the belly button, providing coverage and support to the midsection. The bottoms can range from full coverage to cheeky, depending on the design.

How to Wear High Waisted Bathing Suits

High waisted bathing suits can be worn in various ways, depending on your personal style and comfort level. Here are some tips on how to wear them:

1. Choose the Right Size

It is essential to choose the right size of high waisted bathing suit to avoid any discomfort or unflattering fit. Make sure to measure your waist and hips and refer to the size chart provided by the brand.

2. Mix and Match

You can mix and match the top and bottom of your high waisted bathing suit to create a unique look. You can pair it with a crop top, a tankini, or even a bikini top.

3. Accessorize

You can accessorize your high waisted bathing suit with a hat, sunglasses, and a cover-up to complete your beach or poolside look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear high waisted bathing suits:

Step 1: Choose the Right Size

Measure your waist and hips and refer to the size chart provided by the brand to choose the right size of high waisted bathing suit.

Step 2: Choose a Design

Choose a design that flatters your body type and suits your personal style. You can opt for full coverage or cheeky bottoms.

Step 3: Mix and Match

You can mix and match the top and bottom of your high waisted bathing suit to create a unique look. You can pair it with a crop top, a tankini, or even a bikini top.

Step 4: Accessorize

Accessorize your high waisted bathing suit with a hat, sunglasses, and a cover-up to complete your beach or poolside look.

Tips to Make High Waisted Bathing Suits Flattering

Here are some tips to make high waisted bathing suits flattering:

1. Choose the Right Design

Choose a design that flatters your body type. If you have a pear-shaped body, opt for a high waisted bathing suit with a darker bottom and a lighter top to balance your silhouette.

2. Opt for a High Cut Leg

Choose a high waisted bathing suit with a high cut leg to elongate your legs and create an illusion of height.

3. Wear a Belt

Wear a belt with your high waisted bathing suit to cinch your waist and create an hourglass figure.

FAQs

Q. Are high waisted bathing suits only for certain body types?

A. No, high waisted bathing suits can be worn by anyone regardless of their body type. It is essential to choose the right design and size that flatters your body type.

Q. Can high waisted bathing suits be worn for water sports?

A. Yes, high waisted bathing suits provide coverage and support to the midsection, making them suitable for water sports.

Q. Can high waisted bathing suits be worn by older women?

A. Yes, high waisted bathing suits can be worn by women of all ages. They provide coverage and support to the midsection, making them a suitable option for older women.

Hair Care Tips for Beach and Pool Days

While enjoying your high waisted bathing suit, make sure to protect your hair from the sun, saltwater, and chlorine. Here are some hair care tips for beach and pool days:

1. Wear a Hat

Wear a hat to protect your hair from the sun and reduce the amount of exposure to harmful UV rays.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner before and after swimming to protect your hair from the damaging effects of saltwater and chlorine.

3. Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming to remove any salt or chlorine residue.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair after swimming as it can further damage your hair. Let your hair air dry or use a low heat setting on your blow dryer.

In conclusion, high waisted bathing suits can be flattering if worn correctly. Follow the tips and steps mentioned in this blog post to wear them with confidence and enjoy your beach or poolside day. Don't forget to protect your hair from the sun, saltwater, and chlorine to maintain healthy and shiny locks.
