Short Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 70

Written by Eveline Nov 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out. However, this doesn't mean that we can't have stylish and trendy haircuts. In fact, there are many short hairstyles for thin hair over 70 that can make you look and feel younger. In this article, we will be discussing how to choose the right short hairstyle, how to style it, and hair care tips to maintain healthy hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

Choosing the right short hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you have thin hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the right one:

Consider your face shape

The first thing you need to consider when choosing a short hairstyle is your face shape. If you have a round face, you might want to go for a short haircut with layers to add volume. If you have a long face, a pixie cut or a bob can work well for you.

Think about your hair texture

Thin hair can be fine, coarse, or somewhere in between. If you have fine hair, you might want to go for a layered haircut to add volume. If you have coarse hair, a choppy bob can work well for you.

Consider your lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be a factor when choosing a short hairstyle. If you are a busy person who doesn't have much time to style your hair, a low maintenance haircut might be the best option for you.

How to Style Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 70

Styling short hairstyles for thin hair over 70 is relatively easy. Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

Add volume

To add volume to your hair, you can use a volumizing mousse or a root lifter. Apply the product to your roots and blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.

Use the right styling tools

The right styling tools can make a big difference in how your hair looks. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume to your hair. You can also use a flat iron to add texture and waves.

Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. You can wear your hair straight, curly, or wavy. You can also try different partings or add accessories to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 70

Here is a step by step guide to help you achieve the perfect short hairstyle for thin hair over 70:

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

Choose a short haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob or a pixie cut can work well for thin hair.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Apply a volumizing mousse or root lifter to your roots to add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume. Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

Step 5: Style your hair

Style your hair using a flat iron, curling iron, or any other styling tool you prefer. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 70

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

Use a heat protectant

When using styling tools, always use a heat protectant to protect your hair from damage.

Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage.

Protect your hair from the sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for thin hair over 70?

The best short hairstyle for thin hair over 70 depends on your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob or a pixie cut can work well for thin hair.

How can I add volume to my thin hair?

You can add volume to your thin hair by using a volumizing mousse or root lifter. Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 70

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

Avoid using too much heat

Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Use a lower heat setting on your styling tools to prevent damage.

Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for thin hair over 70 can be stylish and trendy. Choose the right haircut, style it well, and maintain healthy hair to look and feel your best.
