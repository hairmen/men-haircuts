Best Short Haircut For Long Oval Face

Written by Petter Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long oval face, then you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect haircut. The wrong haircut can make your face look even longer and narrower, which is not what anyone wants. Fortunately, there are several short haircuts that work well with long oval faces. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircut for long oval face, how to style it, and how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best short haircut for long oval face is the pixie cut. This haircut is perfect for women who want to keep their hair short and stylish. The pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that is perfect for women with long oval faces. The layers help to add volume and texture to the hair, which makes it look fuller and more voluminous.

How to

To get the best short haircut for long oval face, you need to find a good hairstylist who can cut your hair to suit your face shape. When you go to the salon, bring a picture of the pixie cut that you like or ask your stylist for recommendations. Your stylist will analyze your face shape and recommend the best style for you.

Step by step

Here are the steps to getting the best short haircut for long oval face:

  1. Find a good hairstylist
  2. Bring a picture of the pixie cut you like
  3. Discuss your face shape with your stylist
  4. Let your stylist cut your hair
  5. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best short haircut for long oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short
  • Add layers to your hair to create volume and texture
  • Keep your hair styled and groomed
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircut for long oval face:

Q: What is the best short haircut for long oval face?

A: The best short haircut for long oval face is the pixie cut.

Q: How do I style my pixie cut?

A: You can style your pixie cut with a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: You should get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Hair Care

To maintain your pixie cut, you need to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair regularly
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your style

By following these tips, you can maintain your pixie cut and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for long oval face is the pixie cut. This haircut is perfect for women who want to keep their hair short and stylish. With the right styling and hair care, you can keep your pixie cut looking beautiful and stylish.
