How To See What Hairstyles Look Good On You

Written by Robby Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but unsure of what will look good on you? It can be challenging to decide on a new look, but there are ways to figure out what styles will suit you best. In this article, we will go through some tips and steps on how to see what hairstyles look good on you.

Table of Content

  1. How to Determine What Hairstyles Suit You
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle
  3. Tips for Choosing a Flattering Hairstyle
  4. Frequently Asked Questions

How to Determine What Hairstyles Suit You

The first step in determining what hairstyles suit you is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes will look better with different hairstyles. Here are some guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, hairstyles that add height at the crown or have long layers will elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, soft layers and waves will soften your angles.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, hairstyles that add volume at the chin and jawline will balance your features.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will suit you.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. Your hair texture will determine how certain hairstyles will look on you. For example, if you have thick hair, a short bob may look too heavy, while if you have fine hair, a long hairstyle may look too thin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide to finding your perfect hairstyle:

  1. Consider your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Look for inspiration from magazines, online sources, and social media.
  3. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different styles will look on you.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion and advice.
  5. Choose a hairstyle that you love and feel confident in.

Tips for Choosing a Flattering Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for choosing a flattering hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to devote to styling your hair each day.
  • Think about the level of maintenance the hairstyle will require, including regular trims and styling products.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and personal style.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and interest to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine the shape of your face in a mirror. You can also trace the outline of your face onto a piece of paper and compare it to the four basic face shapes: round, square, heart, and oval.

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It is possible to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that flatters the dominant face shape.

What if I have a unique hair texture?

If you have a unique hair texture, consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with your hair type. They can provide advice and recommend hairstyles that will suit you best.

Hair Care Tips to Keep Your Hairstyle Looking Great

Once you have found your perfect hairstyle, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant product if you do.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and steps, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for their professional advice, and maintain your hairstyle with proper hair care.
