How To Take Care Of Permed Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Perming hair has been a popular trend among men for a long time. It gives a unique texture and style to your hair that you can enjoy for several months. However, taking care of permed hair can be quite challenging if you don't know the right steps. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about how to take care of permed hair guys.

Description

Perming hair involves the use of chemicals to change your hair's structure and make it curly or wavy. The process can be quite harsh on your hair, which is why you need to take extra care of it. Permed hair requires specific maintenance routines to keep it healthy, shiny, and bouncy.

How to Take Care of Permed Hair Guys

Here are some essential tips for taking care of permed hair guys:

1. Avoid using harsh shampoos

Permed hair is already fragile, and using harsh shampoos can strip away its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use mild, sulfate-free shampoos that are gentle on your hair and scalp.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it hydrated and nourished. Look for conditioners that contain natural oils and proteins that can strengthen your hair and prevent breakage.

3. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair every day can strip away its natural oils, leaving it dry and dull. Try to limit washing your hair to 2-3 times a week.

4. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage permed hair, making it frizzy and prone to breakage. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners, curling irons, and blow dryers. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray first.

5. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun's UV rays can damage permed hair, making it dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF protection when you're out in the sun.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to take care of permed hair guys:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a mild shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to clean your hair and scalp. Massage gently and rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Deep condition your hair

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and comb through with a wide-tooth comb. Leave it on for 5-10 minutes, then rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Avoid towel-drying your hair

Instead of rubbing your hair with a towel, gently squeeze out excess water using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.

Step 4: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair, focusing on the ends. This will help keep your hair hydrated and protected.

Step 5: Air-dry your hair

Avoid using heat tools to dry your hair. Instead, let it air dry naturally or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Tips for Maintaining Permed Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining permed hair:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks can help prevent split ends and breakage.

2. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help prevent frizz and breakage by reducing friction between your hair and the pillow.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids, buns, and ponytails can pull on your hair, causing breakage and damage. Opt for looser styles that don't put too much tension on your hair.

FAQs

Q. How often should I deep condition my permed hair?

A. You can deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.

Q. Can I color my permed hair?

A. It's best to wait at least 2 weeks after perming your hair before coloring it.

Q. Can I use oil on my permed hair?

A. Yes, you can use natural oils like coconut oil, argan oil, and jojoba oil to moisturize your hair and scalp.

Hair Care Routine for Permed Hair Guys

Here's a hair care routine for permed hair guys:

1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo 2-3 times a week

2. Deep condition your hair once a week

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair

4. Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer

5. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks

6. Protect your hair from the sun

7. Avoid heat styling

8. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

9. Avoid tight hairstyles

10. Use natural oils to moisturize your hair and scalp

With these tips and a proper hair care routine, you can maintain healthy, shiny, and bouncy permed hair. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling. If you have any questions or concerns, consult a hairstylist or a dermatologist.
