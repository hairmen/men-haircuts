Does Pomade Make Hair Look Thicker

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of thin and limp hair? Do you want to add more volume to your hair without spending a fortune on expensive hair products? If your answer is yes, then pomade might be the answer to your hair problems. In this article, we will discuss whether pomade makes hair look thicker and how you can use it to create a voluminous hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Pomade to Make Hair Look Thicker
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description

Pomade is a hair styling product that can add hold and shine to your hair. It is usually made from wax, oil, or a combination of both. Pomade can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, from slicked back to messy and textured. But does pomade make hair look thicker? The answer is yes, pomade can make hair look thicker by adding volume and texture to the hair. Pomade can also help to control frizz and flyaways, which can make hair appear thinner.

How to Use Pomade to Make Hair Look Thicker

If you want to use pomade to make your hair look thicker, there are a few things you need to keep in mind. First, choose the right type of pomade for your hair type. If you have fine hair, choose a lightweight pomade that will not weigh down your hair. If you have thick or curly hair, choose a heavy pomade that will provide more hold and control.

Next, apply pomade to dry hair. Wet hair will dilute the pomade and make it less effective. Start with a small amount of pomade, and add more as needed. Rub the pomade between your palms to warm it up, and then apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Use your fingers to work the pomade into your hair, creating texture and volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use pomade to make your hair look thicker:

  1. Choose the right type of pomade for your hair type.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Warm up a small amount of pomade between your palms.
  4. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  5. Use your fingers to work the pomade into your hair, creating texture and volume.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your pomade:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade, and add more as needed. You can always add more pomade, but it is difficult to remove it once it has been applied.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • If you have oily hair, choose a water-based pomade instead of an oil-based one.
  • If you have dry hair, choose a pomade that contains moisturizing ingredients.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about using pomade to make hair look thicker:

Q: Will pomade make my hair greasy?

A: It depends on the type of pomade you use. Oil-based pomades can make hair look greasy and oily, while water-based pomades are less likely to do so.

Q: How often should I use pomade?

A: You can use pomade as often as you like, but it is recommended that you do not use it every day. Overuse of pomade can cause buildup on the scalp and hair, which can lead to dandruff and other scalp issues.

Q: Can pomade cause hair loss?

A: No, pomade does not cause hair loss. However, if you use pomade too frequently or do not wash it out properly, it can cause buildup on the scalp, which can lead to hair loss over time.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and voluminous hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt, oil, and buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage the hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dye, as they can damage the hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body and texture to your hair.

In conclusion, pomade can make hair look thicker by adding volume and texture to the hair. However, it is important to choose the right type of pomade for your hair type and use it correctly to achieve the desired results. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can create a voluminous hairstyle that will make you look and feel your best.
