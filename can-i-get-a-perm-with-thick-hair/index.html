Can I Get A Perm With Thick Hair Everything You Need To Know

Getting a perm with thick hair might seem like a daunting task, but it is definitely possible. In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about getting a perm with thick hair, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that adds curls or waves to your hair. It works by breaking down the protein bonds in your hair and then reforming them in a new shape. A perm can last for several months, depending on how well you take care of your hair.

How to Get a Perm with Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, you can still get a perm. However, you need to be aware that the process might take longer and might require more chemicals. Here are the steps to getting a perm with thick hair:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

The first step in getting a perm with thick hair is to consult with a professional stylist. They will be able to assess your hair and determine if it is healthy enough for a perm. They will also be able to recommend the best type of perm for your hair type.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before your perm, your hairstylist will prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any product buildup and ensure that your hair is clean and ready for the perm solution.

Step 3: Apply the Perm Solution

The perm solution will be applied to your hair in sections. Your hairstylist will use a perm rod to wrap your hair around and then apply the solution. The solution will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and the desired curl pattern.

Step 4: Rinse and Neutralize

After the perm solution has been on your hair for the desired amount of time, it will be rinsed off with warm water. Then, a neutralizing solution will be applied to your hair to stop the chemical process. Your hair will be left to sit for a few minutes before it is rinsed off again.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair has been neutralized, your hairstylist will apply a leave-in conditioner and style your hair. You can choose to air dry or blow dry your hair, depending on your preference.

Tips for Getting a Perm with Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a perm with thick hair:

  • Make sure your hair is healthy and moisturized before getting a perm.
  • Be patient â the process might take longer than it would with thin hair.
  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience working with thick hair.
  • Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your hairstylist.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a perm with thick hair:

Can I get a perm with thick hair?

Yes, you can get a perm with thick hair. However, the process might take longer and might require more chemicals.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, depending on how well you take care of your hair.

What is the best type of perm for thick hair?

The best type of perm for thick hair is a larger curl pattern, such as a loose spiral or body wave.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a perm:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, for the first few weeks.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, for at least a month after getting a perm.

In conclusion, getting a perm with thick hair is definitely possible. However, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist and follow their recommendations for the best type of perm for your hair type. With proper care and maintenance, your perm can last for several months and give you the curls or waves you desire.
