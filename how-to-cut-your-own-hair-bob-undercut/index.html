How To Cut Your Own Hair Bob Undercut

Written by Frank Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon every time you need a trim? Well, we have good news for you. You can now cut your own hair bob undercut at home with just a few simple steps. In this blog post, we will walk you through the process of achieving the perfect bob undercut haircut at home. So, let’s get started!

How to Cut Your Own Hair Bob Undercut

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it can be easy and cost-effective. Before you start, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Clippers with guard sizes 1 and 2
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair clips
  • Hair ties

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect bob undercut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an accurate haircut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Starting with the back section, use the clippers with guard size 2 to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Use the clippers with guard size 1 to trim the hair above the nape. Blend the two sections together using scissors.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections. Use the clippers with guard size 2 to trim the hair on the sides of your head. Blend the hair into the back section using scissors.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. You can also use the clippers with guard size 2 to add texture to the hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use hair ties to create a sleek ponytail or add some product to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bob undercut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair as needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Take your time and don’t rush.
  • Use hair clips to keep sections of hair out of the way as you cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair bob undercut?

Yes, you can cut your own hair bob undercut. With the right tools and technique, it can be easy and cost-effective.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair bob undercut?

You will need clippers with guard sizes 1 and 2, scissors, a comb, a mirror, hair clips, and hair ties.

How do I section my hair for a bob undercut?

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Congratulations! You have successfully learned how to cut your own hair bob undercut. With practice, you will become a pro at cutting your own hair and saving money on salon visits. Happy cutting!
