How to Cut Your Own Hair Bob Undercut

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it can be easy and cost-effective. Before you start, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Clippers with guard sizes 1 and 2
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair clips
  • Hair ties

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect bob undercut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an accurate haircut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Starting with the back section, use the clippers with guard size 2 to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Use the clippers with guard size 1 to trim the hair above the nape. Blend the two sections together using scissors.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections. Use the clippers with guard size 2 to trim the hair on the sides of your head. Blend the hair into the back section using scissors.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. You can also use the clippers with guard size 2 to add texture to the hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use hair ties to create a sleek ponytail or add some product to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bob undercut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair as needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Take your time and donât rush.
  • Use hair clips to keep sections of hair out of the way as you cut.
