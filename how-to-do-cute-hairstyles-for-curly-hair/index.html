How To Do Cute Hairstyles For Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. But with the right techniques and products, you can create cute hairstyles that showcase your natural curls. In this guide, we'll show you how to do cute hairstyles for curly hair, from simple updos to intricate braids. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to switch up your everyday look, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads.

  2. How to
  3. Step-by-Step

Hair Care

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about hair care. Curly hair requires a bit more TLC than straight hair, so it's important to use products that are specifically formulated for your hair type. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to wet hair to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid friction and tangles.

By following these tips, you'll be able to keep your curls healthy and hydrated, which will make styling them much easier.

How to

Now that you know how to take care of your curly hair, let's get into the fun part: styling! Here are some cute hairstyles for curly hair:

Half-Up Half-Down

This is a simple and classic hairstyle that works well for any occasion.

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Optional: tease the crown of your hair for extra volume.

Voila! You're ready to rock your half-up half-down hairstyle.

Braided Crown

This hairstyle is perfect for a bohemian or romantic look.

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and braid it.
  3. Wrap the braid around the crown of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side.

You now have a beautiful braided crown hairstyle.

Step-by-Step

Low Bun

This hairstyle is perfect for a chic and polished look.

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Optional: pull out a few strands of hair around your face for a softer look.

You now have a stylish low bun hairstyle.

Twisted Ponytail

This hairstyle is a fun twist on the classic ponytail.

  1. Start by creating a low ponytail.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the ponytail and twist it.
  3. Wrap the twisted section around the base of the ponytail and secure it with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.

You now have a cute and playful twisted ponytail.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, scarves, and clips, to add some flair to your hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create soft waves in the morning.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people with curly hair find that washing their hair once or twice a week is sufficient. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant and to not straighten it too frequently to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How can I tame frizz?

A: Frizz is a common issue for people with curly hair. To tame frizz, use a smoothing serum or oil on wet or dry hair, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be able to show off your beautiful curly hair with confidence. Have fun experimenting with different looks, and don't be afraid to try something new!
