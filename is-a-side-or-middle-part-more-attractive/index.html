Is A Side Or Middle Part More Attractive

Written by Aprilia Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you wondering whether a side or middle part is more attractive? Well, the answer is not as simple as you might think. Both styles have their own unique charm and appeal, and it ultimately depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. In this blog post, we'll explore the pros and cons of both styles and help you decide which one is best for you.

Description

First, let's define what a side and middle part are. A side part is when you part your hair on one side, while a middle part is when you part your hair in the center. Simple enough, right? But, there's more to it than just the placement of your part.

How To

If you're not sure which part to choose, try experimenting with both styles. Use a comb or your fingers to create a side or middle part and see which one looks better on you. You can also ask your hairstylist for their opinion.

Step by Step

If you want to try a side part, here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp.
  2. Use a comb to create a diagonal line from the crown of your head to the front hairline on one side.
  3. Brush the hair on the smaller side of the part towards your ear and use a bobby pin to hold it in place.
  4. Style the rest of your hair as desired.

If you prefer a middle part, here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it's damp.
  2. Use a comb to create a straight line down the center of your head.
  3. Brush the hair on either side of the part towards your ears.
  4. Style the rest of your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a part:

  • Consider your face shape. A side part can help balance out a round face, while a middle part can elongate a square face.
  • Think about your hair texture. A side part can add volume to fine hair, while a middle part can work well with thick hair.
  • Try different parts with different hairstyles. A side part can look great with loose waves, while a middle part can work well with a sleek ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair parts:

Q: Can I switch between a side and middle part?

A: Yes, you can switch between a side and middle part depending on your mood or outfit.

Q: Will a side part work with bangs?

A: Yes, a side part can work with bangs. Just make sure to style them in the same direction as your part.

Q: How do I know if a middle part will suit me?

A: A middle part can suit most face shapes, but it's best to try it out and see how it looks on you.

Hair Care

Regardless of which part you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat styling tools. It's also a good idea to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, both a side and middle part can be attractive, and it ultimately depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. Try out both styles to see which one suits you best, and don't forget to take care of your hair!
