Best Hairstyle For Long Face Over 60

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its natural luster. However, that doesn't mean we can't still look fabulous. Choosing the right hairstyle can make all the difference, especially if you have a long face. In this article, we'll discuss the best hairstyle for long face over 60, how to achieve it, and tips for hair care.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face, the goal is to create balance and proportion. A long face can be defined by a high forehead, prominent chin, and narrow cheekbones. The best hairstyle for long face over 60 is one that adds volume to the sides, creates softness around the jawline, and adds height to the crown.

How to

The first step in achieving the best hairstyle for long face over 60 is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that works best with your face shape and hair type. You may also want to consider adding layers or highlights to add dimension and movement to your hair.

Step by step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

3. Section your hair and use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting the roots to add volume.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends.

5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can make your face look even longer.

- Bangs can be a great way to add softness to your face, but make sure they are not too thick or heavy.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and movement to your hair.

- Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long, but make sure to add layers and volume to the sides to create balance.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous on top.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Make sure to use products that are designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for long face over 60 is one that adds volume to the sides, softness around the jawline, and height to the crown. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you, and make sure to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
