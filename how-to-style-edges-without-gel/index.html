How To Style Edges Without Gel In Relaxed English Language

Written by Robby Sep 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of using gel to style your edges? Do you want to try a more natural approach? In this article, we will discuss how to style edges without gel. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Edges Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Edges Without Gel
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Edges Without Gel

Styling your edges without gel is easier than you might think. All you need is a few simple tools and products.

Tools:

  • Soft-bristle brush
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hair clips or bobby pins
  • Satin scarf or bonnet

Products:

  • Edge control cream
  • Edge control brush
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you have your tools and products ready, let's start styling your edges without gel.

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Before you start styling your edges, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or product buildup.

Step 2: Apply edge control cream

Take a small amount of edge control cream and apply it to your edges. Use your edge control brush to smooth the cream along your hairline.

Step 3: Brush your edges

Use your soft-bristle brush to brush your edges in the direction you want them to go. Keep brushing until your edges are smooth and sleek.

Step 4: Use a rat-tail comb

Use a rat-tail comb to create a part in your hair if you want a specific style. You can also use the comb to shape your edges and create a more defined look.

Step 5: Secure your edges

If you want your edges to stay in place, use hair clips or bobby pins to secure them. Place the clips or pins behind your ears or at the nape of your neck.

Step 6: Wrap your hair

Wrap your hair with a satin scarf or bonnet to keep your edges in place while you sleep. This will also help prevent breakage and frizz.

Tips for Styling Edges Without Gel

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look when styling your edges without gel.

  • Use a small amount of edge control cream to avoid buildup and greasiness.
  • Brush your edges gently to avoid breakage.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Wrap your hair every night to maintain your style and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Can I style my edges without any products?

Yes, you can style your edges without any products. However, using edge control cream can help you achieve a sleeker and more defined look.

Can I use a regular brush to style my edges?

No, it is best to use a soft-bristle brush or an edge control brush to style your edges. A regular brush can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know how to style edges without gel, you can try this natural approach and achieve a sleek and defined look. Remember to use the right tools and products, follow the step-by-step instructions, and take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful mane.
