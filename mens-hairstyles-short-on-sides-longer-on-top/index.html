Mens Hairstyles Short On Sides Longer On Top The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's easy to manage and looks great, then you'll want to try a mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top. This style is perfect for any man who wants to look stylish and trendy without spending hours in front of the mirror.

Description

The mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top is a classic cut that has been around for decades. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways to suit your personal style. This cut features short hair on the sides and back, while the top is left longer.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting this style, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who is experienced in cutting mens hair. Once you've found someone you trust, it's time to discuss the style and how you want it to look. Here are the steps to getting the perfect mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the look you're going for. Some men prefer a longer top, while others prefer a shorter top. Discuss your options with your barber or hairstylist to find the right length for you.

Step 2: Determine the Fade

The next step is to determine the fade you want on the sides and back of your head. A fade is when the hair gradually gets shorter as it goes down the head. There are many different types of fades, including low fades, high fades, and skin fades. Discuss your options with your barber or hairstylist to find the right fade for you.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once the top and sides have been cut, it's time to style the top. There are many different ways to style the top of a mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top, including slicked back, messy, or textured. Discuss your options with your barber or hairstylist to find the right style for you.

Tips for Styling

Once you have your mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top, it's important to know how to style it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

  • Use a good quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to hold your hair in place
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair in the direction you want it to go
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy

FAQ

What face shape is best for a mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top?

A mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top works well for most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on men with square or oval faces.

What is the best way to maintain a mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top?

The best way to maintain this style is to get regular trims to keep the sides and back short, and to use a good quality styling product to keep the top in place.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, this style is versatile and can be styled in many different ways to suit different occasions. For example, you could slick it back for a formal event or leave it messy for a casual day out.

Hair Care

To keep your mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a mens hairstyle short on sides longer on top with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a classic look or something more modern and edgy, this cut is sure to turn heads and make you feel great.
