What Hairstyles Suit Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles and never finding one that suits your round face? Look no further, as we bring you a comprehensive guide on what hairstyles suit round faces. Read on to find out more.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a wider forehead, rounded chin, and fuller cheeks. Finding the right hairstyle that flatters your features can be a challenge. But with the right knowledge, you can easily find a hairstyle that complements your face shape.

How to

Here's how to determine if you have a round face:

  1. Measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If they are all similar in width, you have a round face.
  2. Look at your face in the mirror and see if it appears circular.

Once you have determined that you have a round face, it's time to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Opt for hairstyles that add height to your face, such as a high ponytail or an updo.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a blunt fringe or a bob that ends at the chin.
  3. Choose hairstyles that have layers or angles, as they can create the illusion of a longer face.
  4. Try out hairstyles that have side-swept bangs, as they can help to elongate your face.
  5. Consider a hairstyle that has volume at the crown, as it can help to balance your face shape.
  6. Go for hairstyles that have length, as they can help to elongate your face.
  7. Avoid hairstyles that have too much volume at the sides, as they can make your face look wider.
  8. Consider getting highlights or lowlights that frame your face, as they can help to create the illusion of a longer face.
  9. Try out different hairstyles and see what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find a hairstyle that flatters your features.
  • Consider getting a haircut that suits your hair texture as well as your face shape.
  • Be mindful of the products you use on your hair, as some can weigh it down and make it appear flat.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair while blow-drying.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyles suit round faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a blunt fringe or a bob that ends at the chin.

What hairstyles flatter a round face?

Hairstyles that add height to your face, have layers or angles, and have side-swept bangs can flatter a round face.

Can I wear my hair short if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair short if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features.

Hair Care

Maintain healthy hair by washing it regularly, conditioning it, and using hair products that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge, you can easily find a hairstyle that flatters your features. Try out different hairstyles and consult with your hairstylist to find a hairstyle that suits you best. Remember to maintain healthy hair by following a proper hair care routine.
