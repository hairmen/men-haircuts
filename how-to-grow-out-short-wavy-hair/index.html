How To Grow Out Short Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short wavy hair and want to grow it out, you're not alone. Many people with this hair type struggle with growing it out because it can be difficult to manage and style in the awkward in-between stages. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can grow out your short wavy hair and achieve long, beautiful locks.

Description

Short wavy hair can be a challenge to grow out because it often requires more maintenance than other hair types. However, with the right care and styling techniques, you can achieve your desired length and style. It's important to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, as it can take time to see results.

How to Grow Out Short Wavy Hair

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Foundation

The key to growing out any hair type is to start with a healthy foundation. This means eating a balanced diet, staying hydrated, and taking care of your hair and scalp. Make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Step 2: Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow out faster. This is because it helps to prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth process. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Step 3: Experiment with Styles

One of the biggest challenges of growing out short wavy hair is finding a style that works for you in the in-between stages. Experiment with different styles, such as braids, updos, and half-up hairstyles, to find a look that you love. This will also help to keep your hair healthy and protected while you grow it out.

Step 4: Use the Right Products

Using the right products can also make a big difference when it comes to growing out short wavy hair. Look for products that are designed to add moisture and nourishment to your hair, such as a leave-in conditioner or hair oil. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.

Tips for Growing Out Short Wavy Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you grow out your short wavy hair:

  • Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage.
  • Avoid using tight hair elastics or clips, as these can cause breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb to prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out short wavy hair:

Q: How long does it take to grow out short wavy hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to grow out short wavy hair, depending on your individual hair growth rate.

Q: Can I still color or highlight my hair while growing it out?

A: Yes, but it's important to be gentle and avoid harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Consider using a semi-permanent or demi-permanent hair color to minimize damage.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from looking frizzy while growing it out?

A: Use a hair oil or serum to add moisture and shine to your hair, and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Consider getting a keratin treatment or other smoothing treatment to help tame frizz.

Hair Care for Short Wavy Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for short wavy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add moisture and shine to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can grow out your short wavy hair and achieve the long, beautiful locks you've always wanted.
