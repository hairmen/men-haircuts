How To Get Wet Curly Hair Look A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your same old curly hair routine? Want to switch things up and try out the wet curly hair look? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the trendy wet curly hair look. We will also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain healthy and bouncy curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Wet Curly Hair Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Wet Curly Hair Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair

How to Get Wet Curly Hair Look

The wet curly hair look is achieved by applying a hair gel or mousse to damp hair and scrunching the curls to create a defined and bouncy effect. This hairstyle has been trending on social media platforms and is the perfect look for a night out or a summer day at the beach.

What You Will Need:

  • Hair gel or mousse
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Microfiber towel
  • Hair dryer with a diffuser attachment (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect wet curly hair look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to retain moisture in your hair and prevent it from drying out.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair. Do not rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Hair Gel or Mousse

Apply a quarter-sized amount of hair gel or mousse to your damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to create defined curls. Continue scrunching until your hair is fully dry.

Step 5: Optional - Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you have a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment, you can use this to speed up the drying process. Set your hair dryer to the lowest heat setting and use the diffuser to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands.

Tips for Achieving the Wet Curly Hair Look

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect wet curly hair look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply hair gel or mousse to damp hair to help define your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to create defined curls.
  • Do not touch your hair while it is drying to prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process.

FAQs

Is the wet curly hair look suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the wet curly hair look can be achieved on all hair types. However, it may look different depending on your hair's texture and length.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain the wet curly hair look?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain the wet curly hair look. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair

To maintain healthy and bouncy curls, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from rubbing against cotton and causing friction.

With these tips and step-by-step guide, you can easily achieve the trendy wet curly hair look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and bouncy.
