How To Cut Your Own Messy Bob

Written by Luffy Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few weeks to get your hair trimmed? Do you want to save some money and cut your own hair at home? If you have a messy bob hairstyle and want to give it a fresh trim, then this article is for you. In this post, we'll show you how to cut your own messy bob in five easy steps.

Description

A messy bob is a popular hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, casual look. It's a short haircut that is typically chin-length or shorter and has layers throughout. The messy bob is great for those who have naturally straight or wavy hair.

How to Cut Your Own Messy Bob

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair-cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. This will make it easier to cut and style. You can also add some product to your hair to make it more manageable.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and clip the two sections away from each other. Then, take a small section of hair from the back and cut it to your desired length. This will be your guide for the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Starting with one side of your head, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the back and working your way towards the front. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it to your liking. You can use a curling iron or straightener to add some texture and volume to your hair. You can also add some product to give your hair some hold.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little bit at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't undo a bad haircut.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small trim and work your way up to a bigger cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so make sure you give yourself some rest.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair if it's curly. Just make sure you cut it when it's dry, as wet curly hair can be longer than when it's dry.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy and fresh look.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a salon to fix it or wait for it to grow out. It's also a good idea to have someone else check your hair after you've cut it.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. You can also use a hair mask once a week to give your hair some extra nourishment.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these five easy steps, you can give yourself a fresh and stylish messy bob haircut. Just remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and have fun!
