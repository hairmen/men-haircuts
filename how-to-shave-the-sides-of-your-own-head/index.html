How To Shave The Sides Of Your Own Head A Step By Step Guide

Shaving the sides of your own head can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done easily and efficiently. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to shave the sides of your own head. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair post-shave, as well as answer some frequently asked questions.

  • How to Shave the Sides of Your Own Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Shave the Sides of Your Own Head

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it is important to have the proper tools. Here are the items you will need:

  • Electric hair clippers
  • Mirror
  • Hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Shaving cream or gel
  • Razor
  • Towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have the necessary tools, let's go through the steps of how to shave the sides of your own head:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Using the hair cutting scissors, trim any excess hair on the sides of your head.
  3. Using the electric hair clippers with the desired guard length, start at the bottom of your head and work your way up, making sure to use a fluid motion.
  4. Repeat step 3 on the other side of your head.
  5. Remove the guard from the electric hair clippers and use them to trim the hair around your ears.
  6. Apply shaving cream or gel to the sides of your head and use the razor to shave any remaining hair.
  7. Rinse your head with cool water and towel-dry it.

Tips for Shaving Your Own Head

Here are some tips to help make the process of shaving the sides of your own head easier:

  • Use a mirror to ensure you are shaving evenly.
  • Start with a longer guard length and work your way down if needed.
  • Use short, fluid motions when shaving with the electric hair clippers.
  • Apply shaving cream or gel to avoid razor burn.
  • Take breaks if needed to avoid fatigue in your arms and hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions when it comes to shaving the sides of your own head:

Is it better to shave wet or dry?

It is recommended to shave wet as it helps to soften the hair and avoid razor burn.

What guard length should I use?

The guard length will depend on personal preference, but starting with a longer guard length and working your way down is a good way to ensure you don't cut too much hair at once.

How often should I shave the sides of my head?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 2-4 weeks is a good timeframe.

Hair Care Post-Shave

Now that you have successfully shaved the sides of your own head, it is important to take care of your hair post-shave. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo to avoid irritation.
  • Apply a soothing lotion or balm to avoid razor burn.
  • Moisturize your scalp to avoid dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair for at least a few days.

Shaving the sides of your own head can be a fun and empowering experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a fresh and clean look. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if needed.
