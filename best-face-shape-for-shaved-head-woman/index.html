Best Face Shape For Shaved Head Woman Find Your Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Nov 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking about shaving your head but wondering if it will suit your face shape? Don't worry, we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best face shape for shaved head woman and some tips to help you rock your new look confidently. Let's dive right in.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best face shape for shaved head woman, it's important to determine your face shape first. Here are the steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker to outline the shape of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the outline of your face.
  4. Compare the outline to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose the best shaved head look that suits you.

Best Face Shape for Shaved Head Woman - Oval

Lucky you! If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off any shaved head look effortlessly. An oval face shape is balanced and symmetrical, so you can choose any style that you like.

Best Face Shape for Shaved Head Woman - Round

If you have a round face shape, you want to avoid adding volume at the top of your head. Opt for a shaved head look that is shorter on the sides and longer on the top. This will create an illusion of length and balance out your face shape.

Best Face Shape for Shaved Head Woman - Square

For square face shapes, you want to soften your features with a shaved head look that has a lot of texture and volume. A buzz cut with longer layers on top or a side-swept fringe will create a more balanced look.

Best Face Shape for Shaved Head Woman - Heart

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your forehead and jawline. A buzz cut with longer layers on the sides or a textured pixie cut will create a more balanced look for your face shape.

Best Face Shape for Shaved Head Woman - Diamond

A diamond face shape is angular and narrow at the forehead and jawline. A shaved head look that adds volume at the top and sides will create a more balanced look. A textured crop or a buzz cut with longer layers on top will suit a diamond face shape perfectly.

Tips to Rock Your Shaved Head Look

  • Keep your scalp moisturized and protected from the sun.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, hats, and scarves to add some personality to your look.
  • Try different makeup looks to accentuate your features.
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention to your face.
  • Confidence is key – embrace your new look and own it!

FAQs

Will a shaved head look suit me?

Yes, a shaved head look can suit anyone regardless of their face shape or hair type. It's all about finding the right style that suits you.

Will my hair grow back the same after shaving my head?

Yes, your hair will grow back the same after shaving your head. However, the texture and thickness of your hair may change over time.

How often do I need to shave my head?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. You may need to shave your head every two weeks or every month.

Hair Care for Shaved Head Women

Just because you have shaved your head doesn't mean you should neglect your hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your scalp healthy:

  • Moisturize your scalp with oils or creams to prevent dryness and itching.
  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun with hats or sunscreen.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol or harsh chemicals.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

Now that you know the best face shape for shaved head woman, it's time to embrace your new look with confidence. Remember, it's all about finding the right style that suits you and owning it. Happy shaving!
