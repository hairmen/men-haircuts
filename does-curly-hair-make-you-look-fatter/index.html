Does Curly Hair Make You Look Fatter The Truth Revealed

Written by Petter Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair has always been a topic of discussion when it comes to how it affects your appearance. One of the common questions asked is whether curly hair makes you look fatter or not. Well, the answer is not as simple as a yes or no. It depends on various factors that we will discuss in this article. So, if you have been wondering about the same question, keep reading.

Description

Curly hair has a tendency to add volume to your hair, which can make your face look rounder and fuller. This is why people often think that curly hair makes them look fatter. However, the truth is that it's not the curls that make you look fat, but how you style them. The way you cut and style your curly hair can either make you look slimmer or add more weight to your face.

How to Determine If Curly Hair Makes You Look Fatter

The easiest way to determine if your curly hair makes you look fatter is by looking at your face shape. If you have a round face, then curly hair can add more volume to your hair, making your face look fuller. On the other hand, if you have an oval or heart-shaped face, then curly hair can complement your face shape and make you look slimmer.

Another way to determine if curly hair makes you look fatter is by looking at the length of your hair. If you have long curly hair, then it can add more weight to your face and make you look heavier. However, if you have short curly hair, then it can give you a slimmer look.

Step by Step Guide to Achieve the Perfect Curly Hair Look

To achieve the perfect curly hair look, follow these simple steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to your damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips to Style Curly Hair without Looking Fat

Here are some tips to style your curly hair without looking fat:

  • Get a haircut that complements your face shape.
  • Avoid heavy layers that can add more volume to your hair.
  • Use lightweight products that won't weigh down your curls.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can make your face look rounder.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for you.

FAQs

Q. Can curly hair make your face look slimmer?

A. Yes, curly hair can make your face look slimmer if styled correctly.

Q. Does the length of your hair affect how curly hair makes you look?

A. Yes, the length of your hair can affect how curly hair makes you look. Short curly hair can give you a slimmer look, while long curly hair can add more weight to your face.

Q. Can a different hair color make you look slimmer with curly hair?

A. Yes, a different hair color can make you look slimmer with curly hair. Darker hair colors tend to make your face look slimmer, while lighter hair colors can make your face look rounder.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your curls.

In conclusion, curly hair does not necessarily make you look fatter. It depends on various factors such as your face shape, hair length, and how you style your curls. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can achieve the perfect curly hair look without adding more weight to your face. Remember to take care of your curls by following a proper hair care routine to keep them healthy and hydrated.
