Haircut According To Face Shape Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 · 5 min read

As a man, getting a haircut is one of those things that you just have to do every few weeks. However, have you ever considered that the way you cut your hair might depend on the shape of your face? That's right, your face shape can have a huge impact on how your hair looks and what styles will suit you best. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting a haircut according to your face shape as a male.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different haircuts for various face shapes, it's essential to determine what your face shape is. Here are the different face shapes and how to identify them:

Round Face

If your face is circular, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a round face.

Square Face

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, you have a square face.

Oval Face

If your face is longer than it is wide and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have an oval face.

Heart-Shaped Face

If your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your face narrows towards your chin, you have a heart-shaped face.

Diamond-Shaped Face

If your forehead and jawline are narrower than your cheekbones, you have a diamond-shaped face.

Haircuts for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you'll want to avoid haircuts that add extra volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for haircuts that add height to your hair and elongate your face. Some great haircuts for round faces include the pompadour, the side part, and the slicked-back undercut.

Haircuts for Square Faces

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head. Some great haircuts for square faces include the textured crop, the messy fringe, and the side-swept undercut.

Haircuts for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Almost any haircut will look good on you. However, if you want to accentuate your features, try a short back and sides with a longer top or a messy quiff.

Haircuts for Heart-Shaped Faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to balance out your features with a haircut that adds volume to the top and sides of your head. Some great haircuts for heart-shaped faces include the messy crop, the classic side part, and the textured fringe.

Haircuts for Diamond-Shaped Faces

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to balance out your features with a haircut that adds volume to the top and sides of your head. Some great haircuts for diamond-shaped faces include the comb-over, the classic side part, and the messy fringe.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your face shape, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help style your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still get a haircut that doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! Ultimately, it's up to you to decide what haircut you want. However, keep in mind that certain haircuts may not be as flattering as others, depending on your face shape.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and what style you have. Typically, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care for Men

In addition to getting the right haircut for your face shape, it's essential to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

In conclusion, getting the right haircut for your face shape can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. By following the tips outlined in this article, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and maintain it properly.
