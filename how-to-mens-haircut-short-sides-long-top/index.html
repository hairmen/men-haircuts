How To Men s Haircut Short Sides Long Top

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, the short sides long top style is a great choice. This haircut features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on the top that can be styled in various ways. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look and give you some tips on how to maintain it.

How to Achieve the Short Sides Long Top Haircut

The short sides long top haircut is a popular style among men because it's easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways. To achieve this look, you'll need to cut the sides and back of your hair short while leaving the top longer.

This style works well with different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. You can also experiment with different hair lengths on the top, depending on your preference.

What You'll Need

To achieve the short sides long top haircut, you'll need the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair wax or gel

Alternatively, you can visit a professional hairstylist to get the haircut done.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair

If you're planning to cut your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Using clippers, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. You can start with a higher guard and gradually decrease it until you achieve the desired length.
  3. Blend the sides and back with scissors. Comb your hair upwards and use scissors to cut any stray hairs that stick out.
  4. Cut the hair on the top to your desired length. You can use scissors or clippers to achieve the desired length. Make sure to leave enough length on the top to style your hair.
  5. Style your hair using hair wax or gel. You can experiment with different styles, such as a side part or a messy fringe.

Remember to take your time while cutting your hair, and use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've achieved the short sides long top haircut, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Visit a hairstylist every four to six weeks to trim the sides and back of your hair.
  • Use hair wax or gel to style your hair, and avoid using too much product, which can make your hair look greasy.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and updated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I achieve the short sides long top haircut with curly hair?

Yes, this haircut works well with curly hair. You can leave more length on the top to showcase your curls.

What face shape does the short sides long top haircut suit?

This haircut works well with different face shapes, including oval, square, and round faces.

How do I choose the right length for the sides and back of my hair?

The length you choose depends on your preference and the shape of your head. You can start with a higher guard and gradually decrease it until you achieve the desired length.

Hair Care Tips for Short Sides Long Top Haircut

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short sides long top haircut healthy and shiny:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove excess oil.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip away natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your short sides long top haircut looking stylish and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve the short sides long top haircut and maintain it. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured style, this haircut offers endless possibilities. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your new haircut!
