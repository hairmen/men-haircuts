Are you looking for a new haircut that will flatter your round face? Short curly haircuts might just be the perfect match for you! In this guide, we will show you some pictures of short curly haircuts for round faces and give you some tips on how to style and care for your hair. Let's get started!

Description

Short curly haircuts are a great choice for round faces because they add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out the roundness of the face. The curls can also help elongate the face and accentuate the cheekbones. There are many different types of short curly haircuts to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How to

When choosing a short curly haircut for a round face, it's important to keep in mind the length and shape of the haircut. Ideally, you want a haircut that will elongate the face and create the illusion of more height. A layered bob or a shaggy pixie cut are great options for achieving this look.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your haircut can make a big difference in how it flatters your face shape. For round faces, shorter haircuts are often the best choice because they create the illusion of more height. A pixie cut or a bob that falls just below the chin are both great options.

Consider the Shape

The shape of your haircut is also important. A layered bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back can help elongate the face, while a shaggy pixie cut with lots of layers can add volume and texture to the hair. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make the face look rounder.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when styling your short curly haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and protect your curls.
  2. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  3. If you want more definition, apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or a gel, to your hair and scrunch it gently with your hands.
  4. Finish off your style with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place without weighing them down.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

What face shape is best for short curly haircuts?

Short curly haircuts can work for many different face shapes, but they are especially flattering for round faces. The curls can help elongate the face and create the illusion of more height.

What length should I choose for my short curly haircut?

For round faces, shorter haircuts are often the best choice because they create the illusion of more height. A pixie cut or a bob that falls just below the chin are both great options.

How can I style my short curly haircut?

To style your short curly haircut, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls. If you want more definition, apply a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair gently with your hands. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for taking care of your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and some inspiration from our pictures of short curly haircuts for round faces, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and style it with confidence. Happy styling!
