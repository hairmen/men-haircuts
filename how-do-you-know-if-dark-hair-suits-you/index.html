How Do You Know If Dark Hair Suits You

Written by Aprilia Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Dark hair can be a bold statement, but how do you know if it's the right choice for you? In this article, we'll discuss how to determine if dark hair suits you, step-by-step tips, and hair care advice to keep your dark locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine If Dark Hair Suits You
  2. Step-by-Step Tips
  3. Hair Care Advice
  4. FAQ

How to Determine If Dark Hair Suits You

The first step in determining if dark hair suits you is to consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, you may want to opt for a warmer shade of dark hair, such as chestnut or chocolate brown. If you have a cool skin tone, you may want to go for a cooler shade of dark hair, such as jet black or ash brown.

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, it's more likely that dark hair will suit you. However, if you have lighter hair, you may want to gradually darken your hair to see how it looks on you before committing to a full color change.

Finally, consider your personal style and lifestyle. Dark hair can be a dramatic change, so make sure it aligns with your overall aesthetic. Additionally, consider the maintenance and upkeep required for dark hair, such as regular root touch-ups and color-safe hair care products.

Step-by-Step Tips

If you've decided that dark hair is right for you, here are some step-by-step tips to help you achieve your desired look:

1. Choose the Right Shade

As we mentioned earlier, it's important to choose a shade of dark hair that complements your skin tone. Consult with a hair stylist or colorist to determine the best shade for you.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Before coloring your hair, make sure it's in good condition. Deep condition your hair leading up to your salon appointment to ensure it's healthy and strong enough to withstand the coloring process.

3. Trust the Professionals

Although it may be tempting to color your hair at home, it's best to trust the professionals. A licensed hair stylist or colorist can help you achieve your desired look while minimizing damage to your hair.

4. Maintain Your Color

Once you've achieved your desired dark hair color, make sure to maintain it with color-safe hair care products and regular root touch-ups.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your dark hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Use Color-Safe Hair Care Products

Regular shampoos and conditioners can strip color from your hair, so make sure to use color-safe hair care products to maintain your dark hair color.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Aim to deep condition your hair at least once a week.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause your dark color to fade. Use heat protectant products and avoid using hot tools on high heat settings.

FAQ

Q: Will dark hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Dark hair can be a timeless and elegant choice, regardless of age.

Q: Can I dye my hair dark at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair dark at home, it's best to consult with a professional to ensure you achieve your desired look without damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable your regrowth is. Aim to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks, or as needed.

Now that you know how to determine if dark hair suits you, follow these tips and hair care advice to achieve and maintain your desired look. Whether you opt for a subtle dark brown or a bold black, dark hair can be a striking and flattering choice.
