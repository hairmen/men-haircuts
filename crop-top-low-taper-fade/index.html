Crop Top Low Taper Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut? Look no further than the crop top low taper fade! This haircut has been making waves in the fashion industry and is a favorite among men of all ages. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about the crop top low taper fade, including how to get the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

The crop top low taper fade is a modern take on the classic crop top haircut. It features short hair on top that is styled with a messy, textured look, and a gradual fade on the sides and back. The low taper fade starts just above the ear and gradually fades to the skin at the nape of the neck. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and edgy look that is easy to maintain.

How to Get the Look

If you want to get the crop top low taper fade, you will need to visit a professional barber or hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Here are the steps involved in getting the crop top low taper fade:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your haircut, you will have a consultation with your barber or hairstylist. During this time, you should discuss the look you want to achieve and any concerns you may have. Your barber or hairstylist will also examine your hair type and face shape to determine the best style for you.

Step 2: Cutting the Top

The top of your hair will be cut with scissors to the desired length. You can choose to have your hair cut short or longer, depending on your preference. Your barber or hairstylist will also add texture to the hair to achieve the messy, textured look.

Step 3: Fading the Sides and Back

The sides and back of your hair will be faded gradually using clippers. The fade will start just above the ear and gradually blend into the skin at the nape of the neck. Your barber or hairstylist will use different clipper guards to achieve the desired length and blend.

Step 4: Styling

Once the haircut is complete, your barber or hairstylist will style your hair using a hair product, such as pomade or wax. The product will help achieve the messy, textured look and hold the hair in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you want to rock the crop top low taper fade:

  • Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair looking great all day long.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.
  • Keep your hair clean and healthy by washing it regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.

FAQs

What face shape is best suited for the crop top low taper fade?

The crop top low taper fade is suitable for most face shapes, including round, square, and oval. Your barber or hairstylist can help determine the best style for you based on your face shape.

What hair type is best suited for the crop top low taper fade?

The crop top low taper fade is suitable for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly. Your barber or hairstylist can help determine the best style for you based on your hair type.

How often should I visit the barber or hairstylist for a trim?

You should visit the barber or hairstylist every 3-4 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking great, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair looking great all day long.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the crop top low taper fade, it's time to get your own! With the right cut, styling, and care, you can rock this trendy and stylish haircut with confidence.
