How Do I Know If I Have Curly Hair Male

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is a popular hair type for men. However, not all men are aware that they have curly hair. It can be confusing for some because curly hair can be difficult to spot, especially when it's not styled. In this article, we'll go through the signs that can help you determine if you have curly hair as a male.

Table of Contents

  • Signs of Curly Hair
  • Hair Type Chart
  • How to Care for Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Signs of Curly Hair

The first sign of curly hair is the shape of the hair follicles. If the hair follicles are oval, the hair is likely to be curly. In contrast, if the hair follicles are round, the hair is likely to be straight. Another sign is the texture of your hair. Curly hair tends to be coarser and thicker than straight hair. It also tends to have more volume and can be harder to manage.

If you're still unsure, there are a few other signs that can help you determine if you have curly hair. One is if your hair tends to frizz or if it's difficult to comb or brush. Additionally, if your hair is prone to tangles, you may have curly hair. Finally, if your hair has a natural wave or curl, it's likely that you have curly hair.

Hair Type Chart

It can be helpful to determine your hair type to better understand how to care for it. The hair type chart includes four categories: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Curly hair is further divided into three subcategories: loose, classic, and tight.

Loose curls are the largest curls and tend to be more relaxed. Classic curls are medium-sized curls that are tighter than loose curls. Tight curls are the smallest curls and are the most defined.

You can determine your hair type by taking a strand of hair and examining it closely. If you have straight hair, the hair will be completely straight. If you have wavy hair, the hair will have a slight "S" shape. If you have curly hair, the hair will have a spiral or coil shape. Finally, if you have coily hair, the hair will be tightly coiled or have a zig-zag pattern.

How to Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be challenging, but it's essential to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often and use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers, flat irons, and curling irons as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and add moisture to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's essential to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your curly hair once or twice a week to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I brush my curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid using a brush on curly hair as it can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

In conclusion, determining if you have curly hair as a male can be challenging, but there are a few signs that can help you identify your hair type. Once you know your hair type, it's essential to care for it correctly to keep it healthy and looking its best.
