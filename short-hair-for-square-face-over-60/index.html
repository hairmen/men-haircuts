Short Hair For Square Face Over 60 Tips How To And Hair Care

If you are looking for a new hairstyle to complement your square face over 60, short hair can be a great option. With the right cut and style, short hair can flatter your face shape and make you look more youthful and vibrant. In this article, we will share tips, how-tos, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect short hair for your square face over 60.

Description

Short haircuts for square faces over 60 should balance out the strong jawline and angular features of the face. The length of the hair should be kept above the chin and should not be too voluminous on the sides. A good rule of thumb is to keep the hair close to the head and avoid adding too much height on top. Short haircuts also work well for women with thinning hair as they can add volume and texture to the hair.

How to

The first step in getting the perfect short hair for your square face over 60 is to find a good hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience working with mature hair and can provide you with a consultation to determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture.

When choosing a haircut, consider a bob or a pixie cut. These styles are classic and timeless and can be customized to fit your individual style. A bob can be cut to chin-length or slightly shorter and can be styled with layers and texture to add volume and movement. A pixie cut can be very short or slightly longer and can be styled with bangs or layers to soften the face.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect short haircut for your square face over 60:

  1. Find a good hairstylist who has experience working with mature hair.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Choose a classic and timeless style such as a bob or a pixie cut.
  4. Customize the cut with layers and texture to add volume and movement.
  5. Style the hair with a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  6. Finish the style with a light hairspray or styling product to hold the shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short hair for your square face over 60:

  • Avoid adding too much height on top of the head as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Choose a side part to soften the angular features of the face.
  • Add texture and layers to the hair to add volume and movement.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to the hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and fullness to the hair.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a square face over 60?

The best short haircuts for square faces over 60 are bobs and pixie cuts. These styles balance out the strong jawline and angular features of the face and can be customized to fit your individual style.

How do I choose the right short haircut for my square face over 60?

Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with mature hair to determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture. Consider a classic and timeless style such as a bob or a pixie cut and customize the cut with layers and texture to add volume and movement.

Hair Care

Short hair is easy to maintain, but it still requires regular care and maintenance to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair and use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.
  • Trim the hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to the hair.
  • Avoid using too much product on the hair as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Short hair for square faces over 60 can be a great way to update your look and feel more youthful and vibrant. With the right cut and style, short hair can flatter your face shape and make you feel more confident and beautiful.
