What Haircut Suits Me Test A Guide For Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Aprilia Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to switch things up, but don't know which haircut would suit you best? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people struggle with finding the right haircut that complements their face shape, hair texture, and personal style. However, with the help of the "what haircut suits me test", you can easily discover your perfect hairstyle. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of taking the test and provide tips on how to care for your hair once you've found your ideal haircut.

What is the "What Haircut Suits Me Test"?

The "what haircut suits me test" is a series of questions that assess your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Based on your answers, the test recommends a range of hairstyles that are best suited for you. This test is widely available online and can be taken for free. It's a great tool to use if you're unsure about what haircut to get and want to make an informed decision.

How to Take the Test

Taking the "what haircut suits me test" is simple and straightforward. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

To take the test, you'll need a clear picture of your face and a measuring tape. Make sure the picture is well-lit and shows your face from the front.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Use the measuring tape to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Based on these measurements, determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Step 3: Answer the Questions

Take the "what haircut suits me test" and answer the questions honestly. The questions will ask about your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Make sure to answer all the questions to get the most accurate results.

Step 4: Get Your Results

Once you've completed the test, you'll receive a list of recommended hairstyles that are best suited for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Take some time to review the options and choose the one that you like the most.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, use products that are designed for curly hair. This will help to enhance your hair's natural texture and keep it looking healthy.

Tip 2: Get Regular Trims

To maintain your hairstyle, it's important to get regular trims. This will help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and prevent split ends from forming.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools. Also, avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "what haircut suits me test":

What if I'm not happy with the recommended hairstyles?

If you're not happy with the recommended hairstyles, you can retake the test and answer the questions differently. You can also consult a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Can I take the test if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still take the test if you have short hair. The test takes into account your hair texture and face shape, so it can recommend hairstyles that are best suited for you, regardless of your hair length.

How often should I take the test?

You can take the test as often as you like, but we recommend taking it every time you want to switch up your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Having a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Follow these tips to maintain healthy hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that's suitable for your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Tip 2: Use a Conditioner

Use a conditioner after shampooing to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent tangles.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF. This will help to prevent sun damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

In conclusion, the "what haircut suits me test" is a great tool to use if you're looking for a new hairstyle. By taking the test and following the tips for maintaining your new haircut, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that complements your face shape and personal style.
