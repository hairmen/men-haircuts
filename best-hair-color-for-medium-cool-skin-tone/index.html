Best Hair Color For Medium Cool Skin Tone The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 27, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re a medium cool skin tone girl, then you’re in luck! As there are a plethora of hair color options that can accentuate your skin tone and make you look stunning. Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be overwhelming, but don’t worry, we’re here to help.

Description

Medium cool skin tone is characterized by having pink or blue undertones. This skin tone looks great with cool-toned hair colors that have blue or purple undertones. Hair colors that are too warm or golden can make the skin look dull and washed out.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When choosing the right hair color for medium cool skin tone, you should consider the following factors:

  • The undertones of your skin
  • Your natural hair color
  • Your personal style and preferences

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the perfect hair color for your medium cool skin tone:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, then you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, then you have a warm skin tone. If you can’t tell, then you might have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Choose Your Undertone

Once you’ve determined your skin tone, you need to choose the right undertone for your hair color. For medium cool skin tone, choose hair colors that have blue or purple undertones.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

If you’re going for a drastic change, you might need to bleach your hair first. If you want to go for a subtle change, choose a hair color that is close to your natural hair color.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you’re not sure what hair color will suit you best, it’s always best to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can recommend the best hair color for your skin tone and help you achieve your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color for medium cool skin tone:

  • Avoid hair colors that are too warm or golden
  • Choose hair colors that have blue or purple undertones
  • Consider your natural hair color
  • Consult with a professional hair stylist

FAQ

Q: What hair colors are best for medium cool skin tone?

A: Hair colors that have blue or purple undertones are best for medium cool skin tone. Some examples include ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, and black.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: It’s possible to dye your hair at home, but it’s always best to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose the right hair color and ensure that it’s applied correctly.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking vibrant and healthy for longer.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color for medium cool skin tone can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. Remember to consider your skin tone, undertone, natural hair color, and personal style when choosing a hair color. And don’t forget to take care of your hair after coloring it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.
