How To Do A Formal Low Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a simple yet elegant hairstyle for your next formal event? Look no further than the low ponytail! A timeless classic, the low ponytail is a versatile and chic hairstyle that can be dressed up or down to suit any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to create a formal low ponytail that is perfect for weddings, proms, and other special events.

How to Create a Formal Low Ponytail

Here's what you'll need to create a formal low ponytail:

  • A comb
  • A brush
  • A hair tie
  • Hairspray
  • Bobby pins (optional)

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first for a sleeker look.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Using your comb, create a deep side part in your hair. This will give your ponytail a more formal, elegant look.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Next, use your brush to smooth out any tangles or knots in your hair. Make sure your hair is completely smooth and sleek before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Gather Your Hair

Using your comb, gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure, but not so tight that it pulls on your scalp.

Step 5: Secure Your Ponytail

Wrap a hair tie around your ponytail several times to secure it in place. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your ponytail in place, but not so tight that it creates a dent in your hair.

Step 6: Smooth Out Your Ponytail

Use your brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your ponytail. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

Step 7: Add Hairspray

Finally, spritz some hairspray over your ponytail to help it stay in place throughout the day or evening.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Low Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect low ponytail:

  • If you have thin hair, try backcombing your hair at the crown of your head before creating your ponytail. This will give your hair more volume and make your ponytail look fuller.
  • If you have thick hair, use a hair tie that is strong enough to hold your hair in place.
  • If you want to add some extra glamour to your ponytail, try wrapping a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it.
  • If you have bangs, you can keep them loose or sweep them to the side for a more sophisticated look.

FAQ

Can I wear a low ponytail to a formal event?

Yes! A low ponytail is a classic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for formal events like weddings, proms, and galas. Just make sure your hair is smooth and sleek, and use hairspray to help your ponytail stay in place throughout the evening.

What if my hair is too short for a low ponytail?

If your hair is too short for a low ponytail, you can try a half-up, half-down hairstyle instead. This will give you a similar look, but with less hair. You can also try using hair extensions to add length and volume to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair ties that are too tight, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a stunning formal low ponytail that is sure to turn heads. Whether you're attending a wedding, prom, or other special event, this classic hairstyle is sure to make you look and feel like a million bucks.
