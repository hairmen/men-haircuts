How To Get Curls For Black Males A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 22, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're a black male looking to get curls, you've come to the right place. Getting curls can be a tricky process, but with the right steps and products, you can achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get curls for black males, from the basics to advanced techniques.

Table of Contents:

  • The Basics of Getting Curls
  • How to Get Curls for Black Males
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curls
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

The Basics of Getting Curls

Before we dive into how to get curls for black males, it's important to understand the basics of curly hair. Curly hair is characterized by its shape, which is usually a spiral or zigzag pattern. This shape is caused by the hair follicles being oval or elliptical instead of round, which causes the hair to grow in a curved direction.

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but there are a few things you can do to help keep your curls looking their best. First, make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. This may include shampoo, conditioner, and styling products specifically designed for curly hair.

It's also important to avoid using heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness. Instead, opt for air-drying or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

How to Get Curls for Black Males

Now that you understand the basics of curly hair, let's dive into how to get curls for black males. There are a few different methods you can try, depending on your hair type and desired look.

Method 1: Twist-Out

The twist-out method is a popular way to achieve curls for black males. To do a twist-out, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section of hair tightly, working from the root to the tip.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the twists to reveal your curls.

This method can take some practice to perfect, but it can create beautiful, defined curls.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another method for getting curls for black males. To create Bantu knots, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Twist each section of hair tightly, working from the root to the tip.
  4. Wrap each twisted section around itself to create a knot.
  5. Secure each knot with a bobby pin or hair elastic.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  7. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the knots to reveal your curls.

This method can create more voluminous, less defined curls than the twist-out method.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curls for black males:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Choose a curling method (twist-out or Bantu knots).
  5. Follow the steps for your chosen method to create curls.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  7. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the twists or knots to reveal your curls.
  8. Use a light hold styling product, such as a curl defining gel or mousse, to help set your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curls

Once you've achieved your desired curls, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Refresh your curls between washes by spritzing them with water and applying a small amount of curl defining product.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your curls, as this can cause them to lose their shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get curls if I have relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can still get curls if you have relaxed hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve defined curls, as relaxed hair is typically straighter than natural hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain my curls?

A: How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every few days, while others wash it once a week or less often.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to get curls?

A: Yes, you can use hair extensions to add curls to your hair. Look for extensions made from human hair, as these will blend more seamlessly with your natural hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can strip your hair of moisture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls as a black male. Remember to be patient and practice your chosen method to perfect your curling routine.
