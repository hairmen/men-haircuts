How To Cut Men s Hair Short On Sides Long On Top With Clippers

Written by Aprilia Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle that's low-maintenance, then you might want to consider cutting your hair short on the sides and long on top. This style is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's hair short on sides long on top with clippers in relaxed English language.

Description

The short on sides long on top haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. It's also a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling, making it a popular choice for men with busy lifestyles.

How to

To cut men's hair short on sides long on top with clippers, you'll need a few tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper guards

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide how short you want the sides and how long you want the top. This will help you determine which clipper guard to use.

Step 3: Use the Clippers

Start by using the clippers to cut the sides of your hair. Use a clipper guard that's appropriate for the length you want. Start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up to the top of your head. Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Once you've cut the sides, use scissors to blend the hair on top with the sides. This will create a smooth transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a hair product of your choice to style the hair on top. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy and textured.

Tips

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter one if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair too short on the sides, as this can make your head look disproportionate.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be challenging, so don't rush it.

FAQ

Is it easy to cut men's hair short on sides long on top with clippers?

It can be challenging to cut your own hair, but with practice, you can get the hang of it.

What kind of clippers should I use?

You can use any clippers that are designed for cutting hair. Look for clippers that come with a variety of guards so you can choose the right length for your hair.

Can I style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

Yes, the short on sides long on top haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to wash it regularly with a gentle shampoo. Use a conditioner to keep it soft and manageable. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you're going to use a hair product, choose one that's designed for your hair type and use it sparingly.
