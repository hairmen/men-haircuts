How To Make Your Hair Look Fuller In The Front

Written by Christine Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume to the front of your hair? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make your hair look fuller in the front. From the right haircut to styling techniques, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve a full and voluminous look.

The Right Haircut

The first step to achieving fuller hair in the front is to get the right haircut. A layered cut with long layers will add volume and movement to your hair. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in a way that will frame your face and add volume to the front. If you have bangs, ask your stylist to cut them in a way that will add volume rather than weighing them down.

Products

Using the right products can also help you achieve a full and voluminous look. Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. After washing your hair, apply a volumizing mousse or root lifter to the roots of your hair. This will help lift your hair and add volume. You can also try using a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.

Blow-drying Techniques

Blow-drying your hair in the right way can also help you achieve a fuller look. Start by blow-drying your hair upside down. This will help lift your roots and add volume. Use a round brush to curl the front sections of your hair away from your face. This will create a voluminous look. Once your hair is dry, flip it back and use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways.

Teasing

Teasing your hair can also help you achieve a fuller look. Take a small section of hair from the front of your hair and backcomb it gently using a teasing comb. Repeat this process on all the sections of your hair that you want to add volume to. Once you've teased your hair, use a brush to smooth the top layer and create a polished look.

Curling

Curling your hair can also add volume and dimension to your hair. Use a curling iron to curl the front sections of your hair away from your face. This will create a voluminous and bouncy look. Once you've curled your hair, use a brush to gently brush out the curls and create a more natural look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve a fuller look:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create volume and texture.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days. Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat and lifeless.

What kind of products should I use?

You should use volumizing products such as mousse, root lifter, and dry shampoo to add volume to your hair. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to maintain its health and volume. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a full and voluminous look in the front of your hair. Remember to use the right products, blow-dry your hair in the right way, and take care of your hair to maintain its health and fullness.
