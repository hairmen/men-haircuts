Does Growing Facial Hair Make It Thicker

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are one of those men who are trying to grow a beard or mustache, you might be wondering if growing facial hair makes it thicker. Well, the answer to this question is not straightforward, but we will try to explain it in detail in this article. Here is what we will cover:

Description

The thickness of your facial hair depends on various factors, including genetics, age, hormones, and lifestyle. Some men are blessed with naturally thick and full beards, while others struggle to grow even a patchy beard. So, the answer to the question "does growing facial hair make it thicker?" is not a simple yes or no.

However, there are some steps you can take to promote thicker and fuller facial hair growth. In the following sections, we will discuss these steps in detail.

How to Grow a Thicker Beard

If you want to grow a thicker beard, you need to be patient and persistent. Growing facial hair is a slow process, and it can take several weeks or even months to see significant results. Here are some steps you can follow to promote thicker beard growth:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Your diet plays a crucial role in the health of your facial hair. Make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods like eggs, nuts, fish, and leafy greens can promote hair growth and make your beard thicker.

2. Exercise Regularly

Regular exercise can help improve blood circulation, which can promote hair growth. Make sure to engage in physical activity for at least 30 minutes every day.

3. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is crucial for your overall health, including your facial hair. Lack of sleep can lead to stress, which can affect your hormone levels and slow down beard growth. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep every night.

4. Manage Stress

Stress can have a negative impact on your beard growth. Practice stress-reducing activities like yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises to promote beard growth.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to growing a thicker beard:

Step 1: Stop Shaving

The first step to growing a beard is to stop shaving. Let your facial hair grow for at least four weeks without trimming or shaping it.

Step 2: Keep it Clean

Make sure to keep your beard clean by washing it regularly with a mild shampoo or beard wash. This will prevent dirt and bacteria buildup that can hinder beard growth.

Step 3: Use Beard Oil

Applying beard oil can help moisturize your facial hair and promote growth. Massage a few drops of beard oil into your beard every day.

Step 4: Brush Your Beard

Brushing your beard regularly can help stimulate blood circulation and promote beard growth. Use a boar bristle brush to brush your beard gently.

Step 5: Trim and Shape Your Beard

Once your beard has grown to the desired length, you can trim and shape it to your liking. Use a pair of sharp scissors or a beard trimmer to trim your beard evenly.

Tips to Grow a Thicker Beard

Here are some additional tips to help you grow a thicker and fuller beard:

1. Be Patient

Growing facial hair takes time, so be patient and don't give up. It can take several weeks or even months to see significant results.

2. Don't Overwash Your Beard

Washing your beard too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can hinder beard growth. Aim to wash your beard every two or three days.

3. Avoid Using Harsh Products

Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your beard, as they can damage your facial hair and slow down growth.

4. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your facial hair hydrated and promote growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does shaving make facial hair thicker?

No, shaving does not make facial hair thicker. This is a common myth. Shaving only removes the hair from the surface of the skin, and it has no effect on the thickness or density of the hair.

Can I use hair growth products on my beard?

Yes, you can use hair growth products like minoxidil on your beard. However, make sure to follow the instructions carefully and consult with a doctor before using any such products.

Can genetics affect beard growth?

Yes, genetics play a significant role in beard growth. Some men are genetically predisposed to have thicker and fuller beards, while others may struggle to grow even a patchy beard.

Facial Hair Care

Proper care is essential for healthy and thick facial hair growth. Here are some tips to take care of your beard:

1. Wash Your Beard

Wash your beard regularly with a mild shampoo or beard wash to keep it clean and prevent dirt buildup.

2. Apply Beard Oil

Applying beard oil can help moisturize your facial hair and prevent dryness and itching.

3. Trim Your Beard

Regular trimming can help keep your beard neat and tidy and promote growth.

4. Brush Your Beard

Brushing your beard regularly can help stimulate blood circulation and promote growth.

In conclusion, growing facial hair does not necessarily make it thicker. However, by following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can promote thicker and fuller beard growth. Remember to be patient and persistent, and take good care of your facial hair.
