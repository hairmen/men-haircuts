How To Thin Extremely Thick Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're someone with extremely thick hair, you know the struggle of trying to manage it. It can be time-consuming and difficult to style, especially during the hot summer months. One solution to this problem is to thin out your hair. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to thin extremely thick hair so that you can manage it more easily.

Table of Contents

  • How to Thin Extremely Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thinning Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Hair

How to Thin Extremely Thick Hair

There are several ways to thin out extremely thick hair, including:

  • Thinning shears
  • Razor cut
  • Point cutting
  • Texturizing scissors

Each method has its own pros and cons, so it's important to choose the one that's right for you. Thinning shears are the most common method used to thin out hair, so we'll focus on that method.

Materials Needed:

  • Thinning shears
  • Hair clips
  • Comb or brush
  • Spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to thin out your hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off your hair.
  3. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers.
  5. Use the thinning shears to cut into the hair, starting from the middle and working your way down to the end.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  7. Once you've finished thinning out your hair, style it as desired.

Make sure to only use the thinning shears on the ends of your hair. Avoid using them on the roots, as this can cause your hair to look thin and stringy.

Tips for Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out your hair:

  • Start with a small section of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Don't overdo it. You can always thin out more hair later.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair before cutting it. This will make it easier to cut.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once. You can always go back and cut more later.
  • Comb your hair regularly to prevent tangles.

FAQs

Is it safe to use thinning shears on my hair?

Yes, it is safe to use thinning shears on your hair. However, it's important to use them correctly to avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I thin out my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to thin out your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use a razor to thin out my hair?

Yes, you can use a razor to thin out your hair. However, it's important to use a razor that's specifically designed for thinning hair to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair when wet.
  • Don't wash your hair every day. This can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Thinning out your hair can be a great way to manage your thick hair. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that's easy to manage.
