Women s Haircuts For Round Faces 2020

Written by Daniel Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but don't know what would suit your round face? It can be challenging to find a haircut that flatters your face shape, but don't worry, we have got you covered. In this post, we will discuss the best women's haircuts for round faces in 2020. So, let's get started!

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are usually proportional in length and width. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is round. The goal when choosing a haircut is to create an illusion of a longer face to balance out the roundness. So, the ideal haircut for round faces is one that adds height and volume to the crown while keeping the sides sleek and not too bulky.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Round Faces?

Choosing the right haircut can be overwhelming, but it's essential to choose one that flatters your face shape. Here are some tips to keep in mind before going to the salon:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Know your face shape - Determine if you have a round face by measuring the length and width of your face.
  2. Find inspiration - Look for hairstyles that flatter round faces and save them for reference.
  3. Consult with your stylist - Discuss your options with your stylist and get their opinion on which haircut would suit your face shape.
  4. Consider your lifestyle - Choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces in 2020

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut let's look at some of the best haircuts for round faces in 2020:

1. Long Layered Cut

A long layered cut is perfect for round faces as it creates an illusion of length and gives volume to the crown. The layers should be long and feathery to add movement and texture to the hair.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short haircut that is perfect for round faces. It adds height to the crown and keeps the sides sleek and not too bulky, creating an illusion of length. This cut is effortless to maintain and perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another excellent option for round faces. It adds height to the crown and keeps the sides sleek, creating an illusion of length. A bob cut can be customized to suit your face shape, such as an asymmetrical bob or a layered bob.

Tips for Styling Haircuts for Round Faces

Styling your haircut is just as important as choosing the right one. Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling haircuts for round faces:

  • Add volume to the crown - Use a volumizing mousse or spray to create volume at the crown.
  • Side-swept bangs - Side-swept bangs can help create an illusion of length and balance out the roundness of the face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts - Blunt cuts can make the face appear rounder, so it's best to avoid them.
  • Use a round brush - Use a round brush to create volume and movement in the hair.

FAQ

Q. Can I get a short haircut if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have a round face, but make sure it adds height to the crown and keeps the sides sleek.

Q. What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A. The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height to the crown and keeps the sides sleek.

Q. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A. No, you can have bangs if you have a round face, but it's best to go for side-swept bangs that create an illusion of length.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Now that you have a new haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a round face can be challenging, but with the right tips and inspiration, you can find the perfect hairstyle. Remember to consider your face shape, lifestyle, and consult with your stylist before making a decision. With the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your new look.
