How To Start Dreads With Straight Short Hair

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of rocking the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Starting dreadlocks might be the perfect hairstyle for you! Dreadlocks are a unique and stylish way to express yourself and your personality. If you have straight short hair, you might be wondering how to start dreads. In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step.

Description

Dreadlocks, commonly known as "dreads," are strands of hair that are matted and knotted together to form a rope-like appearance. They can be created with any hair type, including straight hair. However, starting dreads with straight short hair can be a bit challenging. It requires a lot of patience and dedication, but the end result is worth it.

How to Start Dreads with Straight Short Hair

Step 1: Decide on the Method

There are several methods to start dreads, including the twist and rip method, backcombing method, and natural method. For straight short hair, the twist and rip method is the most recommended. It involves sectioning your hair and twisting and ripping each section to create a knot.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is crucial when starting dreads. Use a comb to divide your hair into small, equal sections. The size of the sections will determine the thickness of your dreads. For short hair, smaller sections are recommended.

Step 3: Twist and Rip

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly. Then, use your fingers to rip the hair in two directions, creating a knot. The tighter you twist and rip, the faster your dreads will form. Repeat this process for all sections of hair.

Step 4: Maintenance

Starting dreads is just the beginning. Maintaining them is essential to keep them healthy and looking their best. You will need to wash your dreads regularly and use a residue-free shampoo. You can also use a crochet hook to tighten loose hairs and form the dreads' shape.

Tips

  • Be patient. It takes time for dreads to form, especially with short hair.
  • Avoid using products that contain wax or heavy oils as they can clog your pores and cause buildup.
  • Protect your dreads while sleeping by wearing a silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories once your dreads have formed.

FAQ

Can I start dreads with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can start dreads with relaxed hair. However, you will need to wait until your hair has grown out a bit before starting the process.

How long does it take for dreads to form?

It can take several months for dreads to form, depending on your hair type and the method you use.

Can I wash my dreads?

Yes, you can wash your dreads. It is important to use a residue-free shampoo and avoid scrubbing too hard.

Hair Care

Starting dreads with straight short hair requires proper hair care to keep your dreads looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten loose hairs and form the dreads' shape.
  • Avoid using products that contain wax or heavy oils as they can cause buildup.
  • Protect your dreads while sleeping by wearing a silk scarf or bonnet.

Conclusion

Starting dreads with straight short hair might seem daunting, but with the right method and proper hair care, you can achieve the hairstyle of your dreams. Remember to be patient and take good care of your dreads, and they will look amazing in no time.
