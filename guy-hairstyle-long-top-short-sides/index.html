Guy Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides A Trending Look For Men

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle, then the guy hairstyle long top short sides is the way to go. This hairstyle has been around for a few years and has gained popularity among men of all ages. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways, making it perfect for any occasion. In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at this hairstyle, how to style it, and some tips and tricks to keep it looking great.

Description

The guy hairstyle long top short sides is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the top is typically styled in a messy or textured way, while the sides and back are faded or tapered. This gives the hairstyle a clean and modern look. The length of the hair on the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's usually around 3-4 inches long.

How to Style

Styling the guy hairstyle long top short sides is fairly easy and can be done in a few simple steps. Here's how:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oil buildup and make your hair easier to style.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to the top of your hair. This can be a pomade, wax, or gel, depending on your hair type and desired look.
  3. Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair on the top in the desired way. You can go for a messy, textured look or a slicked-back style, depending on your preference.
  4. Next, use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back. You can go for a low or high fade, depending on how short you want the hair to be.
  5. Finally, use a trimmer to clean up the hairline and sideburns. This will give your hairstyle a clean and polished look.

Remember to use a good quality styling product that will hold your hair in place throughout the day. You can also experiment with different styles and product combinations to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the guy hairstyle long top short sides:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to the top of your hair.
  3. Style your hair on the top in the desired way using your fingers or a comb.
  4. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back.
  5. Use a trimmer to clean up the hairline and sideburns.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your guy hairstyle long top short sides looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep the hair on the sides and back looking clean and neat.
  • Use a good quality styling product that is suited for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles and product combinations to find the one that works best for you.
  • Try blow-drying your hair on the top for added volume and texture.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the guy hairstyle long top short sides:

What face shape is best for this hairstyle?

The guy hairstyle long top short sides can work on any face shape, but it's particularly flattering on oval or round faces.

What hair type is best for this hairstyle?

The guy hairstyle long top short sides can work on any hair type, but it's best suited for hair that is thick and straight or wavy.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair on the sides and back looking clean and neat.

Hair Care

To keep your guy hairstyle long top short sides looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a good quality styling product that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair too much, as this can cause breakage and damage.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your guy hairstyle long top short sides looking great for weeks to come.
