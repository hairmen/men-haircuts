How To Style Your Hair Korean

Written by Daniel Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a fan of K-pop and K-dramas? Do you love the way Koreans style their hair? If yes, then this article is for you! Here, we will guide you on how to style your hair Korean style in easy and simple steps. So, let's get started!

How to

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to know that Korean hairstyles are all about simplicity and elegance. They prefer natural-looking hairstyles that are easy to maintain. So, let's begin with the basics:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to getting a Korean hairstyle is to get the right haircut. You can either show your hairstylist a picture of the hairstyle you want or ask for their recommendations. The most popular Korean haircuts are the bob, the lob, and the shaggy haircut. Make sure to choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hair Color

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to choose the right hair color. Koreans prefer natural-looking hair colors like black, brown, and dark blonde. If you want to add some color, you can go for highlights or balayage. Just make sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone.

Step 3: Get the Right Hair Products

To achieve the perfect Korean hairstyle, you need to have the right hair products. Koreans prefer lightweight and non-greasy products that won't weigh down their hair. Some of the must-have hair products for Korean hairstyles are hair oil, hair serum, texturizing spray, and hairspray.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that you have the right haircut, hair color, and hair products, it's time to style your hair. For a natural-looking Korean hairstyle, you can go for a simple middle part or a side part. You can also add some waves or curls using a curling iron or a straightener. Don't forget to use a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your hair. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect Korean hairstyle:

  • Don't wash your hair too often. Koreans prefer to wash their hair every two to three days to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Koreans prefer to air dry their hair or use a hair dryer on a cool setting to avoid damaging their hair.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush. Koreans prefer using a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to detangle their hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Korean hairstyles:

Q: Can I get a Korean hairstyle if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a Korean hairstyle even if you have curly hair. Just make sure to get a haircut that suits your curly hair type and use the right hair products to keep your curls healthy and defined.

Q: Can I get a Korean hairstyle if I have short hair?

A: Yes, there are many Korean hairstyles that are suitable for short hair. You can go for a bob or a pixie cut and add some waves or curls to create a natural-looking hairstyle.

Q: How do I maintain my Korean hairstyle?

A: To maintain your Korean hairstyle, make sure to use the right hair products and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You should also get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

With these simple steps and tips, you can now achieve the perfect Korean hairstyle that suits your personality and style. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
