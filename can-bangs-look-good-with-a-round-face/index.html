Can Bangs Look Good With A Round Face

Written by Christine Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might be hesitant to try bangs. You may be wondering whether they will accentuate your face shape or make it look even rounder. However, the good news is that bangs can look great on round faces!

Description

In this blog post, we will explore how bangs can complement a round face, the different types of bangs you can try, and some tips on how to style them.

How to

First, it's important to understand that bangs can help to balance out the proportions of a round face. By adding some structure and angles to your hair, bangs can create the illusion of a longer and more oval-shaped face.

There are several types of bangs that can work well with a round face:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces as they can help to create angles and draw attention away from the cheeks. They can be styled straight or with a slight curve, depending on your preference.

Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can also look good on round faces, but it's important to make sure they are cut slightly longer at the sides to avoid adding too much width to the face. They can be styled straight or with a slight curve.

Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs are a softer option that can still create some structure and angles around the face. They can be styled straight or with a slight curve, and are a good option if you want a more low-maintenance look.

Step by Step

If you're considering getting bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Research different types of bangs to find the one that will best suit your face shape and hair type.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your bangs.
  3. Maintain your bangs with regular trims to keep them looking polished and flattering.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your bangs:

  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs to create a smooth and polished look.
  • Apply a lightweight styling product to help keep your bangs in place and prevent frizz.
  • If you're feeling self-conscious about your round face, try pairing your bangs with a longer hairstyle to create balance.
  • If you're not sure whether bangs are right for you, try experimenting with clip-in bangs or a faux fringe before committing to a permanent cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs and round faces:

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily! The right type of bangs can actually help to balance out the proportions of a round face.

Q: What type of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid full, straight-across bangs as they can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept, blunt, or wispy bangs.

Q: How often should I get my bangs trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking polished and flattering.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, and use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.

By following these tips and experimenting with different types of bangs, you can find a style that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
