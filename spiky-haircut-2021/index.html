Spiky Haircut 2021 The Trending Hairstyle Of The Year

Written by Petter Sep 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read
If you're looking for a hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd, the spiky haircut might be what you need. This trendy hairstyle has been around for years and is still going strong in 2021. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to get the perfect spiky haircut, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this edgy hairstyle.

How to Get a Spiky Haircut

Getting a spiky haircut is easy, but it's important to choose the right length and style. You can opt for a short or medium-length haircut, depending on your personal preference. The hair on the sides and back of your head should be kept short, while the hair on top should be left longer. Before you head to the salon, make sure you have a clear idea of the style you want. You can browse online for inspiration, or bring a photo of your desired haircut to show to your hairstylist.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Spiky Haircut

Once you have your spiky haircut, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect spiky look: 1. Start with clean, dry hair. 2. Apply a styling product, such as gel or wax, to your hair. 3. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes on the top of your head. 4. Use a blow dryer to set the spikes in place. 5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Tips for Maintaining a Spiky Haircut

To keep your spiky haircut looking sharp, here are some tips to follow: - Use a high-quality styling product to hold the spikes in place. - Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy. - Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your spiky look.

FAQ About Spiky Haircuts

Q: Can anyone get a spiky haircut? A: Yes, anyone can get a spiky haircut. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. Q: How long does it take to style a spiky haircut? A: It usually takes about 10-15 minutes to style a spiky haircut, depending on the length and thickness of your hair. Q: Can you wear a spiky haircut to work? A: It depends on the dress code of your workplace. Some workplaces may have strict guidelines on hairstyles, while others may allow more freedom.

Hair Care for Spiky Haircuts

To keep your spiky haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to follow: - Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair. - Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf. In conclusion, a spiky haircut is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve the perfect spiky look. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
