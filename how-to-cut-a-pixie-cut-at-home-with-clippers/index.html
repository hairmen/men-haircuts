How To Cut A Pixie Cut At Home With Clippers

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for a shorter, edgier look, a pixie cut might be just what you need. While getting a pixie cut at the salon can be pricey, you can achieve the look at home with clippers. In this blog post, we'll go over how to cut a pixie cut at home with clippers, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Pixie Cut at Home with Clippers
  • Step by Step

How to Cut a Pixie Cut at Home with Clippers

Before we get started, it's important to note that cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you don't have experience doing so. With that being said, if you're feeling confident and up for the challenge, let's get started!

The first step in cutting a pixie cut at home with clippers is to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, hair scissors, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle with water to dampen your hair as you cut it.

Choosing the Right Clippers

When it comes to choosing the right clippers, you want to look for a pair that is lightweight and easy to handle. You'll also want to make sure they come with different guard sizes so you can achieve the length you want for your pixie cut.

Step by Step

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to start cutting your pixie cut at home with clippers. Follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. It's best to cut your hair when it's clean and dry so you can get an accurate idea of how it will look.
  2. Use your comb to section off the top of your hair. Clip it out of the way for now.
  3. Start with the sides of your head. Use the clippers with a guard to start cutting your hair to the desired length. Work your way up from the bottom, using the comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut.
  4. Once you've finished the sides, move on to the back of your head. Use the same technique to cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. Now it's time to move on to the top of your hair. Remove the clip and use your hair scissors to trim the top to your desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  6. Once you've finished cutting your hair, use the clippers without a guard to clean up the hairline around your neck and ears.
  7. Take a step back and inspect your new pixie cut. Make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you can achieve a salon-worthy pixie cut at home:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to achieve your desired length.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair as you cut to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush. It's better to go slow and make small adjustments than to cut too much off at once.
  • When in doubt, leave it a little longer. You can always go back and cut more off later.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting a pixie cut at home with clippers:

Can I cut a pixie cut with just scissors?

While it's possible to cut a pixie cut with just scissors, clippers will make the process much easier and more efficient.

What if I mess up?

Don't panic! If you make a mistake, you can always go to a professional stylist to have it fixed. Or, you can try to fix it yourself by making small adjustments.

How often do I need to cut my pixie cut?

Pixie cuts require regular maintenance, so plan on getting it trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Now that you have your new pixie cut, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Cutting a pixie cut at home with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and steps, you can achieve a stylish and edgy look from the comfort of your own home.
