How To Change The Texture Of Your Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your hair's texture and want to change it up? You're not alone. Many people want to change their hair texture, whether it's to make it more manageable or to try out a new style. In this article, we'll go over how to change the texture of your hair and provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Why Change Your Hair Texture?

There are many reasons why someone might want to change the texture of their hair. One of the most common reasons is to make it more manageable. If you have curly or frizzy hair, you may want to straighten it to make it easier to style and maintain. On the other hand, if you have straight hair, you may want to add some curls or waves for a different look.

How to Change Your Hair Texture

There are several ways to change the texture of your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments, such as relaxers and perms, can permanently alter the texture of your hair. Relaxers are used to straighten curly or kinky hair, while perms are used to add curls or waves to straight hair. These treatments should only be done by a professional, as they can cause damage if not done correctly.

Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons, curling irons, and hot rollers, can temporarily change the texture of your hair. Straightening irons can be used to straighten curly or frizzy hair, while curling irons and hot rollers can add curls or waves to straight hair. However, excessive heat styling can cause damage to your hair, so it's important to use these tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

Hair Extensions

Hair extensions can also be used to change the texture of your hair. If you have short or thin hair, you can add extensions to make it longer and fuller. You can also choose extensions with a different texture than your natural hair, such as curly or wavy extensions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to changing the texture of your hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Desired Texture

Before you start, determine the texture you want to achieve. Do you want straight hair, curly hair, or something in between? This will help you choose the right method for your hair.

Step 2: Choose Your Method

Choose the method you want to use to change your hair texture. If you want a permanent change, consider a chemical treatment. If you want a temporary change, consider heat styling or hair extensions.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you're using a chemical treatment, follow the instructions carefully and make sure to protect your scalp and skin from the chemicals. If you're using heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Step 4: Start Changing Your Hair Texture

Depending on the method you choose, start changing your hair texture. Follow the instructions carefully and take your time. Don't rush the process, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step 5: Maintain Your New Texture

Once you've achieved your desired texture, maintain it by using the right hair care products. For example, if you have curly hair, use products designed for curly hair to keep your curls looking their best.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Texture

Here are some tips to make the process of changing your hair texture easier:

  • Consult a professional if you're not sure how to achieve the texture you want.
  • Use high-quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Be patient and take your time. Changing your hair texture can take time and effort.
  • Protect your hair from damage by using a heat protectant spray and avoiding excessive heat styling.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can I change the texture of my hair without using chemicals?

Yes, you can change the texture of your hair without using chemicals. Heat styling tools and hair extensions can be used to achieve a different texture without permanently altering your natural hair.

How often should I use heat styling tools?

You should use heat styling tools sparingly to prevent damage to your hair. Try to limit heat styling to once or twice a week, and always use a heat protectant spray.

What should I do if my hair gets damaged from heat styling?

If your hair gets damaged from heat styling, give it a break and let it rest for a while. Use deep conditioning treatments and avoid heat styling until your hair is healthy again.

Hair Care for Different Hair Textures

Regardless of the texture of your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for different hair textures:

Straight Hair

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for straight hair. Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.

Curly Hair

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls looking their best.

Kinky Hair

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for kinky hair. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Conclusion

Changing the texture of your hair can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. Whether you want to straighten your curly hair or add some curls to your straight hair, there are many methods to choose from. Just remember to take good care of your hair and protect it from damage.
