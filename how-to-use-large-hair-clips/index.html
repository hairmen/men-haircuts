How To Use Large Hair Clips For Easy Hair Styling

Written by Luffy Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your hair, large hair clips are a great option. Not only do they hold your hair securely in place, but they also come in a variety of styles and sizes, making it easy to find the perfect clip for your hair type and style. In this article, we'll show you how to use large hair clips and share some tips for getting the most out of these versatile hair accessories.

Description of Large Hair Clips

Large hair clips are typically made from plastic, metal, or a combination of both materials. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes, from basic rectangular clips to more ornate designs with intricate patterns and embellishments. Some clips feature teeth or ridges that help grip your hair, while others are smooth and rely on the tension of the clip to hold your hair in place.

How to Use Large Hair Clips

Using large hair clips is easy once you know the basics. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clip

Before you start styling your hair, choose the right clip for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. If you have thick hair, you may need a larger clip with more tension to hold your hair securely in place. If you have fine hair, a smaller clip may be a better option to avoid weighing down your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section off the part of your hair that you want to clip. This could be a small section of hair near your face, or a larger section of hair at the back of your head. Use your fingers or a comb to create a clean part and gather the hair you want to clip together.

Step 3: Position the Clip

Once you have the section of hair you want to clip, position the clip vertically or horizontally over the hair. Depending on the style you want to achieve, you may want to place the clip at the root of your hair or further down the hair shaft.

Step 4: Secure the Clip

Gently squeeze the clip to open it, then position it over the section of hair you want to hold. Release the clip so that it grips your hair securely. If you have a clip with teeth or ridges, make sure they are facing towards your scalp for the best hold.

Tips for Using Large Hair Clips

Here are some tips for using large hair clips:

  • Experiment with different clip sizes and styles to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.
  • If your hair is prone to slipping out of clips, try spritzing it with a little hairspray or dry shampoo before clipping it.
  • For a more secure hold, twist the section of hair you want to clip before positioning the clip over it.
  • Use large hair clips to create a variety of styles, from half-up half-down styles to messy buns and top knots.
  • When removing a clip, gently squeeze it to release the tension and avoid pulling on your hair.

FAQs About Large Hair Clips

Q. Can I use large hair clips on wet hair?

A. It's best to avoid using large hair clips on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Wait until your hair is completely dry before using clips to style it.

Q. How long can I leave a clip in my hair?

A. Large hair clips can be left in your hair for several hours, but it's best to remove them before you go to bed to avoid damaging your hair while you sleep.

Q. How do I clean my hair clips?

A. To clean your hair clips, simply wipe them down with a damp cloth or rinse them under running water. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that could damage the clip's surface.

Hair Care Tips for Using Large Hair Clips

Here are some hair care tips for using large hair clips:

  • Avoid using clips that are too tight or that pull on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Take breaks from using hair clips and other hair accessories to give your hair a chance to breathe and recover.
  • Maintain a healthy hair care routine, including regular washing, conditioning, and moisturizing, to keep your hair strong and healthy.

Conclusion

Large hair clips are a versatile and easy-to-use hair accessory that can help you create a variety of styles. By following these tips and techniques, you can use large hair clips to achieve the perfect look for any occasion, while keeping your hair healthy and strong.
