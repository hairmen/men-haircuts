Best Slick Back Pomade For A Polished Look

Written by Aprilia Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to achieve a slick and polished look for your hair? A high-quality slick back pomade can help you achieve that look effortlessly. In this article, we will discuss the best slick back pomade for a polished look, how to use it, step by step guide, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents:

  • Best Slick Back Pomades
  • How to Use Slick Back Pomade
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Slick Back Pomade
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Best Slick Back Pomades:

The market is flooded with a variety of slick back pomades, making it quite challenging to choose the best one. Here are some of the best slick back pomades in the market:

1. Suavecito Pomade:

Suavecito Pomade is a popular choice among men due to its strong hold and excellent fragrance. It is water-soluble, making it easy to wash out. This pomade has a medium shine, making it perfect for a polished look.

2. Layrite Superhold Pomade:

Layrite Superhold Pomade is another popular choice among men due to its strong hold and matte finish. It is water-soluble, making it easy to wash out. This pomade is perfect for a natural-looking polished look.

3. Baxter of California Clay Pomade:

Baxter of California Clay Pomade is a high-quality pomade that provides a strong hold and a matte finish. It is water-soluble, making it easy to wash out. This pomade is perfect for a modern and edgy polished look.

How to Use Slick Back Pomade:

Using a slick back pomade is quite easy. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step by Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair.

Step 2: Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your hands.

Step 3: Apply the pomade evenly to your hair, starting from the back and moving towards the front.

Step 4: Use a comb to slick back your hair, ensuring that there are no clumps or bumps.

Step 5: Finish off with a hairspray for extra hold.

Tips for Using Slick Back Pomade:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slick back look:

  • Use a small amount of pomade to avoid clumps and bumps.
  • Apply the pomade evenly to your hair to achieve a polished look.
  • Use a comb to slick back your hair for a neat and polished look.
  • Use a hairspray for extra hold.
  • Experiment with different pomades to find the one that suits your hair type and style.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Can I use slick back pomade on wet hair?

A: It is recommended to use slick back pomade on dry hair for best results. Wet hair can dilute the pomade and affect its hold.

Q: How often should I use slick back pomade?

A: It is recommended to use slick back pomade sparingly, especially if you have fine hair. Overuse of pomade can lead to hair damage and breakage.

Q: Can I use slick back pomade on colored hair?

A: Yes, slick back pomade can be used on colored hair. However, it is recommended to use a pomade that is specifically formulated for colored hair to avoid fading or damage.

Hair Care Tips:

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for a trim and to keep your hair looking healthy.

With the right slick back pomade and proper hair care, you can achieve a polished and neat look effortlessly. Try out different pomades to find the one that suits your hair type and style. Follow the tips and guidelines in this article to achieve a perfect slick back look.
