How To Cut A Stacked Bob Haircut Video Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 10, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are looking for a chic and versatile haircut, a stacked bob may be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle features shorter layers at the back of the head, gradually increasing in length towards the front. It is a trendy and low-maintenance haircut that can suit different face shapes and hair textures. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut a stacked bob haircut video, along with tips and hair care advice.

Description

A stacked bob is a haircut that has shorter layers at the back of the head, creating a stacked effect. It is also known as an inverted bob, as the hair is cut shorter at the nape of the neck and longer towards the front. This haircut can add volume and texture to the hair, while still maintaining a sleek and modern look. It can be customized to suit different hair types, lengths, and face shapes.

How to Cut a Stacked Bob Haircut Video

If you want to learn how to cut a stacked bob haircut video, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools, such as hair cutting scissors, a comb, clips, and a spray bottle. You may also want to have a mirror and a hair cutting cape or towel to protect your client's clothes.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: the top section, two side sections, and the back section. Use clips to secure each section, leaving the top section loose.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Begin cutting the back section by combing the hair downwards and cutting it at an angle towards the nape of the neck. Use the shorter layers as a guide, gradually increasing the length towards the front. Repeat this process on both sides of the back section, making sure to keep the hair even and balanced.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Release one of the side sections and comb the hair downwards. Cut the hair at an angle towards the back, using the shorter layers as a guide. Repeat this process on the other side section, making sure to keep the hair even and balanced.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Release the top section and comb the hair downwards. Cut the hair at an angle towards the front, using the longer layers as a guide. Blend the top section with the side and back sections, making sure to keep the hair even and balanced.

Step 6: Check and Refine

Once you have finished cutting, check the hair for any uneven or stray hairs. Refine the haircut as needed, using your scissors or a razor to create texture and shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect stacked bob haircut:

  • Start with clean and dry hair, as wet hair may shrink or appear longer when it dries.
  • Use sharp and clean scissors to ensure a precise cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time, to avoid mistakes or unevenness.
  • Consult with your client about their desired length and style, and make adjustments accordingly.
  • Finish the haircut with a blow dryer and styling products, such as a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray.

FAQ

Can I cut a stacked bob haircut video on myself?

It is possible to cut a stacked bob haircut on yourself, but it may be challenging, especially if you are not experienced with hair cutting. We recommend seeking the help of a professional hair stylist, who can provide you with the best results and advice.

How often should I trim my stacked bob haircut?

It depends on your hair growth and maintenance needs, but generally, we recommend trimming your stacked bob every 6-8 weeks, to maintain its shape and style.

Can a stacked bob haircut suit any face shape?

A stacked bob can suit different face shapes, depending on how it is customized. For example, a chin-length stacked bob can flatter round or square faces, while a longer stacked bob can complement oval or heart-shaped faces. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best stacked bob style for your face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain a healthy and stylish stacked bob haircut, we recommend following these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, suited for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using too much heat or styling products, as they can damage your hair and affect its texture.
  • Cut your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from sun exposure and harsh weather conditions, by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet, to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Now that you know how to cut a stacked bob haircut video, you can try this trendy and versatile hairstyle on yourself or your clients. Remember to follow the steps carefully and use the proper tools and techniques, to achieve a flawless and chic result. Happy styling!
