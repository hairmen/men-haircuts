How To Wear A Face Mask Properly With Glasses Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Nov 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wearing a face mask is essential in protecting ourselves and others from airborne viruses and bacteria. However, for those who wear glasses, it can be a bit challenging to wear a mask without fogging up their lenses. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to wear a face mask properly with glasses.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Face Mask Properly with Glasses
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Wear a Face Mask Properly with Glasses

The first step in wearing a face mask properly with glasses is to find a mask that fits your face well. The mask should cover both your nose and mouth and fit snugly against your face. This will help prevent air from escaping and fogging up your glasses.

Next, make sure that your glasses are clean and free from any smudges or debris. This will help prevent any additional fogging that may be caused by dirty lenses.

Another tip is to adjust the fit of your mask by pulling it higher up on your face. This will help create a seal between the mask and your skin, preventing any air from escaping and fogging up your glasses.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a face mask properly with glasses:

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water or use hand sanitizer.
  2. Choose a mask that fits your face well and covers both your nose and mouth.
  3. Put on your mask, making sure it fits snugly against your face.
  4. Adjust the fit of your mask by pulling it higher up on your face.
  5. Make sure your glasses are clean and free from any smudges or debris.
  6. Position your glasses on top of your mask, pressing down on the bridge of your nose to create a seal.
  7. If your glasses still fog up, adjust the fit of your mask or try using anti-fogging products on your lenses.
  8. Remove your mask by the ear loops, avoiding touching the front of the mask.
  9. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water or use hand sanitizer after removing your mask.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to wear a face mask properly with glasses:

  • Use a mask with a nose wire to help create a seal between the mask and your face.
  • Try using anti-fogging products on your lenses, such as anti-fogging sprays or wipes.
  • Make sure your glasses fit well and are not too loose or tight.
  • Consider using contact lenses instead of glasses when wearing a mask.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a face mask with glasses:

  • Why do my glasses fog up when wearing a mask? Glasses fog up when warm air escapes from the top of the mask and meets the cooler lenses, causing condensation to form.
  • Can I wear a face shield instead of a mask? While face shields provide some protection, they are not as effective as masks in preventing the spread of airborne viruses and bacteria.
  • Should I wear my glasses over or under my mask? It's best to wear your glasses over your mask, pressing down on the bridge of your nose to create a seal.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a mask for extended periods can cause discomfort and irritation to your ears and hair. Here are some hair care tips to help alleviate these issues:

  • Use a hair tie or headband to keep your hair away from your face.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to prevent breakage caused by the friction of the mask.
  • Avoid using hair products that may stick to the mask, such as hairspray or gel.

In conclusion, wearing a face mask properly with glasses may take some adjusting, but it is essential in protecting yourself and others from airborne viruses and bacteria. By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your mask comfortably and efficiently without fogging up your glasses.
