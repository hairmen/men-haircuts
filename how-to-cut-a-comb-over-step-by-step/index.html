How To Cut A Comb Over Step By Step

Description

A comb over is a classic men's hairstyle that involves combing hair to one side to create a sleek, polished look. It's a versatile style that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. Cutting a comb over can be tricky, especially if you're new to cutting hair. In this blog post, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut a comb over, along with some helpful tips and tricks.

How to

Before you begin cutting a comb over, it's important to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. It's also a good idea to have some hair gel or styling product on hand to help create the finished look.

Step by Step

Here's how to cut a comb over step by step:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair to remove any excess product or debris.
  2. Part your hair on the side where you want the comb over to be. Use a comb to create a clean part.
  3. Clip the hair on the opposite side of the part to keep it out of the way.
  4. Using your clippers, create a fade on the lower half of your head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard. Make sure to blend the fade evenly.
  5. Trim the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. Use scissors to create texture and layers.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair gel or styling product to the hair on the top of your head.
  7. Using your comb, comb the hair on the top of your head to the side opposite of the part. This will create the comb over look.
  8. Use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you cut a perfect comb over:

  • Start with a longer guard when creating the fade, and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard. This will help you achieve a more natural-looking fade.
  • Don't be afraid to use scissors to create texture and layers on the top of your head.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure your cut is even.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a comb over:

  • Can I cut a comb over on my own? Yes, you can cut a comb over on your own, but it can be tricky if you're not experienced with cutting hair. We recommend getting help from a friend or visiting a professional stylist.
  • What hair type is best for a comb over? A comb over can be worn by men with all hair types, but it works best on hair that is thick and full.
  • How often should I cut my hair? We recommend cutting your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your comb over.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your comb over, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.

Cutting a comb over can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish, polished look that's perfect for any occasion. Follow these step-by-step instructions and helpful tips to get the perfect comb over every time.
