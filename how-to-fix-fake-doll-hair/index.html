How To Fix Fake Doll Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of seeing your doll's hair looking tangled, matted, and just plain messy? If so, you're not alone. Many doll owners struggle with fixing fake doll hair, but there are solutions! In this blog post, we'll be sharing tips and tricks on how to fix fake doll hair so that it looks smooth, shiny, and beautiful again.

How to Fix Fake Doll Hair

Before you start, it's important to know that not all doll hair can be fixed. Synthetic hair, especially cheap quality ones, may not be able to be restored to its original state. However, if your doll has high-quality synthetic or human hair, it's definitely worth a try.

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to fix fake doll hair in a few easy steps:

  1. Fill a bowl or sink with warm water and a small amount of mild shampoo.
  2. Gently comb through the hair with a wide-tooth comb or brush to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Place the doll's head or hair into the water, being careful not to get the body or clothing wet.
  4. Swirl the hair around in the water to help remove any dirt or debris.
  5. Rinse the hair thoroughly with clean water.
  6. Apply a small amount of conditioner to the hair, avoiding the roots.
  7. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with clean water.
  8. Gently squeeze out any excess water with a towel.
  9. Let the hair air dry, or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.
  10. Once the hair is dry, gently comb through it again to remove any remaining tangles or knots.

Repeat this process as needed until the hair is looking smooth and shiny again.

Tips for Fixing Fake Doll Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you fix fake doll hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Be gentle when washing and conditioning the hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can damage the hair.
  • Don't use a hair straightener or curling iron on synthetic hair, as it can melt the fibers.
  • If the hair is still tangled after washing and conditioning, try using a detangling spray or serum.

FAQs About Fixing Fake Doll Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing fake doll hair:

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on doll hair?

Yes, you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on doll hair. Just be sure to use a mild formula and avoid getting the roots of the hair wet.

Can I use a hair dryer on doll hair?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer on doll hair, but be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging the hair.

How often should I wash my doll's hair?

It depends on how often the doll is played with and how dirty the hair gets. As a general rule, you should wash the hair every few months or as needed.

Hair Care for Dolls

Finally, to keep your doll's hair looking its best, be sure to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Store the doll in a cool, dry place to avoid heat damage.
  • Brush or comb the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Avoid exposing the hair to direct sunlight, as it can fade the color.
  • If the hair gets wet, be sure to dry it thoroughly to avoid mold or mildew.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your doll's hair looking beautiful and well-groomed for years to come.

Remember, not all doll hair can be fixed, but it's always worth a try. With a little bit of patience and care, you can restore your doll's hair to its former glory.
