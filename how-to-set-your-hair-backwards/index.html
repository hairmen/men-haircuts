How To Set Your Hair Backwards A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Setting your hair backwards is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Not only is it a timeless look, but it is also a great way to keep your hair out of your face. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to set your hair backwards. We will also offer some tips and advice on how to maintain this hairstyle and keep it looking great all day long.

Description

Setting your hair backwards is a simple yet effective hairstyle that involves brushing your hair away from your face and slicking it back. It is a great option for those who want to keep their hair out of their face or for those who want to achieve a sleek and polished look. This hairstyle works well for both men and women and can be done on any hair type.

How to Set Your Hair Backwards

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect backwards hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and let it air dry or blow dry it. If your hair is still damp, it will be more difficult to style.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product such as gel or pomade to your hair. This will help to hold your hair in place and add shine. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Brush your hair backwards

Using a comb or brush, brush your hair backwards away from your face. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, making sure to smooth down any flyaways or bumps.

Step 4: Secure your hair with clips

If necessary, use hair clips to secure your hair in place. This will help to keep your hair from falling forward and maintain the backwards style.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish by spraying your hair with hairspray. This will help to hold your style in place and prevent your hair from becoming frizzy or unruly throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect backwards hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to ensure that your hair stays in place all day long.
  • Invest in a good comb or brush to make styling your hair easier.
  • Experiment with different hair clips to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to add some volume to your hair by using a volumizing product or teasing your hair at the roots.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about setting your hair backwards:

Q: Can I set my hair backwards if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a backwards hairstyle with short hair. Simply brush your hair back and use a styling product to hold it in place.

Q: Will setting my hair backwards damage it?

A: No, setting your hair backwards should not cause any damage to your hair. However, using too much product or pulling your hair too tightly can cause breakage over time.

Q: How long will my backwards hairstyle last?

A: The length of time that your backwards hairstyle will last depends on a variety of factors, including the type of product you use and the weather conditions. However, with the right products and techniques, you should be able to maintain your style for several hours.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dye, which can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Setting your hair backwards is a great way to achieve a classic and polished look. With the right products and techniques, you can maintain this hairstyle all day long. Follow the steps outlined in this article to achieve the perfect backwards hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
