How To Style Long Layered Hair With Side Bangs

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try out? Long layered hair with side bangs is a classic choice that can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we'll show you how to style long layered hair with side bangs and share some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.

How to Style Long Layered Hair with Side Bangs

The first step in styling long layered hair with side bangs is to decide what kind of look you want to achieve. Here are a few options:

1. Straight and Sleek

If you want a polished, sophisticated look, try straightening your hair with a flat iron. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and work in small sections for best results. Finish with a shine serum or spray to add extra sheen.

2. Beachy Waves

For a more relaxed, bohemian vibe, try creating loose, beachy waves. You can achieve this look with a curling iron or by braiding your hair before bed and unraveling it in the morning. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you want to show off your layers and bangs, try pulling the top half of your hair back into a half-up, half-down style. You can add a cute clip or ribbon for extra flair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling long layered hair with side bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is prone to frizz or flyaways, apply a smoothing serum or cream.
  2. If you want to straighten your hair, section it off and use a flat iron to smooth out each section. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  3. If you want to create waves, section off your hair and use a curling iron to create loose curls. Hold the curling iron vertically for a more natural look.
  4. If you're doing a half-up, half-down style, use a comb to section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or elastic.
  5. If you have side bangs, use a round brush and blow dryer to style them. Aim the dryer downwards to avoid creating too much volume.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your long layered hair with side bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as a good shampoo and conditioner, to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, embrace your texture and try a curly style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long layered hair with side bangs:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can probably go longer between washes. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

How can I prevent my bangs from getting greasy?

Try using dry shampoo on your bangs between washes. You can also use a small amount of baby powder or cornstarch to absorb oil.

Hair Care

To keep your long layered hair with side bangs looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to moisturize your hair.

With these tips and styling ideas, you'll be able to rock your long layered hair with side bangs with confidence and ease. Experiment with different looks and find the one that works best for you!
