How To Manage Very Curly Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have very curly thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage. But with the right techniques and products, you can make your hair look amazing every day. In this guide, we’ll show you how to manage very curly thick hair, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Manage Very Curly Thick Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Managing Very Curly Thick Hair

Description

Very curly thick hair is characterized by tight, coiled curls and a lot of volume. This type of hair can be difficult to manage because it tends to frizz easily and can be prone to tangling. However, with the right techniques and products, you can keep your hair looking healthy, shiny, and well-defined.

How to Manage Very Curly Thick Hair

Managing very curly thick hair requires a combination of techniques, products, and styling tools. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

When choosing a shampoo and conditioner for very curly thick hair, look for products that are designed to moisturize and nourish the hair. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, which can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz. Conditioning is crucial for curly hair. A good conditioner will detangle and soften your curls while providing moisture and shine.

2. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than twice a week, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and refresh your curls.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb or Your Fingers to Detangle

When detangling very curly thick hair, it’s best to use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently detangling as you go. Avoid using a brush, which can cause breakage and frizz.

4. Apply Products While Your Hair is Wet

Applying products to very curly thick hair while it’s still wet will help to lock in moisture and define your curls. Start by applying a leave-in conditioner or curl cream, then follow up with a styling gel or mousse to hold your curls in place.

5. Use a Diffuser When Blow Drying

Blow drying very curly thick hair can cause frizz and damage. To minimize these issues, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and define your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to managing very curly thick hair:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.

4. Apply a styling gel or mousse to your hair, working it in with your fingers.

5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.

6. Once your hair is dry, use a serum or oil to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips for Managing Very Curly Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your very curly thick hair:

1. Sleep on a satin pillowcase to minimize frizz.

2. Use a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair while you sleep.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.

4. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten very curly thick hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten very curly thick hair, but it’s important to use heat protectant products and to avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Q: How often should I trim my very curly thick hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I color very curly thick hair?

A: Yes, you can color very curly thick hair, but it’s important to use products that are gentle and moisturizing to prevent damage and dryness.

Hair Care for Very Curly Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your very curly thick hair:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

3. Use a protein treatment once a month to strengthen your hair and prevent breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can manage your very curly thick hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful every day.
