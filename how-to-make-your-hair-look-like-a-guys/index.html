How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Guy s

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you want to achieve the effortless and cool look that guys seem to have with their hair? With the right styling techniques and products, you can achieve that look too. Here are some tips on how to make your hair look like a guy's.

Description

Having hair that looks like a guy's can give you a relaxed and carefree look. It can also make you stand out from the crowd and give you a unique style. A guy's hair is usually shorter, messier, and more textured than women's hair. Achieving this look requires using the right products and styling techniques.

How to

Here are the steps to follow if you want to make your hair look like a guy's:

Step 1: Cut Your Hair Short

The first step to achieving a guy's hair look is to cut your hair short. Go for a layered haircut that adds texture and movement to your hair. You can also opt for a pixie cut or a bob that is shorter in length. Short hair is easier to style and manage, making it perfect for a relaxed look.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is crucial in achieving a guy's hair look. Use a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your hair to create a messy and tousled look. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

The key to styling your hair like a guy is to keep it simple and effortless. Use your fingers to create a messy and tousled look. Scrunch your hair to create waves and curls. You can also use a hair straightener to create loose waves or a messy bob. Avoid using a brush as it can make your hair look too neat and polished.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a guy's hair look:

  • Wash your hair less often to maintain the texture and volume
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as it can damage your hair
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you

FAQ

Can I achieve a guy's hair look with long hair?

It is possible to achieve a guy's hair look with long hair, but it may require more effort and styling techniques. You can try adding layers to your hair to create texture and movement. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and a small amount of wax to create a messy look.

What type of products should I use to achieve a guy's hair look?

You should use texturizing sprays, sea salt sprays, pomades, and waxes to achieve a guy's hair look. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage. Avoid using hot tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a guy's hair look that is effortless and cool. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture.
