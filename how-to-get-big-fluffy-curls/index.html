How To Get Big Fluffy Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and want big, fluffy curls, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect big, fluffy curls that will turn heads. From the tools you need to the best techniques to use, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before we dive into the steps, let's first go over the tools you'll need to achieve big, fluffy curls. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, dry it thoroughly. It's important to start with completely dry hair to ensure that your curls hold their shape.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from damage caused by the heat of the curling iron.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections using a wide-tooth comb. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping each section out of the way as you go.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Now it's time to start curling your hair. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue this process, working your way through each section of hair.

Step 5: Shake Out Your Curls

Once you've curled your entire head of hair, use your fingers to gently shake out your curls. This will create a more natural, fluffy look.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

To ensure that your curls stay in place all day, apply a light mist of hairspray to your hair. Be sure to hold the can at least 6 inches away from your head to avoid any sticky residue.

Tips for Getting Perfect Big, Fluffy Curls

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you work on achieving big, fluffy curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel size to create bigger curls.
  • Don't hold the curling iron on your hair for too long or you risk damaging your hair.
  • Be sure to shake out your curls for a more natural look.
  • Apply hairspray in a light mist to avoid making your hair stiff or crunchy.

FAQ

How long will my big, fluffy curls last?

Your curls should last all day if you apply hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much.

Can I achieve big, fluffy curls on short hair?

Yes, you can achieve big, fluffy curls on short hair. Use a smaller curling iron barrel size and work in smaller sections.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use heat styling tools, always apply a heat protectant spray to your hair first.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve big, fluffy curls in no time. Have fun experimenting with different styles and techniques until you find the perfect look for you!
