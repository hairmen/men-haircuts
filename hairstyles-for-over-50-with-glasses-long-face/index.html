Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses Long Face

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are over 50 and have a long face, you may be wondering which hairstyles will look good on you. And if you wear glasses, finding the right hairstyle can be even more challenging. But don't worry! In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for over 50 women with glasses and long faces. We will also share some tips and tricks to help you maintain your hair and keep it looking great. So, let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its volume. This can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that looks good and is easy to maintain. If you have a long face, you may also be concerned about finding a hairstyle that complements your features. And if you wear glasses, you want a hairstyle that doesn't compete with your frames or get in the way of your vision. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles that are perfect for women over 50 with glasses and long faces.

How to

Before we get into specific hairstyles, let's talk about some general tips for choosing a hairstyle. First, consider your face shape. If you have a long face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. Instead, opt for a medium-length hairstyle that falls just below your shoulders. This will help to balance out your face and make it appear shorter. Second, consider your glasses. If you have large frames, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head. Instead, choose a hairstyle that is sleek and streamlined.

Step by step

Now, let's look at some specific hairstyles that are perfect for women over 50 with glasses and long faces. Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve these looks:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For women over 50 with glasses and long faces, a layered bob can help to add volume and movement to the hair, while also framing the face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob with lots of layers. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add volume, and finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Pixie Cut

If you are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to maintain, a pixie cut may be the perfect choice for you. For women over 50 with glasses and long faces, a pixie cut can help to highlight your features and create a youthful, edgy look. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a short, layered pixie cut. Use a styling gel to add texture and definition, and finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Shoulder-Length Waves

If you prefer longer hair, a shoulder-length hairstyle with soft, loose waves can be a great choice. This hairstyle is perfect for women over 50 with glasses and long faces because it helps to create volume and movement without adding too much bulk to the sides of the head. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair, and finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your hair and keep it looking great:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Consider using a hair serum or oil to add shine and manageability to your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for over 50 women with glasses and long faces:

What is the best hairstyle for a long face?

The best hairstyle for a long face is a medium-length haircut with layers. This helps to balance out the face and create a more proportional look.

What is the best hairstyle for women over 50?

The best hairstyle for women over 50 depends on your personal style and preferences. However, a layered bob or shoulder-length hairstyle with waves are both popular choices.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by using volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray. You can also try blow-drying your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.

Hair Care

Finally, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or color treatments on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it is wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best as you age. Whether you prefer a short pixie cut or a shoulder-length hairstyle with waves, there is a hairstyle that is perfect for you. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
