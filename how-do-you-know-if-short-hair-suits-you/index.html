How Do You Know If Short Hair Suits You

Written by Eveline Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is a bold and daring look that can completely transform your appearance. But how do you know if it's the right style for you?

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding if short hair suits you is to determine your face shape. Some short haircuts may look better on certain face shapes than others. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: Pixie cuts and short bobs with layers can help elongate your face.
  • Square face: Soft, layered bobs or short cuts with side-swept bangs can soften your features.
  • Oval face: Lucky you! Almost any short haircut will look great on an oval face.
  • Heart-shaped face: Chin-length bobs or pixie cuts with volume on top can balance out your features.

Remember, these are just suggestions. Ultimately, the best short haircut is one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Consider Your Hair Type

While face shape is important, your hair type also plays a big role in determining if short hair suits you. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, a short cut may require a lot of maintenance to keep it looking neat and styled. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, a short cut may add volume and make it look fuller.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Short hair can be a great option if you have a busy lifestyle and don't have a lot of time to spend on styling. However, if you enjoy experimenting with different styles and updos, short hair may not be the best choice for you. Consider your daily routine and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair before making a decision.

Reflect on Your Personality

Your hairstyle can say a lot about your personality. Short hair can give off a bold, confident vibe, while longer hair may convey a softer, more romantic look. Think about your personal style and what kind of image you want to project before opting for a short cut.

Try On Wigs or Use Virtual Tools

If you're still unsure if short hair suits you, try on wigs or use virtual tools to see how you would look with a shorter cut. There are many websites and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually.

Get a Professional Consultation

If you're still unsure, it's always best to consult with a professional stylist. They can give you expert advice on what short hairstyles would look best on you based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

FAQ

Can I still look feminine with short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair can be both edgy and feminine, depending on the cut and styling. Pixie cuts and bobs can be styled with soft, romantic waves or sleek and sophisticated straight styles.

Will short hair make my face look wider?

Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. It's all about finding the right cut and style for your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair may require less maintenance than longer hair, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness to fine hair.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as a pomade or mousse, to help hold your style in place.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying, flat ironing, or curling, to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you opt for short hair or long locks, wear it with pride and confidence.
