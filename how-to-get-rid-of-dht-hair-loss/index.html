How To Get Rid Of Dht Hair Loss A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss, you may be wondering what is causing it. One common cause of hair loss is DHT, or dihydrotestosterone. DHT is a hormone that can cause hair follicles to shrink, leading to hair loss. However, there are steps you can take to get rid of DHT hair loss. In this guide, we will provide tips and steps you can take to get rid of DHT hair loss.

Description

DHT hair loss is a common condition that affects many people. It is caused by the hormone dihydrotestosterone, which can cause hair follicles to shrink and eventually fall out. There are many factors that can contribute to DHT hair loss, including genetics, age, and hormones. However, there are steps you can take to prevent and treat DHT hair loss.

How to Get Rid of DHT Hair Loss

Step 1: Use Hair Loss Shampoo

One of the first steps you should take in getting rid of DHT hair loss is to use a hair loss shampoo. These shampoos are specially designed to help reduce DHT levels in the scalp, which can help prevent hair loss. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like ketoconazole or saw palmetto, which are known to help reduce DHT levels.

Step 2: Take Supplements

In addition to using hair loss shampoo, you can also take supplements that help reduce DHT levels. Saw palmetto, biotin, and zinc are all supplements that have been shown to help reduce DHT levels in the scalp. Be sure to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements to make sure they are safe for you.

Step 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can also help reduce DHT levels. Foods that are high in antioxidants, like berries and leafy greens, can help reduce inflammation in the scalp and promote healthy hair growth. In addition, foods that are high in vitamins and minerals, like iron and vitamin D, can also help promote healthy hair growth.

Step 4: Manage Stress

Stress can also contribute to DHT hair loss. When you are stressed, your body produces more cortisol, which can increase DHT levels in the scalp. To help manage stress, try practicing relaxation techniques like meditation or yoga.

Tips

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like hair dyes and styling products.
  • Drink plenty of water to help keep your scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, like braids and ponytails, which can pull on the hair and cause damage.
  • Get plenty of sleep to help promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Can DHT hair loss be reversed?

While DHT hair loss cannot be reversed, there are steps you can take to prevent further hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

Can medications help reduce DHT levels?

Yes, there are medications that can help reduce DHT levels in the scalp. Talk to your doctor about whether medication is right for you.

How long does it take to see results from DHT hair loss treatments?

It can take several months to see results from DHT hair loss treatments. Be patient and consistent in your treatment regimen.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is important in preventing DHT hair loss. Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair, and avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid rubbing your hair with a towel when drying it. Instead, gently blot your hair with a towel to remove excess moisture.

By following these steps and taking good care of your hair, you can help prevent and treat DHT hair loss.
