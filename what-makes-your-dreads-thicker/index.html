Keeping your scalp clean and free of buildup can promote healthy hair growth. Aim to wash your hair at least once a week, using a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid buildup.

Using a Scalp Massager

A scalp massager can help improve blood flow and circulation to your scalp, which can promote healthy hair growth. Use a scalp massager regularly to help make your dreads thicker.
