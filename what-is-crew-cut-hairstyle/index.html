What Is Crew Cut Hairstyle

Written by Petter Oct 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A crew cut hairstyle is a popular short haircut style that is typically seen on military personnel, athletes, and actors. This hairstyle is characterized by its short length on the sides and back, while the top is left slightly longer. The hair on the top is usually styled forward or to the side, and the overall look is clean and neat.

How To Get A Crew Cut Hairstyle?

If you are interested in getting a crew cut hairstyle, there are a few things to consider before heading to the barber. First, you should decide on the length of the cut you want, as there are different variations of this style. Then, you should choose a barber who is experienced in cutting crew cuts, as it requires precision and skill to create a clean and even look.

When getting a crew cut, the barber will typically use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. The hair on the top will then be cut with scissors, and styled according to your preference.

Step By Step Guide To Getting A Crew Cut Hairstyle

Step 1: Choose The Right Length

Before getting a crew cut, you should decide on the length of the cut you want. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Generally, crew cuts are between 1/2 inch to 2 inches in length on the top, while the sides and back are usually buzzed with clippers.

Step 2: Find A Skilled Barber

Once you have decided on the length of your crew cut, you should find a barber who is experienced in cutting this style. Look for someone who has experience with military haircuts or has a portfolio of crew cuts they have done in the past.

Step 3: Get The Cut

When you arrive at the barbershop, explain the length and style you want for your crew cut. The barber will then use clippers to buzz the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. They will then use scissors to cut the hair on the top to the desired length and style it according to your preference.

Tips For Maintaining A Crew Cut Hairstyle

Once you have a crew cut, there are a few things you can do to maintain the look. First, you should get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the length even and prevent the hair from becoming too long. You should also use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Another tip for maintaining a crew cut is to use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to keep the hair on the top in place. This will give your crew cut a polished and finished look.

FAQ About Crew Cut Hairstyle

What is the history of the crew cut?

The crew cut originated in the military during World War II, where it was worn by soldiers for its practicality and ease of maintenance. It later became popular among civilians in the 1950s and 1960s, and has remained a classic short hairstyle ever since.

Can anyone get a crew cut?

While crew cuts are generally a good option for men with shorter hair, they may not be suitable for everyone. For example, if you have a round face shape, a crew cut may make your face look even rounder. It is best to consult with a barber to see if a crew cut is a good option for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care For Crew Cut Hairstyle

When it comes to hair care for a crew cut, it is important to keep the hair healthy and hydrated. This can be done by using a quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. You should also avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair and make it look dull.

In addition, you should consider using a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to keep the hair on the top in place and give your crew cut a polished and finished look.

Conclusion

A crew cut hairstyle is a classic short haircut style that is popular among military personnel, athletes, and actors. It is characterized by its short length on the sides and back, while the top is left slightly longer. If you are interested in getting a crew cut, be sure to choose a skilled barber and maintain the look with regular trims and quality hair care products.
