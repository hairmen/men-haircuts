How To Shave The Sides Of Your Head For A Mullet

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting a mullet? One of the most characteristic features of this hairstyle is the short sides. If you want to achieve the look, you'll need to know how to shave the sides of your head. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step, provide you with some tips, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

A mullet is a hairstyle that's short on top and long in the back. The sides of the head are typically shaved or cut short, while the hair on top and in the back is left longer. The mullet has been around since the 1970s, but it became especially popular in the 1980s. Today, some people still rock the mullet as a bold and retro look.

How To

Before you start shaving, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a razor, and shaving cream. It's also a good idea to have a mirror or two so you can see the back of your head.

Step 1: Trim the Longer Hair

Start by trimming the longer hair on top and in the back of your head. Use the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length. The length will depend on your personal preference and the style you're going for.

Step 2: Section Off the Sides

Next, use a comb to section off the sides of your head. The section should start just above your ears and go up to the top of your head. Clip the longer hair on top out of the way so you can focus on the sides.

Step 3: Shave the Sides

Apply shaving cream to the sides of your head. Use the razor to shave the hair off. Be careful not to cut yourself, and go slowly to ensure a smooth shave. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Clean Up the Neckline

Finally, clean up the neckline. Use the razor to shave any hair below the hairline on the back of your neck. You can also use the clippers to trim the hair on the back of your head to the desired length.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet:

  • Start with longer hair so you have more to work with.
  • Use a mirror or two to see the back of your head.
  • Go slowly and be careful not to cut yourself.
  • Clean your tools after each use.

FAQ

How long should the sides of a mullet be?

The length of the sides will depend on your personal preference and the style you're going for. Some people prefer a shorter, buzzed look, while others like a slightly longer length.

Can I shave my own head?

Yes, you can shave your own head. Just be sure to use the right tools and go slowly to avoid any cuts or nicks.

How often do I need to shave the sides?

The frequency of shaving will depend on how quickly your hair grows. You may need to shave the sides every few days or once a week to maintain the look.

Hair Care

After shaving the sides of your head, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. You can also use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.

Now that you know how to shave the sides of your head for a mullet, you're ready to rock this bold and retro hairstyle. Remember to take your time, be careful, and have fun with it!
