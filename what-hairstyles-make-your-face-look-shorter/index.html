What Hairstyles Make Your Face Look Shorter

Written by Luffy Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long face shape? Do you want to create the illusion of a shorter face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles that make your face look shorter. Stick around and find a hairstyle that suits your face shape.

Description

Long face shapes can be a challenge when it comes to choosing a flattering hairstyle. The right hairstyle can create the illusion of a shorter face, making it appear more balanced and proportionate. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face and avoids lengthening your face shape.

How to

Choosing a hairstyle that makes your face look shorter depends on your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Once you determine your face shape, you can select a hairstyle that enhances your features and balances your proportions.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Oval faces are versatile and can pull off most hairstyles. However, to create the illusion of a shorter face, go for a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face. Consider a layered bob or a side-swept bang.

Round Face Shape

For a round face shape, choose a hairstyle that creates height and volume at the crown of your head. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Consider a high ponytail or a layered pixie cut.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and wide forehead. To soften the angles of your face, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and sides of your face. Consider a layered shag or a side-swept fringe.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you have a wide forehead and narrow chin. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the lower half of your face, such as a chin-length bob or beachy waves.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. To balance your features, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and creates width at the temples. Consider a short pixie cut or a voluminous updo.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that makes your face look shorter, follow these steps to achieve the look:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose curls or waves.
  4. Apply a volumizing mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle that makes your face look shorter:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as this can elongate your face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth in your hair.
  • Try out different hairstyles before committing to a new look.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice on what hairstyles suit your face shape.

FAQ

What hairstyle is best for a long face?

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face are best for a long face shape. Consider a layered bob, a side-swept fringe, or beachy waves.

Can bangs make your face look shorter?

Yes, bangs can make your face look shorter if they are styled correctly. For a long face shape, go for a side-swept fringe or choppy bangs that add volume to the sides of your face.

Does hair color affect the way your face looks?

Yes, hair color can affect the way your face looks. Highlights or lowlights can create dimension and depth in your hair, making your face appear more balanced and proportionate.

Hair Care

After choosing a hairstyle that makes your face look shorter, it's important to maintain healthy hair. Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyles make your face look shorter, go ahead and try out a new look! Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your features. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you'll have healthy, beautiful hair that flatters your face shape.
