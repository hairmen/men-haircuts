How To Get Rid Of Red Hair Tones A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have ever dyed your hair red, you know that it can be a difficult color to maintain. Over time, the color can fade and turn brassy, leaving you with unwanted red tones. But don't worry, there are several ways to get rid of red hair tones and restore your hair's natural color. In this guide, we will go over the best methods for removing red hair tones and provide tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Red Hair Tones
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Tones

The first step in getting rid of red hair tones is to determine the cause of the problem. Red tones can occur due to a variety of factors, including exposure to sunlight, heat styling, and chemical treatments. Once you have identified the cause, you can choose the best method to remove the red tones.

Here are some of the most effective ways to get rid of red hair tones:

Use a Color-Correcting Shampoo

One of the easiest ways to get rid of red hair tones is to use a color-correcting shampoo. These shampoos are designed to neutralize red and orange tones in the hair, leaving you with a more natural color. Look for a shampoo that contains blue or purple pigments, as these colors are opposite to red on the color wheel and will cancel out the red tones.

Try a DIY Vinegar Rinse

Vinegar rinses are a natural and effective way to remove red hair tones. Mix equal parts water and apple cider vinegar and pour the mixture over your hair. Let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing out. The acidity of the vinegar will help to remove the red tones and restore your hair's natural color.

Use a Hair Toner

Hair toners are another effective way to get rid of red hair tones. Toners are typically used to neutralize unwanted brassy or yellow tones in the hair, but they can also be used to remove red tones. Look for a toner that is specifically designed for your hair color and follow the instructions carefully.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting rid of red hair tones:

  1. Identify the cause of the red tones.
  2. Choose the best method to remove the red tones.
  3. Follow the instructions carefully.
  4. Repeat the process as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair after removing red tones?

Yes, you can dye your hair after removing red tones. However, it is best to wait a few days to allow your hair to recover from the treatment.

How often should I use a color-correcting shampoo?

You can use a color-correcting shampoo as often as needed, but be careful not to overuse it, as it can dry out your hair.

Can I use a vinegar rinse on colored hair?

Yes, you can use a vinegar rinse on colored hair. However, it is best to check with your hairstylist first to ensure that it will not damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and using the methods outlined in this guide, you can easily get rid of red hair tones and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
