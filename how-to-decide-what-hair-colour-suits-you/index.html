How To Decide What Hair Colour Suits You A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience, but it can also be a daunting task. With so many colours and shades to choose from, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. Fortunately, there are a few things you can do to help you decide what hair colour suits you best. In this article, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to make an informed decision.

  2. Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have a yellow undertone, while cool skin tones have a pink or blue undertone. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones, and if they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Next, consider your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, it may be difficult to achieve a light colour without damaging your hair. If you have light hair, it may be easier to achieve a darker colour. It's also important to consider any previous colour treatments you've had, as they can impact the outcome of your new colour.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

When choosing a new hair colour, think about your personal style. Are you someone who likes to stand out, or do you prefer a more natural look? Do you want a dramatic change or a subtle one? Consider your lifestyle as well. If you have a job that requires a more conservative appearance, you may want to stick with a natural colour.

Step 4: Take The Plunge!

Once you've determined your skin tone, considered your natural hair colour, and thought about your personal style, it's time to take the plunge! Visit a trusted hairstylist to discuss your options and get their professional opinion. They can help you choose a colour that will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider the maintenance required for your new colour. Some colours require more upkeep than others.
  • Think about the season. In the summer, you may want a lighter colour, while in the winter, a darker colour can be more flattering.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! If you've always had dark hair, try going lighter. You may be surprised at how much you love it.

FAQs

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change damage my hair?

A: It's possible that a drastic colour change can damage your hair, especially if you're going from a dark colour to a light one. However, a skilled hairstylist can help minimize the damage by using high-quality products and techniques.

Q: How often will I need to touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on the colour you choose and how fast your hair grows. Some colours may require touch-ups every four to six weeks, while others can go longer between appointments.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to help prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and working with a skilled hairstylist, you can find a colour that will make you look and feel your best.
