How To Style Wavy Thick Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Wavy thick hair guys often face the challenge of managing their hair, but with the right styling techniques, it can be a great asset. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to style wavy thick hair guys, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Description

Wavy thick hair guys have hair that is naturally textured, which means it can be styled in a variety of ways. However, the thickness of the hair can make it challenging to manage and style. This guide will provide you with the tools and techniques you need to style your hair with confidence.

How to Style Wavy Thick Hair Guys

The key to styling wavy thick hair is to find a style that works with the natural texture of your hair. Here are some popular styles for wavy thick hair guys:

1. The Wavy Pompadour

The wavy pompadour is a classic style that works well with wavy thick hair. To achieve this style, you’ll need to cut your hair short on the sides and longer on top. Style the top of your hair into a pompadour by using a strong hold pomade or wax.

2. The Messy Waves

The messy waves style is perfect for guys who want a more relaxed, casual look. To achieve this style, apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it with your hands. This will give your hair a tousled, beachy look.

3. The Slicked Back Waves

The slicked back waves style is a great option for guys who want a more polished, put-together look. To achieve this style, apply a styling cream or pomade to your hair and comb it back. This will give your hair a sleek, sophisticated look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style wavy thick hair guys:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy thick hair. This will help to hydrate your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Once you’ve washed your hair, towel dry it until it is slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a sea salt spray, pomade, or styling cream. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a comb, your fingers, or a hair dryer. Experiment with different techniques until you find a style that works for you.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips for Styling Wavy Thick Hair Guys

Here are some tips to help you style your wavy thick hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to style your hair, as this will help to maintain the natural texture of your hair.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause frizz and damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my wavy thick hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy thick hair using a flat iron. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy thick hair?

A: It’s best to wash your wavy thick hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: What’s the best way to dry my wavy thick hair?

A: The best way to dry your wavy thick hair is to let it air dry. If you need to use a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment to avoid frizz.

Hair Care for Wavy Thick Hair Guys

Here are some tips to keep your wavy thick hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy thick hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to style your wavy thick hair with confidence. Remember to experiment with different styles and products until you find the ones that work best for you.
