Easy Hairstyles For Mid Length Wavy Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have mid length wavy hair, you are in luck! There are so many easy hairstyles that you can try to switch up your look. From messy buns to beachy waves, there are endless possibilities. In this article, we will go over some of the best easy hairstyles for mid length wavy hair, as well as tips on how to achieve them.

Description

Mid length wavy hair is perfect for those who are looking for a versatile hairstyle. It is not too long or too short, and the waves add texture and volume to the hair. However, styling mid length wavy hair can be a bit challenging at times. That's why it is important to have some easy hairstyles up your sleeve for those days when you don't have a lot of time or energy to spend on your hair.

How to

Here are some easy hairstyles that you can try with your mid length wavy hair:

1. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a classic that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, simply take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail. You can leave the rest of your hair down, or you can curl it for some extra volume and texture.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is perfect for those days when you don't feel like doing much with your hair. To achieve this look, simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy, effortless look.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for a day at the beach or a night out. To achieve this look, simply curl your hair with a curling iron or wand, and then run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls. You can also use a salt spray to add some texture and volume to your hair.

4. Braids

Braids are a great way to switch up your look and add some texture to your hair. You can try a simple three-strand braid, a French braid, or a fishtail braid. The possibilities are endless!

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions for achieving some of these easy hairstyles:

1. Half Up Half Down

  1. Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  2. Tease the crown of your hair for some extra volume.
  3. Curl the bottom section of your hair for some texture.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  3. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Beachy Waves

  1. Curl your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  2. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  3. Spray with salt spray to add texture and volume.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Braids

  1. Choose the type of braid you want to try.
  2. Section off the hair you want to braid.
  3. Braid the hair, starting at the top and working your way down.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving the best results with these easy hairstyles:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or wand.
  • Tease the crown of your hair for extra volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.
  • Use a hairspray that provides a strong hold.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for mid length wavy hair:

1. How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

You can use a serum or oil to help prevent your hair from getting frizzy. You can also try using a diffuser when blow drying your hair.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It is recommended that you wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets.

3. How do I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by teasing the crown or using a volumizing spray or mousse.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these easy hairstyles and tips, you can achieve a variety of looks with your mid length wavy hair. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there is a hairstyle that will work for you.
