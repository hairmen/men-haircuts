Cute Updo Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have shoulder length hair, you might find it challenging to style it in an updo. But don't worry, there are many cute updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair that you can try. In this article, we will show you some easy and stylish hairdos that you can wear for any occasion. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many ways. However, when it comes to updos, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type and face shape. In this article, we will show you some cute updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair that are easy to do and perfect for any occasion.

How to

To create a cute updo hairstyle for shoulder length hair, you need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Decide on the updo hairstyle you want to create.
  4. Gather the necessary tools and accessories, such as bobby pins, hair ties, and hairspray.
  5. Follow the step-by-step instructions to create your updo hairstyle.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

Step by step

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a popular updo hairstyle that is easy to do and looks effortless. Here's how to create a messy bun:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Tie your hair in a high ponytail.
  3. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

2. Half Updo

A half updo is a cute hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Here's how to create a half updo:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head.
  3. Tie the section with a hair tie.
  4. Twist the hair tie to create a small bun.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

3. Braided Updo

The braided updo is a stylish hairstyle that looks elegant and sophisticated. Here's how to create a braided updo:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head.
  3. Braid the section of hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  4. Repeat the process on the other side of your head.
  5. Gather the braids and the rest of your hair and tie them in a low ponytail.
  6. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  7. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect updo hairstyle for shoulder length hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Add accessories, such as flowers or hairpins, to enhance your updo hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I create an updo hairstyle with shoulder length hair?

A: Yes, there are many cute updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair that you can try. From messy buns to braided updos, there's a hairstyle for every occasion.

Q: Do I need special tools to create an updo hairstyle?

A: No, you don't need special tools to create an updo hairstyle. All you need are bobby pins, hair ties, and hairspray.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair healthy and shiny, you need to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these cute updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair and hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking beautiful and stylish. Try these hairstyles for your next event or night out, and you're sure to turn heads!
