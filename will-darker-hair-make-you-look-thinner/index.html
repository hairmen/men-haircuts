Will Darker Hair Make You Look Thinner

Written by Aprilia Oct 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying to look slimmer but not seeing any results? Have you ever thought that changing your hair color could be the answer to your problem? In this article, we will explore whether darker hair can make you look thinner and give you some tips on how to achieve this look.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Changing your hair color can make a huge difference in how you look. Darker hair can create the illusion of a slimmer face and body. This is because darker hair tends to absorb light, making your features look more defined and your body appear smaller. The contrast between your skin and hair color can also make your face look more angular, which can give the appearance of a slimmer face.

How to

Before you decide to dye your hair darker, there are a few things you should consider. Firstly, you need to choose the right shade of dark hair color that will complement your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, go for warmer shades of brown or black. If you have a cool skin tone, opt for cooler shades of brown or black.

Secondly, you need to consider the maintenance of your new hair color. Darker hair colors tend to fade faster than lighter ones, so you will need to touch up your roots more often. You should also invest in good quality hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to get darker hair that will make you look thinner:

  1. Choose the right shade of dark hair color that will complement your skin tone.
  2. Make sure your hair is in good condition before dyeing it.
  3. Apply the hair color according to the instructions on the box.
  4. After dyeing your hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color.
  5. Touch up your roots regularly to keep your hair looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the look of thinner hair:

  • Consider getting a haircut that will complement your new hair color.
  • Use makeup to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Wear clothes that flatter your body type.
  • Exercise regularly and eat a healthy diet to maintain a healthy weight.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether darker hair can make you look thinner:

Will darker hair make my face look slimmer?

Yes, darker hair can create the illusion of a slimmer face by making your features look more defined and your jawline more angular.

What shade of dark hair color should I choose?

You should choose a shade of dark hair color that complements your skin tone. If you have warm skin tones, opt for warmer shades of brown or black. If you have cool skin tones, go for cooler shades of brown or black.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent fading
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Changing your hair color can be a simple yet effective way to make yourself look slimmer. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve the look you desire.
