How To Get The Wet Hair Look On Straight Weave

Written by Petter Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to switch up your straight weave hairstyle? The wet hair look is a popular trend that gives off a sleek and edgy vibe. With a few simple steps, you can achieve this look on your straight weave. In this article, we'll go over how to get the wet hair look on straight weave, along with some tips and FAQs to help you perfect the style.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get the Wet Hair Look on Straight Weave
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Wet Hair Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for the Wet Hair Look

Step by Step Guide

Before we get started, make sure your straight weave is clean and dry. Here's how to achieve the wet hair look on your straight weave:

Step 1: Apply Hair Gel

Start by applying a small amount of hair gel to your weave, focusing on the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair to avoid any clumps or buildup.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb through your hair to distribute the gel and create a sleek look. Comb your hair in the direction that you want it to lay.

Step 3: Apply Hair Spray

Next, apply a light mist of hair spray to your hair to set the style in place. Be sure to hold the hairspray bottle about 10-12 inches away from your head to avoid any buildup or stickiness.

Step 4: Add a Shine Serum

To add some extra shine to your wet hair look, apply a small amount of shine serum to your hair. Focus on the ends to avoid any greasiness or buildup.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on low heat to speed up the drying process. Avoid using a blow dryer on high heat as this can cause damage to your weave.

Tips for Achieving the Wet Hair Look

Here are some additional tips to help you perfect the wet hair look on your straight weave:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel and hairspray to avoid any buildup or stickiness.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a slicked-back ponytail or a deep side part.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray if you choose to use a diffuser or blow dryer on low heat.
  • Use a small amount of shine serum to avoid any greasiness or buildup.

FAQs

Can I achieve the wet hair look on curly or wavy weave?

Yes, you can achieve the wet hair look on curly or wavy weave. However, you may need to use more hair gel and hairspray to control the texture of your hair.

How long will the wet hair look last?

The wet hair look can last up to a day or two, depending on the amount of hair gel and hairspray you use. Touch up your hair throughout the day with a light mist of hairspray to maintain the style.

Will the hair gel and hairspray damage my weave?

No, as long as you use high-quality hair products and avoid using too much product, your weave should not be damaged by the hair gel and hairspray.

Hair Care for the Wet Hair Look

To maintain the health of your straight weave and prevent any damage, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid any buildup or oiliness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on high heat to prevent damage to your weave.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair to prevent any tangling or matting of your weave.

With these tips and steps, you can easily achieve the wet hair look on your straight weave. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the perfect look for you. Happy styling!
