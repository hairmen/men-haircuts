How To Put Hair In Small Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and easy hairstyle that can keep your hair out of your face? Then a small man bun might be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle not only looks great, but it's also practical and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to put hair in small man bun.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Put Hair in Small Man Bun
  2. Tips for Styling Small Man Bun
  3. Frequently Asked Questions
  4. Hair Care for Small Man Bun

How to Put Hair in Small Man Bun

Follow these simple steps to create a perfect small man bun:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying some styling product. This will help keep your hair in place and make it easier to manage.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to gather your hair into a bun.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Use your hands to gather all of your hair at the crown of your head. Hold it tightly to prevent any stray hairs from escaping.

Step 4: Tie Your Hair

Using an elastic band, tie your hair into a ponytail. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it hurts your scalp.

Step 5: Create the Bun

Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun. Keep twisting until the bun feels tight and secure.

Step 6: Secure the Bun

Use another elastic band to secure the bun in place. Make sure it's positioned at the base of the bun to keep it from slipping down.

Step 7: Finish the Look

Use some hairspray or styling gel to tame any flyaways and add some shine to your hair. You can also use a comb or your fingers to create a more textured look.

Tips for Styling Small Man Bun

Here are some tips to help you style your small man bun:

  • Don't tie your hair too tight. This can cause hair breakage and damage your scalp.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color. This will make it less noticeable and give you a more natural look.
  • Experiment with different bun sizes and positions. You can place the bun at the top or back of your head, depending on your preference.
  • Accessorize your bun with a headband or bandana for a more stylish look.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does my hair need to be to create a small man bun?

Your hair should be at least 3 inches long to create a small man bun. If your hair is shorter, you can still create a mini-bun or opt for a different hairstyle.

Can I wear a small man bun to work or formal events?

Yes, a small man bun can be a stylish and professional hairstyle for any occasion. Just make sure it's neat and well-maintained.

How often should I wash my hair if I wear a small man bun?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup and maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care for Small Man Bun

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and in great shape:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners. This can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a perfect small man bun that looks great and keeps your hair under control. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
