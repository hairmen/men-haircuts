How Do I Ask My Barber For A Taper

Written by William Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut but not sure how to explain it to your barber? Asking for a taper can be confusing, especially if you're not familiar with the terminology. In this post, we'll break down exactly what a taper is, and give you some tips on how to ask your barber for the perfect taper.

What is a Taper?

A taper is a popular hairstyle that involves gradually shortening the hair from the top of the head down to the neck. The hair is left longer at the top and shorter at the sides and back. This creates a smooth, blended look that is perfect for any occasion.

How to Ask for a Taper

If you're not sure how to ask your barber for a taper, don't worry. Here are some steps you can follow to ensure that you get the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Show Your Barber a Picture

The easiest way to explain what you want is to show your barber a picture of the style you're looking for. This can be a picture of a celebrity or a model, or even a picture of yourself from a previous haircut.

Step 2: Explain the Length You Want

Once you've shown your barber a picture, explain the length you want at the top of your head. This will help your barber determine how much hair to leave at the top of your head.

Step 3: Explain the Fade or Taper

Next, explain the type of taper you want. Do you want a low fade, a high fade, or something in between? Your barber will need to know this in order to create the perfect taper for you.

Step 4: Be Specific

It's important to be as specific as possible when explaining what you want. Use terms like "shorter on the sides" or "a gradual fade" to ensure that your barber understands exactly what you're looking for.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Taper

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect taper:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Barber

Make sure you choose a barber who is experienced in creating tapers. Look for a barber who has a portfolio of previous work that includes tapers.

Tip 2: Be Clear About What You Want

Don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with the way your taper is turning out. Your barber wants you to be happy with your haircut, so be clear about what you want.

Tip 3: Maintain Your Taper

Once you've gotten the perfect taper, it's important to maintain it. Visit your barber regularly to keep your taper looking fresh.

FAQ

What's the difference between a taper and a fade?

A taper involves gradually shortening the hair from the top of the head down to the neck, while a fade involves a more dramatic transition from longer hair at the top to shorter hair at the sides and back.

How long does a taper last?

A taper can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care

After getting a taper, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips for hair care:

Tip 1: Shampoo and Condition Regularly

Make sure you shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair, so try to avoid using heat tools as much as possible.

By following these tips and asking your barber for a taper, you'll be well on your way to the perfect haircut. Remember to be specific and communicate clearly with your barber, and don't be afraid to ask for what you want.
