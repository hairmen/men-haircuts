Allergies

Allergies are one of the most common reasons why dogs lose hair and develop bald spots. These can be caused by environmental factors such as pollen, dust mites, and mold, or by food allergies. If your dog is scratching and biting at their skin, this can lead to hair loss and bald spots.

Symptoms

Some common symptoms of allergies in dogs include:

Symptoms

Some common symptoms of skin infections in dogs include:

  • Bumps or scabs
