Short Hairstyles That Suit Round Faces

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but none seem to complement your round face shape? Look no further as we have compiled a list of short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces. Read on to find out more!

Description

A round face shape is characterized by fullness in the cheeks, soft and curved lines, and a width that is almost equal to the height. The goal of a short hairstyle for a round face is to create height and length to the face to make it appear more oval-shaped. A good short hairstyle for a round face should have volume at the crown and the sides should be kept close to the head.

How to

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror. If your face is widest at your cheeks and has a rounded chin, then you have a round face shape.

Next, choose a short hairstyle that has volume on top and is tapered at the sides. A pixie cut, a bob with layers, or a short shag are all great options for round faces. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face as this will only make your face appear rounder.

When getting your hair cut, ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to your hair to add volume to the crown. If you want to add some height to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or root lifter before blow-drying your hair.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Choose a short hairstyle that has volume on top and is tapered at the sides

3. Ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to your hair to add volume to the crown

4. Use a volumizing mousse or root lifter before blow-drying your hair to add height

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face

2. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume to the crown

3. Use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair

FAQ

1. Can I still wear bangs with a round face?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides to create a diagonal line that slims the face.

2. Will a short hairstyle make my face look even rounder?

No, a short hairstyle that is tapered at the sides and has volume on top will create height and length to the face, making it appear more oval-shaped.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using hot tools such as a flat iron or curling iron too often. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Now that you know what short hairstyles suit round faces, it's time to book a hair appointment and try out a new look. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
