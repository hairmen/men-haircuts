How to Change Your Hair Type Permanently: A Comprehensive Guide

Description

Many people are unhappy with their natural hair type, whether it be curly, straight, or somewhere in between. If you're one of those people, you may be wondering if it's possible to change your hair type permanently. The answer is yes, it is possible, but it requires a lot of time, effort, and money.

How to

The process of changing your hair type permanently involves chemical treatments that alter the structure of your hair. There are two main methods for doing this: relaxing and texturizing.

Relaxing

Relaxing is the process of chemically straightening curly or kinky hair. It involves applying a relaxer, which is a type of cream or lotion that contains chemicals that break down the protein bonds in your hair, making it straight. The process can take several hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Texturizing

Texturizing is the process of chemically altering straight hair to create curls or waves. It involves applying a texturizer, which is a type of cream or lotion that contains chemicals that loosen the protein bonds in your hair, allowing it to curl or wave. The process can take several hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Step by step

Before you decide to change your hair type permanently, it's important to do your research and find a reputable stylist who specializes in chemical treatments. Here are the basic steps involved:

  1. Consult with a stylist to determine the best method for your hair type and desired outcome
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it
  3. Apply the chemical treatment, being careful to follow the instructions carefully
  4. Rinse out the chemical treatment
  5. Apply a neutralizer to stop the chemical process
  6. Rinse out the neutralizer
  7. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Changing your hair type permanently is a big decision, so here are some tips to help you prepare:

  • Do your research and find a reputable stylist
  • Be prepared to spend several hours at the salon
  • Make sure you understand the risks and potential side effects of chemical treatments
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products to maintain your new hair type

FAQ

Is it safe to change your hair type permanently?

Chemical treatments can be damaging to your hair and scalp, so it's important to find a skilled stylist who uses high-quality products and follows proper safety procedures.

How long does it take to change your hair type permanently?

The process can take several hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

How much does it cost to change your hair type permanently?

The cost varies depending on the method used, the length and thickness of your hair, and the location of the salon. Expect to pay several hundred dollars.

Hair Care

Maintaining your new hair type requires a different approach to hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use products specifically designed for your new hair type
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

Changing your hair type permanently is a big decision, but with the right stylist and proper care, you can achieve the look you've always wanted.
