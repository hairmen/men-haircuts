How To Keep Curly Hair From Getting Frizzy Overnight Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep your curls looking defined and free from frizz. While there are many factors that can contribute to frizz, one of the most frustrating is waking up to a head full of unruly hair after a good night's sleep. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to keep curly hair from getting frizzy overnight.

  • How to Keep Curly Hair from Getting Frizzy Overnight
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Keep Curly Hair from Getting Frizzy Overnight

There are a few things you can do to prevent your curly hair from getting frizzy overnight:

1. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are great for curly hair because they reduce friction and help prevent tangles. They also help to keep your hair moisturized, which can help prevent frizz.

2. Pineapple Your Hair

Pineappling is a technique where you gather all of your curls on the top of your head and secure them with a scrunchie or a silk scarf. This helps to keep your curls from getting squished and frizzy while you sleep.

3. Use a Satin Bonnet or Scarf

If you don't want to pineapple your hair, you can also use a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your curls while you sleep. Satin is a smooth material that helps to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

4. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Before going to bed, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep curly hair from getting frizzy overnight:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Pineapple Your Hair or Use a Satin Bonnet or Scarf

Next, either pineapple your hair by gathering all of your curls on the top of your head and securing them with a scrunchie or a silk scarf, or use a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your curls while you sleep.

Step 4: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Finally, sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangles. This will help to keep your curls looking defined and free from frizz.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for keeping your curly hair from getting frizzy overnight:

1. Use a Microfiber Towel

When drying your hair, use a microfiber towel instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on your hair and help to reduce frizz.

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

Try not to touch your hair too much while you sleep. The more you touch your hair, the more likely it is to get frizzy.

3. Use a Diffuser When Blow Drying

If you blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help prevent frizz. A diffuser helps to distribute the heat more evenly and reduce the amount of air flow, which can help prevent frizz.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly your hair gets oily. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others need to wash their hair every other day.

Q: Can I use a regular pillowcase instead of a silk pillowcase?

A: While a regular pillowcase is better than sleeping on cotton sheets, it's not as good as a silk pillowcase. Silk helps to reduce friction and prevent tangles, while cotton can cause friction and lead to frizz.

Hair Care Routine

Here's an example hair care routine for keeping your curly hair looking its best:

Morning:

  • Refresh your curls with a water-based curl refresher spray
  • Apply a lightweight leave-in conditioner
  • Style your hair as desired

Evening:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner
  • Pineapple your hair or use a satin bonnet or scarf
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase

By following these tips and tricks and establishing a consistent hair care routine, you can keep your curly hair looking defined and free from frizz, even while you sleep.
