Short Hair For Round Face 60 Year Old A Guide

If you are a 60-year-old woman with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can seem like a daunting task. However, with the right guidance, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that complements your facial features. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short hair for round face 60 year old, including how to choose the right cut, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

  2. How to Style Your Hair

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing the right cut for your round face, there are a few things to consider. First, you want to look for a cut that will add height to your face, rather than width. This can be achieved with a short, layered cut that adds volume at the crown of your head.

Another option is a pixie cut, which can be tailored to suit your individual face shape. A side-swept fringe can also help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Ultimately, the best way to choose the right cut is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you find a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right cut, it's important to learn how to style your hair to achieve the best look. Depending on your cut, you may need to use product to add volume or texture to your hair.

If you have a pixie cut, you may want to use a styling cream or wax to add texture and definition to your hair. For a short layered cut, you may want to use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to your crown.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture or waves.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Style

To keep your short hair looking its best, there are a few tips you can follow:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your cut and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage over time.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for round face 60 year old:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short cut can actually elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Q: What is the best way to style a pixie cut?

A: Use a styling cream or wax to add texture and definition to your hair. You can also experiment with different partings and fringe lengths to find the perfect look for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

To keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look with your short hair. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist to find the right cut for your individual face shape and hair type.
