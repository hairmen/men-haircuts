How To Style Hair Away From Your Face A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly having your hair in your face? Do you want to try out new hairstyles that require your hair to be away from your face? Well, look no further! In this article, we will be discussing various techniques and tips on how to style hair away from your face. Whether you have long hair or short hair, there are plenty of options for you to choose from.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Hair Away from Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Hair Away from Your Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Hair Away from Your Face

There are several ways to style hair away from your face. The most common techniques include braids, buns, ponytails, and clips. You can also use styling products such as hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place. Below are some of the most popular ways to style hair away from your face:

Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while adding some style to your look. There are several types of braids to choose from including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. You can also add accessories such as beads or ribbons to your braids to make them unique.

Buns

Buns are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can choose from a high bun, low bun, or messy bun depending on your preference. Buns are also great for keeping your hair off your neck during hot weather.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a quick and easy way to style your hair away from your face. You can choose from a high ponytail, low ponytail, or side ponytail depending on your preference. You can also add accessories such as scrunchies or headbands to your ponytail to add some flair.

Clips

Clips are a simple yet effective way to keep your hair out of your face. You can choose from bobby pins, hair clips, or barrettes depending on your preference. Clips are also great for adding some color or sparkle to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style hair away from your face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Choose your preferred hairstyle (braid, bun, ponytail, or clip)
  3. Gather your hair and secure it with an elastic band
  4. If you are doing a braid, separate your hair into three sections and begin braiding
  5. If you are doing a bun, twist your hair into a bun shape and secure it with bobby pins
  6. If you are doing a ponytail, pull your hair back and secure it with an elastic band
  7. If you are using clips, place them in your hair to keep it away from your face
  8. Use hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place
  9. Add accessories such as beads, ribbons, or headbands to your hairstyle
  10. Enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips for Styling Hair Away from Your Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair away from your face:

  • Use a comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or tangles in your hair
  • Apply styling products such as hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Add accessories such as beads, ribbons, or headbands to make your hairstyle unique
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle

FAQs

Q: Can I style my hair away from my face if I have short hair?

A: Yes, there are several ways to style short hair away from your face. You can try a pixie cut or a bob with bangs pushed to the side. You can also use clips or bobby pins to keep your hair out of your face.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from falling out of my hairstyle?

A: Use hairspray, gel, or bobby pins to keep your hair in place. You can also try using a hair tie that is tight enough to hold your hair, but not too tight that it causes discomfort.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much styling products as they can weigh down your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength

Now that you know how to style hair away from your face, you can try out different hairstyles and experiment with accessories to find your favorite look. Remember to take care of your hair by following proper hair care tips to keep it healthy and strong. Happy styling!
