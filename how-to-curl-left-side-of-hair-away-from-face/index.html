How To Curl Left Side Of Hair Away From Face

Written by Petter Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some volume and texture to your hair, curling it is a great option. However, curling your hair can be tricky, especially when you're trying to curl the left side away from your face. In this article, we'll show you how to curl the left side of your hair away from your face in easy steps.

Description

Curling your hair can be a fun way to switch up your look, but it can be a challenge to get your curls to stay in place the way you want them to. When you're curling the left side of your hair away from your face, it's important to follow a few key steps to ensure that your curls come out looking beautiful and natural. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks for curling the left side of your hair in a way that flatters your face shape and adds body and dimension to your hair.

How to Curl Left Side of Hair Away from Face

Follow these steps to curl the left side of your hair away from your face:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start curling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. It's also a good idea to use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some body to your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable pieces. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide each side into two sections. Clip each section out of the way as you work.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting from the middle of the strand and wrapping it towards the ends. Hold the curling iron for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process with each section of hair.

Step 4: Curl the left side of your hair away from your face

When curling the left side of your hair, it's important to curl the hair away from your face. To do this, hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the hair around it away from your face. Hold the curling iron for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you've curled all the sections of your hair, use a light hairspray to set your curls in place. Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent the curls from falling out.

Tips

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel size that's appropriate for your hair length and texture.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or it will look unnatural.
  • Make sure to curl the left side of your hair away from your face to create a flattering look.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions and vary the size of your curls.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

What's the best way to curl the left side of my hair away from my face?

The best way to curl the left side of your hair away from your face is to hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the hair around it away from your face.

How can I make my curls last longer?

To make your curls last longer, use a light hairspray to set your curls in place. Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent the curls from falling out.

What size curling iron should I use?

The size of the curling iron you use will depend on your hair length and texture. For shorter hair, use a smaller barrel size, and for longer hair, use a larger barrel size.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you do use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Finally, make sure to eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.
