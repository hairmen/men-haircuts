How To Know What Shape Your Face Is

Written by Oliver Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever wonder what the best haircut or makeup look is for your face shape? Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyles and makeup looks. In this article, we’ll show you how to determine your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Determining Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

How to Determine Your Face Shape

When it comes to determining your face shape, there are a few key features to look for:

  • Forehead width
  • Cheekbone width
  • Jawline shape
  • Face length

By measuring these parts of your face, you can narrow down your face shape to one of the following:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to determine your face shape:

  1. Measure your forehead width by measuring across the widest part of your forehead.
  2. Measure your cheekbone width by measuring across your face, starting from the outer corner of one eye to the outer corner of the other.
  3. Measure your jawline shape by measuring from the tip of your chin to the bottom of your ear at the jawline.
  4. Measure your face length by measuring from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.
  5. Compare your measurements to the face shape chart to determine your face shape.

Remember, these measurements may not be exact, so use your best judgment when determining your face shape.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to help you determine your face shape:

  • Look at your face in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a bar of soap or a washable marker.
  • Take a selfie and draw lines on the photo to measure your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and face length.
  • Ask a friend or hairstylist for their opinion on your face shape.

FAQs

What if my face shape doesn’t match any of the options?

It’s possible that your face shape doesn’t fit neatly into one of the six options. You may have a combination of two face shapes or a unique face shape. In this case, focus on the features of your face that are most prominent and use that as a guide for choosing hairstyles and makeup looks.

What if my measurements are between two face shapes?

If your measurements fall in between two face shapes, you may have a combination face shape. Look at the features of each face shape and choose hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter the most prominent features of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can use this information to choose the most flattering hairstyles for your face shape.

Oval Face Shape

People with an oval face shape have balanced proportions and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Some flattering hairstyles for oval face shapes include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves

Round Face Shape

People with a round face shape have full cheeks and a rounded chin. Flattering hairstyles for round face shapes include:

  • Long, layered hair
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bob

Square Face Shape

People with a square face shape have a strong jawline and forehead. Flattering hairstyles for square face shapes include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves

Heart Face Shape

People with a heart face shape have a wider forehead and a pointed chin. Flattering hairstyles for heart face shapes include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Beachy waves

Diamond Face Shape

People with a diamond face shape have a narrow forehead and a pointed chin. Flattering hairstyles for diamond face shapes include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Short bob
  • Soft waves

Triangle Face Shape

People with a triangle face shape have a wide jawline and a narrow forehead. Flattering hairstyles for triangle face shapes include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Soft waves

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyles and makeup looks. Use these tips to determine your face shape and choose hairstyles that flatter your features.
