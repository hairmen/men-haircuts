Do Highlights Make You Look Older Or Younger

Written by Oliver Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Highlights are a great way to add dimension and depth to your hair. They can also be used to create a youthful, vibrant look. However, many people wonder whether highlights make you look older or younger. In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide you with tips on how to achieve a look that is both youthful and stylish.

Description

Highlights are a hair coloring technique that involves coloring small sections of hair. This technique can be used to create a variety of looks, from subtle to bold. Highlights can be added to any hair color, and the results can vary depending on the color and placement of the highlights.

How to

If you are interested in getting highlights, there are a few things you should consider. First, think about the color of your hair and the color you want to achieve. If you have dark hair, you may want to opt for lighter highlights to create contrast. If you have light hair, you may want to choose darker highlights to add depth.

Next, think about the placement of the highlights. Highlights can be added throughout the hair or in specific areas, such as around the face or at the tips of the hair. The placement of the highlights can affect the overall look.

Finally, think about the maintenance required for highlights. Depending on the type of highlights you choose, you may need to visit the salon every few months for touch-ups.

Step by Step

If you decide to get highlights, here are the steps you can expect:

  1. Your stylist will assess your hair and determine the best color and placement for your highlights.
  2. Your hair will be sectioned and the highlights will be applied using foil or a cap.
  3. The highlights will be left on for a certain amount of time, depending on the desired result.
  4. Your hair will be rinsed and conditioned.
  5. Your stylist may trim your hair or style it to show off your new highlights.

Tips

If you want to achieve a youthful, vibrant look with highlights, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose colors that complement your skin tone and eye color.
  • Consider the placement of the highlights to create a flattering look.
  • Avoid over-processing your hair, as this can lead to damage.
  • Use hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair.

FAQ

Do highlights make you look older or younger?

Highlights can make you look younger if they are done in a way that complements your features and skin tone. However, if the highlights are too light or too harsh, they can create an aging effect.

Can anyone get highlights?

Most people can get highlights, but it is important to consider the condition of your hair and any previous color treatments before getting highlights.

How often should I get my highlights touched up?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the type of highlights and your natural hair growth. Generally, most people need touch-ups every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant after getting highlights, it is important to use hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair. These products can help to maintain the color and prevent damage. It is also important to protect your hair from the sun and heat, as these can cause fading and damage.
