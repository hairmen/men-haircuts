What To Tell Your Barber When Getting A Haircut

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Getting a haircut can be a nerve-wracking experience, especially if you're not sure what to tell your barber. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you some tips on what to tell your barber when getting a haircut. So sit back, relax, and let's get started!

Description

Getting a haircut is a routine part of life, but it can be stressful if you don't know what to ask for. Many people struggle to communicate their desired haircut to their barber, which can lead to disappointment and frustration. In this article, we'll provide some helpful tips on what to tell your barber when getting a haircut.

How to

When you arrive at the barbershop, it's important to have a clear idea of what you want. This can be achieved by looking at photos of hairstyles online or in magazines. You can even bring a photo with you to show your barber. Here are some other tips:

Step by Step

1. Start by telling your barber the overall length you want for your hair. Do you want a short, medium, or long haircut?

2. Next, tell your barber about any specific features you want. For example, do you want your hair to be layered or textured?

3. Be sure to mention any areas of your head that you want to focus on. For example, do you want your bangs trimmed or your sideburns shortened?

4. If you have a preferred styling product, let your barber know. They can help you choose a haircut that will work well with your favorite product.

5. Finally, ask your barber for their professional opinion. They may have suggestions for a haircut that would look great on you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

- Be open to your barber's suggestions. They are trained professionals and may have ideas that you haven't considered.

- Don't be afraid to ask questions. If you're not sure what something means, ask for clarification.

- Be specific. Use descriptive words to help your barber understand exactly what you're looking for.

- Relax and enjoy the experience. Getting a haircut should be a relaxing and enjoyable experience.

FAQ

Q: Should I wash my hair before getting a haircut?

A: It's not necessary, but it's a good idea to have clean hair so your barber can see your hair's natural texture.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair's length and how quickly it grows. Most people get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: How much should I tip my barber?

A: It's customary to tip 15-20% of the cost of the haircut.

Hair Care

After you've gotten your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

- Use high-quality hair care products that are designed for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh environmental factors.

Now that you know what to tell your barber when getting a haircut, you can sit back, relax, and enjoy the experience. Remember to be specific and open to your barber's suggestions, and you'll walk out of the barbershop with a great new haircut!
