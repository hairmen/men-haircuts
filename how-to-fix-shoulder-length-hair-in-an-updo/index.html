How To Fix Shoulder Length Hair In An Updo

Written by Oliver Aug 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle with your shoulder length hair? Do you want to switch it up and try something new? Well, you're in luck because we have the solution for you! In this article, we will teach you how to fix shoulder length hair in an updo that will have you looking fabulous. Whether you're going out for a night on the town or attending a wedding, this updo will have you feeling confident and beautiful.

Description

Shoulder length hair can be a bit tricky when it comes to styling, but with the right technique, you can create a beautiful updo that will make you stand out from the crowd. This updo is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face while still looking stylish. It's a great option for both casual and formal events and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Apply a small amount of styling product to help hold the style in place.

Step 1:

Start by parting your hair down the middle and separating it into two sections.

Step 2:

Take one section of hair and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it with bobby pins. Repeat with the other section of hair.

Step 3:

Take the twisted sections of hair and cross them over each other, securing them with bobby pins. Make sure to hide the pins as much as possible.

Step 4:

Take any loose strands of hair and twist them into a small bun at the nape of your neck. Secure it with bobby pins.

Step 5:

Spray your hair with hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips

- Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to help hide them.

- If you have layers in your hair, use a texturizing product to help create more volume and hold.

- Don't be afraid to add accessories to your updo, such as a headband or hair clips.

FAQ

Q: Will this updo work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this updo can work for all hair types, but it may require some modifications depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

Q: How long will this updo last?

A: This updo can last all day and night with the help of hairspray and bobby pins.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools, and try to limit heat styling as much as possible. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to fix your shoulder length hair in an updo that will have you feeling confident and stylish. Give it a try for your next event, and watch as heads turn in admiration.
