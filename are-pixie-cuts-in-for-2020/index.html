Are Pixie Cuts In For 2020

Written by Christine Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and daring hairstyle that screams confidence and independence, then a pixie cut might be perfect for you. Pixie cuts have been around for decades, and they are still a popular choice for women who want to stand out from the crowd. But are pixie cuts in for 2020? Let's find out in this blog post.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the head and styled in a way that creates a spiky or textured look. The length of a pixie cut can vary, but it usually falls between half an inch to three inches. Pixie cuts are usually low-maintenance and easy to style, making them a popular choice for women who lead busy lives.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

If you're interested in getting a pixie cut, here are the steps you can follow:

  1. Research different pixie cut styles and choose one that suits your face shape and hair type.
  2. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience with pixie cuts.
  3. Bring a picture of the pixie cut you want to your hairstylist.
  4. Communicate with your hairstylist about the length and style you want.
  5. Prepare for the cut by washing your hair and avoiding any styling products.
  6. Sit back and let your hairstylist work their magic!

Step by Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is easy and straightforward. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling gel or mousse to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten any unruly pieces.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to create texture and hold.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your cut looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Be confident and embrace your new look!

FAQ

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts look great on women with oval, heart-shaped, and square faces. If you have a round or long face, you may want to consider a different hairstyle.

Can I still style a pixie cut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add even more texture and volume to a pixie cut. Just make sure to use the right styling products and tools to enhance your curls.

Will a pixie cut make my hair look thinner?

Not necessarily. A pixie cut can actually make your hair look thicker and fuller, especially if you add layers and texture.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Even though pixie cuts are low-maintenance, it's still important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your style.

In conclusion, pixie cuts are still a popular choice for women in 2020 and beyond. Whether you want to make a bold statement or simply want a low-maintenance hairstyle, a pixie cut might be perfect for you. With the right cut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can rock a pixie cut with confidence and style.
