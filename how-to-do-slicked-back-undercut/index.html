How To Do Slicked Back Undercut A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and modern hairstyle, the slicked back undercut might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is a combination of a classic slicked back look with a trendy undercut. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by men of all ages and is suitable for both formal and casual occasions.

Description

The slicked back undercut is a hairstyle that features short sides and back with longer hair on top that is slicked back. The hair on top is usually styled with a pomade or wax to create a sleek and polished look. The undercut is created by shaving the sides and back of the head to a very short length.

This hairstyle is popular among men because it's easy to style and maintain. It's also versatile, as it can be worn with a variety of hair types and lengths. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, the slicked back undercut can work for you.

How to Do Slicked Back Undercut

To achieve a slicked back undercut, you'll need to have short hair on the sides and back of your head, with longer hair on top. You'll also need a good quality pomade or wax to style your hair. Follow these steps to create the perfect slicked back undercut:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Work the pomade or wax through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Make sure you distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Using a comb or your fingers, slick your hair back from your forehead to the crown of your head. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or kinks.
  5. Use a hair dryer on a cool setting to set your hair in place. You can also use a hairbrush to smooth out any bumps or kinks.
  6. Finish off your look with a little bit more pomade or wax to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Slicked Back Undercut

To keep your slicked back undercut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a good quality pomade or wax to style your hair.
  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length of your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair products and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back undercut:

Q: Is the slicked back undercut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the slicked back undercut can work for all hair types and textures.

Q: How often do I need to wash my hair with a slicked back undercut?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.

Q: Can I style my slicked back undercut with a hair dryer?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer on a cool setting to set your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide above, you can achieve a perfect slicked back undercut that will have you looking and feeling your best.
