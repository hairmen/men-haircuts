Short Layered Bob With Fringe Over 60 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a chic and easy-to-maintain hairstyle, a short layered bob with fringe might be just what you need. This classic haircut has been popular for years, but with some subtle tweaks and adjustments, it can be customized to flatter any face shape and hair type. In this guide, we'll give you all the information you need to know about the short layered bob with fringe over 60, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and more.

Description: What is a Short Layered Bob with Fringe?

A short layered bob with fringe is a haircut that features a chin-length bob with layers cut into the hair to create movement and texture. The fringe, also known as bangs, is cut to hang just above the eyebrows and can be styled straight or swept to the side. This haircut is versatile and flattering, making it a popular choice for women over 60 who want a low-maintenance but stylish look.

How to: Styling a Short Layered Bob with Fringe

Styling a short layered bob with fringe is simple and straightforward. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup and leave your hair feeling clean and fresh.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair, such as a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray. This will help add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair, lifting the roots to create volume and texture. As you blow dry, use the brush to create a slight curl at the ends of your hair for a soft and feminine look.

Step 4: Style your fringe

Once your hair is dry, style your fringe by either sweeping it to the side or straightening it with a flat iron. If you're going for a more casual look, you can leave your fringe natural and tousled.

Tips: Maintaining a Short Layered Bob with Fringe

To keep your short layered bob with fringe looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

Tip 1: Get regular trims

To maintain the shape of your haircut, it's important to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Tip 2: Use a heat protectant

If you're using heat styling tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Experiment with different styling products

There are a variety of styling products available that can help enhance the texture and volume of your hair. Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and styling needs.

FAQ: Short Layered Bob with Fringe Over 60

Q: Is a short layered bob with fringe a good choice for women over 60?

A: Yes, a short layered bob with fringe can be a great choice for women over 60. It's a low-maintenance and stylish haircut that can be customized to flatter any face shape and hair type.

Q: What face shape is best for a short layered bob with fringe?

A: A short layered bob with fringe looks great on a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I style my short layered bob with fringe differently?

A: Yes, there are many different ways you can style a short layered bob with fringe. You can try adding waves or curls for a more playful look, or straighten your hair for a sleek and sophisticated style.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Short Layered Bob with Fringe Healthy

To keep your hair healthy and vibrant, here are some hair care tips to follow:

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Tip 2: Avoid using too much heat

While it's tempting to use heat styling tools to achieve your desired look, try to limit their use as much as possible. Overuse of heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage and split ends.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

When spending time outdoors, make sure to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays. Wear a hat or scarf to shield your hair from the sun, or use a hair product that contains SPF.

Overall, a short layered bob with fringe over 60 is a classic and versatile haircut that can flatter any face shape and hair type. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish for years to come.
