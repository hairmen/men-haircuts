Does Lighter Hair Make You Look Thinner

Written by William Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to shed a few pounds without hitting the gym? You might want to consider changing your hair color. That's right - lighter hair can make you look thinner! In this article, we'll explore the science behind why lighter hair can give the illusion of a slimmer face and provide tips on how to achieve this look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Lighter Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Lighter Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Lightened Hair

Description

So, how exactly does lighter hair make you look thinner? The answer lies in how our eyes perceive color. Darker colors, including dark hair, tend to absorb light, making them appear more substantial. Lighter colors, on the other hand, reflect light, creating a more expansive and airy appearance. This effect can create the illusion of a slimmer face, especially around the cheeks and jawline.

It's important to note that changing your hair color alone won't necessarily make you look thinner. The key is to find a shade that complements your skin tone and face shape. Opting for a bright, unnatural-looking color can actually draw attention to imperfections and make your face appear wider.

How to Achieve Lighter Hair

The best way to achieve lighter hair is to visit a professional stylist. They can assess your hair type, skin tone, and face shape to recommend a shade that will complement your features. Depending on your starting color and desired outcome, your stylist may suggest a full dye job, highlights, or a balayage technique.

If you prefer to lighten your hair at home, there are plenty of DIY kits available at drugstores and beauty supply stores. Keep in mind that at-home hair dye can be tricky, so it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you decide to lighten your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a natural-looking result:

  1. Choose a shade that is one to two levels lighter than your current hair color.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and do a patch test 48 hours before applying the dye.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to prevent dye from staining your skin.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and apply the dye from the roots to the ends, starting at the back of your head.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time and rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  6. Apply the included conditioner to restore moisture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Lighter Hair

Once you've achieved your desired shade, it's crucial to maintain it properly to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip away the color and natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair and fade the color.

FAQ

Here are answers to some common questions about how lighter hair can affect your appearance:

Does lighter hair make your face look thinner or wider?

Lighter hair can make your face appear thinner, especially around the cheeks and jawline.

What is the best shade of blonde for a slimmer look?

The best shade of blonde depends on your skin tone and face shape. A professional stylist can help you choose a shade that complements your features.

Is it safe to lighten your hair at home?

Lightening your hair at home is generally safe, but it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

Hair Care for Lightened Hair

Lightened hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and fade the color.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or relaxers, which can damage your hair and fade the color.

By following these tips and choosing the right shade of hair color, you can achieve a slimmer-looking face without ever stepping foot in a gym.
