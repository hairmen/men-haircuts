Stunning Hairdos For Long Hair For Weddings

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a bride-to-be looking for the perfect hairstyle for your long hair on your wedding day? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing some stunning hairdos for long hair that will make you feel like a queen on your special day. From elegant updos to romantic loose curls, we have got you covered.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairdo for Your Wedding Day

Before we dive into the specific hairdos, let's talk about how to choose the perfect one for you. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider the Dress

Your hairstyle should complement your dress. If you have a dress with intricate details on the back, you might want to consider an updo to show it off. If your dress is more simple, loose curls or braids can add some texture and interest to your overall look.

Think About the Venue

The venue can also play a role in your hairstyle. If you're having an outdoor wedding in the middle of summer, you might want to choose a hairstyle that won't make you too hot. If you're having an indoor wedding in a grand ballroom, you might want to choose a hairstyle that's more formal.

Consider Your Face Shape

Finally, consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle. Certain styles can accentuate your features while others can detract from them. Experiment with different styles to see what looks best on you.

Step-by-Step Hairdos for Long Hair for Weddings

Elegant Updo

This elegant updo is perfect for a formal wedding. Here's how to achieve it:

1. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron.

2. Tease the crown of your hair to add some volume.

3. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

4. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.

5. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to soften the look.

Boho Braids

If you're having a boho or beach wedding, these loose braids are perfect. Here's how to achieve the look:

1. Part your hair down the middle.

2. Take a small section of hair from the front and braid it loosely.

3. Repeat on the other side.

4. Combine the two braids into a larger braid at the back of your head.

5. Pull out some pieces around your face to create a romantic, undone look.

Half-Up, Half-Down

This half-up, half-down style is perfect for a more casual wedding. Here's how to achieve it:

1. Curl your hair with a curling iron.

2. Take a small section of hair from the front and braid it.

3. Repeat on the other side.

4. Pull the braids back and secure them with a bobby pin.

5. Leave the rest of your hair down in loose curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wedding Day Hairdo

Now that you've chosen your perfect hairstyle, here are some tips for maintaining it throughout the day:

Use Lots of Hairspray

Make sure to use lots of hairspray to keep your hair in place. You can also bring a travel-sized hairspray with you to touch up throughout the day.

Don't Overdo It with the Accessories

Accessorizing is a great way to add some interest to your hairstyle, but don't overdo it. Choose one or two pieces that complement your dress and hairstyle.

Have a Touch-Up Kit Handy

Have a touch-up kit handy with extra bobby pins, hairspray, and a comb. This way, you can fix any flyaways or loose pieces throughout the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Far in Advance Should I Choose My Wedding Day Hairstyle?

You should choose your wedding day hairstyle at least a few months in advance. This will give you enough time to experiment with different styles and find the perfect one for you.

Should I Get a Haircut Before My Wedding?

If you want to get a haircut before your wedding, make sure to do it at least a few weeks in advance. This will give your hair enough time to settle and for you to get used to your new length.

What if I Have Thin Hair?

If you have thin hair, don't worry! There are lots of hairstyles that can add volume and texture to your hair. Talk to your hairstylist about your concerns and they can recommend a style that will work for you.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips for long hair to keep it healthy and beautiful:

Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these hairdos for long hair for weddings and hair care tips, you're sure to have a stunning hairstyle on your special day. Congratulations on your upcoming wedding!
