How To Style Long Hair For Round Face Tips And Tricks

Do you have long hair and a round face? Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing various tips and tricks to help you style your long hair in a way that complements your round face.

How to Style Long Hair for Round Face

When it comes to styling long hair for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved through various hairstyles and techniques, such as:

Layered Haircuts:

Layered haircuts are a great way to add texture and dimension to your long hair. They help to create the illusion of length and angles by framing your face and drawing attention away from your cheeks. Opt for long, subtle layers instead of short, choppy ones to avoid adding volume to your face.

Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs are another great way to add angles to your face. They help to create a diagonal line that draws the eye away from the roundness of your face. Opt for long, wispy bangs that blend seamlessly into your layers for a more natural look.

Face-Framing Layers:

Face-framing layers are layers that are cut around your face to create a flattering frame. They help to elongate your face and add angles to your cheeks. Opt for long, soft layers that blend seamlessly into your hair for a more natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your long hair for a round face:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage. This is especially important if you plan on using heat tools to style your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a sleek look. Use the brush to pull your hair away from your roots to create lift and volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using one of the techniques mentioned above, such as layered haircuts or side-swept bangs. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and create the desired look. Finish off with a hairspray or hair oil to tame any flyaways and add shine.

Tips for Styling Long Hair for Round Face

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long hair for a round face:

  • Opt for hairstyles that elongate your face and add angles to your cheeks.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face, such as blunt cuts and short layers.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Use heat tools sparingly to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face, such as blunt cuts and short layers. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

What hairstyles should I opt for if I have a round face?

You should opt for hairstyles that elongate your face and add angles to your cheeks. Layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and face-framing layers are all great options.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your long hair in a way that flatters your round face. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find the ones that work best for you. Happy styling!
