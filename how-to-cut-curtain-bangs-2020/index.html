How To Cut Curtain Bangs 2020 A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 07, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try out in 2023? Consider cutting curtain bangs! This style has been popular for years and continues to be a trendy choice for many. In this blog post, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut curtain bangs based on the 2020 trend. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted down the middle, creating a curtain-like effect. They can be cut at different lengths and widths to suit your face shape and personal style. This style is versatile and can be worn with various hair lengths and textures.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs 2020: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before beginning the cutting process. This will help you to see the natural fall of your hair and make it easier to cut the bangs evenly.

Step 2: Section off your hair

Part your hair down the middle and separate the front section, which will become your curtain bangs. Use clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Determine the length of your bangs

Decide how long you want your curtain bangs to be. It's best to start with longer bangs and cut them gradually, as you can always cut more later.

Step 4: Cut the bangs

Hold your scissors vertically and cut into the hair at a slight angle, creating a soft, feathered effect. Cut small sections at a time and check the length frequently to ensure you're happy with the result.

Step 5: Blend the bangs

Use a comb to blend the bangs into the rest of your hair, creating a seamless transition. This will also help to remove any harsh lines from the cutting process.

Step 6: Style your bangs

Style your curtain bangs by parting them down the middle and tucking them behind your ears. You can also add some texture by using a curling iron or straightener.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs

- Start with longer bangs and cut gradually
- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
- Cut small sections at a time
- Check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much
- Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair for a seamless look

FAQs About Curtain Bangs

Q: Can curtain bangs be worn with any hair length?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be worn with any hair length. They can be cut to suit your face shape and personal style.

Q: How often do curtain bangs need to be trimmed?

A: Curtain bangs will need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Q: Can I cut curtain bangs myself?

A: While it's possible to cut curtain bangs yourself, it's best to have them cut by a professional stylist to ensure a clean and even cut.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can cause the bangs to become greasy and lose their shape. Regular trims will also help to maintain the length and shape of your curtain bangs.

Cutting curtain bangs is a simple process that can be done by a professional stylist or at home. Use our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the perfect look for you. Happy cutting!
