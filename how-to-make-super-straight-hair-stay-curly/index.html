How To Make Super Straight Hair Stay Curly

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours curling your hair only to have it fall flat after a few hours? If you have super straight hair, it can be a challenge to keep curls in place. But don't worry, there are ways to make your curls last longer. In this article, we'll show you how to make super straight hair stay curly.

  2. Step by Step

How to Make Super Straight Hair Stay Curly

The key to making super straight hair stay curly is to prep your hair properly before styling. Here's what you need to do:

1. Wash your hair

Start with clean hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using products that weigh down your hair, as this will make it harder to hold curls.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Before styling, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent damage from heat styling tools and keep your hair healthy.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This will help add volume to your hair, which will make it easier to hold curls. Make sure your hair is completely dry before moving on to the next step.

4. Use a curling iron

Use a curling iron with a barrel size that is suitable for the type of curls you want. For loose waves, use a larger barrel. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until you've curled all of your hair.

5. Set your curls

Once you've curled your hair, let it cool down for a few minutes. This will help set the curls in place. Then, use a light hold hairspray to hold the curls. Don't use too much hairspray, as this will weigh down your hair and make it harder to hold curls.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make super straight hair stay curly:

  1. Wash your hair with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush.
  4. Use a curling iron to curl your hair.
  5. Let your hair cool down for a few minutes.
  6. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make super straight hair stay curly:

  • Use a high-quality curling iron.
  • Curl small sections of hair at a time.
  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't use too much hairspray.
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes to achieve different types of curls.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The duration of your curls depends on several factors, such as the type of curls you've created, the products you've used, and the weather conditions. Generally, you can expect your curls to last for a few hours to a day.

Q: Can I use rollers to curl my hair?

A: Yes, you can use rollers to curl your hair. This method is gentler on your hair and can create long-lasting curls. However, it takes longer to achieve the desired results.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and make your curls last longer, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a suitable shampoo and conditioner for your hair type.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can make super straight hair stay curly for longer. With a little practice, you'll be able to create beautiful, long-lasting curls that you can show off all day long.
