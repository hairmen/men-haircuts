Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2021

Written by Frank Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for long hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and double chin? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2021. We'll give you some tips on how to style your hair and take care of it. So, let's get started!

Description

Having a round face and double chin can be challenging when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. But with the right haircut, you can create an illusion of a slimmer face and neck. Long hairstyles can be perfect for this purpose. They create a vertical line that elongates your face and neck, making them look slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle?

Choosing the right long hairstyle for your round face and double chin can be tricky. You need to consider the length, texture, and thickness of your hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect long hairstyle:

1. Length

Long hair can be flattering for round faces and double chins, but you need to find the right length. Hair that falls above your shoulders can make your face look wider. So, it's best to keep your hair longer than your shoulders. You can try a layered haircut to add more dimension and texture to your hair.

2. Texture

Adding texture to your hair can make it look more voluminous and less flat. You can try curls or waves to add more body to your hair. A messy bun or a messy braid can also be a great option for a casual look.

3. Thickness

If you have thin hair, you can try a blunt haircut to add more volume. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can try a layered haircut to reduce the bulkiness.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Hair

Now that you have chosen the right long hairstyle, let's talk about how to style it. Here is a step by step guide:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will protect your hair from damage caused by heat.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry. This will add volume and body to your hair.

4. Curl or straighten your hair

Depending on the style you want to achieve, you can use a curling iron or a straightener to style your hair. Remember to apply a hairspray to hold the style in place.

5. Finish with a hair serum

To add shine and reduce frizz, apply a hair serum to your hair. This will give your hair a healthy and glossy look.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

To keep your long hair healthy and shiny, here are some tips:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks will prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair.

FAQ

Q1. What hairstyle is best for a fat face?

A long layered haircut is best for a fat face as it creates an illusion of a slimmer face and neck.

Q2. How can I make my double chin look smaller?

Wearing the right hairstyle can make your double chin look smaller. Long hairstyles that create a vertical line can elongate your face and neck, making them look slimmer.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, so use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can dry it out, so wash it every 2-3 days.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

In conclusion, long hairstyles can be perfect for fat faces and double chins. By choosing the right length, texture, and thickness of your hair, you can create an illusion of a slimmer face and neck. With these tips and tricks, you can style your hair and take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny.
