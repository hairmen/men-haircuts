How To Deal With Really Thick Hair

Written by Frank Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Having thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can make your hair look voluminous and full of life. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you don't know how to care for it properly. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to deal with really thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in dealing with thick hair is to figure out what kind of hair you have. Is it curly, straight, or wavy? This will determine what kind of products and techniques you should be using. Once you have a good understanding of your hair type, you can start to experiment with different styles and products.

One of the best ways to deal with thick hair is to keep it well-moisturized. This will help to reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable. Use a deep conditioner once a week to give your hair a boost of hydration. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and make it easier to comb through.

Products to Use

When it comes to products, there are a few key items that you should have in your arsenal:

  • Shampoo and conditioner: Look for products that are specifically designed for thick hair. These will help to hydrate your hair and make it more manageable.
  • Deep conditioner: Use a deep conditioner once a week to give your hair a boost of hydration.
  • Leave-in conditioner: Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and make it easier to comb through.
  • Hair oil: Use a hair oil to help smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to deal with really thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for thick hair.
  2. Use a deep conditioner once a week to give your hair a boost of hydration.
  3. Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and make it easier to comb through.
  4. Use a hair oil to help smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  6. If you want to straighten your hair, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.
  7. If you want to curl your hair, use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to deal with really thick hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Is it better to wash thick hair every day or every other day?

A: It's generally better to wash thick hair every other day, as washing it too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to breakage.

Q: How can I make my thick hair look thinner?

A: You can make your thick hair look thinner by getting layers cut into it, which will help to remove some of the bulk. You can also use a flat iron to straighten your hair, which can help to make it look less voluminous.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and manageable. In addition to using the right products, you should also be gentle when combing and styling your hair. Avoid using hot tools too often, and consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Dealing with really thick hair can be challenging, but with the right products, techniques, and care, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable hairstyle. Whether you prefer to wear your hair straight or curly, there are plenty of options available to help you look your best.
