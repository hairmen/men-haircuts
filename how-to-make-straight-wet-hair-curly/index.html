How To Make Straight Wet Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to try something new? Why not try making your wet hair curly? In this article, we'll show you how to make straight wet hair curly in just a few easy steps. Whether you have naturally straight or wavy hair, we'll help you achieve the perfect curls you've always wanted. Here's a quick table of content so you can jump to the section you need:

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Straight Wet Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Straight Wet Hair Curly
  • FAQs

How to Make Straight Wet Hair Curly

Before we begin, let's talk about the tools you'll need to make your hair curly. Here's a list of what you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Curling cream or mousse
  • Diffuser attachment for your hair dryer

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your straight wet hair curly:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply curling cream or mousse to your wet hair. Use a generous amount to ensure that your curls will hold.
  3. Comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Use hair clips to section your hair into small pieces. This will make it easier to curl your hair.
  5. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat.
  6. Take a small section of your hair and place it in the diffuser.
  7. Hold the diffuser close to your head and move it around in a circular motion. This will help to create the curls.
  8. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.
  9. Once your hair is completely dry, use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.
  10. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Making Straight Wet Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you make your straight wet hair curly:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated.
  • Apply curling cream or mousse to your hair while it's still wet to ensure that your curls hold their shape.
  • Section your hair into small pieces to make it easier to curl.
  • Use a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer to create defined curls.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place without making them stiff.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair curly without heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair curly without heat by using techniques like braiding, twisting, or using flexi rods. However, these methods may take longer to achieve the desired curls.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls should last for several hours to a day, depending on your hair type and the products you use. To prolong the life of your curls, avoid touching your hair too much and use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Q: Can I make my hair curly if it's already dry?

A: It's best to make your hair curly when it's wet to ensure that the curls hold their shape. However, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls on dry hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and defined. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Now that you know how to make straight wet hair curly, it's time to try it out for yourself! Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips to achieve the perfect curls. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at making your hair curly in no time.
