How To Wear Your Hair After 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Oct 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering how to wear your hair after 50? As we age, our hair changes in texture and thickness, and it's important to find a style that flatters our face shape and enhances our natural beauty. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for styling your hair after 50, from easy updos to chic short cuts. Let's get started!

How to Wear Your Hair After 50: The Basics

Before we dive into specific styles and tips, let's cover some basic guidelines for styling your hair after 50:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Opt for low-maintenance styles that require minimal styling
  • Experiment with hair accessories, such as headbands and clips
  • Invest in quality hair products that nourish and protect your hair

How to Wear Your Hair After 50: Step by Step

Now that we've covered the basics, let's walk through some step-by-step tutorials for styling your hair after 50:

1. Easy Updo

To create an easy updo, start by gathering your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins. For added volume and texture, tease the crown of your head with a comb or brush. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Classic Bob

A classic bob is a timeless hairstyle that flatters women of all ages. To create a bob, ask your stylist to cut your hair to chin length or just above the shoulders. You can opt for a blunt cut or add layers for added movement and texture. Style with a flat iron or curling iron to add body and volume.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a daring and stylish option for women over 50. To create a pixie cut, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving a longer layer on top. Style with a texturizing product to add volume and definition to your hair.

How to Wear Your Hair After 50: Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling your hair after 50:

  • Embrace your natural hair color and texture
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Invest in a high-quality blow dryer and flat iron to style your hair at home
  • Try a new hair color or highlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Experiment with different partings and angles to find a style that flatters your face shape

How to Wear Your Hair After 50: FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling your hair after 50:

1. Is it okay to wear long hair after 50?

Yes, it's absolutely okay to wear long hair after 50! However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Long hair can be more difficult to maintain and style, so opt for low-maintenance styles that require minimal effort.

2. How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of haircuts can vary depending on your hair texture and thickness.

3. Can I still color my hair after 50?

Yes, you can still color your hair after 50! However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Consult with your stylist to find the perfect shade for you.

How to Wear Your Hair After 50: Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy locks and prevent damage. Here are some tips for hair care after 50:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends

Now that you know how to wear your hair after 50, experiment with different styles and find the perfect look for you. Remember, the key is to embrace your natural beauty and choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
