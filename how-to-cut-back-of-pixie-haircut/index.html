How To Cut Back Of Pixie Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a pixie haircut, you know how important it is to maintain its shape and style. Cutting the back of your pixie can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. In this blog post, we'll show you how to cut the back of your pixie haircut with ease.

Description

Pixie haircuts are short, trendy, and easy to maintain. They're perfect for women who want to make a bold statement with their hair. The back of a pixie haircut is often the trickiest part to cut because it's hard to see and reach. However, with a few simple steps, you can cut the back of your pixie with confidence.

How to

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the shape of your haircut and ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Section your hair

Use the comb to create a horizontal parting across the back of your head, from ear to ear. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section.

Step 4: Cut the bottom section

Take a small section of hair from the bottom, comb it straight down, and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process, working your way up to the top section of hair.

Step 5: Cut the top section

Unclip the top section of your hair and repeat the same process, cutting small sections of hair to the desired length. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even.

Step 6: Blend the layers

To create a seamless look, use the scissors to blend the layers together. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut into the ends to create soft, feathered layers.

Step 7: Finishing touches

Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head one last time. Make any final adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time for more control
  • Take breaks to rest your hands and avoid fatigue
  • Use a light touch when cutting to avoid over-cutting
  • If you're unsure, start with a longer length and gradually cut more

FAQ

How often should I cut the back of my pixie haircut?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Can I cut the back of my pixie haircut myself?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut the back of your pixie at home. However, if you're unsure or have a more complicated haircut, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie haircut looking its best, use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and let it air-dry whenever possible. Regular trims will also help maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Conclusion

Cutting the back of your pixie haircut doesn't have to be difficult. With the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. Remember to take your time, cut small sections of hair at a time, and use a light touch. And if you're unsure, don't hesitate to visit a professional stylist.
