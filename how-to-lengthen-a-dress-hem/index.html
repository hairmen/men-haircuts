How To Lengthen A Dress Hem

Written by Luffy Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old dresses because they are too short? Do you have a dress that you love but wish it was a bit longer? Don't worry, you don't have to give up on your favorite dresses just yet. In this post, we'll show you how to lengthen a dress hem and give your wardrobe a new life.

Description

Lengthening a dress hem is a simple process that can be done in a few easy steps. All you need are some basic sewing skills, a few materials, and a bit of patience. By following our guide, you'll be able to transform your dresses and make them longer and more wearable.

How to Lengthen a Dress Hem

Step 1: Choose the Right Fabric

The first step in lengthening a dress hem is to choose the right fabric. You want a fabric that matches the dress and blends in seamlessly. Look for a fabric that is similar in weight, texture, and color to the original dress. If you can't find an exact match, choose a fabric that is as close as possible.

Step 2: Measure and Cut the New Hemline

Measure the length you want to add to the dress and mark it with a pin or a piece of chalk. Add an extra inch to this measurement to allow for hemming. Cut the excess fabric at the bottom of the dress, following the new hemline you marked.

Step 3: Hem the New Fabric

Next, hem the new fabric you cut to the length you need. Fold the bottom edge of the fabric up by 1/4 inch and then another 1/4 inch, pin it in place, and sew it using a straight stitch. This will prevent the fabric from fraying and give it a clean finish.

Step 4: Pin the New Fabric to the Dress

Pin the new fabric to the bottom of the dress, right sides together. Make sure the hemline matches up and the fabric is evenly distributed. Pin the fabric in place every few inches to keep it secure.

Step 5: Sew the New Fabric to the Dress

Using a straight stitch, sew the new fabric to the bottom of the dress. Remove the pins as you go. Once you've sewn all the way around the dress, trim any excess threads and turn the dress right side out.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you lengthen a dress hem:

  • Choose a fabric that matches the dress as closely as possible.
  • Measure carefully to make sure you get the length right.
  • Pin the fabric in place before sewing to avoid any shifting.
  • Use a straight stitch for a clean and secure finish.

FAQ

Can you lengthen a dress hem without sewing?

It is possible to lengthen a dress hem without sewing, but it won't be as secure or long-lasting as sewing. You can use fabric glue or iron-on hem tape to attach a new piece of fabric to the bottom of the dress. However, this method is not recommended for heavy or stretchy fabrics.

How much fabric do I need to lengthen a dress hem?

The amount of fabric you need depends on how much you want to add to the dress. Measure the length you want to add, add an extra inch for hemming, and purchase enough fabric to cover that length.

Hair Care

While you're working on lengthening your dress hem, don't forget about your hair. Keep it healthy and strong by using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can give your dresses a new lease on life and create a wardrobe that works for you. Happy sewing!
