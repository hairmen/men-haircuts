Cute Ponytail Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length hair, you are in luck because you can create a variety of cute ponytail hairstyles. Ponytails are not only easy to create, but they are also versatile and can be worn for any occasion. In this article, we will show you some of the best cute ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair that you can try out.

Description

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that has been around for centuries. They are perfect for those days when you don't have much time to spend on your hair, but still want to look stylish. With medium length hair, you can create different types of ponytails that are perfect for any occasion.

How to Create Cute Ponytail Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Creating cute ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair is easy. All you need is a hair tie, a brush, and some bobby pins. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. You can leave your ponytail as it is or add some volume by teasing the crown of your head.
  4. If you want to add some extra flair to your ponytail, you can braid a section of your hair and wrap it around the hair tie.
  5. Secure the braid with bobby pins and you're done!

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create some of the cutest ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair:

The Messy Ponytail

  1. Start by creating a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Gently tug on the hair above the hair tie to create some volume.
  3. Use your fingers to create a messy look by pulling out some strands of hair.
  4. Secure any loose strands with bobby pins.

The Twisted Ponytail

  1. Create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Split the hair above the hair tie into two sections.
  3. Twist one section over the other and secure with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.

The Braided Ponytail

  1. Create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Braid a section of hair on one side of your head.
  3. Wrap the braid around the hair tie and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side.

Tips for Creating Cute Ponytail Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating cute ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to create more volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  • Experiment with different types of braids to add some texture to your ponytail.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cute ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair:

Can I wear a ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, you can! A sleek and polished ponytail can be a great option for a formal event.

How do I make my ponytail look fuller?

You can use a volumizing spray or mousse before creating your ponytail to add more volume. You can also tease the hair at the crown of your head.

How do I keep my ponytail from slipping?

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands and make sure your hair tie is tight enough.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

To keep your medium length hair healthy and strong, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

With these cute ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair, you can easily switch up your look in no time. Whether you're going to work, school, or a special event, there's a ponytail hairstyle that's perfect for you. So go ahead and try out these hairstyles and don't forget to share your favorite one with us!
