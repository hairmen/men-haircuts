FAQ

What causes frizzy hair?

Frizzy hair is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair. When the hair is dry, it tends to absorb moisture from the air, which causes it to swell and become frizzy. Other factors that can contribute to frizzy hair include using hot tools too often, using harsh hair care products, and genetics.

How do I know if I have fine frizzy wavy hair?

Fine frizzy wavy hair is characterized by hair that is thin, prone to frizz, and has a natural wave or curl to it. If you have trouble getting your hair to hold a curl or if it tends to get frizzy, you likely have fine frizzy wavy hair.

What hair care products should I avoid if I have fine frizzy wavy hair?

If you have fine frizzy wavy hair, you should avoid using hair care products that contain harsh chemicals such as sulfates and alcohol, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause more frizz. You should also avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
