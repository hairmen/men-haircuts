How To Contour A Square Face Shape A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Contouring has become an essential part of makeup routines, especially for those with a square face shape. If you have a square face, you may want to soften your features and make them appear more oval. In this article, we'll walk you through the step-by-step process of contouring a square face shape. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Contour a Square Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Contouring a Square Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Square Face Shapes

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. Contouring can help soften the angles of the face and create a more oval shape. By using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight, you can create a more sculpted and defined look.

How to Contour a Square Face Shape

Before we get started, it's essential to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a contour brush, a highlighter brush, a contour powder or cream, and a highlighter powder or cream. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Face

Start by washing your face and applying your regular moisturizer. Then, apply a primer to help your makeup last longer.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply your foundation as you normally would. Make sure to blend it well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Contour Your Cheeks

Using your contour brush, apply contour powder or cream to the hollows of your cheeks. To find the hollows of your cheeks, suck in your cheeks and apply the contour product where the natural shadow appears. Blend the product well with your brush.

Step 4: Contour Your Jawline

Apply contour powder or cream to your jawline to create a more defined look. Start at the center of your chin and blend the product upwards towards your ears.

Step 5: Contour Your Forehead

Apply contour powder or cream to the sides of your forehead, starting at your temples and blending towards the center of your forehead.

Step 6: Highlight Your Cheekbones

Using your highlighter brush, apply highlighter powder or cream to the tops of your cheekbones. This will help bring them forward and create a more defined look.

Step 7: Highlight Your Nose

Apply highlighter powder or cream to the bridge of your nose to make it appear more slender.

Step 8: Highlight Your Forehead

Apply highlighter powder or cream to the center of your forehead to make it appear more prominent.

Step 9: Blend, Blend, Blend

Use a clean brush or a beauty sponge to blend all of the products together. Make sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 10: Set Your Makeup

Finish off your look by setting your makeup with a setting spray or powder.

Tips for Contouring a Square Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind while contouring a square face shape:

  • Use a light hand when applying contour products to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Blend well to create a natural-looking finish.
  • Choose a contour shade that is only a few shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Choose a highlighter shade that is only a few shades lighter than your skin tone.
  • Apply your contour and highlight products in natural light to avoid any errors.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about contouring a square face shape:

What is the best contour product for a square face shape?

The best contour product for a square face shape is a powder or cream that is only a few shades darker than your skin tone. Avoid using products that are too dark, as they can create a harsh and unnatural look.

What is the best highlighter for a square face shape?

The best highlighter for a square face shape is a powder or cream that is only a few shades lighter than your skin tone. Avoid using products that are too light, as they can create a harsh and unnatural look.

How often should I contour my square face?

You can contour your square face as often as you like. However, it's essential to give your skin a break from makeup occasionally to allow it to breathe.

Hair Care for Square Face Shapes

If you have a square face shape, you may want to consider a hairstyle that softens your features. Here are some hairstyles that can help:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Curly bob
  • Asymmetrical bob

By following these contouring tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can create a more defined and sculpted look for your square face shape.
