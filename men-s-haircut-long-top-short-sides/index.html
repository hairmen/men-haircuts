Men s Haircut Long Top Short Sides The Latest Trend For 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new look for 2023? The men's haircut with a long top and short sides is a popular trend that's been around for a while and shows no sign of slowing down. This versatile style can work for any hair type and face shape, making it a great option for anyone who wants to switch up their look. In this article, we'll go over how to get the perfect men's haircut long top short sides, as well as some tips for maintenance and care.

How to Get the Perfect Men's Haircut Long Top Short Sides

If you're looking to get this trendy haircut, it's important to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect men's haircut long top short sides:

Step 1: Choose your length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top and how short you want it on the sides. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Be sure to communicate your desired length to your barber or hairstylist so they can create the perfect look for you.

Step 2: Section off the hair

Your barber or hairstylist will likely section off your hair to ensure an even cut. They may use clippers or scissors to trim the sides and back of your head while leaving the top longer.

Step 3: Blend the hair

To ensure a natural-looking transition between the longer top and shorter sides, your barber or hairstylist will blend the hair using scissors or clippers. This will create a seamless look that's both stylish and versatile.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your hair has been cut and blended, it's time to style it. You can use a variety of products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to create the perfect look. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Men's Haircut Long Top Short Sides

Now that you've got the perfect men's haircut long top short sides, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

TIP 1: Get regular trims

Even though you may want to grow out your hair, regular trims are important to maintain the shape of your haircut. Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a quick trim to keep your haircut looking fresh.

TIP 2: Use the right products

Using the right hair products can make all the difference in how your haircut looks and feels. Experiment with different products to find ones that work well for your hair type and desired style.

TIP 3: Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly is important for maintaining healthy hair and scalp. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What face shapes work best with the men's haircut long top short sides?

A: This haircut can work for any face shape, but it's especially flattering for those with oval, round, or square faces.

Q2: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Absolutely! The men's haircut long top short sides is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on the occasion.

Q3: Can I still get this haircut if I have curly or wavy hair?

A: Yes! This haircut can work for any hair type, including curly and wavy hair. Your barber or hairstylist can help you achieve the perfect look for your hair type.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Long Top Short Sides

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy hair and a great haircut. Here are some tips for hair care:

TIP 1: Use a heat protectant

If you use heat tools to style your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

TIP 2: Avoid over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage. Try to wash your hair every other day, or every two days if possible.

TIP 3: Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when spending time outdoors.

Overall, the men's haircut long top short sides is a versatile and stylish trend that's perfect for anyone looking to switch up their look in 2023. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, this haircut can work for you. Be sure to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the perfect look, and don't forget to maintain your haircut with regular trims and proper hair care.
