Mens Hairstyles For Medium Length Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this blog post, we will be discussing some of the best mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair. Wavy hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Whether you want a messy, relaxed look, or a sleek, sophisticated style, there is a hairstyle that will work for you. Let's dive in!

Description

Medium length wavy hair is hair that falls between the chin and the shoulders and has a natural wave or curl to it. This type of hair can be challenging to style, as it can become frizzy or unruly if not properly cared for. However, with the right products and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish and flattering hairstyles.

How to Style Medium Length Wavy Hair

The key to styling medium length wavy hair is to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  • Apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl cream, to enhance your natural wave or curl
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz
  • Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place

Step by Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here is a step by step guide to styling medium length wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl cream
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on low heat
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose waves or curls
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray or pomade

Tips for Styling Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your medium length wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush, such as a boar bristle brush, to help distribute natural oils and prevent frizz
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair:

What are some popular mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair?

Some popular mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair include the messy textured look, the slicked back style, and the side swept fringe.

What products should I use to style my medium length wavy hair?

You should use a styling product, such as mousse or curl cream, to enhance your natural wave or curl. You can also use a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

How often should I wash my medium length wavy hair?

You should wash your medium length wavy hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

Conclusion

Medium length wavy hair is a versatile and stylish hair type that can be styled in many different ways. By using the right products and techniques, you can create a variety of flattering hairstyles that will make you look and feel your best. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
