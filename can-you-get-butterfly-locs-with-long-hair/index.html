Can You Get Butterfly Locs with Long Hair? | Tips, How to, FAQ and Hair Care

If you have long hair and are considering getting butterfly locs, you may be wondering if it's possible. The good news is that yes, you can get butterfly locs with long hair! In this article, we will discuss how to get butterfly locs with long hair, step by step instructions, tips, FAQ and hair care.

Description

Butterfly locs are a popular protective style that mimics the look of dreadlocks. It's a bohemian style that features loose, wavy curls at the ends of the locs. Butterfly locs are created by wrapping synthetic hair around your natural hair, giving it a thick and full look. This style is perfect for those who want to give their hair a break from daily styling and manipulation.

How to Get Butterfly Locs with Long Hair

Step 1: Choose Your Synthetic Hair

The first step in getting butterfly locs is choosing the right synthetic hair. Make sure to choose a hair texture that matches your natural hair. You can choose from different colors and lengths to customize your look.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before getting butterfly locs, it's essential to wash and condition your hair. This ensures that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into small, even parts. This step is especially important if you have long hair to ensure that the locs are uniform in size and shape.

Step 4: Wrap the Synthetic Hair Around Your Natural Hair

Take a small section of synthetic hair and wrap it around your natural hair. Use a crochet hook to secure the hair in place. Repeat this step until all sections of your hair are wrapped in the synthetic hair.

Step 5: Style Your Butterfly Locs

Once your hair is wrapped in the synthetic hair, it's time to style your butterfly locs. You can leave the locs loose and wavy or use a curling wand to create more defined curls.

Tips for Getting Butterfly Locs with Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when getting butterfly locs with long hair:

  • Choose synthetic hair that matches your natural hair texture
  • Section your hair into small, even parts to ensure uniformity
  • Consider using a crochet hook to secure the synthetic hair in place
  • Use a curling wand to create defined curls
  • Wrap the synthetic hair tightly around your natural hair to prevent slipping or sliding

FAQ

Can You Get Butterfly Locs with Long Hair?

Yes, you can get butterfly locs with long hair. Just make sure to section your hair into small, even parts to ensure uniformity and choose a synthetic hair texture that matches your natural hair.

How Long Do Butterfly Locs Last?

Butterfly locs can last up to 6-8 weeks with proper care. To make them last longer, avoid excessive styling and manipulation and keep your scalp clean and moisturized.

Hair Care for Butterfly Locs with Long Hair

Proper hair care is essential to keep your butterfly locs looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips to care for your butterfly locs with long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your locs
  • Cover your hair with a satin or silk scarf at night to prevent tangling and breakage
