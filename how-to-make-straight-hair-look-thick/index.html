How To Make Straight Hair Look Thick Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having straight hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it's easy to manage and style, it can also look flat and lifeless. If you're looking to add volume and thickness to your straight hair, there are a few simple tips and tricks you can try. In this article, we'll share some easy ways on how to make straight hair look thick.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Straight Hair Look Thick
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

How to Make Straight Hair Look Thick

If you have straight hair, you might think that it's impossible to make it look thick and voluminous. However, there are several ways to add body and volume to your hair.

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Start with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are formulated to add body and thickness to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One easy way to add volume to your straight hair is to blow dry it upside down. This technique allows the roots of your hair to lift off your scalp, creating a fuller look. Use a round brush to help lift and curl the ends of your hair.

3. Use a Volumizing Spray or Mousse

Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your hair before blow drying. These products can help add body and texture to your hair. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

4. Tease Your Hair

If you want even more volume, try teasing your hair. Use a fine-tooth comb to tease the roots of your hair, then smooth the top layer with a brush. This technique can create a more dramatic, voluminous look.

5. Add Layers to Your Haircut

If you're due for a haircut, consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can help create movement and texture, which can make your hair appear thicker. Talk to your stylist about the best layering technique for your hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make straight hair look thick:

  1. Start with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush.
  4. Tease your hair at the roots for extra volume.
  5. Smooth the top layer of your hair with a brush.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips to Maintain Thick Hair

Once you've achieved the thick hair look, you'll want to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can I make my straight hair look thick without using heat styling tools?

Yes, you can. Try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a volumizing spray or mousse, and teasing your hair at the roots to add volume. You can also consider adding layers to your haircut to create movement and texture.

What is the best haircut for straight hair?

The best haircut for straight hair depends on your face shape and hair type. However, adding layers to your hair can help create volume and texture, which can make your hair appear thicker.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, you'll want to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this can prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry it out.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your straight hair look thick and voluminous. Experiment with different styling techniques and hair products to find what works best for you. With a little effort and the right products, you can achieve the thick hair look you've always wanted.
