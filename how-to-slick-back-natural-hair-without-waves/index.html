How to Slick Back Natural Hair Without Waves

Slicking back natural hair without waves requires some effort and patience. The process involves using hair products and a comb to create a smooth and sleek look. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Before slicking back your hair, it's essential to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo to cleanse your hair and scalp thoroughly. Make sure to remove any product buildup or dirt that can make your hair look dull and lifeless.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

After shampooing, apply a moisturizing conditioner to your hair. Leave it on for a few minutes to allow it to penetrate the hair shaft and provide hydration. Rinse thoroughly with warm water to remove all the conditioner.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide extra moisture and prevent breakage. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Apply a Styling Gel

To slick back your hair, you need a strong-hold styling gel. Apply a small amount of gel to your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Use a comb to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back starting from the hairline. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction of the back of your head to create a smooth and sleek look. Repeat the combing process until your hair is completely slicked back.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you need a visual guide to slick back your natural hair without waves, here are the detailed instructions:

  1. Start with clean and conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to provide extra moisture.
  3. Apply a strong-hold styling gel to your hair.
  4. Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back starting from the hairline.
  5. Repeat the combing process until your hair is completely slicked back.
  6. Smooth out any bumps or frizz using your fingers or a brush.
  7. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair.
  8. Set your hair with a hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Your Natural Hair

Here are some tips to make the process of slicking back your natural hair without waves easier:

  • Start with clean and conditioned hair to prevent product buildup.
  • Use a strong-hold styling gel to keep your hair in place.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a smooth and sleek look.
  • Smooth out any bumps or frizz using your fingers or a brush.
  • Set your hair with a hairspray to keep it in place.
  • Don't use too much product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQs

Can I slick back my natural hair without using gel?

Yes, you can use a pomade or a cream to slick back your natural hair without using gel. Pomades and creams provide a softer hold and can be easier to apply than gel.

Can I slick back my natural hair without using a comb?

No, using a comb is essential to create a smooth and sleek look when slicking back your natural hair. A comb helps to distribute the product evenly and create a uniform texture.

How often should I slick back my natural hair?

You can slick back your natural hair as often as you like, but it's essential to give your hair a break in between to prevent breakage and damage.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Hair

When you slick back your natural hair, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product to prevent buildup.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline.
  • Use a silk scarf or pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

Slicking back natural hair without waves can be a bit challenging, but with the right products and technique, you can achieve a smooth and sleek look. Follow the steps and tips in this article to get the perfect slicked-back hairstyle for your natural hair.
