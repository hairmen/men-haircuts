How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Boy Without Cutting It

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and try something new, but don't want to commit to a drastic chop, then making your hair look like a boy's might be the perfect solution for you. This look can be edgy, chic, and versatile, and it's easy to achieve without sacrificing your length. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair look like a boy without cutting it, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Look Like a Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Boyish Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Your Boyish Mane

How to Make Your Hair Look Like a Boy

Making your hair look like a boy's can be achieved through a few different techniques. Here are some key elements to consider:

  • Shorter length on the sides and back
  • Longer length on top
  • Textured or messy styling
  • Side-swept bangs or a fringe
  • Minimal or no layers

You don't have to follow all of these guidelines to achieve a boyish look, but incorporating some of these elements can help you achieve the aesthetic you're going for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair look like a boy's:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and lift to your locks.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a texturizing product, like a sea salt spray or styling cream, to add texture and hold.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and waves to your hair. You can also create messy, tousled curls by twisting small sections of hair around your fingers and pinning them to your scalp until they cool.
  5. Create side-swept bangs or a fringe by parting your hair to one side and sweeping the front section of hair across your forehead.
  6. Finish the look with a light hold hairspray or texturizing spray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Boyish Look

Here are some tips for keeping your boyish hairstyle looking fresh and on-trend:

  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add volume to your roots.
  • Invest in a good quality texturizing product to add hold and texture to your locks.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, like braids or top knots, to keep your look fresh and interesting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about making your hair look like a boy's:

Can I achieve this look with any hair type?

Yes, this look can be achieved with any hair type, from straight to curly to wavy.

Do I need to use any specific products?

Using a texturizing product, like a sea salt spray or styling cream, can help add texture and hold to your hair.

Do I need to have short hair?

No, you can achieve a boyish look with long hair by incorporating the key elements we mentioned earlier, like side-swept bangs and textured styling.

Hair Care for Your Boyish Mane

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping your scalp of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and incorporating the key elements of a boyish hairstyle, you can achieve a trendy and versatile look without sacrificing your length. Have fun experimenting with different styling techniques and finding the perfect look for you!
