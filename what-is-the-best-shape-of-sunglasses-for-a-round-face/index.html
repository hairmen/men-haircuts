What Is The Best Shape Of Sunglasses For A Round Face

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect pair of sunglasses can be a bit of a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of options out there that can help you look your best while keeping your eyes protected from the sun's harmful rays. In this article, we'll explore what the best shape of sunglasses is for a round face and provide some tips and advice to help you choose the right pair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Choose Sunglasses for Round Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips and Advice
  5. FAQs

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose sunglasses that help balance out your features and create more definition. The best shape of sunglasses for a round face is one that is angular and has sharp lines, as this helps to create a more structured look. You'll also want to choose sunglasses that are wider than they are tall, as this will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

How To Choose Sunglasses for Round Face

When choosing sunglasses for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

1. Look for Angular Shapes

Angular shapes like square or rectangular frames help to create more definition and structure in a round face. They also help to balance out the soft, curved lines of a round face.

2. Choose Wide Frames

Wide frames help to elongate a round face and make it appear slimmer. Look for frames that are wider than they are tall, as this will help to balance out the proportions of your face.

3. Avoid Round Frames

Round frames can make a round face appear even rounder. If you have a round face, it's best to avoid round frames and opt for angular shapes instead.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the best shape of sunglasses for your round face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start shopping for sunglasses, it's important to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking in the mirror and examining the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you'll have soft, curved lines with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin.

2. Look for Angular Shapes

Once you've determined your face shape, look for sunglasses with angular shapes like square or rectangular frames. These shapes help to create more definition and structure in a round face.

3. Choose Wide Frames

Choose sunglasses with wide frames that are wider than they are tall. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

4. Avoid Round Frames

Avoid round frames, as they can make a round face appear even rounder. Opt for angular shapes instead.

Tips and Advice

Here are a few additional tips and pieces of advice to help you choose the best shape of sunglasses for your round face:

1. Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing sunglasses, consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, look for frames in warm colors like brown, gold, or tortoiseshell. If you have cool undertones, look for frames in cool colors like black, silver, or blue.

2. Find the Right Fit

Make sure your sunglasses fit properly and are comfortable to wear. They should sit snugly on your nose and ears without pinching or sliding down.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Don't be afraid to try on different styles and shapes of sunglasses to see what looks best on you. You might be surprised by what you like!

FAQs

Q: What are some good brands of sunglasses for round faces?

A: Some good brands of sunglasses for round faces include Ray-Ban, Oakley, Maui Jim, and Persol.

Q: Can round faces wear aviator sunglasses?

A: Yes, round faces can wear aviator sunglasses. Look for aviators with angular frames to help balance out the soft, curved lines of a round face.

Q: What color lenses are best for sunglasses?

A: The best color lenses for sunglasses depend on the conditions in which you'll be wearing them. Generally, gray, green, and brown lenses are best for everyday wear, while yellow, orange, and red lenses are best for low light conditions.

Hair Care Tips

When choosing sunglasses for a round face, it's also important to consider your hairstyle. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose a Hairstyle with Volume

Choosing a hairstyle with volume can help to balance out the soft, curved lines of a round face. Consider a short, layered cut or a long, voluminous style.

2. Avoid Flat Styles

Avoid flat hairstyles that can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for styles with volume and texture.

3. Consider Your Hair Color

When choosing sunglasses, consider your hair color. If you have dark hair, look for frames in black or tortoiseshell. If you have light hair, look for frames in lighter colors like white or beige.

By following these tips and advice, you can choose the best shape of sunglasses for your round face and look your best while keeping your eyes protected from the sun's harmful rays.
