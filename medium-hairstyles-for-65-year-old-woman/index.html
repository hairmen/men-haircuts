Medium Hairstyles For 65 Year Old Women

Written by Luffy Oct 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Getting older doesn't mean you have to give up your style. In fact, there are plenty of medium hairstyles for 65 year-old women that are both fashionable and easy to maintain. Whether you're looking for something classic, trendy, or low-maintenance, there's a medium hairstyle that will suit your needs.

Description

Medium hairstyles are a great option for women over 65 who want a versatile look that can be dressed up or down. These hairstyles typically fall somewhere between the chin and the shoulders, offering enough length to play with different styles without requiring a lot of maintenance.

How To

The first step in choosing a medium hairstyle is to consider your face shape. Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and length, while square faces benefit from soft, layered cuts. Heart-shaped faces look great with chin-length bobs, while oval faces can pull off just about any medium-length style.

Once you've determined your face shape, consider the texture of your hair. Fine hair benefits from layers and choppy cuts that add volume, while thick hair looks great with blunt cuts that emphasize the natural texture of the hair.

Step By Step

1. Determine your face shape and hair texture.

2. Browse online or in magazines for inspiration.

3. Consult with your hairstylist to find a style that suits you.

4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

5. Use styling products like mousse or hairspray to add volume or hold your style in place.

Tips

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors.

- Consider investing in a quality hair dryer or flat iron to help you achieve your desired look.

- Regular trims are essential to maintaining healthy hair.

FAQ

Q: Can women over 65 wear bangs?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to update your look and add depth to your hairstyle.

Q: What are some low-maintenance medium hairstyles?

A: Shoulder-length cuts with soft layers or textured waves are a great option for women who want a low-maintenance style.

Hair Care

Regular washing and conditioning are essential to maintaining healthy hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair over time. If you must use these tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand.

Finally, protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside for extended periods of time.
