How To Keep Side Swept Bangs Out Of Your Face A Guide

Written by William Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of glamour to any look. However, they can also be a nuisance when they constantly fall into your eyes. If you're tired of constantly pushing your bangs out of your face, read on to learn how to keep side swept bangs out of your face!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Keep Side Swept Bangs Out of Your Face

There are several ways to keep your side swept bangs out of your face:

  • Use bobby pins
  • Apply hairspray
  • Try a headband
  • Create a twist or braid
  • Use a styling cream or gel

Each of these methods can work well, but some may work better for you than others. Read on for a step-by-step guide on each method.

Step-by-Step Guide

Using Bobby Pins

Here's how to use bobby pins to keep your side swept bangs out of your face:

  1. Brush your bangs to one side.
  2. Use two bobby pins to secure your bangs in place, making an X shape with the pins.
  3. Spray with hairspray to keep in place.

Applying Hairspray

If you don't want to use bobby pins, hairspray can be a good alternative:

  1. Brush your bangs to one side.
  2. Spray hairspray onto your fingertips.
  3. Gently smooth your bangs down and to the side, using your hairspray-coated fingers to help keep them in place.
  4. Repeat as necessary throughout the day to keep your bangs in place.

Trying a Headband

A headband can be a cute and stylish way to keep your side swept bangs out of your face:

  1. Choose a headband that matches your outfit or style.
  2. Slide the headband onto your head, positioning it just behind your bangs.
  3. Push your bangs back into the headband, using it to hold them in place.

Creating a Twist or Braid

A twist or braid can be a more intricate way to keep your side swept bangs out of your face:

  1. Brush your bangs to one side.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the opposite side of your head and twist it away from your face.
  3. Continue twisting until you reach the end of the section of hair.
  4. Bring the twisted section of hair over your bangs and secure it with a bobby pin.

Using a Styling Cream or Gel

A styling cream or gel can help keep your side swept bangs in place all day:

  1. Apply a small amount of styling cream or gel to your fingertips.
  2. Gently smooth your bangs down and to the side, using your product-coated fingers to help keep them in place.
  3. Repeat as necessary throughout the day to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Keeping Side Swept Bangs in Place

Here are some additional tips to help keep your side swept bangs out of your face:

  • Blow dry your bangs to the side using a round brush to help set them in place.
  • Use a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil, which can make your bangs fall flat.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your bangs at the right length.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Will using bobby pins damage my hair?

A: No, bobby pins should not cause any damage to your hair as long as you use them correctly. Be sure to use high-quality pins and avoid pulling your hair too tightly.

Q: Can I use any type of hairspray?

A: It's best to use a hairspray that is specifically formulated for your hair type. Look for a product that provides a flexible hold without leaving your hair feeling stiff or crunchy.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Keeping your side swept bangs in place is important, but so is taking care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for side swept bangs:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to keep them clean and fresh.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons on your bangs, as this can cause damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and using the methods outlined above, you can keep your side swept bangs out of your face and looking great all day long!
