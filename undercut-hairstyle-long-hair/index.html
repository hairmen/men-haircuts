Undercut Hairstyle Long Hair A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd, then an undercut hairstyle for long hair might be just what you need. This edgy hairstyle has been popular for a few years now, and it's not going out of style anytime soon. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the undercut hairstyle for long hair, how to achieve it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The undercut hairstyle for long hair is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that involves shaving the sides and back of your head, while leaving the top long. This creates a contrast between the shaved sections and the longer hair on top, making for a bold and eye-catching look. This hairstyle can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and tousled.

How to Achieve an Undercut Hairstyle for Long Hair

The first step in achieving an undercut hairstyle for long hair is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. This is not a hairstyle that you can easily do yourself, so it's important to find someone who has experience with this type of cut.

When you go to the hair salon, bring pictures of the undercut hairstyle you want to achieve. This will help your stylist understand your vision and give you the best possible result. Be prepared to spend a few hours at the salon, as this is not a quick or easy haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving an undercut hairstyle for long hair:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Section off the top portion of your hair.
  3. Clip the top section out of the way.
  4. Using a clipper, buzz the sides and back of your head.
  5. Blend the buzzed sections into the longer hair on top.
  6. Release the clipped top section and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining an Undercut Hairstyle for Long Hair

Once you've achieved your undercut hairstyle for long hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your undercut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the best one for your hair type.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get an undercut hairstyle for long hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still get an undercut hairstyle for long hair if you have curly hair. However, it's important to find a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair, as this type of hair requires special attention and care.

Will an undercut hairstyle for long hair look good on me?

An undercut hairstyle for long hair can look good on anyone, regardless of their face shape or hair texture. However, it's important to find a style that complements your features and personality.

How often do I need to get a trim for my undercut?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your undercut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Undercut Hairstyle Long Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for maintaining an undercut hairstyle for long hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hair elastics that can cause breakage and damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know all about the undercut hairstyle for long hair, it's time to book your appointment with a skilled stylist and try it out for yourself. With the right care and maintenance, this bold and edgy hairstyle can be the perfect way to express your personal style and stand out from the crowd.
