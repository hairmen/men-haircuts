How To Layer Your Own Very Short Hair At Home

Written by Frank Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for salon visits every time you need a haircut? Do you want to learn how to layer your own very short hair at home? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to layer your own very short hair at home.

Description

Layering your own hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you have very short hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a layered look that's both stylish and easy to maintain. Layering can add depth and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

How to Layer Your Own Very Short Hair at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in layering your own very short hair at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure that your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure that it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Once you've washed your hair, dry it with a towel or a blow dryer until it's completely dry.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Next, divide your hair into several sections using your comb and hair clips. The number of sections will depend on the thickness and length of your hair. For very short hair, you may only need two or three sections.

Step 4: Cut the First Section

Starting with the bottom section, comb your hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use your scissors to make small, upward cuts along the length of your hair. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can create uneven layers.

Step 5: Repeat on the Remaining Sections

Once you've finished cutting the first section, release it and move on to the next section. Repeat the same process, making small, upward cuts along the length of your hair. Remember to cut each section evenly, so that your layers are consistent.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you've finished cutting all the sections, use your comb to blend the layers together. Comb your hair from the roots to the ends, making sure that the layers are evenly distributed.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering your own very short hair at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up, as it's easier to cut more hair than it is to add it back.
  • Use a mirror or ask someone to help you, especially if you're cutting the back of your hair.
  • Be patient and take your time, as rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect layered look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering very short hair:

Is it easy to layer very short hair?

Layering very short hair can be a bit more challenging than layering longer hair, but it's still possible to achieve a layered look at home with the right tools and techniques.

What's the best way to cut very short hair?

When cutting very short hair, it's best to make small, upward cuts along the length of your hair. This will create a layered look that's both subtle and stylish.

Can I layer my own hair at home?

Yes, you can layer your own hair at home, but it's important to have the right tools and techniques. If you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After layering your very short hair at home, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat or chemical treatments. Regular trims can also help to keep your layers looking fresh and well-maintained.

Learning how to layer your own very short hair at home can save you time and money, while still allowing you to achieve a stylish and professional look. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a layered hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
