How to Get Your Curls Back Perm

Choose the Right Perm Solution

When it comes to perming your hair, not all solutions are created equal. There are different types of perm solutions, including alkaline, acid, and exothermic. Alkaline solutions are best for coarse, resistant hair, while acid solutions are ideal for fine, delicate hair. Exothermic solutions are a combination of both and are suitable for all hair types.

Section Your Hair

Before you begin perming your hair, make sure to section it off into small, manageable sections. This will ensure that the perm solution is applied evenly and that all of your hair is covered.

Apply the Perm Solution

Using a perm rod, apply the perm solution to each section of hair. Make sure to saturate the hair thoroughly to ensure that the curl takes hold.

After the perm solution has set, rinse your hair with warm water to remove any excess solution. Then, apply a neutralizer to your hair to stop the perming process and set the curls.
