How to do Side Swept Fringe - Step by Step Guide

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can make you stand out in the crowd? Side swept fringe is the perfect choice for you! This hairstyle has been in trend for years and continues to be popular among women of all ages. In this article, we will guide you on how to do side swept fringe in a step-by-step manner.

Table of Contents

  • How to do Side Swept Fringe
  • Step by Step Guide

How to do Side Swept Fringe

Side swept fringe is a versatile hairstyle that can be done in various ways. Whether you have long or short hair, you can create this look. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Part your hair on one side.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it towards the opposite side.
  4. Use a hair straightener to straighten the section of hair. This will help to create a smooth and sleek look.
  5. Once the section of hair is straightened, comb it towards the side where you parted your hair.
  6. Use a bobby pin to secure the section of hair on the side of your head.
  7. Repeat the same steps on the other side of your head.
  8. Apply some hairspray to keep the hair in place.
  9. You can also use a hair curler to add some waves to your hair for a more textured look.
  10. Your side swept fringe is ready!

Tips for Perfect Side Swept Fringe

Here are some tips to keep in mind while doing side swept fringe:

  • Use a good quality hair straightener to create a smooth and sleek look.
  • Comb your hair properly before straightening it to avoid tangles and knots.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to avoid them being visible.
  • Apply hairspray from a distance to avoid making your hair sticky.
  • Use a hair curler with a barrel size that matches the length of your hair to create perfect waves.

FAQs

  1. Can I do side swept fringe with curly hair?
    Yes, you can! But you may need to straighten the section of hair first to create a sleek look.
  2. Will side swept fringe suit my face shape?
    Side swept fringe suits most face shapes. But you can consult with your hairstylist to get the best advice.
  3. How often should I trim my side swept fringe?
    It depends on how fast your hair grows. But generally, you should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care for Side Swept Fringe

Here are some hair care tips to keep your side swept fringe looking perfect:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a hair straightener or curler.
  • Trim your fringe regularly to maintain the style.
  • Apply some hair serum to keep your hair shiny and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily create a perfect side swept fringe. Go ahead and try this trendy hairstyle today!
