How To Put Up Layered Medium Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Layered medium hair is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, putting up your layered medium hair is a quick and easy way to switch up your style. In this guide, we'll show you how to put up layered medium hair in a relaxed English language, from simple ponytails to more intricate updos.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Up Layered Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Putting Up Layered Medium Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Layered Medium Hair

How to Put Up Layered Medium Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here are some popular ways to put up layered medium hair:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Braids
  • Top knot
  • Twist

Step-by-Step Guide

For this tutorial, we'll be showing you how to do a simple ponytail and a twisted updo.

Simple Ponytail

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and hold it with one hand.
  3. With the other hand, take a hair tie and secure your hair into a ponytail.
  4. Adjust the height of the ponytail to your liking.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it.
  6. Secure the wrapped section of hair with a bobby pin.
  7. Optional: Pull out a few face-framing pieces of hair for a softer look.

Twisted Updo

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from one side of your head and twist it away from your face.
  3. Bring the twisted section of hair towards the back of your head and secure it with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Gather the remaining hair at the nape of your neck and twist it upwards.
  6. Secure the twisted hair with bobby pins, making sure to hide the pins as much as possible.
  7. Optional: Pull out a few face-framing pieces of hair for a softer look.

Tips for Putting Up Layered Medium Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting up your layered medium hair:

  • Use hair ties and bobby pins that match your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Loosen your ponytail or updo for a more relaxed and effortless look.
  • Add hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to dress up your hairstyle.
  • Use hairspray or texturizing spray to give your hair more hold and texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting up layered medium hair:

1. Can I put up my layered medium hair if it's too short?

Yes, you can still put up your layered medium hair even if it's too short for a ponytail or bun. Try doing a half-up, half-down style or braiding your hair instead.

2. How do I prevent my hair from falling out of the ponytail or updo?

Make sure to use hair ties and bobby pins that are strong enough to hold your hair in place. You can also use hairspray or texturizing spray for extra hold.

Hair Care for Layered Medium Hair

Layered medium hair requires some extra TLC to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layers and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to put up layered medium hair, you can switch up your hairstyle in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these styles are perfect for any occasion. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new styles!
