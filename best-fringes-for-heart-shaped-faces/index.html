Best Fringes For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. The good news is that there are plenty of fringes that can flatter your face shape! In this article, we'll go over the best fringes for heart shaped faces, how to style them, and some tips for taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrow chin. The goal with any hairstyle is to balance out these proportions and create a more oval shape. Fringes can be a great way to do this! They can help to soften the forehead and draw attention away from a pointy chin.

How to

When choosing a fringe for a heart-shaped face, you want to look for styles that are either side-swept or have a soft, rounded shape. Avoid straight-across fringes, as these can emphasize the width of the forehead and make the face appear top-heavy.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best fringes for heart shaped faces:

Side-swept fringe

A side-swept fringe is a great option for heart-shaped faces. This style helps to balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention away from a pointy chin. To style a side-swept fringe, simply blow dry it to the side using a round brush. You can also use a flat iron to add some extra curl or wave.

Curly fringe

A curly fringe can also be a good option for heart-shaped faces. This style helps to soften the forehead and add some volume to the top of the head. To style a curly fringe, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create some loose curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.

Long, layered fringe

A long, layered fringe can be a great way to add some texture and movement to a heart-shaped face. This style works best with longer hair, as it helps to create a more balanced and proportionate look. To style a long, layered fringe, use a round brush to blow dry it straight or add some loose waves with a curling iron.

Tips

Here are some tips for choosing and styling a fringe for a heart-shaped face:

  • Choose a fringe that is either side-swept or has a soft, rounded shape.
  • Avoid straight-across fringes, as these can emphasize the width of the forehead.
  • Blow dry your fringe to the side using a round brush.
  • Add some curl or wave to your fringe with a curling iron or flat iron.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • If you have longer hair, consider a long, layered fringe to add some texture and movement.

FAQ

What is a heart-shaped face?

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrow chin.

What fringes are best for heart-shaped faces?

The best fringes for heart-shaped faces are either side-swept or have a soft, rounded shape.

Can I have a straight-across fringe with a heart-shaped face?

It's best to avoid straight-across fringes with a heart-shaped face, as these can emphasize the width of the forehead.

Hair Care

To keep your fringe looking its best, be sure to take good care of your hair. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using heat protectant products when styling with hot tools. You can also use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your fringe between washes.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect fringe for your heart-shaped face! Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape and fits your personal style.
