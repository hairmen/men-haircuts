How To Do Half Up Half Down Curled Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Half up half down curled hair is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. Whether you're attending a wedding, prom, or just want to switch up your look, this hairstyle is perfect. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of achieving this gorgeous hairstyle, step by step.

How to

To achieve half up half down curled hair, you'll need a few tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray

Once you have all the necessary tools, you can begin the styling process. Here's how:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Next, it's time to curl your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue curling your entire head of hair until all of it is curled.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Once you've curled all of your hair, use hair clips to section off the top half of your hair.

Step 4: Create Your Half Up Half Down Style

Take the top half of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip. You can leave some hair around your face for a more natural look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your hairstyle stays in place all day or night, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect half up half down curled hair:

  • Use a curling iron with a thicker barrel for looser curls, or a thinner barrel for tighter curls.
  • Don't curl too close to your roots to avoid creating a puffy look.
  • Use hair clips to keep your hair in place while curling.
  • Experiment with different ways to style the top half of your hair, such as braiding or twisting it.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about half up half down curled hair:

Q: Can I achieve this hairstyle with naturally straight hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve this hairstyle with naturally straight hair. You may need to use a stronger hold hairspray to ensure that your curls stay in place.

Q: How can I make my curls last longer?

A: To make your curls last longer, apply hairspray to each section of hair before curling. Once you've curled your entire head of hair, finish with a strong hold hairspray.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect half up half down curled hair. Whether you're headed to a special event or just want to switch up your look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
