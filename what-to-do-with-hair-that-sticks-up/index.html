What To Do With Hair That Sticks Up Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jun 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have hair that sticks up, you may feel frustrated and unsure of what to do with it. It's a common problem, but there are ways to manage it. In this article, we'll explore different tips and tricks to help you style your hair and keep it in place. Whether you have short or long hair, curly or straight, these tips will work for you. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Hair That Sticks Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Hair That Sticks Up
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Hair That Sticks Up

How to Style Hair That Sticks Up

Styling hair that sticks up can be a challenge, but there are several things you can do to manage it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Strong Hold Product

One of the best ways to keep hair in place is to use a strong hold product, such as a gel or pomade. These products are designed to provide extra hold and can be applied to wet or dry hair. Use a small amount and work it through your hair, focusing on the areas that tend to stick up.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

If you have straight or wavy hair, blow drying it can help smooth it down and keep it in place. Use a round brush to pull your hair straight as you blow dry it. For curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls without causing frizz.

3. Use a Flat Iron

For hair that sticks up at the roots, using a flat iron can help smooth it down. Use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections, pulling the flat iron through your hair from root to tip. Be careful not to use too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling hair that sticks up:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

3. Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of a strong hold styling product, such as a gel or pomade, to your hair. Work it through your hair, focusing on the areas that tend to stick up.

4. Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush, pulling your hair straight as you dry it. For curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

5. Flat iron your hair (optional)

If your hair sticks up at the roots, use a flat iron to smooth it down. Use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections, pulling the flat iron through your hair from root to tip.

6. Finish with a hairspray

Finish by applying a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place. Avoid using too much, as this can cause your hair to look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Managing Hair That Sticks Up

Here are some additional tips for managing hair that sticks up:

1. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

2. Use a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and breakage. Silk is gentle on your hair and helps prevent it from rubbing against rough cotton fibers.

3. Avoid using hot tools too often

Using hot tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, too often can damage your hair and cause breakage. Use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

FAQ

1. Why does my hair stick up?

There are several reasons why hair can stick up, including genetics, humidity, and damage from heat styling or chemical treatments.

2. Can I use a hair mask for hair that sticks up?

Yes, using a hair mask can help nourish and hydrate your hair, reducing frizz and flyaways.

3. How do I prevent my hair from sticking up in humid weather?

Using a strong hold styling product and blow drying your hair can help prevent it from sticking up in humid weather. You can also use a frizz-fighting serum or oil to smooth down any flyaways.

Hair Care for Hair That Sticks Up

Caring for hair that sticks up requires a gentle touch. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner

Applying a leave-in conditioner can help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Work it through your hair after washing and towel drying.

3. Avoid using too much heat

Using too much heat can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Use heat styling tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair healthy and strong.

With these tips and tricks, you can manage hair that sticks up and keep your locks looking their best. Whether you prefer a sleek and smooth look or embrace your natural texture, there's a solution for every hair type.
