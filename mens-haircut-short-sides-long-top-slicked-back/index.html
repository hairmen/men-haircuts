Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back A Trendy Style For 2023

Written by Eveline Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men in 2023, then the short sides long top slicked back style might be just what you're looking for. This style has been popular for several years, and it's still going strong. It's a versatile haircut that can be worn in various ways, depending on your preferences and hair type. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description of Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back

The mens haircut short sides long top slicked back is a style that features short hair on the sides and long hair on the top that is combed back. It's a classic and timeless look that has been popular for decades. In recent years, it's become even more popular due to the rise of the hipster and modern gentleman style.

How to Get Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back

If you're interested in getting the mens haircut short sides long top slicked back, you'll need to find a stylist who is experienced in this style. The stylist will start by cutting the sides of your hair short, usually with clippers. The length of the sides can vary depending on your preference, but a common length is around a 2 or 3 clipper guard. The top of the hair will be left longer, and the stylist will use scissors to trim and shape it. Finally, the hair will be combed back with a styling product such as pomade or wax.

Step by Step Guide for Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back

Here is a step-by-step guide for getting the mens haircut short sides long top slicked back:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced in this style.
  2. Discuss the desired length and style with the stylist.
  3. The stylist will start by cutting the sides of your hair short with clippers.
  4. The top of the hair will be left longer, and the stylist will use scissors to trim and shape it.
  5. The hair will be combed back with a styling product such as pomade or wax.
  6. Adjust the style to your preference.

Tips for Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling the mens haircut short sides long top slicked back:

  • Use a good quality styling product such as pomade or wax.
  • Blow-dry your hair before applying styling product for a more polished look.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Maintain the style by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use a comb or brush to style the hair.

FAQs About Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short sides long top slicked back:

1. Is this hairstyle easy to maintain?

Yes, this hairstyle is relatively easy to maintain. You'll need to get regular trims to keep the sides short and the top of the hair in shape. You'll also need to use a styling product to keep the hair slicked back.

2. Can this hairstyle work for any hair type?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, the length and style may need to be adjusted depending on your hair type.

3. What styling product should I use?

You should use a good quality styling product such as pomade or wax. These products will help keep the hair slicked back and in place.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Slicked Back

Here are some tips for hair care for the mens haircut short sides long top slicked back:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying on high heat or using hot styling tools regularly.
  • Maintain the style by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use a good quality styling product such as pomade or wax.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Overall, the mens haircut short sides long top slicked back is a trendy and stylish haircut that can work for most hair types. With the right styling product and hair care, you can maintain this style and look sharp and polished. So, if you're looking for a new haircut in 2023, give this style a try and see how it works for you!
