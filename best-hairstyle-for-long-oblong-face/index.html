Best Hairstyle For Long Oblong Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a long oblong face and are looking for the best hairstyle to flatter your features? Look no further, as we have compiled the ultimate guide to help you find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about the best hairstyle for long oblong face, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

A long oblong face is characterized by its elongated shape and straight lines. This face shape is longer than it is wide, with a defined jawline, forehead, and cheekbones. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a long oblong face is to add width and volume to the sides of the face, while minimizing the length. The right hairstyle can balance out your features and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing the right cut for a long oblong face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to look for hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, and curls. These styles will help to balance out the length of your face and create the illusion of more width.

Second, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as these can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for cuts that are shoulder length or shorter, and add texture and movement to your hair.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for choosing the right hairstyle for your long oblong face:

  1. Identify your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face.
  2. Look for hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, and curls.
  3. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight.
  4. Choose a cut that is shoulder length or shorter.
  5. Add texture and movement to your hair with layers, bangs, or curls.
  6. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing the best hairstyle for your long oblong face:

  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to shorten the length of your face.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse, texturizing spray, or sea salt spray.
  • Try out different partings to change up your look and add volume to the crown of your head.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for long oblong face:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oblong face?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as these can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for cuts that are shoulder length or shorter, and add texture and movement to your hair.

What are some hairstyles that flatter a long oblong face?

Hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, and curls, are most flattering for a long oblong face. You can also add bangs to your hairstyle to shorten the length of your face.

Do I need to consult with a hairstylist to find the right cut?

While it's not necessary to consult with a hairstylist, it can be helpful to get their opinion on what cut would be most flattering for your face shape. They can also provide you with tips and recommendations for styling your hair at home.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it's important to use products that are suited to your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, opt for lightweight products that won't weigh your hair down, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray. If you have thick or curly hair, use products that help to define your curls and control frizz, such as curl-enhancing cream or anti-frizz serum.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to keep your hair healthy by washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling or chemical treatments. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hairstyle for a long oblong face can be challenging, but with the right cut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and flatter your features. Use the tips and advice in this article to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, and remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
