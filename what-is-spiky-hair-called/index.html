What Is Spiky Hair Called

Written by Daniel Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Spiky hair is a hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is a hairstyle that involves raising the hair in small, sharp spikes. This type of hairstyle is commonly seen as a bold and edgy style that is often associated with punk rock and alternative culture. In this article, we will explore what spiky hair is called in relaxed English, as well as how to achieve this style, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description

Spiky hair, also known as spiked hair, is a hairstyle that involves raising the hair in small, sharp spikes. This type of hairstyle is commonly seen as a bold and edgy style that is often associated with punk rock and alternative culture. Spiky hair can be achieved using a variety of methods, including hair gel, hairspray, and hair wax.

How to Achieve Spiky Hair

There are several steps to achieving spiky hair. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Apply a hair styling product, such as hair gel, hairspray, or hair wax, to your hair. Use a small amount of product and work it evenly through your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair into small, sharp spikes. Be sure to work the product through your hair evenly to ensure that your spikes stay in place.

Tips for Maintaining Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your spiky hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair styling product that is specifically designed for spiky hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Be gentle when styling your hair, as rough handling can cause damage to your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

FAQ

What is the best hair product for spiky hair?

The best hair product for spiky hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Hair gel, hairspray, and hair wax are all popular choices for achieving spiky hair. Choose a product that is specifically designed for spiky hair and that provides a strong hold.

Can spiky hair be achieved on all hair types?

Spiky hair can be achieved on most hair types. However, it may be more difficult to achieve on very fine or thin hair. If you have fine or thin hair, consider using a volumizing product to add body to your hair before styling it into spikes.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important, especially if you regularly style your hair into spikes. To keep your hair healthy, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, too often
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and free from split ends

Overall, spiky hair is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can be achieved using a variety of methods. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle and maintain healthy hair.
