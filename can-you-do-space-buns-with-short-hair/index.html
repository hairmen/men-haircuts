Can You Do Space Buns With Short Hair

Written by Petter Jul 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're wondering whether you can do space buns with short hair, the answer is yes! Short hair doesn't have to limit your styling options, and space buns are a great way to add some fun and creativity to your hairstyle.

Description

Space buns are a popular hairstyle that involves creating two buns on the top of your head. They can be styled in different ways, such as messy buns or sleek buns, and can be worn for various occasions, from casual outings to formal events.

How to

Here's how you can do space buns with short hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take one section of hair and twist it into a bun, securing it in place with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side.
  3. If your hair is too short to create a full bun, you can create a mini bun or use hair extensions to add volume.
  4. Accessorize your space buns with clips, ribbons, or other hair accessories to add some extra flair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating space buns with short hair:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Part your hair down the middle using a comb.
  3. Take one section of hair and twist it into a bun, making sure it's tight and secure.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. If your hair is too short to create a full bun, create a mini bun by twisting a small section of hair and securing it in place with bobby pins.
  7. Accessorize your space buns with hair clips, ribbons, or other hair accessories.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect space buns with short hair:

  • Use hair extensions to add volume and length to your hair if it's too short to create a full bun.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as messy buns or sleek buns, to find the look that suits you best.
  • Accessorize your space buns with hair clips, ribbons, or other hair accessories to add some extra flair.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about space buns with short hair:

Can you do space buns with shoulder-length hair?

Yes, you can do space buns with shoulder-length hair. However, you may need to use hair extensions to add volume and length to your hair.

Can you do space buns with curly hair?

Yes, you can do space buns with curly hair. However, you may need to straighten your hair first to make it easier to style into buns.

How long does it take to do space buns with short hair?

It usually takes around 10-15 minutes to do space buns with short hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can create the perfect space buns with short hair and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. So go ahead and experiment with different styles and accessories to find the look that suits you best!
