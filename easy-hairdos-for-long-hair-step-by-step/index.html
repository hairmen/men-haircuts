Easy Hairdos For Long Hair Step By Step

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Look no further because we have compiled a list of easy hairdos for long hair step by step. Whether you're going to school, work, or a special event, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to

Before we dive into the easy hairdos for long hair step by step, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting any hairstyle. Secondly, keep all necessary tools such as hair ties, bobby pins, and hair spray within reach. Lastly, take your time and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles!

Step by Step

1. Braided Bun

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle and create two braids on each side.

Step 2: Twist the braids together to create a bun shape and secure with bobby pins.

Step 3: Spray hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Step 1: Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie.

Step 2: Tease the ponytail with a comb to add volume.

Step 3: Wrap a small piece of hair around the hair tie to hide it and secure with bobby pins.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Messy Bun

Step 1: Gather all of your hair and tie it into a high ponytail.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun shape.

Step 3: Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

Step 4: Spray hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Side Braid

Step 1: Part your hair to one side and create a braid on the opposite side.

Step 2: Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

Step 3: Secure the braid with a hair tie and spray hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Use hair accessories such as headbands or clips to add a pop of color to your hairstyle.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different braids and twists to create unique hairstyles.

- If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume.

FAQ

Q: Can these hairstyles be done on short hair?

A: Some of these hairstyles may be possible on short hair, but they are mostly designed for long hair.

Q: Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

A: Absolutely! These hairstyles work on all hair types.

Q: How long do these hairstyles take to do?

A: The time it takes to do these hairstyles varies, but most can be done in under 10 minutes.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and strength. Here are a few tips:

- Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools such as a straightener or curling iron.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

- Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you have a list of easy hairdos for long hair step by step, it's time to try them out! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles. Happy hairstyling!
