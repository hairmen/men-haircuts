How To Cut Shaggy Medium Length Bob Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 09, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great? Look no further than the shaggy medium length bob! This versatile haircut is perfect for any face shape or hair type, and can be customized to fit your personal style. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of how to cut a shaggy medium length bob, as well as give you some tips on how to care for and style your new do.

How to Cut a Shaggy Medium Length Bob

Before we get started, it's important to note that cutting your own hair can be tricky. If you're not confident in your abilities, it may be best to head to a salon and let a professional handle it. However, if you're feeling brave, here's how to cut a shaggy medium length bob:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. You want to work with clean, dry hair for the most accurate cut.
  2. Section your hair. Use clips to separate your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back.
  3. Cut the back. Starting with the back section, use a comb to create a straight line across the nape of your neck. Then, use scissors to cut along that line.
  4. Cut the sides. Moving on to the side sections, use your comb to create a diagonal line from the back of your head to the front. Then, use scissors to cut along that line.
  5. Cut the top. Finally, cut the top section of your hair. Use your comb to create a horizontal line across the crown of your head. Then, use scissors to cut along that line.
  6. Create layers. To give your shaggy bob some texture, use scissors to add layers throughout your hair. Start at the front and work your way back, taking small sections of hair and cutting at different lengths.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Shaggy Bob

Now that you've got your shaggy bob, here are some tips for keeping it looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Trim your hair regularly. To keep your shaggy bob looking fresh, it's important to trim it every 6-8 weeks.
  • Invest in some quality styling products. Texturizing spray, wax, and mousse can help add definition and hold to your shaggy bob.
  • Experiment with different styles. Your shaggy bob is versatile and can be worn sleek and straight, tousled and messy, or somewhere in between.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaggy medium length bobs:

Can a shaggy bob be worn with bangs?

Absolutely! Bangs can add a fun, flirty touch to your shaggy bob. Consider getting wispy, textured bangs that blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

What face shapes work best with a shaggy bob?

A shaggy bob can work for any face shape, but it's especially flattering on oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, consider adding more layers to create a more angular look.

Can a shaggy bob be styled for formal events?

Yes! A shaggy bob can be styled sleek and straight for a chic, elegant look. Add some statement earrings or a bold lip to dress it up even more.

Hair Care Tips for Your Shaggy Bob

To keep your shaggy bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often. Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair. Washing too frequently can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Congratulations, you now know how to cut a shaggy medium length bob! With a little practice and some styling products, you'll be rocking your new do in no time.
