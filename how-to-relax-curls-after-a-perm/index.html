How To Relax Curls After A Perm Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your tight curls after a perm? Do you want to relax them a bit? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll show you how to relax curls after a perm step by step. We’ll also provide some tips and tricks for hair care. Let’s get started!

How to Relax Curls after a Perm

Relaxing curls after a perm is a process that requires some patience and care. There are several methods you can use to achieve a relaxed look. These include:

  • Using a straightening iron
  • Blow-drying with a round brush
  • Using a hair relaxer

Each method has its advantages and disadvantages. You should choose the one that suits your hair type and texture. Keep in mind that any method can damage your hair if not done properly. Let’s take a closer look at each method.

Using a Straightening Iron

A straightening iron is a popular tool for relaxing curls. It is easy to use and can provide instant results. However, it can also damage your hair if not used correctly. Here’s how to use a straightening iron:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Dry it with a towel.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will prevent damage from the heat of the iron.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Run the iron over each section from root to tip. Be careful not to hold it in one place for too long.
  5. Repeat the process until you’ve straightened all sections.
  6. Apply a hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

Using a straightening iron can provide a sleek and smooth look. However, it can also cause damage if used too often. Use it sparingly and always apply a heat protectant spray.

Blow-drying with a Round Brush

Blow-drying with a round brush is another method for relaxing curls. It is less damaging than using a straightening iron, but it requires more time and effort. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Dry it with a towel.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Take a section and wrap it around the round brush.
  5. Blow-dry the section while pulling it downwards with the brush.
  6. Repeat the process until you’ve dried all sections.
  7. Apply a hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

Blow-drying with a round brush can provide a natural and bouncy look. However, it requires some practice and patience. Be gentle with your hair and don’t use too much heat.

Using a Hair Relaxer

A hair relaxer is a chemical treatment that can permanently straighten your hair. It is the most effective method for relaxing curls, but it also has the highest risk of damage. Here’s how to use a hair relaxer:

  1. Choose a hair relaxer that suits your hair type and texture. Read the instructions carefully.
  2. Apply the relaxer to your hair, avoiding the scalp and the roots.
  3. Leave it on for the recommended time. Don’t exceed the time limit.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical reaction.
  6. Apply a deep conditioner to moisturize your hair.

Using a hair relaxer can provide a permanent straightening effect. However, it can also cause severe damage if not done properly. Read the instructions carefully and don’t exceed the time limit.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the methods for relaxing curls after a perm, let’s summarize the steps:

  1. Choose a method that suits your hair type and texture.
  2. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Dry it with a towel.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections.
  5. Use a straightening iron, a round brush, or a hair relaxer to relax the curls.
  6. Apply a hair serum or a deep conditioner to add shine and prevent frizz.

Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid excessive heat or chemicals. Patience and care are key to achieving a relaxed and healthy look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for relaxing curls after a perm:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using a straightening iron or a blow dryer.
  • Use a round brush with natural bristles to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Avoid using a hair relaxer too often, as it can cause severe damage.
  • Apply a hair serum or a deep conditioner to moisturize and protect your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a relaxed and healthy look without damaging your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing curls after a perm:

Can I relax my curls without using heat or chemicals?

Yes, you can use natural methods like braiding or twisting your hair. However, these methods may not provide the same effect as using a straightening iron or a hair relaxer.

How often should I relax my curls?

It depends on your hair type and texture. Generally, you should avoid using a hair relaxer more than once every 3-6 months. Use a straightening iron or a round brush sparingly to avoid heat damage.

Can relaxing curls cause hair loss?

Yes, excessive use of heat or chemicals can cause hair loss or breakage. Always be gentle with your hair and avoid using too much heat or chemicals.

Hair Care

Relaxing curls after a perm requires some extra care and attention. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a hair serum or a deep conditioner to moisturize and protect your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat or chemicals, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these hair care tips, you can maintain a relaxed and healthy look.

Conclusion

Relaxing curls after a perm can be a challenging process, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a relaxed and healthy look. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and avoid excessive heat or chemicals. Follow our tips and tricks, and take good care of your hair. Good luck!
