Do Fat Faces Suit Fringes

Written by Bardi Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, fringes or bangs are still a popular hairstyle choice for many people. However, one question that often comes up is whether or not fringes suit those with a "fat face." In this blog post, we'll explore this topic and provide you with some tips and advice to help you decide if fringes are right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine if Fringes Suit Your Fat Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Fringes for Your Fat Face
  • Tips for Styling Fringes on a Fat Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Fringes on a Fat Face

Description

The term "fat face" is often used to describe a round or chubby face shape. People with this face shape may feel self-conscious about their appearance and may be hesitant to try new hairstyles, such as fringes. However, the good news is that fringes can actually work well on a fat face, as long as they are styled correctly.

How to Determine if Fringes Suit Your Fat Face

Before getting fringes, it's important to determine if they will suit your face shape. To do this, you can follow these simple steps:

  1. Take a look at your face shape in the mirror. Is it round or chubby?
  2. Consider the length of your forehead. If you have a short forehead, fringes may not be the best choice for you.
  3. Think about your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, fringes may be difficult to maintain.
  4. Look at photos of people with similar face shapes to yours who have fringes. Do you like the way they look?

If you've considered these factors and still think fringes will suit your face shape, then it's time to move onto the next step.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Fringes for Your Fat Face

If you've decided that fringes are the right choice for you, then follow these steps to get the perfect fringe:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine the best type of fringe for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose the length of your fringe. Shorter fringes work well on fat faces, as they help to elongate the face.
  3. Decide on the style of your fringe. Side-swept fringes are a great option for fat faces, as they help to create a more angular look.
  4. Maintain your fringe by trimming it every four to six weeks. This will help to keep it looking fresh and prevent it from becoming too long.

By following these steps, you can ensure that your fringe will look great on your fat face.

Tips for Styling Fringes on a Fat Face

Once you have your fringe, it's important to style it correctly. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Blow dry your fringe straight to create a sleek, polished look.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your fringe.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade, to your fringe to help hold it in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy, textured look or a sleek, straight look.

By following these tips, you can create a variety of different looks with your fringe.

FAQs

Q: Will fringes make my face look even rounder?

A: No, if styled correctly, fringes can actually help to elongate a fat face and create a more angular look.

Q: Can I still wear fringes if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, but you may need to straighten your fringe with a flat iron to achieve the desired look.

Q: How often should I trim my fringe?

A: It's recommended to trim your fringe every four to six weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Fringes on a Fat Face

Finally, it's important to take care of your fringe to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you do just that:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent your fringe from becoming oily.
  • Avoid touching your fringe too often, as this can cause it to become greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow drying or straightening your fringe.
  • Avoid using too much product on your fringe, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

By following these hair care tips, you can ensure that your fringe looks great all the time.

Conclusion

In conclusion, fringes can work well on a fat face, as long as they are styled correctly. By following the tips and advice in this blog post, you can determine if fringes are right for you and get the perfect fringe for your fat face.
