How To Cut Asymmetrical Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to change up your hairstyle without going for a full-on haircut, cutting asymmetrical bangs might be the perfect solution for you. Asymmetrical bangs are a trendy and edgy look that can add some dimension and personality to your hair. In this guide, we'll go over the steps to cutting asymmetrical bangs at home, as well as some tips and FAQ to help you achieve the perfect look for you.

How to Cut Asymmetrical Bangs

Before you start cutting, it's important to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and clips to section off your hair. Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose your part

The first step to cutting asymmetrical bangs is to decide where you want your part to be. This will determine which side your bangs will be longer on. Use your comb to create a straight line from your part to the opposite side of your head.

Step 2: Section off your hair

Next, clip away the hair you don't want to cut. Use your comb to create a triangle shape with the hair you want to cut, with the point of the triangle at your part and the base of the triangle at the top of your head.

Step 3: Cut your bangs

Now it's time to start cutting. Hold the triangle of hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut straight across. For an asymmetrical look, start cutting shorter on the side of your head where your bangs will be shorter, and cut longer towards the opposite side.

Step 4: Check your work

Once you've cut your bangs, unclip the rest of your hair and check your work. Use your comb to make sure your bangs are even and adjust any uneven spots with your scissors.

Tips for Cutting Asymmetrical Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair off than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. It's better to start longer and cut more later if you want a shorter look.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Consider getting a friend to help you if you're nervous about cutting your own hair.

FAQ

How do I know if asymmetrical bangs will look good on me?

Asymmetrical bangs can look good on a variety of face shapes and hair types, but it's always a good idea to consult with a stylist before making a big change to your hair. They can help you determine if asymmetrical bangs are the right look for you.

Should I cut my bangs wet or dry?

It's generally easier to cut hair when it's wet, but you can also cut your bangs dry if you prefer. Just make sure your hair is completely detangled before you start cutting.

How often should I trim my bangs?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look uneven. As a general rule, aim to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care for Asymmetrical Bangs

To keep your asymmetrical bangs looking their best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat styling tools, and avoid over-washing or over-styling your hair to prevent damage. If you notice any split ends or damage to your hair, it's a good idea to get a trim to keep your hair healthy.

Cutting asymmetrical bangs at home can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and some patience, you can achieve a trendy and stylish look that's all your own. Follow these steps, tips, and FAQ to get started, and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect asymmetrical bangs for you.
