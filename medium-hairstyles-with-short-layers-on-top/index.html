Medium Hairstyles With Short Layers On Top A Relaxed Guide

Written by Petter Nov 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that can work for any occasion, medium hairstyles with short layers on top might be the answer to your hair woes. Whether you have straight or curly hair, this style can be customized to suit your unique features and personality. In this guide, we'll break down how to achieve this look, offer some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant, and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular style.

Description

Medium hairstyles with short layers on top are a popular choice for women who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. This style typically involves layers at the top of the head, with the length gradually increasing towards the ends. This creates a flattering frame around the face, while also adding volume and texture to the hair. The layers can be cut to suit your face shape and hair type, making this style suitable for almost anyone.

How to

If you're interested in trying out this style, here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add loose waves or curls to the ends of your hair.
  5. Finally, use a texturizing spray or wax to add definition to the layers at the top of your head.

Voila! You now have a gorgeous medium hairstyle with short layers on top.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in achieving this style:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type. This will help to cleanse your hair of any dirt and buildup, while also keeping it hydrated and nourished.

Step 2: Towel-Dry and Apply Heat Protectant

After washing your hair, gently towel-dry it to remove excess water. Then, apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume. Work in sections, starting at the back of your head and moving towards the front.

Step 4: Add Loose Waves or Curls

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add loose waves or curls to the ends of your hair. This will give your hair a soft, feminine look that complements the layers at the top of your head.

Step 5: Add Texture

Finally, use a texturizing spray or wax to add definition to the layers at the top of your head. This will create a tousled, beachy look that's perfect for everyday wear.

Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your medium hairstyle with short layers on top looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about medium hairstyles with short layers on top:

Q: What face shapes are best suited for this style?

A: This style can work for almost any face shape, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can this style work for curly hair?

A: Absolutely! This style can be customized to suit any hair type, including curly hair. Just make sure to use products that are designed for curly hair to keep your curls looking their best.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed to maintain this style?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep the layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and beautiful is essential for maintaining any hairstyle, including medium hairstyles with short layers on top. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your medium hairstyle with short layers on top looking its best for years to come!
