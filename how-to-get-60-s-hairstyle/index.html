How To Get 60 S Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Are you a fan of the iconic hairstyles of the 60s? Do you want to rock a retro look for a special occasion or just for fun? Look no further! In this post, we will guide you through the steps on how to achieve a 60s hairstyle that will make you feel like a vintage queen.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get 60's Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect 60's Hairstyle
  • FAQs

How to Get 60's Hairstyle

The 60s was a decade of iconic hairstyles that are still popular today. From the bouffant to the beehive, there are many styles to choose from. Here are some of the most popular 60s hairstyles:

  • Bouffant
  • Beehive
  • Flip
  • Chignon
  • Twiggy cut

To achieve a 60s hairstyle, you will need the right tools and products. Here are some of the things you will need:

  • Hot rollers or curling iron
  • Hairspray
  • Teasing comb
  • Hair pins

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need, it's time to start styling your hair. Follow these steps to achieve a 60s hairstyle:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots. This will help give your hair the volume it needs for a 60s hairstyle.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Using hot rollers or a curling iron, curl your hair in small sections. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face. Once you have curled all of your hair, let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing comb, tease the crown of your head. This will help give your hair the height it needs for a 60s hairstyle. Spray the teased section with hairspray to hold it in place.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair

Take the top section of your hair and pin it at the crown of your head. This will create a bouffant or beehive look. Use hair pins to secure the section in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to keep it in place. You can also use a shine spray to give your hair a glossy finish.

Tips for a Perfect 60's Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair the volume it needs.
  • Tease your hair at the crown for a bouffant or beehive look.
  • Use hot rollers or a curling iron to create soft curls.
  • Use hairspray to hold your hair in place.
  • Accessorize with a headband or scarf for an extra retro touch.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve a 60s hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve a Twiggy cut with short hair. Use a flat iron to create a sleek look and add some volume with a volumizing mousse.

Q: How long does it take to achieve a 60s hairstyle?

A: It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour to achieve a 60s hairstyle, depending on the style and your hair length.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy while achieving a 60s hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot rollers or a curling iron.
  • Don't tease your hair too much as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and crunchy.

Now that you know how to achieve a 60s hairstyle, it's time to try it out for yourself! Have fun experimenting with different styles and adding your own personal touch to make it your own.
