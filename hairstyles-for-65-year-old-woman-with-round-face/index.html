Hairstyles For 65 Year Old Woman With Round Face

Written by Frank Jul 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

A round face is characterized by its soft and curved lines. Women who have a round face shape often want to create an illusion of a slimmer and longer face. As we age, our hair becomes thinner, and it may lose its natural shine and texture. But that doesn't mean we can't have a fabulous hairstyle. In this article, we will show you some of the best hairstyles for 65-year-old women with round face shapes that will make you look and feel beautiful.

Description

As we age, our face shape may change, and we may develop a rounder face. A round face is characterized by a circular shape, with the width and length being almost the same. The cheeks are usually full, and the jawline is not very defined. The key to finding the right hairstyle for a round face is to create an illusion of length and slimness.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following:

  • Oval: The length of the face is longer than the width, and the forehead is slightly wider than the jawline.
  • Round: The width and length of the face are almost the same, and the cheeks are usually full.
  • Square: The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same width, giving the face a boxy look.
  • Heart: The forehead is wider than the jawline, and the chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: The face is widest at the cheekbones, and the forehead and jawline are narrower.

Step by Step Guide: Hairstyles for 65 Year Old Woman with Round Face

1. Bob with Side Bangs

A bob is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For a round face shape, a bob with side bangs is an excellent option. The side bangs create an illusion of length, and the bob frames the face, making it look slimmer. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush, and use a curling iron to add some volume and texture.

2. Layered Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that looks great on women with a round face shape. A layered pixie cut adds volume to the crown of the head, making the face look longer. To style, use a texturizing spray to add some texture and volume to your hair.

3. Medium Length Shag

A medium length shag is a layered hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For a round face shape, a shag with layers that frame the face is an excellent option. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush, and use a curling iron to add some volume and texture.

4. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for women who prefer longer hairstyles. Long layers add movement and texture to the hair, making it look more voluminous. To style, use a curling iron to add some waves to your hair, and use a texturizing spray to add some volume.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face and create an illusion of length.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight, as they can make your face look rounder.

FAQ: Hairstyles for 65 Year Old Woman with Round Face

1. What is the best hairstyle for a 65-year-old woman with a round face shape?

A bob with side bangs, a layered pixie cut, a medium length shag, and long layers are all great options for a 65-year-old woman with a round face shape.

2. How do I determine my face shape?

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 65

As we age, our hair may become thinner and lose its natural shine and texture. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can damage it.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as it can cause breakage and damage.

Remember, age is just a number, and there is no reason why you can't have a fabulous hairstyle at 65. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape and rock it with confidence!
