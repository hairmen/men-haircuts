Mens Hairstyles Long On Top Short Sides And Back The Ultimate Guide

Looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle for men? Look no further than the timeless mens hairstyles long on top short sides and back! This hairstyle has been popular for years and continues to be a go-to for guys of all ages and styles. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this classic hairstyle, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and much more.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintenance
  • FAQs

Description

The long on top short sides and back hairstyle is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head, which is typically styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. The sides and back are kept short, usually with a fade or undercut. This creates a sharp contrast between the longer top and shorter sides and back, resulting in a stylish and trendy look that's perfect for any occasion.

How to Style

Styling a long on top short sides and back haircut is relatively easy and can be done in a few simple steps. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean and dry, apply a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel to the top of your hair, and use your fingers or a comb to style it as desired. For the sides and back, use clippers or scissors to trim your hair to the desired length, depending on the type of fade or undercut you prefer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a long on top short sides and back haircut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair to remove any dirt or buildup
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product to the top of your hair
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to style the top of your hair as desired
  4. Trim the sides and back of your hair with clippers or scissors, depending on the type of fade or undercut you prefer
  5. Style the sides and back of your hair to complete the look

With these simple steps, you can achieve a stylish and trendy long on top short sides and back hairstyle.

Tips for Maintenance

Keeping your long on top short sides and back haircut looking great requires some maintenance and care. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back of your hair to maintain the contrast with the longer top
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Apply a styling product to the top of your hair to keep it in place throughout the day
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish

FAQs

Here are some common questions about long on top short sides and back hairstyles:

  • Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes? Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape.
  • What type of styling product is best for this hairstyle? It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade, wax, or gel can be used to achieve different looks.
  • How often should I trim my hair? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the contrast between the top and sides grows out. Generally, a trim every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle, including long on top short sides and back. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and buildup
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers or straighteners too frequently, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth from the inside out

In conclusion, the long on top short sides and back hairstyle is a versatile and trendy option for men of all ages and styles. With the right maintenance and care, you can keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish for any occasion. Use the tips and advice in this guide to achieve the perfect long on top short sides and back hairstyle for you!
