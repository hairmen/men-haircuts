Men s Mop Top Haircut A Relaxed Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Nov 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will be discussing the popular men's mop top haircut. This hairstyle is a classic that has been around for decades, but it has recently made a comeback. We will be covering everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care.

Description

The men's mop top haircut is a hairstyle that is characterized by long hair on top and short hair on the sides and back. The hair on top is usually styled to fall over the forehead and ears, giving it a messy, undone look. This haircut is often associated with the Beatles, who popularized it in the 1960s.

How to Get the Men's Mop Top Haircut

To get the men's mop top haircut, you will need to visit a hairstylist or barber who is experienced in this style. It is important to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to achieve so that your stylist knows exactly what you are looking for.

The haircut typically involves cutting the sides and back of the hair short with clippers, while leaving the hair on top long. The stylist will then use scissors to layer the hair on top, creating a messy, textured look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a comb to section off the hair on top of your head. This will be the part that is cut and styled.

3. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short.

4. Use scissors to layer the hair on top, creating a messy, textured look.

5. Use a hair dryer and your fingers to style the hair on top, pushing it forward over your forehead and ears.

Tips

1. Be sure to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to achieve to your stylist.

2. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help hold the hair in place and create a textured look.

FAQ

How long does the men's mop top haircut last?

The men's mop top haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can I style the men's mop top haircut in different ways?

Yes, the men's mop top haircut can be styled in different ways, depending on the occasion. You can slick it back for a more formal look or leave it messy for a casual look.

Is the men's mop top haircut suitable for all hair types?

The men's mop top haircut is suitable for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, if you have very thin or fine hair, this hairstyle may not be the best choice for you.

Hair Care

To keep your men's mop top haircut looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. This includes using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.

With proper care and maintenance, the men's mop top haircut can be a stylish and versatile choice for any man.
