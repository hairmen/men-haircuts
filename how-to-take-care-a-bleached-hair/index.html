How To Take Care Of Bleached Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Aug 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have recently bleached your hair or are planning to, it is important to know how to properly take care of it. Bleached hair requires a lot of attention and care because it is more prone to damage and breakage. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks on how to take care of bleached hair.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Take Care of Bleached Hair

The first step in taking care of bleached hair is to avoid washing it too often. This is because frequent washing can strip the hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Instead, wash your hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

It is also important to avoid using hot tools on bleached hair, such as straighteners and curling irons. These can cause further damage and breakage to the hair. If you must use hot tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Another way to take care of bleached hair is to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. This will help to restore moisture and nourishment to the hair, leaving it looking shiny and healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to take care of bleached hair:

  1. Wash your hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid using hot tools on your hair, or use a heat protectant spray.
  3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  4. Avoid over-brushing or combing your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  5. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  6. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Tips for Maintaining Bleached Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining bleached hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can cause further damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and provide extra moisture.
  • Avoid using hair elastics or clips that can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about taking care of bleached hair:

Q: Can I still color my hair if it has been bleached?

A: Yes, but it is important to wait at least four weeks after bleaching before coloring your hair again. This will help to prevent further damage and breakage.

Q: How can I prevent my bleached hair from turning yellow?

A: Use a purple shampoo and conditioner once a week to help neutralize any yellow tones in your hair.

Q: Is it okay to blow dry my bleached hair?

A: Yes, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat. Use a low heat setting and hold the blow dryer at least six inches away from your hair.

Bleached Hair Care Products

Here are some recommended hair care products for bleached hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Purple shampoo and conditioner

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can keep your bleached hair looking healthy and beautiful.
