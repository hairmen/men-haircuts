How To Decide Bridesmaid Hair Tips Ideas And Hair Care

Bridesmaid hairstyles are just as important as the bride's hairstyle. As a bride, you want your bridesmaids to look fabulous on your big day. But how do you decide on the perfect hairstyle for your bridesmaids? Here are some tips, ideas, and hair care tips to help you decide on the perfect bridesmaid hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Decide Bridesmaid Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing Bridesmaid Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Bridesmaids

How to Decide Bridesmaid Hair

The first thing you need to consider is the type of wedding you are having. Is it a formal or casual wedding? Is it indoors or outdoors? The type of wedding will determine the hairstyle you choose for your bridesmaids.

Next, you need to consider the bridesmaids' hair length and texture. If your bridesmaids have short hair, you can opt for a bob or pixie cut. If they have long hair, you can go for a braided or curly hairstyle.

Another factor to consider is the bridesmaids' face shape. Different hairstyles will suit different face shapes. For example, a long bob will look great on a round face, while an updo will suit an oval face.

Finally, you need to consider the bridesmaids' personal style. You want your bridesmaids to feel comfortable and confident on your big day, so choose a hairstyle that they will love and feel good in.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to decide on the perfect bridesmaid hair:

  1. Consider the type of wedding you are having
  2. Consider the bridesmaids' hair length and texture
  3. Consider the bridesmaids' face shape
  4. Consider the bridesmaids' personal style
  5. Look for inspiration online and in bridal magazines
  6. Talk to your hairstylist about the different options
  7. Book a hair trial for your bridesmaids
  8. Get feedback from your bridesmaids
  9. Make a final decision on the hairstyle
  10. Book your hairstylist for the big day

Tips for Choosing Bridesmaid Hair

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect bridesmaid hair:

  • Consider the bridesmaids' hair length and texture
  • Consider the bridesmaids' face shape
  • Look for inspiration online and in bridal magazines
  • Talk to your hairstylist about the different options
  • Book a hair trial for your bridesmaids
  • Get feedback from your bridesmaids
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your wedding dress
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits the bridesmaids' personal style

FAQs

What are some popular bridesmaid hairstyles?

Some popular bridesmaid hairstyles include updos, braids, half up/half down styles, and loose waves.

How much does it cost to get bridesmaid hair done?

The cost of getting bridesmaid hair done will depend on the hairstylist and the type of hairstyle you choose. On average, bridesmaid hair can cost anywhere from $50 to $150 per person.

How far in advance should I book my hairstylist?

You should book your hairstylist at least 3-6 months in advance to ensure that they are available on your wedding day.

Hair Care for Bridesmaids

It's important for bridesmaids to take care of their hair leading up to the wedding day. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools

By following these tips and ideas, you can decide on the perfect bridesmaid hair for your big day. Remember to consider the type of wedding, the bridesmaids' hair length and texture, their face shape, and personal style. Book a hair trial and get feedback from your bridesmaids to make a final decision. And don't forget to take care of your hair leading up to the wedding day!
