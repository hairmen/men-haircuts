How To Measure Your Face To See What Shape It Is

Written by Robby Aug 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Knowing your face shape is important when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and even glasses. In this article, we will guide you on how to measure your face to see what shape it is. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Measure Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Measuring Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

How to Measure Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand the different face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Now, let's move on to the measuring process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to measure your face shape:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back and away from your face.
  2. Take a measuring tape and measure across your forehead, from the widest point to the other widest point.
  3. Measure your cheekbones, from the widest point to the other widest point.
  4. Measure your jawline, from the widest point to the other widest point.
  5. Measure the length of your face, from your hairline to your chin.

Once you have all these measurements, compare them to determine your face shape.

Tips for Measuring Your Face Shape

Here are some tips to keep in mind when measuring your face shape:

  • Make sure you are measuring the widest part of each section.
  • Use a flexible measuring tape to ensure accurate measurements.
  • Don't pull the measuring tape too tight or too loose.
  • Measure each section twice to ensure accuracy.

FAQs

Q: What if my measurements fall in between two face shapes?

A: If your measurements fall in between two face shapes, you may have a combination of both. In this case, choose the face shape that is closest to your measurements.

Q: What if my face shape is not listed?

A: If your face shape is not listed, it may be a unique shape or a combination of multiple shapes. In this case, it is best to consult a hairstylist or makeup artist for personalized advice.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.
  • Oval face: lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will suit your face shape.
  • Square face: soften your angles with wavy or curly hair, or a side parting.
  • Heart face: balance out your forehead with side-swept bangs, and add volume to the lower half of your face with layers.
  • Diamond face: add width to your forehead with bangs, and soften your cheekbones with layered hair.
  • Triangle face: balance out your jawline with volume at the top of your head, such as a messy bun or high ponytail.

There you have it, a complete guide on how to measure your face to see what shape it is. Use these tips and tricks to choose the right hairstyle and makeup for your face shape, and rock your look with confidence!
