Men s Hairstyles Fade Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle that will make you stand out, you can't go wrong with the men's hairstyles fade sides long top. This hairstyle has been popular for years and it's not going away anytime soon. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips to maintain it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The men's hairstyles fade sides long top is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut short, gradually blending into longer hair on the top of the head. This hairstyle can be customized to fit any hair type or face shape, making it a popular choice among men of all ages.

How to Get the Men's Hairstyles Fade Sides Long Top

If you're interested in getting the men's hairstyles fade sides long top, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found a professional, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting the men's hairstyles fade sides long top is to choose the right length for your hair. Depending on your hair type and face shape, you may want to go for a shorter or longer cut. It's important to discuss your options with your barber or hairstylist to ensure that you get the best cut for your individual needs.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

The next step is to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short. The length should gradually decrease from the top of your head to the bottom. This is where the "fade" comes in. The shorter the hair, the more defined the fade will be.

Step 3: Blend the Top

Once the sides and back are cut, your barber or hairstylist will blend the top of your hair into the shorter hair on the sides and back. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 4: Style the Top

The final step is to style the top of your hair. Depending on your personal preference and hair type, you can choose to slick it back, leave it messy, or part it to the side. The options are endless!

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Hairstyles Fade Sides Long Top

To keep your men's hairstyles fade sides long top looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in a quality hair styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Consider getting a hairline trim to keep your edges looking sharp.

FAQ about Men's Hairstyles Fade Sides Long Top

Q: Is the men's hairstyles fade sides long top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can be customized to fit any hair type, from straight to curly.

Q: How often do I need to visit the barber for touch-ups?

A: It's recommended to visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups.

Q: Can I style the top of my hair differently every day?

A: Yes, the men's hairstyles fade sides long top is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways.

Hair Care for Men's Hairstyles Fade Sides Long Top

To keep your men's hairstyles fade sides long top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes:

  • Using a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoiding over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage.
  • Using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Using a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools on your hair.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock the men's hairstyles fade sides long top with confidence and style.
