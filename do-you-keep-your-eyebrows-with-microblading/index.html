Do You Keep Your Eyebrows With Microblading

Written by Eveline Aug 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Microblading is a popular technique for enhancing eyebrows. It’s a semi-permanent makeup procedure that involves using a handheld tool to create small, hair-like strokes on the surface of the skin. The result is natural-looking, fuller eyebrows that can last up to two years with proper care. In this article, we’ll answer the question “Do you keep your eyebrows with microblading?” and provide tips on how to maintain your microbladed eyebrows.

Description

Microblading is a type of cosmetic tattooing that involves using a special tool to deposit pigment into the skin. The tool has a series of fine needles that create hair-like strokes on the surface of the skin. The pigment is then absorbed by the skin, creating the appearance of fuller, thicker eyebrows. The procedure typically takes around two hours to complete and can last up to two years with proper care.

How to

If you’re considering microblading your eyebrows, it’s important to find a qualified and experienced technician. Look for someone who is licensed and has a good reputation in your area. Before the procedure, your technician will discuss your desired eyebrow shape and color with you. They will then numb the area with a topical anesthetic to minimize discomfort.

During the procedure, your technician will use the microblading tool to create hair-like strokes on the surface of your skin. They will carefully choose the color of pigment that best matches your natural eyebrow hair. After the procedure, you may experience some redness and mild swelling. You’ll need to avoid getting your eyebrows wet for a few days and apply a healing ointment to the area.

Step by Step

Here are the steps involved in getting microbladed eyebrows:

  1. Research and find a qualified technician
  2. Discuss your desired eyebrow shape and color with your technician
  3. Numb the area with a topical anesthetic
  4. Use the microblading tool to create hair-like strokes on the skin
  5. Choose the color of pigment that best matches your natural eyebrow hair
  6. Avoid getting your eyebrows wet for a few days after the procedure
  7. Apply a healing ointment to the area

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your microbladed eyebrows:

  • Avoid getting your eyebrows wet for a few days after the procedure
  • Avoid touching your eyebrows or picking at scabs
  • Avoid using makeup or skincare products on your eyebrows for at least a week after the procedure
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds
  • Apply sunscreen to your eyebrows when you’re outside
  • Avoid swimming or sweating excessively
  • Touch up your eyebrows every 12-18 months to maintain their appearance

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about microblading:

How long does microblading last?

Microblading can last up to two years with proper care. However, you may need to touch up your eyebrows every 12-18 months to maintain their appearance.

Is microblading painful?

The procedure can be mildly uncomfortable, but your technician will numb the area with a topical anesthetic to minimize any pain or discomfort.

Can I wear makeup on my eyebrows after microblading?

You should avoid using makeup or skincare products on your eyebrows for at least a week after the procedure. After that, you can apply makeup as usual, but be gentle when removing it.

Is microblading safe?

Microblading is generally safe when performed by a qualified and experienced technician. However, there is always a risk of infection or allergic reaction, so it’s important to follow proper aftercare instructions.

Hair Care

If you have microbladed eyebrows, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain their appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals or fragrances that could irritate your skin
  • Avoid using hair dyes or bleach on your eyebrows
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your eyebrows
  • Avoid using heated styling tools near your eyebrows
  • Protect your eyebrows from sun exposure by wearing a hat or using sunscreen

Overall, microblading can be a great way to enhance your eyebrows and achieve a more polished look. By following proper aftercare instructions and taking care of your hair, you can maintain your microbladed eyebrows for up to two years or more.
