How To Turn Straight Hair Curly Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch up your look? Well, you're in luck because we're here to show you how to turn straight hair curly guys! From using products to heat styling, we've got you covered. Here's a breakdown of everything you need to know.

How to Turn Straight Hair Curly Guys

Before we get started, you should know that there's no one-size-fits-all solution when it comes to turning straight hair curly. Everyone's hair is different, and what works for one person may not work for another. With that said, there are a few methods that you can try.

Method 1: Use Products

The easiest way to turn straight hair curly guys is by using products. Here are a few products that you can try:

  • Curl Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Curl Defining Cream or Gel
  • Hair Mousse

When using these products, make sure to follow the instructions carefully. Typically, you'll need to apply the product to damp hair and then scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation. You can then either air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Method 2: Heat Styling

If you want more dramatic curls, you can try heat styling. Here's how:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair and use a curling iron or wand to curl each section.
  4. Once you've curled all sections, use a hairspray to set the curls.

When using heat styling tools, be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair. You should also give your hair a break from heat styling every few days to prevent damage.

Tips for Turning Straight Hair Curly Guys

Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Experiment with different products and methods to find what works best for you.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair after applying products, as this can disrupt the curl formation.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's designed for curly hair. This can help enhance your curls and make them easier to style.

FAQ About Turning Straight Hair Curly Guys

Q: Can I turn my straight hair curly permanently?

A: Yes, you can. However, the only way to do this is by getting a perm, which is a chemical process that can be damaging to your hair.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on the method you use and how well you maintain your curls. If you use products, your curls may last a day or two. If you use heat styling, your curls may last several days.

Q: Will turning my straight hair curly damage it?

A: If you're using heat styling tools or chemicals, there is a risk of damage. However, if you take care of your hair and use protective products, you can minimize the risk of damage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a different type of care than straight hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to turn straight hair curly guys, it's time to give it a try! Remember to be patient and experiment with different methods until you find what works best for you.
