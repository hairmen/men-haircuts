Best Long Hairstyles for Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is versatile and can carry off a variety of hairstyles. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. In this article, we have curated a list of the best long hairstyles for oval face that will help you narrow down your choices.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is longer than the width. This face shape is considered to be the most balanced and proportionate, and almost all hairstyles suit it. However, certain long hairstyles can accentuate your best features and enhance your overall look.

How to Choose the Best Long Hairstyle for Oval Face?

When choosing the best long hairstyle for oval face, consider the following factors:

  • Face shape
  • Hair texture
  • Personal style
  • Length of hair

Keep in mind that you don't have to stick to one particular style. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that you feel most confident in.

Step by Step Guide for Best Long Hairstyles for Oval Face

1. Layered Cut

A layered cut works well for oval face shape as it adds volume and movement to the hair. Start by blow-drying your hair and then use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish off with a texturizing spray to add definition to the layers.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and highlight your best features. Sweep your bangs to one side and use a round brush to blow-dry them. Finish off with a hairspray to keep them in place.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for a casual and effortless look. Apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair and create natural-looking waves.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Oval Face

  • Regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip natural oils and cause dryness

FAQs

1. Can oval face shape carry off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval face shape is the most versatile face shape and can carry off almost any hairstyle.

2. Is it necessary to have long hair for oval face shape?

No, oval face shape can carry off short, medium, or long hairstyles. It depends on your personal style and preference.

3. Which is the best hairstyle for oval face shape?

There is no one best hairstyle for oval face shape. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style.

Hair Care for Long Hairstyles for Oval Face

Long hairstyles require extra care and attention to keep them healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Apply a leave-in conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends. Avoid using hot styling tools frequently and use a heat protectant spray before using them. Finally, maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle to keep your hair healthy and strong.
