How To Take Care Of Your Weave Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on hair extensions or weave hair only for it to get damaged after a few weeks? Taking care of your weave hair is essential to ensure it lasts long and looks great. In this guide, we will walk you through how to take care of your weave hair, step by step.

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Weave Hair

Description

Weave hair is a popular hair extension technique that involves sewing hair extensions onto a braided base. It is a great way to add length and volume to your hair without damaging your natural hair. However, weave hair requires proper care to ensure it lasts long, looks great, and does not cause damage to your natural hair. Taking care of your weave hair involves regular washing, conditioning, and styling.

How to Take Care of Your Weave Hair

Here are some tips on how to take care of your weave hair:

1. Detangle Your Weave Hair Regularly

Detangle your weave hair regularly to prevent tangling and matting. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently comb through your weave, starting from the ends and working your way up.

2. Wash Your Weave Hair

Wash your weave hair every 2-3 weeks to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your weave hair clean and hydrated.

3. Condition Your Weave Hair

Condition your weave hair after every wash to keep it soft, smooth, and tangle-free. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a deep conditioner and let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

4. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools on your weave hair as it can cause damage and shorten its lifespan. If you must use heat, apply a heat protectant spray and use a low heat setting.

5. Protect Your Weave Hair at Night

Tie your weave hair in a satin or silk scarf at night to prevent tangling and frizz. You can also use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your weave hair while you sleep.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to take care of your weave hair:

Step 1: Detangle Your Weave Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently detangle your weave hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

Step 2: Wet Your Weave Hair

Wet your weave hair with warm water, making sure it is thoroughly saturated.

Step 3: Apply Shampoo

Apply a sulfate-free shampoo to your scalp and massage it into your weave hair, working up a lather. Rinse your weave hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 4: Apply Conditioner

Apply a moisturizing conditioner to your weave hair, focusing on the ends. Let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out with warm water.

Step 5: Dry Your Weave Hair

Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water from your weave hair. Let it air dry or use a low heat setting on your blow dryer.

Step 6: Style Your Weave Hair

Style your weave hair using heat-free methods such as braiding, twisting, or using flexi rods. Avoid using heat styling tools on your weave hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Weave Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your weave hair:

1. Avoid Using Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products such as gels, hairsprays, and pomades on your weave hair as they can cause buildup and weigh it down.

2. Protect Your Weave Hair from Chlorine

If you swim regularly, wear a swim cap or apply a leave-in conditioner before getting into the pool to protect your weave hair from chlorine.

3. Don't Scratch Your Scalp

Avoid scratching your scalp as it can cause damage to your weave hair and natural hair. Use a scalp oil or a soothing spray to relieve itching.

FAQs

1. How long can I keep my weave hair in?

You can keep your weave hair in for 6-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of it.

2. Can I wash my weave hair every week?

It is not recommended to wash your weave hair every week as it can cause damage and shorten its lifespan. Wash your weave hair every 2-3 weeks instead.

3. Can I use oil on my weave hair?

Avoid using heavy oils on your weave hair as they can cause buildup and weigh it down. Use a lightweight oil or a serum instead.

Hair Care Products for Your Weave Hair

Here are some hair care products that you can use to take care of your weave hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo
  • Moisturizing conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Deep conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Scalp oil
  • Soothing spray
  • Lightweight oil or serum

By following these tips and using the right products, you can take care of your weave hair and ensure it lasts long and looks great.
