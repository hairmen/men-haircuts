How To Stop Relaxed Hair From Breakage And Shedding

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have relaxed hair, you know that breakage and shedding can be a common problem. But don't worry, there are steps you can take to prevent this from happening. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to stop relaxed hair from breakage and shedding.

How to

First and foremost, it's important to understand that relaxed hair is fragile and needs to be handled with care. This means avoiding harsh chemicals, excessive heat, and rough handling.

Step 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Start by using a gentle shampoo that doesn't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that is specifically designed for relaxed hair.

Step 2: Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning is essential for relaxed hair. It helps to restore moisture and strengthen the hair. Use a deep conditioner at least once a week, and leave it on for at least 30 minutes.

Step 3: Avoid Excessive Heat

Heat can be damaging to relaxed hair, so it's important to avoid excessive heat. This means limiting the use of flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers.

Step 4: Use Protective Styles

Protective styles like braids, twists, and buns can help to prevent breakage and shedding. These styles keep your hair protected and give it a break from styling.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help prevent breakage and shedding:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to stop relaxed hair from breakage and shedding:

Q: How often should I deep condition my relaxed hair?

A: It's recommended to deep condition your relaxed hair at least once a week.

Q: Can I still use heat on my relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can still use heat on your relaxed hair, but it's important to use it sparingly and always use a heat protectant.

Q: How do I know if my hair is breaking or shedding?

A: Breaking hair will have a clean break, while shedding hair will have a white bulb at the end.

Hair Care

Taking care of your relaxed hair is essential for preventing breakage and shedding. Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid using too many hair products
  • Don't overwash your hair

By following these tips and taking care of your relaxed hair, you can prevent breakage and shedding and keep your hair healthy and strong.
