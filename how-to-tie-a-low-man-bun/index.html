How To Tie A Low Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and practical way to manage your long hair, the low man bun might be just what you need. It's a classic hairstyle that looks great on any occasion, whether you're going to work or out with friends. In this post, we'll show you how to tie a low man bun in a few easy steps. We'll also share some tips and hair care advice to help you keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Gather Your Hair
  • Step 2: Tie a Ponytail
  • Step 3: Wrap Your Hair around the Elastic Band
  • Step 4: Secure the Bun with Bobby Pins
  • Tips for Tying a Low Man Bun
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

The first step in tying a low man bun is to gather your hair at the nape of your neck. Use your fingers to comb through your hair and remove any tangles. Then, brush your hair back and use an elastic band to tie a ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 2: Tie a Ponytail

Next, take the ponytail and twist it around the elastic band. Keep twisting until the ponytail forms a tight spiral. Once you've twisted the ponytail, use another elastic band to tie it in place. This will create the base for your low man bun.

Step 3: Wrap Your Hair around the Elastic Band

Now it's time to create the bun itself. Take the twisted ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band. Keep wrapping until you've formed a tight bun. Make sure the bun is positioned low on the back of your head, just above your neck. You may need to adjust the size of the bun by loosening or tightening the elastic band.

Step 4: Secure the Bun with Bobby Pins

To keep your low man bun in place, you'll need to use some bobby pins. Take a few bobby pins and insert them into the bun, securing it to your head. Make sure the bobby pins are hidden from view and that your bun looks neat and tidy.

Tips for Tying a Low Man Bun

  • Use a brush or comb to make sure your hair is free from tangles before tying your man bun.
  • Experiment with different bun sizes to find the one that looks best on you.
  • If you have particularly long or thick hair, you may need to use more than one elastic band to secure your bun.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray or other products to keep your bun looking neat and tidy throughout the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I tie a low man bun if I have short hair?

No, the low man bun is a style that works best for men with medium to long hair. If you have short hair, you may want to consider a different hairstyle.

Is it okay to wear a low man bun to work?

Yes, the low man bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of settings, including the workplace. However, if you work in a very conservative environment, you may want to check with your employer before trying this style.

How often should I wash my hair when wearing a low man bun?

It's important to keep your hair clean and healthy when wearing a low man bun. Depending on your hair type and lifestyle, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With these simple tips and steps, you can create a stylish low man bun that looks great and keeps your long hair under control. Whether you're going to work or out with friends, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
