Best Hairstyles For Round Faces And Thin Hair

Written by Petter Oct 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face and thin hair, you might feel like you're limited in your hairstyle choices. But fear not, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your features and give your hair some volume and texture. In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for round faces and thin hair, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Best Hairstyles for Round Faces and Thin Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hairstyles for your face shape is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and look at your face in the mirror. There are several common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

If you have a round face, your face is widest at the cheeks and you have a rounded chin.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces and Thin Hair

Now that you know your face shape, let's talk about the best hairstyles for round faces and thin hair. These hairstyles are designed to flatter your features and give your hair some volume and texture:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help add volume and texture to thin hair, while also elongating the face and creating a more flattering shape. Consider adding layers that start at the cheekbones and gradually get longer.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a great choice for round faces, as it can help create the illusion of a longer face. The side-swept bangs also help to frame the face and add some texture.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice, but it can also be incredibly flattering on round faces. Opt for a layered pixie cut with lots of texture to add some volume and dimension to your hair.

4. Shoulder-Length Hair with Layers

Shoulder-length hair with layers can be a great choice for round faces, as it can add volume and texture without overwhelming your features. Consider adding face-framing layers to add some dimension to your hair.

5. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle can be a great choice for round faces, as it can help elongate the face and create a more flattering shape. Consider adding some volume to the top of your hair to create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that works for your face shape and hair type, it's important to know how to style it properly to get the best results. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you have thin hair, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture and body.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Now that you know how to style your hair, let's talk about some tips for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face with thin hair?

The best haircut for a round face with thin hair is one that adds volume and texture, such as long layers, a bob with side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut with lots of texture.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face and thin hair?

Absolutely! Long hair can be a great choice for round faces, as long as you add some volume and texture to your hair to prevent it from falling flat.

What products should I use to add volume to my hair?

You can use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a texturizing spray or mousse, to add volume to your hair.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round faces and thin hair, as well as some tips for maintaining healthy hair, you can confidently rock your new look. Remember to always listen to your hair and choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
