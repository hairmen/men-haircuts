Men s Haircut Longer On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle that will make you stand out, the men's haircut longer on top might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been around for a while and it's still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect men's haircut longer on top, how to take care of it and answer some of the most frequently asked questions about this style.

Description

The men's haircut longer on top is a hairstyle where the hair on the top of the head is left longer than the hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast that makes the hair on the top stand out. This style is versatile and can be adapted to different hair types and face shapes.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Longer on Top

If you want to get the men's haircut longer on top, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who knows how to cut this style. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair on the top will depend on your preference and the shape of your face. Some men prefer a longer, more dramatic look, while others prefer a more subtle look. Talk to your barber or stylist about what length would be best for you.

Step 2: Decide on the Style

The men's haircut longer on top can be styled in different ways. You can go for a messy look, a slicked-back look, a quiff, or a pompadour, among others. Your barber or stylist can help you decide which style would look best on you.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on the Sides and Back

The hair on the sides and back should be cut shorter than the hair on the top. Your barber or stylist will use clippers to achieve the desired length. The length will depend on your preference and the style you've chosen.

Step 4: Cut the Hair on the Top

The hair on the top should be cut to the desired length using scissors. Your barber or stylist may use texturizing shears to add texture to the hair and create a more natural look.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once the hair is cut, your barber or stylist will style it according to your preference. They may use products like pomade, wax, or gel to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Haircut Longer on Top

Here are some tips to help you maintain the men's haircut longer on top:

- Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable. - Use a comb or brush to style your hair. - Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the style you've chosen. - Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups.

FAQs

Q: Is the men's haircut longer on top suitable for all face shapes?

A: The men's haircut longer on top can be adapted to different face shapes. Your barber or stylist can help you choose a style that suits your face shape.

Q: How often should I visit my barber or stylist for touch-ups?

A: It's recommended to visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups.

Q: Can I style the men's haircut longer on top myself?

A: Yes, you can style the men's haircut longer on top yourself. However, it's recommended to get some advice from your barber or stylist on how to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

The men's haircut longer on top requires some maintenance to keep it looking good. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening. - Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF. - Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the men's haircut longer on top, it's time to book an appointment with your barber or stylist and try this trendy style for yourself. With the right cut and care, your hair can look stylish and versatile all year round.
