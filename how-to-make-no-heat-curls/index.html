How To Make No Heat Curls

Written by Aprilia Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of damaging your hair with heat styling tools? Look no further, because we have the solution for you! No heat curls are a great way to achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without any heat damage. In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to make no heat curls, as well as tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Make No Heat Curls

There are several methods to make no heat curls, and we will be discussing some of the most popular ones.

Method 1: Overnight Braids

This method involves braiding your hair before going to bed, and waking up with beautiful curls in the morning.

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry until slightly damp.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections, and start braiding each section tightly. The tighter the braid, the tighter the curl.

Step 3: Secure the end of each braid with a hair tie, and go to bed.

Step 4: In the morning, gently undo the braids and finger comb your hair to achieve your desired look.

Method 2: Twist and Pin

This method involves twisting your hair and pinning it in place to create curls.

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry until slightly damp.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections, and twist each section tightly.

Step 3: Secure each twist with a bobby pin, and leave them in place for a few hours or overnight.

Step 4: Remove the bobby pins and gently finger comb your hair to achieve your desired look.

Method 3: Headband Curls

This method involves using a headband to create curls.

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry until slightly damp.

Step 2: Place a headband over your head, and tuck your hair into the headband.

Step 3: Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the headband, tucking the ends under. Continue until all of your hair is wrapped around the headband.

Step 4: Leave the headband in place for a few hours or overnight, then remove it and gently finger comb your hair to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Making No Heat Curls

- Use a styling product to help hold the curls in place.

- For tighter curls, braid or twist your hair tightly. For looser curls, braid or twist your hair loosely.

- Sleep on a satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz.

- If you have naturally straight hair, try using a salt spray or mousse to help create texture before braiding or twisting your hair.

FAQ

Q: How long do I need to leave the braids/twists/headband in place?

A: It depends on the thickness of your hair and the tightness of the braids/twists/headband. Generally, it's best to leave them in for at least a few hours, or overnight for best results.

Q: Will no heat curls work on all hair types?

A: Yes, no heat curls can work on all hair types, but the results may vary depending on the texture of your hair.

Q: Will no heat curls damage my hair?

A: No, no heat curls are a great way to achieve beautiful curls without any heat damage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially when using styling products or tools. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Use a heat protectant if you do use heat styling tools.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

- Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, no heat curls without damaging your hair. Give these methods a try and see which one works best for you!
