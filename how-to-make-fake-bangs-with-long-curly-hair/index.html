How To Make Fake Bangs With Long Curly Hair

Written by Frank Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long curly hair and want to switch things up without committing to a haircut, fake bangs may be the perfect solution for you. In this article, we'll go over how to make fake bangs with long curly hair, as well as tips for styling and caring for your new look.

Description

Fake bangs are a great way to experiment with your look without making a permanent change. They can be created using your own hair or with the help of extensions. With long curly hair, the process may seem a bit intimidating, but it's actually quite simple.

How to

Here's how to make fake bangs with long curly hair:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning off the front portion of your hair that you want to use for your bangs. Use a comb or your fingers to create a triangular section of hair that starts at your hairline and ends at the crown of your head.

Step 2: Create Your Bangs

Once you have your section of hair, you can start creating your bangs. Take small sections of hair at a time and twist them tightly. Then, use bobby pins to secure the twists in place, creating a curved shape that mimics the look of bangs. Continue this process until all of your hair is twisted and pinned in place.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now that your fake bangs are in place, you can style the rest of your hair as desired. You can leave your curls loose or pull them back into a ponytail or bun. You can also add accessories like headbands or clips to accentuate your new look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating and styling your fake bangs:

  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair for a seamless look.
  • Experiment with the placement of your bangs to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Use a curl-defining product to enhance your natural curls and add definition to your bangs.
  • Add a bit of hairspray to your bangs to help them hold their shape throughout the day.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about fake bangs:

Can I use extensions to create fake bangs?

Yes, you can use clip-in or tape-in extensions to create fake bangs. Simply clip or tape the extensions into the front portion of your hair, then style them as desired.

How long will my fake bangs last?

Your fake bangs should last until you wash your hair. To prolong their lifespan, avoid touching or playing with them too much throughout the day.

Can I wear fake bangs with any hairstyle?

Yes, fake bangs can be worn with a variety of hairstyles, from loose curls to updos. Just be sure to style your hair in a way that complements your new bangs.

Hair Care

When wearing fake bangs, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain their appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily create fake bangs with long curly hair and rock a new look without the commitment of a haircut. Have fun experimenting with different styles and finding the perfect look for you!
