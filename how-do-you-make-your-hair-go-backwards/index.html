How To Make Your Hair Go Backwards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch it up a bit? One way to do that is by making your hair go backwards. It's a simple and sleek hairstyle that can be achieved with just a few steps. In this post, we'll be discussing how to make your hair go backwards, tips for maintaining the style, and answers to some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Go Backwards
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • FAQ

How to Make Your Hair Go Backwards

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to making your hair go backwards is to wash your hair thoroughly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type to ensure that your hair is clean and healthy. Rinse your hair with cold water to help seal the cuticles and add shine to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel as this can damage your hair. Instead, pat your hair dry to remove excess water.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a gel, pomade, or wax. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Backwards

Using a comb, comb your hair backwards. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back. Use your fingers to style your hair in the direction that you want it to go.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, blow dry your hair in the direction that you combed it. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style it the way you want it to look. You can use a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your backwards hairstyle:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I make my hair go backwards if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still make your hair go backwards if you have short hair. Use a styling product that is suited for short hair and comb your hair backwards.

Can I make my hair go backwards without using styling product?

It is possible to make your hair go backwards without using styling product, but it may not hold the style for very long. Using a styling product will help hold the style in place for longer.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving any hairstyle. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as this can make your hair look greasy.

There you have it, a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair go backwards. We hope that this post has been helpful in achieving the hairstyle that you desire. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful hair.
