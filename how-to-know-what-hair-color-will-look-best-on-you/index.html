How To Know What Hair Color Will Look Best On You

Written by Aprilia Aug 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking of changing your hair color, you may be wondering which shade will suit you best. With so many colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to determine what hair color will look best on you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining what hair color will look best on you is to consider your skin tone. There are three basic skin tones - cool, warm, and neutral. If you have cool undertones, you'll look best in cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown shades. If you have warm undertones, you'll look best in warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and warm brown shades. Those with neutral undertones can wear both warm and cool hair colors with ease.

Next, consider your eye color. Your eye color can also help determine which hair color will compliment you best. If you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colors like platinum, ash blonde, and cool brown shades will look great on you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and warm brown shades will be more flattering.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to determine what hair color will look best on you:

  1. Determine your skin tone - cool, warm, or neutral.
  2. Consider your eye color - blue or green eyes look best with cool hair colors, while brown or hazel eyes look best with warm hair colors.
  3. Think about your natural hair color - if you have light hair, you may want to go for a lighter shade, while those with dark hair can experiment with darker shades.
  4. Consider your personality - if you're outgoing and adventurous, you may want to try bold hair colors like red or purple. If you're more reserved, you may prefer more natural-looking hair colors.
  5. Consult with a professional - a hair stylist can help you determine what hair color will look best on you based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider the maintenance involved - if you're not willing to spend a lot of time and money on upkeep, choose a hair color that will grow out gracefully.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment - it's just hair, and it will grow back!
  • Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to get an idea of what different hair colors will look like on you.
  • Take good care of your hair to keep your color looking vibrant and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair color:

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones, try to identify the dominant one. If you're still unsure, consult with a professional.

What if I have a cool skin tone but warm eyes?

Don't worry too much about matching your eye color to your hair color - it's more important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.

What if I don't like the hair color I chose?

Hair color can always be changed - if you don't like the color you chose, you can always try a different shade or go back to your natural color.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a hair color, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause your color to fade.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and taking the time to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color, you can be sure that you'll look and feel your best with your new hair color.
