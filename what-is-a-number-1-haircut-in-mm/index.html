What Is A Number 1 Haircut In Mm

Written by Daniel Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're new to getting haircuts or are looking for a new style, you may have heard about the "number 1" haircut. But what exactly does that mean? In this article, we'll explain what a number 1 haircut is in millimeters (mm) and give you some tips on how to achieve this style.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Get a Number 1 Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Number 1 Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Description:

A number 1 haircut is a very short buzz cut that is achieved using clippers. The number 1 refers to the guard that is attached to the clippers, which is usually about 1/8 of an inch or 3 mm in length. This style is popular among men who want a low-maintenance, easy-to-maintain look.

How to Get a Number 1 Haircut:

The best way to get a number 1 haircut is to visit a professional barber or hair stylist. They will have the tools and expertise needed to give you an even, clean cut. However, if you're feeling brave, you can also try cutting your hair at home using clippers.

Tools Needed:

  • Clippers with a number 1 guard
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here's how to achieve a number 1 haircut at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It's easier to cut hair when it's dry, so make sure your hair is completely dry before you start.
  2. Attach the number 1 guard to your clippers.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up, using the clippers to cut your hair to an even length.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers missed.
  5. Use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.
  6. Clean up the hairline around your ears and neck using the clippers or scissors.
  7. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even and clean.

Remember, it's always better to start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always go back and trim more if you want a shorter look.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 1 Haircut:

Now that you have a number 1 haircut, here are some tips for keeping it looking great:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 2-3 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that are heavy or greasy, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider using a styling product like hair gel or pomade to add texture and volume to your hair.

FAQs:

Q: Will a number 1 haircut look good on me?

A: A number 1 haircut is a very short style that works well for men with a round or oval face shape. It may not be the best choice for men with a square or angular face shape.

Q: Will a number 1 haircut make my hair grow back thicker?

A: No, cutting your hair short will not make it grow back thicker. This is a common myth.

Q: Can I style my number 1 haircut?

A: Yes, you can use styling products like hair gel or pomade to add texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair:

Short hair requires a different type of hair care than longer hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can dry it out and make it brittle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know what a number 1 haircut is in mm and how to achieve it, you can decide if this style is right for you. Whether you visit a professional barber or cut your hair at home, a number 1 haircut is a low-maintenance, stylish look that is perfect for men who want to keep their hair short and simple.
