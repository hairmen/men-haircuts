How To Cut The Back Of Your Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting the back of your hair straight can be a challenging task, especially if you're doing it on your own. It's essential to take the proper steps to ensure that your hair is cut evenly and doesn't look lopsided. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your hair straight.

How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your hair straight:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step is to gather all the necessary tools you'll need to cut your hair. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. Make sure that your scissors are sharp to ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before starting the cutting process. Ensure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and to create a smooth surface for cutting.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. Create a central parting and divide your hair into two equal sections at the back of your head. Secure each section with a hair clip.

Step 5: Cut the Hair in the Middle

Start with the middle section of your hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Be sure to cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Cut the Hair on the Left Side

Untie the hair clip on the left side and comb through the hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across, following the line of the hair in the middle section. Repeat this process on the right side.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Check the back of your hair in the handheld mirror to ensure that it's even. If you notice any areas that need to be trimmed, repeat the cutting process until you achieve the desired length.

Step 8: Add Texture

If you want to add texture to your hair, use the tip of the scissors to create small, angled cuts. This will give your hair a natural and textured look.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

After cutting the back of your hair straight, style your hair as desired. You can blow-dry your hair for a sleek look or let it air dry for a natural look.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Hair Straight

Here are some tips to help you cut the back of your hair straight:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure an even cut
  • Check the back of your hair in a handheld mirror to ensure evenness
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid any mistakes
  • Take your time and be patient

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I cut the back of my hair straight on my own?

Yes, you can cut the back of your hair straight on your own. However, it's essential to take the proper steps to ensure an even and straight cut.

What tools do I need to cut the back of my hair straight?

You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror to cut the back of your hair straight.

How can I ensure that my hair is cut evenly?

You can ensure that your hair is cut evenly by cutting small sections of hair at a time, checking the back of your hair in a handheld mirror, and taking your time.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth

Now that you know how to cut the back of your hair straight, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to ensure an even and straight cut.
