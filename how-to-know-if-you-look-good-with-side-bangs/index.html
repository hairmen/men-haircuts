How To Know If You Look Good With Side Bangs

Written by Petter Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Side bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of sophistication and style to your look. However, not everyone can pull off the look. So, how do you know if you look good with side bangs? In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks to help you determine if side bangs are right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Side bangs are a hairstyle where the hair is cut to hang over one side of the forehead, rather than being swept back or to the side. Side bangs can be short or long, and can be cut to blend into the rest of the hair or left as a distinct section. The key to determining if side bangs are right for you is to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How To

The first step in determining if you look good with side bangs is to consider your face shape. Side bangs are flattering on most face shapes, but work particularly well on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid side bangs, as they can make your face appear fuller. If you have a square face, you may want to opt for longer side bangs that blend into the rest of your hair, rather than shorter, more distinct bangs.

The next step is to consider your hair texture. Side bangs work well on most hair types, but may require some styling if you have thick or curly hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for shorter, more textured side bangs, rather than longer, heavier bangs that can weigh down your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to determine if you look good with side bangs:

  1. Consider your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture
  3. Look at pictures of hairstyles with side bangs to get an idea of what they look like
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if side bangs are right for you
  5. If you decide to get side bangs, work with your hairstylist to choose a length and style that flatters your face shape and hair texture

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you decide to get side bangs:

  • Start with shorter side bangs and work your way up to longer bangs, if you're not sure how you'll like the look
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to help blend the side bangs into the rest of your hair
  • Try styling your side bangs in different ways to find a look that works for you
  • Use a flat iron or round brush to style your side bangs, depending on the look you want to achieve

FAQ

Can I wear side bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can wear side bangs if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use a flat iron or round brush to style your bangs to get the look you want.

What length should I choose for my side bangs?

The length of your side bangs will depend on your face shape and personal style. As a general rule, shorter side bangs work well on round faces, while longer side bangs work well on square faces.

Hair Care

If you decide to get side bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep your bangs looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools
  • Trim your side bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to help extend the life of your side bangs between washes

Overall, side bangs can be a great way to add some style and sophistication to your look. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can determine if side bangs are right for you. If you decide to get side bangs, work with your hairstylist to choose a length and style that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and take care of your hair to keep your bangs looking their best.
