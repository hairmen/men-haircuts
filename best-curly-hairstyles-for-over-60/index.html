Best Curly Hairstyles For Over 60

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural volume and bounce. However, that doesn't mean we can't have fabulous curls even in our 60s. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock gorgeous curls that will make you look and feel younger. In this article, we'll explore the best curly hairstyles for over 60 and provide tips on how to maintain them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair

If you don't have naturally curly hair, don't worry. You can still achieve beautiful curls with the help of styling tools such as a curling iron or hot rollers. Before curling your hair, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Another option is to get a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that involves wrapping your hair around rods to create curls. However, perms can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to consult with a professional stylist before getting one.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

To style curly hair, you'll need to follow a few simple steps. First, wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Then, apply a curl-defining product such as mousse or gel to wet hair.

Next, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz. If you don't have a diffuser, you can also air dry your hair.

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to define your curls further. You can also use a curling wand to create loose, beachy waves. Finish off with a hairspray or hair oil for shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

To keep your curls looking their best, follow these tips:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair is one that is layered and cut to suit your face shape. A layered haircut will help enhance your curls and add volume.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so you don't need to wash it as often. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week.

Can I still wear my hair up with curly hair?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with curly hair. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style to show off your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair looking its best, it's important to use hair care products that are designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients such as coconut oil or shea butter.

Avoid using hot styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. Deep condition your hair once a week, and use a leave-in conditioner to keep it hydrated.

With the right care and styling techniques, you can rock gorgeous curls even in your 60s. Try out some of these curly hairstyles and see which ones work best for you.
