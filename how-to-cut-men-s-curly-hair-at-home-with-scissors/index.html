How To Cut Men s Curly Hair At Home With Scissors

Written by Daniel Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to save some money and cut your curly hair at home? With the right techniques and tools, it’s possible to achieve a great haircut without visiting a salon. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut men's curly hair at home with scissors. Whether you want to trim your hair, give it a new shape, or just maintain your current style, these tips will help you achieve a clean and professional-looking cut.

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to cut because it tends to bounce and curl as it’s being trimmed. However, with the right approach and tools, you can cut curly hair like a pro. One of the most important things to keep in mind when cutting curly hair is to avoid cutting it too short. Curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries, so if you cut it too short, it may end up looking much shorter than you intended.

How to

Here’s how to cut men's curly hair at home with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will allow you to see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by parting it down the middle and then dividing it into four sections; top, left and right sides, and the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start with the sides of your hair. Using a comb, gently comb out the hair in the section. Use the scissors to cut the hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to cut only small sections at a time, and avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Next, move on to the top of your hair. Comb your hair straight up and start trimming the ends with the scissors. Again, make sure to cut only small sections at a time.

Step 5: Trim the Back

Finally, trim the hair at the back of your head. Use a mirror to see the back of your head, and start trimming the hair from the bottom and working your way up.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Don’t cut your hair too short. Remember that curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.
  • Work slowly and carefully. Take your time and avoid rushing the process.
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head. This will help you achieve a more even cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair while it’s wet?

It’s best to cut curly hair when it’s dry, as wet hair tends to look longer and can be harder to cut accurately.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, you should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always trim a little more hair to even out the cut. If you’re unsure about cutting your hair, it’s best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After cutting your curly hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair, and avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.

With these tips, you can achieve a great haircut at home and save money on salon visits. Remember to take your time, work carefully, and have fun!
