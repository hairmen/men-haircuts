How to Fade Men's Hair with Scissors - Step by Step Guide

If you're looking for a fresh and stylish haircut, then a faded haircut is the way to go. Fading the hair involves tapering the hair length gradually from the top of the head towards the sides and back. It gives a clean and sharp look that suits any face shape. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to fade men's hair with scissors.

How to Fade Men's Hair with Scissors - Step by Step Guide

Before we begin, you'll need a pair of good quality hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Fading Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the fade. You can choose from high, mid, or low fade. High fade means the hair is faded almost to the scalp, mid fade means the hair is faded halfway, and low fade means the hair is faded just above the ears.

Step 2: Cut the Top Hair

Start by cutting the top hair to the desired length. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a vertical direction, holding the hair between your fingers and cutting a little at a time. Make sure to blend the hair in with the faded sides and back.

Step 3: Start Fading the Sides and Back

Use the comb to separate the hair on the sides and back from the top hair. Start fading the hair by cutting the hair at the desired length and blend it in with the top hair. Cut a little at a time and use the comb to check the length and blend.

Step 4: Create a Defined Line

Once you've faded the hair to the desired length, create a defined line between the faded hair and the longer hair on top. Use your scissors to create a straight line and blend the hair in with the faded hair.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Check the hair for evenness by using a mirror. Look at the hair from all angles and make sure it's blended and faded evenly. If you notice any unevenness, go back and blend the hair in.

Tips for Fading Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind while fading men's hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors for a clean cut.
  • Work on one side at a time to ensure evenness.
  • Blend the hair in slowly and gradually.
  • Use a mirror to check for evenness.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.

FAQ - How to Fade Men's Hair with Scissors

What is a faded haircut?

A faded haircut involves tapering the hair length gradually from the top of the head towards the sides and back. It gives a clean and sharp look that suits any face shape.

Can you fade men's hair with scissors?

Yes, you can fade men's hair with scissors. It requires a good pair of hair cutting scissors and a comb to blend the hair in.

Is fading hair difficult?

Fading hair can be difficult for beginners. It requires patience, practice, and attention to detail. However, with time and practice, anyone can master the art of fading hair.

Hair Care After Fading Hair

After fading your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the faded look.

Now that you know how to fade men's hair with scissors, you can try it out and achieve a stylish and fresh look. Follow the steps and tips mentioned above and take your time to get it right. Happy fading!
