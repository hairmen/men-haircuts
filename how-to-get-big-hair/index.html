How To Get Big Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to get big hair! Whether you have naturally fine hair or just want to add some volume and texture to your locks, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to achieve big, voluminous hair that will turn heads. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Get Big Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Big Hair

How to Get Big Hair

If you’re wondering how to get big hair, there are a few things you can do to achieve the look. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing products, like mousse or root-lifting spray, to add texture and lift to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to add height and body.
  • Use hot rollers or a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  • Add extensions or a hair piece for extra volume.

With these simple tips, you can achieve big, beautiful hair in no time!

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to get big hair, let’s dive into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help add texture and lift to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply volumizing product

Apply a volumizing product, like mousse or root-lifting spray, to your damp hair. This will help add texture and lift to your locks.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift and separate your locks. This will help create volume at the roots.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Using a teasing comb, tease your hair at the crown to add height and body. Be sure to only tease the top layer of your hair, and use a light touch to avoid damaging your locks.

Step 5: Curl your hair

Using hot rollers or a curling iron, create loose waves or curls throughout your hair. This will add volume and texture to your locks.

Step 6: Add extensions (optional)

If you want even more volume, consider adding extensions or a hair piece. This will give you the big, voluminous hair you desire!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for getting big hair:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create even more volume.
  • Set your curls with hairspray to help them last longer.
  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to second-day hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting big hair:

Can I get big hair with fine hair?

Yes! With the right products and styling techniques, even those with fine hair can achieve big, voluminous locks.

How often should I wash my hair?

This varies from person to person, but most people can get away with washing their hair every other day. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Hair Care for Big Hair

Now that you’ve achieved big, voluminous hair, it’s important to take care of your locks. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve big, beautiful hair that will turn heads wherever you go. Happy styling!
