Best Women s Hairstyles For Square Faces In 2023

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what hairstyles will flatter your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of options to choose from! In this article, we'll explore the best women's hairstyles for square faces in 2023, including how to style them, tips for maintaining your hair, and more.

What is a Square Face?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and prominent cheekbones. If you have a square face, your features are angular and well-defined. Your goal when choosing a hairstyle should be to soften these angles and create balance.

Best Hairstyles for Square Faces

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face, you want to go for styles that soften your features and create balance. Here are some of the best women's hairstyles for square faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for square faces because they help to soften your features and create movement. To style long layers, add some loose waves or curls with a curling iron or use a straightener for a sleek look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for square faces. They help to create a diagonal line across your face, which softens your features and adds balance. To style side-swept bangs, blow dry them to the side and use a round brush to create volume.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a great option for square faces because it helps to elongate your face and create balance. To style a bob, add some texture with a texturizing spray or use a straightener for a sleek look.

Updo

An updo can also be a great option for square faces. A messy bun or a low ponytail can help to soften your features and create a more feminine look. To style an updo, use bobby pins and hairspray to keep your hair in place.

How to Style Your Hair

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing mousse to add some lift. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a curl-enhancing cream to define your curls.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of your face. If you have a square face, you want to create softness and movement with your hair. This might mean adding some loose waves or curls, or using a round brush to create volume.

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products! The more you play around with your hair, the more you'll learn about what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle for your square face, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Limit your use of hot tools to prevent damage
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

If you have a square face, you should avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline. This might include blunt bangs, a straight bob, or a center part.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a square face?

If you have a square face, you may want to avoid a middle part. A side part can help to create balance and soften your features.

Hair Care

Remember, the key to finding the perfect hairstyle for your square face is to create balance and soften your features. With the right products and styling tools, you can achieve any look you want!
