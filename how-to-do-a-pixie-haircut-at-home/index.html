How To Do A Pixie Haircut At Home

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to transform your look, a pixie haircut may be just what you need. This short and sassy style is perfect for those who want to embrace their natural beauty and show off their unique features. In this article, we'll show you how to do a pixie haircut at home, step by step, so you can save time and money while achieving a stylish new look.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that typically features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. This style is popular among women of all ages and can be customized to suit your unique features and personal style. With a little practice and some basic tools, you can easily do a pixie haircut at home and achieve a trendy, low-maintenance look.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to gather all the necessary tools and materials. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, straighten it using a flat iron to make it easier to cut. Section your hair into four parts, clipping the top part away from the sides and back.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut upward in small sections, making sure to cut at an angle to create a tapered effect. Repeat this process on both sides and the back, working your way up to the top of your head.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Release the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Using your scissors, trim the hair to your desired length, making sure to cut in small sections to avoid cutting too much at once. You can also use your fingers to create texture and movement in the hair.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Use your mirror to check your work from all angles, making sure the hair is evenly cut and blended. Make any necessary adjustments, and use your scissors to trim any stray hairs that may have been missed.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and gradually work your way up to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a comb to comb the hair straight before cutting to ensure an even cut.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting abilities, start with a longer length and gradually cut more as you become more comfortable.
  • Be patient and take your time, as rushing can result in an uneven cut.

FAQ

Can I do a pixie haircut at home?

Yes, with the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can easily do a pixie haircut at home.

What tools do I need to do a pixie haircut at home?

You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, hair clips, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror.

How do I know what length to cut my hair?

It's best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more until you achieve your desired length. You can also consult with a hairstylist or look up pictures for inspiration.

Hair Care

To maintain your pixie haircut, it's important to keep your hair clean and well-groomed. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage. You can also use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily do a pixie haircut at home and achieve a stylish new look. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style or want to show off your unique features, a pixie haircut is a great choice for women of all ages and hair types.
