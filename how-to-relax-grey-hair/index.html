How To Relax Grey Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly grey hair? Relaxing your hair can help to tame it and make it more manageable. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to relax grey hair:

How To

Step 1: Choose the Right Product

When relaxing grey hair, it’s important to choose a product that is specifically designed for grey hair. Look for a relaxer that is gentle on the hair and scalp, and that contains conditioning ingredients to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying the relaxer, you’ll need to prepare your hair. This involves washing your hair thoroughly with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Then, section your hair into four equal parts and detangle each section with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 3: Apply the Relaxer

Now it’s time to apply the relaxer. Follow the instructions on the product carefully, and be sure to wear gloves to protect your hands. Apply the relaxer to each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Rinse Out the Relaxer

After leaving the relaxer on your hair for the recommended amount of time, it’s time to rinse it out. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water, making sure to get all of the product out.

Step 5: Neutralize Your Hair

After rinsing out the relaxer, you’ll need to neutralize your hair to stop the chemical process. Apply the neutralizing shampoo that came with the relaxer, and massage it into your hair and scalp. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Step 6: Condition Your Hair

After neutralizing your hair, it’s important to condition it to restore moisture and prevent breakage. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Tips

  • Do a strand test before applying the relaxer to your entire head of hair to ensure that it won’t cause any damage or breakage.
  • Don’t apply the relaxer to your scalp – only apply it to your hair.
  • Wait at least 2 weeks before coloring or perming your hair after relaxing it.

FAQ

How often should I relax my grey hair?

You should only relax your hair every 8-10 weeks to prevent over-processing and damage.

Can I relax my grey hair at home?

Yes, you can relax your grey hair at home, but it’s important to follow the instructions carefully and do a strand test beforehand.

Will relaxing my grey hair damage it?

Relaxing your hair can cause damage if not done properly or if done too often. It’s important to use a gentle relaxer and to wait at least 8-10 weeks between relaxer applications.

Hair Care

After relaxing your grey hair, it’s important to take good care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and be gentle when brushing or styling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
