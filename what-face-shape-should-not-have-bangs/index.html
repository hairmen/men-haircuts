What Face Shape Should Not Have Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering getting bangs, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape. While certain styles can be flattering on some face shapes, they can look unflattering on others. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at what face shape should not have bangs and why.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Bangs
  4. Tips for Styling Bangs
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Description

When it comes to bangs, not all face shapes are created equal. While some styles can enhance your features and flatter your face shape, others can make your face look wider, shorter, or longer than it actually is. In general, people with square and round face shapes should avoid bangs, while those with oval and heart-shaped faces can pull off a variety of styles.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into which face shapes should avoid bangs, let's first determine how to determine your face shape. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a closer look at your face and observe the following:
    • The width of your forehead
    • The width of your cheekbones
    • The length of your face
    • The shape of your jawline
  3. Once you've observed these features, compare them to the following face shapes:
    • Oval
    • Round
    • Square
    • Heart
    • Diamond
    • Triangle
  4. Determine which face shape you have and proceed to the next section.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Bangs

Now that you know your face shape, here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right bangs:

  1. Oval Face Shape: People with oval faces can pull off a variety of bang styles, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy. Choose a style that complements your hair type and personal style.
  2. Round Face Shape: People with round faces should avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or layered bangs that add height to your face.
  3. Square Face Shape: People with square faces should avoid straight, blunt bangs, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that soften your features.
  4. Heart Face Shape: People with heart-shaped faces can pull off a variety of bang styles, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy. Choose a style that complements your hair type and personal style.
  5. Diamond Face Shape: People with diamond faces should avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look longer. Instead, opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that soften your features.
  6. Triangle Face Shape: People with triangle faces should avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or layered bangs that add height to your face.

Tips for Styling Bangs

Whether you're a bangs newbie or a seasoned pro, here are some tips for styling your bangs:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors and trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and create a sleek look.
  • Tousle your bangs with a texturizing spray to create a messy, beachy look.
  • Experiment with different styles and see what works best for your face shape and personal style.

FAQ

Can people with round faces have bangs?

Yes, people with round faces can have bangs, but they should avoid blunt styles that make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or layered bangs that add height to the face.

Can people with square faces have bangs?

Yes, people with square faces can have bangs, but they should avoid straight, blunt styles that make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that soften the features.

Can people with heart-shaped faces have bangs?

Yes, people with heart-shaped faces can have bangs, and they can pull off a variety of styles, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your bangs looking great:

  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to avoid over-washing and drying them out.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become greasy or out of place.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

By following these tips and choosing the right bangs for your face shape, you can rock this stylish and versatile look with confidence.
