How To Make A Pixie Cut Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to try out a new hairstyle without committing to a drastic cut, a pixie cut wig is a great option. Making your own wig can be a fun and rewarding project. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of making a pixie cut wig.

Table of Contents

  • Materials
  • Preparation
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Sewing the Wig Cap
  • Attaching the Hair
  • Styling the Wig
  • Tips for Making a Pixie Cut Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut Wig

Materials

Before you begin, gather the following materials:

  • A wig cap
  • Wefted hair extensions
  • A wig stand
  • A wig head or mannequin head
  • A needle and thread
  • A pair of scissors
  • A comb or brush

Preparation

Start by placing the wig cap on your wig stand or mannequin head. Make sure it is secure and stretched taut. This will ensure that the wig fits properly when you are finished.

Cutting the Hair

Using your scissors, cut the hair extensions to the desired length of your pixie cut. For a classic pixie cut, the hair should be cut very short on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. If you're not sure how short to go, start with longer hair and trim it down gradually until you achieve the desired length.

Sewing the Wig Cap

Thread your needle and begin sewing the hair onto the wig cap. Start at the back of the wig cap and work your way forward. Be sure to sew the hair securely, using small stitches to keep it in place. Continue sewing the hair until the entire wig cap is covered.

Attaching the Hair

Once you have sewn the hair onto the wig cap, it's time to attach it to the wig stand or mannequin head. Use pins or clips to secure the wig in place, making sure it is stretched taut. This will help to shape the wig and ensure that it fits properly.

Styling the Wig

Now that your wig is complete, it's time to style it. Use a comb or brush to style the hair into the desired shape. You can also use a hair dryer or flat iron to shape the hair. Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect look.

Tips for Making a Pixie Cut Wig

  • Use high-quality hair extensions for the best results.
  • Be patient and take your time when sewing the hair onto the wig cap.
  • Experiment with different hair colors and textures to achieve different looks.
  • Consider adding a lace front to your wig for a more natural look.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to make a pixie cut wig?

A: The time it takes to make a pixie cut wig can vary depending on your skill level and the materials you are using. Generally, it can take several hours to complete.

Q: Can I make a pixie cut wig without a wig cap?

A: While it is possible to make a wig without a wig cap, it is not recommended. A wig cap helps to keep the hair in place and ensures that the wig fits properly.

Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut Wig

To keep your pixie cut wig looking its best, be sure to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heated styling tools too often. Store the wig on a wig head or mannequin head when not in use to help maintain its shape.

Conclusion

With a little patience and some basic sewing skills, you can make your own pixie cut wig. Follow these steps and tips to create a wig that looks and feels great. Whether you're trying out a new look or just looking for a fun project, making a wig can be a rewarding experience.
