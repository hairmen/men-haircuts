How To Flatten Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, wavy hair? Do you find it difficult to style your hair the way you want it because of its natural waves? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with taming their wavy hair, but there are ways to achieve a sleek, straight look. In this article, we'll show you how to flatten wavy hair with a step-by-step guide, along with some tips for maintaining your straight hair. Let's get started!

  • How to Flatten Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Flatten Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand the basics of how to flatten wavy hair. There are a few methods you can use, including:

  • Using a straightener
  • Blow-drying with a round brush
  • Chemical treatments, such as a keratin treatment

In this article, we'll focus on using a straightener to flatten wavy hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve sleek, straight hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel-dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the straightener.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back and working your way to the front.
  4. Take a 1-inch section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root.
  5. Gently glide the straightener down the length of the hair, pulling it straight as you go.
  6. Repeat this process on each section of hair until all of your hair is straight.
  7. Apply a smoothing serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

And that's it! You should now have sleek, straight hair that will last all day.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Now that you've achieved straight hair, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about flattening wavy hair:

  • Can I use a straightener on wet hair? No, you should only use a straightener on dry hair to prevent damage.
  • How often should I straighten my hair? It's best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage.
  • Will a keratin treatment permanently straighten my hair? No, a keratin treatment will only last for a few months and will gradually wear off.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

If you have naturally wavy hair, it's important to take steps to care for it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to reduce frizz and add shine.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can achieve sleek, straight hair and maintain it for longer. Good luck!
