How To Cut Hair Straight Across Yourself A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you don't have any experience. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair straight across yourself, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Cut Hair Straight Across Yourself
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Cutting Hair Straight Across Yourself
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care After Cutting

Description

Before we jump into the steps, let's take a moment to understand what it means to cut hair straight across. This style is also known as a blunt cut and involves cutting the hair at the same length all the way around the head. It's a simple and classic look that works well for all hair types and lengths.

How to Cut Hair Straight Across Yourself

To cut your hair straight across yourself, you'll need a few tools, including:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair straight across:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. It's easier to cut clean hair, so make sure to wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. Use your comb to divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Clip each section away from the others.
  3. Start with the back sections. Take one of the back sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers at the length you want to cut. Use your scissors to cut straight across. Repeat on the other back section.
  4. Move to the front sections. Take one of the front sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers at the length you want to cut. Use your scissors to cut straight across. Repeat on the other front section.
  5. Check for symmetry. Once you've cut all four sections, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your hair is even.

Tips for Cutting Hair Straight Across Yourself

Follow these tips to achieve the best results:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Sharp scissors will make the cutting process easier and produce better results.
  • Start with small cuts. It's easier to cut more hair than to fix a mistake from cutting too much.
  • Take breaks. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take a break if you need to.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair straight across if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight across if you have curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair can shrink when it dries, so you may need to cut it slightly longer than you want it to be.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always make adjustments by cutting a little more hair or using hair clips to hold the hair in place while you fix it.

Can I cut my hair straight across if it's layered?

If your hair is layered, cutting it straight across may not be the best option. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best way to cut your hair.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain the style. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh.

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful blunt cut at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a gorgeous new haircut in no time.
