Short Sides Long Top Haircut For Women The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short sides long top haircut for women has been a popular hairstyle for a long time. This edgy and stylish haircut is perfect for those who want to try something different and bold. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to do it, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care.

Description

The short sides long top haircut for women is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. It is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance haircut that is still fashionable and chic.

How to Do Short Sides Long Top Haircut for Women

If you want to do a short sides long top haircut for women, you will need some basic hair cutting tools such as scissors, clippers, and a comb. Here are the steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. Use clips to separate the top section from the sides and back.
  3. Using clippers, shave the sides and back of your head to your desired length.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top using scissors.
  5. Trim the hair on the top to your desired length.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips to Maintain Short Sides Long Top Haircut for Women

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short sides long top haircut for women:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use hair products such as hair gel or mousse to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes suit short sides long top haircut for women?

Short sides long top haircut for women suits most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I style my hair differently with short sides long top haircut?

Yes, short sides long top haircut for women is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can try a messy or slicked-back look, or even add some braids or curls to the top section.

Hair Care for Short Sides Long Top Haircut for Women

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, short sides long top haircut for women is a stylish and edgy hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. Follow the steps and tips in this guide to achieve the perfect haircut and maintain healthy hair.
