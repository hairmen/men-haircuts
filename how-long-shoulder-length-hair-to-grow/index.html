How Long Does It Take To Grow Shoulder Length Hair

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of short hair and looking to grow it out into a shoulder length style? It can be frustrating to wait for your hair to grow, but with patience and the right hair care routine, you can achieve your desired length. In this article, we'll discuss how long it typically takes to grow shoulder length hair and provide some tips on how to speed up the process.

How to Measure Shoulder Length Hair

Before we dive into the details, let's define what we mean by "shoulder length hair." To measure your hair length, start at the center of the back of your neck and measure down to the point where you want your hair to fall. This is usually around the top of your shoulders. For most people, this length is around 12-14 inches.

How Long Does It Take to Grow Shoulder Length Hair?

The rate at which your hair grows can vary depending on several factors, including genetics, age, and overall health. On average, hair grows around half an inch per month, which means it can take approximately one year to grow shoulder length hair from a short style. However, this can differ for each individual, and some people may see faster or slower growth.

How to Speed Up Hair Growth

If you're looking to speed up the hair growth process, there are several things you can do to promote healthy growth:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a significant impact on your hair health. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet to support healthy hair growth. Foods like eggs, nuts, fruits, and vegetables are all great choices.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and slow down growth. Try to limit your use of these tools or use a heat protectant spray before styling.

3. Get Regular Trims

It may seem counterintuitive, but getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster. Trimming off split ends can prevent breakage and make your hair appear healthier.

4. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can stimulate hair follicles and promote growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

FAQ About Growing Shoulder Length Hair

How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days between washes. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils and slow down growth.

Can I Use Hair Extensions to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add length and volume to your hair while you're waiting for it to grow. Just be sure to choose high-quality extensions and have them installed by a professional to avoid damage to your natural hair.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Shoulder Length Hair

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that's gentle and free of harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed to promote hair growth.

2. Condition Regularly

Conditioning your hair regularly can help keep it healthy and prevent breakage. Choose a conditioner that's nourishing and hydrating.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and slow down growth. Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection when you're spending time outdoors.

Conclusion

While it can take some time to grow shoulder length hair, the process can be sped up with the right hair care routine. Eat a healthy diet, avoid heat styling, get regular trims, and massage your scalp to promote healthy growth. And remember, be patient â good things come to those who wait!
