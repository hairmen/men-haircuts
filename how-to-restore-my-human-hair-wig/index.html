How To Restore My Human Hair Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Human hair wigs, whether synthetic or natural, can be a fantastic investment in your appearance. They can enhance your confidence and make you feel beautiful. However, over time, your wig may become tangled, dry, and damaged. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to restore your human hair wig to its natural shine and beauty.

How to Restore My Human Hair Wig

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

The first step in restoring your human hair wig is to detangle it. Start by gently brushing the wig from the bottom up, using a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush. Be gentle, as the hair may be fragile, and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair.

Step 2: Wash the Wig

After detangling, it's time to wash the wig. Fill a sink or basin with lukewarm water and add a small amount of shampoo. Gently swish the wig in the water, making sure to avoid rubbing or twisting the hair. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water.

Step 3: Condition the Wig

After washing, it's time to condition the wig. Apply a small amount of conditioner to the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Step 4: Dry the Wig

To dry the wig, gently pat it with a towel to remove excess water. Do not rub or twist the hair, as this can cause damage. Allow the wig to air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can cause the hair to become dry and brittle.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Once the wig is dry, it's time to style it. Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently style the hair. You can also use heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Tips for Maintaining Your Human Hair Wig

Here are some tips to help you maintain the beauty and longevity of your human hair wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat, such as from a stove, oven, or hair dryer.
  • Avoid sleeping in your wig, as this can cause tangles and damage.
  • Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and prevent the wig from slipping.

FAQs About Restoring Your Human Hair Wig

How often should I wash my human hair wig?

You should wash your human hair wig every 7-10 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or tangled.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

You should use shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wigs, as regular products can damage the hair.

Can I color or bleach my human hair wig?

Yes, you can color or bleach your human hair wig, but it's best to have it done professionally to avoid damaging the hair.

Hair Care for Your Human Hair Wig

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the beauty and longevity of your human hair wig. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot water, as this can cause the hair to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can cause the hair to become dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to chlorine or saltwater, as this can cause damage.

By following these tips and tricks for restoring and maintaining your human hair wig, you can keep it looking beautiful and natural for years to come.
