Layered Short Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces A Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know how important it is to find the right haircut to flatter your features. A layered short haircut can be a great choice for heart-shaped faces, as it can help balance out a wider forehead and narrow chin. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about layered short haircuts for heart shaped faces, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

How to Choose the Right Layered Short Haircut for Your Heart Shaped Face

Before you head to the salon, it's important to have a clear idea of the type of layered short haircut you want. Here are some tips to help you choose the right cut for your heart-shaped face:

Consider Your Hair Texture

When choosing a layered short haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut with lots of layers to add volume and movement. If you have thicker hair, you may want to go for a longer layered cut to help manage the weight of your hair.

Think About Your Face Shape

While heart-shaped faces have some common features, each face is unique. Consider the length of your face, the width of your forehead, and the shape of your jawline when choosing a layered short haircut.

Look for Inspiration

Before you head to the salon, take some time to browse magazines or Pinterest for inspiration. Look for layered short haircuts that flatter heart-shaped faces and save photos to show your stylist.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Layered Short Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Once you have your layered short haircut, it's important to know how to style it to achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling layered short haircuts for heart shaped faces:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. If your hair tends to be oily, you may want to use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and hold. Work the product through your hair evenly.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create shape. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and pull it away from your face.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Straightener (Optional)

If you want to add more texture to your hair, you can use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or curls.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to hold your style in place. Hold the hairspray about 12 inches from your head and spray lightly to avoid making your hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Short Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your layered short haircut looking great:

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important to keep your layered short haircut looking its best. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair healthy and maintain the shape.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause split ends. Try to limit heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when you do style with heat.

FAQs About Layered Short Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Q: Can I wear bangs with a layered short haircut for a heart shaped face?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great addition to a layered short haircut for a heart shaped face. Side-swept bangs or blunt bangs can help balance out your forehead and make your face appear more oval-shaped.

Q: What types of layered short haircuts are best for heart shaped faces?

A: Layered bobs, pixie cuts, and shaggy cuts can all be great choices for heart shaped faces. The key is to choose a cut that flatters your unique face shape and features.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a layered short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a layered short haircut. Use bobby pins or small hair elastics to secure your hair in place, and add some texture to your hair with a texturizing spray to help the style hold.

Conclusion

A layered short haircut can be a great choice for heart shaped faces, as it can help balance out a wider forehead and narrow chin. By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect layered short haircut for your unique features and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
