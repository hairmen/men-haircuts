How To Get Thicker Longer Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Oct 07, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're someone with curly hair, you might have noticed that your hair tends to look thinner and shorter than it actually is. This can be frustrating, especially if you're trying to grow your hair out or achieve a voluminous look. Don't worry, though - there are plenty of things you can do to get thicker, longer, and more luscious curls!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Thicker Longer Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thicker Longer Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Long Curly Hair

How to Get Thicker Longer Curly Hair

There are several things you can do to get thicker, longer, and more voluminous curls:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can break your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Consider using hair extensions or a volumizing spray to add thickness and volume to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting thicker, longer, and more voluminous curls:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair.
  2. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  4. Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can break your hair and make it look thinner.
  6. Consider using hair extensions or a volumizing spray to add thickness and volume to your hair.
  7. Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  8. Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes that can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  9. Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and make it look thinner.
  10. Be patient - it takes time to grow out your hair and achieve thicker, longer, and more voluminous curls!

Tips for Thicker Longer Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for getting thicker, longer, and more voluminous curls:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look thinner.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished throughout the day.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and protect your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and hydration to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I use heat styling tools if I want thicker, longer curls?

A: It's best to avoid using heat styling tools if you want thicker, longer curls. Heat can damage your hair and make it look thinner, so it's best to air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Q: How often should I deep condition my hair?

A: It's best to deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to add thickness and volume to my hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add thickness and volume to your hair. Just make sure to choose extensions that are made from high-quality hair and blend well with your natural hair.

Hair Care for Thick Long Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your thick, long, curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look thinner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes that can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Be gentle when styling your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve thicker, longer, and more voluminous curls. Remember to be patient, as it takes time to grow out your hair and achieve the look you want. With a little bit of care and attention, you can have the beautiful, healthy curls you've always wanted!
