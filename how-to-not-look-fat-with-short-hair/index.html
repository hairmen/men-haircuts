How To Not Look Fat With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair can be a great way to update your look and feel more confident, but if you're worried about looking fat with short hair, don't worry. There are plenty of tricks and tips that can help you look your best. Keep reading to learn more about how to not look fat with short hair.

Description

When it comes to hair, the length, style, and color can all have an effect on how your face looks. Short hair, in particular, can make some people feel self-conscious about their weight. However, there are ways to style your short hair that will make you look more slender and confident.

How to

Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and features. A layered bob or pixie cut can be a great choice for many people, as it can help elongate the neck and draw attention upwards. Avoid a blunt cut that ends at your chin, as this can make your face look rounder.

Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow dry your hair while flipping your head upside down. This will create volume at the roots and help elongate your face.

Style Your Hair Away from Your Face

Styling your hair away from your face can help create a more slender look. Use a hair clip or bobby pins to pin your hair back, or create a side part to draw attention away from the center of your face.

Accessorize

Accessories can be a great way to draw attention away from your face and create a more balanced look. Try wearing statement earrings or a bold necklace to draw attention upwards. Avoid wearing hats or headbands that sit low on your forehead, as this can make your face look wider.

Step by Step

Step 1

Choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and features.

Step 2

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.

Step 3

Blow dry your hair while flipping your head upside down to create volume at the roots.

Step 4

Style your hair away from your face using bobby pins or a side part.

Step 5

Accessorize with statement earrings or a bold necklace to draw attention upwards.

Tips

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid wearing your hair in a center part, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can short hair make me look fatter?

A: Short hair can make some people feel self-conscious about their weight. However, there are ways to style your short hair that will make you look more slender and confident.

Q: What is the best short haircut for a round face?

A: A layered bob or pixie cut can be a great choice for a round face, as it can help elongate the neck and draw attention upwards.

Q: How can I add volume to my short hair?

A: Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow dry your hair while flipping your head upside down. This will create volume at the roots and help elongate your face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. If you color your hair, be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking vibrant.

By following these tips and tricks, you can feel confident and beautiful with short hair, no matter your body shape or size.
