How To Make Oval Face Look Square Tips And Tricks

If you have an oval face shape, you might find it challenging to style your hair to make it look square. But don't worry, there are many ways to achieve this look! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make oval face look square. We'll also include some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Oval Face Look Square

There are several ways to make your oval face look square:

  • Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Hairstyles with sharp angles and straight lines
  • Hairstyles with bangs that cover your forehead

Let's take a closer look at each of these options.

1. Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face

Adding volume to the sides of your face can help create the illusion of a square face. This can be achieved with hairstyles like:

  • Layered haircuts
  • Bob haircuts with layers
  • Wavy or curly hairstyles

These hairstyles add volume to the sides of your face and make it appear wider, which can help balance out the length of an oval face.

2. Hairstyles with sharp angles and straight lines

Hairstyles with sharp angles and straight lines can also help create the illusion of a square face. This can be achieved with hairstyles like:

  • Asymmetrical haircuts
  • Bob haircuts with blunt ends
  • Pixie haircuts with sharp angles

These hairstyles create a strong jawline and angular features, which can help make an oval face appear more square.

3. Hairstyles with bangs that cover your forehead

Bangs can also help make an oval face look square. Bangs that cover your forehead can help balance out the length of an oval face and create the illusion of a shorter forehead. This can be achieved with hairstyles like:

  • Straight bangs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Blunt bangs

These hairstyles draw attention to the center of your face and create a more square-looking shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair to make an oval face look square:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face or has sharp angles and straight lines.
  2. If you have long hair, consider getting it cut to shoulder length or shorter.
  3. Add layers to your hair to create volume and movement.
  4. If you want to add bangs, choose a style that covers your forehead.
  5. Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall.
  6. If you're using a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to create sharp angles and straight lines.
  7. Finish with a texturizing spray or hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make your oval face look square:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can accentuate the length of an oval face.
  • If you have a high forehead, consider getting bangs to balance it out.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and haircuts to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can makeup help make an oval face look square?

A: Yes, contouring with makeup can help create the illusion of a square face. By using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight certain areas, you can create a more defined jawline and cheekbones.

Q: Will growing out my hair help make my oval face look square?

A: No, growing out your hair won't necessarily make your face look more square. It's all about the cut and style of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your oval face look square and rock any hairstyle you want!
