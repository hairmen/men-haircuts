Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face or a double chin, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your best features and create a flattering look. Here are some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Pay attention to the shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a double chin, you may also have a round face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces and Double Chins

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face or double chin, it's important to choose a cut that elongates the face and draws attention away from the chin. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces and double chins:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces and double chins because they create movement and dimension. The layers help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin. To create this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start at the chin and taper down to the ends.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces and double chins. The bangs help to create angles and draw attention away from the chin. To create this look, ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a great option for round faces and double chins. The key is to choose a bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back. This helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin. To create this look, ask your stylist for a long bob with layers that start at the chin and taper down to the ends.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Product

If you have a round face or double chin, using a volumizing product can help to create lift and add dimension to your hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots before blow-drying your hair.

2. Use a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and movement. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and create volume.

3. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products, such as pomades or waxes, as these can weigh down your hair and make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for lightweight products, such as hairspray or texturizing spray.

FAQ About Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face or double chin?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as these can make your face look rounder. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as these can accentuate your double chin.

Q: What if I have thin hair?

A: If you have thin hair, consider adding layers to create volume and movement. You can also use a volumizing product to create lift and add dimension.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended that you get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces and Double Chins

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling your hair properly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Choose a moisturizing shampoo that will help to keep your hair hydrated and nourished. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, and shea butter.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish and strengthen your hair. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

When styling your hair with heat tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage. Also, try to limit your use of heat tools to avoid over-drying and damaging your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and double chin. Remember, the key is to choose a cut and style that elongates your face and draws attention away from your chin. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
