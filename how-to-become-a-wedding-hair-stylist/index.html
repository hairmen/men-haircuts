How To Become A Wedding Hair Stylist

Written by Oliver Nov 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a passion for hair styling and you love weddings, then becoming a wedding hair stylist might be the perfect career for you. In this article, we will guide you on how to become a wedding hair stylist, step by step.

How to Become a Wedding Hair Stylist

Step 1: Get Certified

The first step to becoming a wedding hair stylist is to get certified. You can attend a cosmetology school or a beauty school to get the necessary training and certification. You can also take online courses or workshops to learn more about wedding hair styling.

Step 2: Build Your Portfolio

Building your portfolio is an essential step in becoming a wedding hair stylist. You can start by practicing on friends and family members and taking pictures of your work. You can also collaborate with local photographers or wedding planners to build your portfolio.

Step 3: Gain Experience

To gain experience, you can work as an assistant in a salon or a wedding hair stylist. This will give you the opportunity to learn from experienced professionals and gain valuable skills and knowledge.

Step 4: Market Yourself

Marketing yourself is crucial in becoming a successful wedding hair stylist. You can create a website or a social media page to showcase your portfolio and services. You can also attend bridal shows or network with wedding planners and photographers to market your services.

Tips for Becoming a Wedding Hair Stylist

Here are some tips that can help you become a successful wedding hair stylist:

  • Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in wedding hair styling.
  • Be punctual and reliable.
  • Communicate effectively with your clients to understand their needs and preferences.
  • Have a positive attitude and be flexible.
  • Invest in high-quality products and tools to deliver excellent results.

FAQs

What skills do I need to become a wedding hair stylist?

You need to have excellent hair styling skills, good communication skills, creativity, and attention to detail.

How much money can I make as a wedding hair stylist?

The average salary for a wedding hair stylist varies depending on the location and experience. According to ZipRecruiter, the average salary for a wedding hair stylist in the United States is $48,000 per year.

Hair Care Tips for Brides

As a wedding hair stylist, it's important to educate your clients on how to take care of their hair before and after the wedding. Here are some hair care tips for brides:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment a week before the wedding to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons a few days before the wedding to prevent damage.
  • Wash the hair a day before the wedding to allow the natural oils to penetrate the hair and make it easier to style.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or styling products that can weigh down the hair.
  • Consider using hair extensions or accessories to add volume and texture to the hair.

Becoming a wedding hair stylist requires dedication, hard work, and passion. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve your dream of becoming a successful wedding hair stylist.
