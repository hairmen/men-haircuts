How To Wear Your Hair With A Round Face Tips Tricks And Hair Care

If you have a round face, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape. But don't worry, we got you covered! In this blog post, we will share tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you wear your hair with a round face confidently.

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to

The key to wearing your hair with a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. You want to avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder and fuller. Here are some hairstyles that will flatter your round face:

Long Layers

Long layers add volume and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of length. This style works best for long hair, and it's perfect for those who want to keep their hair long but still want to add some texture to it.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and create angles. They draw attention away from the roundness of your face and make it appear slimmer. Make sure to have your stylist cut your bangs at an angle to create a diagonal line that flatters your face shape.

Updos

Updos are perfect for special occasions, and they also work well for those with a round face. Opt for a messy bun or a low ponytail to create the illusion of length. This style also highlights your cheekbones and jawline, making your face appear slimmer.

Step by step

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to create a side-swept bang:

  1. Start by parting your hair to the side.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut for your bangs.
  3. Comb the hair forward and hold it between your index and middle finger.
  4. Cut the hair at an angle, following the diagonal line of your cheekbone.
  5. Continue cutting until you reach your desired length.
  6. Repeat on the other side.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair with a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin.
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers or angles.
  • Try adding volume to your crown to elongate your face.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands or clips to add interest to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a bob with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob with a round face, but make sure it's a long bob that ends below your chin. Also, have your stylist cut it at an angle to create angles that flatter your face shape.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts with a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Short haircuts can work for those with a round face, but make sure to add texture and layers to create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin as they can make your face look fuller.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips, tricks, and hair care advice, you can confidently wear your hair with a round face. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you. And most importantly, embrace your unique beauty!
