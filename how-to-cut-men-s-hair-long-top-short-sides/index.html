How To Cut Men s Hair Long Top Short Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Description

If you're looking to give your hair a trendy and stylish look, then the long top short sides haircut is the way to go. This haircut features long hair on top and shorter hair on the sides, giving you a sleek and modern appearance. However, getting this haircut requires some skill and precision. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting men's hair long top short sides, step-by-step.

How to Cut Men's Hair Long Top Short Sides

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair products like pomade or wax.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use clips to separate them.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using the clippers, start cutting the sides. Use a #2 or #3 guard for a shorter look. Run the clippers against the grain, starting at the bottom and moving upwards.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the sides, blend them into the top. Use a #4 or #5 guard to create a gradual fade.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Using the scissors, start cutting the top. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to your desired length.

Step 6: Texturize the Top

To add texture to the top, use the scissors to create choppy layers. This will give your hair a messy and natural look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Apply hair products like pomade or wax to style your hair. Use your fingers to create a messy and tousled look.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to get the desired length. - Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting. - Take your time and don't rush the process. - If you're not confident in cutting your own hair, ask a friend or a professional hairstylist for help.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair long top short sides?

Yes, you can cut your own hair long top short sides. However, it requires some skill and precision. Take your time and don't rush the process.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use hair products like pomade or wax to style your hair. These products will give your hair a sleek and polished look.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair. And don't forget to trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair long top short sides, you can give yourself a trendy and stylish haircut. Just remember to take your time and don't rush the process. And if you're not confident in cutting your own hair, don't hesitate to ask for help from a friend or a professional hairstylist.
