How To Get One Big Curl Ponytail Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is simple yet stylish? Look no further than the one big curl ponytail. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get one big curl ponytail. We will also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure that your hairstyle looks great and lasts all day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get One Big Curl Ponytail
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Ponytails

How to Get One Big Curl Ponytail

To achieve the one big curl ponytail, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Blow Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, and then blow dry your hair. Use a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Next, use a curling iron to create one big curl. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron for 10-15 seconds and release the hair. Repeat this process until you have curled all of your hair.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair into a Ponytail

Gather all of your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place. Make sure that the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 4: Tease the Ponytail

Take a comb and tease the hair in the ponytail to create volume. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to hold the ponytail in place. Use your fingers to smooth out any flyaways, and you're all set!

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect one big curl ponytail:

  • Use a high-quality curling iron to create a smooth, long-lasting curl.
  • Tease the hair in the ponytail to create volume and thickness.
  • Use hairspray to hold the ponytail in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Choose a hair tie that is strong and sturdy to keep the ponytail secure.

FAQs

Q. Can I achieve this hairstyle if I have short hair?

A. Unfortunately, this hairstyle works best on medium to long hair. If you have short hair, you may need to consider using hair extensions to achieve the same look.

Q. How long will this hairstyle last?

A. With the right hair care and products, this hairstyle can last all day and into the night.

Q. Can I add accessories to my ponytail?

A. Absolutely! You can add a headband, ribbon, or hair clip to your ponytail to add some extra flair.

Hair Care for Ponytails

When wearing a ponytail, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while wearing a ponytail:

  • Avoid using hair ties that are too tight or too loose.
  • Use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair, such as a fabric or scrunchie hair tie.
  • Don't wear your hair in a ponytail every day to prevent breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Now that you know how to get one big curl ponytail, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve a simple yet stylish hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage, and have fun experimenting with different accessories to add some extra flair to your ponytail.
