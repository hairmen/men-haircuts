How to Style Short Pixie Cut Messy

Styling short pixie cut messy is quite easy, and the best part about it is that you don't need any special tools or products. Here is how you can style your short pixie cut messy:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help create volume and texture.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and rub it into your hair.
  4. Using your fingers, tousle your hair to create a messy texture. You can also use a comb to create more defined sections.
  5. Finish off the look by applying a hairspray to hold the style in place.

And that's it! Your short pixie cut messy is ready.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short pixie cut messy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil or product buildup from your hair.

Step 2: Blow-dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help create volume and texture in your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Wax or Pomade

Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and rub it into your hair. This will help create a messy texture in your hair.

Step 4: Tousle Your Hair

Using your fingers, tousle your hair to create a messy texture. You can also use a comb to create more defined sections in your hair.

Step 5: Apply Hairspray

Finish off the look by applying a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Pixie Cut Messy

Here are some tips that will help you style your short pixie cut messy:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to create volume and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to use hair wax or pomade to create a messy texture in your hair.
  • Tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, carefree look.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQs

Can I style short pixie cut messy on my own?

Yes, you can easily style short pixie cut messy on your own. All you need is a good quality shampoo, conditioner, hair wax or pomade, and hairspray.

Will short pixie cut messy suit my face shape?

Short pixie cut messy is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape. However, it is always best to consult with your hairstylist before getting a new haircut.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Cut Messy

Here are some hair care tips that will help you maintain your short pixie cut messy:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Short pixie cut messy is a fun and easy hairstyle that can give you a youthful and carefree look. By following these tips and steps, you can easily style your short pixie cut messy at home. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain the health and shine of your new hairstyle.
