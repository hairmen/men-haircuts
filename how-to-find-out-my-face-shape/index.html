How To Find Out My Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Knowing your face shape is essential when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories. However, not everyone knows how to determine their face shape. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of finding out your face shape, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Introduction
  2. Taking Measurements
  3. Types of Face Shapes
  4. Face Shape Test
  5. Tips for Finding Your Face Shape
  6. Frequently Asked Questions
  7. Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Introduction

Before we dive into the process of finding out your face shape, it's essential to understand why it's crucial to know your face shape. Your face shape determines the best hairstyles, makeup looks, and accessories that complement your natural features. It's the foundation of creating a personalized style that enhances your unique beauty.

Taking Measurements

The first step in finding out your face shape is to take accurate measurements of your face. You will need a flexible measuring tape, a mirror, and a pen and paper to note down your measurements. Here are the measurements you need to take:

  • Forehead width: measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the other.
  • Cheekbone width: measure across your cheekbones, starting from the outer corner of one eye to the outer corner of the other eye.
  • Jawline width: measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone starts to angle up.
  • Face length: measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Types of Face Shapes

Once you have taken your measurements, you can determine your face shape by comparing them against the following face shape types:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Heart-shaped
  • Square
  • Diamond
  • Pear-shaped

Face Shape Test

If you're still unsure about your face shape, you can take a face shape test. There are various online face shape tests available that ask you a series of questions about your facial features and measurements. These tests provide a quick and easy way to determine your face shape.

Tips for Finding Your Face Shape

Here are some tips that can help you in finding out your face shape:

  • Make sure you take accurate measurements using a flexible measuring tape.
  • Take your time and compare your measurements against the different face shapes.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with the measurements.
  • Take into account any asymmetry or unique features in your face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can my face shape change?

A: No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which doesn't change.

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes, some people have a combination of two or more face shapes.

Q: Is it essential to know my face shape?

A: Yes, knowing your face shape helps in choosing the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that complement your natural features.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose hairstyles that flatter your features. Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

  • Oval: You can rock any hairstyle, but avoid styles that cover your forehead.
  • Round: Go for hairstyles that add height and volume on top.
  • Heart-shaped: Choose hairstyles that add volume on the sides and create a soft, round shape.
  • Square: Soften your features with hairstyles that add volume and texture.
  • Diamond: Add width to your forehead and chin with hairstyles that create volume on top and bottom.
  • Pear-shaped: Create balance by adding volume to the top of your head with layers and bangs.

Finding out your face shape is the first step in creating a personalized style that enhances your unique beauty. Use the tips and techniques mentioned in this blog post to determine your face shape and choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that complement your natural features.
