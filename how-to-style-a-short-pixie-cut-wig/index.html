How To Style A Short Pixie Cut Wig

Description

A short pixie cut wig is a great way to switch up your look without having to commit to a drastic haircut. It’s also perfect for those who want to experiment with different colors without damaging their natural hair. This wig style is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice among women of all ages.

How to Style a Short Pixie Cut Wig

If you’re new to wearing a short pixie cut wig, you may be wondering how to style it. The good news is that it’s easy to style and can be done in just a few minutes. Here’s how:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your wig, make sure it’s clean and dry. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to wash your wig and let it air dry overnight. This will ensure that your wig is clean and ready to be styled.

2. Use a wig cap

Using a wig cap will help keep your wig in place and prevent it from slipping. Place the wig cap over your head and tuck in any stray hairs.

3. Comb through your wig

Use a wide-toothed comb to gently comb through your wig. This will help detangle any knots and make it easier to style.

4. Apply styling products

Apply a small amount of mousse or gel to your wig to help hold its shape. You can also use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat styling tools.

5. Style your wig

Use your fingers or a comb to style your wig as desired. You can create a messy look, slick it back, or add volume to the crown.

6. Finish with hairspray

Once you’ve styled your wig, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your style in place throughout the day.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style a short pixie cut wig:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair.

Apply styling products to help hold your style.

Step 5:

Style your wig using your fingers or a comb.

Step 6:

Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling a Short Pixie Cut Wig

Here are some tips to help you style your short pixie cut wig:

  • Use a wig stand to help you style your wig.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use heat styling tools sparingly to prevent damage to your wig.
  • Try using hair accessories such as headbands or clips to add some extra flair to your style.

FAQs

Can I use heat styling tools on my short pixie cut wig?

Yes, you can use heat styling tools on your wig, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

How often should I clean my wig?

You should clean your wig after every 10-12 wears or when it starts to look dirty.

Can I dye my short pixie cut wig?

Yes, you can dye your wig, but be sure to use a wig dye specifically designed for synthetic wigs.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Cut Wigs

Here are some tips to help you care for your short pixie cut wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand to help maintain its shape.
  • Wash your wig with a wig shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and soft.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your wig.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat or direct sunlight.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your short pixie cut wig in a variety of ways and enjoy a new look every day!
