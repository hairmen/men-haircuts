How To Cut Your Own Pixie Style Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance and stylish haircut, a pixie cut might be the perfect choice for you. However, going to a salon to get your hair cut can be expensive and time-consuming. Luckily, with a little bit of practice and the right tools, you can learn how to cut your own pixie style haircut at home. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step and provide some helpful tips along the way.

How to Cut Your Own Pixie Style Haircut

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right preparation and tools, you can achieve a salon-worthy pixie cut in the comfort of your own home. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Clipper set with guards
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair clips or elastics
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape to protect your clothes

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own pixie style haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the shape of your hair and ensure an even cut.
  2. Section off the top of your hair using clips or elastics. This will help you focus on the sides and back of your hair first.
  3. Using your clippers with the appropriate guard, start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use a comb to lift the hair and clip it to your desired length. You can start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.
  4. Once you've finished the back and sides, unclip the top section of your hair. Use your scissors to carefully cut the hair to your desired length. Start with longer pieces and work your way shorter. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to create texture.
  5. Blend the sides and back with the top sections. Use your scissors to carefully cut any longer pieces and create a seamless transition.
  6. Check your hair in the mirror from all angles to ensure an even cut. Make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your hair as desired using a styling product.

Tips for a Successful Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. It's easier to take off more hair than to add it back on.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair before cutting it. This will help you achieve an even cut and prevent any choppy layers.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair can shrink up to an inch when it dries, which can result in a shorter-than-intended cut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, start with small snips and work your way up to a full cut.

FAQs About Cutting Your Own Pixie Cut

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own pixie cut:

How often should I trim my pixie cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows, but most people need a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of their pixie cut.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always make adjustments or style your hair differently to hide any uneven spots. And remember, hair grows back.

Do I need to use special scissors to cut my hair?

Yes, it's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure an even cut. Using regular scissors can result in choppy layers and split ends.

Hair Care After Your Pixie Cut

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the shape and health of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your pixie cut
  • Use a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair

Cutting your own pixie style haircut can be a fun and empowering experience. With the right tools and preparation, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut in the comfort of your own home. Good luck!
