Mens Hairstyles For Thinning Hair On Top

Written by Aprilia Nov 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem among men. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, age, medication, and stress. There are many hairstyles that can help men with thinning hair on top look their best. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right hairstyle for thinning hair on top, how to style your hair, and how to take care of your hair to prevent further thinning.

Description

Thinning hair on top is a common problem among men. It can make them feel self-conscious and affect their confidence. However, there are many hairstyles that can help men with thinning hair on top look their best. These hairstyles can be achieved with the right cut, styling, and hair care.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for thinning hair on top can be challenging. You want a style that will make your hair look fuller and thicker, but you also want a style that will suit your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a style that adds height to the top of your head. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a style that is longer on top and shorter on the sides.

Choose a Hairstyle that Suits Your Hair Type

It is also important to choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that is shorter on top and longer on the sides.

Consult with Your Barber or Stylist

Your barber or stylist can help you choose the right hairstyle for your thinning hair on top. They can recommend styles that will suit your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your hair.

How to Style Your Hair

Styling your hair is an important part of achieving the right look for thinning hair on top. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help you achieve the right look for your thinning hair on top. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add hold and texture to your hair.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help you achieve a fuller look for your thinning hair on top. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can also help you achieve the right look for your thinning hair on top. Choose a style that adds height and texture to your hair. Avoid styles that are flat and straight.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair for thinning hair on top:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair. This will add hold and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb. Choose a style that adds height and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling your hair for thinning hair on top:

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Avoid overwashing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look thinner.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and full. A good haircut can make a big difference in the appearance of thinning hair on top.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for thinning hair on top?

The best hairstyle for thinning hair on top is one that adds height and texture to your hair. A short, textured cut, such as a crew cut or a buzz cut, can be a good option.

Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair on top?

You can still have long hair if you have thinning hair on top, but it may not be the best option. Long hair can make thinning hair on top more noticeable. A shorter cut with texture and volume can be a better option.

What hair products should I use for thinning hair on top?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add hold and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to prevent further thinning. Here are some tips for hair care:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

Get Enough Sleep

Get enough sleep to promote healthy hair growth. Lack of sleep can lead to stress, which can cause hair loss.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that can help men with thinning hair on top look their best. Choosing the right hairstyle, styling your hair, and taking care of your hair are important steps to achieve the right look for your thinning hair on top. By following these tips, you can look and feel your best.
