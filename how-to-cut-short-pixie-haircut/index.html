How To Cut Short Pixie Haircut Step By Step Guide

Are you thinking about getting a short pixie haircut but don't know how to cut it? Pixie haircuts are great for those who want to have a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and manage. In this article, we will show you how to cut short pixie haircut in easy steps, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

  • Description
  • How to Cut Short Pixie Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips

Description

A short pixie haircut is a hairstyle that is cut very short, typically no longer than two inches. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from a messy, tousled look to a sleek, polished style. Pixie haircuts are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and manage.

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help wet your hair and make it easier to cut.

How to Cut Short Pixie Haircut

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own short pixie haircut:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to part your hair down the middle, from the front to the back of your head. Then, use the comb to create a part on each side of your head, from the center part to your ears. You should have four sections of hair.
  2. Wet your hair. Use the spray bottle to dampen your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see the shape of your haircut more clearly.
  3. Starting with one section of hair, comb it straight up from your head. Use your fingers to hold the hair in place at the length you want to cut it. Snip the ends of the hair with the scissors.
  4. Continue cutting each section of hair, working your way around your head. Make sure to check the length of each section against the others to make sure they are even.
  5. Once you have cut all of your hair, use the scissors to clean up any stray hairs or uneven edges. If you want a more textured look, you can use the scissors to cut into the hair, creating choppy layers.
  6. Style your hair. Use a hair dryer and a brush to style your hair as desired. You can add volume by blow-drying your hair upside down or use a flat iron for a sleek look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own short pixie haircut:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more if you want a shorter haircut.
  • Use sharp scissors to get clean, even cuts. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven edges.
  • Take your time and don't rush. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, so it's important to be patient and take your time to get the best results.
  • It's always a good idea to have a backup plan in case you don't like the way your haircut turns out. You can always go to a professional hairstylist to fix any mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short pixie haircuts:

  • How often should I cut my short pixie haircut?

    • You should plan to get your short pixie haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and polished.

  • Can I cut my own hair if I have curly hair?

    • Cutting curly hair can be tricky, so it's best to leave it to a professional hairstylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

  • What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

    • If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional hairstylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

Once you have cut your short pixie haircut, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.
  • Style your hair with a lightweight hair product, such as a mousse or gel, to keep it in place.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often. These can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and polished.

Cutting your own short pixie haircut can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for any occasion.
