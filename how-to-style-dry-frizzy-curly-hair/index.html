How To Style Dry Frizzy Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look beautiful and voluminous, it can also be dry and frizzy, making it difficult to style. If you're struggling with how to style dry frizzy curly hair, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect curly hairstyle.

  • How to Style Dry Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

Before we dive into the styling tips, it's important to note that the foundation of any great hairstyle is healthy hair. If your hair is dry and damaged, it's important to start with a good hair care routine that includes regular conditioning and deep conditioning treatments. Once your hair is healthy, you can start experimenting with different styles.

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

One of the best ways to combat dry, frizzy hair is to use a leave-in conditioner. A leave-in conditioner can help to nourish your hair and provide it with the moisture it needs to look its best. Apply the conditioner after washing your hair, and focus on the ends to prevent split ends.

2. Try a Diffuser

If you have curly hair, you know that using a blow dryer can be a disaster. The heat can cause your hair to frizz, leaving you with a mess of untamed curls. However, using a diffuser attachment can help to distribute the heat evenly, reducing the risk of frizz. Just be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Use the Right Products

When it comes to curly hair, using the right products is key. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid anything that contains alcohol, which can dry out your hair even more. A good styling cream or serum can help to define your curls and tame frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Gently blot your hair dry with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to avoid damaging your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. Focus on the ends and mid-lengths of your hair, as these areas are most prone to dryness and damage.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use a low heat setting to dry your hair. Be sure to scrunch your hair with the diffuser to create defined curls.

Step 4: Apply a Styling Cream or Serum

Apply a styling cream or serum to your hair to define your curls and tame frizz. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create volume.

Tips and Tricks

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Try a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

What is the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce the risk of frizz. Be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips for Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

Aside from styling, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent dryness and frizz. Here are some hair care tips for dry frizzy curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your dry frizzy curly hair with ease. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products until you find what works best for you. Remember, healthy hair is the foundation of any great hairstyle, so be sure to take care of your hair with a good hair care routine.
