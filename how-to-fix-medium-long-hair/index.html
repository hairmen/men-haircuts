How To Fix Medium Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of your medium long hair looking dull and lifeless? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to fix medium long hair and achieve a healthy, shiny and voluminous look. Here's what we'll be covering:

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to fix medium long hair, it's important to understand the basics of hair care. Here are some tips on how to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.

How to Fix Medium Long Hair

Now that you have a good understanding of hair care, let's move on to how to fix medium long hair. Here are some things you can do:

  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and give it some extra TLC.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Try different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, or a half-up, half-down look.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fix medium long hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a hair mask, leaving it on for the recommended time.
  3. Rinse out the hair mask and apply a leave-in conditioner.
  4. Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  5. Use a round brush to add some shape and smooth out any frizz.
  6. Once your hair is dry, apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  7. Try different hairstyles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down look.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.
  9. Enjoy your healthy, shiny and voluminous hair!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to fix medium long hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is less damaging than a brush.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have fine hair, avoid using heavy oils or serums that can make your hair look flat.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment when blow drying to enhance your natural curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to fix medium long hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often than if you have dry hair. Generally speaking, washing your hair every other day or every two to three days is a good rule of thumb.

What's the best way to prevent split ends?

The best way to prevent split ends is to trim your hair regularly. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Can I use a hair mask every day?

No, you should only use a hair mask once a week. Using it more often can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Should I use a heat protectant before using hot tools?

Yes, it's important to use a heat protectant before using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons. This can help prevent damage to your hair.

What's the best way to add volume to my hair?

Blow drying your hair upside down and using a volumizing mousse or spray are great ways to add volume to your hair.

Hair Care is Key

Remember, the key to fixing medium long hair is to take good care of it. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve healthy, shiny and voluminous hair that looks and feels amazing. So go ahead, give your hair the love it deserves!
