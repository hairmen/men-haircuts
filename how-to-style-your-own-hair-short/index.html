How To Style Your Own Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Short hair can be incredibly versatile and easy to style, but it can also be challenging to get it just right when you're doing it yourself. Whether you're looking for an everyday look or something more special for a night out, there are plenty of ways to style your own short hair. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step by step, offer some tips and tricks along the way, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Your Own Hair Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Your Own Hair Short

Before you start styling, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a good quality hairbrush, a hairdryer, a flat iron or curling iron, and some styling products like hairspray or mousse. Once you have everything you need, you can start styling your hair.

1. Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in styling your own short hair is to determine your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and techniques to achieve the look you want. If you have thin or fine hair, you'll want to use lightweight products that won't weigh down your hair. If you have thick or curly hair, you'll need heavier products to help control frizz and add definition.

2. Choose Your Style

Next, you'll need to choose your style. There are plenty of options when it comes to short hair, from a sleek and smooth bob to a messy and tousled pixie cut. Take some time to browse through magazines or online for inspiration, and choose a style that works with your hair type and face shape.

3. Prep Your Hair

Once you've chosen your style, it's time to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, using products that are appropriate for your hair type. Then, towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a hairdryer and a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use the brush to smooth out any kinks or bumps, and aim the dryer nozzle down to prevent frizz.

2. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired. If you're going for a sleek and smooth look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. If you want to add some texture and volume, use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.

3. Finish with Products

Finally, finish your look with some styling products like hairspray or mousse. Use a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing it down, or use a volumizing mousse to add some texture and body.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect short hair style:

  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to add volume and body.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Use a light hand when applying styling products to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques until you find what works for you.

FAQ

Q: What if I have naturally curly hair?

A: If you have naturally curly hair, you may want to consider embracing your curls instead of trying to straighten them. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and add some shine, and avoid using a brush or comb to prevent frizz.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: How often you wash your short hair will depend on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, you can wash your hair every 2-3 days, but if you have very oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or hair dyes that can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your own short hair like a pro in no time. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a fun and flirty style, there are plenty of options to choose from. So grab your tools, get creative, and have fun with your new short hair style!
