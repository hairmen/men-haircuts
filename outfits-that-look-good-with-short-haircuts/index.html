Outfits That Look Good With Short Haircuts

Written by William Nov 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short haircuts have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. They're low-maintenance, stylish, and can be incredibly versatile. If you've recently cut your hair short, you might be wondering what outfits will look best with your new 'do. In this article, we'll explore some outfit ideas that will complement your short haircut perfectly, whether you're heading to the office or out for a night on the town.

  • Professional Outfits
  • Evening Outfits

Casual Outfits

When it comes to casual outfits, short haircuts are incredibly versatile. You can go for a laid-back, bohemian vibe with flowy dresses and sandals, or opt for a more edgy look with leather jackets and combat boots. Here are a few outfit ideas to get you started:

Crop Tops and High-Waisted Shorts

A crop top paired with high-waisted shorts is a great option for a casual summer day. This outfit will show off your shoulders and collarbone, which will draw attention to your new haircut. Add a pair of sandals or sneakers, and you're good to go!

Flowy Dresses

Flowy dresses are perfect for summer days when you want to look cute and feel comfortable. Opt for a dress with a fun print or bright color to add some extra personality to your outfit. Pair with strappy sandals or ankle boots depending on the occasion.

Leather Jackets and Jeans

If you're going for an edgier look, try pairing a leather jacket with skinny jeans and ankle boots. This outfit will accentuate your short haircut and give you a cool, confident vibe.

Professional Outfits

Just because you have a short haircut doesn't mean you can't look professional. In fact, short haircuts can give off a powerful, confident vibe that's perfect for the office. Here are a few outfit ideas for professional settings:

Pencil Skirts and Blouses

A pencil skirt paired with a blouse is a classic professional outfit that will never go out of style. This outfit will show off your waist and draw attention to your face and neckline, which will highlight your short haircut. Add a pair of heels or loafers to complete the look.

Tailored Pants and Blazers

If you prefer pants to skirts, try pairing tailored pants with a blazer. This outfit will give you a polished, put-together look that's perfect for the office. Add a pair of heels or oxfords to complete the outfit.

Evening Outfits

Short haircuts can be incredibly glamorous, especially when paired with the right evening outfit. Whether you're going to a fancy dinner or a night out with friends, here are a few outfit ideas that will make you feel like a movie star:

Little Black Dress

A little black dress is a classic evening outfit that never goes out of style. This outfit will show off your legs and collarbone, which will draw attention to your short haircut. Add a pair of statement earrings and high heels to complete the look.

Jumpsuits

Jumpsuits are a great alternative to dresses if you're looking for a more unique evening outfit. Opt for a jumpsuit with a plunging neckline or cut-out details to show off your short haircut. Add a pair of strappy heels or sandals to complete the look.

Accessories to Complete Your Look

Accessories are the finishing touch to any outfit, and they can make all the difference when it comes to showing off your short haircut. Here are a few accessories that will complement your new 'do perfectly:

Statement Earrings

Statement earrings are a great way to draw attention to your face and neckline, which will highlight your short haircut. Opt for earrings with bold colors or interesting shapes to add some extra personality to your outfit.

Hats

Hats can be a great accessory for short haircuts, especially if you're having a bad hair day. Try a floppy hat or a fedora to add some extra style to your outfit.

FAQs About Outfits for Short Haircuts

What kind of tops look good with short haircuts?

Off-the-shoulder tops, crop tops, and v-neck tops are all great options for showing off your short haircut.

Can I still wear long necklaces with a short haircut?

Yes! Long necklaces can actually look great with short haircuts, especially if they're layered.

What kind of shoes should I wear with a short haircut?

The shoes you wear will depend on the occasion and your personal style. Sneakers, sandals, loafers, and heels can all look great with short haircuts.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require a different kind of hair care than longer haircuts. Here are a few tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

Wash Your Hair Less Often

Short haircuts can get greasy more quickly than longer haircuts, so try washing your hair every other day instead of every day. This will help your hair retain its natural oils and keep it looking healthy.

Invest in a Good Styling Product

A good styling product can make all the difference when it comes to short haircuts. Try a pomade, wax, or paste to add texture and hold to your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require more frequent trims than longer haircuts. Try to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Short haircuts can be incredibly stylish and versatile, and with the right outfits and accessories, you can show off your new 'do in style. Whether you're heading to the office or out for a night on the town, there's an outfit that will complement your short haircut perfectly. Just remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and good styling products, and you'll be rocking your short haircut with confidence in no time!
