Does Long Hair Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Petter Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a round-faced beauty wondering if long hair is a good look for you? Look no further! In this article, we will answer the age-old question, “Does long hair look good on round faces?” We’ll provide you with tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions on how to rock long locks with your lovely round face.

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a forehead and jawline that are of similar width. If you have a round face, you know that certain hairstyles can make your face appear even rounder. That’s why it’s important to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

How-To

To determine if long hair is a good look for your round face, follow these steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a soft measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Look at Celebrities with Round Faces

Look at celebrities with round faces who have rocked long hair. Some examples include Chrissy Teigen, Emma Stone, and Selena Gomez. Take note of how they style their hair to flatter their face shape.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Texture

Long hair looks different on every person based on their hair texture. Consider if your hair is straight, curly, or wavy. Certain textures may make your face appear rounder, while other textures may elongate your features.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can provide you with personalized recommendations based on your face shape and hair texture. They can help you determine the best length, layers, and styling techniques to flatter your round face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling long hair with a round face:

  • Stay away from blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones. These cuts can make your face appear fuller.
  • Opt for layers to add dimension and movement to your hair. Soft, face-framing layers can elongate your features.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to balance out your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the top of your head. This can make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and ponytails, to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can round faces wear long hair?

Yes, round faces can wear long hair! The key is to find a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length, layers, and styling techniques for your round face.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that elongates your features. Consider adding layers, side-swept bangs, and volume at the crown of your head. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones.

Can bangs look good on round faces?

Yes, bangs can look good on round faces! Side-swept bangs, in particular, can balance out a round face shape. Consider consulting with a hairstylist to determine the best bang style for your face shape.

Hair Care

Long hair requires extra care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep your long hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis. Instead, air dry your hair or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to enhance your natural texture.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Now that you know the answer to the question “Does long hair look good on round faces?” it’s time to take the plunge and try out a new hairstyle! With the right cut and styling techniques, you can rock long hair and enhance your natural beauty.
