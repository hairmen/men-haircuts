Does Short Hair Make Your Head Look Bigger

Written by Robby Jul 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking about chopping off your locks but worried that it might make your head look bigger? Don't fret, you're not alone! Many people have this same concern, especially those with round or oval-shaped faces. In this blog post, we'll explore whether or not short hair really does make your head look bigger and provide tips on how to rock a short hairstyle with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can definitely make your head look bigger if it's not styled properly. When hair is cut short, it can expose more of your face and head, making it appear more prominent. However, there are ways to style short hair to make your head appear smaller and more proportional.

How To

The key to making short hair work for you is to find a style that suits your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help to elongate your face and make your head look smaller. If you have an oval-shaped face, a pixie cut can work well as it adds dimension and texture to your hair.

Step 1: Consult with a Hair Stylist

Before chopping off your locks, it's important to consult with a hair stylist who can guide you on the best style for your face shape and hair type. They can also provide tips on how to style your hair to make your head appear smaller.

Step 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Once you've found a style that works for you, don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles. Adding layers or highlights can help to create the illusion of more volume and texture, making your head appear smaller.

Step 3: Use Hair Products to Add Volume

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your short hair looks. For example, using a volumizing mousse or spray can help to add volume and texture to your hair, making your head look smaller.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make short hair work for you:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Add layers or highlights to create dimension
  • Use hair products to add volume and texture
  • Accessorize with hats, headbands, or scarves to draw attention away from your head
  • Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you

FAQ

Does short hair make your head look bigger?

Short hair can make your head look bigger if it's not styled properly. However, there are ways to style short hair to make your head appear smaller and more proportional.

What hairstyle suits a round face?

A short bob with layers can help to elongate a round face and make your head look smaller.

What hairstyle suits an oval face?

A pixie cut can work well for those with an oval-shaped face as it adds dimension and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your short hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

In conclusion, short hair doesn't have to make your head look bigger if it's styled properly. Consult with a hair stylist to find a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles. With the right styling techniques and hair care routine, you can rock a short hairstyle with confidence!
