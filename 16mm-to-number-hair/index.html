16Mm To Number Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Description

16mm to number hair is a common hair measurement system used in many countries. It involves measuring the length of hair in millimeters and then converting it into a corresponding number. The number system is usually based on a scale of 1 to 10, with 1 being the shortest and 10 being the longest. This system is used by many hairdressers and barbers to ensure that they cut the hair to the desired length.

How to Convert 16mm to Number Hair

Converting 16mm to number hair is relatively simple. First, you need to understand the number system used by your hairdresser or barber. Most hairdressers use the following number system:

1 = 3mm

2 = 6mm

3 = 9mm

4 = 12mm

5 = 15mm

6 = 18mm

7 = 21mm

8 = 24mm

9 = 27mm

10 = 30mm

Using this system, you can easily convert 16mm to number hair by dividing 16 by 3 and rounding up to the nearest whole number. In this case, 16mm is equivalent to a number 6 haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to convert 16mm to number hair:

  1. Measure your hair using a ruler or measuring tape.
  2. Convert the measurement from millimeters to centimeters if necessary.
  3. Divide the measurement by 3.
  4. Round up to the nearest whole number.
  5. Refer to the number system used by your hairdresser or barber to determine the corresponding haircut number.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when converting 16mm to number hair:

  • Always measure your hair when it's dry and straight to get an accurate measurement.
  • If you're unsure about the number system used by your hairdresser or barber, ask them to explain it to you.
  • Remember that hair grows at an average rate of 1cm per month, so you may need to adjust your haircut frequency depending on your desired length.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 16mm to number hair:

What if my hair is longer than 30mm?

If your hair is longer than 30mm, you can still use the number system by dividing the length by 3 and rounding up to the nearest whole number. For example, if your hair is 45mm long, it would be equivalent to a number 8 haircut.

What if I want a specific length?

If you want a specific length, you can always ask your hairdresser or barber to cut your hair to that length in millimeters. It's always better to be clear about what you want to avoid any miscommunication.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair, such as bleaching and perming.

By following these tips and tricks, you can convert 16mm to number hair with ease and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
