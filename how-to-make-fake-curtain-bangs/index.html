How To Make Fake Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of trying out curtain bangs but not quite ready to commit to a haircut? Or maybe you have shorter hair and can't quite achieve the look? Fear not, because we have a solution for you: fake curtain bangs! In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to make fake curtain bangs that look just as good as the real thing.

Description: What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a trendy hairstyle that involves long, wispy bangs that frame the face. They got their name because they resemble a curtain that is parted in the middle. Curtain bangs can be worn by people with any hair type or length and add a stylish touch to any hairstyle.

How to Make Fake Curtain Bangs: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section at the crown of your head, with the point of the triangle towards the front of your head.

Step 2: Clip the rest of your hair away

Clip the rest of your hair away so that you have a clear view of the triangle-shaped section you created.

Step 3: Create your bangs

Using a comb, create a part in the middle of the triangle-shaped section. Starting at the part, create two sections of hair on either side of your face. These sections will be your fake curtain bangs.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Tease the hair in both sections to create volume and texture. This will make your fake bangs look more natural and blend in with the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Use bobby pins

Use bobby pins to secure your fake bangs in place. Pin them back towards the crown of your head, making sure they are even on both sides.

Step 6: Style your hair

Style the rest of your hair as desired. You can leave it down or pull it back into a ponytail or bun.

Tips for Making Fake Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips to help you make your fake curtain bangs look as natural as possible:

  • Use a comb to create a precise part in the middle of your hair.
  • Tease your hair to create volume and texture.
  • Pin your fake bangs back towards the crown of your head to keep them in place.
  • Blend your fake bangs in with the rest of your hair by styling it as desired.

FAQs

How long will fake curtain bangs last?

Fake curtain bangs will last until you take them out. They are a temporary solution and can be easily removed at the end of the day.

Can I use hair extensions to make fake curtain bangs?

Yes, you can use hair extensions to make fake curtain bangs. Simply clip them in place and follow the same steps as outlined above.

Can I make fake curtain bangs if I have short hair?

Yes, you can make fake curtain bangs even if you have short hair. Simply create your bangs using the hair at the front of your head and follow the same steps as outlined above.

Hair Care Tips

Remember to take care of your hair when creating fake curtain bangs. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Don't use too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a nourishing conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

And there you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to make fake curtain bangs. Now you can enjoy the trendy hairstyle without committing to a haircut.
