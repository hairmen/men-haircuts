How To Make Curly Thick Frizzy Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly thick frizzy hair, you know how challenging it can be to tame it and make it look good. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can have gorgeous, defined curls that are frizz-free and easy to style. In this article, we will share with you our top tips and tricks on how to make curly thick frizzy hair look good.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

The first step in making curly thick frizzy hair look good is to understand your hair type and its needs. Curly hair tends to be dry, and frizz is often caused by lack of moisture. Therefore, it's essential to use products that provide hydration and nourishment to your hair.

The following are the steps to follow to make your curly thick frizzy hair look good:

Step 1: Start with a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter. These ingredients help to moisturize and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and manageable.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair. This will help to lock in moisture and keep your curls defined. Look for a lightweight leave-in conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

Step 3: Use a Curl-Defining Cream

Apply a curl-defining cream to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Look for a cream that is specifically designed for curly hair and contains natural ingredients like aloe vera or argan oil.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser attachment.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Oil

After your hair is dry, apply a hair oil to the ends to add shine and reduce frizz. Look for a lightweight hair oil that won't weigh down your hair. Apply a small amount and work it through the ends of your hair.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to help you make your curly thick frizzy hair look good:

  • Don't wash your hair too often. Curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it too frequently can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about making curly thick frizzy hair look good:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a regular conditioner instead of a leave-in conditioner?

A: While you can use a regular conditioner, a leave-in conditioner provides more moisture and helps to define your curls.

Q: What should I do if I have a lot of frizz?

A: Try using a hair serum or oil to tame frizz. Apply a small amount to the ends of your hair and work it through with your fingers.

Hair Care:

Proper hair care is essential for making curly thick frizzy hair look good. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to provide extra hydration to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can have beautiful, defined curls that are frizz-free and easy to style.
