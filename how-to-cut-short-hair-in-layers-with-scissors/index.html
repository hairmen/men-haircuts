How To Cut Short Hair In Layers With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add texture and movement to your short hair, layers are the perfect solution. And the best part? You can do it yourself at home with just a pair of scissors. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut short hair in layers with scissors, including tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.

How to Cut Short Hair in Layers with Scissors

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and clips to hold your hair in place. It's also a good idea to have a mirror or two, so you can see what you're doing from different angles.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

It's best to start with clean, dry hair, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that work well for your hair type, and blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use your comb to section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Clip the top part out of the way for now.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Starting with the bottom layer, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the scissors vertically and cut into the hair, making small snips to create texture and movement. Continue this technique throughout the bottom layer, working your way up to the top layer.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Once you've finished the bottom layer, unclip the top layer and repeat the same technique. Take small sections of hair and cut into them vertically, creating layers that blend seamlessly with the bottom layer.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

After you've cut both layers, it's time to blend them together. Use your scissors to snip into the hair at an angle, creating a soft, blended look. Be sure to use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, it's important to check your work from all angles. Use your mirrors to make sure your layers are even and well-blended. If you notice any areas that need a little more attention, go back and make small adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair in Layers with Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your short hair in layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once
  • Hold your scissors vertically to create texture and movement
  • Use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair
  • Check your work from all angles to ensure even, well-blended layers

FAQ: Cutting Short Hair in Layers

Can I cut my short hair in layers with scissors at home?

Yes! With the right tools and technique, you can cut your short hair in layers at home. Just be sure to start with clean, dry hair and use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair.

How do I know how many layers to cut?

The number of layers you cut depends on the length of your hair and the look you're going for. If you're not sure, start with just a few layers and add more as needed.

Hair Care for Short Hair in Layers

Once you've cut your short hair in layers, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that work well for your hair type, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair soft and hydrated. Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can also help maintain your layers and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily cut short hair in layers with scissors at home. Just remember to start with clean, dry hair, use a light touch, and check your work from all angles for the best results.
