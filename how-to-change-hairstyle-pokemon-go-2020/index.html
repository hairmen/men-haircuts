How To Change Hairstyle In Pokemon Go 2020

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re an avid Pokemon Go player, you may have noticed that your avatar’s hairstyle has been the same for quite some time. Fortunately, changing your avatar’s hairstyle in Pokemon Go is a simple process. This guide will walk you through the steps of changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go 2020.

Description

Changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is an easy way to customize your avatar and make it stand out from the rest. With the recent updates, players can now choose from a variety of hairstyles, colors, and accessories to make their avatar truly unique.

How to Change Hairstyle in Pokemon Go 2020

Follow these steps to change your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

Step 1: Open the Game

Open the Pokemon Go app on your device and log in to your account.

Step 2: Go to the Avatar Customization Screen

Tap on the Pokeball icon at the bottom of the screen to open the main menu. Select the "Settings" option, then tap on "Style" and "Customize" to access the avatar customization screen.

Step 3: Change Your Hairstyle

Swipe left or right to browse through the available hairstyles. Tap on the hairstyle you want, then select the color you prefer. You can also choose from a variety of accessories, such as hats, glasses, and backpacks.

Step 4: Save Your Changes

Once you’ve chosen your new hairstyle and accessories, tap on the “Save” button to apply your changes. Your new avatar will now be visible to other players in the game.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and colors to find the perfect look for your avatar.
  • Consider adding accessories like hats or glasses to make your avatar stand out even more.
  • Don’t forget to save your changes before exiting the customization screen.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

Can I change my avatar’s gender?

Yes, you can change your avatar’s gender by going to the customization screen and selecting the “Gender” option.

Do I have to pay to change my avatar’s hairstyle?

No, changing your avatar’s hairstyle is completely free.

Can I change my avatar’s hairstyle multiple times?

Yes, you can change your avatar’s hairstyle as many times as you want.

Hair Care

While changing your avatar’s hairstyle in Pokemon Go is simple and fun, it’s important to remember to take care of your own hair as well. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your own hair looking healthy and beautiful while enjoying the ability to change your avatar’s hairstyle in Pokemon Go.
