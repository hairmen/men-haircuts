How To Do Men s Haircut Short On Sides Long On Top

Written by Bardi Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men? The short on sides and long on top hairstyle is a popular choice for men of all ages. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style. In this article, we will show you how to do a men's haircut short on sides long on top in relaxed English language.

Description

The short on sides and long on top haircut is characterized by shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair short and low maintenance on the sides while having enough length on the top to style and experiment with. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to

Here are the steps to follow for a men's haircut short on sides long on top:

Step 1: Gather the tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the necessary tools, including hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle with water.

Step 2: Cut the sides

Start by cutting the hair on the sides to your desired length. Use a clipper guard to ensure an even cut. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 3: Cut the top

Next, cut the hair on the top to your desired length. Use scissors to create texture and layers for a more natural look.

Step 4: Blend the hair

Blend the hair on the sides and the top using scissors or clippers. This will create a seamless transition between the shorter sides and longer top.

Step 5: Style the hair

Finally, style the hair on the top using a comb or your fingers. You can use hair products like wax, pomade, or gel to create different styles and textures.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a men's haircut short on sides long on top:

- Start with longer hair and gradually cut it shorter to avoid any mistakes. - Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure the haircut is even on both sides. - Use hair products to create different styles and textures. - Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut short on sides long on top:

Q: How long does this haircut last?

A: This haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks depending on the rate of your hair growth.

Q: Can this haircut be customized to suit my face shape?

A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit your face shape. Consult with your barber or stylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Hair Care

To maintain this haircut, you should visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. Use hair products like shampoo, conditioner, and hair oil to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Conclusion

The men's haircut short on sides long on top is a stylish and trendy haircut that can be customized to suit your personal style. Follow the steps and tips in this article to achieve the perfect haircut. Remember to visit your barber or stylist regularly for maintenance and use hair products to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
