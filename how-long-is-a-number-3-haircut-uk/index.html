How Long Is A Number 3 Haircut Uk

Written by Robby Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about how long is a number 3 haircut in the UK! If you're someone who is looking to try out a new hairstyle or just curious about different haircuts, then this post is for you. In this article, we'll be discussing the length of a number 3 haircut in the UK, how to get it, and some tips for maintaining your new haircut.

Description

A number 3 haircut in the UK is a popular choice for men who want a short, but not too short, haircut. It is typically cut with clippers and leaves the hair 3/8 of an inch long (or about 9.5mm). This length is longer than a number 2 haircut (1/4 inch or 6.4mm) and shorter than a number 4 (1/2 inch or 12.7mm).

How to Get a Number 3 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a number 3 haircut, the first step is to find a trusted hairdresser or barber. Once you've found someone you trust, you can ask for a number 3 haircut and they will use clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.

If you're cutting your own hair, you'll need a good quality set of clippers and a steady hand. Start by trimming the sides of your head with a number 3 guard attached to the clippers. Work your way around your head, making sure to keep the clippers level and to avoid cutting too much hair in one go.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide for getting a number 3 haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Attach a number 3 guard to your clippers.
  3. Start at the sides of your head, moving the clippers up and down in a smooth motion.
  4. Move to the back of your head and repeat the same motion.
  5. Trim the hair around your ears and neckline with scissors or clippers.
  6. Use a comb to blend any uneven spots.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your number 3 haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 3 haircuts:

How long is a number 3 haircut in mm?

A number 3 haircut in the UK is approximately 9.5mm long.

What is the difference between a number 2 and a number 3 haircut?

A number 2 haircut is 6.4mm long, while a number 3 haircut is 9.5mm long. The difference is subtle, but noticeable.

Can you get a number 3 haircut with scissors?

A number 3 haircut is typically done with clippers, but it is possible to achieve the same length with scissors. However, it may take longer and require more skill.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after washing to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Visit a hairdresser or barber regularly for trims and touch-ups.

So there you have it, everything you need to know about how long is a number 3 haircut in the UK. Whether you're getting your hair cut by a professional or doing it yourself, a number 3 haircut is a great option for a stylish and low-maintenance look.
