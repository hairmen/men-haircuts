How To Style Medium Length Hair With Long Layers

Written by Eveline Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length hair with long layers is a versatile hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to add volume and movement to their hair. It can be styled in various ways, from sleek straight hair to beachy waves. In this blog post, we will show you how to style medium length hair with long layers in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

Description

Medium length hair with long layers is a hairstyle that is cut to shoulder length and has layers that start from the mid-length to the ends of the hair. This hairstyle adds volume and movement to the hair, making it perfect for those who have thin or flat hair. With the right styling techniques, medium length hair with long layers can be transformed into various hairstyles that suit any occasion.

How to Style Medium Length Hair with Long Layers

There are many ways to style medium length hair with long layers, from sleek and straight to beachy waves. Here are some popular hairstyles that you can try:

Sleek and Straight

To achieve a sleek and straight look, start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush, and then use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves. Finish off with a light hair serum for added shine.

Beachy Waves

For a beachy wave look, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Apply a salt spray to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance the waves. Finish off with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their long layers. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron, and then take the top section of your hair and tie it into a half ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands, and then finish off with a hairspray for added hold.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style medium length hair with long layers:

  1. Wash your hair with the appropriate shampoo and conditioner for the desired look.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush or diffuser attachment.
  3. Style your hair according to the desired look, such as sleek and straight or beachy waves.
  4. Finish off with a hair serum or texturizing spray for added shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair with Long Layers

Here are some tips for styling medium length hair with long layers:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to prevent hair breakage.
  • Don't wash your hair every day to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Use hair accessories such as hairbands and clips to add a pop of color or sparkle to your hairstyle.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my hair if I have medium length hair with long layers?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to prevent drying out your hair.

2. Can I still style my hair if I have thin or flat hair?

Yes, you can still style your hair if you have thin or flat hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and use volumizing products such as salt sprays and texturizing sprays to add volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair with Long Layers

To maintain the health and shape of your medium length hair with long layers, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent hair damage.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

With these styling tips and hair care tips, you can achieve a versatile and trendy hairstyle with medium length hair with long layers. Experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your hair!
