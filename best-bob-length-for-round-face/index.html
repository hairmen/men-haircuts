The Best Bob Length For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that will flatter your round face? Look no further than the bob! This versatile and timeless haircut can be customized to suit any face shape, including round faces. But with so many bob lengths to choose from, it can be hard to know which one will be the most flattering for you. In this guide, we'll explore the best bob length for round faces, as well as how to style and care for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Bob Length for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Bob Haircut
  • Tips for Styling Your Bob
  • FAQs about Bobs for Round Faces
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Bob Haircut

How to Choose the Best Bob Length for Round Face

When it comes to bobs for round faces, the key is to create the illusion of length and angles. This means avoiding blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones, which can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a longer bob that grazes your collarbone or even your shoulders. This length will elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Another option is to add layers to your bob, which will create movement and texture. Layers can also help to frame your face and create angles. However, be careful not to add too many layers, as this can make your hair look stringy and thin.

Ultimately, the best bob length for you will depend on your personal style, hair type, and face shape. It's always a good idea to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect length for your bob.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Bob Haircut

Ready to take the plunge and get a bob haircut? Here's what to expect:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bob length for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Sit down in the salon chair and let your hairstylist section your hair.
  4. Using sharp scissors, your hairstylist will cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. If you're adding layers, your hairstylist will use a razor or shears to create texture and movement.
  6. Once your haircut is complete, your hairstylist will blow-dry and style your hair.
  7. Voila! You now have a chic and stylish bob haircut.

Tips for Styling Your Bob

Now that you have a bob haircut, it's time to style it! Here are some tips to help you get the perfect look:

  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron, depending on your preferred style.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and texture to fine or thin hair.
  • Try a sleek and straight look for a sophisticated vibe, or tousled waves for a more relaxed look.
  • Accessorize with a headband, scarf, or statement earrings to add some personality to your hairstyle.

FAQs about Bobs for Round Faces

Q: Can I still get a bob if I have thick hair?

A: Absolutely! In fact, bobs can be a great choice for thick hair, as they can help to lighten up your locks and make them more manageable. Your hairstylist can also add layers to your bob to create movement and texture.

Q: Will a bob haircut make my face look wider?

A: It depends on the length and style of the bob. As we mentioned earlier, a longer bob that grazes your collarbone or shoulders can actually elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. However, a blunt bob that ends at your chin or cheekbones can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a bob haircut?

A: Absolutely! While bobs can be shorter than traditional hairstyles, you can still wear your hair up in a ponytail, bun, or half-up style. You may need to use bobby pins or a hair tie to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Your Bob Haircut

Now that you have a bob haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for your new 'do:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat tools every day, as this can damage your hair. Try air-drying your hair or using heatless styling methods instead.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your bob looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking your new bob haircut in no time. Whether you opt for a sleek and straight look or tousled waves, your bob will flatter your round face and make you feel confident and stylish.
