What to Do If Wedding Dress Is Too Short - Tips and FAQ

If you are a bride-to-be, you know that one of the most important things you need to have on your wedding day is a perfect dress. But what if you find out that your wedding dress is too short? Don't panic! There are several things you can do to make your dress longer and more comfortable. In this article, we'll share some tips and frequently asked questions to help you solve this problem.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Wedding Dress Longer
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Avoid This Problem
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

How to Make Your Wedding Dress Longer

If your wedding dress is too short, there are several things you can do to make it longer. Here are some options:

Alter the Hemline

You can ask a professional tailor or seamstress to alter the hemline of your dress. This is a common solution for brides who have bought a dress that is too short. They can add extra fabric or lace to the bottom of the dress to make it longer, while still maintaining the overall design and style of the dress.

Add a Petticoat

Another option is to add a petticoat, which is a type of underskirt that can add volume and length to your dress. This is a great solution if you have a short dress that doesn't have much volume or if you want to add some extra flair to your dress.

Wear Heels

If you don't want to alter your dress or add a petticoat, you can always wear heels to make your dress appear longer. This is a simple solution, but it can make a big difference in how your dress looks and feels.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you decide to alter your dress or add a petticoat, here is a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

  1. Find a professional tailor or seamstress who specializes in wedding dresses.
  2. Try on your dress and discuss your options with the tailor or seamstress.
  3. If you are altering the hemline, the tailor or seamstress will measure the length of your new hemline and mark it on the dress.
  4. The tailor or seamstress will then cut off the excess fabric and sew the new hemline in place.
  5. If you are adding a petticoat, the tailor or seamstress will measure your dress and create a custom petticoat that fits underneath.
  6. Try on your dress with the alterations or petticoat and make sure you are happy with the results.

Tips to Avoid This Problem

Here are some tips to help you avoid buying a wedding dress that is too short:

  • Measure yourself carefully and compare your measurements to the size chart provided by the dress designer.
  • If you are ordering your dress online, make sure to read the reviews and look at customer photos to see how the dress fits on other brides.
  • Try on dresses in person before you buy them to make sure they fit well and are the right length.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear flats with my wedding dress?

A: Yes, you can wear flats with your wedding dress, but keep in mind that they may make your dress appear shorter. If you are worried about the length of your dress, it's best to wear heels.

Q: How much does it cost to alter a wedding dress?

A: The cost of altering a wedding dress can vary depending on the extent of the alterations needed and the location of the tailor or seamstress. It's best to get a quote from a professional before you commit to any alterations.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

Finally, don't forget about your hair on your wedding day! Here are some tips to keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy:

  • Start a hair care routine at least a month before your wedding day to ensure that your hair is in top condition.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Consider hiring a professional hairstylist to create a beautiful hairstyle for your big day.

By following these tips and solutions, you can ensure that your wedding dress is the perfect length and that your hair looks beautiful on your special day. Congratulations and best wishes!
