What Haircut Suits A Round Face

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying out different haircuts and not being able to find the right one for your round face? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on what haircut suits a round face, how to get it, and tips on how to maintain it.

Description

A round face has soft features with a circular shape and fuller cheeks. It is essential to choose a hairstyle that compliments your face shape and enhances your features. The right haircut can make your face appear longer and slimmer while balancing your features.

How To

The first step in getting the right haircut is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you should look for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown while keeping the sides and back short. This creates an illusion of length and makes your face look slimmer.

Some of the best haircuts for a round face include a long bob, layered bob, pixie cut, and a side-swept fringe. These haircuts are versatile and can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and occasion.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape
2. Look for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown
3. Keep the sides and back short
4. Choose a haircut that suits your personality and occasion
5. Consult with your hairstylist for the best haircut for your face shape

Tips

1. Avoid haircuts that add width to your face
2. Add layers to your hair to create movement and texture
3. Avoid center parting as it can make your face appear wider
4. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best
5. Use hair products that enhance your haircut and add volume to your hair

FAQs

1. Can I get a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have a round face. The key is to keep the sides and back short and add height and volume to the crown.

2. Is a middle parting suitable for a round face?

No, a middle parting can make your face appear wider. It is best to avoid center parting and opt for a side-swept fringe instead.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the look and style of your haircut. Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style. Use a hairbrush to detangle your hair and avoid using hot tools too often as it can damage your hair. Protect your hair from the sun and use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know what haircut suits a round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get the perfect haircut for your face shape. Follow our tips and take care of your hair to maintain your new look.
