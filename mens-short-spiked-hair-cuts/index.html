Mens Short Spiked Hair Cuts A Guide For 2023

Written by Aprilia Jun 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle in 2023? Mens short spiked hair cuts are a popular choice for those who want a bold, edgy look. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to achieve the perfect spiked hair cut, along with tips and tricks for maintaining and styling your new 'do.

Description

A spiked hair cut is characterized by short, choppy hair that is styled upwards to create spikes. This look is achieved by cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving some length on top. The hair on top is then styled upwards with the use of styling products, such as hair gel or pomade.

How To

The first step in achieving a mens short spiked hair cut is to find a reputable barber or hair stylist who has experience with this type of cut. Once you have found your stylist, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, it's important to have a consultation to discuss the type of spiked hair cut you want. Bring in pictures of styles you like to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Step 2: Cutting

Your stylist will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head short, typically with clippers. The length on top will be left longer, usually around 2-3 inches.

Step 3: Styling

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will apply styling product to your hair and style it upwards to create spikes. You can choose to style your spikes in different directions, such as forward or to the side, depending on your personal preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens short spiked hair cut:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your spikes in place
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you
  • Maintain your hair cut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens short spiked hair cuts:

Q: How do I style my spikes?

A: Apply styling product to your hair and use your fingers or a comb to style your hair upwards to create spikes. You can also experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended that you get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your hair cut and keep your spikes looking sharp.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair if you have thinning hair. Use a volumizing product to add body to your hair and create the appearance of thicker hair.

Hair Care

To keep your mens short spiked hair cut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect mens short spiked hair cut and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
