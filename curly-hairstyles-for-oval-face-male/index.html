Are you an oval-faced man looking for the best curly hairstyles? Curly hair can be a challenge for men of all face shapes, but with the right cut and style, you can make your curls work to your advantage. In this article, we'll show you some of the best curly hairstyles for oval face males, along with tips, how-to guides, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Tips for Choosing the Right Curly Hairstyle

When choosing a curly hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have an oval-shaped face, you're lucky because most hairstyles will look great on you. However, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height at the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Choose styles that add volume on the sides of your head, as this will balance out your face shape.
  • Consider getting a layered cut, as this will help to define your curls and add texture.

How-to: Curly Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

Here are some of the best curly hairstyles for oval face males:

The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well with curly hair. To achieve this look, part your hair on one side and sweep it to the other side. Use a hair product to keep your curls in place.

The Messy Top

The messy top is a great option if you want a more relaxed, casual look. Simply let your curls fall naturally and use a hair product to add volume and texture.

The Short and Textured Cut

A short and textured cut is a good choice if you want a low-maintenance style. This cut works well with curly hair because it adds texture and definition.

The Long and Layered Cut

If you prefer longer hair, consider a long and layered cut. This style works well with curly hair because it adds volume and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide for Curly Hairstyles

Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving the perfect curly hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and desired style.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to keep your curls in place.

FAQs About Curly Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

Q: Will a short or long hairstyle work best for oval face males with curly hair?

A: Either style can work well for oval face males with curly hair. It depends on your personal preference and the amount of maintenance you're willing to do.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner for my curly hair?

A: It's best to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help to keep your curls defined and reduce frizz.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant product and not to straighten your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause frizz.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have a better idea of the best curly hairstyles for oval face males, as well as tips, how-to guides, FAQs and hair care advice, you can rock your curls with confidence. Remember to choose a style that works well with your face shape, and use the right products and techniques to keep your curls looking their best.
