Which Hair Colour Will Suit Me Best In 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair colour, you might be wondering which one will suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the perfect shade for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. Read on to learn more!

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hair Colour

Description

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. However, selecting the perfect shade for your skin tone and personality can make all the difference in how you look and feel. In this guide, we'll go over the factors you should consider when selecting a hair colour and provide tips for keeping your locks looking vibrant and healthy.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. Generally, people fall into one of three categories: warm, cool, or neutral. If you have a warm skin tone, you'll look best in hair colours that have golden, red, or amber undertones. Cool skin tones, on the other hand, pair well with hair colours that have blue or violet undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off a wide range of hair colours.

Next, consider your eye colour. This can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, you'll look great with cool-toned hair colours like ash blonde or platinum. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warmer shades like caramel or honey will complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hair Colour

1. Determine your skin tone and eye colour.

2. Consider your natural hair colour and the level of maintenance you're willing to commit to.

3. Look for inspiration photos and consult with a stylist.

4. Consider any lifestyle or career factors that may impact your hair colour choice.

5. Choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and beautiful!

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

1. Use colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.

2. Avoid excessive heat styling, which can cause damage to coloured hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

4. Schedule regular touch-ups with your stylist to keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change look good on me?

A: It depends on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. Consult with a stylist and look for inspiration photos to help you make the decision.

Q: How often will I need to touch up my hair colour?

A: This varies depending on the type of hair colour you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid using hot tools on damp hair, as this can cause damage.

3. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

4. Avoid chlorine and saltwater, which can cause coloured hair to fade.

Now that you know how to choose the right hair colour and maintain it, you're ready to take the plunge and try something new! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Good luck!
