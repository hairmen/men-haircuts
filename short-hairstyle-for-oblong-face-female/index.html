Short Hairstyle For Oblong Face Female A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Frank Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you an oblong-faced female looking for a new hairstyle? Look no further! This guide will provide tips and step-by-step instructions to help you find the perfect short haircut to flatter your face shape. From pixies to bobs, we've got you covered.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a high forehead, prominent chin, and straight jawline. The goal of a short hairstyle for an oblong face is to add width to the face, while also balancing out its length. Short haircuts can help achieve this by adding volume and texture to the sides of the face.

How-To

When choosing a short haircut for an oblong face, consider the following:

1. Texture

Add texture to your hair with layers and choppy cuts. This will help break up the length of your face and add fullness to the sides.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out the length of your face by drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Opt for long, wispy bangs that fall diagonally across your forehead.

3. Volume

Add volume to the sides of your hair with waves or curls. This will help widen your face and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step

Ready to try out a new short haircut? Follow these steps:

1. Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and fits your personal style. They can also provide tips on how to style your hair at home.

2. Choose a Cut

Choose a short haircut that adds volume and texture to the sides of your hair. This can include bobs, pixies, or shags.

3. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a texturizing spray or mousse. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to the sides of your hair.

Tips

Follow these tips for maintaining your short haircut:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep their shape. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use Heat Protectant

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

3. Experiment with Products

Try out different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and texture. This can include texturizing sprays, mousses, and pomades.

FAQ

What Short Haircut is Best for an Oblong Face?

A short haircut with layers and texture is best for an oblong face. This can include bobs, pixies, or shags.

What Should I Avoid with an Oblong Face?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as slicked-back styles or high ponytails. These can elongate your face and make it appear even longer.

Hair Care

Follow these hair care tips to keep your short haircut looking its best:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and formulated for your hair type.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Apply conditioner from mid-length to ends, avoiding the roots.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find a short hairstyle that flatters your oblong face shape. Happy styling!
