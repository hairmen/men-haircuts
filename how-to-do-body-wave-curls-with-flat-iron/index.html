How To Do Body Wave Curls With Flat Iron A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 19, 2023 · 3 min read

Creating body wave curls with a flat iron is an easy and quick way to achieve beautiful, wavy hair. This technique is perfect for those who want to add volume, texture, and movement to their hair without using a curling iron. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of creating body wave curls with a flat iron.

Description

Body wave curls are loose, flowing waves that add a subtle, natural-looking texture to your hair. This style is perfect for those who want to add a bit of volume and movement to their hair without going overboard. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve this look in no time.

How to

To create body wave curls with a flat iron, you will need the following:

  • A flat iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Sectioning clips

Now, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section your hair into small sections using sectioning clips. The smaller the sections, the better the curls will turn out. Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up to the top.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the root of your hair. Twist the flat iron away from your face and slowly glide it down the length of your hair. Repeat this process until you have curled all of your hair.

Step 4: Set your curls

Once you have curled all of your hair, use a hairspray to set the curls in place. Hold the hairspray can about 10 inches away from your hair and spray lightly to avoid weighing down your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect body wave curls:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Section your hair into small sections to achieve more defined curls.
  • Twist the flat iron away from your face to create a natural-looking wave.
  • Use a hairspray with a flexible hold to keep your curls in place without weighing them down.

FAQ

Q: Can I create body wave curls with a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can. However, using a flat iron is a quicker and easier way to achieve this look.

Q: Can I create body wave curls on short hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to adjust the size of the sections to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

To maintain your body wave curls, use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed to enhance waves and curls. Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage to your hair. Instead, opt for heatless styling techniques such as braids or buns.

Now that you know how to create body wave curls with a flat iron, you can achieve this look anytime you want. With a bit of practice and the right tools, you can have beautiful, wavy hair in no time.
