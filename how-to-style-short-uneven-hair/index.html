How To Style Short Uneven Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short uneven hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a chic and trendy look that is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to style short uneven hair, including step-by-step instructions, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking healthy and vibrant.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Uneven Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Short Uneven Hair

The first step in styling short uneven hair is to determine the type of cut you have. Is it a layered bob, a pixie cut or a choppy bob? Knowing the type of cut you have will help you determine the best styling techniques and products to use.

Once you have determined your hair cut, you can then start experimenting with different styling techniques that work for your hair type and texture. Some of the most popular styles for short uneven hair include messy waves, sleek and straight, textured pixie, and tousled layers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your short uneven hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum.
  3. Using a hairdryer and a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use the brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a straightener to create the desired style. For messy waves, use a curling iron and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel. For a sleek and straight style, use a straightener to smooth out your hair.
  5. Finish off your style with a texturizing spray or a hair wax to add definition and hold.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect style for your short uneven hair:

  • Invest in a good quality heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and lift.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques. Short uneven hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short uneven hair:

Q: Can I style short uneven hair if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, you can style short uneven hair even if you have fine hair. Just be sure to use lightweight styling products that won't weigh your hair down.

Q: How often should I wash my short uneven hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less often. A good rule of thumb is to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I still style short uneven hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still style short uneven hair if you have curly hair. Just be sure to use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curls.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short uneven hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your short uneven hair and keep it looking healthy and fabulous. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the ones that work best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new. Your short uneven hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, so have fun with it!
