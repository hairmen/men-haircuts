Great Books For 50 Year Old Woman A Must Read List

Written by Petter Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a 50-year-old woman and you love reading, you are in the right place. In this article, we will provide you with a list of the best books that you should read. These books are perfect for women who are looking for inspiration, motivation, and some great entertainment. So, without further ado, let's dive into the list.

Table of Content

  • Fiction Books
  • Non-Fiction Books
  • Self-Help Books
  • Memoirs

Fiction Books

Fiction books are perfect for women who love to lose themselves in a good story. Here are some of the best fiction books that you should read:

The Nightingale by Kristin Hannah

This book is set in France during World War II and tells the story of two sisters who are separated by the war. It is a heartwarming and heartbreaking story that will leave you in tears.

The Great Alone by Kristin Hannah

Another great book by Kristin Hannah, this one is set in the 1970s in Alaska. It tells the story of a family who moves to Alaska in search of a better life. However, they quickly realize that life in Alaska is not as easy as they thought it would be.

The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides

This psychological thriller is about a woman who is accused of killing her husband. She refuses to speak and the story follows her therapist as he tries to uncover the truth.

Non-Fiction Books

If you are looking for something more informative, non-fiction books are perfect for you. Here are some of the best non-fiction books that you should read:

Becoming by Michelle Obama

This is a memoir by former First Lady Michelle Obama. It is an inspiring story of how she grew up in Chicago and became one of the most powerful women in the world.

The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks by Rebecca Skloot

This book tells the story of Henrietta Lacks, a woman whose cancer cells were taken without her permission and used in medical research. It is a fascinating and thought-provoking read.

Quiet: The Power of Introverts in a World That Can't Stop Talking by Susan Cain

This book is perfect for introverted women who feel like they don't fit in. It is an empowering read that celebrates the strengths of introverts.

Self-Help Books

If you are looking for some inspiration and motivation, self-help books are perfect for you. Here are some of the best self-help books that you should read:

The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen R. Covey

This book provides a step-by-step guide to help you become more effective in your personal and professional life. It is a must-read for anyone who wants to improve themselves.

You Are a Badass: How to Stop Doubting Your Greatness and Start Living an Awesome Life by Jen Sincero

This book is all about overcoming self-doubt and living your best life. It is an empowering read that will leave you feeling motivated and inspired.

The Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle

This book is all about living in the present moment and letting go of the past and future. It is a great read for anyone who wants to find inner peace and happiness.

Memoirs

Memoirs are perfect for women who love true stories. Here are some of the best memoirs that you should read:

Wild: From Lost to Found on the Pacific Crest Trail by Cheryl Strayed

This memoir is about Cheryl Strayed's journey hiking the Pacific Crest Trail. It is a beautiful and inspiring story of self-discovery and healing.

The Glass Castle by Jeannette Walls

This memoir tells the story of Jeannette Walls' childhood growing up with dysfunctional parents. It is a heartbreaking and inspiring story of resilience and hope.

Just Kids by Patti Smith

This memoir is about Patti Smith's relationship with the artist Robert Mapplethorpe. It is a beautiful and poetic story of their friendship and the 1970s art scene in New York City.

Conclusion

These are just a few of the great books for 50-year-old women. Whether you love fiction, non-fiction, self-help, or memoirs, there is something for everyone on this list. So, go ahead and pick up one of these books today. You won't regret it!
