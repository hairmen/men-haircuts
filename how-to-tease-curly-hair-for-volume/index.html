How To Tease Curly Hair For Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be challenging to achieve volume. Teasing your hair can be a great way to add volume and texture to your curls. In this guide, we’ll show you how to tease curly hair for volume in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner. We’ll cover the basics, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • The Basics
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Teasing Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Teased Curly Hair

The Basics

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of teasing your curls, let’s go over some basic information about teasing. Teasing, also known as backcombing or ratting, is a technique used to add volume and texture to hair. It involves using a comb or brush to backcomb sections of hair at the roots, creating a “teased” effect.

When teasing curly hair, it’s important to use the right tools and products. You’ll need a fine-toothed comb, a light-hold hairspray, and possibly a volumizing powder or mousse.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps to tease your curly hair for volume:

  1. Start with dry, detangled hair. Comb through your curls to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Section off the top layer of your hair, leaving the bottom layer loose. Clip the top layer out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair at the crown of your head and hold it straight up.
  4. Using your fine-toothed comb, gently backcomb the section of hair at the roots. Start at the mid-lengths and work your way down to the roots.
  5. Spray the teased section lightly with hairspray to hold the volume.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the remaining sections of hair on the top layer.
  7. Release the clipped-up top layer of hair and repeat steps 3-5 on the sections underneath.
  8. Once you’ve teased all the sections of hair, gently smooth the top layer over the teased sections to hide the backcombing.
  9. Spray your entire head lightly with hairspray to hold the volume in place.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

It’s important to tease your hair gently and not to backcomb too aggressively, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Tips for Teasing Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when teasing your curly hair:

  • Use a fine-toothed comb to tease your curls, as this will help prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Start small and work in sections, teasing one section at a time.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to hold the volume, as heavy products can weigh down your curls.
  • Consider using a volumizing powder or mousse to add extra lift and texture to your curls.
  • Don’t over-tease your hair, as this can damage your curls and make them frizzy.

FAQs

Here are some common questions people ask about teasing curly hair:

Is teasing bad for curly hair?

Teasing can be damaging to curly hair if done too aggressively or too often. It’s important to tease your hair gently and to use a light-hold hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Can you tease wet curly hair?

No, you should never tease wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage to your curls. Make sure your hair is completely dry before teasing.

How long does teased curly hair last?

The amount of time your teased curls will last depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, teased hair will last for a day or two, but you can touch it up with a little hairspray if needed.

Hair Care for Teased Curly Hair

Teasing can be damaging to hair, so it’s important to take care of your curls after teasing them. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair too much, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls soft and healthy.

By following these tips and using the right techniques and products, you can achieve voluminous, textured curls that are sure to turn heads!
