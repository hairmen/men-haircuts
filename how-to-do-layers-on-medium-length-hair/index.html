How To Do Layers On Medium Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have medium length hair and you’re looking for a way to add more volume and texture to your locks, then layers might just be the solution you’re looking for. Layers can give your hair more movement and shape, making it look fuller and more vibrant.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Layers on Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Layering Your Hair

How to Do Layers on Medium Length Hair

Layering your hair is not as difficult as it may sound. You can easily create layers on your own at home or you can visit a salon to have a professional do it for you. Here are some things to keep in mind before you start layering your hair:

  • Consider the thickness of your hair. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid creating too many layers as it can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, try adding just a few layers to give your hair some movement.
  • Think about the length of your hair. If you have shorter hair, you may want to create shorter layers. If you have longer hair, you can create longer layers.
  • Consider the shape of your face. Layers can be used to accentuate certain features of your face, such as your cheekbones or your jawline. Talk to your stylist to determine which layers would work best for your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to create some layers on your medium length hair? Follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to work with.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  3. Separate your hair into sections. You can do this by using hair clips or hair ties to keep each section separate.
  4. Take one section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the section of your hair between your fingers, about an inch or two from the end.
  5. Cut the hair that is sticking out from between your fingers. This will create a shorter layer of hair.
  6. Repeat this process with each section of your hair, making sure to cut each section to the same length.
  7. When you’re finished, shake out your hair to see the layers you’ve created. You can also trim any uneven sections to create a more even look.

Tips for Layering Your Hair

Layering your hair can be a great way to add volume and texture to your locks. Here are some tips to help you create the perfect layered look:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can create uneven layers, which can make your hair look choppy and messy.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once. It’s better to take smaller sections of hair and cut them a little at a time to create a more natural look.
  • Don’t be afraid to mix up the length of your layers. Shorter layers can add volume and texture to the top of your hair, while longer layers can give your hair more movement.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to accentuate your new layers.

FAQs

1. How often should I get my hair layered?

You should get your hair layered every 8-12 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows. This will help to maintain your layered look and prevent split ends.

2. Can I layer my hair if it’s curly?

Yes! In fact, layering can be a great way to enhance your natural curls and give your hair more shape and movement.

3. Will layering my hair make it look thinner?

Not necessarily. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid creating too many layers as it can make your hair look even thinner. However, adding a few layers can give your hair some movement and make it look fuller.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

Once you’ve layered your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair some extra nourishment.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain your layered look.

With these tips and tricks, you can create beautiful layers on your medium length hair and enjoy a fresh new look that’s full of volume and texture. Whether you choose to do it at home or visit a salon, layering your hair can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your style.
