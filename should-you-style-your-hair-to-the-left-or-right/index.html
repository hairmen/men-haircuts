Should You Style Your Hair To The Left Or Right

Written by Daniel Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to styling your hair, there are endless possibilities. But one question that often arises is whether you should style your hair to the left or right. The answer isn't always straightforward, as it depends on a variety of factors. In this blog post, we'll explore the pros and cons of both options and give you some tips on how to decide which direction is best for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The direction in which you style your hair can have a significant impact on your overall look. It can change the way your face appears, make your hair look fuller or thinner, and even affect the way people perceive you. So, it's essential to choose the right direction for your hair.

How to

Before deciding which direction to style your hair, you need to consider a few factors. These include your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences.

Hair Type

Thick, coarse hair tends to look better when styled to the left, while finer hair can benefit from being styled to the right. However, this isn't a hard and fast rule, and it's always best to experiment to find what works for you.

Face Shape

Your face shape can also play a role in determining which direction to style your hair. For example, if you have a round face, styling your hair to the left can help elongate your face and make it look slimmer. On the other hand, if you have a square face, styling your hair to the right can soften your features and give your face a more rounded appearance.

Personal Preferences

Ultimately, the direction in which you style your hair is a matter of personal preference. While it's essential to consider your hair type and face shape, you should also choose the direction that makes you feel the most confident and comfortable.

Step by Step

If you're still unsure which direction to style your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you decide:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair straight.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a few photos of yourself with your hair styled to the left and to the right.
  4. Compare the photos and see which direction you prefer.
  5. If you're still undecided, try asking a friend or family member for their opinion.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when deciding which direction to style your hair:

  • Consider the occasion. Some hairstyles may be more appropriate for formal events, while others are better suited for casual occasions.
  • Experiment with different products. Using different styling products can help you achieve different looks and make it easier to style your hair in a particular direction.
  • Don't be afraid to switch things up. If you've been styling your hair to the left for years, try switching it up and styling it to the right. You may be surprised at the results.

FAQ

Should I style my hair to the left or right if I have a widow's peak?

If you have a widow's peak, it's usually best to style your hair away from it. This means styling your hair to the opposite side of your peak. For example, if your widow's peak is on the left, you should style your hair to the right.

Can I style my hair in the middle?

Yes, you can style your hair in the middle. However, it's worth noting that this style works better for some people than others. If you have a round face, styling your hair in the middle can make your face appear even rounder. On the other hand, if you have an oval face, a middle part can be very flattering.

Hair Care

No matter which direction you choose to style your hair, it's essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

So, should you style your hair to the left or right? Ultimately, the choice is yours. By considering your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences, you can find the direction that works best for you. And with a little experimentation and some good hair care, you'll be able to rock your new style with confidence.
