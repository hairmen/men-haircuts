How To Ask For A Tapered Haircut In Relaxed English Language

Written by Oliver Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning to get a new haircut and want to try a tapered cut? Knowing how to communicate with your hairstylist is essential to achieve the look you want. In this article, we’ll guide you on how to ask for a tapered haircut in relaxed English language, including how to prepare for the cut, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • How to Ask for a Tapered Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining a Tapered Haircut
  • FAQs

Preparation

Before getting a tapered haircut, it’s important to do some research and find inspiration photos online or in magazines. Look for images of people with similar hair types and face shapes. This will give you an idea of what to ask for and help your hairstylist understand your vision.

It’s also a good idea to bring your own photos to show your hairstylist. This will give them a better idea of what you want and help avoid any miscommunication.

How to Ask for a Tapered Haircut

When you’re at the salon, speak up and communicate clearly with your hairstylist. Here are some phrases you can use to ask for a tapered haircut:

  • “I would like a tapered cut, please.”
  • “Can you give me a tapered haircut?”
  • “I want a haircut with shorter sides and longer length on top.”
  • “Please taper the back and sides of my hair.”

Be specific about the length you want and how gradual you want the taper to be. You can also ask for a fade, which is a more gradual taper. For example:

  • “Can you give me a low fade on the sides and back?”
  • “I want a high fade with a longer length on top.”

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get a tapered haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Discuss your desired length and style with your hairstylist.
  3. Your hairstylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair shorter than the length on top.
  4. The length of the taper will depend on your preference.
  5. If you want a fade, your hairstylist will gradually blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer length on top.
  6. Once the taper is complete, your hairstylist will style your hair with product.

Tips for Maintaining a Tapered Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your tapered haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with the right products for your hair type.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about tapered haircuts:

Q: Can I get a tapered haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, a tapered haircut can work for curly hair. Just be sure to discuss your desired length and style with your hairstylist, and ask for recommendations on products and styling techniques.

Q: How often should I get a touch-up for my tapered haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the taper grows out. Generally, a touch-up every 2-4 weeks is recommended.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated for healthy hair.

Now you know how to ask for a tapered haircut and how to maintain it. With these tips and advice, you’ll have a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you!
