How To Make Frizzy Hair Smooth Instantly

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Frizzy hair can be a nightmare to deal with, especially when you're in a hurry. Fortunately, there are ways to make frizzy hair smooth instantly. In this article, we will go over how to make frizzy hair smooth instantly and provide tips for maintaining smooth hair.

Description

Frizzy hair can be caused by a multitude of factors, including humidity, damage, and genetics. When the hair cuticle is raised, it allows moisture to enter and causes the hair to become frizzy. Additionally, when the hair is damaged, it can become more prone to frizz.

How to Make Frizzy Hair Smooth Instantly

Here are some quick and easy ways to make frizzy hair smooth instantly:

1. Use a Smoothing Serum

Apply a smoothing serum to your hair to help tame frizz. Look for serums that contain ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil, which can help to smooth the hair cuticle. Apply the serum to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush for best results.

2. Use a Flat Iron

A flat iron can help to smooth frizzy hair instantly. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using the flat iron to prevent damage. Starting at the roots, clamp a section of hair between the plates and slowly glide the flat iron down the length of the hair. Repeat until all sections of hair are straight.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Applying a hair mask can help to moisturize and smooth frizzy hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients like shea butter or keratin, which can help to strengthen and smooth the hair. Apply the mask to damp hair and leave on for the recommended amount of time before rinsing.

4. Use a Boar Bristle Brush

A boar bristle brush can help to distribute natural oils throughout the hair, which can help to smooth frizz. Start by brushing your hair from the roots to the ends, and then brush from the ends to the roots. This will help to detangle any knots and smooth the hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make frizzy hair smooth instantly:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin or argan oil, which can help to smooth the hair.

2. Apply Smoothing Serum

Apply a smoothing serum to your damp hair, focusing on the ends. Use a quarter-sized amount for short hair and a dime-sized amount for long hair.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through the hair as you go.

4. Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Starting at the roots, clamp a section of hair between the plates and slowly glide the flat iron down the length of the hair. Repeat until all sections of hair are straight.

5. Apply a Hair Mask

Apply a hair mask to your hair once a week to help moisturize and smooth frizz. Leave on for the recommended amount of time before rinsing.

6. Use a Boar Bristle Brush

Use a boar bristle brush to smooth any remaining frizz. Brush from the roots to the ends and then from the ends to the roots.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining smooth hair:

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair and cause frizz. - Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair, as it is more gentle on the hair. - Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the hair and cause frizz. - Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair from becoming tangled and frizzy while you sleep.

FAQ

What causes frizzy hair?

Frizzy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, damage, and genetics.

How can I prevent frizzy hair?

To prevent frizzy hair, avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a smoothing serum or hair mask to help moisturize and smooth the hair.

What are some good ingredients to look for in hair products for frizzy hair?

Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, argan oil, or coconut oil, which can help to moisturize and smooth the hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for preventing frizz. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping the hair of its natural oils. - Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. - Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated. - Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
