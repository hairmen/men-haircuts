Best Short Hairstyles For Oblong Faces In 2023

Written by Aprilia Aug 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds width to the face and balances out the length. Lucky for you, we've put together a list of the best short hairstyles for oblong faces in 2023!

Description

Oblong faces are longer than they are wide, with a prominent chin and high forehead. The goal of a short hairstyle for an oblong face is to add width to the face and create balance. This can be achieved with a variety of hairstyles, from pixie cuts to bobs.

How to

The first step in finding the best short hairstyle for your oblong face is to determine your face shape. Once you've done that, you can start exploring different hairstyles that will flatter your features. Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, like layered bobs or textured pixie cuts. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as these can make your face appear even longer.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Research hairstyles that flatter oblong faces.

3. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.

4. Consult with your hairstylist to ensure the hairstyle will work for you.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face, keep in mind that the length should be no shorter than chin-length. This will help balance out the length of your face.

Additionally, consider adding layers or texture to your hairstyle to create volume and movement. This will help add width to your face and make it appear more balanced.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for oblong faces?

The best short hairstyles for oblong faces are those that add width to the face and create balance. This can be achieved with layered bobs, textured pixie cuts, and long bobs with layers.

How do I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your jawline is rounded, you have a round face. If your face is longer than it is wide and your jawline is square, you have an oblong face.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use a styling product, like mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and texture to your hair. And visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your oblong face in 2023.
