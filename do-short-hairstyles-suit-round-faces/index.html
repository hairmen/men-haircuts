Description

Short hairstyles can be a great way to switch up your look, but if you have a round face, you may be wondering if they will suit you. The answer is yes! There are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter a round face shape and enhance your features.

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, the key is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, such as layering, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face

If you have a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Look for hairstyles that create height and length in the crown area.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or boxy, as these can make the face appear even rounder.
  • Consider adding layers, which can create movement and texture while also slimming the face.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to create angles and draw attention away from the width of the face.
  • Asymmetrical cuts, such as a bob with one side longer than the other, can also help to create the illusion of length and angles.

Examples of Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

Here are some short hairstyles that can flatter a round face:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob with side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Textured crop
  • Short shag

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Here's how to style short hair for a round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a slight curl at the ends. This will help to create the illusion of length.
  4. If you have bangs, blow-dry them to the side to create angles.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any sections that need it.
  6. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as wax or pomade, to create texture and hold.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • When blow-drying, use a low heat and avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can damage it.
  • Consider investing in a good quality flat iron and heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

FAQs

What short hairstyles suit round faces?

Short hairstyles that create height and length in the crown area, such as a pixie cut with side-swept bangs or a layered bob with side-swept bangs, can flatter a round face. Asymmetrical cuts and textured crops can also help to create the illusion of length and angles.

Can I still have bangs with a round face?

Yes, but it's best to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can accentuate the roundness of your face. Side-swept bangs are a great option, as they can create angles and draw attention away from the width of the face.

Do I need to use special products for short hair?

Yes, it's important to use products that are designed specifically for short hair, as they are formulated to provide the right amount of hold and texture without weighing down your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Round Faces

Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can dry it out and make it prone to breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Overall, short hairstyles can definitely suit round faces, as long as you choose the right style and take care of your hair. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can enhance your features and look your best.
