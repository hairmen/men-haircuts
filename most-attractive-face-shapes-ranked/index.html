Most Attractive Face Shapes Ranked

Written by Oliver Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you curious about which face shapes are considered the most attractive? Here, we will rank the top face shapes and explore why they are so appealing. If you're looking to enhance your natural beauty, understanding your face shape can help you make informed decisions about hairstyles, makeup, and more. Let's dive in!

1. Oval Face Shape

The oval face shape is often considered the most attractive because of its balanced proportions. This face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly curved jawline. Many hairstyles and makeup looks flatter this face shape, so it's a great canvas for experimentation.

2. Heart Face Shape

The heart face shape has a wider forehead and a narrower chin, creating a heart-like shape. This face shape is often associated with a youthful, feminine look. Hairstyles that draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones can be especially flattering on this face shape.

3. Square Face Shape

The square face shape has a strong, angular jawline and a forehead that is roughly the same width as the jaw. This face shape is often associated with strength and confidence. Softening the jawline with hairstyles and makeup can create a more balanced and appealing look.

4. Round Face Shape

The round face shape is characterized by a wide hairline and a full, rounded cheekbone area. This face shape is often associated with a friendly, approachable look. Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head can help elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

5. Diamond Face Shape

The diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. This face shape is often associated with a graceful, elegant look. Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head and create width at the temples can balance out the face and enhance its natural beauty.

6. Long Face Shape

The long face shape is characterized by a high forehead and a long, narrow shape. This face shape is often associated with sophistication and elegance. Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face can create the illusion of width and balance out the proportions.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape can be a helpful first step in enhancing your natural beauty. To determine your face shape, start by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in a mirror. Consider the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, as well as the length of your face. Based on these proportions, you can determine which of the above face shapes best describes your features.

Tips for Enhancing Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, there are many ways to enhance your natural beauty. Some tips to consider include:

  • Choosing hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  • Using makeup to highlight your best features
  • Wearing clothing that balances out your proportions
  • Considering cosmetic treatments to enhance your natural beauty

FAQ

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, focus on enhancing the features that are most prominent and balancing out any contrasting proportions.

Can I change my face shape?

While you can't change your natural face shape, there are many ways to enhance your features and create a more balanced, attractive look. Consider consulting with a beauty professional to explore your options.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

When it comes to hair care, choosing the right style for your face shape can make all the difference. Some tips to consider include:

  • For oval face shapes, try a layered cut or a side-swept fringe
  • For heart face shapes, try a pixie cut or a chin-length bob
  • For square face shapes, try a long, layered cut or a side-parted bob
  • For round face shapes, try a layered cut with volume at the crown
  • For diamond face shapes, try a layered cut with volume at the temples
  • For long face shapes, try a layered cut with volume at the sides

By understanding your face shape and choosing the right hairstyles, makeup, and clothing, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
