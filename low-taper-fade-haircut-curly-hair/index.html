Description

A low taper fade haircut for curly hair is a style that gradually fades from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. The taper is low, meaning that it doesn't go too high up the head, and the fade is gradual, giving a more natural look. This style works well for curly hair because it allows the curls to be more defined and adds dimension to the hair.

How to Get a Low Taper Fade Haircut for Curly Hair

Getting a low taper fade haircut for curly hair is a simple process. You can either go to a barber or stylist or do it yourself at home. Here are the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry.
  2. Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face.
  3. Use clippers to start the taper, starting at the bottom of the hairline and working your way up. The length of the hair should gradually get shorter as you move up the head.
  4. Blend the hair between the taper and the longer hair on top using scissors or clippers, depending on your skill level.
  5. Trim the top of your hair to your desired length.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining a Low Taper Fade Haircut for Curly Hair

To keep your low taper fade haircut looking fresh, here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the shape of the style.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when wet.
  • Avoid brushing your curls when dry, as it can lead to frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the low taper fade haircut for curly hair:

What face shape does this style work best on?

This style works well on most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Can I still wear my hair curly with this style?

Yes! This style works well with curly hair and can actually enhance your curls.

Is this style high maintenance?

Not particularly. Regular trims and a good hair care routine will keep this style looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and defined. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid towel drying your hair, as it can lead to frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.
