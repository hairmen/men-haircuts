How To Make Hair Hold Style Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Sep 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're struggling to keep your hair in place throughout the day, you're not alone. Many people find it difficult to achieve lasting hold, especially if they have fine or thin hair. But with the right techniques and products, you can create a style that stays put all day long. Follow these simple tips to make your hair hold style:

Description

When it comes to styling hair, everyone wants their look to last all day. Unfortunately, for many of us, the style we create in the morning is gone by lunchtime. Whether you're dealing with fine or thin hair or you simply can't get your style to hold, there are plenty of ways to improve your chances of success.

How to Make Hair Hold Style

Choose the Right Products

When it comes to making hair hold style, the products you use are key. Look for products that offer strong hold without leaving your hair feeling sticky or crunchy. Start with a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and fullness to your hair, then use a strong-hold hairspray to lock your style in place.

Prep Your Hair Properly

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is more difficult to work with and is less likely to hold a style. If you have fine or thin hair, use a lightweight volumizing spray or mousse to add texture and hold. If you have thick or curly hair, use a smoothing serum or cream to tame frizz and flyaways.

Use the Right Tools

The tools you use to style your hair can make a big difference in how well your style holds up. Use a high-quality blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle to direct the airflow and create a smooth, polished finish. If you're using hot tools like a curling iron or flat iron, make sure they're the right size for your hair and that you're using them correctly.

Section Your Hair

When styling your hair, it's important to work in sections. This will help you create a more polished finish and ensure that your style is evenly distributed throughout your hair. Use clips or hair ties to section your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Keep Your Style in Place

Once you've created your desired style, use a strong-hold hairspray to keep it in place. Hold the can 8-10 inches away from your head and mist lightly over your hair. Don't overdo it, as too much hairspray can leave your hair feeling stiff and unnatural.

Step by Step

Follow these step-by-step instructions to make your hair hold style:

Step 1: Prep your hair by washing and drying it.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and fullness.

Step 3: Section your hair using clips or hair ties.

Step 4: Use a high-quality blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle to create a polished finish.

Step 5: Use hot tools like a curling iron or flat iron to create your desired style.

Step 6: Use a strong-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make your hair hold style:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your style to fall out.
  • If you have fine or thin hair, avoid heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help define your curls.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to refresh your style throughout the day.

Hair Care

Remember that the key to making hair hold style is to start with a healthy foundation. This means taking care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding damaging practices like excessive heat styling and harsh chemicals. Be sure to use a deep conditioner or hair mask regularly to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair hold style and keep your look polished and perfect all day long.
