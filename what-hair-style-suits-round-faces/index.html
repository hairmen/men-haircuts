What Hair Style Suits Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. While this face shape is often associated with youthfulness, many people with round faces struggle to find the right hair style to flatter their features. The key to finding a hair style that suits a round face is to create the illusion of length and angularity. In this article, we will explore the different hair styles that work best for round faces, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair to maintain your new look.

How to

When choosing a hair style for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and angularity. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including:

  • Adding layers to create volume at the crown of the head
  • Creating asymmetrical cuts to create the illusion of length
  • Opting for a side-swept fringe to elongate the face
  • Choosing a hair style with height at the crown and a tapered back to create the illusion of length

It is also important to consider the texture of your hair when choosing a hair style. Those with fine hair may want to add layers to create volume, while those with thick hair may want to opt for a blunt cut to create a sleeker look.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the most flattering hair styles for round faces:

Long layers

  1. Start with freshly washed hair, and towel dry until damp
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair, and comb through to distribute evenly
  3. Blow dry your hair using a large round brush to create volume at the crown of your head
  4. Create long layers around your face, starting just below your chin
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

Side-swept fringe

  1. Part your hair to the side, and comb through to remove any tangles
  2. Create a deep side part, and use a round brush to blow dry your hair away from your face
  3. Create a side-swept fringe by cutting your hair at an angle from your part to your cheekbone
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth your hair, and finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a hair style that suits a round face:

  • Opt for a hair style with height at the crown to create the illusion of length
  • Choose a hair style that is longer than chin length to elongate the face
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face appear rounder
  • Avoid center parts, which can make the face appear wider
  • Consider adding highlights to create dimension and draw attention away from the face

FAQ

What hair colors are best for round faces?

While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, warm hair colors such as honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut brown can help to create the illusion of length and dimension. It is best to consult with a professional stylist to determine which hair color will work best for you.

Can short hair work for round faces?

Yes, short hair can work for round faces. The key is to create the illusion of length and angularity through techniques such as asymmetrical cuts and side-swept fringes.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect hair style for your round face, it is important to take good care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons on a daily basis
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your style
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair

With the right hair style and proper care, those with round faces can achieve a look that is both flattering and stylish.
