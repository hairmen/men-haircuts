How To Know What You Would Look Like With Bangs

Written by William Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs, but unsure if they'll suit you? It's a common concern, as bangs can drastically change your appearance. Fortunately, there are several ways to get an idea of what you would look like with bangs before committing to the cut. In this article, we'll explore some of those methods and provide tips for ensuring your new bangs look great.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Know What You Would Look Like with Bangs
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Know What You Would Look Like with Bangs

1. Use a Virtual Makeover Tool

One of the easiest ways to see what you would look like with bangs is to use a virtual makeover tool. There are several apps and websites that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including bangs. This gives you a realistic idea of what you would look like with bangs, without the commitment.

2. Try Clip-In Bangs

If you're not ready to commit to a permanent bangs haircut, try clip-in bangs. These are fake bangs that clip onto your hair, giving you a temporary fringe. Clip-in bangs are a great way to experiment with different bang styles and lengths before committing to a cut.

3. Ask Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is an expert in hair and can give you advice on whether bangs would suit you. They can also recommend different bang styles and lengths that would flatter your face shape and hair type. Make sure to bring photos of bangs you like to your appointment.

Step by Step

1. Research Bang Styles

Before getting bangs, research different styles to find one that suits your face shape and hair type. Some popular bang styles include blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and choppy bangs.

2. Consider Your Face Shape

Not all bang styles look great on every face shape. For example, blunt bangs can make a round face appear rounder, while side-swept bangs can soften square jawlines. Consider your face shape when choosing a bang style.

3. Get a Professional Cut

Unless you're experienced with cutting hair, it's best to get your bangs cut by a professional. A hairstylist can ensure your bangs are the right length and shape for your face, and can make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

1. Start with Longer Bangs

If you're unsure about bangs, start with longer bangs that can be swept to the side. This gives you the option to pin them back if you don't like them, or to gradually cut them shorter if you do.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Bangs can look different depending on your hair texture. If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to opt for longer, side-swept bangs that blend in with your curls. If you have straight hair, blunt bangs may be a good option.

3. Use Styling Products

Styling products can help keep your bangs in place and prevent them from becoming greasy or oily. Use a small amount of hair gel, mousse, or hairspray to style your bangs.

FAQ

Q: How do I know if bangs will suit me?

A: Consider your face shape and hair type. Your hairstylist can also give you advice on whether bangs would flatter your features.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: Bangs typically need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Hair Care

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to prevent your bangs from becoming oily or greasy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Bangs

Blow dry your bangs after washing them to ensure they dry straight and smooth. Use a round brush to help shape them.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat tools to style your bangs, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

By following these tips and methods, you can get a good idea of what you would look like with bangs and make an informed decision about whether to get the cut. Remember to always consult with a professional hairstylist and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
