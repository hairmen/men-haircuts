Is Feathered Hair Coming Back

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, many people are wondering if feathered hair is making a comeback. Feathered hair was a popular hairstyle in the 1970s and 1980s, but it fell out of favor in the 1990s. However, with the resurgence of vintage fashion trends, it's not surprising that feathered hair is once again on people's minds.

Description

Feathered hair is a layered haircut that creates a feather-like appearance. It was popularized by celebrities like Farrah Fawcett and Priscilla Presley in the 1970s. The style involves cutting the hair in layers and then using a razor or scissors to feather the ends. This creates a soft, wispy effect that frames the face.

How To

If you want to try feathered hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to find a good stylist who has experience with this type of haircut. Feathered hair requires a certain level of skill to execute properly, so don't be afraid to ask for references or look at the stylist's portfolio.

Once you've found a stylist, you can discuss the details of the haircut. Feathered hair works best on medium to long hair, so if your hair is shorter, you may need to grow it out first. You'll also want to consider the shape of your face and the texture of your hair. Feathered hair looks great on most face shapes, but it may not work as well on very curly or thick hair.

During the haircut, your stylist will likely use a razor or scissors to create the feathered effect. They may also add layers to give the hair more volume and movement. Once the haircut is finished, you can style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer to create the feathered look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting feathered hair:

  1. Find a stylist who has experience with feathered haircuts
  2. Discuss the details of the haircut, including the length, shape, and texture of your hair
  3. During the haircut, your stylist will likely use a razor or scissors to create the feathered effect
  4. Once the haircut is finished, style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer to create the feathered look

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining feathered hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking full and bouncy
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to create a messy, beachy look
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about feathered hair:

Is feathered hair easy to maintain?

Feathered hair can be relatively easy to maintain, depending on the length and texture of your hair. If you have straight hair, you may be able to simply blow dry it and go. If you have curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling it.

What face shapes look best with feathered hair?

Feathered hair can look great on most face shapes, but it's especially flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces. The layers help to frame the face and accentuate the cheekbones.

Can I get feathered hair if my hair is very thin?

Feathered hair can work on thin hair, but you'll want to make sure that your stylist doesn't remove too much volume. You may also want to consider adding some layers or highlights to give your hair more dimension.

Hair Care

To keep your feathered hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can damage the cuticle

Overall, feathered hair is a fun and trendy hairstyle that can work on a variety of hair types and face shapes. If you're thinking about trying it out, make sure to find a skilled stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful!
