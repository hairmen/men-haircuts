Best Bangs For Heart Shaped Face 2021

Written by Robby Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a heart-shaped face? Do you love bangs but don't know which ones will suit you the best? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best bangs for heart-shaped faces in 2021. Whether you have long hair or short hair, there is a perfect bang for everyone. So, let's get started!

Description

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones with a pointed chin. The aim of bangs is to balance out the face shape and draw attention to your eyes. The right kind of bang can make your face appear more symmetrical and proportionate. With the right cut, you can accentuate your best features and create an overall polished look.

How to Choose the Best Bangs for Heart Shaped Face

Before we dive into the best bangs for heart-shaped faces, let's take a look at how to choose the right type of bang. The first thing to consider is your hair texture. The second consideration is your face shape.

For heart-shaped faces, the best type of bangs are long side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and choppy bangs. These styles soften the forehead and balance out the chin. Straight-across bangs should be avoided as they highlight the forehead and make the chin appear more pointed.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieve the Best Bangs for Heart Shaped Face

Once you've decided on the type of bangs you want, it's time to achieve the perfect look. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the best bangs for heart-shaped faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Comb the hair straight down over your forehead.
  4. Use hair-cutting scissors to cut the bangs at an angle, starting from the middle and working your way outwards.
  5. Trim the bangs to your desired length.
  6. Use thinning shears to create a choppy look if desired.
  7. Style the bangs as desired using a blow dryer and round brush.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Now that you've got the perfect bangs, it's time to maintain them. Here are some tips on how to keep your bangs looking great:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs oil-free between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum to keep your bangs smooth and shiny.

FAQs About Best Bangs for Heart Shaped Face 2021

What are the best bangs for heart-shaped faces?

The best bangs for heart-shaped faces are long side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and choppy bangs. Straight-across bangs should be avoided.

How do I cut my bangs for a heart-shaped face?

To cut your bangs for a heart-shaped face, section off the hair you want to cut, comb it straight down, and cut at an angle from the middle to the outer edges. Use thinning shears to create a choppy look if desired.

How do I style my bangs for a heart-shaped face?

To style your bangs for a heart-shaped face, blow dry them using a round brush and style as desired. Use a small amount of hair serum to keep them smooth and shiny.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Face

In addition to maintaining your bangs, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips on how to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Now that you know the best bangs for heart-shaped faces in 2021, it's time to head to the salon and get a new look. Whether you choose long side-swept bangs or choppy bangs, you're sure to love your new style. Remember to maintain your bangs and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
