How To Do Half Up Half Down Hairstyles With Extensions

Written by Aprilia Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that allows you to show off your locks while keeping them out of your face, then the half up half down hairstyle is perfect for you. Adding extensions to this hairstyle gives you an extra oomph and volume to your hair. In this article, we'll teach you how to do half up half down hairstyles with extensions, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Half Up Half Down Hairstyles with Extensions
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare your hair extensions

Before you start styling your hair, make sure that you have the right hair extensions for your hair type. Clip-in extensions are the easiest to use for this hairstyle. Prepare your extensions by brushing them and making sure they're free from tangles.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts horizontally, starting from the top of your ears. Clip the top section of your hair up to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Attach the extensions

Attach the extensions to the bottom section of your hair. Start by attaching one clip at a time, starting from the middle and working your way out to the sides.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Tease the crown section of your hair for volume. Use a comb or a teasing brush to backcomb your hair gently.

Step 5: Create your half up half down hairstyle

Take the top section of your hair and pull it back. Secure it with a hair tie or a clip. You can leave a few strands of hair out to frame your face.

Step 6: Blend your extensions

Blend your extensions with your natural hair by gently combing or brushing them together.

Step 7: Style your hair

You can curl or straighten your hair, depending on the look you're going for. You can also add accessories like hair pins or headbands to complete your look.

Tips

  • Choose hair extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Don't over-tease your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it difficult to comb later.
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

What type of extensions should I use?

Clip-in extensions are the easiest to use for this hairstyle. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.

How do I blend my extensions with my natural hair?

Gently comb or brush your extensions with your natural hair to blend them together. You can also use a hair straightener or curling iron to blend them together.

How do I choose the right hair tie or clip for my hairstyle?

Choose a hair tie or clip that matches your hair color and is strong enough to hold your hair in place. Avoid using tight hair ties or clips that can damage your hair.

Hair Care

Extensions require extra care to keep them looking good. Here are some tips to keep your extensions and natural hair healthy:

  • Brush your hair gently to avoid tangles.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to do half up half down hairstyles with extensions, you can create a variety of looks for any occasion. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you.
