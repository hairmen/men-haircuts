How To Safely Get Hair Done During Covid

Written by Luffy Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you worried about getting your hair done during the Covid pandemic? You're not alone. Many people are concerned about the safety of visiting a salon or stylist during these times. But with the right precautions, it is possible to get your hair done safely. Here's how:

How to

1. Book an Appointment

First, make sure to book an appointment in advance. Many salons and stylists are limiting the number of clients they see each day, so it's important to secure your spot ahead of time. When you book, ask about the salon's safety protocols and what measures they are taking to protect clients.

2. Wear a Mask

When you arrive at the salon, make sure to wear a mask. Most salons require clients to wear masks during their appointment. If you don't have a mask, the salon may provide one for you. Make sure to wear the mask properly, covering your nose and mouth at all times.

3. Wash Your Hands

Before your appointment, wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water. This will help prevent the spread of germs. When you arrive at the salon, you may also be asked to use hand sanitizer.

4. Maintain Social Distance

While you're at the salon, try to maintain a safe distance from other clients and staff. This may mean waiting in your car until your appointment time, or sitting in a designated waiting area that is spaced out from other clients. Follow any signage or directions provided by the salon.

5. Avoid Touching Your Face

During your appointment, try to avoid touching your face. This will help prevent the spread of germs. If you need to adjust your mask or touch your face for any reason, make sure to wash your hands immediately afterwards.

Step by Step

Step 1: Book an Appointment

Call the salon or stylist you want to visit and ask about their safety protocols. Book an appointment in advance.

Step 2: Wear a Mask

When you arrive at the salon, put on a mask and wear it properly throughout your appointment.

Step 3: Wash Your Hands

Before your appointment, wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water. Use hand sanitizer when you arrive at the salon.

Step 4: Maintain Social Distance

Follow any signage or directions provided by the salon to maintain a safe distance from other clients and staff.

Step 5: Avoid Touching Your Face

Try not to touch your face during your appointment. If you need to, wash your hands immediately afterwards.

Tips

- If you're feeling unwell or have been in contact with someone who has Covid-19, reschedule your appointment.

- Consider bringing your own water bottle and snacks to avoid using communal items at the salon.

- Ask your stylist to wear a mask and gloves, if it makes you feel more comfortable.

FAQ

Q: Can I bring a friend or family member to my appointment?

A: Many salons are limiting the number of people allowed inside, so it's best to check with the salon before bringing someone with you.

Q: What if I can't wear a mask due to medical reasons?

A: If you can't wear a mask, speak with the salon to see if there are any alternative options available.

Q: Can I get a haircut or color done safely during Covid?

A: Yes, with the right precautions in place, it is possible to get your hair done safely. Follow the steps outlined above and check with your salon or stylist about their safety protocols.

Hair Care

After your appointment, make sure to continue taking care of your hair at home. Use good hygiene practices, such as washing your hair regularly and not sharing hair tools with others. If you have any concerns or questions about hair care during Covid, speak with your stylist.

Getting your hair done during Covid can be stressful, but with the right precautions in place, it is possible to do so safely. Remember to follow the steps outlined above, take care of your hair at home, and stay informed about any updates or changes in guidelines.
