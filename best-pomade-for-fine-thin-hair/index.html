Best Pomade For Fine Thin Hair Top Picks In 2023

Written by Luffy Nov 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have fine, thin hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a styling product that provides hold and volume without weighing your hair down. Pomade is an excellent option for those with fine, thin hair because it provides the necessary hold and texture while also adding volume and thickness to your hair. In this article, we will discuss the best pomade for fine thin hair, how to use it, and tips for hair care.

  • How to Use Pomade on Fine Thin Hair

Best Pomade for Fine Thin Hair

When it comes to finding the best pomade for fine thin hair, it's essential to look for a product that provides hold and texture without weighing your hair down. Here are our top picks:

1. American Crew Pomade

American Crew Pomade is a classic pomade that provides excellent hold and shine without weighing your hair down. It's perfect for fine, thin hair because it adds volume and texture, making your hair appear thicker and fuller.

2. Baxter of California Clay Pomade

Baxter of California Clay Pomade is a lightweight, matte-finish pomade that provides hold and texture without adding any shine. It's perfect for fine, thin hair because it adds volume and thickness without weighing your hair down.

3. Suavecito Pomade

Suavecito Pomade is a versatile pomade that provides a medium hold and a natural, matte finish. It's perfect for fine, thin hair because it adds volume and texture without weighing your hair down.

How to Use Pomade on Fine Thin Hair

Using pomade on fine thin hair is easy, but there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Apply a small amount of pomade to your fingertips
  • Rub the pomade between your palms to warm it up
  • Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up
  • Style your hair as desired

Step-by-step Guide for Applying Pomade

Here is a step-by-step guide for applying pomade to your fine thin hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up
  4. Style your hair as desired
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to create volume and texture

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your fine thin hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and volume to your hair
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

Frequently Asked Questions

Can pomade make my hair look greasy?

It's possible for pomade to make your hair look greasy if you use too much product. To avoid this, start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed.

How often should I wash my hair if I use pomade?

It's best to wash your hair every day or every other day if you use pomade regularly. This will help prevent product buildup and keep your hair looking fresh and clean.

Can I use pomade on wet hair?

No, it's best to use pomade on dry hair. Applying pomade to wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Overall, using pomade on fine thin hair can be a game-changer. With the right product and technique, you can add volume and texture to your hair while keeping it looking healthy and natural. Try out one of our top picks for the best pomade for fine thin hair and see the difference for yourself!
