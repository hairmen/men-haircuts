Best Bob For Rectangular Face A Guide To Flattering Your Face Shape

Written by Luffy Aug 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your rectangular face shape? Look no further than the classic bob! With its sleek lines and flattering angles, the bob is the perfect style for those with a rectangular face. In this article, we'll explore the best bob for rectangular face, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Best Bob for Rectangular Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bob
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Bob
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Rectangular Face Shape

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long, straight jawline and forehead. It is similar to an oval face shape, but with more angular features. The key to finding the best bob for rectangular face is to choose a style that softens the angles of the face and creates balance.

How to Achieve the Best Bob for Rectangular Face

The best bob for rectangular face is a chin-length or shoulder-length style that has soft layers and gentle curves. The layers will create movement and volume, while the curves will soften the angles of the face. The bob should also be styled with a side part, which will create the illusion of width across the forehead and balance out the proportions of the face.

Types of Bobs for Rectangular Face Shape

There are several types of bobs that work well for rectangular face shape:

  • The classic bob
  • The layered bob
  • The asymmetrical bob
  • The lob (long bob)

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bob

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your bob:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  3. Using a curling iron, curl the ends of your hair under to create a soft curve.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to create movement and hold.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob

Here are some tips for maintaining your bob:

  • Get regular trims to keep your bob looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and soft.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • When washing your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with a bob for rectangular face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a bob for rectangular face, but make sure they are side-swept and not too heavy. Bangs can help soften the angles of the face and create balance.

Q: Can I wear a bob if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob if you have thin hair. In fact, a bob can create the illusion of volume and thickness. Just make sure to add layers to create movement and avoid a flat, lifeless look.

Hair Care for Rectangular Face Shape

Here are some general hair care tips for those with a rectangular face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the angles of your face, such as a blunt bob or a center part.
  • Choose hairstyles with soft, rounded edges to create balance.
  • Keep your hair moisturized and healthy with regular conditioning treatments.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect bob for rectangular face and rock it with confidence!
