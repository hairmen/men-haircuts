Short Hairstyles For Over 60S With Thick Hair

Written by Robby Sep 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more difficult to manage. However, for those with thick hair, the opposite can be true. Thick hair can be a blessing, providing more styling options and volume. If you're over 60 and have thick hair, you may be wondering what short hairstyles are best suited for your hair type and age. In this article, we'll explore some popular short hairstyles for over 60s with thick hair.

Description

Thick hair can be challenging to style, especially as we age. Short hairstyles can be a great option for those looking for a low-maintenance, chic look. With the right cut and style, short hair can flatter and enhance your natural features while providing volume and texture.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A good stylist can help you find a cut that complements all these factors. It's also important to consider how much time you want to spend styling your hair each day. Short hair can be low maintenance, but some styles may require more effort than others.

Step by Step

1. Start by researching short hairstyles that appeal to you. Look for styles that complement your face shape and hair texture.

2. Find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts and make an appointment.

3. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style with your stylist. They can recommend a cut that flatters your features and meets your needs.

4. Once you've settled on a cut, ask your stylist for tips on styling your hair at home. They can recommend products and techniques to make styling easy.

Tips

- Consider adding layers to your short haircut to add texture and movement to your hair.

- Use a volumizing product to add volume to your hair.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.

- Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for over 60s with thick hair?

Some popular short hairstyles for over 60s with thick hair include the pixie cut, bob, and shag haircut.

Can short haircuts be low maintenance?

Yes, short haircuts can be low maintenance, but some styles may require more effort than others. Speak to your stylist about finding a cut that meets your needs.

How can I add volume to my short hair?

Use a volumizing product, such as a mousse or root lifter, to add volume to your hair. Blow drying your hair upside down can also add volume.

Hair Care

When caring for your short hair, it's essential to keep it healthy and hydrated. Use a conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools too frequently. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, short hairstyles can be a great option for over 60s with thick hair. With the right cut and style, short hair can flatter and enhance your natural features while providing volume and texture. Speak to your stylist about finding a cut that meets your needs and fits your personal style.
