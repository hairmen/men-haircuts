Best Volumizing Hair Products 2021 Give Your Hair The Boost It Needs

Written by Christine Sep 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we all know, having voluminous hair is a dream come true for many of us. It adds that extra oomph to our overall look and makes us feel confident. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally voluminous hair. That's where volumizing hair products come in handy. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best volumizing hair products of 2021 that are worth investing in.

How to Choose the Right Volumizing Hair Product?

Choosing the right volumizing hair product is crucial in achieving your desired look. Here are some factors that you should consider when choosing a volumizing hair product:

Hair Type:

It's important to choose a volumizing hair product that is suitable for your hair type. For instance, if you have thin hair, you should opt for a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can go for a heavier product that will give your hair the extra volume it needs.

Ingredients:

The ingredients used in the product can make a huge difference in the results you get. Look for products that contain ingredients such as collagen, keratin, and biotin, as these can help strengthen your hair and add volume.

Hold:

The hold of the product is also important. If you want your hair to stay voluminous all day long, you should opt for a product with a strong hold. However, if you prefer a more natural look, a product with a lighter hold would be more suitable.

Top 5 Volumizing Hair Products of 2021

1. Moroccanoil Extra Volume Shampoo and Conditioner:

Moroccanoil Extra Volume Shampoo and Conditioner is a great option for those with fine hair. This product is formulated with antioxidant-rich argan oil and revitalizing nutrients that help promote body and shine, leaving your hair looking fuller and healthier.

2. Living Proof Full Thickening Cream:

The Living Proof Full Thickening Cream is perfect for those with thin hair. This product is formulated with a patented Thickening Molecule that creates a microscopic pattern of thickening dots on each hair strand, adding volume and fullness to your hair.

3. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray:

The Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray is a versatile product that can be used on all hair types. This product is formulated with polymers that coat each hair strand, adding volume and body to your hair.

4. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray:

The Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray is a great option for those with thick hair. This product is formulated with keratin amino acids and hydrolyzed vegetable protein that help strengthen and thicken your hair, leaving it looking fuller and healthier.

5. Redken Guts 10 Volume Spray Foam:

The Redken Guts 10 Volume Spray Foam is perfect for those who want a product that provides a strong hold. This product is formulated with a directional spray nozzle that allows for precise application, adding volume and fullness to your hair.

Tips for Using Volumizing Hair Products

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your volumizing hair products:

  • Use the right amount of product. Using too much product can weigh your hair down, while using too little may not give you the desired results.
  • Apply the product to the roots of your hair. This is where you want the most volume, so make sure to apply the product directly to the roots.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down. This will help lift your hair at the roots and create more volume.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair. This will help create more volume and body.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can volumizing hair products damage your hair?

No, volumizing hair products are generally safe to use. However, it's important to use them in moderation and not to rely on them too heavily, as overuse can lead to dryness and damage.

Can volumizing hair products be used on all hair types?

Yes, there are volumizing hair products that are suitable for all hair types. However, it's important to choose a product that is specifically designed for your hair type to get the best results.

How often should I use volumizing hair products?

It depends on the product and your hair type. Some products can be used daily, while others should be used only a few times a week. It's best to follow the instructions on the product label.

Hair Care: Volumizing Hair Products

While volumizing hair products can give your hair the boost it needs, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Investing in the right volumizing hair products can make a huge difference in the way your hair looks and feels. With the above mentioned best volumizing hair products of 2021 and tips on how to use them, you can now achieve the voluminous hair of your dreams.
