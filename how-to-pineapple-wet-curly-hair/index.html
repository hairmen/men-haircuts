How To Pineapple Wet Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of waking up to frizzy, tangled curls in the morning? If so, the pineapple method may be just what you need to keep your curls intact overnight. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps of how to pineapple wet curly hair, as well as provide tips and FAQ to help you achieve the best results.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Pineapple Wet Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ about Pineappling Wet Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Pineapple Wet Curly Hair

The pineapple method is a technique used by curly girls to preserve their curls overnight. Essentially, it involves gathering your hair at the crown of your head (like a pineapple), securing it with a loose hair tie, and sleeping with your curls in this position. Here’s how to pineapple wet curly hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with wet hair. The pineapple method works best on wet hair, so wash and condition your hair as usual.

2. Detangle your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your curls, starting from the ends and working your way up.

3. Flip your head upside down. Lean forward so that your head is facing the ground.

4. Gather your curls at the crown of your head. Use your hands to gather your curls at the very top of your head, near your forehead.

5. Secure your hair with a loose hair tie. Use a scrunchie or soft hair tie to loosely secure your hair in place. Make sure not to pull your hair too tight, as this can cause creases and breakage.

6. Sleep on a satin pillowcase. Satin pillowcases are gentle on curly hair and help prevent frizz and breakage. If you don’t have a satin pillowcase, consider wrapping your hair in a satin scarf or bonnet.

Tips for Pineappling Wet Curly Hair

1. Use a leave-in conditioner. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before pineappling to help keep your curls moisturized overnight.

2. Avoid using metal hair ties. Metal hair ties can cause breakage and creases in your hair, so opt for a soft scrunchie or hair tie instead.

3. Experiment with different pineapple heights. Depending on your hair length and texture, you may need to adjust the height of your pineapple to achieve the best results.

FAQ about Pineappling Wet Curly Hair

Q: How long should I pineapple my hair for?

A: You can pineapple your hair for as long as you’d like, but most curly girls pineapple their hair overnight.

Q: Will pineappling my hair cause breakage?

A: If done correctly with a loose hair tie, pineappling should not cause breakage. However, if you notice breakage or creases in your hair, adjust the height or tightness of your pineapple.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip curly hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz.

2. Deep condition regularly. Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.

3. Avoid heat styling. Heat styling tools can damage curly hair and cause frizz. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

4. Embrace your curls. Curly hair is beautiful, so embrace your natural texture and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and products.

By following these tips and using the pineapple method, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls that last all day and night. Happy styling!
