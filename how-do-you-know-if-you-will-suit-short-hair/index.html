Description

Short hair is a bold and daring statement that not everyone can pull off. It takes a certain level of confidence, personality, and facial features to make it work. Some people are hesitant to try short hair because they are afraid it won't suit them. But how do you know if you will suit short hair?

How to Determine If You Will Suit Short Hair

Determining if you will suit short hair is a combination of personal preference and objective analysis. Here are some factors to consider:

  • Face Shape: Short hair looks best on people with oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, you may want to avoid short hair as it can make your face appear wider.
  • Hair Texture: Short hair works best on people with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, it can be more challenging to maintain a short style.
  • Personality: Short hair is a statement style that requires confidence and a bold personality. If you are someone who likes to blend in, short hair may not be for you.
  • Lifestyle: Short hair requires more maintenance than long hair. If you have a busy lifestyle or prefer low-maintenance hair, short hair may not be the best option.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you are still unsure whether short hair will suit you, here is a step-by-step guide to help you determine:

  1. Take a photo of yourself with your hair pulled back or up, exposing your face.
  2. Print out the photo and draw different short hairstyles over your hairline and around your face.
  3. Look at each hairstyle and evaluate how it looks with your face shape and features.
  4. Consider your personality and lifestyle and eliminate any styles that don't fit your criteria.
  5. Try on different wigs or use a virtual hairstyle app to see how you look with short hair.
  6. Take the plunge and get a short haircut!

Tips When Considering Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when considering short hair:

  • Bring photos of short hairstyles you like to your hairdresser to ensure they understand your vision.
  • Consider getting a consultation with a hairdresser before getting a short haircut.
  • Be open-minded and willing to try something new.
  • Take care of your short hair with regular trims and styling.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair suit me if I have a round face?

A: Short hair can make a round face appear wider, so it may not be the best option. However, you can still try short hair with a side-swept fringe or layers to elongate your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with short hair. You may need to use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or hair wax, to find what works for your hair type.
  • When blow-drying your hair, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

So, how do you know if you will suit short hair? It ultimately comes down to personal preference and whether you feel comfortable and confident with the style. Use our guide to help you determine whether short hair is right for you.
