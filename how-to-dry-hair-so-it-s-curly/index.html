How To Dry Hair So It s Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having naturally curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful when styled properly, curly hair can be difficult to manage and style. One of the key factors in achieving gorgeous curls is knowing how to dry your hair properly. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to dry your hair so it's curly.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Drying Curly Hair
  4. FAQs

How to Dry Hair So It's Curly

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand the basics of drying curly hair. First and foremost, you want to avoid using a towel to dry your hair. The rough texture of a towel can cause frizz and breakage, which is the last thing you want when trying to achieve soft, bouncy curls. Instead, opt for a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry.

Additionally, it's important to use the right products for your hair type. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl defining creams or mousse. These products will help to enhance your natural curls and keep them looking defined and bouncy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Wet Hair

The first step in drying curly hair is to start with wet hair. After washing your hair, gently blot it dry with a microfiber towel or old t-shirt. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Apply Your Products

Next, apply your preferred hair products to your hair. This could be a curl defining cream, mousse, or any other product that works well for your hair type. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Once your hair is properly coated in product, divide it into sections. The number of sections you create will depend on the thickness and length of your hair. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and turn it on to a low heat setting. Begin drying each section of your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use the diffuser to gently scrunch your hair as you dry it, which will help to enhance your natural curls.

Step 5: Finish with Cool Air

Once your hair is almost dry, switch your hair dryer to the cool air setting. Use the cool air to set your curls in place and reduce any frizz. Hold the cool air over each section of your hair for a few seconds before moving onto the next section.

Tips for Drying Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when drying curly hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Try not to use a hair dryer on its highest heat setting, as this can damage your hair and cause it to become frizzy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help nourish and protect your hair while it's drying.
  • Be patient - drying curly hair can take longer than straight hair, but the end result is worth it!

FAQs

Q: Can I air dry my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can air dry your curly hair. However, be aware that this can take a long time and may not result in the same level of curl definition as using a diffuser.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid using a regular towel to dry curly hair, as the rough texture can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, opt for a microfiber towel or old t-shirt.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Proper hair care is crucial for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Try to avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your curls less defined.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and using the right techniques to dry your hair, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that you'll love showing off.
