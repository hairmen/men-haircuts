How To Cut Long Hair Straight At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting long hair is a daunting task, especially when you want to achieve a straight and even look. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your long hair straight at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting long hair straight at home.

  1. How to Cut Long Hair Straight at Home
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Long Hair Straight at Home
  4. FAQs

How to Cut Long Hair Straight at Home

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have the following tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips or hair ties
  • Mirror

Once you have all the tools, you can follow the step-by-step guide to cut your long hair straight at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and let it air dry or blow dry it. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair to remove any tangles and make sure that your hair is smooth and straight.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use clips or hair ties to secure each section.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair by trimming the ends. Hold a small section of your hair between your fingers and cut it using the scissors. Cut straight across and avoid cutting at an angle.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Take one of the front sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat it with the other front section.

Step 6: Cut the Back

Take one of the back sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat it with the other back section.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

After cutting each section, check for evenness. Make sure that both sides are even and the length is the same.

Step 8: Trim the Bangs (Optional)

If you have bangs, you can trim them using the scissors. Comb your bangs straight down and hold them between your fingers. Cut them straight across and avoid cutting too much.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. Use a flat iron or a curling iron to add some texture and volume.

Step 10: Clean Up

After cutting your hair, clean up the area and wash the hair off the floor.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair Straight at Home

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little at a time.
  • Do not cut your hair when it is wet as it may result in uneven cut.
  • Make sure that you are cutting your hair straight across and not at an angle.
  • Take breaks in between to avoid fatigue and to check for evenness.

FAQs

Can I cut my long hair straight at home?

Yes, you can cut your long hair straight at home with the right tools and techniques.

How often should I cut my long hair?

You should cut your long hair every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends and maintain its health.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, stop and take a break. You can always trim it again later or seek professional help.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Hair Straight at Home

After cutting your hair, make sure that you take proper care of it to maintain its health and shine. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and keep it looking fresh.

Cutting long hair straight at home may seem intimidating at first, but with practice and patience, you can easily achieve a salon-worthy look. Follow the step-by-step guide and tips mentioned above to cut your long hair straight at home and flaunt your new look with confidence!
