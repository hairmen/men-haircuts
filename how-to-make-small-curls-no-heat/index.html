How To Make Small Curls No Heat

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to create small curls without using heat, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll teach you how to make small curls without any heat styling tools. From using braids to twists, there are many ways to get the perfect curls without damaging your hair. So, let's get started!

Description

Heat styling tools such as curling irons and flat irons can cause damage to your hair over time. Constantly using heat on your hair can lead to breakage, split ends, and dryness. That's why it's important to find alternative methods to style your hair. In this article, we'll show you how to make small curls without any heat, so you can have beautiful, healthy hair.

How to Make Small Curls No Heat

There are many ways to make small curls without using heat. Here are some of our favorite techniques:

1. Braids

One of the easiest ways to create small curls without heat is to braid your hair. You can create small braids all over your head, or just a few in strategic places. Leave the braids in overnight, and when you take them out in the morning, you'll have beautiful, natural-looking curls.

2. Twists

Another great way to create small curls without heat is to twist your hair. Take small sections of hair and twist them tightly. Secure the ends with a hair elastic or bobby pin, and leave them in overnight. When you wake up in the morning, take the twists out and you'll have small, bouncy curls.

3. Pin Curls

Pin curls are a classic way to create small curls without heat. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around your finger. Secure the curl with a bobby pin, and leave it in overnight. When you wake up in the morning, take the pins out and you'll have small, defined curls.

Step by Step

1. Braids

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair.
Step 2: Divide your hair into small sections.
Step 3: Braid each section tightly.
Step 4: Leave the braids in overnight.
Step 5: Take the braids out in the morning.
Step 6: Use your fingers to separate the curls.
Step 7: Apply hairspray to hold the curls in place.

2. Twists

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair.
Step 2: Divide your hair into small sections.
Step 3: Twist each section tightly.
Step 4: Secure the ends with a hair elastic or bobby pin.
Step 5: Leave the twists in overnight.
Step 6: Take the twists out in the morning.
Step 7: Use your fingers to separate the curls.
Step 8: Apply hairspray to hold the curls in place.

3. Pin Curls

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair.
Step 2: Divide your hair into small sections.
Step 3: Wrap each section around your finger.
Step 4: Secure the curl with a bobby pin.
Step 5: Leave the pins in overnight.
Step 6: Take the pins out in the morning.
Step 7: Use your fingers to separate the curls.
Step 8: Apply hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect small curls:

  • Use a lightweight hairspray to hold the curls in place.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after creating the curls, as this can cause them to fall out.
  • If you have naturally straight hair, you may want to apply some mousse or curl-enhancing product before creating the curls.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQ

Can I make small curls without any products?

Yes, you can make small curls without using any products. However, using a lightweight hairspray can help hold the curls in place.

How long do I need to leave the braids/twists/pin curls in?

You should leave the braids, twists, or pin curls in overnight to ensure that the curls set properly.

Can I use these techniques on short hair?

Yes, you can use these techniques on short hair. However, you may need to create smaller sections to ensure that the curls are tight and defined.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair while using these techniques. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a heat protectant if you do use heat styling tools. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to make small curls without any heat, you can create beautiful, healthy curls whenever you want. Whether you prefer braids, twists, or pin curls, there's a technique that will work for you. So, go ahead and try it out!
