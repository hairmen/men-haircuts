How To Wear Medium Length Hair Up A Relaxing Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length hair is the perfect length for those who want versatility in their hairstyles. It's not too long that it becomes unmanageable, but it's not too short that you're limited to a few styles. One of the best things about medium length hair is that you can wear it up or down, depending on your mood and occasion. In this tutorial, we'll be focusing on how to wear medium length hair up in a relaxed and effortless way. Keep reading to learn how!

How to Wear Medium Length Hair Up

Before we dive into the tutorial, it's important to note that there are many ways to wear medium length hair up. It's all about finding the style that works best for you and your hair type. Here are some popular ways to wear medium length hair up:

  • Messy bun
  • Half-up half-down
  • Low ponytail
  • Braided updo
  • Top knot

In this tutorial, we'll be focusing on the messy bun and the half-up half-down styles. These styles are perfect for those who want a relaxed and effortless look.

Step by Step Guide

Messy Bun

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a loose bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and pull it back into a ponytail.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  4. Tease the crown of your head to add volume.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Wearing Medium Length Hair Up

Here are some tips to help you rock your medium length hair up:

  • Use bobby pins to secure your hairstyle in place.
  • Add volume to your hair by teasing the crown.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to hide it.
  • Don't worry about making your hairstyle perfect. Messy and imperfect styles are in!
  • Accessorize with a cute hair clip or headband to add some flair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing medium length hair up:

Q: Is it okay to wear medium length hair up every day?
A: Yes, it's okay to wear your hair up every day as long as you're not pulling it too tight or using harsh hair accessories. Q: How do I prevent my hairstyle from falling out?
A: Use bobby pins and hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place. You can also try using hair ties that have a strong grip. Q: Can I wear my hair up if it's thin?
A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if it's thin. Just be sure to tease the crown of your head to add volume and use bobby pins to secure your hairstyle in place.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know how to wear medium length hair up, it's time to try out some of these styles for yourself. Whether you're running errands or going out for a night on the town, these relaxed and effortless styles are sure to turn heads. Have fun experimenting with different looks and don't forget to take care of your hair!
