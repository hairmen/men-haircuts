How To Get Rid Of Heart Shaped Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having a heart shaped face? Do you want to know how to get rid of it? Look no further as we provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of your heart shaped face.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This shape is often considered attractive, but some people may not like the way it looks on them. If you're one of those people, don't worry! There are ways to minimize the appearance of a heart shaped face and achieve the look you desire.

How to

The following are steps on how to get rid of heart shaped face:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you can get rid of your heart shaped face, you need to determine if it is indeed your face shape. There are many online resources that can help you with this. Once you're sure that you have a heart shaped face, you can proceed with the next step.

Step 2: Find the right hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in the appearance of your face. For heart shaped faces, it's best to go for hairstyles that add volume to the chin area. This can include layers, side-swept bangs, and long bobs.

Step 3: Contour your face

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you achieve a more defined face shape. For heart shaped faces, contouring the chin area can help balance out the face. Use a matte bronzer to create a shadow under the chin and blend it well.

Step 4: Choose the right accessories

Accessories can also help balance out your face shape. For heart shaped faces, earrings that are wider at the bottom can help draw attention away from the forehead and cheekbones. Avoid wearing chokers or necklaces that sit high on the neck as they can make the face look more narrow.

Step 5: Consider cosmetic procedures

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, cosmetic procedures like fillers or surgery can help reshape your face. However, these procedures can be expensive and come with risks, so it's important to do your research and consult with a qualified professional before making any decisions.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get rid of your heart shaped face:

  • Wear makeup that emphasizes your eyes instead of your cheeks
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or too voluminous at the top
  • Choose glasses with frames that are wider at the bottom
  • Practice good skin care to keep your face looking healthy and radiant

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of a heart shaped face:

Can I change my face shape?

While you can't change your bone structure, there are ways to minimize the appearance of a heart shaped face.

Do fillers hurt?

Fillers can cause some discomfort, but it's usually minimal and can be managed with the use of numbing cream.

How long do fillers last?

The duration of fillers depends on the type of filler used and the area it's injected in. Generally, fillers can last anywhere from 6 months to 2 years.

Hair Care

Proper hair care can also help you achieve the look you desire. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips and steps, you can get rid of your heart shaped face and achieve the look you desire. Remember, it's important to embrace your unique features and love yourself no matter what!
