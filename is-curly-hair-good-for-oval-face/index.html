Oval faces are considered the ideal face shape because they have balanced proportions and can pull off many different hairstyles. Curly hair can add volume and texture to an oval face, making it look even more beautiful. The natural curls can frame the face and enhance its features, giving it a youthful and playful look.

How to Style Curly Hair for Oval Faces

When it comes to styling curly hair for oval faces, the key is to embrace the natural texture of your hair. Long Layers

Long layers can add movement and shape to curly hair. They can also help to prevent your curls from becoming too heavy and flat. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in long layers that frame your face and enhance your natural curls.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can soften the angles of an oval face and draw attention to your eyes. They can also help to balance out the volume of curly hair. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle that complements your face shape.

Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on oval faces. It can be customized to suit your hair texture and face shape. A bob with blunt ends can add structure to curly hair, while a layered bob can add movement.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair for Oval Faces

Hereâs a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair for oval faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dryer than straight hair, so itâs important to use products that moisturize your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse. This will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.
  3. Flip your hair upside down and diffuse it with a diffuser attachment. This will add volume to your curls and enhance their shape.
  4. If you want to add more volume, use a pick to lift your roots and add height.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls or waves. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair for Oval Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair for oval faces:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, which can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, try washing your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you must use a heat tool, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
