What To Put On Scalp After Chemo Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Oct 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Going through chemotherapy can be a tough experience for anyone, and hair loss is one of the most common side effects that people experience. While there are many ways to cope with hair loss, one of the most effective is to take care of your scalp. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on what to put on your scalp after chemo to help with hair regrowth and overall scalp health.

Why Take Care of Your Scalp After Chemo?

Chemotherapy drugs work by targeting rapidly dividing cells in the body, which includes hair follicles. This is why hair loss is such a common side effect of chemo. While hair loss is usually temporary, it can still be a distressing experience. Taking care of your scalp after chemo can help with hair regrowth and overall scalp health.

How to Take Care of Your Scalp After Chemo: Step by Step

Step 1: Keep Your Scalp Clean

Keeping your scalp clean is important for overall scalp health. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid irritation. Avoid using hot water on your scalp, as this can further irritate it.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Scalp

Chemotherapy can leave your scalp dry and itchy. Use a gentle moisturizer to keep your scalp hydrated. Look for products that are specifically designed for sensitive skin.

Step 3: Use Essential Oils

Essential oils can help with hair regrowth and overall scalp health. Some of the best essential oils for hair growth include peppermint, rosemary, and lavender. Dilute the essential oil with a carrier oil, such as coconut oil, and massage into your scalp.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Scalp After Chemo

Tip 1: Protect Your Scalp from the Sun

Chemotherapy can make your scalp more sensitive to the sun. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your scalp when you're outside.

Tip 2: Be Gentle with Your Hair

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes or perms. Instead, opt for gentle hair products that are designed for sensitive skin.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help with hair regrowth and overall scalp health. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

FAQ About What to Put on Scalp After Chemo

What Products Should I Use on My Scalp After Chemo?

Use gentle shampoo and conditioner, a moisturizer designed for sensitive skin, and essential oils diluted with a carrier oil.

When Will My Hair Start to Regrow?

Hair regrowth after chemo varies from person to person. It can take several months to a year for hair to start regrowing.

Can I Use Hair Dye After Chemo?

It's best to avoid using hair dye until your hair has fully regrown. When you do start using hair dye again, opt for gentle, ammonia-free products.

Conclusion

Taking care of your scalp after chemo is important for hair regrowth and overall scalp health. Keep your scalp clean, moisturized, and protected from the sun. Use essential oils and eat a healthy diet to help with hair regrowth. And remember, be gentle with your hair!
