How To Slick Back Thick Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick natural hair, you may find it challenging to style your hair in a slicked-back look. However, with the right tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that will turn heads. In this guide, we'll show you how to slick back thick natural hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

  • How to Slick Back Thick Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks

How to Slick Back Thick Natural Hair

The key to slicking back thick natural hair is to have the right products and tools on hand. Here are the things you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Boar bristle brush
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair spray
  • Hair ties or bobby pins

Once you have these items, you're ready to start styling your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to slick back your thick natural hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Apply a generous amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a boar bristle brush to brush your hair back away from your face. Make sure to brush your hair in the direction you want it to lay.
  4. Continue brushing your hair back until it is smooth and sleek. Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure any loose strands in place.
  5. Once your hair is in place, spray it with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place throughout the day.

And voila! You now have a sleek and stylish slicked-back hairstyle.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to make slicking back your thick natural hair easier:

  • Use a water-based hair gel or pomade that won't dry out your hair or leave it feeling greasy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too vigorously as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Don't be afraid to add some accessories like headbands or hair clips to enhance your style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back thick natural hair:

Can I slick back my hair without using hair products?

While you can slick back your hair without using hair products, it may not stay in place for very long. Using hair gel or pomade can help hold your hair in place and give it a sleeker look.

What kind of hair gel or pomade should I use?

Look for a water-based hair gel or pomade that won't dry out your hair or leave it feeling greasy. You may need to experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

Can I slick back my hair if it's not thick?

Absolutely! You can slick back hair of any texture or thickness. The key is to have the right products and tools on hand to help you achieve the look you want.

Hair Care for Thick Natural Hair

Thick natural hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using chemical treatments like relaxers or texturizers as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your thick natural hair looking healthy and beautiful while also achieving a sleek and stylish slicked-back look.
