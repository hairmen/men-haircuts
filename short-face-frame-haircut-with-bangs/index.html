Short Face Frame Haircut With Bangs A Fresh Look For 2023

Written by Daniel Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a new and trendy hairstyle this year, a short face frame haircut with bangs might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is versatile and flattering for all face shapes, and it's low maintenance as well. In this article, we'll explore how to achieve the perfect short face frame haircut with bangs, hair care tips, and frequently asked questions.

Description

A short face frame haircut with bangs is a style that features short layers around the face that frame the features, and a set of bangs that fall across the forehead. It's a fresh and youthful look that can be customized to suit your personal style, whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or a more textured and tousled style.

How to Achieve

To achieve the perfect short face frame haircut with bangs, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with Your Stylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine if this style is right for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. They can also recommend the best products to maintain your new look.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The length of your haircut will depend on your personal preference and face shape. Generally, the layers around the face should be no longer than chin-length, and the bangs should fall just above the eyebrows. However, your stylist can help you determine the best length for your particular features.

Step 3: Create the Layers

To create the layers, your stylist will likely use a combination of scissors and a razor. They will cut the hair around your face in short, choppy layers to create texture and movement. The layers should be blended seamlessly into the rest of your hair for a natural look.

Step 4: Add the Bangs

After creating the layers, your stylist will add the bangs. They may use scissors or a razor to create a soft, wispy effect. The bangs should be cut straight across and then blended into the rest of the hair for a seamless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short face frame haircut with bangs:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to create a tousled, beachy look.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short face frame haircuts with bangs:

Can I wear this style if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! This style works well with all hair textures. Just be sure to use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

Will this style work for a round face?

Yes, it can be very flattering for a round face. The layers around the face will add definition and help elongate the face.

How often should I get this style trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your haircut. This will also help keep your bangs looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your short face frame haircut with bangs looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your short face frame haircut with bangs with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a fun and flirty vibe, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
