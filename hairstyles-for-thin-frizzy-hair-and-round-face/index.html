Hairstyles For Thin Frizzy Hair And Round Face

Written by Christine Nov 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Having thin frizzy hair and a round face can be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. However, with the right cut, styling tools, and products, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for thin frizzy hair and round face.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Thin frizzy hair can be a result of genetics, hormonal changes, or excessive styling. It is characterized by a lack of volume, texture, and shine. A round face, on the other hand, has a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The challenge is to find a hairstyle that can add height and dimension to the hair while balancing the roundness of the face.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to consult with a professional hairstylist who can assess your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Based on your consultation, your hairstylist can recommend the best haircut and styling techniques that will work for you.

Step by step

The following are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for thin frizzy hair and round face:

1. Bob with Layers

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.

Step 3: Blow-dry the hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Use a flat iron to add some waves to the hair.

Step 5: Finish with a medium hold hairspray.

2. Pixie Cut

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair.

Step 2: Apply a texturizing spray to the roots.

Step 3: Blow-dry the hair using your fingers to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Use a flat iron to create soft waves.

Step 5: Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

The following are some tips to keep in mind when styling thin frizzy hair and round face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to the hair.
  • Avoid over-styling the hair, which can cause further damage and frizz.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Invest in a quality hair dryer and flat iron that can help reduce frizz.

FAQ

The following are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for thin frizzy hair and round face:

1. Can I wear my hair long if I have thin frizzy hair and a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair long, but it is important to add layers and texture to avoid a flat and lifeless look.

2. What is the best haircut for thin frizzy hair and round face?

A bob with layers, pixie cut, or shaggy lob are some of the best haircuts for thin frizzy hair and round face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. The following are some tips on how to care for thin frizzy hair and round face:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and excessive heat styling.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering hairstyle that complements your thin frizzy hair and round face.
