What Does A Number 8 Haircut Look Like A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut but not sure which one to get, you might be wondering what a number 8 haircut looks like. A number 8 haircut is one of the longer clipper settings, and it can be a great option for those who want a bit of length but not too much. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about a number 8 haircut.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Number 8 Haircut
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining a Number 8 Haircut

Description

A number 8 haircut is a clipper setting that leaves your hair 1 inch long. This length is longer than a number 1 or 2 haircut, which are typically very short, but shorter than a number 4 or 5 haircut, which leave your hair around 1.5 to 2 inches long. A number 8 haircut can be a great option for those who want a bit of length but still want a low-maintenance style.

How to Get a Number 8 Haircut

To get a number 8 haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced with using clippers. You can bring a photo of the style you want, or you can ask the stylist for their recommendation based on your hair type and face shape. Be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length you want and any other preferences you have.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 8 haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Sit in the barber's chair and put on a cape to protect your clothing.
  3. Discuss the length and style you want with your stylist.
  4. The stylist will start by using a longer clipper guard, such as a number 6, to remove the majority of the length.
  5. They will then switch to a number 8 guard and go over your hair again to achieve the desired length.
  6. The stylist may use scissors to trim any areas that need extra attention, such as your bangs or the back of your neck.
  7. Once the cut is complete, the stylist will use a brush and blow dryer to style your hair.
  8. You can ask for any finishing touches, such as a beard trim or hairline shaping.
  9. Pay and tip your stylist, and schedule your next appointment.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 8 Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your number 8 haircut looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • If you have a beard, keep it trimmed and groomed to complement your haircut.
  • Consider using a styling product, such as a pomade or clay, to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQs

What face shapes suit a number 8 haircut?

A number 8 haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, but it works particularly well for those with square or oval faces. If you have a round face, you may want to consider a shorter haircut to balance out your features.

Can I get a number 8 haircut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! A number 8 haircut can work well with curly hair, but keep in mind that the length may appear shorter due to the curls. Be sure to discuss your hair type with your stylist to ensure that they can achieve the look you want.

Hair Care for a Number 8 Haircut

Maintaining a number 8 haircut is fairly simple, but here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to add moisture.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy.

In conclusion, a number 8 haircut is a great option for those who want a bit of length without committing to a longer style. By following these tips and working with an experienced stylist, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look that suits your face shape and hair type.
