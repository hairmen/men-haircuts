Medium Length Hair Oval Face How To Style Your Hair Perfectly

Written by Bardi Jul 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oval face and medium-length hair, you are lucky! You can get away with almost any style. However, it can be challenging to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your beauty. In this blog post, we will provide you with some tips and tricks to style your medium-length hair for an oval face shape.

Description

An oval face is considered the ideal face shape because it is perfectly balanced. It has a gently rounded hairline, a slightly wider forehead, and a rounded chin. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with different hairstyles without worrying about looking unbalanced or disproportionate.

Medium-length hair is also a versatile length. It is long enough to style in different ways, yet short enough to be low-maintenance. It is perfect for women who want the best of both worlds.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for an Oval Face

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your medium-length hair for an oval face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and curl at the ends.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or beachy curls.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium-length hair for an oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles with heavy bangs or blunt cuts. They can make your face look shorter and wider.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of a longer or shorter face.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and bobby pins to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium-length hair for an oval face:

What are the best hairstyles for an oval face with medium length hair?

The best hairstyles for an oval face with medium length hair are layered cuts, long bobs, and shoulder-length styles. They enhance your natural features and give you a polished look.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oval face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oval face. Just make sure to add some volume at the crown and curl the ends slightly to avoid a flat look.

How often should I trim my medium-length hair?

You should trim your medium-length hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your medium-length hair healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your medium-length hair for an oval face like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun!
