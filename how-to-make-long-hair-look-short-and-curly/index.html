How To Make Long Hair Look Short And Curly

Written by Christine Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having long, straight hair and want to switch things up? Well, you're in luck because in this article, we will be discussing how to make long hair look short and curly. Whether you have a special occasion coming up or just want to try out a new look, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the desired style.

Description

Making long hair look short and curly can seem like a daunting task, especially if you have never attempted it before. The key is to start by determining the type of curls you want and then choosing the right tools and products to achieve that look. In this article, we will cover how to create both tight and loose curls, as well as how to make your long hair appear shorter.

How to

To make long hair look short and curly, you will need a few basic tools and products. These include a curling iron, hair clips, hairspray, and possibly a hair tie or headband. Follow these steps to achieve your desired look:

Step 1: Determine the Type of Curls

The first step is to determine the type of curls you want. Do you want tight, spiral curls, or loose, beachy waves? This will help you choose the right size curling iron and products needed to achieve the desired style.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once you have determined the type of curls you want, section your hair into manageable parts using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that all of your hair is styled evenly.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using your curling iron, curl each section of hair in the direction you want your curls to go. For tight curls, use a smaller curling iron and hold the hair on the iron for a longer period of time. For loose waves, use a larger curling iron and hold the hair for a shorter amount of time.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your curls stay in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also add some shine to your hair and prevent any frizz from forming.

Tips

Here are a few extra tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure that your curls hold.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • If you want to make your hair appear shorter, try tying it up in a messy bun or using a headband to push it back.
  • Experiment with different products, such as mousse or curl-enhancing cream, to achieve the desired look.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different curl sizes and directions to create a more natural-looking style.

FAQ

Can I make long hair look short without cutting it?

Yes, you can make long hair look shorter by tying it up in a bun or using a headband to push it back. This will give the illusion of shorter hair without actually cutting it.

What size curling iron should I use?

The size of the curling iron you use will depend on the type of curls you want. For tight curls, use a smaller curling iron, and for loose waves, use a larger curling iron.

How do I prevent my curls from falling out?

To prevent your curls from falling out, use a light mist of hairspray after curling each section of hair. You can also use a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to help hold the curls in place.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair, especially if you are using heat tools to style it. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools.
  • Avoid using heat tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can cause buildup and make your hair appear greasy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to make long hair look short and curly, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your new look!
