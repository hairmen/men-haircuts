Best Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair And Long Face

Written by Bardi Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thick wavy hair and a long face can be a beautiful combination, but it can also be a challenge to find the right haircut that flatters your features. In this article, we will explore the best haircuts for thick wavy hair and long face, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. When combined with a long face shape, it can be challenging to find a haircut that flatters your features. The ideal haircut for thick wavy hair and long face is one that adds volume to the top of your head, while also elongating your face.

How to

When choosing a haircut for thick wavy hair and long face, it is important to consider your face shape and hair type. A layered haircut can add volume and texture to your hair, while also elongating your face. This can be achieved by adding layers to the top and sides of your hair, while keeping the length at the bottom. Another option is a long bob or lob, which can help to elongate your face and add volume to your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that flatters your features.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to ensure that the haircut you choose is suitable for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Maintain your haircut with regular trims and hair care.

Tips

When styling your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves and curls.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair and long face?

A: The ideal haircut for thick wavy hair and long face is one that adds volume to the top of your head, while also elongating your face. A layered haircut or long bob can achieve this look.

Q: How do I style my hair if I have thick wavy hair and long face?

A: Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves and curls.

Hair Care

Caring for your thick wavy hair is essential to maintaining the health and appearance of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, and use a heat protectant spray when using them. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, the best haircuts for thick wavy hair and long face are those that add volume to the top of your head while elongating your face. A layered haircut or long bob can achieve this look, and it is important to care for your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use the tips and advice in this article to find the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair and long face.
