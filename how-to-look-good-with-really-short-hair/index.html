How To Look Good With Really Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

In today’s world, short hair has become a trend, and it’s not just for men anymore. Women are also sporting short haircuts, and it’s a great way to show off your facial features and enhance your natural beauty. Short haircuts are freeing, liberating, and chic. So, if you’re considering chopping off your locks, here are some tips on how to look good with really short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Look Good with Really Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Look Good with Really Short Hair

If you have really short hair or you’re planning to get one, it’s important to know how to style it properly to look your best. Follow these tips on how to look good with really short hair:

1. Choose the Right Cut

It’s essential to choose the right cut that will flatter your face shape and features. A skilled stylist will be able to recommend the best haircut for you.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

With short hair, you have the freedom to experiment with different styles. Play around with texture, waves, curls, and braids to find your perfect look.

3. Keep It Clean

Short hair requires more maintenance than longer hair. Make sure to wash your hair frequently and keep it clean to avoid buildup and greasiness.

4. Accessorize

Use accessories to add some flair to your short hair. Try headbands, hair clips, or hairpins to add some sparkle to your hair.

5. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Don’t fight your natural texture. Embrace your curls, waves, or straight hair for a unique and beautiful look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style your short hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Choose a product that will help hold your style in place.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a flat iron, curling iron or wand to style your hair. Experiment with different styles until you find a look you love.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for looking good with really short hair:

1. Choose the Right Color

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural features and make your short hair look even better.

2. Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are specifically designed for short hair. Choose products that will add volume, texture, and hold to your hair.

3. Don’t Be Afraid to Experiment

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles, colors, and accessories. Short hair is versatile, and you can create many different looks with it.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will really short hair suit me?

Yes, really short hair can suit anyone, but it’s important to choose the right cut that will flatter your face shape and features.

2. How often should I wash my short hair?

Short hair requires more maintenance than long hair, so it’s important to wash your hair frequently to avoid buildup and greasiness. Aim to wash your hair every other day.

3. Can I still wear a ponytail with really short hair?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with really short hair. You can use hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length to your hair and create a ponytail.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Here are some tips on how to take care of your short hair:

1. Moisturize Your Hair

Short hair can become dry and brittle, so it’s important to moisturize your hair regularly. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum before using styling tools like flat irons, curling irons or wands.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Looking good with really short hair is all about choosing the right cut, experimenting with different styles, and taking care of your hair. Follow these tips and tricks to rock your short hair with confidence.
