Does Korean Bangs Suit Round Face

Written by Aprilia Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your round face? Do you want to change your hairstyle and try something new? If yes, then Korean bangs can be your new style statement. Korean bangs are very popular among women of all ages. They can transform your look in seconds. However, if you have a round face, you might be wondering if Korean bangs suit you or not.

Description

Korean bangs are a type of bangs that cover the forehead and part of the cheeks. They are straight and usually cut just above the eyebrows. Korean bangs can make your face look slimmer and longer. They also give a youthful and fresh look to your face. However, if you have a round face, you need to be careful while choosing the length and style of your Korean bangs.

How to

If you have a round face and want to try Korean bangs, here's how you can do it:

1. Consult with a hairstylist

The first and foremost step is to consult with a hairstylist. They can guide you better about the length and style of Korean bangs that suit your face shape. They can also suggest you the right hair products to maintain your bangs.

2. Choose the right length

If you have a round face, you should avoid very short or very long bangs. Opt for medium-length Korean bangs that cover your forehead and part of your cheeks. The length of your bangs should be just above your eyebrows.

3. Go for soft and wispy bangs

Soft and wispy Korean bangs can add volume and texture to your hair. They can also make your face look slimmer and longer. Avoid blunt and heavy bangs if you have a round face.

4. Style your bangs properly

Styling your Korean bangs properly can make a lot of difference. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs. You can also use a flat iron to straighten them. Avoid using too much hair products as they can weigh down your bangs.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting Korean bangs for a round face:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair completely using a towel.

2. Section your hair

Section your hair from the crown to the ears. Clip the rest of the hair.

3. Cut your bangs

Take a small section of your hair and comb it. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight just above your eyebrows.

4. Trim the sides

Trim the sides of your bangs to make them blend with the rest of your hair.

5. Style your bangs

Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush. Use a flat iron to straighten them. You can also curl them using a curling iron.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while getting Korean bangs for a round face:

  • Choose the right length and style that suits your face shape.
  • Opt for soft and wispy bangs.
  • Style your bangs properly to avoid a messy look.
  • Avoid heavy hair products that can weigh down your bangs.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your bangs.

FAQ

Q. Can Korean bangs make my face look slimmer?

A. Yes, Korean bangs can make your face look slimmer and longer.

Q. What length of Korean bangs should I go for if I have a round face?

A. If you have a round face, opt for medium-length Korean bangs that cover your forehead and part of your cheeks.

Q. How do I style my Korean bangs?

A. You can blow-dry your bangs using a round brush. You can also use a flat iron to straighten them or curl them using a curling iron.

Hair Care

To maintain your Korean bangs, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much hair products on your bangs.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and style of your bangs.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as it can cause hair breakage.

So, if you have a round face and want to try Korean bangs, go ahead and give it a try. Just make sure to choose the right length and style that suits your face shape. With the right styling and hair care, Korean bangs can be your new style statement.
