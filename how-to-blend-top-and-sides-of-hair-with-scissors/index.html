How To Blend Top And Sides Of Hair With Scissors

Written by Oliver Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking uneven and choppy? It's time to learn how to blend the top and sides of your hair with scissors. This technique will give you a seamless and natural-looking style. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to achieve this look.

Description

Blending the top and sides of your hair is a crucial step in achieving a cohesive and polished hairstyle. This technique involves using scissors to create a smooth transition between the longer hair on the top of your head and the shorter hair on the sides. By mastering this technique, you can create a variety of hairstyles, from a classic undercut to a modern textured cut.

How to Blend Top and Sides of Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into three parts: the top, the left side, and the right side. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair. It's important to remember that the hair on the sides should be shorter than the hair on the top. Use a comb to separate a small section of hair on the side, and hold it between your fingers at the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on the Sides

Using your scissors, cut the hair on the sides to the desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, making sure to cut small sections at a time.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once the sides are cut, it's time to blend the hair. Take a small section of hair on the side and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, creating a soft and seamless transition between the top and sides. Repeat this process on both sides until the hair is blended.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Now that the sides are blended, it's time to cut the top of your hair. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, making sure to blend it with the hair on the sides.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut and blended, it's time to style it. Use a brush and hair products to create the desired look. You can choose to create a sleek and polished style or a messy and textured look.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Take your time and be patient. Blending hair requires precision and attention to detail.

FAQ

How often should I blend my hair?

It's recommended to blend your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I blend my hair at home?

Yes, you can blend your hair at home with the right tools and techniques. However, it's always recommended to visit a professional hairdresser for the best results.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining a healthy and beautiful head of hair. Make sure to use high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims and styling can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to blend the top and sides of your hair with scissors, you can achieve a variety of hairstyles with ease. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always seek professional help if you're unsure about the process. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at blending your hair in no time.
