How To Fix Extremely Dry Frizzy Hair

Written by Robby Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have extremely dry frizzy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try and tame it. It can feel like no matter what you do, your hair just won't cooperate. But don't worry, there are steps you can take to help fix your dry frizzy hair. In this article, we'll go over the best ways to fix extremely dry frizzy hair and get your hair looking healthy and moisturized again.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Extremely Dry Frizzy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Dry Frizzy Hair

How to Fix Extremely Dry Frizzy Hair

The first step in fixing extremely dry frizzy hair is to identify the cause. There are many factors that can contribute to dry, frizzy hair, including:

  • Excessive heat styling
  • Over-washing
  • Chemical treatments, such as coloring or relaxing
  • Environmental factors, such as humidity

Once you identify the cause of your dry frizzy hair, you can take steps to address it. Here are some tips to help fix extremely dry frizzy hair:

Step by Step Guide

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Using a moisturizing shampoo can help add moisture back into your hair. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, or shea butter. These ingredients can help nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair is essential for keeping it moisturized. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help repair and hydrate your hair. Look for conditioners that contain ingredients like keratin, which can help strengthen your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can be very damaging to your hair, especially if you have dry, frizzy hair. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray first.

4. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Look for leave-in conditioners that contain ingredients like aloe vera, which can help soothe and hydrate your hair.

5. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day. If you must wash your hair more frequently, use a sulfate-free shampoo, which can be less drying.

6. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun's UV rays can be very damaging to your hair, especially if it's already dry and frizzy. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to help protect your hair from the sun.

Tips for Managing Dry Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing dry frizzy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing it, as this can cause frizz
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can be drying

FAQs

What causes dry frizzy hair?

Dry frizzy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including excessive heat styling, over-washing, chemical treatments, and environmental factors like humidity.

Can you fix dry frizzy hair?

Yes, you can fix dry frizzy hair. By following the tips outlined in this article, you can help restore moisture and nourishment to your hair, making it look healthy and shiny again.

How often should you use a deep conditioning treatment?

You should use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help repair and hydrate your hair.

Hair Care Routine for Dry Frizzy Hair

If you have dry frizzy hair, it's important to take extra care of it to keep it healthy and moisturized. Here's a sample hair care routine for dry frizzy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a moisturizing shampoo
  • Condition your hair with a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun's UV rays with a hat or UV-protectant spray

By following these tips and taking extra care of your hair, you can help fix extremely dry frizzy hair and get your hair looking healthy and shiny again.
