How To Workout With Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Working out is an essential part of a healthy lifestyle, but for those with curly hair, it can be a bit of a challenge. Sweat, frizz, and tangles are just some of the issues that can arise during a workout. However, this doesn't mean you have to sacrifice your hair for your fitness goals. Here are some tips and tricks on how to workout with curly hair.

  • Hair Care
  • How to Workout with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Hair Care

Before diving into how to workout with curly hair, it's important to establish a good hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a wide-tooth comb or finger-detangling to prevent breakage, and moisturizing your hair with a leave-in conditioner or oil.

It's also essential to use products specifically designed for curly hair that will provide hold and definition without weighing your hair down. Avoid using products with alcohol or sulfates as they can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

How to Workout with Curly Hair

Now that you have established a good hair care routine, let's dive into how to workout with curly hair. The key is to find a hairstyle that will keep your hair out of your face and prevent excessive sweating.

1. High Bun

A high bun is a classic hairstyle that works well for curly hair. Start by gathering your hair into a ponytail at the top of your head. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins. This hairstyle will keep your hair off your face and prevent sweat from accumulating.

2. Braids

Braids are another great hairstyle option for curly hair. French braids or cornrows will keep your hair secure and prevent tangles. Plus, they can be left in for a few days, making them ideal for those who workout regularly.

3. Pineapple

The pineapple hairstyle is perfect for those with longer curly hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the top of your head, leaving some curls out. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie and let the curls fall naturally. This hairstyle will keep your hair out of your face and prevent tangles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for a quick and easy workout hairstyle:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the top of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun or leave it as a ponytail.
  3. Secure the hairstyle with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use a headband to keep any flyaways out of your face.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you workout with curly hair:

  • Use a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain moisture.
  • Avoid touching your hair during your workout to prevent excessive sweating.
  • Try using dry shampoo or a co-wash instead of washing your hair after every workout.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair without causing frizz.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every other day while others can go a week or more without washing. Pay attention to how your hair feels and adjust your washing schedule accordingly.

Q: Can I workout with wet hair?

A: It's not recommended to workout with wet hair as it can cause breakage and damage. Always make sure your hair is dry before working out.

Q: Will wearing a hat or headband damage my curly hair?

A: It's unlikely that wearing a hat or headband will damage your curly hair, as long as they are not too tight or pulling on your hair. However, it's important to choose a breathable material to prevent excessive sweating.

With these tips and tricks, you can workout with curly hair without sacrificing your style or health. Remember to establish a good hair care routine and find a hairstyle that works for you. Happy sweating!
