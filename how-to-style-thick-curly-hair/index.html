How To Style Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. From frizz to tangles, managing your locks can be a daunting task. But fear not, we’ve compiled a comprehensive guide to help you style your thick curly hair with ease.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Thick Curly Hair

Styling thick curly hair requires a bit of patience and experimentation. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls that last all day. Here are some steps to follow when styling your thick curly hair.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to styling your thick curly hair is to wash it thoroughly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out. Rinse your hair with cold water to help lock in moisture and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Once your hair is clean, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

To dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help minimize frizz and preserve your curls’ shape. Focus the dryer on the roots of your hair and work your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Finish with Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a hair oil to add shine and combat frizz. Use a small amount and work it through your hair with your fingers.

Step-by-Step Guide

To help you achieve the perfect curly hair, we’ve broken down the styling process step by step.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to clean your hair. Rinse with cold water.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Focus on the roots and work your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Apply Hair Oil

Apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair to add shine and combat frizz.

Tips for Styling Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your thick curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQs

How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

It’s best to wash your hair once or twice a week to prevent it from drying out.

What’s the best way to comb my thick curly hair?

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Avoid brushing it when it’s dry as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your thick curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you can style your thick curly hair with confidence. Experiment with different products and styling methods to find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
