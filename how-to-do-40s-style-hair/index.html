How To Do 40S Style Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

The 1940s were known for their classic hairstyles and glamorous looks. If you're looking to add some vintage flair to your hair, then look no further than the iconic 40s style. In this guide, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of achieving the perfect 40s hairdo.

Description

40s style hair is all about creating a classic and elegant look. This style is characterized by soft waves, curls, and voluminous shapes. The key to achieving this look is to use the right products and tools, as well as to have a bit of patience and practice.

How to

Here's how to do 40s style hair in a few easy steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, then apply a heat protectant spray or serum. This will help protect your hair from damage when using hot tools.

Step 2: Create Curls

Next, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create soft curls throughout your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping each curl in place as you go. Once all of your hair is curled, let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 3: Brush Out Curls

After your curls have cooled, use a soft-bristled brush to gently brush them out. This will create soft waves and give your hair volume and texture.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair

Take the top section of your hair and pin it up, leaving the bottom section loose. This will help create a voluminous shape and give your hair that classic 40s look.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Finally, add the finishing touches to your hairdo. You can add a small hair accessory, such as a flower or a clip, to complete the look. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect 40s style hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair to add extra volume.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use bobby pins to pin your curls in place while they cool. This will help them hold their shape.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to gently brush out your curls for a soft, natural look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 40s style hair:

What products should I use to achieve 40s style hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a heat protectant spray or serum. You may also want to use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair.

What tools do I need to create 40s style hair?

You'll need a curling iron or hot rollers, as well as a soft-bristled brush and bobby pins.

How do I keep my 40s style hair in place?

Use a light hairspray to hold your hair in place, and consider using bobby pins to secure any loose curls.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using hot tools too often. Be gentle when brushing or styling your hair, and avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect 40s style hair for any occasion. Whether you're attending a wedding or just want to add some vintage flair to your everyday look, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
