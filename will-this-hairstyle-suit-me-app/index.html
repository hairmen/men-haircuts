Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use the "Will This Hairstyle Suit Me App":

The first step is to download and install the "Will This Hairstyle Suit Me App" on your smartphone. You can find the app on the App Store or Google Play Store.

Step 2: Upload a Photo of Yourself

Once you've installed the app, open it and upload a photo of yourself. It's best to use a photo where your face is clearly visible and without any accessories like sunglasses or hats.
