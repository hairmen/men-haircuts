How To Do Bangs With Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks effortlessly beautiful, it can also be difficult to style. One of the most popular styles for wavy hair is bangs. However, many people are hesitant to try bangs with wavy hair because they are not sure how to style them. In this article, we will show you how to do bangs with wavy hair and give you some tips and tricks to make your bangs look their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Bangs with Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Do Bangs with Wavy Hair

The first step in styling bangs with wavy hair is to determine the type of bangs that will work best for your face shape. There are several types of bangs, including blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and wispy bangs. Once you have chosen the type of bangs you want, you can begin styling them.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style bangs with wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally wavy, you can enhance your waves with a curl-enhancing product.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on the type of bangs you want.
  3. Section off the hair you want to be your bangs. This can be a small section or a larger section, depending on how thick you want your bangs to be.
  4. If you have straight hair, you can use a flat iron to straighten your bangs. If you have wavy hair, you can use a curling iron to add some waves to your bangs.
  5. If you want blunt bangs, cut them straight across while they are dry. If you want side-swept or wispy bangs, cut them while they are wet.
  6. Style your bangs with your fingers or a round brush. If you want more volume, you can blow-dry your bangs with a round brush.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style bangs with wavy hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to enhance your natural waves.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, you can straighten your bangs with a flat iron.
  • If you have a cowlick, you may need to style your bangs in a certain direction to keep them in place.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your bangs.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush for a sleek look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling bangs with wavy hair:

Can I cut my own bangs?

While it is possible to cut your own bangs, it is best to visit a professional stylist. They can help you choose the best type of bangs for your face shape and give you a precise cut.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It is recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

What if I don't like my bangs?

If you don't like your bangs, you can grow them out or visit a stylist to have them recut. In the meantime, you can style your bangs in different ways to make them look more flattering.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires some extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily style bangs with wavy hair and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
