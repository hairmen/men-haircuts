Step-by-Step Instructions for Updos

Here are some popular updo styles for shoulder length straight hair:

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with a hair tie
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. Pull out some strands of hair around your face for a messy look
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style

French Twist

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Tease the crown of your head to add volume
  3. Pull your hair back and twist it upwards, securing with bobby pins
  4. Tuck the ends of your hair under the twist and secure with more bobby pins
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style

Braided Updo

  1. Part your hair down the middle and braid each section
  2. Wrap the braids around the back of your head and secure with bobby pins
  3. Pull out some strands of hair around your face for a soft look
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style

Tips for Caring for Shoulder Length Straight Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your shoulder length straight hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or hair products that contain UV protection
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hair ties that can damage your hair
