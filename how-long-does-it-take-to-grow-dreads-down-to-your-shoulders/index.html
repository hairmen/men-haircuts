How Long Does It Take To Grow Dreads Down To Your Shoulders

Are you thinking about growing dreadlocks? One of the most common questions people ask is how long does it take to grow dreads down to your shoulders. The answer to this question depends on several factors. In this article, we will discuss how long it takes to grow dreads down to your shoulders, how to care for your hair during the process, and some tips to help you achieve your desired length.

How to Grow Dreads Down to Your Shoulders

Growing dreads down to your shoulders requires patience, dedication, and commitment. The length of time it takes to grow dreads down to your shoulders depends on several factors, including your hair type, the thickness of your hair, and how fast your hair grows. On average, it takes 1-2 years to grow dreads down to your shoulders.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Dreads Down to Your Shoulders

Here is a step-by-step guide to growing dreads down to your shoulders:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Avoid using any conditioner or styling products.
  2. Section your hair into small, even sections using a comb or your fingers. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your dreads to be.
  3. Twist each section of hair in a clockwise direction until it starts to knot up. Use a small amount of beeswax or dread wax to help the hair lock up.
  4. Repeat the twisting process for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in dreads.
  5. Once your dreads have formed, avoid washing your hair too often. Over-washing can cause your dreads to loosen and fall out.
  6. Use a residue-free shampoo to wash your hair every 1-2 weeks. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid product buildup.
  7. Consider using a dreadlock accelerator to help your dreads grow faster. These products contain essential oils and vitamins that promote hair growth.
  8. Be patient. Growing dreads down to your shoulders takes time, but the end result is worth the effort.

Tips for Growing Dreads Down to Your Shoulders

Here are some tips to help you grow dreads down to your shoulders:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends, which can slow down the growth of your dreads.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can weaken the hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Be gentle when washing and styling your hair to avoid pulling on your dreads and causing them to loosen or fall out.

Frequently Asked Questions About Growing Dreads

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing dreads:

  • Can anyone grow dreads? Yes, anyone can grow dreads. However, people with straight hair may find it more difficult to form and maintain dreads.
  • Do dreads have to be a certain length? No, dreads can be any length you want. Some people prefer shorter dreads, while others prefer longer ones.
  • Do dreads require a lot of maintenance? Yes, dreads require regular maintenance to keep them clean and prevent them from loosening or falling out.
  • Can I still swim with dreads? Yes, you can still swim with dreads, but be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly afterwards to remove any chlorine or salt water.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Here are some tips for caring for your dreadlocks:

  • Use a residue-free shampoo to wash your hair every 1-2 weeks.
  • Avoid using conditioner, as it can loosen your dreads.
  • Use a dreadlock-specific hair oil or spray to keep your dreads moisturized and prevent them from drying out.
  • Wear a scarf or hat to protect your dreads from the sun and wind.
  • Be gentle when washing and styling your hair to avoid pulling on your dreads and causing them to loosen or fall out.

In conclusion, growing dreads down to your shoulders takes time and dedication, but the end result is worth it. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve the length and style you desire. Remember to be patient and enjoy the journey!
