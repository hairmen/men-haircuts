How To Curl The Left Side Of Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, curling is a great option. However, it can be tricky to get the left side of your hair to curl just as nicely as the right. But fear not! In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to curl the left side of your hair like a pro.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Perfect Curls
  4. FAQs

How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair

Curling the left side of your hair starts with choosing the right curling iron. You want a curling iron with a barrel size that suits your hair length and the type of curl you want to achieve. For shorter hair, a smaller barrel is ideal, while longer hair can handle larger barrels.

Next, you'll need to divide your hair into sections. You can do this by parting your hair down the middle and then splitting it into two sections. Clip the right side of your hair out of the way to start with the left.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, it's important to prep your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and hold a hairspray to keep your curls in place. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat until you've curled all the sections on the left side of your hair.

Step 3: Repeat on the Other Side

Unclip the right side of your hair and repeat the process on the other side.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've finished curling, use a hairspray to set your curls in place. Spray lightly to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips for Perfect Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve perfect curls:

  • Use a curling iron with adjustable temperature settings to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron in one spot for too long, as this can create unwanted kinks and damage your hair.
  • For looser, beachy curls, wrap larger sections of hair around the curling iron barrel.
  • For tighter, bouncier curls, use smaller sections of hair and hold the curling iron in place for longer.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as twisting the hair before wrapping it around the barrel, to achieve different types of curls.

FAQs

How often should I curl my hair?

It's best to avoid curling your hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Aim to curl your hair no more than once or twice a week.

What's the best curling iron for my hair type?

The best curling iron for your hair type depends on your hair length and the type of curl you want to achieve. For shorter hair, a smaller barrel is ideal, while longer hair can handle larger barrels. If you want tight, bouncy curls, opt for a curling iron with a smaller barrel. For looser, beachy waves, choose a larger barrel size.

How can I make my curls last longer?

Use a hairspray to set your curls in place and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun to help your curls last longer.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo designed for curly hair to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush on your curls, as this can lead to frizz and damage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to curl the left side of your hair like a pro in no time! Remember to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best.
