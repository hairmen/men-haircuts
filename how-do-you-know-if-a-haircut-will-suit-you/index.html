How Do You Know If A Haircut Will Suit You

Written by Oliver Jul 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing a new haircut can be exciting, but it can also be daunting. A bad haircut can leave you feeling self-conscious and uncomfortable, so it's important to know how to choose a style that suits you. In this article, we will discuss how to know if a haircut will suit you.

How to

The first step in choosing a new haircut is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's important to choose a style that complements your features.

Step 1: Determine your face shape

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Oblong

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start to look at hairstyles that will suit you.

Step 2: Research hairstyles

Research different hairstyles online or in magazines that suit your face shape. Consider the length, layers, and texture of each style, as well as whether it will suit your hair type and lifestyle.

Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist

Once you have a few styles in mind, consult with your hairstylist. They will be able to advise you on which styles will suit you best, taking into account your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose a haircut that will suit you:

  • Consider your hair texture: Straight, wavy, or curly hair will suit different hairstyles.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new: A new haircut can be a great way to change up your look and boost your confidence.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a haircut that will suit you:

How do I know if a short haircut will suit me?

Short haircuts can suit many face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Consult with your hairstylist to find a short haircut that will suit you.

How do I know if a long haircut will suit me?

Long hair can be flattering on many face shapes, but it's important to choose a length that suits your features. Consult with your hairstylist to find a long haircut that will suit you.

Can I change my haircut if I don't like it?

Yes, if you don't like your new haircut, you can always change it. Talk to your hairstylist about what changes you would like to make.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your new haircut looking its best.
