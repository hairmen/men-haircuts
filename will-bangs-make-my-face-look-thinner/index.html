Will Bangs Make My Face Look Thinner

Written by Oliver Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who's looking for a way to make your face look slimmer, you might be wondering whether bangs can help you achieve that. In this article, we'll answer the question "will bangs make my face look thinner?" and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your look and frame your face. But can they really make your face look thinner? The answer is yes, they can! Depending on the type of bangs you choose, they can visually elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline.

How to

Choosing the right type of bangs for your face shape is key to achieving a slimming effect. Here are some tips:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will create the illusion of length and slim down your face.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you'll want to choose bangs that are longer in the middle and shorter on the sides. This will soften your features and create a more oval-like shape.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, just like with a round face. This will balance out your features and create a more proportional look.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! You can pretty much pull off any type of bangs. Just make sure they're not too heavy or thick, as this can overwhelm your face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape
  3. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut at an angle
  4. Repeat on the other side, making sure the length and angle are even
  5. Blend the edges with scissors or a razor

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your bangs:

  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs for a smooth finish
  • Avoid cutting your bangs too short, as this can make your face look wider
  • Experiment with different types of bangs to find the one that works best for you
  • Consider getting bangs that can be styled in different ways, such as side-swept or parted in the middle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs and face shape:

Do bangs work for all face shapes?

No, bangs don't work for all face shapes. You'll want to choose a style that complements your features and balances out your face shape.

Can bangs make my face look wider?

If you choose the wrong type of bangs for your face shape, they can make your face look wider. That's why it's important to do your research and choose a style that flatters your features.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

You'll need to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and to prevent them from getting too long and heavy.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your strands
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

So, will bangs make your face look thinner? The answer is yes, if you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Use the tips and advice in this article to find the perfect style for you and rock those bangs with confidence!
