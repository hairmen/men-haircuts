The Best Thing For Frizzy Hair In Humidity A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Oct 24, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have frizzy hair, you know the struggle of trying to tame it in humid weather. But fear not! We’ve compiled the best tips and tricks to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek, no matter the humidity level. From products to techniques, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

Best Products for Frizzy Hair in Humidity

The right products can make all the difference in taming frizzy hair in humid weather. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, and shea butter to help smooth your hair and keep it frizz-free. Here are some of our top picks:

1. MoroccanOil Treatment

This lightweight oil is perfect for taming frizz and adding shine. Apply a small amount to damp hair before styling.

2. Living Proof No Frizz Nourishing Styling Cream

This styling cream contains a patented Healthy Hair Molecule that blocks humidity and smooths hair. Apply to damp hair before blow-drying.

3. Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser’s Invisible Oil Primer

This primer helps to detangle hair, protect against heat damage, and smooth frizz. Spray onto damp hair before styling.

Best Techniques for Frizzy Hair in Humidity

Aside from using the right products, there are some techniques you can use to help keep frizz at bay in humid weather. Here are some of our top tips:

1. Air-Dry Your Hair

Avoid blow-drying your hair if possible, as the heat can cause frizz. Instead, let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair gently.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage, as it causes less friction on your hair than a cotton pillowcase.

Step-by-Step Guide to Taming Frizzy Hair in Humidity

Follow these steps for a frizz-free hairstyle in humid weather:

1. Start with Clean, Conditioned Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help combat frizz.

2. Apply a Frizz-Fighting Product

Apply a frizz-fighting product, like MoroccanOil Treatment or Living Proof No Frizz Nourishing Styling Cream, to damp hair.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb to Detangle Your Hair

Gently comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.

4. Air-Dry Your Hair or Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Blow Dryer

Avoid blow-drying your hair if possible, but if you need to, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair gently.

5. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleep on a silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.

Expert Tips for Frizzy Hair in Humidity

Here are some additional tips from hair experts:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Try washing your hair every other day instead.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount to damp hair before styling.

3. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz. Try to avoid playing with your hair throughout the day.

FAQs About Frizzy Hair in Humidity

1. Can I use hairspray to tame frizzy hair in humidity?

Yes, you can use hairspray to help keep your hair in place in humid weather. Look for a hairspray that is specifically formulated for frizz control.

2. How often should I use a frizz-fighting product?

You can use a frizz-fighting product every time you wash your hair, or as needed throughout the day.

3. Will wearing a hat help prevent frizz in humidity?

Wearing a hat can help protect your hair from humidity and prevent frizz. Look for a hat made from breathable materials, like cotton or linen.

Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Hair in Humidity

Here are some additional hair care tips to help prevent frizz in humid weather:

1. Avoid Using Hot Tools Too Often

Using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often can damage your hair and lead to frizz. Try to limit your use of these tools to once or twice a week.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and smooth.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can cause damage to your hair and lead to frizz. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun’s rays.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your frizzy hair under control in humid weather. Say goodbye to bad hair days and hello to smooth, sleek locks!
