How To Control Hair Fall In Summer In Hindi Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Do you experience hair fall during summer? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience hair fall during the hot season due to various reasons such as sweat, dust, pollution, and exposure to the sun. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to control hair fall in summer in Hindi. So, let's get started!

How to Control Hair Fall in Summer

There are various ways to control hair fall during summer. Here are some of them:

1. Keep Your Scalp Clean

During summer, our scalp tends to get oily and sweaty, which can lead to hair fall. Therefore, it's essential to keep your scalp clean by washing it regularly with a mild shampoo. A clean scalp will prevent the accumulation of dust and dirt, which can clog hair follicles and lead to hair fall.

2. Oil Your Hair

Oiling your hair regularly can help prevent hair fall during summer. Oil nourishes your hair and scalp, keeping them healthy and hydrated. You can use coconut oil, almond oil, or any other oil that suits your hair type. Massage the oil onto your scalp and hair, leave it on for an hour or two, and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner after shampooing your hair can help prevent hair fall. Conditioners hydrate your hair and make it more manageable, preventing tangles and breakage. Use a conditioner that suits your hair type and apply it only to the ends of your hair.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

During summer, it's best to avoid heat styling tools such as straighteners, curling irons, and hair dryers. Heat styling can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally, or use a cool setting on your hair dryer.

5. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle, leading to hair fall. Therefore, it's essential to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or a scarf. You can also use a hair serum or spray that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to control hair fall in summer:

Step 1: Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.

Step 2: Oil your hair once or twice a week.

Step 3: Use a conditioner after shampooing your hair.

Step 4: Avoid heat styling tools.

Step 5: Protect your hair from the sun.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall during summer:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help prevent hair fall. Include foods such as leafy greens, nuts, seeds, and fish in your diet.

2. Drink Plenty of Water

Staying hydrated is essential for healthy hair. Drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your body and hair hydrated.

3. Exercise Regularly

Regular exercise can improve blood circulation, which is essential for healthy hair growth. Exercise for at least 30 minutes a day to promote healthy hair.

4. Reduce Stress

Stress can lead to hair fall. Therefore, it's essential to manage stress through activities such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

FAQs

Q. Does wearing a hat cause hair fall?

A. No, wearing a hat doesn't cause hair fall. In fact, wearing a hat can protect your hair from the sun's UV rays, which can cause hair fall.

Q. Can hair fall be reversed?

A. Yes, hair fall can be reversed through proper hair care and a healthy lifestyle.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. Therefore, it's essential to manage stress through activities such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

Hair Care Tips for Summer

Here are some additional hair care tips for summer:

1. Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You can make a hair mask at home using ingredients such as egg, yogurt, and honey.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends, which can lead to hair fall. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can prevent tangles and breakage, which can lead to hair fall. Start combing your hair from the ends and work your way up to the roots.

4. Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid chemical treatments such as coloring, perming, or relaxing your hair during summer. Chemical treatments can damage your hair and lead to hair fall.

In conclusion, hair fall during summer is a common problem, but it can be prevented through proper hair care and a healthy lifestyle. Follow the tips and tricks mentioned in this article to control hair fall in summer in Hindi. Happy hair care!
