What To Do With Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, your hair is naturally voluminous and full of personality. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. In this guide, we'll explore some tips and tricks for what to do with curly hair to help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How To Style Curly Hair
  3. Step-By-Step Guide

Hair Care

The first step in managing curly hair is taking care of it properly. Curly hair tends to be more fragile than straight hair, so it's important to use gentle products and avoid over-washing. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair over time.
  • Consider getting a regular trim to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

How To Style Curly Hair

Now that you know how to take care of your curly hair, let's talk about how to style it. There are a few different methods you can use to enhance your curls and achieve the look you want:

  • Plopping: This method involves using a t-shirt or microfiber towel to gently scrunch your hair and remove excess water. After plopping for 20-30 minutes, you can release your curls and let them air dry.
  • Diffusing: If you prefer to use a hair dryer, try using a diffuser attachment to help distribute heat evenly and minimize frizz.
  • Bantu knots: This style involves twisting sections of hair into small knots and securing them with bobby pins. After leaving the knots in overnight, you can unravel them in the morning for defined curls.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help hydrate your curls.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  4. Choose your preferred styling method (plopping, diffusing, or Bantu knots).
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing curly hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to minimize friction and prevent breakage.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve your curls overnight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about what to do with curly hair:

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on curly hair?
A: It's best to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair, as these products are gentler and won't strip your hair of natural oils. Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?
A: This can vary depending on your hair type and texture, but in general, it's best to wash curly hair no more than once or twice a week. Q: How can I prevent my curls from becoming frizzy?
A: To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much, use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil, and consider using a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair.

By following these tips and tricks for what to do with curly hair, you can achieve healthy, defined curls that look and feel great.
