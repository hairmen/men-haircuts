How To Make Thin Wavy Hair Look Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have thin wavy hair, you may feel like your hair lacks volume and thickness. However, there are ways to make your hair look thicker and fuller. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make thin wavy hair look thicker.

Description

Thin wavy hair can be challenging to style because it tends to lack volume and thickness. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create the illusion of thicker hair. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make thin wavy hair look thicker.

How to Make Thin Wavy Hair Look Thicker

Here are some tips and tricks on how to make thin wavy hair look thicker:

1. Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to add volume and thickness to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help create volume and lift at the roots. Start by flipping your hair upside down and blow-drying it until it is about 80% dry. Then, flip your hair back up and use a round brush to finish drying your hair.

3. Use a volumizing mousse

A volumizing mousse can help add thickness and volume to your hair. Apply a small amount of mousse to your roots and work it through your hair. Then, blow-dry your hair as usual.

4. Use a root lifting spray

A root lifting spray can help add volume and lift to your hair. Spray the product onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush.

5. Try a dry shampoo

Dry shampoo can help absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair. Spray the product onto your roots and work it in with your fingers. Then, use a brush to distribute the product through your hair.

6. Use a thickening spray

A thickening spray can help add volume and thickness to your hair. Spray the product onto your roots and work it in with your fingers. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush.

7. Use a curling iron

Curling your hair can help create the illusion of thicker hair. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls. Then, use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.

8. Get a layered haircut

A layered haircut can help add volume and movement to your hair. Talk to your hairstylist about getting a layered haircut that is tailored to your hair type and face shape.

9. Avoid heavy products

Avoid using heavy products like oils and serums, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for lightweight products that are specifically designed to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make thin wavy hair look thicker:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair upside down until it is about 80% dry.

Step 3: Apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your roots and work it through your hair.

Step 4: Use a root lifting spray to add volume and lift to your hair.

Step 5: Try a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.

Step 6: Use a thickening spray to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 7: Curl your hair using a curling iron to create volume and movement.

Step 8: Get a layered haircut tailored to your hair type and face shape.

Step 9: Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make thin wavy hair look thicker:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

2. Avoid brushing your hair too much, as it can cause breakage and make your hair look thin.

3. Use a heat protectant spray before using heated styling tools.

4. Sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

5. Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and thickness.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hair extensions to make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and thickness to your hair. However, make sure to choose high-quality extensions that match your hair color and texture.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have thin wavy hair, you may want to wash it every other day to avoid weighing it down with excess oil.

Q: Will cutting my hair make it look thicker?

A: Yes, getting a haircut can help remove split ends and add volume to your hair. Talk to your hairstylist about getting a haircut that is tailored to your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some tips for taking care of thin wavy hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.

2. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

5. Get regular haircuts to remove split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thin wavy hair look thicker and fuller. Remember to take good care of your hair and use the right products and styling techniques for your hair type. With a little effort, you can have the voluminous, luscious hair of your dreams.
