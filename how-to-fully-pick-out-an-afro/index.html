How To Fully Pick Out An Afro A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 · 4 min read
How to Fully Pick Out an Afro: A Step-by-Step Guide | Hair Care Tips 2023

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on how to fully pick out an afro. This guide is perfect for anyone looking to achieve a full and voluminous afro hairstyle. We’ll cover everything from how to prepare your hair, to the tools you’ll need, to the step-by-step process of picking out your afro. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Tools You’ll Need
  • The Process: How to Fully Pick Out an Afro
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Afro
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Afro Hair

Preparation

Before you begin the process of picking out your afro, it’s important to properly prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, using products that are designed for curly or kinky hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and make it easier to pick out.

Once you’ve washed and conditioned your hair, gently towel-dry it to remove excess water. You can also use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as these materials are gentler than regular towels and can help to prevent frizz.

Tools You’ll Need

To fully pick out your afro, you’ll need a few key tools:

  • A wide-tooth comb
  • A pick or Afro comb
  • A hair pick or Afro pick
  • A hair oil or moisturizer

Make sure that you have all of these tools on hand before you begin the process of picking out your afro.

The Process: How to Fully Pick Out an Afro

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Begin by washing and conditioning your hair, then allow it to air dry or use a diffuser to dry it gently. Once your hair is completely dry, it’s ready to be picked out.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your wide-tooth comb, gently section your hair into small, manageable sections. This will make it easier to pick out your afro and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

Step 3: Begin Picking Out Your Hair

Starting at the ends of your hair, use your pick or Afro comb to gently lift and separate your curls. Move slowly and carefully, working your way up to the roots of your hair.

As you pick out your hair, use your hair oil or moisturizer to help smooth and separate your curls. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 4: Continue Picking Out Your Hair

Once you’ve picked out one section of your hair, move on to the next section and repeat the process. Continue picking out your hair until your entire head is full and voluminous.

Tips for Maintaining Your Afro

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates and parabens.

FAQs

Can I pick out my afro every day?

While you can pick out your afro every day if you’d like, it’s not necessary. In fact, over-picking can lead to breakage and damage to your hair. It’s best to pick out your afro every few days, or as needed to achieve your desired look.

What should I do if my hair gets tangled while I’m picking it out?

If you encounter a tangle while picking out your hair, stop and gently detangle it with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb. Avoid using force or pulling on the tangle, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Is it okay to use a hair pick or Afro pick on wet hair?

No, it’s not recommended to use a hair pick or Afro pick on wet hair. Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so it’s best to wait until your hair is dry before picking it out.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Afro Hair

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly using products designed for curly or kinky hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can cause damage and breakage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates and parabens.

Congratulations! You now know how to fully pick out an afro. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a full and voluminous afro hairstyle that’s sure to turn heads. Remember to take care of your hair and use gentle products to keep it healthy and strong.
