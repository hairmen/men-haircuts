What Is The Best Shape Of Sunglasses For A Square Face

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may find it challenging to find the right pair of sunglasses that flatters your features. Luckily, there are plenty of options available that can enhance your look and protect your eyes from harmful UV rays. In this article, we'll explore the best shape of sunglasses for a square face and provide some tips on how to choose the perfect pair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose Sunglasses for a Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Pair of Sunglasses

Description

A square face shape is defined by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. The best shape of sunglasses for a square face is one that balances out these strong features and softens the overall look of the face. The ideal pair of sunglasses for a square face will have round or oval frames that contrast with the square angles of the face.

How to Choose Sunglasses for a Square Face

When choosing sunglasses for a square face, look for frames that are wider than your cheekbones and have a rounded or oval shape. Avoid frames that are too angular or square, as they will only accentuate your features and make your face appear more boxy. Look for frames that have a gentle curve and a soft, rounded edge.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Pair of Sunglasses

Follow these steps to find the perfect pair of sunglasses for your square face:

  1. Measure the width of your face from temple to temple.
  2. Determine your face shape by looking in the mirror and noting the angles and proportions of your features.
  3. Choose frames that are wider than your cheekbones and have a rounded or oval shape.
  4. Try on different styles and shapes to find the perfect fit.
  5. Consider the color and material of the frames, as well as the shade of the lenses.
  6. Make sure the sunglasses fit comfortably and securely on your face.
  7. Choose sunglasses that complement your personal style and enhance your overall look.

Tips for Choosing Sunglasses for a Square Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing sunglasses for a square face:

  • Look for frames that have a gentle curve and a soft, rounded edge.
  • Avoid frames that are too angular or square.
  • Choose frames that are wider than your cheekbones to balance out your features.
  • Consider frames with embellishments or detailing to add interest and soften your look.
  • Choose sunglasses with lenses that are wider at the top and taper down towards the bottom to create a flattering shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing sunglasses for a square face:

What are the best materials for sunglasses frames?

The best materials for sunglasses frames are lightweight and durable. Look for frames made from materials like titanium, acetate, or stainless steel.

Do I need to consider the color of my skin when choosing sunglasses?

Yes, the color of your skin can affect how certain colors and shades of sunglasses look on you. If you have a warm skin tone, look for sunglasses with warm colors and golden hues. If you have a cool skin tone, look for sunglasses with cool colors and silver tones.

Should I choose polarized or non-polarized lenses?

Polarized lenses are a great choice for reducing glare and improving visibility, but they can also make it difficult to see certain digital screens. Non-polarized lenses are a good choice for everyday wear.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses with a Square Face

If you have a square face shape, wearing sunglasses can be tricky when it comes to your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great while wearing sunglasses:

  • Choose sunglasses with a low profile to avoid interfering with your hair.
  • Wear your hair in a style that complements your sunglasses, such as a sleek updo or loose waves.
  • Avoid wearing sunglasses on top of your head, as this can cause your hair to become tangled and frizzy.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

By following these tips and choosing the right pair of sunglasses for your square face, you can enhance your look and protect your eyes from the sun's harmful rays.
