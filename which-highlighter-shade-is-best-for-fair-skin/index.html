Which Highlighter Shade Is Best For Fair Skin

Written by Eveline Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone with fair skin, you might have found it challenging to find a highlighter shade that complements your skin tone. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss which highlighter shades work best for fair skin and provide you with some tips to help you get the perfect glow.

Description

A highlighter is a makeup product that is used to accentuate the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, nose, and brow bone. It helps to create a luminous, healthy-looking glow on your skin. However, choosing the right shade of highlighter can be a bit tricky, especially if you have fair skin.

How to Choose the Right Highlighter Shade for Fair Skin

When choosing a highlighter shade for fair skin, it's important to look for shades that have a pearl or champagne undertone. These shades work best for fair skin as they provide a soft glow without looking too harsh or overpowering.

Here are some tips to help you choose the right highlighter shade:

Consider Your Skin Undertone

If you have cool undertones, you should look for highlighters with a pink or silver undertone. On the other hand, if you have warm undertones, you should look for highlighters with a gold or peach undertone. If you have neutral undertones, you can opt for a highlighter with a champagne undertone.

Try Before You Buy

It's always a good idea to try the highlighter shade before you buy it. You can test the shade on the back of your hand or on your cheekbones to see how it looks on your skin.

Consider the Finish

The finish of the highlighter can also make a difference in how it looks on your skin. If you have dry skin, you should opt for a cream or liquid highlighter as it will provide a more natural-looking finish. If you have oily skin, you should opt for a powder highlighter as it will provide a more matte finish.

Step by Step Guide to Applying Highlighter on Fair Skin

Now that you know which highlighter shade works best for fair skin, let's take a look at how to apply it:

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before applying highlighter, it's important to prep your skin. Start by cleansing and moisturizing your face. You can apply a primer to help your makeup last longer.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Next, apply your foundation as you normally would. Make sure to blend it well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Apply Highlighter

Using a small brush, apply the highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, nose, and brow bone. Start with a small amount and build it up gradually. Make sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Set Your Makeup

Finally, set your makeup with a setting spray to make it last longer.

Tips for Applying Highlighter on Fair Skin

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect glow:

Use a Light Hand

When applying highlighter, it's important to use a light hand to avoid looking too shiny or glittery.

Avoid Applying Too Much

Applying too much highlighter can make you look oily and shiny. Start with a small amount and build it up gradually.

Blend Well

Make sure to blend your highlighter well to avoid any harsh lines or patches.

FAQ

Q: Can fair skin wear gold highlighters?

A: Yes, fair skin can wear gold highlighters. However, it's important to choose a gold highlighter with a champagne undertone to avoid looking too yellow or brassy.

Q: Can fair skin wear pink highlighters?

A: Yes, fair skin can wear pink highlighters. However, it's important to choose a pink highlighter with a pearl undertone to avoid looking too pink or rosy.

Q: How often should I apply highlighter?

A: You can apply highlighter every day if you like. However, it's important to use it sparingly to avoid looking too shiny or glittery.

Hair Care for Fair Skin

When it comes to hair care for fair skin, it's important to protect your skin from the sun's harmful rays. You can do this by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF. It's also important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid irritating your skin.

In conclusion, choosing the right highlighter shade for fair skin can be a bit tricky, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get the perfect glow in no time!
