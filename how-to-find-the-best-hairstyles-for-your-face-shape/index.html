How To Find The Best Hairstyles For Your Face Shape In 2023

Written by William Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don’t seem to suit you? Do you feel like you’re missing out on the perfect look because you’re not sure what styles will flatter your face shape? Well, the good news is that finding the best hairstyles for your face shape doesn’t have to be a daunting task. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks to help you determine which hairstyles will suit you the best.

How To:

First things first, you need to determine your face shape. This can be done by pulling your hair back and examining your face shape in the mirror. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Oval Face Shape:

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Long, short, curly or straight, almost any style will suit you. However, try to avoid hairstyles that are too long and cover up your face as this can make your features appear smaller.

Round Face Shape:

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length. Long layered hairstyles, side swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts are all great options to elongate your face. Avoid blunt cuts and chin-length hairstyles as they can make your face appear wider.

Square Face Shape:

For a square face shape, you want to soften your features. Shoulder-length hairstyles with layers or soft curls can help create a more rounded look. Avoid straight, blunt cuts and short hairstyles as they can make your face appear boxy.

Heart Face Shape:

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to draw attention away from your forehead and create balance. Side-swept bangs, long layered hairstyles, and chin-length bobs are all great options for this face shape. Avoid short, blunt cuts and styles that add volume to the crown of your head.

Diamond Face Shape:

Diamond face shapes are characterized by narrow foreheads and jawlines with wide cheekbones. Soft layers and chin-length bobs can help soften your features and create balance. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head.

Oblong Face Shape:

If you have an oblong face shape, you want to create the illusion of width. Short to medium-length hairstyles with layers, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs are great options. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and add volume to the crown of your head.

Step by Step:

1. Determine your face shape
2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape
3. Consider your hair type and texture
4. Consult with a hairstylist
5. Experiment with different styles

Tips:

- Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles
- Consider your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair
- Take into account your hair’s natural texture and thickness
- Be open to trying new haircuts and styles

FAQ:

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn’t necessarily flatter my face shape?
A: Of course! Ultimately, your hairstyle should make you feel confident and comfortable. If you love a certain haircut or style, go for it!

Q: Do certain hair colors or highlights flatter different face shapes?
A: While there are no hard and fast rules, highlights around the face can help draw attention to your features and create a more youthful appearance.

Hair Care:

Remember to take care of your hair by using products that are suited for your hair type and texture. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
