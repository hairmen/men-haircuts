How To Make Your Box Braids Last Longer

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Box braids are a popular hairstyle that can last for several weeks. However, if you want to keep your braids looking fresh and neat for as long as possible, there are a few things you can do. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make your box braids last longer.

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to make your box braids last longer, it's important to talk about hair care. Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle, including box braids. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons. Heat can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

How to Make Your Box Braids Last Longer

Now that you know how to take care of your hair, let's talk about how to make your box braids last longer. Here are some tips:

1. Choose the Right Size

The size of your box braids can affect how long they last. Larger braids tend to unravel more quickly, while smaller braids can last longer. However, smaller braids can also take longer to install and can be more difficult to maintain.

2. Use High-Quality Hair Extensions

The quality of the hair extensions you use can also impact how long your box braids last. Look for extensions made from 100% human hair or high-quality synthetic hair. Cheaper hair extensions can tangle and frizz, causing your braids to look messy and unkempt.

3. Keep Your Scalp Clean and Moisturized

A clean and moisturized scalp can help your braids last longer. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse your scalp and keep it hydrated. You can also use a scalp oil or moisturizer to prevent dryness.

4. Avoid Excessive Manipulation

The more you touch and manipulate your braids, the more likely they are to unravel. Avoid playing with your braids or pulling them too tightly. You should also avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as ponytails or buns, as these can put pressure on your braids and cause them to loosen.

5. Sleep with a Satin or Silk Scarf

Sleeping with a satin or silk scarf can help prevent frizz and tangling. Simply wrap your scarf around your head before bed to protect your braids while you sleep.

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your box braids last longer:

Step 1: Choose the Right Size

Choose a size that is comfortable for you and that fits your lifestyle. Larger braids tend to unravel more quickly, while smaller braids can last longer.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help make your box braids last longer:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your braids to keep them hydrated.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products on your braids, as these can weigh them down and cause them to unravel.
  • Try not to get your braids wet too often, as this can cause them to loosen and unravel.
  • If your braids start to look fuzzy or frizzy, you can use a small amount of oil or gel to smooth them down.

FAQ

How long do box braids usually last?

Box braids can last anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the size, quality of the hair extensions, and how well you take care of them.

Can I wash my box braids?

Yes, you can wash your box braids. However, you should avoid getting them too wet or using heavy products that can weigh them down and cause them to unravel.

Can I exercise with box braids?

Yes, you can exercise with box braids. However, you should avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can put pressure on your braids and cause them to loosen.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help make your box braids last longer and keep them looking fresh and neat for weeks to come.
