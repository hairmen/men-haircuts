How To Cut Men s Hair Short On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to give yourself or a loved one a fresh new haircut, cutting the hair short on top can be a great option. This style is both versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a stylish and modern short haircut. Let's get started!

How to Cut Men's Hair Short on Top

Cutting men's hair short on top is a fairly simple process, but it does require some basic knowledge and tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircut cape or towel
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a cleaner cut.
  2. Using the clippers, cut the sides and back of the hair to the desired length. A number 3 or 4 guard is usually a good length for a short haircut.
  3. Next, use the scissors to cut the hair on top. Start at the front and work your way towards the back, cutting small sections at a time.
  4. Be sure to blend the sides and top together by cutting at a slight angle.
  5. Once you've finished cutting, use the comb to check for any uneven spots or stray hairs. Trim as needed.
  6. Finally, use the clippers to clean up the neckline and around the ears.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Getting a great haircut takes more than just following the steps above. Here are some additional tips to consider:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of the hair.
  • Use a comb to section off the hair and make it easier to cut.
  • Don't be afraid to take your time and make small cuts. It's better to go slow and get it right than rush and make mistakes.
  • Blend the sides and top together smoothly for a clean and polished look.
  • Keep the hair slightly longer on top for more styling options.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting men's hair short on top:

Q: What's the best length for a short haircut?

A: This depends on personal preference, but a number 3 or 4 guard is typically a good length for a short haircut.

Q: Can I cut my own hair short on top?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair short on top with the right tools and technique. Just be sure to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: This varies depending on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it starts to look unkempt. Generally, a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended to keep the style looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've cut your hair short on top, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a styling product like hair wax or pomade to create different looks.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.

Cutting men's hair short on top is a great way to achieve a modern and stylish look. With the right tools and technique, you can easily create a professional-looking haircut at home. Just remember to take your time, follow the steps carefully, and take care of your hair afterwards for best results. Happy cutting!
