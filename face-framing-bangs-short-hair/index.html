Face Framing Bangs For Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and looking for a way to spice up your short hair? Look no further than face framing bangs! This trendy style can add a touch of sophistication and edge to your look. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve face framing bangs for short hair, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve Face Framing Bangs for Short Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Face framing bangs are a popular hairstyle that can complement any face shape and add dimension to short hair. These bangs typically start at the crown of the head and angle down to frame the face, creating a soft and flattering look. Face framing bangs can be customized to fit your personal style, whether you prefer a blunt cut or a more textured look.

How to Achieve Face Framing Bangs for Short Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you begin, it's important to note that achieving face framing bangs for short hair may require some professional help. If you're uncomfortable cutting your hair yourself, consider visiting a hairstylist who can help you achieve the perfect look. However, if you're feeling confident, follow these steps to achieve face framing bangs:

  1. Start with dry hair that has been styled as desired. This will make it easier to see where your bangs should fall.
  2. Section off the hair at the crown of your head, leaving the hair around your hairline free.
  3. Using a comb, create a triangular section of hair at the crown of your head. This section should be about 1-2 inches wide at the top and taper down to a point near the sides of your head.
  4. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  5. Using sharp scissors, cut the triangular section of hair to your desired length. Keep in mind that the longer your bangs are, the more dramatic the face framing effect will be.
  6. Release the rest of your hair and check to make sure your bangs are even. Make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your bangs as desired. For a more textured look, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition.

Tips for Maintaining Face Framing Bangs

Once you've achieved the perfect face framing bangs, it's important to maintain them to keep your look fresh and polished. Here are some tips for maintaining your face framing bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at your desired length.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener.
  • Avoid over-styling your bangs, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.

FAQs

Q: Will face framing bangs work for my face shape?
A: Yes! Face framing bangs can work for any face shape. Talk to your hairstylist about the best way to customize your bangs to complement your face shape. Q: Can I achieve face framing bangs without cutting my hair?
A: Yes, you can achieve a similar effect with clip-in bangs or by styling your hair to create the illusion of bangs. However, cutting your hair is the most effective way to achieve face framing bangs.

Hair Care for Face Framing Bangs

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful face framing bangs. Here are some tips for caring for your bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your bangs every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid over-brushing your bangs, as this can lead to frizz and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain beautiful face framing bangs for short hair. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a dramatic new look, face framing bangs are the perfect way to switch up your style.
