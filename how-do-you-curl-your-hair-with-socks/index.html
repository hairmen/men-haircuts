How Do You Curl Your Hair With Socks

Written by Luffy Jul 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a heat-free and affordable way to curl your hair, then this tutorial on how to curl your hair with socks is just for you! This method is perfect for those who want to achieve soft, bouncy curls without damaging their hair with heat styling tools. Plus, it's a great way to use up old socks that you no longer wear.

Description

Curling your hair with socks is a simple and easy process that involves wrapping your hair around a sock and sleeping with it overnight. This method is also known as the "sock bun" or "sock curls" technique, and it's been around for a long time. It's a great way to create natural-looking curls, and it works on all hair types and lengths.

How to Curl Your Hair With Socks

Here's how to curl your hair with socks:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair or dampen it with a spray bottle.
  2. Part your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on how thick your hair is.
  3. Take a sock and roll it up into a donut shape.
  4. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the sock, starting at the ends and rolling it up towards your scalp.
  5. Secure the sock in place by tying the ends together.
  6. Repeat steps 4-5 until all sections of your hair are wrapped around socks.
  7. Sleep with the socks in your hair overnight.
  8. In the morning, remove the socks and gently finger comb your hair to loosen the curls.
  9. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the curls.

And voila! You now have beautiful, heat-free curls.

Step by Step

Let's go through each step in more detail:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's damp. You can also dampen your hair with a spray bottle if you don't want to wash it.

Step 2: Part your hair into sections

Use a comb to part your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on how thick your hair is. For thin hair, you can create 4-6 sections. For thick hair, you may need to create more sections.

Step 3: Take a sock and roll it up into a donut shape

Take a clean sock and roll it up into a donut shape. You can use any type of sock, but a longer sock will give you more curl. Make sure the sock is clean and free of any holes or tears.

Step 4: Take a section of hair and wrap it around the sock

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the sock, starting at the ends and rolling it up towards your scalp. Make sure the hair is wrapped tightly around the sock to create a defined curl.

Step 5: Secure the sock in place

Tie the ends of the sock together to secure it in place. Make sure the sock is tightly secured so it doesn't come loose while you sleep.

Step 6: Repeat steps 4-5

Repeat steps 4-5 until all sections of your hair are wrapped around socks. Make sure the sections are even and the same size to create uniform curls.

Step 7: Sleep with the socks in your hair overnight

Go to bed and sleep with the socks in your hair overnight. This will give the curls time to set and create a natural-looking wave.

Step 8: Remove the socks

In the morning, remove the socks by untying the ends and gently sliding the sock out of your hair.

Step 9: Finger comb your hair

Gently finger comb your hair to loosen the curls and create a natural-looking wave.

Step 10: Spray your hair with hairspray

Spray your hair with hairspray to set the curls and keep them in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair with socks:

  • Use smaller sections of hair for tighter curls and larger sections for looser curls.
  • For more defined curls, twist the hair as you wrap it around the sock.
  • Make sure the socks are tightly secured to prevent them from coming loose while you sleep.
  • If you have trouble sleeping with the socks in your hair, try using a silk scarf to wrap your hair instead.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling your hair with socks:

Is it bad to sleep with socks in your hair?

No, it's not bad to sleep with socks in your hair. The socks are soft and won't damage your hair, unlike heat styling tools.

How long do you leave the socks in your hair?

You should leave the socks in your hair overnight to give the curls time to set and create a natural-looking wave.

Can you curl your hair with any type of socks?

You can curl your hair with any type of socks, but longer socks will give you more curl. Make sure the socks are clean and free of any holes or tears.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially when heat styling. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

So there you have it! A simple and easy way to curl your hair with socks. Give it a try and see how it works for you. You'll be surprised at how easy it is to achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without any heat damage.
