How To Choose The Right Color For Your Hair

Written by Robby Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect hair color that suits you best. From how to choose the right color to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered. Here’s a table of contents for easy navigation:

  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Choose the Right Color for Your Hair

When choosing the right hair color, there are a few factors to consider:

Skin Tone

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color is your skin tone. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and dark brown. Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and auburn.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also influence your choice of hair color. If you have light hair, you can go for lighter shades of blonde. If you have dark hair, you can opt for darker shades of brown or black.

Eye Color

Eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, cool shades of blonde or brown can complement your eyes. If you have brown eyes, warm shades of brown or red can make your eyes pop.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to choosing the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Before choosing a hair color, determine your skin tone. To do this, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

Step 2: Choose a Hair Color

Based on your skin tone and natural hair color, choose a hair color that complements your features. If you’re not sure, consult with a professional hair stylist.

Step 3: Test the Color

Before committing to a new hair color, it’s important to test it out first. You can do this by applying a small amount of hair dye to a hidden section of your hair or by using temporary hair color products.

Step 4: Maintenance

Once you’ve chosen your new hair color, it’s important to maintain it. Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair color vibrant, and avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and whether the hair color requires frequent touch-ups.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your wardrobe.
  • Consult with a professional stylist for advice on choosing the right hair color.
  • Consider using temporary hair color products to test out a new hair color before committing to it.

FAQs

What’s the best way to determine my skin tone?

The best way to determine your skin tone is by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

Can I dye my hair a lighter color without bleach?

No, you cannot dye your hair a lighter color without bleach. Bleach is necessary to lighten the hair before applying a lighter hair color.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

This depends on the type of hair color and your natural hair growth rate. Consult with a professional stylist for advice on touch-up frequency.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or hair products that contain UV protection.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color, you can choose a hair color that complements your features. Remember to test the color before committing to it, and maintain your hair color with proper hair care techniques. With these tips, you’ll be able to choose the perfect hair color that suits you best.
