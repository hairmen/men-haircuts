Mens Hairstyles Short Back And Sides Long On Top The Perfect Combination

Short back and sides, long on top is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It has continued to be trendy and popular among men of all ages because of its versatility and modern look. In this blog post, we'll dive into the details of mens hairstyles short back and sides long on top and give you some tips on how to style and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Style
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Mens hairstyles short back and sides long on top is a combination of a short, tapered cut on the back and sides of the head, and a longer, layered cut on the top. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a modern, edgy look that's easy to maintain. It's also versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from slicked-back to messy and textured.

How To Style

To achieve this hairstyle, you'll need to visit a professional stylist or barber. They'll be able to give you a precise cut that's tailored to your face shape and hair type. Once you have the right cut, you can style your hair in many different ways. Here are a few popular options:

  • Slicked-back: Use a pomade or gel to slick your hair back for a polished look.
  • Messy and textured: Use a texturizing spray or wax to add volume and texture to your hair for a more casual, laid-back look.
  • Side-parted: Use a comb to create a side part and style your hair neatly to one side.

Step by Step

To style your hair in a slicked-back look, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your fingertips and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Smooth the product over your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.
  4. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the front and working your way to the back.
  5. Use your fingers to smooth any flyaways or bumps.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling mens hairstyles short back and sides long on top:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to achieve the look you want.
  • Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Keep your hair clean and well-maintained to ensure it looks its best.
  • Visit your stylist or barber regularly to maintain your cut and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles short back and sides long on top:

  • Q: Can this hairstyle work for all hair types?
  • A: Yes, this hairstyle can work for all hair types, but the cut may need to be tailored to your hair type.
  • Q: How often should I visit my stylist or barber to maintain this hairstyle?
  • A: It's recommended to visit your stylist or barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your cut and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know all about mens hairstyles short back and sides long on top, you're ready to rock this trendy look. Remember to take care of your hair properly and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. With the right cut and styling, you'll look and feel your best.
