How To Slick Your Hair Up Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 08, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle, but don't want to use gel, you're in luck. There are several ways to slick your hair up without gel. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this classic look without any sticky products. Here's what you need to know:

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Up Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Your Hair Up Without Gel
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Slicked-Back Hair

How to Slick Your Hair Up Without Gel

There are several ways to slick your hair up without gel:

  • Using pomade or wax
  • Using water-based hair products
  • Using hairspray
  • Using natural oils

Each of these methods can give you a sleek and sophisticated look without the use of gel. Let's take a closer look at each option.

Using Pomade or Wax

Pomade and wax are two excellent alternatives to gel. They provide a strong hold and a shiny finish, making them perfect for slicked-back hairstyles.

To use pomade or wax, start with dry or slightly damp hair. Take a small amount of product and warm it up between your palms. Then, apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a sleek, polished look.

Using Water-Based Hair Products

Water-based hair products, like styling cream or mousse, can also be used to slick your hair up. These products provide a strong hold without the stickiness of gel.

To use water-based hair products, start with damp hair. Apply a small amount of product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a sleek, polished look.

Using Hairspray

Hairspray is another option for slicking your hair up without gel. It provides a strong hold and can be used on dry or damp hair.

To use hairspray, hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a sleek, polished look.

Using Natural Oils

Natural oils, like coconut oil or argan oil, can also be used to slick your hair up. These oils provide a light hold and a natural shine, making them perfect for a more casual look.

To use natural oils, start with dry or slightly damp hair. Take a small amount of oil and warm it up between your palms. Then, apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a sleek, polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to slick your hair up without gel:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose your product – pomade or wax, water-based hair product, hairspray, or natural oil.
  3. If using pomade or wax, warm up a small amount of product in your palms.
  4. If using water-based hair product, apply a small amount to your hair.
  5. If using hairspray, hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair.
  6. If using natural oil, warm up a small amount of oil in your palms.
  7. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.
  8. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a sleek, polished look.
  9. Style your hair as desired.
  10. Finish with hairspray, if desired.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Up Without Gel

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, dry hair for best results.
  • Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a sleek, polished look.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.
  • If your hair is thin or fine, use a light product to avoid weighing it down.
  • If your hair is thick or coarse, use a heavier product for a stronger hold.
  • Don't use too much product – a little goes a long way.
  • Be patient – it may take some practice to get the perfect slicked-back look.

FAQs

Can I slick my hair up without any product?

Yes, you can slick your hair up without any product, but it may be more difficult to achieve a polished look. Using a product can help provide a strong hold and a sleek finish.

What's the best product to use for slicked-back hair?

The best product to use for slicked-back hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Pomade, wax, water-based hair products, hairspray, and natural oils are all good options to consider.

How do I remove product buildup from my hair?

To remove product buildup from your hair, use a clarifying shampoo or apple cider vinegar rinse. These will help remove any excess product and leave your hair feeling clean and refreshed.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked-Back Hair

Here are some tips to help keep your hair healthy and looking great when wearing it slicked back:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like flat irons or curling irons, too frequently.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Slicking your hair up without gel is a great way to achieve a classic, polished look without any sticky products. Whether you prefer pomade, wax, water-based hair products, hairspray, or natural oils, there's a product out there that will work for you. Experiment with different options and find the one that gives you the perfect slicked-back style.
