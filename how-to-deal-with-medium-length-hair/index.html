Description

Medium length hair is a very versatile length for hair. It's not too short and not too long, and it can be styled in many different ways. However, it can also be difficult to manage at times. In this article, we will discuss how to deal with medium length hair and provide some tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking its best.

How to

Dealing with medium length hair starts with finding the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. You can consult with a professional hairstylist to help you choose the best cut for your hair.

Once you have the right cut, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles. There are many hairstyles that work well with medium length hair, such as waves, curls, braids, and updos.

Step by step

Here are the step by step instructions for dealing with medium length hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's almost dry, and then let it air-dry the rest of the way.
  3. Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.
  5. Try braiding your hair for a chic and easy hairstyle.
  6. Create an updo for a more formal occasion.

Tips

Here are some tips for dealing with medium length hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about dealing with medium length hair:

Q: How often should I wash my medium length hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or every few days.
Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with medium length hair?
A: Yes, you can wear a ponytail with medium length hair. You can also experiment with different ponytail styles, such as a low or high ponytail, or a side ponytail.
Q: How can I add volume to my medium length hair?
A: You can add volume to your medium length hair by blow-drying it upside down, using a volumizing mousse, or teasing the roots.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for keeping your medium length hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
