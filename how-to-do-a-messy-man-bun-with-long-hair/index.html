How To Do A Messy Man Bun With Long Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair and you're looking for a quick and easy style that's perfect for any occasion, a messy man bun might be just what you need! Not only is it simple to create, but it's also trendy and stylish. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to achieve the perfect messy man bun with long hair, along with tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step

How to

The messy man bun is a popular hairstyle for guys with long hair. It's casual, easy to create, and perfect for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

What You'll Need

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair elastic

Step-by-Step

Follow these simple steps to create a messy man bun:

  1. Start by brushing or combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, making sure it's centred on the top of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair elastic, making sure it's not too tight.
  5. Use your fingers to gently pull on the bun to loosen it and create a messy, relaxed look.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating a messy man bun:

  • Don't worry about making the bun too perfect â the messier, the better!
  • Experiment with different bun sizes and positions to find the look that works best for you.
  • If you have shorter pieces of hair that don't quite fit into the bun, try tucking them in with bobby pins.
  • For a more polished look, use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to smooth down any frizz or flyaways.

FAQ

How do I make my man bun look fuller?

To make your man bun look fuller, try backcombing your hair before creating the bun. This will add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to create a full, messy bun.

What if my hair is too short for a man bun?

If your hair is too short for a man bun, you can still achieve a similar look by creating a half-up, half-down style. Simply gather the top half of your hair into a small bun or knot, leaving the bottom half loose.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can create the perfect messy man bun with long hair. Whether you're heading to the office or hitting the beach, this style is sure to turn heads and keep you looking cool and confident all day long.
