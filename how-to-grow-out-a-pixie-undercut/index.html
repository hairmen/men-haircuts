How To Grow Out A Pixie Undercut

Written by Bardi Dec 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Pixie haircuts are a great way to change up your look and feel confident, but eventually, you may want to grow out your pixie undercut. Growing out a short haircut can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge and tools, it can be a fun and exciting experience. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to grow out a pixie undercut.

Table of Content:

  • How to Grow Out a Pixie Undercut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Be Patient

First things first, growing out a pixie undercut takes time and patience. It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow out your hair to shoulder length. So, be patient and don't give up!

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly, every 6-8 weeks, will help to promote healthy hair growth and prevent split ends. By trimming your hair, you'll also be able to shape your hair as it grows out, making the transition easier.

3. Experiment with Hair Accessories

As your hair grows out, experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and hair ties. These accessories can help to keep your hair out of your face and make the growing-out process more manageable.

4. Try Different Hairstyles

As your hair grows out, try different hairstyles to find what works best for you. You can experiment with buns, braids, and ponytails to keep your hair looking stylish and chic.

5. Use Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help to keep your hair healthy and promote hair growth. Look for products that contain biotin, keratin, and vitamin E to nourish your hair and promote healthy growth.

Hair Care Tips

1. Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean without stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to nourish your hair and prevent breakage. Look for a treatment that contains natural oils such as coconut oil, jojoba oil, or argan oil.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and slow down hair growth. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to grow out a pixie undercut?

It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow out your hair to shoulder length.

2. How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to promote healthy hair growth and prevent split ends.

3. What hair products should I use to promote hair growth?

Look for products that contain biotin, keratin, and vitamin E to nourish your hair and promote healthy growth.

Now that you know how to grow out a pixie undercut, it's time to start your hair growth journey. Remember to be patient, take care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with new hairstyles along the way!
