Best Haircuts For Round Faces And Thick Hair In 2023

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face and thick locks? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best haircuts for round faces and thick hair in 2023. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type. How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Faces and Thick Hair

When choosing a haircut for round faces and thick hair, it is important to keep in mind that you want a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face. Here are some tips to consider:

  • Look for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.
  • Consider long layers to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Choose a style that works with your hair's natural texture.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces and Thick Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, here are some of the best haircuts for round faces and thick hair:

  • Long layers with face-framing highlights
  • Long bob with side-swept bangs
  • Blunt bob with choppy layers
  • Messy pixie cut with long bangs
  • Shoulder-length curls with side part

Step-by-Step Guide for Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you have chosen your perfect haircut, it's time to get it. Here is a step-by-step guide for getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Bring a picture of the haircut you want to your stylist.
  2. Communicate with your stylist about what you want and what you don't want.
  3. Trust your stylist's expertise.
  4. Maintain your haircut with regular trims.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions about Haircuts for Round Faces and Thick Hair

What is the best haircut for a round face and thick hair?

The best haircut for a round face and thick hair is one that adds height to the crown of your head and avoids adding width to the sides of your face. Long layers, a long bob with side-swept bangs, a blunt bob with choppy layers, a messy pixie cut with long bangs, and shoulder-length curls with a side part are all great options.

Can I get a short haircut if I have a round face and thick hair?

Yes! A messy pixie cut with long bangs is a great short haircut option for round faces and thick hair.

How often should I get my haircut?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces and Thick Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind for round faces and thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for thick hair to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair to prevent frizz.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

With these tips and haircuts, you're sure to find the perfect style for your round face and thick hair in 2023. Remember to communicate with your stylist about what you want and what you don't want, and trust their expertise. With regular maintenance and proper hair care, you'll be rocking your new haircut in no time.
