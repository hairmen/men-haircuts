In this guide, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to style and care for your long hair on top, and we'll also recommend some of the trendiest hairstyles for men with long hair. Description

Men's hairstyles long hair on top are all the rage right now. This particular hairstyle features long hair on the top of the head, while the sides and back are trimmed shorter. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many ways, from a slick-back to a messy, textured look. Men's hairstyles long hair on top can be achieved with different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How to Style Men's Hairstyles Long Hair On Top

The key to styling men's hairstyles long hair on top is to find the right products that work for your hair type and desired look. Here are some of the products you may need:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair dryer
  • Styling cream or pomade
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Men's Hairstyles Long Hair On Top

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, but leave it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  4. Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair in the desired direction.
  5. Blow-dry your hair using a hair dryer on low heat, while continuing to style it with the brush or comb.
  6. Apply a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Hairstyles Long Hair On Top

To keep your men's hairstyles long hair on top looking great, here are some tips to follow:

  • Regularly trim the hair on the sides and back to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the strands.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the natural oils from your scalp.

FAQs About Men's Hairstyles Long Hair On Top

What face shapes are best suited for men's hairstyles long hair on top?

Men's hairstyles long hair on top can work for different face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on men with square and oval faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid this hairstyle, as it can make your face appear wider.

What are some of the trendiest men's hairstyles long hair on top?

Some of the trendiest men's hairstyles long hair on top include the slick-back, the quiff, the textured crop, and the messy, tousled look.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair On Top

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with long hair on top:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too much shampoo, as it can dry out your scalp and strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Wear a hat or a bandana when you're out in the sun, as UV rays can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can promote healthy hair growth.

So, there you have it - a complete guide to men's hairstyles long hair on top. Whether you're looking for a sleek and polished look or a more relaxed, messy style, there's a hairstyle out there that's perfect for you. Just remember to take good care of your hair, and you'll be rocking your long locks in no time!
