Description

Medium length curly hair is a popular choice for men in 2023. It is a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways, from loose and natural to more structured and defined. This style is perfect for those with naturally curly hair, as it allows for the natural texture of the hair to shine through, while still being manageable.

How To Cut Medium Length Curly Hair for Men

The key to a successful medium length curly hair cut is to work with the natural texture of the hair. The following steps will guide you through the process:

Step 1: Consultation

Before starting the cut, it is important to have a consultation with the client to discuss their desired look and any concerns they may have. This will help to ensure that both the client and the stylist are on the same page.

Step 2: Preparation

Next, wet the hair and comb it through to remove any tangles. Apply a styling product that will help to define the curls and provide hold.

Step 3: Cutting

Starting at the nape of the neck, use scissors to cut the hair in small sections. Work your way up to the crown of the head, taking care to maintain the natural shape of the hair. Use thinning shears to remove excess bulk and create texture.

Step 4: Finishing

Once the cut is complete, apply a styling product to the hair and use your fingers to shape the curls. Use a diffuser attachment on a blow dryer to dry the hair, taking care not to disturb the curls too much.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Medium Length Curly Hair Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length curly hair cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using a brush on curly hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens medium length curly hair cut:

What is the best product for defining curls?

A curl defining cream or gel is the best product for defining curls. Look for a product that is specifically designed for curly hair.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It is recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the cut and keep the hair healthy.

Hair Care for Mens Medium Length Curly Hair Cut

Here are some tips for caring for your mens medium length curly hair cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using a brush on curly hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, mens medium length curly hair cut is a popular and versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways. By working with the natural texture of the hair and following the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve a great looking cut that is easy to maintain.
