Severe Hair Loss Due To Thyroid Causes Symptoms And Treatments

Written by Frank Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thyroid disorders are a common cause of hair loss in both men and women. If you are dealing with severe hair loss due to thyroid, you know how frustrating it can be. In this article, we'll explore the causes, symptoms, and treatments for severe hair loss due to thyroid.

What is Thyroid?

The thyroid is a gland located in the neck that produces hormones that regulate your body's metabolism. When the thyroid gland is not functioning properly, it can lead to a variety of health problems, including hair loss.

What Causes Severe Hair Loss Due to Thyroid?

Severe hair loss due to thyroid is caused by an imbalance of hormones in the body. When the thyroid gland produces too much or too little of certain hormones, it can lead to hair loss. This is because the hair follicles require a certain amount of hormones to grow and stay healthy.

What are the Symptoms of Severe Hair Loss Due to Thyroid?

The symptoms of severe hair loss due to thyroid can include thinning hair, bald patches, and hair that breaks easily. You may also notice changes in the texture and quality of your hair, such as dryness or brittleness.

How is Severe Hair Loss Due to Thyroid Treated?

The treatment for severe hair loss due to thyroid will depend on the underlying cause of the condition. If the hair loss is due to an overactive thyroid, medications may be prescribed to regulate hormone levels. If the hair loss is due to an underactive thyroid, hormone replacement therapy may be recommended.

How Can I Prevent Severe Hair Loss Due to Thyroid?

While there is no guaranteed way to prevent severe hair loss due to thyroid, there are steps you can take to support healthy hair growth. Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, getting regular exercise, and managing stress levels can all help support healthy hair growth.

Hair Care Tips for Severe Hair Loss Due to Thyroid

If you are dealing with severe hair loss due to thyroid, there are a few hair care tips you can follow to help support healthy hair growth:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for products that are free of harsh chemicals and sulfates.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners can damage your hair and make hair loss worse. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible.

3. Take care when brushing and combing

Be gentle when brushing and combing your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and scalp. Wear a hat or use a protective hair product when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

Q: Can thyroid medication cause hair loss?

A: Yes, some medications used to treat thyroid disorders can cause hair loss as a side effect.

Q: Will my hair grow back after thyroid treatment?

A: In many cases, hair will grow back after thyroid treatment. However, it may take several months for hair to return to its normal thickness and texture.

Q: Is there a cure for severe hair loss due to thyroid?

A: While there is no cure for severe hair loss due to thyroid, there are treatments that can help manage the condition and support healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Severe hair loss due to thyroid can be a frustrating and challenging condition to deal with. However, with the right treatment and hair care, it is possible to support healthy hair growth and manage the condition. If you are experiencing severe hair loss due to thyroid, talk to your healthcare provider about your treatment options.
