How To Thin Out Thick Hair With Thinning Scissors

Written by Eveline Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning out thick hair can be a challenge for many people. It can feel like your hair is too heavy, bulky, and difficult to manage. Luckily, thinning scissors can help you achieve a more manageable and lighter hairstyle. In this article, we will guide you through how to thin out thick hair with thinning scissors, step by step.

Description

Thinning scissors, also known as texturizing shears, are a type of scissors used to remove bulk from hair. These scissors have teeth or notches on one or both blades, which cut the hair in a way that removes some of its thickness while leaving the length intact. Thinning scissors are an excellent tool for people with thick hair who want to achieve a more textured and layered look.

How to Thin Out Thick Hair with Thinning Scissors

Thinning out hair with thinning scissors is not difficult, but it does require some skill and practice. Here is how to do it:

1. Wash and dry your hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it before using thinning scissors. This will make it easier to see the natural texture of your hair and the areas where it needs thinning.

2. Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable parts, starting at the back of your head. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

3. Choose the right thinning scissors

Choose thinning scissors with the right number of teeth or notches. The more teeth or notches the scissors have, the more hair they will remove. Start with scissors that have fewer teeth or notches, and work your way up as needed.

4. Start thinning your hair

Hold a section of hair between your fingers, close to the scalp. Open the thinning scissors and place them horizontally on the hair, about halfway down the section. Close the scissors while moving them down to the ends of the hair. Repeat this process until you have thinned out the desired amount of hair.

5. Check your progress

Check your progress regularly by releasing the section of hair and comparing it to the rest of your hair. This will help you avoid over-thinning or under-thinning your hair.

6. Repeat on the remaining sections

Repeat the thinning process on the remaining sections of your hair, working from the back of your head to the front.

7. Blend the layers

Blend the thinned-out sections with the rest of your hair by combing it gently and using regular scissors to trim any uneven spots.

8. Style your hair

Style your hair as desired, and enjoy your newly thinned-out hair!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when thinning out your hair with thinning scissors:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up to avoid over-thinning.
  • Use a comb to separate the hair you want to thin out to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Thinning scissors work best on dry hair, so avoid using them on wet hair.
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid making mistakes.

FAQ

Can thinning scissors damage my hair?

Thinning scissors are safe to use if you use them correctly. However, if you over-thin your hair or use them on wet hair, they can cause damage and breakage.

What is the difference between thinning scissors and regular scissors?

Thinning scissors have teeth or notches on one or both blades, which remove bulk from hair without affecting its length. Regular scissors, on the other hand, cut hair straight across and can create blunt lines.

Hair Care

Thinning out your hair can make it easier to manage and style, but it's essential to take care of it to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its health and prevent split ends.

Thinning out thick hair with thinning scissors is a simple process that can help you achieve a more manageable and lighter hairstyle. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the best results and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
