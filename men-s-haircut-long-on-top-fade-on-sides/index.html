Men s Haircut Long On Top Fade On Sides A Trendy And Stylish Look

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and modern haircut that will make you stand out in the crowd? If so, then the men's haircut long on top fade on sides is the perfect choice for you. This trendy hairstyle has been popular among men for many years and is still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we will discuss how to get this haircut, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions about it.

Description

The men's haircut long on top fade on sides is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your individual style. It features longer hair on the top of the head that is gradually faded into shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a clean and polished look that is perfect for any occasion.

How to Get Men's Haircut Long on Top Fade on Sides

To get the men's haircut long on top fade on sides, you will need to find a skilled barber or stylist who specializes in this type of haircut. They will be able to customize the haircut to suit your individual needs and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the men's haircut long on top fade on sides:

  1. Choose the length you want on the top of your head.
  2. Determine how short you want the sides and back to be faded.
  3. Ask your barber or stylist to gradually blend the longer hair on top into the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  4. Trim any excess hair and style the hair on top as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Haircut Long on Top Fade on Sides

To maintain your men's haircut long on top fade on sides, you should follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to create a polished and sleek look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs About Men's Haircut Long on Top Fade on Sides

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long on top fade on sides:

Q: Is the men's haircut long on top fade on sides suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does it take to get the men's haircut long on top fade on sides?

A: The length of time it takes to get this haircut will depend on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the skill level of your barber or stylist. However, on average, it takes around 30-45 minutes to complete.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with this haircut?

A: You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Long on Top Fade on Sides

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, you should follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Overall, the men's haircut long on top fade on sides is a stylish and modern haircut that is perfect for any occasion. By following these tips and maintaining proper hair care, you can keep your haircut looking fresh and polished for weeks to come.
