How To Decide Which Hair Colour Suits You In 2023

Written by Aprilia Nov 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be both exciting and stressful. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which hair color suits you best. Here are some tips to help you make the right decision.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Decide Which Hair Colour Suits You
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips to Consider
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Decide Which Hair Colour Suits You

The first step in deciding which hair color suits you is to consider your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a combination of both.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can then look at your natural hair color. If you have warm skin tones, you may want to consider warmer hair colors such as golden blonde or caramel. If you have cool skin tones, you may want to consider cooler hair colors such as ash blonde or platinum. If you have neutral skin tones, you can choose from a wide range of hair colors.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. Your eye color can help you determine which hair color will complement your features. For example, if you have blue eyes, you may want to consider hair colors with warm undertones to make your eyes pop.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you decide which hair color suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Consider your natural hair color
  3. Look at your eye color
  4. Research different hair colors
  5. Consult with a professional stylist

Tips to Consider

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a new hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you are willing to do
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see how different colors will look on you
  • Keep in mind that some hair colors may require more frequent touch-ups
  • Consider the health of your hair and whether it can handle the coloring process

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new hair color:

Q: What hair color suits fair skin?

A: Cool blonde, ash brown or rich red are good options for fair skin.

Q: What hair color suits olive skin?

A: Warm caramel, honey blonde or chestnut brown are good options for olive skin.

Q: What hair color suits dark skin?

A: Deep red, dark brown or black with warm undertones are good options for dark skin.

Hair Care Tips

After you have chosen your new hair color, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Deep condition your hair once a week

Choosing a new hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color and eye color, you can find a color that will complement your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. Don't forget to take care of your hair after coloring to maintain its health and vibrancy.
