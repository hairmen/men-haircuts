How To Fix Frontal Wig With Glue A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wearing a frontal wig is a great way to achieve a natural and flawless look. However, it can be frustrating when your wig starts to shift or come off during the day. One solution to this problem is to use glue to secure your frontal wig in place. In this article, we will show you how to fix frontal wig with glue in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Fix Frontal Wig with Glue
  2. Tips for Fixing Frontal Wig with Glue
  3. FAQs
  4. Hair Care Tips for Frontal Wig Wearers

How to Fix Frontal Wig with Glue

Before you begin, make sure you have the following items:

  • Frontal wig
  • Scissors
  • Wig glue
  • Comb
  • Baby wipes or rubbing alcohol
  • Cotton swabs

Follow these steps to fix your frontal wig with glue:

Step 1: Prepare your hairline

Use baby wipes or rubbing alcohol to clean your forehead and hairline. This will remove any oils or dirt that could prevent the glue from sticking properly.

Step 2: Cut the lace

Using scissors, carefully cut the lace around the hairline of your frontal wig. Be careful not to cut any of the hairs on the wig.

Step 3: Apply the glue

Apply a thin layer of wig glue along your hairline using a cotton swab. Be sure to apply the glue evenly and avoid getting it on your hair.

Step 4: Place the wig

Carefully place the wig on your head, starting from the front and working your way back. Press the wig firmly onto your scalp to ensure it sticks properly.

Step 5: Style the wig

Using a comb, style the wig as desired. You can also use a hair dryer on low heat to help set the glue.

Step 6: Let the glue dry

Allow the glue to dry completely before touching or styling your hair. This can take anywhere from 15 to 30 minutes, depending on the type of glue you are using.

Tips for Fixing Frontal Wig with Glue

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your frontal wig with glue:

  • Use a wig cap to help keep your natural hair in place and prevent it from getting tangled with the wig.
  • Use a hair dryer on low heat to help set the glue and prevent it from melting in hot weather.
  • Avoid using too much glue, as this can cause irritation and damage to your scalp and hairline.
  • Choose a high-quality wig glue that is specifically designed for frontal wigs.

FAQs

How long will the glue hold my wig in place?

The length of time the glue will hold your wig in place depends on the type of glue you are using and your activity level. Generally, wig glue can hold a wig in place for 1-2 days.

Can I swim or shower with my wig on?

It is not recommended to swim or shower with your wig on, as this can cause the glue to loosen and the wig to come off.

How do I remove the wig glue?

You can use rubbing alcohol or wig glue remover to remove the glue from your scalp and wig. Apply the remover to the affected area and let it sit for a few minutes before gently wiping it away with a cotton ball or swab.

Hair Care Tips for Frontal Wig Wearers

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your frontal wig:

  • Wash your wig regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig to prevent damage to the hair fibers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.
  • Protect your wig from direct sunlight and heat to prevent fading and damage.

By following these tips and steps, you can easily fix your frontal wig with glue and enjoy a natural and flawless look all day long.
