How to Know What Face Shape You Have Male

Do you want to know what face shape you have? Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyle, beard style, and glasses that will enhance your features. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to determine your face shape if you are a male.

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Before we start, let's first discuss the different face shapes. There are six basic face shapes, which are:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and proportions. Once you know your face shape, you can use this information to enhance your features and create a more balanced look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are several ways to determine your face shape:

  • Look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a bar of soap or a washable marker.
  • Take a selfie and trace the outline of your face using a photo editing app.
  • Ask someone to take a photo of you facing forward and another photo of you in profile. Print out the photos and trace the outline of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face using a bar of soap or a washable marker.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you traced.
  4. Compare it to the six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle.
  5. Take note of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  6. An oval face shape has a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and prominent cheekbones.
  7. A round face shape has a soft jawline and full cheeks.
  8. A square face shape has a strong jawline and a forehead that is approximately the same width as the jawline.
  9. A heart face shape has a wide forehead and a narrow jawline.
  10. A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones.
  11. A triangle face shape has a wide jawline and a narrow forehead.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure your hair is pulled back so you can see your entire face shape.
  • Use natural light when tracing your face shape to avoid any distortion caused by artificial lighting.
  • Don't worry if your face shape is not a perfect match to the six basic face shapes. Most people have a combination of two or more face shapes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

  • What if my face shape is a combination of two or more shapes?

    • If your face shape is a combination of two or more shapes, focus on the dominant shape and use that as a guide for choosing hairstyles, beards, and glasses.

  • What if I am still unsure about my face shape?

    • If you are still unsure about your face shape, ask a hairstylist or a makeup artist for help. They can give you professional advice on how to enhance your features.

Hair Care Based on Face Shape

Once you know your face shape, you can choose hairstyles that will flatter your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval face shape: Almost any hairstyle will work for an oval face shape.
  • Round face shape: Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as pompadours or quiffs, are ideal for a round face shape.
  • Square face shape: Short hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, such as buzz cuts or crew cuts, work well for a square face shape.
  • Heart face shape: Hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as chin-length bobs or messy waves, are great for a heart face shape.
  • Diamond face shape: Short hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, such as textured crops or messy fringes, are ideal for a diamond face shape.
  • Triangle face shape: Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head, such as slicked-back styles or side partings, work well for a triangle face shape.

Knowing your face shape can help you make better decisions when it comes to choosing hairstyles, beards, and glasses. Use the tips and guidelines in this article to determine your face shape and enhance your features.
