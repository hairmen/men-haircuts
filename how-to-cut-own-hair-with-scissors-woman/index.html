How To Cut Your Own Hair With Scissors A Guide For Women

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Whether you’re looking to save money or just want to try something new, cutting your own hair can be a daunting task. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a bit of patience, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. In this guide, we’ll go over the steps for how to cut your own hair with scissors as a woman, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair with Scissors
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Tools You’ll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair clips or ties
  • Mirror (preferably one that can be adjusted for different angles)

It’s important to use sharp scissors, as dull ones can cause split ends and damage to your hair. You can find hair cutting scissors at most drug stores or beauty supply stores.

How to Cut Your Own Hair with Scissors

Before you start cutting, it’s important to have a plan. Decide on the length and style you want, and consider your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the basic steps for cutting your own hair with scissors:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or ties.
  4. Start with the bottom section and cut across the ends. You can use a comb to hold the hair taut and create a straight line.
  5. Move up to the next section, making sure to cut evenly across the ends.
  6. Continue cutting in sections until you’ve reached the desired length.
  7. If you want to add layers, take a small section of hair from the top and cut at an angle. Repeat with other sections until you achieve your desired layered look.
  8. Check your work in the mirror, and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

  • Start with small cuts, as you can always cut more if needed.
  • Be patient and take breaks if needed to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • If you’re unsure, consider consulting a professional stylist or watching tutorial videos online.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can cut your own curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink in length when dry, so cut less than you think you need to.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can usually fix it by making small adjustments or trimming off the damaged ends.

How often should I cut my own hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows, but most people cut their hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Cutting your own hair with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following the steps in this guide and taking proper care of your hair, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut without leaving your home.
