How To Make A Fancy High Ponytail Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 30, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Welcome to our latest hair tutorial on how to make a fancy high ponytail! If you're looking for a chic and stylish hairstyle that can be easily done at home, then this tutorial is perfect for you. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to make a fancy high ponytail that will turn heads wherever you go. So let's get started!

Description

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. However, if you want to add a little bit of glam and sophistication to your ponytail, then a fancy high ponytail is the way to go. This hairstyle is perfect for special occasions, such as weddings, proms, or even just a night out with friends. A fancy high ponytail is easy to achieve, and it doesn't require any special skills or tools. All you need is a hairbrush, some hair elastics, and a few bobby pins.

How to Make a Fancy High Ponytail

Follow these step by step instructions to make a fancy high ponytail:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling it.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles. Use a comb to create a straight parting in the middle of your head.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair in a High Ponytail

Take a hair elastic and tie your hair in a high ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 4: Add Some Volume

To add some volume to your ponytail, take a small section of hair from underneath the ponytail and tease it with a comb. This will create a poofy effect.

Step 5: Create a Braid

Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and create a braid. Secure the end of the braid with a hair elastic.

Step 6: Wrap the Braid Around the Ponytail

Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins. This will create a twisted effect.

Step 7: Add Some Hair Accessories

To add some extra flair to your fancy high ponytail, you can add some hair accessories, such as a headband or a flower clip.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the perfect fancy high ponytail:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches the color of your hair.
  • Tease your hair lightly to avoid damaging it.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the braid and the twisted effect.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to find the perfect look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fancy high ponytails:

What hair length is suitable for a fancy high ponytail?

A fancy high ponytail can be done on any hair length, from short to long. However, it looks best on medium to long hair.

Can I use hairspray to hold the ponytail in place?

Yes, you can use hairspray to hold the ponytail in place. However, make sure you don't use too much hairspray, as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

How long does it take to make a fancy high ponytail?

A fancy high ponytail can be done in less than 10 minutes, depending on your hair length and thickness.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure you follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a mild shampoo, conditioning it, and using a hair mask once a week. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

That's it for our step by step guide on how to make a fancy high ponytail. We hope you found this tutorial helpful, and we can't wait to see your fabulous ponytails!
