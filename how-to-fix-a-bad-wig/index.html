How To Fix A Bad Wig Easy Steps To Bring Back Your Confidence

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Wigs are a great way to change up your look, but sometimes they can be more trouble than they're worth. If you've ever had a bad wig day, you know how frustrating it can be. But don't worry, fixing a bad wig is easier than you think!

Description

There are a few common issues that can make a wig look bad. It might be too shiny, too bulky, or not fit properly. Whatever the problem, there's always a solution. In this article, we'll go over some easy steps to fix a bad wig and get it looking great again.

How to

Before you start fixing your wig, you'll need a few things:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Wig stand or mannequin head
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Straightening iron or curling iron (optional)

Step by Step

Follow these easy steps to fix a bad wig:

  1. Place your wig on a stand or mannequin head. This will help you see what you're doing and prevent damage to the wig.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle the wig. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.
  3. If your wig is too shiny, spray it with a heat protectant spray and then gently comb through it with your fingers. This will help to reduce the shine.
  4. If your wig is too bulky, use a straightening iron or curling iron to flatten or curl the hair as needed. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  5. If your wig doesn't fit properly, try adjusting the straps or clips inside the wig. You can also take it to a professional wig stylist for alterations.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep your wig looking great:

  • Don't use hot water on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Don't brush your wig when it's wet, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Store your wig on a stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape and prevent tangles.
  • Wash your wig regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing a bad wig:

Q: Can I dye my wig?

A: It depends on the type of wig. Synthetic wigs cannot be dyed, but human hair wigs can be dyed with the proper products and techniques.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It's recommended to wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Q: Can I style my wig with heat?

A: It depends on the type of wig. Synthetic wigs cannot be styled with heat, but human hair wigs can be styled with heat tools such as straightening irons and curling irons.

Hair Care

Whether you wear a wig or not, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair and your wig looking great!
