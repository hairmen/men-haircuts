How To Revive Your Human Hair Wig

Written by William Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you worried about the dullness and lifelessness of your human hair wig? Do you want to bring it back to its original shine and luster? Well, you are in the right place! In this article, we will guide you on how to revive your human hair wig and make it look like new again. We will cover different aspects such as cleaning, conditioning, and styling. So, let's get started!

Description

A human hair wig is an investment that requires proper care and maintenance. Wearing it regularly, exposure to the sun, and frequent styling can take a toll on its quality and appearance. However, with the right techniques and products, you can revive your wig and keep it looking beautiful for a long time.

How to Revive Your Human Hair Wig

The following steps will help you revive your human hair wig:

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

Before you begin washing your wig, make sure to detangle it gently. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to remove any knots or tangles. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Avoid pulling or tugging the hair, as this can damage the wig.

Step 2: Wash the Wig

Fill a sink or basin with lukewarm water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Place the wig in the water and gently swirl it around for a few minutes. Do not rub the hair or squeeze it tightly, as this can cause tangling and damage. Rinse the wig thoroughly with clean water.

Step 3: Condition the Wig

Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair and distribute it evenly with your fingers. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it off with cool water. Conditioner helps to restore the moisture and shine of the hair, making it soft and silky.

Step 4: Dry the Wig

Use a towel to gently blot the excess water from the wig. Do not rub or twist the hair, as this can cause damage. Place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head and let it air dry. Avoid using a hairdryer or other heat sources, as this can damage the wig.

Tips for Maintaining Your Human Hair Wig

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight, as this can fade the color and dry out the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your wig with hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners.
  • Do not sleep in your wig, as this can cause tangling and damage.
  • Wash your wig every 2-4 weeks, depending on how often you wear it.

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my human hair wig?

A: No, you should use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for wigs. Regular products can contain harsh chemicals that can damage the hair and cause tangling.

Q: Can I dye my human hair wig?

A: Yes, you can dye your human hair wig. However, it is best to have it done by a professional stylist to ensure that the color is applied evenly and the hair is not damaged.

Hair Care for Your Human Hair Wig

Here are some additional tips for maintaining the quality and appearance of your human hair wig:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle your wig gently.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your wig, as this can damage the hair.
  • Protect your wig from chlorine and saltwater, as these can cause tangling and damage.
  • Do not use oily or greasy products on your wig, as these can weigh down the hair and cause tangling.
  • Trim the ends of your wig regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and techniques, you can revive your human hair wig and keep it looking beautiful for a long time. Remember to be gentle when handling the hair, use the right products, and store it properly when not in use. A little care and attention can go a long way in maintaining the quality and appearance of your wig.
