How To Make Curly Hair Look Bigger Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have naturally curly hair, but find that it lacks volume and body? If so, you're not alone. Many people with curly hair struggle to make it look full and voluminous. But don't worry – there are plenty of ways to enhance your curls and make them look bigger and more beautiful. In this article, we'll share our top tips and tricks for making curly hair look bigger and more voluminous.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Curly Hair Look Bigger
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Curly Hair Look Bigger

There are several ways to make curly hair look bigger and more voluminous. Here are some of the most effective methods:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to damp hair before styling.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Tease your hair gently at the roots to create lift and volume.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create bigger, more defined curls.
  • Clip your hair up at the roots while it dries to create lift and volume.
  • Use a texturizing spray or powder to add volume and texture to your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your curly hair look bigger and more voluminous:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rough it up too much.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to damp hair, working it through from roots to tips.
  4. Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  5. Tease your hair gently at the roots to create lift and volume.
  6. Use a curling iron or wand to create bigger, more defined curls.
  7. Clip your hair up at the roots while it dries to create lift and volume.
  8. Finish with a texturizing spray or powder to add volume and texture to your curls.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for making curly hair look bigger and more voluminous:

  • Avoid using heavy oils and serums, which can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun to preserve your curls overnight and create more volume.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thin and lackluster.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls – they're beautiful just the way they are!

FAQ

Here are some common questions and answers about making curly hair look bigger:

Q: Can I make my curly hair look bigger without heat styling?

A: Yes, you can. Try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, or clip your hair up at the roots while it dries to create lift and volume.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture, but most people with curly hair can get away with washing it every 2-3 days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it look dull and lifeless.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for taking care of your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair after washing to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your curly hair look bigger and more voluminous than ever before. Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find the ones that work best for your hair, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls. With a little bit of care and attention, your curly hair can look absolutely stunning!
