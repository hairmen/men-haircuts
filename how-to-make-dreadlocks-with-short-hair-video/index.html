How To Make Dreadlocks With Short Hair Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering how to make dreadlocks with short hair video? Don't worry; we've got you covered. Whether you're a first-timer or looking for a new way to style your hair, this guide will help you create the perfect dreadlocks.

Description

Dreadlocks are a type of hairstyle where the hair is matted or twisted into tight, rope-like strands. This style has been around for centuries and is popular with people of all ages, races, and genders. If you have short hair and want to try out this style, follow these simple steps to create your own dreadlocks.

How to

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in creating dreadlocks is to wash your hair thoroughly. Use a good quality shampoo to remove any dirt, oil, or residue from your hair. This will help your hair to knot and tangle more easily.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into small sections using a comb. The size of the sections will depend on how thick or thin you want your dreadlocks to be. Remember that smaller sections will result in thinner locks, while larger sections will create thicker locks.

Step 3: Apply Wax or Gel

Apply a small amount of hair wax or gel to each section of hair. This will help the hair to stick together and create the dreadlocks. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can make your hair greasy and difficult to work with.

Step 4: Twist and Roll

Once you've applied the wax or gel, twist each section of hair tightly in one direction. Then, roll the hair between your palms in the opposite direction to create a tight, rope-like strand. Repeat this process for each section of hair until all of your hair is twisted and rolled.

Step 5: Secure the Dreadlocks

Once you've created all of your dreadlocks, secure them in place using hair ties or rubber bands. Make sure to tie them tightly to prevent them from unraveling. You can also use a hair dryer or leave them to air dry to help set the dreadlocks.

Tips

- Be patient; creating dreadlocks can take several hours or even days, depending on the length of your hair and the thickness of the locks.

- Avoid using too much product or wax, as this can make your hair greasy and difficult to work with.

- Don't wash your hair for the first few weeks after creating your dreadlocks, as this can cause them to unravel.

FAQ

Q: Can you make dreadlocks with short hair?

A: Yes, you can make dreadlocks with short hair. Just follow the steps outlined in this guide to create your own dreadlocks.

Q: How long does it take to create dreadlocks?

A: It can take several hours or even days, depending on the length of your hair and the thickness of the locks.

Hair Care

After you've created your dreadlocks, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips to keep your dreadlocks looking great:

- Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using too much product or wax, as this can make your hair greasy and difficult to work with.

- Don't use a hairbrush on your dreadlocks; instead, use your fingers to gently separate and maintain them.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create beautiful, long-lasting dreadlocks with short hair. Just remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you'll have a stunning new style in no time.
