How To Stop Hair Fall For Oily Scalp A Complete Guide

Are you tired of having a greasy scalp and losing hair at the same time? If yes, then you are not alone. Oily scalp is a common problem that many people face, and it can lead to hair fall if not taken care of properly. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall for oily scalp and provide you with some essential tips and tricks to maintain healthy hair.

Description

Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women, and it can be caused due to various factors. One of the primary reasons for hair fall is an oily scalp. An oily scalp can lead to clogged hair follicles, which in turn can cause hair fall. Additionally, excess sebum production can make hair greasy, heavy, and difficult to manage. Therefore, it is essential to address the issue of oily scalp to prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.

How to Stop Hair Fall for Oily Scalp

Here are some effective ways to stop hair fall for oily scalp:

  • Use a gentle shampoo that is specifically designed for oily scalp. Look for ingredients such as salicylic acid, tea tree oil, and witch hazel that help regulate sebum production and unclog hair follicles.
  • Avoid using hot water for hair wash as it can stimulate sebum production. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Do not apply conditioner to your scalp as it can make hair greasy. Only apply it to the ends of your hair.
  • Do not wash your hair too frequently as it can strip natural oils from your scalp and lead to excess sebum production. Wash your hair every other day or every third day.
  • Avoid using styling products such as gels and sprays as they can make hair greasy and lead to hair fall.
  • Do not brush your hair when it is wet as it can cause hair breakage. Use a wide-toothed comb instead.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can pull on the hair follicles and cause hair fall.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. Nutrients such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your scalp hydrated and prevent excess sebum production.
  • Manage stress levels as stress can trigger excess sebum production and hair fall.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to stop hair fall for oily scalp:

  1. Choose a gentle shampoo that is specifically designed for oily scalp.
  2. Wet your hair with lukewarm water.
  3. Apply the shampoo to your hair and massage your scalp gently with your fingertips.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  5. Avoid applying conditioner to your scalp. Only apply it to the ends of your hair.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  7. Gently pat your hair dry with a towel.
  8. Avoid using styling products on your hair.
  9. Comb your hair with a wide-toothed comb.
  10. Eat a balanced diet that is rich in nutrients.
  11. Drink plenty of water and manage stress levels.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall for Oily Scalp

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall for oily scalp:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage hair and lead to hair fall.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and bleaches.
  • Avoid using tight hair accessories such as hair ties and clips as they can pull on the hair follicles and cause hair fall.
  • Massage your scalp regularly with warm oil to improve blood circulation and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my hair if I have an oily scalp?

You should wash your hair every other day or every third day if you have an oily scalp. Washing your hair too frequently can strip natural oils from your scalp and lead to excess sebum production.

2. Can stress cause hair fall for oily scalp?

Yes, stress can trigger excess sebum production and hair fall for oily scalp. Therefore, it is essential to manage stress levels to prevent hair fall.

3. What are some natural remedies for oily scalp?

Some natural remedies for oily scalp include using apple cider vinegar, lemon juice, and aloe vera gel. These ingredients help regulate sebum production and unclog hair follicles.

Hair Care for Oily Scalp

Here are some essential hair care tips for oily scalp:

  • Choose a gentle shampoo that is specifically designed for oily scalp.
  • Avoid using hot water for hair wash.
  • Do not apply conditioner to your scalp.
  • Do not wash your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using styling products such as gels and sprays.
  • Do not brush your hair when it is wet.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Manage stress levels.

In conclusion, an oily scalp can lead to hair fall if not taken care of properly. Therefore, it is essential to follow the above-mentioned tips and tricks to maintain healthy hair. Additionally, if you experience severe hair fall, it is recommended to consult a dermatologist to identify the underlying cause and get appropriate treatment.
