How To Make Thick Wavy Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick wavy hair is a blessing, but it can also be a curse if you don't know how to style it properly. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to make your thick wavy hair look good. Whether you have short, medium or long hair, these tips will help you transform your locks into a beautiful and stylish look.

  • How to Style Thick Wavy Hair

Hair Care

The first step to making your thick wavy hair look good is to take care of it. This means using the right products and treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to nourish it and keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to styling thick wavy hair, there are many options to choose from. You can go for a sleek and straight look, or embrace your waves and go for a more relaxed and natural style. Here are some popular hairstyles for thick wavy hair:

  • Beachy Waves: This is a popular and easy-to-achieve look that involves using a curling iron to create loose waves. You can use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Braids: Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish. You can go for a simple braid, a fishtail braid, or a French braid.
  • Top Knot: This is a simple and chic hairstyle that involves twisting your hair into a bun on the top of your head. You can leave some strands loose for a more relaxed look.
  • Half-Up Half-Down: This is a versatile hairstyle that works well for both casual and formal occasions. You can leave some waves loose around your face for a more romantic look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your thick wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to protect it from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls, if desired.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  6. Style your hair into your desired look, such as a beachy wave, braids, top knot, or half-up half-down.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for making your thick wavy hair look good:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash it more often. Otherwise, you can wash it every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.

Q: What kind of hair products should I use for thick wavy hair?

A: Look for products that are designed for curly or wavy hair, such as curl enhancing creams, mousses, and gels. Avoid products that contain sulfates or alcohol, as these can dry out your hair.

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can make your thick wavy hair look good and feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair, experiment with different hairstyles, and have fun with your locks. Whether you rock beachy waves, braids, top knots, or half-up half-down styles, you can embrace your natural beauty and shine bright.
