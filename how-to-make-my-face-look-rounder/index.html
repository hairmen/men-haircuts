How To Make My Face Look Rounder Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a naturally angular face or simply want to achieve a more youthful and softer look, there are a number of ways to make your face appear rounder. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best tips and tricks for creating a fuller, rounder face shape.

Description

There are several factors that can contribute to the appearance of a rounder face shape. These include facial exercises, makeup techniques, and even certain hairstyles. By combining these strategies, you can create a more balanced and symmetrical look that enhances your natural beauty.

How To

The following steps will help you achieve a rounder face shape:

Step 1: Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are a great way to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, which can help create a more defined and rounder appearance. Some effective exercises include cheek plumping, puckering your lips, and smiling while pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth. Repeat these exercises regularly to see results.

Step 2: Contouring with Makeup

Contouring with makeup is a popular technique for creating a slimmer, more defined face shape. However, you can also use makeup to create the opposite effect by applying a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to the outer edges of your face and a lighter shade to the center. This will help create the illusion of a fuller, rounder face.

Step 3: Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also play a role in creating a rounder face shape. Avoid sleek, straight styles that can emphasize angular features and instead opt for soft, layered cuts that frame your face. Adding volume to your hair can also help create a more rounded appearance. Consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to enhance your hair's natural texture.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a rounder face shape:

  1. Start by performing facial exercises regularly to tone and tighten your facial muscles.
  2. Apply a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to the outer edges of your face and a lighter shade to the center.
  3. Choose a soft, layered hairstyle that frames your face and adds volume.
  4. Consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to enhance your hair's natural texture.
  5. Use a blush or bronzer to add warmth and dimension to your cheeks.
  6. Avoid harsh lines and angles in your makeup application and instead opt for soft, blended looks.
  7. Consider using a highlighter to enhance your natural glow and create the illusion of a fuller, rounder face.
  8. Experiment with different makeup techniques and hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  9. Remember to stay hydrated and take care of your skin to maintain a healthy, youthful appearance.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for creating a rounder face shape:

  • Avoid wearing your hair in tight ponytails or buns, as these can emphasize angular features.
  • Use a moisturizer and sunscreen daily to protect your skin and prevent premature aging.
  • Choose clothing and accessories that complement your face shape and enhance your natural features.
  • Consider consulting with a professional stylist or makeup artist for personalized advice and recommendations.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating a rounder face shape:

Q: Are there any risks associated with facial exercises?

A: While facial exercises are generally safe, it's important to use proper technique to avoid injury. Avoid overexerting your facial muscles and stop immediately if you experience any pain or discomfort.

Q: Can contouring with makeup be used on any skin tone?

A: Yes, contouring techniques can be adapted for any skin tone. However, it's important to choose shades that complement your natural skin tone and avoid overly dark or light shades that can create an unnatural appearance.

Q: Can certain foods or supplements help create a rounder face shape?

A: While there is no definitive evidence that specific foods or supplements can create a rounder face shape, maintaining a healthy diet and exercise regimen can help improve overall health and well-being, which can in turn contribute to a more youthful and vibrant appearance.

Hair Care

When it comes to creating a rounder face shape, your hairstyle can play a key role. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using excessive heat or styling products that can damage your hair and contribute to hair loss.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and nutrients that support hair health.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Visit a professional stylist regularly for trims and treatments that will keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a rounder, fuller face shape that enhances your natural beauty and confidence.
