Best Haircuts For Thinning Hair On Top Of Head

Written by William Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair on top of your head? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience hair loss as they age, and it can be frustrating to find hairstyles that work for your changing hair. But don't worry, there are plenty of haircuts that can help you feel confident and stylish. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for thinning hair on top of the head, how to achieve them, and some tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Haircut
  • Tips for Hair Care
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Choose the Best Haircut

When choosing a haircut for thinning hair on top of the head, you want to focus on styles that create volume and texture. Avoid styles that are too short or too long, as they can draw attention to the thinning areas. Here are some of the best haircuts for thinning hair on top of the head:

1. The Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a great option for those with thinning hair on top of the head, as it's short and low maintenance. The buzz cut also creates an even appearance, which can help to camouflage any thinning areas.

2. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another great option for those with thinning hair on top of the head. This style is short on the sides and longer on top, which creates volume and texture. You can also add some texture to the top by using a styling product.

3. The Side Part

The side part is a classic style that works well for those with thinning hair on top of the head. This style involves parting the hair to one side and combing it over. You can also add some volume to the top by blow-drying it in the opposite direction of the part.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving the crew cut:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Choose your Clippers

You'll need a pair of clippers to achieve the crew cut. Choose a pair with guards that allow you to adjust the length of the cut.

3. Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair with a shorter guard. Use a longer guard on the top of your head to create volume and texture.

4. Blend the Sides and Top

Once you've finished cutting, use a blending tool to blend the sides and top together.

5. Style your Hair

Apply a styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume on the top of your head.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for thinning hair:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for thinning hair. These products can help to strengthen and nourish your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair on top of my head?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have thinning hair on top of your head. However, it's important to choose a style that works for your hair type and face shape.

2. Will a buzz cut make my thinning hair more noticeable?

No, a buzz cut can actually help to camouflage thinning hair on top of the head. The short length creates an even appearance, which can make your hair look fuller.

3. How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by using volumizing shampoos and styling products. You can also try blow-drying your hair in the opposite direction of the part to create more volume.

Now that you know the best haircuts for thinning hair on top of the head, it's time to try them out for yourself. Remember to choose a style that works for your hair type and face shape, and to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
