How To Look Stylish At 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in your 60s and wondering how to look stylish and chic? Many women in their 60s struggle with finding the perfect balance between comfort and fashion. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a stylish and modern look without sacrificing comfort. In this article, we will share some of the best tips to help you look your best at 60.

How to

The first step to looking stylish at 60 is to understand your body and style. You need to figure out what works for you and what doesn’t. This will help you choose clothes that flatter your body and make you feel confident. Here are some tips on how to look stylish at 60:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clothes

When it comes to choosing clothes, it’s important to pick ones that flatter your body and suit your personal style. Avoid clothes that are too tight or too baggy. Instead, opt for clothes that fit you well and show off your best features. You can also experiment with different styles and fabrics to find what works best for you.

Step 2: Accessorize Wisely

Accessories can make or break an outfit. When it comes to accessorizing, less is more. Choose simple and elegant pieces that complement your outfit. You can add a scarf, a statement necklace, or a pair of earrings to elevate your look.

Step 3: Invest in Quality Shoes

Comfortable shoes are a must-have for any woman in her 60s. However, that doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice style. Invest in quality shoes that are both comfortable and stylish. A pair of loafers or ankle boots can add a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to look stylish at 60:

TIP 1: Stick to Classic Pieces

Classic pieces never go out of style. Invest in timeless pieces such as a well-tailored blazer, a pair of black trousers, or a little black dress. These pieces can be dressed up or down and can be worn for any occasion.

TIP 2: Experiment with Colors

Don’t be afraid to experiment with colors. While black and white are timeless classics, adding a pop of color can instantly brighten up your outfit. You can opt for bold hues such as red, pink, or green or go for softer shades such as pastels.

TIP 3: Pay Attention to Details

Details such as buttons, zippers, and pockets can make a big difference in your outfit. Opt for clothes with interesting details that add a touch of sophistication to your look. A blazer with gold buttons or a dress with lace details can instantly elevate your outfit.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear jeans in my 60s?

A: Yes, you can still wear jeans in your 60s. However, it’s important to choose the right fit and style. Opt for high-waisted jeans that flatter your waistline and choose darker washes that are more slimming.

Q: Should I wear makeup in my 60s?

A: Yes, wearing makeup can help you look and feel your best. However, it’s important to choose makeup that complements your skin tone and doesn’t look too heavy. Stick to neutral shades and opt for a lightweight foundation that doesn’t settle into fine lines and wrinkles.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It’s recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Regular haircuts can also help you manage split ends and prevent damage.

Hair Care

When it comes to looking stylish at 60, your hair plays a big role. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and stylish:

TIP 1: Choose a Flattering Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. Short haircuts such as a pixie cut or a bob can be a great option for women in their 60s.

TIP 2: Use a Gentle Shampoo

As you age, your hair becomes more fragile and prone to damage. Use a gentle shampoo that nourishes your hair and doesn’t strip it of its natural oils. Opt for sulfate-free shampoos that are gentle on your hair and scalp.

TIP 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause breakage. Protect your hair by using a heat protectant spray before heat styling and avoid using these tools too often.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily look stylish and chic in your 60s. Remember, it’s all about finding the right balance between comfort and fashion. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your fashion choices.
