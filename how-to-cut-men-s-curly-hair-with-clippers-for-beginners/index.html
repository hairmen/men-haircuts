How To Cut Men s Curly Hair With Clippers For Beginners

Written by Bardi Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to cut, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this article, we will be discussing how to cut men's curly hair with clippers for beginners. Whether you're a barber or just looking to give your friend or family member a haircut, this guide will provide you with the necessary steps and tips for a successful cut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Men's curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair. The natural curls can make it difficult to achieve a clean and even cut. Clippers are a popular tool for cutting men's hair and can be used for curly hair as well. With the right technique and a little practice, you can achieve a great haircut for men with curly hair.

How To

The first step in cutting men's curly hair with clippers is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a good quality clipper with different guard sizes, a comb, scissors, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also important to have a clear and clean workspace.

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to wash and dry the hair. This will remove any dirt or product buildup and make the hair easier to cut. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Once the hair is washed, towel dry it and use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry the hair.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

When using clippers, it's important to choose the right guard size for the length you want to achieve. Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller size if needed.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair and make it easier to cut. Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Use the clipper to trim the sides and back of the head, using the chosen guard size.

Step 4: Cut the Top of the Hair

Use the scissors to cut the top of the hair. It's important to cut the hair when it's dry and in its natural state. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use the clipper to blend the hair and create a smooth transition between the different lengths. Start at the top and work your way down, using a smaller guard size.

Step 6: Clean up the Edges

Use the clipper to clean up the edges, such as the hairline and sideburns. Use a smaller guard size or no guard at all for a clean and precise cut.

Tips

  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to keep the hair damp and easier to cut.
  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller size for a gradual and natural look.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.
  • Always cut the hair when it's dry and in its natural state.
  • Practice makes perfect - don't be afraid to try different techniques and find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I cut curly hair with clippers?

Yes, clippers can be used to cut men's curly hair. However, it's important to use the right technique and guard size for a clean and even cut.

What's the best way to cut curly hair?

The best way to cut curly hair is to use a combination of clippers and scissors. Clippers can be used for the sides and back of the head, while scissors are best for the top of the hair. It's also important to cut the hair when it's dry and in its natural state.

How often should I cut curly hair?

It depends on the length and style of the hair. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the hair.

Hair Care

To keep men's curly hair looking its best, it's important to use the right products and techniques. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair and avoid products with harsh chemicals. Use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry the hair and avoid rubbing the hair with a towel. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair and avoid brushing it when it's dry.

Now that you know how to cut men's curly hair with clippers for beginners, you can give your friends and family members a great haircut. Remember to use the right tools and technique for a clean and even cut. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut for men with curly hair.
