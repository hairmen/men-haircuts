How To Hide Chin Hair With Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Having chin hair can be a source of embarrassment for some people, especially women. While there are various hair removal methods, makeup can also help hide chin hair. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to hide chin hair with makeup.

How to:

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, here’s a quick rundown of the steps on how to hide chin hair with makeup:

  1. Prep your skin
  2. Apply primer
  3. Use color corrector
  4. Apply foundation
  5. Conceal
  6. Set with powder
  7. Apply blush and highlighter
  8. Finish with setting spray

Step by Step:

Now, let’s get into the details of each step:

1. Prep your skin

Wash your face with a gentle cleanser and moisturize your skin. This will ensure that your skin is clean and hydrated, making it easier to apply makeup.

2. Apply primer

Apply a primer to your chin area. This will help your makeup last longer and prevent it from creasing or smudging.

3. Use color corrector

If you have dark chin hair, use a color corrector to cancel out the darkness. Use a green color corrector for redness and a peach or orange color corrector for dark hair.

4. Apply foundation

Apply foundation to your chin area. Make sure to blend it well into your skin to avoid any harsh lines.

5. Conceal

If you still see some hair, use a concealer that matches your skin tone to cover it up. Use a small brush to apply the concealer precisely on the hair.

6. Set with powder

Set your makeup with a translucent powder to prevent it from smudging or creasing. Use a fluffy brush to apply the powder.

7. Apply blush and highlighter

Add some color to your face by applying blush to your cheeks and highlighter to your cheekbones.

8. Finish with setting spray

Finish off your makeup by applying a setting spray to lock it in place.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you hide chin hair with makeup:

  • Use a small brush to apply makeup precisely on the hair.
  • Make sure to blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Choose a foundation and concealer that matches your skin tone.
  • Use a matte finish foundation to avoid drawing attention to your chin area.
  • Try using a hair growth inhibitor to reduce hair growth in the chin area.

FAQ:

Here are some common questions about hiding chin hair with makeup:

1. Can I use regular makeup to hide chin hair?

Yes, you can use regular makeup to hide chin hair. You don’t need any special makeup products to do this.

2. Will makeup completely hide my chin hair?

No, makeup can only hide chin hair to a certain extent. It won’t completely cover the hair, but it can make it less noticeable.

3. How long does makeup last when hiding chin hair?

Makeup can last up to 8 hours when hiding chin hair, but it depends on the products you use and your skin type.

Hair Care:

Aside from using makeup, it’s also important to take care of your chin hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle hair removal method like threading or waxing.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your chin area.
  • Moisturize your chin area daily to keep the skin hydrated.
  • Try using a hair growth inhibitor to reduce hair growth in the chin area.

Now that you know how to hide chin hair with makeup, you can confidently rock any look you want. Remember to take care of your skin and hair to ensure that you always look and feel your best.
