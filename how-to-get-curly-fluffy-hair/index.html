How To Get Curly Fluffy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 09, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some bounce and volume to your hair, curly fluffy hair might be just the thing for you. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this fun and playful look without having to rely on complicated styling methods. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get curly fluffy hair:

Description

Curly fluffy hair is a popular hairstyle that features loose, bouncy curls and plenty of volume. This look is often achieved using a combination of heat styling tools, styling products, and hair accessories.

How to Get Curly Fluffy Hair

Getting curly fluffy hair starts with the right preparation. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. This will help to remove any buildup and condition your hair for styling.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that is designed to add volume and hold to your hair, such as a mousse or volumizing spray. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair until it is about 80% dry. You can use a round brush to help create volume and shape as you blow dry.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Using a curling iron, curl small sections of your hair in alternating directions. This will help to create loose, bouncy curls that add volume and dimension to your hair.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing brush or comb, gently tease the roots of your hair to create additional volume and lift. Be sure to tease your hair in small sections, working from the roots to the ends.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look by applying a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place and add additional volume and texture.

Tips for Getting Curly Fluffy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your curly fluffy hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage when using heat styling tools.
  • Experiment with different curling iron sizes to find the perfect curl for your hair type.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create additional lift and volume.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help create more defined curls.
  • Accessorize your hair with cute hair clips or headbands to add even more volume and texture.

FAQ About Curly Fluffy Hair

Q: Can I get curly fluffy hair if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can. You'll just need to use heat styling tools and styling products to help create the curls and volume.

Q: Will curly fluffy hair work on short hair?

A: Yes, it can. Just be sure to use a smaller curling iron and focus your teasing on the roots of your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Fluffy Hair

Proper hair care is important to keep your curly fluffy hair healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful curly fluffy hair that is full of volume and bounce. So go ahead and try it out for yourself!
