Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2020

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your round face and double chin? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women struggle with finding the right hairstyle that flatters their face shape and minimizes their double chin. In this article, we'll go over some of the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2020.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, it's important to determine your face shape. Here are the most common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Determining your face shape can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. The easiest way to determine your face shape is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Alternatively, you can take a selfie and use an app or website that allows you to upload a photo and analyze your face shape.

Long Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some of the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2020:

Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts are great for women with round faces and double chins because they add volume and movement to the hair. Layers can also help to soften angular features and draw attention away from the chin. Opt for long layers that start at the jawline and frame the face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to draw attention away from the chin and create a more balanced look. When getting bangs, ask your stylist to cut them at an angle so that they are longer on one side and gradually get shorter on the other. This will create a more natural look and help to elongate the face.

Wavy Hairstyles

Wavy hairstyles are perfect for women with round faces and double chins because they add texture and dimension to the hair. Waves can also help to create the illusion of a longer face by adding volume at the top and bottom of the head. To achieve this look, use a curling iron or hair rollers to create loose waves.

Low Ponytails

Low ponytails are a great way to create a sleek and polished look while also drawing attention away from the chin. To achieve this look, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. Leave a few strands of hair loose to frame your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for your face shape, it's time to learn how to style them. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  3. If you have thick hair, use a straightener to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  4. If you have thin hair, use a volumizing spray or mousse to add texture and volume.
  5. If you're going for a wavy hairstyle, use a curling iron or hair rollers to create loose waves.
  6. If you're going for a low ponytail, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. Leave a few strands of hair loose to frame your face.
  7. Finish off your look with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking fresh and healthy:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Can I still wear my hair in a bun if I have a round face and double chin?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a bun if you have a round face and double chin. Just make sure to leave a few strands of hair loose to frame your face and create a more balanced look.

Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a round face and double chin?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too straight as they can accentuate the roundness of your face and draw attention to your chin. Instead, opt for longer hairstyles with layers and volume.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Don't tie your hair up too tightly as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.

Now that you know the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2020, it's time to try them out for yourself. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and to maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care.
