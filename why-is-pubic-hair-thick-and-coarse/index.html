Why Is Pubic Hair Thick And Coarse

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Pubic hair is a natural and normal part of the human body. It serves a purpose, not only in terms of protecting the skin but also in sexual attraction. However, many people wonder why pubic hair is thick and coarse compared to other hair on the body. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this phenomenon.

What Causes Pubic Hair to be Thick and Coarse?

Pubic hair is thick and coarse because of its location and purpose. The pubic region experiences more friction and heat than other parts of the body, so the hair needs to be dense and strong to protect the skin from irritation and infection. Additionally, pubic hair is an evolutionary adaptation that helps to trap pheromones, which are scents that attract potential partners.

Another factor that contributes to the thickness and coarseness of pubic hair is hormones. During puberty, the body produces more androgens, which are male hormones that stimulate hair growth. Androgens also cause the hair to become thicker and darker. This is why pubic hair tends to be thicker and coarser in adults than in children.

How to Take Care of Pubic Hair

While pubic hair serves a purpose, not everyone wants to keep it. If you choose to remove your pubic hair, there are several methods you can use, including shaving, waxing, and laser hair removal. However, it's important to be gentle and use proper hygiene to prevent irritation and infection.

When shaving, use a clean razor and shaving cream to avoid cuts and razor burn. After shaving, apply a soothing lotion or oil to calm the skin. Waxing and laser hair removal are more permanent methods of hair removal, but they can be painful and expensive.

FAQ About Pubic Hair

1. Is it normal to have pubic hair?

Yes, it is entirely normal to have pubic hair. Pubic hair serves a purpose in protecting the skin and trapping pheromones.

2. Why does pubic hair sometimes grow in different directions?

Pubic hair can grow in different directions because the hair follicles are not all aligned in the same direction. This is normal and nothing to worry about.

3. Can pubic hair be too thick?

While pubic hair can be thick and coarse, there is no such thing as "too thick." If you are uncomfortable with the thickness of your pubic hair, there are various methods you can use to remove it, such as shaving or waxing.

Conclusion

Pubic hair is thick and coarse for a reason. It is an evolutionary adaptation that serves a purpose in protecting the skin and attracting potential partners. While there is no such thing as "too thick" pubic hair, if you choose to remove it, be sure to use proper hygiene and gentle methods to prevent irritation and infection.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a gentle soap or body wash when washing your pubic hair to avoid irritation.

2. Avoid tight clothing that can cause friction and irritation in the pubic area.

3. Trim your pubic hair regularly to keep it neat and tidy.

4. Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your pubic hair.

5. Consider using a moisturizing lotion or oil to keep the skin and hair hydrated.

Step by Step Guide: How to Shave Pubic Hair

1. Trim the hair with scissors or clippers to a short length.

2. Soak in a warm bath or shower to soften the hair and open the pores.

3. Apply shaving cream or gel to the pubic area.

4. Use a clean razor with a single blade to avoid irritation.

5. Shave in the direction of hair growth, using short strokes.

6. Rinse the razor frequently to avoid clogging.

7. After shaving, rinse the area with cool water and apply a soothing lotion or oil.
