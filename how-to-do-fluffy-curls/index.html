How To Do Fluffy Curls Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a way to add volume and texture to your hair? Fluffy curls may just be what you need. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some bounce to their hair without damaging it. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of achieving fluffy curls, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Do Fluffy Curls
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Fluffy Curls
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Fluffy Curls

How to Do Fluffy Curls

Before we get started, let's talk about the tools you'll need. To get fluffy curls, you'll need a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or larger, a heat protectant spray, and a hairbrush. Once you have these tools, you're ready to start.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve fluffy curls:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections depends on the thickness of your hair. If you have thin hair, you may only need four sections. If you have thick hair, you may need up to eight sections.
  3. Take a section of hair and brush it out to remove any tangles. Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the section of hair around the barrel, away from your face. Hold the curl for 10-15 seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat step 3 for each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls. This will give you a more natural and voluminous look.
  5. Once you've curled all of your hair, brush it out with a hairbrush. This will blend the curls together and create a fluffy, voluminous effect.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Fluffy Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best fluffy curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a large barrel. The larger the barrel, the bigger the curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Looser curls will give you a more natural and voluminous look.
  • Alternate the direction of your curls. This will give your hair more texture and volume.
  • Brush out your curls after curling. This will create a more natural and fluffy look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about fluffy curls:

  • Can I achieve fluffy curls with a flat iron? Yes, you can achieve fluffy curls with a flat iron by wrapping your hair around the plates and pulling it through.
  • How long do fluffy curls last? Fluffy curls can last up to two days if you use a strong hold hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much.
  • Can I achieve fluffy curls on short hair? Yes, you can achieve fluffy curls on short hair by using a curling iron with a smaller barrel size.

Hair Care for Fluffy Curls

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy while achieving fluffy curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve fluffy curls that will add volume and texture to your hair while keeping it healthy.
