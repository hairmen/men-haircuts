How Do I Find The Right Hairstyle For My Face Shape

Written by Bardi Aug 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Having a hairstyle that flatters your face shape can make all the difference in your overall appearance. However, with so many hairstyles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know which one is right for you. In this article, we will go over how to find the right hairstyle for your face shape in a step-by-step guide, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Find Hairstyles for Your Face Shape
  • Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type and Lifestyle
  • Tips for Finding the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Hairstyle

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and take note of the following:

  • Measure your face's length and width with a tape measure or ruler.
  • Take note of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead.
  • Compare your measurements and facial features to the descriptions of the six face shapes.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Find Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. Here are some guidelines for each face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle. However, you may want to avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of your head.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length. Hairstyles that add height at the crown or length to the face, such as long layers or a side-swept bang, are great options.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften the angles of your face. Hairstyles with soft layers or curls will help to achieve this. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs that will accentuate your square jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you want to balance out your forehead and narrow chin. Hairstyles with side-swept bangs and layers around the cheekbones will help to achieve this. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that will make your forehead appear wider.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you want to emphasize your cheekbones and soften your forehead and chin. Hairstyles with layers around the cheekbones and a side-swept bang will help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you want to create the illusion of width. Hairstyles with layers around the cheekbones and chin or a side-swept bang are great options. Avoid hairstyles with too much length, as they will elongate your face even more.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type and Lifestyle

Now that you have an idea of what hairstyles will flatter your face shape, you also need to consider your hair type and lifestyle. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Your hair type: Straight, curly, wavy, thick, or thin hair will all have different styling requirements.
  • Your lifestyle: If you're always on the go, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle. If you work in a professional setting, you may need a more polished look.
  • Your personal style: Your hairstyle should reflect your personal style and make you feel confident.

Take all of these factors into consideration when choosing a hairstyle.

Tips for Finding the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for finding the right hairstyle:

  • Look for inspiration in magazines, online, or on social media.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist.
  • Be open to trying something new, but also trust your instincts.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes, so don't worry if you don't fit perfectly into one category. You can still use the guidelines for each face shape to find a flattering hairstyle.

What if I don't like any of the hairstyles for my face shape?

Remember that these guidelines are just suggestions. Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and reflects your personal style.

Hair Care for Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips, you can maintain a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident.
