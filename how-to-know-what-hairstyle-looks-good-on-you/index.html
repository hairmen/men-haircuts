Determining Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the perfect hairstyle for you is to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you narrow down the hairstyles that will best suit you. The four main face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the widest part of your forehead, which is usually at the hairline.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have your measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Round face: Forehead and jawline are about the same width, with a rounded chin.
  • Oval face: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and the forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Square face: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, with a square chin.
  • Heart-shaped face: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, and the chin comes to a point.

Knowing your face shape will give you a good starting point for choosing a hairstyle that will flatter your features.
