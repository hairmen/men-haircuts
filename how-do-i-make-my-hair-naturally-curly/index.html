How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly

Written by Aprilia Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools to create curls and waves in your hair? Or maybe you have naturally straight hair and want to switch things up with some curls. Whatever your reason may be, making your hair naturally curly is possible with a few tips and tricks. In this post, we will guide you through the process of making your hair naturally curly.

Table of Content:

  1. How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips

Step by Step

The first step in making your hair naturally curly is to start with clean hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream that is specifically designed to enhance curls. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends where the curls tend to be more defined.

Divide your hair into sections and twist each section around your finger to create a curl. Be sure to twist in the direction you want your curls to go. Continue twisting until all sections of your hair are curled.

Once your hair is completely curled, leave it to air dry. Avoid touching your hair during this time, as this can disrupt the curls and cause frizz. If you are in a rush, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process.

After your hair is completely dry, gently separate the curls with your fingers. This will create more volume and definition in your curls. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or a pick to gently separate the curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair naturally curly:

  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain your curls overnight.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Try different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that creates the most natural-looking curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making hair naturally curly:

Can I make my hair naturally curly if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can make your hair naturally curly even if you have naturally straight hair. Follow the steps outlined above and use a curl-enhancing product to help create curls.

How long will my natural curls last?

Your natural curls should last until your next wash day. To help maintain your curls, avoid touching your hair too much and sleep on a satin pillowcase.

Can I use heat styling tools to create curls?

While heat styling tools can create curls, they can also cause damage to your hair. We recommend using the methods outlined in this post to make your hair naturally curly without using heat.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, naturally curly locks. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair naturally curly, you can try out this look for yourself. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without using heat styling tools. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, hydrated locks.
