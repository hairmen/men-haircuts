Hairstyles That Suit Round Faces Male Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit challenging. You want to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of options that can work for you. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles that suit round faces male.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have soft and curvy features, which can make the face appear wider and fuller. The goal of choosing a hairstyle is to create the illusion of a longer face shape and add some angularity to balance out the roundness. The best hairstyles for round faces male are those that have some height and volume on top and some length on the sides. Avoid styles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look rounder.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair type, texture, and personal style. It's essential to work with a stylist who understands your face shape and can recommend the best style for you. Bring in some pictures of hairstyles that you like and discuss them with your stylist. They can help you choose a style that works for your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles that suit round faces male:

1. The Side Part

This classic style is perfect for round faces. The side part adds some length to the face and creates a more angular look. Use a comb to create a clean part and apply some pomade or wax to style the hair.

2. The Quiff

The quiff is a popular hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of the head. This style works well for round faces as it creates the illusion of a longer face shape. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style the hair and finish with some hairspray.

3. The Undercut

The undercut is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that works well for round faces. This style features short sides and a longer top, which adds some height and volume to the face. Use some pomade or wax to style the hair on top and keep the sides short.

4. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a low-maintenance style that can work well for round faces. This style features short hair all over the head, which can help to balance out the roundness of the face. Use clippers to trim the hair to your desired length.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round faces:

  • Avoid styles that are too short or too long.
  • Choose styles that add height and volume to the top of the head.
  • Consider your hair type and texture.
  • Work with a stylist who understands your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles that suit round faces male:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Also, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the head, as this can make the face appear wider.

What hairstyles work best for round faces?

Hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of the head and have some length on the sides work best for round faces. Examples include the side part, quiff, undercut, and buzz cut.

Hair Care

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, it's essential to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it harder to style. Use a styling product that works for your hair type and follow the directions on the label.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be a bit challenging, but with the right guidance, you can find a style that works for you. Remember to consider your hair type, texture, and personal style, and work with a stylist who understands your face shape. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best.
