If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both stylish and low-maintenance, then medium length wavy hair might be the perfect option for you. In this guide, we'll take a look at how to style mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair, as well as some tips for hair care and maintenance. Whether you're looking for a casual or formal look, we've got you covered.

Description

Mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair is a versatile and popular option for men of all ages. This hairstyle is characterized by hair that falls between the chin and shoulders, with natural waves and texture. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and sophisticated.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles Medium Length Wavy Hair

Styling mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair is relatively easy and straightforward. The key is to work with your natural waves and texture, rather than trying to fight against them. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

Use the Right Products

When it comes to styling wavy hair, the right products can make all the difference. Look for products that are designed for wavy or curly hair, such as curl-enhancing creams or gels. Avoid heavy products that can weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look dry and frizzy. Try to limit washing your hair to every other day, or even every few days if possible. When you do wash your hair, use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of curl-enhancing cream or gel to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair, distributing the product evenly.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently tousle and shape your waves.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Mens Hairstyles Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair:

  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can disrupt your natural waves and texture.
  • If you have particularly frizzy or unruly hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to tame your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • If you want to add some extra volume to your hair, try blow-drying your hair upside down using a diffuser attachment.

FAQs

What face shapes suit mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair?

Mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair can work well for a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, consider adding some layers to your hair to create more definition and structure.

Can I get mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair if my hair is naturally straight?

Yes, you can still achieve mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair even if your hair is naturally straight. Simply use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves and texture in your hair.

Hair Care and Maintenance

To keep your medium length wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care and maintenance:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in nutrients and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth from the inside out.

With the right styling techniques and hair care routine, mens hairstyles medium length wavy hair can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for men of all ages. Experiment with different products, styles, and techniques to find the look that works best for you.
