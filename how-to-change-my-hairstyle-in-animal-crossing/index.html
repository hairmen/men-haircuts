How To Change Your Hairstyle In Animal Crossing

Written by Frank Nov 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're an avid Animal Crossing player, you know that changing your hairstyle is essential to keeping your character looking fresh and stylish. Whether you're bored with your current hairstyle or just want to switch things up, changing your hair is easy and fun. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing, step by step.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a simple process, but it does require a bit of patience. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Visit Harriet's Salon

The first step in changing your hairstyle is to visit Harriet's Salon. Harriet is a poodle who runs a hair salon in the game. Her salon is located on Main Street, and you can visit it by taking the bus from the town plaza. Once you enter the salon, Harriet will greet you and ask if you want to change your hairstyle.

Step 2: Choose Your New Hairstyle

After you've told Harriet that you want to change your hairstyle, she'll ask you to choose a new style from her catalog. The catalog includes a variety of hairstyles, ranging from short and simple to long and elaborate. Take your time and browse through the catalog to find a style that you like.

Step 3: Sit in the Chair

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, Harriet will ask you to sit in the chair. The chair is located in the center of the salon, and it's where Harriet will work her magic. Take a deep breath, sit back, and relax.

Step 4: Let Harriet Work Her Magic

Harriet will begin working on your hair as soon as you sit in the chair. She'll use her scissors, combs, and other tools to create your new hairstyle. This process can take a few minutes, so be patient and enjoy the experience.

Step 5: Admire Your New Look

Once Harriet is finished, she'll ask you to stand up and take a look in the mirror. You'll be able to see your new hairstyle from all angles, and you can take as much time as you need to admire it.

Tips for Changing Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  • Take your time and choose a style that you like
  • Be patient during the process
  • Enjoy the experience
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hairstyle more than once?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want. Just visit Harriet's Salon whenever you're ready for a new look.

Do I have to pay to change my hairstyle?

Yes, you'll need to pay a fee to change your hairstyle. The fee varies depending on the style you choose.

Can I change my hair color?

No, you can't change your hair color in Animal Crossing. However, there are a variety of hair colors to choose from in Harriet's catalog.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

If you want to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny in Animal Crossing, you'll need to take care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit Harriet's Salon regularly to keep your hairstyle looking fresh
  • Use a hairbrush to prevent tangles
  • Wear a hat or other headwear to protect your hair from the sun
  • Use a hair accessory, like a bow or ribbon, to add some flair to your hairstyle

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a fun and exciting experience. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your character looking stylish and fresh. So, what are you waiting for? Visit Harriet's Salon and get ready for a new look!
