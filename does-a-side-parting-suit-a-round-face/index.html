Does A Side Parting Suit A Round Face

Written by Petter Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but unsure if a side parting suits your round face? We’ve got you covered! In this article, we'll explore how to determine if a side parting is right for you, how to create the perfect side part, and some hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if a Side Parting Suits a Round Face
  • How to Create the Perfect Side Parting
  • Hair Care Tips for Side Parting Hairstyles
  • FAQs

How to Determine if a Side Parting Suits a Round Face

Round faces have soft, rounded chins and wide cheekbones. The goal of any hairstyle is to create balance and elongate the face. A side parting can be a great option for those with round faces, but it depends on the individual's features.

To determine if a side parting is right for you, look at the proportions of your face. If your cheeks are the widest part of your face, a side parting can help create the illusion of length. Additionally, if you have a high forehead, a side parting can draw attention away from it.

What to Avoid

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or center parts. These styles can make your face appear rounder.

How to Create the Perfect Side Parting

Creating a side parting is easy and can be done in a few simple steps.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Clean hair is easier to part and style. Wash and condition your hair with products that work well for your hair type.

Step 2: Determine Your Parting

Use the arch of your eyebrow as a guide to determine where to part your hair. Use a comb to create a straight line from the arch of your eyebrow to the crown of your head.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by using a comb to create a part from the crown of your head to the back of your ear.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Finish with Product

Use a small amount of styling product to finish your style. This will help hold the part and keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Side Parting Hairstyles

To keep your side parting looking great, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type. If you have dry hair, look for products that add moisture. If you have oily hair, look for products that help control oil.

Tip 2: Use a Heat Protectant

Heat styling tools can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.

Tip 3: Visit Your Stylist Regularly

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair in top shape.

FAQs

Q: Can a side parting be worn with any hair length?

A: Yes, a side parting can be worn with any hair length.

Q: Can a side parting be worn with bangs?

A: Yes, a side parting can be worn with bangs. However, make sure the bangs are not too blunt and that they blend well with the rest of your hair.

Q: How can I make my side parting look fuller?

A: Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.

In conclusion, a side parting can be a great option for those with round faces. Use our tips to determine if it's right for you, and follow our steps to create the perfect side part. With the right hair care, your side parting will look great and stay healthy.
