How To Do Long Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Long wavy hair is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. Whether you want to achieve beachy waves or glamorous curls, this guide will show you how to do long wavy hair in no time. In this article, we’ll cover everything from how to prep your hair to the best products and tools to use. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Tools and Products
  • Technique
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, you need to prep it properly. This means washing your hair with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup and add moisture to your hair. After washing, gently towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your strands from heat damage.

Once your hair is prepped and ready, it’s time to move on to the next step.

Tools and Products

When it comes to styling long wavy hair, the right tools and products can make all the difference. Here are some of the must-have items:

  • A curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Texture spray
  • Sea salt spray

Make sure to choose a curling iron or wand that is the right size for your hair. A larger barrel will create looser waves, while a smaller barrel will create tighter curls.

Technique

Now that you have all your tools and products ready, it’s time to start styling your hair. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Divide your hair into two sections – top and bottom – and clip the top section out of the way.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the bottom section and spray it with heat protectant spray.
  3. Wrap the section of hair around the curling iron or wand, holding it in place for a few seconds.
  4. Release the curl and spray it lightly with hairspray.
  5. Repeat this process until you’ve curled all the hair in the bottom section.
  6. Release the top section of hair and repeat the curling process.
  7. Once all your hair is curled, use a texture spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your waves.
  8. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold your waves in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect long wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don’t over-curl your hair. Aim for loose, natural-looking waves.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray before styling.
  • If you have thick hair, use a smoothing serum or cream before styling.
  • Experiment with different partings to create different looks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to do long wavy hair:

1. Can you curl long hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can curl long hair with a straightener. Simply twist the hair as you run the straightener down the length of your hair.

2. How long do waves last in long hair?

Waves can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days in long hair, depending on your hair type and the products you use. Using a texture spray or sea salt spray can help your waves last longer.

3. Can you sleep with wavy hair?

Yes, you can sleep with wavy hair. To preserve your waves, loosely braid your hair before bed and secure it with a scrunchie or hair tie.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful long wavy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools every day.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair mask.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect long wavy hair in no time. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful!
