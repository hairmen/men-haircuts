Why Does My Hair Only Curl At The Bottom

Written by Aprilia Oct 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re someone who has naturally straight or wavy hair, you may have experienced the frustrating phenomenon of your hair only curling at the bottom. This can happen whether you’re using heat styling tools or trying to create curls with rollers or braids. In this blog post, we’ll explore why this happens and what you can do about it.

Description

When your hair only curls at the bottom, it means that the curls or waves you’ve created don’t hold throughout the length of your hair. Instead, the curls start at the ends and gradually loosen as they move up towards the roots. This can leave you with a style that looks uneven or unfinished.

How to

If you want to create curls that last throughout your hair, there are a few things you can try:

Use Smaller Sections

When you’re curling your hair, make sure you’re working with small sections at a time. This will allow you to apply heat or product more evenly, which can help your curls hold better. You may also want to try using smaller curling or styling tools.

Use a Stronger Hold Product

If you’re not already using a strong hold product, it may be worth trying one out. Look for a hairspray, mousse, or gel that is specifically designed to hold curls in place. Apply it to your hair before styling, and make sure you’re distributing it evenly.

Try a Different Technique

If you’re using a curling iron or wand, try experimenting with different techniques. For example, you may want to try wrapping your hair in a spiral pattern instead of just curling the ends. You may also want to try curling your hair in smaller sections or using a different heat setting.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide for creating curls that last throughout your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant product if you’re using heat styling tools.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Apply a strong hold product to each section, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Curl each section using a curling iron or wand, wrapping your hair in a spiral pattern from the roots to the ends.
  5. Hold each curl in place for a few seconds before releasing it.
  6. Repeat until all sections are curled.
  7. Allow your curls to cool completely before touching them.
  8. Use a light hold hairspray to set your curls in place.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for creating curls that last:

  • Don’t brush or comb your curls after styling them. This can cause them to loosen and fall out.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day. The oils from your hands can weigh down your curls and cause them to lose their shape.
  • If you’re using heat styling tools, make sure you’re using a high-quality tool that heats evenly and has adjustable heat settings.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create more defined curls.

FAQ

Q: Why does my hair only curl at the bottom?

A: There are a few reasons why this might happen. It could be due to the length or thickness of your hair, the type of styling tools you’re using, or the products you’re using to create your curls.

Q: Can I create curls that last without using heat styling tools?

A: Absolutely! You can try using foam rollers, braids, or twists to create curls without using heat. Just make sure you’re using a strong hold product and allowing your curls to set for a few hours before removing the rollers or braids.

Hair Care

Remember that styling your hair with heat or products can cause damage over time. To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure you’re taking good care of it. This means washing it regularly, using a nourishing conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemical treatments like bleach or relaxers.


In conclusion, if you’re struggling with hair that only curls at the bottom, there are plenty of things you can try to create curls that last throughout your hair. Experiment with different techniques, products, and tools until you find a method that works for you. And don’t forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
