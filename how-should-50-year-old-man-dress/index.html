How Should 50 Year Old Man Dress A Guide To Relaxed Style

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As men age, their fashion sense tends to evolve. What may have been trendy in their 20s and 30s may no longer be appropriate or even comfortable in their 50s. But that doesn't mean they have to sacrifice style for comfort. In this guide, we'll explore how should 50 year old man dress in a relaxed and stylish manner. Whether you're going for a casual look or dressing up for a special occasion, we've got you covered.

How To Dress As a 50 Year Old Man

Dressing as a 50 year old man doesn't have to be difficult. The key is to find a balance between comfort and style. Here are some tips to help you dress well and feel good:

1. Invest in Quality Clothing

Quality clothing is worth the investment. It lasts longer, looks better, and feels more comfortable. Look for clothes made from natural materials like cotton, wool, and silk. These fabrics breathe well and feel good against the skin. Avoid synthetic materials like polyester, which can be uncomfortable and trap heat.

2. Choose a Comfortable Fit

As you get older, comfort becomes increasingly important. Choose clothes that fit well and feel good. Avoid tight or restrictive clothing, which can be uncomfortable and unflattering. Look for clothes with a relaxed fit, which are comfortable without looking sloppy.

3. Stick to Classic Styles

Classic styles never go out of fashion. Stick to timeless pieces like tailored suits, blazers, and dress shoes. These items are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Step by Step Guide

If you're unsure how to dress as a 50 year old man, here's a step by step guide:

1. Start with the Basics

Begin by building a foundation of basic pieces like t-shirts, jeans, and chinos. These items are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Choose neutral colors like black, white, and grey, which are easy to mix and match.

2. Add Some Color

While neutral colors are a safe bet, don't be afraid to add some color to your wardrobe. Choose colors that complement your skin tone and hair color. Stick to one or two bold colors per outfit to avoid looking too busy.

3. Invest in a Tailored Suit

A tailored suit is a must-have for any 50 year old man. Choose a classic style like a navy or grey suit, which can be worn to a variety of occasions. Make sure the suit fits well and is tailored to your body.

4. Don't Forget the Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit. Invest in a few key pieces like a leather belt, dress shoes, and a watch. These items add a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

Tips For Dressing As a 50 Year Old Man

Here are some additional tips to help you dress well as a 50 year old man:

1. Dress for the Occasion

Make sure to dress appropriately for the occasion. If you're going to a formal event, wear a suit and tie. If you're going to a casual event, wear something more relaxed like a polo shirt and jeans.

2. Invest in Good Shoes

Good shoes are essential for any outfit. Invest in a few pairs of high-quality dress shoes and sneakers. Make sure they're comfortable and versatile enough to wear with a variety of outfits.

3. Keep Your Hair Neat

Your hair is an important part of your overall look. Keep it neat and well-groomed. If you're balding, consider shaving your head or keeping your hair short. If you have a full head of hair, keep it well-styled and trimmed.

FAQs

1. Can 50 year old men still wear jeans?

Yes, 50 year old men can still wear jeans. However, it's important to choose the right style and fit. Look for jeans that are straight-legged or bootcut, and avoid skinny or overly baggy styles.

2. Can 50 year old men wear sneakers?

Yes, 50 year old men can wear sneakers. However, make sure to choose a pair that's appropriate for your age and style. Avoid overly flashy or trendy sneakers, and opt for classic styles like Converse or Adidas.

3. How should 50 year old men dress for business casual?

For business casual, 50 year old men should wear a collared shirt, dress pants or chinos, and dress shoes. Avoid wearing jeans, t-shirts, or sneakers.

Hair Care Tips For 50 Year Old Men

As men age, their hair tends to thin and grey. Here are some hair care tips for 50 year old men:

1. Embrace Your Grey Hair

Grey hair can look sophisticated and stylish. Embrace it by keeping it well-groomed and styled.

2. Keep Your Hair Short

Short hair is easier to manage and looks more youthful. Consider a short, classic style like a buzz cut or crew cut.

3. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

As you age, your hair may become drier and more brittle. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated and healthy.

In conclusion, dressing as a 50 year old man can be both comfortable and stylish. Invest in quality clothing, choose a comfortable fit, and stick to classic styles. Dress appropriately for the occasion, invest in good shoes, and keep your hair neat and well-groomed. By following these tips, you'll look and feel great at any age.
