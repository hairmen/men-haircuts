How Can I Bring Out My Natural Curls

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you are lucky! You have a unique and beautiful texture that many people envy. However, sometimes it can be challenging to bring out your curls, especially if you have relaxed your hair or have been using heat styling tools regularly. In this article, we will discuss how you can bring out your natural curls and enjoy your beautiful texture.

Description

Natural curls are a beautiful texture that many people wish they had. However, not everyone is born with curly hair, and some people need to use styling tools or chemical treatments to achieve the look. If you have relaxed hair, it can be challenging to bring out your curls, but it's not impossible. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks to help you bring out your natural curls and embrace your unique texture.

How-To: Bring Out Your Natural Curls

Follow these simple steps to bring out your natural curls:

Step 1: Use the Right Products

Start by using the right products for your hair. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking healthy and bouncy.

Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons. These tools can damage your hair and make it harder to bring out your natural curls. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Step 3: Air Dry Your Hair

Air dry your hair instead of using a blow dryer. Blow drying can cause frizz and damage your hair, making it harder to bring out your natural curls. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair gently.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch your hair to bring out your natural curls. Use your hands to scrunch your hair gently from the ends to the roots. This will help define your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 5: Use a Curling Cream or Gel

Use a curling cream or gel to help define your curls and keep them in place. Apply a small amount of product to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. Use your hands to scrunch your hair again to distribute the product evenly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you bring out your natural curls:

  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it harder to bring out your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bringing out natural curls:

Q: Can I use a curling iron to bring out my natural curls?

A: It's best to avoid using a curling iron if you want to bring out your natural curls. Curling irons can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a natural look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to bring out my natural curls?

A: It's best to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week to bring out your natural curls. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it harder to achieve a natural look.

Q: Can I bring out my natural curls if I have relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can still bring out your natural curls if you have relaxed hair. Follow the steps above, and use products that are specifically designed for relaxed hair.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is essential if you want to bring out your natural curls. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can bring out your natural curls and embrace your unique texture. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and enjoy your beautiful curls!
