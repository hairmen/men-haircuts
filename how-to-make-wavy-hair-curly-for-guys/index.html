How To Make Wavy Hair Curly For Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're a guy with wavy hair who wants to switch up your look, making your hair curly could be the way to go. While it may seem like a daunting task, there are actually several ways to make wavy hair curly. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to make wavy hair curly for guys.

Description

Curly hair is a popular hairstyle among guys, and for good reason. It's a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways. However, not everyone is born with curly hair. If you have wavy hair, you can still achieve curly hair with the right techniques and products. In this article, we'll show you how to make wavy hair curly for guys.

How to Make Wavy Hair Curly for Guys

There are several ways to make wavy hair curly. Here are some of the most popular techniques:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using a perm
  • Using a curl-enhancing product
  • Braiding your hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Using a Curling Iron

Here's how to use a curling iron to make wavy hair curly:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Section your hair into small sections.
  5. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  6. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds.
  7. Release the curling iron and let the curl cool.
  8. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  9. Once all of your hair is curled, use a light-hold hairspray to set the curls in place.

Using a Perm

Here's how to use a perm to make wavy hair curly:

  1. Visit a salon and consult with a stylist about the type of perm you want.
  2. Wash your hair and let it air dry.
  3. Apply a perm solution to your hair.
  4. Wrap your hair around perm rods.
  5. Apply a neutralizer to your hair to set the curls.
  6. Wait for 24-48 hours before washing your hair.

Using a Curl-Enhancing Product

Here's how to use a curl-enhancing product to make wavy hair curly:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair from the ends up to the roots.
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Braiding Your Hair

Here's how to braid your hair to make wavy hair curly:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Braid your hair into several small braids.
  3. Let your hair dry completely.
  4. Unbraid your hair and use your fingers to loosen the waves.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make wavy hair curly:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Choose a perm that is suitable for your hair type and length.
  • Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without damaging the curls.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

FAQs

Can wavy hair be made curly?

Yes, wavy hair can be made curly with the right techniques and products. Using a curling iron, getting a perm, using a curl-enhancing product, or braiding your hair can all help make wavy hair curly.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3-6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your hair.

Can I still style my curly hair?

Yes, curly hair can be styled in many different ways. You can try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz, or use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for guys with curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make wavy hair curly for guys. Whether you choose to use a curling iron, get a perm, use a curl-enhancing product, or braid your hair, you'll be able to achieve the curly hairstyle you want.
