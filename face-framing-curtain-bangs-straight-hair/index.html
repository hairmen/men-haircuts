Face Framing Curtain Bangs Straight Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both chic and versatile, then look no further than face framing curtain bangs for straight hair. This style is all about creating a soft and feminine look that can work for any occasion, whether it's a casual weekend brunch or a fancy night out. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this trendy look in a few easy steps, as well as answer some common questions and provide tips for maintaining your new 'do.

Description

Face framing curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted in the middle and swept to the sides, creating a curtain-like effect around the face. This style is perfect for those with straight hair, as it adds texture and movement to otherwise flat locks. It's also a great way to change up your look without committing to a full-on haircut or color.

How to Achieve Face Framing Curtain Bangs

The key to achieving face framing curtain bangs is to start with a good haircut. You'll want to go to a stylist who has experience cutting bangs, as they can be tricky to get just right. Once you have your bangs cut, you can style them in a few different ways depending on your preference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your face framing curtain bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and tease it lightly with a comb or brush.
  4. Smooth the teased section down and pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  5. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and comb it forward.
  6. Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  7. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  8. Remove the bobby pin from the top of your head and gently comb your hair back into place.
  9. Finish with hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips for keeping your curtain bangs looking their best:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting too long and heavy.
  • Use a flat iron to touch up any flyaways or frizz.
  • Avoid washing your bangs every day, as this can strip them of their natural oils and make them look dull.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about face framing curtain bangs:

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Can I wear my curtain bangs with a ponytail?

Absolutely! Curtain bangs look great with a high ponytail or messy bun.

Do curtain bangs work with all face shapes?

Yes, curtain bangs can be customized to work with any face shape. Talk to your stylist about the best way to cut your bangs based on your features.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's free of sulfates and parabens, and avoid heat styling your hair too often. You can also use a hair mask once a week to give your locks a boost of hydration.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect face framing curtain bangs for straight hair in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
