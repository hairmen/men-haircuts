Best Hair Straighteners For Thick Hair 2021

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a hair straightener that can handle your strands. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hair straighteners for thick hair in 2021.

Description

Thick hair can be challenging to straighten. It requires a hair straightener that can handle the volume and thickness of the hair without damaging it. The best hair straighteners for thick hair are those that can reach high temperatures quickly and evenly distribute heat throughout the hair.

How to Choose the Best Hair Straightener for Thick Hair

When choosing a hair straightener for thick hair, there are a few things to consider. First, you want to look for a straightener with adjustable temperature settings. This will allow you to choose the right temperature for your hair type. Second, you want to look for a straightener with wide plates. This will allow you to straighten more hair at once, which will save you time. Lastly, you want to look for a straightener with tourmaline or ceramic plates. These materials will help protect your hair from heat damage.

Step by Step Guide to Using a Hair Straightener on Thick Hair

Now that you have chosen the best hair straightener for your thick hair, it's time to learn how to use it. Here is a step by step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before using a straightener. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start straightening.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply heat protectant to your hair before using a straightener. This will help protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections before using a straightener. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Start at the roots and slowly glide the straightener down your hair. Repeat until all sections of your hair are straightened.

Step 5: Finish Your Look

Finish your look with a hair serum or spray to add shine and protect your hair from humidity.

Tips for Straightening Thick Hair

Straightening thick hair can be challenging, but with these tips, you can achieve a sleek, straight look:

  • Use a straightener with adjustable temperature settings
  • Section your hair into manageable sections
  • Use a heat protectant
  • Start at the roots and slowly glide the straightener down your hair
  • Finish your look with a hair serum or spray to add shine and protect your hair from humidity

FAQs

What temperature should I use to straighten thick hair?

You should use a temperature of 350-400°F to straighten thick hair. However, you should always start with a lower temperature and gradually increase it until you find the right temperature for your hair type.

Can I straighten my hair every day?

Straightening your hair every day can damage your hair. It's best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

In addition to using the best hair straightener for your hair type, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care for thick hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools every day
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much product
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair

Overall, the best hair straighteners for thick hair in 2021 are those that can handle the volume and thickness of your hair without damaging it. Look for a straightener with adjustable temperature settings, wide plates, and tourmaline or ceramic plates. Follow our step by step guide and tips for straightening thick hair to achieve a sleek, straight look without damaging your hair.
