Best Stuff To Slick Hair Back

Written by Petter Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best stuff to slick your hair back? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the top products and techniques to achieve a sleek and stylish look. Whether you have long or short hair, these tips and tricks will work for you. Let's get started!

Description

Slicking your hair back is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. It is a popular choice for men and women alike and can be achieved with a variety of products. The key is to find the best stuff for your hair type and desired look. In this article, we will be exploring the different options and how to use them.

How To

The first step in slicking your hair back is to start with clean, dry hair. If you have oily hair, you may want to use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Once your hair is clean and dry, you can begin to apply your product of choice.

Step 1: Choose Your Product

There are many products available for slicking hair back, including pomades, gels, waxes, and creams. Depending on your hair type and desired look, you may prefer one over the other. Pomades are a popular choice for their high shine and strong hold. Gels are great for a wet look and can provide a firm hold. Waxes are ideal for thicker hair and can help to tame frizz. Creams are a more lightweight option and can be used for a more natural look.

Step 2: Apply the Product

Once you have chosen your product, begin by applying a small amount to your fingertips. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Starting at the crown of your head, comb your hair back with your hands. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. Continue to work the product through your hair until you achieve your desired look.

Step 3: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your style stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to lock in the product and prevent any flyaways. Be sure to hold the hairspray at least 12 inches away from your head to avoid a sticky or crunchy finish.

Tips

- Start with a small amount of product and build up as needed to avoid a greasy or weighed-down look.

- Use a comb or brush to help distribute the product evenly through your hair.

- If you have long hair, consider using a hair tie or clip to keep it out of your face while styling.

FAQ

Q: Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

A: Yes! You may need to use a stronger product to tame your curls, but slicking your hair back can work for any hair type.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

A: It's best to avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

When slicking your hair back, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and promote healthy growth. Be sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-styling or using heat tools too often. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Conclusion

Slicking your hair back is a classic and stylish look that can be achieved with the right products and techniques. Whether you prefer a high shine or a more natural look, there is a product out there for you. Follow these tips and take care of your hair to achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle.
