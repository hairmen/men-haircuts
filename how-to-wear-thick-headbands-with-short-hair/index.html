How To Wear Thick Headbands With Short Hair

Written by Christine Nov 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that wearing a thick headband is not possible. However, with the right techniques and style, you can easily pull off this look. In this article, we will show you how to wear thick headbands with short hair in a stylish and comfortable way.

Table of Contents

  • How to wear thick headbands with short hair
  • Step by step guide
  • Tips for wearing thick headbands with short hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair care tips

How to wear thick headbands with short hair

Before we get into the details of how to wear thick headbands with short hair, it's essential to choose the right headband. You can choose from various materials such as fabric, metal or plastic. Choose a headband that is comfortable and fits your head well.

The following are the steps to follow when wearing a thick headband with short hair:

Step by step guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to create volume and texture.
  3. Place the headband on your head and adjust it until it fits comfortably.
  4. Push the headband back slightly, so it's not sitting directly on your forehead.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure the headband in place at the back of your head.
  6. Pull out a few strands of hair from the front to create a messy and effortless look.
  7. You can also add some accessories such as hair clips or flowers to enhance the look.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for wearing thick headbands with short hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing thick headbands with short hair:

  • Choose a headband that complements your outfit and style.
  • If you have short bangs, try pushing them to the side or pin them back.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles such as messy buns or braids.
  • Don't be afraid to try different colors and patterns of headbands.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.

FAQs

Q: How do I choose the right headband for my short hair?

A: Choose a headband that is comfortable and fits your head well. You can also experiment with different materials such as fabric, metal or plastic.

Q: Can I wear a thick headband with short bangs?

A: Yes, you can. Try pushing them to the side or pin them back to create a stylish look.

Hair care tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily wear thick headbands with short hair in a stylish and comfortable way. Experiment with different styles and colors to find the perfect headband for you.
