How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a professional man is to consider the industry you work in. For example, a creative industry such as advertising may allow for more bold and trendy hairstyles, while a conservative industry such as law may require a more classic and traditional look. It's important to also consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

Some popular hairstyles for professional men in 2023 include the slicked back undercut, the textured crop, and the side part. The slicked back undercut is a modern take on the classic side part and involves shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving longer hair on top. The textured crop is a short and messy cut that works well for those with wavy or curly hair. The side part is a timeless and versatile look that involves combing hair to one side and keeping the sides and back shorter.

Step by Step Styling Instructions

To style the slicked back undercut, start by applying a small amount of pomade or gel to damp hair. Use a comb to slick hair back and away from the face, working from the front to the back. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place.

To style the textured crop, start by applying a texturizing spray to damp hair. Use your fingers to tousle hair and create a messy, undone look. Use a hair dryer on low heat to add volume and texture.

To style the side part, start by applying a small amount of pomade or wax to damp hair. Use a comb to part hair to one side and smooth down any flyaways. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place.

Helpful Tips

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when styling your hair as a professional man:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products that work well for your hair type.
  • Schedule regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh and polished.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you.
  • Use a hair dryer on low heat to set your style in place without damaging your hair.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a professional man?

The best hairstyle for a professional man depends on the industry you work in, your face shape, and your personal style. Some popular options include the slicked back undercut, the textured crop, and the side part.

How often should a professional man get a haircut?

It's recommended that a professional man get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep their style looking fresh and polished.

Hair Care Advice

Taking care of your hair is crucial to maintaining a professional and polished appearance. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

We hope this guide on hair style for professional man in 2023 has been helpful. Remember to choose a hairstyle that works for your industry, face shape, and personal style, and to take good care of your hair to maintain a polished and professional appearance.
