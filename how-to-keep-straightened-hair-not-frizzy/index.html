How To Keep Straightened Hair Not Frizzy Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Straightening your hair can be a great way to switch up your look, but dealing with frizz can be a real struggle. Luckily, there are some tips and tricks you can use to keep your straightened hair looking sleek and smooth for longer. In this article, we'll go over the best ways to prevent frizz and keep your hair looking fabulous.

How to Keep Straightened Hair Not Frizzy

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of preventing frizz, it's important to start with a good foundation. Make sure you're using a high-quality straightening iron that's appropriate for your hair type. Thin or damaged hair requires a lower heat setting, while thick or coarse hair can handle higher temperatures. You should also be using a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair to minimize damage.

Once you've straightened your hair, there are a few things you can do to keep it from getting frizzy:

1. Choose the Right Products

Products are key when it comes to keeping your hair smooth and frizz-free. Look for a smoothing serum or oil that's specifically designed for straightened hair. You should also consider using a leave-in conditioner to help prevent damage and keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid Humidity

Humidity is the enemy of straightened hair. If you're going to be outside on a humid day, try to wear a hat or use an umbrella to shield your hair from the moisture. You can also use a humidity-blocking hairspray to help keep your hair in place.

3. Don't Touch Your Hair

It can be tempting to run your fingers through your hair or brush it, but doing so can cause frizz. Try to keep your hands off your hair as much as possible, and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle it instead of a brush.

4. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and lead to frizz. Switching to a silk pillowcase can help keep your hair smooth and prevent breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to keeping your straightened hair looking sleek:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to help prevent frizz.

2. Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Spray a heat protectant on your hair before straightening it to minimize damage.

3. Straighten Your Hair in Sections

Divide your hair into sections and straighten each section one at a time to ensure that you get every strand.

4. Apply a Smoothing Serum or Oil

Apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair after straightening to help keep it smooth and frizz-free.

5. Avoid Touching Your Hair

Avoid touching your hair as much as possible to prevent frizz.

6. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and keep your hair smooth.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for keeping your straightened hair looking great:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends, which can lead to frizz.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and lead to frizz.

FAQ

How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to limit how often you straighten your hair to prevent damage. If you have thin or damaged hair, you should only straighten it once a week. If you have thick or coarse hair, you can straighten it up to three times a week.

What's the best way to prevent damage when straightening my hair?

Use a high-quality straightening iron with adjustable heat settings, and always use a heat protectant spray beforehand. You should also avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using the tips and tricks we've outlined above, there are some general hair care tips you should follow to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your straightened hair looking smooth and frizz-free for longer.
