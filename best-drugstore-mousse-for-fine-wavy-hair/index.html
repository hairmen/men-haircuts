Best Drugstore Mousse For Fine Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have fine wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right products to give you the volume and texture you crave. Luckily, drugstore mousses can be a great option for achieving the perfect beachy waves without breaking the bank. In this guide, we'll break down the best drugstore mousses for fine wavy hair, as well as provide tips and tricks for using them effectively.

Why Use Mousse?

Mousse is a great option for adding volume and texture to fine wavy hair. It's lightweight and won't weigh your hair down like some other styling products, making it perfect for achieving a natural, tousled look. Additionally, mousse can help define your waves and prevent frizz, making it a must-have in any wavy-haired person's styling arsenal.

How to Use Mousse

Using mousse is easy, but there are a few tips and tricks to keep in mind to ensure you get the best results possible:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before applying mousse, make sure your hair is clean and towel-dried. It's also a good idea to apply a heat protectant spray if you plan on using a hair dryer or other heat styling tools.

Step 2: Apply the Mousse

Dispense a golf ball-sized amount of mousse into your hand and evenly distribute it throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Once the mousse is distributed evenly, scrunch your hair with your hands to help define your waves and create volume.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air-Dry

Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying to prevent frizz.

Tips for Using Mousse on Fine Wavy Hair

While mousse can be a great option for fine wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure you get the best results:

- Use a lightweight mousse to avoid weighing your hair down. - Don't overdo it – a little bit of mousse goes a long way. - Focus on the roots and mid-lengths of your hair, as these are the areas that will benefit most from the added volume and texture. - Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying to prevent frizz. - Experiment with different application techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

What is the Best Drugstore Mousse for Fine Wavy Hair?

There are a lot of great drugstore mousses for fine wavy hair, but some of our favorites include:

- Herbal Essences Totally Twisted Curl Boosting Mousse - Aussie Instant Freeze Sculpting Mousse - Garnier Fructis Sky-Hi Volume Mousse - TRESemmé Thermal Creations Volumizing Mousse

Can Mousse be Used on Straight Hair?

Yes, mousse can be used on straight hair to add volume and texture. However, it may not be as effective at defining waves or curls.

Hair Care for Fine Wavy Hair

Using the right products is important for achieving the perfect beachy waves, but it's also important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are a few tips for caring for fine wavy hair:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for fine hair. - Limit heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when you do style your hair. - Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage. - Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve the perfect beachy waves that will make you feel like you just stepped off the sand.
