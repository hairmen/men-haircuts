Mens Hairstyles For Medium Length Wavy Hair A Guide

Medium length wavy hair has become a popular hairstyle among men in recent years. With its versatility and easy maintenance, it's no wonder why so many men are opting for this style. In this guide, we will explore the best mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair, how to achieve them, and tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Medium length wavy hair is typically defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders and has a natural wave or curl to it. This type of hair is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, making it a popular choice among men. Whether you're looking for a casual or formal look, there is a mens hairstyle for medium length wavy hair that will suit your needs.

How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles for Medium Length Wavy Hair

Achieving mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair is relatively easy. The key is to find a style that works best for your hair type and face shape. Some popular mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair include the messy fringe, the side part, and the textured crop. These styles can be achieved with the right haircut and styling products.

Haircut

When getting a haircut for medium length wavy hair, it's important to communicate with your stylist to ensure they understand the look you're going for. A layered cut is often the best option for wavy hair, as it helps to add texture and volume. To achieve a messy fringe or textured crop, ask your stylist to cut your hair shorter on the sides and longer on top. For a more classic look, a side part can be achieved with a slightly longer cut on top and shorter sides.

Styling Products

To achieve mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair, you will need to invest in some styling products. A good hair mousse or sea salt spray can help to enhance your hair's natural waves and add texture. For a more polished look, a styling cream or pomade can be used to create a more defined style.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the messy fringe mens hairstyle for medium length wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply a hair mousse or sea salt spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  4. Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a styling cream or pomade to create a messy fringe.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Wavy Hair

To keep your medium length wavy hair looking its best, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet to avoid breakage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I straighten my medium length wavy hair?

While it is possible to straighten your medium length wavy hair, it's not recommended as it can damage your hair. If you must straighten your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant and a low-heat setting on your styling tool.

How often should I wash my medium length wavy hair?

It's best to wash your medium length wavy hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Medium Length Wavy Hair

Taking care of your medium length wavy hair is essential to keeping it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet to avoid breakage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, mens hairstyles for medium length wavy hair are versatile and easy to maintain. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a variety of looks that suit your personal style. Just remember to take care of your hair by using moisturizing products and avoiding heat damage.
