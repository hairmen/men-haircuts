How To Cut Your Long Hair Evenly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've been growing your hair out for a while, you may be in need of a trim. But how can you cut your long hair evenly without heading to the salon? In this article, we'll walk you through the process of cutting your own hair at home. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the best techniques for creating an even cut. So, let's get started!

What You'll Need

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools on hand:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A hair tie or clip
  • A handheld mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. You want your hair to be completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into two equal sections by parting it down the middle. Secure one section with a hair tie or clip so it's out of the way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take the remaining section of hair and hold it straight up with your non-dominant hand. Use your scissors to cut the hair straight across at the desired length. Make sure you're cutting straight across and not at an angle.

Step 4: Check Your Work

After you've cut the first section, release the other section of hair and compare the lengths. If one side is longer, use your scissors to trim it until both sides are even.

Step 5: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you've achieved an even cut on one side, repeat the process on the other side of your head. Remember to keep checking your work as you go to ensure both sides are even.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Evenly

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your hair:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Don't pull your hair too tightly as you cut, as this can result in an uneven cut.
  • Be patient and take your time. It's better to make small cuts and check your work frequently than to rush and end up with an uneven cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair evenly without a mirror?

It's possible, but it's not recommended. It's important to be able to see what you're doing as you cut your hair to ensure an even cut.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be deceptive and appear longer than it actually is.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. In general, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've successfully cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting your long hair evenly at home may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable. Remember to take your time, be patient, and check your work frequently. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a perfectly even cut every time!
