How To Cut Short Side Swept Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to try something new? Cutting short side swept bangs is a great way to change up your look without having to commit to a drastic haircut. But before you grab a pair of scissors and start chopping away, follow these simple steps to ensure that you get the perfect side swept bangs.

How to Cut Short Side Swept Bangs at Home

Cutting your own hair may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or bobby pins
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect short side swept bangs:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into two parts – the bangs section and the rest of your hair. Use hair clips or bobby pins to hold the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 2: Wet Your Bangs

Use a water spray bottle to wet your bangs. This will make them easier to cut and ensure that they stay in place.

Step 3: Comb Your Bangs

Use a comb to comb your bangs straight down. Make sure they are evenly distributed and there are no tangles.

Step 4: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length of your bangs. Remember, it’s always better to start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter. You can always cut more, but you can’t replace what you’ve already cut.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Hold the scissors at a slight angle and start cutting the hair at the desired length. Make small cuts and check your progress frequently to ensure that your bangs are even.

Step 6: Check the Length

Once you have cut one side, check the length and make sure it’s even with the other side. If it’s not, make small adjustments until both sides are even.

Step 7: Style Your Bangs

Use a blow dryer to style your bangs. Use a round brush to create a slight curve and give your bangs some volume.

Step 8: Final Touches

Once you’re happy with the length and style of your bangs, make any final touches with the scissors. Trim any stray hairs or uneven ends.

Tips for Cutting Short Side Swept Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short side swept bangs:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter
  • Make small cuts and check your progress frequently
  • Hold the scissors at a slight angle to create a natural curve
  • Use a blow dryer and round brush to style your bangs
  • Trim any stray hairs or uneven ends at the end

FAQs

Can I cut my own bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own bangs. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

How can I make sure my bangs are even?

Check your progress frequently and make small adjustments as needed. If you’re having trouble, consider asking a friend or family member for help.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always adjust the length or style of your bangs. If you’re really unhappy with the result, consider visiting a professional hairstylist to fix the problem.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you’ve cut your short side swept bangs, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

With these simple tips, you can achieve the perfect short side swept bangs and keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy.
