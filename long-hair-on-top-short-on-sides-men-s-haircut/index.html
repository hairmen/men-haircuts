Long Hair On Top Short On Sides Men s Haircut

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can give you a bold and confident look? If yes, then the long hair on top short on sides men's haircut is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has gained a lot of popularity in recent years, especially among young men.

Description

The long hair on top short on sides men's haircut is a versatile hairstyle that involves leaving the hair on top long while keeping the sides and back short. This haircut is often referred to as an undercut, and it can be styled in various ways depending on your preference.

How to

Getting a long hair on top short on sides men's haircut is easy, and you can do it at home or at a salon. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your hair. You can choose to keep the hair on top long, medium, or short, depending on your preference. The sides and back should be kept short, usually buzzed with clippers.

Step 2: Get the Haircut

Once you have decided on the length, it's time to get the haircut. You can either do it yourself or go to a salon. If you're doing it yourself, use a pair of clippers to buzz the sides and back. Then, use scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the haircut, you can style your hair in different ways. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy. Use some hair product to keep your hair in place and give it some texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a long hair on top short on sides men's haircut:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair in place.
  • Visit a professional hairdresser if you're not confident about doing it yourself.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q. Is a long hair on top short on sides men's haircut suitable for all hair types?

A. Yes, this haircut can be done on all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

Q. How often should I get a long hair on top short on sides men's haircut?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q. Can I style my long hair on top short on sides men's haircut differently every day?

A. Yes, you can experiment with different styles and change your look every day.

Hair Care

To maintain your long hair on top short on sides men's haircut, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it in shape and prevent split ends.

With the right care and styling, a long hair on top short on sides men's haircut can give you a unique and stylish look that will turn heads wherever you go.
