How To Cut Short Curly Hair With Bangs A Complete Guide

If you have short curly hair with bangs, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut that works for you. But fear not, because in this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your short curly hair with bangs. We will also give you some tips on how to maintain your curls and keep your hair healthy. So, let's dive in!

How to Cut Short Curly Hair with Bangs

Before we start, it's important to note that cutting short curly hair with bangs requires some skill and patience. If you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to visit a professional stylist. But if you're up for the challenge, here's what you need to do:

1. Start with Dry Hair

It's essential to cut your hair when it's dry because curly hair tends to shrink when it dries. If you cut your hair when it's wet, you may end up cutting more than you intended, and your hair may look shorter than you wanted.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place. Start with the back sections first.

3. Cut the Back Sections

Using hair-cutting scissors, start cutting the back sections. Start with the ends and work your way up, cutting small sections at a time. Be sure to cut the hair in a straight line, keeping the length consistent.

4. Cut the Front Sections

Once you've finished cutting the back sections, move on to the front sections. Use the back sections as a guide and cut the front sections to the same length. Be sure to cut the bangs to the desired length as well.

5. Check for Symmetry

After cutting your hair, check for symmetry by comparing both sides. If one side is longer than the other, even it out by trimming the longer side.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut short curly hair with bangs:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Cut the back sections first.
  4. Cut the front sections, including the bangs.
  5. Check for symmetry.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting short curly hair with bangs:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair-cutting scissors.
  • Start with small sections and cut slowly.
  • Don't cut too much off at once.
  • Check for symmetry throughout the cutting process.
  • Don't be afraid to seek professional help if you're not confident in your abilities.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

No, it's best to cut your hair when it's dry because curly hair tends to shrink when it dries. If you cut your hair when it's wet, you may end up cutting more than you intended, and your hair may look shorter than you wanted.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your haircut. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair with Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for short curly hair with bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut short curly hair with bangs, you can confidently style your hair at home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and seek professional help if needed. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to maintain healthy, beautiful curls that you'll love.
