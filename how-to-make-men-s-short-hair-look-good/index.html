How To Make Men s Short Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a man with short hair, you may be wondering how to make it look good. While you may think that having short hair limits your styling options, there are actually plenty of ways to create a stylish and polished look. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you make the most of your short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair for Men
  • Step-by-Step Guide: How to Style Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs about Men's Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair for Men

There are a few key things to keep in mind when styling short hair:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Consider your hair type and texture (e.g. curly, straight, thick, thin)
  • Think about your personal style and preferences

Some popular short hairstyles for men include:

  • The classic buzz cut
  • The crew cut
  • The textured crop
  • The slicked-back undercut
  • The side-parted comb-over

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Style Short Hair

Here's a general guide to styling short hair:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair
  2. Apply a styling product (e.g. pomade, wax, gel) to your hair
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style
  4. Finish with hairspray or another finishing product (optional)

Keep in mind that the specific steps may vary depending on your chosen hairstyle and hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (e.g. blow dryers, straighteners)
  • Consider using a hair mask or other deep conditioning treatment once a week

FAQs about Men's Short Hair

Here are some common questions about men's short hair:

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preferences, but most people can get away with washing their hair every 2-3 days.

Q: How do I choose a short hairstyle that suits me?

A: Consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a short hairstyle. Your barber or stylist can also give you recommendations.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if it's thinning or receding?

A: Yes, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can help disguise thinning or receding hairlines. Talk to your barber or stylist for suggestions.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair (e.g. bleach)
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your short hair look its best. Whether you prefer a classic buzz cut or a modern textured crop, there are plenty of options to suit your style and preferences.
