How To Contour Square Face Shape

Written by Bardi Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Contouring is a makeup technique that helps define your facial features. It's especially helpful for those with square face shapes. If you have a square face shape, you have a strong jawline and a wide forehead. Contouring can help soften these features and create a more balanced look. In this article, we'll show you how to contour your square face shape step by step.

Face Shape Tips

Before we get started with contouring, it's important to know your face shape. As we mentioned earlier, square face shapes have a strong jawline and a wide forehead. Here are some tips to help you identify if you have a square face shape:

  • Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width
  • Your jawline is sharp and defined
  • Your face is angular and has minimal curves

If you have a square face shape, contouring can help soften your features and create a more balanced look.

Contouring Tips

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here are some contouring tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a contour shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone
  • Blend, blend, blend! Blending is key to creating a natural-looking contour
  • Use a matte contour shade to avoid any shimmer or shine on the areas you want to define
  • Apply your contour in natural light to ensure it looks even and natural

Step by Step Guide

Now, let's get into the step-by-step guide on how to contour your square face shape:

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before you start contouring, make sure your skin is prepped and ready. Start with a clean, moisturized face. Apply your foundation as you normally would.

Step 2: Apply Contour

Using a contour brush, apply your contour shade to the areas you want to define. For square face shapes, this typically includes the temples, the hollows of your cheeks, and your jawline. Apply the contour in a "3" shape, starting at your temples, moving down to your cheeks, and then down to your jawline.

Step 3: Blend

Using a blending brush or a damp beauty sponge, blend out your contour to create a natural-looking finish. Focus on blending the edges to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Highlight

Next, apply a highlight shade to the areas you want to bring forward. For square face shapes, this typically includes the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and the tops of your cheekbones.

Step 5: Blend Again

Using the same blending brush or beauty sponge, blend out your highlight to create a seamless finish. Make sure to blend the edges where your contour and highlight shades meet.

Step 6: Finish with Powder

To set your contour and highlight, apply a translucent powder all over your face. This will help your makeup last longer and prevent any unwanted shine.

FAQ

What products do I need to contour my square face shape?

You'll need a contour shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone, a highlight shade that is one or two shades lighter than your natural skin tone, a contour brush, a blending brush or beauty sponge, and a translucent powder.

Can I contour my square face shape without using a contour shade?

You can still contour your square face shape without a contour shade, but it may not be as effective. Using a contour shade helps define your features and create a more balanced look.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you know how to contour your square face shape, here are some hair care tips to help enhance your new look:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your square face shape. Soft waves or layers can help soften your features
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline, such as slicked-back ponytails or buns

Contouring your square face shape can help soften your features and create a more balanced look. Remember to choose a contour shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone, blend your contour and highlight shades seamlessly, and set your makeup with a translucent powder. With these steps, you'll have a perfectly contoured square face shape in no time!
