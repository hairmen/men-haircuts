Guy Hairstyles Long Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

In recent years, men's hairstyles have become more diverse and trendy. One of the most popular styles for men is the long top short sides haircut. This hairstyle offers a perfect balance between a classic and modern look, making it a favorite among men of all ages.

Description

The long top short sides haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. This hairstyle features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair is usually styled with a bit of volume on the top, and the sides are tapered or faded for a clean, sharp look.

This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a classic yet modern look. It's easy to maintain and can be worn in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. It's also suitable for different occasions, from a casual day out to a formal event.

How to Get the Guy Hairstyles Long Top Short Sides

The long top short sides haircut can be achieved with the help of a professional barber or stylist. You can also do it yourself if you have the right tools and skills. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on the top and how short on the sides. The length will depend on your face shape, hair type, and personal preference. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to keep the sides shorter to create a more angular look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wet hair can be misleading, and you might end up cutting too much. It's also a good idea to use a styling product to keep your hair in place while cutting.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Start by cutting the hair on the sides and back. Use clippers or scissors to taper or fade the hair, depending on the style you want. You can also create a hard part or a soft part to add some interest.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back, move on to the top. Use scissors or a razor to trim the hair to the desired length. You can also add layers or texture for a more dynamic look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Apply some styling product to the top, and use your fingers or a comb to create the desired look. You can also use a blow dryer to add some volume or texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're not confident in cutting your hair, it's best to go to a professional barber or stylist. Here is a step-by-step guide for getting the long top short sides haircut:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with your barber or stylist about the style you want. They can recommend the best length and style based on your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Wash and Prep

Next, your hair will be washed and prepared for cutting. This will involve shampooing and conditioning to remove any dirt and oil from your hair.

Step 3: Cutting the Sides and Back

The barber or stylist will use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back. They will taper or fade the hair to create a clean, sharp look.

Step 4: Cutting the Top

Once the sides and back are done, they will move on to the top. The hair will be trimmed to the desired length and styled to create volume and texture.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Finally, the barber or stylist will add some finishing touches to your hair, such as a part or some texture. They will also give you some tips on how to maintain the style at home.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

The long top short sides haircut is easy to maintain, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place, such as pomade, wax, or gel.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

FAQ

What face shape is best for the long top short sides haircut?

The long top short sides haircut is suitable for most face shapes, but it works particularly well for those with oval or square faces. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the sides shorter to create a more angular look.

Can I style my hair differently with the long top short sides haircut?

Yes, the long top short sides haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways. You can slick it back for a classic look, mess it up for a casual look, or add some texture for a more modern look.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. This will also help maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Aside from maintaining your long top short sides haircut, it's also essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for men:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't rub your hair with a towel after washing; instead, pat it dry gently.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.

In conclusion, the long top short sides haircut is a versatile and trendy style that is perfect for men of all ages. Whether you do it yourself or go to a professional barber or stylist, this haircut is easy to maintain and can be styled in various ways. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
