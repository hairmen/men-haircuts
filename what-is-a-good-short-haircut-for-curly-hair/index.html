What Is A Good Short Haircut For Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Nov 23, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. While long, flowing locks may be the go-to look for many curly-haired folks, sometimes a shorter cut can be a refreshing change. So, what is a good short haircut for curly hair? Let’s explore some options.

Description

A good short haircut for curly hair should be easy to manage, low maintenance, and accentuate your natural curls. It should be versatile enough to be styled in different ways and flatter your face shape. The right haircut can make all the difference in how your curls look and feel.

How to

Before deciding on a short haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Short haircuts work best on oval, heart-shaped, and square faces. If you have a round face, consider a longer cut that falls below your chin to elongate your face. If you have thick, coarse hair, a layered cut can help to remove some of the bulk and make your hair more manageable. If you have fine hair, a bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture.

Step by Step

  1. Consult with a stylist who specializes in curly hair. They can help you determine the best cut for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Bring in photos of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.
  3. Consider the length and style of the cut. Do you want a short bob, pixie cut, or something in between?
  4. Think about how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair each day. A low-maintenance cut may be best if you’re always on the go.
  5. Ask your stylist for tips on how to maintain and style your new haircut.

Tips

Once you’ve found the right short haircut for your curly hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind to help maintain your new look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your natural curls.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Can I still straighten my hair with a short haircut?
A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair with a short haircut. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and a flat iron with adjustable temperature settings to avoid damage.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?
A: It depends on the cut. If you have a round face, a shorter cut that falls below your chin can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

With the right short haircut and proper hair care, you can embrace your natural curls and rock a low-maintenance, stylish look.
