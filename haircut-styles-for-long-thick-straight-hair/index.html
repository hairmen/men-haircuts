Haircut Styles For Long Thick Straight Hair

Written by Robby Oct 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long thick straight hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's beautiful and enviable, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. Finding the right haircut can make all the difference in achieving a look that works for you. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircut styles for long thick straight hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your long thick straight hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and complements your overall look. Here are some tips for determining your face shape:

  • Oval: Your forehead is slightly wider than your chin, and your face is longer than it is wide.
  • Round: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a soft, circular shape.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, giving your face a strong, angular appearance.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your chin, and your face tapers down to a point at your chin.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are both narrower.

Haircut Styles for Long Thick Straight Hair

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut that works for you. Here are some of the best haircut styles for long thick straight hair:

The Blunt Cut

The blunt cut is a classic style that works well for long thick straight hair. This cut is characterized by a straight, even line across the bottom of your hair, with no layers or angles. It works well for most face shapes, and it's easy to maintain.

The Layered Cut

If you're looking for a more versatile style, the layered cut is a great option. This cut features layers of varying lengths that add movement and dimension to your hair. It works well for most face shapes, and it's perfect for those who want to add some texture to their long thick straight hair.

The Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to add some interest to your haircut without sacrificing length, side-swept bangs are a great option. These bangs are swept to one side, framing your face and adding some softness to your look. They work well for most face shapes, but they're especially flattering for those with square or heart-shaped faces.

The Long Bob

The long bob, or "lob," is a popular haircut style that works well for long thick straight hair. This cut features a length that falls somewhere between your chin and your shoulders, with subtle layers that add movement and texture. It works well for most face shapes, and it's a great option for those who want a shorter style without sacrificing too much length.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Straight Hair

Now that you've chosen a haircut style, it's important to know how to maintain your long thick straight hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircut styles for long thick straight hair:

What is the best haircut for long thick straight hair?

The best haircut for long thick straight hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Some popular options include the blunt cut, the layered cut, side-swept bangs, and the long bob.

How often should I trim my long thick straight hair?

You should trim your long thick straight hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Long Thick Straight Hair

Maintaining long thick straight hair requires a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for long thick straight hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can lead to breakage.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can keep your long thick straight hair looking healthy and beautiful. Whether you prefer a classic blunt cut or a trendy long bob, there's a haircut style that will work for you.
