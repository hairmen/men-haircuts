Would I Look Good Platinum Blonde

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Platinum blonde is a bold and daring hair color that can transform your look. However, not everyone can pull off this striking shade. If you're considering going platinum blonde, you may be wondering if it would suit you. In this article, we'll explore whether or not you would look good with platinum blonde hair, as well as provide tips on how to achieve and maintain this color.

Description

Platinum blonde is a light, almost white shade of blonde. It is a high-maintenance color that requires regular touch-ups and can be damaging to your hair. However, if done correctly, platinum blonde can be a stunning and head-turning look.

How to Go Platinum Blonde

If you've decided that you want to go platinum blonde, it's important to go to a professional colorist. Bleaching your hair to this extreme shade can be damaging, and it's crucial to have an experienced stylist who knows how to minimize the damage. Your colorist will likely need to bleach your hair multiple times to achieve the desired level of lightness, and they will also need to tone your hair to remove any brassy tones.

Step by Step Guide:

1. Find a professional colorist who has experience with platinum blonde hair.
2. Discuss your desired shade with your colorist.
3. Prepare your hair by minimizing any damage before the appointment.
4. Your colorist will bleach your hair to achieve the desired level of lightness.
5. Your colorist will tone your hair to remove any brassy tones.
6. Maintain your platinum blonde hair with regular touch-ups and proper hair care.

Tips for Maintaining Platinum Blonde Hair

Once you've achieved your platinum blonde hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent further damage to your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Will platinum blonde hair suit me?

Platinum blonde hair can look great on anyone, but it's important to consider your skin tone and eye color. If you have a warm skin tone, platinum blonde may wash you out. Those with cool skin tones tend to look best with platinum blonde hair.

Is platinum blonde high maintenance?

Yes, platinum blonde hair is high maintenance. It requires regular touch-ups and can be damaging to your hair. It's important to go to a professional colorist who knows how to minimize the damage.

Can I achieve platinum blonde hair at home?

We do not recommend attempting to achieve platinum blonde hair at home. Bleaching your hair to this extreme shade can be damaging, and it's crucial to have an experienced stylist who knows how to minimize the damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when it comes to platinum blonde hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent further damage to your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Overall, whether or not you would look good with platinum blonde hair depends on your skin tone and eye color. If you have a cool skin tone, platinum blonde can be a stunning and head-turning look. However, it's important to consider the high maintenance and potential damage that comes with this color. If you're willing to commit to the upkeep, platinum blonde can be a show-stopping look that's sure to turn heads.
