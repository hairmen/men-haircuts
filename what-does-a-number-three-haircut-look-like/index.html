What Does A Number Three Haircut Look Like

Written by Christine Jun 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you thinking of getting a number three haircut but don't know what to expect? Look no further! In this post, we will discuss what a number three haircut looks like, how to get it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

A number three haircut is a popular clipper guard size that leaves hair 3/8 of an inch long. This haircut is perfect for those who want a short, low-maintenance style that still has some length. The number three clipper guard is usually used on the sides and back of the head, leaving the top longer for styling.

How to Get a Number Three Haircut

To get a number three haircut, you will need to visit a barber or hair stylist who has experience with clippers. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the desired length and guard size (in this case, a number three).
  3. Begin at the sides and back of the head, using the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length.
  4. Move to the top of the head and use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length and style.

It is important to communicate clearly with your stylist to ensure you get the haircut you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a number three haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the desired style for reference.
  • Ask your stylist for their opinion on whether a number three will work for your hair type and face shape.
  • Maintain the haircut by getting regular trims every four to six weeks.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or styling cream, to add texture and hold.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number three haircuts:

Is a number three haircut good for thinning hair?

Yes, a number three haircut can be a good option for those with thinning hair because it leaves some length on top to create the illusion of fullness.

Can I get a number three haircut at home?

Yes, you can purchase clippers with a number three guard and attempt the haircut at home. However, it is recommended to visit a professional for best results.

What face shape is best suited for a number three haircut?

A number three haircut can work for most face shapes, but is particularly flattering on those with oval or round faces.

Hair Care

To maintain your number three haircut, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips, you can keep your number three haircut looking fresh and stylish.

Conclusion

A number three haircut is a versatile and low-maintenance style that is perfect for those who want a short length with some texture. By following the steps, tips, and hair care advice in this post, you can confidently rock your number three haircut.
