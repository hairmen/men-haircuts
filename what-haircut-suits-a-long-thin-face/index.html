What Haircut Suits A Long Thin Face

Written by Robby Oct 10, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long, thin face, you may find it difficult to choose a haircut that flatters your features. However, with the right haircut, you can enhance your facial structure and make your face appear fuller. In this article, we will give you tips on what haircut suits a long thin face.

Description

A long thin face is characterized by a long forehead, an elongated chin, and a narrow jawline. This facial structure can make you look older than your actual age and can also make your face appear more angular. However, with the right haircut, you can soften your facial features and create a more balanced look.

How to Choose the Right Haircut?

When choosing a haircut for a long thin face, the key is to create the illusion of width. You should choose haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face and create a fuller look. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

1. Add Layers to Your Hair

Adding layers to your hair is a great way to create the illusion of volume. Layers can add movement and texture to your hair and make it appear fuller. However, be careful not to add too many layers, as this can make your hair look too thin.

2. Get a Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a great option for a long thin face. A chin-length bob can add width to your face and create a more balanced look. You can also add layers to your bob haircut to create more volume.

3. Try a Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can also add width to your face and create a more balanced look. You can try long side-swept bangs that start from the crown of your head and fall diagonally across your forehead. This will make your face appear wider and shorter.

4. Avoid Center Parting

Avoid center parting if you have a long thin face. Center parting can make your face appear even longer and narrower. Instead, try a side parting, which can add volume to the sides of your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right haircut for a long thin face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. A long thin face is characterized by a long forehead, an elongated chin, and a narrow jawline.

2. Research Haircuts

Research different haircuts that flatter a long thin face. Look for haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face and create a fuller look.

3. Consult a Hairstylist

Consult a hairstylist who has experience working with long thin faces. They can recommend haircuts that flatter your facial structure and create a more balanced look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right haircut for a long thin face:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid haircuts that make your face appear longer and narrower
  • Add layers to your hair to create the illusion of volume
  • Try a bob haircut or side-swept bangs
  • Avoid center parting

FAQ

What haircut makes a long thin face look fuller?

A bob haircut or side-swept bangs can make a long thin face look fuller. Adding layers to your hair can also create the illusion of volume.

What haircut should I avoid if I have a long thin face?

Avoid haircuts that make your face appear longer and narrower. Center parting can also make your face appear longer and narrower.

Hair Care

After choosing the right haircut for your long thin face, it is important to take care of your hair. Use products that add volume to your hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a long thin face can seem daunting, but with the right tips and advice, you can enhance your facial structure and create a more balanced look. Remember to consult a hairstylist who has experience working with long thin faces and take care of your hair to maintain its volume and texture.
