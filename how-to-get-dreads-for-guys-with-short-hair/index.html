How To Get Dreads For Guys With Short Hair

If you're a guy with short hair and you're considering getting dreads, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll show you how to get dreads even if you have short hair. We'll cover everything from the preparation to the maintenance of your new hairstyle. Let's get started!

Preparation

Before you start the process of getting dreads, you'll need to make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. It's also a good idea to trim any split ends or damaged hair. This will make the process of creating dreads easier and more effective.

It's important to note that getting dreads is a commitment. You'll need to have patience and be willing to put in the effort to maintain your new hairstyle. With that said, let's move on to the next section.

How to Get Dreads for Guys with Short Hair

Contrary to popular belief, you don't need long hair to get dreads. In fact, short hair can be easier to work with when creating dreads. The key is to use a technique called backcombing.

Backcombing involves teasing small sections of hair towards the scalp, creating knots that will eventually form into dreads. You'll need a small comb or a dread comb to do this effectively. You'll also need a good dread wax or locking gel to help keep your dreads in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get dreads for guys with short hair:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using hair ties or clips.
  3. Take one section of hair and backcomb it towards the scalp using your comb.
  4. Apply a small amount of dread wax or locking gel to the section of hair and continue to backcomb until the section starts to knot up.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section of hair until you have created dreads all over your head.
  6. Use a hair dryer on low heat to help set the dreads in place.
  7. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to help keep your dreads in place.

Tips for Keeping Your Dreads Looking Great

Now that you've got your dreads, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a good dread shampoo.
  • Avoid using regular shampoo or conditioners as they can loosen your dreads.
  • Use a residue-free dread wax or gel to help keep your dreads in place.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat as it can damage your dreads.
  • Be patient. It can take several months for your dreads to fully mature and lock up.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about getting dreads:

Can I get dreads if I have short hair?

Yes, you can get dreads even if you have short hair. Backcombing is a great technique for creating dreads on short hair.

Do I need to shave my head before getting dreads?

No, you don't need to shave your head before getting dreads. In fact, it's better to have some hair to work with when creating dreads.

How long does it take to get dreads?

It can take several months for your dreads to fully mature and lock up. However, the length of time can vary depending on your hair type and the method used to create your dreads.

Hair Care for Dreads

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your dreads:

  • Avoid using combs or brushes on your dreads as they can loosen them.
  • Use a residue-free dread shampoo to wash your dreads.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach on your dreads as they can weaken your hair.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water as it can damage your dreads.

That's it! Now you know how to get dreads for guys with short hair. Remember, getting dreads is a commitment, but with the right care and maintenance, you can enjoy your new hairstyle for years to come.
