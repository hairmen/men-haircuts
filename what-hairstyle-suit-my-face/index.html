What Hairstyle Suit My Face

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles just to end up looking unflattering? The truth is, not all hairstyles suit every face shape. It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features and enhances your overall appearance. In this article, we'll be discussing how to determine what hairstyle suits your face, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what hairstyles suit your face, it's essential to determine your face shape. The four common face shapes are oval, round, heart, and square. One way to determine your face shape is by pulling your hair back and using a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Oval Face

An oval face is characterized by a forehead that's slightly wider than the chin, and prominent cheekbones. Oval faces can pull off various hairstyles, including short, medium, and long haircuts.

Round Face

If you have a round face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all the same width. Hairstyles that add height on the top and length on the sides, such as long layers, bobs, and pixie cuts, are great for round faces.

Heart Face

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and cheekbones, and a pointed chin. Hairstyles that add volume at the bottom, such as bob, lob, and long layers, are ideal for heart-shaped faces.

Square Face

Square faces have a wide forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all the same width. Softening the angles of the face with wavy or curly hairstyles, such as long layers and side-swept bangs, can complement square-shaped faces.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle. Follow these step-by-step instructions to find a hairstyle that suits your face.

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Consider your hair type, texture, and length.

3. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape.

4. Visit a hairstylist and ask for their opinion.

5. Choose a hairstyle that you love and feel comfortable with.

6. Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

- Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair.

- Choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors and styles.

- Look for inspiration from celebrities and online hair trends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A: Hairstyles that add height on the top and length on the sides, such as long layers, bobs, and pixie cuts, are great for round faces.

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off short hairstyles such as bobs and lobs.

Q: What hairstyle suits a square face?

A: Softening the angles of the face with wavy or curly hairstyles, such as long layers and side-swept bangs, can complement square-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

- Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

- Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to have fun and experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one.
