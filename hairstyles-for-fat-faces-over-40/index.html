As we age, our faces change, and sometimes they may become rounder or fuller. For women with fat faces over 40, it can be challenging to find hairstyles that flatter their face shape. In this article, we will cover tips, ideas, and hair care for hairstyles for fat faces over 40.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is essential, regardless of your age. However, it becomes even more critical as you age, and your face shape changes. Here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape.

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another crucial factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that require a lot of volume. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much bulk.

3. Look for Inspiration

Once you have determined your face shape and hair texture, it's time to look for inspiration. Look for hairstyles that flatter your face shape and hair texture. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by consulting with a hairstylist.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape let's go through a step-by-step guide to styling your hair.

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in styling your hair is to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause damage.

3. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help it hold its shape. Use a product that is suitable for your hair type and the hairstyle you have chosen.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a hairdryer and a round brush. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

5. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it according to the hairstyle you have chosen. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or straighten your hair.

Hairstyle Ideas for Fat Faces Over 40

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle and how to style your hair let's look at some hairstyle ideas for fat faces over 40.

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for women with fat faces over 40. A bob haircut can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

2. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is another great option for women with fat faces over 40. Layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair, which can help to balance out your face shape.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance hairstyle that can be flattering for women with fat faces over 40. A pixie cut can help to elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you have chosen the right hairstyle and know how to style your hair let's go through some tips for maintaining your hair.

1. Use a Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

1. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Women with fat faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to their cheeks, such as blunt bangs or a full bob.

2. Can I wear long hair if I have a fat face?

Yes, you can wear long hair if you have a fat face. Just be sure to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

3. How can I make my face look slimmer?

You can make your face look slimmer by choosing a hairstyle that elongates your face, such as a bob or layered haircut.


In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for fat faces over 40 is all about finding a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Use the tips and ideas in this article, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
