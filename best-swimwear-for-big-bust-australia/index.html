How to

When shopping for swimwear, there are a few things to consider to ensure you find the best fit for your big bust. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Look for Underwire

Underwire is an excellent feature to look for in swimwear as it provides additional support for your bust. Underwire can help lift your bust and provide a more flattering shape.

2. Opt for Wide Straps

Wide straps are a great option for big busts as they distribute the weight of your bust more evenly. This can help prevent discomfort and shoulder pain.

3. Consider a Halter Neck

A halter neck is another great option for big busts as it provides additional support and lift. Halter necks can also help create a more flattering shape for your bust.

Hair Care

When spending time at the beach or pool, it's essential to take care of your hair. Saltwater and chlorine can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to take steps to protect it. Here are some tips:

1. Wet Your Hair

Before getting into the water, wet your hair with fresh water. This will help prevent your hair from absorbing too much saltwater or chlorine.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help protect your hair from the damaging effects of saltwater and chlorine. Apply the conditioner before getting in the water.

3. Rinse Your Hair After Swimming

After swimming, rinse your hair with fresh water to remove any saltwater or chlorine.
