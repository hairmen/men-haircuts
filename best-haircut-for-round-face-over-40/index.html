Best Haircut For Round Face Over 40

Written by Oliver Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our face shape may change, and it's important to find the right hairstyle that complements our features. If you have a round face and are over 40, it can be challenging to find the best haircut that accentuates your facial structure. In this article, we'll explore the best haircut for round face over 40, how to achieve it, and some tips and FAQ to help you maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have a round face, you want to create an illusion of length and slimness. The best haircut for a round face over 40 is one that adds height and volume to the crown while elongating the face. Layered haircuts are perfect for round faces as they create the appearance of length and volume without adding bulk to the sides of the face. Additionally, bangs that are swept to the side can help to elongate the face and draw attention to the eyes.

How to

The best way to achieve the perfect haircut for a round face over 40 is to work with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the right length and style that complements your facial structure. Before your appointment, do some research and bring pictures of hairstyles you like. This will help your stylist understand your preferences and guide them in creating a look that works for you.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting the best haircut for a round face over 40:

  1. Find a professional stylist who has experience working with round faces and aging hair.
  2. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  3. Discuss your preferences and hair type with your stylist.
  4. Let your stylist know if you want any layers or bangs added to your haircut.
  5. Be open to your stylist's suggestions and recommendations.
  6. After your haircut, maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care.

Tips

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and complements your features.
  • Consider adding layers or bangs to your haircut to create the illusion of length and volume.
  • Use volumizing products to add height and volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add bulk to the sides of your face as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face over 40?

The best haircut for a round face over 40 is one that adds height and volume to the crown while elongating the face. Layered haircuts and side-swept bangs are perfect for round faces as they create the appearance of length and volume without adding bulk to the sides of the face.

Should I add layers or bangs to my haircut?

Add layers or bangs to your haircut to create the illusion of length and volume. Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, while bangs can draw attention to your eyes and elongate your face.

How often should I get a trim?

Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Hair Care

Maintain your new haircut with proper hair care. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add height and volume to the crown of your head. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create lift and movement. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and definition to your hair. Avoid using too much product or over-styling your hair as this can weigh it down and make it look flat.

With these tips and suggestions, you can find the perfect haircut for your round face over 40. Embrace your features and find a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
