Does A Side Part Or Middle Part Look Better

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Deciding between a side part or middle part for your hairstyle can be a tough decision. In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of both styles to help you determine which one might look better on you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Both side parts and middle parts have been popular hairstyles for decades, and they continue to be popular today. A side part involves combing your hair to one side, while a middle part involves splitting your hair down the middle. Both styles can be worn with a variety of hair lengths and types, but they can create different looks depending on your face shape and hair texture.

How to

To achieve a side part, start by washing and drying your hair. Then, use a comb to create a part on one side of your head. Comb your hair to the opposite side of the part, and use a styling product, such as gel or pomade, to keep your hair in place. To achieve a middle part, start by washing and drying your hair. Then, use a comb to create a straight part down the middle of your head. Comb your hair to each side of the part, and use a styling product to keep your hair in place.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving a side part or middle part:

Side Part

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Use a comb to create a part on one side of your head
  3. Comb your hair to the opposite side of the part
  4. Use a styling product, such as gel or pomade, to keep your hair in place

Middle Part

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Use a comb to create a straight part down the middle of your head
  3. Comb your hair to each side of the part
  4. Use a styling product to keep your hair in place

Tips

Here are some tips for choosing between a side part or middle part:

  • If you have a round face, a middle part can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer
  • If you have a square or angular face, a side part can soften your features
  • If you have thick or curly hair, a middle part can help distribute the weight of your hair evenly
  • If you have fine or straight hair, a side part can help create the illusion of volume

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side parts and middle parts:

Which part is better for my face shape?

The best part for your face shape depends on your individual features. Generally, a middle part can help elongate a round face, while a side part can soften a square or angular face.

Can I switch between a side part and middle part?

Yes, you can switch between a side part and middle part as often as you like. Experiment with different parts to find the one that looks best on you.

Hair Care

Regardless of which part you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF

So, does a side part or middle part look better? The answer is that it depends on your individual features and hair texture. Try both styles to see which one suits you best, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
