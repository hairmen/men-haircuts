How To Get Rid Of Brassy Gold Hair Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of your hair looking brassy and gold instead of the beautiful blonde or light color you were going for? Don't worry! In this article, we'll go over how to get rid of brassy gold hair, step-by-step, and provide tips and tricks to help keep your hair looking its best.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Gold Hair

Getting rid of brassy gold hair involves neutralizing the yellow or orange tones that have formed in your hair. There are a few different methods you can try to achieve this:

Toning Shampoo or Conditioner

A toning shampoo or conditioner is a great option for getting rid of brassy gold hair. These products are designed to neutralize yellow or orange tones, leaving your hair looking more cool-toned. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair color, whether that's blonde, silver, or another shade.

Hair Toner

A hair toner is a more intensive way to get rid of brassy gold hair. Toners are often used in salons but can also be done at home with a kit. A toner will help neutralize the yellow or orange tones and can also add shine to your hair.

Bleach Bath

If your hair is severely brassy and toning products aren't doing the trick, a bleach bath may be necessary. This involves mixing bleach with shampoo and water to create a gentle, diluted bleach solution. This should only be done if you're comfortable working with bleach and have experience with it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for getting rid of brassy gold hair:

  1. Choose the method that's right for you - toning shampoo or conditioner, hair toner, or bleach bath.
  2. Follow the instructions on the product carefully.
  3. Start with clean, dry hair.
  4. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas.
  5. Leave the product on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.
  7. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to help restore moisture to your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your brassy gold hair removal:

  • Use a purple shampoo or conditioner in between treatments to help maintain your hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as much as possible, as heat can cause your hair color to fade and become brassy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Consider going to a professional hair stylist for a more intensive treatment if you've tried everything else and still can't get rid of the brassiness.

FAQs

Can I use toning products on colored hair?

Yes, toning products can be used on colored hair. Just make sure to choose a product that's specifically designed for your hair color and follow the instructions carefully.

How often should I use toning products?

This will depend on your hair and the toning product you're using. Some toning shampoos and conditioners can be used daily, while others should only be used once or twice a week.

Can I use a toner at home?

Yes, you can use a toner at home with a kit. Just make sure to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the toner to your entire head of hair.

Hair Care Regimen

To keep your hair looking its best after getting rid of brassy gold tones, it's important to have a good hair care regimen. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of brassy gold hair and keep your hair looking its best.
