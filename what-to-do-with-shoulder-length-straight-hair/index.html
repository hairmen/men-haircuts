What To Do With Shoulder Length Straight Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have shoulder-length straight hair, you might find it difficult to style it in different ways. However, there are plenty of hairstyles and tricks that you can try to make your hair look chic and stylish. In this post, we will share some tips and tricks on what to do with shoulder length straight hair.

Description

Shoulder-length straight hair can be challenging to style, but it also offers versatility. You can try different hairstyles and haircuts that suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. From sleek bobs to messy waves, there are plenty of options to explore.

How-To

Here are some tips on what to do with shoulder length straight hair:

1. Add layers

Adding layers to your hair can add volume and movement to your straight hair. You can opt for long layers or choppy layers, depending on how much volume you want. Layers also make it easier to style your hair in different ways.

2. Experiment with bangs

Bangs can transform your look and add a touch of personality to your hair. You can try blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or wispy bangs, depending on your face shape and hair texture. Bangs also help to frame your face and add dimension to your hair.

3. Go for a bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on shoulder-length straight hair. You can opt for a sleek bob or a messy bob, depending on your mood and occasion. Bobs are easy to style and maintain, which makes them a popular choice for busy women.

4. Try a ponytail

A ponytail is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that you can do in minutes. You can choose a high ponytail, a low ponytail, or a side ponytail, depending on your preference. You can also add accessories like scrunchies or hair clips to make your ponytail look more interesting.

Step-By-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your shoulder-length straight hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product like mousse or serum to your hair to add volume and shine. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a cool setting to set the style.

4. Style your hair

You can style your hair in different ways depending on your preference. You can try a sleek bob, messy waves, or a ponytail. Use a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder-length straight hair:

1. Use the right products

Use products that suit your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

2. Protect your hair

Use heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools like flat irons or curling irons. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged and keep it healthy.

3. Get regular haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will also help to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

FAQ

Q. Can I curl my shoulder-length straight hair?

A. Yes, you can use a curling iron or a straightening iron to curl your hair. However, make sure to use heat protectant spray and not to apply too much heat to your hair.

Q. Can I wear my hair in a bun?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair in a bun. You can try a high bun, a low bun, or a messy bun, depending on your preference.

Q. How can I add volume to my shoulder-length straight hair?

A. You can add volume to your hair by adding layers, using volumizing products, and blow-drying your hair using a round brush.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your shoulder-length straight hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Brush your hair gently

Brush your hair gently using a wide-toothed comb or a brush. Avoid pulling or tugging your hair as it can cause breakage and damage.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum that contains SPF. This will prevent your hair from getting dry and damaged.

In conclusion, shoulder-length straight hair offers versatility and plenty of styling options. By adding layers, experimenting with bangs, going for a bob, or trying a ponytail, you can elevate your look and make your hair look chic and stylish. Just remember to use the right products, protect your hair, and get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
