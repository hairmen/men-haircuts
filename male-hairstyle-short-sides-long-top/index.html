If you're looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle, then the male hairstyle short sides long top may be the perfect choice for you. What is the Male Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top?

The male hairstyle short sides long top is a popular haircut that features short hair on the sides and long hair on the top. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways, making it a favorite among men of all ages. The length of the top hair can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference.

How to Achieve the Male Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

To achieve the male hairstyle short sides long top, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to give you the right cut and shape that will suit your face shape and hair type. You can also show them a picture of the hairstyle to give them a better idea of what you want.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Male Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

Step 1: Decide on the length of the top hair. Step 2: Visit a professional hairstylist. Step 3: Show them a picture of the hairstyle you want. Step 4: Let them cut the sides short while leaving the top hair long. Step 5: Style the top hair according to your preference.

Tips for Maintaining the Male Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

- Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. - Use a hair styling product to keep the top hair in place. - Get regular trims to keep the sides short. - Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.

FAQ's about the Male Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

How Often Should You Get a Haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the hairstyle.

How Do You Style the Long Top?

You can style the long top in various ways, such as slicking it back, parting it to the side, or leaving it messy for a more casual look.

Can You Get This Hairstyle if You Have Curly Hair?

Yes, you can get this hairstyle even if you have curly hair. However, it may require more styling products and effort to keep the top hair in place.

How Do You Keep the Sides Short?

Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can help keep the sides short. You can also use clippers to trim the sides at home.

Hair Care Tips for the Male Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on the top hair. - Avoid using too much hair product, as it can make the hair greasy and weigh it down. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy and strong. In conclusion, the male hairstyle short sides long top is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that is still going strong in 2023. With the right cut, styling, and maintenance, you can rock this hairstyle and turn heads wherever you go. So, go ahead and try it out for yourself!
