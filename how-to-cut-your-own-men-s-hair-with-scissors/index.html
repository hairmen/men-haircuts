How To Cut Your Own Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're tired of paying for expensive haircuts or simply prefer the convenience of cutting your own hair at home, then learning how to cut your own men's hair with scissors is a skill worth mastering. Not only will it save you time and money, but it can also be a fun and satisfying experience. In this guide, we'll walk you through the entire process, from preparation to clean-up.

How to Cut Your Own Men's Hair with Scissors

The first thing you'll need to do is gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have clippers, you can use them to trim the sides and back of your hair, but they are not necessary.

Next, you'll want to choose a good location to cut your hair. We recommend doing it in a well-lit room with a large mirror. You'll also want to cover the floor with a towel or sheet to catch any hair clippings.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need, it's time to start cutting.

  1. Begin by combing your hair straight down over your forehead.
  2. Using your scissors, make small, vertical cuts into your hair, starting at the hairline and moving backwards. Be sure to cut small sections at a time and avoid cutting too much at once.
  3. Once you've finished the front section, move on to the sides of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair up and outwards, and then cut the hair at an angle.
  4. For the back of your head, it's best to use a mirror to ensure that you're cutting the hair evenly. Use the comb to lift the hair up and then cut it straight across. Be sure to check the mirror frequently to make sure you're cutting the hair evenly.
  5. Once you've finished cutting your hair, use the comb to brush away any loose hairs and then style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Here are a few tips to keep in mind to help ensure a successful haircut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to ensure even cutting in the back.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own men's hair with scissors:

  • Can I use clippers instead of scissors?
  • Yes, clippers can be used to trim the sides and back of your hair.
  • Do I need any special scissors?
  • No, any sharp scissors will do.
  • How often should I cut my hair?
  • This depends on personal preference, but most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a moisturizing hair product to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim any split ends as needed.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Learning how to cut your own men's hair with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a great haircut without ever leaving your home.
