How Can I Make My Hair Curly Without A Perm

Written by William Sep 25, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re looking for a way to add some curls to your hair without using harsh chemicals or heat tools, you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll be discussing how you can make your hair curly without a perm, using natural methods that are safe and easy to try at home.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that alters the structure of your hair, making it curly or wavy. While perms can give you the curls you’ve always wanted, they can also damage your hair and scalp. If you’re looking for a more natural way to curl your hair, there are several methods you can try.

How to Make Your Hair Curly Without a Perm

Here are some natural ways you can make your hair curly without a perm:

1. Braid Your Hair

One of the easiest ways to get curly hair without a perm is to braid your hair. Simply divide your hair into sections, braid each section, and leave it in overnight. In the morning, take the braids out and you’ll have beautiful, natural-looking curls!

2. Twist Your Hair

Another way to get curly hair without a perm is to twist your hair. Divide your hair into sections, twist each section, and leave it in overnight. In the morning, take the twists out and you’ll have soft, bouncy curls.

3. Use Rollers

You can also use rollers to make your hair curly without a perm. Choose the size of rollers you want (smaller rollers will give you tighter curls), and roll your hair onto the rollers. Leave the rollers in for a few hours, or overnight, and then take them out for beautiful curls!

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curly without a perm:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is damp, it won’t hold the curls as well.

Step 2:

Divide your hair into sections, depending on how big or small you want your curls to be.

Step 3:

If you’re braiding your hair, braid each section and secure the end with a hair tie. If you’re twisting your hair, twist each section and secure the end with a hair tie.

Step 4:

If you’re using rollers, roll each section of hair onto the rollers and secure them in place.

Step 5:

Leave your hair in braids, twists, or rollers overnight.

Step 6:

In the morning, take out the braids, twists, or rollers and gently finger-comb your hair to loosen the curls.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curly Without a Perm

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when making your hair curly without a perm:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you’re using heat tools to curl your hair (like a curling iron or straightener), make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Don’t Overdo It

Be careful not to overdo it when trying to make your hair curly. Overusing heat tools or styling products can damage your hair and make it brittle.

3. Experiment with Different Methods

Not every method will work for every hair type, so experiment with different methods to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type and the method you use. Braids and twists will generally last longer than curls created with rollers or heat tools.

Q: Can I make my hair curly without any products?

A: Yes, you can make your hair curly without using any products. Braiding, twisting, or using rollers are all natural ways to curl your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Whether you have naturally curly hair or you’ve created curls using one of the methods above, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it’s important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls looking their best.

2. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day, or every few days, to keep it healthy.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your curls moisturized and prevent frizz. Apply it to damp hair and let it air dry for the best results.

4. Avoid Heat Tools

Heat tools can damage your hair and make it brittle, so try to avoid them as much as possible. If you must use a heat tool, make sure to use a heat protectant and don’t use it every day.

With these tips and methods, you can make your hair curly without a perm and keep it healthy and beautiful. Try out different methods and find what works best for you!
