How to Cut a Deathhawk: A Step-by-Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

If you're looking for something edgy and unique, a deathhawk might be perfect for you. This hairstyle combines the classic mohawk style with longer hair on the sides, creating a dramatic, eye-catching look. In this step-by-step guide, we'll teach you how to cut a deathhawk in just a few easy steps. Read on for our tips on how to achieve this daring hairstyle.

Description

A deathhawk is a hairstyle that combines elements of a mohawk with longer hair on the sides. The hair on top is typically styled straight up, while the hair on the sides can be styled in a variety of ways, such as spiked or slicked back. This style is popular among punk rockers and those who want a bold, edgy look.

How to Cut a Deathhawk

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary supplies on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair scissors, a comb, hair clippers, and hair ties.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Starting at the front of the head, use a comb to section off the hair that will become the mohawk. Use hair ties to hold the sections in place.

Step 3: Trim the Sides

Using hair clippers, trim the hair on the sides to the desired length. If you want a more dramatic look, leave the sides shorter. If you prefer a softer look, leave the sides longer.

Step 4: Cut the Mohawk

Using sharp hair scissors, cut the hair on top to the desired length. Start at the front of the head and work your way back, cutting the hair in small sections. Make sure to leave enough length so that the hair can be styled straight up.

Step 5: Style the Hair

With the hair cut, it's time to style it into a deathhawk. Use a strong-hold hair gel or wax to style the hair on top straight up. For the sides, you can either spike the hair up or slick it back, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Tips

- Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut.

- Start with longer hair and gradually cut it shorter until you achieve the desired length.

- If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQ

Q: How long does a deathhawk last?

A: A deathhawk can last for several weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

Q: Can I dye my deathhawk?

A: Yes, you can dye your deathhawk in any color you like. Just make sure to use a hair dye that is safe for your hair type and follow the instructions carefully.

Hair Care

To keep your deathhawk looking its best, it's important to take proper care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips, you'll be able to rock a deathhawk with confidence. Remember, this style is all about being bold and daring, so don't be afraid to experiment and make it your own. Happy cutting!
