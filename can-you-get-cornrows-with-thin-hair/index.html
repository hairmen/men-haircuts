Can You Get Cornrows With Thin Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 28, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to protective hairstyles, cornrows are a popular choice for many women. However, if you have thin hair, you may be wondering if you can even get cornrows. The answer is yes, you can get cornrows with thin hair. In this article, we’ll discuss how to achieve this style and offer some tips for maintaining it.

Description

Cornrows are a traditional African hairstyle that involves braiding the hair close to the scalp. The braids are usually straight and can be arranged in various patterns. Cornrows are often worn as a protective style to help promote hair growth and prevent breakage. They can be worn for several weeks at a time, making them a low-maintenance hairstyle.

How to

If you have thin hair, getting cornrows may require a bit more effort than if you have thicker hair. Here are the steps to getting cornrows with thin hair:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before getting cornrows, make sure your hair is clean and free of any tangles. If your hair is too oily, it may be difficult for the stylist to braid it. You can wash your hair the day before or the morning of your appointment.

Step 2: Moisturize your hair

Thin hair tends to be more fragile and prone to breakage, so it’s important to keep it moisturized. Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair before getting it braided. This will help keep your hair healthy and reduce the risk of damage.

Step 3: Choose the right stylist

Not all stylists are created equal when it comes to braiding. Look for a stylist who has experience working with thin hair and can create cornrows that are not too tight. Tight braids can cause damage and breakage, so it’s important to find a stylist who can braid your hair gently.

Step 4: Communicate with your stylist

When getting your hair braided, make sure to communicate with your stylist about your hair type and any concerns you may have. Let them know if your hair is thin or fragile, and ask them to braid your hair gently. If the braids are too tight, don’t be afraid to speak up and ask them to loosen them.

Step 5: Maintain your cornrows

Once you have your cornrows, it’s important to maintain them properly. Keep your scalp clean and moisturized, and avoid pulling or tugging on the braids. You can also use a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for getting cornrows with thin hair:

  • Don’t get your hair braided too tightly
  • Moisturize your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Avoid wearing your cornrows for too long

FAQ

Can you get cornrows with short hair?

Yes, you can get cornrows with short hair. However, the length of your hair will affect the size of the braids. If your hair is very short, the braids may be small and close together.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last for several weeks, depending on how well you maintain them. It’s important to keep your scalp clean and moisturized, and avoid pulling or tugging on the braids.

Can cornrows cause hair loss?

Tight braids can cause hair loss and breakage, so it’s important to find a stylist who can braid your hair gently. It’s also important to take breaks between wearing cornrows to allow your hair to rest and recover.

Hair Care

Getting cornrows with thin hair requires a bit more effort than if you have thicker hair. It’s important to keep your hair moisturized and avoid tight braids that can cause damage. With proper maintenance, cornrows can be a great protective style for thin hair.
