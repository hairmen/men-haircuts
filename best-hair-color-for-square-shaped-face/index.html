Best Hair Color For Square Shaped Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square shaped face, you may be wondering what hair color will suit you best. While there are many factors to consider when choosing a hair color, the shape of your face is an important one. In this article, we'll explore the best hair color for square shaped face and provide you with tips and tricks to make your hair look its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair Color for Square Shaped Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

Description

A square shaped face is characterized by strong, angular features such as a prominent jawline and forehead. It is important to choose a hair color that will soften these features and enhance your natural beauty. The best hair color for square shaped face is one that will add depth and dimension to your hair without overwhelming your face.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color for Square Shaped Face

When choosing a hair color for square shaped face, consider your skin tone and eye color. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm shades such as caramel, honey, or golden blonde. If you have cool undertones, go for cool shades such as ash blonde, platinum, or black. If you have neutral undertones, you can experiment with both warm and cool shades.

Another factor to consider is the level of contrast between your hair color and skin tone. If you have a light skin tone, choose a hair color that is not too dark or too light. If you have a dark skin tone, opt for a hair color that is rich and vibrant.

Lastly, consider your lifestyle and personal style. If you have a bold and edgy style, you may want to choose a hair color that reflects that. If you have a more classic and timeless style, opt for a hair color that is elegant and sophisticated.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

Before you color your hair, it is important to prepare it properly. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Then, apply a deep conditioning treatment to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Next, choose a hair color that suits your face shape and skin tone. Mix the hair color according to the instructions on the box. Apply the hair color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the hair color on for the recommended time, then rinse it out with cool water.

After rinsing out the hair color, apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water. Pat your hair dry with a towel and style it as desired.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Hair Color

To maintain your hair color, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner. Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause the color to fade faster. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight, as this can cause the color to fade. If you are going to be outside for an extended period of time, wear a hat or use a hair care product with SPF protection.

If you want to touch up your roots, use a root touch-up kit that matches your hair color. Apply the root touch-up according to the instructions on the box, then rinse it out with cool water.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color suits a square shaped face?

The best hair color for square shaped face is one that will add depth and dimension to your hair without overwhelming your face. Consider your skin tone, eye color, and personal style when choosing a hair color.

What hair color makes a square face look slimmer?

Highlights and lowlights can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Opt for a hair color that has a mix of light and dark shades to add dimension to your hair.

Can you dye your hair if you have a square shaped face?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a square shaped face. Just be sure to choose a hair color that suits your face shape and skin tone.

Hair Care for Square Shaped Face

Aside from choosing the right hair color, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong. Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, you can choose the best hair color for square shaped face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
