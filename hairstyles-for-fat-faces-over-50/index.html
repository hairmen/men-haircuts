How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, your face shape is the most important factor to consider. For those with a round or chubby face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and slimness. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that are longer than chin-length, as shorter cuts can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider layers, which can add movement and volume to your hair, creating a more youthful look.
  • A side-swept fringe can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.
