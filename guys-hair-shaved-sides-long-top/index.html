Guys Hair Shaved Sides Long Top A Trendy Hairstyle For Men

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy hairstyle that can make you stand out? Guys hair shaved sides long top might be the perfect hairstyle for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity among men in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. With its edgy and versatile look, guys hair shaved sides long top is perfect for any occasion, whether it's casual or formal.

Description

The guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle is characterized by short or shaved sides and long hair on top. The contrast between the shaved sides and the long top creates a bold and edgy look that can be styled in various ways. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn in different lengths, textures, and colors, depending on your preference and face shape.

How to

If you want to achieve the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle, you can follow these easy steps:

  1. Decide on the length and style of the long top. You can choose to have it straight, wavy, curly, or textured.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist and tell them what you want. They will shave or trim the sides and leave the top long.
  3. Style your hair using your preferred hair products. You can slick it back, create a pompadour, or leave it messy.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose the Length and Style of the Long Top

Decide on the length and style of the long top that you want. You can choose to have it straight, wavy, curly, or textured. Consider your face shape and hair type when deciding on the length and style.

Step 2: Visit a Professional Hairstylist

Visit a professional hairstylist and tell them what you want. They will shave or trim the sides and leave the top long. Make sure to communicate clearly with your hairstylist to achieve the desired look.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your preferred hair products. You can slick it back, create a pompadour, or leave it messy. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips

If you want to maintain the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle, here are some tips for you:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep the sides trimmed or shaved.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and style.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and trendy.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing the length and style of the long top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys hair shaved sides long top:

Is the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle can be adapted to suit different face shapes. You can adjust the length and style of the long top to complement your face shape.

Can I style my hair differently every day with the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle?

Yes, the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways. You can slick it back, create a pompadour, or leave it messy, depending on your mood and preference.

What hair products should I use for the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle?

Use hair products that suit your hair type and style. You can use pomade, wax, gel, or hairspray to style your hair. Make sure to choose high-quality products to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

If you want to keep your hair healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips for you:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or coloring your hair too often.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the guys hair shaved sides long top hairstyle with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle is sure to make you stand out from the crowd.
