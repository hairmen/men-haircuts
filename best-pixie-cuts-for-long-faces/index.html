Best Pixie Cuts For Long Faces

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face and want to try out a pixie cut, you might be wondering which styles will suit you best. Pixie cuts are a great way to add some edge and personality to your look, but not all cuts are created equal. In this article, we'll explore the best pixie cuts for long faces and offer some tips on how to care for your new style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by high foreheads, elongated chins, and prominent cheekbones. The best pixie cuts for this face shape will help balance out the proportions and add some width to the face. Some popular styles include:

Side-Swept Pixie

The side-swept pixie is a classic style that works well for long faces. This cut features longer bangs that are swept to one side, creating the illusion of width across the forehead. The rest of the hair is cut short, with layers added to add volume and texture.

Textured Pixie

The textured pixie is a great option for those who want a more modern, edgy look. This style features choppy, layered hair that is styled with product to create a messy, tousled effect. The textured layers add volume to the hair and help balance out the proportions of a long face.

Asymmetrical Pixie

The asymmetrical pixie is a bold, daring style that can work well for long faces. This cut features one side of the hair cut shorter than the other, with longer layers on the opposite side. The asymmetrical shape helps balance out the proportions of a long face and adds some interest to the style.

How to

If you're thinking of getting a pixie cut for your long face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consult with your stylist to find the best style for your face shape and hair texture
  • Consider adding layers to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Invest in quality styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to help you create your desired look
  • Experiment with different partings and styling techniques to find the most flattering look for your face shape

Step by Step

If you're interested in styling your own pixie cut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair
  2. Apply a dime-sized amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots and tips
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling wand to add texture and shape to your hair
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your pixie cut:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous

FAQ

What if I don't like my pixie cut?

If you're not happy with your pixie cut, don't worry - hair grows back! Talk to your stylist about options for growing out your hair or experimenting with different styles.

Can I still style my pixie cut if my hair is thin or fine?

Absolutely! Pixie cuts can work well for those with thin or fine hair, as long as the right styling products are used to add volume and texture.

Hair Care

When caring for your pixie cut, it's important to use products that will keep your hair healthy and nourished. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for your hair type, and use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated. It's also a good idea to avoid using hot styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Now that you know the best pixie cuts for long faces, it's time to book your appointment with your stylist and try out a new look!
