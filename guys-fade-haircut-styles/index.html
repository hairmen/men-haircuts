Guys Fade Haircut Styles A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then you should definitely try a fade. Fades are one of the most popular hairstyles for men, and they come in many different styles. In this article, we will guide you through the different types of guys fade haircut styles, how to achieve them, tips, and hair care. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Types of Guys Fade Haircut Styles
  • How to Achieve a Guys Fade Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining a Guys Fade Haircut

Types of Guys Fade Haircut Styles

There are several types of guys fade haircut styles, and each one has its own unique look. Here are some of the most popular types:

Low Fade

The low fade is a subtle hairstyle that gradually fades from the hairline to the neck. This is a popular choice for guys who want a clean and professional look. It works well with both short and long hair.

High Fade

The high fade is a bold and edgy hairstyle that fades from the hairline to the top of the head. This style is perfect for guys who want a dramatic look. It works well with short hair and looks great with a textured top.

Mid Fade

The mid fade is a versatile hairstyle that fades from the hairline to the middle of the head. This style works well with both short and long hair and is great for guys who want a modern and stylish look.

Bald Fade

The bald fade is a dramatic hairstyle that fades from the hairline to the skin. This style works well for guys who want a bold and edgy look. It looks great with short hair and can be paired with a textured top.

Temple Fade

The temple fade is a subtle hairstyle that fades from the hairline to the temples. This style works well with short hair and is great for guys who want a clean and polished look.

How to Achieve a Guys Fade Haircut

Now that you know the different types of guys fade haircut styles, let's talk about how to achieve them. Here are the steps:

  1. Choose the type of fade you want.
  2. Decide on the length of your hair on top.
  3. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head.
  4. Blend the fade by gradually changing the length of the clippers.
  5. Use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining a Guys Fade Haircut

Maintaining a guys fade haircut requires some effort, but it's worth it to keep your hair looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your fade looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to keep the look in place.
  • If you have a textured top, use a sea salt spray to add volume and texture.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat, as this can damage your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about guys fade haircut styles:

How often should I get a fade?

You should get a fade every 2-4 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Can I get a fade with long hair?

Yes, you can get a fade with long hair. However, you will need to keep the top of your hair longer than the sides and back.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Hair Care for Guys Fade Haircut Styles

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons.

With these tips in mind, you can rock your guys fade haircut style with confidence and ease. Whether you prefer a low fade, high fade, mid fade, bald fade, or temple fade, there's a style that's perfect for you. So, go ahead and try a fade today!
