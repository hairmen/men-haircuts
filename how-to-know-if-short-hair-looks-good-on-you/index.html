How To Know If Short Hair Looks Good On You

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair is becoming increasingly popular among women, especially since it is a more convenient and low-maintenance hairstyle. However, before you chop your locks off, it is essential to determine whether short hair will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to know if short hair looks good on you and provide you with tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Determine Your Hair Texture
  • Step 3: Assess Your Personal Style
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining whether short hair looks good on you. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart, and diamond.

If you have a round face, you may want to avoid short hair since it can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a longer bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers. On the other hand, if you have an oval face, you can experiment with different short hairstyles since this face shape is versatile and can pull off most styles.

If you have a square face, you may want to soften your features with a textured pixie cut or a side-swept bob. If you have a heart-shaped face, you can balance out your features with a chin-length bob or a layered shag cut. Lastly, if you have a diamond-shaped face, you can create the illusion of a fuller face with a textured pixie cut or a short bob.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another crucial factor to consider when determining whether short hair looks good on you. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid very short haircuts since they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for a layered bob or a long pixie cut with textured layers.

If you have thick hair, you can pull off most short hairstyles, but you may want to opt for a layered cut to avoid a boxy look. If you have curly hair, you may want to avoid very short haircuts since they can make your curls look frizzy and unmanageable. Instead, opt for a shoulder-length cut with layers or a curly bob.

Step 3: Assess Your Personal Style

Finally, you need to assess your personal style to determine whether short hair looks good on you. If you prefer a more feminine and sophisticated look, you may want to opt for a chin-length bob or a textured pixie cut with side-swept bangs. If you prefer a more edgy and daring look, you can experiment with a spiky pixie cut or a shaved undercut.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Maintaining short hair can be easy and low-maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Use a lightweight, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Style your hair with a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or a pomade, to add texture and volume.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Get creative with accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to add some flair to your short hair.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can make you look more mature and sophisticated, but it will not necessarily make you look older. The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape and personal style.

Q: Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

A: Yes, you can style your short hair for special occasions with the help of hair accessories, such as hair pins and headbands, or by using styling products, such as hair wax or hairspray. You can also experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls, to create a unique and stylish look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair is low-maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair less frequently to prevent it from drying out.
  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb to avoid breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know how to determine whether short hair looks good on you, it is time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish.
