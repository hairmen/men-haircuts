Definition

A number 3 guard in MM refers to a specific attachment for a hair clipper. The number 3 guard is 3/8 of an inch or 9.5 millimeters long, which means that it cuts hair to a length of 9.5 millimeters when attached to the clipper. It is one of the most commonly used clipper guards and is ideal for creating buzz cuts, crew cuts, and other short hairstyles.

How to Use a Number 3 Guard in MM

Using a number 3 guard in MM is a simple and straightforward process. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Attach the number 3 guard to your hair clipper. Make sure that it is securely in place.
  2. Start at the back of your head and move the clipper up towards the top of your head. Use a slow, steady motion to ensure an even cut.
  3. Repeat this process on the sides of your head.
  4. Move to the top of your head and cut the hair in the same direction as the growth pattern.
  5. Blend the sides and top of your hair with a higher guard or without a guard to create a seamless look.

Tips for Using a Number 3 Guard in MM

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when using a number 3 guard in MM:

  • Use a comb to help guide the clipper and ensure an even cut.
  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Don't push too hard with the clipper, as this can result in an uneven cut or irritation to the scalp.
  • Clean and oil your clipper regularly to ensure optimal performance.

FAQs

Q: What hairstyles can I create with a number 3 guard in MM?

A: A number 3 guard in MM is ideal for creating buzz cuts, crew cuts, and other short hairstyles.

Q: Can I use a number 3 guard in MM on any type of hair?

A: Yes, a number 3 guard in MM can be used on all hair types, but it may not be suitable for very curly or kinky hair.

Q: How often should I replace my clipper guard?

A: It's recommended to replace your clipper guard every 6-12 months, depending on how often you use it.

Hair Care Tips

After using a number 3 guard in MM, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a moisturizing hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips and using a number 3 guard in MM correctly, you can achieve a stylish and well-maintained short hairstyle.
