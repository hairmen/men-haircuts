How To Cut Curly Men s Hair At Home With Scissors

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of visiting the salon every few weeks to get your curly hair trimmed? With a little bit of practice, you can easily cut your own hair at home with a pair of scissors. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut curly men's hair at home with scissors.

How to:

Before we start, you need to have the right tools. Here are the basic tools you will need:

  • A pair of sharp haircutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step by Step:

Follow the steps below to cut your own curly hair at home:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Dry it completely before cutting.
  2. Section your hair into parts. Use a comb to divide your hair into four equal parts: front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.
  3. Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold it between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat the process for the entire back section.
  4. Move on to the side sections. Comb a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut it at an angle, following the shape of your head. Repeat the process for the other side.
  5. Finally, move on to the front section. Comb your hair forward and cut it at an angle, following the shape of your forehead. Make sure to cut it slightly longer than the side sections.
  6. Check the length of your hair. Make sure that all sections are cut evenly. If you notice any uneven sections, trim them until you achieve your desired length.
  7. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start by cutting a small section of hair. You can always cut more, but you cannot undo a bad cut.
  • Make sure to cut your hair when it is dry. Wet hair can stretch, making it difficult to cut evenly.
  • Invest in a good pair of haircutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it difficult to cut.
  • Take breaks if you get tired. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks if you need to.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly men's hair at home:

  • Can I cut my hair when it is wet?
  • You can cut your hair when it is wet, but it can be difficult to get an even cut. We recommend cutting your hair when it is dry.
  • What if I make a mistake?
  • If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the section until it looks even.
  • How often should I cut my hair?
  • It depends on how fast your hair grows. We recommend trimming your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can easily cut your own curly hair at home with scissors. Remember to practice and be patient, as it may take a few tries to get the perfect cut.
