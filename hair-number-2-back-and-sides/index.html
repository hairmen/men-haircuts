Hair Number 2 Back And Sides

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a low maintenance haircut that still looks stylish, the hair number 2 back and sides might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a classic look that has been popular for decades, and it's easy to achieve and maintain. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the hair number 2 back and sides, including how to get it, how to maintain it, and some tips for styling it.

Description

The hair number 2 back and sides is a short haircut that is typically achieved with clippers. The "number 2" refers to the length of the blade used on the clippers, which is approximately 1/4 inch. The sides and back of the head are trimmed with the number 2 blade, while the top of the head can be left longer or trimmed to the same length.

How to get the Hair Number 2 Back and Sides

The hair number 2 back and sides is a simple haircut that can be achieved at home or by a professional barber. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers with a number 2 blade to trim the sides and back of the head.
  3. Blend the sides and back into the top of the head using scissors or clippers with a longer blade.
  4. Style the hair on top as desired, using a comb or your fingers.

If you're unsure about cutting your hair at home, it's always a good idea to visit a professional barber. They can help you achieve the perfect haircut and give you tips for maintaining it at home.

Step by Step Guide

If you're new to cutting hair, or just want a more detailed guide on how to achieve the hair number 2 back and sides, here's a step by step guide:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair on top of the head using hair clips or a hair tie.
  2. Use clippers with a number 2 blade to trim the sides and back of the head. Start by trimming the hair at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown of the head.
  3. Blend the sides and back into the top of the head using scissors or clippers with a longer blade. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any long pieces that stick out.
  4. Remove the hair clips or tie from the top of the head and trim the hair as desired. You can leave it longer for a more textured look, or trim it to the same length as the sides and back for a more uniform look.
  5. Style the hair on top as desired, using a comb or your fingers.

Remember to take your time and go slowly when cutting hair. It's always better to start with a longer length and gradually trim it shorter, rather than cutting too much off at once.

Tips for Styling the Hair Number 2 Back and Sides

The hair number 2 back and sides is a versatile haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some tips for styling it:

  • For a more textured look, use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, and work it through the hair with your fingers. This will give the hair a slightly messy, tousled look.
  • For a more polished look, use a comb to create a side part and smooth the hair down.
  • If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, embrace the texture and let it do its thing. You can use a curl-enhancing product to define the curls and keep them from frizzing.

FAQ

How often do I need to get a haircut with the hair number 2 back and sides?

The hair number 2 back and sides is a low maintenance haircut that can last for several weeks before needing a touch-up. However, if you prefer to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, you may want to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Can I achieve the hair number 2 back and sides with curly hair?

Absolutely! The hair number 2 back and sides can be achieved with any hair type, including curly hair. Just be sure to find a barber who is experienced with cutting curly hair, as it requires a different approach than straight hair.

Is the hair number 2 back and sides a good choice for balding men?

The hair number 2 back and sides can be a good choice for men who are experiencing hair loss or balding. By keeping the hair short and uniform, it can help to disguise thinning hair and create the illusion of more volume.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best with the hair number 2 back and sides, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep the hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hair number 2 back and sides and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
