How To Give Yourself Cornrows Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read
Are you looking to give yourself some fresh cornrows but not sure where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of giving yourself cornrows as a guy in relaxed English language.

How to Give Yourself Cornrows Guys

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle for guys that can be achieved with some practice and patience. Here's what you'll need to get started:
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hair clips
  • Hair gel or wax
  • Small rubber bands

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to give yourself cornrows:
  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is long, consider trimming it before starting.
  2. Use the rat-tail comb to part your hair into sections. Start at the front of your head and create a straight part down the middle. Then, create another part on each side of the center part. Clip the hair in each section out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of the center part. Divide it into three equal sections.
  4. Begin braiding the hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section. As you braid, add a small section of hair from each side to the braid. This will create the cornrow pattern.
  5. Continue braiding the hair in this pattern, adding small sections of hair as you go, until you reach the nape of your neck. Secure the end of the braid with a small rubber band.
  6. Repeat this process on the other side of the center part, creating a matching cornrow.
  7. Continue creating cornrows along the sides of your head, working your way towards the back. Be sure to keep the sections of hair even and the braids tight.
  8. When you reach the back of your head, you can either continue creating cornrows or switch to a different braiding pattern.
  9. Once all of the cornrows are complete, use hair gel or wax to smooth down any flyaways and give the style a polished look.
  10. Take out the hair clips and admire your new cornrow style!

Tips for Giving Yourself Cornrows

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving yourself cornrows:
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't be discouraged if your first attempt doesn't turn out perfectly. Keep practicing and you'll get better over time.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before starting. Wet hair can be more difficult to braid and may not hold the style as well.
  • Use hair gel or wax to help control flyaways and keep the style in place.
  • Don't braid too tightly. While it's important to keep the braids secure, braiding too tightly can cause pain and damage to your hair and scalp.
  • Take breaks as needed. Braiding your hair can be time-consuming and tiring, so don't be afraid to take breaks and come back to it later.

FAQs

How long does it take to give yourself cornrows?

The amount of time it takes to give yourself cornrows will depend on your skill level and the complexity of the style. Generally, it can take anywhere from 2-4 hours to complete a full head of cornrows.

Can I cornrow my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still cornrow your hair if it's short. Just make sure to start with clean, dry hair and use smaller sections to create the braids.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last anywhere from 2-6 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them and how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care Tips for Cornrows

To keep your cornrows looking great, here are some hair care tips to follow:
  • Wash your hair regularly, but be gentle when shampooing and avoid rubbing the braids too hard.
  • Use a lightweight oil, such as coconut or jojoba oil, to moisturize your scalp and hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or using a silk or satin pillowcase.
  • Don't leave your cornrows in for too long. After a few weeks, it's important to take them out and give your hair a break to prevent damage and breakage.
Now that you know how to give yourself cornrows as a guy, it's time to give it a try! With some practice and patience, you'll be rocking a fresh new style in no time.
