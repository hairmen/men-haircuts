How To Make Your Hair Look Short Curly A Guide For Relaxed Hair

Written by Christine Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight and boring hair? Do you want to switch things up and try a new hairstyle? If so, you might want to consider making your hair look short curly. Short curly hair can give you a fun and playful look that is perfect for any occasion. Plus, it's relatively easy to achieve, even if you have relaxed hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to make your hair look short curly in a few simple steps.

How to Make Your Hair Look Short Curly

If you're ready to make your hair look short curly, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The first step is to start with clean and moisturized hair. You can either wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo or co-wash your hair with a conditioner. Make sure to detangle your hair while it's wet to minimize breakage.

Step 2: Apply a curling cream or mousse

Apply a curling cream or mousse to your hair to help define your curls. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots to the tips.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. A diffuser attachment helps to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use the diffuser attachment on low or medium heat and scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp.

Step 4: Finger comb your hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to comb through your hair to break up any clumps or curls. Avoid using a brush or comb, as it can cause your hair to frizz.

Step 5: Add finishing touches

Add some finishing touches to your hair by using a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz. You can also use a hairspray or hair gel to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Making Your Hair Look Short Curly

Here are some additional tips for making your hair look short curly:

  • Don't overuse heat styling tools, as it can cause damage to your hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause your curls to lose shape
  • Consider getting a haircut to help define your curls and give your hair more shape

FAQ About Making Your Hair Look Short Curly

Here are some common questions about making your hair look short curly:

Can I make my relaxed hair look short curly?

Yes, you can make your relaxed hair look short curly by using curling creams or mousses and diffusing your hair with a blow dryer.

How long does short curly hair last?

The duration of short curly hair depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, short curly hair can last for a few days to a week.

Can I make my hair look short curly without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair look short curly without heat by using twist-outs or braid-outs. These styles help to define your curls without using heat styling tools.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

To keep your short curly hair healthy and well-maintained, here are some hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Avoid using sulfates and harsh chemicals in your hair products
  • Protect your hair at night by using a silk or satin bonnet or scarf
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

By following these tips, you can make your hair look short curly and keep it healthy and beautiful. So go ahead and give this hairstyle a try, and see how it can transform your look!
