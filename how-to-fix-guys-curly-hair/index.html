How To Fix Guys Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with curly hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style and manage your locks. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll go over how to fix guys curly hair with some simple steps and tips. Whether you're looking to tame your curls or enhance them, we've got some great advice for you.

How to Fix Guys Curly Hair

Here are some steps you can take to fix your curly hair:

Step 1: Use the Right Products

The first step in fixing your curly hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking great all day long.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to condition your hair regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls looking healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

When drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Don't Touch Your Hair

Once you've styled your hair, try not to touch it too much. This can cause frizz and disrupt the curls.

Tips for Fixing Guys Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help fix your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your curls looking great all day long. Apply a small amount to your hair after washing and conditioning, and leave it in.

Tip 2: Use a Curling Gel

A curling gel can help to define your curls and keep them looking great. Apply a small amount to your hair and scrunch it in.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to keep your curly hair looking neat and tidy. Ask your stylist to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep your curls looking their best.

FAQ about Fixing Guys Curly Hair

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: Should I comb or brush my curly hair?

A: It's best to use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curly hair. Brushing can cause frizz and disrupt the curls.

Hair Care for Guys with Curly Hair

Taking care of your curly hair is essential to keeping it looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

Tip 1: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and keep your curls looking great.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your curly hair, so it's best to avoid it. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can cause damage to your hair, so it's important to protect it. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to keep your curls looking great.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to fix your curly hair and keep it looking great. Remember to use the right products, condition your hair regularly, and avoid touching it too much. And don't forget to get regular haircuts to keep your curls looking neat and tidy.
