Description

A taper fade is a popular haircut that involves gradually decreasing the length of hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on the top longer. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in different ways, and it looks great on all hair types and face shapes. Getting a taper fade at a salon or barbershop can be expensive, but you can save money by learning how to self cut taper fade at home. In this article, we will show you how to do it yourself in a few easy steps.

How to Self Cut Taper Fade

The key to self cutting taper fade is to have the right tools and techniques. You will need a good quality clipper, scissors, comb, mirror, and a steady hand. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide how short you want the sides and back of your hair to be. Use a clipper with the appropriate guard to cut the hair to the desired length. Start from the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion to create a fade effect.
  3. Blend the hair between the top and the sides by using a scissor-over-comb technique. Take a small section of hair between your comb and scissors and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process until the hair is blended evenly.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 with a shorter guard to create a more dramatic fade effect. Make sure to blend the hair well between the different lengths.
  5. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. Cut it in an even, horizontal line. You can use your fingers to gauge the length of the hair.
  6. Blend the hair between the top and the sides by using a scissor-over-comb technique. Take a small section of hair between your comb and scissors and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process until the hair is blended evenly.
  7. Check your hair in the mirror from different angles to ensure that it is even and properly blended.
  8. Clean up any stray hairs around the hairline and ears by using a trimmer or scissors.
  9. Shower and rinse your hair to remove any loose hair. Style your hair as desired.
  10. Practice makes perfect! It may take a few tries to get it right, but don't get discouraged. Once you master the technique, you can save a lot of money by self cutting taper fade at home.

Tips for Self Cutting Taper Fade

Self cutting taper fade can be challenging, but it is not impossible. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Invest in a good quality clipper and scissors. Cheap tools can lead to uneven cuts and frustration.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a more gradual fade.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Watch tutorial videos online to learn different techniques and styles.

FAQs

Q: How often should I self cut taper fade?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the style. On average, you can self cut taper fade every 2-4 weeks.

Q: Can I self cut taper fade if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, it may be more challenging to blend the hair evenly. It is recommended to use a scissor-over-comb technique and work with smaller sections of hair.

Q: Can I use hair clippers to trim my beard?

A: Yes, you can. However, make sure to use the appropriate guard and be careful around sensitive areas such as the neck and chin.

Hair Care After Self Cutting Taper Fade

After self cutting taper fade, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use hair products such as wax or pomade to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair in good condition and maintain the style.

Congratulations, you have learned how to self cut taper fade! With practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish.
