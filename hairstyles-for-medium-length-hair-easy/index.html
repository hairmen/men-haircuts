Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Easy - Best Tips and Tricks

If you have medium length hair and are looking for some easy hairstyles to try out, then you have come to the right place! In this article, we will be sharing some of the best hairstyles for medium length hair that are super easy to create and require minimal effort. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Length Hair
  • Step by Step Guide for Easy Hairstyles
  • Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to know how to style medium length hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Blow dry your hair on a low setting to avoid damaging it
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage
  • Apply hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz

Step by Step Guide for Easy Hairstyles

Now, let's take a look at some easy hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Here's how:

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look

2. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want your hair out of your face but still want to show off your length. Here's how:

  1. Take the top section of your hair and pull it back
  2. Secure the section with a hair tie or bobby pins
  3. Leave the rest of your hair down

3. Low Ponytail

The low ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that works for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck
  2. Tie it with a hair tie
  3. Take a small section of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie
  4. Secure the section with a bobby pin

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

FAQs

1. Can I curl medium length hair?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to create curls or waves in your medium length hair.

2. How do I prevent my medium length hair from getting frizzy?

Apply a hair oil or serum to your hair before styling to reduce frizz. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Here are some products that are great for maintaining and caring for medium length hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner - look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients
  • Deep conditioning treatment - use once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Hair oil or serum - apply before styling to add shine and reduce frizz
  • Heat protectant spray - use before using any hot tools to prevent damage

So there you have it, some easy hairstyles for medium length hair and tips for maintaining and caring for it. With these simple tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful all year round!
