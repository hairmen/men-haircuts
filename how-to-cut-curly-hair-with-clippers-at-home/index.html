How To Cut Curly Hair With Clippers At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that finding the right stylist to cut your hair can be a challenge. But what if you could learn how to cut your own hair at home with clippers? In this article, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to cut your curly hair with clippers at home.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair with Clippers

Description

Cutting curly hair with clippers can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a great way to save money and get the style you want. Clippers are designed to cut through hair quickly and easily, making them a great choice for curly hair that can be difficult to manage.

How To Cut Curly Hair with Clippers at Home

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a set of clippers with guards, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Once you have everything you need, you can start the cutting process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to wash and dry it. This will help you see the natural curl pattern of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Clippers come with different guards that determine how short the hair will be cut. For curly hair, it's best to start with a guard that leaves the hair longer, like a 2 or 3. You can always go shorter if you need to, but it's harder to go back if you cut too much off.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Starting at the back of your head, use the clippers to cut your hair in a straight line. Move the clippers up to the next section of hair and repeat. Continue cutting around your head, making sure to keep the clippers level with your head.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back of your head, it's time to move on to the sides. Use the same guard you used on the back, but start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a blending technique to create a smooth transition between the sides and the back.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Finally, it's time to trim the top of your hair. Use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Start by cutting small sections at a time and work your way around your head.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair with Clippers

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, but these tips will help make the process easier:

  • Always start with a longer guard and work your way down if necessary.
  • Use a blending technique to create a smooth transition between different sections of hair.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use scissors to trim the top of your hair for a more natural look.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with clippers if it's really curly?

Yes, you can cut curly hair with clippers. Just make sure to use a longer guard and cut small sections of hair at a time.

Can I use regular scissors to cut curly hair?

While you can use regular scissors to cut curly hair, it's best to use clippers with guards for a more even cut.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your curly hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid using heat tools that can damage your hair.

Cutting your curly hair with clippers at home can be a great way to save money and get the style you want. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-quality cut in the comfort of your own home.
