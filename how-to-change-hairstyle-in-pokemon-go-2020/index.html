How To Change Hairstyle In Pokemon Go 2020 A Step By Step Guide

If you're a Pokemon Go player who wants to switch up your in-game appearance, you might be wondering how to change your hairstyle. Fortunately, it's a relatively easy process that can be done in a few simple steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go 2020.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go 2020

The first step to changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is to make sure you have the necessary items. You'll need a Mirror, which can be purchased from the Shop for 200 PokeCoins. Once you have the Mirror, follow these steps:

Step 1: Open the Main Menu

Open the main menu by tapping the Poke Ball icon at the bottom of the screen.

Step 2: Select the Style Icon

Next, select the Style icon, which looks like a hairbrush and can be found on the right side of the screen.

Step 3: Use the Mirror

Once you're in the Style menu, select the Mirror item from your inventory. You'll be prompted to use the item, and once you confirm, your character will hold up the Mirror to their face.

Step 4: Choose a New Hairstyle

With the Mirror in hand, you can now select a new hairstyle from the options presented on the screen. Scroll through the different hairstyles until you find one you like.

Step 5: Confirm Your Selection

After you've chosen a new hairstyle, you'll be prompted to confirm your selection. If you're happy with your new look, select Yes to save your changes.

Tips for Changing Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go 2020

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  • Make sure you have enough PokeCoins to purchase the Mirror
  • Take your time when selecting a new hairstyle
  • Experiment with different looks to find one you like
  • Remember that changing your hairstyle won't affect your Pokemon's stats or abilities

FAQ About Changing Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go 2020

Can I change my hairstyle multiple times?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you like. However, you'll need to purchase a new Mirror each time.

Will changing my hairstyle affect my Pokemon in any way?

No, changing your hairstyle won't have any impact on your Pokemon's stats or abilities.

Do I need to be a certain level to change my hairstyle?

No, there are no level requirements for changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go.

Hair Care in Pokemon Go 2020

While changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is a fun way to mix up your in-game appearance, it's important to remember that hair care is still important. Here are a few tips to keep your virtual locks looking their best:

  • Wear a hat or headband to protect your hair from the sun
  • Use a virtual shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Comb your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots
  • Visit the virtual salon to get a trim or treatment if your hair needs some extra care

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to keep your in-game hairstyle looking great and change it up whenever you want!
