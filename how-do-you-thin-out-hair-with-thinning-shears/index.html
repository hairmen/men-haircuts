How Do You Thin Out Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning shears are a hairstylist's best friend. They are a handy tool that can be used to remove bulk from hair and create texture. Thinning shears are also great for people who have thick hair and want to thin it out. In this article, we will discuss how to thin out hair with thinning shears.

Description

Thinning shears are scissors that have teeth, which are used to cut hair at different lengths. They are also known as texturizing shears or blending shears. Thinning shears can be used to remove bulk from hair and create texture. They are also useful for people with thick hair who want to thin it out without losing too much length. Thinning shears are available in different sizes and shapes, and the number of teeth on the shears varies.

How to Thin Out Hair With Thinning Shears

Thinning out hair with thinning shears is a straightforward process. Follow the steps below:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry or blow-dry it. It is essential to have clean hair to get the best results.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts, using hair clips. This will make it easier to work with your hair.

Step 3: Hold the Shears Correctly

Hold the thinning shears correctly. To hold the shears, place your thumb in one of the holes and your ring and middle fingers in the other hole. Your index finger should be on the outside of the shears, near the teeth.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair with the thinning shears. Hold a section of hair between your fingers and cut the hair with the shears, moving them towards the end of the hair. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears:

  • Use the right size of thinning shears for your hair type.
  • Do not use thinning shears on wet hair.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Do not cut too much hair at once.
  • Keep the shears clean and sharp.

FAQ

Can thinning shears damage your hair?

Thinning shears can cause damage if they are not used correctly. It is essential to use the right size of shears and to cut small sections of hair at a time. Do not use thinning shears on wet hair, as this can cause the hair to break.

How often should you thin out your hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. If you have thick hair, you may need to thin it out every six weeks. If you have fine hair, you may need to thin it out every three months.

Can you use thinning shears on curly hair?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on curly hair. However, it is essential to be careful not to cut too much hair, as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care

After thinning out your hair with thinning shears, it is essential to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. It is also essential to get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Conclusion

Thinning shears are a useful tool for people who want to remove bulk from their hair and create texture. By following the steps outlined above and keeping the tips in mind, you can thin out your hair with thinning shears without causing damage. Remember to take care of your hair after thinning it out to keep it healthy and looking great.
