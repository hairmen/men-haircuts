How To Middle Part Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you interested in trying out a new hairstyle? One that has been gaining popularity in recent years is the middle part for men. This style can be versatile and work for a variety of hair types and lengths. In this article, we’ll go over how to middle part hair male and give you some tips for maintaining this style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Middle Part Hair Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Middle Part
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for the Middle Part Style

How to Middle Part Hair Male

The first step in middle parting your hair is to determine if it’s the right style for you. This style works well for those with a symmetrical face shape and balanced facial features. It’s also great for those with oval or oblong face shapes.

To determine your ideal parting location, use your nose as a guide. Place a comb or your finger at the center of your nose and run it through your hair. This is where your part should be.

Next, you’ll need to prep your hair. Wash and condition your hair with a product that suits your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create the perfect middle part:

  1. Start by dividing your hair into two equal sections with a comb.
  2. Use the comb to create a straight line down the center of your head, starting from the hairline and going towards the back of your head.
  3. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth each section of hair down, making sure to keep the part straight.
  4. If you have longer hair, you may want to use a hair tie to keep one side out of the way while you work on the other.
  5. Continue smoothing and styling your hair until you achieve your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining the Middle Part

Here are a few tips to keep in mind to ensure your middle part looks great all day long:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Touch up your part throughout the day if needed.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction of your part to help keep it in place.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about middle parting hair for men:

Q. Is the middle part only for long hair?

A. No, the middle part can work for hair of any length.

Q. Will the middle part work for all face shapes?

A. No, the middle part works best for those with symmetrical or oval/oblong face shapes.

Q. Can I still style my hair if I have a middle part?

A. Yes, you can still style your hair in a variety of ways with a middle part.

Hair Care for the Middle Part Style

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great with a middle part:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair nourished.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to middle part hair male, give it a try and see if it’s the right style for you. With a little practice and some styling products, you can rock this versatile and trendy look.
