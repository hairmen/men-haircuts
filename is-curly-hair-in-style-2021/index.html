Is Curly Hair In Style 2021

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to hair trends, one of the most popular questions people ask is whether curly hair is in style. Well, we have good news for all the curly-haired folks out there - curly hair is definitely in style for 2021! In fact, curly hair has been trending for the past few years and it doesn't seem to be going anywhere.

Why is Curly Hair in Style?

Curly hair has gained popularity over the years because it's a versatile hair type that can be styled in many ways. It's also a natural hair type for many people, so it's a great way to embrace and celebrate one's natural beauty. Additionally, curly hair adds volume and dimension to any hairstyle, making it a great choice for those who want to add some flair to their look.

How to Embrace Your Curly Hair

If you have curly hair and want to embrace it, there are a few things you can do to enhance your curls:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and disrupt your curls' natural pattern.
  4. Consider getting a haircut that complements your curls, such as layers or a Deva cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or curling iron to touch up any curls that need a little extra definition.
  5. Finish with a lightweight hair spray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair can be a challenge, but here are some tips to help:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Refresh your curls in between washes with a curl refresher spray.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can dry out your curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best products for curly hair?

There are many great products for curly hair, but some of the most popular ones include leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and curl refresher sprays.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use heat protectant products and to avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.

What is a Deva cut?

A Deva cut is a haircut specifically designed for curly hair. It involves cutting the hair while it's dry and in its natural state, with the goal of creating a shape that enhances the hair's natural curl pattern.

Conclusion

Curly hair is definitely in style for 2021, and there are many ways to embrace and enhance your curls. Whether you're using curl creams, diffusers, or getting a Deva cut, there are many options for styling and maintaining curly hair. So, if you have curly hair, embrace it and show off your natural beauty!
