Mens Short Haircuts Longer On Top A Guide To The Latest Trend

If you're looking for a low-maintenance but stylish haircut, then you might want to consider mens short haircuts longer on top. This haircut has been popular among men of all ages and styles for a few years now, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this article, we'll give you a complete guide on how to get this look, how to maintain it, and some tips on styling it.

Description

Mens short haircuts longer on top is a style that involves cutting the sides and back of the hair short while keeping the hair on top longer. This hairdo is versatile and can be styled in many ways, including messy, slicked back, or combed over. It's also very low maintenance and easy to style, which makes it a popular choice among men who don't want to spend too much time on their hair.

How to Get Mens Short Haircuts Longer on Top

The first step in getting this look is to find a good barber or hairstylist who is experienced in cutting mens hair. Make sure to bring a photo of the style you want so that your stylist can get an idea of what you're looking for.

When you get to the salon, your stylist will first cut the sides and back of your hair short, usually with clippers. Then, they'll leave the hair on top longer and cut it to the desired length. You can specify how long you want your hair on top, but generally, it should be at least 2-3 inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get mens short haircuts longer on top:

  1. Find a good barber or hairstylist
  2. Bring a photo of the style you want
  3. Discuss your preferences with your stylist
  4. Get the sides and back of your hair cut short
  5. Leave the hair on top longer and cut it to the desired length
  6. Ask your stylist to blend the sides and top together for a seamless look
  7. Style your hair as desired

Tips on Styling Mens Short Haircuts Longer on Top

Now that you have your mens short haircuts longer on top, it's time to style it. Here are some tips on how to style this trendy haircut:

  • Use a good quality pomade or wax to style your hair
  • Try different hairstyles to find what suits you best
  • Use a hairdryer to add volume to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different textures and lengths

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens short haircuts longer on top:

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, mens short haircuts longer on top can be styled in many ways. For a casual look, you can leave it messy, while for a formal occasion, you can slick it back.

Hair Care for Mens Short Haircuts Longer on Top

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining any hairstyle, including mens short haircuts longer on top. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils

So, there you have it - a complete guide to mens short haircuts longer on top. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle or a trendy look, this haircut is a great option. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can rock this style with confidence.
