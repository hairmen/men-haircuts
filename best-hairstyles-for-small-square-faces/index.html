Best Hairstyles For Small Square Faces

Written by Petter Aug 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a small square face, you might feel like your hairstyle options are limited. However, there are many hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for small square faces and give you tips on how to style them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A small square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and create balance. This can be achieved through layers, texture, and volume.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a small square face, consider the length and texture of your hair. Shorter haircuts tend to work better for this face shape, as they can help balance out the broad forehead and strong jawline. Textured or layered styles can also add volume and movement to the hair, creating a more balanced look.

Step by Step

1. Start by determining the length of hair you prefer. Shorter haircuts tend to work better for small square faces.

2. Consider adding layers or texture to your hair to create movement and volume.

3. Choose a hairstyle that balances out your face shape, such as a bob or a shag.

4. Use styling products to enhance your hair's texture and volume, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even smaller.

2. Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as they can help balance out a broad forehead.

3. Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a small square face?

A: While shorter haircuts tend to work better for small square faces, you can still wear your hair long if you prefer. Just be sure to add layers or texture to create volume and movement.

Q: Should I avoid straightening my hair if I have a small square face?

A: Straightening your hair can actually help elongate your face and balance out a strong jawline. However, be sure to add volume and movement to your hair as well to avoid a flat, lifeless look.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair, be sure to use products that nourish and protect your strands. This can include shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type, as well as heat protectant spray if you use heat styling tools. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Overall, there are many hairstyles that can flatter a small square face. By considering the length, texture, and balance of your hair, you can find a style that highlights your best features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
