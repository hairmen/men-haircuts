How To Cut The Top Of Your Hair For A Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to rock a mullet but not sure how to get the perfect cut? Fear not, because we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be guiding you through the process of cutting the top of your hair for a mullet, step by step.

Description

A mullet is a popular haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on top and in the back. While it may have had a bad reputation in the past, mullets are making a comeback in recent years and can be a trendy and stylish look.

How to Cut the Top of Your Hair for a Mullet

Cutting the top of your hair for a mullet can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done easily. Here’s how:

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you start cutting, you’ll need to gather a few tools. These include:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips (optional)
  • Water spray bottle

Make sure your tools are clean and in good condition before starting.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

Before cutting, it’s important to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a more even cut. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type, and dry your hair completely with a towel or hair dryer.

Step 3: Section your hair

Using your comb or sectioning clips, divide your hair into two sections: top and bottom. The top section should start at the crown of your head and extend to your forehead.

Step 4: Cut the top section

Starting at the back of your head, use your scissors to cut the top section of your hair. The length of the cut will depend on the style you’re going for, but generally, you’ll want to leave the hair longer at the front and gradually shorten it towards the back.

If you’re using clippers, use a guard attachment to achieve an even cut. Start at the back and work your way towards the front, using a comb to guide the clippers.

Step 5: Blend the top and bottom sections

Once you’ve finished cutting the top section, use your comb to blend it with the bottom section. This will create a seamless transition between the shorter sides and longer top.

Step 6: Check for any uneven spots

After you’ve finished cutting, carefully inspect your hair for any uneven spots or stray hairs. Use your scissors to trim any areas that need it.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make your mullet cutting experience easier:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually shorten it as you go.
  • If you’re not confident in your cutting skills, consider getting help from a friend or professional stylist.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring!

FAQ

Can I cut my own mullet?

Yes, you can definitely cut your own mullet! Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

What length should I cut my hair?

The length of your hair will depend on the style you’re going for. Generally, the top section should be longer than the sides and back.

Do I need to use clippers?

No, clippers are not necessary, but they can make the process easier and more precise.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its shape and health. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to cut the top of your hair for a mullet like a pro. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with it!
