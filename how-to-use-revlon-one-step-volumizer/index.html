How To Use Revlon One Step Volumizer

Written by Christine Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to add volume and bounce to your hair, the Revlon One Step Volumizer is a great tool to have. This versatile hair styling tool combines the power of a hair dryer and a round brush to give you a salon-quality blowout at home. In this article, we'll walk you through how to use the Revlon One Step Volumizer and share some tips and tricks to help you get the best results.

Description

The Revlon One Step Volumizer is a hair styling tool that features a round brush and a hair dryer in one. Its unique design allows you to dry and style your hair simultaneously, which can save you time and effort in your beauty routine. The brush is designed with nylon pins and tufted bristles to detangle and smooth your hair, while the ionic technology helps to reduce frizz and static.

How to Use Revlon One Step Volumizer

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before using the Revlon One Step Volumizer, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. This will help you achieve a more even and voluminous style.

Step 3: Turn on the Revlon One Step Volumizer

Plug in your Revlon One Step Volumizer and turn it on. Select the heat and speed settings that are appropriate for your hair type. For thicker hair, use a higher heat and speed setting, while for finer hair, use a lower heat and speed setting.

Step 4: Start styling

Take a section of hair and place the Revlon One Step Volumizer at the roots. Use your other hand to hold the hair taut as you glide the brush through your hair. Continue to brush and style your hair until it is dry and smooth. Repeat this process for each section of hair until you've styled your entire head.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results with the Revlon One Step Volumizer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat
  • Section your hair for more even and voluminous styling
  • For more volume, use the brush to lift your hair at the roots as you style
  • For a sleeker look, use the brush to smooth your hair as you style
  • Experiment with different heat and speed settings to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Can I use the Revlon One Step Volumizer on wet hair?

No, the Revlon One Step Volumizer is designed to be used on dry hair. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before using the tool.

Is the Revlon One Step Volumizer suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Volumizer is suitable for all hair types. However, you may need to adjust the heat and speed settings based on your hair thickness and texture.

How do I clean my Revlon One Step Volumizer?

Allow the tool to cool down completely before cleaning. Use a damp cloth to wipe down the brush and body of the tool. Do not submerge the tool in water or use harsh chemicals to clean it.

Hair Care

Using the Revlon One Step Volumizer can be a great way to add volume and style to your hair, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Limit the use of hair products that contain harsh chemicals
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair

With these tips and the Revlon One Step Volumizer, you can achieve a salon-quality blowout at home. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
