Pictures Of Best Hairstyles For Round Face Shape

Written by Frank Sep 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you someone with a round face shape and finding it difficult to choose the perfect hairstyle to flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for round face shape with pictures and tips to help you achieve your best look.

Description

Round face shapes often have wider cheekbones and a rounded jawline. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle?

It’s important to consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair while choosing the perfect hairstyle. A hairstyle that adds volume to the crown of your head and elongates your face will be the most flattering for round face shapes.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to choosing the perfect hairstyle for round face shapes:

  1. Identify your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Determine the length of your hair and decide if you want to keep it short, medium or long.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the crown of your head and elongates your face.
  4. If you have curly hair, opt for a hairstyle that adds layers to the bottom of your hair to create definition and movement.
  5. Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to create the illusion of a longer face.

Tips for Round Face Shapes

Here are some tips to keep in mind while choosing the perfect hairstyle for round face shapes:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short and accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that create angles and definition to your face.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with headbands, hair clips, or hair scarves to create the illusion of a longer face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round face shapes:

Q. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face shape. However, it’s important to add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Q. Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face shape. However, make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Q. Should I avoid bobs if I have a round face shape?

A. No, you can wear bobs if you have a round face shape. However, make sure to choose a longer bob that adds volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Hair Care for Round Face Shapes

It’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips for round face shapes:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular hair treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you have a better understanding of the best hairstyles for round face shapes, it’s time to choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Remember to consider your hair length, texture, and volume while choosing your hairstyle. Happy styling!
