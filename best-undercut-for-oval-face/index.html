Best Undercut For Oval Face A Guide To The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Frank Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face, you are one of the lucky ones. Your face is balanced and symmetrical, and you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you want to try something new, an undercut can be a great option. In this article, we will discuss the best undercut for oval face and provide you with some useful tips and tricks to style it.

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut short while the hair on top is left longer. This creates a sharp contrast between the two lengths and makes the top hair stand out. The best thing about an undercut is that it can be customized to suit any face shape.

How to

If you want to get an undercut, the first step is to find a good hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience in cutting undercuts and can give you the perfect style. Once you have found the right stylist, follow these steps:

Step 1: Decide on the length

The length of the undercut depends on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with different lengths, from a short buzz cut to a longer, textured top. Discuss your options with your stylist and choose the length that suits you best.

Step 2: Choose the style

There are many styles to choose from when it comes to undercuts. You can go for a classic slicked-back look, a messy textured top, or a side-swept fringe. Again, consider your face shape and personal style when choosing the right style for you.

Step 3: Maintain your undercut

An undercut requires regular maintenance to keep it looking sharp. You will need to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. You can also use hair products such as wax or pomade to style your hair and keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best undercut for oval face:

  • Consider your hair type when choosing the length and style of your undercut. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a shorter cut to make it more manageable.
  • Experiment with different styles before settling on one. You may find that a certain style suits you better than others.
  • Ask your stylist for advice on how to style your undercut. They may have some useful tips and tricks that you can use at home.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. An undercut can be a great way to change up your look and express your personal style.

FAQ

Can anyone get an undercut?

Yes, anyone can get an undercut. However, you should consider your hair type and face shape when choosing the length and style of your undercut.

How often do I need to maintain my undercut?

You will need to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. You can also use hair products to style your hair and keep it in place.

What products should I use to style my undercut?

You can use hair wax, pomade, or gel to style your undercut. Choose a product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking its best, you should follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a mild shampoo, using conditioner to keep it soft and smooth, and avoiding heat styling tools such as straighteners or curling irons. You should also protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Conclusion

An undercut can be a great hairstyle for those with oval faces. It can be customized to suit any personal style and is easy to maintain. Follow our tips and tricks to get the best undercut for oval face and enjoy your new look!
