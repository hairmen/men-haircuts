How To Thin Curly Hair Without Thinning Shears A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have thick, curly hair that needs some thinning out? While thinning shears can be a quick and easy solution, not everyone has access to them or wants to use them. Luckily, there are other ways to thin curly hair without thinning shears. In this guide, we’ll go over how to thin curly hair without thinning shears, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Thin Curly Hair Without Thinning Shears
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Thinning Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Thin Curly Hair Without Thinning Shears

Thinning shears can be a quick way to thin out curly hair, but they can also damage your hair if not used properly. If you don’t have access to thinning shears or prefer not to use them, there are other ways to thin curly hair:

  • Use a razor comb
  • Use regular scissors
  • Use a texturizing spray or cream
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying

Each of these methods can help thin out curly hair without causing damage or creating an uneven look.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s how to thin curly hair without thinning shears:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It’s easier to see and cut individual strands when hair is dry.
  2. Decide on the method you want to use. Razor combs and regular scissors work for most hair types, but texturizing sprays and diffusers may work better for some.
  3. Section your hair. Use clips or hair ties to separate your hair into sections. This will make it easier to thin out each section evenly.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of one of the sections you’ve created.
  5. Hold the section of hair taut with one hand.
  6. Use your other hand to cut into the section of hair with your chosen tool. For razor combs and regular scissors, start at the ends of the hair and work your way up, cutting small pieces at a time. For texturizing sprays, spray onto the section of hair and use your fingers to separate and thin out the hair. For diffusers, use on a low heat setting and cup the hair in the diffuser, lifting it up towards the roots to create volume and thin out the hair.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 on each section of hair until you’ve thinned out your hair to your desired level.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Remember to take your time and be careful when thinning out your hair. It’s better to go slow and cut less hair at a time than to cut too much and create an uneven look.

Tips for Thinning Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for thinning curly hair without thinning shears:

  • Start with a little bit of hair and work your way up. It’s easier to thin out more hair than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head. It’s easy to miss spots when you’re only looking at the front.
  • Don’t thin out your hair too much. Curly hair needs some weight to help it hold its shape.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you’re unsure about thinning out your hair yourself.

FAQs

Can I thin out curly hair with regular scissors?

Yes, you can use regular scissors to thin out curly hair. Just be sure to cut small pieces at a time and start at the ends of your hair.

Will thinning out curly hair damage it?

Thinning out curly hair can cause damage if not done properly. Be sure to use a sharp tool and cut small pieces at a time to avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I thin out my curly hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should only need to thin out your hair every few months.

Hair Care for Thin Curly Hair

Once you’ve thinned out your curly hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can thin out your curly hair without thinning shears and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
