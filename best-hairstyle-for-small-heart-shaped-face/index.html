Best Hairstyle For Small Heart Shaped Face In 2023

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 · 2 min read

If you have a small heart-shaped face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But don’t worry! In this article, we’ll share the best hairstyle for small heart-shaped face and provide you with useful tips and tricks to keep your hair looking fabulous.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Find the Best Hairstyle for Small Heart Shaped Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Small Heart Shaped Face

Description

A small heart-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and a pointed chin. The best hairstyle for this face shape is one that adds width to the forehead and creates volume around the jawline to balance out the chin. The ideal haircut should also be able to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, making them the focal point of your face.

How to Find the Best Hairstyle for Small Heart Shaped Face

When searching for the best hairstyle for small heart-shaped face, you need to consider the length, texture, and thickness of your hair. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a layered cut or a side-swept fringe.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even smaller.
  • Opt for hairstyles with soft waves or curls, as they can add texture and dimension to your hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or straight, as they can accentuate the narrowness of your forehead.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the best hairstyle for small heart-shaped face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  3. Create soft waves or curls using a curling iron or a hair straightener.
  4. Add volume to the sides of your head using a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse.
  5. Create a side-swept fringe to add width to your forehead.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp condition.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for small heart-shaped face:

  • Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a small heart-shaped face?
  • A: While a pixie cut can look great on some people with small heart-shaped face, it can accentuate the narrowness of your forehead and make your face look even smaller. It’s best to consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your face shape.
  • Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a small heart-shaped face?
  • A: While straight hair can look great on some people with small heart-shaped face, it can accentuate the narrowness of your forehead and make your face look even smaller. It’s best to add volume and texture to your hair to create the illusion of width.
  • Q: What is the best hair color for small heart-shaped face?
  • A: The best hair color for small heart-shaped face depends on your skin tone and personal preference. However, highlights or lowlights can create depth and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Hair Care for Small Heart Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers, as they can damage your hair and scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best hairstyle for small heart-shaped face and keep your hair looking fabulous all year round. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and always take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
