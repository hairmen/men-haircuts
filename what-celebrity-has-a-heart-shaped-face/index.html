What Celebrity Has A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Christine Jul 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a heart shaped face and you're looking for hairstyle inspiration, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing what celebrity has a heart shaped face, and providing you with some tips and tricks for styling your hair to flatter your face shape.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. This face shape is also known as inverted triangle.

How to Identify if You Have a Heart Shaped Face?

If you're not sure if you have a heart shaped face, take a look in the mirror. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin comes to a point, you likely have a heart shaped face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Heart Shaped Face

Step 1: Consider a Side Part

A side part is a great option for heart shaped faces, as it helps to balance out the wider forehead.

Step 2: Try Bangs

Bangs can also be a great option for heart shaped faces, as they help to balance out the wider forehead. Opt for bangs that are swept to the side, rather than straight across, to avoid making your forehead appear even wider.

Step 3: Embrace Layers

Layers can be a great way to add texture and movement to your hair, while also helping to balance out a wider forehead.

Tips for Styling Your Hair if You Have a Heart Shaped Face

Tip 1: Avoid Volume at the Crown

Avoid adding too much volume at the crown of your head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.

Tip 2: Keep Your Hair Below Chin Length

Keeping your hair below chin length can help to balance out a narrow chin, while also avoiding making your forehead appear even wider.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) About Heart Shaped Faces

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume at the crown of your head, as well as hairstyles that are too short or too long.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a heart shaped face?

A: Absolutely! Straight hair can look great on heart shaped faces, just be sure to avoid adding too much volume at the crown of your head.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Tip 1: Use a Volumizing Shampoo

Using a volumizing shampoo can help to add body and texture to your hair, without adding too much volume at the crown of your head.

Tip 2: Avoid Overusing Heat Styling Tools

Overusing heat styling tools can damage your hair, making it harder to style and less healthy overall.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh, while also helping to maintain a balanced shape.

In conclusion, if you have a heart shaped face, there are plenty of hairstyle options that can flatter your face shape. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you'll be well on your way to finding a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
