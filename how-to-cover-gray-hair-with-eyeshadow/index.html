How To Cover Gray Hair With Eyeshadow A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of seeing those pesky gray hairs peeking out of your roots? Of course, you are! But don't worry, we have the solution for you. In this post, we'll teach you how to cover gray hair with eyeshadow, a quick and easy fix that anyone can do at home. Keep reading to learn how to transform your gray roots into a flawless mane.

Description

Gray hair is a part of life, but that doesn't mean you have to live with it. Coloring your hair can be expensive, time-consuming, and damaging to your locks. That's why using eyeshadow to cover gray hair is a great option. It's affordable, temporary, and easy to do. Plus, you can experiment with different shades to find the perfect match for your hair color.

How to Cover Gray Hair with Eyeshadow

Follow these simple steps to cover your gray hair with eyeshadow:

Step 1: Choose Your Eyeshadow

The first step is to choose an eyeshadow that matches your hair color. Look for a matte eyeshadow that is the same shade as your roots. If you have highlights, you can use a lighter shade of eyeshadow to match those areas.

Step 2: Apply Eyeshadow to Your Roots

Dip a small, angled brush into the eyeshadow and tap off any excess powder. Then, lightly dab the brush onto your roots, focusing on areas where the gray hair is most visible. Use short strokes to blend the eyeshadow into your hair, making sure to cover all the gray strands.

Step 3: Blend and Set

Use a clean makeup brush to blend the eyeshadow into your hair, making sure there are no harsh lines. Then, spray a light mist of hairspray over the area to set the eyeshadow in place. This will keep the color from rubbing off onto your clothes or pillowcase.

Tips for Covering Gray Hair with Eyeshadow

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your eyeshadow gray hair cover-up:

  • Choose a powder eyeshadow, not a cream or liquid formula.
  • Use a small, angled brush to apply the eyeshadow to your hair.
  • Focus on the areas where the gray hair is most visible, such as your part or hairline.
  • Blend the eyeshadow into your hair using a clean makeup brush.
  • Use a light mist of hairspray to set the eyeshadow in place.
  • Experiment with different shades of eyeshadow to find the perfect match for your hair color.

FAQ

Q: Will eyeshadow damage my hair?

A: No, eyeshadow will not damage your hair. It's a temporary solution that can be washed out easily with shampoo.

Q: How long will the eyeshadow last in my hair?

A: The eyeshadow will last until you wash your hair. However, it may rub off onto your clothes or pillowcase if you don't set it with hairspray.

Q: Can I use eyeshadow on all hair types?

A: Yes, eyeshadow can be used on all hair types and colors. Just make sure to choose a shade that matches your hair color.

Hair Care Tips

While using eyeshadow to cover gray hair is a great temporary solution, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further graying. Here are some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and youthful:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

Now that you know how to cover your gray hair with eyeshadow, you can enjoy a youthful look without spending a fortune. Try it out for yourself and let us know how it goes!
