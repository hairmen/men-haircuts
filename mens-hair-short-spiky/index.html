Mens Hair Short Spiky A Guide To The Trendy Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Daniel Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle in 2023, you might want to consider the mens hair short spiky look. This trendy hairstyle has been around for years and continues to be popular among men of all ages. In this guide, we'll discuss how to achieve the perfect short spiky hairstyle, provide tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Short spiky hair is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short and styling it to create spikes or points. This hairstyle is versatile and can be achieved with different hair types, lengths, and textures. It's a popular choice for men who want to have a trendy, low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style and maintain.

How To

To achieve the short spiky hairstyle, you'll need to have short hair on the sides and back of your head and slightly longer hair on the top. You can achieve this look by going to a barber or hairstylist who's experienced in this type of haircut. Ask for a short fade on the sides and back and leave the hair on top long enough to style into spikes or points.

Once you have the right haircut, you can style your hair into spikes using a styling gel or wax. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up. Use your fingers to shape the hair into spikes or points, depending on your preference.

Step by Step

1. Start with a short haircut on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top longer.

2. Apply a small amount of styling gel or wax to your fingertips.

3. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.

4. Use your fingers to shape the hair into spikes or points.

5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

1. Use a quality styling product that's designed for short hair.

2. Don't use too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

3. Experiment with different styles and shapes to find the one that works best for you.

4. Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

FAQ

How long does it take to style short spiky hair?

It only takes a few minutes to style short spiky hair. The key is to use the right styling product and work it through your hair evenly.

Can short spiky hair work with all hair types?

Yes, short spiky hair can work with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair. The key is to have the right haircut and use the right styling product.

How often do I need to visit the barber or hairstylist to maintain short spiky hair?

You should visit your barber or hairstylist every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and length of your short spiky hair.

Hair Care

To keep your short spiky hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, mens hair short spiky is a trendy hairstyle that's easy to achieve and maintain. With the right haircut and styling product, you can create a look that's both stylish and low-maintenance. Follow our tips and advice, and you'll be rocking the short spiky hairstyle in no time.
