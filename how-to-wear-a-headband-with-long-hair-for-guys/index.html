How To Wear A Headband With Long Hair For Guys

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a guy with long hair who wants to incorporate a headband into your hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will go over all the steps necessary to wear a headband with long hair for guys. Whether you're going for a casual, athletic, or trendy look, we have got you covered.

How to Wear a Headband with Long Hair for Guys

Step 1: Choose the Right Headband

The first step in wearing a headband with long hair for guys is to choose the right headband. There are a variety of headbands available on the market, so it's essential to choose one that fits your style and hair type. For guys with long hair, we recommend choosing a headband that is at least one inch wide to ensure that it stays in place.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you put on your headband, it's essential to prepare your hair. Long hair can be prone to tangles and knots, so it's best to comb or brush your hair before wearing a headband. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a leave-in conditioner to prevent frizz.

Step 3: Position the Headband

Once your hair is prepared, it's time to position the headband. Place the headband on the top of your head, positioning it about an inch behind your hairline. Make sure the headband is snug, but not too tight, as you don't want it to cause any discomfort.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your headband is in place, it's time to style your hair. For a casual look, you can leave your hair down or pull it back into a low ponytail. For a trendy look, you can try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun. If you're going for an athletic look, you can slick your hair back with gel or pomade.

Tips for Wearing a Headband with Long Hair for Guys

Now that you know how to wear a headband with long hair for guys, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Experiment with different headband styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Avoid wearing a headband too tightly, as this can cause headaches.
  • Choose a headband made of a breathable material, such as cotton, to prevent sweat buildup.
  • Consider wearing a headband during outdoor activities, such as hiking or running, to keep your hair out of your face.

FAQ About Wearing a Headband with Long Hair for Guys

Can guys with short hair wear headbands?

Yes, guys with short hair can wear headbands. However, they may want to choose a narrower headband to ensure that it stays in place.

How can I prevent my headband from slipping?

If your headband is slipping, you may want to try using bobby pins to secure it in place. You can also try using hairspray or texturizing spray to add grip to your hair.

Hair Care for Guys with Long Hair

Wearing a headband with long hair is not only a great style choice but also helps keep hair healthy. Here are some hair care tips for guys with long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to wear a headband with long hair for guys, it's time to try out some new styles. Remember to choose the right headband, prepare your hair, position the headband correctly, and style your hair to achieve the perfect look. Happy styling!
