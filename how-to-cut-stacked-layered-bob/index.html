How To Cut Stacked Layered Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a chic and versatile hairstyle that can take your look to the next level? The stacked layered bob is a trendy haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair while keeping it short and manageable. If you want to learn how to cut stacked layered bob, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of creating this stylish haircut, from choosing the right tools and products to trimming and layering your hair. Let's get started!

Description

The stacked layered bob is a short haircut that features stacked layers at the back of the head and longer layers at the front. This haircut creates a dramatic and flattering shape that accentuates your facial features and adds volume to your hair. The stacked layers at the back create a rounded shape that lifts the hair, while the longer layers at the front frame your face and soften your features. This haircut is perfect for women who want to add some edge and sophistication to their look without sacrificing the length of their hair.

How to Cut Stacked Layered Bob

Step 1: Gather Your Tools and Products

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools and products on hand. You'll need:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Clips
  • A spray bottle with water
  • Styling products (optional)

Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends, so it's important to use high-quality scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before cutting it. Your hair should be clean and free of tangles, as this will make it easier to cut and style. If you have curly or wavy hair, consider straightening it before cutting it to get a more precise cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Clip the top section of your hair on top of your head, so it's out of the way. Then, divide the bottom section of your hair into two equal parts, one on each side of your head. Clip the left section of your hair away from the right section.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Start by cutting the back of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the right section and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across, using the fingers as a guide. Cut the hair at an angle, so it's slightly shorter at the back and longer at the front. Repeat this process with the rest of the hair on the right side, then do the same on the left side.

Step 5: Cut the Front

Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, so it's longer at the front and shorter at the back. Continue cutting small sections of hair at an angle, working your way from the front to the back of your head. Make sure the layers are even on both sides.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Use your scissors to blend the layers together. Take small sections of hair and cut into them at an angle, so the layers blend together seamlessly. This will create a smooth and natural transition between the different layers of your hair.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make sure the layers are even and the shape of your hair is balanced. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them carefully with your scissors.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect stacked layered bob:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections, as you become more comfortable with the process.
  • Use a spray bottle with water to keep your hair damp while cutting it. This will make it easier to control and cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to cut small amounts of hair and check your work as you go, rather than cutting too much and having to start over.
  • Consider using styling products to enhance the volume and texture of your hair. For example, you can use mousse or a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a stacked layered bob:

Q: Can I cut a stacked layered bob on my own?

A: While it's possible to cut a stacked layered bob on your own, we recommend going to a professional stylist for best results. A stylist can help you achieve the perfect shape and balance for your hair, and can also suggest styling products and techniques to maintain your haircut.

Q: How often should I get my stacked layered bob trimmed?

A: To maintain the shape and texture of your stacked layered bob, we recommend getting it trimmed every 6-8 weeks. Regular trims will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Q: Is the stacked layered bob suitable for all hair types?

A: The stacked layered bob can work well on most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it may not be the best choice for very fine or thin hair, as it can make the hair appear even thinner. If you have very thick or coarse hair, you may need to have your hair thinned out to achieve the desired shape.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining your stacked layered bob:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you need to use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Cutting a stacked layered bob can be a fun and rewarding experience that can transform your look and boost your confidence. Follow these simple steps and tips, and you'll be rocking a chic and stylish haircut in no time!
