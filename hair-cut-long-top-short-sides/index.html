Hair Cut Long Top Short Sides A Guide To This Stylish Men s Haircut

Written by Frank Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then the hair cut long top short sides is definitely worth considering. Also known as the disconnected undercut, this style features longer hair on the top that gradually fades into shorter sides and back. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

Description

The hair cut long top short sides is a variation of the classic undercut hairstyle. It involves keeping the hair on top of the head long and disconnected from the shorter sides and back. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it’s typically between 2 to 6 inches. The sides and back are usually cut short, ranging from a buzz cut to a fade. This creates a sharp contrast between the long top and short sides, giving the hairstyle a modern and edgy look.

How to

If you’re interested in getting a hair cut long top short sides, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose the Length of Your Hair on Top

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your hair type and face shape. If you have thick hair, you can go for a longer length. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the hair shorter on top to create a more angular look.

Step 2: Cut the Hair on Top

The hair on top should be cut evenly, following the natural shape of your head. You can choose to have layers added to create texture and movement. The stylist will use scissors or a razor to achieve the desired length and style.

Step 3: Fade the Sides and Back

The sides and back should be faded gradually, starting with a longer length at the top and getting shorter towards the bottom. The fade can be a low fade, mid fade, or high fade, depending on your preference. The stylist will use clippers to achieve the desired length and style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair using a product of your choice. You can go for a slicked-back look, a messy, textured style, or a classic side part.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you’re considering a hair cut long top short sides:

- Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your stylist. - Ask your stylist for advice on the best length and style for your hair type and face shape. - Invest in a good styling product to keep your hair in place and looking sharp. - Schedule regular appointments with your stylist to maintain the length and style of your hair.

FAQ

Will a hair cut long top short sides suit my face shape?

The hair cut long top short sides can be customized to suit different face shapes. Talk to your stylist about the best length and style for your face shape.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair. Choose a product that provides the hold and shine you want.

How often should I get a haircut?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools. - Avoid over-styling your hair with too much product or heat. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Overall, the hair cut long top short sides is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different preferences and face shapes. Talk to your stylist about how you can achieve this look and keep your hair looking its best.
