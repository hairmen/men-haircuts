How To Make Ponytail Look Higher Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read
Are you tired of your ponytail looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to know how to make your ponytail look higher and more voluminous? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make your ponytail look higher and fuller without using any hair extensions or complicated styling tools. Let's get started!

Description

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. It's simple, elegant, and versatile, making it perfect for any occasion. However, not everyone knows how to make their ponytail look higher and more voluminous. Some people struggle with flat or limp ponytails, which can be frustrating. Fortunately, there are several tips and tricks that you can try to make your ponytail look higher and fuller.

How to Make Ponytail Look Higher: Step by Step

If you want to make your ponytail look higher and more voluminous, follow these simple steps: 1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make it clean and soft. 2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots. 3. Apply some volumizing mousse or spray to your hair to add texture and hold. 4. Brush your hair back and gather it into a high ponytail. 5. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band. 6. Use a comb or your fingers to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add more volume. 7. Smooth the top layer of your hair with a brush or your fingers. 8. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it. 9. Secure the wrapped section of hair with a bobby pin. 10. Spray some hairspray over your ponytail to hold it in place.

Tips to Make Ponytail Look Higher

Here are some tips to make your ponytail look higher and more voluminous: 1. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable. 2. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. 3. Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it. 4. Use a boar bristle brush to smooth the top layer of your hair. 5. Pull your ponytail tight to make it look higher.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my ponytail look higher without teasing my hair? A: Yes, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold to your hair instead of teasing it. Q: How do I hide the elastic band in my ponytail? A: Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it. Secure the wrapped section of hair with a bobby pin.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips: 1. Wash and condition your hair regularly. 2. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. 3. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair. 4. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth. 5. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. In conclusion, making your ponytail look higher and more voluminous is easy with these tips and tricks. Whether you're going to work, a party, or a casual outing, a high ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can elevate your look. Try these steps and see the difference for yourself!
