Best Pixie Cuts For Fine Hair 2020

Written by Petter Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you might think that a pixie cut is not for you. However, there are many pixie cuts that can work well for fine hair, giving you a stylish and low-maintenance look. In this article, we will discuss the best pixie cuts for fine hair in 2020, including how to style them and how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Pixie cuts are short hairstyles that are typically cut close to the scalp. They are great for fine hair because they can give the illusion of thickness and volume. In 2020, the best pixie cuts for fine hair include:

1. The Classic Pixie

The classic pixie is a timeless look that will never go out of style. It involves short layers that are cut close to the scalp, with longer layers on top that can be styled in a variety of ways.

2. The Textured Pixie

The textured pixie is a more modern take on the classic pixie. It involves adding texture to the hair with choppy layers and a messy, tousled look.

3. The Layered Pixie

The layered pixie is a great option for fine hair because it can add volume and movement to the hair. It involves longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides and back.

How to

To style a pixie cut for fine hair, you will need a few basic tools, including a hair dryer, a flat iron, and some styling products. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie cut:

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the roots.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and definition to your hair.
  6. Use your fingers to style your hair into your desired look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your pixie cut:

  • Choose a pixie cut that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling iron.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your pixie cut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut make my fine hair look thinner?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make your fine hair look thicker and fuller, especially if you choose a layered or textured style.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with a pixie cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people with a pixie cut can wash their hair every other day or every three days.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, there are many great pixie cuts for fine hair in 2020. By choosing the right style and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance look that complements your unique style and personality.
