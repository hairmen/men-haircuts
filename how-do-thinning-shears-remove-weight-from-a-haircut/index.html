How Do Thinning Shears Remove Weight From A Haircut

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to remove excess weight from your hair without sacrificing length, thinning shears may be the solution you're looking for. These specialized scissors are designed to remove bulk and texture from the hair, creating a lighter, more manageable style. In this article, we'll explore the ins and outs of how thinning shears work and how you can use them to achieve your desired look.

What are Thinning Shears?

Thinning shears, also known as texturizing shears or blending shears, are scissors that have been designed with teeth on one or both blades. These teeth are designed to remove hair in a more gradual and natural way than traditional scissors, which can create blunt or choppy edges. Thinning shears are most commonly used to remove bulk from thick hair, create layers, blend different lengths together, or create texture in a style.

How Do Thinning Shears Work?

Thinning shears work by cutting some, but not all, of the hair in a given section. As the teeth on the scissors move through the hair, they remove some strands while leaving others intact. This creates a more natural, blended effect that removes bulk without sacrificing length. The amount of hair removed depends on the spacing and size of the teeth on the scissors, as well as the angle and technique used by the stylist.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Thinning Shears

If you're interested in using thinning shears to remove weight from your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Section your hair into smaller sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Hold the thinning shears at a slight angle and comb through a small section of hair.
  4. Close the shears partway and move them down the length of the hair, opening and closing the blades as you go.
  5. Repeat this process throughout the rest of your hair, taking care to blend the cut sections with the uncut sections.
  6. Style your hair as desired, using heat protectant products if using heat styling tools.

It's important to note that while thinning shears can be a useful tool for removing weight from the hair, they should be used with caution. Overuse or improper technique can lead to uneven or choppy results, or even damage to the hair.

Tips for Using Thinning Shears

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when using thinning shears:

  • Use a light touch and take small sections of hair at a time.
  • Start with a small amount of hair and gradually increase the amount you're cutting as you gain experience.
  • Use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type and desired result.
  • Take breaks throughout the process to step back and assess your progress.
  • Practice on mannequin heads or willing friends before attempting to use thinning shears on your own hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about using thinning shears:

  • Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?
  • How often should thinning shears be used on the hair?
  • Are thinning shears damaging to the hair?
  • Can thinning shears be used to create layers?
  • How long does it take to learn how to use thinning shears effectively?

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're using thinning shears or not, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant product when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote strong, healthy hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors.

In conclusion, thinning shears can be a useful tool for removing weight from the hair and creating a lighter, more manageable style. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking results that will leave you feeling confident and stylish.
