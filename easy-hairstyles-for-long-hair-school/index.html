Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair School

Written by Luffy Oct 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will be discussing easy hairstyles for long hair school. We understand that school mornings can be busy and hectic, leaving you with little time to style your hair. These hairstyles are quick, easy, and perfect for those rushed mornings. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we jump into the hairstyles, here are some quick tips to help you get started:

  • Invest in some hair ties and bobby pins to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to your ends to keep them from drying out.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Step by Step

The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for school. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself.
  3. Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the ponytail and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, effortless look.

The Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still show off their length. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Gather the top section of your hair, starting from your temples.
  2. Secure the hair with a hair tie or clip at the back of your head.
  3. If desired, add a cute hair accessory such as a bow or headband.

The Side Braid

The side braid is a chic and effortless hairstyle that is perfect for school. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Brush your hair to one side and divide it into three sections.
  2. Start braiding your hair, incorporating more hair from the section as you go.
  3. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Use dry shampoo to freshen up your hair on those days when you don't have time to wash it.
  • Take care of your hair by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your friends for help if you're struggling with a particular hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: Unfortunately, there is no magic solution to make your hair grow faster. However, you can promote healthy hair growth by eating a balanced diet, getting enough sleep, and taking care of your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go a few days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash it every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

We hope these easy hairstyles for long hair school have given you some inspiration for your next school day. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!
