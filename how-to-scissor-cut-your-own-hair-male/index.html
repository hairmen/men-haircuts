How To Scissor Cut Your Own Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to scissor cut your own hair male. With salons and barbershops closed due to the pandemic, many men have taken it upon themselves to groom their hair. Scissor cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools, techniques and a little bit of practice, you can achieve professional-looking results. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to

The first step in scissor cutting your own hair is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, hair clippers, and a mirror. It is important to use sharp scissors as dull ones can cause split ends and make the haircut look uneven.

Next, decide on the length you want your hair to be. It is always better to start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's gone.

Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Comb it out to remove any tangles and knots. It is best to cut your hair when it is dry as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

Begin by sectioning your hair. Use the comb to create a part in the middle of your head and clip one side away. Start cutting one section at a time, beginning at the back of your head and working your way towards the front.

Use the comb to lift a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to trim the hair in a straight line. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting off too much hair at once.

Continue cutting sections of hair until you have cut all of the hair on one side of your head. Repeat the process on the other side of your head, making sure to keep the hair length even on both sides.

Step by step

Step 1: Gather all the necessary tools, including sharp scissors, a comb, hair clippers, and a mirror.

Step 2: Decide on the length you want your hair to be and start with a longer length.

Step 3: Wash and towel-dry your hair, comb it out to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 4: Section your hair, starting at the back of your head and working your way towards the front.

Step 5: Use the comb to lift a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers.

Step 6: Use the scissors to trim the hair in a straight line, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 7: Continue cutting sections of hair until you have cut all of the hair on one side of your head.

Step 8: Repeat the process on the other side of your head, making sure to keep the hair length even on both sides.

Tips

- Invest in a good pair of scissors

- Start with a longer length

- Cut hair in small sections at a time

- Keep the hair length even on both sides

- Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated

FAQ

Q: Can I scissor cut my own hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, it may be more difficult to cut curly hair evenly. It is best to cut curly hair when it is dry to avoid uneven cuts.

Q: Can I use hair clippers instead of scissors?

A: Yes, you can. However, hair clippers may not be as precise as scissors and may not give you the same level of control.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using products that are suitable for your hair type. If you have long hair, you may want to consider using a leave-in conditioner to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to scissor cut your own hair male, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. It may take some practice, but with patience and the right techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
