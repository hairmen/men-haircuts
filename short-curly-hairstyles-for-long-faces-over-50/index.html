Short Curly Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 50 A Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its thickness and volume, making it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that will complement our facial features. For women with long faces over 50, adding volume and texture to their hair is crucial in creating a flattering look. Short curly hairstyles are an excellent option for women who want to add volume and texture to their hair while emphasizing their facial features.

How to Choose the Right Short Curly Hairstyle for Long Faces Over 50

Choosing the right short curly hairstyle for your long face over 50 can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Hair Texture

Curly hair tends to have more volume, making it an excellent option for women with long faces over 50. However, it's essential to consider your hair texture before choosing a hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut to add volume and texture to your hair.

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a short curly hairstyle for your long face, it's essential to consider your face shape. A short curly bob or a pixie cut with textured layers is an excellent option for women with long faces. These hairstyles add volume to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a significant role in choosing the right short curly hairstyle for your long face over 50. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that requires minimal maintenance.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair for Long Faces Over 50

Styling short curly hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and voluminous look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short curly hair for long faces over 50:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's essential to use products that hydrate and nourish your hair.

Step 2: Apply Hair Products

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or cream. This will help define your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or wand to define your curls further. You can also use a styling cream or pomade to add texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair for Long Faces Over 50

Maintaining short curly hair requires regular maintenance to keep your curls looking voluminous and healthy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly hair:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape and style of your short curly hair. Aim to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep your curls looking healthy and voluminous.

Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products is crucial for maintaining healthy and voluminous curls. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and add volume to your hair.

Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days to keep your curls looking healthy and voluminous.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best short curly hairstyles for long faces over 50?

The best short curly hairstyles for long faces over 50 include a curly bob, a pixie cut with textured layers, and a curly shag haircut.

How do I style my short curly hair for a special occasion?

You can style your short curly hair for a special occasion by adding accessories, such as a hair clip or headband, or by wearing your hair in an updo.

What hair products should I use for my short curly hair?

You should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a curl-enhancing product, and a styling cream or pomade to maintain your short curly hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair for Long Faces Over 50

Follow these hair care tips to keep your short curly hair looking healthy and voluminous:

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and help define your curls.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using hair products with SPF. The sun can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.

Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep your curls looking healthy and voluminous. Use a deep conditioning treatment that is specifically designed for curly hair.

In conclusion, short curly hairstyles are an excellent option for women with long faces over 50. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and voluminous look that will complement your facial features. Remember to maintain your short curly hair regularly by trimming it, using the right hair products, and avoiding overwashing it.
