Do Hazel Eyes Look Better With Dark Or Light Hair

Written by Robby Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing the perfect hair color, many people often wonder if hazel eyes look better with dark or light hair. Hazel eyes are a unique blend of green, brown, and gold, which makes them incredibly versatile and able to complement a range of hair colors. In this article, we'll explore the best hair colors for hazel eyes and provide you with some tips on how to make the most of your gorgeous eye color.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone?

Before choosing a hair color that complements your hazel eyes, it's important to determine your skin tone. There are three basic skin undertones: cool, warm, and neutral. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't determine whether your veins appear blue or green or if you have a mix of blue and green, you have a neutral undertone.

Best Hair Colors for Hazel Eyes Based on Skin Tone

For Cool Undertones:

If you have a cool undertone, the best hair colors for hazel eyes are cool shades of brown, blonde, and black. Some examples of cool brown shades include ash brown, cool dark brown, and cool light brown. For blonde shades, try platinum blonde or ash blonde. For black shades, opt for cool black or blue-black.

For Warm Undertones:

If you have a warm undertone, the best hair colors for hazel eyes are warm shades of brown, blonde, and red. Some examples of warm brown shades include golden brown, honey brown, and copper brown. For blonde shades, try golden blonde or honey blonde. For red shades, try auburn or strawberry blonde.

For Neutral Undertones:

If you have a neutral undertone, you're lucky because you can pull off a wide range of hair colors! Some great options for hazel eyes include warm chocolate brown, caramel blonde, and dark red.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Best Hair Color for Hazel Eyes

1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist.

2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your hazel eyes.

3. Consider your lifestyle and maintenance level when choosing a hair color.

4. Consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure about which hair color to choose.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've chosen the perfect hair color for your hazel eyes, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

- Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair color.

- Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and fade the color.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

- Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as this can damage your hair and fade the color.

FAQs

Q: Can hazel eyes look good with any hair color?

A: Yes, hazel eyes are incredibly versatile and can look good with a wide range of hair colors.

Q: Do warm or cool hair colors look better with hazel eyes?

A: It depends on your skin tone. If you have a warm undertone, warm hair colors will look better on you. If you have a cool undertone, cool hair colors will look better on you.

Q: Can hazel eyes change color based on the hair color you choose?

A: No, hazel eyes won't change color based on the hair color you choose. However, certain hair colors can enhance the green, brown, or gold tones in your eyes and make them stand out more.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there's no right or wrong answer when it comes to choosing the best hair color for hazel eyes. It all comes down to your skin tone and personal preference. Whether you choose a warm or cool hair color, the key is to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your beautiful hazel eyes. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
