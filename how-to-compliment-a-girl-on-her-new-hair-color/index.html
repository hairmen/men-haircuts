How To Compliment A Girl On Her New Hair Color Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to compliment a girl on her new hair color but not sure what to say? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to compliment a girl on her new hair color in a way that will make her feel amazing. Whether you're a guy or a girl, these tips will help you give the perfect compliment.

How to Compliment a Girl on Her New Hair Color

Complimenting someone on their new hair color can be tricky. You want to make sure that your compliment is genuine and not offensive. Here are some tips that you can use to compliment a girl on her new hair color:

1. Be Specific

Instead of just saying "I like your hair," be specific about what you like. For example, you can say "Your new blonde highlights really suit you" or "Your new red hair brings out the color of your eyes." Being specific shows that you have noticed the change and appreciate it.

2. Be Sincere

Girls can tell when a compliment is insincere. Make sure that your compliment is genuine and comes from the heart. If you're not sure what to say, think about what you genuinely like about the new hair color and express that.

3. Use Positive Language

Using positive language can make a big difference in how your compliment is received. Instead of saying "Your hair isn't as bad as it used to be," say "Your new hair color looks great on you." Positive language can make the person feel good about themselves and the change.

4. Don't Compare

Comparing the new hair color to the old one can come across as negative. Instead, focus on the positive aspects of the new hair color and how it suits the person. For example, you can say "Your new hair color is so vibrant and really suits your skin tone" instead of "Your old hair color was dull."

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to compliment a girl on her new hair color:

1. Notice the Change

Make sure that you notice the change in her hair color. If you're not sure, ask her if she's done something different with her hair.

2. Be Genuine

Make sure that your compliment is genuine and comes from the heart. Think about what you genuinely like about the new hair color and express that.

3. Be Specific

Be specific about what you like about the new hair color. This shows that you have noticed the change and appreciate it.

4. Use Positive Language

Use positive language to make the person feel good about themselves and the change.

5. Don't Compare

Focus on the positive aspects of the new hair color and how it suits the person. Don't compare the new hair color to the old one.

Tips for Complimenting a Girl on Her New Hair Color

Here are some additional tips for complimenting a girl on her new hair color:

1. Be Confident

Confidence is key when giving a compliment. Speak clearly and with confidence.

2. Be Respectful

Make sure that your compliment is respectful and appropriate.

3. Be Mindful of the Setting

Make sure that the setting is appropriate for giving a compliment. For example, it may not be appropriate to give a compliment in a professional setting.

FAQs

What if I don't like the new hair color?

If you don't like the new hair color, it's best to keep your opinion to yourself. You don't want to hurt the person's feelings.

What if I'm not good at giving compliments?

Practice makes perfect. Start by giving compliments to your friends and family members. Over time, you'll become more comfortable giving compliments.

Is it okay to touch someone's hair when giving a compliment?

No, it's not okay to touch someone's hair without their permission. Stick to verbal compliments.

Hair Care

Changing your hair color can be a fun way to switch up your look. However, it's important to take care of your hair after making a change. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

1. Use a Shampoo and Conditioner for Color-Treated Hair

Using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair can help preserve the color and prevent fading.

2. Avoid Hot Tools

Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage the hair and cause the color to fade.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can cause the color to fade. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Complimenting a girl on her new hair color is a great way to show that you're paying attention and that you appreciate the change. Remember to be specific, sincere, and use positive language. With these tips, you'll be able to give the perfect compliment every time.
