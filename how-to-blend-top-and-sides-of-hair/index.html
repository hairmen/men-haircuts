How To Blend Top And Sides Of Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 26, 2023 ¬∑ 3 min read

If you are looking to give your hair a new look, blending the top and sides of your hair can be a great option. However, blending hair can be challenging, especially if you are not familiar with the process. In this guide, we will discuss how to blend top and sides of hair in a step-by-step manner. We will also provide tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect look for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Blend Top and Sides of Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Blending Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Blend Top and Sides of Hair

Blending the top and sides of hair involves cutting the hair in a way that creates a smooth transition between the two areas. There are different methods to blend hair, depending on the length and texture of your hair. Here are some general tips to follow:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp, quality scissors or clippers.
  • Take your time and work in small sections.
  • Blend hair gradually, to avoid any sudden changes in length or texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to blend top and sides of hair:

  1. Start by dividing your hair into three sections: top, sides, and back.
  2. Clip or tie the back section, so it does not interfere with your work.
  3. Start with the sides, using clippers or scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Blend the sides with the top, by cutting the hair at an angle and using scissors to thin out any thick areas.
  5. Work on the back section next, using the same technique to blend it with the sides and top.
  6. Check your work in the mirror, and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Wash and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Blending Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect blend for your hair:

  • Experiment with different lengths and angles to find the best look for your hair.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors or clippers, and to ensure even cuts.
  • If you are unsure about cutting your hair, consider going to a professional stylist.
  • Take good care of your hair, by using quality products and avoiding excessive heat or chemical treatments.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about blending top and sides of hair:

  • How often should I blend my hair? It depends on the length and texture of your hair. Generally, you may need to blend your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your look.
  • Can I blend my hair at home? Yes, you can blend your hair at home, but it may be a good idea to seek professional help if you are unsure about your cutting skills.
  • What if I make a mistake while blending my hair? Do not panic. You can always make adjustments or go to a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid overusing heat styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated, to promote healthy hair growth.

Blending top and sides of hair can be a fun and rewarding way to change up your look. By following the tips and steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve a seamless blend that looks great and is easy to maintain. Remember to take good care of your hair, and enjoy your new style!
