The Trending Hair Cut Short Back Longer Front

Written by Robby Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is edgy and trendy? Look no further than the short back longer front haircut. This style has been popular for years and shows no signs of slowing down. Keep reading to learn more about this hairstyle, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The short back longer front haircut, also known as an asymmetrical bob, is a style where the back of the hair is cut short, while the front is left longer. This creates a dramatic and unique look that is perfect for those who want to stand out. The length of the front can vary, from just below the chin to shoulder length.

How to Get It

The best way to get this haircut is to go to a professional stylist. They will be able to create the perfect shape and length for your face shape and hair type. When you go to the salon, bring in pictures of the style you want to ensure that you and your stylist are on the same page.

During the haircut, your stylist will start by cutting the back of your hair short. They will then work on the front, cutting it to the desired length. The key to this style is to keep the front longer than the back, so make sure your stylist understands that.

Step by Step

1. Find a professional stylist who has experience with the short back longer front haircut.

2. Bring in pictures of the style you want.

3. Your stylist will start by cutting the back of your hair short.

4. They will then work on the front, cutting it to the desired length.

5. Your stylist will then style your hair, using products to enhance the texture and shape.

Tips

- If you have thin hair, ask your stylist to add layers to create more volume.

- Use a texturizing spray to add texture and movement to your hair.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to enhance the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this style. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

Q: Will this style work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this style can be customized to work for all face shapes. Your stylist will be able to create a shape that flatters your face.

Hair Care

The key to maintaining this haircut is to keep it trimmed regularly. The back of the hair will grow out faster than the front, so make sure to schedule regular appointments with your stylist to keep the shape intact.

When styling your hair, use products that enhance the texture and movement of your hair. Texturizing sprays, sea salt sprays, and dry shampoos are all great options. Avoid heavy products that will weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

In conclusion, the short back longer front haircut is a trendy and edgy style that is perfect for those who want to stand out. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can rock this style with confidence.
