How To Part Curly Hair To The Side A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to part curly hair to the side. If you’re looking to switch up your hairstyle, a side part can be a great way to add some volume and texture to your curls. In this post, we’ll walk you through the process of parting your hair to the side, as well as some tips for maintaining healthy, hydrated curls.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Part Curly Hair to the Side
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls
  4. FAQs

How to Part Curly Hair to the Side

Parting your curly hair to the side is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps:

Step 1: Determine Your Part

Before you start, decide which side you want your part to be on. It’s best to part your hair on the side that your curls naturally fall, as this will help your hair look more natural and less forced.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into two sections along the line where you want your part to be. Make sure the line is straight and even, as this will help your hair look more polished.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

With your hair divided into two sections, you can now style your hair as you normally would. You can leave your curls loose and natural, or you can use a curling iron or other styling tool to create more defined curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to part curly hair to the side, let’s walk through the process step by step:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Hydrated Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to make sure it’s clean and hydrated. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for curly hair, and make sure to apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Determine Your Part

Decide which side you want your part to be on. As we mentioned earlier, it’s best to part your hair on the side that your curls naturally fall.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into two sections along the line where you want your part to be. Make sure the line is straight and even.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

Apply your favorite styling products to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream depending on your hair type and the look you’re going for.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

With your hair divided into two sections, you can now style your hair as you normally would. You can leave your curls loose and natural, or you can use a curling iron or other styling tool to create more defined curls.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls

Now that you know how to part curly hair to the side, here are some tips for maintaining healthy, hydrated curls:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair is naturally more prone to dryness, so it’s important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Hair Oil

To keep your curls hydrated and healthy, apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil after washing your hair. This will help seal in moisture and prevent frizz.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Try to avoid using a curling iron or other heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray if you do.

Tip 4: Sleep with a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause friction and tangle your curls. Try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase instead to help keep your curls smooth and frizz-free.

FAQs

Q: Can I part my curly hair down the middle?

A: Absolutely! While parting your hair to the side can help add volume and texture, you can also part your hair down the middle if that’s the look you’re going for.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your curly hair no more than once or twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I still straighten my curly hair if I’ve parted it to the side?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair even if you’ve parted it to the side. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid heat styling too often to prevent damage.

We hope this guide has been helpful in teaching you how to part your curly hair to the side. Remember to take care of your curls by using gentle products and avoiding heat styling too often. Happy styling!
