What Hairstyle For A Round Face Flattering Cuts And Styles

Written by Eveline Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what hairstyle is best for a round face? Look no further! We've got you covered with tips and tricks to help you find the perfect cut and style to flatter your face shape.

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines with the widest point being at the cheeks. The length and width of the face are almost equal, creating a circular shape. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face shape and create the illusion of angles.

How To

When considering a new hairstyle, it's important to keep your face shape in mind. Here are some tips for finding the perfect cut and style for a round face:

1. Consider the Length

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how your face appears. Long hair can elongate the face, while short hair can make the face appear wider. For a round face, a length that falls between the chin and shoulder will be most flattering.

2. Add Layers

Layers can help to create the appearance of angles and add texture to your hair. Long layers that start at the chin can help to elongate the face, while shorter layers around the crown can add volume and height.

3. Avoid Blunt Bangs

Straight, blunt bangs can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to create the illusion of angles and draw attention to your eyes.

4. Embrace Waves and Curls

Soft waves and curls can help to add volume and texture to your hair, which can be especially flattering for a round face. Beachy waves or loose curls that start around the chin can help to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance.

Step by Step

Ready to try out a new hairstyle for your round face? Follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. Use a tape measure to measure the length and width of your face, then compare the measurements to determine if you have a round face.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that are flattering for your face shape. Look for cuts and styles that elongate the face and create the illusion of angles.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

If you're unsure which hairstyle is best for your round face, consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a cut and style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture and lifestyle.

Step 4: Experiment with Products

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, experiment with products to help enhance the look. Use volumizing products to add height and texture, or smoothing products to create sleek, straight styles.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face:

  • Try a side part to create the illusion of angles
  • Avoid styles that add volume at the sides of the face, such as wide headbands or bouffants
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and cuts until you find the perfect one for you

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that make the face appear wider, such as blunt bangs or styles with volume at the sides of the face.

What length is best for a round face?

A length that falls between the chin and shoulder is most flattering for a round face.

Can I wear my hair straight with a round face?

Yes! Straight hair can be flattering for a round face, as long as the length and cut are right for your face shape.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining a flattering hairstyle. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
