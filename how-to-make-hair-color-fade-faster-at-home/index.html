How To Make Hair Color Fade Faster At Home A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’ve recently colored your hair and want to speed up the fading process, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll share tips and tricks to help you make your hair color fade faster at home. Whether you’re looking to fix a hair color disaster or want to switch to a new shade, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When you color your hair, the dye penetrates the hair shaft and alters the color. Over time, the color fades as the dye molecules break down and wash out of the hair. However, some factors can speed up the fading process, such as exposure to sunlight, chlorine, and heat styling tools.

If you want to fade your hair color faster, you can use a variety of methods at home. These include washing your hair with hot water, using clarifying shampoos, and applying vitamin C to your hair. While these methods can help fade your hair color, keep in mind that they can also damage your hair if used too frequently.

How to

If you want to make your hair color fade faster at home, there are several methods you can try. Here are some of the most effective:

1. Wash your hair with hot water

Hot water can help open up the hair cuticle and allow the dye molecules to escape more quickly. To use this method, simply wash your hair with hot water instead of warm or cold water. However, keep in mind that hot water can also strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage, so use this method sparingly.

2. Use a clarifying shampoo

Clarifying shampoos are designed to remove buildup from your hair, including excess dye. To use this method, simply wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo once or twice a week. However, keep in mind that clarifying shampoos can be harsh on your hair, so be sure to use a deep conditioner afterwards.

3. Apply vitamin C to your hair

Vitamin C is a natural hair lightener and can help fade your hair color over time. To use this method, crush up some vitamin C tablets and mix them with shampoo to create a paste. Apply the paste to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair color fade faster at home:

  1. Wash your hair with hot water.
  2. Use a clarifying shampoo once or twice a week.
  3. Create a paste with crushed vitamin C tablets and shampoo.
  4. Apply the paste to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes.
  5. Rinse out the paste with warm water.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair color fade faster at home:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause your hair color to fade more slowly.
  • Avoid exposure to sunlight and chlorine, as they can also slow down the fading process.
  • Use a deep conditioner to help restore moisture to your hair after using clarifying shampoos.
  • Consider using a color remover product if you want to remove your hair color completely.

FAQ

How long does it take for hair color to fade naturally?

It can take anywhere from 4-12 weeks for hair color to fade naturally, depending on the type of dye used and how often you wash your hair.

Can I use baking soda to fade my hair color?

Baking soda can be effective at removing buildup from your hair, but it can also be harsh and cause damage if used too frequently. It’s best to use a clarifying shampoo instead.

Will washing my hair with hot water damage it?

Washing your hair with hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage if done too frequently. It’s best to use warm or cool water instead.

Hair Care

If you want to keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid over-processing your hair with dyes and chemicals.

By following these tips, you can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best, whether you’re trying to fade your hair color or maintain a natural shade.
