Mid Length Grey Hairstyles Over 50 A Guide To Styling Your Hair

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle, mid length grey hairstyles might be the perfect choice for you. Not only is this length versatile and easy to maintain, but it can also help you embrace your natural grey hair in a stylish way. Whether you want to keep your hair straight, wavy, or curly, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll show you how to rock mid length grey hairstyles over 50.

Description

Mid length grey hairstyles over 50 can be worn by women of all ages and hair types. This length is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long enough to style but short enough to avoid the hassle of maintaining longer hair. Additionally, grey hair can be beautiful and stylish, and mid length hairstyles are perfect for embracing your natural grey hair.

How to Style Mid Length Grey Hair

Styling mid length grey hair is easy and there are many ways to do it. You can wear your hair straight, wavy, or curly to suit your personal style. Here are some tips for styling your mid length grey hair:

1. Straight

To wear your hair straight, start by washing it and applying a heat protectant spray. Then, use a straightener to create sleek, smooth strands. Finish with a shine spray to add a healthy glow to your hair.

2. Wavy

For a wavy look, start by washing your hair and applying a texturizing spray. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your waves in place.

3. Curly

To wear your hair curly, start by washing it and applying a curl-enhancing cream. Then, use a diffuser to dry your hair and create defined curls. Finish with a lightweight hair oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your mid length grey hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Apply products

Apply any styling products you want to use, such as heat protectant spray, texturizing spray, or curl-enhancing cream.

3. Style your hair

Use your preferred styling tool to create the look you want, whether that's straight, wavy, or curly.

4. Finish with product

Finish with a light hairspray, shine spray, or hair oil to keep your style in place and add shine.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Grey Hair

Here are some tips for styling mid length grey hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools
  • Invest in a high-quality straightener, curling iron, or diffuser
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Try a new hairstyle to switch up your look
  • Use a lightweight hair oil to prevent frizz and add shine

FAQ

What are some popular mid length grey hairstyles over 50?

Some popular mid length grey hairstyles over 50 include layered cuts, blunt bobs, and long bangs. These styles can be customized to suit your personal style and hair type.

How often should I wash my mid length grey hair?

You should wash your mid length grey hair as often as you need to. If your hair is oily, you may need to wash it more frequently. If your hair is dry, you may be able to wash it less often.

How can I prevent my mid length grey hair from looking dull?

To prevent your mid length grey hair from looking dull, use a shine spray or hair oil to add a healthy glow. Additionally, make sure you're using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Hair Care for Mid Length Grey Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your mid length grey hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy

Conclusion

Mid length grey hairstyles over 50 are a great option for women who want to embrace their natural grey hair in a stylish way. Whether you prefer straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are many ways to style your mid length grey hair. Use the tips and tricks in this article to rock your mid length grey hair with confidence.
