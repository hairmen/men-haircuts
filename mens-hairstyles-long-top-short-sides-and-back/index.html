Mens Hairstyles Long Top Short Sides And Back A Guide To The Perfect Haircut

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's trendy, versatile, and easy to maintain, then the mens hairstyles long top short sides and back may be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity among men of all ages, and for good reason. It's a modern and stylish look that can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about mens hairstyles long top short sides and back, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The mens hairstyles long top short sides and back is a haircut that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair on the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's usually long enough to be styled in different ways, such as a quiff, pompadour, or slicked back. The sides and back are trimmed short, usually with clippers, to create a contrast with the longer hair on the top. This style is often referred to as an undercut or fade haircut, depending on the length of the hair on the sides and back.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting a mens hairstyles long top short sides and back, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style. You can bring a picture of the style you want to show your stylist, or you can describe it in detail. Make sure to communicate your preferences clearly, such as the length of the hair on the top, the type of fade or undercut you want, and any other details that are important to you.

Once you're in the chair, your stylist will use a combination of scissors and clippers to create the cut. They may start by cutting the hair on the top to the desired length, and then move on to the sides and back. Depending on the style you want, they may create a gradual fade by using different clipper guards, or they may create a more dramatic contrast by shaving the hair very short on the sides and back. The process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the cut.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mens hairstyles long top short sides and back:

  1. Choose a good barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style.
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want, or describe it in detail.
  3. Sit in the chair and let the stylist work their magic.
  4. They will start by cutting the hair on the top to the desired length.
  5. Then, they will move on to the sides and back, using scissors and clippers to create the desired length and contrast.
  6. They may use different clipper guards to create a fade, or they may shave the hair very short for a more dramatic contrast.
  7. Once the cut is complete, they will style it to your liking using products such as pomade or wax.
  8. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the perfect mens hairstyles long top short sides and back:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Communicate your preferences clearly to your stylist.
  • Maintain the style by getting regular trims and using quality hair products.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Be confident and rock your new look!

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about mens hairstyles long top short sides and back:

  • What face shape is best suited for this style?
  • Can this style be worn with different hair types?
  • How often should I get a trim?
  • What kind of products should I use to style my hair?
  • Can I wear this style to work or formal events?

Hair Care

Maintaining a mens hairstyles long top short sides and back is relatively easy, but it does require some care and attention. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a styling product that's suitable for your hair type, such as pomade or wax.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

So there you have it, everything you need to know about mens hairstyles long top short sides and back. Whether you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut or just want to switch up your look, this style is definitely worth considering. With the right care and attention, you can rock this look with confidence and style!
