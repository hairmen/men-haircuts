What Size Curling Iron For Beach Waves Short Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair and want to achieve beach waves, curling irons can be your best friend. But with so many sizes and types of curling irons available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is right for you. In this article, we'll discuss what size curling iron you should use for beach waves on short hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Beach waves are loose, casual, and effortless-looking curls that are perfect for short hair. To achieve this look, you need a curling iron that can create loose curls or waves. The size of the curling iron barrel determines the size of the curls. So, for beach waves, you need a curling iron with a larger barrel.

But how large should the barrel be? Generally, a curling iron with a barrel size of 1-2 inches is ideal for beach waves on short hair. However, the size of the barrel also depends on your hair length, texture, and thickness.

How To

The first step to achieving beach waves on short hair is to prep your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

Once your hair is dry, divide it into sections. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold the hair for a few seconds and release it. Repeat this process all over your head.

Step By Step

1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.

3. Divide your hair into sections.

4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel.

5. Hold the hair for a few seconds and release it.

6. Repeat the process all over your head.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect beach waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel size.
  • Wrap your hair around the curling iron barrel in different directions to create a natural look.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the ends to create a more natural look.
  • Use a sea salt spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Finish with a hairspray to keep your beach waves in place.

FAQ

Can I use a curling wand instead of a curling iron for beach waves on short hair?

Yes, you can use a curling wand instead of a curling iron. A curling wand is similar to a curling iron, but without the clamp. It can create loose waves and curls, which are perfect for beach waves on short hair.

What size curling iron should I use for beach waves on short hair?

A curling iron with a barrel size of 1-2 inches is ideal for beach waves on short hair. However, the size of the barrel also depends on your hair length, texture, and thickness.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Don't use hot tools on wet hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, a curling iron with a barrel size of 1-2 inches is ideal for beach waves on short hair. However, the size of the barrel also depends on your hair length, texture, and thickness. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to achieve perfect beach waves on your short hair.
