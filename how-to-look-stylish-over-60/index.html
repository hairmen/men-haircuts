How To Look Stylish Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Are you over 60 and looking to revamp your style? It's never too late to update your wardrobe and feel confident in your appearance. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to look stylish over 60.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Fashion Tips
  • Accessories

Hair Care

Your hairstyle can make a big impact on your overall look. As we age, our hair may become thinner and lose its luster, so it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

1. Invest in a Good Haircut

A good haircut can do wonders for your appearance. Find a stylist who specializes in styling hair for seniors, and work with them to find a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.

2. Use Quality Hair Products

Using quality hair products, such as shampoos, conditioners, and serums, can help improve the texture and shine of your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for aging hair.

3. Consider Coloring Your Hair

Many women over 60 choose to color their hair to cover gray or to add dimension to their hair. Consider working with a stylist to find a color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty.

Fashion Tips

When it comes to fashion, there are some general tips that can help you look stylish and put-together:

1. Invest in Quality Basics

Investing in quality basics, such as a well-fitting pair of jeans, a classic blazer, and a comfortable pair of shoes, can help you create a solid foundation for your wardrobe. Look for pieces that fit well and can be mixed and matched with other items in your closet.

2. Embrace Color and Pattern

Just because you're over 60 doesn't mean you have to stick to neutral colors and plain patterns. Embrace color and pattern in your wardrobe, and have fun with your fashion choices. Just make sure to choose colors and patterns that complement your skin tone and body shape.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Accessorize

Accessories can take an outfit from basic to stylish in an instant. Consider adding a statement necklace, a colorful scarf, or a bold pair of earrings to your outfit to add some personality.

Accessories

Speaking of accessories, there are some specific accessories that can help you look stylish over 60:

1. Comfortable Shoes

Comfortable shoes are a must-have for anyone over 60. Look for shoes that provide support and cushioning, while still being stylish. Ballet flats, loafers, and low-heeled boots are all great options.

2. A Classic Handbag

A classic handbag, such as a leather tote or a crossbody bag, can add sophistication to any outfit. Look for a bag that is big enough to hold your essentials, but not so big that it overwhelms your frame.

3. Sunglasses

Sunglasses not only protect your eyes from the sun, but they also add a stylish touch to your outfit. Look for sunglasses that complement your face shape and personal style.

Makeup

Makeup can also play a role in how stylish you look. Here are some makeup tips for women over 60:

1. Moisturize

As we age, our skin tends to become drier. Moisturizing regularly can help keep your skin looking healthy and radiant.

2. Choose a Flattering Foundation

Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and provides the right amount of coverage for your skin. Avoid heavy, cakey foundations, which can settle into fine lines and wrinkles.

3. Add Some Color

Adding some color to your cheeks and lips can help you look more vibrant and youthful. Choose a blush and lipstick that complement your skin tone.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear trendy clothes over 60?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose trends that work for your body shape and personal style.

Q: Should I avoid wearing certain colors over 60?

A: There are no hard and fast rules when it comes to color. Just make sure to choose colors that complement your skin tone and make you feel confident.

Q: How can I find a hairstylist who specializes in styling hair for seniors?

A: Ask for recommendations from friends and family members, or look for stylists who advertise themselves as specializing in senior hair care.

By following these tips and tricks, you can look stylish and confident at any age. Remember to have fun with your fashion choices and choose pieces that make you feel good.
