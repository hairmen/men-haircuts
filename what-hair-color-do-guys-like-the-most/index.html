What Hair Color Do Guys Like The Most A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to dating and relationships, it's no secret that physical attraction plays a big role. And while there are many factors that contribute to someone's attractiveness, hair color is one of the most noticeable and impactful. So, what hair color do guys like the most?

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as every guy has their own unique preferences. However, there are some general trends and patterns that can be observed when it comes to hair color and attraction.

According to a survey conducted by dating app Zoosk, the most attractive hair color for women, as rated by men, is blonde. This is followed by brown, black, and red. However, it's important to note that these preferences can vary based on the individual and their cultural background.

How To

If you're looking to change your hair color to attract more guys, it's important to do so in a way that suits your personal style and preferences. Here are some tips:

  • Consider your skin tone: Certain hair colors may complement your skin tone better than others. For instance, if you have a warm skin tone, you may look better with auburn or honey blonde hair, while cool skin tones may suit ash blonde or black hair better.
  • Consult a professional stylist: Changing your hair color can be a complex process, and it's best to seek the advice of a professional stylist. They can help you choose a color that flatters your face shape, skin tone, and eye color.
  • Choose a shade that matches your personality: Your hair color should reflect your personality and personal style. If you're bold and outgoing, you may want to try a bright red or platinum blonde. If you're more laid-back, a natural-looking brown or caramel may be a better fit.

Step By Step

If you're considering changing your hair color, here are the basic steps involved:

  • Choose a color: Consider your skin tone, eye color, and personal style when choosing a color.
  • Prepare your hair: If you're going lighter, you may need to bleach your hair first. If you're going darker or adding highlights, you may not need to do anything special.
  • Apply the color: Follow the instructions on the box carefully, or have a professional stylist apply the color.
  • Care for your hair: After coloring your hair, it's important to use products that are designed for color-treated hair, and to avoid excessive heat styling and exposure to the sun.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a hair color that guys will love:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you have a busy schedule and don't have time for frequent touch-ups, choose a low-maintenance color that won't require a lot of upkeep.
  • Think about your wardrobe: Your hair color should complement the colors and styles of clothing you wear most often.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment: If you're feeling bold, try a trendy new hair color like pastel pink or mermaid blue.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair color and attraction:

Q: Can changing my hair color really make me more attractive to guys?

A: While hair color is just one factor that contributes to attractiveness, it can make a noticeable difference in how you're perceived. However, it's important to choose a color that suits your personal style and complements your features.

Q: Do guys prefer natural hair colors or more unusual shades?

A: This can vary based on the guy and their personal preferences. Some may prefer natural-looking colors like blonde or brown, while others may find more unusual shades like pink or purple to be attractive.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home, or do I need to go to a salon?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's generally safer and more effective to have it done by a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use products designed for color-treated hair: These products are formulated to protect your hair color and keep it from fading.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling: Heat can damage your hair and cause your color to fade more quickly. Use low heat settings on your styling tools, or let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can cause your hair color to fade and become brassy. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when you're spending time outdoors.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your color look dull and faded.

In conclusion, there is no one "right" hair color when it comes to attracting guys. However, by considering your personal style, skin tone, and lifestyle, you can choose a color that makes you feel confident and attractive. And by taking good care of your hair, you can keep your color looking fresh and vibrant for as long as possible.
