How Do You Stop Hair Loss From Lupus Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Oct 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are living with lupus, you may have noticed that one of the most common symptoms is hair loss. This autoimmune disease can cause hair to fall out in patches, thinning hair, and even total hair loss. While there is no cure for lupus, there are ways to manage your symptoms, including hair loss. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to stop hair loss from lupus.

Table of Contents

  • Causes of Hair Loss in Lupus
  • Hair Care Tips for Lupus Patients
  • Treatment Options for Hair Loss in Lupus
  • FAQs

Causes of Hair Loss in Lupus

Hair loss in lupus is caused by inflammation that attacks hair follicles. This inflammation can be caused by lupus itself, medications used to treat lupus, or stress. Here are some of the most common causes of hair loss in lupus:

  • Lupus itself
  • Medications used to treat lupus, such as hydroxychloroquine and methotrexate
  • Stress
  • Hormonal changes
  • Nutritional deficiencies

If you are experiencing hair loss, it is important to speak with your doctor to determine the underlying cause and create a treatment plan.

Hair Care Tips for Lupus Patients

While there is no cure for hair loss in lupus, there are some steps you can take to care for your hair and prevent further damage. Here are some hair care tips for lupus patients:

  • Be gentle with your hair. Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to prevent damage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your scalp from the sun.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, especially vitamin D and iron.

It is also important to talk to your doctor about any hair care products you use. Some products may contain harsh chemicals that can further damage your hair.

Treatment Options for Hair Loss in Lupus

If you are experiencing hair loss from lupus, there are several treatment options available. Here are some of the most common:

  • Topical treatments, such as corticosteroids and minoxidil
  • Oral medications, such as hydroxychloroquine and methotrexate
  • Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy
  • Hair transplant surgery

It is important to talk to your doctor about which treatment option is best for you based on the underlying cause of your hair loss and your overall health.

FAQs

Can lupus cause permanent hair loss?

While hair loss from lupus can be temporary, it can also be permanent if the hair follicles are permanently damaged. However, with proper treatment, hair loss can often be managed.

Can stress cause hair loss in lupus?

Yes, stress can trigger hair loss in lupus patients.

Are there any natural remedies for hair loss in lupus?

While there is no cure for hair loss in lupus, there are some natural remedies that may help promote hair growth, such as essential oils and scalp massage. However, it is important to talk to your doctor before using any natural remedies.

In conclusion, hair loss is a common symptom of lupus, but there are ways to manage it. By taking good care of your hair, talking to your doctor about treatment options, and managing stress, you can reduce hair loss and promote hair growth.
