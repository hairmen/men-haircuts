How To Dress 50S Style Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 01, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're into vintage fashion, then you might have already thought about dressing in the 50s style. 50s fashion was all about femininity, elegance, and a refined look. To dress 50s style, you need to pay attention to the details, the silhouette, and the fabrics. In this guide, we'll show you how to dress 50s style step by step, from head to toe.

Description

The 50s was a decade of glamour, sophistication, and feminine style. It was the era of the hourglass silhouette, full skirts, and tailored jackets. Women's fashion was all about accentuating curves, showing off the waistline, and looking polished at all times. To dress 50s style, you need to embrace the feminine aesthetic and pay attention to every detail, from the hair to the shoes.

How to Dress 50s Style: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Silhouette

The 50s silhouette was all about the hourglass shape, so you need to choose a dress or a skirt that accentuates your waistline. Look for dresses with a fitted bodice and a full skirt, or pencil skirts that hug your hips and thighs.

Step 2: Choose the Right Fabric

The 50s fashion was all about luxurious fabrics, such as silk, satin, and taffeta. Look for dresses and skirts in these fabrics, or opt for cotton, linen, or wool for a more casual look.

Step 3: Choose the Right Colors

The 50s fashion was all about bold colors, such as red, blue, green, and yellow. You can also opt for pastel colors, such as pink, mint, and baby blue. Avoid black and white, as they were not very common in the 50s.

Step 4: Accessories

To complete your 50s look, you need to add the right accessories. Look for retro-style sunglasses, gloves, pearls, and a small handbag. You can also add a scarf to your hair or wear a brooch on your dress.

Step 5: Hair and Makeup

The 50s hair was all about curls and waves, so you need to curl your hair with rollers or use a curling iron. You can also add a headband or a scarf to your hair. For makeup, go for a bold red lipstick, winged eyeliner, and a rosy blush.

Tips

- Choose the right underwear to create the hourglass silhouette - Don't forget to wear stockings or tights - Wear high heels or retro-style flats - Look for vintage-inspired clothing or shop at vintage stores - Mix and match modern and vintage pieces for a unique look

FAQ

What was the 50s fashion?

The 50s fashion was all about the hourglass silhouette, full skirts, tailored jackets, and feminine details. It was a decade of glamour, sophistication, and elegance.

What colors were popular in the 50s?

Bold colors, such as red, blue, green, and yellow, were popular in the 50s. Pastel colors, such as pink, mint, and baby blue, were also common.

What kind of accessories should I wear with a 50s dress?

You can wear retro-style sunglasses, gloves, pearls, and a small handbag with a 50s dress. You can also add a scarf to your hair or wear a brooch on your dress.

Hair Care

To achieve the 50s hair look, you need to curl your hair with rollers or use a curling iron. Make sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray. You can also use a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your curls. Don't forget to use a hairspray to keep your curls in place.
