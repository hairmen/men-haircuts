How To Do 60S Hair Straighteners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve that classic 60s straight hair look, you've come to the right place. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to do 60s hair straighteners using modern tools and techniques. From prepping your hair to adding finishing touches, we've got you covered.

Description

Straight hair was all the rage in the 60s, and it's still a classic look today. Whether you're going for a retro vibe or just want to switch up your style, this guide will help you achieve that sleek, straight hair look.

How to

To start, you'll need a few basic tools and products. You'll need a good quality flat iron, heat protectant spray, and a comb. Depending on your hair type, you may also want to use a straightening serum or oil for added protection and shine.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start straightening, it's important to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual, then apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. This will help you work on one section at a time and ensure that you don't miss any spots. Clip up the sections you're not working on to keep them out of the way.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Start by taking a small section of hair and running the flat iron over it from root to tip. Repeat this process until you've straightened all the sections of your hair. Be sure to move the flat iron slowly and evenly through each section to avoid creating kinks or creases.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

Once you've straightened all your hair, you can add some finishing touches. Use a small amount of straightening serum or oil to add shine and tame any flyaways. You can also use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s straight hair look:

  • Use a good quality flat iron
  • Work in small sections
  • Move the flat iron slowly and evenly
  • Use a heat protectant spray
  • Use a straightening serum or oil for added shine
  • Use hairspray to hold your style in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 60s hair straighteners:

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can straighten curly hair with a flat iron. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to limit how often you straighten your hair to avoid heat damage. Try to straighten your hair no more than once a week.

Q: How do I keep my hair straight all day?

A: Use a hairspray or straightening serum to hold your style in place. You can also try wrapping your hair in a silk scarf before going to bed to help maintain your straight hair look.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a good quality conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently
  • Use a heat protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 60s straight hair look in no time. Whether you're going for a retro vibe or just want to switch up your style, straight hair is a classic look that never goes out of style.
