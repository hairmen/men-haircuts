How To Style Your Hair While Growing It Out For Guys

Written by Eveline Nov 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to growing out your hair as a guy, it can be a frustrating and awkward process. You may find yourself struggling with how to style your hair during this in-between stage. But fear not, we have put together a guide on how to style your hair while growing it out for guys.

Description

This article will provide you with tips and tricks on how to style your hair while growing it out as a guy. We will cover everything from the best hair products to use to how to style your hair for different occasions.

How to

1. Invest in the Right Hair Products

One of the most important things you can do when growing out your hair is to invest in the right hair products. You want to make sure you have a good shampoo and conditioner that will keep your hair healthy and nourished. You may also want to consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair smooth and manageable.

2. Consider a New Haircut

While you are growing out your hair, it may be a good idea to get a haircut every once in a while. This will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and can also help prevent split ends. Consider getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

3. Try Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles while you are growing out your hair. You may find that certain hairstyles work better for you than others. Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

4. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be a great way to keep your hair looking neat and stylish while you are growing it out. Consider using headbands, bandanas, or hats to keep your hair out of your face and looking great.

5. Embrace Your Natural Texture

While you are growing out your hair, it's important to embrace your natural texture. If you have curly hair, try using a curl defining cream to enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, consider using a hair straightener to achieve a sleek look.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Step 2: Apply Hair Products

Apply any hair products that you may be using, such as a leave-in conditioner or hair oil. This will help keep your hair smooth and manageable.

Step 3: Decide on a Hairstyle

Decide on a hairstyle that you want to try out. You may want to consider a new hairstyle every once in a while to keep things fresh and interesting.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Accessories

Finish off your hairstyle with hair accessories such as headbands, bandanas, or hats. This will help keep your hair looking neat and stylish.

Tips

- Be patient - growing out your hair takes time.
- Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
- Use hair products to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
- Consider getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

What are the best hair products to use when growing out your hair?

The best hair products to use when growing out your hair are a good quality shampoo and conditioner, a leave-in conditioner, and a hair oil. These products will help keep your hair healthy and nourished.

How often should I get a haircut when growing out my hair?

You should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks when growing out your hair. This will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and can also help prevent split ends.

What are some good hairstyles to try when growing out my hair?

Some good hairstyles to try when growing out your hair include the messy bun, the slicked back look, and the man bun. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

When growing out your hair, it's important to take good care of it. This means using good quality hair products, getting regular haircuts, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You should also avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can successfully grow out your hair and look great while doing it. Remember to be patient and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles!
