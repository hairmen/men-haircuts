How Do You Determine What Shape Your Face Is

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Have you ever wondered what shape your face is? Determining your face shape is essential for choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and even accessories that will complement your features. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine what shape your face is.

Table of Content

  • Step 1: Take a photo
  • Step 2: Identify facial features
  • Step 3: Compare
  • Tips for Determining Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Step 1: Take a photo

The first step in determining your face shape is to take a photo of yourself. Make sure that you take a photo of your face straight on and with no angle. This will help you see the proportions of your face more clearly.

Step 2: Identify facial features

Identify your facial features by looking at the photo you took. Look at the width of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and chin. Take note of the shape of your nose, lips, and eyes. These features will help you determine your face shape.

Step 3: Compare

After identifying your facial features, compare them to the different face shapes. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Look at the characteristics of each face shape and compare them to your features.

For example, if your forehead and jawline have the same width, with a narrow chin and high cheekbones, then you have an oval face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have the same width, with a round chin and full cheeks, then you have a round face shape.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

Here are some tips to help you determine your face shape:

  • Ask a friend to help you identify your facial features
  • Use a mirror to look at your face from different angles
  • Take note of the length and width of your face
  • Look at the shape of your hairline
  • Take into consideration the prominence of your cheekbones and jawline

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining face shape:

Q: Can face shape change over time?

A: No, your face shape is determined by bone structure and will not change over time.

Q: What if my face shape is in between two shapes?

A: It is possible to have a combination of two face shapes. Identify the features that are dominant and choose the face shape that is closest to your features.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle that will flatter your features. Here are some hair care tips for each face shape:

  • Oval: Try a layered bob or long waves
  • Round: Go for a long, layered cut or a side-swept bang
  • Square: Opt for a soft, layered cut or a side part
  • Heart: Choose a side-swept bang or a chin-length bob
  • Diamond: Try a short, layered cut or a side part

Determining your face shape is crucial for choosing the right hairstyle and makeup that will enhance your features. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be on your way to looking your best!
