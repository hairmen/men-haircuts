How To Make Your Hair Look Short Easy

Written by Luffy Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who loves the look of short hair but isn't quite ready to take the plunge, there are plenty of easy ways to make your hair look short without having to cut it all off. In this article, we'll be going over some simple tips and tricks for achieving the appearance of short hair without ever having to pick up a pair of scissors.

Description

Short hair has always been a popular trend, but not everyone is ready to commit to a drastic haircut. Whether you're growing out your hair, or just want to mix up your look without the commitment, there are plenty of ways to make your hair look short and stylish. We'll be covering easy techniques that will transform your long locks into a short and chic style in no time.

How-To

To make your hair look short, there are a few different techniques you can try:

1. Tuck and Pin

This method involves tucking your hair under itself and securing it with bobby pins. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom. Then, take the bottom section and roll it up towards the nape of your neck. Secure the roll with bobby pins, and then release the top section of your hair to cover the pins. This will give the illusion of a short bob.

2. Faux Bob

A faux bob is another easy way to make your hair look short without actually cutting it. Start by creating a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck. Then, tuck the ponytail under itself and secure it with bobby pins. Use your fingers to gently pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it. This style works great for special occasions or a night out.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you have long hair, a half-up, half-down style can help make it look shorter. Start by pulling the top half of your hair back into a ponytail or bun. Leave the bottom half loose, and add some waves or curls to create texture. This style is versatile and can be dressed up or down.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the tuck and pin style:

Step 1:

Separate the top section of your hair from the bottom.

Step 2:

Roll the bottom section up towards the nape of your neck.

Step 3:

Secure the roll with bobby pins.

Step 4:

Release the top section of your hair to cover the pins.

Step 5:

Use hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating these styles:

1. Use the Right Tools

Make sure you have the right tools on hand, such as bobby pins, hair ties, and a curling iron or straightener.

2. Add Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help make it look shorter. Try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and create a messy, undone look.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Accessories like headbands, scarves, and hair clips can add a fun and stylish touch to your short hair look.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about making your hair look short:

1. Will these styles work on all hair types?

Yes, these styles can work on all hair types, but some may require more bobby pins or extra styling products to keep the style in place.

2. How long will these styles stay in place?

These styles should stay in place for several hours with the help of hairspray and bobby pins. However, if you're planning on being active or outdoors, you may need to touch up the style throughout the day.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Overall, there are plenty of easy ways to make your hair look short without having to cut it all off. Whether you're looking for a temporary change or just want to experiment with different styles, these techniques are a great way to switch up your look without the commitment.
