How To Wear A Large Hair Clip A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Large hair clips are a popular hair accessory that can add a touch of elegance and style to any hairstyle. However, many people are unsure of how to wear a large hair clip, and end up not using them to their full potential. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to wear a large hair clip, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear a Large Hair Clip
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing a Large Hair Clip
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Advice

How to Wear a Large Hair Clip

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some basic tips for wearing a large hair clip:

  • Choose a hair clip that matches your hair color or complements your outfit
  • Start with a smaller hair clip if you are new to wearing them
  • Experiment with different styles and placements to find what works best for you

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let's get into the step-by-step guide on how to wear a large hair clip:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Choose the placement for your hair clip. You can place it on the side of your head, at the back, or on top of your head.
  3. Take a section of hair and twist it slightly.
  4. Place the hair clip over the twisted section of hair.
  5. Secure the hair clip by snapping it closed.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 with additional sections of hair until you achieve your desired look.

That's it! With these simple steps, you can easily wear a large hair clip and add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle.

Tips for Wearing a Large Hair Clip

Here are some additional tips for wearing a large hair clip:

  • Use a hair tie to secure your hair before placing the hair clip for added hold
  • Experiment with different sizes and styles of hair clips to find what works best for you
  • Try using multiple hair clips for a more intricate hairstyle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a large hair clip:

Can I wear a large hair clip with short hair?

Yes, you can! Simply take a small section of hair and follow the same steps for placement and securing as you would with longer hair.

Can I wear a large hair clip with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, a large hair clip can help to define and enhance your curls.

How do I clean my hair clip?

You can clean your hair clip by gently wiping it with a damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or cleaning products that could damage the clip.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, here are some tips for caring for your hair while wearing a large hair clip:

  • Don't wear a hair clip that is too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid wearing a hair clip in the same spot every day, as this can lead to hair breakage and thinning.
  • Be gentle when removing the hair clip to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.

With these tips and advice, you can confidently wear a large hair clip and add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle. Happy styling!
