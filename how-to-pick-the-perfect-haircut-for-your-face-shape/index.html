How To Pick The Perfect Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Daniel Sep 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, but it's crucial to pick one that complements your facial features. The right haircut can highlight your best features and detract from any flaws. In this guide, we'll show you how to pick the perfect haircut for your face shape.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Pick the Perfect Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step to picking the perfect haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that complements your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any haircut. However, to show off your features, consider a haircut that falls above or below your chin.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds angles and lengthens your face. A long bob or layered cut that falls below your chin can achieve this look. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt, as they can make your face look rounder.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face, choose a haircut that softens your angles. A layered cut that falls just below your chin or a long bob with side-swept bangs can achieve this look. Avoid blunt cuts or styles that are too short.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a haircut that adds width to your chin and jawline. A bob or lob that falls just below your chin or a layered cut with side-swept bangs can achieve this look. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt, as they can make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, choose a haircut that adds width to your forehead and jawline. A layered cut with side-swept bangs or a chin-length bob can achieve this look. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt, as they can make your face look too angular.

Long Face Shape

If you have a long face, choose a haircut that adds width to your face. A layered cut that falls just below your chin or a bob with side-swept bangs can achieve this look. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can make your face look even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, follow these steps to pick the perfect haircut:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape
  3. Consult with your stylist to find the best haircut for you
  4. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a haircut
  5. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your new haircut at home

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect haircut:

  • Consider your hair type and texture
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist
  • Be open to your stylist's suggestions and advice
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and style.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

Don't panic! Hair grows back, and your stylist can always make adjustments to your haircut. Communicate your concerns with your stylist and work together to find a solution.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair daily
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape and style
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny

Choosing the perfect haircut can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and consulting with your stylist, you can find a haircut that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style.
