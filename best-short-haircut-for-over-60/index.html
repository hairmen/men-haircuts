Best Short Haircut For Over 60 A Guide To Look Fabulous

Written by Christine Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle that will make you look and feel fabulous? Look no further! In this guide, we will explore the best short haircut for over 60, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining and styling your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair changes in texture and thickness. It's important to find a hairstyle that complements these changes and enhances our natural beauty. The best short haircut for over 60 is one that is easy to maintain, flattering, and versatile.

Short haircuts are ideal for those over 60 as they are low maintenance and can make you look younger. They also work well with thinning hair, as they can create the illusion of volume and thickness. There are many short haircuts to choose from, so it's important to find one that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How to

When choosing the best short haircut for over 60, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be very flattering. If you have a square-shaped face, a short layered haircut with soft waves can balance out your features. If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Almost any short haircut will suit you.

It's also important to consider your hair type. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut with choppy layers can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a short blunt cut can create a sleek and sophisticated look.

Step by step

1. Find inspiration by browsing through magazines or online galleries for short haircuts that appeal to you.

2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which short haircut will suit your face shape and hair type.

3. Be prepared to invest in regular haircuts to maintain your new hairstyle.

4. Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

Tips

1. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to thinning hair.

2. Use a smoothing serum or cream to tame frizz and flyaways.

3. Experiment with different styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, to create different looks.

4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily! A short haircut can actually make you look younger and more modern.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your short hairstyle.

Q: Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in many ways, from sleek and sophisticated to playful and tousled.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your short haircut, it's important to use the right products and techniques. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and flat irons, too often as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry whenever possible, and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to keep your short haircut looking fresh and fabulous.

Now that you know the best short haircut for over 60, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out your new look. Remember to have fun and embrace your natural beauty!
