How To Keep Long Hair Swept Back

Written by Luffy Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair can be both beautiful and challenging to care for. One of the most popular ways to style long hair is to sweep it back. This look can be achieved with a variety of hair types and lengths, but it can be tricky to keep your hair in place all day. In this blog post, we'll give you tips and tricks for keeping your long hair swept back effortlessly.

  • How To

Description

Sweeping your hair back is a popular style that can be achieved with different hair types and lengths. Keeping your hair in place can be a challenge, especially if you're dealing with frizz or flyaways. In this blog post, we'll give you tips and tricks for keeping your long hair swept back effortlessly.

How To

Here are the steps to keep your long hair swept back.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

Step 2: Use Hair Products

Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair. This will help to control flyaways and frizz. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Using a bristle brush, brush your hair back from your forehead. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the nape of your neck. Make sure that your hair is completely brushed back.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair at the back of your head. Make sure that the pins or clips are hidden from view. You can also use a hair elastic to create a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a few bobby pins to hold any loose strands in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep your long hair swept back.

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair.
  5. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  6. Using a bristle brush, brush your hair back from your forehead.
  7. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the nape of your neck.
  8. Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair at the back of your head.
  9. Use a few bobby pins to hold any loose strands in place.
  10. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your long hair swept back.

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to control flyaways and frizz.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair at the back of your head.
  • Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place all day.

FAQ

How long does it take to sweep long hair back?

It takes around 10-15 minutes to sweep long hair back.

Can I sweep my hair back if I have frizzy hair?

Yes, you can sweep your hair back even if you have frizzy hair. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to control frizz and flyaways.

Can I sweep my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can sweep your hair back even if you have curly hair. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair and apply a styling cream or pomade to control frizz.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair healthy and strong, make sure to wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.

Keeping your long hair swept back can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve this look effortlessly. Follow our tips and tricks to keep your hair in place all day and look fabulous!
