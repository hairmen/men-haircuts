How To Get Silver Grey Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you ready to rock the silver grey hair trend? It’s a bold and beautiful look that’s taking the world by storm. But how do you achieve this stunning shade? In this guide, we’ll take you through the process step-by-step, from pre-lightening to toning, and everything in between. So, let’s get started!

Description

Silver grey hair is a beautiful, trendy hair color that’s become increasingly popular in recent years. It’s a cool-toned shade that ranges from a light, icy grey to a darker, more muted hue. Getting silver grey hair requires pre-lightening your hair to a pale blonde shade, and then toning it with a grey or silver dye to achieve the desired color.

How to Get Silver Grey Hair: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Pre-Lightening

The first step to achieving silver grey hair is to pre-lighten your hair to a pale blonde shade. This is necessary because the silver dye won’t show up on darker hair. You can do this at home using a high-quality bleach kit, or you can go to a salon to have it done professionally.

Tip: Be sure to follow the instructions on your bleach kit carefully, and don’t leave it on for longer than recommended. Bleaching your hair can be damaging, so it’s important to take care of your hair during this process.

Step 2: Toning

Once you’ve achieved a pale blonde shade, it’s time to tone your hair with a silver or grey dye. You can find these dyes at your local beauty supply store or online. Follow the instructions carefully and leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time. Rinse it out thoroughly and voila! You now have silver grey hair.

Tip: If you want a lighter shade of silver, you can dilute the dye with conditioner to achieve a more pastel hue.

Step 3: Maintenance

Now that you’ve achieved your desired shade of silver grey hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out, and avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Tips for Getting Silver Grey Hair

  • Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to keep your silver hair from turning yellow.
  • Be patient – achieving silver grey hair can take several rounds of bleaching and toning.
  • Consider going to a professional if you’re not confident in your ability to bleach and tone your hair.
  • Experiment with different shades of silver to find the one that looks best on you.

FAQ

Can I achieve silver grey hair on dark hair?

No, silver grey hair requires pre-lightening to a pale blonde shade before toning.

How long does it take to achieve silver grey hair?

It can take several rounds of bleaching and toning to achieve silver grey hair, so be patient.

Is it damaging to bleach my hair?

Bleaching your hair can be damaging, so it’s important to take care of your hair during the process and use a deep conditioning treatment afterwards.

Hair Care for Silver Grey Hair

Now that you have silver grey hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible.
  • If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to keep your silver hair from turning yellow.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be rocking the silver grey hair trend in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and be patient during the process. Good luck!
