How To Straighten Short Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short curly hair, you may be wondering how to straighten it for a different look. Straightening your hair can be a great way to switch up your style and give yourself a new look. In this guide, we’ll go through everything you need to know about how to straighten short curly hair.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Straighten Short Curly Hair

Straightening short curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Cleanse and Condition Your Hair

Before you start straightening your hair, it’s important to cleanse and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage caused by the straightening iron.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it is completely dry. This will help prepare your hair for straightening.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Starting with the bottom sections of your hair, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair. Make sure to use a low to medium heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Serum

Once you have straightened all of your hair, finish with a hair serum to add shine and keep your hair looking smooth.

Tips for Straightening Short Curly Hair

Straightening short curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a low to medium heat setting to avoid damaging your hair
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that you don’t miss any sections
  • Finish with a hair serum to add shine and keep your hair looking smooth

FAQs

Can I straighten my hair if it’s wet?

No, it’s important to straighten your hair when it is completely dry. Wet hair can be damaged by the heat from the straightening iron.

How often can I straighten my hair?

It’s best to limit the amount of heat styling you do on your hair to avoid damage. Aim to straighten your hair no more than once a week.

Hair Care for Straightened Short Curly Hair

After straightening your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to avoid damage
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and keep your hair looking smooth

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look for your short curly hair. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated.
