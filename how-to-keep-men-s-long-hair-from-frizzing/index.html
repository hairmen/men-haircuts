How To Keep Men s Long Hair From Frizzing Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair can be a great look for men, but it can also be a hassle to keep under control. One of the biggest issues that men with long hair face is frizz. Frizz can make your hair look unkempt and messy, but it doesn't have to be this way. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to keep men's long hair from frizzing.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Men's Long Hair from Frizzing
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Men with Long Hair

How to Keep Men's Long Hair from Frizzing

The first step in keeping men's long hair from frizzing is to understand what causes frizz in the first place. Frizz is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair, which can be due to a number of factors, including:

  • Using the wrong hair products
  • Blow drying your hair too much
  • Using hot styling tools too often
  • Not washing your hair often enough

To keep your hair from frizzing, you need to keep it moisturized. This means using the right hair products, avoiding hot styling tools, and washing your hair regularly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to keep men's long hair from frizzing:

  1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter.
  2. Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons or curling irons. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray first.
  3. Let your hair air dry as much as possible. If you need to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment and set it on a low heat setting.
  4. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day.
  5. Avoid touching your hair too much. The oils from your hands can make your hair more prone to frizz.
  6. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  7. Avoid using harsh hair products like gels or hairsprays. These can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.
  8. Protect your hair from the elements. If it's raining or humid outside, wear a hat or use an umbrella to keep your hair as dry as possible.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep men's long hair from frizzing:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after you shower. Instead, gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.
  • If you have curly hair, consider using a satin or silk pillowcase. These materials are gentler on your hair and can help prevent frizz.
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I still use styling products if I want to keep my hair from frizzing?

A: Yes, you can still use styling products, but choose products that are lightweight and won't weigh your hair down. Look for products that contain ingredients like jojoba oil or glycerin, which can help keep your hair moisturized.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can probably get away with washing it once or twice a week.

Q: What should I do if my hair starts to frizz during the day?

A: Keep a small bottle of hair oil or leave-in conditioner with you and apply it to your hair when needed. You can also try using a little bit of water to smooth down any frizz.

Hair Care for Men with Long Hair

Keeping men's long hair from frizzing is just one part of a good hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for men with long hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products like gels or hairsprays. These can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, which can help promote hair growth and make your hair stronger.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your men's long hair looking healthy, shiny, and frizz-free.
