How To Keep Long Hair Off Your Neck The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful, it can also be a hassle to manage, especially during the hot summer months. If you're tired of constantly feeling a sweaty, sticky mess, it's time to learn how to keep long hair off your neck. In this guide, we'll show you everything you need to know to keep your locks looking great and feeling cool.

How to Keep Long Hair Off Your Neck

There are several ways to keep long hair off your neck, including:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Braid
  • Headband
  • Clip

Each of these options has its pros and cons, so it's important to find the one that works best for your hair type and personal style.

Ponytail

The ponytail is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for keeping long hair off your neck. To create a ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, just above your neck.
  3. Tie your hair with an elastic band. Make sure it's not too tight, as this can cause headaches.
  4. Adjust your ponytail so it's in the position you want.

A high ponytail can create a sleek, sophisticated look, while a low ponytail is more casual and relaxed.

Bun

The bun is another classic hairstyle that's perfect for keeping long hair off your neck. To create a bun, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, just above your neck.
  3. Twist your hair into a tight coil.
  4. Wrap the coil around itself to create a bun.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.

A low bun is perfect for a sophisticated look, while a high bun is more playful and casual.

Braid

The braid is a versatile hairstyle that's perfect for keeping long hair off your neck. To create a braid, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, just above your neck.
  3. Divide your hair into three equal sections.
  4. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  5. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you reach the end of your hair.
  7. Tie the end of your braid with an elastic band.

A fishtail braid is perfect for a boho-chic look, while a French braid is more elegant and sophisticated.

Headband

A headband is a simple and stylish way to keep long hair off your neck. To wear a headband, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a headband that fits your head comfortably.
  2. Place the headband on your head, just behind your hairline.
  3. Gather your hair at the back of your head, just above your neck.
  4. Tuck your hair into the headband.

A wide headband is perfect for a boho-chic look, while a thin headband is more elegant and sophisticated.

Clip

A clip is a simple and stylish way to keep long hair off your neck. To wear a clip, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a clip that fits your hair type and personal style.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, just above your neck.
  3. Twist your hair into a tight coil.
  4. Clip the coil with the clip.

A barrette clip is perfect for a sophisticated look, while a claw clip is more playful and casual.

Step-by-Step Guide

To keep long hair off your neck, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that works for your hair type and personal style.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Create your hairstyle using the steps above.
  4. Adjust your hairstyle as needed throughout the day.

With these simple steps, you can keep your long hair off your neck and stay cool and comfortable all day long.

Tips for Keeping Long Hair Off Your Neck

Here are some additional tips for keeping long hair off your neck:

  • Use a hair tie or clip that won't damage your hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works for your hair type and personal style.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your hair looking clean and fresh.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage with a hat or scarf.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair looking great and feeling cool and comfortable all day long.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

A: Brush your hair regularly and use a detangling spray or conditioner. You can also braid your hair before bed to prevent tangles while you sleep.

Q: Can I wear a hat or scarf with my hairstyle?

A: Yes! A hat or scarf can add a stylish touch to your hairstyle while also protecting your hair from the sun and heat damage.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
