How To Cut A Pixie Haircut At Home With Clippers

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon and paying a huge amount of money for a simple pixie haircut? Why not try cutting your own hair at home with clippers? With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking pixie haircut without leaving your house. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut a pixie haircut at home with clippers.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that is usually cut into layers and can be worn by anyone regardless of their age, face shape, or hair texture. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to

Step 1: Gather the tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, hair clips, a mirror, and a towel.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

It is important to wash your hair before cutting it to ensure that it is clean and free from any product buildup. Once you have washed your hair, dry it completely before you start cutting.

Step 3: Section your hair

Using the comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip each section of hair separately to keep it out of the way.

Step 4: Start cutting

Start with the back of your head, using the clippers to cut the hair from the nape of your neck up to the crown of your head. Use the longest guard on your clippers to start with and then gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to create layers.

Move on to the sides of your head, using the clippers to trim the hair from the bottom up. Use the comb to guide the clippers and create a straight line.

Finally, move on to the top of your head, using the clippers to trim the hair from front to back. Use the comb to lift the hair and create layers.

Step 5: Blend and refine

Once you have cut all the sections of your hair, blend and refine the layers by using the clippers and comb to remove any uneven sections.

Step 6: Clean up

Clean up any loose hairs and trim the edges using scissors if needed. Wash your hair again to remove any loose hairs and style it as desired.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and gradually work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and create layers.
  • Be patient and take your time to ensure that you are cutting your hair evenly.
  • If you are unsure about cutting your own hair, ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While it is possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it is not recommended as they may not be sharp enough to create clean lines.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I color my hair after cutting it?

Yes, you can color your hair after cutting it. However, it is recommended to wait at least 24 hours before coloring your hair to allow your scalp to recover from the cutting process.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and shiny. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.

Now that you know how to cut a pixie haircut at home with clippers, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Just remember to be patient and take your time to ensure that you are cutting your hair evenly. Happy cutting!
