How to Get a Messy Pixie Cut - Step by Step Guide

A pixie cut is a short haircut that can be both chic and edgy. If you are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling, a messy pixie cut may be the perfect fit for you. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get a messy pixie cut.

How to Get a Messy Pixie Cut

Step 1: Choose the Right Pixie Cut

The first step to getting a messy pixie cut is to choose the right cut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which type of pixie cut will work best for you. Some popular pixie cuts include the classic pixie, textured pixie, and undercut pixie.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you go in for a haircut, make sure to wash and dry your hair. This will give your hairstylist a better idea of how your hair naturally falls and how to cut it accordingly.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Hairstylist

When you are at the salon, make sure to communicate with your hairstylist about the type of messy pixie cut you want. Show them pictures of hairstyles that you like and explain what you want to achieve with your haircut.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will begin cutting your hair, starting from the back and working their way to the front. They may use scissors or clippers, depending on the type of pixie cut you have chosen. Make sure to let them know if you want more or less hair taken off.

Step 5: Add Texture and Layers

Once your hair is cut, your hairstylist will add texture and layers to create a messy look. This may involve using thinning shears, razors, or texturizing scissors. They may also add layers to create movement and dimension in your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut and textured, it's time to style your hair. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or hair wax to your hair and use your fingers to create a tousled, messy look. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy pixie cut:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.
  • Invest in a good texturizing spray or hair wax to add texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to change up your look.

FAQ

How long does a messy pixie cut last?

A messy pixie cut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. It's important to maintain your haircut to keep it looking fresh and stylish.

Is a messy pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?

A messy pixie cut can work for most face shapes, but it's important to choose the right type of pixie cut that flatters your features. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which type of pixie cut will work best for you.

Can I style my messy pixie cut differently?

A messy pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in different ways. Experiment with different hair products and accessories to create different looks.

Hair Care for Messy Pixie Cut

Here are some hair care tips for a messy pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to get a messy pixie cut, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist. With the right cut and styling, a messy pixie cut can be a fun and low-maintenance hairstyle that will turn heads.
