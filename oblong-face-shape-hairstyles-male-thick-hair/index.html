Oblong Face Shape Hairstyles For Men With Thick Hair

Written by William Jul 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oblong face shape and thick hair, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. Fortunately, there are many options available that can enhance your natural features and flatter your face shape. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on oblong face shape hairstyles for men with thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Description

The oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent forehead and chin. Men with this face shape tend to have a more rectangular or elongated appearance. Hairstyles that work well for oblong face shapes typically involve creating more volume on the sides of the face to balance out the length.

Thick hair is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Men with thick hair have a lot of hair to work with, which means they can create a variety of different styles. However, it can also be difficult to manage and style thick hair, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape and thick hair, it's essential to consider the following factors:

  • Length: Hairstyles that are too short can make the face look even longer, while hairstyles that are too long can add more length and make the face appear even narrower. Medium length hairstyles typically work well for oblong face shapes.
  • Volume: Adding volume to the sides of the face can help balance out the length of the face. Hairstyles with layers or textured ends can create more volume and movement.
  • Face shape: It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your natural features and flatters your face shape. Hairstyles that create a more oval or square appearance tend to work well for oblong face shapes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some popular oblong face shape hairstyles for men with thick hair:

1. Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless hairstyle that works well for most face shapes, including oblong. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Apply a medium hold styling product to your hair.
  3. Using a comb, create a deep side part on one side of your head.
  4. Comb the hair on the longer side of the part to the side and back, creating volume and height at the front.
  5. Comb the hair on the shorter side of the part down and to the side.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that works well for oblong face shapes. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Apply a styling cream or mousse to your hair.
  3. Using a comb, create a side part on one side of your head.
  4. Use your fingers to tousle and style your hair, creating a textured and messy look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

3. Quiff

The quiff is a stylish and classic hairstyle that works well for oblong face shapes. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair.
  3. Using a comb, create a side part on one side of your head.
  4. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to create volume and height at the front.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle and style your hair, creating a messy and textured look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your oblong face shape hairstyle:

  • Regular trims: Regular trims will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and hairstyle, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.
  • Avoid over-styling: Over-styling your hair can lead to damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and harsh chemicals.
  • Protect your hair: Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about oblong face shape hairstyles for men with thick hair:

What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face shape?

Hairstyles that create volume on the sides of the face, such as the classic side part or textured crop, tend to work well for oblong face shapes.

How do I know if I have an oblong face shape?

Oblong face shapes are characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent forehead and chin. If your face is longer than it is wide, you may have an oblong face shape.

How do I style thick hair?

Thick hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including with layers, textures, and volume. Use a styling product that is specifically designed for your hair type and style, and avoid over-styling to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Washing your hair regularly will help keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a conditioner: Conditioner will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and limit your use.
  • Trim regularly: Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and choosing the right oblong face shape hairstyle for your thick hair, you can create a stylish and flattering look that complements your natural features.
