How To Fake Gray Hair Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Bardi Aug 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your same old hair color and want to add some sophistication and style to your look? Well, why not try faking gray hair? Gray hair is all the rage these days, and it’s not just for the elderly. With the right techniques and products, you too can achieve the silvery, edgy look that’s taking the fashion world by storm. Here’s how.

How to Fake Gray Hair

There are several ways to fake gray hair, from temporary sprays and powders to more permanent dyes and extensions. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Temporary Hair Color Spray

This is one of the easiest and most convenient ways to fake gray hair. Simply apply the spray to your hair and style as usual. This method can last up to a few days, depending on how often you wash your hair. Try L’Oreal Paris Colorista Hair Makeup Temporary 1-Day Hair Color Spray in Silver for a subtle yet stylish gray look.

Gray Hair Powder

Another temporary method is to use a gray hair powder. This is a great option if you have dark hair and want a more dramatic effect. Apply the powder to your hair with a brush and style as usual. The powder will wash out with shampoo. Try Bumble and Bumble Bb. Color Stick in Misty Dawn for a quick and easy gray look.

Semi-Permanent Hair Dye

If you want a longer-lasting gray look, try a semi-permanent hair dye. This type of dye will last for several washes and gradually fade over time. It’s a good option if you’re not ready to commit to a permanent dye. Try Clairol Natural Instincts Semi-Permanent Hair Color in Lightest Cool Brown for a subtle gray look.

Permanent Hair Dye

If you’re ready to commit to a full-on gray look, go for a permanent hair dye. This method will require more maintenance and touch-ups, but it will give you the most authentic gray look. Try L’Oreal Paris Feria Multi-Faceted Shimmering Permanent Hair Color in Smokey Silver for a bold and edgy look.

Gray Hair Extensions

If you’re not ready to dye your hair or want a more temporary solution, try gray hair extensions. You can clip them in and style as usual for a quick and easy gray look. Try GOO GOO 20 Inch Clip in Hair Extensions in Ash Blonde for a subtle gray look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Whether you choose a temporary or permanent method, here’s a step-by-step guide to faking gray hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair as usual. If you’re using a temporary spray or powder, make sure your hair is completely dry before applying. If you’re using a semi-permanent or permanent dye, follow the instructions on the package for preparing your hair.

Step 2: Apply the Product

Apply the product of your choice to your hair, making sure to cover all areas evenly. Use a brush or your fingers to distribute the product and blend it in. If you’re using a spray or powder, be sure to hold the can or container a few inches away from your hair to avoid clumping.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as usual, using a hair dryer or flat iron if desired. If you’re using extensions, clip them in and style as usual. Be sure to blend the extensions in with your natural hair for a seamless look.

Step 4: Maintain Your Gray Look

Depending on the method you choose, your gray look may require some maintenance. Temporary sprays and powders may need to be reapplied every few days, while semi-permanent and permanent dyes may require touch-ups every few weeks. Be sure to follow the instructions on the product and take good care of your hair.

Tips for Faking Gray Hair

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect gray look:

  • Choose a shade of gray that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  • Experiment with different methods and products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look dull and lifeless.
  • Take good care of your hair, using nourishing shampoos and conditioners to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Consider going to a professional stylist for more complex methods, such as extensions or permanent dyes.

FAQs

Can I fake gray hair if I have dark hair?

Yes, you can. Try using a gray hair powder or a semi-permanent dye for a more dramatic effect.

Will faking gray hair damage my hair?

Not if you use the right products and follow the instructions carefully. Be sure to use nourishing shampoos and conditioners to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

How long will my gray look last?

It depends on the method you choose. Temporary sprays and powders can last up to a few days, while semi-permanent and permanent dyes can last for several washes or weeks.

Hair Care for Gray Hair

If you decide to go for a permanent gray look, or if you already have natural gray hair, here are some tips for taking care of it:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your hair from turning yellow or brassy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips, tricks, and techniques, you can fake gray hair like a pro. Whether you’re looking for a temporary change or a more permanent look, there’s a method that’s right for you. So go ahead, embrace the gray, and rock that edgy, sophisticated look.
