Hairstyles for Long Faces - How to Choose the Perfect One

Do you have a long face and struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your facial features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for long faces, how to choose the perfect one, step-by-step tutorials, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for Long Faces

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle for long faces, the rule of thumb is to add width to your face. This can be achieved by adding layers, bangs, or choosing a hairstyle with volume at the sides. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as this can elongate your face even more. Instead, go for hairstyles that hit at your collarbone or above.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have thin hair, avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or voluminous, as this can make your hair appear flat. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, opt for hairstyles that add movement and texture to your hair.

Bob Hairstyles for Long Faces

Bob hairstyles are a great option for long faces, as they add width and volume to your face. The key is to choose a bob that hits at your chin or above, as this will draw attention to your jawline and add balance to your face. You can also add layers or bangs to your bob for added texture and dimension.

Bangs for Long Faces

Bangs are a great way to add width to your face and draw attention to your eyes. For long faces, go for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs, as this will soften your features and add balance to your face. Avoid blunt or straight across bangs, as this can make your face appear longer.

Layered Hairstyles for Long Faces

Layered hairstyles are perfect for long faces, as they add volume and movement to your hair. The key is to add layers around your cheekbones or chin, as this will add width to your face and draw attention to your best features. You can also add texture to your layers with a choppy or razor cut.

Updos for Long Faces

Updos are a great way to show off your facial features and add elegance to your look. For long faces, opt for updos that add volume at the sides, such as a messy bun or braided crown. Avoid sleek or tight updos, as this can elongate your face.

Step-by-Step Tutorials

Here are some step-by-step tutorials for hairstyles that are perfect for long faces:

Bob with Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair to the side
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, adding volume at the roots
  4. Curl small sections of your hair away from your face using a curling iron
  5. Add texturizing spray to your hair for added volume and texture
  6. Cut bangs that hit at your eyebrows or above
  7. Style your bangs using a round brush for a soft, side-swept look
  8. Finish with hairspray

Layered Lob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Section your hair into layers, starting at your cheekbones and ending at your collarbone
  4. Use a razor or scissors to cut your layers, adding texture and movement to your hair
  5. Add texturizing spray to your hair for added volume and texture
  6. Curl small sections of your hair away from your face using a curling iron
  7. Finish with hairspray

Tips for Styling Long Faces

  • Add volume and width to your face with layers, bangs, or hairstyles with volume at the sides
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as this can elongate your face
  • Choose a hairstyle that hits at your collarbone or above
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle
  • Use texturizing spray for added volume and texture
  • Avoid sleek or tight hairstyles, as this can elongate your face

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as this can elongate your face even more. Also, avoid blunt or straight across bangs, as this can make your face appear longer.

What hairstyles are best for long faces?

Hairstyles that add volume and width to your face, such as bob hairstyles, layered hairstyles, and hairstyles with side-swept or curtain bangs are best for long faces.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using texturizing spray, blow drying your hair with a round brush, or adding layers to your hair.

Hair Care Advice for Long Faces

When it comes to hair care for long faces, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools. Also, make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and choosing the perfect hairstyle for your long face, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your look.
