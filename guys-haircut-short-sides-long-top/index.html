Guys Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for men that is easy to maintain? Look no further than the guys haircut short sides long top. This versatile hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we will provide you with everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

Description

The guys haircut short sides long top is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This haircut is also known as the undercut, as it typically involves shaving or clipping the hair on the sides and back to create a sharp contrast with the longer hair on top. This haircut can be styled in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

How to Get the Guys Haircut Short Sides Long Top

If you're interested in getting the guys haircut short sides long top, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. When you go to the salon, bring a picture of the style you like to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for. Once you've found a stylist you trust, follow these steps to get the perfect guys haircut short sides long top:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step in getting the guys haircut short sides long top is to decide on the length of your hair. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it should be at least a few inches long to create the desired contrast with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 2: Clip or Shave the Sides and Back

The next step is to clip or shave the hair on the sides and back of your head. This will create the sharp contrast between the short sides and the longer hair on top. The length of the hair on the sides and back can vary depending on your preference, but it is usually clipped or shaved to a length of 1/4 inch to 1/2 inch.

Step 3: Style the Hair on Top

Once the sides and back have been clipped or shaved, the hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular styles include slicked-back hair, messy texture, or a pompadour. Use a high-quality styling product to achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Guys Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Now that you have your guys haircut short sides long top, it's time to learn how to style it. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and easy to manage.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Once your hair is clean and dry, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. The type of product you use will depend on the style you want to achieve. For a slicked-back style, use a pomade or gel. For a messy, textured look, use a wax or clay.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. Start at the front and work your way back, making sure to smooth down any flyaways or stray hairs. If you want a more textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create some volume.

Tips for Maintaining the Guys Haircut Short Sides Long Top

To keep your guys haircut short sides long top looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep the sides and back of your hair trimmed.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

What face shape is best for the guys haircut short sides long top?

The guys haircut short sides long top looks great on a variety of face shapes, but it is particularly flattering on those with oval or square faces.

Can I style the guys haircut short sides long top in different ways?

Yes, the guys haircut short sides long top is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

Hair Care for the Guys Haircut Short Sides Long Top

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the guys haircut short sides long top with confidence and style. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you.
