Which Hair Cut Will Suit Me

Written by Luffy Nov 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you unsure about which hair cut will suit you? Choosing a hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. In this article, we will help you find the perfect hair cut by considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Read on to discover your ideal hairstyle.

Description

When it comes to choosing a hair cut, it's important to consider your face shape. The right hair cut can enhance your features and balance your proportions. If you have a round face, opt for a cut that adds height and lengthens your face, such as a long bob or layered cut. If you have a square face, soften your features with a side-swept fringe or textured layers. If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pull off almost any style.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help control the volume. If you have curly hair, a cut that keeps the length and adds layers can enhance your natural texture.

How To

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and trace your face shape onto a mirror with lipstick. Then, compare your shape to the following:

  • Round: widest at the cheeks and has a rounded jawline.
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar width.
  • Oval: slightly wider at the forehead and narrow at the jawline.
  • Heart: widest at the forehead and narrows at the jawline.
  • Diamond: widest at the cheekbones and narrows at both the forehead and jawline.

Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that flatter your shape. Bring pictures to your hairstylist to ensure they understand your vision.

Step by Step

Before getting a hair cut, it's important to communicate with your hairstylist. Be honest about your hair texture, daily routine, and personal style. Your hairstylist can recommend a cut that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.

During the appointment, your hairstylist will likely wash your hair and assess your hair texture. They will then cut your hair to the desired length and shape. After the cut, they may style your hair with products or tools to show you how to recreate the look at home.

Tips

  • Consider your personal style and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair daily.
  • Be open to trying new things, but also know your limits.
  • Bring pictures to your hairstylist to ensure clear communication.
  • Invest in quality hair products to maintain your new cut.

FAQ

How often should I get a hair cut?

It's recommended to get a hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Can I still get a trendy hair cut if I have a conservative job?

Yes! Talk to your hairstylist about how to style your hair in a professional manner. A trendy cut can still be versatile and appropriate for the workplace.

What if I don't like my hair cut?

Communicate with your hairstylist about your concerns. They may be able to make adjustments or offer solutions. Remember, hair grows back!

Hair Care

After getting a hair cut, it's important to maintain the health of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using heat tools daily and opt for air-drying when possible. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. And finally, be patient. It may take time to adjust to your new cut, but with proper care, you'll love it in no time.
