How To Style Medium Length Mens Hair Thick

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length hair that is thick, you might find it difficult to style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of looks that will suit your style and personality. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style medium length mens hair thick.

Description

Medium length hair that is thick can be challenging to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of hairstyles that will suit your style and personality. Whether you prefer a slick back look, messy texture, or something in between, there are many options to choose from.

How to Style Medium Length Mens Hair Thick

Here are the steps to follow to style medium length men's hair that is thick:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in styling your hair is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick hair to help control frizz and add moisture. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. A pomade or wax is ideal for thick hair, as it will provide hold and control. Rub a small amount of product in your palms and work it through your hair evenly.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while styling it. Use a round brush to create volume and direction in your hair. Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go, whether that's back, to the side or up. Use a cool setting to set the style in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold it in place and prevent frizz. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium length mens hair thick:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find one that suits you.
  • Consider getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.
  • Invest in high-quality products that are designed for thick hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length mens hair thick:

Q: What is the best styling product for thick hair?

A: Pomade or wax is ideal for thick hair, as it provides hold and control.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but most men should wash their hair every 2-3 days.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my hair?

A: Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick hair to help control frizz. Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, and finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your medium length mens hair thick:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your medium length mens hair thick in a variety of ways. Experiment with different styles to find one that suits your personality and lifestyle.
