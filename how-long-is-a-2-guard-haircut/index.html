How Long Is A 2 Guard Haircut

Written by Robby Aug 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you wondering how long a 2 guard haircut is? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about a 2 guard haircut length.

Description

A 2 guard haircut refers to a specific blade attachment used on a clipper. The guard number corresponds to the length of hair that will be left on your head after the cut. The number 2 guard is a popular choice for those who want a shorter, yet still manageable, haircut.

How To

Getting a 2 guard haircut is easy! Here are the steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Attach the number 2 guard to your clipper.
  3. Begin at the bottom of your head and work your way up, using the clipper to cut your hair evenly.
  4. Continue until you have cut all the hair on your head.
  5. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs or to shape your haircut as desired.

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to get a 2 guard haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is slightly damp.
  3. Attach the number 2 guard to your clipper.
  4. Turn on the clipper and begin at the nape of your neck.
  5. Run the clipper up the back of your head, moving against the grain of your hair.
  6. Repeat this process on the sides of your head, starting at the bottom and moving upward.
  7. Continue to work your way around your head, using the clipper to cut your hair evenly.
  8. Once you have finished cutting your hair with the clipper, use scissors to trim any longer hairs or to shape your haircut as desired.
  9. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a 2 guard haircut:

  • Be sure to use a quality clipper and blade attachment for best results.
  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter length if you are unsure of what length you want.
  • Use scissors to add texture and movement to your haircut.
  • Take your time and go slowly to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist if you are unsure about cutting your hair yourself.

FAQ

What length is a 2 guard haircut?

A 2 guard haircut leaves approximately 1/4 inch or 6.35mm of hair on your head.

Can I use a 2 guard on any hair type?

Yes, a 2 guard can be used on any hair type, but the results may vary depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How often should I get a 2 guard haircut?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your haircut. Generally, a 2 guard haircut can be maintained every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After getting a 2 guard haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can cause damage.
  • Use a moisturizing hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

Now that you know how long a 2 guard haircut is, you can confidently go to your barber or cut your hair yourself. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling healthy!
