What Is A Good Short Hairstyle For A 60 Year Old Woman

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a 60 year old woman looking for a new hairstyle, you might be wondering what the best short hairstyle is for you. With so many options out there, it can be tough to know where to start. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for women over 60, and give you tips on how to choose the right one for you.

Description

Short hair can be a great option for women over 60, as it can be low-maintenance, youthful, and stylish. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular short hairstyles for women over 60 include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle, there are several factors to consider. Here are some tips to help you find the right one:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in determining which short hairstyle will look best on you. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to opt for a longer bob that falls just below your chin. If you have a square face, a pixie cut with lots of layers can soften your features.

2. Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also impact which short hairstyle is best for you. If you have thick, coarse hair, a layered bob can help to thin it out and make it more manageable. If you have fine hair, a pixie cut with lots of texture can add volume and dimension.

3. Consider Your Personal Style

Finally, it's important to choose a short hairstyle that reflects your personal style. If you're someone who likes to keep things simple and classic, a sleek bob might be a good option. If you're more adventurous, a spiky pixie cut can add some edge to your look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve some of the most popular short hairstyles for women over 60:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered hairstyle that's both low-maintenance and stylish. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to create a side part.
  3. Using scissors or clippers, cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short.
  4. Leave the hair on top of your head slightly longer, and add layers for texture.
  5. Style with a bit of pomade or wax for hold.

Bob

A bob is a classic, timeless hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to create a center or side part.
  3. Cut the hair to chin-length, and add layers for texture.
  4. Style with a bit of mousse or hairspray for hold.

Lob

A lob, or long bob, is a slightly longer version of the classic bob. It's a great option if you want a bit more length, but still want a short hairstyle. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to create a center or side part.
  3. Cut the hair to shoulder-length or just above, and add layers for texture.
  4. Style with a bit of mousse or hairspray for hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair:

  • Visit your hairstylist for regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent hair breakage.

FAQs

What is the Best Short Hairstyle for a 60 Year Old Woman?

The best short hairstyle for a 60 year old woman depends on several factors, including face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular options include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob.

Is Short Hair Flattering on Older Women?

Short hair can be very flattering on older women, as it can be low-maintenance and youthful. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

How Often Should I Get a Trim?

You should visit your hairstylist for a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hairstyle looking its best.

Hair Care

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair.

Overall, a good short hairstyle for a 60 year old woman is one that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob, be sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
