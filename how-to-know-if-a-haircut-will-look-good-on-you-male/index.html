How To Know If A Haircut Will Look Good On You Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of getting a bad haircut? Do you want to know how to choose a haircut that will suit your face shape and style? Look no further! In this article, we will give you tips on how to know if a haircut will look good on you male. Our guide includes steps, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you make the right decision.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture
  • Step 3: Look for Inspiration
  • Step 4: Consult with Your Barber or Stylist
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. There are several face shapes including oval, round, square, diamond, heart, and oblong. Each face shape has specific hairstyles that will complement it. For instance, if you have a round face, you should consider a short, layered cut to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, a textured, messy cut will soften your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture

Your hair type and texture will play a significant role in determining the right haircut for you. If you have thick, coarse hair, you should consider a short, layered cut to add volume and texture. If you have thin hair, a longer, layered cut will create the illusion of fullness.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Before heading to the barber or stylist, look for inspiration. Browse through magazines or search online for hairstyles that you like. This will give your barber or stylist an idea of what you are looking for and help them suggest the right haircut for you.

Step 4: Consult with Your Barber or Stylist

Finally, consult with your barber or stylist. They are experts in their field and can suggest the best haircut for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Be open to their suggestions, but also communicate what you want. A good barber or stylist will listen to your needs and work with you to achieve the perfect haircut.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. You can always grow your hair out or change the style.
  • Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your barber or stylist.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your personality.
  • Consider the amount of time and effort you are willing to put into styling your hair.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face?

A: A short, layered cut is best for a round face as it creates the illusion of length.

Q: What is the best haircut for thick, coarse hair?

A: A short, layered cut with texture is best for thick, coarse hair as it adds volume and texture.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and style. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Use a styling product that suits your hair type and style.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, but with our guide, you can now make an informed decision. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type and texture, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut. Don't be afraid to experiment and consult with your barber or stylist. With the right haircut, you can look and feel your best.
