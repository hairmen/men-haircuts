How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately Home Remedies In Hindi

Written by Aprilia Nov 23, 2023 · 3 min read

If you are experiencing hair fall, it can be a very frustrating experience. However, there are many home remedies that you can use to stop hair fall immediately. In this article, we will discuss some of the best home remedies to stop hair fall in Hindi, which will help you achieve healthy and strong hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Stop Hair Fall
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Preventing Hair Fall
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Routine

How to Stop Hair Fall

Here are some effective home remedies to stop hair fall:

1. Amla

Amla is one of the best home remedies to stop hair fall. It is rich in vitamin C, which helps in strengthening hair follicles and promoting hair growth. You can use amla in the form of oil or powder.

2. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a great home remedy to stop hair fall. It is rich in fatty acids and vitamins, which helps in nourishing the scalp and promoting hair growth. Massage warm coconut oil on your scalp and leave it overnight for best results.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is another effective home remedy to stop hair fall. It is rich in sulfur, which helps in promoting hair growth. Apply onion juice on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in protein and nicotinic acid, which helps in promoting hair growth and preventing hair fall. Soak fenugreek seeds overnight and grind them into a paste. Apply the paste on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to stop hair fall immediately:

  1. Choose a home remedy that suits your hair type.
  2. Prepare the home remedy as per the instructions.
  3. Apply the home remedy on your scalp and hair.
  4. Leave it for the recommended time.
  5. Wash it off with a mild shampoo.
  6. Repeat the home remedy once a week for best results.

Tips for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some tips that can help in preventing hair fall:

  • Follow a healthy and balanced diet.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to get rid of split ends.

FAQs

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. It can disrupt the natural hair growth cycle and lead to hair fall.

Q. How many times should I use a home remedy in a week?

A. You can use a home remedy once a week for best results.

Q. Can hair fall be reversed?

A. Yes, hair fall can be reversed with the help of proper hair care and home remedies.

Hair Care Routine

Here is a hair care routine that can help in preventing hair fall:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo twice a week.
  • Apply a conditioner after shampooing to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons.
  • Massage your scalp with warm oil once a week.
  • Get regular trims to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet.

By following these home remedies and tips, you can stop hair fall immediately and achieve healthy and strong hair.
