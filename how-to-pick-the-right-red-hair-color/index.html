How To Pick The Right Red Hair Color A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Red hair is a bold and beautiful choice that can make a statement wherever you go. But with so many shades of red to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is right for you. In this guide, we’ll show you how to pick the right red hair color that will complement your skin tone and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 3: Choose the Right Shade of Red
  • Step 4: Test the Color
  • Tips for Maintaining Red Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in picking the right red hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are two main types of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones.

If you have a warm skin tone, you’ll want to choose a red hair color with warm undertones. This can include shades like copper, auburn, or strawberry blonde. If you have a cool skin tone, you’ll want to choose a red hair color with cool undertones, such as burgundy or cherry red.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in choosing the right shade of red. If you have light or blonde hair, you may want to opt for a lighter shade of red, such as strawberry blonde or light copper. If you have darker hair, a darker shade of red, such as burgundy or auburn, may be a better fit.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shade of Red

Once you’ve determined your skin tone and natural hair color, it’s time to choose the right shade of red. Here are some popular shades of red and who they work best for:

  • Copper: Works well for warm skin tones and light hair
  • Auburn: Works well for warm skin tones and darker hair
  • Strawberry blonde: Works well for warm skin tones and light hair
  • Burgundy: Works well for cool skin tones and darker hair
  • Cherry red: Works well for cool skin tones and darker hair

Step 4: Test the Color

Before committing to a new hair color, it’s important to test it out first. You can do this by using a temporary hair dye or trying on a wig in the color you’re considering. This will give you a better idea of how the color will look on you and whether or not it’s the right choice.

Tips for Maintaining Red Hair

Once you’ve chosen your perfect shade of red, it’s important to take care of it to ensure it stays vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining red hair:

  • Use a color-protecting shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners
  • Wear a hat or use a UV-protecting spray when spending time in the sun

FAQ

Q: Will red hair look good on me?

A: Red hair can look good on anyone, regardless of skin tone or hair color. It’s all about finding the right shade that complements your individual features.

Q: How often will I need to touch up my red hair?

A: This will depend on your hair growth and how quickly your hair fades. On average, you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair red at home?

A: It is possible to dye your hair red at home, but it’s recommended to visit a professional stylist to ensure the color is applied evenly and well-maintained.

Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Red hair can be more delicate than other hair colors, so it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for red hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or peroxide
  • Avoid excessive heat styling

With these tips in mind, you’ll be able to choose the perfect shade of red hair that complements your personal style and skin tone. Remember to take care of your new color with proper hair care and maintenance to keep it looking vibrant and healthy.
