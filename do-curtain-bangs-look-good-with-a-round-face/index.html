Do Curtain Bangs Look Good With A Round Face

Written by Aprilia Jul 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you someone with a round face and considering getting curtain bangs? You might be wondering whether it's the right hairstyle for you. Curtain bangs have been trending for a while now and for good reason. They're versatile, low-maintenance, and look good on most people. But do curtain bangs look good with a round face? Let's find out.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle, framing the face on either side like curtains. They're longer than traditional bangs and can be styled in various ways, making them a popular choice among many women. Round faces have soft, curved features, and their width is almost the same as their length. The goal is to create an illusion of length on the face, which can be done with the right hairstyle.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

Getting curtain bangs is easy, but it's always recommended to visit a professional hairstylist to get the best results. Here's how to get curtain bangs:

  1. Start by deciding on the length and thickness of your bangs.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs longer than your eyebrows and make them thinner towards the ends.
  3. Part your bangs in the middle and comb them to each side.
  4. Style them as desired. You can use a straightener or curling iron to create soft waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style curtain bangs:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle and comb your bangs to each side.
  3. Use a straightener or curling iron to create soft waves on the ends of your bangs.
  4. Apply hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when getting curtain bangs:

  • Make sure your bangs are long enough to be parted in the middle.
  • Consider the thickness of your hair before getting curtain bangs. If you have thick hair, you might want to go for thinner bangs.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. Longer bangs look better on round faces.
  • Style your bangs with soft waves to create an illusion of length on your face.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs and round faces:

Do curtain bangs suit everyone?

Yes, curtain bangs suit almost everyone as long as they're cut and styled correctly.

Can curtain bangs make a round face look slimmer?

Yes, curtain bangs can make a round face look slimmer by creating an illusion of length on the face.

How often do curtain bangs need to be trimmed?

Curtain bangs need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their length and shape.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent greasiness and keep your bangs fresh.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs as it can damage them.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to your bangs to keep them soft and shiny.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your bangs and prevent breakage.

Conclusion

In conclusion, curtain bangs can look good on round faces as long as they're cut and styled correctly. They're an easy and low-maintenance hairstyle that can add a touch of glamour to any look. Consider the tips and advice in this article before getting curtain bangs, and you'll be sure to rock this trendy hairstyle.
