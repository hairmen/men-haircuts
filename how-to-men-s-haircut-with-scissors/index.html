How To Men s Haircut With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut? Using scissors is a great way to achieve a classic, timeless look. However, it can be intimidating if you've never done it before. In this guide, we'll show you how to men's haircut with scissors in a relaxed, easy-to-understand way.

How to Men's Haircut with Scissors

Cutting hair with scissors takes a bit of practice, but it's not as hard as it seems. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather a few things:

  • Sharp scissors (preferably hair cutting scissors)
  • A comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A towel or cape to catch hair

Step 2: Assess the Hair

Before you start cutting, take a good look at the hair. What style is the person looking for? Is the hair thick or thin? Is it curly or straight? Take note of any cowlicks or areas that might need extra attention.

Step 3: Wet the Hair

Using the spray bottle, wet the hair down. This will make it easier to cut and help the hair lay flat.

Step 4: Section the Hair

Using the comb, section off the hair into manageable sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Using the scissors, start cutting the hair in small sections. Work your way through each section, cutting as evenly as possible. Use the comb to guide the scissors and make sure the hair is staying straight.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the sections, blend the hair together. Use the scissors to trim any uneven areas and make sure the hair is all the same length.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Finally, style the hair using the comb and any products desired. Use the clippers to trim any hair around the neck or ears if necessary.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed breakdown of each step:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Make sure you have everything you need before you start cutting. Having to stop midway through to grab something can be frustrating and can throw off your rhythm.

Step 2: Assess the Hair

Before you start cutting, take a few minutes to evaluate the hair. This will help you determine the best cutting technique and ensure that you're able to achieve the desired look.

Step 3: Wet the Hair

Wetting the hair down will make it easier to cut and help you see any uneven areas that need to be addressed.

Step 4: Section the Hair

Sectioning the hair will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any areas. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start at the bottom of the hair and work your way up. Use the comb to guide the scissors and make sure the hair is staying straight. Be sure to cut evenly and take your time.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the sections, blend the hair together. Use the scissors to trim any uneven areas and make sure the hair is all the same length.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Style the hair as desired using the comb and any products. Use the clippers to trim any hair around the neck or ears if necessary.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with scissors:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They'll make a big difference in the quality of the cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush. Cutting hair is a delicate process and it's important to take your time to avoid mistakes.
  • Ensure that the hair is wet but not too wet. Cutting hair that is too wet can result in an uneven cut.
  • Use the comb to guide the scissors and keep the hair straight. This will help you achieve a more even cut.
  • Ask the person you're cutting for feedback along the way. This will help ensure that you're on the right track.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors. However, it's important to take your time and be patient to avoid mistakes.

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it?

Yes, wetting the hair down will make it easier to cut and help you achieve a more even cut.

What kind of scissors should I use?

Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They're designed specifically for cutting hair and will make a big difference in the quality of the cut.

Hair Care After Your Haircut

After your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (e.g. hairdryers, straighteners) as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

Cutting hair with scissors takes practice, but it's a skill that anyone can learn. By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve a great haircut every time.
