Why Does No Hairstyle Suit Me Understanding Your Hair Type And Face Shape

Written by Eveline Sep 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles but none of them seem to suit you? It can be frustrating and demoralizing to see a gorgeous hairstyle on someone else and not being able to replicate the same on yourself. However, it's important to understand that every person's hair type and face shape are unique, which is why not every hairstyle suits everyone.

Description

In this post, we will discuss the reasons behind why no hairstyle suits you and provide tips on how you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your unique features. We will cover different face shapes, hair types, and styling techniques to help you achieve a look that suits you.

How To

Before we dive into the reasons behind why no hairstyle suits you, let's first discuss how you can determine your face shape and hair type. This will help you choose the right hairstyle that enhances your natural features.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six different face shapes, including:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long

You can determine your face shape by examining the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have determined your face shape, you can look for hairstyles that suit your features.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Type

The next step is to determine your hair type. There are four different hair types, including:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

You can determine your hair type by examining the texture and curl pattern of your hair. Once you have determined your hair type, you can look for hairstyles that work best for your hair texture.

Tips

Now that you have determined your face shape and hair type, here are some tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle
  • Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Take good care of your hair to ensure it looks healthy and vibrant

FAQ

Why Does No Hairstyle Suit Me?

No hairstyle suits everyone because every person's face shape and hair type are unique. It's important to find a hairstyle that complements your natural features to enhance your appearance.

How Can I Find the Right Hairstyle?

You can find the right hairstyle by determining your face shape and hair type and considering them when choosing a hairstyle. You can also consult with a hairstylist for professional advice.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and vibrant, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with products

Conclusion

Understanding your face shape and hair type is the key to finding the perfect hairstyle that suits you. By following the tips and hair care advice in this post, you'll be able to rock a hairstyle that enhances your natural features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
