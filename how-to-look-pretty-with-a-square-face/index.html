How To Look Pretty With A Square Face

Written by Aprilia Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a square face, you may feel like your features are too harsh or angular. But with a few simple beauty tips and tricks, you can highlight your best features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we’ll show you how to look pretty with a square face using makeup, hair styling, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Pretty with a Square Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Beauty Tips for Square Faces
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Square Faces

How to Look Pretty with a Square Face

The key to looking pretty with a square face is to soften your features and create a more balanced look. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape
  • Use makeup to contour your face and highlight your best features
  • Wear clothing that flatters your body shape
  • Take care of your skin to enhance your natural beauty

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to look pretty with a square face:

  1. Start with a clean, moisturized face
  2. Apply a primer to smooth out your skin and create a base for your makeup
  3. Use a foundation that matches your skin tone to even out your complexion
  4. Apply a concealer to any blemishes or dark circles under your eyes
  5. Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows under your cheekbones and along your jawline to soften your features
  6. Add a touch of blush or bronzer to the apples of your cheeks to add warmth to your face
  7. Define your eyebrows with a pencil or powder to frame your face
  8. Apply eyeshadow in neutral tones to enhance your eyes without overpowering your face
  9. Use a black or brown eyeliner to define your eyes
  10. Finish with a coat of mascara to make your lashes look longer and fuller

Beauty Tips for Square Faces

Here are some beauty tips for square faces:

  • Choose a hairstyle with soft layers or waves to soften your features
  • Wear earrings that are long and dangly to draw attention away from your jawline
  • Try a bold lip color to add dimension to your face
  • Avoid wearing sunglasses with square frames that can compete with your face shape
  • Use a highlighter on the high points of your face to bring out your best features

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about square faces:

What is a square face?

A square face has a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square or angular shape.

What hairstyles are good for square faces?

Hairstyles with soft layers, waves, or curls are good for square faces. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face look more angular.

What makeup should I use for a square face?

Use contouring powder or cream to create shadows under your cheekbones and along your jawline to soften your features. Add a touch of blush or bronzer to the apples of your cheeks to add warmth to your face. Use neutral eyeshadow tones and define your eyes and eyebrows to frame your face.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Here are some hair care tips for square faces:

  • Choose a hairstyle with soft layers or waves to soften your features
  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face look more angular
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can emphasize your jawline
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

By following these beauty tips and hair care tips, you can enhance your natural beauty and look pretty with a square face. Remember to choose hairstyles, makeup, and clothing that flatter your face shape and make you feel confident and beautiful.
