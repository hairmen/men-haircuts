How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Boy Tiktok

Written by William Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you want to achieve that boyish look for your hair, then you’re in luck because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair look like a boy tiktok. By following these simple steps and tips, you’ll be able to rock that stylish and edgy look in no time.

How to Make Your Hair Look Like a Boy Tiktok

The boy tiktok hairstyle is all about having a messy yet stylish look. To achieve this look, you need to have the right haircut, use the right hair products, and style your hair properly. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Get the Right Haircut

The first step in achieving the boy tiktok look is to get the right haircut. You need to have short hair that’s cut in layers. The top should be longer than the sides and the back. You can show a picture of the style you want to your hairdresser to get the right cut.

Step 2: Use the Right Hair Products

After your haircut, it’s time to use the right hair products. You need to use a pomade or a wax to create that messy look. Apply the product on your fingertips and run them through your hair. You can also use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right haircut and the right products, it’s time to style your hair. Use your fingers to create that messy look. You can also use a comb or a brush to create some texture. Remember, the boy tiktok look is all about looking effortless and messy.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your hair look like a boy tiktok:

  1. Get the right haircut
  2. Use the right hair products
  3. Style your hair using your fingers, a comb or a brush
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To maintain the boy tiktok look, you need to follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it off its natural oils
  • Use a dry shampoo to freshen up your hair in between washes
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh your hair down
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I achieve the boy tiktok look with long hair?

No, the boy tiktok look is all about having short hair. You need to have a haircut that’s cut in layers to achieve the look.

What hair products should I use?

You should use a pomade or a wax to create that messy look. You can also use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it off its natural oils. You can use a dry shampoo to freshen up your hair in between washes.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently
  • Eat a balanced diet to keep your hair healthy from the inside out
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these simple steps and tips, you can easily make your hair look like a boy tiktok. Whether you want to rock that edgy and stylish look for a night out or just for everyday wear, this hairstyle is perfect for you. So go ahead and give it a try!
