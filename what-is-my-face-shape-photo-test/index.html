What Is My Face Shape Photo Test

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've ever wondered what your face shape is, there's a simple and easy test you can do using just a photo. This test is called the "What is My Face Shape Photo Test," and it can help you determine your face shape in just a few minutes. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about this test, including how to do it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice based on your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do the Face Shape Photo Test
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Determining Your Face Shape
  • FAQs About Face Shape Photo Test
  • Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

How to Do the Face Shape Photo Test

The first step in determining your face shape using a photo test is to take a clear, straight-on photo of your face. Make sure your hair is pulled back and your face is free of any accessories, such as glasses or hats. You can take the photo with a smartphone or a camera, as long as it's clear and high-quality.

Next, you'll need to upload the photo to a face shape photo test website or app. There are several options available online, such as FaceShapeApp.com or the What's My Face Shape app. Once you upload the photo, the test will analyze your facial features and provide you with your face shape.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions for doing the face shape photo test:

  1. Take a clear, straight-on photo of your face.
  2. Upload the photo to a face shape photo test website or app.
  3. Wait for the test to analyze your facial features.
  4. View your face shape result.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

If you prefer not to use a face shape photo test, there are a few tips you can use to determine your face shape on your own. These include:

  • Looking in the mirror and tracing the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick.
  • Measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a tape measure.
  • Comparing your face shape to common face shape charts or diagrams online.

FAQs About Face Shape Photo Test

Q: Can a face shape photo test be inaccurate?

A: Yes, a face shape photo test may not always be 100% accurate, as it relies on facial recognition software. However, it can still give you a general idea of your face shape.

Q: Is it possible to have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes, it's possible to have a combination of face shapes, such as a square face with rounded features or an oval face with a prominent jawline.

Q: Do face shapes change over time?

A: No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which doesn't change over time. However, your facial features may change due to aging, weight gain or loss, or other factors.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Knowing your face shape can also help you choose the most flattering hairstyles and haircuts. Here are some hair care tips based on different face shapes:

  • Round face: Choose hairstyles that add height and volume on top, such as a pixie cut or a layered bob. Avoid chin-length haircuts or blunt bangs, which can emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Square face: Soften your features with wavy or layered hairstyles. Avoid straight, blunt cuts or center parts, which can make your face appear more angular.
  • Heart-shaped face: Opt for styles that add volume to the sides, such as long layers or side-swept bangs. Avoid short, choppy cuts or high ponytails, which can make your face look top-heavy.
  • Oval face: Most hairstyles look good on oval faces, so experiment with different lengths and styles. Avoid hairstyles that cover your face, such as heavy bangs or long veils.

Now that you know everything about the face shape photo test, you can determine your face shape and choose the most flattering hairstyles and haircuts for your features.
