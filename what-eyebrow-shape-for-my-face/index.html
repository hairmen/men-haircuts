What Eyebrow Shape Is Best For My Face

Written by Luffy Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to shaping your eyebrows, it's important to consider your face shape. Not all eyebrow shapes will flatter every face shape, so it's important to find the right shape for you. In this article, we'll go over what eyebrow shape is best for your face shape, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new shape.

Description

When deciding on what eyebrow shape is best for your face, it's important to first understand your face shape. Your face shape will determine the most flattering eyebrow shape for you. The most common face shapes are round, oval, heart, square, and diamond.

Round Face Shape

For those with a round face shape, it's best to go for a high arch eyebrow shape. This will help elongate your face and create the illusion of a longer face shape. Avoid rounded eyebrows as it can make your face look even rounder.

Oval Face Shape

Those with an oval face shape are lucky as most eyebrow shapes will flatter their face. However, a soft angled eyebrow shape is best as it will complement the natural curves of your face.

Heart Face Shape

For those with a heart face shape, it's best to go for a rounded eyebrow shape. This will help balance out your face shape and soften your features. Avoid high arches as it can make your forehead look even larger.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, a soft angled or rounded eyebrow shape is best. This will help soften your features and create a more feminine look.

Diamond Face Shape

For those with a diamond face shape, a curved or rounded eyebrow shape is best. This will help balance out your features and soften your jawline. Avoid high arches as it can make your face look even longer.

How To

Now that you know what eyebrow shape is best for your face shape, it's time to start shaping your eyebrows. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Brush Your Eyebrows

Brush your eyebrows upward using a spoolie brush. This will help you see the natural shape of your eyebrows.

Step 2: Determine Your Arch

Using a brow pencil, determine where your arch should be. The arch should be at the highest point of your eyebrow and should align with the outer edge of your iris.

Step 3: Remove Unwanted Hair

Using tweezers, remove any unwanted hair outside of your natural eyebrow shape. Be careful not to overpluck as this can lead to sparse eyebrows.

Step 4: Trim Your Eyebrows

If your eyebrows are long, trim them using a pair of scissors. Brush your eyebrows upward and trim any hairs that extend beyond the natural shape of your eyebrow.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new eyebrow shape:

- Use a brow gel to keep your eyebrows in place. - Avoid overplucking as it can lead to sparse eyebrows. - Fill in any sparse areas using a brow pencil or powder. - Avoid using a magnifying mirror as it can distort your natural eyebrow shape.

FAQ

How often should I shape my eyebrows?

You should shape your eyebrows every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired shape.

What should I do if I overplucked my eyebrows?

If you overplucked your eyebrows, avoid tweezing for a few weeks to allow the hairs to grow back. In the meantime, fill in any sparse areas using a brow pencil or powder.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your eyebrows just like you take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy eyebrows:

- Avoid using hot tools such as a hair dryer on your eyebrows. - Use a brow serum to promote hair growth. - Avoid rubbing your eyebrows as this can lead to hair loss. - Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and eyebrows hydrated.

Now that you know what eyebrow shape is best for your face shape, it's time to start shaping your eyebrows. Remember to take your time and be patient. It may take a few tries to get your desired shape, but once you do, you'll be amazed at how much of a difference it can make to your overall appearance.
