How To Style Your Hair With Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who has recently got bangs, you might be wondering about the best ways to style them. Styling your hair with bangs can be a little tricky, but with the right techniques, you can make them look amazing. In this article, we'll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair with bangs.

How to Wash Your Hair with Bangs

The first step to styling your hair with bangs is to wash them. While you can use any shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, it's important to ensure that you don't get the shampoo in your eyes while washing your bangs. To avoid this, you can use a headband or clip your bangs back while washing your hair.

Blow-Drying Your Hair with Bangs

After washing your hair, it's time to blow-dry them. While blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. This will help you achieve a sleek and smooth look.

Styling Your Bangs with a Flat Iron

If you want to style your bangs with a flat iron, start by sectioning your hair. Take a small section of your bangs and run the flat iron over them while pulling them down. This will give you a straight and sleek look.

Styling Your Bangs with a Curling Iron

If you want to add some curls to your bangs, you can use a curling iron. Start by sectioning your hair and taking a small section of your bangs. Wrap it around the curling iron and hold it for a few seconds. This will add some bounce and volume to your bangs.

Adding Volume to Your Bangs

If you have thin or fine hair, you might want to add some volume to your bangs. To do this, you can use a volumizing spray or mousse before blow-drying your hair. You can also use a teasing comb to tease your bangs slightly for added volume.

Putting Your Bangs Up

If you want to change up your look, you can put your bangs up. You can use bobby pins or a clip to secure your bangs in place. This will give you a sleek and polished look.

Using Hair Accessories with Bangs

You can also use hair accessories to style your hair with bangs. Headbands, hair clips, and barrettes can add some extra flair to your bangs. You can experiment with different hair accessories to find the ones that suit your style.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. How often should I wash my hair with bangs?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you might need to wash your hair with bangs more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair with bangs less often.

Q. How do I prevent my bangs from getting oily?

A. You can use dry shampoo or baby powder to absorb excess oil from your bangs. You can also use a blotting paper to remove oil from your bangs.

Q. Can I style my bangs without heat tools?

A. Yes, you can style your bangs without heat tools. You can try air-drying your hair with bangs or using a hair roller to add some curls.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with Bangs

  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs for a sleek look.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs.
  • Use hair accessories to add some extra flair to your bangs.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that suit your style.

Hair Care for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking great, you need to take care of them. You can trim your bangs regularly to prevent split ends. You can also use a hair mask or deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your hair with bangs like a pro. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a more casual and effortless look, you can experiment with different styles to find the ones that suit you.
