Men s Haircut Long On Top Short Sides Tutorial

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? The men's haircut long on top short sides is the perfect choice for you! This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and has become a classic look for men of all ages. In this tutorial, we will guide you through the process of getting a men's haircut long on top short sides, step by step. So, let's get started!

How to

The men's haircut long on top short sides is a combination of short hair on the sides and long hair on the top. This haircut is also known as the undercut, and it is popular among men who want a stylish and modern look. Here is how to get this haircut:

Step by step

Step 1: Choose the right length

The first step in getting a men's haircut long on top short sides is to choose the right length. You will need to decide how short you want the sides to be and how long you want the top to be. It's essential to have a clear idea of what you want before you visit your hairstylist.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Before you get your haircut, it's essential to wash your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair smoothly and evenly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to clean your hair and remove any dirt or oil.

Step 3: Start with the sides

The next step is to start cutting the sides of your hair. Your hairstylist will use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. They will gradually decrease the guard size to create a fade effect. The shorter the guard, the closer the haircut will be to the scalp.

Step 4: Cut the top

After trimming the sides, it's time to cut the top. Your hairstylist will use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length. They may also use a razor to thin out the hair and create texture. The length of the top should be longer than the sides to create a contrast.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once your hair has been cut, it's time to style it. You can use a hair product, such as gel or pomade, to create a slicked-back look or a messy, textured look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's haircut long on top short sides:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a hair product to create a slicked-back look or a messy, textured look.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to get a men's haircut long on top short sides?

A: It usually takes around 30 minutes to an hour to get a men's haircut long on top short sides, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I get a men's haircut long on top short sides if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a men's haircut long on top short sides if you have curly hair. Your hairstylist will adjust the length and texture of your hair to create a suitable style for you.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut long on top short sides looking great, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair product, such as gel or pomade, to style your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your men's haircut long on top short sides with confidence and style!
