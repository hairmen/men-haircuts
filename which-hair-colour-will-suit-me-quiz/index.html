Are you tired of your current hair colour and want to try something new? Or are you unsure which hair colour will suit you best? Take our hair colour quiz to find out!

Description

Our hair colour quiz is designed to help you discover the perfect hair colour for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal preferences. The quiz will ask you a series of questions about your current hair colour, skin tone, eye colour, and desired outcome. Once you have answered all the questions, you will receive a recommendation for your perfect hair colour.

How to Take the Quiz

To take the quiz, simply click on the link and follow the instructions. It should take you no more than 5-10 minutes to complete. Make sure you answer the questions honestly and to the best of your ability to get the most accurate results.

Step by Step Guide

1. Click on the link to access the quiz.
2. Read the instructions and click on the "Start Quiz" button.
3. Answer the questions about your current hair colour, skin tone, eye colour, and desired outcome.
4. Once you have answered all the questions, click on the "Submit" button.
5. Your recommended hair colour will be displayed on the screen.
6. Take note of your recommended hair colour and consider booking an appointment with your hairdresser to achieve the look.

Tips

FAQ

What if I don't like the recommended hair colour?

The recommended hair colour is just a suggestion based on your answers. It's important to remember that the final decision is up to you. If you don't like the recommended hair colour, you can always retake the quiz or consult with your hairdresser for more options.

Is the quiz accurate?

The quiz is designed to give you a general idea of which hair colour may suit you best. However, it's important to keep in mind that everyone's skin tone and eye colour are unique, and the final decision should be based on your personal preferences and consultation with your hairdresser.

Can I take the quiz more than once?

Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like to explore different hair colour options.

Hair Care

Once you have achieved your new hair colour, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Use products specifically designed for coloured hair, and avoid excessive heat styling and sun exposure. Regularly visit your hairdresser for touch-ups and trims to keep your hair looking its best.
