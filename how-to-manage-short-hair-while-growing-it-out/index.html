How To Manage Short Hair While Growing It Out

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’ve recently cut your hair short and are now regretting the decision, you’re not alone. Many people have felt the same way after getting a short haircut. The good news is that there are ways to manage your short hair while growing it out. In this article, we’ll provide you with tips and tricks on how to manage short hair while growing it out.

How to Manage Short Hair While Growing it Out

The first step to managing short hair while growing it out is to embrace the awkward phase. It’s normal for your hair to look a little funky during this time, so don’t stress too much about it. Here are some tips to help you manage your hair:

1. Accessorize

One of the easiest ways to manage short hair while growing it out is to accessorize. Try wearing headbands, barrettes, and hair clips to keep your hair out of your face. This will also help to add some personality to your hairstyle.

2. Experiment with Hairstyles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Try braiding your hair, creating a messy bun or adding some waves. These hairstyles will help to add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more manageable.

3. Invest in Hair Products

Investing in the right hair products can make all the difference when it comes to managing short hair while growing it out. Look for products that will add volume and texture to your hair, such as dry shampoo, sea salt spray, and texturizing spray. These products will help to make your hair look fuller and more manageable.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to managing short hair while growing it out:

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing short hair while growing it out:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Be patient. Growing out your hair takes time, so don’t expect overnight results.
  • Keep your hair healthy by eating a balanced diet, drinking plenty of water, and avoiding heat styling tools.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for advice on how to manage your hair during this transition period.

FAQ

How long does it take to grow out short hair?

It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year or more to grow out short hair.

Can I still color my hair while growing it out?

Yes, you can still color your hair while growing it out. Just be sure to use a color that matches your natural hair color to avoid any noticeable roots.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it healthy and avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair while growing it out:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can manage your short hair while growing it out and make the transition period much easier. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and before you know it, you’ll have long, beautiful locks!
