How To Shape Eyebrows Professionally A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of struggling with your eyebrows every day? Do you want to learn how to shape your eyebrows professionally? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to shape your eyebrows like a pro. From choosing the right tools to maintaining your brows, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Tools
  • Preparing Your Eyebrows
  • Mapping Your Eyebrows
  • Trimming Your Eyebrows
  • Plucking Your Eyebrows
  • Aftercare Tips
  • FAQs

Choosing the Right Tools

The first step in shaping your eyebrows like a pro is choosing the right tools. You'll need:

  • Eyebrow brush or spoolie
  • Eyebrow scissors
  • Eyebrow tweezers
  • Eyebrow pencil or powder

Make sure that your tweezers are slanted and have a good grip. Your scissors should be small and sharp. Your eyebrow pencil or powder should match your hair color.

Preparing Your Eyebrows

Before you start shaping your eyebrows, make sure that your skin is clean and dry. Use an alcohol wipe to sanitize your tweezers. Brush your eyebrows upwards and trim any long hairs with scissors.

Mapping Your Eyebrows

The key to shaping your eyebrows like a pro is to map them out first. Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to mark the following points:

  • The beginning of your eyebrow (where the eyebrow starts at the bridge of your nose)
  • The arch of your eyebrow (where the eyebrow is highest)
  • The end of your eyebrow (where the eyebrow ends at the outer corner of your eye)

Connect the dots with your eyebrow pencil or powder.

Trimming Your Eyebrows

Use your scissors to trim any hairs that are longer than the rest. Brush your eyebrows downwards and trim any long hairs that stick out.

Plucking Your Eyebrows

Now it's time to start plucking. Hold your skin taut with your fingers and use your tweezers to pluck any stray hairs that fall outside of your mapped out shape. Start with the hairs underneath your eyebrow and work your way up. Be careful not to over-pluck!

Aftercare Tips

After you've finished shaping your eyebrows, apply a soothing gel or cream to reduce redness and inflammation. Avoid touching your eyebrows for a few hours to prevent further irritation. If you have sensitive skin, apply a cold compress to your eyebrows.

FAQs

How often should I shape my eyebrows?

You should shape your eyebrows every four to six weeks. If you have fast-growing hair, you may need to shape them more often.

Should I use wax to shape my eyebrows?

Waxing is not recommended for shaping eyebrows because it can cause irritation and damage to the skin. Stick to tweezing or threading instead.

Can I shape my eyebrows at home?

Yes, you can shape your eyebrows at home as long as you have the right tools and technique. However, if you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to visit a professional.

Eyebrow Hair Care

Now that you know how to shape your eyebrows like a pro, it's important to take care of them. Use an eyebrow brush or spoolie to brush your eyebrows daily. Apply castor oil or coconut oil to your eyebrows to promote hair growth. Avoid over-plucking and harsh chemicals.

By following these tips, you'll have perfectly shaped eyebrows in no time!
