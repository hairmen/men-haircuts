What Is The Best Type Of Human Hair Wig To Buy

Written by Daniel Aug 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering purchasing a human hair wig but don't know where to start? With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for you. In this article, we will break down the different types of human hair wigs and help you determine which one is right for you.

Description

Human hair wigs are a popular choice for those who want a natural-looking and durable wig. They are made from real human hair, which allows them to be styled and treated just like your natural hair. There are several different types of human hair wigs available, each with its own unique characteristics and benefits.

How to Choose the Right Human Hair Wig

When choosing a human hair wig, there are several factors to consider:

  • Cap construction
  • Hair texture
  • Hair length
  • Hair color

The cap construction refers to how the wig is constructed and how it fits on your head. Some common cap constructions include full lace, lace front, and monofilament. The hair texture can be straight, wavy, or curly, and the hair length can range from short to long. Finally, consider the hair color that best matches your natural hair or the look you are going for.

Step-by-Step Guide to Buying a Human Hair Wig

Follow these steps to ensure you choose the best human hair wig for your needs:

  1. Determine your budget
  2. Decide on cap construction
  3. Select hair texture and length
  4. Choose a hair color
  5. Read reviews and compare brands
  6. Consult with a wig specialist

By following these steps, you can feel confident in your decision and know that you have chosen the best human hair wig for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Human Hair Wig

Once you have purchased your human hair wig, it's important to take proper care of it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your wig:

  • Use a wig stand to store your wig when not in use
  • Wash your wig every 7-10 wears
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner designed for human hair wigs
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig
  • Protect your wig from sunlight and heat

By following these tips, you can keep your human hair wig looking beautiful and natural for years to come.

FAQs

Q: Can I color my human hair wig?

A: Yes, you can color your human hair wig just like you would your natural hair. However, it's important to have a professional colorist do it to ensure the best results.

Q: How long will my human hair wig last?

A: With proper care and maintenance, a human hair wig can last up to a year or more.

Q: Can I swim or shower with my human hair wig?

A: We do not recommend swimming or showering with your human hair wig, as it can cause damage to the hair and the cap.

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing the best type of human hair wig to buy, it's important to consider your budget, cap construction, hair texture, length, and color. By following our step-by-step guide and taking proper care of your wig, you can enjoy a natural-looking and durable wig for years to come.
