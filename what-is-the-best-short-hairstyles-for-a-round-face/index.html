What Is The Best Short Hairstyles For A Round Face

Are you looking for the perfect short hairstyle for your round face? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for a round face and give you tips on how to maintain your new look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The best short hairstyles for a round face are those that elongate the face and create the illusion of a longer, more oval shape. These hairstyles should also add volume to the crown of the head and create height at the top.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. Some people may prefer a sleek, straight look while others may prefer a more voluminous, textured look. Consult with your stylist to determine which style will work best for you.

Face Shape

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine the outline of your face in the mirror. If your face is widest at the cheeks and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you have a round face.

Length

Short hairstyles come in a variety of lengths, from a pixie cut to a bob. When choosing a length, consider the shape of your face and the features you want to highlight. A shorter cut can help elongate the face, while a longer cut can add volume and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in choosing a short hairstyle. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and create the illusion of thickness. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help tame the volume and create a more polished look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing the best short hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture
  3. Choose a length that flatters your features
  4. Consult with your stylist

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo to add volume to the crown of the head
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for a round face:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that elongates the face and adds volume to the crown of the head. This can be achieved with a layered cut, a side-swept bang, or a textured pixie cut.

Can a round face pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, a round face can pull off a pixie cut. The key is to add texture and volume to the crown of the head to create height and elongate the face.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools too often. Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut, and experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the best short hairstyle for a round face can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and guidance, you can find the perfect style to flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. Consult with your stylist, consider your face shape and hair texture, and experiment with different lengths and styles until you find the perfect look for you.
