Is Short Hair Good For Oblong Face

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Oblong faces are characterized by their long and narrow shape. If you have an oblong face, you may be wondering if short hair is a good choice for you. The good news is that short hair can be a great option for oblong faces! In this article, we will explore why short hair is a good choice for oblong faces, how to choose the right short hairstyle, and how to care for your short hair.

Why Short Hair is Good for Oblong Faces

Short hair can actually help balance out an oblong face. When you have long hair, it can elongate your face even more. Short hair, on the other hand, can create the illusion of width and make your face appear more proportional.

Another benefit of short hair for oblong faces is that it can help draw attention to your facial features. With long hair, your hair may be the focal point of your look. With short hair, your face becomes the focal point, which can be a great way to showcase your features.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle for your oblong face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Length: Look for hairstyles that are chin-length or shorter. This will help create the illusion of width.
  • Layers: Layers can also help create the illusion of width. Look for hairstyles with layers around the face.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a great way to break up the length of your face. Consider getting side-swept bangs or blunt bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a short haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research hairstyles that you like and that will flatter your oblong face.
  2. Find a stylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  4. Communicate with your stylist about what you're looking for and any concerns you may have.
  5. Relax and enjoy your new look!

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Oblong Faces

Once you have your short haircut, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Use volumizing products: To create the illusion of width, use volumizing products like mousse or root-lifting spray.
  • Try different partings: Experiment with different partings to find one that flatters your face shape.
  • Accessorize: Adding a headband, barrette, or other hair accessory can help add width to your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different type of maintenance than long hair. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Short hair can get greasy quickly, so make sure to wash it every other day or so.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner: Short hair doesn't need heavy conditioners. Look for a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims: To keep your short haircut looking fresh, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look too round?

A: No, short hair can actually help balance out an oblong face and create the illusion of width.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, there are plenty of ways to style short hair up. Talk to your stylist about options like braids, buns, and twists.

Q: Do I need to use different styling products for short hair?

A: It's a good idea to use lightweight styling products that won't weigh your hair down. Look for products like mousse, root-lifting spray, and texturizing spray.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for oblong faces. It can help create the illusion of width, draw attention to your facial features, and be a fun and stylish change. Just make sure to choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape and take good care of your short hair.
