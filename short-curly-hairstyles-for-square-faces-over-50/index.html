Short Curly Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our face shape changes, and it's essential to choose a hairstyle that complements our features. Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, and the width of the forehead and cheekbones are almost equal. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short curly hairstyles for square faces over 50.

Description

Short curly hairstyles are trendy and low-maintenance, making them perfect for mature women. They add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and bouncier. However, not all hairstyles suit square faces as they can accentuate the angular features, making them appear harsh.

How to

Before you choose a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the results. If they are almost equal, you have a square face. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step by Step

1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in short curly hairstyles for mature women. They can give you personalized advice on the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair. Short curly hairstyles, such as a pixie cut or a bob, are perfect for square faces. They add height to the crown, making your face appear longer.

3. Consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can soften the angular features of your face, making it appear more feminine.

4. Use hair products that enhance your curls. Curly hair tends to be dry and frizzy, so it's essential to use hair products that moisturize and define your curls.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs. They can make your face appear boxy.

2. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair. They can make your hair look fuller and more vibrant.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles and hair products until you find the perfect look.

FAQ

Q: Can women over 50 wear short curly hairstyles?

A: Yes, short curly hairstyles are perfect for mature women as they add volume and texture to their hair, making it look fuller and bouncier.

Q: What is the best short curly hairstyle for a square face?

A: The best short curly hairstyle for a square face is a pixie cut or a bob. They add height to the crown, making your face appear longer.

Hair Care

Short curly hairstyles require minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to define your curls and add moisture.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

4. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Short curly hairstyles for square faces over 50 can be flattering and stylish. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect look for you.
