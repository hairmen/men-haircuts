How To Know If Shoulder Length Hair Will Suit You A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Shoulder length hair is a popular choice among women, and for good reason. It's a versatile length that can work with a variety of hair types and face shapes. However, not everyone can pull off shoulder length hair. If you're considering making the switch, it's important to know whether or not it will suit you. In this post, we'll go through the steps to determine if shoulder length hair is right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Think About Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle
  • Step 4: Try a Virtual Makeover

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding whether or not shoulder length hair will suit you is your face shape. Here are some guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, shoulder length hair can help elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, shoulder length hair can soften your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any length of hair.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, shoulder length hair can balance out your features.

Of course, these are just general guidelines. Ultimately, the best way to determine if shoulder length hair will suit you is to try it out and see how you feel.

Step 2: Think About Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Here are some guidelines:

  • If you have thin hair, shoulder length hair can give the illusion of thicker hair.
  • If you have curly hair, shoulder length hair can be a great length to show off your curls.
  • If you have thick hair, shoulder length hair can help lighten the weight of your hair.
  • If you have straight hair, shoulder length hair can give you a sleek and polished look.

Again, these are just general guidelines. Your hair type is unique to you, so it's important to consider how shoulder length hair will work with your specific hair type.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when deciding on a haircut. If you're someone who doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair, shoulder length hair may not be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you're someone who enjoys experimenting with different hairstyles, shoulder length hair can be a great length to work with.

Step 4: Try a Virtual Makeover

If you're still unsure about whether or not shoulder length hair will suit you, try a virtual makeover. There are plenty of websites and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles. This can give you a better idea of what you'll look like with shoulder length hair.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

If you do decide to go for shoulder length hair, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep things fresh.

FAQs

Will shoulder length hair look good on me if I have a double chin?

Shoulder length hair can actually be a great length to help conceal a double chin. It can help draw attention away from your chin and towards your shoulders.

Can I wear my hair up if I have shoulder length hair?

Absolutely! Shoulder length hair is a great length for wearing your hair up in a variety of styles, from a simple ponytail to a fancy updo.

Is shoulder length hair easy to maintain?

It depends on your hair type and how you choose to style it. Some people find shoulder length hair to be easy to maintain, while others may find it more challenging. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for shoulder length hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush that won't damage your hair.

Overall, shoulder length hair can be a great choice for many women. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can determine whether or not it will suit you. If you do decide to make the switch, be sure to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
