Description

Long faces and big noses can be challenging when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. However, short hairstyles are a great option as they can help to balance out your features and draw attention away from your nose. In this article, we will be sharing some of the best short hairstyles for long faces and big noses, as well as tips on how to style and care for your hair.

How to

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a long face and big nose, it's important to consider your face shape and hair type. A layered bob or pixie cut can help to add volume and texture to your hair, while a side-swept fringe can help to balance out your features. You may also want to consider highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

Step by Step

If you're unsure about which short hairstyle to go for, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type, as well as provide advice on how to maintain your new look at home.

When styling your hair at home, start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray before blow-drying your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. You can then use a straightener or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a long face and big nose:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type
  • Add volume and texture to your hair with layers
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and texture
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using a straightener or curling iron
  • Use a hairspray to hold your style in place

FAQ

Q: Are there any short hairstyles that should be avoided for a long face and big nose?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too short or flat as they can make your nose appear larger. It's also best to avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or straight as they can elongate your face.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a long face and big nose?

A: Yes, a side-swept fringe can help to balance out your features and draw attention away from your nose.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly with products that are suitable for your hair type, as well as using a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. You may also want to consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, there are many short hairstyles that can help to balance out a long face and big nose. By choosing a style that complements your face shape and hair type, adding volume and texture to your hair, and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look.
