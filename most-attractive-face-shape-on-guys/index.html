The Most Attractive Face Shape On Guys A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering what the most attractive face shape on guys is? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the top face shapes that are considered the most attractive and how to achieve them. We will also provide tips on how to take care of your facial hair and maintain your desired look. So, let’s dive in!

The Most Attractive Face Shape on Guys

According to various studies and surveys, the most attractive face shape on guys is the square face. This is because a square face is defined by a strong jawline, prominent cheekbones, and a broad forehead. These features are considered to be masculine and are associated with strength and dominance.

However, this doesn’t mean that other face shapes aren’t attractive. Some other popular face shapes include oval, round, and heart-shaped faces. Each of these shapes has its own unique features that can be accentuated to create an attractive look.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face is characterized by a slightly rounded jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles and beard styles.

Round Face Shape

A round face is characterized by a soft jawline, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. To make this face shape more attractive, it is recommended to keep the hair short on the sides and longer on top to create the illusion of a longer face. A well-groomed beard can also help to add definition to the jawline.

Heart-Shaped Face

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. To make this face shape more attractive, it is recommended to keep the hair longer on the sides and shorter on top to balance out the width of the forehead. A light beard or stubble can also help to add definition to the chin.

How to Achieve the Perfect Face Shape

If you want to achieve the perfect face shape, there are a few things you can do:

  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly to keep your face and body in shape
  • Use skincare products to keep your skin looking healthy and clear
  • Consider cosmetic procedures such as rhinoplasty or jawline contouring to enhance your features

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Desired Look

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve your desired look:

  1. Choose the face shape that you want to achieve
  2. Consult with a hairstylist or barber to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape
  3. Experiment with different beard and mustache styles to find the one that suits you best
  4. Use grooming products such as beard oil and wax to keep your facial hair looking neat and tidy
  5. Consider cosmetic procedures if you want to enhance your features

Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your facial hair:

  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat
  • Use beard oil to keep your facial hair moisturized and soft
  • Invest in a good quality beard trimmer to make grooming easier
  • Try different styles to see what works best for your face shape
  • Consider using a beard comb to keep your facial hair tangle-free

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most attractive beard style?

The most attractive beard style varies depending on your face shape. Generally, a well-groomed beard that complements your features is considered to be the most attractive.

What is the best haircut for a square face?

The best haircut for a square face is one that is short on the sides and longer on top. This helps to accentuate the jawline and forehead.

How can I make my face look more masculine?

You can make your face look more masculine by accentuating your jawline and forehead. This can be achieved through a well-groomed beard, a short haircut, and cosmetic procedures such as jawline contouring.

Hair Care for Men

When it comes to hair care for men, it is important to choose the right products for your hair type. Here are some tips:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for men’s hair
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to avoid tangles
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat
  • Use styling products such as gel or pomade to achieve your desired look
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can cause damage

Conclusion

Overall, the most attractive face shape on guys is the square face. However, other face shapes such as oval, round, and heart-shaped can also be attractive when accentuated correctly. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve your desired look and maintain it with ease. Remember to take care of your facial hair and hair in general to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
