Curly Top Low Taper Fade A Relaxed Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new haircut that combines length and style? Look no further than the curly top low taper fade! This trendy cut features a longer, curly top that gradually fades into shorter sides and back. It's a versatile style that works for all hair types and face shapes, and it's easy to maintain with regular trims. Here's everything you need to know about getting and caring for a curly top low taper fade.

Description: What is a Curly Top Low Taper Fade?

A curly top low taper fade is a haircut that blends a longer, curly top with shorter sides and back. The top is typically left longer and styled with natural curls or waves, while the sides and back are tapered down to create a fade effect. The fade can be high or low, depending on your preference, and the length of the fade can vary as well. This cut is popular among men, but it can also work for women with curly or wavy hair.

How to: Getting a Curly Top Low Taper Fade

If you're interested in getting a curly top low taper fade, here's what to expect:

Step 1: Find a skilled barber or stylist.

Not all barbers or stylists are experienced with curly hair, so it's important to find someone who knows how to work with your hair type. Look for reviews or ask for recommendations from friends or family members who have curly hair.

Step 2: Decide on the length and style of the top.

Before getting the cut, think about how long you want the top to be and how you want it styled. Do you prefer loose, natural curls or tighter, defined curls? Bring pictures or examples to show your barber or stylist.

Step 3: Choose the length and style of the fade.

The fade can be high, medium, or low, and the length of the fade can vary as well. Some people prefer a more gradual fade, while others like a more dramatic contrast between the top and sides. Again, bring pictures or examples to show your barber or stylist.

Step 4: Sit back and relax.

Once you've discussed your preferences with your barber or stylist, sit back and let them work their magic. The cut should take about 30-45 minutes, depending on the complexity of the style.

Tips: Caring for a Curly Top Low Taper Fade

Once you have your curly top low taper fade, here are some tips for maintaining and styling it:

Tip 1: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.

Tip 2: Apply a curl-defining product.

To enhance your curls and prevent frizz, apply a curl-defining product such as a curl cream or gel to damp hair. Scrunch the product into your curls and let it dry naturally or use a diffuser attachment on a blow dryer.

Tip 3: Schedule regular trims.

To maintain the shape and style of your curly top low taper fade, schedule regular trims with your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is a curly top low taper fade suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a curly top low taper fade can work for all hair types, but it's especially flattering for those with curly or wavy hair.

Q: How long does a curly top low taper fade last?

A: The length of time between cuts will vary depending on the individual's hair growth and styling preferences, but generally, the cut will last 4-6 weeks.

Q: How do I style my curly top low taper fade?

A: To style your curly top, apply a curl-defining product and scrunch your curls with your fingers. For the sides and back, use a comb or brush to style them in the desired direction.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

A curly top low taper fade is a stylish, low-maintenance cut that's perfect for those who want length and style without the fuss. With the right products and regular trims, you can keep your curls looking healthy and defined. So go ahead and try out this trendy cut â you won't be disappointed!
