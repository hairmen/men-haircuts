How To Dress With A Shaved Head Woman Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Dressing with a shaved head woman can seem daunting at first, but it doesn't have to be. Whether you've recently shaved your head or you're considering it, there are plenty of ways to dress confidently and stylishly. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to accessorize to what to wear for different occasions.

How to Dress with a Shaved Head Woman

Step 1: Choose the Right Accessories

Accessories are a great way to add personality and flair to your outfits. When you have a shaved head, earrings, necklaces, and hats become even more important. To draw attention to your face, choose statement earrings or a bold necklace. If you're wearing a hat, make sure it fits snugly and doesn't slip off your head.

Step 2: Embrace Your Style

Just because you have a shaved head doesn't mean you have to dress a certain way. Embrace your personal style and wear what makes you feel confident and comfortable. Whether you prefer bohemian or minimalist fashion, there's no one "right" way to dress with a shaved head.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Silhouettes

A shaved head can be a bold statement, so why not pair it with bold silhouettes? Experiment with different styles, like oversized blazers or flowy dresses, to find what works best for you. Don't be afraid to step out of your comfort zone and try something new.

Tips for Dressing with a Shaved Head Woman

Tip 1: Balance Your Outfit

When you have a shaved head, it's important to balance your outfit so your head doesn't look too bare. For example, if you're wearing a sleeveless top, add a statement necklace or a scarf to draw attention to your face.

Tip 2: Play with Color

A shaved head can be a blank canvas for bold colors. Experiment with different hues and prints to make a statement with your outfit.

Tip 3: Don't Be Afraid to Show Skin

With a shaved head, you have the opportunity to show off your neck and collarbone. Don't be afraid to wear off-the-shoulder tops or scoop necklines to accentuate these features.

FAQ: Dressing with a Shaved Head Woman

Q: Can I still wear hats with a shaved head?

A: Absolutely! Hats can be a great way to protect your scalp from the sun and add some extra style to your outfit. Just make sure the hat fits snugly and doesn't slip off your head.

Q: Should I wear more makeup with a shaved head?

A: It's entirely up to you! Some women prefer to wear more makeup with a shaved head, while others choose to go more natural. Ultimately, the choice is yours.

Q: Can I wear my hair extensions with a shaved head?

A: If you have hair extensions, you can still wear them with a shaved head! Simply clip them onto the remaining hair on the sides and back of your head.

Hair Care for Shaved Head Women

Finally, it's important to take care of your shaved head. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp moisturized, and consider using a scalp oil to prevent dryness. If you're going outside, make sure to wear a hat or use sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun.

In conclusion, dressing with a shaved head woman can be fun and empowering. By choosing the right accessories, embracing your personal style, and experimenting with different silhouettes, you can create outfits that make you feel confident and stylish.
