Saw Palmetto

Saw palmetto is a natural supplement that is derived from the berries of the saw palmetto plant. It is commonly used to treat hair loss in men and women. Saw palmetto works by blocking the production of DHT, a hormone that is linked to hair loss. You can find saw palmetto supplements in capsule or liquid form at your local health food store.

How to use:

Take 320 milligrams of saw palmetto daily.

Tips:

  • Consult with your doctor before taking saw palmetto supplements.
  • Take saw palmetto with food to avoid stomach upset.
  • Do not take saw palmetto if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.

FAQ:

  • Q: Can saw palmetto be used topically?
  • A: No, saw palmetto supplements should be taken orally.

Rosemary Oil

Rosemary oil is an essential oil that is derived from the rosemary plant. It is commonly used to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Rosemary oil works by stimulating blood circulation in the scalp, which helps to nourish hair follicles. You can find rosemary oil at your local health food store.

How to use:

Mix 2-3 drops of rosemary oil with 1 tablespoon of carrier oil (such as coconut oil or olive oil). Massage the mixture into your scalp and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it out.

Tips:

  • Do not use rosemary oil if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • Do a patch test before using rosemary oil to ensure that you are not allergic to it.
  • Use a shower cap to cover your hair while the oil is on your scalp.

FAQ:

  • Q: Can rosemary oil be used on all hair types?
  • A: Yes, rosemary oil can be used on all hair types.
