How To Make Natural Curly Hair Curlier For Guys

Written by Aprilia Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a guy with natural curly hair, you may be wondering how you can make your curls even curlier. While genetics play a big role in determining the type of curl pattern you have, there are several things you can do to enhance your natural curls. In this article, we will discuss how to make natural curly hair curlier for guys.

Description

Natural curly hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you look at it. Some guys love the way their curls look and embrace them, while others wish their curls were curlier. If you fall into the latter category, don't worry. There are several things you can do to enhance your natural curls and make them curlier.

How to Make Natural Curly Hair Curlier for Guys

1. Use a Curl Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help define your curls and make them more pronounced.

2. Apply a Curl Enhancing Cream or Gel

After washing your hair, apply a curl enhancing cream or gel. This will help define your curls and make them curlier. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Scrunch Your Hair While It's Wet

Once you have applied the curl enhancing product, scrunch your hair while it's still wet. This will help create more defined curls and make them curlier. Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to gently scrunch your hair.

4. Diffuse Your Hair with a Blow Dryer

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and make them curlier. Be sure to use a low heat setting and don't dry your hair completely. Leave it slightly damp.

5. Use a Curling Iron

If you want to make your curls even curlier, you can use a curling iron. Choose a curling iron that is the same size as your curls. Wrap small sections of your hair around the curling iron and hold for a few seconds. This will help create more defined curls.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
2. Apply a curl enhancing cream or gel evenly throughout your hair.
3. Scrunch your hair while it's still wet.
4. Diffuse your hair with a blow dryer using a low heat setting.
5. Use a curling iron to create more defined curls.

Tips

- Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.
- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can break up your curls.
- Don't touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.
- Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz.

FAQ

Can I make my curls curlier without using heat?

Yes, you can make your curls curlier without using heat. Try using a curl enhancing product and scrunching your hair while it's wet. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls without using too much heat.

What type of curling iron should I use?

Choose a curling iron that is the same size as your curls. If you have tight curls, use a smaller curling iron. If you have loose curls, use a larger curling iron.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your natural curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for guys with curly hair:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
- Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it moisturized.
- Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can enhance your natural curls and make them even curlier. Embrace your natural hair and experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.
