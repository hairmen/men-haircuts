What Hair Colour Makes Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Daniel Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a simple and easy way to make your face look slimmer, changing your hair colour can be a great option. The right hair colour can help to highlight your best features and soften your face shape, making it appear slimmer and more defined.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

The key to choosing the right hair colour to make your face look slimmer is to find a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural features. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair colour for your face shape:

  • For round faces, choose darker shades such as chocolate brown or black to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • For square faces, opt for lighter shades such as blonde or caramel to soften your jawline and create a more balanced look.
  • For heart-shaped faces, choose warm shades such as honey blonde or copper to enhance your cheekbones and soften your chin.
  • For oval faces, almost any hair colour will work, but try to avoid overly bright or bold colours that can overwhelm your features.

By taking your face shape and skin tone into account, you can choose a hair colour that will complement your natural beauty and make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

Step-by-Step Guide to Changing Your Hair Colour

If you are ready to change your hair colour, here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect shade:

  1. Choose the right hair dye for your hair type and colour.
  2. Do a strand test to make sure the colour looks good on your hair.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to avoid staining your skin.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and apply the dye evenly from roots to tips.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  6. Apply conditioner to your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

By following these simple steps, you can achieve a new hair colour that will enhance your natural beauty and make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you have changed your hair colour, it is important to take care of your new look to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hair colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons that can damage your hair and fade your colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Touch up your roots regularly to keep your colour looking fresh.
  • Visit a professional hair stylist for regular trims and treatments to keep your hair healthy and strong.

By following these simple tips, you can keep your new hair colour looking fresh and vibrant for weeks to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colour makes your face look slimmer?

The best hair colour to make your face look slimmer depends on your face shape and skin tone. Darker shades such as chocolate brown or black can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face for round faces, while lighter shades such as blonde or caramel can soften a square face and enhance cheekbones for heart-shaped faces.

How often should I touch up my roots?

It is recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair colour looking fresh and vibrant.

Can changing my hair colour damage my hair?

While changing your hair colour can cause some damage to your hair, using a high-quality dye and taking proper care of your hair can help to minimize the damage and keep your locks healthy and strong.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to choosing the right hair colour to make your face look slimmer, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Visit a professional hair stylist regularly for trims and treatments.

By following these simple tips, you can maintain healthy and strong hair that will complement your new hair colour and make your face appear slimmer and more defined.
