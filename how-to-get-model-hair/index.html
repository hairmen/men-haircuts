How To Get Model Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to achieve model-worthy hair, you’ve come to the right place! Model hair is all about achieving a healthy, shiny, and voluminous mane that turns heads. Whether you have long locks or a short bob, anyone can achieve model hair with the right tools and techniques. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect model hair look.

Table of Contents

  1. How To Get Model Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Achieving Model Hair
  4. Model Hair FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips for Model Hair

How To Get Model Hair

Model hair is all about creating volume, texture, and shine. Here are the basic steps to achieve the perfect model hair:

  • Start with a healthy scalp and nourished hair
  • Blow-dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to create volume
  • Add texture with a curling iron or straightener
  • Apply a volumizing product to your roots
  • Finish with a shine-enhancing serum

Step by Step Guide

Ready to get started? Here’s a more detailed guide to achieving model hair:

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Scalp and Nourished Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to make sure it’s healthy and well-nourished. This means taking care of your scalp and using products that will help strengthen and moisturize your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup, and apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair in Sections

Once your hair is clean and towel-dried, it’s time to start blow-drying. Divide your hair into sections and use a round brush to lift each section at the roots while blow-drying. This will create volume and lift at the roots, which is key to achieving model hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the blow dryer.

Step 3: Add Texture with a Curling Iron or Straightener

Once your hair is dry, it’s time to add some texture. You can use a curling iron to create loose waves or a straightener to create beachy waves. Alternate the direction of the curl or wave to create a more natural, effortless look. Don't forget to use heat protectant spray.

Step 4: Apply a Volumizing Product to Your Roots

Now it’s time to add some volume to your hair. Apply a volumizing product to your roots, and use your fingers to massage it in. You can also use a teasing brush to create even more volume at the roots.

Step 5: Finish with a Shine-Enhancing Serum

The final step in achieving model hair is to add some shine. Apply a shine-enhancing serum to the ends of your hair, and use your fingers to distribute it evenly. This will give your hair a healthy, glossy finish.

Tips for Achieving Model Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve model-worthy hair:

  • Use a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and lift to your hair
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage
  • Don’t over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness

Model Hair FAQ

Here are some common questions and answers about achieving model hair:

Q: Can I achieve model hair with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Model hair is all about creating volume and texture, so even if you have a short bob, you can still achieve this look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to achieve model hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and texture, but in general, it’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Q: What’s the best way to create volume at the roots?

A: The best way to create volume at the roots is to blow-dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to lift each section at the roots while blow-drying.

Hair Care Tips for Model Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your hair and maintain your model-worthy locks:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can lead to damage and dryness
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve model-worthy hair that turns heads everywhere you go. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and strong, and you’ll be well on your way to achieving the perfect model hair look.
