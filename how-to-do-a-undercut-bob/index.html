How To Do An Undercut Bob

Written by Oliver Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a fresh and edgy hairstyle? An undercut bob might just be the perfect look for you! This hairstyle features a shorter length at the nape of the neck, with longer layers on top. It's a versatile look that can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to do an undercut bob step by step, along with some tips and hair care advice.

How to

Step 1: Section Your Hair

To start, section your hair into four even parts: two on top and two on the bottom. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Shave the Nape

With a pair of clippers, shave the hair at the nape of your neck. You can use a #2 or #3 guard for a shorter look, or a #4 or #5 guard for a longer look. Make sure to shave the hair evenly and follow the natural curve of your head.

Step 3: Create the Bob

Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair on top of your head into a bob shape. Keep the hair longer in the front and gradually shorter towards the back. Use a razor to add texture and soften the edges.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Use thinning shears to blend the layers on top of your head with the shorter hair at the nape of your neck. This will create a seamless transition and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, style your hair as desired. You can add waves or curls using a curling iron, or keep it straight with a flat iron. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and definition to your layers.

Tips

- Before attempting this haircut, make sure you have the proper tools and experience. If you're unsure, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting. This will ensure that your haircut is even and balanced.

- If you have thick hair, consider removing some of the bulk before starting the undercut bob. This will make it easier to manage and style.

FAQ

How often should I get my undercut bob trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your undercut bob.

Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut bob?

Yes! While the shorter hair at the nape of your neck might be visible, you can still wear your hair up in a ponytail or bun.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut bob looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat when styling, and use a heat protectant spray if necessary. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know how to do an undercut bob, it's time to try it out for yourself! With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a bold and stylish new look that's sure to turn heads.
