How To Make My Frizzy Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Nov 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who has naturally curly or wavy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try and keep it straight. Frizz, humidity, and other factors can make it seem impossible to achieve that sleek, straight look you're after. But fear not - with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to make your frizzy hair straight and smooth. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve straight, manageable hair that you'll love.

How to Make My Frizzy Hair Straight

The first step in making your frizzy hair straight is to choose the right tools and products. You'll need a good quality straightener or flat iron, as well as a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage to your hair. You may also want to invest in a smoothing serum or cream to help tame any flyaways and keep your hair looking sleek.

Once you have your tools and products, it's time to get started. Follow these steps to make your frizzy hair straight:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to help tame frizz and make your hair more manageable. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup, which can cause your hair to look dull and lifeless.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing, gently towel dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, gently blot your hair to remove excess moisture.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using your straightener, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from damage caused by the heat of the straightener. Be sure to evenly distribute the spray throughout your hair, paying special attention to the ends.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to straighten each section and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Starting with the bottom sections, use your straightener to straighten your hair. Be sure to move the straightener slowly and evenly through each section, taking care not to leave it in one spot for too long. You may need to go over each section a few times to get it completely straight.

Step 6: Apply Smoothing Serum

After straightening, apply a smoothing serum or cream to your hair. This will help to tame any flyaways and keep your hair looking sleek and shiny.

Step 7: Finish and Style

Finally, finish your look with a light hold hairspray or other styling products. This will help to keep your hair in place and maintain your sleek, straight look throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Once you've made your frizzy hair straight, there are a few things you can do to help maintain your straight look:

  • Avoid getting your hair wet or exposing it to humidity, as this can cause it to frizz up again.
  • Use a silk pillowcase, which can help to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause oils and dirt to build up and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Aim to straighten your hair no more than 2-3 times per week, and be sure to use a heat protectant spray each time.

Q: What kind of straightener should I use?

A: Look for a straightener with ceramic plates, which will help to distribute heat evenly and prevent hot spots. You may also want to consider a straightener with adjustable heat settings, so you can customize the temperature to suit your hair type.

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it's wet?

A: No, it's important to only straighten dry hair. Wet hair is more prone to damage and breakage, and can also cause your straightener to sizzle and steam.

Hair Care Products for Straight Hair

If you're looking to make your frizzy hair straight, there are a few key products you'll want to have on hand:

  • Smoothing shampoo and conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Flat iron or straightener
  • Smoothing serum or cream
  • Light hold hairspray or other styling products

By using these products and following the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve straight, smooth hair that you'll love. So go ahead and give it a try - you might just be surprised at how easy it is to make your frizzy hair straight!
