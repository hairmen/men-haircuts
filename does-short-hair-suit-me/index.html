Does Short Hair Suit Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you contemplating cutting your hair short but unsure if it will look good on you? It's a common dilemma that many women face. In this article, we'll help you answer the question, "Does short hair suit me?" by providing you with a how-to guide, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Description

Short hair is a trendy and versatile look that can be styled in various ways to suit different face shapes and personal styles. However, not everyone can pull off the short hair look. The suitability of short hair depends on several factors, including face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we'll help you determine if short hair is right for you.

How to Determine if Short Hair Suits You?

Determining if short hair suits you involves considering several factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here's how to determine if short hair is right for you:

Step 1: Identify Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if short hair suits you is to identify your face shape. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles. Short hair looks best on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a square or round face, consider longer haircuts that frame your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you is your hair texture. Short hair looks best on women with straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have very curly or coarse hair, short hair may require more maintenance and styling to look good.

Step 3: Determine Your Personal Style

Personal style is another factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you. Short hair requires confidence and a bold personality. If you prefer a more conservative or traditional look, short hair may not be for you.

Step-by-Step Guide on Getting Short Hair

If you've decided that short hair suits you, here's a step-by-step guide on how to get short hair:

Step 1: Research Short Haircuts

Research different short haircuts and styles to find one that suits your face shape and hair texture. Look for inspiration online or consult with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Book a Consultation

Book a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss the best short haircut for you. Your hairstylist can provide you with recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step 3: Prepare for Your Haircut

Before your haircut, wash your hair and avoid using any styling products. Wear clothes that you don't mind getting hair on and bring a photo of the haircut you want.

Step 4: Get Your Haircut

During your haircut, communicate with your hairstylist to ensure that you're happy with the length and style. Your hairstylist may also provide you with styling tips and recommendations for hair care products.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Maintaining short hair requires regular trims and proper hair care. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair texture.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron to style your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Consider getting a hair treatment or mask to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

1. Will short hair make my face look rounder?

Short hair can make your face look rounder if you have a round face shape. Consider a longer haircut that frames your face if you're concerned about this.

2. Can I still style my short hair?

Yes, short hair can be styled in various ways, including straight, wavy, or curly. Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron to style your hair.

3. Will short hair require more maintenance?

Short hair may require more maintenance than longer haircuts to keep it looking fresh. Regular trims and proper hair care are essential to maintain short hair.

Conclusion

So, does short hair suit you? The answer depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Follow our guide to determine if short hair is right for you and how to maintain it. Remember to consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.
