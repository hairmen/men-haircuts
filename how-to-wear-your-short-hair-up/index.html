How To Wear Your Short Hair Up

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair is a great way to showcase your face, neck, and shoulders. But what about when you want to switch things up and wear your hair up? Don't fret, because we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to wear your short hair up.

Description

Wearing your short hair up is a great way to add some variety to your hairstyle. It's perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face or when you want to show off your earrings or neck. Plus, there are plenty of ways to style short hair, whether it's with bobby pins, hair ties, or hair accessories.

How to

If you're not sure how to wear your short hair up, here are some easy steps to get you started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the type of updo you want to create. Do you want a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided style?
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun, using bobby pins or hair ties to secure it in place.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add volume and texture.
  5. Add hair accessories, such as a headband, barrette, or flower, to add some extra flair to your updo.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Let's break down the steps into more detail:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be harder to style and may not hold the shape you want. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair if necessary.

Step 2: Decide on the type of updo you want to create.

There are many different types of updos you can create with short hair, so take some time to decide on the look you want to achieve. You can go for a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided style. Look for inspiration online or in magazines and choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun.

Once you've decided on the style you want to create, gather your hair into a ponytail or bun. Use hair ties or bobby pins to secure the hair in place. If you're going for a messy look, don't worry about making it too neat. For a sleeker look, use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 4: Tease the hair at the crown of your head.

If you want to add some volume and texture to your updo, use a comb or your fingers to tease the hair at the crown of your head. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look. Be gentle when teasing your hair, so you don't damage it.

Step 5: Add hair accessories.

To add some extra flair to your updo, use hair accessories such as a headband, barrette, or flower. This can help to dress up your style and make it look more polished. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and personal style.

Step 6: Spray with hairspray.

Finally, spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place. This will help to keep your updo looking fresh and neat throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your short hair up:

  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use hairpins that match your hair color to keep your updo looking seamless.
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your hair with a curling iron or flat iron before you style it.
  • Use a light hairspray to hold your updo in place without weighing it down.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you experiment with different updos, the better you'll get at creating them.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing short hair up:

Can you wear short hair up?

Yes, you can definitely wear short hair up! There are plenty of ways to style short hair, including ponytails, buns, and braids.

What is the easiest way to wear short hair up?

The easiest way to wear short hair up is to gather it into a ponytail or bun using hair ties or bobby pins. This is a quick and easy way to keep your hair out of your face.

What hair accessories can you use to wear short hair up?

You can use a variety of hair accessories to wear short hair up, including headbands, barrettes, flowers, and hairpins. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and personal style.

Hair Care

When wearing your short hair up, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when wearing it up, as this can cause breakage and damage to the hair follicles.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful while still enjoying the versatility of wearing it up.
