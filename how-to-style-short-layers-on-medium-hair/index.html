How To Style Short Layers On Medium Hair

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have medium length hair with short layers, you may be wondering how to style it to make the most of your layers. Short layers can add volume and texture to your hair, but they can also be tricky to style. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks for styling short layers on medium hair.

Description

Medium length hair with short layers is a popular hairstyle that can be versatile and easy to maintain. Short layers can add movement and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. However, styling short layers can be challenging, especially if you're not sure how to make the most of them.

How to Style Short Layers on Medium Hair

Here are some tips and tricks for styling short layers on medium hair:

1. Blow-dry your hair upside down

One of the best ways to add volume to your short layers is to blow-dry your hair upside down. This will help lift your hair at the roots and create more volume and texture. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair as you blow-dry it, and finish with a blast of cool air to set your style.

2. Use a volumizing mousse

If you want to add even more volume to your short layers, use a volumizing mousse before you blow-dry your hair. Apply the mousse to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers. This will help create more lift and texture, making your short layers look fuller and more voluminous.

3. Try a messy bun

A messy bun is a great way to style your short layers on medium hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, leaving out your short layers. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Then, use your fingers to pull out some pieces of hair around your face and your short layers. This will create a messy, textured look that's perfect for casual occasions.

4. Use a texture spray

A texture spray can help add definition and texture to your short layers. Spray the texture spray onto your hair and use your fingers to work it through your layers. This will help create a tousled, beachy look that's perfect for summer.

5. Try a half-up hairstyle

A half-up hairstyle is a great way to show off your short layers while still keeping your hair out of your face. Gather the top half of your hair, leaving out your short layers, and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Then, use a curling iron to curl your short layers and the rest of your hair. This will create a soft, romantic look that's perfect for special occasions.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short layers on medium hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help create more volume and texture in your hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers. This will help create more lift and texture in your short layers.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush to smooth out your hair as you go. Finish with a blast of cool air to set your style.

Step 4: Use a texture spray

Spray a texture spray onto your hair and use your fingers to work it through your layers. This will help create a tousled, textured look.

Step 5: Try a half-up hairstyle

Gather the top half of your hair, leaving out your short layers, and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Then, use a curling iron to curl your short layers and the rest of your hair. This will create a soft, romantic look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short layers on medium hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for you.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your short layers looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

1. Can I still straighten my hair if I have short layers?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have short layers. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a flat iron with adjustable heat settings.

2. How often should I get my hair trimmed if I have short layers?

It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks if you have short layers. This will help keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your short layers looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools too often. Be sure to get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

Styling short layers on medium hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of looks that showcase your layers. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the ones that work best for you.
