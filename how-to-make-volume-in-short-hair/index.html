How To Make Volume In Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Short hair can be challenging to style, especially if you want to add some volume. But don't worry, there are several ways to create volume in short hair. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to make volume in short hair.

How to Make Volume in Short Hair

Here are some ways to add volume to your short hair:

1. Use volumizing products

Start with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to add volume and lift to your hair. Then, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair before blow-drying.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-drying your hair upside down will add volume to your roots. Use a round brush to lift your hair and direct the airflow towards the roots.

3. Tease your hair

Teasing your hair is an excellent way to add volume to your roots. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb your hair at the roots. Then, smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased section.

4. Use hot rollers

Hot rollers are an easy way to add volume to your short hair. Roll your hair in small sections, starting at the roots. Leave the rollers in for at least 20 minutes before removing them.

5. Get a layered haircut

A layered haircut can add volume and texture to your hair. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers, with shorter layers at the top.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make volume in short hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and lift to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair.

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair before blow-drying. This will help add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair upside down.

Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to your roots. Use a round brush to lift your hair and direct the airflow towards the roots.

Step 4: Tease your hair.

Tease your hair at the roots using a fine-tooth comb. Then, smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased section.

Step 5: Use hot rollers.

Roll your hair in small sections using hot rollers. Leave the rollers in for at least 20 minutes before removing them.

Tips for Maintaining Volume in Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining volume in short hair:

1. Use dry shampoo

Dry shampoo can help absorb oil and add volume to your hair. Use it between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.

2. Avoid heavy products

Avoid using heavy products like wax or pomade, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

3. Use a volumizing hairspray

Finish off your style with a volumizing hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and maintain volume throughout the day.

FAQs

Q: Can I add volume to short hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can add volume to short hair without using heat. Try using a volumizing mousse and blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Short hair needs to be trimmed regularly to maintain its shape and style.

2. Use a heat protectant

When using heat tools like blow dryers and flat irons, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you can add volume to your short hair and achieve the look you want. Experiment with different techniques to find the ones that work best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and use products that are gentle and nourishing.
