Picking The Right Haircut For Your Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right haircut can make all the difference in your appearance. However, with so many hairstyles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. One of the best ways to narrow down your options is to consider your face shape. In this article, we'll walk you through how to pick the right haircut for your face shape, provide tips for styling, and answer some common questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Picking the Right Haircut
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in picking the right haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the descriptions above. You can also use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide to Picking the Right Haircut

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. Here are some tips:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and minimize the width of your face. Hairstyles that work well for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Shoulder-length cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cuts

Oval Face

Oval faces are considered the "ideal" face shape because they are balanced and proportional. Almost any hairstyle will work well for an oval face, but some popular options include:

  • Long layers
  • Bob haircuts
  • Pixie cuts
  • Side-swept bangs

Square Face

Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead. The goal is to soften these features and create the illusion of length. Hairstyles that work well for square faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Soft, side-swept bangs
  • Curly or wavy hair
  • Shoulder-length cuts

Heart Face

Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The goal is to balance these proportions and create the illusion of a more oval face shape. Hairstyles that work well for heart-shaped faces include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Long layers
  • Curly or wavy hair

Diamond Face

Diamond faces are widest at the cheekbones and have a narrow forehead and chin. The goal is to create the illusion of a more oval face shape. Hairstyles that work well for diamond-shaped faces include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Long layers
  • Curly or wavy hair

Triangle Face

Triangle faces have a wider jawline and a narrower forehead. The goal is to balance these proportions and create the illusion of a more oval face shape. Hairstyles that work well for triangle-shaped faces include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Long layers
  • Curly or wavy hair

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've picked the right haircut for your face shape, there are some tips to keep in mind for styling:

  • Use the right products for your hair type
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall
  • Use a round brush to add volume
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't necessarily flatter my face shape?

A: Of course! At the end of the day, it's your hair and you should wear it in a way that makes you feel confident and happy.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. On average, most people get their hair cut every 6-8 weeks.

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: Unfortunately, there is no magic solution for making your hair grow faster. However, maintaining a healthy diet, reducing stress, and using quality hair care products can help promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and picking the right haircut for your face shape, you can achieve a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident.
