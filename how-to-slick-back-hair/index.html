How To Slick Back Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking to rock a classic slicked back hairstyle? Whether you're going for a sleek and professional look or a more casual and edgy vibe, learning how to slick back your hair is an essential skill. In this guide, we'll go through the steps you need to follow to achieve this timeless look.

How to Slick Back Hair

Slicking back your hair involves using a hair product to create a smooth, streamlined look. Here are the basic steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Product

The first step in slicking back your hair is choosing the right product. There are a few different options to consider:

  • Pomade: A pomade is a waxy, water-based product that provides a strong hold and a shiny finish.
  • Gel: Hair gel is a thick, sticky product that can create a wet look and hold your hair in place.
  • Hairspray: If you prefer a more natural look, you can use hairspray to hold your hair in place without adding any extra shine.

Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before slicking back your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then dry it with a towel or blow dryer.

Step 3: Apply the Product

Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair back in the direction you want it to go. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, smoothing out any bumps or flyaways as you go.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can style it however you like. You can leave it as is for a sleek, classic look, or add some volume at the roots for a more modern twist.

Tips for Slicking Back Your Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You can always add more, but it's harder to remove excess product once it's in your hair.
  • If you have thicker hair, you may need to use a stronger product to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different comb styles to find the one that works best for you. A fine-toothed comb is best for achieving a sleek, streamlined look.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with your hairstyle to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

How long will a slicked back hairstyle last?

A slicked back hairstyle can last for several hours or even all day, depending on the product you use and your hair type. To make your hairstyle last longer, use a strong-hold product and avoid touching your hair throughout the day.

Can I slick back my hair if it's thin or fine?

Absolutely! If you have thin or fine hair, use a lightweight product and avoid applying too much. You can also add some volume at the roots to create a fuller look.

Hair Care Tips

Just like any hairstyle, maintaining a slicked back look requires some basic hair care. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you should be well on your way to rocking a sleek and stylish slicked back hairstyle.
