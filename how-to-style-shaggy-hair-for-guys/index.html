How To Style Shaggy Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy with shaggy hair, figuring out how to style it can be a challenge. Shaggy hair can be a great look, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks for styling shaggy hair, as well as some hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

  • How to Style Shaggy Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Shaggy Hair for Guys

The first step to styling shaggy hair is to get a good haircut. Make sure your hair is cut in layers to add volume and texture. You can also ask your stylist to thin out your hair if it's too thick, which will make it easier to style.

When it comes to styling shaggy hair, the key is to embrace the messiness. Shaggy hair is meant to look undone, so don't worry if it doesn't look perfect. Here are some tips for styling shaggy hair:

1. Use the Right Products

When styling shaggy hair, it's important to use the right products. A lightweight styling cream or texturizing spray can help add volume and texture to your hair. Avoid using heavy products like pomade or gel, as they can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.

2. Embrace the Messiness

Don't be afraid to embrace the messiness of shaggy hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look. If you're going for a more polished look, you can use a comb to smooth down the front of your hair and create a side part.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

One of the great things about shaggy hair is that there are so many different ways to style it. You can experiment with different lengths, textures, and parting styles to find the look that works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling shaggy hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

2. Apply Styling Cream or Texturizing Spray

Apply a small amount of styling cream or texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to create volume and texture.

3. Tousle Your Hair

Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look. If you want to create a more polished look, use a comb to smooth down the front of your hair and create a side part.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Shaggy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling shaggy hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style. Try to wash your hair every other day, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it harder to style in the long run. Try to avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners, or use them sparingly.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and manageable. Aim to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your shaggy hair looking its best.

FAQ

1. Can I style shaggy hair with a hairdryer?

Yes, you can use a hairdryer to style shaggy hair. Use a diffuser attachment to add volume and texture, and avoid using high heat or too much airflow, which can make your hair frizzy.

2. How can I add more volume to my shaggy hair?

You can add more volume to your shaggy hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a lightweight styling cream or texturizing spray. You can also try blow-drying your hair upside down to add more lift.

Hair Care for Shaggy Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your shaggy hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that's gentle and designed for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals or products that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and make it harder to style. Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection to keep your hair healthy.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair strong and healthy. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables to give your hair the nutrients it needs.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your shaggy hair with confidence. Remember to embrace the messiness, experiment with different styles, and take good care of your hair for the best results.
