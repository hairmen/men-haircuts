How To Decrease Volume In Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have naturally voluminous hair, you know how hard it can be to manage. It can take hours to blow dry, and even then, it might not stay in place. Fortunately, there are several ways to decrease the volume in your hair. In this post, we’ll provide you with tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions on how to decrease volume in hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Decrease Volume in Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Decrease Volume in Hair

Here are five ways to decrease volume in hair:

Cut Your Hair

One of the easiest ways to decrease the volume in your hair is to get a haircut. A layered haircut can help to thin out your hair and make it easier to manage. If you’re not ready to go for a drastic cut, ask your stylist to add some long layers to your hair.

Straighten Your Hair

Straightening your hair is another way to decrease volume. You can use a flat iron or a hair straightener to do this, but make sure you use a heat protectant spray before you start. Straightening your hair can cause damage, so it’s important to take precautions to protect your hair.

Use a Hair Serum

A hair serum can help to decrease volume and make your hair more manageable. Look for a serum that is specifically designed to tame frizz and control volume. Apply the serum to damp hair before blow-drying or air-drying.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to decrease volume and make your hair more manageable. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, and make sure you dry it in small sections. This will help to smooth out your hair and reduce frizz.

Use Hair Products

There are several hair products on the market that can help to decrease volume. Look for products that are specifically designed to control volume and tame frizz. Some popular products include anti-frizz serums, mousses, and hair sprays.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to decrease volume in your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a hair serum to damp hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  4. Straighten your hair using a flat iron or hair straightener.
  5. Apply a hair spray or anti-frizz serum to finish.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you decrease volume in your hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose braid to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help control frizz and decrease volume.

FAQs

Can I decrease volume in my hair without cutting it?

Yes, you can decrease volume in your hair without cutting it. Here are a few ways to do it:

  • Straighten your hair using a flat iron or hair straightener.
  • Use a hair serum to tame frizz and control volume.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  • Use hair products specifically designed to control volume.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care Routine

Here’s a simple hair care routine to help you decrease volume in your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a hair serum to damp hair and blow dry using a round brush.
  • Straighten your hair using a flat iron or hair straightener.
  • Apply a hair spray or anti-frizz serum to finish.
  • Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can decrease volume in your hair and make it more manageable. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding excessive heat styling.
