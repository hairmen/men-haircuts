How To Pick A Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Robby Jun 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing the right haircut that suits your face shape can be a daunting task. With so many styles to choose from, it can be hard to know where to begin. In this article, we'll show you how to pick a haircut for your face shape, step by step, and provide some hair care tips to keep your new haircut looking great.

  • How to Pick a Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step by Step

How to Pick a Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in picking a haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Trace the outline of your face on the mirror with a washable marker or lipstick.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the most proportionate and versatile. You can pull off almost any haircut, from short to long. Consider adding layers or bangs to add dimension and texture to your hair.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape has soft, curved lines with the width and length in the same proportions. To elongate your face, look for hairstyles that add height at the crown, like a high ponytail or updo. Layers that start below the chin will also help to create a more angular look.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and forehead, with the width and length in the same proportions. Look for hairstyles that soften the angles of your face, like a long bob or side-swept bangs. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs that will emphasize your strong features.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. Look for hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of your face, like a bob or long layers. Side-swept bangs will also help to balance out your features.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones. Look for hairstyles that add width to your forehead and jawline, like a chin-length bob or side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the crown, as this will make your face look longer.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape has a long, narrow shape with a high forehead and prominent chin. Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, like a layered bob or curls. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the crown, as this will make your face look even longer.

Step by Step

Now that you know what your face shape is and what hairstyles will suit you best, it's time to pick a haircut. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape
  2. Find pictures of hairstyles you like
  3. Show the pictures to your hairdresser
  4. Discuss any concerns or questions with your hairdresser
  5. Trust your hairdresser's professional opinion
  6. Enjoy your new haircut!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair
  • Think about your hair texture and how it will affect the way your hair looks when cut
  • Ask your hairdresser for styling tips and product recommendations
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

What haircut suits a round face?

Hairstyles that add height at the crown, like a high ponytail or updo, are great for elongating a round face. Layers that start below the chin will also help to create a more angular look.

What haircut suits a square face?

Hairstyles that soften the angles of a square face, like a long bob or side-swept bangs, are great. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs that will emphasize strong features.

What haircut suits an oval face?

Almost any haircut will suit an oval face, from short to long. Consider adding layers or bangs to add dimension and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Now that you have a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week

By following these tips, you can keep your new haircut looking great and feeling healthy. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and comfortable!
