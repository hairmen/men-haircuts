How To Straighten Short Wavy Hair Without Heat

Written by Eveline Oct 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Having short wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s easy to manage, it can also be challenging to style. If you’re looking for a way to straighten your short wavy hair without heat, you’ve come to the right place. In this guide, we’ll show you how to get straight hair without damaging heat tools.

Why Avoid Heat?

Heat styling tools like flat irons and hair straighteners can damage your hair in the long run. They can dry out your hair, cause split ends, and even break your hair. If you’re looking for a way to straighten your hair without causing damage, try these methods instead.

How to Straighten Short Wavy Hair Without Heat

Method 1: Blow Dry and Flat Iron

If you’re in a hurry, you can blow dry your hair straight and then use a flat iron to smooth out any remaining waves. Here’s how:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Use the cool setting on your blow dryer to avoid heat damage.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any remaining waves.
  5. Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Method 2: Hair Rollers

If you have some time to spare, you can use hair rollers to straighten your hair without heat. Here’s how:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections and roll each section around a hair roller.
  4. Leave the rollers in your hair for a few hours or overnight.
  5. Remove the rollers and brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  6. Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Straightening Short Wavy Hair Without Heat

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when straightening your short wavy hair without heat:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage.
  • Don’t use too much product on your hair as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Be patient – these methods take longer than using a flat iron or hair straightener.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you straighten short wavy hair without heat?

Yes, you can straighten short wavy hair without heat. You can use hair rollers or blow dry and flat iron your hair to get straight hair without using heat tools.

How can I straighten my hair without damaging it?

You can straighten your hair without damaging it by avoiding heat tools and using alternative methods like hair rollers or blow drying and flat ironing your hair. Always use a heat protectant spray and apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

How long will my hair stay straight?

The length of time your hair stays straight depends on your hair type and the method you use to straighten it. If you use hair rollers, your hair may stay straight for a few days. If you blow dry and flat iron your hair, it may stay straight until your next wash.

Hair Care for Short Wavy Hair

To keep your short wavy hair healthy, try these tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment.

By following these tips and using our methods for straightening short wavy hair without heat, you can achieve the straight hair you desire without causing damage to your hair.
