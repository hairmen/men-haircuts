Haircuts Short Sides Long Top The Perfect Style For Men In 2023

Written by William Aug 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain, then the haircuts short sides long top is the perfect option for you. This haircut has been a popular choice among men for several years now, and it doesn't seem to be going out of style anytime soon.

Description

The haircuts short sides long top is characterized by short hair on the sides and the back, and long hair on the top. This style is versatile and can be worn with different hair types and textures. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and cool look without having to spend too much time styling their hair.

How to

If you want to achieve the haircuts short sides long top, you need to visit a professional hairstylist who can give you the perfect cut. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this style:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting the haircut, you need to have a consultation with your hairstylist. Discuss your hair type, texture, and the look you want to achieve. Your hairstylist will give you recommendations on the best haircut that will suit your face shape and overall style.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and the Back

Using a pair of clippers, your hairstylist will cut the sides and the back of your hair. The length of the hair will depend on your preference, but the general rule is to keep it short. The hair on the sides and the back will be blended together smoothly for a seamless transition.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The hair on the top will be left longer than the sides and the back. Your hairstylist will use scissors to cut the hair on the top to your desired length. The hair on the top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircuts short sides long top:

  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts short sides long top:

Q: Is this style suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style is suitable for all hair types and textures.

Q: Can I style my hair on the top differently?

A: Yes, you can style your hair on the top in different ways depending on your preference.

Q: Do I need to use a particular product to style my hair?

A: No, you can use any styling product that suits your hair type and texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.

The haircuts short sides long top is a stylish and trendy haircut that is perfect for men who want a cool and low-maintenance look. With proper care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking great and stylish for a long time.
