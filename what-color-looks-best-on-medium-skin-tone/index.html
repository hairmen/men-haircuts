What Color Looks Best On Medium Skin Tone Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for the best color that will complement your medium skin tone? You’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll share tips and ideas on how to find the perfect color that will enhance your complexion. We’ll also answer frequently asked questions and provide hair care tips to complete your look.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the right color for your medium skin tone is to determine your undertone. There are two undertones: warm and cool. Warm undertones have yellow or golden hues, while cool undertones have pink or blue hues. Here are some ways to determine your skin tone:

  • Check your veins: If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones, and if they appear blue, you have cool undertones.
  • Look at your jewelry: If you look better in gold jewelry, you have warm undertones, and if you look better in silver jewelry, you have cool undertones.
  • Examine your skin after being in the sun: If your skin turns golden brown, you have warm undertones, and if your skin turns pink or red, you have cool undertones.

Once you determine your undertone, you can start choosing colors that will complement your skin tone.

Tips for Choosing the Best Colors

When choosing colors for medium skin tone, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Stick to colors that complement your undertone: Warm colors such as red, orange, and yellow look great on warm undertones, while cool colors such as blue, green, and purple look great on cool undertones.
  • Avoid colors that clash with your skin tone: Colors like beige and pastels can make medium skin tones look dull and ashy.
  • Experiment with different shades: Just because a color is warm or cool doesn’t mean it will look good on your skin. Try different shades to find the perfect match.
  • Consider the occasion: Some colors are more appropriate for certain occasions. For example, bright colors like red and orange are great for parties and events, while muted colors like beige and brown are great for work or business meetings.

Warm Colors for Medium Skin Tone

If you have warm undertones, you can try these warm colors:

  • Red: Shades of red like brick, coral, and tomato are great for warm skin tones.
  • Orange: Shades of orange like peach, tangerine, and burnt orange look great on warm skin tones.
  • Yellow: Shades of yellow like mustard, lemon, and goldenrod complement warm skin tones.
  • Brown: Shades of brown like caramel, chocolate, and coffee look great on warm skin tones.

Cool Colors for Medium Skin Tone

If you have cool undertones, you can try these cool colors:

  • Blue: Shades of blue like navy, cobalt, and baby blue look great on cool skin tones.
  • Green: Shades of green like emerald, olive, and mint are great for cool skin tones.
  • Purple: Shades of purple like lavender, grape, and plum complement cool skin tones.
  • Pink: Shades of pink like fuchsia, magenta, and blush are great for cool skin tones.

Metallic Colors for Medium Skin Tone

Metallic colors like gold, silver, and bronze can also look great on medium skin tones. If you have warm undertones, go for gold and bronze, while if you have cool undertones, go for silver.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear black if I have medium skin tone?

A: Yes, you can wear black. However, it’s not the best color for medium skin tones as it can make your skin look dull. If you want to wear black, you can pair it with a bright accessory or lipstick to add some color to your look.

Q: What color should I avoid if I have medium skin tone?

A: Colors like beige, pastels, and ash tones can make medium skin tones look dull and ashy.

Q: Can I wear bold colors if I have medium skin tone?

A: Yes, you can wear bold colors like red, orange, and yellow if you have warm undertones, and blue, green, and purple if you have cool undertones. Just make sure to choose the right shade that complements your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Skin Tone

Choosing the right hair color can also enhance your medium skin tone. Here are some tips:

  • Stick to warm or cool colors that complement your undertone.
  • Consider the color of your eyes: If you have brown eyes, warm colors like caramel, honey, and chestnut will complement your skin tone. If you have green or blue eyes, cool colors like ash blonde, platinum, and icy brown will complement your skin tone.
  • Experiment with highlights: Highlights can add dimension to your hair and complement your skin tone.
  • Consult a professional: If you’re not sure which hair color will look best on your skin tone, consult a professional stylist.

Choosing the right color for your medium skin tone can enhance your natural beauty and make you look radiant. Use these tips and ideas to find the perfect color that will complement your skin tone and complete your look.
