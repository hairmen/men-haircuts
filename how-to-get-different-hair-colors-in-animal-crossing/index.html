How To Get Different Hair Colors In Animal Crossing

Are you tired of your character's default hair color in Animal Crossing? Do you want to switch it up and add some flair to your virtual persona? Look no further! In this article, we will go over how to get different hair colors in Animal Crossing, step-by-step.

How to Get Different Hair Colors in Animal Crossing

There are multiple ways to get different hair colors in Animal Crossing, but the most common method is by visiting Harriet, the hair stylist, at Shampoodle. Shampoodle is a beauty salon that can be built in your town after you meet certain requirements.

Another way to change your hair color is by using hair dye, which can be purchased at the Able Sisters' shop. However, hair dye may only be used once and will only last for one day.

Requirements for Shampoodle

Before you can visit Shampoodle, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Have at least 10,000 Bells in your bank account
  • Have played for at least seven days
  • Have at least one other player character in your town

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you have met the requirements, follow these steps to get different hair colors in Animal Crossing:

  1. Go to Main Street in your town.
  2. Find the Shampoodle salon and enter.
  3. Talk to Harriet and choose "Makeover."
  4. Choose "Change hair color."
  5. Select the hair color you want and confirm your choice.
  6. Pay the fee of 3,000 Bells.
  7. Voila! Your character now has a new hair color.

Tips for Choosing Hair Colors

While changing your hair color can be exciting, it's important to choose a color that fits your character's personality and style. Here are some tips to consider:

  • Think about your character's favorite colors and clothing style.
  • Consider the season and choose a color that matches the theme.
  • Try out different colors before committing to one.
  • Don't be afraid to go bold and try something new!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about getting different hair colors in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hair color more than once?

Yes, you can visit Shampoodle as many times as you want to change your hair color.

Can I get different hair colors without Shampoodle?

Yes, you can purchase hair dye from the Able Sisters' shop, but the color will only last for one day.

Can I change the style of my hair?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle and hair color at Shampoodle.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Now that you have a new hair color, it's important to take care of it! Here are some tips:

  • Visit Shampoodle regularly to maintain your hair color.
  • Avoid getting caught in the rain, as this can wash out your hair color.
  • Use a hat or umbrella to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Try out different hairstyles to keep your look fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to switch up your hair color in Animal Crossing and keep your virtual persona looking stylish and fresh. Happy gaming!
