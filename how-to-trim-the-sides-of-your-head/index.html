How To Trim The Sides Of Your Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to trim the sides of your head at home? Trimming the sides of your head can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's easy to do yourself. In this article, we'll show you how to trim the sides of your head like a pro.

How to Trim the Sides of Your Head

Before we get started, here's a list of tools you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Now, let's get started!

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start trimming, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can cause the clippers to pull or snag your hair. Use a towel to dry your hair thoroughly before you start.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want to trim your hair to. Use the clippers to trim the sides of your head to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve the desired length. Make sure to use the same guard on both sides to ensure evenness.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the hair on the sides of your head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to create a seamless blend between the shorter and longer hair. Make sure to blend both sides evenly.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Use the mirror to check your work. Make sure both sides are even and that the hair is blended seamlessly. If you notice any unevenness or rough spots, use the scissors to make adjustments.

Tips for Trimming the Sides of Your Head

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect trim:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard. It's easier to take more hair off than it is to put it back on.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair before you cut it. This will help you get a more even cut.
  • Take your time. Rushing can lead to unevenness and mistakes.
  • Be patient. Trimming the sides of your head can be tricky, but with practice, you'll get better.

FAQ

How often should I trim the sides of my head?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Generally, trimming every 2-3 weeks is recommended.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! Mistakes happen. If you accidentally cut too much hair, you can always go shorter or wait for it to grow back. If you're unhappy with the results, you can always go to a professional for help.

Can I use a mirror to trim the sides of my head?

Yes, using a mirror is essential when trimming the sides of your head. Make sure you have a good view of both sides of your head while you're trimming.

Hair Care

After you're done trimming the sides of your head, make sure to wash your hair again to remove any loose hair. Apply a small amount of hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Trimming the sides of your head can be a bit daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it's easy to do at home. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a professional-looking trim that will save you time and money in the long run.
