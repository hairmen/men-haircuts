Best Hat For Oblong Face Shape Tips And Recommendations

Written by Eveline Nov 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hat that flatters your features. The key is to find a hat that balances out the length of your face and adds width to it. In this article, we will share some tips and recommendations on the best hat for oblong face shape.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. This face shape is also known as a rectangular face shape. If you have an oblong face, you should avoid hats that are too tall or too narrow as they will make your face look longer. Instead, look for hats that add width to your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

How to Choose the Best Hat for Oblong Face Shape

Choosing the best hat for oblong face shape can be challenging, but with the right tips, you can find the perfect hat that flatters your features. Here are some things to consider when choosing a hat:

1. Hat Style

The hat style plays a crucial role in determining whether a hat will flatter your oblong face shape. Look for hats that add width to your face, such as fedoras, floppy hats, bucket hats, and berets. These hats have a wider brim that creates the illusion of a more oval shape. Avoid hats that are too tall or too narrow, such as top hats, as they will make your face look longer.

2. Hat Size

The size of the hat is also important when choosing the best hat for oblong face shape. Look for hats that fit snugly on your head and do not slide down over your forehead. A hat that is too small will make your face look longer, while a hat that is too big will overwhelm your features.

3. Hat Color

The color of the hat can also make a difference in how it flatters your oblong face shape. Look for hats in darker colors, such as black, navy, or brown, as they create a slimming effect that balances out the length of your face. Avoid hats in light colors, such as white or pastel shades, as they will make your face look longer.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to choose the best hat for oblong face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and examine the length and width of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape.

Step 2: Look for Hats that Add Width

Look for hats that add width to your face, such as fedoras, floppy hats, bucket hats, and berets. These hats have a wider brim that creates the illusion of a more oval shape.

Step 3: Avoid Hats that are Too Tall or Too Narrow

Avoid hats that are too tall or too narrow, such as top hats, as they will make your face look longer. Look for hats that have a wider brim that balances out the length of your face.

Step 4: Choose the Right Hat Size

Choose a hat that fits snugly on your head and does not slide down over your forehead. A hat that is too small will make your face look longer, while a hat that is too big will overwhelm your features.

Step 5: Consider the Hat Color

Consider the color of the hat when choosing the best hat for oblong face shape. Look for hats in darker colors, such as black, navy, or brown, as they create a slimming effect that balances out the length of your face.

Tips for Wearing Hats with an Oblong Face Shape

Here are some additional tips for wearing hats with an oblong face shape:

1. Wear the Hat at an Angle

Wearing the hat at an angle can help balance out the length of your face and create a more oval shape.

2. Avoid Hats with a High Crown

Avoid hats with a high crown, such as top hats or cowboy hats, as they will make your face look longer.

3. Experiment with Different Hat Styles

Experiment with different hat styles to find the one that flatters your features the most.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Hats

Here are some hair care tips for wearing hats:

1. Use a Leave-in Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner before wearing a hat to prevent dryness and damage to your hair.

2. Choose a Hat with a Lining

Choose a hat with a lining to prevent friction and breakage of your hair.

3. Avoid Tight-Fitting Hats

Avoid tight-fitting hats as they can cause hair breakage and damage.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hat for oblong face shape can be challenging, but with the right tips and recommendations, you can find the perfect hat that flatters your features. Remember to look for hats that add width to your face, avoid hats that are too tall or too narrow, and choose the right hat size and color. Experiment with different hat styles to find the one that suits you the most, and follow the hair care tips to prevent damage to your hair. With these tips in mind, you can rock any hat with confidence and style!
