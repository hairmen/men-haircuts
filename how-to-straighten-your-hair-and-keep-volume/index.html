How to Straighten Your Hair and Keep Volume

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions, let's talk about the tools you'll need to achieve this look. You'll need a high-quality straightening iron, heat protectant spray, and volumizing hair products.

Step 1: Start with freshly washed hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the straightening iron.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

Step 3: Section your hair into manageable sections. This will ensure that you straighten every strand of hair.

Step 4: Take each section of hair and run the straightening iron over it. Hold the straightening iron vertically to add volume at the roots.

Step 5: Once you've straightened each section of hair, apply a volumizing product to the roots. This will add extra volume and hold.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in straightening your hair while keeping volume:

Step 1: Start with Freshly Washed Hair

It's important to start with freshly washed hair when straightening your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before blow-drying to prevent damage from the straightening iron.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume. Section your hair into manageable sections and focus the blow-dryer on the roots to create volume.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections. This will ensure that you straighten every strand of hair. Use hair clips to keep each section of hair separate.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Take each section of hair and run the straightening iron over it. Hold the straightening iron vertically to add volume at the roots.

Make sure to straighten each section of hair thoroughly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Apply Volumizing Product

Once you've straightened each section of hair, apply a volumizing product to the roots. This will add extra volume and hold.

Massage the product into your roots using your fingertips.

Tips for Straightening Your Hair and Keeping Volume

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect straightened hair with volume:

  • Invest in a high-quality straightening iron that has adjustable heat settings.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the straightening iron.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  • Section your hair into manageable sections to ensure that you straighten every strand of hair.
  • Hold the straightening iron vertically to add volume at the roots.
  • Apply a volumizing product to the roots after straightening to add extra volume and hold.
  • Don't over-straighten your hair. Straighten each section of hair thoroughly but don't hold the straightening iron in one place for too long.

FAQ

How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to avoid straightening your hair every day as this can cause damage. Aim to straighten your hair no more than once or twice a week.

Can I straighten my hair without a straightening iron?

While a straightening iron is the most effective tool for straightening hair, you can also use a hair dryer and round brush to achieve a similar effect.

How can I keep my hair straight overnight?

To keep your hair straight overnight, wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase. This will prevent your hair from getting frizzy or tangled while you sleep.

Hair Care for Straightened Hair

It's important to take care of your hair after straightening it to prevent damage and maintain volume. Here are some tips:

  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve straightened hair while keeping volume. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy, voluminous locks.
