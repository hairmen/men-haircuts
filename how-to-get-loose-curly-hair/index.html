How To Get Loose Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and bounce to your hair, loose curls are the perfect choice. But achieving the perfect curl can be challenging, especially if you have naturally straight or fine hair. In this article, we'll show you how to get loose curly hair in a few simple steps. We'll cover everything from styling tips to hair care routines, so you can achieve the perfect curl every time.

How to Get Loose Curly Hair

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are several ways to achieve loose curls. Some people prefer to use heat styling tools, while others opt for natural methods. Regardless of which method you choose, the key is to use the right products and techniques to create long-lasting, bouncy curls.

Heat Styling

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to get loose curls, heat styling is the way to go. Here's how:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking soft and shiny.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and use a curling iron to create loose curls. Start from the bottom and work your way up, wrapping small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron.
  4. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a more natural, tousled look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Natural Methods

If you prefer to avoid heat styling tools, there are several natural methods you can use to get loose curls:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or curl cream, to damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a loose bun. Secure with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry completely before removing the buns.
  5. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more natural, tousled look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to get loose curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking soft and shiny.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or curl cream, to damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat.
  5. Use a curling iron to create loose curls. Start from the bottom and work your way up, wrapping small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a more natural, tousled look.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Loose Curls

Once you've achieved the perfect curl, it's important to take the right steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid brushing your hair once it's curled. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more natural, tousled look.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and preserve your curls overnight.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage to your hair and lead to breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make your curls look frizzy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to get loose curly hair:

Q: How can I make my curls last longer?

A: To make your curls last longer, use a light hold hairspray to keep them in place. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to maintain the curl overnight.

Q: Can I use a curling iron on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a curling iron on wet hair. This can cause damage to your hair and lead to breakage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a curling iron.

Q: What should I do if my curls look frizzy?

A: If your curls look frizzy, try using a curl-defining product or serum to smooth them out. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat.

Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to have a hair care routine that's tailored to your hair type. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent frizz and keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or curl cream, to damp hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can easily achieve loose curly hair that looks soft, bouncy, and beautiful.
