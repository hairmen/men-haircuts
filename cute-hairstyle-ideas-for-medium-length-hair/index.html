Cute Hairstyle Ideas For Medium Length Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Medium length hair is the perfect length for versatility when it comes to styling. Whether you’re going to work, school, or a special event, there are plenty of cute hairstyle ideas for medium length hair. In this guide, we’ll provide you with some inspiration for your next look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Tutorials

Description

Medium length hair can be defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and collarbone. It’s a popular length because it’s easy to manage, style, and maintain. Additionally, medium length hair is neither too long nor too short, making it a versatile option for any occasion.

How to Style Medium Length Hair

There are countless ways to style medium length hair, but the most important thing is to choose a style that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Some popular styles for medium length hair include:

  • Beachy waves
  • Braids
  • Top knots
  • Half-up, half-down styles
  • Sleek and straight
  • Curly

No matter what style you choose, make sure to use heat protectant and styling products to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your medium length hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear updos with medium length hair?

A: Yes! There are plenty of updos that work well with medium length hair, such as top knots, messy buns, and braided updos.

Q: How do I add volume to my medium length hair?

A: You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a root-lifting spray.

Q: Will cutting my hair to medium length make it look thinner?

A: No, cutting your hair to medium length won’t make it look thinner. In fact, medium length hair can create the illusion of thicker hair because it has more movement and texture than longer hair.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

When it comes to hair care, it’s important to choose products that are specifically designed for your hair type and concerns. Here are some products that work well for medium length hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for your hair type (e.g. curly, straight, fine, thick)
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair oil or serum
  • Hairspray or hair gel

By following these tips and trying out different cute hairstyle ideas for medium length hair, you’ll be able to find a look that works for you and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
