Hairstyles For Long Thick Straight Hair

Written by Frank Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle for your long, thick, straight hair? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with some great hairstyles for long thick straight hair, along with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Description

Long, thick, straight hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of hair to work with, which means you can create a variety of styles. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you don't have the right tools and techniques.

How to

If you're looking for a new hairstyle for your long thick straight hair, there are several options to choose from. Here are some of our favorites:

1. Sleek Ponytail

A sleek ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well for long thick straight hair. To achieve this look, brush your hair back and secure it at the nape of your neck with a hair tie. Use a small amount of hair gel or serum to smooth down any flyaways.

2. Beachy Waves

If you want a more relaxed, casual look, try creating beachy waves in your long thick straight hair. Start by applying a heat protectant to your hair, then use a curling wand or flat iron to create loose waves. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your long thick straight hair while keeping it out of your face. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair down and smooth it out with a straightening iron.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating beachy waves in your long thick straight hair:

  1. Start by applying a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the bottom.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling wand or flat iron.
  4. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  6. Use your fingers to gently tousle your curls for a more natural look.
  7. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your long thick straight hair looking its best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long thick straight hair:

1. What are some good hairstyles for long thick straight hair?

Some good hairstyles for long thick straight hair include a sleek ponytail, beachy waves, and a half-up half-down style.

2. How do I keep my long thick straight hair healthy?

To keep your long thick straight hair healthy, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, trim your hair regularly, and avoid using hot tools every day.

3. Can I create beachy waves in my long thick straight hair?

Yes! You can create beachy waves in your long thick straight hair by using a curling wand or flat iron and finishing with a texturizing spray.

Hair Care

To keep your long thick straight hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools every day. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, and trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles for long thick straight hair, you'll be able to switch up your look and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Try one of these styles today and see the difference it can make!
