10 Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Wavy Hair

Written by Robby Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length wavy hair, you're in luck! There are plenty of easy hairstyles that you can try out. Whether you're looking for a casual, everyday look or something a little more formal, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing 10 easy hairstyles for medium length wavy hair that you can try out today.

Description

Medium length wavy hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. With the right techniques and tools, you can create a variety of hairstyles that will make you look and feel your best. From braids to updos, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to

To create these easy hairstyles for medium length wavy hair, you'll need a few basic tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair spray
  • Curling iron (optional)

Once you have your tools and products ready, follow the step-by-step instructions below to create each hairstyle.

Step by Step

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a classic and easy hairstyle that's perfect for medium length wavy hair.

  1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a small ponytail.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  4. Gently pull on the hair at the top of the ponytail to create some volume.

You can leave the rest of your hair down or you can curl it for some extra texture.

2. Messy Bun

This is a quick and easy hairstyle that's perfect for when you're in a rush.

  1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Braided Crown

This is a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for special occasions.

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and braid it.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take one of the braids and wrap it over the top of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat with the other braid, tucking the ends under the first braid.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you create these easy hairstyles for medium length wavy hair:

  • Use a curling iron to add some extra texture to your hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help them blend in.
  • Experiment with different braids and twists to create unique hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to add hair accessories like headbands or clips to your hairstyles.
  • Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I create these hairstyles on straight hair?

A: Yes, these hairstyles can be adapted for straight hair as well. You may need to add some texture to your hair with a curling iron or texturizing spray.

Q: How long does it take to create these hairstyles?

A: Most of these hairstyles can be created in 10-15 minutes, depending on your skill level and the complexity of the style.

Q: Do I need to use all the products and tools listed to create these hairstyles?

A: No, you can adapt these hairstyles to use the tools and products that you have on hand.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right tools and products, you can keep your medium length wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful.
