How to Get Wet Hair Look with Wig - Tips and Tricks 2023

If you're looking for a way to achieve a wet hair look with your wig, you've come to the right place. This trendy and chic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and can be easily achieved with the right products and techniques. In this article, we'll be sharing with you some tips and tricks on how to get a wet hair look with your wig that will have you turning heads.

How to Get Wet Hair Look with Wig

Before we dive into the steps on how to get a wet hair look with your wig, it's important to note that not all wigs are created equal. You'll want to make sure that you have a wig that is made of synthetic hair or human hair that can withstand moisture. Here are the steps to achieving a wet hair look with your wig:

Step 1: Start with a Clean Wig

The first step to achieving a wet hair look with your wig is to start with a clean and dry wig. You'll want to make sure that your wig is free of any tangles or knots before you start styling it. Use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Gel

Next, you'll want to apply a styling gel or mousse to your wig. This will help to create the wet look and keep your hair in place. Make sure to use a product that is designed for wigs or synthetic hair to avoid damaging your wig. Apply the product evenly throughout your wig, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.

Step 3: Add Water

Once you've applied the styling gel or mousse, it's time to add water to your wig. You can use a spray bottle to mist your wig with water or you can wet your hands and run them through your wig. Make sure to distribute the water evenly throughout your wig to achieve a uniform wet look.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Now that your wig is wet and has product in it, it's time to style it. You can use your fingers to create a slicked-back look or you can use a comb to create a side part. If you want to add some volume or texture to your wig, you can scrunch it up with your hands or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create waves.

Step 5: Allow Your Wig to Dry

Once you've styled your wig to your liking, it's important to let it dry completely before you wear it. You can either let it air dry or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the drying process. Avoid using a high heat setting as this can damage your wig.

Tips for Achieving a Wet Hair Look with Your Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect wet hair look with your wig:

  • Use a gel or mousse that is designed for wigs or synthetic hair to avoid damaging your wig.
  • Don't overdo it with the water. You want your wig to look wet, not drenched.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig as this can damage the hair fibers.
  • Store your wig properly to maintain its shape and style.

FAQs

Can I achieve a wet hair look with a human hair wig?

Yes, you can achieve a wet hair look with a human hair wig. Just make sure to use products that are designed for human hair to avoid damaging the hair fibers.

How long will the wet hair look last?

The wet hair look will last until your wig dries completely. You can use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the drying process if you don't want to wait for your wig to air dry.

Hair Care for Your Wig

In order to maintain the quality and style of your wig, it's important to take proper care of it. Here are some tips for caring for your wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Wash your wig regularly with a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig as this can damage the hair fibers.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight or heat sources as this can cause the hair fibers to become brittle.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect wet hair look with your wig. Remember to use products that are designed for wigs or synthetic hair and to take proper care of your wig to maintain its quality and style.
