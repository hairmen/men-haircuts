How Will I Know What Hairstyle Will Suit Me In 2023

Are you tired of sporting the same hairstyle for years? Are you looking to switch things up and try something new? Finding the right hairstyle can be a challenging task, especially if you're not sure what suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we'll discuss how to determine the best hairstyle for you in 2023.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining the best hairstyle for you. Here are the basic face shapes:

Oval Face

An oval face has balanced proportions and is slightly longer than it is wide. This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle with ease.

Round Face

A round face has full cheeks and a rounded chin. Hairstyles that create height at the crown or elongate the face work best for this face shape.

Square Face

A square face has a strong jawline and forehead. Softening the edges with layers and waves can complement this face shape.

Heart-Shaped Face

A heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and a narrow chin. Layered hairstyles that add volume to the chin area work best for this face shape.

Long Face

A long face has a narrow chin and forehead. Hairstyles that add width and volume to the sides work best for this face shape.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and trace the outline of your face in a mirror with lipstick or an eyeliner. Compare your traced shape to the above descriptions to determine your face shape.

Consider Your Hair Texture and Type

Your hair texture and type play a significant role in determining the best hairstyle for you. Here are the basic hair types:

Straight Hair

Straight hair is smooth and lacks volume. Layered bobs or long, straight hairstyles work best for this hair type.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair has a natural wave pattern that adds volume and texture. Shoulder-length layered haircuts or long, beachy waves work best for this hair type.

Curly Hair

Curly hair has a spiral or ringlet shape and can be challenging to manage. Short, layered hairstyles or long, loose waves work best for this hair type.

Kinky Hair

Kinky hair has tight coils and requires a lot of moisture to maintain. Short, tapered haircuts or long, protective styles work best for this hair type.

Consider your hair texture and type when choosing a hairstyle. A style that works for straight hair may not work for curly hair and vice versa.

Consider Your Personal Style Preferences

Your personal style preferences are an essential factor in determining the best hairstyle for you. Here are some things to consider when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Do you prefer a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hairstyle?
  • Do you like to experiment with different colors and styles?
  • Do you have any hair-related insecurities (e.g., thinning hair, receding hairline)?
  • Do you prefer a classic, trendy, or edgy hairstyle?

Consider your personal style preferences when choosing a hairstyle. Your stylist can help you choose a style that complements your personal style and preferences.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Once you've determined your face shape, hair texture and type, and personal style preferences, it's time to choose a hairstyle. Here are some tips for a great haircut:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist.
  • Communicate your hair-related insecurities to your stylist.
  • Choose a style that complements your face shape, hair texture and type, and personal style preferences.
  • Consider the amount of maintenance your chosen style requires.
  • Ask your stylist for recommendations on hair care products and styling techniques.

FAQ

How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

Determine your face shape, hair texture and type, and personal style preferences. Choose a style that complements these factors and communicates your preferences to your stylist.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the style you choose. Shorter hairstyles require more frequent trims, while longer hairstyles can go longer between cuts. Ask your stylist for recommendations on how often to get a haircut.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

Communicate your concerns to your stylist. They may be able to adjust the cut or suggest a different style that complements your preferences.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use hair care products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but by considering your face shape, hair texture and type, and personal style preferences, you can find a style that complements your features and fits your lifestyle. Use the tips outlined in this article to choose a hairstyle that you love and can maintain with ease.
