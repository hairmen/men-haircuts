Description

Short haircuts come in many different lengths and styles, and some of these styles can make your face look rounder. The reason for this is that short hairstyles tend to draw attention to your face, which can accentuate its shape. However, there are also many short hairstyles that can actually make your face look slimmer and more angular.

How To

The key to finding a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape is to consider your faceâs proportions. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this will elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right short hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to take the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure from your hairline to the fullest part of your forehead.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone begins to angle upwards.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Step 2: Find Short Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Once youâve determined your face shape, you can start looking for short hairstyles that flatter your features. Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

  • Round face: Look for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head and create volume at the crown. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Square face: Look for hairstyles with soft, rounded edges that soften your jawline. Avoid hairstyles with harsh angles or straight bangs, as these can make your face look more angular.
  • Heart-shaped face: Look for hairstyles that balance out your face shape by adding volume to the lower half of your face. Avoid hairstyles with heavy layers or volume at the top of your head, as these can make your forehead look wider.
  • Oval face: Lucky you! Oval faces can wear almost any short hairstyle and look great.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape:

  • Consider your hair texture. Some short hairstyles work better with curly or wavy hair, while others work better with straight hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. Some short hairstyles require more maintenance than others, so make sure you choose a style that fits your lifestyle.
  • Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you find the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q: Does cutting your hair short make your face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. It depends on the specific hairstyle you choose and how it complements your face shape.

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

A: Look for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head and create volume at the crown. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

So, does shorter hair make your face look rounder? The answer is: it depends. By choosing the right short hairstyle for your face shape and hair type, you can create a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and stylish.
