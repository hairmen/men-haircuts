Which Hair Cut Suits On Round Chubby Face

Written by Robby Oct 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut to suit your round chubby face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss different haircuts that would look great on you. We will also give you some tips on how to maintain your hair so it always looks its best.

Description

People with round chubby faces often have a harder time finding the right haircut that flatters their face shape. The aim is to choose a style that slims and elongates your face to create an illusion of a more defined jawline. You also want to choose a haircut that complements your features and your personality.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut, the first thing you need to consider is the shape of your face. A round face is characterized by soft curves and no sharp angles. The key to finding the right haircut is to create the illusion of more length and less width. This can be achieved with layers, texture, and volume.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a haircut:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Research different haircuts
  3. Consult with your hairstylist
  4. Take into account your hair texture and thickness
  5. Choose a style that complements your features and personality

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when choosing a haircut:

  • Avoid haircuts that add width to your face
  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the crown of your head
  • Layer your hair to create texture and movement
  • Avoid a blunt cut that ends at your chin
  • Consider adding bangs to create the illusion of length

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round chubby faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a blunt cut that ends at your chin or a bob that falls at your jawline. Instead, opt for a layered haircut that adds texture and volume.

Should I add bangs?

Adding bangs can help create the illusion of length and balance out your face shape. Consider getting side-swept bangs or a curtain fringe.

What hair texture works best with a round face?

Textured hair works best with a round face. Adding layers and texture can help create movement and volume, which can elongate your face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair

Now that you know which haircut suits a round chubby face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to choose a style that complements your features and personality, and follow our hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best.
