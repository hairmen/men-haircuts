How To Make Straight Hair Super Curly Without Heat A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're tired of using heat styling tools to achieve curly hair, you're not alone. Not only can heat damage your hair, but it can also be time-consuming and expensive to maintain. Luckily, there are ways to achieve super curly hair without using any heat. In this article, we'll explore how to make straight hair super curly without heat, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

How to Make Straight Hair Super Curly Without Heat

There are a few methods you can use to make straight hair super curly without heat. The most popular techniques include using rollers, braiding, and twisting. Each method will create a different type of curl, so it's important to choose the one that best suits your desired look.

Rollers

Rollers are a great way to create bouncy, voluminous curls without using heat. There are several types of rollers to choose from, including foam, velcro, and magnetic rollers.

To use rollers, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, apply a styling product to help hold the curls in place. Next, section your hair and roll each section of hair onto the rollers. Once all of your hair is in rollers, allow it to air dry overnight or use a hairdryer on a cool setting to speed up the process. Once your hair is dry, remove the rollers and style as desired.

Braiding

Braiding is another popular technique for creating curly hair without heat. To create curls with braids, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, divide your hair into small sections and braid each section tightly. Once all of your hair is braided, allow it to air dry overnight or use a hairdryer on a cool setting to speed up the process. Once your hair is dry, remove the braids and style as desired.

Twisting

Twisting is another technique that can be used to create super curly hair without heat. To twist your hair, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly. Once all of your hair is twisted, allow it to air dry overnight or use a hairdryer on a cool setting to speed up the process. Once your hair is dry, remove the twists and style as desired.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for using rollers, braiding, and twisting to make straight hair super curly:

Rollers

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to help hold the curls in place.
  3. Section your hair and roll each section of hair onto the rollers.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry overnight or use a hairdryer on a cool setting to speed up the process.
  5. Remove the rollers and style your hair as desired.

Braiding

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry overnight or use a hairdryer on a cool setting to speed up the process.
  4. Remove the braids and style your hair as desired.

Twisting

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry overnight or use a hairdryer on a cool setting to speed up the process.
  4. Remove the twists and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Achieving Super Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve super curly hair without heat:

  • Use a styling product to help hold the curls in place.
  • Allow your hair to air dry completely before removing the rollers, braids, or twists.
  • For best results, sleep with your hair in rollers, braids, or twists to allow your hair to set overnight.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate the curls and add volume.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.

FAQs

Can I use heat to speed up the process?

No, using heat defeats the purpose of trying to achieve super curly hair without heat. Heat can damage your hair and disrupt the curl pattern.

How long will the curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the method you used to create the curls. Generally, curls created with rollers or braids will last longer than curls created with twists.

What products should I use to help hold the curls in place?

You can use a variety of products to help hold the curls in place, including mousse, gel, or hairspray. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve super curly hair without using any heat. Experiment with different methods and products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired look.
