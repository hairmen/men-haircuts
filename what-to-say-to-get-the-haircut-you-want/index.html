What To Say To Get The Haircut You Want

Written by Bardi Jul 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Getting the perfect haircut can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Knowing what to say to your hairdresser can make all the difference in getting the cut that you want. In this article, we will walk you through how to communicate effectively with your hairdresser to ensure that you get the haircut you want.

Description

Have you ever gone to the hairdresser and left feeling disappointed with your haircut? It's a common experience and can be frustrating. However, it's important to remember that your hairdresser isn't a mind reader. They need clear communication from you to understand exactly what you want. In this article, we will provide you with tips on what to say to your hairdresser to ensure you get the haircut you want.

How to Get the Haircut You Want

Getting the haircut you want requires clear communication with your hairdresser. Here are some tips on what to say to your hairdresser:

1. Bring a Picture

If you have a specific haircut in mind, bring a picture with you to show your hairdresser. A picture can be worth a thousand words and can help your hairdresser understand exactly what you want. You can find pictures of haircuts in magazines, online, or even on social media.

2. Use Specific Terminology

When describing your desired haircut, use specific terminology. For example, if you want a bob, be sure to use that term rather than just saying you want your hair shorter. Using specific terminology will help your hairdresser understand exactly what you want.

3. Be Honest about Your Hair

Be honest with your hairdresser about your hair. If your hair is fine, thick, curly, or straight, be sure to let your hairdresser know. This information will help them determine the best way to cut your hair to achieve the desired result.

4. Communicate Your Lifestyle

Tell your hairdresser about your lifestyle. If you're a busy mom, you may not have time for a high-maintenance haircut. If you're an athlete, you may need a haircut that can withstand sweat and activity. Your hairdresser can recommend a haircut that works for your lifestyle.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to say to your hairdresser, let's walk through the steps to ensure you get the haircut you want.

Step 1: Research

Research the type of haircut you want. Look for pictures online or in magazines and bring them with you to your appointment.

Step 2: Use Specific Terminology

When you're describing your desired haircut, use specific terminology. Be clear about the length, layers, and style you want.

Step 3: Be Honest About Your Hair

Be honest with your hairdresser about your hair type, texture, and any problem areas you may have. This information will help them determine the best way to cut your hair.

Step 4: Communicate Your Lifestyle

Tell your hairdresser about your lifestyle. This information will help them recommend a haircut that works for you and your daily routine.

Step 5: Ask Questions

Don't be afraid to ask questions. If you're unsure about something, ask your hairdresser to clarify. It's better to ask questions before the cut than to be unhappy with the result.

Tips for Getting the Haircut You Want

Here are a few additional tips to ensure you get the haircut you want:

1. Be Open to Suggestions

Your hairdresser may have suggestions for a different cut or style that will work better for you. Be open to their suggestions and take them into consideration.

2. Don't Be Afraid to Speak Up

If you're unhappy with the way your haircut is going, speak up. It's better to address any concerns during the cut than to leave unhappy with the result.

3. Schedule a Consultation

If you're unsure about what type of haircut you want, schedule a consultation with your hairdresser. They can provide you with recommendations and help you find the perfect cut.

FAQ

Q: What should I bring to my hair appointment?

A: Bring a picture of the haircut you want, and be sure to communicate any specific requests or concerns you have.

Q: How much should I tip my hairdresser?

A: A standard tip for a hairdresser is 15-20% of the total cost of the service.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on your hair type and the style you have. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care

Now that you have your perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are a few tips:

1. Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. These products can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using heat protectant sprays or serums before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, knowing what to say to your hairdresser can make all the difference in getting the perfect haircut. Be specific about what you want, communicate honestly about your hair, and be open to suggestions. By following these tips, you'll be sure to leave the salon with the haircut you want.
