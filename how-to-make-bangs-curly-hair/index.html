How To Make Bangs Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a way to add some volume and bounce to your bangs? If you have naturally curly hair or want to create curls in your bangs, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to make bangs curly hair in a few simple steps.

Description

Bangs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, if you have straight hair, you may find that your bangs fall flat and lack volume. Fortunately, there are ways to make your bangs curly hair without having to use heat styling tools or chemicals.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to make bangs curly hair in a few simple steps:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it’s important to keep it moisturized.

2. Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, focusing on your bangs. Look for a product that’s specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl cream or gel.

3. Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your bangs. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

4. Twirl Your Bangs

Once your bangs are dry, use your fingers to twirl them around your finger. This will help to create defined curls.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hairspray to help hold your curls in place.

Tips for Making Bangs Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you make your bangs curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls without causing damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making bangs curly hair:

Can I make my bangs curly hair without heat styling tools?

Yes, you can make your bangs curly hair without heat styling tools by using curl-enhancing products and diffusing your hair dryer.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It’s best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to keep it hydrated and healthy.

What type of hair is best for making bangs curly hair?

Any hair type can be made curly, but naturally curly or wavy hair will be easier to style.

Hair Care for Curly Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider using a satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your bangs curly hair and achieve the perfect curly look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you.
