Best Haircut For Square Face Curly Hair A Complete Guide

If you have a square face and curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape while also showcasing your beautiful curls. In this guide, we'll give you tips and tricks for finding the best haircut for square face curly hair.

Description

Square faces have strong, angular jawlines and foreheads. If you have curly hair, you want a style that softens these features and highlights your curls. The best haircut for square face curly hair is one that adds volume and movement to your locks while accentuating your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Square Face Curly Hair

When choosing the best haircut for square face curly hair, consider the following:

Length

The length of your hair can have a big impact on your face shape. For square faces, longer hair can help soften your features. However, if you have tight curls, long hair can be difficult to manage. Consider a mid-length or short haircut that adds volume and movement to your curls.

Layers

Layers can help add volume and depth to curly hair. For square faces, long layers that start at the cheekbones can help soften your jawline. Shorter layers can add volume and movement to your curls.

Bangs

Bangs can help balance out a square face. Consider side-swept bangs that start at the brow bone. This will draw attention to your eyes and soften your forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Best Haircut for Square Face Curly Hair

Follow these steps to get the best haircut for square face curly hair:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Look for inspiration online or in magazines. Find pictures of haircuts that you like and that flatter square faces with curly hair.

Step 2: Consult with a Stylist

Book a consultation with a stylist who specializes in curly hair. Bring your inspiration pictures and discuss your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Choose a Length

Based on your consultation, choose a length that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Consider mid-length or short haircuts that add volume and movement to your curls.

Step 4: Add Layers

If your stylist recommends layers, choose the length and type of layers that will add volume and depth to your curls. Long layers that start at the cheekbones can help soften your jawline.

Step 5: Consider Bangs

If you want to add bangs to your haircut, consider side-swept bangs that start at the brow bone. This will draw attention to your eyes and soften your forehead.

Tips for Maintaining Your Square Face Curly Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your square face curly haircut:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of natural oils and dry out your curls. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Condition regularly

Curly hair can be prone to dryness and frizz. Use a moisturizing conditioner regularly to help keep your curls hydrated and defined.

3. Use a diffuser

A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use it on a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

4. Avoid brushing your hair

Brushing curly hair can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.

FAQs About Best Haircuts for Square Face Curly Hair

What is the best haircut for square face curly hair?

The best haircut for square face curly hair is one that adds volume and movement to your locks while accentuating your face shape. Consider mid-length or short haircuts with layers and side-swept bangs.

How do I choose the right length for my square face curly hair?

Choose a length that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Mid-length or short haircuts can add volume and movement to curly hair while also softening square face features.

What should I consider when adding layers to my square face curly haircut?

Consider long layers that start at the cheekbones to help soften your jawline. Shorter layers can add volume and movement to your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for square face curly hair:

1. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and keep your curls defined overnight.

2. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair and cause frizz. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the best haircut for square face curly hair and keep your curls looking healthy and defined.
