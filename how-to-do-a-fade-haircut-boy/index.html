How To Do A Fade Haircut Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to give your boy a fresh new haircut? A fade haircut boy is a popular and trendy style that can give your child a stylish and modern look. In this guide, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of how to do a fade haircut boy.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A fade haircut is a hairstyle that gradually fades from short hair at the bottom to longer hair at the top. It is a popular hairstyle for boys of all ages and is often seen in sports and other activities. A fade haircut boy can be done with clippers, scissors, or a combination of both.

How to

Before you start cutting your boy's hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a set of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to have a cape or towel to drape over your boy's shoulders to catch the hair.

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to do a fade haircut boy:

  1. Start with a longer guard on the clippers and cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length.
  2. Use a shorter guard on the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head, gradually fading it into the longer hair on top.
  3. Blend the hair between the short and long sections using scissors or clippers.
  4. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs on top that may be sticking out.
  5. Use the comb to check for any uneven sections and trim as needed.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 with shorter guards until you achieve the desired fade.
  7. Use the clippers to clean up the hairline around the ears and neck.
  8. Style the hair on top as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a fade haircut boy:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Take breaks and step back to look at the haircut from different angles to ensure it is even.
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid making mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a fade haircut boy:

Q: How long does a fade haircut boy last?

A: A fade haircut boy can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks depending on how fast the hair grows.

Q: Can I do a fade haircut boy at home?

A: Yes, a fade haircut boy can be done at home with the right tools and technique.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: If you make a mistake, try to blend the hair or trim it to make it look even. If all else fails, you can always take your boy to a professional barber to fix the mistake.

Hair Care

To keep your boy's hair looking fresh after a fade haircut, make sure to wash it regularly and use a conditioner to keep it soft and healthy. You can also use a styling product to keep the hair in place and add texture.

Now that you know how to do a fade haircut boy, you can give your child a stylish and modern look from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
