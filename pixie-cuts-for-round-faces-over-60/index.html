Pixie Cuts For Round Faces Over 60 A Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a woman over 60 with a round face, you may be wondering if a pixie cut is the right hairstyle for you. The answer is yes! Pixie cuts are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be tailored to suit any face shape, including round faces. In this guide, we will discuss how to choose the right pixie cut for your face shape, tips for styling and caring for your hair, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut for Round Faces Over 60

When choosing a pixie cut, it's essential to consider your face shape. For round faces, you want to avoid styles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for cuts that elongate your face and create the illusion of height. Here are some styles to consider:

Short Layered Pixie Cut

A short, layered pixie cut can be an excellent choice for round faces over 60. The layers add texture and volume to your hair, while the short length elongates your face. This style is easy to maintain and can be styled in many ways.

Asymmetrical Pixie Cut

An asymmetrical pixie cut is a stylish and edgy option for round faces. This cut features longer hair on one side of your head, which creates an illusion of length and height. This style is perfect for women who want a bold, modern look.

Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut is a great way to add volume and movement to your hair. This style features short, choppy layers that add texture and dimension to your hair. This style is perfect for women with fine or thinning hair.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Pixie Cuts for Round Faces Over 60

Styling a pixie cut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited for your hair type. Use a towel to gently dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a hairbrush or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a hairdryer, blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to fall. Use your fingers or a hairbrush to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool such as a straightener or curling iron to create the desired look. Finish with a styling product such as hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Pixie Cuts for Round Faces Over 60

Here are some tips for keeping your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape of your pixie cut. Aim to visit your hairdresser every four to six weeks for a trim.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are suited for your hair type and needs. Avoid using styling products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol, as these can damage your hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Before using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant product to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ about Pixie Cuts for Round Faces Over 60

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look wider?

A: No, a properly styled pixie cut can elongate your face and create the illusion of height.

Q: How do I choose the right pixie cut for my face shape?

A: Consider your face shape and choose a style that elongates your face and creates the illusion of height.

Q: Can I still style my pixie cut if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes! A textured pixie cut is an excellent option for women with fine or thinning hair.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts for Round Faces Over 60

Here are some hair care tips to keep your pixie cut looking healthy:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush, as this can damage your hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

With these tips and styles in mind, you can confidently rock a pixie cut at any age. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits your face shape and personality best. Remember, a great haircut can make you feel confident and beautiful, so embrace your new look with confidence!
