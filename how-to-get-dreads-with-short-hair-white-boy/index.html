How To Get Dreads With Short Hair White Boy

Written by Bardi Oct 05, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you are a white boy with short hair and looking to get dreads, you may be wondering where to start or if it is even possible. Well, the good news is that it is definitely possible, and this guide will show you how to do it.

Description

Dreadlocks, or simply dreads, are a hairstyle that involves the matting and twisting of hair to create rope-like strands. They are popular among people of different cultures and races, including white boys. However, getting dreads with short hair can be challenging, but not impossible.

How to

Here are the steps to follow if you want to get dreads with short hair:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair

You will need to grow your hair to a certain length to create dreadlocks. Ideally, your hair should be at least 3-4 inches long, and the longer it is, the easier it will be to create dreads.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start creating dreads, you need to prepare your hair. This involves washing your hair with a residue-free shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils that may hinder the locking process. You can also use a clarifying shampoo to ensure your hair is clean and ready for the next step.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

After washing your hair, you need to section it into parts. This involves dividing your hair into small, equal sections using a comb. The sections should be about the same size as the dreadlocks you want to create.

Step 4: Backcomb Your Hair

Backcombing is a technique used to create dreads by teasing the hair strands in the opposite direction of hair growth. To backcomb your hair, you will need a fine-tooth comb and a bit of patience. Start at the roots and work your way down, combing each section until it starts to form a dread.

Step 5: Twist and Seal Your Dreads

After backcombing, twist each dread in the direction you want it to go. You can also palm roll your dreads to help them tighten up. Finally, seal your dreads using a locking gel or wax to keep them in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when creating dreads with short hair:

  • Use a residue-free shampoo to wash your hair
  • Section your hair evenly to create uniform dreadlocks
  • Backcomb slowly and gently to avoid damaging your hair
  • Twist and palm roll your dreads regularly to help them tighten up
  • Be patient and give your hair time to lock up

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting dreads with short hair:

Can I get dreads with really short hair?

It is possible to create dreads with very short hair, but it may take longer to lock up. You will need to backcomb your hair carefully and twist the dreads as they start to form.

Do I need to use wax or gel to create dreads?

No, you don't have to use any products, but using a locking gel or wax can help to keep your dreads in place and speed up the locking process.

How long does it take to create dreads with short hair?

The time it takes to create dreads with short hair depends on factors such as your hair type, length, and how often you twist and palm roll your dreads. Generally, it can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more to form mature, fully locked dreads.

Hair Care

Once you have created your dreads, it is important to take care of them to keep them healthy and looking good. Here are some hair care tips for dreads:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo and warm water
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can weigh down your dreads
  • Dry your dreads thoroughly after washing to prevent mold or mildew
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Protect your dreads from harsh weather conditions, such as extreme heat or cold

By following these tips and taking care of your dreads, you can enjoy this unique and stylish hairstyle for years to come.
