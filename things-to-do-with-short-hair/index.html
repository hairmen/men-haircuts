Things to Do With Short Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

Short hair is versatile and easy to maintain, but it can sometimes feel limiting when it comes to styling. However, there are plenty of things you can do with short hair to switch up your look and add some variety to your style. In this article, we'll go over some tips, tricks and how-to's for different styles and looks you can achieve with short hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Short Hair Styling
  3. Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  4. FAQ for Short Hair Styling

How to Style Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy, and there are many different styles you can achieve with a little bit of effort. Some popular styles for short hair include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Layered cut
  • Textured cut

When deciding on a style, consider your hair type and face shape. Talk to your stylist about which style will work best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Short Hair Styling

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving different styles with short hair:

Pixie Cut

To style a pixie cut:

  1. Apply a styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to damp hair.
  2. Use your fingers to style your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Bob

To style a bob:

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, pulling the hair away from your face.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Layered Cut

To style a layered cut:

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, flipping out the ends of your hair.
  3. Use a curling iron to add more texture and definition to your layers.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Textured Cut

To style a textured cut:

  1. Apply a salt spray to damp hair.
  2. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create texture and definition.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in good quality styling products to help hold your style in place.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from styling products.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQ for Short Hair Styling

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair:

  • Can I still wear my hair up with short hair?
  • How often should I wash my short hair?
  • What should I do if my short hair is too thick or too thin?

Hair Care for Short Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a variety of different styles with your short hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair type.
