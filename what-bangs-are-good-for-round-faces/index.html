What Bangs Are Good For Round Faces A Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your round face making you feel like you can't rock certain hairstyles? Well, fear not! Bangs are a great way to frame your face and add some dimension to your hair. However, not all bangs are created equal when it comes to round faces. In this guide, we'll explore what bangs are good for round faces and provide some tips for styling and care.

What Are Bangs?

Bangs refer to the hair that falls over the forehead, typically cut shorter than the rest of the hair. They come in different lengths, thicknesses, and styles. Bangs can be parted in the middle, swept to the side, or cut straight across. They're a great way to change up your look without having to cut all your hair.

What Bangs Are Good for Round Faces?

When it comes to round faces, the goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of more angles. Here are some types of bangs that work well with round faces:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces because they create the illusion of length. They're also versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can sweep them to the side or part them in the middle. They work well with both short and long hair.

Long, Layered Bangs

Long, layered bangs that blend into the rest of the hair are another option for round faces. They add texture and movement to the hair, which can help break up the roundness of the face. They work well with medium to long hair.

Asymmetrical Bangs

Asymmetrical bangs are cut at an angle, with one side longer than the other. They create the illusion of more angles and can help balance out a round face. They work well with short to medium hair.

How to Style Bangs for Round Faces

Once you've decided on the type of bangs that work best for your face shape, it's important to know how to style them. Here are some tips:

Blow-Dry Your Bangs

Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. This will help smooth out any frizz or kinks and give them a polished look.

Straighten Them

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may need to straighten your bangs to achieve the look you want. Use a flat iron on low heat to gently straighten them.

Use Product

Use a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to help define your bangs and keep them in place.

Step-by-Step Guide for Cutting Bangs for Round Faces

If you're considering cutting your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry, styled hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape.
  3. Use scissors to cut the hair straight across, starting from the middle of the triangle and working your way outwards.
  4. Blend the edges of the bangs into the rest of your hair by point-cutting with scissors.
  5. Check for symmetry by parting your bangs in the middle and adjusting as needed.

Tips for Caring for Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

Trim Them Regularly

Depending on the length of your bangs, you may need to trim them every 4-6 weeks to keep them from getting too long.

Wash Them Often

Because bangs tend to get greasy faster than the rest of your hair, it's important to wash them often. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying them out.

Blow-Dry Them Immediately

After washing your hair, blow-dry your bangs immediately to avoid any kinks or dents from forming.

FAQs

Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?

Absolutely! You just need to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

How do I know which type of bangs will work best for me?

It's best to consult with a hairstylist who can assess your face shape and recommend the best type of bangs for you.

Do I need to use special products to style my bangs?

No, you can use the same products you use on the rest of your hair. Just make sure to use a small amount and avoid weighing down your bangs.

In conclusion, bangs are a great way to add some dimension to your hair and frame your face. If you have a round face, opt for side-swept, long layered, or asymmetrical bangs to create the illusion of more angles. With the right styling and care, your bangs will look great and flatter your face shape.
