How To Make Big Hair Bows With Ribbon A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Big hair bows are a fun and stylish accessory that can add a pop of color and personality to any outfit. Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or just want to add some flair to your everyday look, making your own big hair bows with ribbon is surprisingly easy. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create your own beautiful, oversized hair bows.

Description

In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make big hair bows with ribbon. We will discuss the materials you will need, how to choose the right ribbon, and tips and tricks for making your bows look their best. We will also answer some frequently asked questions about hair bows and provide some advice on how to care for them.

How to Make Big Hair Bows with Ribbon

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

The first step in making big hair bows is to gather your materials. You will need:

  • Ribbon (we recommend using a ribbon that is at least 2 inches wide)
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread
  • Hot glue gun
  • Bobby pins or hair clips

Step 2: Choose Your Ribbon

When choosing your ribbon, you'll want to select a material that is sturdy enough to hold its shape but still pliable enough to work with. Satin, grosgrain, and velvet ribbons are all good choices. You should also consider the color and pattern of the ribbon, as well as the width. For big hair bows, we recommend using a ribbon that is at least 2 inches wide.

Step 3: Cut Your Ribbon

Cut a length of ribbon that is twice the size you want your finished bow to be. For example, if you want a 6-inch bow, cut a length of ribbon that is 12 inches long.

Step 4: Create the Bow Loops

To create the bow loops, fold the ribbon in half and make a loop on each side. Pinch the loops in the center and secure with a small piece of thread. Trim the ends of the ribbon at an angle to create a V-shape.

Step 5: Make the Center Knot

Take a small piece of ribbon and tie it around the center of the bow loops. This will create the center knot. Secure the knot tightly with a knot at the back of the bow.

Step 6: Add the Finishing Touches

Using your hot glue gun, attach a bobby pin or hair clip to the back of the bow. This will allow you to easily attach the bow to your hair. You can also add additional embellishments, such as rhinestones or beads, to make your bow even more unique.

Tips for Making Big Hair Bows with Ribbon

  • Use a high-quality ribbon to ensure that your bow looks its best.
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns to create unique bows that match your style.
  • Practice your bow-making technique on smaller bows before attempting larger ones.
  • Don't be afraid to get creative with your embellishments!

FAQ

What kind of ribbon should I use to make big hair bows?

We recommend using a sturdy but pliable ribbon, such as satin, grosgrain, or velvet. Choose a ribbon that is at least 2 inches wide.

How do I attach the bow to my hair?

Use a bobby pin or hair clip to attach the bow to your hair. You can also attach the bow to a headband for a different look.

How do I care for my hair bow?

Store your hair bow in a cool, dry place to prevent it from getting crushed or damaged. If your bow gets dirty, spot-clean it with a damp cloth and allow it to air dry.

Hair Care

When wearing big hair bows, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong. Consider using a heat protectant spray if you frequently use hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, on your hair.

Making big hair bows with ribbon is a fun and easy DIY project that can add some personality and flair to your style. With these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to create your own beautiful hair bows in no time.
