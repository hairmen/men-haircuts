Side Swept Bangs Look Good On Round Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Side swept bangs are a timeless hairstyle that can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to any look. But did you know that they can also be the perfect style for round faces? In this guide, we’ll explore how side swept bangs can enhance the beauty of round faces, how to style them, and how to care for them. So, let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Side Swept Bangs on Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Side Swept Bangs

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved edges and a lack of prominent angles. When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, it’s important to create balance and symmetry. Side swept bangs are a great way to achieve this, as they draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, creating an elongated look.

Side swept bangs work particularly well on round faces because they help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. They also soften the overall look of the face, making it appear more feminine and delicate. In short, side swept bangs can be a game-changer for those with round faces.

How to Style Side Swept Bangs on Round Faces

Styling side swept bangs is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps. Here’s how:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will ensure that your bangs stay in place throughout the day.

2. Section your hair

Section off the front part of your hair that you want to turn into bangs. Use a comb to create a straight line from the highest point of your eyebrow to the other eyebrow.

3. Cut your bangs

Using scissors, cut your hair in a straight line, starting from the outer corner of one eye and ending at the outer corner of the other eye. Make sure to cut your hair below your eyebrows, as this will create a softer look.

4. Style your bangs

Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs in a sweeping motion to the side. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.

5. Finish with hairspray

To ensure your bangs stay in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Still not sure how to style your side swept bangs? Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you begin styling.

2. Section off your hair

Section off the front part of your hair that you want to turn into bangs. Use a comb to create a straight line from the highest point of your eyebrow to the other eyebrow.

3. Pin back the rest of your hair

Use a hair clip or bobby pins to pin back the rest of your hair. This will ensure that you can focus on styling your bangs.

4. Cut your bangs

Using scissors, cut your hair in a straight line, starting from the outer corner of one eye and ending at the outer corner of the other eye. Make sure to cut your hair below your eyebrows, as this will create a softer look.

5. Style your bangs

Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs in a sweeping motion to the side. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.

6. Finish with hairspray

To ensure your bangs stay in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Swept Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your side swept bangs looking great:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become greasy or flat.
  • Use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look oily.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about side swept bangs:

Can anyone pull off side swept bangs?

Side swept bangs can look great on a variety of face shapes, but they work particularly well on round faces.

How do I know if side swept bangs are right for me?

If you have a round face, side swept bangs can help to create balance and symmetry. It’s also important to consider your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

How do I style my side swept bangs?

Start with clean, dry hair and use a round brush to sweep your bangs to the side. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to ensure they stay in place all day.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your side swept bangs looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your bangs from becoming greasy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair and bangs looking healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock the perfect side swept bangs for your round face. Just remember to keep them trimmed, styled, and well-maintained for the best results!
