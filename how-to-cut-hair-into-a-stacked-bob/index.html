How To Cut Hair Into A Stacked Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's both stylish and easy to maintain, a stacked bob might just be the perfect option for you. This trendy cut features stacked layers at the back of the head, with shorter layers framing the face. Not sure how to achieve this look? Keep reading for a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair into a stacked bob.

Description

A stacked bob is a popular haircut that features layers at the back of the head, with shorter layers framing the face. This cut is perfect for those who want a stylish and low-maintenance look. It can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types, making it a versatile option.

How To

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here's how to cut hair into a stacked bob:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts â two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 2: Cut the Back

Starting with the back sections, comb the hair straight down and cut a straight line across the bottom. Then, create a guide by taking a small section of hair from the center of the back and cutting it at a diagonal. Repeat this process on both sides, cutting at a slightly steeper angle each time.

Step 3: Cut the Front

Move on to the front sections, combing the hair straight down and cutting it to the desired length. For a more dramatic look, you can angle the scissors upwards while cutting to create a shorter, more textured look.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Using a blending technique, blend the shorter layers from the front into the longer layers at the back. This will create a seamless transition between the two sections.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting, go back and check for any uneven sections or stray hairs. Make any necessary adjustments and add finishing touches, such as texturizing or layering, to achieve your desired look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting hair into a stacked bob:

  • Start with dry, straightened hair for the most accurate cut.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean, precise cut.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to avoid mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to adjust the length as you go â it's always better to err on the side of caution.

FAQ

Q: Is a stacked bob suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a stacked bob can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and angle for your face.

Q: How often should I get my stacked bob cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but most people find that a trim every 6-8 weeks keeps their stacked bob looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your stacked bob looking its best, make sure to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting hair into a stacked bob is a great way to update your look and achieve a stylish, low-maintenance haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this trendy look at home or with the help of a professional stylist.
