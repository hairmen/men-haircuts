How To Style Curly Hair On Little Boy

Written by Luffy Jul 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair on little boys can be a challenge to style, but with the right products and techniques, you can create a look that is both stylish and manageable. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style curly hair on little boys. We will also give you some tips on how to care for curly hair and answer some frequently asked questions.

  • Hair Care for Curly Hair
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair on Little Boy

How to Style Curly Hair on Little Boy

The first thing you need to do when styling curly hair on a little boy is to choose the right haircut. A layered haircut will help to define the curls and make them easier to manage. You can also ask your hairstylist to add some texture to the hair to create more volume.

Once you have the right haircut, you can follow these steps to style curly hair on a little boy:

Step 1: Wash the Hair

Start by washing the hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils, which can make curly hair dry and frizzy. Use warm water to wash the hair and rinse it thoroughly.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to the hair to help detangle it and keep it moisturized. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout the hair.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to the hair to define the curls and hold the style in place. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse, depending on the texture of the hair. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout the hair.

Step 4: Style the Hair

You can style curly hair in a variety of ways, depending on the length and texture of the hair. Here are some popular styles for little boys with curly hair:

  • Cropped Cut: This is a short, low-maintenance style that works well for active little boys. It involves cutting the hair close to the scalp and leaving some length on top to create a messy, textured look.
  • Curly Afro: This is a bold and stylish look that involves letting the hair grow out and shaping it into a round, voluminous afro.
  • Curly Mohawk: This is a fun and edgy style that involves shaving the sides of the hair and leaving the top long and curly.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair is essential to keeping it healthy, strong, and manageable. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo: Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy. Look for a shampoo that is free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.
  • Condition regularly: Conditioner helps to keep curly hair moisturized and detangled. Use a leave-in conditioner after washing and a deep conditioner once a week.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools can damage curly hair and make it frizzy. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  • Protect the hair at night: Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage. You can also wrap the hair in a silk scarf or bonnet.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair on Little Boy

Here are some additional tips for styling curly hair on little boys:

  • Don't overwash: Curly hair doesn't need to be washed as often as straight hair. Overwashing can strip the hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb: A wide-tooth comb is gentler on curly hair than a brush and helps to detangle the hair without causing breakage.
  • Embrace the curls: Don't try to straighten or tame curly hair too much. Embrace the natural texture of the hair and work with it to create a style that suits your little boy's personality.

FAQ

How often should I wash my little boy's curly hair?

Curly hair doesn't need to be washed as often as straight hair. Aim to wash your little boy's hair once or twice a week, depending on how active he is and how much he sweats.

What is the best way to detangle curly hair?

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle curly hair. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots, being careful not to pull or tug on the hair.

Can I use a brush on curly hair?

It's best to avoid using a brush on curly hair, as it can cause breakage and frizz. If you must use a brush, use a wide-tooth brush or a brush with natural bristles.

What is the best styling product for curly hair?

The best styling product for curly hair depends on the texture of the hair. A curl cream or gel is a good choice for thick, coarse hair, while a mousse is better for fine, thin hair.

How can I prevent frizz in curly hair?

To prevent frizz in curly hair, avoid touching or playing with the hair too much. Use a satin or silk pillowcase, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Can I cut my little boy's curly hair myself?

If you have experience cutting hair, you can cut your little boy's curly hair yourself. However, if you are not comfortable cutting hair, it's best to leave it to a professional hairstylist.
