How To Pick Color At Nail Salon Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever found yourself at the nail salon, staring blankly at the wall of nail polish colors, unsure of which one to choose? Picking the right color can be a daunting task, especially when there are so many options available. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to pick color at nail salon.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick Color at Nail Salon
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Color
  • FAQs

How to Pick Color at Nail Salon

The first thing you need to do is decide what type of look you want to achieve. Do you want something bold and bright, or something more subtle and understated? Consider your skin tone and the occasion you'll be wearing the nail polish for. Once you've narrowed down your options, it's time to start browsing the colors available.

Look at Swatches

Most nail salons will have a swatch book or display of all the colors they offer. This is a great place to start. Look through the swatches and see which colors catch your eye. You can also hold the swatches up to your skin to get an idea of how the color will look on you.

Ask for Recommendations

If you're still unsure, don't be afraid to ask your nail technician for recommendations. They have experience working with different colors and can help you find the perfect shade for your skin tone and the occasion.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to pick color at nail salon:

  1. Decide what type of look you want to achieve.
  2. Browse the swatches and see which colors catch your eye.
  3. Hold the swatches up to your skin to get an idea of how the color will look on you.
  4. Ask your nail technician for recommendations.
  5. Consider the occasion you'll be wearing the nail polish for.
  6. Choose a color that complements your skin tone.
  7. Think about the finish you want (matte, glossy, glittery, etc).
  8. Consider the season (bright colors for summer, darker shades for fall and winter).
  9. Don't be afraid to try something new!
  10. Trust your gut and choose a color that makes you happy.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Color

Here are some additional tips for choosing the perfect color:

  • Bring in a photo of an outfit you'll be wearing the nail polish with to help you choose a color that complements it.
  • Consider the length and shape of your nails when choosing a color (darker colors tend to look better on shorter nails).
  • Take into account your lifestyle and job (if you work in a conservative environment, you may want to stick to neutral colors).
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match colors or try nail art.

FAQs

What if I don't like the color I picked?

If you don't like the color you picked, don't worry. You can always ask your nail technician to remove it and choose a different color.

How often should I change my nail color?

It's up to you how often you want to change your nail color. Some people change it weekly, while others go longer. It's important to give your nails a break in between polish changes to prevent damage.

What if I'm on a budget?

If you're on a budget, consider purchasing your own nail polish and bringing it to the salon. This way, you can ensure you're getting the color you want and you can use the polish at home as well.

Can I bring my own nail polish to the salon?

Yes, most salons will allow you to bring your own nail polish. Just be sure to check with them beforehand.

Now that you know how to pick color at nail salon, go ahead and try out some new shades! Remember, it's all about having fun and expressing yourself.
