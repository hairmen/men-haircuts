What Haircuts Make Your Face Look Thinner A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Oct 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to make your face look slimmer without going under the knife? Haircuts can be a great solution! By choosing the right hairstyle, you can create the illusion of a more sculpted jawline and a slimmer face. In this article, we’ll share some tips on what haircuts make your face look thinner.

Haircuts for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you want to choose a haircut that creates the illusion of length. You should avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for layered haircuts that fall past your shoulders. Long, side-swept bangs can also help elongate your face.

A long bob, also known as a “lob,” is a great haircut for round faces. The length of the hair elongates the face, while the layers add movement and texture. A pixie cut can also work well, as long as the layers are kept long and choppy.

Haircuts for Square Faces

If you have a square face, you want to soften your strong jawline. A layered haircut with soft, wispy bangs can help achieve this. Long, face-framing layers can also help soften your features.

A shoulder-length bob with layers can be a great haircut for square faces. The layers add movement and texture, while the length elongates the face. A long, layered shag can also work well, as long as the layers are kept soft and wispy.

Haircuts for Heart-Shaped Faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your wide forehead and narrow chin. A haircut with layers that fall around your chin can help achieve this. Long, side-swept bangs can also help balance out your features.

A long, layered haircut with face-framing layers can be a great option for heart-shaped faces. The layers add movement and texture, while the face-framing layers help balance out your features. A lob with long, side-swept bangs can also work well.

Haircuts for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face, you’re in luck! Almost any haircut will look great on you. However, if you want to create the illusion of a slimmer face, you can try a long, layered haircut with face-framing layers. Side-swept bangs can also help create a more sculpted jawline.

Tips for Getting the Right Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a haircut
  • Ask your stylist for their opinion on what would look best on you
  • Be open to trying new styles
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking its best

FAQ

Q: Can hair color make my face look thinner?

A: Yes! Highlights or lowlights can create the illusion of depth and dimension, which can help make your face look thinner. Talk to your stylist about which hair color would be best for you.

Q: What should I avoid if I want my face to look thinner?

A: Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face look wider. You should also avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the crown, as they can add width to your face.

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes! The key is to choose a layered haircut with choppy layers that fall around your face. A pixie cut with long, choppy layers can also work well.

Hair Care Tips for Thinner-Looking Face

In addition to choosing the right haircut, there are some hair care tips you can follow to create the illusion of a slimmer face:

  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make your face look wider
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the crown
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your roots
  • Avoid wearing your hair in a tight ponytail or bun, as it can make your face look wider
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and definition to your hair

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your face shape, you can create the illusion of a slimmer, more sculpted face without the need for surgery or drastic measures.
