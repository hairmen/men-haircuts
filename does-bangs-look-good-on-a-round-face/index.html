Does Bangs Look Good On A Round Face

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering whether bangs would look good on you. The truth is, bangs can be a great way to complement a round face shape, but it all depends on the type of bangs you choose.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. It is a beautiful face shape that can be enhanced with the right haircut and hairstyle. Bangs can be a great way to add dimension to a round face and accentuate your best features.

How to

The key to making bangs work for a round face is to choose the right type of bangs. Here are some options:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great choice for a round face. They help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular jawline. When getting side-swept bangs, make sure they are long enough to reach the outer corner of your eye.

Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can also work well on a round face. However, they should be cut just above the eyebrows to avoid making your face look shorter and rounder. Blunt bangs can also be paired with layers to add texture and movement to your hair.

Textured Bangs

Textured bangs are another great option for a round face. They add volume and movement to your hair, which can help to balance out the roundness of your face. Textured bangs can be cut at varying lengths to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

If you decide to get bangs, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Bangs

As mentioned earlier, side-swept, blunt, and textured bangs are great choices for a round face. Choose the type of bangs that you feel most comfortable with and that will work best with your hair type.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before getting bangs, it is important to consult with your hairstylist. They can help you determine which type of bangs will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

When getting bangs, you should also consider your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle or work in a profession that requires a lot of movement, you may want to opt for side-swept or textured bangs that can be easily styled and maintained.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you have your bangs, you can style them in a variety of ways. Side-swept bangs can be swept to the side with a round brush and blow dryer. Blunt bangs can be straightened with a flat iron, while textured bangs can be scrunched with a styling product.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting bangs:

  • Choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  • Consult with your hairstylist before getting bangs.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing bangs.
  • Style your bangs to suit your face shape and personal style.

FAQ

Q. Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A. It depends on the type of bangs you choose. Side-swept, blunt, and textured bangs can help to balance out a round face shape and make it look more angular.

Q. How do I style my bangs?

A. You can style your bangs in a variety of ways, depending on the type of bangs you have. Side-swept bangs can be swept to the side with a round brush and blow dryer, while blunt bangs can be straightened with a flat iron.

Q. How often should I trim my bangs?

A. You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, you should wash and condition them regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. You can also use a styling product to keep your bangs in place and add shine.

Overall, bangs can be a great way to enhance a round face shape. Just make sure to choose the right type of bangs and style them to suit your face shape and personal style.
