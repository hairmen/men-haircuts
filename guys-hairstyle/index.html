Guys Hairstyle A Guide To The Latest Trends And Tips

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy who's looking to update your hairstyle, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll cover the latest trends in guys hairstyle, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents:

  • Trends
  • How to
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Trends

The latest trends in guys hairstyle include fades, undercuts, textured crops, and long hair. Fades can be high or low, and can be paired with short or long hair on top. Undercuts involve shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving a longer length on top. Textured crops are short on the sides and longer on top, with a messy, textured look. Long hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy man buns to slicked back styles.

High Fade with Long Hair on Top

This style involves shaving the sides and back of the head to create a high fade, leaving a longer length of hair on top. The longer hair can be styled in a messy, textured look or slicked back for a more polished appearance.

Textured Crop

The textured crop involves short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The hair on top is styled in a messy, textured look for a relaxed and casual appearance.

Man Bun

The man bun involves growing out the hair on top and back of the head, and tying it back into a bun. This style can be messy and casual or slicked back for a more polished look.

How to

Depending on the style you're going for, achieving a new guys hairstyle can involve a trip to the barber or stylist, or simply changing up your styling routine at home. For more complex styles, it's best to consult with a professional who can help you achieve your desired look.

High Fade with Long Hair on Top

To achieve a high fade with long hair on top, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced with this style. They will use clippers to shave the sides and back of your head, leaving a longer length of hair on top. From there, you can style the longer hair using a texturizing product for a messy, casual look or a pomade for a more polished appearance.

Textured Crop

The textured crop can be achieved by asking your barber or stylist for a short cut on the sides and back of your head, with longer hair on top. From there, you can use a texturizing product to create a messy, textured look. Simply apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers to style.

Man Bun

To achieve a man bun, you'll need to grow out the hair on top and back of your head to a length that can be tied back. From there, you can tie your hair back into a bun using a hair tie or elastic band. For a messy look, simply pull the hair back with your hands and tie it into a loose bun. For a more polished appearance, slick the hair back using a pomade or gel before tying it back.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new guys hairstyle:

  • Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type and desired style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys hairstyle:

What should I consider before getting a new hairstyle?

Before getting a new hairstyle, consider your lifestyle, hair type, and desired level of maintenance. Some hairstyles require more upkeep than others, so be sure to choose a style that works for your daily routine.

What products do I need to maintain my new hairstyle?

The products you'll need to maintain your new hairstyle will depend on the style you choose. For most styles, you'll need a quality shampoo and conditioner, as well as a styling product like a pomade or texturizing spray.

How often should I get a haircut?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on your desired style and how quickly your hair grows. For most guys, getting a trim every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish mane. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top shape:

  • Wash your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health, such as biotin and vitamin E.

With these tips and trends in mind, you're sure to find a guys hairstyle that suits your style and personality. Whether you're looking for a casual, messy look or a polished, professional appearance, there's a style out there for everyone. So go ahead and experiment with different cuts and products until you find the perfect look for you!
