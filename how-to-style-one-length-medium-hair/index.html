How To Style One Length Medium Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and easy-to-manage hairstyle, one-length medium hair might be the perfect choice for you. This style is suitable for most hair types and face shapes, and it can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. In this post, we'll show you how to style one-length medium hair in different ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and wavy.

How to Style One Length Medium Hair

One-length medium hair is a timeless and classic style that can be worn in different ways. Here are some popular ways to style this haircut:

Straight and Sleek

To achieve a straight and sleek look, start by applying a heat protectant to your hair. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your hair in small sections. Finish with a shine serum or spray to add a healthy sheen to your locks.

Tousled and Wavy

If you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, try tousled and wavy hair. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair and use a curling wand to create loose waves. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to add more texture, and finish with a sea salt spray for a beachy vibe.

Sleek Ponytail

A sleek ponytail is a simple and elegant way to style one-length medium hair. Start by brushing your hair back and securing it with an elastic band. Use a bit of hair gel or pomade to smooth out any flyaways, and wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it.

Braids

Braids are a fun and versatile way to style one-length medium hair. You can try a simple three-strand braid, a fishtail braid, or a Dutch braid. You can also add braids to your ponytail or half-up style for a more intricate look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style one-length medium hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and bounce.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any hot tools.
  4. Decide on the style you want to create, whether it's straight, wavy, or braided.
  5. Use the appropriate tools, such as a flat iron, curling wand, or hair elastics, to create your desired look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining One Length Medium Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your one-length medium hair:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about one-length medium hair:

Is one-length medium hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, one-length medium hair is a versatile style that can flatter most face shapes. However, you may need to adjust the length or layers to suit your features.

Can I style one-length medium hair if I have fine or thin hair?

Absolutely! One-length medium hair can add volume and texture to fine or thin hair. You can also use volumizing products or teasing to create the illusion of thicker hair.

How can I add more dimension to my one-length medium hair?

You can add layers, highlights, or lowlights to create more depth and dimension to your one-length medium hair. Talk to your stylist about your options.

Hair Care Tips for One Length Medium Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for one-length medium hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant before styling.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your one-length medium hair in different styles and keep it healthy and shiny. Experiment with different looks and have fun with your hair!
