How To Angle The Sides Of Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same old hairstyle? Do you want to add some edge and dimension to your look? Angling the sides of your hair is a great way to achieve a fresh and modern style. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to angle the sides of your hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Angle the Sides of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Angling Your Hair

How to Angle the Sides of Your Hair

Angling the sides of your hair involves cutting the hair at an angle to create a tapered look. This technique adds depth and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. Angling the sides of your hair also helps to frame your face and accentuate your features.

Tools You Will Need

Before getting started, here are the tools you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the middle using a comb. This will help you create a symmetrical cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by clipping the top half of your hair up. This will help you focus on the sides of your hair.

Step 4: Wet Your Hair

Use a water spray bottle to wet the sides of your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a clean cut.

Step 5: Cut Your Hair at an Angle

Starting from the bottom, use your scissors to cut your hair at an angle. Cut the hair shorter towards the back and longer towards the front. Make sure to cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes. Repeat this process on both sides of your hair.

Step 6: Blend Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting both sides of your hair, use your scissors to blend the hair together. This will create a seamless transition between the angled sides and the rest of your hair.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a hair straightener, curling iron, or hair products to add texture and volume to your hair.

Tips for Angling Your Hair

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid any mistakes
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Wet your hair before cutting to ensure a smooth and clean cut
  • Blend your hair to create a seamless transition between the angled sides and the rest of your hair
  • Style your hair as desired to add texture and volume

FAQs

How often should I angle my hair?

You can angle your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Can I angle my hair if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Angling your hair can add more depth and dimension to your curly hair. Just make sure to cut your hair when it is dry to avoid any shrinkage.

Can I angle my hair if I have short hair?

Yes, you can angle your hair no matter the length. Just make sure to cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair

Angling the sides of your hair is a great way to add some edge and dimension to your look. By following this step-by-step guide and using our tips, you can achieve a modern and stylish hairstyle. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using proper hair care techniques and products. Happy styling!
