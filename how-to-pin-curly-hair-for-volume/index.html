How To Pin Curly Hair For Volume

Written by Petter Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to get the volume you want. Fortunately, there are ways to add volume to your curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to pin curly hair for volume.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pin Curly Hair for Volume
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Pinning Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Pin Curly Hair for Volume

Pinning curly hair for volume is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps. Here’s what you need to do:

What You’ll Need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to pin curly hair for volume:

Step 1: Brush or comb your hair

Use a hairbrush or comb to brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to work with your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the more volume you’ll have. Use bobby pins to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Twist and pin

Take one section of hair and twist it. Pin it to your scalp with a bobby pin. Repeat this process with the remaining sections of hair.

Step 4: Add hairspray (optional)

If you want your curly hair to stay in place, you can add hairspray. Spray a light mist over your hair to help hold the style.

Tips for Pinning Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you pin curly hair for volume:

Tip 1: Use bobby pins

Bobby pins are great for pinning curly hair because they’re small and won’t damage your hair.

Tip 2: Section your hair

Sectioning your hair will make it easier to work with and will give you more volume.

Tip 3: Twist your hair

Twisting your hair before pinning it will help give you more volume.

Tip 4: Use hairspray

Hairspray can help hold your style in place and prevent frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I pin curly hair when it’s wet?

A: It’s best to pin curly hair when it’s dry. Wet hair is more fragile and can break easily.

Q: How long will my pin-curled hair last?

A: Pin-curled hair can last for several hours or even overnight. It depends on your hair type and how well you’ve secured the pins.

Q: Can I use other types of pins?

A: You can use other types of pins, but bobby pins are the best choice for pinning curly hair because they’re small and won’t damage your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and frizzy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy.

Tip 2: Condition your hair regularly

Conditioning your hair regularly can help keep it healthy and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls looking their best.

Tip 3: Use a wide-tooth comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on curly hair than a brush. Use it to detangle your hair after washing.

Tip 4: Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons.

Tip 5: Protect your hair while sleeping

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while sleeping. This will help prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and using the pinning technique we’ve outlined, you can achieve beautiful, voluminous curls without damaging your hair.
