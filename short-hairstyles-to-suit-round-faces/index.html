Short Hairstyles To Suit Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles only to find out that they don't suit your round face? Well, worry no more! We have compiled a list of the best short hairstyles to suit round faces that will flatter your features and make you look fantastic. Read on to find out more.

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

Round faces have soft features and a circular shape. The best short hairstyles for round faces are those that add height and volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks. Short hairstyles with layers and textures are great for round faces as they create movement and dimension.

How to

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the widest point of your face, which is usually at the cheeks, and the length of your face from the hairline to the chin. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for short hairstyles that suit it.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best short hairstyles for round faces:

Pixie Cut

1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the back, the sides, and the top.

2. Use scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back short, leaving the hair on the top longer.

3. Use a razor to add texture and layers to the hair on the top.

4. Style the hair using a hairdryer and a styling product to add volume and texture to the crown.

Bob

1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the back, the sides, and the top.

2. Use scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back short, leaving the hair on the top longer.

3. Add layers to the hair on the top using scissors or a razor to create movement and dimension.

4. Style the hair using a hairdryer and a styling product to add volume and texture to the crown.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers and textures to create movement and dimension.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make the face appear rounder.

What hairstyles are best for round faces?

Short hairstyles with layers and textures are great for round faces, as they create movement and dimension. Pixie cuts and bobs are also great options.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Use a styling product to add volume and texture to the crown, and use a hairdryer to style your hair. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best short hairstyle to suit your round face and look your best every day. So, go ahead and try out these hairstyles and see which one suits you the best.
