How To Do Makeup To Look Slimmer Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Sep 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to look slimmer, then applying makeup can be a great option. With just a few simple tricks, you can use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a more slender physique. In this article, we'll show you how to do makeup to look slimmer, step by step. We'll also share some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you get the most out of your makeup routine.

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

The first step to any makeup routine is to prep your skin. Start by cleansing your face and applying moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated. Then, use a primer to help your makeup last longer and create a smooth base for your foundation. Choose a primer that's designed to blur imperfections and minimize pores for the best results.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Contouring is the key to creating a slimmer-looking face. Use a matte bronzer to shade the areas of your face that you want to minimize, such as your temples, cheekbones, and jawline. Apply the bronzer in a "3" shape on each side of your face, starting at your temples, sweeping down to your cheekbones, and then down to your jawline. Blend well to avoid harsh lines.

Step 3: Highlight Your Features

Highlighting is the opposite of contouring, and it helps to bring out your best features. Use a highlighter to add shimmer to the areas of your face that you want to accentuate, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. Apply the highlighter in a "C" shape around your eyes, starting at your brow bone, sweeping down to your cheekbones, and then up to the bridge of your nose. Blend well for a natural look.

Step 4: Apply Blush

Blush adds a pop of color to your cheeks and gives you a healthy, youthful glow. Choose a blush shade that complements your skin tone and apply it to the apples of your cheeks, blending upward toward your temples. Avoid applying too much blush or applying it too low on your cheeks, as this can make your face look wider.

Step 5: Define Your Brows

Well-defined brows can help to frame your face and make it look slimmer. Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in any sparse areas and create a defined shape. Brush your brows upward and outward for a natural look.

Step 6: Apply Eye Makeup

Eye makeup can help to draw attention to your eyes and make them look bigger and brighter. Use a neutral eyeshadow palette to create a subtle, natural look. Apply a light shade on your lid, a medium shade in your crease, and a dark shade along your lash line. Use mascara to lengthen and volumize your lashes.

Step 7: Finish with Lips

Finish your makeup look with a flattering lip color. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and coordinates with your outfit. Use a lip liner to define your lips and prevent bleeding, and then apply lipstick or lip gloss to finish the look.

Tips for Looking Slimmer with Makeup

  • Use matte products instead of shimmery products to create a more natural-looking contour.
  • Blend your makeup well to avoid harsh lines and make your makeup look more natural.
  • Choose a foundation shade that matches your skin tone exactly to avoid looking too pale or too dark.
  • Use a setting spray to keep your makeup in place and prevent it from melting or smudging throughout the day.
  • Don't forget to blend your makeup down your neck and onto your chest to avoid an obvious makeup line.

FAQs About Makeup to Look Slimmer

What kind of makeup should I use to look slimmer?

Matte products are best for creating a slimming effect, as they create a natural-looking shadow without adding any extra shine or shimmer.

How can I make my cheekbones look higher?

Use a matte bronzer to shade just underneath your cheekbones, blending upward to create the illusion of higher cheekbones.

Can makeup really make me look slimmer?

Yes, makeup can create the illusion of a slimmer face by contouring and highlighting your features in strategic ways.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Slimming Makeup

While makeup can help to make your face look slimmer, your hairstyle can also play a role in creating a more slender appearance. Try these hair care tips to complement your slimming makeup:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, such as a long bob or side-swept bangs.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as big, bouncy curls or a voluminous blowout.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add dimension and movement to your hair without adding extra width to your face.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair without making it look too full or wide.

With these makeup and hair care tips, you can create a slimmer-looking face and feel confident and beautiful every day. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to look your best, these tips can help you achieve your desired look.
