How To Wear A Hat With Short Curly Hair Guys

Written by Petter Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Short curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially when it comes to wearing hats. However, with the right technique and style, you can easily rock a hat with your short curly hair. Here’s a guide on how to wear a hat with short curly hair guys.

Description

Wearing a hat is a great way to add style to your outfit and protect your head from the sun. However, it can be tricky to find the right hat that suits your short curly hair. The key is to choose a hat that complements your hair texture and face shape. In this article, we’ll give you tips on how to choose the right hat and how to style it with your short curly hair.

How to Choose the Right Hat

When it comes to choosing the right hat for your short curly hair, you need to consider your hair texture and face shape. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Choose a hat that fits your head

When buying a hat, make sure it fits your head properly. A hat that is too tight or too loose can ruin your hairstyle and make you look unattractive.

Tip #2: Consider your face shape

Choose a hat that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, go for a hat with a wide brim to balance out your face. If you have a square face, go for a hat with a curved brim to soften your features.

Tip #3: Choose a hat that matches your hair texture

For short curly hair, choose a hat that matches your hair texture. A beanie or a baseball cap is a great choice for curly hair as it won’t flatten your curls.

Step by Step Guide on How to Wear a Hat with Short Curly Hair

Now that you’ve chosen the right hat, it’s time to style it with your short curly hair. Here’s a step by step guide:

Step #1: Prep your hair

Wash and condition your hair using products that define your curls. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.

Step #2: Style your hair

Style your hair as usual. If you want to wear a beanie, leave your hair loose or tie it into a loose bun at the nape of your neck.

Step #3: Position the hat

Position the hat on your head. Make sure it sits comfortably and doesn’t flatten your curls.

Step #4: Adjust the hat

Adjust the hat to make sure it sits properly on your head. If you’re wearing a baseball cap, adjust the strap to fit your head.

Step #5: Tuck in any loose hair

If you’re wearing a beanie, tuck in any loose hair around your face. This will give you a neater look.

Tips for Wearing a Hat with Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for wearing a hat with short curly hair:

Tip #1: Choose the right fabric

Choose a hat made from a breathable fabric like cotton or wool. This will prevent your head from getting too hot and sweating.

Tip #2: Avoid wearing a hat for too long

Avoid wearing a hat for too long as it can flatten your curls and create a frizzy look.

Tip #3: Experiment with different styles

Experiment with different styles of hats to find what suits you best. Try a fedora, a bucket hat, or a snapback.

FAQ

Q1. Can I wear a hat with short curly hair?

A1. Yes, you can wear a hat with short curly hair. Just make sure to choose the right hat that complements your hair texture and face shape.

Q2. How do I prevent my curls from flattening when wearing a hat?

A2. To prevent your curls from flattening, choose a hat that doesn’t sit too tightly on your head. You can also wear a beanie or a baseball cap instead of a fitted hat.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short curly hair:

Tip #1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Tip #2: Avoid using heat styling tools

Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your curls. Instead, air dry your hair or use a diffuser.

Tip #3: Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.

Wearing a hat with short curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right hat and styling technique, you can easily pull off the look. Follow these tips and tricks to rock a hat with your short curly hair.
