How To Cut Medium Length Layered Haircuts

Written by Bardi Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that’s both trendy and versatile? Look no further than the medium length layered haircut! It’s a great option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair without sacrificing too much length. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut medium length layered haircuts in an easy-to-follow guide.

  • Description
  • How to Cut Medium Length Layered Haircuts
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Medium Length Layered Haircuts

Description

The medium length layered haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your individual preferences. Whether you want long layers or short layers, this haircut can be adjusted to suit your needs. It’s a great option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair without sacrificing too much length. This style works well with all hair types, from straight to curly, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to Cut Medium Length Layered Haircuts

To cut a medium length layered haircut, you’ll need a few basic tools: a comb, hair cutting shears, and hair clips. Follow these steps to create the perfect layered look:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of your hair, which will make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four equal parts: top, bottom, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use your hair cutting shears to trim off any split ends, and then cut the hair to the desired length. Work your way around the entire bottom layer, making sure to cut evenly.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the top section of your hair and let it fall over the middle layer. Use your hair cutting shears to cut the middle layer to the desired length. Make sure to cut evenly around the entire section.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Release the top section of your hair and let it fall over the middle and bottom layers. Use your hair cutting shears to cut the top layer to the desired length. Make sure to cut evenly around the entire section.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Use your hair cutting shears to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless transition from one layer to the next.

Tips for Cutting Medium Length Layered Haircuts

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting medium length layered haircuts:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Use sharp hair cutting shears. Dull shears can damage your hair and create split ends.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet. Wet hair can shrink up to 10%, which can lead to uneven cutting.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair can be a time-consuming process, but the end result will be worth it.

FAQ

How often should I get a medium length layered haircut?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Can I cut my own medium length layered haircut?

While it’s possible to cut your own hair, it’s recommended to visit a professional stylist to ensure the best results.

Will a medium length layered haircut work for my hair type?

Yes, a medium length layered haircut works well with all hair types, from straight to curly.

Hair Care for Medium Length Layered Haircuts

After cutting your medium length layered haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a beautiful, versatile medium length layered haircut that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re going for a casual, relaxed look or a more formal style, this haircut is sure to turn heads.
