Pixie Hairstyles For Over 60 Round Face A Guide For Mature Women

Written by Robby Jun 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you in your 60s with a round face and looking for a chic and easy-to-manage hairstyle? Look no further than the pixie cut! Pixie hairstyles for over 60 round face are not only trendy but also practical for mature women who want to embrace their natural beauty. In this guide, we'll show you how to rock a pixie cut with confidence and offer tips on hair care and styling.

Why Choose a Pixie Cut?

Pixie cuts are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. They're also low-maintenance and can save you time and money on styling products and salon visits. Pixie hairstyles for over 60 round face can accentuate your facial features, create volume and movement, and bring out your inner sassiness.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut for Your Round Face

Not all pixie cuts are created equal, especially when it comes to round faces. The key is to create balance and elongation by adding height, angles, and layers to your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

If you have thin or fine hair, opt for a layered pixie cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick or curly hair, choose a tapered or undercut pixie cut that reduces bulk and defines your curls.

Focus on the Top of Your Head

Avoid pixie cuts that are too short or flat on top, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, go for pixie cuts with longer layers or bangs that can be swept to the side or teased for height.

Avoid Full Fringe

While bangs can be flattering for round faces, full fringe can make your face look shorter and wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that frame your face and soften your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

Now that you've chosen your ideal pixie cut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner if needed. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your fingertips and rub them together. Then, distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and crown.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush and a blow dryer, lift and smooth your hair at the roots to create volume and shape. Use the brush to direct your hair away from your face and create angles and texture.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to tousle and define your hair. You can also add a small amount of hair wax or pomade to create separation and hold. Finish with a light hairspray to set your style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

Visit Your Stylist Regularly

Depending on your hair growth rate, you may need to visit your stylist every 4-8 weeks to trim and shape your pixie cut. This will prevent split ends and maintain the integrity of your hair.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

When using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent breakage and damage. Use a low heat setting and avoid over-styling your hair.

Experiment with Accessories

Accessorize your pixie cut with headbands, scarves, or hair clips to add color and flair to your hairstyle. You can also experiment with different hair colors or highlights to enhance your features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Wear a Pixie Cut with Glasses?

Absolutely! Pixie hairstyles for over 60 round face can complement your glasses and create a chic and sophisticated look. Choose glasses that flatter your face shape and coordinate with your hair color.

Can I Dye My Pixie Cut?

Yes, you can dye your pixie cut to match your skin tone, eye color, or personal style. However, keep in mind that frequent dyeing can damage your hair and cause dryness or breakage. Consult with your stylist before making any drastic changes.

Can I Wear a Pixie Cut with Bangs?

Yes, you can wear a pixie cut with bangs as long as they're not too thick or heavy. Side-swept or wispy bangs can soften your features and add dimension to your hairstyle.

The Bottom Line

Pixie hairstyles for over 60 round face can be a game-changer for mature women who want to feel confident and stylish. By choosing the right pixie cut, styling your hair with care, and maintaining your hair health, you can rock a pixie cut like a pro. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your new look!
