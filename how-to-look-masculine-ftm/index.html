How to Look Masculine FTM: A Step-by-Step Guide

In today's world, it's important to be true to yourself and express who you are. For many trans men, looking masculine is an important part of that expression. However, it can be difficult to know where to start. This guide will give you step-by-step instructions on how to look masculine FTM, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Masculine FTM
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Looking Masculine FTM
  • FAQs

How to Look Masculine FTM

There are many ways to look masculine FTM, including changing your hairstyle, wearing masculine clothing, and working out to build muscle. It's important to remember that everyone's journey is unique, and what works for one person may not work for another. Here are some general tips to help you look more masculine:

Clothing

One of the easiest ways to look more masculine is to wear clothing designed for men. This includes jeans, t-shirts, button-up shirts, and suits. Look for clothing with a more structured fit and avoid anything too flowy or feminine. Clothing can also help create the illusion of broader shoulders or a more defined waist.

Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can play a big role in how masculine you look. Short haircuts, like a buzz cut or undercut, are popular choices for trans men. If you're not ready to go that short, try a more masculine style like a slicked-back pompadour or a messy quiff.

Body Language

The way you carry yourself can also affect how masculine you appear. Stand up straight with your shoulders back and avoid crossing your legs or arms. Take up space and use hand gestures when you speak. These small changes can make a big difference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to look masculine FTM:

  1. Choose clothing designed for men with a more structured fit.
  2. Experiment with different hairstyles, including short cuts or more masculine styles like a pompadour or quiff.
  3. Focus on building muscle through weight lifting or bodyweight exercises like push-ups and pull-ups.
  4. Pay attention to your body language and try to present yourself with confidence and assertiveness.

Tips for Looking Masculine FTM

Here are some additional tips to help you look more masculine:

  • Invest in a good binder to flatten your chest and create a more masculine silhouette.
  • Accessorize with masculine jewelry, like a watch or cufflinks.
  • Avoid wearing makeup or jewelry that is traditionally seen as feminine.
  • Experiment with different scents, like cologne or aftershave, to find one that makes you feel more masculine.

FAQs

Q: Is it necessary to have surgery to look more masculine?

A: No, surgery is not necessary to look more masculine. Many trans men choose to have top surgery or hormone therapy to help them achieve the appearance they want, but these are not required.

Q: Can I wear makeup and still look masculine?

A: Yes, you can wear makeup and still look masculine. However, it's important to avoid anything too feminine, like bright lipstick or heavy eye makeup. Stick to more natural-looking products, like a tinted moisturizer or brow gel.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and masculine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo designed for men.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Try using a hair styling product, like gel or pomade, to create a more masculine look.
  • Visit a barber regularly to keep your hair looking sharp.

By following these tips and advice, you can start to look more masculine FTM and feel more confident in your own skin. Remember, the most important thing is to be true to yourself and express who you are.
