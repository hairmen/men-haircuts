How To Shorten A Bridesmaid Dress Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

As a bridesmaid, you want to look your best in a dress that complements the bride and the wedding theme. However, sometimes the dress may be too long for you, especially if you are petite. Don't worry; you can always shorten the dress to fit you perfectly. In this article, we will show you how to shorten a bridesmaid dress and make it look like it was made for you.

How to Shorten a Bridesmaid Dress: Step by Step Guide

Before you start, there are some things you need to prepare:

  • Scissors
  • Seam ripper
  • Measuring tape
  • Sewing machine or needle and thread
  • Iron and ironing board

Step 1: Measure the length of the dress

Put on the dress and measure how much you need to shorten it. Use the measuring tape to measure from the waistline to where you want the dress to end. Make sure to add an extra inch for hemming.

Step 2: Remove the hem

Using the seam ripper, carefully remove the stitches from the hemline. Be careful not to cut the fabric or damage the dress.

Step 3: Cut the excess fabric

Using the scissors, cut the excess fabric from the bottom of the dress. Make sure to cut it evenly and straight.

Step 4: Hem the dress

Using the iron, press the hemline to create a clean and even fold. Then, using the sewing machine or needle and thread, sew the hemline in place. Make sure to sew it securely and evenly.

Step 5: Finish the seams

Using the sewing machine or needle and thread, finish the raw edges of the fabric to prevent fraying. You can use a zigzag stitch or an overlock stitch to finish the seams.

Step 6: Iron the dress

Using the iron, press the dress to remove any wrinkles or creases. Make sure to iron it on the right setting to avoid damaging the fabric.

Tips for Shortening a Bridesmaid Dress

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shortening a bridesmaid dress:

  • Always measure twice before cutting.
  • Use sharp scissors to cut the fabric evenly.
  • Iron the fabric before hemming to create a clean and even fold.
  • Use a matching thread to sew the hemline to make it less noticeable.
  • Try on the dress after hemming to make sure it fits perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I shorten a bridesmaid dress without sewing?

Yes, you can use fabric tape or hemming tape to shorten a bridesmaid dress without sewing. However, this method may not be as secure and long-lasting as sewing.

How much does it cost to shorten a bridesmaid dress?

The cost of shortening a bridesmaid dress depends on the length of the dress, the fabric, and the tailor's rate. On average, it can cost anywhere from $20 to $50.

Can I shorten a bridesmaid dress myself?

Yes, you can shorten a bridesmaid dress yourself if you have the right tools and skills. Follow the steps outlined in this article, and you should be able to do it successfully.

Hair Care for Bridesmaids

Aside from shortening your dress, you also want to make sure your hair looks great on the wedding day. Here are some hair care tips for bridesmaids:

  • Start preparing your hair weeks before the wedding by getting regular trims and conditioning treatments.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the dress and the wedding theme.
  • Use heat protectant products before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Use a hairspray or hair gel to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Bring a touch-up kit with you, including bobby pins, hair ties, and a small bottle of hairspray.

By following these tips, you can look your best on the wedding day and feel confident in your bridesmaid dress.
