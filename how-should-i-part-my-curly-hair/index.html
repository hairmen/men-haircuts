How Should I Part My Curly Hair

Written by Petter Nov 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but parting it correctly can make a huge difference. In this article, we will discuss how to part your curly hair to achieve the best results. We will also provide tips and hair care advice to keep your curls looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Part Your Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Parting Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Part Your Curly Hair

Before we dive into how to part your curly hair, it’s important to understand that there is no one “right” way to do it. The best part for you will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. With that said, there are a few general guidelines that can help you find the perfect part.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can play a role in determining the best part for you. Here are a few guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, a side part can help elongate your face.
  • If you have a square or rectangular face, a middle part can soften your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any part.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also influence the best part for you. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • If you have fine hair, a middle part can help create the illusion of more volume.
  • If you have thick hair, a side part can help balance out the volume.
  • If you have curly hair, a side part can help your curls fall more naturally.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have a general idea of what to consider when choosing a part, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to create a part down the middle of your head.
  3. Take a look in the mirror and see how it looks. If you’re not happy with it, try moving the part slightly to the left or right.
  4. Once you’ve found the perfect spot, use a comb to make the part more precise.
  5. If you’re going for a side part, use the arch of your eyebrow as a guide.
  6. Use a comb to create a diagonal part from the arch of your eyebrow to the crown of your head.
  7. Again, take a look in the mirror and adjust as needed.

Tips for Parting Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Experiment with different parts to find what works best for you.
  • Don’t be afraid to switch things up – a new part can give your hair a whole new look.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and keep them looking their best.
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.

FAQ

What if I can’t decide on a part?

Try starting with a middle part and then switching to a side part after a few days. This can give you a chance to experiment and see what you like best.

Can I part my hair when it’s wet?

Yes, you can part your hair when it’s wet. However, keep in mind that your hair may dry differently than it looks when wet, so be prepared to make adjustments once your hair is dry.

How often should I change my part?

There’s no set rule for how often you should change your part. Some people like to switch things up every few weeks, while others prefer to stick with the same part for years. Ultimately, it’s up to you and what you feel most comfortable with.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Parting your hair is just one piece of the puzzle when it comes to managing curly hair. Here are a few additional tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry – this can cause frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to part your curly hair with confidence and keep your curls looking their best.
