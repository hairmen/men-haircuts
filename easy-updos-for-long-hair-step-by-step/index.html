Easy Updos For Long Hair Step By Step

Written by Robby Jun 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful when left open, it can be a hassle to manage on a daily basis. This is where easy updos for long hair step by step come in handy. With just a few simple steps, you can transform your long locks into a stunning updo that is perfect for any occasion.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to:

The first step to creating an easy updo for long hair is to ensure that your hair is clean and free of tangles. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and then apply a leave-in conditioner to keep it smooth and manageable.

Next, decide on the type of updo you want. Do you want a messy bun, a sleek chignon, or a braided updo? Once you have decided, gather the necessary tools and accessories. You will need bobby pins, hair ties, a hairbrush, a comb, and any hair accessories you wish to use.

Step by Step:

1. Messy Bun

Step 1: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 2: Twist your hair around the hair tie to create a bun.

Step 3: Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 4: Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

2. Sleek Chignon

Step 1: Brush your hair back and gather it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step 2: Twist your hair to create a rope-like shape.

Step 3: Loop the twisted hair around the hair tie to create a chignon.

Step 4: Secure the chignon with bobby pins.

3. Braided Updo

Step 1: Part your hair in the middle and create two braids on either side of your head.

Step 2: Cross the braids over the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.

Step 3: Gather the remaining hair into a low ponytail.

Step 4: Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Tips:

1. Use hairspray to keep your updo in place.

2. Add a hair accessory to dress up your updo.

3. Don't be afraid to experiment with different types of updos to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ:

Q: How long does it take to create an easy updo for long hair?

A: It usually takes around 10-15 minutes, depending on the type of updo and your hair length.

Q: Can I create an updo with short hair?

A: Yes, there are many updos that can be created with short hair. However, they may require different techniques and accessories.

Hair Care:

It is important to take care of your hair to ensure that it remains healthy and beautiful. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and trimming. You should also use heat protectant products when using heat styling tools and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

In conclusion, easy updos for long hair step by step are a great way to add variety to your hairstyle without too much effort. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stunning updo that is perfect for any occasion.
