Short Curly Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you searching for a new hairstyle that will suit your short curly locks and glasses? Look no further as we have gathered the best short curly hairstyles for over 60 with glasses. These hairstyles are not only stylish but also easy to maintain.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and less voluminous. Short curly hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 as they add volume and texture to the hair. Glasses are also an essential accessory for many women over 60, and choosing a hairstyle that complements them is crucial.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Curly Hairstyle for Over 60 with Glasses

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short curly hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, go for a short curly bob that falls below the chin. If you have an oval-shaped face, try a layered curly hairstyle that will add volume to your hair.

Choose a Low Maintenance Hairstyle

Short curly hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 as they are low maintenance. Choose a hairstyle that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

Consider Your Hair Type

Choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type. If you have thin hair, go for a layered curly hairstyle that will add volume to your hair. If you have thick hair, try a short curly bob that will give your hair a neat and polished look.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle. If you lead an active lifestyle, go for a short curly hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style. If you have a more formal lifestyle, try a short curly hairstyle that can be styled for formal occasions.

Step by Step Guide to Short Curly Hairstyles for Over 60 with Glasses

Here is a step by step guide to some of the best short curly hairstyles for over 60 with glasses:

Short Curly Bob

The short curly bob is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for women over 60. Here is how to achieve the look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  5. Use a curling iron to touch up any loose curls.
  6. Style your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Layered Short Curly Hairstyle

The layered short curly hairstyle adds volume and texture to your hair. Here is how to achieve the look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  5. Use a curling iron to touch up any loose curls.
  6. Style your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.
  7. Get layers cut into your hair to add volume and texture.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hairstyles for Over 60 with Glasses

Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  • Use a curling iron to touch up any loose curls.
  • Style your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQs

Can I wear glasses with short curly hairstyles?

Yes, you can wear glasses with short curly hairstyles. Choose glasses that complement your face shape and hairstyle.

Are short curly hairstyles suitable for women over 60?

Yes, short curly hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 as they add volume and texture to the hair.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hairstyles for Over 60 with Glasses

Here are some hair care tips for short curly hairstyles:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Now you have a range of short curly hairstyles to choose from that will suit your over 60s look with glasses. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. With these tips, you can achieve a stylish and low maintenance hairstyle that will make you look and feel great.
