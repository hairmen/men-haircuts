Which Cutting Is Best For Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wavy hair is versatile and beautiful if styled correctly. However, finding the right haircut for wavy hair can be a daunting task. In this article, we will discuss which cutting is best for wavy hair, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining your wavy locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Best Cutting for Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair
  • FAQ

Description

The best cutting for wavy hair is a layered cut. Layers add movement and texture to wavy hair, making it look bouncy and voluminous. The key is to find the right length and layering technique that suits your face shape and hair type.

There are different types of layering techniques, such as feathering, undercutting, and razor cutting. Your stylist can recommend the best technique for your hair texture and personal style.

How to Achieve the Best Cutting for Wavy Hair

The first step in achieving the best cutting for wavy hair is to find a stylist who specializes in wavy hair. Not all stylists are trained to cut and style wavy hair correctly. Look for a stylist who has experience with wavy hair and can recommend the best cutting technique for your hair type.

Before your appointment, gather pictures of hairstyles that you like and show them to your stylist. This will help your stylist understand the look you want and make recommendations based on your hair texture and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the best cutting for wavy hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and cut the hair in a V-shape. The longest point should be at the bottom of the hair and the shortest at the top.
  4. Continue with the side sections, cutting the hair in the same V-shape.
  5. Finish with the top section, cutting the hair straight across.
  6. Add layers to the hair by cutting diagonally. The layers should start at the chin or below, depending on your face shape.
  7. Blend the layers by cutting them at an angle.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your wavy locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's damp.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to add moisture and shine.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best length for wavy hair?

The best length for wavy hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Generally, shoulder-length hair works well for most people with wavy hair.

Can I add bangs to my wavy hair?

Yes, you can add bangs to your wavy hair. However, make sure to choose a bang style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

What is the difference between feathering and layering?

Feathering is a layering technique that creates a soft, wispy effect. The hair is cut at an angle to create a feather-like texture. Layering, on the other hand, involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create movement and texture.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful wavy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair two to three times a week to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair. Avoid using a regular towel, as it can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using too many styling products on your hair. Use a lightweight styling cream or mousse to enhance your waves.

Now that you know which cutting is best for wavy hair, you can make an informed decision on your next haircut. Remember to find a stylist who specializes in wavy hair, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
