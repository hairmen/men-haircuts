How To Style Shoulder Length Curly Hair With Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Nov 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have shoulder length curly hair with bangs, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that brings out its natural beauty. Thankfully, there are many ways to style curly hair that are both easy and elegant. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to style shoulder length curly hair with bangs, from how to wash and care for your hair to the best products and techniques for styling.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Shoulder Length Curly Hair with Bangs
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Hair Care:

Before you start styling your curly hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and hydrated:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly:

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner every other day or as needed. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair:

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Leave the conditioner in for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb:

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it’s wet. Avoid brushing your hair, as it can cause breakage and damage.

How to Style Shoulder Length Curly Hair with Bangs:

Here are some easy and elegant ways to style your shoulder length curly hair with bangs:

1. Half-Up Half-Down:

Separate the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Twist the top section and secure it with bobby pins. Let the rest of your curls fall freely.

2. Low Ponytail:

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Wrap a section of hair around the elastic band to hide it. Leave your bangs loose.

3. Messy Bun:

Gather your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a messy bun. Leave your bangs loose.

Step by Step Guide:

Here’s a step by step guide for styling your shoulder length curly hair with bangs:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair:

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Deep condition your hair once a week.

2. Detangle Your Hair:

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it’s wet. Avoid brushing your hair.

3. Apply Styling Product:

Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair while it’s still wet. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

4. Style Your Hair:

Choose one of the styling methods above or create your own. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair:

Here are some additional tips for styling your curly hair:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair:

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle. Try washing your hair every other day or as needed.

2. Use a Microfiber Towel:

Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair. Avoid using a regular towel, as it can cause frizz.

3. Use a Diffuser Attachment:

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1. How Often Should I Wash My Curly Hair?

You should wash your curly hair every other day or as needed. Avoid washing it too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Should I Brush My Curly Hair?

No, you should avoid brushing your curly hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead.

3. How Can I Reduce Frizz in My Curly Hair?

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, avoid using hot water when washing your hair, and use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair.

Styling shoulder length curly hair with bangs can be a breeze with these easy tips and techniques. Remember to take care of your hair properly and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you. Happy styling!
