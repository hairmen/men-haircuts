How To Style Guys Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for guys. On one hand, it gives you a unique look that stands out from the crowd. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. But fear not, because with the right techniques and products, you can turn your curly hair into a stylish and manageable masterpiece. In this guide, we'll show you how to style guys curly hair from start to finish.

  • How to Style Guys Curly Hair

How to Style Guys Curly Hair

The first step to styling guys curly hair is to embrace your natural texture. Trying to fight against your curls will only lead to frustration and disappointment. Instead, work with your curls to create a style that works for you. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Find the right haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to styling curly hair. Look for a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair and ask for a cut that will enhance your natural texture. A layered cut can help add volume and definition to your curls.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products is essential for styling curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners, styling creams, and gels. Avoid products that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it frizzy.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works for you. Try out different partings, lengths, and textures to see what looks best with your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of styling curly hair, let's dive into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair. Gently massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, then rinse thoroughly. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair, then comb it through with a wide-toothed comb. Rinse out the conditioner with cool water.

Step 2: Apply styling product

While your hair is still damp, apply a curl-enhancing styling cream or gel to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair and a quarter-sized amount for longer hair. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Define your curls

To define your curls, use your fingers to twist and scrunch your hair. This will help enhance your natural texture and create defined curls. If you have longer hair, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Once you've defined your curls, finish off your style with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling curly hair:

  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I use a regular shampoo on my curly hair?

A: It's best to use a shampoo that's specifically designed for curly hair, as these products are formulated to be gentle and moisturizing.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on your hair than cotton.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your guys curly hair with confidence and ease. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
