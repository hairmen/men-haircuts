How To Make Styling Cream For Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've got curly hair, you know how important it is to find the right styling products. Unfortunately, many store-bought products are filled with harmful chemicals that can damage your hair and scalp. That's why we recommend making your own styling cream at home. Not only is it cheaper and healthier, but you can also customize it to suit your specific hair needs. In this article, we'll show you how to make styling cream for curly hair in just a few easy steps.

Description

Styling cream is a versatile hair product that can help define curls, reduce frizz, and add moisture to dry hair. However, many commercial styling creams contain silicones, sulfates, and other harsh chemicals that can damage your hair over time. By making your own styling cream at home, you can avoid these harmful ingredients and create a product that is tailored to your hair type and preferences.

How to Make Styling Cream for Curly Hair

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup shea butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons jojoba oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vitamin E oil
  • 10 drops of essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt the shea butter and coconut oil in a double boiler or microwave until fully melted.
  2. Add the jojoba oil, honey, and vitamin E oil to the melted mixture, and stir to combine.
  3. If desired, add 10 drops of your favorite essential oil for fragrance.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool for a few minutes, then transfer it to a container with a lid.
  5. Let the mixture set for a few hours until it solidifies into a creamy consistency.

Tips

  • You can adjust the ingredients to suit your specific hair needs. For example, if your hair is dry, you may want to add more coconut oil or jojoba oil. If your hair is oily, you may want to use less shea butter.
  • Be sure to use raw, unrefined shea butter and coconut oil for the best results. Processed oils may not have the same benefits for your hair.
  • If you don't have a double boiler, you can make one by placing a heat-safe bowl over a pot of boiling water.
  • Store your styling cream in a cool, dry place to prevent it from melting or spoiling.

FAQ

How long does homemade styling cream last?

If stored properly in a cool, dry place, homemade styling cream can last up to six months.

Can I use this styling cream on other hair types?

While this recipe is specifically designed for curly hair, you can certainly experiment with it on other hair types. Just be aware that the ingredients may not work as well for straight or wavy hair.

Can I use this styling cream on wet or dry hair?

You can use this styling cream on either wet or dry hair, depending on your preference. For best results, apply a small amount to damp hair and scrunch your curls to define them.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair after washing to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.

By following these tips and using a homemade styling cream, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
