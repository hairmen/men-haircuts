Medium Haircut For Curly Hair And Round Face

Written by Petter Jun 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have curly hair and a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be challenging. However, with the right medium haircut, you can accentuate your best features and create a stunning look. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect medium haircut for curly hair and round face.

Description

A medium haircut for curly hair and round face is a hairstyle that falls between the chin and shoulders. It is perfect for people with naturally curly hair and a round face shape. This type of haircut helps to elongate the face and create a slimming effect. It also helps to bring out your natural curls, giving your hair a bouncy and voluminous look.

How To

The first step in getting the perfect medium haircut for curly hair and round face is to find a hairstylist who specializes in cutting curly hair. They will be able to help you determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it is still wet. This will help to prevent breakage and split ends.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Cut your hair in small sections, starting with the back. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut your hair at an angle, following the natural curve of your head.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. This will help to enhance your curls and create volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your medium haircut for curly hair and round face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage
  • Cut your hair in small sections to ensure an even cut
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair and round face?

The best haircut for curly hair and round face is a medium haircut that falls between the chin and shoulders. This helps to elongate the face and create a slimming effect.

Should I use a diffuser on my hairdryer?

Yes, using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer will help to enhance your curls and create volume.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and instructions, you can achieve the perfect medium haircut for curly hair and round face. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
