How To Shape Long Wavy Hair A Guide For Relaxed Styling

Are you tired of your long wavy hair looking messy and unshaped? Do you want to learn how to style your hair in a relaxed way that still looks put-together? Look no further! In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to shape your long wavy hair into a relaxed and stylish look.

  • How to Shape Long Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaping Long Wavy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Shape Long Wavy Hair

Shaping long wavy hair can seem daunting, but it doesn't have to be! By following a few simple steps, you can achieve a relaxed and stylish look for your hair.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Having clean, healthy hair will ensure that your styling products work properly and your hair looks its best.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a dime-sized amount of styling cream or mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, then comb through with a wide-tooth comb to detangle any knots.

Step 3: Divide Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into three sections: top, middle, and bottom. Clip the top and middle sections out of the way, leaving the bottom section loose.

Step 4: Curl the Bottom Section

Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or wand, holding for 10-15 seconds before releasing. Continue curling small sections of hair in the bottom section, working your way around your head.

Step 5: Curl the Middle Section

Release the middle section of your hair and repeat the curling process, working your way around your head. Be sure to curl the hair away from your face for a more natural look.

Step 6: Curl the Top Section

Release the top section of your hair and repeat the curling process, working your way around your head. For a more relaxed look, only curl the top section halfway up the hair shaft.

Step 7: Shake Out Your Hair

Once you have finished curling all sections of your hair, shake out your hair with your fingers to break up any tight curls and create a more relaxed look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for shaping long wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean hair
  2. Apply styling products
  3. Divide hair into sections
  4. Curl the bottom section
  5. Curl the middle section
  6. Curl the top section
  7. Shake out your hair

Tips for Shaping Long Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you shape your long wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Apply styling products evenly throughout your hair
  • Divide your hair into sections to make the curling process easier
  • Curl your hair away from your face for a more natural look
  • Shake out your hair with your fingers to create a relaxed look

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaping long wavy hair:

How often should I wash my long wavy hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Can I use a diffuser attachment on my blow dryer?

Yes, using a diffuser attachment can help enhance your natural waves and create a more relaxed look.

What styling products should I use?

Styling cream or mousse can help enhance your natural waves and keep your hair in place.

Hair Care for Long Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your long wavy hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet to prevent breakage

By following these tips and steps, you can shape your long wavy hair into a relaxed and stylish look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
