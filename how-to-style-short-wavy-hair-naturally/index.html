How To Style Short Wavy Hair Naturally

Written by Frank Oct 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to tame your short wavy hair? Do you want to embrace your natural waves without damaging your hair? If you said yes to both questions, then this blog post is for you! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style short wavy hair naturally. From easy hairstyles to hair care tips, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Short wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's low maintenance and looks effortlessly cool. On the other hand, it can be frizzy, undefined, and difficult to style. If you're looking for natural ways to style your short wavy hair, you're in luck. By using the right products, techniques, and hairstyles, you can enhance your waves and make them look their best.

How to Style Short Wavy Hair Naturally

The key to styling short wavy hair naturally is to embrace your waves and work with them, not against them. Here are some tips on how to do it:

1. Use a Curl-Enhancing Product

If you want to enhance your waves and make them look more defined, you need a curl-enhancing product. Look for a lightweight, non-sticky formula that won't weigh down your hair. Some good options include curl creams, mousses, and gels.

2. Scrunch Your Hair

Once you've applied your curl-enhancing product, scrunch your hair with your hands. This will help create more defined waves and add volume to your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it.

3. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is a great way to enhance your waves and create a beachy, bohemian look. To do it, divide your hair into two sections and braid each section loosely. Leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight, then take them out and shake your hair out. You'll have beautiful, natural-looking waves.

4. Use a Salt Spray

A salt spray can help create a tousled, beachy look that's perfect for short wavy hair. Look for a salt spray that's infused with nourishing ingredients like sea kelp or coconut oil, so it won't dry out your hair. Spray it on damp hair and scrunch your hair to create waves.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short wavy hair naturally:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Follow up with a conditioner that's designed for curly or wavy hair.

Step 2: Apply Your Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a small amount of curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. Work it through your hair from roots to ends, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to create more defined waves. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it.

Step 4: Braid Your Hair

If you want to create more defined waves, braid your hair loosely and leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight.

Step 5: Use a Salt Spray

When your hair is dry, spray a salt spray on your hair and scrunch it to create a tousled, beachy look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short wavy hair naturally:

  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make your waves look frizzy and undefined.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my short wavy hair without using any products?

A: Yes, you can style your short wavy hair without using any products. However, using a curl-enhancing product can help enhance your waves and make them look more defined.

Q: Can I straighten my short wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short wavy hair, but it's not recommended. Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make your waves look frizzy and undefined.

Q: How do I prevent my waves from looking frizzy?

A: To prevent your waves from looking frizzy, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry and use a silk pillowcase to prevent friction while you sleep.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your short wavy hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can learn how to style short wavy hair naturally and embrace your natural waves. Remember to be patient and experiment with different techniques and products until you find what works best for you. Happy styling!
