Does Short Hair Look Good On Oblong Faces

Written by Christine Oct 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair has always been a popular choice for those who want to change their look. However, not all haircuts look great on all face shapes. If you have an oblong face, you might be wondering whether short hair will suit you. In this article, we will answer the question "does short hair look good on oblong faces?" and provide tips on how to choose the right haircut for your face shape.

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and forehead. This face shape can sometimes look unbalanced, but the right haircut can create balance and make the face look more proportionate. Short haircuts can work well on oblong faces, but it depends on the specific haircut and how it complements your facial features.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Oblong Faces

The key to choosing the right haircut for an oblong face is to create width and volume around the sides of the face. Short haircuts that have volume and texture around the crown and sides can help to balance out the length of the face. Avoid haircuts that are too long or flat, as they can make the face look even longer.

Some short haircuts that can work well on oblong faces include:

  • Pixie cuts with volume at the crown
  • Bob haircuts with layers and texture
  • Cropped cuts with side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cuts with volume on one side

It's important to consult with a stylist who is experienced in working with oblong face shapes. They can help you choose a haircut that complements your facial features and works well with your hair texture and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide for Short Haircuts on Oblong Faces

If you have an oblong face and want to try a short haircut, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consult with a stylist who is experienced in working with oblong face shapes.
  2. Choose a haircut that adds volume and texture to the sides of the face.
  3. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to your haircut.
  4. If you have thin hair, consider adding highlights or lowlights to create the illusion of more volume.
  5. Style your hair with volumizing products and a round brush to create volume at the crown.
  6. If you have bangs, consider side-swept bangs that add width to the forehead.
  7. Use a texturizing spray to add texture to the sides of the hair.
  8. If you have a longer face, consider keeping some length at the sides of the hair to create width.
  9. Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and volume of your haircut.
  10. Enjoy your new short haircut!

Tips for Taking Care of Short Hair on Oblong Faces

Short hair on oblong faces requires regular maintenance to keep the shape and volume of the haircut. Here are some tips for taking care of short hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create volume at the crown.
  • Style your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and volume of your haircut.
  • If you have bangs, trim them regularly to keep them at the right length.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to maintain the shape and volume of your haircut.

FAQs

Can short hair make an oblong face look even longer?

Short haircuts can work well on oblong faces if they have volume and texture around the sides of the face. However, if the haircut is too flat or too short, it can make the face look even longer. It's important to choose a haircut that complements your facial features and adds width and volume to the sides of the face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

If you have an oblong face, you should avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as they can make the face look even longer. Avoid hairstyles with a middle part, as this can also make the face look longer. Instead, choose hairstyles that add volume and texture to the sides of the face.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have an oblong face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have an oblong face. However, consider wearing your ponytail lower on your head or to the side, as this can create the illusion of more width around the face. You can also add volume to the sides of your hair before putting it in a ponytail.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Oblong Faces

Short hair on oblong faces requires regular maintenance to keep the shape and volume of the haircut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Style your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and volume of your haircut.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to maintain the shape and volume of your haircut.

Overall, short hair can look great on oblong faces if you choose the right haircut and maintain it properly. Consult with a stylist who is experienced in working with oblong face shapes to find the perfect haircut for you.
