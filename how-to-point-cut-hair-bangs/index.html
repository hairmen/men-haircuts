How To Point Cut Hair Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having the same boring hairstyle? Do you want to add some edge to your look? One simple solution is to point cut your hair bangs. Point cutting is a technique where you angle your scissors and cut the hair at an angle instead of straight across. This creates texture and adds movement to your hairstyle. In this article, we will guide you through the process of point cutting your hair bangs.

How to Point Cut Hair Bangs

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors and a comb. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into the desired bangs. Use your comb to create a straight line where you want your bangs to start. Then, use the comb to section off the hair you want to cut.

Step 2: Angle Your Scissors

Hold your scissors at a 45-degree angle. This angle will allow you to cut the hair at an angle instead of straight across.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair at the desired length. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle. Make sure you don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you have finished cutting, comb your hair and check your work. If you need to make any adjustments, go back and cut a little more.

Tips for Point Cutting Hair Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect point cut bangs:

  • Start with small sections of hair
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Use sharp scissors
  • Hold your scissors at a 45-degree angle
  • Check your work as you go

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about point cutting hair bangs:

Can you point cut your own hair?

Yes, you can point cut your own hair. However, it's best to have someone else do it for you to avoid mistakes.

Do point cut bangs work for all hair types?

Point cut bangs work for most hair types. However, if you have curly or thick hair, you may need to go to a professional to avoid mistakes.

How often should you trim your point cut bangs?

You should trim your point cut bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care Tips

After point cutting your hair bangs, make sure you take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools
  • Don't use too much product
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style

Point cutting your hair bangs is a great way to add some edge to your look. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve the perfect point cut bangs. Just remember to take care of your hair after the cut to maintain the style.
