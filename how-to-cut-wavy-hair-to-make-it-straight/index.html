How To Cut Wavy Hair To Make It Straight

Written by William Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have wavy hair and want to make it straight, you might be looking for a way to cut your hair to achieve the desired look. In this article, we will show you how to cut wavy hair to make it straight. We will discuss everything from how to do it, step by step, tips, frequently asked questions, and how to take care of your hair afterward.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Wavy Hair to Make it Straight
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Cut Wavy Hair to Make it Straight

The first step in cutting wavy hair to make it straight is to find the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a hair straightener. The scissors should be sharp and suitable for cutting hair. The comb should be sturdy and have fine teeth. The hair straightener should be of good quality and have adjustable heat settings.

The next step is to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair and conditioning it. Once you have washed your hair, towel dry it and comb it to get rid of any tangles. Then, blow-dry your hair and straighten it using your hair straightener. This will make it easier to cut your hair and ensure that the cut is even.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut your wavy hair to make it straight:

  1. Divide your hair into sections. Start by dividing your hair into two equal sections. Then, divide each section into two more sections, creating a total of four sections. Secure each section with a hair clip.
  2. Start cutting your hair. Begin by cutting the bottom section of your hair. Hold the hair straight and cut it straight across. Continue cutting each section of your hair, moving up each section until you reach the top of your head.
  3. Check the length of your hair. Once you have finished cutting your hair, check the length of your hair to ensure that it is even. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until it is even.
  4. Style your hair. Once you have cut your hair, style it using your hair straightener. This will help to create a sleek and straight look.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting wavy hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure that you get a clean cut.
  • Start by cutting a small amount of hair and work your way up. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • When cutting your hair, make sure to hold it straight to ensure that you get an even cut.
  • Take your time when cutting your hair. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my wavy hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your wavy hair yourself. However, it is important to have the right tools and to take your time to ensure that you get an even cut.

How often should I cut my wavy hair?

You should cut your wavy hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Can cutting my hair make it straight?

No, cutting your hair will not make it straight. However, cutting your hair can help to create a more even and sleek look.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your wavy hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and bleach.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut wavy hair to make it straight, you can achieve the sleek and straight look you've always wanted. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and take care of your hair afterward. With these tips, you will be able to create a beautiful and healthy look that will turn heads.
