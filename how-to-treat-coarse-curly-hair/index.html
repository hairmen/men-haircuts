How To Treat Coarse Curly Hair Tips Steps And Faqs

Written by Aprilia Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have coarse curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to maintain and style. Coarse hair is often dry, prone to frizz, and difficult to detangle. However, with the right hair care routine and products, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. In this article, we'll share some tips, steps and FAQs on how to treat coarse curly hair. Let's get started!

Hair Care Routine

The first step to treating coarse curly hair is to establish a hair care routine that works for you. This routine should consist of shampooing, conditioning, detangling, and styling your hair. Here are the steps you can follow:

Shampooing

When shampooing your hair, choose a sulfate-free shampoo that is gentle on your curls. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Wet your hair thoroughly and apply the shampoo to your scalp. Massage your scalp gently with your fingertips, and then work the shampoo down the length of your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Conditioning

Conditioning is essential to keeping your curls hydrated and soft. Choose a conditioner that is specifically formulated for curly hair. Apply the conditioner to the length of your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly through your hair. Leave the conditioner on for 2-3 minutes, and then rinse your hair thoroughly.

Detangling

Detangling your hair can be a challenge, especially if your hair is coarse and curly. To make the process easier, use a detangling spray or conditioner. Apply the detangler to your hair, and then use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be patient and gentle, as pulling or tugging on your hair can cause breakage.

Styling

Styling your hair can be fun and creative. However, it's important to use products that are formulated for curly hair, as they will help define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply a curl defining cream or gel to your hair, and then scrunch your curls upward to enhance their shape. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair gently and evenly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you treat your coarse curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Experiment with different hair care products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about treating coarse curly hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how active you are. If you have coarse curly hair, you can wash your hair once or twice a week to prevent it from becoming too dry.

Can I use regular hair products on my curly hair?

No, it's best to use hair products that are specifically formulated for curly hair. These products will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

How can I prevent frizz?

To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, use a sulfate-free shampoo, and apply a curl defining cream or gel to your hair.

Should I use a hair mask?

Yes, a hair mask can help hydrate and nourish your curls. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and steps, you can keep your coarse curly hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and experiment with different hair care products to find the ones that work best for you. Happy styling!
