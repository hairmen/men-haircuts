What Sunglasses Suit A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of sunglasses that suit your heart-shaped face? Look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you through everything you need to know about finding the right sunglasses for your face shape. From the best styles to the top brands, we have you covered.

Description

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal when choosing sunglasses for this face shape is to balance the proportions of the face. You want to create a more defined jawline by drawing attention away from the forehead and towards the lower half of the face.

How to Choose Sunglasses for a Heart Shaped Face

When choosing sunglasses for a heart-shaped face, there are a few key features to look for:

1. Frame Shape

The best frame shapes for heart-shaped faces are cat-eye, round, aviator, and butterfly. These styles complement the face's natural shape and balance out the proportions. Avoid frames with sharp angles or heavy top rims as they can make the forehead appear larger.

2. Frame Size

Make sure to choose sunglasses that are proportional to the face size. Oversized frames can overpower a heart-shaped face and make it appear smaller. Look for frames that sit comfortably on the face and cover the eyebrows without extending too far beyond the temples.

3. Color

When it comes to color, heart-shaped faces can pull off a variety of shades. However, it's best to avoid frames with bright colors or bold patterns as they can draw unwanted attention to the forehead. Stick to neutral or classic colors like black, tortoise, or brown.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to find the perfect sunglasses for a heart-shaped face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. If you have a heart-shaped face, you will have a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin.

Step 2: Choose the Right Frame Shape

Look for frames that are cat-eye, round, aviator, or butterfly. These styles complement the face's natural shape and balance out the proportions.

Step 3: Check the Frame Size

Make sure to choose sunglasses that are proportional to the face size. Oversized frames can overpower a heart-shaped face and make it appear smaller.

Step 4: Consider the Color

Stick to neutral or classic colors like black, tortoise, or brown. Avoid frames with bright colors or bold patterns as they can draw unwanted attention to the forehead.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when shopping for sunglasses:

- Try on different styles and shapes to see what looks best on you. - Look for sunglasses with polarized lenses to reduce glare and improve visibility. - Consider the UV protection level of the lenses to protect your eyes from harmful sun rays. - Don't be afraid to invest in a quality pair of sunglasses that will last you for years to come.

FAQ

Can heart-shaped faces wear oversized sunglasses?

Heart-shaped faces can wear oversized sunglasses, but it's important to make sure they are proportional to the face size. Avoid frames that are too big and overpower the face.

What are the best brands for sunglasses for heart-shaped faces?

Some of the best brands for sunglasses for heart-shaped faces include Ray-Ban, Maui Jim, Oakley, and Gucci.

Can heart-shaped faces wear sporty sunglasses?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can wear sporty sunglasses. Look for frames that are proportional to the face size and have a flattering shape.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care for heart-shaped faces, the goal is to balance out the proportions of the face. Here are some tips:

- Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead, like bangs or heavy layers. - Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of the face, like side-swept bangs or chin-length bobs. - Consider a deep side part to draw attention away from the forehead and towards the eyes and cheekbones.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect pair of sunglasses for a heart-shaped face can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. By choosing the right frame shape, size, and color, you can balance out the proportions of your face and look your best. Keep these tips in mind and don't be afraid to try on different styles until you find the perfect pair.
