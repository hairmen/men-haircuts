What Product Can I Use To Thicken My Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 29, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’ve been looking for a way to add more volume to your hair, you may be wondering what product you can use to thicken it. There are many products on the market that claim to thicken hair, but not all of them are created equal. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the best products for thickening hair and give you tips on how to use them effectively.

Description

Thinning hair can be a frustrating issue for many people, but there are products on the market that can help. Thickening products are designed to add volume to hair and give it a fuller appearance. These products come in a variety of forms, including shampoos, conditioners, and styling products.

How to Thicken Your Hair

There are several ways to thicken your hair, and using the right products is one of them. Here are some tips on how to thicken your hair:

1. Use a thickening shampoo and conditioner

One of the easiest ways to thicken your hair is to use a thickening shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to add volume to your hair and make it appear thicker. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

2. Use a volumizing mousse or spray

If you want to add even more volume to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray. These products are designed to lift your hair at the roots and give it a fuller appearance. Apply the product to damp hair and blow dry it using a round brush for best results.

3. Try a hair thickening powder

If you have fine or thinning hair, a hair thickening powder can be a game changer. These powders are designed to cling to your hair and make it appear thicker. Simply sprinkle the powder onto your roots and gently massage it in with your fingers.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to use thickening products to achieve thicker hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a thickening shampoo

Start by washing your hair with a thickening shampoo. Massage the product into your scalp and hair, focusing on the roots. Rinse thoroughly and follow up with a thickening conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray

Next, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your damp hair. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Step 3: Use a hair thickening powder

If you want even more volume, use a hair thickening powder. Apply the powder to your roots and gently massage it in with your fingers. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Thicker Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving thicker hair:

1. Avoid heat styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling irons.

2. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This can make your hair appear thicker and fuller.

3. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can help promote hair growth and thickness. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

What is the best product for thickening hair?

There are many products on the market that claim to thicken hair, but some of the best include thickening shampoos and conditioners, volumizing mousses and sprays, and hair thickening powders.

Can thickening products damage my hair?

Thickening products are generally safe to use, but using too much product or using it too frequently can cause buildup on your scalp and hair. This can make your hair appear greasy and flat.

How often should I use thickening products?

It’s best to follow the instructions on the product packaging, but in general, you can use thickening products every time you wash your hair. Just be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Once you’ve achieved thicker hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, thick hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Excessive sun exposure can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when you’re out in the sun.

3. Avoid over-processing your hair

Over-processing your hair can cause damage and make it appear thinner. Avoid excessive coloring, perming, or relaxing.

Thickening your hair can be a great way to boost your confidence and achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. With the right products and care, you can achieve thicker, healthier hair in no time!
