Short Haircuts For Thick Frizzy Hair And Round Faces

Written by Petter Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for those who have thick, frizzy hair and round faces. We understand that finding the perfect haircut can be a frustrating and time-consuming process, especially when you have specific hair types and face shapes to consider. That's why we have compiled a list of short haircuts that will not only flatter your face but also make your hair more manageable. Let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Thick, frizzy hair can be difficult to manage, especially when you have a round face. The wrong haircut can make your hair look even more voluminous and unmanageable. However, the right short haircut can help you achieve a more put-together look while also making your hair more manageable. The key is finding a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

How to

The first step to finding the perfect short haircut for your thick, frizzy hair and round face is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine which haircut will flatter your face shape and hair type the most. Additionally, you can research different haircuts and bring pictures to your hairstylist to give them a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step by Step

The following are the best short haircuts for those who have thick, frizzy hair and round faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short haircut that is perfect for those who have thick, frizzy hair and round faces. It is a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and maintain. It also helps to elongate the face, making it appear less round.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another short haircut that is perfect for those who have thick, frizzy hair and round faces. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in several ways. The bob cut also helps to frame the face, making it appear less round.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for those who have thick, frizzy hair and round faces. Layers help to add dimension and movement to the hair, making it appear less voluminous. This haircut also helps to elongate the face, making it appear less round.

Tips

The following are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for thick, frizzy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to help tame frizz and add shine to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my hair appear even more voluminous?

A: No, the right short haircut can actually help to make your hair more manageable and less voluminous. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which haircut will flatter your face shape and hair type the most.

Q: Can I still style my hair with a short haircut?

A: Yes, short haircuts can still be styled in several ways. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which styling techniques will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining a healthy and manageable hair. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

In conclusion, finding the perfect short haircut for your thick, frizzy hair and round face can be a daunting task. However, with the right haircut and proper hair care, you can achieve a more put-together and manageable look. Don't be afraid to consult with a hairstylist and try out different haircuts until you find the one that works best for you.
