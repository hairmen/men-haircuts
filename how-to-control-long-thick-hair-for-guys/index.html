How To Control Long Thick Hair For Guys

Written by Petter Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long, thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for guys. While it can give you a cool, rockstar look, it can also be a nightmare to maintain and style. If you're struggling to control your long, thick hair, don't worry! In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to manage your mane and look your best.

How to Control Long Thick Hair for Guys

Controlling long, thick hair starts with a good haircut. Make sure you go to a stylist who understands your hair type and can give you a cut that will work with your natural texture. Once you have the right cut, follow these steps to keep your hair under control:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair regularly is important for keeping it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and avoid over-washing, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Choosing the right hair products can make a big difference when it comes to controlling your long, thick hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as a smoothing serum or a texturizing cream. Avoid heavy products like pomades, which can weigh your hair down and make it greasy.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Drying your hair properly is key to controlling it. Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair, and use a round brush to smooth out any kinks or tangles. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, which can cause frizz and breakage.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Use a comb or brush to create the style you want, and use a light hold hairspray to keep it in place. Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to controlling your long, thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  2. Use a towel to gently pat your hair dry.
  3. Apply a smoothing serum or texturizing cream to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting, using a round brush to smooth out any kinks or tangles.
  5. Style your hair using a comb or brush, and finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for controlling your long, thick hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, which can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styles and products until you find what works for you.

FAQs

Q: What's the best way to dry my long, thick hair?

A: The best way to dry your hair is to use a blow dryer on a low heat setting, using a round brush to smooth out any kinks or tangles. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, which can cause frizz and breakage.

Q: Should I use a pomade to style my long, thick hair?

A: Pomades can be heavy and greasy, and can weigh your hair down. Instead, try using a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Hair Care for Long, Thick Hair

Long, thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your long, thick hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your long, thick hair under control and look your best. With a little effort and the right products, you can rock the long hair look like a pro.
