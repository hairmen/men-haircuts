How To Style Long Bangs Away From Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long bangs, you know that keeping them out of your face can be a challenge. Whether you're working out, running errands, or just want a change in your hairstyle, styling your long bangs away from your face can be a refreshing and practical solution. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Long bangs can be a great addition to any hairstyle, but sometimes they can get in the way. Whether you have a sensitive forehead or just want to change up your look, styling long bangs away from your face can give you a new, fresh style.

How to Style Long Bangs Away from Face

Styling long bangs away from your face can be done in a few simple steps. With the right tools and products, you can achieve a variety of styles that will suit your needs.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to styling your long bangs away from your face is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that will give your hair volume and texture. This will make it easier to style your hair and keep it in place.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you've washed your hair, blow dry it using a round brush. This will give your hair volume and make it easier to style. Blow dry your bangs away from your face, using the brush to pull them in the opposite direction of your forehead.

Step 3: Use Hair Products

After you've blow dried your hair, you can use hair products to keep it in place. Use a hairspray or hair gel to keep your hair away from your face. You can also use bobby pins to pin your hair back.

Step 4: Try a Headband

If you want a quick and easy solution to styling your long bangs away from your face, try a headband. Choose a headband that complements your outfit and use it to push your bangs back. You can also use a scarf or bandana to achieve the same look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your long bangs away from your face:

  • Use hair products that will keep your hair in place, such as hairspray or hair gel.
  • Blow dry your hair away from your face to give it volume and texture.
  • Try a headband or scarf to push your bangs back.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long bangs away from your face:

How do I keep my bangs out of my face when I'm working out?

Use a headband or bobby pins to keep your bangs out of your face when you're working out. You can also try a ponytail or bun to keep your hair off your face.

Can I style my long bangs away from my face without using hair products?

Yes, you can use a headband or scarf to push your bangs back without using hair products. However, using hair products can help keep your hair in place and make it easier to style.

What are some hairstyles that will keep my bangs away from my face?

You can try a high ponytail, bun, or braid to keep your bangs away from your face. You can also use hair accessories such as headbands, scarves, or bobby pins to push your bangs back.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Be gentle when brushing your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on your bangs. When styling your hair, use products that will nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

Conclusion

Styling long bangs away from your face can be a great way to change up your look and keep your hair out of your face. By following these simple steps and using the right products, you can achieve a variety of styles that will suit your needs. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you.
