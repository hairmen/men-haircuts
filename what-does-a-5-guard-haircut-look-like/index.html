What Does A 5 Guard Haircut Look Like A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you planning to get a haircut and wondering what a 5 guard haircut looks like? If so, you have come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the 5 guard haircut, from its description to how to get it and hair care tips. So, let's get started!

Description

The 5 guard haircut is a type of buzz cut that leaves hair 5/8 inch or roughly 16mm long. It is a popular choice among men who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. The 5 guard haircut is also versatile and can be styled in various ways. It works well for those with thinning hair, as the short length helps create an illusion of thickness.

How to Get a 5 Guard Haircut

Getting a 5 guard haircut is relatively easy, and you can do it at home with the right tools. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Gather the Tools

You'll need a clipper with a 5 guard attachment, a comb, and scissors. If you don't have clippers at home, you can buy them at a local store or online. Make sure to choose a good quality clipper to ensure a smooth and even cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will help you achieve a more accurate and precise cut.

Step 3: Attach the 5 Guard

Attach the 5 guard to your clipper and make sure it is securely in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the sides and work your way up to the top. Use the clipper in an upward motion, going against the direction of your hair growth. Make sure to keep the clipper flat against your head to avoid uneven cuts. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair and blend it with the sides.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you've finished cutting your hair, clean up any loose hairs around your neck and ears. Take a shower to wash off any remaining hair and style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect 5 guard haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut
  • Don't rush the process and take your time to ensure an even cut
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself, visit a professional stylist

FAQ

Is a 5 guard haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a 5 guard haircut works well for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get a 5 guard haircut?

A 5 guard haircut is low-maintenance, and you can go up to six weeks between haircuts. However, if you want to maintain a more polished look, you can get a haircut every four weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a healthy and good-looking hair. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

  • Shampoo your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and manageable
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and UV rays by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

That's everything you need to know about the 5 guard haircut. It's a simple and easy-to-maintain haircut that works well for all hair types. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve a perfect 5 guard haircut.
