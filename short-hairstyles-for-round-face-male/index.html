Short Hairstyles For Round Face Male

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a male with a round face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape? Look no further! This article will provide you with all the information you need to know about short hairstyles for round face male.

Description

A round face is characterized by its soft curves and a lack of angular features. The width and length of the face are almost equal, making it important to find a hairstyle that adds some dimension and shape to the face. Short hairstyles can work wonders for a round face male, as they help to create the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face male, it's important to take into consideration the texture of your hair and the overall vibe you are going for. Here are some short hairstyles that are perfect for a round face male:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for any face shape, including a round face. It's low maintenance, stylish, and can be easily customized to suit your personal style. This hairstyle involves shaving the hair close to the scalp using clippers. You can choose to keep the hair the same length all around, or add some texture by leaving the top slightly longer.

The High and Tight

The high and tight is a military-inspired hairstyle that is perfect for a round face male. This haircut involves shaving the sides and back of the head, while leaving the hair on top slightly longer. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back or spiked up.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a versatile hairstyle that works well for a round face male. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top slightly longer. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including messy or slicked back.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve the perfect short hairstyle for a round face male:

  1. Choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  3. Have your hair cut to the desired length using scissors or clippers.
  4. Style your hair using a styling product, such as wax or pomade.
  5. Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face male:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds some height and volume to the top of the head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face male?

The best short hairstyle for a round face male is one that adds height and volume to the top of the head. The buzz cut, high and tight, and crew cut are all great options.

Can a round face male pull off a short hairstyle?

Absolutely! Short hairstyles can work wonders for a round face male, as they help to create the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain my short hairstyle?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your short hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to style your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Now that you have all the information you need about short hairstyles for round face male, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look!
