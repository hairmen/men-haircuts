Description

Short haircuts have always been popular among men. They are low-maintenance, easy to style, and can look great on all hair types. In 2021, we saw some new and exciting short haircuts for men that are worth trying. From buzz cuts to textured crops, there was something for everyone. In this article, we will discuss the best men's short haircuts of 2021 and how to style them.

How to

Before getting into the different short haircuts, it's important to know how to style them. Here are some general tips:

  • Use a good quality styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Always start with clean and dry hair.
  • If using a hair dryer, use the low heat setting.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Step by step

Here are the steps to style some of the best men's short haircuts of 2021:

Buzz Cut

  1. Start with a clipper with a #1 or #2 guard.
  2. Trim your hair evenly all over your head.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to keep your hair in place.

Textured Crop

  1. Start with a #2 or #3 guard on your clipper.
  2. Trim the top of your hair to about 2-3 inches in length.
  3. Use scissors to add texture to the ends of your hair.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling product to add volume and texture.

Side Part

  1. Start with a #2 or #3 guard on your clipper.
  2. Leave the top of your hair longer, about 3-4 inches in length.
  3. Part your hair to one side using a comb.
  4. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling product to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice on which short haircut would suit you best.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that suits your hair type and style.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a short haircut.
  • Be confident and rock your new haircut!

FAQ

What are the best short haircuts for men?

Some of the best short haircuts for men in 2021 include the buzz cut, textured crop, side part, and French crop.

What face shapes suit short haircuts?

Short haircuts can suit all face shapes, but some styles may be more flattering than others. For example, a buzz cut can look great on a square face, while a textured crop can flatter a round face.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

It's recommended to get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to keep your short haircut looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Consider using a hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

By following these tips and trying out some of the best men's short haircuts of 2021, you can have a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle.
