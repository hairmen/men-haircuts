What Makeup Suits My Skin Tone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Oct 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we all know, makeup is an essential part of many people's daily routine. However, choosing the right makeup for your skin tone can be challenging. With so many different products and shades available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we will be discussing how to determine your skin tone and what makeup suits it best. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Makeup for Cool Undertones
  • Makeup for Warm Undertones
  • Makeup for Neutral Undertones

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining what makeup suits your skin tone is to figure out your undertone. Your undertone is the underlying hue of your skin, and it can be cool, warm, or neutral. Here are a few ways to determine your undertone:

  • Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they look green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones.
  • Try on jewelry. If you look better in silver, you have cool undertones. If gold looks better, you have warm undertones. If both look good, you may have neutral undertones.
  • Consider how your skin reacts to the sun. If you burn easily and don't tan, you have cool undertones. If you tan easily, you have warm undertones. If you burn then tan, you may have neutral undertones.

Once you have determined your undertone, you can start looking for makeup that complements it.

Makeup for Cool Undertones

If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blueish hue. To complement your skin tone, look for makeup with blue or pink undertones. Here are some makeup products that work well for cool undertones:

  • Foundation: Look for shades with cool undertones, such as "porcelain" or "ivory."
  • Blush: Choose shades of pink or berry.
  • Lipstick: Look for shades of pink, berry, or blue-based reds.
  • Eyeshadow: Choose cool colors such as blues, purples, and grays.

Makeup for Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or golden hue. To complement your skin tone, look for makeup with warm undertones, such as peach or golden colors. Here are some makeup products that work well for warm undertones:

  • Foundation: Look for shades with warm undertones, such as "beige" or "honey."
  • Blush: Choose shades of peach or coral.
  • Lipstick: Look for shades of peach, coral, or warm reds.
  • Eyeshadow: Choose warm colors such as golds, bronzes, and browns.

Makeup for Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a balance of pink and yellow hues. To complement your skin tone, look for makeup with neutral undertones, such as beige or nude colors. Here are some makeup products that work well for neutral undertones:

  • Foundation: Look for shades labeled "neutral" or "natural."
  • Blush: Choose shades of peachy-pink or nude.
  • Lipstick: Look for shades of nude, pink, or soft reds.
  • Eyeshadow: Choose neutral colors such as taupes, browns, and grays.

Tips for Choosing Makeup

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing makeup for your skin tone:

  • Always test makeup in natural light to ensure it complements your skin tone.
  • Consider your eye color when choosing eyeshadow shades.
  • When choosing lipstick shades, consider your skin tone as well as your teeth color. Some shades can make your teeth appear more yellow.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and products to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about choosing makeup for different skin tones:

What makeup suits fair skin?

For fair skin, look for makeup with cool undertones such as pink or blue. Shades of pink or berry for blush and lipstick work well, and cool colors such as blues and purples for eyeshadow.

What makeup suits medium skin?

For medium skin, look for makeup with warm undertones such as peach or golden colors. Shades of peach or coral for blush and lipstick work well, and warm colors such as golds and bronzes for eyeshadow.

What makeup suits dark skin?

For dark skin, look for makeup with rich, deep colors. Shades of deep red, burgundy, or plum for blush and lipstick work well, and colors such as deep blues and purples for eyeshadow.

Hair Care Tips for Different Skin Tones

Just as makeup can complement your skin tone, so can hair color. Here are some hair care tips for different skin tones:

What hair color suits fair skin?

For fair skin, look for hair colors with cool undertones such as ash blonde or platinum blonde.

What hair color suits medium skin?

For medium skin, look for hair colors with warm undertones such as caramel or honey highlights.

What hair color suits dark skin?

For dark skin, look for hair colors with rich, deep hues such as chocolate brown or burgundy.

Now that you know how to determine your skin tone and what makeup suits it best, you can confidently choose products that will complement your natural beauty. Remember to always test makeup in natural light and don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and products until you find what works best for you.
