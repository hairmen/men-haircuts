Best Hairstyles For Narrow Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, one size doesn't fit all. Different face shapes require different hairstyles to make the most of their features. If you have a narrow face, you might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape. But don't worry! We've got you covered. In this article, we'll share the best hairstyles for narrow faces that will complement your features and make you look stunning.

Description

A narrow face is characterized by a longer length than width, with a pointed chin and high cheekbones. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a narrow face is to create an illusion of width and balance the face. You can achieve this by adding volume to the sides of your face and avoiding styles that make your face appear longer.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Narrow Face

The right hairstyle can make all the difference in how your face looks. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for a narrow face:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as curls, waves, layers, and bobs.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear longer, such as straight and long hair.
  • Choose hairstyles that have a side-swept fringe or bangs to create the illusion of width.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Narrow Face

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a narrow face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides of your face.
  3. If you have long hair, consider getting a layered haircut to add volume and movement to your hair.
  4. If you have short hair, consider getting a bob with side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.
  5. If you have medium-length hair, consider getting loose curls or waves to add volume to the sides of your face.
  6. Finish off your hairstyle with a volumizing hairspray or mousse to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your narrow face, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz and damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for narrow faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a narrow face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear longer, such as straight and long hair, center parts, and updos.

What hairstyles are best for a narrow face?

The best hairstyles for a narrow face are those that add volume to the sides of your face, such as curls, waves, layers, and bobs.

Can I wear a ponytail if I have a narrow face?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail if you have a narrow face. However, make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the length.

Hair Care Tips for Narrow Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage and dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a narrow face can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
