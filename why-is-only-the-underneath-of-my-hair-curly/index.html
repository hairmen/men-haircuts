Why Is Only The Underneath Of My Hair Curly

Written by Eveline Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who has naturally straight hair, but only the underneath part is curly, you may be wondering why. It's a common concern among women and men with straight hair, and it can be frustrating when you're trying to achieve a uniform look. In this article, we'll explore why this happens and what you can do about it.

Description

Many people with straight hair have experienced having the underneath part of their hair curl, while the top remains straight. This can be due to a variety of reasons, and it's important to understand the cause to find the best solution for you.

How To

The first step in dealing with curly hair underneath straight hair is to identify the cause. Some possible reasons include genetics, hormonal changes, or damage from heat styling or chemical treatments. Once you have identified the cause, you can take steps to address it and achieve a more uniform look.

Step by Step

1. Identify the cause of the curly hair underneath your straight hair.

2. Address the root cause by making changes to your hair care routine, such as avoiding heat styling or using products that promote hair health.

3. Consider getting a hair cut that will help to even out the curl, such as a layered cut or a shorter style.

4. Experiment with different hair styling techniques, such as braids or twists, to help blend the curls with the rest of your hair.

5. Consider using hair extensions or wigs to achieve a more uniform look.

Tips

There are several tips you can follow to help manage curly hair underneath straight hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid damaging it.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help tame frizz and keep your hair looking smooth.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten the curly hair underneath my straight hair?

A: Yes, you can use a flat iron or other heat styling tool to straighten the curly hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant product to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Will cutting my hair help to even out the curl?

A: Yes, getting a haircut can help to even out the curl and achieve a more uniform look. Consider a layered cut or a shorter style to help blend the curls with the rest of your hair.

Q: Can I use hair extensions or wigs to achieve a more uniform look?

A: Yes, hair extensions or wigs can be a great option for achieving a more uniform look. Just be sure to choose extensions or wigs that are made from high-quality hair and are a good match for your natural hair color and texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for achieving a uniform look when dealing with curly hair underneath straight hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help tame frizz and keep your hair looking smooth.

In conclusion, having curly hair underneath straight hair is a common concern, but there are many ways to manage it and achieve a more uniform look. By following these tips and taking steps to address the root cause of the curl, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that looks its best.
