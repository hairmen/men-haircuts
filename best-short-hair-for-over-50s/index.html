Best Short Hair For Over 50S A Guide For A Youthful And Stylish Look

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle that will make you look and feel younger? Short hair can be a great option for women over 50, as it is easy to maintain and can help to create a more youthful and stylish look. In this article, we will share with you some of the best short haircuts for over 50s, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Description

As we age, our hair starts to lose its elasticity and becomes thinner, which can make it difficult to style. However, with the right haircut and hair care routine, you can achieve a youthful and stylish look. Short haircuts are a great option for women over 50, as they can help to create volume and texture, which can make your hair look thicker and fuller.

How to

Step 1: Find the Right Haircut

The first step in achieving a great short haircut is to find the right style that suits your face shape and hair type. Some of the best short haircuts for over 50s include the pixie cut, bob, and layered haircuts. The pixie cut is a short and low-maintenance haircut that can create a youthful and edgy look. The bob is a classic and versatile haircut that can be tailored to suit your face shape and hair type. Layered haircuts can help to create volume and texture, which can make your hair look thicker and more youthful.

Step 2: Choose the Right Color

The right hair color can also help to create a more youthful and stylish look. As we age, our hair starts to lose its natural color, and can become dull and lifeless. Choosing the right hair color can help to brighten up your complexion and make your hair look healthier and more vibrant. Some of the best hair colors for over 50s include blonde, caramel, and copper highlights, which can help to create a more youthful and vibrant look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for over 50s:

Q: Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Short hair can be tailored to suit all face shapes, but it is important to choose the right style that flatters your features.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can actually make you look younger and more stylish, as long as you choose the right style and color that suits your face shape and skin tone.

Q: How often should I visit my hairstylist?

A: It is recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray if you need to use hot tools.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for women over 50 who want to achieve a youthful and stylish look. By choosing the right haircut and hair color, as well as following a proper hair care routine, you can keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful.
