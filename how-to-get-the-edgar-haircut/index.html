How To Get The Edgar Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, the Edgar haircut might be just what you need. This haircut is characterized by short, choppy layers and a tapered neckline, giving it a unique and edgy look. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get the Edgar haircut, including tips and hair care advice to keep your new style looking great.

Description of the Edgar Haircut

The Edgar haircut is a popular style for men that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This haircut is characterized by short, choppy layers that are cut in a way that gives the hair a lot of texture and movement. The layers are typically cut to different lengths, giving the style a messy and edgy look. The neckline is tapered, which means that the hair is cut shorter at the back and gradually gets longer towards the top of the head. This gives the haircut a clean and polished look while still maintaining its edgy vibe.

How to Get the Edgar Haircut

Getting the Edgar haircut is a fairly simple process. However, it's important to go to a skilled and experienced barber or stylist who knows how to create this specific style. Here are the steps you can expect when getting the Edgar haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The Edgar haircut can be customized to suit your individual preferences and face shape. You can choose to have the layers cut shorter or longer, depending on the look you're going for. Talk to your barber or stylist about your preferences and they can help you determine the best length for your hair.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before getting the Edgar haircut, it's important to wash your hair and make sure it's clean and free of any styling products. This will make it easier for your barber or stylist to cut your hair and ensure that they get the style just right.

Step 3: Cut the Layers

The next step is to cut the layers. Your barber or stylist will use scissors to cut the hair into short, choppy layers. They will likely cut the layers at different lengths to give your hair that textured look.

Step 4: Taper the Neckline

Once the layers are cut, your barber or stylist will taper the neckline. This means that they will cut the hair shorter at the back of your head and gradually make it longer towards the top. This gives the haircut a polished and clean look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, your barber or stylist will style your hair using products like wax, pomade, or gel. They will likely use their fingers to create a messy and textured look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Edgar Haircut

To keep your Edgar haircut looking great, there are a few tips you should follow:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to keep the haircut looking fresh and clean.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shape is best suited for the Edgar haircut?

The Edgar haircut can work well with a variety of face shapes, but it tends to look best on oval or square-shaped faces.

How often should I get my Edgar haircut trimmed?

It's recommended to get your Edgar haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Can the Edgar haircut work for women?

While the Edgar haircut is traditionally a men's haircut, it can be adapted for women who want a short and edgy look.

What products should I use to style my Edgar haircut?

You can use a variety of products to style your Edgar haircut, including wax, pomade, or gel. Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for the Edgar Haircut

The Edgar haircut is a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep. However, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to keep the haircut looking fresh and clean.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your Edgar haircut looking great for weeks to come.
