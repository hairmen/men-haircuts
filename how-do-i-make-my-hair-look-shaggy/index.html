How Do I Make My Hair Look Shaggy

Written by Frank Jun 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a relaxed and effortless hairstyle, the shaggy look might be the perfect choice for you. This style is characterized by its messy and layered appearance, which can add texture and volume to your hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of achieving the shaggy look, step by step.

How To Make Your Hair Look Shaggy?

The shaggy look is all about creating texture and volume in your hair, so you'll need to start by using the right products. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, you'll need to wash it thoroughly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and grease from your hair, leaving it clean and fresh.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Texturizing Spray

To create the shaggy look, you'll need to add texture and volume to your hair. You can do this by applying a small amount of mousse or texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. This will help to give your hair more body and hold.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Next, use a hair dryer to blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to tousle your hair as you go. This will help to create that messy, shaggy look that you're after.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Straightener

If you want to add more texture to your hair, you can use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or curls. Simply wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron or straightener, leaving the ends straight for that shaggy look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To hold your shaggy hairstyle in place, you can finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair looking tousled and textured throughout the day.

Tips for Making Your Hair Look Shaggy

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect shaggy look:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't overdo it with the styling products - a little goes a long way.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and layers to find the best shaggy look for you.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to create even more texture.

FAQs About Making Your Hair Look Shaggy

Q. Will the shaggy look work on all hair types?

A. Yes, the shaggy look can work on all hair types, although it may require some tweaking to get it just right for your particular hair texture and length.

Q. Do I need to use heat styling tools?

A. No, you don't need to use heat styling tools to achieve the shaggy look. You can create texture and volume using just your fingers and the right styling products.

Q. How often should I wash my hair to maintain the shaggy look?

A. It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain the shaggy look. This will help to keep your hair clean and fresh, without stripping away too much of the natural oils that give your hair its texture and volume.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining the Shaggy Look

To keep your hair looking shaggy and stylish, it's important to take good care of your locks. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your locks and make them look dull and lifeless.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair without causing damage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the shaggy look that you've always wanted. Whether you have long or short hair, thick or fine, the shaggy look is a versatile and timeless hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. So why not give it a try today?
