Man Bun Curly Hair Tutorial How To Master The Look

Written by William Jun 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to elevate your style and rock a trendy, yet practical hairstyle, the man bun is the perfect option. And if you have curly hair, don't worry, you can still achieve the look. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to create the perfect man bun for curly hair in just a few easy steps.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to the Perfect Man Bun

How to Create a Man Bun with Curly Hair

The secret to a great man bun is to make sure your hair is long enough to pull back. For curly hair, this means waiting until your curls are at least 6 inches long. Once you have the length, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. This will ensure your curls are defined and easy to manage. After washing, towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help control frizz and define your curls. A leave-in conditioner or curl cream works well for this step. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Using a brush, gently brush your hair back, away from your face. This will help to smooth out any bumps or tangles in your hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

Gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure the band is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 5: Create Your Bun

Take the hair you've gathered in the elastic band and twist it into a bun shape. Wrap the twisted hair around the elastic band and secure it in place with another elastic band or bobby pins.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Perfect Man Bun

For a more detailed guide on creating the perfect man bun with curly hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. This will ensure your curls are defined and easy to manage. After washing, towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help control frizz and define your curls. A leave-in conditioner or curl cream works well for this step. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your hair back, away from your face. This will help to smooth out any bumps or tangles in your hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

Gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure the band is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 5: Twist Your Hair

Take the hair you've gathered in the elastic band and twist it into a bun shape. Twist it as tight or as loose as you prefer.

Step 6: Secure Your Bun

Wrap the twisted hair around the elastic band and secure it in place with another elastic band or bobby pins. Tuck any loose ends under the bun and adjust as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Your Man Bun

Now that you've achieved the perfect man bun, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to help:

  • Use a hair tie without metal to avoid damaging your hair
  • Don't pull your hair too tight when creating your bun
  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my hair is too short for a man bun?

If your hair isn't long enough for a full man bun, you can try a half bun. This involves pulling back only the top half of your hair and securing it with an elastic band.

Can I wear a man bun to work?

Man buns are generally accepted in most workplaces. However, if you're unsure, it's always best to check with your HR department or supervisor.

Do I need to use specific products for curly hair?

While you don't necessarily need to use products designed specifically for curly hair, they can help to enhance and define your curls. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and aloe vera.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition your hair every time you wash it
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this tutorial, you'll be able to create the perfect man bun with your curly hair. So go ahead, give it a try and see how it can elevate your style.
