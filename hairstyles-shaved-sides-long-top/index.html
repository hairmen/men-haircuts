Hairstyles Shaved Sides Long Top - A Trendy Look for 2023

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle, then the shaved sides long top is a great option. This hairstyle has been around for a while and is still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get this look, provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Shaved Sides Long Top Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get the Shaved Sides Long Top Look

The shaved sides long top hairstyle is a popular look for men and women. It's a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways. To get this look, you'll need to have the sides of your head shaved and leave the top longer. The length of the top can vary depending on your preference, but it's typically longer than the sides.

What You'll Need

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Styling product

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to get the shaved sides long top look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair.
  3. Use the clippers to shave the sides of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.
  4. Use the scissors to trim the top of your hair to the desired length.
  5. Style your hair using a comb and styling product. You can slick it back, create a messy look or something in between.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have the shaved sides long top look, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Get regular trims to keep the top of your hair looking neat.
  • Use a styling product that works for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a design or pattern shaved into the sides for a unique look.

FAQs

Is the shaved sides long top hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for any face shape. It's all about finding the right length and style for your features.

Do I need to have thick hair to pull off this look?

No, you don't need to have thick hair to pull off this look. However, if you have thin hair, you may need to use a volumizing product to add some body to the top of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
