Good Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone with a heart-shaped face looking for the perfect haircut to complement your features? Look no further! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best haircuts for heart-shaped faces, as well as tips on how to make the most of your unique face shape.

Table of Contents:

  • Description of Heart Shaped Faces
  • How to Determine If You Have a Heart Shaped Face
  • Good Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Tips for Styling Heart Shaped Face Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Description of Heart Shaped Faces

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and temples, a narrow jawline and chin, and a pointed chin. People with heart shaped faces often have high cheekbones and a defined jawline, which makes them a great canvas for a variety of hairstyles.

How to Determine If You Have a Heart Shaped Face

If you’re not sure whether you have a heart shaped face or not, there are a few characteristics to look out for. Take a look in the mirror and see if you have:

  • A wide forehead and temples
  • A narrow jawline and chin
  • A pointed chin
  • High cheekbones

If you have all of these features, then you most likely have a heart shaped face.

Good Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you know what a heart shaped face looks like, let’s talk about the best haircuts to complement your features. Here are some options:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for heart shaped faces because they add volume to the lower half of your face, balancing out your features. You can wear your hair straight or curly with this cut.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for heart shaped faces. They help to soften the forehead and make the face appear more symmetrical.

Bob

A chin-length or shoulder-length bob is a great option for heart shaped faces because it helps to balance out the wide forehead and narrow chin. You can wear your bob straight or wavy.

Pixie Cut

If you’re feeling daring, a pixie cut can look great on a heart shaped face. It helps to accentuate the cheekbones and draw attention away from the forehead.

Tips for Styling Heart Shaped Face Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Don’t add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead look even wider.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead look even more prominent.
  • Try to add volume to the lower half of your face with curls or waves.
  • Consider wearing your hair in a side part to balance out your features.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for heart shaped faces:

  • Can heart shaped faces pull off short haircuts? Yes, heart shaped faces can look great with short haircuts like pixie cuts or bobs.
  • What hairstyles should heart shaped faces avoid? Heart shaped faces should avoid adding too much volume to the top of their head, as this can make their forehead look even wider.
  • Can heart shaped faces wear blunt bangs? It’s best for heart shaped faces to avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the forehead look even more prominent.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a heart shaped face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the lower half of your face.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your strands and make your hair look less healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and haircut options, you’re sure to find a look that complements your heart shaped face perfectly. Happy styling!
