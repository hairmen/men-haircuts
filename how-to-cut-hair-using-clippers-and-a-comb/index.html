How To Cut Hair Using Clippers And A Comb A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? Do you want to learn how to cut hair using clippers and a comb? Look no further! In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to Get Started

Before you begin, ensure that you have the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, scissors, and a spray bottle. Additionally, make sure that the person whose hair you are cutting is seated comfortably and that their hair is clean and dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have all the tools, let's get started!

Step 1: Decide on the length of the haircut.

Use the numbered guide combs that come with your clippers to determine the length of the haircut. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be.

Step 2: Section the hair.

Use the comb to section the hair. Start by parting the hair down the middle from the front to the back. Then, section the hair from the top of the head to the bottom of the ears.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using the clippers, start cutting the hair. Begin with the sides and work your way up to the top of the head. Use the numbered guide combs to achieve an even cut.

Step 4: Blend the hair.

Use the clippers without a guide comb to blend the hair on the sides and back of the head. Use short, upward strokes to achieve a natural-looking blend.

Step 5: Cut the top of the head.

Using scissors, cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 6: Blend the top.

Using the scissors, blend the hair on the top of the head with the hair on the sides and back. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to achieve a natural-looking blend.

Step 7: Cut the bangs.

If the person you are cutting has bangs, use the scissors to cut them to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it straight across.

Step 8: Finish the haircut.

Use the clippers to clean up the neckline and around the ears. Use a comb to ensure that all the hair is even.

Tips for Cutting Hair Using Clippers and a Comb

Here are some tips to make your haircutting experience easier:

  • Start with a longer guide comb and work your way down to a shorter length if needed.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it in small, even sections.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair if it is too dry to cut.
  • Always cut hair when it is dry to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take your time and cut small sections at a time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair using clippers and a comb:

Can I cut my hair using clippers and a comb by myself?

Yes, you can. However, it may be easier to have someone else cut your hair for you.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your hair to be. Generally, it is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a healthy length.

Can I use clippers to cut long hair?

Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to use scissors to cut long hair to achieve a more natural look.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
