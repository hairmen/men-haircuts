If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both stylish and versatile, look no further than the mens haircut long on top and back short on sides. This popular trend has been gaining traction in recent years, offering a modern and edgy look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll take a closer look at everything you need to know about this popular men's hairstyle, from how to get it to tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

The mens haircut long on top and back short on sides is a popular hairstyle that involves keeping the hair on top of the head longer in length, while the sides and back are trimmed short. This asymmetrical cut is often styled with a side part or a slicked-back look, offering a modern and edgy style that's perfect for any occasion. This style is also versatile, allowing men to style their hair in a variety of ways depending on their mood or the event.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Long on Top and Back Short on Sides

To get the mens haircut long on top and back short on sides, you'll want to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style. The first step is to determine how long you want your hair on top to be, as well as how short you want the sides and back to be trimmed. Your stylist can help you determine the right length based on your facial structure, hair texture, and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens haircut long on top and back short on sides:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or buildup.
  2. Sit in your barber or hairstylist's chair and explain the style you're looking for.
  3. Your stylist will begin by cutting the sides and back of your hair short using a clipper.
  4. Next, your stylist will use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length.
  5. Once the hair has been trimmed, your stylist will use a comb and scissors to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  6. Finally, your stylist will style your hair using a product like pomade or wax to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Long on Top and Back Short on Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut long on top and back short on sides:

  • Visit your stylist regularly to keep the length and shape of your hair looking sharp.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as a side part or a slicked-back look, to find the style that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut long on top and back short on sides:

Is the mens haircut long on top and back short on sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work with a variety of hair types, from straight to curly, thick to thin.

How often should I get my mens haircut long on top and back short on sides trimmed?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Long on Top and Back Short on Sides

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens haircut long on top and back short on sides looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

By following these tips and maintaining your mens haircut long on top and back short on sides, you can enjoy a stylish and modern look that's perfect for any occasion.
