How To Fix A Balding Frontal Wig

Written by Daniel Jun 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've been wearing a wig for a while, you may have noticed that the hairline is starting to thin out or bald. This can happen due to wear and tear or just natural shedding. However, don't worry! There are ways to fix a balding frontal wig and make it look good as new. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you out.

Description

A balding frontal wig is a wig that has lost hair from the front hairline. This can be due to natural shedding or wear and tear. It can make the wig look unnatural and unattractive. However, you can fix it with some simple steps and make it look as good as new.

How to Fix a Balding Frontal Wig

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

Before you start fixing the balding frontal wig, you need to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb and start from the ends, working your way up. This will prevent any further damage to the hair.

Step 2: Apply Glue

Apply glue to the balding areas of the wig. Use a small brush to apply the glue evenly. Make sure you apply enough glue to cover the balding areas.

Step 3: Add Hair

Take a small bundle of hair and place it on the glue. Make sure it covers the balding areas. Continue adding hair until you cover all the balding areas.

Step 4: Dry the Glue

Wait for the glue to dry completely. This may take a few hours or overnight. Make sure the wig is completely dry before wearing it.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Once the glue is dry, you can style the wig as you normally would. Use a wig brush to style the hair and make it look natural. You can also use heat tools like a curling iron or flat iron to add more volume and style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix a balding frontal wig:

  • Use a good-quality wig glue for best results.
  • Make sure the glue is completely dry before wearing the wig.
  • Detangle the wig before fixing the balding areas.
  • Style the wig after the glue is completely dry.

FAQ

Is it possible to fix a balding frontal wig?

Yes, it is possible to fix a balding frontal wig with some simple steps.

What glue should I use to fix a balding frontal wig?

You should use a good-quality wig glue to fix a balding frontal wig.

Can I style the wig after fixing the balding areas?

Yes, you can style the wig after fixing the balding areas. Use a wig brush or heat tools to style the hair.

Hair Care

To prevent your wig from balding, you need to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Store the wig in a cool, dry place when not in use.
  • Wash the wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Detangle the wig gently with a wide-tooth comb.
  • Avoid using heat tools on the wig too often.

By following these tips, you can keep your wig looking beautiful and prevent it from balding.
