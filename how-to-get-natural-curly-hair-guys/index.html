How To Get Natural Curly Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Whether you were born with curly hair or you're trying to achieve a natural curly hairstyle, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get natural curly hair guys. From the basics of curly hair care to styling tips, we've got you covered.

Hair Care

Before we get into how to get natural curly hair guys, let's talk about the basics of curly hair care. Caring for curly hair is different than caring for straight hair. Curly hair tends to be more prone to dryness and frizz, so it's important to keep it moisturized.

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Curly hair tends to be dry, so you don't want to use a harsh shampoo that will strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's sulfate-free and contains moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter.

2. Condition regularly

Conditioning is essential for curly hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage curly hair and cause it to become frizzy. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a diffuser to minimize damage.

4. Don't brush your hair when it's dry

Curly hair is prone to frizz, and brushing it when it's dry can make it even frizzier. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

5. Sleep on a satin pillowcase

Satin pillowcases are gentle on curly hair and can help prevent frizz and breakage.

How To Get Natural Curly Hair Guys

If you weren't born with natural curly hair, don't worry. There are several things you can do to achieve a natural curly hairstyle.

1. Get a perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that can give you permanent curls. If you're considering getting a perm, make sure you go to a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. A bad perm can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.

2. Use curl-enhancing products

There are several products on the market that can enhance your natural curls. Look for products that contain ingredients like aloe vera, shea butter, or argan oil.

3. Use a diffuser

A diffuser is an attachment that you can put on your hair dryer. It helps to distribute the heat evenly and minimize frizz. To use a diffuser, start by applying a styling product to your hair. Then, use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

4. Try the plopping method

The plopping method is a technique for drying curly hair that can help enhance your natural curls. To plop your hair, start by applying a styling product to your hair. Then, use a cotton T-shirt to wrap your hair, scrunching it as you go. Leave the T-shirt on your hair for at least 20 minutes, then remove it and let your hair air dry.

Step by Step Guide

If you want to get natural curly hair guys, follow these steps:

1. Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Pat your hair dry with a towel, but don't rub it, as this can cause frizz.

2. Apply curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. Work it through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

3. Use a diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

4. Finish with a hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Natural Curly Hair

Now that you know how to get natural curly hair guys, it's important to know how to maintain it. Follow these tips to keep your curls looking their best:

1. Don't wash your hair every day

Curly hair tends to be dry, so you don't want to wash it too often. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

2. Sleep with your hair in a pineapple

A pineapple is a loose ponytail that sits at the top of your head. It can help preserve your curls while you sleep. To do a pineapple, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie.

3. Use a silk or satin pillowcase

Silk and satin pillowcases are gentle on curly hair and can help prevent frizz and breakage.

4. Avoid touching your hair too much

The more you touch your hair, the more likely it is to become frizzy. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day.

FAQs

Q: Can I get natural curly hair if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can get natural curly hair even if you have straight hair. You can get a perm or use curl-enhancing products to achieve curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: You should wash your curly hair every other day or every few days. Curly hair tends to be dry, so you don't want to wash it too often.

Q: Can I use a regular hair dryer on curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a regular hair dryer on curly hair, but it's best to use a diffuser attachment to minimize frizz.

Now that you know how to get natural curly hair guys, it's time to start experimenting with different styles. Remember to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
