Can You Get A Curly Hair Perm Everything You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia Sep 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and dreaming of bouncy curls? If so, you might be considering a curly hair perm. But can you really get a perm if your hair is already relaxed? In this blog post, we'll answer that question and more. Keep reading to find out everything you need to know about getting a curly hair perm.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Curly Hair Perm
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Perm
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After a Perm

Description

A curly hair perm is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair to create curls or waves that last for several months. The process involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that make your hair straight. Once the bonds are broken down, your hair is wrapped around perm rods and a neutralizing solution is applied to reform the bonds in a curly or wavy pattern. The result is a head full of bouncy curls or waves that can last for several months.

How to Get a Curly Hair Perm

The first step in getting a curly hair perm is finding a reputable salon that specializes in perms. Look for a stylist with experience in perming hair and who uses high-quality products. Once you've found a stylist, schedule a consultation. During the consultation, you can discuss your hair type, the type of curls you want, and any concerns or questions you may have.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a curly hair perm:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and wrap each section around a perm rod.
  3. Apply the perm solution to each rod, making sure to saturate each section of hair.
  4. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and let the perm solution process for the recommended time.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove all the perm solution.
  6. Apply the neutralizing solution to your hair to reform the bonds in a curly or wavy pattern.
  7. Let the neutralizing solution process for the recommended time.
  8. Remove the perm rods and rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  9. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair to restore moisture.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Successful Perm

Here are some tips to help you get a successful curly hair perm:

  • Don't wash your hair for 48 hours before getting a perm.
  • Avoid using any hair products before getting a perm.
  • Make sure your hair is in good condition before getting a perm. If your hair is damaged, the perm solution can cause further damage.
  • Follow your stylist's instructions for aftercare to ensure your curls last as long as possible.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a curly hair perm:

Can you get a perm if your hair is relaxed?

Yes, you can get a perm if your hair is relaxed. However, it's important to wait at least six weeks after getting a relaxer before getting a perm to avoid damaging your hair.

How long does a curly hair perm last?

A curly hair perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how well you take care of your hair and how fast your hair grows.

Can you color your hair before or after a perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. You should also wait at least two weeks after coloring your hair before getting a perm.

Hair Care After a Perm

After getting a curly hair perm, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure your curls last as long as possible. Here are some tips for hair care after a perm:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as it can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

Now that you know everything there is to know about getting a curly hair perm, it's time to make your dream of bouncy curls a reality. Remember to choose a reputable salon and follow your stylist's instructions for aftercare to ensure your curls last as long as possible.
