How To Cut Long Bangs On A Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wigs are a great way to switch up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. However, sometimes you may want to customize your wig to suit your unique style. One way to do this is by cutting long bangs on your wig. In this article, we'll take you through the steps to cut long bangs on a wig, as well as provide some tips and answers to frequently asked questions to guide you along the way.

  • How to Cut Long Bangs on a Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips

How to Cut Long Bangs on a Wig

Before we get started, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a comb, a pair of sharp scissors, and a spray bottle filled with water. Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Put the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help you see the wig's front.
  2. Using the comb, section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. This will help you to avoid accidentally cutting too much hair.
  3. Begin by cutting the hair slightly longer than you want it to be. You can always go back and trim more if necessary.
  4. Using the comb, lift the hair up and out from the head so that you can see the natural fall of the hair.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and gently pull it down to the length you want it to be.
  6. Trim the hair in a straight line, being careful not to cut too much at once.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 until you have cut all of the hair you want to be your bangs.
  8. Once you have cut the bangs to the desired length, use the scissors to add texture to the ends by cutting into them at a slight angle. This will give the bangs a more natural, wispy look.
  9. Use the spray bottle to lightly mist the hair and style it as desired.

Tips

Cutting long bangs on a wig can be tricky, so here are a few tips to help you along the way:

  • Start with a longer length than you think you want. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting a wig can be time-consuming, but rushing through it can result in a botched cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your wig, consider taking it to a professional stylist who has experience working with wigs.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long bangs on a wig:

Q: Can I cut bangs on a synthetic wig?
A: Yes, you can cut bangs on a synthetic wig. However, be aware that synthetic hair cannot be heat styled, so you won't be able to use a curling iron or straightener to style your bangs. Q: How do I blend my bangs with the rest of the wig?
A: Use the comb to blend the bangs in with the rest of the wig by gently backcombing the hair at the roots. This will help to create a seamless transition between the bangs and the rest of the wig.

Hair Care

After cutting your wig's long bangs, it's important to take care of the hair to ensure it stays looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly using a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on synthetic wigs, as the hair cannot withstand high temperatures.
  • Store your wig on a mannequin head or wig stand when not in use to help it maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight, as this can cause the color to fade.

Now that you know how to cut long bangs on a wig, you can customize your wig to suit your unique style. Remember to take your time, be patient, and use the right tools for the job. Happy cutting!
