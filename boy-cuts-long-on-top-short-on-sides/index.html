Boy Cuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Trendy Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Petter Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for boys, then the boy cut long on top short on sides is an excellent option to consider. This haircut features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides, creating a sharp and edgy look that is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about boy cuts long on top short on sides, including how to get the haircut, styling tips, and hair care.

What is a Boy Cut Long on Top Short on Sides?

A boy cut long on top short on sides is a popular hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides. This haircut is often referred to as an undercut, as the hair on the sides is usually cut very short, almost to the scalp. The longer hair on the top can be styled in a variety of ways, making this haircut versatile and perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Boy Cut Long on Top Short on Sides?

If you're interested in getting a boy cut long on top short on sides, then the first step is to find a reputable and experienced hairstylist. You can bring a picture of the haircut to your stylist to ensure that they understand exactly what you're looking for. The stylist will then use clippers to cut the hair on the sides, while leaving the hair on top longer. The length of the hair on top can be tailored to your preferences, and the stylist can also add texture and layers to create a more dynamic look.

Styling Tips for Boy Cuts Long on Top Short on Sides

One of the great things about boy cuts long on top short on sides is that they are very versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are a few styling tips to help you get the most out of your haircut:

  • For a sleek and stylish look, you can use a pomade or hair wax to slick back the hair on top.
  • If you want a more casual and laid-back look, you can leave the hair on top messy and tousled.
  • To add volume and texture, you can blow-dry the hair on top using a round brush.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Boy Cut Long on Top Short on Sides

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a boy cut long on top short on sides:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product to the hair on top. Pomade or hair wax works well for this step.
  3. Using a comb, slick back the hair on top, starting from the front and working your way to the back.
  4. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to blow-dry the hair on top, creating volume and texture.
  5. Finally, use a bit of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Boy Cut Long on Top Short on Sides

To keep your boy cut long on top short on sides looking great, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep the hair on the sides short and neat.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and to keep your haircut looking sharp.

FAQs

Q. What face shape is best suited for a boy cut long on top short on sides?

A. This haircut works well on a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond.

Q. How often should I get a trim for my boy cut long on top short on sides?

A. It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair on the sides short and neat.

Q. Can I style my boy cut long on top short on sides differently for different occasions?

A. Yes, this haircut is very versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions.

The Bottom Line

Boy cuts long on top short on sides are a trendy and stylish haircut that is perfect for boys of all ages. Whether you want a sleek and sophisticated look or a more casual and laid-back style, this haircut is versatile and easy to maintain. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can keep your boy cut long on top short on sides looking great and enjoy a trendy and fashionable haircut that is sure to turn heads.
