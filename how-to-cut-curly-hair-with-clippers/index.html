How To Cut Curly Hair With Clippers A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to cutting it. But with the right tools and technique, it's possible to give yourself a great haircut at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting curly hair with clippers.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Curly Hair with Clippers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair with Clippers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Cut Curly Hair with Clippers

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here are some essential things you'll need:

  • A pair of hair clippers with guard attachments
  • A comb or brush
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • A towel or cape to catch hair

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to give yourself a great haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet or damp, it can be difficult to get an even cut.
  2. Decide on the length you want to cut your hair. Use the guard attachments on your clippers to achieve the desired length.
  3. Begin by cutting the sides and back of your head. Use the longest guard attachment and work your way down to shorter attachments until you reach the desired length.
  4. Use your comb or brush to lift the hair on the top of your head. Cut the hair with the clippers, using the guard attachment that matches the length you want.
  5. Blend the sides and back with the top. Use a shorter guard attachment on the clippers to blend the hair between the top and sides.
  6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to add texture to your hair.
  7. Check your haircut in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair with Clippers:

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down. It's easier to take more hair off than it is to put it back on.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. Curly hair tends to bounce up when it dries, so it will look shorter than it actually is.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head. It can be difficult to see what you're doing, but it's important to make sure the back looks even.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make sure you're cutting your hair evenly.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair with clippers:

  • Can I use clippers on wet hair? It's not recommended, as wet hair can be difficult to cut evenly.
  • What guard attachment should I use for curly hair? It depends on the length you want. Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down.
  • Can I cut my hair myself? Yes, but it can be difficult to cut the back of your head. Consider asking a friend or family member for help.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair:

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair looking great:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to give yourself a great haircut with clippers. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
