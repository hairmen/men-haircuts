How To Get Spiral Curls Without A Perm A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly damaging your hair with perms just to achieve those perfect spiral curls? Fear not, because there are ways to achieve those bouncy, voluminous curls without the use of harmful chemicals. In this article, we will be discussing how to get spiral curls without a perm, along with some tips and hair care advice to ensure that your curls stay healthy and vibrant.

How to

There are various methods to achieve spiral curls without a perm, but we will be discussing the most effective ones below:

Method 1: Using Curling Irons

One of the easiest ways to achieve spiral curls is by using a curling iron. Start by washing and drying your hair, and then section it into small parts. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it for about 10-15 seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled, and then run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up.

Method 2: Using Flexi Rods

Another effective way to get spiral curls without a perm is by using flexi rods. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, and then section it into small parts. Take a flexi rod and wrap a small section of hair around it, rolling it up towards the roots. Once you reach the roots, bend the ends of the flexi rod to secure the hair in place. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled, and then leave the flexi rods in overnight. In the morning, remove the rods and run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up.

Now that we've discussed the methods, let's break down the steps for each:

  7. Leave the flexi rods in overnight
  8. In the morning, remove the rods and run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiral curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after curling, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve spiral curls without any heat styling tools?

A: Yes! You can achieve spiral curls without any heat styling tools by using hair rollers or braids. Simply roll or braid your hair and leave it in overnight, then remove them in the morning to reveal your curls.

Q: Will curling my hair damage it?

A: Curling your hair can cause damage if done excessively or without proper hair care. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent damage.

Hair Care

To keep your spiral curls looking healthy and vibrant, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

With these methods and tips, you'll be able to achieve those beautiful spiral curls without any harm to your hair. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for you!
