Why Does One Side Of My Hair Curl Better Than The Other

Written by Petter Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're like many people, you may have noticed that one side of your hair curls better than the other. This can be frustrating, especially if you're trying to create a specific hairstyle or look. In this article, we'll explore some of the reasons why this might be happening and what you can do about it.

Table of Contents

  • Genetics
  • Hair Care
  • Styling Techniques
  • Tips for Better Curling
  • FAQs

Genetics

One of the most common reasons why one side of your hair curls better than the other is genetics. Hair texture, thickness, and curl pattern are all determined by your genes, which means that you may have inherited a stronger curl pattern on one side of your head than the other.

Additionally, the shape of your head can also affect how your hair curls. If one side of your head is flatter or has a different shape than the other, it can cause your hair to curl differently on each side.

Hair Care

The way you care for your hair can also play a role in how well it curls. If you're not properly moisturizing your hair or using heat styling tools too often, it can lead to damage and breakage, which can make it harder for your hair to hold a curl.

Another factor to consider is the length of your hair. If one side of your hair is shorter than the other, it may be more difficult to curl it evenly. This is especially true if the shorter side has layers or is cut at an angle.

Styling Techniques

The way you style your hair can also affect how well it curls. If you're using different techniques on each side of your head, it can lead to uneven curl patterns.

For example, if you're using a curling iron on one side of your head and a flat iron on the other, it can cause one side to curl better than the other. Additionally, if you're not using the same amount of heat or applying the same amount of tension to each section of hair, it can lead to uneven curls.

Tips for Better Curling

If you're struggling with uneven curls, there are a few things you can try to even them out:

  • Use the same styling tools and techniques on each side of your head
  • Ensure that your hair is properly moisturized and not damaged
  • Try using a different curling technique, such as twisting or braiding your hair instead of using a curling iron
  • Consider getting a haircut that is more even on both sides

FAQs

Can I train my hair to curl evenly?

While you can't change your natural curl pattern, you can try different styling techniques and hair care methods to help even out your curls. It may take some experimentation to find what works best for your hair.

Is it normal for one side of my hair to curl better than the other?

Yes, it's perfectly normal for one side of your hair to curl better than the other. As we mentioned earlier, genetics and hair care habits can both play a role in this.

Can I use hair products to help even out my curls?

Yes, using the right hair products can help to enhance your natural curl pattern and make your curls more even. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care

Overall, there are many reasons why one side of your hair may curl better than the other. By understanding the factors that can contribute to uneven curls, you can take steps to even them out and achieve the look you want.
