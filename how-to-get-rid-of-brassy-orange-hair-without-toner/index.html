How To Get Rid Of Brassy Orange Hair Without Toner

Written by Eveline Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with brassy orange hair? It can be frustrating to dye your hair, only to find out that the result isn't what you were expecting. While toner is a popular solution for getting rid of brassy orange hair, not everyone wants to use it. So, what can you do if you want to get rid of brassy orange hair without toner? Here are some tips and tricks that can help.

Description

Brassy orange hair is a common problem that occurs when you dye your hair blonde or light brown. This is because the underlying pigment in your hair is usually red or orange, which can become more prominent when you lighten your hair. The result is hair that looks orange or brassy, which can be unflattering and difficult to style. While toner is a popular solution for getting rid of brassy hair, there are other ways to fix the problem if you don't want to use toner.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Orange Hair Without Toner

If you want to get rid of brassy orange hair without toner, here are some steps you can follow:

Step 1: Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a great way to tone down brassy hair without using toner. It works by neutralizing the orange and yellow tones in your hair, leaving you with a cooler, more toned look. To use purple shampoo, wet your hair and apply the shampoo, making sure to massage it into your scalp and throughout your hair. Leave the shampoo on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

A hair mask can help to hydrate and nourish your hair, while also toning down brassy tones. Look for a hair mask that contains ingredients like argan oil or keratin, which can help to restore your hair's natural shine and luster. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Use Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural remedy that can help to remove brassy tones from your hair. Mix equal parts apple cider vinegar and water, and apply the mixture to your hair. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out. The acidic properties of the vinegar can help to balance the pH of your hair, leaving you with a more even and toned look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've gotten rid of your brassy orange hair, it's important to take steps to maintain your color. Here are some tips to help:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, which can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I use lemon juice to get rid of brassy hair?

Lemon juice is a natural remedy that can help to lighten your hair, but it's not recommended for getting rid of brassy tones. This is because lemon juice can cause your hair to become dry and brittle, which can make the problem worse.

How often should I use purple shampoo?

It's best to use purple shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair. Using it too often can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.

Can I dye my hair again to get rid of brassy tones?

You can dye your hair again to get rid of brassy tones, but it's important to wait at least two weeks between dyeing your hair to avoid damaging it. It's also important to choose a dye that's specifically designed to tone down brassy tones, to avoid making the problem worse.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important, especially if you've dyed it. Here are some tips to help keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling or damaging it.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, which can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of brassy orange hair without toner, and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
