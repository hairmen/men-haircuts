How To Get A Middle Hair Part Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to get a middle hair part! This hairstyle has been around for decades and is still a popular choice among women. It's a great way to switch up your look without making drastic changes to your hair. In this post, we'll take you through the steps on how to achieve a perfect middle part, tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 2: Divide your hair in the middle
  • Step 3: Use a tail comb to create a precise part

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step to getting a middle hair part is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, dry it with a towel or blow dryer. If you're using a blow dryer, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage to your hair.


Step 2: Divide your hair in the middle

Using a comb or your fingers, divide your hair into two equal sections. Make sure the part is in the center of your head, running from your forehead to the back of your head. You can use a mirror to make sure your part is straight.


Step 3: Use a tail comb to create a precise part

To create a precise middle part, use a tail comb. Start at the front of your hairline and slowly comb down to the back of your head, following the part you created in step 2. This will ensure that your part is straight and even. If you have any flyaways, use a bit of hairspray to keep them in place.


Step 4: Secure the rest of your hair

Once you've created your part, use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair. This will make it easier to style the hair on either side of the part. You can also use a hair tie to create a low ponytail or bun to keep your hair out of the way.


Step 5: Style your hair

Now that your part is in place, it's time to style your hair. You can wear your hair down, or try out some new hairstyles such as braids, buns or ponytails. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.


Tips for Maintaining Your Middle Part

Once you've achieved your perfect middle part, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Use a tail comb to touch up your part daily.
  • Apply a small amount of hairspray to keep your part in place.
  • When sleeping, try to avoid sleeping on your part to avoid messing it up.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I touch up my middle part?

You should touch up your middle part daily to ensure that it stays in place. Use a tail comb to gently comb down any flyaways.

What if I have a cowlick?

If you have a cowlick, it can be a bit more challenging to get a perfectly straight middle part. Experiment with different parting techniques until you find one that works for you.

Can I get a middle part if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Curly hair can be styled in a middle part as well. Just make sure to use a comb to create a precise part.


Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair to prevent breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

We hope this guide on how to get a middle hair part was helpful! Remember, practice makes perfect, so keep trying until you find the perfect part for you. Good luck!
