Best Hairstyles For Rectangular Faces

Written by Robby Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a rectangular face shape, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share with you the best hairstyles for rectangular faces, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Rectangular Faces
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a longer face with a strong jawline and forehead. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the features and soften the angles. The best hairstyles for rectangular faces are those that add volume on the sides and crown while keeping the length below the chin.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, here's a quick and easy way to find out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  3. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  4. Step away and look at the shape you've drawn.

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a strong jawline and forehead, then you have a rectangular face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Rectangular Faces

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to pick the perfect hairstyle. Here are some of the best hairstyles for rectangular faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers help to add volume and movement to the hair, which is perfect for balancing out a rectangular face shape. Keep the length below the chin and add layers starting from the cheekbones down.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs help to soften the angles of a rectangular face shape and draw attention away from the forehead. Keep the length below the eyebrows and angle the bangs to the side.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume at the crown is a great way to add width to a rectangular face shape. Keep the length below the chin and add volume with layers and curls.

4. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob (long bob) is a popular hairstyle that works well with rectangular face shapes. The waves help to add volume and soften the angles of the face. Keep the length above the shoulders and add layers for movement.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your hair for a rectangular face shape:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or flat, as they can emphasize the length of your face.
  • Try adding curls or waves to your hair to add volume and movement.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your hair to add volume at the crown.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear longer.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a rectangular face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too straight or flat, as they can emphasize the length of your face.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a rectangular face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a rectangular face shape. Just make sure to add volume at the crown to balance out the length of your face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect look for your rectangular face shape. Remember to embrace your unique features and have fun experimenting with different styles!
