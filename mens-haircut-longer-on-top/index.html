Mens Haircut Longer On Top How To Achieve This Trendy Look In 2023

Written by Frank Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, one that has been popular for a few years now is the mens haircut longer on top. This haircut is a versatile and modern style that allows you to experiment with different lengths and textures. In this article, we'll go over how to achieve this look, step by step, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair after the cut. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Longer on Top

  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ

Description

The mens haircut longer on top is a style where the hair on the top of the head is kept longer than the sides and back. This allows for more flexibility in styling and can be achieved with a variety of hair textures and types. The style has been popularized by celebrities and influencers alike, making it a trendy and fashionable choice for men of all ages.

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Longer on Top

The mens haircut longer on top can be achieved by following a few simple steps. First, you'll need to decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This can range from a few inches to several inches, depending on your personal preference. Once you've decided on the length, you'll need to choose a suitable haircut that will complement your face shape and hair type. A good barber or hairstylist will be able to recommend a cut that will work well for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the mens haircut longer on top:

  1. Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair.
  2. Choose a suitable haircut that will complement your face shape and hair type.
  3. Show your barber or hairstylist a picture of the style you want to achieve.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hair is cut.
  5. Style your hair with a suitable product, such as wax or pomade, to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut longer on top:

  • Keep your hair clean and well-conditioned to prevent breakage and split ends.
  • Use a suitable product to style your hair, such as wax or pomade.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styles and textures to keep your look fresh and modern.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut longer on top:

Is the mens haircut longer on top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens haircut longer on top can be achieved with a variety of hair textures and types.

How often should I visit my barber or hairstylist to maintain my haircut?

You should visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your mens haircut longer on top.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a variety of products to style your hair, such as wax, pomade, or gel. Experiment with different products to find one that works well for your hair type.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, on a daily basis.
  • Eat a well-balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals, such as chlorine and bleach.

With these tips and a little bit of maintenance, you can keep your mens haircut longer on top looking stylish and modern for years to come.
