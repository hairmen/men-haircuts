How To Use Thinning Shears To Blend Men s Hair

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are looking for a way to blend your men's hair without losing length, thinning shears can be a great tool to use. Thinning shears can help you achieve a natural, textured look that is both modern and sophisticated. In this article, we will guide you on how to use thinning shears to blend men's hair.

Description

Thinning shears are special scissors that have teeth on one blade and a straight edge on the other. They are designed to remove bulk and texture hair without cutting too much length. Thinning shears are an excellent choice for men with thick, curly, and wavy hair, as they can help to reduce the volume and create a more even look.

How to

Before using thinning shears to blend your hair, you will need to wash and dry your hair as usual. Comb your hair so that it is easy to work with, and ensure that all knots and tangles are removed.

Step by Step

Step 1: Section your hair

Using clips or hair ties, divide your hair into sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 2: Start with the crown

Begin at the crown of your head and work your way down. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and use the thinning shears to remove some of the bulk. Cut at an angle, working your way from the top down to ensure that you don't cut too much hair.

Step 3: Move to the sides

Once you have finished the crown, move to the sides of your head. Again, hold small sections of hair between your fingers and use the thinning shears to remove bulk. Keep in mind that you want to create a natural, textured look, so avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 4: Blend the back

Finally, move to the back of your head and blend the hair using the same technique. Work from the top down, cutting at an angle, and be sure to blend the hair so that there are no harsh lines.

Tips

- Use only a small amount of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much.

- Cut at an angle to create a natural, textured look.

- Avoid cutting too much hair at once to prevent unevenness.

FAQ

Q: Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

A: Thinning shears work best on thick, curly, and wavy hair. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to avoid using them.

Q: How often should I use thinning shears?

A: It depends on your hair type and length. For shorter hair, you may only need to use them every few months. For longer hair, you may need to use them more frequently.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best after using thinning shears, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals and dyes.

Using thinning shears to blend your men's hair can be an excellent way to achieve a natural, textured look. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can use thinning shears safely and effectively to create a modern and sophisticated hairstyle.
