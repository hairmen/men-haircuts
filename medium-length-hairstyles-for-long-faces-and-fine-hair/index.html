Medium Length Hairstyles For Long Faces And Fine Hair A Guide

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're blessed with a long face and fine hair, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, because we've got you covered with some great medium length hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and add some volume to your locks. In this guide, we'll cover everything from how to choose the right cut to how to style your hair for maximum impact.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing the right cut for a long face and fine hair, you want to look for styles that add volume and width to your face. Medium length hairstyles are perfect for this, as they can be styled in a variety of ways to flatter your face shape.

Some great options to consider include layered cuts, which can add movement and texture to your hair, or a bob with choppy ends, which can create the illusion of thickness. You can also opt for a side-swept fringe or bangs, which can help balance out the length of your face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right medium length hairstyle for your long face and fine hair:

  1. Determine the length that works best for you. Consider how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair, as well as your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Look for styles that add volume and width to your face. Layered cuts, choppy bobs, and side-swept fringes are all great options to consider.
  3. Consult with your stylist to find the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Styling

Once you've chosen the right cut for your long face and fine hair, it's time to think about styling. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use volumizing products to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to create curls or waves that add width to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as these can emphasize the length of your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for long faces and fine hair:

What is the best length for medium length hairstyles?

The best length for medium length hairstyles can vary depending on your face shape and hair texture. Generally, it's best to aim for a length that falls between your chin and your shoulders.

What types of bangs are best for long faces?

Side-swept bangs or fringe are the best options for long faces, as they can help balance out the length of your face.

How can I add volume to my fine hair?

There are several ways to add volume to fine hair, including using volumizing shampoos and conditioners, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush to create curls or waves.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length hairstyle:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect medium length hairstyle for your long face and fine hair. Remember to experiment with different cuts and styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
