Oval Face Shape Hairstyles For Men With Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a man with an oval face shape looking for the perfect hairstyle to complement your features? Look no further! In this guide, we will provide you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and a list of the best oval face shape hairstyles for men with short hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Oval Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Men with Short Hair

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced facial features, with a gently curved jawline and forehead. Men with oval face shapes can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but it's important to choose a style that complements your unique features.

Short hair is a popular choice for men with oval face shapes because it helps to highlight their balanced features. However, there are many different short hairstyles to choose from, and finding the right one can be overwhelming.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Oval Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle for your oval face shape, it's important to consider your hair type, texture, and personal style. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out your features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add texture and movement.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace it! A short, curly hairstyle can look great on an oval face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair, focusing on the top and front sections to add volume.
  4. Use a blow dryer on low heat to set your style in place.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your hair looking great all day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've got the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about oval face shape hairstyles for men with short hair:

Q: Can men with oval face shapes wear bangs?
A: Yes! Men with oval face shapes can pull off bangs as long as they are styled properly. Q: What is the best short hairstyle for an oval face shape?
A: The best short hairstyle for an oval face shape is one that adds volume to the top of the head, such as a textured crop or messy fringe. Q: Can men with oval face shapes wear a buzz cut?
A: Yes, but it's important to leave some length on the top of the head to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Short Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for men with short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Incorporate hair masks and oils into your hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect oval face shape hairstyle for your short hair. Remember to choose a style that complements your unique features and personality, and always take good care of your hair to keep it looking great.
