What Is A Short Layered Bob

Written by Christine Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a chic and trendy haircut that's easy to maintain, then a short layered bob might just be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle is characterized by its short length and graduated layers, which give it volume and movement.

Description

A short layered bob is a haircut that's typically chin-length or shorter and features layers throughout the hair. The layers are cut at different lengths, with the shortest layers framing the face and the longest layers at the back of the head. This creates a graduated effect that adds volume and movement to the hair.

How To

If you're interested in getting a short layered bob, here's what you can expect from the process:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, they'll likely have a consultation with you to discuss your hair type, face shape, and style preferences. This will help them determine the best way to cut your hair to achieve the look you want.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Your stylist will shampoo and condition your hair to ensure that it's clean and free of any product buildup. They may also apply a heat protectant product to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 3: Cut

Your stylist will begin cutting your hair in layers, starting at the back and working their way forward. They'll likely use scissors or a razor to create the graduated effect of the layers.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will blow dry and style it to your liking. They may use a round brush to add volume and movement to the layers, or they may use a flat iron to create a sleeker look.

Tips

If you're considering a short layered bob, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a length that flatters your face shape
  • Ask your stylist for layers that add volume and movement to your hair
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to enhance the layers
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Regular trims are essential to maintaining the shape of your short layered bob

FAQ

Here are some common questions about short layered bobs:

What face shapes are best suited for a short layered bob?

A short layered bob can flatter a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped faces. However, it may not be the best choice for those with square or angular faces.

How often should I get my short layered bob trimmed?

To maintain the shape of your short layered bob, it's recommended that you get it trimmed every 6-8 weeks.

Can I style my short layered bob in different ways?

Absolutely! A short layered bob can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and messy. Experiment with different styling products and tools to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered bob looking its best, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant product
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to avoid tangles and breakage
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

Now that you know what a short layered bob is, why not book an appointment with your stylist and try it out for yourself? With its trendy and versatile look, it's sure to be a hit!
