Short Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 50 Round Face

Written by Robby Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have a round face with thin hair, you might feel like your options for hairstyles are limited. However, there are many short hairstyles that can make you look and feel fabulous. In this article, we will provide you with a guide on how to choose the best short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 round face, along with some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50 Round Face

Description

Women over 50 often experience thinning hair due to hormonal changes. This, combined with a round face shape, can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. However, short hairstyles can be a great option for those with thin hair and a round face, as they can create the illusion of fuller hair and elongate the face.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50 Round Face

When choosing a short hairstyle, there are several factors to consider, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For round faces with thin hair, hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of the head can be particularly flattering. Some popular short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 round face include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob haircut
  • Layered haircut
  • Side-swept bangs

It is also important to consider the length and shape of the hairstyle. Shorter hairstyles tend to be more flattering for those with thin hair, as they create the illusion of fuller hair. Additionally, asymmetrical cuts can add visual interest and balance to a round face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and techniques. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products specifically designed for thin hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, starting at the crown of the head and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten any pieces that need extra smoothing.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50 Round Face

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create the illusion of fuller hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 round face:

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. Short hairstyles can actually elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

Q: What is the best way to add volume to thin hair?

A: Using a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots and blow-drying your hair with a round brush can help add volume and lift to thin hair.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face shape?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great option for those with round faces, as they can create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair Over 50 Round Face

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, taking care of your hair is essential for keeping it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for thin hair over 50 round face:

  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this can help prevent breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down thin hair and make it appear flat.

By following these tips and choosing the right short hairstyle, you can look and feel your best with thin hair over 50 round face. Remember to embrace your unique features and personal style, and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one for you.
