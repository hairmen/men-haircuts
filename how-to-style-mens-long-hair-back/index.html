How To Style Men s Long Hair Back A Guide For Relaxed Effortless Look

Written by Bardi Nov 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair is not just for women anymore. More and more men are rocking long locks, and styling it back is a classic look that never goes out of style. Whether you have wavy, curly, or straight hair, there are different ways to style your hair back that can suit your face shape and personal style. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of how to style men's long hair back for a relaxed, effortless look.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Men's Long Hair Back

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's first discuss the different ways you can style men's long hair back. Here are some popular options:

  • Slicked back - this is a classic look that involves combing your hair straight back and using a hair product to hold it in place.
  • Half-up - this is a more relaxed look that involves tying half of your hair back while leaving the rest down.
  • Ponytail - this is a simple and practical look that involves tying all of your hair back into a ponytail.
  • Bun - this is a stylish and trendy look that involves tying your hair back into a knot or bun.

Now that you know the different ways to style your hair back, let's dive into the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to style men's long hair back:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be harder to style and hold in place.
  2. Comb your hair straight back using a wide-toothed comb or your fingers. Make sure your hair is evenly distributed and there are no knots or tangles.
  3. Apply a hair product of your choice. This could be a pomade, gel, wax, or hairspray. Make sure to use a product that works well with your hair type and texture.
  4. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to get it all the way to the roots. Use more product if you need to, but don't overdo it.
  5. Style your hair back using your hands or a comb. Smooth out any bumps or kinks, and make sure your hair is evenly distributed.
  6. If you're going for a slicked back look, use a fine-toothed comb to create a defined part on one side of your head.
  7. Once you're happy with the style, let your hair dry completely. Don't touch it or mess with it while it's drying.
  8. If you need to, use a little more product to touch up any areas that need it.
  9. Finally, use a hairspray to hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways or frizz.

And that's it! With a little practice and experimentation, you'll be able to find the perfect style for your long hair.

Tips for Styling Men's Long Hair Back

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling men's long hair back:

  • Experiment with different hair products until you find one that works well with your hair type and texture.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.
  • If you have straight hair, try using a round brush and a hair dryer to add volume and texture.
  • If you're going for a slicked back look, make sure to use a comb to create a defined part on one side of your head.
  • Don't overdo it with the hair product. A little goes a long way, and using too much can make your hair look greasy or heavy.

FAQs

Can I style my long hair back if it's thinning or receding?

Yes, you can still style your hair back if it's thinning or receding. Just make sure to use a hair product that provides hold without making your hair look too heavy or greasy. You can also experiment with different styles, such as a half-up or messy bun, to find a look that works best for you.

Do I need to use a specific type of hair product?

No, you can use any type of hair product that works well with your hair type and texture. Just make sure to use a product that provides hold and doesn't make your hair look greasy or heavy.

How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Men's Long Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your men's long hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often. These can cause damage and breakage over time.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hairline.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your men's long hair back with ease and confidence. Whether you're going for a slicked back look or a messy bun, there's a style out there that will suit your personal taste and style. So go ahead and embrace your long locks, and rock that effortless, relaxed look!
