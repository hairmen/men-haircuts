How to Know What Hairstyle Fits You Best - Tips and FAQs

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch it up but don't know where to start? Choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging, but with these tips and FAQs, you can find a hairstyle that fits you best.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Once you know your face shape, you can search for hairstyles that complement it.

Face Shape and Hairstyles

Oval face: This face shape is considered the most versatile because it can pull off almost any hairstyle. You can try a pixie cut, bob, or long layers.

Round face: To elongate your face, try a hairstyle with height at the crown, such as a pompadour or a high ponytail. Avoid a bob that hits at the chin, as it will make your face appear wider.

Square face: Soften your strong jawline with a wavy or curly hairstyle. Long layers or a side-swept bang are also flattering for a square face.

Heart face: Bring attention to your eyes and cheekbones with a side-swept bang or a chin-length bob. Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top.

Diamond face: Create balance by adding width at the temples with a hairstyle that has volume at the sides. A bob or a layered cut are great options for a diamond face.

Long face: Add width to your face with a hairstyle that has volume at the sides, such as a wavy bob or layers that start at the chin. Avoid a hairstyle that is too long and straight.

Step by Step

Now that you know your face shape and which hairstyles flatter it, it's time to choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and personality. Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Consider your hair type and texture. Are you willing to style your hair every day, or do you prefer a low-maintenance look?
  2. Think about your daily routine. Do you work in a professional setting, or do you have an active lifestyle that requires a more practical hairstyle?
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles to find a look that you love.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. They can provide insight into which hairstyles will work best for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Hair grows back, and a new hairstyle can give you a fresh start.
  • Consider your hair's natural texture. It's easier to work with your hair's natural texture than to fight against it.
  • Be realistic about your styling abilities. If you're not comfortable styling your hair every day, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Think about the maintenance required. Some hairstyles require more upkeep than others, so choose one that fits your lifestyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hairstyle:

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your hairstyle loses its shape. On average, most people get a haircut every six to eight weeks.

Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can be flattering on many face shapes, but it ultimately depends on your preferences and comfort level. Consult with a hairstylist to find a short hairstyle that suits you.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

Give it time to grow on you, and try different styling techniques. If you're still not happy with it, consult with your hairstylist to make adjustments.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.


By following these tips and FAQs, you can find a hairstyle that fits you best and keep it looking its best.
