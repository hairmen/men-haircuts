What Does A Diamond Shaped Face Look Like

Written by Daniel Aug 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you curious to know what a diamond-shaped face looks like? If so, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about this unique face shape, from its defining features to the best hairstyles and makeup tips to flatter it. So, let's dive in!

Description of a Diamond Shaped Face

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, sharp cheekbones, and a pointed chin. It's a rare facial shape, making up only about 2% of the population. Celebrities with diamond-shaped faces include Rihanna, Halle Berry, and Victoria Beckham.

The key to identifying a diamond-shaped face is to look for the widest point on the face, which is usually the cheekbones. From there, the face tapers towards the forehead and chin.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure whether you have a diamond-shaped face, here's a quick and easy way to find out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a closer look at your face, paying attention to the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your forehead and chin are both narrower, you likely have a diamond-shaped face.

It's important to note that everyone's face is unique, and some people may have a combination of different face shapes. If you're still unsure about your face shape, consult with a professional stylist or makeup artist for personalized advice.

Tips for Flattering a Diamond Shaped Face

Now that you know what a diamond-shaped face looks like, you may be wondering how to flatter this unique shape. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin, such as bangs or layers. Avoid styles that add volume to the cheekbones, as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Opt for makeup looks that accentuate the eyes and lips, rather than the cheekbones. Use contouring techniques to soften the angles of the face.
  • When selecting glasses or sunglasses, look for frames that are wider than the cheekbones and have a strong brow line to balance the face.

FAQ About Diamond Shaped Faces

Q: Can diamond-shaped faces pull off short hair?

A: Yes! Diamond-shaped faces can actually look great with short hair, as long as the style adds width to the forehead and chin. Consider a pixie cut or a bob with side-swept bangs.

Q: What kind of earrings should I wear with a diamond-shaped face?

A: Earrings that are wider at the bottom than the top can help balance out a diamond-shaped face. Chandelier earrings and teardrop earrings are both great options.

Q: Are there any makeup techniques to avoid with a diamond-shaped face?

A: Yes. Avoid over-contouring the cheekbones, as this can make them appear more angular. Instead, focus on highlighting the eyes and lips.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Faces

If you have a diamond-shaped face, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained to flatter your unique features. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make your face appear wider.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your preferred style and keep split ends at bay.

By following these tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your diamond-shaped face. Remember, every face shape is unique and beautiful in its own way!
