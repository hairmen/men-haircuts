Easy 50S Updo Hairstyles For Long Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a glamorous and vintage hairstyle that can be done easily at home? The 50s updo hairstyles are the perfect choice for those who want to add a touch of elegance to their look. In this blog post, we will guide you through some easy 50s updo hairstyles for long hair that you can wear on any occasion. From classic French twists to Gibson rolls, these hairstyles are perfect for any hair type and can be achieved with minimal effort.

Description

The 50s updo hairstyles are inspired by the glamorous Hollywood era of the 1950s. These hairstyles are characterized by their voluminous and intricate designs that are perfect for long hair. They are versatile and can be worn on any occasion, from weddings to proms or even a night out with friends. With just a few simple tricks and tools, you can create these classic hairstyles at home.

How to

If you want to achieve the perfect 50s updo hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

Classic French Twist

Step 1: Brush your hair and gather it at the nape of your neck.

Step 2: Twist your hair upwards until it reaches the crown of your head.

Step 3: Secure the twist with bobby pins.

Step 4: Tuck any loose ends into the twist and secure them with bobby pins.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Gibson Roll

Step 1: Brush your hair and gather it at the nape of your neck.

Step 2: Roll your hair upwards until it reaches the crown of your head.

Step 3: Pin the roll in place with bobby pins.

Step 4: Pull out a few strands of hair from the front to create a soft look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the classic French twist:

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 50s updo hairstyle:

- Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.

- Use a hairspray with a strong hold to keep the style in place.

- Use a teasing brush to add volume to your hair before styling.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 50s updo hairstyles:

Q: Can I achieve these hairstyles with short hair?

A: These hairstyles are best suited for long hair, but you can still achieve a similar look with shorter hair by using hair extensions.

Q: Can I wear these hairstyles on a daily basis?

A: Yes, you can wear these hairstyles on a daily basis if you want to add a touch of glamour to your everyday look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.

- Avoid using hot tools such as hairdryers and straighteners too often.

- Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these easy 50s updo hairstyles for long hair, you can add a touch of vintage glamour to your look. Whether you're attending a wedding, prom or just a night out with friends, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. Follow our simple steps and tips to achieve the perfect 50s updo hairstyle at home.
