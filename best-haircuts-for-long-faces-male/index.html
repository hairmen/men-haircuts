Best Haircuts For Long Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long faces are characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a long jawline. If you have a long face, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are several hairstyles that can complement your face shape and enhance your features. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for long faces male, along with tips on how to style and maintain them.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Best Haircuts for Long Faces Male

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Long Faces

When it comes to choosing the right haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to create the illusion of width to balance out the length of your face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of your hair or by choosing a style that falls below your chin. Secondly, you want to avoid styles that accentuate your forehead or elongate your face even further. With that said, here are some of the best haircuts for long faces male:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works well for long faces. It adds volume to the sides of your hair, which helps to balance out the length of your face. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and leave it longer on top. Then, use a comb to create a deep side part and sweep your hair to one side.

2. The Quiff

The quiff is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape. It adds volume to the top of your hair, which can help to make your face appear shorter. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and leave it longer on top. Then, use a hairdryer and a brush to style your hair upwards and backwards.

3. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a low-maintenance haircut that works well for long faces. It draws attention away from your forehead and elongated jawline, creating a more balanced look. To achieve this look, ask your barber to use clippers to cut your hair short all over.

4. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a clean and simple haircut that works well for long faces. It adds volume to the top of your hair, which can help to make your face appear shorter. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and leave it slightly longer on top. Then, use a comb to style your hair upwards and backwards.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Best Haircuts for Long Faces Male

Once you have chosen the right haircut for your face shape, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling the best haircuts for long faces male:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup and leave your hair clean and fresh.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a clean towel to gently dry your hair, removing any excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply Product

Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair, depending on the style you are trying to achieve. For a natural look, use a matte finish product, and for a more polished look, use a pomade or wax.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a comb or brush to style your hair into the desired shape. For the side part, create a deep part and sweep your hair to one side. For the quiff, use a hairdryer and a brush to style your hair upwards and backwards. For the buzz cut and crew cut, use a comb to style your hair upwards and backwards.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish by applying a light mist of hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb to help prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQs on Best Haircuts for Long Faces Male

1. Can long faces wear short hair?

Yes, long faces can wear short haircuts such as the buzz cut and crew cut. These haircuts draw attention away from the length of your face and can create a more balanced look.

2. What is the best haircut for a long face and big forehead?

The best haircut for a long face and big forehead is the side part. This haircut adds volume to the sides of your hair, which can help to balance out your forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces Male

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling it properly, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for long faces male:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a long face can be a challenge, but with the right tools and knowledge, it's possible to find a style that complements your features and enhances your overall look. Whether you opt for a classic side part or a trendy quiff, remember to take care of your hair and maintain your style with regular trims and proper styling techniques.
