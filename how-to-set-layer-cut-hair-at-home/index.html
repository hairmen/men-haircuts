How To Set Layer Cut Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to add some volume and movement to your hair? A layer cut might just be what you need. But what if you can’t make it to the salon? Don’t worry, you can still achieve a layered look at home. In this guide, we’ll show you how to set layer cut hair at home in just a few steps.

How to Set Layer Cut Hair at Home

Before we get started, here’s what you’ll need:

  • A pair of hair scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Hair clips
  • A blow dryer (optional)
  • Styling products (optional)

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and let it air dry for a few minutes. If you’re in a hurry, you can use a blow dryer on low heat to speed up the drying process.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to create a middle part, then section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Clip each section to keep it in place.

Step 3: Cut the First Layer

Unclip the front section, then comb it straight down. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it at a slight angle. Repeat this process for the rest of the front section.

Step 4: Cut the Second Layer

Unclip the back section, then comb it straight down. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it at a slightly steeper angle than the first layer. Repeat this process for the rest of the back section.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Unclip the remaining sections and blend the layers together. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle that blends the two layers together. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you’ve finished cutting, check your hair for evenness. Use a mirror to make sure the layers are even on both sides.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you’ve set your layer cut, it’s time to style your hair. You can use a blow dryer and styling products to add volume and movement to your hair. Experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.

Tips for Setting Layer Cut Hair at Home

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting off too much.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors for a clean cut.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it’s important to be careful and start with small sections of hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it’s best to use a pair of hair scissors for a clean cut.

Hair Care for Layer Cut Hair

To keep your layer cut looking fresh and healthy, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layer cut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to set layer cut hair at home, you can achieve a salon-worthy look without leaving your house. Just remember to be patient and take your time, and you’ll end up with beautiful, layered locks.
