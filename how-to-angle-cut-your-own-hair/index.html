Description

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts and want to try cutting your own hair at home? One trendy and easy way to change up your look is by angle cutting your own hair. This technique involves cutting the hair at a slanted angle, creating a chic and edgy look. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to angle cut your own hair like a pro.

How to Angle Cut Your Own Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also recommended to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to angle cut your own hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair as usual. It is important to cut your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can shrink and lead to uneven cuts.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down. This will be your guide for the length of your cut.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and slide your fingers down to the desired length. Make sure to hold the hair at a slanted angle to create the angle cut.
  5. Using sharp scissors, cut the hair at the angle you are holding it. Cut slowly and carefully to ensure an even cut.
  6. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure to match the length and angle of the first side.
  7. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way towards the back of your head. Remember to hold each section at a slanted angle to maintain the angle cut.
  8. When you reach the back of your head, use a handheld mirror to check the length and angle of your cut. Make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Once you are satisfied with your cut, style your hair as desired.
  10. Clean your tools and enjoy your new look!

Tips for Angle Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you angle cut your own hair successfully:

  • Start with a small section of hair to prevent cutting too much at once.
  • Use caution when cutting near your face, as it is easy to make mistakes.
  • Use a comb to straighten out any tangles before cutting.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated, as this will help you avoid mistakes.
  • Watch tutorials or ask a friend for help if you are unsure about the process.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about angle cutting your own hair:

Q: Is it easy to angle cut your own hair?
A: With practice and patience, anyone can angle cut their own hair. Q: How often should I angle cut my hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks. Q: Can I angle cut my hair if it is curly?
A: Yes, but it may be more difficult to see the slanted angle. Consider straightening your hair before cutting to make it easier.

Hair Care Tips

After angle cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can angle cut your own hair with confidence and achieve a stylish and edgy look.
