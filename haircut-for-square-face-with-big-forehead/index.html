Haircut For Square Face With Big Forehead A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your square face with a big forehead? Look no further! We've got you covered with this comprehensive guide that includes everything from description to hair care tips.

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead with equal width. The forehead is the most significant feature of a square face, and if it's big, it can be challenging to find the right haircut that complements it. The ideal cut for a square face with a big forehead is one that softens the strong features and elongates the face.

How to

Before heading to the salon, it's essential to know your face shape and forehead size. Once you've determined that, you can choose from a variety of haircuts that suit your face shape and forehead size. A layered cut with side-swept bangs is ideal for a square face with a big forehead. The layers soften the jawline, while the bangs cover the forehead and create an illusion of a smaller forehead.

Step by Step

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in haircuts for square faces.
  2. Show them pictures of haircuts that you like and ask for their opinion.
  3. Determine the appropriate length and layers for your hair.
  4. Decide on the type of bangs you want, such as side-swept or blunt.
  5. Ask your hairstylist to create a textured look to soften your jawline.

Tips

  • Avoid haircuts that emphasize the jawline, such as blunt bobs or one-length cuts.
  • Embrace layers to add movement and soften the angles of your face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  • Avoid center parts, as they draw attention to the forehead.

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for a square face with a big forehead?

The best haircuts for a square face with a big forehead are layered cuts with side-swept bangs. These haircuts soften the jawline and cover the forehead, creating an illusion of a smaller forehead.

Can I wear my hair up if I have a square face with a big forehead?

Absolutely! You can wear your hair up if you have a square face with a big forehead. Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain your desired style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using hot tools frequently. When blow-drying your hair, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut for a square face with a big forehead can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the right style that complements your features. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and take care of your hair to maintain your desired look.
