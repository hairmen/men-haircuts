How To Find Hair Salons On Instagram A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Instagram is not just a platform for sharing photos and videos, but also a powerful tool for finding hair salons. With over 1 billion monthly active users, it's no surprise that many hair salons are using Instagram to promote their services and showcase their work. In this article, we'll show you how to find hair salons on Instagram, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Use Hashtags
  • Step 2: Search by Location
  • Step 3: Check Instagram's Explore Page
  • Step 4: Ask for Recommendations
  • Tip: Follow Your Favorite Hair Stylists
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Use Hashtags

Hashtags are a great way to find hair salons on Instagram. Simply search for hashtags like #hairsalon, #hairservices, #haircut, #hairstyle, #haircolor, #hairtreatment, and #haircare. You'll see a list of posts that include those hashtags, and many of them will be from hair salons.

Once you find a post that catches your eye, click on the salon's profile and browse their feed. This will give you an idea of their style and the services they offer. If you like what you see, you can follow them or even book an appointment directly through Instagram.

Step 2: Search by Location

If you're looking for a hair salon in your area, you can use Instagram's location search feature. Simply type in your city or neighborhood, and Instagram will show you posts from users in that area. You can also filter your search by using hashtags like #hairsalon + your city or #haircut + your neighborhood.

When you find a salon you like, check their profile and website to see if they have any deals or promotions for new customers. You can also read their reviews on Instagram or other review sites like Yelp to get an idea of their reputation.

Step 3: Check Instagram's Explore Page

Instagram's Explore page is a great way to discover new hair salons. Instagram uses an algorithm to show you posts and profiles that it thinks you'll like based on your interests and activity on the app. To access the Explore page, click on the magnifying glass icon at the bottom of your screen.

Scroll through the posts and profiles that Instagram recommends, and look for hair salons that catch your eye. You can also use the search bar at the top of the page to search for specific keywords like "hair salon" or "haircut."

Step 4: Ask for Recommendations

Finally, don't be afraid to ask for recommendations from your friends and family. Post a status update on Facebook or Instagram asking if anyone knows of a good hair salon in your area. You can also ask for recommendations from your favorite beauty bloggers or influencers on Instagram.

Chances are, someone you know has a great recommendation for you. And if they do, be sure to check out the salon's Instagram profile and website before booking your appointment.

Tip: Follow Your Favorite Hair Stylists

If you have a favorite hair stylist or salon, be sure to follow them on Instagram. Not only will you be able to see their latest work and promotions, but you'll also be the first to know about any new services or products they offer.

FAQs

Q: Can I book an appointment through Instagram?

A: Yes, many hair salons now allow you to book appointments through Instagram. Simply click on the "Book" button on their profile and follow the prompts.

Q: How do I know if a hair salon is reputable?

A: Check their reviews on Instagram or other review sites like Yelp. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or family.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found a hair salon on Instagram, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Choose hair products that are formulated for your hair type.

By following these tips and finding a great hair salon on Instagram, you'll be on your way to healthy, beautiful hair.
