Long Haircuts For Round Faces And Thin Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find a haircut that flatters your round face and thin hair? Look no further! In this guide, we will discuss the best long haircuts for round faces and thin hair, as well as provide tips and tricks for caring for your new 'do.

Table of Contents:

  • How To Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Long Haircuts for Round Faces and Thin Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Round Faces and Thin Hair

How To Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best long haircuts for round faces and thin hair, it's important to determine your face shape. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back and your face clean of any makeup.
  2. Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down each measurement.
  3. Compare the measurements. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your jawline is rounded, you likely have a round face shape.

Best Long Haircuts for Round Faces and Thin Hair

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect long haircut. Here are our top picks:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for adding volume to thin hair and elongating a round face. Ask your stylist for long, face-framing layers that start below the chin.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can break up a round face shape and give the illusion of a longer face. Ask your stylist for long, wispy bangs that start at the arch of your eyebrow and angle down to the side of your face.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can add texture and volume to thin hair, while also softening a round face shape. Ask your stylist to add long layers throughout your hair and use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut

Now that you have your perfect long haircut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to thin hair.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a quality heat protectant spray to use before styling with heat tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about long haircuts for round faces and thin hair:

Can I still have long hair if I have thin hair?

Yes! Long hair can still look great on thin hair, as long as it is properly cared for and styled.

Will long hair make my face look rounder?

No, as long as you choose the right long haircut. Long layers and side-swept bangs can actually elongate a round face shape.

Should I avoid straight hair if I have a round face?

Not necessarily. Straight hair can look great on a round face, as long as it is cut in a flattering long style with face-framing layers.

Hair Care for Round Faces and Thin Hair

Lastly, here are some general hair care tips for those with round faces and thin hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, as brushing can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a quality hair mask to use once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and long haircut options, you'll be sure to find a style that flatters your round face and thin hair. Happy styling!
