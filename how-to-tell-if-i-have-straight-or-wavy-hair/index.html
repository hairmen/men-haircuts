How To Tell If I Have Straight Or Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're not sure if you have straight or wavy hair, don't worry! It can be difficult to tell the difference between the two at times, but understanding your hair type is important for proper hair care and styling. In this article, we'll go over how to tell if you have straight or wavy hair and provide tips for caring for each hair type.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell if You Have Straight or Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Straight and Wavy Hair

How to Tell if You Have Straight or Wavy Hair

The easiest way to tell if you have straight or wavy hair is to look at it when it's wet. Take a shower or wet your hair with a spray bottle and let it air dry without brushing or combing it. Once your hair is dry, look at the texture and shape of your hair.

Wavy hair will have a slight "S" shape to it, with some waves being looser or tighter than others. Straight hair, on the other hand, will have no discernible shape or texture when wet. It will simply hang straight down without any kinks or curls.

If you're still unsure, you can also look at old photos of yourself or ask a stylist for their opinion. They'll be able to tell you definitively what your hair type is.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine your hair type:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry without brushing or combing it.
  2. After your hair is dry, look at the texture and shape of your hair.
  3. If your hair has a slight "S" shape to it, with some waves being looser or tighter than others, you have wavy hair.
  4. If your hair hangs straight down without any kinks or curls, you have straight hair.

It's important to note that some people have hair that falls somewhere in between straight and wavy. This hair type is often referred to as "straight-wavy" or "wavy-straight." If this is the case for you, you'll notice that your hair has a slight wave to it but still falls relatively straight.

Tips for Straight and Wavy Hair

If you have straight hair, you'll want to focus on keeping it shiny and smooth. Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame any frizz and protect your hair from heat damage. Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

For wavy hair, focus on enhancing your natural waves. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your waves and prevent frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your waves and add volume.

FAQs

Can I have both straight and wavy hair?

Yes! Some people have hair that falls somewhere in between straight and wavy, often referred to as "straight-wavy" or "wavy-straight."

Can my hair type change over time?

Yes, your hair type can change due to hormonal changes, aging, and other factors. It's important to regularly reassess your hair type and adjust your hair care routine accordingly.

Hair Care for Straight and Wavy Hair

Regardless of your hair type, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for straight and wavy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Limit heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when you do style your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and understanding your hair type, you'll be able to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
