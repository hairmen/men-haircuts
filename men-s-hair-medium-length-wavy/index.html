Men s Hair Medium Length Wavy A Guide To Styling And Maintenance

Written by Eveline Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with medium length wavy hair, you're in luck! This hair type is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a more polished style, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to style and care for your medium length wavy hair.

Description

Men's hair medium length wavy is hair that falls between the shoulders and the chin and has a natural wave or curl to it. This hair type can be thick or thin and can range from loose waves to tight curls. Medium length wavy hair is a popular choice for men because it's easy to style and can be worn in many different ways.

How To Style

There are many ways to style men's hair medium length wavy. Here are a few popular options:

1. Messy Textured Look

To achieve a messy textured look, start by applying a texturizing product to your damp hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Slicked Back

The slicked back look is a classic style that works well with medium length wavy hair. Start by applying a pomade or gel to your hair and comb it back. Use a blow dryer to set the style in place.

3. Side Part

The side part is a timeless style that's easy to achieve with medium length wavy hair. Start by combing your hair to one side and using a pomade or gel to hold the style in place.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling men's hair medium length wavy:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for wavy hair. This will help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. The type of product you use will depend on the look you're trying to achieve. For a messy textured look, use a texturizing product. For a slicked back look, use a pomade or gel.

3. Style

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair. Tousle it for a messy look, comb it back for a slicked back look, or comb it to one side for a side part.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you style and care for your men's hair medium length wavy:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products designed for wavy hair to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. Try a messy textured look one day and a slicked back look the next.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy. A good haircut can also help make styling your hair easier.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my medium length wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your medium length wavy hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length wavy hair?

A: It's best to wash your medium length wavy hair every two to three days to prevent it from getting too dry.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my medium length wavy hair?

A: To prevent frizz, use a leave-in conditioner and avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing it. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water with a towel or let your hair air dry.

Hair Care

To keep your men's hair medium length wavy looking healthy and hydrated, use products designed for wavy hair and avoid using products that contain alcohol. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
