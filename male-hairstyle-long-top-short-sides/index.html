Male Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut? Do you want to try something new and modern? Then look no further than the male hairstyle long top short sides. This haircut has been growing in popularity over the years and for good reason. It’s stylish, versatile and perfect for any occasion. In this ultimate guide, we’ll go over everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle.

Description

The male hairstyle long top short sides, also known as the undercut, is a hairstyle where the top of the hair is left long while the sides and back are cut short. The result is a sharp contrast between the long top and short sides, creating a bold and edgy look. This haircut can be customized to suit your personal style, whether you want a slick and polished look or a more rugged and messy appearance.

How to

If you’re interested in trying out the male hairstyle long top short sides, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Decide on the length

The first step is to decide on the length of the top of your hair. This can range from a few inches to shoulder-length, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Choose your fade

The next step is to choose your fade. The fade is the gradual transition from the short sides to the longer top. There are different types of fades such as low, mid or high fade. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which fade will suit you best.

Step 3: Cut the sides and back

Once you’ve decided on the length and fade, it’s time to cut the sides and back of your hair. This can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on your hairstylist’s technique.

Step 4: Style the top

After the sides and back are cut, it’s time to style the top. You can choose to slick it back, part it to the side or leave it messy depending on your preference. Use a styling product such as pomade, wax or gel to hold the hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when maintaining your male hairstyle long top short sides:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep the hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the look that suits you best.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent it from drying out.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the male hairstyle long top short sides:

How long does the top need to be?

The length of the top can vary depending on your preference. It can range from a few inches to shoulder-length.

What kind of styling product should I use?

The best styling product for the male hairstyle long top short sides is one that provides a strong hold such as pomade, wax or gel.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

It’s recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair with the male hairstyle long top short sides:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair hydrated and soft.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the male hairstyle long top short sides, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out this trendy and versatile haircut.
