How To Make Hair Curl Inwards Without Heat

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools to get those perfect curls? Using heat on your hair too often can lead to damage and breakage. Fortunately, there are other ways to achieve the same look without heat. In this article, we will show you how to make hair curl inwards without heat.

Description

Having curly hair is a blessing, but not everyone is born with it. However, you can create those bouncy curls right at home without using any heat. Inward curls, also known as barrel curls, are easy to achieve and look stunning. All you need is a few simple tools and the right technique to get the desired results.

How to Make Hair Curl Inwards Without Heat

Here are the steps to make hair curl inwards without heat:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip the natural oils from your hair and make it dry and brittle. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently pat it dry with a towel. Do not rub your hair vigorously as it can cause breakage.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Curl Enhancer

Apply a dollop of mousse or curl enhancer to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will give your hair some hold and make it easier to curl.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that you don't miss any strands.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around your finger, starting at the ends and rolling it towards your scalp. Pin the curl in place using a bobby pin or hair clip. Repeat this step for each section of hair until you have curled your entire head.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls. Do not touch your hair until it is completely dry.

Step 6: Remove the Clips

Remove the clips or bobby pins from your hair and gently finger-comb your curls. Do not use a brush or comb as it can cause frizz.

Step 7: Set Your Curls

Apply a small amount of hair spray to set your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make hair curl inwards without heat:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before curling.
  • Do not use too much product as it can weigh your hair down.
  • Wrap your hair tightly around your finger to create tighter curls.
  • For looser curls, use larger sections of hair.
  • Do not touch your hair too much as it can cause frizz.

FAQs

Q. Can I use this method on all hair types?

A. Yes, this method works on all hair types. However, the results may vary depending on your hair texture and length.

Q. How long will the curls last?

A. The curls can last up to 2-3 days depending on your hair type and how well you set them.

Q. Can I sleep with the curls in my hair?

A. Yes, you can sleep with the curls in your hair. However, it is recommended that you wrap your hair in a silk scarf to prevent frizz and preserve the curls.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties that can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to make hair curl inwards without heat, you can achieve those bouncy curls without damaging your hair. Follow these simple steps and get ready to rock your new look!
