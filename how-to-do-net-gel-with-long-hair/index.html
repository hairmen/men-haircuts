How To Do Net Gel With Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Net gels are an excellent way to keep your long hair in place while you're working or playing sports. But if you've never done net gel with long hair before, you might be wondering how to do it. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to do net gel with long hair, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Do Net Gel with Long Hair

Net gel with long hair can be a little tricky, but with some practice, you'll get the hang of it. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help the net gel hold better and last longer.

Step 2: Apply Gel

Next, apply a generous amount of gel to your hair. You can use any type of hair gel, but make sure it's strong enough to hold your hair in place.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair. Make sure all of your hair is coated with the gel.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, with one part on top of your head and the other part at the back. You can use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 5: Put on the Net

Take the net and put it over your head, making sure it covers all of your hair. The net should be tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it's uncomfortable.

Step 6: Secure the Net

Use hair clips to secure the net in place. Make sure the clips are evenly spaced and hold the net firmly in place.

Step 7: Apply More Gel

Apply more gel to your hair, making sure it's evenly distributed. This will help the net gel hold better and last longer.

Step 8: Wait for the Gel to Dry

Wait for the gel to dry completely before removing the net. This will take around 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how thick your hair is.

Step 9: Remove the Net

Once the gel is dry, carefully remove the net. Be gentle to avoid pulling your hair.

Step 10: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a hairbrush or comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Tips for Doing Net Gel with Long Hair

Here are some tips to make the process of doing net gel with long hair easier:

  • Use a strong hair gel to ensure that your hair stays in place
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before applying the gel
  • Section your hair into two parts to make it easier to apply the net
  • Use hair clips to hold the net in place and prevent it from slipping
  • Wait for the gel to dry completely before removing the net

FAQs About Doing Net Gel with Long Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing net gel with long hair:

Can I use any type of hair gel?

Yes, you can use any type of hair gel. However, make sure it's strong enough to hold your hair in place.

How long does the gel take to dry?

The gel will take around 30 minutes to an hour to dry completely, depending on how thick your hair is.

Can I style my hair after removing the net?

Yes, you can style your hair as desired after removing the net. Use a hairbrush or comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for taking care of long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to do net gel with long hair like a pro. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll have a sleek and stylish look that will last all day.
