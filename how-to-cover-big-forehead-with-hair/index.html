How To Cover Big Forehead With Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your big forehead? Do you wish you could find a way to cover it up with your hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cover your big forehead with hair. From simple hairstyles to tips and tricks, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Big Forehead with Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Cover Big Forehead with Hair

There are many ways to cover a big forehead with hair, but some are more effective than others. The key is to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hides your forehead at the same time. Here are some popular hairstyles that can help you achieve this:

1. Bangs

Bangs are one of the most popular ways to cover a big forehead. They can be worn in a variety of styles, including side-swept, blunt, or layered. The key is to choose the right length and style that suits your face shape.

2. Side part

A side part can also help to cover a big forehead. By sweeping your hair to one side, you can create the illusion of a smaller forehead. This works best with long hair.

3. Updos

Updos are a great way to cover a big forehead, especially if you have long hair. A high bun, for example, can draw attention away from your forehead and towards your face. Other updos, such as braids or twists, can also work well.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what kind of hairstyles can help you cover your big forehead, let’s take a look at how to achieve them:

1. Bangs

Step 1: Decide on the type of bangs you want. Do you want them blunt, layered, or side-swept?

Step 2: Cut your bangs to the desired length. If you’re not confident in cutting your own hair, visit a professional stylist.

Step 3: Style your bangs. You can blow-dry them straight or use a curling iron to add some waves.

2. Side part

Step 1: Decide on which side you want to part your hair.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair to one side.

Step 3: Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

3. Updos

Step 1: Decide on the type of updo you want. Do you want a high bun, braids, or twists?

Step 2: Gather your hair into a ponytail.

Step 3: Twist or braid your hair and secure it with bobby pins.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you cover your big forehead with hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a hair powder or dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Use a light hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

FAQ

Q: What face shape is best for bangs?

A: Bangs can work with any face shape, but the length and style should be chosen based on your face shape. For example, side-swept bangs work well with round or square faces, while blunt bangs work well with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big forehead?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have a big forehead. Just make sure to choose an updo that flatters your face shape and doesn’t draw attention to your forehead.

Hair Care

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, strong locks. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cover your big forehead with hair and feel great about your appearance.
