How To Style Men s Long Hair On Top

Are you a man with long hair on top and struggling to find ways to style it? Look no further! In this article, we will walk you through step-by-step on how to style men's long hair on top. We will also provide you with some tips, hair care advice, and frequently asked questions.

How to Style Men's Long Hair on Top

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand the different types of long haircuts for men. The most popular ones include the man bun, the messy top knot, and the slick back. Choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

When styling long hair on top, it's important to have the right tools. Invest in a good quality comb, brush, and hair products such as hair oil, pomade, and hairspray. These products will help you achieve the desired look and keep your hair healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, oil, and product buildup from your hair.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of hair oil. This will add shine and nourishment to your hair.

3. Use a comb to part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preferred style.

4. Use a brush to smooth out any tangles or knots in your hair.

5. If you're going for a man bun or messy top knot, gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it with an elastic band. For a slick back, use a comb to push your hair back and apply some pomade for hold.

6. Once you've achieved the desired style, use hairspray to set the look and prevent any flyaways.

Tips for Styling Men's Long Hair on Top:

- Experiment with different styles to find what suits your face shape and personal style.

- Use hair products that are specifically designed for men's long hair on top.

- Don't wash your hair too often as this can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Q: How do I prevent split ends?

A: Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can help prevent split ends. Also, avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons too often.

Hair Care for Men's Long Hair on Top:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swimming cap.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach and hair dye as this can damage your hair.

- Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, styling men's long hair on top doesn't have to be complicated. With the right tools, products, and techniques, you can achieve a variety of styles that suit your face shape and personal style. Don't forget to take care of your hair with regular washing, conditioning, and trims.
