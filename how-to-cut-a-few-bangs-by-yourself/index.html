How To Cut A Few Bangs By Yourself

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle, but constantly going to a salon to maintain them can be expensive. Luckily, cutting your own bangs is not as difficult as it may seem! With a few simple steps and some patience, you can easily give yourself a fresh new look. In this article, we will go over how to cut a few bangs by yourself, along with some tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

How to Cut a Few Bangs by Yourself

Cutting your own bangs can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather the Tools

Before you start cutting your own bangs, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A fine-toothed comb
  • Clips to section off your hair

Having these tools on hand will make sure that you are able to cut your bangs without any issues.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

The first step to cutting your own bangs is deciding on the length that you want them to be. You can use a ruler or measuring tape to make sure that they are all the same length, or you can simply eyeball it if you prefer a more natural look.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Once you have decided on the length of your bangs, you will need to section off the hair that you want to cut. Use your comb to create a triangle-shaped section at the front of your head, with the point of the triangle at the crown of your head.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Now it's time to cut your bangs! Hold the section of hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut straight across. Make sure that you are only cutting the hair in the section that you have created, and not any of the surrounding hair.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you have cut your bangs, it's important to check your work. If you notice any stray hairs or unevenness, you can always go back and trim them up.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own bangs:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more off if you want them shorter.
  • Make sure that your scissors are sharp to avoid any unevenness.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Don't cut your bangs when they are wet, as they will look shorter once they dry.

FAQ About Cutting Your Own Bangs

Can I cut my bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own bangs if you have curly hair. Just make sure that you cut them when your hair is dry, and that you cut them a little longer than you want them to be, as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.

What should I do if I mess up my bangs?

If you accidentally cut your bangs too short or unevenly, don't panic! You can always try to style them differently or pin them back until they grow out. Alternatively, you can always go to a salon and have them fixed.

Hair Care Advice for Bangs

Once you have cut your own bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash and blow dry your bangs separately from the rest of your hair.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as hairspray or pomade, to keep them in place.
  • Avoid touching them too much, as this can make them greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain their shape and length.

Cutting your own bangs can be a fun and easy way to change up your hairstyle. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a fresh new look without having to leave your home. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and be patient!
