How To Slick Back Hair Without Product A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Slicked back hair is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It's a versatile look that can be worn for any occasion, from a formal event to a casual day out. However, many people are hesitant to try this style because they don't want to use hair products. The good news is that it is possible to achieve a slicked back look without any product. In this guide, we will show you how to slick back hair without product.

How to Slick Back Hair Without Product

To slick back hair without product, you will need a few things:

  • A comb
  • Water
  • Hair ties or bobby pins (optional)

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

The first step is to wet your hair. You can do this by taking a shower or using a spray bottle filled with water. Make sure your hair is completely wet before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to brush your hair straight back. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay. If you have short hair, you can use a smaller comb. If you have longer hair, use a larger comb.

Step 3: Smooth Your Hair

Once your hair is combed back, use your hands to smooth it down. This will help to eliminate any bumps or waves in your hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

If your hair is long enough, you can tie it back into a ponytail or bun. Use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure your hair in place. If your hair is too short to tie back, skip this step.

Step 5: Allow Your Hair to Dry

Leave your hair to dry naturally. Don't use a hair dryer or towel to dry your hair, as this can create frizz and undo all your hard work.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray (Optional)

If you want to add a little extra hold to your slicked back hair, you can use a light hold hairspray. Hold the can about 12 inches away from your head and spray lightly. Be careful not to use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Tips

  • If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to use a gel or pomade to achieve a slicked back look.
  • Don't use too much water, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a comb with wide teeth to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying, as this can create frizz.

FAQ

Can I slick back my hair without any tools?

Yes, you can use your hands to slick back your hair. Simply wet your hands and run them through your hair, smoothing it back as you go.

How long will my slicked back hair last without product?

This will depend on your hair type and how well you follow the steps above. Generally, slicked back hair without product will last for a few hours.

Can I wear a hat with slicked back hair?

Yes, you can wear a hat with slicked back hair. However, be careful not to mess up your hair while putting on or taking off your hat.

Hair Care

Slicked back hair can be harsh on your hair if you do it too often. It's important to take care of your hair by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools. You can also use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a slicked back hairstyle without using any product. It's a classic look that never goes out of style, and it's perfect for any occasion.
