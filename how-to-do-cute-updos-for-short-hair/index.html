How To Do Cute Updos For Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of wearing your short hair the same way every day? Updos are a great way to switch up your look and add some variety to your hairstyle routine. Contrary to popular belief, short hair can be styled in many different ways, including cute updos. In this blog post, we'll show you how to do cute updos for short hair in a few easy steps.

How to Do Cute Updos for Short Hair

If you have short hair, you may think that your styling options are limited. However, there are many cute updos that you can create with short hair. The key is to use the right products and techniques to get the look you want. Here are some basic steps to follow when creating a cute updo for short hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling, you need to prepare your hair. Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry it gently. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to give it some hold and texture.

Step 2: Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair at the crown to add volume and texture. Use a comb to backcomb small sections of hair at the crown, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased sections.

Step 3: Create a Ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 4: Twist and Pin

Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. You can leave some hair out around your face for a softer look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look by spraying your hair with hairspray to hold it in place. Use a light, flexible hairspray that won't make your hair stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide to creating a cute updo for short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to give it some hold and texture. Use a small amount of product and distribute it evenly through your hair.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair at the crown to add volume and texture. Use a comb to backcomb small sections of hair at the crown, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased sections.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into three parts: the top section, the middle section, and the bottom section. Clip the top and middle sections out of the way for now.

Step 5: Create a Ponytail

Gather the bottom section of your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 6: Twist and Pin

Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. You can leave some hair out around your face for a softer look.

Step 7: Add the Middle Section

Release the middle section of hair and tease it lightly. Smooth the top layer of hair over the teased sections, then gather it into a ponytail just above the first bun. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the first bun, then use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step 8: Add the Top Section

Release the top section of hair and tease it lightly. Smooth the top layer of hair over the teased sections, then gather it into a ponytail just above the second bun. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the second bun, then use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step 9: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look by spraying your hair with hairspray to hold it in place. Use a light, flexible hairspray that won't make your hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Creating Cute Updos for Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating cute updos for short hair:

  • Use the right products: Choose styling products that are appropriate for your hair type and the look you want to create.
  • Tease your hair: Teasing your hair can add volume and texture to your updo.
  • Use bobby pins: Bobby pins are your best friend when it comes to creating updos. Use them to secure your hair in place and add extra hold.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try different updo styles to see what works best for you.

FAQs About Cute Updos for Short Hair

Q: Can I create a cute updo with really short hair?

A: Yes! Even if your hair is very short, you can create a cute updo using bobby pins and hair accessories.

Q: What kind of hair accessories can I use to create a cute updo?

A: Bobby pins, hair ties, and hair clips are all great accessories to use when creating updos for short hair. You can also use headbands, scarves, and flowers to add some extra flair.

Q: Can I create a cute updo without teasing my hair?

A: Yes, you can! Teasing your hair can add volume and texture to your updo, but it's not necessary. If you prefer a smoother look, skip the teasing and use a smoothing product instead.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can create cute updos for short hair in no time. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you. Have fun and enjoy your gorgeous new hairstyle!
