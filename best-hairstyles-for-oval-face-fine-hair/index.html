Best Hairstyles For Oval Face Fine Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Nov 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face with fine hair, then you are lucky as you have a wide range of hairstyles to choose from. The oval face shape is versatile and can pull off most styles with ease. However, finding the perfect hairstyle for fine hair can be a challenge as it requires a style that adds volume and texture without weighing down the hair. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oval face fine hair, how to style them, tips, and hair care.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval face fine hair, it is essential to determine your face shape. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Take a measuring tape and measure the distance from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin.
  2. Measure the width of your cheekbones.
  3. Measure the distance between your temples.
  4. Measure the length of your jawline.
  5. Compare these measurements to identify your face shape.

If the measurements are similar, then you have an oval face shape. Now let's move on to the best hairstyles for oval face fine hair.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Fine Hair

Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face fine hair:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a timeless classic that looks great on oval face shapes. It is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your hair texture and face shape. A blunt bob that ends just above the shoulders adds volume and texture to fine hair.

2. Long Layers

Long layers add movement and volume to fine hair without weighing it down. They also frame the face and accentuate your features. Soft, face-framing layers that start from the chin look great on oval face shapes.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance style that looks great on oval face shapes with fine hair. It adds volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller. A pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs looks great on oval face shapes.

4. Lob Haircut

The lob, or long bob, is a trendy style that looks great on oval face shapes with fine hair. It is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A lob that ends just above the shoulders adds volume and texture to fine hair.

5. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a chic and effortless style that looks great on oval face shapes with fine hair. It adds volume and texture to the hair while keeping it out of your face. A half up half down style with loose waves looks great on oval face shapes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Here's how you can style the above hairstyles:

1. Bob Haircut

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry it with a round brush.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look.
  4. Apply a shine serum to add a glossy finish.

2. Long Layers

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry it with a round brush.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to add texture and volume.

3. Pixie Cut

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a styling cream to your hair and blow-dry it with your fingers.
  3. Use a flat iron to create a sleek look.
  4. Apply a hair wax to add texture and hold.

4. Lob Haircut

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a styling cream to your hair and blow-dry it with a round brush.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to add texture and volume.

5. Half Up Half Down

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry it with a round brush.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  4. Take the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to add texture and volume.

Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQs

1. Can oval face shapes pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval face shapes are versatile and can pull off most hairstyles with ease.

2. What is the best haircut for fine hair?

The best haircut for fine hair is one that adds volume and texture without weighing it down. Bob haircuts, long layers, and pixie cuts are great options for fine hair.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are some hair care tips for fine hair:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval face fine hair, how to style them, tips, and hair care, you can choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Remember to maintain your hairstyle by following the tips mentioned above. With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can have healthy, voluminous hair that looks great every day.
