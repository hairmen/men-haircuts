How To Hide Hair Underneath Extensions Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair. But, what do you do when you want to hide your natural hair underneath the extensions? In this blog post, we will discuss how to hide hair underneath extensions and the best tips and tricks to achieve a seamless look. Let's get started!

How to Hide Hair Underneath Extensions

Before we get into the tips and tricks, let's talk about the different types of extensions. There are clip-ins, sew-ins, tape-ins, and more. Each type of extension has its own method of installation and requires specific techniques to hide your natural hair.

Clip-in Extensions

Clip-in extensions are the easiest to install and remove. They come with clips attached to the weft, which you clip onto your natural hair. To hide your natural hair underneath clip-in extensions, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair where you want to place the clip-ins.
  2. Tease the roots of the section where you'll place the clip-ins to create a secure base.
  3. Clip the weft onto the teased section, making sure to hide your natural hair underneath.
  4. Repeat this process until you've placed all the clip-ins.

Tip: Use a rat-tail comb to create a clean part and blend the extensions with your natural hair.

Sew-in Extensions

Sew-in extensions require a bit more effort to install, but they offer a more secure and long-lasting hold. To hide your natural hair underneath sew-in extensions, follow these steps:

  1. Braid your natural hair in cornrows, making sure they're flat and close to your scalp.
  2. Sew the weft onto the braids, making sure to hide your natural hair underneath.
  3. Repeat this process until you've sewn in all the wefts.

Tip: Use a weaving needle and thread that matches your natural hair color to create a seamless look.

Tape-in Extensions

Tape-in extensions are the newest type of extension on the market. They're easy to install and remove, and they offer a natural-looking finish. To hide your natural hair underneath tape-in extensions, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair where you want to place the tape-ins.
  2. Apply the tape-in weft onto the parted section, making sure to hide your natural hair underneath.
  3. Repeat this process until you've placed all the tape-ins.

Tip: Use a flat iron to blend the tape-in extensions with your natural hair.

Tips and Tricks

Now that you know how to hide your natural hair underneath extensions, here are some tips and tricks to achieve a flawless finish:

  1. Choose extensions that match your natural hair color and texture.
  2. Blend the extensions with your natural hair by using a flat iron or curling wand.
  3. Use dry shampoo to add volume to your natural hair and the extensions.
  4. Trim the extensions to match your natural hair length and style.
  5. Use a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to protect the extensions and your natural hair while sleeping.

FAQ

Can I wash my hair with extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair with extensions. However, it's important to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the extensions. Also, don't wash your hair too frequently as it can loosen the wefts.

How do I remove extensions?

The process of removing extensions depends on the type of extension you have. Clip-in extensions are the easiest to remove, simply unclip them from your hair. Sew-in and tape-in extensions require a bit more effort to remove. It's best to have them removed by a professional stylist.

How do I take care of my natural hair with extensions?

It's important to take care of your natural hair while wearing extensions. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling, and avoiding tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your scalp. Also, make sure to moisturize your natural hair and scalp to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

Extensions can be a great way to change up your hairstyle, but it's important to take care of them and your natural hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the extensions and your natural hair.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to the extensions to keep them moisturized and soft.
  4. Avoid using heavy products on the extensions, as they can weigh them down and cause them to become greasy.
  5. Take breaks between wearing extensions to give your natural hair a chance to rest and breathe.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a seamless look when hiding your natural hair underneath extensions. Remember to take care of your natural hair and the extensions to keep them looking their best.
