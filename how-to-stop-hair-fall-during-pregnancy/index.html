How To Stop Hair Fall During Pregnancy Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall during pregnancy? You're not alone. Hair fall is a common issue faced by many pregnant women due to hormonal changes in the body. However, it can be stressful to see your hair falling out in clumps. Fortunately, there are ways to prevent hair fall during pregnancy. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall during pregnancy with easy-to-follow tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  1. Causes of Hair Fall During Pregnancy
  2. Hair Care During Pregnancy
  3. How to Stop Hair Fall During Pregnancy
  4. Tips to Prevent Hair Fall During Pregnancy
  5. FAQs

Causes of Hair Fall During Pregnancy

Hair fall during pregnancy is caused by hormonal changes in the body. The increase in estrogen levels during pregnancy can cause hair to stay in the growth phase for a longer period of time, leading to thicker and fuller hair. However, after delivery, the estrogen levels drop, causing the hair to enter the resting phase, leading to hair fall.

Other factors that can contribute to hair fall during pregnancy include:

  • Stress
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Genetics
  • Medical conditions
  • Hairstyling practices

Hair Care During Pregnancy

Proper hair care during pregnancy can help prevent hair fall. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

How to Stop Hair Fall During Pregnancy

Here are some tips on how to stop hair fall during pregnancy:

  1. Consult your doctor: If you are experiencing excessive hair fall, it's important to consult your doctor. They can rule out any underlying medical conditions that may be causing hair fall.
  2. Take prenatal vitamins: Prenatal vitamins contain essential nutrients like folic acid, iron, and biotin that can help prevent hair fall.
  3. Use hair growth products: There are many hair growth products available in the market that can help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth.
  4. Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails can cause hair fall. Try to keep your hair loose and avoid tying it up too tightly.
  5. Avoid chemical treatments: Chemical treatments like coloring and perming can damage your hair and cause hair fall. Avoid them during pregnancy.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall During Pregnancy

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall during pregnancy:

  • Avoid using harsh hair products
  • Don't over-wash your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid exposing your hair to sunlight for long periods

FAQs

Q. Can stress cause hair fall during pregnancy?

A. Yes, stress can contribute to hair fall during pregnancy. It's important to manage stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques like yoga and meditation.

Q. Can I color my hair during pregnancy?

A. It's best to avoid chemical treatments like coloring and perming during pregnancy as they can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

Q. Will my hair fall stop after delivery?

A. Hair fall after delivery is normal and temporary. Your hair will eventually grow back to its normal state.

Q. Can I use hair growth products during pregnancy?

A. It's best to consult your doctor before using any hair growth products during pregnancy.

Conclusion

Hair fall during pregnancy can be a stressful experience, but with the right care and attention, it can be prevented. Follow the tips and tricks mentioned in this article to stop hair fall during pregnancy and enjoy healthy, luscious locks.
