Best Hair For Round Face 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Look no further because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best hair for round face 2020. We will cover everything from the description of a round face to the hairstyles that will flatter your face shape, step by step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care. So, let’s get started.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft chin, full cheeks, and a round jawline. The width and length of a round face are almost equal, and the forehead is usually rounded. If you have a round face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it’s essential to consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair. You should also consider your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend on your hair each day.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter a round face.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle is best for you.
  4. Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and styling.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair for round face 2020:

  • Layered hairstyles can help to create the illusion of length and definition.
  • Long hairstyles with choppy layers can help to elongate your face.
  • Short hairstyles with volume at the crown can help to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Hairstyles that are too short or too long can make a round face appear wider.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair for round face 2020:

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or straight hair with no layers. These styles can make your face appear wider.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just be sure to add some layers to create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I wear bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a round face. Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care

It’s essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know the best hair for round face 2020, it’s time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new hairstyle. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
