How To Style Mid Long Hair

Written by William Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of styling your mid long hair the same way every day? Do you want to switch up your look but don't know where to start? You've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to style your mid long hair in various ways to give you a fresh new look.

Description

Mid long hair can be challenging to style because it's not too short nor too long. But with the right techniques and products, you can create various hairstyles that suit different occasions. Whether you're going to work, a party, or just running errands, we've got you covered.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, let's go over some essential tools and products you'll need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Hair spray or hair gel
  • Straightener or curling iron (optional)
  • Heat protectant spray (if using hot tools)

Now that you have your tools and products ready, let's get started with some hairstyles:

1. Beach Waves

Beach waves are perfect for a relaxed, carefree look. Here's how to achieve them:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray and hair gel to your hair.
  3. Section your hair and twist each section away from your face.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray.

Tip: For a more natural look, don't curl the ends of your hair.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Half-up half-down hairstyles are effortless yet elegant. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  3. Tease the ponytail to add volume.
  4. Smooth the top section of your hair and wrap it around the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with hairspray.

Tip: You can also braid the top section of your hair for a different look.

3. Straight and Sleek

Straight and sleek hairstyles are perfect for a polished, sophisticated look. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Wash and blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray and straighten your hair in sections.
  3. Finish with a shine spray.

Tip: Use a boar bristle brush to smooth out any frizz and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your mid long hair:

  • Invest in good quality tools and products.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to use accessories like headbands or hairpins to enhance your hairstyle.
  • Take good care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my mid long hair if it's fine or thin?

A: Absolutely! You can use volumizing products and techniques to add body and fullness to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my mid long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to style your mid long hair, it's time to try out some new looks! Remember, the key is to have fun and experiment with different hairstyles until you find what works best for you. Happy styling!
