Best Haircut For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick coarse wavy hair, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. However, with the right haircut, you can embrace your natural texture and make your hair work for you. In this article, we will explore the best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair and provide tips on how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. Its volume and texture can make it difficult to manage, but it also provides endless styling possibilities. The key to finding the best haircut for your hair type is to work with your natural texture instead of trying to fight it.

The best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair is one that adds definition and shape to your waves while reducing bulk. A layered haircut is ideal for this hair type, as it helps to distribute the weight of the hair and create movement. A layered haircut can also be customized to flatter your face shape and enhance your best features.

How to

The first step in getting the best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair is to find a stylist who is experienced in working with this hair type. Look for someone who listens to your needs and understands your natural texture. Bring in pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss which ones would work best for your hair type.

When getting a layered haircut for thick coarse wavy hair, it's important to avoid thinning shears. Thinning shears can create frizz and make your hair look thin and wispy. Instead, your stylist should use regular shears to create long layers that blend seamlessly into each other.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to getting the best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced in working with thick coarse wavy hair.
  2. Discuss which hairstyles would work best for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Avoid thinning shears and opt for regular shears to create long layers.
  4. Ask your stylist to add texture and movement to your hair.
  5. Style your hair using products that enhance your natural texture.

Tips

Here are some tips for caring for thick coarse wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid over-drying your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to add moisture and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair:

What is the best length for thick coarse wavy hair?

The best length for thick coarse wavy hair depends on your face shape and personal style. However, medium-length hair is often the most flattering for this hair type, as it allows for enough weight to keep the waves in place without becoming too heavy.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have thick coarse wavy hair?

You should avoid hairstyles that create too much volume at the crown, as this can make your hair look bulky. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as these can make your hair look frizzy and unmanageable.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for thick coarse wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and make your hair more prone to frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.
  • Avoid sleeping with your hair in a tight ponytail or bun, as this can create tension on your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and getting the right haircut, you can embrace your natural texture and make your thick coarse wavy hair work for you.
