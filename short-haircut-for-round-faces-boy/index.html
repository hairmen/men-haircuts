Short Haircut For Round Faces Boy

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a boy with a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll be discussing the best short haircuts for round-faced boys. From how to choose the right style to hair care tips, we've got everything you need to know to rock a short haircut with confidence.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The key to finding the right haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and definition. Short haircuts can be a great option for boys with round faces, as they can help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Faces Boy

When choosing a short haircut for a round-faced boy, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: Short haircuts that are too short can make a round face look even rounder. Aim for a style that is short but not too short, such as a buzz cut or a textured crop.
  • Texture: Adding texture to a short haircut can help to create the illusion of length and definition. Consider a style with choppy layers or textured fringe.
  • Shape: A haircut with a tapered back and sides can help to create a more defined jawline. Avoid styles that are too boxy or blunt.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Round Faces Boy

Follow these steps to get the perfect short haircut for a round-faced boy:

  1. Choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the style you want.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to add texture to the haircut.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to taper the back and sides of the haircut.
  5. Style your new haircut with a pomade or wax.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Haircut for Round Faces Boy

Here are some tips for keeping your short haircut looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep the haircut looking sharp.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax for a polished look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for round-faced boys:

  • Q: Can boys with round faces wear short haircuts?
  • A: Yes, short haircuts can be a great option for boys with round faces. The key is to choose a style that creates the illusion of length and definition.
  • Q: What are some popular short haircuts for round-faced boys?
  • A: Some popular short haircuts for round-faced boys include the buzz cut, textured crop, and messy fringe.
  • Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?
  • A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care for Short Haircut for Round Faces Boy

A good hair care routine is essential for keeping your short haircut looking great. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax for a polished look.

With the right short haircut and a good hair care routine, boys with round faces can look and feel their best. So go ahead, rock that short haircut with confidence!
