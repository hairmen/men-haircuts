What Does The Quran Say About Covering Hair

Description

Many women in Muslim countries cover their hair as a sign of religious devotion. But what does the Quran say about covering hair? The Quran does not have a specific verse that commands women to cover their hair, but it does give general guidelines for modesty and dress. In this article, we will explore what the Quran says about covering hair and provide tips for covering hair according to Quranic teachings.

How To Cover Hair According to Quran

The Quran teaches that both men and women should dress modestly and cover their bodies. However, the specifics of how to dress modestly are not defined in great detail. The Quran simply states that clothing should be "covering and decent" (7:26) and that "believing women should lower their gaze and guard their private parts, and should not display their beauty and ornaments except what is apparent of it" (24:31).

While the Quran does not explicitly mention hair covering, it is understood by many Muslims that hair is included in the definition of "beauty and ornaments" that should not be displayed in public. Therefore, many Muslim women cover their hair with a hijab, a scarf that covers the head and neck, as a sign of modesty and religious devotion.

Step by Step Guide to Cover Hair According to Quran

Here is a step by step guide for covering hair according to Quranic teachings:

  1. Choose a hijab that covers the head and neck
  2. Place the hijab over the head, making sure that it covers the hairline and forehead
  3. Wrap the hijab around the head, making sure that it covers the neck and shoulders
  4. Secure the hijab in place with pins or clips
  5. Adjust the hijab so that it is comfortable and does not restrict movement or vision

Tips for Covering Hair

Here are some tips for covering hair according to Quranic teachings:

  • Choose a hijab made from breathable material such as cotton or silk
  • Experiment with different styles of hijab to find the one that is most comfortable and flattering
  • Match the color of the hijab to the rest of your outfit for a cohesive look
  • Accessorize the hijab with jewelry or pins for a personalized touch

FAQs

Q: Do Muslim women have to cover their hair?

A: The Quran does not explicitly state that women have to cover their hair, but it is generally understood that hair is included in the definition of "beauty and ornaments" that should not be displayed in public.

Q: Can non-Muslim women wear a hijab?

A: While non-Muslim women are not required to wear a hijab, they may choose to do so as a sign of respect for Muslim culture and beliefs.

Hair Care Tips While Covering Hair

Wearing a hijab can affect hair health and appearance. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while covering it:

  • Wash hair regularly to prevent buildup of sweat and oils
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or clips that can damage hair
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Massage the scalp regularly to increase blood flow and promote hair growth
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a neat appearance

In conclusion, while the Quran does not explicitly command women to cover their hair, it does provide general guidelines for modesty and dress. Many Muslim women choose to cover their hair with a hijab as a sign of religious devotion and modesty. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, women can cover their hair according to Quranic teachings while maintaining healthy hair.
