How To Cut Top Of Men s Hair With Clippers

Written by Aprilia Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts or just want to cut your hair at home? Cutting the top of men's hair with clippers is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting the top of men's hair with clippers.

Description

Cutting the top of men's hair with clippers is a popular trend among men. It is a simple and quick way to achieve a clean and neat look. Clippers are an essential tool for this process, and they come in different sizes and styles. The size of the clipper blade determines the length of the hair that is cut. For example, a number 1 clipper blade will cut hair to a length of 1/8 inch while a number 4 blade will cut hair to a length of 1/2 inch.

How to Cut Top of Men's Hair with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need to gather the necessary tools. These include a pair of clippers, clipper guards, scissors, comb, and a mirror. Once you have these tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure to wash and dry it. This will help remove any dirt or oils that may interfere with the cutting process.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

The clipper guard you choose will depend on the length you want your hair to be. It's best to start with a longer guard and work your way down. Place the guard on the clippers and make sure it is securely attached.

Step 3: Start at the Sides

Start by cutting the sides of your hair. Use the clippers to trim the hair around your ears and the back of your head. Use a higher guard for the sides and a lower guard for the back of your head.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Once you have finished cutting the sides and back, move on to the top of your head. Use a higher guard for the top of your head and work your way down to a lower guard until you achieve the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and top of your head. This will create a seamless look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the hair around your neckline. This will give you a clean and polished look.

Step 7: Check for Any Uneven Spots

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for any uneven spots. If you notice any, use the clippers or scissors to even them out.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut your hair.
  • Trim the hair around your ears and the back of your head before moving on to the top.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and top of your head for a seamless look.
  • Make sure to clean your clippers after each use to maintain their performance.

FAQ

What size clipper guard should I use?

The size of the clipper guard you use will depend on the length you want your hair to be. It's best to start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it is dry as wet hair can be more difficult to cut and style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Regular trims and maintaining a healthy diet can also help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Cutting the top of men's hair with clippers is a simple and cost-effective way to achieve a clean and polished look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect haircut.
