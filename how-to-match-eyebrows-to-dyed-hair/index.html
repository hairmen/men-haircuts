How To Match Eyebrows To Dyed Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've recently dyed your hair, then you may be wondering how to match your eyebrows to your new hair color. It can be a daunting task, but with a few simple steps, you can achieve a natural and cohesive look. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and FAQs on how to match your eyebrows to your dyed hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Match Eyebrows to Dyed Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Matching Eyebrows to Dyed Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Dyed Hair

How to Match Eyebrows to Dyed Hair

When it comes to matching your eyebrows to your dyed hair, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, it's important to determine the undertones of your new hair color. Is it warm or cool-toned? This will help you choose the right eyebrow product and shade.

Secondly, you'll need to decide whether you want your eyebrows to match your hair color exactly or whether you'd prefer a slightly darker or lighter shade. This will depend on your personal preference and the overall look you're trying to achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to match your eyebrows to your dyed hair:

  1. Determine the undertones of your new hair color
  2. Choose an eyebrow product and shade that matches your hair's undertones
  3. Fill in your eyebrows using light, feathery strokes
  4. Blend the product using a spoolie brush
  5. If necessary, use a concealer to clean up any mistakes or to create a sharper shape
  6. Set your eyebrows with a clear or tinted brow gel

Tips for Matching Eyebrows to Dyed Hair

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect eyebrow and hair color match:

  • If you're unsure about which eyebrow product or shade to use, consult with a professional makeup artist or eyebrow specialist
  • Consider using a slightly darker or lighter shade than your hair color for a more natural look
  • Use a light hand when filling in your eyebrows to avoid a harsh or unnatural look
  • Don't forget to blend your product using a spoolie brush for a seamless finish
  • Use a concealer to clean up any mistakes or to create a sharper shape
  • Set your eyebrows with a clear or tinted brow gel to keep them in place throughout the day

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about matching eyebrows to dyed hair:

Should my eyebrows match my hair color exactly?

It depends on your personal preference and the look you're trying to achieve. If you want a more natural look, consider using a slightly darker or lighter shade than your hair color.

What eyebrow product should I use?

It's best to use a product that matches your hair's undertones. If your hair is warm-toned, choose a warm-toned product. If it's cool-toned, choose a cool-toned product.

How do I clean up mistakes when filling in my eyebrows?

Use a concealer that matches your skin tone to clean up any mistakes or to create a sharper shape.

Hair Care for Dyed Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your dyed hair to ensure that it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to match your eyebrows to your dyed hair effortlessly. Remember to take care of your hair to ensure that your new color stays vibrant and healthy.
