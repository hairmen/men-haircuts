How To Wear A Dad Hat With Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long hair, you might think that wearing a dad hat is not for you. But fear not, there are ways to style your hair and wear a dad hat without compromising on style or comfort. In this blog post, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to wear a dad hat with long hair.

Description

A dad hat is a type of baseball cap that has a slightly curved brim and is often made from cotton or canvas. It is a casual and stylish accessory that can be worn by both men and women. However, if you have long hair, you might find it challenging to wear a dad hat without your hair getting in the way or looking messy. But don't worry, with a few styling tips and tricks, you can wear a dad hat with confidence and style.

How to Wear a Dad Hat with Long Hair

Here are some tips on how to wear a dad hat with long hair:

Step 1: Style your hair

Before you put on your dad hat, you need to style your hair in a way that complements the hat. You can either tie your hair in a low ponytail, a messy bun, or leave it loose. If you have bangs, you can either tuck them under the hat or let them hang out.

Step 2: Adjust the hat

Once you have styled your hair, put on the dad hat and adjust it to fit comfortably on your head. Make sure that the brim of the hat is not covering your eyes and that the hat is not too tight or too loose.

Step 3: Position the hat

How you position the hat on your head can make a big difference in how it looks. You can wear the hat straight on your head, tilted to one side, or even backward. Experiment with different positions to find the one that suits you best.

Step 4: Own the look

Now that you have styled your hair and positioned the hat, it's time to own the look. Wear your dad hat with confidence and style, and don't be afraid to add some accessories like sunglasses or a necklace to complete the look.

Tips for Wearing a Dad Hat with Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a dad hat with long hair:

Tip 1: Choose the right hat

When choosing a dad hat, make sure that it fits your head comfortably and that the brim is not too big or too small. You also want to choose a hat that complements your personal style and wardrobe.

Tip 2: Experiment with different hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles when wearing a dad hat. Try a messy bun, a fishtail braid, or even a half-up, half-down hairstyle to see what works best for you.

Tip 3: Use a hair tie or clips

If you have long hair that keeps falling in your face, use a hair tie or clips to keep it in place. You can also tuck your hair behind your ears or under the hat to keep it out of the way.

Tip 4: Coordinate with your outfit

A dad hat can add a casual and stylish touch to any outfit. Coordinate your hat with your outfit by choosing colors and styles that complement each other.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a dad hat with short hair?

A: Yes, a dad hat can be worn with any hair length, including short hair. Just make sure that the hat fits your head comfortably and that you style your hair in a way that complements the hat.

Q: What types of outfits can I wear with a dad hat?

A: A dad hat can be worn with a variety of outfits, including casual streetwear, athletic wear, and even dresses. Coordinate your hat with your outfit to create a cohesive and stylish look.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a dad hat with long hair can sometimes lead to hair breakage or damage. To prevent this, make sure to take care of your hair by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear a dad hat with confidence and style, no matter your hair length or personal style. Whether you're running errands, hanging out with friends, or going to a concert, a dad hat is a versatile and stylish accessory that can elevate any outfit.
