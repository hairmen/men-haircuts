The Best Brush To Slick Back Natural Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Slicked back hairstyles have been trendy for years now, and they are not going anywhere anytime soon. They are a perfect choice for those who want to look sleek, polished, and elegant. However, finding the best brush to slick back natural hair can be a daunting task. There are so many options out there, and it can be hard to know where to start. In this article, we will discuss the best brush to slick back natural hair and provide you with tips on how to achieve the perfect slicked-back look.

Description

Slicking back natural hair is a great way to show off your hair's natural texture while also giving it a sleek and polished look. To achieve this look, you need the right tools, including the best brush for your hair type. The right brush will help you achieve a smooth and shiny finish without damaging your hair.

How to Slick Back Natural Hair

Slicking back natural hair may seem like a daunting task, but it is actually quite simple. Here are the steps you need to follow to achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step to slicking back natural hair is to start with clean, damp hair. This will make it easier to brush your hair back and achieve a sleek finish.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product such as a gel or pomade to your hair while it is still damp. This will help to hold your hair in place and give it a shiny finish.

Step 3: Brush your hair back

Using the best brush for your hair type, brush your hair back from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Make sure to brush your hair in the direction you want it to stay.

Step 4: Secure your hair

Once you have brushed your hair back, use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure it in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Natural Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Use a comb to create a deep side part for a more dramatic look.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair before slicking it back to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Be gentle when brushing your hair to prevent breakage and damage.

FAQ

What is the best brush to slick back natural hair?

The best brush to slick back natural hair is a paddle brush. Paddle brushes are great for detangling and smoothing out hair, making them perfect for slicking back natural hair.

Can I slick back my natural hair without using any products?

It is possible to slick back natural hair without using any products, but it will be more difficult to achieve a smooth and shiny finish. Using a styling product will help to hold your hair in place and give it a polished look.

Will slicking back my hair damage it?

Slicking back your hair can cause damage if done improperly. It is important to be gentle when brushing your hair and to use a styling product that will not cause damage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

In addition to following a hair care routine, it is also important to use the best brush for your hair type. Using the wrong brush can cause damage and breakage, so make sure to choose a brush that is gentle and appropriate for your hair.

Conclusion

Slicking back natural hair is a great way to achieve a polished and elegant look. By using the best brush for your hair type and following the tips outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle. Remember to be gentle when brushing your hair and to follow a regular hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
