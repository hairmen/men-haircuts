How To Curl Hair Korean Style The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to switch up your hair game, why not try out the Korean style of curling your hair? Korean beauty trends have been taking the world by storm in recent years, and their hair styling techniques are no exception. In this article, we’ll be giving you a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair Korean style, as well as some tips and tricks to make sure your curls last all day.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Curl Hair Korean Style
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care

How to Curl Hair Korean Style

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of how to curl your hair Korean style, let’s talk about what sets this style apart from others. Korean curls are typically looser and more natural-looking than other types of curls. They’re also usually curled away from the face, which helps to open up and frame the face.

The most popular method for achieving Korean-style curls is by using a curling iron or a straightening iron. You’ll also need some hair clips, a heat protectant, and some hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair Korean style:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair, making sure it’s completely dry before you start curling. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting from the back and working your way forward. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or straightening iron, starting from the middle of the section and working your way down to the ends. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when curling your hair Korean style:

  • Use a curling iron or straightening iron with a larger barrel for looser curls
  • Curl away from the face for a more natural look
  • Don’t curl your hair all the way to the roots – leave a few inches at the top for a more relaxed look
  • Use hair clips to keep the curl in place while it cools
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your curls in place

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling your hair Korean style:

What type of curling iron should I use?

For looser curls, use a curling iron or straightening iron with a larger barrel. A 1 ½ inch barrel is a good option.

Should I curl my hair all the way to the roots?

No, leave a few inches at the top for a more relaxed look.

How can I make my curls last all day?

Use a heat protectant before curling, and finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair – use lukewarm or cool water instead
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

Now that you know how to curl your hair Korean style, it’s time to give it a try. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for you. With a little practice, you’ll be able to achieve natural-looking, beautiful curls that last all day.
