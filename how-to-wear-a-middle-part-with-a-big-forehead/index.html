How To Wear A Middle Part With A Big Forehead

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone who is blessed with a big forehead but always struggled with how to style your hair? Worry not, as this article will guide you on how to wear a middle part with a big forehead. This is a popular hairstyle that has been in trend for a while now, and it can be a great way to flaunt your forehead instead of hiding it. Read on to learn how to style your hair in a middle part with confidence.

Description of Middle Part with a Big Forehead

A middle part is a hairstyle where the hair is parted down the center of your head. This style can be done with any hair length or texture, and it can be a great way to frame your face. However, if you have a big forehead, it can be intimidating to try this style. But with a few tips and tricks, you can rock this look with confidence.

How to Wear a Middle Part with a Big Forehead

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Length

The length of your hair can play a huge role in how your forehead looks. If you have a big forehead, it is best to avoid hairstyles that are too short as they can make your forehead look even bigger. Instead, opt for hairstyles that are longer, such as medium-length or long hairstyles.

Step 2: Find the Right Parting

When it comes to a middle part, the placement of the parting is crucial. If you have a big forehead, it is best to avoid a deep part as it can make your forehead look even bigger. Instead, opt for a shallow part that is closer to the center of your head. This will help create the illusion of a smaller forehead.

Step 3: Add Volume to Your Hair

Adding volume to your hair can help balance out your forehead. You can achieve this by using volumizing products such as mousse or dry shampoo. You can also try blow-drying your hair upside down to create more volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right length, parting, and volume, it's time to style your hair. You can opt for loose waves or straight hair, depending on your preference. You can also add some accessories such as a headband or hair clips to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

Tips for Wearing a Middle Part with a Big Forehead

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair in a middle part:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting bangs if you're not comfortable with a middle part.

FAQs

Can I wear a middle part if I have a big forehead?

Yes, you can wear a middle part with a big forehead. Just make sure to choose the right hair length, parting, and volume to balance out your forehead.

What are some hairstyles that I can try if I have a big forehead?

Aside from a middle part, you can try hairstyles with side-swept bangs or a side part. You can also try updos or braids to create a different look.

Hair Care for a Middle Part with a Big Forehead

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a middle part with a big forehead. Remember to choose the right hair length, parting, and volume to balance out your forehead and create a flattering hairstyle.
