How Do I Cut My Own Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Do you want to try cutting your own hair? Thanks to the power of YouTube, you can learn how to cut your own hair from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your own hair using YouTube tutorials. We will cover the How to, Step by Step, Tips, FAQ, and Hair Care aspects of cutting your own hair. So, let's get started!

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Your Own Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you will need to cut your own hair. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have gathered these tools, it's time to find a YouTube tutorial that suits your hair type and desired haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. After finding a YouTube tutorial that suits your hair type and desired haircut, watch it from start to finish to get an idea of the process.

2. Set up your mirror and position it in a way that allows you to see the back of your head.

3. Comb your hair and section it off using clips.

4. Start cutting your hair according to the tutorial's instructions.

5. Take breaks in between sections to check your progress and adjust accordingly.

6. Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it according to your liking.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

- Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.

- Use hair cutting scissors, not regular scissors. Regular scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends.

- Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, and you may end up cutting more than intended.

- Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with practice, it can become second nature.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair without watching a YouTube tutorial?

A: While it's possible to cut your own hair without a tutorial, we highly recommend watching one to ensure you are cutting your hair correctly and safely.

Q: Is it easy to cut my own hair?

A: Cutting your own hair takes practice, but with time and patience, it can become a simple task.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a hair mask once a week, and trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair using YouTube tutorials, it's time to give it a try. Remember to take your time, be patient, and always use the proper tools. Happy cutting!
