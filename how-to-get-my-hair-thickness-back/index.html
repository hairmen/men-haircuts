How To Get My Hair Thickness Back Tips And Tricks

Written by William Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair and wondering how to get your hair thickness back? You're not alone. Hair loss can be caused by various factors, including genetics, stress, and environmental factors. Fortunately, there are several ways to promote hair growth and restore thickness to your locks. In this article, we'll explore some effective tips and tricks for regaining your hair's volume and thickness.

How to Promote Hair Growth

Before we dive into the tips for getting thicker hair, it's essential to understand how hair growth works. The hair growth cycle consists of three phases: anagen, catagen, and telogen. During the anagen phase, hair grows actively, while the catagen and telogen phases involve hair shedding and resting. The average hair growth rate is about half an inch per month, and most people shed 50 to 100 hairs per day.

To promote hair growth, you can try the following tips:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

What you eat can affect your hair's health and thickness. Eating a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Some foods that are good for promoting hair growth include:

  • Salmon
  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Avocado
  • Sweet potatoes

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow to your hair follicles, promoting hair growth. You can use your fingertips to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes each day. You can also use a scalp massager or a soft-bristled brush to massage your scalp.

3. Use Hair Growth Products

There are many hair growth products available on the market, including shampoos, conditioners, and serums. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can promote hair growth and thickness.

How to Style Thin Hair

If you have thin hair, you may feel self-conscious about styling it. However, there are many ways to create the illusion of thicker, fuller hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use Volumizing Products

Volumizing products like mousse, root lifter, and volumizing spray can help add volume to your hair. Apply these products to damp hair before blow-drying for the best results.

2. Try a Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can create the illusion of thicker hair by adding dimension and movement. Talk to your hairstylist about the best layered haircut for your hair type and face shape.

3. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy styling products like pomade or wax, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for lightweight products like hairspray or texturizing spray.

FAQ About Hair Thickness

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you're under stress, your body produces cortisol, which can affect hair growth and lead to hair loss.

Q: Is it normal to lose hair every day?

A: Yes, it's normal to lose 50 to 100 hairs per day. However, if you're losing more hair than usual or noticing bald spots, you may have a hair loss problem.

Q: Can genetics affect hair thickness?

A: Yes, genetics can play a role in hair thickness. If your parents or grandparents have thin hair, you may be more likely to have thin hair as well.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker Hair

Here are some general hair care tips that can help promote hair growth and thickness:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days, or less if your hair is very dry.

2. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling and breaking your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look thin and frizzy. Use a heat protectant spray before using these tools, and avoid using them too often.

In conclusion, if you want to get your hair thickness back, you can try various tips and tricks to promote hair growth, style your hair, and care for your hair. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and don't hesitate to seek professional help if you have severe hair loss or other hair problems.
