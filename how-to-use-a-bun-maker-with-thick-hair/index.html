How To Use A Bun Maker With Thick Hair

Written by Daniel Sep 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of struggling to create the perfect bun with your thick hair? A bun maker can be a lifesaver when it comes to achieving a flawless bun. However, using a bun maker with thick hair can be tricky. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to use a bun maker with thick hair.

Description

A bun maker is a hair accessory that helps you create a perfect bun effortlessly. It is a doughnut-shaped foam or sponge that you wrap your hair around to create a voluminous bun. Bun makers come in different sizes and colors to match your hair color. They can be used for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Use a Bun Maker with Thick Hair

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Make sure your hair is dry and free of any hair products. This will ensure that your bun is smooth and neat.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair into a Ponytail

Gather your hair in a high or low ponytail, depending on where you want your bun to be. Use a hair tie to secure your ponytail in place.

Step 3: Insert the Bun Maker

Take the bun maker and slide it over your ponytail until it reaches the base. Make sure the bun maker is centered and sits snugly against your head.

Step 4: Spread Your Hair

Spread your hair evenly around the bun maker. Make sure you cover the entire bun maker with your hair. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Step 5: Wrap Your Hair Around the Bun

Take the ends of your hair and wrap them around the bun maker. Keep wrapping until you reach the end of your hair. Tuck any excess hair under the bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 6: Secure the Bun

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Make sure you insert the bobby pins in different directions to ensure the bun is secure.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your bun by spraying it with hairspray. This will hold your bun in place and prevent any flyaways.

Tips

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less visible.
  • Don't pull your hair too tightly as it can cause tension headaches.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less visible.
  • Experiment with different bun styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I use a bun maker with short hair?

No, a bun maker is not suitable for short hair. You can use bobby pins or hair clips to create a bun with short hair.

How do I remove the bun maker?

To remove the bun maker, carefully pull it out of your hair. Undo the bobby pins and gently comb out your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using heat tools frequently. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

There you have it, a step-by-step guide on how to use a bun maker with thick hair. With a little practice, you can create a perfect bun in minutes.
