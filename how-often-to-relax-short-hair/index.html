How Often To Relax Short Hair A Complete Guide

Short relaxed hair is a popular hairstyle among African American women. It's easy to maintain, versatile, and can be styled in many ways. However, one question that often comes up is how often to relax short hair. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about relaxing short hair, including how to do it, how often to do it, and tips for maintaining healthy, relaxed hair.

How to Relax Short Hair

Relaxing short hair involves the use of chemicals to break down the natural curl pattern of your hair. This process is also known as a "perm" or "chemical straightening." There are several steps involved in the process, and it's important to follow them carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to relax short hair:

Step 1: Choose a relaxer that is suitable for your hair type. There are different types of relaxers, such as lye and no-lye, and it's important to choose the right one for your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair into four parts and apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to protect your skin from the relaxer.

Step 3: Mix the relaxer according to the instructions on the package. Wear gloves to protect your hands from the chemicals.

Step 4: Apply the relaxer to your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Use a comb to distribute the relaxer evenly and make sure all strands are covered.

Step 5: Leave the relaxer on your hair for the recommended time. This can vary depending on the type of relaxer, so make sure to read the instructions carefully.

Step 6: Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove the relaxer. Do not use shampoo at this stage.

Step 7: Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair to stop the chemical process. Massage your scalp and hair gently and rinse thoroughly.

Step 8: Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 15-20 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Step 9: Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Relaxed Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, relaxed short hair:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly. Relaxed hair tends to be dry, so it's important to keep it moisturized with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling. Heat can damage relaxed hair, so try to limit the use of flat irons and curling irons.
  • Protect your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf or bonnet. This will help to prevent breakage and preserve your style.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: How often should I relax my short hair?

A: It's recommended to relax your hair every 6-8 weeks. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and how quickly it grows.

Q: Can I relax my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can relax your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and take precautions to avoid damaging your hair or skin.

Q: Will relaxing my hair make it grow faster?

A: No, relaxing your hair will not make it grow faster. Hair growth is determined by genetics and other factors, such as diet and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Relaxed Short Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your relaxed short hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Deep condition your hair once a month to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and greases on your hair, as they can clog your pores and cause breakouts.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swimming cap.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can maintain healthy, relaxed short hair that looks and feels great. Remember to always take care of your hair and listen to what it needs.
