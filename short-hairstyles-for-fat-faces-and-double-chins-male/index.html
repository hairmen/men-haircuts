Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins Male Tips And Ideas

Written by William Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having a round face and a double chin can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. Luckily, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can make your face look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we'll explore some tips and ideas for short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins male.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The key to finding the right hairstyle is to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and a slimmer face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Avoid Haircuts That Add Volume

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face can make your face look even rounder. Avoid styles that feature heavy layers or lots of volume on the sides.

2. Opt for Shorter Styles

Shorter hairstyles can help elongate your face and make your features appear more defined. Consider a classic crew cut, a buzz cut, or a short crop.

3. Create Height on Top

Creating height on top of your head can help balance out a round face. Consider a short spiky style or a messy quiff that adds volume and height to the crown of your head.

Short Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins Male

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's explore some short hairstyles that work well for men with round faces and double chins.

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for men with round faces. It's easy to maintain and creates a clean, sharp look. Keep the sides and back short and tapered, and leave a little more length on top for a more modern twist.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic short hairstyle that works well for men with round faces. It's a low-maintenance style that's easy to style and looks great on most face shapes. Keep the sides and back short and tapered, and leave a little more length on top to create some texture.

The Side Part

The side part is a versatile hairstyle that can work for a variety of face shapes, including round faces. Keep the sides and back short and neat, and create a deep side part to add some definition to your face.

The Short Quiff

The short quiff is a modern take on the classic quiff. It's a great option for men with round faces who want to add some height and volume to their hair. Keep the sides short and textured, and create some volume on top with a little bit of product.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've found the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that work well for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For short hairstyles, consider a lightweight wax or pomade that won't weigh your hair down.

2. Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. Visit Your Barber Regularly

Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. A regular trim can help maintain the shape of your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What if I don't like short hairstyles?

While short hairstyles can be flattering for men with round faces and double chins, they're not for everyone. If you prefer longer hair, consider a style that's longer on top and shorter on the sides, like a modern pompadour or a messy fringe.

Can I still wear a beard with a short hairstyle?

A beard can help balance out a round face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Consider a short, groomed beard that complements your hairstyle.

What if my hair is thinning?

If you're experiencing thinning hair, a shorter hairstyle can help create the illusion of thicker hair. Consider a buzz cut or a short crop that's easy to maintain and style.

Conclusion

There are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter men with round faces and double chins. By choosing the right style and taking care of your hair, you can create a look that's both stylish and flattering. Visit your barber regularly for trims and maintenance, and experiment with different products to find what works best for you.
