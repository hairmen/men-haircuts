How To Cut Side Bangs For Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your boring hairstyle and want to switch things up? One way to add some excitement to your look is by cutting side bangs. Side bangs can frame your face, add dimension to your hair, and give you a fresh new style. However, if you have thin hair, cutting bangs can seem daunting. But fear not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will share with you a step by step guide on how to cut side bangs for thin hair.

Description

Side bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle without making a drastic change. They can add some dimension to your hair and frame your face. However, for those with thin hair, cutting side bangs can seem tricky. But with the right technique, you can cut side bangs that will suit and flatter your thin hair.

How to Cut Side Bangs for Thin Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to choose the right length for your bangs. If you have thin hair, it’s best to go for longer side bangs. Short bangs can make your hair look even thinner. The length of your bangs should be around the middle of your forehead or just above your eyebrows.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair so that you only have the hair you want to cut in front of your face. Use a comb or your fingers to section your hair from the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Now it’s time to cut your bangs. Hold your scissors vertically and make small snips. Don’t cut too much at once. Keep checking your bangs to make sure they are even. If you want a more textured look, you can point cut your bangs.

Step 4: Blend Your Bangs

Once you’ve cut your bangs, it’s time to blend them with the rest of your hair. Use a comb to blend your bangs with the rest of your hair. You can also use a razor to add some texture to your bangs.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to cut your bangs
  • Don’t cut too much at once
  • Start with longer bangs and gradually make them shorter
  • Point cut your bangs for a more textured look
  • Blend your bangs with the rest of your hair to make them look natural

FAQ

Can I cut my own side bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own side bangs. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

What if I make a mistake?

If you accidentally cut too much of your bangs, don’t panic. You can always go to a hair salon to get them fixed.

How often do I need to trim my side bangs?

You should trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your side bangs looking their best, you should take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. You can also use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Cutting side bangs for thin hair can seem daunting, but with the right technique and some practice, you can achieve a great new look. Just remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. Good luck!
