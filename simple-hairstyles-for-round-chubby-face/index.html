Simple Hairstyles For Round Chubby Face A Complete Guide

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't suit your round chubby face? Don't worry! We have got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing some simple hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and boost your confidence. Here's a table of content of what we will cover:

Description

When it comes to hairstyles, one size doesn't fit all. A round chubby face requires a certain type of hairstyle to enhance its features and create an illusion of a slimmer face. The key is to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds more volume to the crown area.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Chubby Face

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you have a round chubby face. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

1. Look for hairstyles that add height to the crown area

Hairstyles that add height to the crown area create an illusion of a longer face, which is ideal for round chubby faces. Pixie cuts, layered bobs, and short shags are some of the hairstyles that add height to the crown area.

2. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face

Hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs and heavy bangs, can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that have layers and soft, wispy bangs.

3. Choose hairstyles that frame your face

Hairstyles that frame your face, such as long layered haircuts and side-swept bangs, draw attention to your best features and add dimension to your face.

4. Experiment with different hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. You can try different lengths, textures, and styles until you find the perfect one.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Simple Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let's take a look at some simple hairstyles that you can create at home:

1. Messy Bun

Step 1: Gather your hair into a high ponytail
Step 2: Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun
Step 3: Secure the bun with bobby pins
Step 4: Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look

2. Side Ponytail

Step 1: Part your hair to one side
Step 2: Gather your hair to the opposite side of the part
Step 3: Secure your hair with an elastic band
Step 4: Wrap a strand of hair around the elastic band to conceal it

3. Half-Up Half-Down

Step 1: Take a section of hair from the crown area
Step 2: Secure the section with an elastic band
Step 3: Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look
Step 4: Leave the rest of your hair down

Tips for Maintaining Simple Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face

Here are some tips for maintaining your simple hairstyles:

1. Use the right hair products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. This will help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Don't overdo it with heat styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. It will also help maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQs

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round chubby face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round chubby face. However, it's important to add some volume to the crown area to create an illusion of a longer face.

2. Can I wear a bob if I have a round chubby face?

Yes, you can wear a bob if you have a round chubby face. However, make sure it has layers and soft, wispy bangs to create an illusion of a slimmer face.

Hair Care Tips for Round Chubby Face

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture.

2. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy. You can make a hair mask at home using natural ingredients such as avocado, coconut oil, and honey.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen. The sun can damage your hair and make it look dry and lifeless.

There you have it, some simple hairstyles for round chubby face that you can create at home. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your personality. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
