How To Put Your Hair Up In A High Ponytail Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling on your face while you work out or go about your daily routine? A high ponytail is an easy and stylish solution to keep your hair out of your face. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to put your hair up in a high ponytail with ease.

How to Put Your Hair Up in a High Ponytail

Before we start, gather all the necessary tools you will need: a hairbrush, hair ties, bobby pins, and hair spray. Once you have everything you need, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair Thoroughly

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to put your hair up in a high ponytail.

Step 2: Decide on the Placement of Your Ponytail

Decide where you want to place your ponytail. A high ponytail is usually placed at the crown of your head. However, you can also place it slightly lower if that feels more comfortable for you.

Step 3: Secure Your Hair with a Hair Tie

Gather your hair at the desired location and secure it with a hair tie. Make sure it is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 4: Smooth Out Any Bumps or Lumps

Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair. You can also use a comb or brush to do this.

Step 5: Add Volume to Your Ponytail

If you want to add more volume to your ponytail, use a teasing brush or comb to tease the hair on the crown of your head. This will create the illusion of a fuller ponytail.

Step 6: Wrap a Small Strand of Hair Around the Hair Tie

To give your ponytail a polished look, take a small strand of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie. Secure the end of the strand with a bobby pin.

Step 7: Apply Hair Spray

To keep your ponytail in place, apply hairspray to your hair. This will help to prevent any flyaways or frizz.

Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail

To achieve the perfect high ponytail, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to add volume to your ponytail.
  • Wrap a strand of hair around the hair tie to give your ponytail a polished look.
  • Apply hairspray to prevent flyaways and frizz.

FAQs

How Do I Keep My High Ponytail from Sagging?

To prevent your high ponytail from sagging, make sure to secure it tightly with a hair tie. You can also use bobby pins to keep any loose strands in place.

What if My Hair is Too Short for a High Ponytail?

If your hair is too short for a high ponytail, try using hair extensions to add length. You can also try a half-up, half-down hairstyle instead.

Hair Care for a High Ponytail

A high ponytail can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face, but it can also cause damage if not done correctly. To prevent damage, make sure to:

  • Use a hair tie that does not damage your hair.
  • Do not tie your hair too tightly.
  • Do not wear your hair in a high ponytail for extended periods.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent breakage.

And there you have it! A simple and stylish way to put your hair up in a high ponytail. Follow these steps and tips for a perfect high ponytail every time.
