How To Apply Product To Curly Hair

Written by Christine Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that finding the right products to use can be a challenge. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to figure out what works best for your hair type. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to apply product to curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Product
  • How to Wash Your Hair
  • How to Apply Product
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Product

Before you start applying products to your hair, it's important to choose the right ones for your hair type. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will contain ingredients that help define and tame your curls. Some key ingredients to look for include shea butter, coconut oil, and jojoba oil.

It's also important to consider the thickness and texture of your hair when choosing products. If you have fine hair, you may want to look for lighter products that won't weigh your curls down. If you have thick hair, you may need heavier products to help control frizz and define your curls.

How to Wash Your Hair

Before you apply any products, it's important to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils, and follow up with a conditioner that's designed for curly hair.

When you're washing your hair, be gentle. Don't rub your hair vigorously or use hot water, as this can damage your curls and cause frizz. Instead, use lukewarm water and gently massage your scalp to help distribute the shampoo.

How to Apply Product

Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, it's time to apply product. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  4. If you have thick hair, you may want to use a gel or mousse to help control frizz.
  5. Scrunch your hair to help enhance your curls.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.

Remember to be gentle when applying product. Don't use too much or you'll end up with greasy, weighed-down hair.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Now that you know how to apply product to your curly hair, here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or pineapple to help preserve your curls overnight.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about applying products to curly hair:

Can I use the same products on my curly hair as I do on my straight hair?

No, you should use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Curly hair requires different ingredients to help define and control the curls.

How often should I apply product to my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and the products you're using. Some people may need to apply product every day, while others may only need to apply it every few days.

How can I prevent my curls from getting frizzy?

Use a product that's designed to control frizz, such as a gel or mousse. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or pineapple to help prevent friction and breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to using the right products and applying them correctly, it's important to take good care of your curly hair. Here are some tips for keeping your curls healthy and beautiful:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking its best. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
