How To Stop Hair Loss After Postpartum

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a new mom struggling with hair loss after giving birth? You're not alone. Postpartum hair loss occurs in many women due to hormonal changes in the body. But don't worry, there are steps you can take to prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair loss after postpartum.

How to Stop Hair Loss After Postpartum

The first step in preventing hair loss after postpartum is to focus on your diet. Consuming a healthy and balanced diet is essential for hair growth. Make sure to include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, biotin, zinc, and vitamin C. Foods such as spinach, eggs, salmon, and berries are great options.

In addition to a healthy diet, there are other steps you can take to prevent hair loss after postpartum:

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, and buns as they can pull on the hair and lead to breakage. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles such as a messy bun or a loose braid.

2. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Use gentle hair care products that are free from harsh chemicals such as sulfates and parabens. These chemicals can strip the hair of its natural oils and lead to hair breakage.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp in circular motions for a few minutes each day.

4. Take Supplements

Taking supplements can help prevent hair loss after postpartum. Supplements such as biotin, iron, and vitamin D are great options for promoting hair growth.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair loss after postpartum:

1. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall health and can also help prevent hair loss. Aim for at least 7-8 hours of sleep each night.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can lead to hair loss, so it's important to manage stress levels. Try practicing relaxation techniques such as meditation or yoga.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is important for overall health and can also help prevent hair loss. Aim for at least 8 glasses of water each day.

FAQs

Q: How long does postpartum hair loss last?

A: Postpartum hair loss can last up to 6 months, but it typically resolves on its own.

Q: Can breastfeeding cause hair loss?

A: Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair loss due to hormonal changes in the body.

Q: Can hair loss after postpartum be permanent?

A: No, hair loss after postpartum is usually temporary and resolves on its own.

Best Hair Care Practices

Here are some best hair care practices for preventing hair loss after postpartum:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help remove buildup and prevent hair loss. Aim to wash your hair at least every other day.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will help prevent hair breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage the hair and lead to breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips and steps, you can prevent hair loss after postpartum and promote hair growth. Remember, postpartum hair loss is normal and temporary, so be patient and take good care of your hair.
