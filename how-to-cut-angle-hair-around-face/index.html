How to Cut Angle Hair Around Face - Step by Step Guide

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, cutting your hair at an angle around your face is a great way to add dimension and style. However, cutting your own hair can be scary, especially if you're not used to it. In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut angle hair around your face in easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Angle Hair Around Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Angle Hair Around Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Angle Hair Around Face

Description

Angle hair is a style that involves cutting hair at an angle, creating layers and movement. Cutting your hair at an angle around your face can frame your face and emphasize your best features. It's a great way to add a new look to your hairstyle without changing the length of your hair.

How to Cut Angle Hair Around Face

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb and hair clips to section your hair. It's also important to work on clean, dry hair.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by parting your hair down the middle and clipping the top section away from your face.


2. Take a small section of hair from the side of your face that you want to cut first.


3. Hold the hair between your fingers, at a 45-degree angle away from your face. This will create the angle you want for your hair.


4. Use your scissors to cut the hair at the angle you've created.


5. Repeat steps 2-4 for the other side of your face.


6. Unclip the top section of your hair and repeat steps 2-4 for any sections of hair that frame your face.


7. Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a comb to blend the layers together.

Tips for Cutting Angle Hair Around Face

- Always cut your hair in small sections to ensure you have control over the cut.


- Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts.


- Cut your hair at an angle that complements your face shape.


- If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with small cuts and work your way up.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can stretch and make it difficult to cut accurately.


Q: How often should I trim my angle hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care After Cutting Angle Hair Around Face

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using heat tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting your hair at an angle around your face can add a new dimension to your hairstyle. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect angle hair around your face!
