How To Know Which Hairstyle Fits You Perfectly

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type can be a challenge. With so many options available, it can be hard to know where to start. But, by following these simple tips, you can easily find a hairstyle that suits you perfectly.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Know Which Hairstyle Fits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Know Which Hairstyle Fits You

Knowing which hairstyle suits you best can be tricky, but it all comes down to a few key factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair type
  • Your lifestyle and personality

By considering these factors, you can easily find a hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your style.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to find the perfect hairstyle for you:

  1. Determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Look in the mirror and compare your face to these shapes to determine which one you have.
  2. Consider your hair type. Is your hair straight, wavy, or curly? Is it thick or thin? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that works for you.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and personality. Do you have a high-energy job that requires a low-maintenance hairstyle? Are you more of a free spirit who likes to experiment with different looks? Your lifestyle and personality should factor into your decision.
  4. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, websites, and social media for inspiration. Save photos of hairstyles you like and bring them to your stylist for reference.
  5. Consult with a stylist. A professional stylist can help you determine which hairstyle will work best for you based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your new look.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Be realistic. Choose a hairstyle that you can realistically maintain on a daily basis.
  • Consider your age. As you age, your face shape and hair type may change. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your current features.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. It's okay to try new things and step out of your comfort zone.
  • Ask for advice. Consult with friends, family, and your stylist for their opinions and advice.

FAQs

What hairstyle suits a round face?

A round face looks best with hairstyles that add height and length. Try a long bob or a side-swept bang to elongate your face.

What hairstyle suits an oval face?

An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off most hairstyles. Try a layered cut or a blunt bob for a classic look.

What hairstyle suits a square face?

A square face looks best with hairstyles that soften the angles of your face. Try a side-swept bang or a textured bob to add some movement to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Choose hair products that are free of harsh chemicals and sulfates.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily find a hairstyle that complements your natural beauty and enhances your personal style. Remember to be patient, be open to trying new things, and most importantly, have fun!
