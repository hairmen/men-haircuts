Best Volumizing Mousse For Oily Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have oily hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right products that won't weigh your hair down. One of the best solutions for adding volume to oily hair is using a volumizing mousse. However, not all mousses are created equal, and finding the right one can be overwhelming. In this guide, we will explore the best volumizing mousse for oily hair and provide tips on how to use it effectively.

What is Volumizing Mousse?

Volumizing mousse is a hair styling product that adds volume and body to your hair. It is typically applied to damp hair and then blow-dried to create a fuller, more voluminous look. Most mousses contain polymers that coat the hair shaft, making it appear thicker and fuller.

How Does Volumizing Mousse Work on Oily Hair?

Oily hair tends to be limp and lack volume due to the excess oil on the scalp. Volumizing mousse works by adding texture and lift to the hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous. The polymers in the mousse also help absorb excess oil and give the hair a matte finish, making it less greasy.

How to Use Volumizing Mousse for Oily Hair

Here are some tips on how to use volumizing mousse for oily hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Shake the mousse can well and dispense a small amount into your palm.
  3. Rub your hands together to distribute the mousse evenly.
  4. Apply the mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.
  6. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

Tips for Using Volumizing Mousse on Oily Hair

Here are some additional tips for using volumizing mousse on oily hair:

  1. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove excess oil and buildup from your hair before applying the mousse.
  2. Avoid applying the mousse directly to your scalp, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  3. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume and body to your hair.
  4. Don't use too much mousse, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  5. Use a lightweight, oil-free hairspray to hold the style without adding additional oil to your hair.

FAQ About Volumizing Mousse for Oily Hair

Q: Can I use volumizing mousse on dry hair?

A: It is best to use volumizing mousse on damp hair, as this allows the product to penetrate the hair shaft and create volume.

Q: How often should I use volumizing mousse on oily hair?

A: You can use volumizing mousse on oily hair as often as needed, but it is best to avoid using it every day. Overuse of styling products can cause buildup and make your hair look greasy.

Q: Can volumizing mousse damage my hair?

A: Volumizing mousse is generally safe to use on all hair types, but overuse of the product can cause buildup and damage to your hair. It is important to use the product as directed and avoid using too much.

Hair Care Tips for Oily Hair

In addition to using volumizing mousse, here are some general hair care tips for oily hair:

  1. Wash your hair every other day to prevent excess oil buildup.
  2. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and excess oil from your hair.
  3. Avoid using heavy, oil-based hair products that can weigh your hair down.
  4. Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and extend the time between washes.
  5. Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can transfer oil from your hands to your hair.

In conclusion, volumizing mousse can be a game-changer for adding volume and body to oily hair. With the right product and application techniques, you can achieve a fuller, more voluminous look without weighing your hair down.
