How To Do Bangs Curly Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, and bangs can add a touch of style to any look. But, creating bangs on curly hair can be a challenge. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to do bangs curly hair in a relaxed English language. We will cover everything you need to know, including how to do it, tips, hair care, and FAQ. So, let's get started!

How to Do Bangs Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need. Here's a list of what you'll need to get started:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips
  • Scissors
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to create bangs on curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products suitable for curly hair. Dry your hair with a microfiber towel.
  2. Using a wide-tooth comb, comb your hair to detangle it.
  3. Part your hair in the middle or to your desired side. Use clips to hold the rest of your hair back.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline, and using your fingers, twist it around to create a small curl.
  5. Hold the curl with one hand and use the other hand to cut the hair at the desired length. Remember to cut less hair than you think you'll need.
  6. Repeat the process with the other side of your hairline, ensuring that the length is the same on both sides.
  7. Release the rest of your hair and style as desired. You can use hair spray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Perfect Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind while creating bangs on curly hair:

  • Start with a small section of hair to avoid cutting too much.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry to ensure that you don't cut too much.
  • Cut your hair gradually, checking the length after each cut.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating bangs on curly hair:

Q: Can I create bangs on my own?

A: Yes, you can create bangs on your own. Just follow the step-by-step guide above.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. You can trim your bangs every two to three weeks or as needed.

Q: Can I straighten my bangs?

A: Yes, you can straighten your bangs if you want to. Just remember to use a heat protectant before using any heat tools.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use products specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners, curling irons, and hair dryers.
  • Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.

Creating bangs on curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, it's possible. Follow the step-by-step guide above and use the tips and hair care advice to keep your curls looking their best.
