How To Cut Men s Medium Long Hair With Scissors

Written by Christine Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to cut your own or someone else's medium or long hair with scissors? It can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps for cutting men's medium/long hair with scissors.

Description

Men's medium/long hair can be difficult to cut, especially if you're not a professional. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve a great haircut with just scissors. This guide will take you through how to cut men's medium/long hair with scissors, step by step.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also helpful to have a mirror and a hair clip to keep hair out of the way.

Step 1: Start by wetting the hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and will help you see any unevenness in the hair.

Step 2: Use the comb to part the hair where you want to start cutting. It's best to start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to trim the hair at an angle. It's important to cut at an angle to avoid creating a blunt edge.

Step 4: Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up the head. Make sure to cut evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Once you've cut all the hair, use the comb to blend the layers together. This will create a more natural look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid any pulling or tugging on the hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Blend the layers together for a more natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

What kind of scissors should I use?

Use a pair of sharp scissors designed for cutting hair. This will ensure a clean cut without any pulling or tugging on the hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut men's medium/long hair with scissors. Remember to take your time and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
