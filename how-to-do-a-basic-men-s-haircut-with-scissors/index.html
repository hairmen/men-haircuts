How To Do A Basic Men s Haircut With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts and want to try cutting your own hair at home? With a little practice and the right tools, you can easily do a basic men's haircut with scissors. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps and provide helpful tips to achieve a professional-looking cut.

How To

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair-cutting scissors, a comb, hair clippers (optional), and a mirror.

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length.

Step 3: Use the comb to separate the hair on top of your head from the trimmed sides and back.

Step 4: Take a small section of hair from the top and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.

Step 5: Move on to the next section of hair and repeat until you've cut all the hair on top of your head.

Step 6: Blend the hair on top with the trimmed sides and back by combing the hair down and cutting any stray hairs.

Step 7: Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs around the ears and neckline.

Step 8: Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great-looking haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the comb to guide your scissors and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process. It's better to make small cuts and adjust as you go.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with hard-to-reach areas like the back of your head.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

A: No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and may result in uneven or damaged hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and desired length, but most men need a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always visit a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling with a blow dryer or flat iron. Regular trims will also help keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently do a basic men's haircut with scissors at home. Practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment and try new styles. Happy cutting!
