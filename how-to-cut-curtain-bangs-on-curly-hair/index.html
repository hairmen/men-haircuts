How To Cut Curtain Bangs On Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair getting in the way of your bangs? Curtain bangs are a great solution for curly hair, as they frame your face without getting in the way. Cutting your own curtain bangs can be daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily at home. In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to cut curtain bangs on curly hair.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Curtain Bangs on Curly Hair

Curtain bangs are a trendy and flattering hairstyle that work well with curly hair. Here’s what you’ll need to get started:

  • Sharp hair-cutting scissors
  • Claw clips or hair ties
  • A wide-tooth comb

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. It’s also a good idea to start with a longer length, as you can always cut more if needed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning the hair you want to cut into a triangular shape. Use the wide-tooth comb to create a middle part and separate the hair into two sections. Clip the back section out of the way with a claw clip or hair tie.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length of your curtain bangs. Hold the front section of your hair at the desired length and cut the hair straight across. Take your time and cut slowly to avoid mistakes.

Step 3: Create the Curtain Effect

Take a small section of hair from each side of the front section and pull them forward. Use the length of the front section as a guide and cut the side sections at the same length. This will create the curtain effect.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Release the back section of your hair and check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to the length of your bangs.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs on Curly Hair

Tip 1: Start with a Longer Length

It’s always better to start with a longer length and cut more if needed. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can’t add hair back once it’s been cut.

Tip 2: Use Sharp Scissors

Sharp scissors will make your job a lot easier and give you a cleaner cut. Dull scissors can create split ends and make your hair look frizzy.

Tip 3: Cut Slowly

Take your time and cut slowly to avoid making mistakes. It’s better to cut a little at a time than to cut too much at once.

FAQs

Q: Should I cut my hair when it’s wet or dry?

A: It’s best to cut curly hair when it’s dry. Cutting curly hair when it’s wet can cause it to shrink up, resulting in a shorter cut than anticipated.

Q: Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

A: Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

Hair Care for Curly Hair with Curtain Bangs

Now that you’ve cut your curtain bangs, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut curtain bangs on curly hair at home. Remember to take your time and use the right tools, and you’ll have a trendy and flattering new hairstyle in no time.
