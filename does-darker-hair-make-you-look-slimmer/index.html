Does Darker Hair Make You Look Slimmer

Written by Robby Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hair color, there are a lot of myths and misconceptions floating around. One of the most popular beliefs is that darker hair makes you look slimmer. But is this really true? In this article, we'll take a closer look at the science behind this claim and give you some tips on how to choose the best hair color for your body shape.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

It's a common belief that darker hair makes you look slimmer. The idea behind this is that darker colors create more contrast between the hair and the face, which can make the face appear slimmer. In addition, darker hair can draw attention away from problem areas, such as a double chin or chubby cheeks.

While there is some truth to these claims, it's important to remember that hair color alone won't make you look slimmer. Your overall body shape, clothing choices, and posture all play a role in how you are perceived by others.

How to

If you're looking to use hair color to create the illusion of a slimmer face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a color that complements your skin tone. This will help to create a natural, cohesive look that doesn't draw too much attention to your hair.
  • Avoid drastic color changes. Going from blonde to black in one go can be jarring and may not look natural. Gradual changes, such as going from light brown to dark brown, are often more flattering.
  • Consider getting highlights. Highlights can add depth and dimension to your hair, which can help to create a slimming effect.
  • Consult a professional. If you're not sure what color will look best on you, or if you're considering a drastic change, it's always a good idea to consult a stylist.

Step by step

If you've decided to go darker with your hair color, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a color that complements your skin tone
  2. Decide on the level of darkness you want
  3. Consider getting highlights to add dimension
  4. Find a reputable stylist to do the job
  5. Maintain your new color with regular touch-ups and proper hair care

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Remember that hair color alone won't make you look slimmer. You'll still need to pay attention to your overall body shape and clothing choices.
  • Consider using makeup to contour your face and create a slimming effect.
  • Experiment with different shades of your chosen color to find the one that looks best on you.
  • Be prepared for some maintenance. Darker hair colors may require more frequent touch-ups to maintain the look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether darker hair makes you look slimmer:

Q: Will dark hair make my face look slimmer?

A: It's possible, but it's not a guarantee. Your overall body shape, clothing choices, and posture all play a role in how you are perceived by others.

Q: What's the best hair color for a round face?

A: While there is no one "best" hair color for a round face, darker colors can create more contrast and draw attention away from problem areas.

Q: Can I go from blonde to black in one go?

A: While it's technically possible, it's not recommended. Gradual changes, such as going from light brown to dark brown, are often more flattering and look more natural.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

So, does darker hair make you look slimmer? The answer is yes, but with some caveats. While darker hair can create the illusion of a slimmer face, it's not a magic solution. Your overall body shape, clothing choices, and posture all play a role in how you are perceived by others. That said, choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone and adds depth and dimension can be a great way to enhance your natural beauty and feel more confident in your appearance.
