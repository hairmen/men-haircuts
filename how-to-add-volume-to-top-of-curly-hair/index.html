How To Add Volume To Top Of Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless curls? Do you struggle to get volume at the top of your head? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with curly hair struggle to add volume at the roots. But with the right techniques and products, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy curls that last all day. In this article, we'll show you how to add volume to the top of curly hair with simple tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Add Volume to Top of Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Add Volume to Top of Curly Hair

The key to adding volume at the roots of your curly hair is to create lift and support. Here are some simple steps you can follow:

1. Use the Right Products

Start with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a boost. Look for products that are designed for fine or thin hair, as these will help to add volume without weighing your hair down.

2. Apply Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots, working it in with your fingers. This will give your hair lift and support, helping to create volume at the top of your head.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots. This will help to create volume and prevent your hair from falling flat.

4. Use a Diffuser

If you have naturally curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and create volume. This will help to distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for voluminous, bouncy curls:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently squeeze out excess water.

2. Apply Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of volumizing mousse to your roots, working it in with your fingers. This will help to create lift and support.

3. Flip Your Head Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and tangling.

4. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer on low heat, dry your hair in sections, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

5. Use a Diffuser

If you have naturally curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and create volume. Hold the diffuser close to your roots and scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for adding volume to the top of curly hair:

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add extra lift.
  • Avoid heavy, oil-based styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun on the top of your head to create volume while you sleep.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can go longer between washes. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Can I add volume to curly hair without using heat?

Absolutely! You can try using a volumizing mousse or foam and letting your hair air dry. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer on low heat.

Hair Care for Voluminous Curls

To keep your curly hair looking voluminous and healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as relaxers and perms, which can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can add volume to the top of curly hair and achieve bouncy, voluminous curls that last all day. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type and texture. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at adding volume to your curly hair in no time!
