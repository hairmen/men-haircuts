How To Fix Medium Layered Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to style your medium layered hair? Don't worry, we've got you covered! Here are some tips and tricks to fix your hair and make it look amazing.

Description

Medium layered hair is one of the most popular hairstyles today. It's versatile, easy to style, and looks great on most face shapes. However, fixing it can be challenging, especially if you don't know where to start. This blog post will guide you on how to fix medium layered hair with ease.

How To

Fixing medium layered hair can be done in many ways. Here are some ways to style your hair:

1. Straighten your hair

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, straightening your hair can give you a sleek and polished look. Use a straightener to smooth out your hair and apply a heat protectant before styling.

2. Add curls

Adding curls to your hair can give you a soft and romantic look. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or tight curls, depending on your preference. Don't forget to apply a heat protectant before styling.

3. Create volume

If you have thin or flat hair, adding volume can make a huge difference. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to create volume at the roots. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fix medium layered hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel dry your hair and comb it gently to remove any tangles.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse, gel, or serum, to your hair to help hold the style. Use a small amount and distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape. Use a diffuser attachment to create curls or waves.

4. Style your hair

Style your hair according to your preference. You can straighten your hair, curl it, or create waves. Use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix your medium layered hair:

1. Use the right products

Use products that suit your hair type and the style you want to create. For example, use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools, and use a volumizing product to create volume.

2. Don't overdo it

Don't use too much product or style your hair too often, as it can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy.

3. Get a regular trim

Get a regular trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is medium layered hair?

Medium layered hair is a hairstyle that features layers of different lengths, starting from the chin to the shoulders. It's a popular hairstyle that suits most face shapes.

How do I fix my medium layered hair?

To fix your medium layered hair, start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a styling product and blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Style your hair according to your preference, and use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

What products should I use to fix my medium layered hair?

Use products that suit your hair type and the style you want to create. For example, use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools, and use a volumizing product to create volume.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools, such as a straightener or curling iron. This will help prevent damage to your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix your medium layered hair and make it look amazing. Remember to take care of your hair regularly to keep it healthy and looking great.
