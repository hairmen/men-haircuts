How To Wear Short Hair Off Your Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Oct 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have short hair, you know how challenging it can be to keep it off your face. It's frustrating when your hair keeps falling into your eyes, especially during hot and humid weather. Fortunately, there are many ways to style short hair off your face, whether you have a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to wear short hair off your face, so you can look and feel your best.

How to Wear Short Hair off Your Face

There are several ways to wear short hair off your face, depending on your hair type, length, and personal style. Here are some popular options:

1. Side Part

A side part is a classic and effortless way to keep short hair off your face. Use a comb or your fingers to create a deep side part, and sweep your hair to one side. You can add a hairpin or a hair clip to secure the hair behind your ear.

2. Headband

A headband is a stylish and practical accessory that can keep your short hair in place. Choose a thin or wide headband that matches your outfit, and place it on your forehead. You can wear the headband over your hair or push it back slightly to create a mini bouffant.

3. Bobby Pins

Bobby pins are a lifesaver when it comes to styling short hair. Use them to create a half-up hairstyle, a twisted bun, or a braided crown. You can also use bobby pins to pin back your bangs or side pieces.

4. Top Knot

A top knot is a chic and easy way to keep your short hair off your face. Gather your hair on top of your head, twist it into a bun, and secure it with a hair tie. You can leave some strands loose for a messy look or slick them back with a pomade or a hair gel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear short hair off your face:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with products that suit your hair type. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream to add moisture and hold.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush or a paddle brush. Create volume at the roots and smooth the ends. You can also air-dry your hair if you prefer a natural look.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

Select a style that suits your face shape and personal taste. You can try a side part, a headband, bobby pins, a top knot, or other styles.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Create your chosen style using a comb, a brush, or your fingers. Use hairpins, hair ties, or hair accessories to secure your hair in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hairspray to keep it in place. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it greasy.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you wear short hair off your face:

1. Use Dry Shampoo

If your hair tends to get oily or greasy, use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add texture. Spray a small amount of dry shampoo on your roots and massage it in with your fingers.

2. Experiment with Accessories

Try different hair accessories, such as headbands, barrettes, scrunchies, or scarves. They can add color, texture, and interest to your hairstyle.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace your natural texture and use products that enhance it. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create defined curls or waves.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear short hair off my face if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can. Try a side part or a low bun to create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Q: How do I keep my short hair from falling into my eyes?

A: Use bobby pins or hair clips to pin back your hair, or try a headband or a scarf to keep it in place.

Q: Can I wear short hair off my face if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, you can. Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to add body and texture to your hair. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires special care to stay healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Use Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Choose a sulfate-free shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Sulfates can strip away natural oils and cause dryness and irritation.

2. Condition Regularly

Apply a conditioner or a hair mask to your hair once a week to add moisture and nourishment. Leave it on for at least 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons, curling irons, or blow dryers, too often. If you need to use them, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair first.

4. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients, such as protein, iron, and omega-3 fatty acids. These nutrients can promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear short hair off your face with confidence and style. Experiment with different styles and accessories, and don't be afraid to try new things. With a little creativity and care, you can rock any short hairstyle.
