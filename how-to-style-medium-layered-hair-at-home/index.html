How To Style Medium Layered Hair At Home

Written by Christine Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Whether you have just gotten a fresh cut or you are looking for new ways to style your medium layered hair, you have come to the right place. Styling your hair at home can be a fun and easy way to switch up your look without breaking the bank. In this article, we will go over some tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions on how to style medium layered hair at home.

Description

Medium layered hair is a versatile and popular haircut that can be styled in many ways. This haircut features layers of varying lengths that create volume and movement in the hair. Styling medium layered hair at home can be easy and quick once you know the right techniques and products to use. With a few simple steps, you can create a variety of looks that are perfect for any occasion.

How to Style Medium Layered Hair at Home

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in styling your medium layered hair is to start with clean hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type to ensure that your hair is healthy and nourished. Towel-dry your hair and detangle it gently with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 2: Apply Hair Products

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair before using any heat styling tools. This will protect your hair from heat damage and keep it looking healthy. Depending on the style you want to achieve, you may also want to apply a styling mousse, gel, or serum to add volume, texture, or hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. For extra volume, flip your hair upside down and blow dry it in that position.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Flat Iron

If you want to add curls or waves to your hair, use a curling iron or flat iron to create the desired look. Start at the roots and wrap your hair around the iron, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. For a more natural look, alternate the direction of the curls and use different sizes of curling irons or flat irons.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques.
  • Invest in high-quality heat styling tools to ensure that your hair stays healthy and damage-free.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft, shiny, and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my medium layered hair without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can style your hair using braids, twists, or buns to create a variety of looks without using heat styling tools.

Q: How often should I wash my medium layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how active you are. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair, it is important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends. With proper care and styling, your medium layered hair can look beautiful and stylish all year long.
