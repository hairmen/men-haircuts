Short Hair Styles For Round Fat Faces Flattering And Stylish Options

Are you tired of hiding behind long locks and looking for a short hair style that flatters your round fat face? Look no further! In this article, we will explore a variety of short hair styles that are perfect for round fat faces. From how to style your hair to hair care tips, we have got you covered.

Description

Short hair styles are an excellent choice for women with round fat faces as they can help to create an illusion of length and slimness. By choosing the right short hair style, you can accentuate your best features and draw attention away from areas you may want to downplay.

When choosing a short hair style for a round fat face, it is important to consider the shape of your face, hair texture, and personal style. Some short hair styles may not work for everyone, but with the right cut and styling techniques, you can find a short hair style that works for you.

How to Choose the Best Short Hair Style for Your Round Fat Face

When choosing a short hair style for your round fat face, keep in mind that your goal is to create the illusion of length and slimness. Consider the following tips:

  • Choose a short hair style that has layers or texture to add volume to the top of your head and create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider a side-swept bang to draw attention away from the width of your face.
  • Choose a hair style that highlights your best features, such as your eyes or cheekbones.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a short hair style. If you have fine hair, a pixie cut may work well for you. If you have thick hair, a bob may be a better option.

Short Hair Style Ideas for Round Fat Faces

Here are some short hair style ideas that are perfect for round fat faces:

  • Pixie cut with textured layers
  • Bob with layers and a side-swept bang
  • Asymmetrical bob with longer layers on one side
  • Long bob with angled layers
  • Short shag with choppy layers

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy. Follow these steps for a polished and stylish look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add curls or waves if desired.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair Style

To keep your short hair style looking fresh and stylish, consider the following tips:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Can I still wear a short hair style if I have a round fat face?

Absolutely! With the right cut and styling techniques, a short hair style can be very flattering for women with round fat faces. Just be sure to choose a style that adds volume to the top of your head and creates the illusion of length.

What hair texture works best for short hair styles?

Short hair styles can work well for a variety of hair textures, but the style you choose may depend on your hair type. If you have fine hair, a pixie cut may work well for you. If you have thick hair, a bob or an angled cut may be a better option.

How often should I visit my stylist for a trim?

To maintain your short hair style, it is recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. This will help to keep your shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Styles

Short hair styles can be low maintenance, but it is still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Consider the following hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or serum to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.

In conclusion, short hair styles can be a great option for women with round fat faces. By choosing the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering and stylish look that highlights your best features. Remember to visit your stylist regularly, take care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
