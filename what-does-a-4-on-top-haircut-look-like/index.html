What Does A 4 On Top Haircut Look Like

Written by Christine Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about what a 4 on top haircut looks like. If you are looking for a new haircut and wondering what a 4 on top haircut is, then you have come to the right place. In this post, we will explain everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, what it looks like, and how to take care of it.

Description

A 4 on top haircut is a popular hairstyle among men that features hair that is cut to four inches in length on the top of the head. The hair on the sides and back is usually shorter, with a fade or taper that blends the hair from short to long. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

How to Get a 4 on Top Haircut

If you are interested in getting a 4 on top haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can give you the cut you want. You should bring a picture of the haircut you like to your appointment, as this will help your barber understand what you are looking for. During your appointment, your barber will use clippers and scissors to cut your hair to the desired length on top and taper the sides and back to your preference.

Step by Step

1. Find a skilled barber or stylist.

2. Bring a picture of the haircut you want.

3. Communicate with your barber about what you want.

4. Sit back and let your barber work their magic.

5. Style your hair at home using your favorite products.

Tips

If you want to keep your 4 on top haircut looking great, there are a few tips you should keep in mind. First, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Second, use high-quality hair products to style your hair and keep it looking healthy. Finally, experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What face shapes does a 4 on top haircut suit?

A 4 on top haircut can suit many different face shapes, but it is particularly flattering on those with oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces.

How often should I get a 4 on top haircut?

You should get a 4 on top haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

What products should I use to style my 4 on top haircut?

You should use high-quality hair products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to style your 4 on top haircut. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair type and styling needs.

Hair Care

To keep your 4 on top haircut looking great, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. If you follow these tips, your 4 on top haircut will look great for weeks to come.

Conclusion

A 4 on top haircut is a stylish and versatile haircut that looks great on many different men. To get this haircut, you should find a skilled barber or stylist, bring a picture of the haircut you want, and communicate clearly about your preferences. With regular trims and high-quality hair products, you can keep your 4 on top haircut looking great for weeks to come.
