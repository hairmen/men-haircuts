How To Give Boy Haircut With Scissors A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a parent or a hairstylist, giving a boy a haircut with scissors can be a bit intimidating. But with the right technique and tools, it can be a simple process. In this guide, we'll take you through each step of how to give a boy's haircut with scissors.

Description

A boy's haircut with scissors is a classic haircut that can be done at home or in a salon. It involves using scissors to cut the hair to a desired length and style. This type of haircut is perfect for boys of all ages and hair types.

How to Give Boy Haircut with Scissors

Step 1: Gather the necessary tools

Before starting, make sure you have all the necessary tools for the haircut. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair.

Step 2: Decide on the haircut style

Before you start cutting, decide on the style you want to give the boy. Look at pictures online or in magazines for inspiration. You could also consider asking the boy what style he wants.

Step 3: Wash and dry the hair

Before cutting the hair, wash it with shampoo and conditioner. Then, use a towel to dry the hair thoroughly. If the hair is too wet, it will be difficult to cut.

Step 4: Section the hair

Use hair clips to section the hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward. This will make it easier to cut each section of hair.

Step 5: Start cutting the hair

Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and straight.

Step 6: Continue cutting the hair

Work your way around the head, cutting each section of hair to the desired length. Make sure to take your time and cut the hair evenly. If you're unsure about cutting a certain section of hair, leave it longer and come back to it later.

Step 7: Blend the hair

To blend the hair, use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle. This will help to create a more natural look. Work your way around the head, blending the hair as you go.

Step 8: Trim the sides and back

Use the scissors to trim the sides and back of the head. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 9: Finish the haircut

Once you've finished cutting the hair, use the comb to check for any uneven sections. If you find any, trim them with the scissors. Then, style the hair as desired.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors for a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut the hair evenly.
  • Don't be afraid to leave the hair a bit longer if you're unsure about cutting it.
  • Blending the hair is important for a natural look.
  • Always check the hair for any uneven sections.

FAQ

How often should I give my boy a haircut?

It depends on the boy's hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it's recommended to give a boy a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

What if I make a mistake while cutting the hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting the hair, don't panic. You can always trim the hair a bit shorter or try to blend it in with the rest of the hair.

Can I use clippers instead of scissors?

Yes, you can use clippers instead of scissors. However, clippers are better for shorter haircuts and may not work as well for longer styles.

Hair Care

After giving a boy a haircut, it's important to take care of the hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or a styling product to keep the hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to give a boy's haircut with scissors, you can give your little boy a fresh new look any time. Just remember to take your time and cut the hair evenly for the best results.
