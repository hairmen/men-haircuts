How To Cut Layered Side Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look, why not try cutting layered side bangs? This style is perfect for those who want to add some movement and volume to their hair, while still keeping some length. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to cut layered side bangs at home. Here's what you'll learn:

Description

Layered side bangs are a great way to add some interest to your hair without committing to a full-on haircut. This style consists of longer layers that are cut at an angle to create a âVâ shape, which frames your face and adds volume to your hair. The side bangs are cut at an angle to blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

How to Cut Layered Side Bangs

Before you start cutting, you'll need a few supplies:

  • Sharp hair-cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own layered side bangs:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Part your hair where you normally wear it. If you usually wear a middle part, part your hair down the center. If you wear a side part, part your hair on the side that you prefer.
  3. Brush your hair forward so that your bangs are in your face. Use your comb to separate the section of hair that you want to cut as your bangs.
  4. Hold the section of hair that you want to cut between your index and middle finger, and bring it to the front of your face.
  5. Take your scissors and cut the hair at an angle. Start at the outer edge of your eyebrows and cut downwards towards the tip of your nose. Be sure to cut at an angle so that the hair is longer towards the back of your head and shorter towards the front.
  6. Repeat this process on the other side.
  7. Trim the edges of your side bangs until they are the desired length.
  8. Blend your side bangs into the rest of your hair by cutting some longer layers into the sides of your hair.
  9. Use your comb to check that your bangs are even on both sides.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Layered Side Bangs

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own layered side bangs:

  • Start with small cuts and trim more if necessary. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back!
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry. Wet hair can shrink when it dries, which can leave your bangs shorter than you intended.
  • Take your time and don't rush. It's better to take your time and get it right than to rush and end up with uneven bangs.

FAQ

Do I need to have a certain hair type to have layered side bangs?

No, layered side bangs can work for any hair type, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How often should I trim my side bangs?

It's best to trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my side bangs myself?

Yes, you can cut your own side bangs at home by following the steps outlined in this article.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Side Bangs

Now that you've cut your layered side bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools on your hair.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting layered side bangs is a great way to change up your look without making a drastic change. With the right tools and some patience, you can achieve this style at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and take care of your hair to keep your layered side bangs looking their best.
