How To Air Dry Wavy Hair Without Frizz A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to dry it without frizz. Blow drying can be damaging, and using a hair dryer can leave your hair feeling dry and brittle. Luckily, there’s a solution: air drying. In this guide, we’ll show you how to air dry wavy hair without frizz.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Air Dry Wavy Hair Without Frizz
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Air Drying Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Air Dry Wavy Hair Without Frizz

The key to air drying wavy hair without frizz is to start with wet hair that has been washed and conditioned. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, gently towel dry your hair by blotting it with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream to your hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots. This will help to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying as this can cause frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help speed up the drying process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to air drying wavy hair without frizz:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair by blotting it with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream to your hair
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair
  5. Let your hair air dry naturally

Tips for Air Drying Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you air dry wavy hair without frizz:

  • Avoid touching your hair while it's drying
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream to keep your hair hydrated
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help speed up the drying process
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet as this can cause breakage and frizz

FAQs

How long does it take to air dry wavy hair?

The amount of time it takes to air dry wavy hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to 2 hours.

Can I use a hair dryer to speed up the drying process?

If you want to use a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment on low heat to help speed up the drying process. Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat as this can cause damage to your hair.

What type of products should I use to prevent frizz?

Use a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of wavy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Air drying wavy hair without frizz is possible with the right technique and products. Follow these steps and tips to achieve beautiful, frizz-free waves.
