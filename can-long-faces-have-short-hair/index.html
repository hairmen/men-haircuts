Can Long Faces Have Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long faces are often associated with having long hair, but the truth is that long faces can look great with short hair too! In this blog post, we will explore whether long faces can have short hair, and provide tips on how to make the most of this hairstyle.

Description

A long face is typically characterized by a high forehead, a narrow chin, and a lengthened appearance. This face shape can be beautiful, but it can also be challenging when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. Short haircuts are often thought to be unsuitable for long faces, but this is not necessarily true.

How To

The key to pulling off short hair with a long face is to choose a style that balances out the length of your face. Here are some tips:

1. Consider a bob haircut

Bobs are a classic haircut that can be customized to suit any face shape. For long faces, a bob that falls at the jawline or just above it can be particularly flattering. This cut draws attention to the jawline, creating the illusion of width.

2. Try a pixie cut

Pixie cuts are short and sassy, and they can work well for long faces if they are cut with layers that add volume to the sides of the face. This style is particularly good for those with a smaller forehead, as it draws attention away from this area.

3. Add bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out the length of a long face. Side-swept bangs that fall just below the eyebrows can help to shorten the appearance of the forehead, while blunt bangs can add width to the face.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut with a long face:

Step 1: Choose your desired style

Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and choose a short haircut that you love. Consider the texture of your hair and the amount of time you are willing to spend styling it.

Step 2: Consult with your stylist

Bring a picture of your desired style to your hairstylist, and discuss whether it will work well for your face shape. Your stylist may suggest modifications to the cut to make it more flattering.

Step 3: Get the cut

Sit back and relax as your stylist cuts your hair. Enjoy the transformation as your long locks are replaced with a chic short cut.

Step 4: Style your hair

Experiment with different styling techniques to find a look that suits you. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture, or try a volumizing spray to add volume to the sides of your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut with a long face:

1. Consider your hair texture

If you have curly hair, a short haircut may require more maintenance to keep it looking polished. Straight hair may require more styling to add volume and texture.

2. Consider your lifestyle

If you are an active person who enjoys sports or outdoor activities, a short haircut may be more practical than long hair.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to try different styling techniques to find a look that suits you. Use Pinterest or Instagram for inspiration.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

A: It depends on the style of the haircut. A bob that falls at the jawline or just above it can help to balance out the length of a long face. A pixie cut with layers can also add volume to the sides of the face, creating a more balanced look.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a short haircut?

A: Yes! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and braids.

Q: Is a short haircut easy to maintain?

A: It can be! Short hair requires less time to wash and dry, and it can be easier to style than long hair. However, some short haircuts may require more maintenance to keep them looking polished.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, be sure to visit your stylist for regular trims. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny, and consider using a styling product to add volume and texture.

With the right cut and styling techniques, a short haircut can be a great look for those with long faces. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find a look that suits you!
