How To Cut Long Hair With Short Layers On Top

Written by Aprilia Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to add some dimension and movement to your long hair, short layers on top can be a great option. This style can help frame your face, add volume, and give your hair an overall lighter feel. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut long hair with short layers on top.

Description

Cutting long hair with short layers on top involves creating layers that are shorter than the rest of your hair. This can be done using scissors or a razor, and the layers can be blended or left more distinct depending on the desired look. This style works well for those with straight or wavy hair, and can be customized to suit individual face shapes and hair textures.

How To

Before starting, it's important to have the right tools on hand, including sharp scissors or a razor, a comb, and hair clips. It's also important to work on clean, dry hair that has been brushed or combed out.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. You can do this by parting your hair down the middle and then dividing it into two sections, or by parting it into three or four sections for more precision. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the top layer of your hair, starting at the crown of your head. Use your comb to lift a small section of hair up, and then cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process, working your way down the section of hair until you have cut all of the top layer to the same length.

Step 3: Blend the Layers

If you want a more blended look, use your scissors or razor to feather the ends of the top layer of hair. This will help create a gradual transition between the shorter layers and the longer hair below.

Step 4: Move on to the Next Section

Once you have finished cutting the top layer of hair, release the next section and repeat the process until you have cut all of your hair. Make sure to check your progress in a mirror as you go, and adjust the length of the layers as needed.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure even cutting
  • Be patient and take your time
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself, consider consulting with a professional stylist

FAQ

Can I cut short layers on top of curly hair?

Yes, short layers on top can work well with curly hair. However, it's important to be mindful of how much hair you're cutting, as curly hair can shrink up when cut too short.

Will short layers on top make my hair look thinner?

No, short layers on top can actually help add volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using heat protectant products when using hot styling tools. You can also consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your layers and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut long hair with short layers on top, it's time to give it a try! Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with your new style.
