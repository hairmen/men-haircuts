What To Do With Thick Long Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick, long hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a luxurious mane that can turn heads and make a statement. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially when you're in a rush. In this article, we'll cover what to do with thick long hair, including how to style it, care for it, and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Thick Long Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Long Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Thick Long Hair

When it comes to styling thick long hair, there are a few key tips and tricks to keep in mind:

1. Use the Right Products

Thick hair requires products that can provide hold and control without weighing it down. Look for styling products that are formulated for thick hair, such as mousses, gels, and pomades. These products can help you achieve the look you want without making your hair look greasy or limp.

2. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

With thick long hair, the possibilities are endless when it comes to hairstyles. From braids to buns to loose waves, there are plenty of options to choose from. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you and your hair type.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Get a Trim

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair look and feel healthier. By removing split ends and damaged hair, you can promote growth and prevent breakage. Plus, a fresh haircut can help you feel more confident and put-together.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Long Hair

To keep your thick long hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Shampoo and Condition Regularly

Thick hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated. Aim to shampoo and condition your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When brushing your hair, opt for a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This can help prevent breakage and tangles, especially when your hair is wet.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

While it may be tempting to reach for your flat iron or curling wand, heat styling can be damaging to your hair, especially when it's already thick and long. Try to air-dry your hair as much as possible and use heat styling tools sparingly.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I wash my thick long hair?

It's generally recommended to shampoo and condition your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and personal preference.

2. Should I use a hair mask on my thick long hair?

Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Look for a mask that's formulated for thick hair and contains nourishing ingredients like coconut oil or argan oil.

3. Can I still wear my hair down if it's thick and long?

Absolutely! Thick long hair can be stunning when worn down, especially when it's healthy and shiny. Try adding some loose waves or braids for a bohemian vibe.

In conclusion, having thick long hair can be a challenge, but with the right styling techniques and hair care tips, you can keep your locks looking gorgeous and healthy. Experiment with different styles, use the right products, and don't forget to take good care of your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments.
