How To Refresh My Wig A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking for a way to refresh your wig and make it look as good as new? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to refresh your wig. From quick fixes to more in-depth cleaning methods, we have you covered. So, let's get started!

How to Refresh My Wig

Refreshing your wig can be done in a few different ways. Here are some of the most common methods:

  • Quick fix with dry shampoo
  • Cool water rinse
  • Deep clean with wig shampoo

Which method you choose will depend on the type of wig you have and how dirty it is. Let's take a closer look at each method and when to use it.

Quick Fix with Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great way to quickly refresh your wig without having to wash it. This method is best for wigs that are only slightly dirty and just need a little pick-me-up.

To use dry shampoo on your wig, simply spray it onto the hair and then use a brush or comb to distribute the product evenly. This will help to absorb any excess oil and leave your wig looking and smelling fresh.

Cool Water Rinse

If your wig is a bit dirtier, a cool water rinse may be in order. This method is great for synthetic wigs, as it will help to remove any buildup without damaging the fibers.

To do a cool water rinse, simply fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Swish the wig around in the water for a few minutes, being careful not to rub or scrub the hair too vigorously. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and then gently squeeze out any excess water.

Deep Clean with Wig Shampoo

If your wig is very dirty, a deep clean with wig shampoo may be necessary. This method is best for human hair wigs, as it will help to remove any buildup or product residue.

To deep clean your wig, fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swish the wig around in the water for a few minutes, being careful not to rub or scrub the hair too vigorously. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and then gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to refresh your wig:

  1. Start by gently brushing out any tangles or knots in the hair.
  2. Decide which method you will use to refresh your wig (dry shampoo, cool water rinse, or deep clean with wig shampoo).
  3. If using dry shampoo, spray it onto the hair and then use a brush or comb to distribute the product evenly.
  4. If doing a cool water rinse, fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Swish the wig around in the water for a few minutes, being careful not to rub or scrub the hair too vigorously. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and then gently squeeze out any excess water.
  5. If doing a deep clean with wig shampoo, fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swish the wig around in the water for a few minutes, being careful not to rub or scrub the hair too vigorously. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and then gently squeeze out any excess water.
  6. If necessary, repeat the above steps until the wig is clean and refreshed.
  7. Gently blot the wig dry with a towel and then lay it flat to air dry.
  8. Once the wig is dry, gently brush it out again and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

Here are some tips for maintaining your wig and keeping it looking great:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use to help maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools on synthetic wigs, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight, as this can damage the fibers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I refresh my wig?

This will depend on how often you wear your wig and how dirty it gets. As a general rule, you should refresh your wig every 2-4 weeks.

Can I use regular shampoo on my wig?

No, regular shampoo is not recommended for use on wigs. You should always use a wig-specific shampoo to avoid damaging the fibers.

Can I sleep in my wig?

No, it is not recommended to sleep in your wig as this can cause tangling and damage to the fibers.

Hair Care for Your Wig

In addition to refreshing your wig, it is important to take good care of it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your wig:

  • Wash your wig regularly using a wig-specific shampoo.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools on synthetic wigs.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight.

By following these tips and refreshing your wig regularly, you can keep it looking great for years to come!
