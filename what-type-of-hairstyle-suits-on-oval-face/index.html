What Type Of Hairstyle Suits On Oval Face

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face, you are lucky enough to have a versatile face shape that can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that accentuates your best features and complements your face shape. In this article, we'll take a look at the best types of hairstyles for oval faces, how to style them, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking fabulous.

Description

An oval face is characterized by a gently rounded hairline and jawline, with the widest point being the cheekbones. This face shape has balanced proportions and is considered the ideal face shape. With an oval face, you can experiment with a variety of hairstyles, from short to long, straight to curly, and everything in between.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for your oval face, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some of the best types of hairstyles for an oval face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval faces as they add volume and movement to your hair. You can wear your hair straight, wavy, or curly, and the layers will enhance your natural texture. Long layers also frame your face beautifully, drawing attention to your cheekbones and eyes.

2. Bob Haircut

A classic bob haircut is an excellent choice for oval faces as it accentuates your jawline and cheekbones. You can choose a chin-length bob or a longer bob, depending on your preference. The bob can be styled straight or with loose waves, giving you a variety of styling options.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add dimension to your hair and draw attention to your eyes. They also balance out your face shape by creating a diagonal line across your forehead. You can choose to have long or short side-swept bangs, depending on your preference.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that looks great on an oval face. The short layers and asymmetrical style draw attention to your cheekbones and eyes, creating a dramatic look. You can also experiment with different colors and highlights to add more dimension to your hair.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to get the perfect hairstyle for your oval face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, make sure you know your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face with a tape measure or by looking at a picture of yourself.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a significant role in choosing a hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you may want to go for a layered cut to add volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut may work better.

Step 3: Choose a Hairstyle

Once you know your face shape and hair texture, you can choose a hairstyle that works best for you. Look for pictures of hairstyles that you like and take them to your stylist for inspiration.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your new hairstyle, it's time to style it. Follow the instructions from your stylist or experiment with different styling products and tools to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

1. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a curling iron or flat iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray. This will prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy.

2. Get Regular Trims

To keep your hair looking fresh and healthy, make sure to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

3. Experiment with Products

Try different hair products like hairspray, mousse, and styling creams to find what works best for your hair type and style.

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle with an oval face?

A: Yes, you can. Oval faces can pull off short hairstyles like a pixie cut or a bob with ease.

Q: What type of bangs should I get with an oval face?

A: Side-swept bangs are a great choice for an oval face. They add dimension to your hair and balance out your face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part with an oval face?

A: Yes, you can. Oval faces can pull off a middle part or a side part with ease.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking healthy. Choose a hair mask that's appropriate for your hair type and follow the instructions.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so protect it by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

In conclusion, an oval face is a versatile face shape that can pull off almost any hairstyle. Consider your hair texture, personal style, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle, and experiment with different products and tools to keep your hair looking its best.
