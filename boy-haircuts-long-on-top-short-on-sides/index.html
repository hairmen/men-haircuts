Boy Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your little boy, you can't go wrong with the "long on top short on sides" style. This popular haircut has been around for years and has evolved into various styles that suit different face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about boy haircuts long on top short on sides, including the how-to, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Description

The boy haircuts long on top short on sides style is a versatile haircut that looks great on boys of all ages. It features long hair on the top of the head that is styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or combed over. The sides and back are trimmed short, typically with a fade or undercut. This haircut is perfect for boys who want a trendy and stylish look that is easy to maintain.

How to

If you're a parent looking to give your son a boy haircut long on top short on sides, it's important to find a good barber or stylist who has experience with this style. They will be able to create the perfect cut that suits your son's face shape and hair type. You can also use online guides and tutorials to learn how to cut hair at home, but be sure to use the right tools and techniques to avoid any accidents.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your son's hair in the boy haircuts long on top short on sides style:

  1. Wash and dry your son's hair.
  2. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of the head to the desired length.
  3. Blend the hair from the sides to the top of the head using scissors or clippers.
  4. Leave the hair on top of the head longer and style it in your desired way.
  5. Use a comb and scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a clean look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving your son a boy haircut long on top short on sides:

  • Make sure you have the right tools, including clippers, scissors, and combs.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from all angles.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your son's face shape and hair type.
  • Use hair products, such as gel or wax, to style the hair on top of the head.

FAQ

What face shapes suit boy haircuts long on top short on sides?

The boy haircuts long on top short on sides style suits most face shapes, including oval, square, and round faces. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your son's face shape and hair type.

What hair types suit boy haircuts long on top short on sides?

The boy haircuts long on top short on sides style suits most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that works with your son's hair type and texture.

Hair Care

To maintain your son's boy haircut long on top short on sides, it's important to keep the hair clean and well-groomed. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a small amount of hair product to the hair on top of the head to style it.
  • Trim the hair on the sides and back regularly to maintain the style.
  • Visit a barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep the haircut looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, your son's boy haircut long on top short on sides will look great and be easy to maintain. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your son's hair type and personality.
