Fringe Makes You Look Younger How To Get The Perfect Fringe

Written by Oliver Jun 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to look younger without going under the knife, then a fringe might be just what you need. A fringe, also known as bangs, is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short over the forehead. It's a simple and effective way to change your look and make you look younger. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect fringe and give you some tips on how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Perfect Fringe
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get the Perfect Fringe

The first step in getting the perfect fringe is to choose the right style. There are many different types of fringes, including blunt, side-swept, and choppy. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a fringe.

Next, you'll need to decide how thick you want your fringe to be. A thick fringe can make you look younger, while a thinner fringe can be more subtle. You'll also need to decide how long you want your fringe to be. A shorter fringe will be easier to maintain, while a longer fringe can be styled in different ways.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by sectioning off the hair at the front of your head. Use a comb to create a straight line where you want your fringe to start.

2. Use scissors to cut the hair along the line you created. Start by cutting a little at a time, and be sure to keep the hair wet so you can see how it will look when it's dry.

3. Once you've cut the hair, use a blow dryer to dry it and style it. You can use a round brush to add volume, or a straightening iron to make it sleek.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe

1. Trim your fringe regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. You can do this yourself with a pair of scissors, or visit a hairdresser for a professional trim.

2. Use a dry shampoo to keep your fringe looking fresh between washes. This will also help to prevent it from becoming greasy.

3. Avoid using too much heat on your fringe, as this can cause damage and make it look dry and brittle. Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use a blow dryer or straightening iron.

FAQs

Q: Will a fringe suit my face shape?

A: There is a fringe style to suit every face shape. Talk to your hairdresser to find the right one for you.

Q: How often should I trim my fringe?

A: You should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips

1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it look dry and frizzy. Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use a blow dryer or straightening iron.

A fringe is a simple and effective way to change your look and make you look younger. With the right style and care, you can have the perfect fringe that will make you look and feel great. Follow our tips and advice to get the most out of your fringe and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
