Mens Haircut Neck Taper A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a man who loves to keep his hair looking sharp and well-groomed, then you must have heard about the mens haircut neck taper. This haircut is a popular style in 2023, and it looks good on all hair types and lengths. In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about the mens haircut neck taper from the description, how to, step by step, tips, and hair care.

Description

The mens haircut neck taper is a classic style that involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head to a shorter length while leaving the hair on top longer. The hair at the neckline is then tapered gradually to create a smooth and seamless transition between the shorter hair at the back and the longer hair on top. The result is a neat and clean look that is perfect for any occasion.

How to

The mens haircut neck taper can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly and towel-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 2: Clip your hair

Clip the hair on the top of your head and comb the sides and back of your hair downwards.

Step 3: Start cutting

Using a pair of scissors or clippers, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, cutting the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Taper the neckline

Once you have finished cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head, it’s time to taper the neckline. Use a pair of clippers to taper the hair gradually, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Blend the hair on the sides and back of your head with the hair on top by using scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired using your favorite hair products.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens haircut neck taper:

  • Make sure you choose a skilled and experienced barber to achieve the best results.
  • Be clear about the length and style you want before you start cutting your hair.
  • Use the right tools, including sharp scissors and high-quality clippers.
  • Take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut neck taper:

Q: How often should I get a mens haircut neck taper?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently with a mens haircut neck taper?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in various ways with a mens haircut neck taper. You can slick it back, part it to the side, or leave it messy and textured.

Q: Is the mens haircut neck taper suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the mens haircut neck taper is a versatile style that looks good on all face shapes.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut neck taper looking sharp and well-groomed, you need to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the style and keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know everything about the mens haircut neck taper, it’s time to head to your barber and get the look you deserve. With the right tools, skills, and care, you can rock this classic style and look your best every day.
