How To Curl Left Side Of Hair With Tyme Iron

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having limp and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and texture to your hair? Well, you're in luck because today we're going to teach you how to curl the left side of your hair with Tyme Iron.

Description

The Tyme Iron is a revolutionary hair styling tool that can curl, straighten, and create waves with just one tool. It has a unique design that allows you to curl your hair without having to wrap it around the iron, which means less damage to your hair. The Tyme Iron is perfect for those who want to achieve salon-quality curls at home.

How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair with Tyme Iron

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Then, brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, the left side and the right side. Clip the right side of your hair away from your face so that you can focus on curling the left side.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Tyme Iron

Turn on your Tyme Iron and let it heat up to the desired temperature. The Tyme Iron has a digital display that shows you the temperature, so you can adjust it according to your hair type.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair on the left side of your head and place the Tyme Iron at the root of your hair. Close the iron and twist it away from your face. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds and release it. Repeat this process until you've curled all the hair on the left side of your head.

Step 5: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you've finished curling the left side of your head, unclip the right side of your hair and repeat the same process to curl the right side of your head.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

- Adjust the temperature of your Tyme Iron according to your hair type.

- Take small sections of hair to ensure even curls.

- Twist the Tyme Iron away from your face to create natural-looking curls.

- Hold the Tyme Iron in place for a few seconds to ensure the curls set.

FAQ

Q: Can I use the Tyme Iron on wet hair?

A: No, you should only use the Tyme Iron on dry hair.

Q: How often should I use the Tyme Iron?

A: It's best to use the Tyme Iron once or twice a week to avoid heat damage to your hair.

Q: How do I clean my Tyme Iron?

A: You can clean your Tyme Iron by wiping it with a damp cloth after it has cooled down.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

- Don't use hot tools on your hair every day.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

With these tips and the Tyme Iron, you can achieve salon-quality curls at home. So go ahead and try it out for yourself!
