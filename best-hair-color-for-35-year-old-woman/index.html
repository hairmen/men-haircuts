Description

At the age of 35, you are in a unique position where you can experiment with different hair colors that complement your style and personality. However, it can be challenging to choose the right hair color that will flatter your skin tone and eye color. You need a hair color that will enhance your natural beauty and make you stand out from the crowd.

When choosing the best hair color for 35 year old woman, itâs essential to consider your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. You want a hair color that will complement your skin tone and bring out the natural color of your eyes. Additionally, you need to choose a color that will fit your lifestyle and career. For instance, if you work in a corporate environment, you may want to avoid bold and vibrant hair colors.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be overwhelming, especially if you have never colored your hair before. However, the following tips will help you select the best hair color for 35 year old woman:

  • Determine your skin tone - Your skin tone will play a significant role in determining the best hair color for you. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and honey brown. If you have cool undertones, you should choose cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, and burgundy.
  • Consider your eye color - Your eye color will also influence the best hair color for you. If you have blue eyes, you will look great with cool hair colors like ash blonde and platinum. If you have brown eyes, you will look great with warm hair colors like caramel and chestnut brown.
  • Think about your lifestyle - Your lifestyle will determine the best hair color for you. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair color that requires minimal upkeep.

Step-by-Step Guide on Coloring Your Hair

If you have decided to color your hair, the following step-by-step guide will help you achieve the perfect hair color:

  1. Choose the right hair color - Consider the tips we discussed earlier to choose the best hair color for you.
  2. Do a patch test - Before applying the hair color all over your hair, do a patch test to ensure that you are not allergic to the product.
  3. Prepare your hair - Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Dry your hair and comb it thoroughly.
  4. Apply the hair color - Apply the hair color from the roots to the ends of your hair, following the instructions on the product packaging. Use a timer to ensure that you leave the color on for the recommended time.
  5. Rinse your hair - Rinse your hair with lukewarm water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair color to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  6. Dry and style your hair - Dry your hair with a towel and style it as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

After coloring your hair, itâs essential to take good care of it to ensure that it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner - Sulfate can strip the color from your hair, so itâs crucial to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often - The more you wash your hair, the faster the color will fade. Try to wash your hair every two to three days.
  • Protect your hair from the sun - The sun can cause your hair color to fade, so itâs essential to protect your hair with a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools - Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause the color to fade. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair color for 35 year old woman:

What is the best hair color for women over 35?

The best hair color for women over 35 depends on their skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. However, warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and honey brown are generally flattering for women in their mid-30s.

Can I color my hair if I have gray hair?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have gray hair. However, you may want to choose a hair color that will cover the gray hair and blend in with your natural hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires extra care to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips for colored hair:

  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can cause the color to fade.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and relaxers on your colored hair.

Choosing the best hair color for 35 year old woman can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you can achieve the perfect hair color that complements your age and style. Remember to take good care of your colored hair to maintain its vibrancy and health.
