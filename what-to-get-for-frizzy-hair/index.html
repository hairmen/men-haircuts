What To Get For Frizzy Hair

Written by Christine Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Frizzy hair can be frustrating to deal with, but there are products and techniques that can help tame those unruly strands. In this article, we'll explore what to get for frizzy hair, including how to choose the right products and tips for keeping your hair looking smooth and sleek.

Description

Frizzy hair is characterized by strands that are dry, brittle, and prone to flyaways. This type of hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, humidity, and heat damage from styling tools. If left untreated, frizzy hair can become increasingly difficult to manage and may even lead to breakage.

How to Choose the Right Products

When it comes to choosing the right products for frizzy hair, it's important to look for ingredients that will provide moisture and help smooth the hair cuticle. Here are some key ingredients to look for:

Argan Oil

Argan oil is rich in antioxidants and essential fatty acids, which can help nourish and hydrate frizzy hair. Look for products that contain pure argan oil for the best results.

Shea Butter

Shea butter is another ingredient that can help moisturize and protect the hair. It's especially beneficial for those with coarse or thick hair that tends to be dry.

Glycerin

Glycerin is a humectant that can help attract and retain moisture in the hair. It's a great ingredient to look for in styling products like gels and mousses.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to help tame frizzy hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to help add moisture and smooth the hair cuticle.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help hydrate and protect the hair. Focus on the ends of the hair, which tend to be the driest.

Step 3: Use a Smoothing Serum

Apply a smoothing serum to dry hair to help tame flyaways and add shine. Use a small amount and focus on the mid-lengths and ends of the hair.

Step 4: Style with Heat

Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray first to help prevent damage.

Tips for Keeping Frizzy Hair Under Control

Here are some additional tips for keeping frizzy hair under control:

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days instead.

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage the hair and make it more prone to frizz. Try to limit your use of hot tools and always use a heat protectant spray.

Avoid Brushing Wet Hair

Wet hair is more prone to breakage, so avoid brushing it when it's wet. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair after washing.

FAQ

Q: Can Frizzy Hair Be Fixed?

A: While frizzy hair can't be completely fixed, it can be managed with the right products and techniques.

Q: What Causes Frizzy Hair?

A: Frizzy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, humidity, and heat damage from styling tools.

Q: How Can I Prevent Frizz?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid over-washing your hair, limit your use of heat styling tools, and use products that contain moisturizing and smoothing ingredients.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for managing frizzy hair. Make sure to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and avoid harsh chemicals that can strip the hair of its natural oils. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends, which can contribute to frizz. With the right products and care, you can keep your frizzy hair looking smooth and healthy.
