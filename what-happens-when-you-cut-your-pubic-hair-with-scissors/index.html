What Happens When You Cut Your Pubic Hair With Scissors

Written by Robby Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Pubic hair is a natural occurrence in both men and women, and it grows around the genitals, anus, and pubic bone. Some people prefer to shave or trim their pubic hair, and some others choose to let it grow naturally. However, if you decide to trim your pubic hair, you must do it carefully and using the right tools. Cutting your pubic hair with scissors can have some consequences and may cause injuries. In this article, we will explain what happens when you cut your pubic hair with scissors, how to do it correctly, and how to take care of your pubic hair afterward.

How to Cut Your Pubic Hair with Scissors

If you decide to cut your pubic hair with scissors, you must do it correctly to avoid injuries and infections. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Clean the Area

Before trimming your pubic hair, you must clean the area with warm water and soap. This will help remove any bacteria or dirt that may be on the skin or hair. You can also use a wet cloth or towel to wipe the area gently.

Step 2: Choose the Right Scissors

When cutting your pubic hair, you should use scissors that are sharp, clean, and have rounded tips. Do not use regular scissors or razors as they may cause injuries or irritations. You can find special scissors for pubic hair trimming in drugstores or online shops.

Step 3: Trim the Hair

Hold the hair between two fingers and cut it slowly and carefully. You can also use a comb to separate the hair and cut it evenly. Avoid cutting too close to the skin or pulling the hair as this may cause pain or bleeding. Trim the hair to your desired length but be careful not to cut too short.

Step 4: Clean the Area Again

After trimming your pubic hair, you must clean the area again with warm water and soap. This will help remove any loose hair or debris that may be on the skin. You can also apply a moisturizer or aloe vera gel to soothe the skin.

Tips for Cutting Your Pubic Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to follow when cutting your pubic hair with scissors:

  • Use clean and sharp scissors
  • Trim the hair slowly and carefully
  • Do not cut too close to the skin or pull the hair
  • Clean the area before and after trimming
  • Apply a moisturizer or aloe vera gel to soothe the skin

FAQ about Cutting Pubic Hair with Scissors

Can you get infections from cutting pubic hair with scissors?

Yes, cutting pubic hair with unclean or dull scissors may cause infections. It can also cause ingrown hairs, irritation, or bleeding.

How often should you trim your pubic hair?

It depends on your preference and hair growth. Some people trim their pubic hair once a week, while others do it once a month.

Is it better to shave or trim pubic hair?

It depends on your preference and comfort. Shaving may cause more irritation and ingrown hairs, while trimming may be less painful and easier to maintain.

How to Take Care of Your Pubic Hair

Whether you trim or let your pubic hair grow naturally, you must take care of it. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Clean the area regularly with warm water and soap
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or fragrances on the skin
  • Avoid wearing tight clothes or underwear that may cause friction
  • Apply a moisturizer or aloe vera gel to soothe the skin
  • Trim or shave the hair carefully and using the right tools

Conclusion

Cutting your pubic hair with scissors can have some consequences, but if you do it correctly and using the right tools, you can avoid injuries and infections. Remember to clean the area before and after trimming, use sharp and clean scissors, and trim the hair slowly and carefully. Also, take care of your pubic hair by cleaning it regularly and applying a moisturizer or aloe vera gel to soothe the skin.
