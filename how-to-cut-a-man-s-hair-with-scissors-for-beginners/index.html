How To Cut A Man s Hair With Scissors For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a beginner looking to cut a man's hair with scissors, you might feel intimidated at first. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can give a great haircut that will leave your client feeling satisfied. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting a man's hair with scissors, as well as provide tips and hair care advice to ensure the best results. So grab your scissors and let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting, it's important to have the right tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors - choose a pair of sharp, high-quality scissors specifically designed for cutting hair
  • Comb - a wide-tooth comb works best for detangling hair
  • Clipper - if you plan to use clippers, make sure you have a set with adjustable guards
  • Hair cape - to protect your client's clothing from hair clippings
  • Spray bottle - to wet the hair and make it easier to cut
  • Hair dryer - to dry the hair after cutting

How to Cut a Man's Hair with Scissors

Cutting a man's hair with scissors involves several steps. Here's a brief overview:

  1. Wash and dry the hair
  2. Section the hair into manageable parts
  3. Comb and cut each section, starting with the sides and back
  4. Blend the hair using scissors or clippers
  5. Finish the haircut by trimming the top of the hair
  6. Style the hair as desired

Step-by-Step Guide

Now let's dive into the details of each step:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is harder to cut and can lead to uneven results. If the hair is particularly long or thick, you may want to blow dry it to make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Section the Hair into Manageable Parts

Using your comb, divide the hair into sections that are easy to work with. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Comb and Cut Each Section, Starting with the Sides and Back

Start with the sides and back of the head, as these areas are easier to cut. Comb each section of hair straight down and cut the ends with your scissors. Use the previous sections as a guide for cutting the remaining sections.

Step 4: Blend the Hair Using Scissors or Clippers

Once the sides and back are cut, you'll want to blend the hair to avoid any harsh lines. This can be done using scissors or clippers with adjustable guards. Use a comb to blend the sections together, making sure to cut any longer hairs that stick out.

Step 5: Finish the Haircut by Trimming the Top of the Hair

Now it's time to tackle the top of the head. Comb the hair straight up and trim the ends with your scissors. You can use your fingers to measure the length or a comb as a guide. Take your time and cut small sections at a time until you achieve the desired length.

Step 6: Style the Hair as Desired

Once the haircut is complete, it's time to style the hair as desired. Use a hair dryer to dry the hair and apply any styling products as needed. Don't forget to clean up any loose hairs around the neck and ears!

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed
  • Trim the hair in the direction it naturally falls
  • Use thinning shears to remove bulk from thick hair
  • Take breaks and step back to assess your progress
  • Practice, practice, practice!

FAQs

Can I cut a man's hair with regular scissors?

You can, but it's not recommended. Regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can lead to uneven results.

How often should a man get his hair cut?

It depends on the length and style of the hair. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain a neat and tidy appearance.

What if I make a mistake while cutting?

Don't panic! Mistakes happen. If you accidentally cut off more than you intended, try blending the hair to make it less noticeable. If necessary, you can always go shorter or even out the hair with clippers.

Hair Care Advice

After the haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or blow dryers too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated for healthy hair from the inside out

With these tips and techniques, you can give a great haircut using scissors. Remember to take your time, practice, and have fun!
