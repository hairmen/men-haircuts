What Is Sexier Curly Or Straight Hair

Written by Petter Nov 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to hair, there's always been a debate on what's sexier: curly or straight hair. This is a topic that has been discussed for years and there is no clear answer. Some people prefer curly hair while others prefer straight hair. In this blog post, we will discuss the pros and cons of each hair type and let you decide which is sexier.

Curly Hair

Curly hair is often associated with a fun, carefree, and wild personality. It's a hair type that is full of life, movement, and texture. Curly hair can be styled in many ways, from tight ringlets to loose waves. While curly hair can be seen as sexy, it can also be difficult to manage. Curly hair is prone to frizz, tangles, and dryness. It requires more maintenance and products to keep it healthy and defined.

How to Style Curly Hair

1. Start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for curly hair.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair. This could be a curl cream, gel, or mousse.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a serum or oil to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Tips for Curly Hair Care

- Avoid brushing or combing curly hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and breakage.

- Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.

- Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

FAQ about Curly Hair

Q: Can I straighten curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten curly hair with a flat iron. Just make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat.

Straight Hair

Straight hair is often associated with a sleek, sophisticated, and polished look. It's a hair type that is easy to manage and style. Straight hair can be worn in many different styles, from a blunt cut to layers. While straight hair can be seen as sexy, it can also be flat and lack volume. It can also be prone to oiliness and can look greasy if not washed frequently.

How to Style Straight Hair

1. Start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for straight hair.

2. Apply a volumizing product to damp hair. This could be a mousse or root-lifting spray.

3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and smoothness.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any sections that need it.

Tips for Straight Hair Care

- Use dry shampoo between washes to absorb oil and add volume.

- Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

- Use a heat protectant when using hot tools.

FAQ about Straight Hair

Q: Can I curl straight hair?

A: Yes, you can curl straight hair with a curling iron. Just make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat.

Conclusion

So, what is sexier: curly or straight hair? The answer is subjective and depends on personal preference. Both hair types have their pros and cons, and it's up to you to decide which one you prefer. No matter what hair type you have, taking care of it is key to keeping it healthy and beautiful.
