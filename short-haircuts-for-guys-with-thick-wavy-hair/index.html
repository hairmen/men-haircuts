Short Haircuts For Guys With Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline Sep 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that it can be difficult to manage. Finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge, but don't worry! We've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to choose the perfect short haircut for guys with thick wavy hair.

Description

Thick wavy hair is characterized by its volume and texture. It's a great hair type to work with, but it can also be challenging. Finding the right haircut is essential to make the most of your hair. Short haircuts are an excellent option for guys with thick wavy hair as they are easy to maintain and give a stylish look.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut

Choosing the right short haircut for guys with thick wavy hair can be a bit tricky. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect haircut:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape. The right haircut can accentuate your best features and hide your flaws. For example, if you have a round face, a short haircut with volume on top can make your face look longer.

2. Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance haircut that requires little styling might be the best option for you. On the other hand, if you have more time to style your hair, a more intricate cut may be a better choice.

3. Consider Your Hair Texture

Thick wavy hair can be challenging to style, but the right haircut can make it easier. Consider your hair texture when choosing a short haircut. A layered cut can help reduce volume and make your hair more manageable.

Step by Step Guide to Short Haircuts for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to choose the perfect short haircut let's take a look at some of the best short haircuts for guys with thick wavy hair.

1. The Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a classic short haircut that's perfect for guys with thick wavy hair. It's easy to maintain and requires no styling. The buzz cut is also a great option if you're balding or have a receding hairline.

2. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic short haircut that's perfect for guys with thick wavy hair. This style features short hair on the sides and back and slightly longer hair on top. The crew cut is a great option if you want a stylish and professional look.

3. The High and Tight

The high and tight is a popular military-inspired short haircut that's perfect for guys with thick wavy hair. This style features short hair on the sides and back and slightly longer hair on top. The high and tight is a great option if you want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish.

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect short haircut, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling short haircuts for guys with thick wavy hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for styling short haircuts for guys with thick wavy hair. A lightweight styling product like a mousse or a light hold gel can help keep your hair in place without weighing it down.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help reduce volume and make it more manageable. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create natural-looking waves.

3. Use a Comb or Brush

Using a comb or brush can help you style your hair and create the look you want. A wide-tooth comb is a great tool for detangling and creating volume, while a brush can help smooth out your hair and create a more polished look.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for guys with thick wavy hair?

The best short haircut for guys with thick wavy hair depends on your face shape, lifestyle, and hair texture. Buzz cuts, crew cuts, and high and tights are all great options.

How do you style short haircuts for guys with thick wavy hair?

Use the right products, blow dry your hair, and use a comb or brush to style your hair.

Hair Care for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for guys with thick wavy hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's specifically designed for thick, wavy hair.

2. Condition Regularly

Conditioning is essential for keeping your hair soft and manageable. Use a conditioner that's designed for thick, wavy hair and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible.

Choosing the right short haircut for guys with thick wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find the perfect style. Remember to consider your face shape, lifestyle, and hair texture when choosing a haircut. Use the right products and styling tools to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. With the right care, your thick wavy hair can be your best asset.
