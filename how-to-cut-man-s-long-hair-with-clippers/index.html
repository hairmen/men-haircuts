How To Cut Man s Long Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut from the comfort of your own home? With the right tools and techniques, cutting a man's long hair with clippers can be a breeze. This step-by-step guide will provide you with all the information you need to achieve a professional-looking haircut that will leave you feeling confident and stylish.

Description

Before we dive into the details of how to cut man's long hair with clippers, let's first discuss why you might want to do this. Cutting your own hair or someone else's can save you time and money, and it can also be a fun and creative way to experiment with different hairstyles. With clippers, you have the ability to control the length and texture of the hair, allowing you to achieve a personalized look that is tailored to your preferences.

How to

Here are the steps you should follow to cut a man's long hair with clippers:

  1. Start by gathering all the tools you will need, including clippers, guards, a comb, scissors, and a mirror.
  2. Decide on the length you want to cut the hair. Attach the appropriate guard to the clippers to achieve the desired length.
  3. Begin by trimming the sides and back of the head. Use the clippers to create a clean line around the bottom of the hairline.
  4. Move to the top of the head and use the comb to lift sections of hair. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length, starting at the front and working your way towards the back.
  5. Blend the sides and top of the hair together using the clippers and a comb.
  6. Use scissors to trim any longer pieces of hair that were missed by the clippers. Be sure to blend these into the rest of the hair to create a seamless look.
  7. Trim the hair around the ears and the neckline using the clippers and a comb.
  8. Check the hair in the mirror to ensure that it is even and balanced on both sides.
  9. Clean up any stray hairs and give the hair a final comb through to remove any loose pieces.
  10. Finish the haircut by washing and styling the hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

Let's break down each of these steps in more detail:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin cutting the hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. This includes clippers, guards, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. You may also want to have a towel or cape to protect clothing from hair clippings.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Determine the length you want to cut the hair and attach the appropriate guard to the clippers. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Begin by using the clippers to trim the sides and back of the head. Use a longer guard to start and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to create a tapered look. Create a clean line around the bottom of the hairline to give the haircut a polished look.

Step 4: Trim the Top

Use the comb to lift sections of hair on the top of the head. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length, starting at the front and working your way towards the back. Be sure to blend the sides and top together for a seamless look.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use the clippers and a comb to blend the hair together for a natural look. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the clippers to create a gradual transition from the sides to the top of the head.

Step 6: Trim with Scissors

Use scissors to trim any longer pieces of hair that were missed by the clippers. Be sure to blend these into the rest of the hair to create a seamless look.

Step 7: Trim Around the Ears and Neckline

Use the clippers and a comb to trim the hair around the ears and neckline. Be sure to create a clean line for a polished look.

Step 8: Check for Balance

Check the hair in the mirror to ensure that it is even and balanced on both sides. Make any necessary adjustments to achieve a symmetrical look.

Step 9: Clean Up Stray Hairs

Clean up any stray hairs with the clippers or scissors. Give the hair a final comb through to remove any loose pieces.

Step 10: Wash and Style

Finish the haircut by washing and styling the hair as desired. Use a styling product to add texture and hold to the hair.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when cutting a man's long hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a comb to lift sections of hair and create a uniform look.
  • Blend the sides and top of the hair together for a natural look.
  • Check for balance and symmetry throughout the haircut.
  • Trim any longer pieces of hair with scissors to create a seamless look.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to the hair.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting a man's long hair with clippers:

  • Can you cut long hair with clippers?

    • Yes, you can cut long hair with clippers, but you will need to use longer guards to achieve the desired length.

  • What kind of clippers should I use?

    • You should use a high-quality pair of clippers with a variety of guards and a sharp blade.

  • What length should I cut the hair?

    • This will depend on your personal preference and the style you are trying to achieve. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to find the right length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and free of split ends.

Cutting a man's long hair with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut that will leave you feeling confident and stylish. Follow the steps in this guide and you'll be on your way to a great haircut in no time!
