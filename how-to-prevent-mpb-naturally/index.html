How To Prevent Mpb Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're struggling with male pattern baldness (MPB), you're not alone. It's estimated that 50% of men will experience some level of hair loss by the age of 50. While there are many medications and treatments available, there are also natural ways to prevent MPB. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for preventing MPB naturally.

What is MPB?

Male pattern baldness (MPB) is a genetic condition that causes hair loss in men. It's characterized by a receding hairline and thinning hair on the crown of the head. MPB is caused by a hormone called dihydrotestosterone (DHT), which is a byproduct of testosterone. DHT causes hair follicles to shrink, leading to hair loss.

How to prevent MPB naturally?

1. Eat a balanced diet

Eating a balanced diet can help prevent hair loss. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods that are rich in protein include eggs, lean meats, and beans. Foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals include leafy greens, nuts, and whole grains.

2. Exercise regularly

Exercise is important for overall health, but it can also help prevent hair loss. Exercise increases blood flow to the scalp, which can help promote hair growth.

3. Manage stress

Stress can cause hair loss, so it's important to manage stress levels. Try meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises to reduce stress.

4. Use natural hair products

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair. Use natural hair products that are gentle on your scalp and hair.

5. Massage your scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood flow and promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

6. Get enough sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall health, but it can also help prevent hair loss. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep each night.

7. Don't smoke

Smoking can cause hair loss, so it's important to quit smoking if you're a smoker.

FAQ

Can MPB be reversed?

While there is no cure for MPB, there are treatments that can help slow down or stop hair loss. These include medications like finasteride and minoxidil, as well as hair transplant surgery.

At what age does MPB start?

MPB can start as early as your teenage years, but it's most common in men over the age of 50.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to the tips above, here are some hair care tips to help prevent hair loss:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, like ponytails and braids.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your scalp of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can also strip your scalp of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid pulling on your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help prevent MPB naturally. Remember to be patient, as it may take time to see results. If you're concerned about hair loss, talk to your doctor or a dermatologist.
