Best Haircuts For Oval Faces Men

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a man with an oval face shape and struggling to find the perfect haircut for you? Look no further! This article will guide you through the best haircuts for oval faces men, along with tips and tricks on how to maintain and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Oval Faces Men
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Men with Oval Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for oval faces men, it's important to determine if you actually have an oval face shape. Oval faces are characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the jawline, high cheekbones, and a rounded chin. To determine your face shape, use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you likely have an oval face shape.

Best Haircuts for Oval Faces Men

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect haircut for you. The good news is that men with oval faces can pull off a variety of different haircuts, so you have plenty of options to choose from.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts work well for men with oval faces because they highlight the natural shape of the face. A few popular short haircuts for men with oval faces include:

  • The buzz cut
  • The crew cut
  • The high and tight

Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts are another great option for men with oval faces. They add texture and volume to the hair, which can help balance out the face shape. Some popular medium length haircuts for men with oval faces include:

  • The side part
  • The textured crop
  • The slick back

Long Haircuts

Men with oval faces can also pull off long haircuts, as long as they are kept well-groomed and styled. Some popular long haircuts for men with oval faces include:

  • The man bun
  • The shoulder-length cut
  • The layered cut

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Regardless of which haircut you choose, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to maintain and care for your hair:

  • Regularly wash and condition your hair to keep it healthy and clean
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or gel, to hold your hairstyle in place
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups

FAQs

What if I don't like any of the recommended haircuts?

That's okay! The haircuts listed above are just suggestions. If you don't like any of them, feel free to experiment and find a haircut that suits your personal style and preferences.

Can men with oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, men with oval faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to make sure the bangs are cut in a way that complements your face shape and doesn't make your forehead appear too short or too long.

Hair Care for Men with Oval Faces

In addition to regular washing and conditioning, men with oval faces should also consider using hair products that are specifically designed for their hair type. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you may want to use a product that helps define your curls and reduce frizz. If you have fine or thinning hair, you may want to use a product that adds volume and thickness.

It's also important to protect your hair from damage by avoiding excessive heat styling and using a heat protectant spray when you do use heat tools. Additionally, wearing a hat or using a sunscreen spray can help protect your scalp from sun damage.

By following these tips and choosing a haircut that complements your face shape, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
