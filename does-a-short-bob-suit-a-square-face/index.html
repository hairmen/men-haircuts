Does A Short Bob Suit A Square Face

Description

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that has been around for over a century. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes, hair types, and personal styles. However, some people may wonder if a short bob suits a square face, which is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. The answer is yes, a short bob can look flattering on a square face if it is tailored to complement the face shape and highlight its best features. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks to help you rock a short bob with confidence and style, regardless of your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the specifics of a short bob on a square face, let's first review how to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that complement your natural features.

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape or ruler to take the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part of your forehead, from one temple to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting from the outer corner of your eye to the edge of your nose.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following face shape categories:

  • Oval: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and forehead is greater than the jawline. The angle of the jaw is rounded rather than sharp.
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement. The angle of the jaw is soft and less defined.
  • Square: All measurements are fairly similar. The angle of the jaw is sharp.
  • Heart: Forehead is greater than the cheekbones and jawline. The chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Face length is the greatest measurement. Then, in descending order: forehead, cheekbones, and smallest is jawline. The chin is pointed.
  • Triangle: Jawline measures greater than cheekbones, which measure larger than forehead.

If you're still not sure what your face shape is, you can try some online face shape quizzes or consult a hairstylist for professional advice.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Bob

If you have determined that you have a square face and want to try a short bob, here is a step-by-step guide to getting the haircut:

  1. Choose the right length: A short bob can range from chin-length to just above the shoulders, depending on your preference and face shape. For a square face, it's best to avoid a blunt cut that ends at the jawline, as it can accentuate the squareness of the face. Instead, opt for a slightly longer bob that grazes the collarbone or a layered bob with soft, angled edges.
  2. Choose the right style: A short bob can be styled in many ways, from straight and sleek to tousled and textured. For a square face, it's best to add some volume and movement to soften the angular features. You can achieve this by using a round brush to blow-dry your hair and create a slight curl at the ends, or by using a texturizing spray or wax to create a messy, beachy look.
  3. Choose the right color: A short bob can be a great canvas for experimenting with different hair colors and highlights. For a square face, it's best to avoid bold, contrasting colors that can draw attention to the jawline. Instead, opt for subtle highlights or a monochromatic color that blends seamlessly with your skin tone.
  4. Maintain the cut: A short bob requires regular maintenance to keep its shape and style. Depending on your hair type and growth rate, you may need to get a trim every 4-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the length and shape of the haircut.

Tips for Styling a Short Bob on a Square Face

Now that you have a short bob haircut, here are some tips for styling and maintaining it:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create a smooth, bouncy finish.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to create a messy, tousled look.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Experiment with different partings to create different looks and highlight different features.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some flair to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short bobs and square faces:

Q: Can a short bob make a square face look even more square?

A: It depends on the length and style of the bob. A blunt, chin-length bob can accentuate the squareness of a face, while a longer, layered bob can soften the angles and create a more oval-like shape.

Q: What are some other hairstyles that suit a square face?

A: Some other hairstyles that complement a square face include long layers, side-swept bangs, and soft waves or curls.

Q: Can a short bob suit any hair type?

A: Yes, a short bob can be tailored to suit different hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly. However, the styling techniques and products may vary depending on the hair texture and density.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Short Bob

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining a short bob:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons every day, as they can cause heat damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a short bob on a square face and showcase your natural beauty and style. Remember, the most important thing is to feel comfortable and confident in your own skin, no matter what hairstyle or face shape you have!
