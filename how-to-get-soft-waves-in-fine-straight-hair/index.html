How to Get Soft Waves in Fine Straight Hair

There are several ways to get soft waves in fine straight hair, including:

1. Braids

Braiding your hair overnight is a great way to get soft waves without using heat. Simply divide your hair into sections, braid each section, and secure the ends with a hair tie. Sleep on your braids overnight, then unravel them in the morning for soft waves.

2. Twist and Pin

Another way to get soft waves is to twist your hair into sections and secure them with bobby pins. Twist each section away from your face, then pin it in place. Leave the pins in for a few hours or overnight, then remove them for soft waves.

3. Hair Rollers

Using hair rollers is a classic way to get soft waves in fine straight hair. Choose rollers that are appropriate for your hair length and thickness, then roll small sections of hair around each roller. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight, then remove them for soft waves.
