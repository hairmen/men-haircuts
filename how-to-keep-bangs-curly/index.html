How To Keep Bangs Curly Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have naturally curly hair or love to curl your locks, but always struggle with keeping your bangs curly throughout the day? Don't worry; you're not alone! Many people with curly hair find that their bangs tend to straighten out quickly, leaving them with an awkward fringe that doesn't match the rest of their hairstyle. But fear not, because there are some simple tips and tricks you can use to keep your bangs curly all day long. In this post, we'll share our best advice for how to keep bangs curly and maintain your perfect hairstyle.

How to Keep Bangs Curly: Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you keep your bangs curly:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Moisturized Hair

The key to keeping your bangs curly is to start with clean, moisturized hair. Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to wash and condition your hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and nourished. This will help your curls stay defined and bouncy.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Before styling, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This could be a curl-defining cream, mousse, or gel. Apply a small amount of product to your bangs, making sure to distribute it evenly. This will help your curls hold their shape and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

Next, style your bangs as desired. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while scrunching your bangs with your hands. Alternatively, you can let your hair air dry and use your fingers to scrunch your curls periodically. You can also use a curling iron or wand to add more definition to your curls. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've styled your bangs, finish with a light hairspray to help hold your curls in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Instead, opt for a lightweight hairspray that will provide hold without stiffness.

Tips for Keeping Your Bangs Curly

Here are some additional tips for keeping your bangs curly:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt your curls and cause frizz.
  • Refresh your curls throughout the day by applying a small amount of water or curl refreshing spray.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause them to lose their shape.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.

FAQs About Keeping Bangs Curly

How do I keep my bangs curly overnight?

You can keep your bangs curly overnight by using a satin or silk pillowcase, applying a small amount of product to your hair before bed, and sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or pineapple style. This will help prevent your curls from getting flattened or frizzy while you sleep.

Can I use hairspray to keep my bangs curly?

Yes, you can use hairspray to help hold your curls in place. Just make sure to use a lightweight hairspray and avoid applying too much, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Can I use a diffuser to dry my bangs?

Yes, using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer can help dry your bangs while maintaining their shape. Just make sure to use a low heat setting and avoid touching your hair too much while drying.

Final Thoughts on Keeping Your Bangs Curly

Keeping your bangs curly can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, it's definitely doable. Remember to start with clean, moisturized hair, apply a curl-enhancing product, style your bangs carefully, and finish with a light hairspray. And don't forget to use a satin or silk pillowcase, avoid touching your hair too much, and refresh your curls as needed throughout the day. With these tips and tricks, you can rock your curly bangs with confidence!
