How To Get Round Face Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Nov 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to get a rounder face naturally, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing some tips and tricks that can help you achieve a more circular face shape without resorting to surgery or other invasive procedures. From facial exercises to hair care, we'll cover everything you need to know to get started.

What Causes a Round Face?

Before we dive into how to get a round face naturally, let's first discuss what causes a round face shape. Genetics plays a big role in determining the shape of our faces, but factors such as weight gain, hormonal changes, and age can also contribute to a more circular face.

How to Get Round Face Naturally: Tips and Tricks

Tip #1: Facial Exercises

One of the most effective ways to get a rounder face naturally is through facial exercises. These exercises can help to tone the muscles in your face and promote circulation, which can lead to a more defined and rounded face shape. Some popular facial exercises include smiling, puffing out your cheeks, and pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth.

Tip #2: Massage

Massaging your face can also help to promote circulation and reduce puffiness, which can contribute to a more circular face shape. You can use your fingers or a facial roller to gently massage your face in circular motions, focusing on the cheeks, jawline, and forehead.

Tip #3: Diet and Exercise

Eating a healthy diet and exercising regularly can also help to promote a more rounded face shape. Incorporating foods that are rich in antioxidants and vitamins, such as fruits and vegetables, can help to reduce inflammation and promote skin health. Additionally, exercises such as running, cycling, and swimming can help to burn fat and promote muscle tone, which can contribute to a more defined and circular face shape.

Tip #4: Makeup

Using makeup can also be a great way to enhance your facial features and create the illusion of a rounder face. For example, using a bronzer to contour the cheeks can help to create a more defined and circular appearance. Additionally, using a highlighter on the high points of the face, such as the cheekbones and forehead, can help to create a more lifted and rounded effect.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a round face naturally:

Step 1: Incorporate Facial Exercises

Start incorporating facial exercises into your daily routine. Some popular exercises include smiling, puffing out your cheeks, and pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth.

Step 2: Massage Your Face

Use your fingers or a facial roller to gently massage your face in circular motions, focusing on the cheeks, jawline, and forehead.

Step 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Incorporate foods that are rich in antioxidants and vitamins, such as fruits and vegetables, into your diet. This can help to reduce inflammation and promote skin health.

Step 4: Exercise Regularly

Incorporate exercises such as running, cycling, and swimming into your daily routine to burn fat and promote muscle tone.

Step 5: Use Makeup

Use makeup to enhance your facial features and create the illusion of a rounder face. For example, using a bronzer to contour the cheeks can help to create a more defined and circular appearance.

Tips for Maintaining a Round Face

Here are some tips for maintaining a round face shape:

Tip #1: Avoid Over-Exfoliating

Over-exfoliating can strip the skin of its natural oils and lead to inflammation, which can contribute to a more angular face shape. Instead, opt for gentle exfoliation once or twice a week.

Tip #2: Moisturize Regularly

Moisturizing regularly can help to keep the skin hydrated and plump, which can contribute to a more rounded face shape.

Tip #3: Protect Your Skin from the Sun

Exposure to the sun's UV rays can lead to premature aging and a more angular face shape. Be sure to protect your skin with sunscreen and wear a hat or sunglasses when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

Q: Can I get a round face naturally without surgery?

A: Yes, there are several natural methods you can use to promote a more rounded face shape, including facial exercises, massage, diet and exercise, and makeup.

Q: How long does it take to get a round face naturally?

A: The amount of time it takes to achieve a more rounded face shape naturally will depend on a variety of factors, including your genetics, age, and lifestyle habits. Consistency is key, so be sure to incorporate these tips into your daily routine and be patient.

Q: Are there any risks associated with getting a round face naturally?

A: No, there are no risks associated with getting a round face naturally through methods such as facial exercises, massage, diet and exercise, and makeup. However, if you are considering surgery or other invasive procedures, it's important to consult with a qualified medical professional to discuss the risks and benefits.

Hair Care

Your hairstyle can also play a role in the appearance of your face shape. To enhance a round face, opt for hairstyles that add volume and fullness around the cheeks and jawline. For example, a layered bob or a side-swept bang can help to create a more circular appearance. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as these can elongate the face and create a more angular appearance.

And that's it! By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a more rounded face shape naturally. Remember to be patient and consistent, and you'll be on your way to a more defined and circular appearance in no time.
