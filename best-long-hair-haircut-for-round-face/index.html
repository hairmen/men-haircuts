Best Long Hair Haircut For Round Face

Written by Frank Jun 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying different haircuts that don't flatter your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best long hair haircut for round face that will complement your features and enhance your look. We'll cover everything from the description of the haircut to the tips and hair care routine. So, let's get started.

Description

The best long hair haircut for round face is the layered haircut. This haircut involves adding layers to your long hair, which creates volume and texture. The layers are strategically placed to frame your face and soften your features. This haircut is perfect for round faces as it creates an illusion of length and slims down your face.

How to

The layered haircut for round face involves the following steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and explain what you want to achieve with your haircut.
  2. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  3. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and start cutting the layers. The layers should be longer in the front and shorter in the back.
  5. Once you're done cutting the layers, style your hair using a curling iron or a straightener.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while getting a layered haircut for round face:

  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience in cutting hair for round faces.
  • Don't go for a haircut that's too short as it will make your face look rounder.
  • Keep the layers long and wispy to create a slimming effect.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Don't overdo the layers as it can make your hair look choppy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best long hair haircut for round face:

Q. Will the layered haircut suit all hair types?

A. Yes, the layered haircut is suitable for all hair types, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly.

Q. How often should I trim my hair?

A. It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the layers.

Q. Can I style my hair in different ways with a layered haircut?

A. Yes, you can style your hair in different ways with a layered haircut, such as a messy bun, a ponytail, or a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your hair after getting a layered haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

So, that's all about the best long hair haircut for round face. Follow these tips and take care of your hair to maintain the shape of the layers and enhance your look. If you have any more questions, feel free to ask your hairstylist.
