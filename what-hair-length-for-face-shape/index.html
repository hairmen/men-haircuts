What Hair Length For Face Shape A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Sep 04, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, one of the most important things to consider is your face shape. Different hair lengths can flatter different face shapes, so it's important to find the right one for you. In this guide, we'll go over what hair length for face shape is best and give you some tips for finding your perfect look.

  2. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Length

Description

Choosing the right hair length for your face shape can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. A flattering hairstyle can bring out your best features, while an unflattering one can make you look less put-together. By understanding your face shape and the best hair lengths for it, you can choose a style that complements your natural beauty.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hair length for your face shape is determining what your face shape actually is. There are five main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, and diamond.

To figure out your face shape, start by taking a photo of yourself facing straight ahead. Next, trace the outline of your face on the photo using a marker or highlighter. Finally, compare the shape you've traced to the five face shape categories to determine which one best describes your face.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hair length that elongates your face and draws attention away from your cheeks.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly narrower jawline. If you have an oval face, you're in luck – this face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to hair length. You can experiment with a variety of styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape is characterized by a strong, square jawline and a forehead that's roughly the same width as the jaw. If you have a square face, you'll want to choose a hair length that softens the angles of your face and draws attention upward.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape is characterized by a wide forehead and a narrow, pointed chin. If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a hair length that balances out your forehead and chin, such as a bob or a mid-length style.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with wider cheekbones. If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to choose a hair length that adds fullness to the temples and jawline, such as a layered cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Length

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hair length that flatters it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by looking at hairstyles on people with the same face shape as you. This can give you an idea of what looks good.
  2. Consider your hair texture – certain lengths may work better for fine or thick hair.
  3. Think about your lifestyle – are you willing to put in the time and effort to style a longer cut?
  4. Visit a professional stylist for their opinion and recommendations.
  5. Choose a length that you feel confident and comfortable in!

Tips for Styling Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape

Once you've chosen a hair length that flatters your face shape, it's important to style it in a way that accentuates your features. Here are some tips:

  • Round face shape: Add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  • Oval face shape: Experiment with different parts and textures to find what looks best on you.
  • Square face shape: Soften angles with layers and side-swept bangs.
  • Heart face shape: Add volume to the chin area with curls or waves.
  • Diamond face shape: Emphasize the cheekbones with a side part and layered cut.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about hair length and face shape:

What hair length looks best on a round face?

A longer, layered cut is generally flattering on a round face, as it elongates the face and draws attention away from the cheeks.

Can an oval face shape pull off any hair length?

Yes – oval face shapes are considered the most versatile when it comes to hair length, so you can experiment with a variety of styles.

What hair length looks best on a square face?

Soft, layered cuts that add movement to the hair are flattering on a square face. A side-swept fringe can also help to soften the angles of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Style

Once you've found the perfect hair length for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Finding the right hair length for your face shape can take some trial and error, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to a flattering and beautiful hairstyle. Remember to choose a length that makes you feel confident and comfortable, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
