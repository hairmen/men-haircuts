Are you someone who is self-conscious about the roundness of your face? Do you feel like it makes you look heavier or younger than you actually are? If so, don't worry! There are some simple hair tricks you can use to make your face appear less round. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to make face look less round hair.

How to Make Your Face Look Less Round with Hair

The key to making your face appear less round with hair is to create angles and volume around your face. This can be achieved through haircuts, hairstyles, and hair accessories.

Haircuts

When it comes to haircuts, there are a few things to keep in mind if you want to make your face appear less round.

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin. This will only emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Instead, opt for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement around your face.
  • Consider getting an angled bob or a long layered haircut that hits below your chin. This will create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

Hairstyles

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles that flatter a round face. Here are some ideas:

  • Part your hair to the side. This will create a diagonal line that adds angles to your face.
  • Avoid center parts, which can make your face appear more round.
  • Try a messy bun or top knot. This will add height to your hair and create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Experiment with loose waves or curls. This will add volume and movement around your face.

Hair Accessories

Finally, hair accessories can also help create angles and volume around your face. Here are some options to consider:

  • Use a headband to push your hair back and create height at the crown of your head.
  • Try a scarf or bandana tied around the top of your head. This will add volume and create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Experiment with hair clips or barrettes that create angles and movement around your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for how to make face look less round hair:

  1. Start with a layered haircut that adds volume and movement around your face.
  2. Part your hair to the side to create a diagonal line that adds angles to your face.
  3. Avoid center parts, which can make your face appear more round.
  4. Experiment with messy buns, top knots, loose waves, or curls to add volume and movement around your face.
  5. Use a headband, scarf, or bandana to create height and volume at the crown of your head.
  6. Try hair clips or barrettes that create angles and movement around your face.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for how to make your face appear less round with hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as this will emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for you.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new things! It may take some trial and error to find the perfect haircut and hairstyle for your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have curly or frizzy hair?

Curly or frizzy hair can be challenging to manage, but the same principles apply for making your face appear less round. Consider getting a layered haircut that adds volume and movement, and experiment with loose curls or waves. You can also try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help tame frizz and create defined curls.

What if I have a short haircut?

Short haircuts can still be flattering for a round face! Consider getting a pixie cut with plenty of volume on top, or a short, layered bob that hits at the jawline. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as this will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

Hair Care for a Round Face

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair if you want it to look its best. Here are some tips for hair care with a round face:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it more difficult to style.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your face appear less round and feel more confident in your appearance. Remember to experiment with different haircuts, hairstyles, and accessories to see what works best for you.
