How To Cut Your Own Short Hair Style

Written by Eveline Sep 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short hair can be a great way to update your look without having to cut too much off. However, going to a hair salon can be expensive, so why not try cutting your own hair at home? With the right tools and techniques, it’s possible to create a stylish short haircut from the comfort of your own home. This guide will provide you with tips and tricks on how to cut your own short hair style.

How To

Before you start, you will need to gather the right tools. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It’s also a good idea to have some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 1: Decide on Your Style

The first step is to decide on the style you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines and decide on the length and shape you want. It’s important to be realistic about your abilities and choose a style that you can achieve at home.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly before you start cutting. This will make it easier to see the natural shape of your hair and to cut it evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into manageable sections. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the hair at the back of your head. Use small sections and cut in a straight line. Work your way around your head, cutting the hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Once you have cut the back, move on to the sides. You can use a mirror to guide you and make sure that both sides are even.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, unclip the top section of your hair and cut it to the desired length. Use your comb to lift the hair up and cut it in a straight line.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own short hair style:

  • Start with small sections and take your time
  • Always cut in a straight line
  • Use a mirror to guide you
  • Don’t cut too much off at once
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to keep it looking neat

FAQ

Can I cut my own short hair style?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, it’s possible to cut your own short hair style at home.

What tools do I need to cut my own short hair style?

You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It’s also a good idea to have some hair clips to section off your hair.

How do I choose the right short hair style for me?

Look for inspiration online or in magazines and decide on the length and shape you want. It’s important to be realistic about your abilities and choose a style that you can achieve at home.

Hair Care

After cutting your own short hair style, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Trim the ends of your hair regularly to keep it looking neat, and visit a hair salon for a professional cut every few months.

Cutting your own short hair style can be a great way to save money and update your look. With the right tools and techniques, it’s possible to achieve a stylish haircut from the comfort of your own home. Follow these tips and tricks to get the best results and take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
