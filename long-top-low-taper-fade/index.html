Long Top Low Taper Fade A Trendy Hairstyle For Men

Written by Aprilia Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to rock in 2023? Long top low taper fade might just be the look for you! This trendy hairstyle combines the best of both worlds: a longer, textured top and a clean, tapered fade on the sides and back. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this look, give you some tips for maintenance, and answer some frequently asked questions about this style.

How to Achieve the Long Top Low Taper Fade

The long top low taper fade requires some skill and precision, so we recommend going to a professional barber or stylist for best results. However, if you want to attempt it at home, here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Grow Out the Top

The top of your hair should be several inches long to achieve the textured, layered effect. Don't be afraid to let it grow out for a few weeks or months if necessary.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of the Fade

The fade can be different lengths depending on your preference. A low fade starts just above the ear and gradually fades into your skin, while a high fade starts at the temple and goes all the way to the top.

Step 3: Use Clippers to Create the Fade

Start with a longer guard on your clippers and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard as you move towards the bottom. Use a comb to help guide the clippers and create a smooth, even fade.

Step 4: Texturize the Top

Use scissors or a razor to add texture and layers to the top of your hair. This will give it a more natural, tousled look.

Step 5: Style as Desired

Use a styling product like pomade or wax to create the desired look. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy and textured.

Tips for Maintenance

Once you have the long top low taper fade, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking fresh:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a beard trim or shaping to complement your new hairstyle.

FAQ

How long does my hair need to be for the long top low taper fade?

The top of your hair should be several inches long, while the fade can be as short as you want it.

Is the long top low taper fade suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this style can work for a variety of face shapes. However, you may want to consult with your barber or stylist to determine the best length and shape for your features.

Can I still wear a hat with this hairstyle?

Yes, a hat can still be worn with this style. Just be sure to style your hair in a way that won't get flattened or messed up by the hat.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is crucial for maintaining this hairstyle. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid overwashing or using too much heat on your hair. You may also want to consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your locks looking their best.

With the long top low taper fade, you can rock a stylish, modern look that's both versatile and easy to maintain. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be turning heads in no time!
