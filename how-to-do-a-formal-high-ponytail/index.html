How To Do A Formal High Ponytail

Written by William Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a classic hairstyle that's perfect for any formal occasion, a high ponytail is a great option. This sleek and sophisticated style is easy to achieve and can be customized to suit your individual preferences. In this article, we'll show you how to do a formal high ponytail step by step, along with some tips and FAQs to ensure you get the perfect look every time.

How to Do a Formal High Ponytail

The first step to achieving a perfect high ponytail is to start with clean, dry hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first to achieve a sleeker look. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. Use a comb to ensure your hair is smooth and straight.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section off the top portion of your hair using a hair tie or clip. This will make it easier to create a high ponytail without any bumps or lumps.

Step 3: Create the ponytail

Gather the remaining hair at the back of your head and use a hair tie to create a ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 4: Tease the crown

Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and use a comb to tease it lightly. This will create volume at the crown of your head and give your ponytail a more polished look.

Step 5: Wrap the hair tie

Take a small piece of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it. Use a bobby pin to secure the end of the hair in place.

Step 6: Finishing touches

Finally, use a small amount of hairspray to hold your ponytail in place and smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect formal high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Add hair extensions if you want to create a longer or fuller ponytail.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as bows or ribbons, to add some extra flair to your ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a formal high ponytail:

How high should I wear my ponytail?

A formal high ponytail should be positioned at the crown of your head, just above the nape of your neck.

How do I prevent my ponytail from slipping down?

Make sure your hair tie is tight and secure, and use bobby pins to anchor any loose strands in place. You can also use a little bit of hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.

Can I do a formal high ponytail with curly hair?

Absolutely! You may want to straighten your hair first to achieve a sleeker look, but you can also embrace your natural curls and create a more voluminous ponytail.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after doing a formal high ponytail. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools, such as a straightener or curling iron.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

In conclusion, a formal high ponytail is a classic and sophisticated hairstyle that's perfect for any formal occasion. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect high ponytail every time.
