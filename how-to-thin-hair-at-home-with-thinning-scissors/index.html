How To Thin Hair At Home With Thinning Scissors A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning hair can be a real problem for many people. It can make hair appear limp, lifeless, and lacking in volume. Fortunately, there is a solution to this problem. You can thin your hair at home with thinning scissors. This guide will show you how to do it step-by-step.

Description

Thinning scissors are a special type of scissors that have teeth or notches on one of the blades. These teeth or notches can be used to cut hair in a way that thins it out without removing too much length. Thinning scissors can be used on any type of hair, but they are particularly helpful for those with thick, heavy hair.

How to Thin Hair at Home with Thinning Scissors

Thinning hair at home with thinning scissors is an easy and cost-effective way to achieve a more voluminous look. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely.
  2. Comb your hair and remove any knots or tangles.
  3. Part your hair down the middle and divide it into two sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and hold it up with your fingers.
  5. Using the thinning scissors, cut into the section of hair about halfway down the length of the hair. Do not cut all the way through the hair.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you have thinned out the entire section of hair.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 on the other side of your head.
  8. Check the back of your head in a mirror to make sure the hair is evenly thinned out.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Thinning Hair at Home

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning your hair at home:

  • Use sharp, high-quality thinning scissors.
  • Thinning scissors work best on dry hair, so make sure your hair is completely dry before you start.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure that your hair is evenly thinned out.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair with each snip.

FAQs

Here are some common questions people have about thinning hair with thinning scissors:

Can I use regular scissors to thin my hair?

No, regular scissors are not designed for thinning hair. They can create blunt lines and remove too much hair, which can make your hair appear uneven and choppy.

How often should I thin my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should thin your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the desired look.

Can thinning scissors damage my hair?

No, thinning scissors are designed to thin hair without causing damage. However, if you use them incorrectly or on wet hair, they can cause breakage and split ends.

Hair Care After Thinning

After thinning your hair with thinning scissors, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and maintain the desired length.

Now that you know how to thin your hair at home with thinning scissors, you can achieve the voluminous, healthy-looking hair you've always wanted. Follow these steps and tips for best results and enjoy your new look!
