How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to give yourself or your loved one a fresh haircut? Cutting men's hair with clippers fade is a popular and stylish option that you can easily do at home. In this guide, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of achieving a perfect fade with clippers. Let's get started!

Description

A fade is a hairstyle that gradually transitions from short to long hair. It is a popular style for men of all ages and can be achieved with clippers. Cutting men's hair with clippers fade is a relatively simple process that requires some practice and patience. In this guide, we will show you how to do it yourself or with the help of a friend or family member.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers Fade

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a set of clippers, a comb, hair scissors, and a mirror. You may also want to have some hair clips or ties to keep the hair out of the way.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want the hair to be. You can use different guard sizes for different lengths. Start with a higher guard size and gradually work your way down for a fade effect. You can also use scissors to blend the hair between different guard sizes.

Step 3: Start from the Bottom

Start trimming the hair from the bottom of the head, around the neck area. Use a higher guard size and work your way up in a straight line. Make sure to trim evenly on both sides.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Next, move to the sides of the head. Use a lower guard size and start from the bottom, working your way up. Use the comb to blend the hair between different guard sizes for a smooth transition.

Step 5: Work on the Top

Finally, work on the top of the head. Use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length and blend it with the sides. Use the comb to make sure the hair is even and natural-looking.

Step 6: Clean Up

Clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neck with the clippers or scissors. Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers Fade

  • Practice on a mannequin head or a willing volunteer before you try cutting your own hair.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and make sure the hair is even.
  • Start with a higher guard size and gradually work your way down for a fade effect.
  • Blend the hair between different guard sizes for a smooth transition.
  • Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of the head and blend it with the sides.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers fade?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers fade. However, it may be easier to have someone else do it for you, especially if you're not experienced with cutting hair.

What guard size should I use for a fade?

Start with a higher guard size, such as 4 or 6, and gradually work your way down to 1 or 2 for a fade effect.

How do I blend the hair between different guard sizes?

Use scissors to blend the hair between different guard sizes. Cut the hair at an angle and use the comb to make sure it's even.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers fade, make sure to wash it thoroughly with shampoo and conditioner. You can also style it with hair products, such as gel or wax, for a polished look.

Cutting men's hair with clippers fade is a popular and stylish option that you can easily do at home. With some practice and patience, you can achieve a perfect fade that will make you look and feel great. Use this step-by-step guide and tips to get started and enjoy your new haircut!
