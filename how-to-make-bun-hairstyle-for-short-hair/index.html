How To Make Bun Hairstyle For Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 27, 2023 · 3 min read

Short hair can be challenging to style, especially when it comes to creating a bun. However, with a little creativity and the right technique, you can achieve a beautiful and elegant bun hairstyle. In this article, we’ll show you how to make bun hairstyle for short hair step by step, provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

A bun is a classic hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion, from casual to formal events. It is a versatile style that can be adapted to suit different hair lengths and textures. However, many people believe that buns are only for long hair, but that’s not true. Short hair can also be styled into a bun, and it can look just as stunning as long hair.

How to

Here’s how to make bun hairstyle for short hair:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply some heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Blow-dry your hair and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Divide your hair

Divide your hair into two sections, the top half and the bottom half. Secure the top half with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Create a bun with the bottom half

Twist the bottom half of your hair into a tight bun, and secure it with bobby pins. Make sure the bun is tight and secure.

Step 4: Create a bun with the top half

Take the top half of your hair and twist it around the bun you created with the bottom half. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 5: Finishing touches

Use a hairspray to keep your bun in place. You can also use a hair accessory, such as a headband or hairpin, to add some extra flair to your bun.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your bun hairstyle look even better:

  • Use a hair donut to create a fuller and more voluminous bun.
  • Add some texture to your hair by curling or braiding it before creating the bun.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

FAQ

Can I make a bun with really short hair?

Yes, you can still make a bun with really short hair. Use a hair donut or a sock bun to create the illusion of longer hair.

Can I make a bun with thin hair?

Yes, you can still make a bun with thin hair. Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some texture and volume to your hair.

How long does it take to make a bun hairstyle?

It depends on your hair length and the style of bun you want to create. It can take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes.

Hair Care

It’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying and straightening.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a beautiful and elegant bun hairstyle for short hair. Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you.
