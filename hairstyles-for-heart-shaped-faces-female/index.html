Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces Female

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you have a wider forehead and a narrower chin, creating a beautiful and unique facial structure. However, when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle, it can be challenging to find one that flatters your features. In this article, we will provide you with a guide to the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces female, along with tips and tricks to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for you.

Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, a narrower chin, and high cheekbones. The goal of finding the perfect hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the proportions and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. The ideal hairstyles for heart shaped faces female are those that add volume and width at the jawline while softening the forehead and temples.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for your heart shaped face, it is essential to consider your hair texture, length, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

It is crucial to identify your face shape before choosing a hairstyle. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture and Length

Your hair texture and length will determine which hairstyles will look best on you. For example, if you have thin hair, you may want to choose a shorter hairstyle that adds volume and texture.

3. Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is an essential factor when choosing a hairstyle. If you prefer a low-maintenance look, you may want to choose a hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces Female

Now that you know how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face, here are some of the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces female:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for heart shaped faces because they add volume and width at the jawline, creating a balanced look. Soft waves or curls can also help soften the forehead and temples.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is an excellent choice for heart shaped faces because it adds volume and width at the jawline while keeping the focus on the eyes and cheekbones. A chin-length bob with layers can also help soften the forehead and temples.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for heart shaped faces because they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while softening the forehead. Make sure the bangs are long enough to sweep to the side and blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and trendy option for heart shaped faces. It adds volume and width at the jawline while keeping the focus on the eyes and cheekbones. A pixie cut with longer layers on top can also help soften the forehead and temples.

5. Half-Up, Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for heart shaped faces because it adds volume and width at the jawline while keeping the focus on the eyes and cheekbones. Soft waves or curls can also help soften the forehead and temples.

FAQ

What hairstyles should heart shaped faces avoid?

Heart shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top of the head, such as high ponytails or top knots. These hairstyles can make the forehead appear even wider and throw off the balance of the face.

Can heart shaped faces wear short hair?

Yes, heart shaped faces can wear short hair. However, it is essential to choose a style that adds volume and width at the jawline while keeping the focus on the eyes and cheekbones.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your hairstyle, it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for heart shaped faces:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you use styling tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

4. Use Hair Masks

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Look for a mask that is suitable for your hair type and needs.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face can be challenging, but with these tips and hairstyles for heart shaped faces female, you can find a style that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
