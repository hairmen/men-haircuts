How To Get Messy Short Hair Look

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a trendy, low-maintenance hairstyle, the messy short hair look might be perfect for you. It’s a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, and it’s easy to achieve with the right products and techniques. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the messy short hair look.

Description

The messy short hair look is a popular hairstyle that’s characterized by its tousled, textured appearance. It’s a low-maintenance style that’s perfect for people who want a trendy look without the hassle of daily styling. It’s also a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it suitable for a variety of occasions.

How to Get Messy Short Hair Look

To achieve the messy short hair look, you’ll need the right products and tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Texturizing spray or cream
  • Hairspray
  • Pomade or wax
  • Blow dryer
  • Round brush

Follow these steps to get the messy short hair look:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, then use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray or Cream

Apply a texturizing spray or cream to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, creating a tousled, messy look.

Step 3: Add Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hairspray to help hold the texture in place. You don’t want your hair to feel stiff or crunchy, so use a light hand with the hairspray.

Step 4: Use Pomade or Wax

Take a small amount of pomade or wax and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help create definition and separation, giving your hair a messy, textured look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a final spritz of hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect messy short hair look:

  • Don’t overdo it with the products. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use your fingers to create texture and separation in your hair. Don’t be afraid to mess it up a little!
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays and creams to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse before blow drying to add extra lift and body.

FAQ

How long does the messy short hair look last?

The messy short hair look can last for up to three days with the right products and techniques. However, you may need to touch up your hair with a bit of pomade or hairspray each day to maintain the style.

Can I achieve the messy short hair look with long hair?

The messy short hair look is designed for short hair, but you can achieve a similar look with long hair by creating layers and using the right products.

Do I need to use a blow dryer to achieve the messy short hair look?

A blow dryer can help add volume and texture to your hair, but it’s not essential. You can achieve the messy short hair look by using your fingers to create texture and separation in your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the trendy, low-maintenance messy short hair look that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re heading to work or out for a night on the town, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
