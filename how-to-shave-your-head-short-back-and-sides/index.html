How To Shave Your Head Short Back And Sides

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to try something new? Shaving your head short back and sides is a great option for a fresh and stylish look. In this article, we will guide you through the process of shaving your head and provide some tips for hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Shave Your Head Short Back and Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Shave
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a good quality electric shaver or hair clippers, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

If your hair is long, use the clippers to trim it down to a manageable length. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Wet your hair with warm water to soften it and make shaving easier. You can also apply shaving cream or gel if you prefer.

Step 3: Shave the Back of Your Head

Start at the back of your head and work your way up. Use the clippers or shaver to shave against the grain, moving from the bottom of your head to the top.

Step 4: Shave the Sides of Your Head

Next, move to the sides of your head. Use the same technique as before, shaving against the grain from the bottom up.

Step 5: Shave the Top of Your Head

Finally, shave the top of your head. Use the comb to lift your hair and shave against the grain, moving from the front to the back of your head.

Tips for a Perfect Shave

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect shave:

  • Use a sharp blade or clippers to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Shave in a well-lit room with a mirror to see what you're doing.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to avoid missing spots.
  • Apply a moisturizer or aftershave lotion to soothe your skin after shaving.

FAQs

Can I shave my head with a razor?

You can, but it's not recommended. Razors can cause cuts, razor burn, and ingrown hairs. Electric shavers or hair clippers are a safer and more efficient option.

How often do I need to shave my head?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Most people need to shave their head every 1-2 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care After Shaving

After shaving your head, it's important to take care of your scalp and hair to prevent irritation and dryness. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your scalp with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and oil.
  • Apply a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid exposing your scalp to direct sunlight for long periods of time.
  • Wear a hat or use sunscreen to protect your scalp from UV rays.

Congratulations, you now know how to shave your head short back and sides! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that will turn heads. Remember to take care of your scalp and hair to keep them healthy and looking great.
