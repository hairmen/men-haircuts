How To Make Long Guy Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair on men has become increasingly popular in recent years. However, it can be challenging to style and maintain, especially if you're new to having long hair. In this article, we will be discussing how to make long guy hair look good, from hair care tips to styling tricks.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. Styling
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ

Hair Care

Before we get into styling long guy hair, it's essential to establish a good hair care routine. Long hair requires more attention and care than short hair, so it's crucial to invest in the right products.

First, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type. If you have dry hair, look for products with hydrating ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove excess oil buildup.

It's also essential to use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Brush your hair regularly using a wide-tooth comb or a brush designed for detangling long hair. Finally, consider getting regular trims every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Styling

Now that you have a good hair care routine in place, it's time to start styling your long guy hair. The key to making long hair look good is to find a style that complements your facial features and hair type. Here are a few popular long hairstyles for men:

Man Bun

The man bun is a classic long hairstyle that involves pulling your hair back into a bun at the crown of your head. This style works well for men with thick, wavy hair and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Half-Up Half-Down

If you want to show off your long locks while keeping your hair out of your face, try the half-up half-down style. This involves pulling the top half of your hair back into a ponytail or bun while leaving the rest down. This style works well for men with straight or wavy hair.

Layered

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, consider getting a layered haircut to add more texture and volume to your hair. This style involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create layers and can be styled with a side part or left natural.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for making long guy hair look good:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • If you're new to having long hair, be patient. It takes time for hair to grow, so don't expect to have a long mane overnight.
  • If you're struggling to style your hair, consider visiting a hairstylist who specializes in long hair.

FAQ

How often should I wash my long guy hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently than someone with dry hair. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Do I need to use a different shampoo and conditioner for long hair?

Not necessarily. However, if you have long hair, you may want to invest in a shampoo and conditioner designed for dry or damaged hair to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

Can I still style my hair if it's thinning?

Absolutely. There are many hairstyles that work well for men with thinning hair, including the classic buzz cut or a short textured crop. Talk to your hairstylist about which style would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Conclusion

Maintaining long guy hair takes time and effort, but with the right hair care routine and styling tips, you can make it look good. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to seek help from a hairstylist if you're struggling to achieve the look you want.
