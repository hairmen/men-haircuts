History

The religion that only wears long skirts is called Apostolic Pentecostalism. It started in the early 20th century in the United States and is now practiced worldwide. The religion is based on the Pentecostal movement and emphasizes the importance of holiness and spiritual purity.

Apostolic Pentecostals believe in the Holy Trinity, salvation through Jesus Christ, and the baptism of the Holy Spirit. They also believe in the gift of speaking in tongues and divine healing. The religion is known for its strict adherence to the Bible and its teachings.

Beliefs

Apostolic Pentecostals believe that the Bible is the literal word of God and should be followed in every aspect of life. They believe in modesty, purity, and separation from the world. They also believe in the importance of spiritual gifts and the power of prayer.

Customs

Apostolic Pentecostals have several customs that they follow. They believe in dressing modestly, refraining from drinking alcohol and smoking, and avoiding worldly activities such as dancing and watching movies. They also believe in attending church regularly and participating in religious activities such as prayer and fasting.

Why They Only Wear Long Skirts

One of the most distinctive customs of Apostolic Pentecostals is their dress code. Women are required to wear long skirts and avoid wearing pants or shorts. This custom is based on the belief that women should dress modestly and not draw attention to themselves.

Apostolic Pentecostals believe that wearing pants or shorts is immodest and inappropriate for women. They believe that women should cover their bodies and not reveal too much skin. Wearing long skirts is seen as a way to show respect for God and to avoid tempting men.

How to Dress Appropriately

If you are interested in dressing appropriately for an Apostolic Pentecostal church service or event, there are a few guidelines to follow. Women should wear long skirts that cover their ankles and avoid wearing pants or shorts. They should also wear modest tops that cover their shoulders and chest.

Men should wear dress pants and a dress shirt. They should avoid wearing jeans, t-shirts, or casual wear. It's also important to avoid wearing jewelry or makeup that is too flashy or attention-grabbing.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to dress appropriately for an Apostolic Pentecostal church service or event:

  1. Choose a long skirt that covers your ankles.
  2. Wear a modest top that covers your shoulders and chest.
  3. Avoid wearing pants or shorts.
  4. Wear dress shoes or sandals.
  5. Avoid wearing jewelry or makeup that is too flashy or attention-grabbing.

Tips for Dressing Modestly

If you want to dress modestly but still look fashionable, there are a few tips to keep in mind. You can wear long skirts that have a flowy or A-line silhouette, which can be both stylish and modest. You can also wear tops with high necklines or long sleeves to cover your arms and chest.

Accessorizing with scarves or jewelry can also add a touch of personality to your outfit without being too flashy. Finally, choose shoes that are comfortable and stylish, such as ballet flats or ankle boots.

FAQs

Q: Can women wear pants or shorts in Apostolic Pentecostalism?

A: No, women are required to wear long skirts and avoid wearing pants or shorts.

Q: Can men wear jeans to church in Apostolic Pentecostalism?

A: No, men are required to wear dress pants and a dress shirt and avoid wearing jeans, t-shirts, or casual wear.

Q: Why do Apostolic Pentecostals dress modestly?

Hair Care Tips

For women in Apostolic Pentecostalism, hair care is an important part of their appearance. They often wear their hair long and avoid cutting it. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid damaging your hair.

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
