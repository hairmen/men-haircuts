How To Find Out If Bangs Suit You A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're considering getting bangs, it's important to know whether or not they will suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to find out if bangs are right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Assessing Your Personal Style
  • Analyzing Your Facial Proportions
  • Trying On Different Bang Styles
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether bangs will suit you is to determine your face shape. Some face shapes are more suited to bangs than others, so it's important to know which category you fall into. The most common face shapes are:

Oval:

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is the most versatile and can pull off most haircuts and styles, including bangs.

Round:

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to avoid blunt bangs that sit straight across your forehead. Instead, opt for wispy, side-swept bangs that will help to elongate your face.

Square:

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs. Instead, opt for soft, wispy bangs that will help to soften your features.

Heart:

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that sit straight across your forehead. Instead, opt for wispy, side-swept bangs that will help to balance out your features.

Long:

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that sit straight across your forehead. Instead, opt for wispy, side-swept bangs that will help to shorten the appearance of your face.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when determining whether bangs will suit you is your hair type. Some hair types are more suited to bangs than others, so it's important to know which category you fall into. The most common hair types are:

Straight:

If you have straight hair, you're in luck! Straight hair is the easiest to style and can pull off most bang styles.

Wavy:

If you have wavy hair, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that sit straight across your forehead. Instead, opt for wispy, side-swept bangs that will help to blend in with your natural texture.

Curly:

If you have curly hair, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that sit straight across your forehead. Instead, opt for soft, wispy bangs that will help to complement your curls.

Assessing Your Personal Style

Your personal style is an important factor to consider when determining whether bangs will suit you. If you have a more edgy or trendy style, you may be able to pull off more daring bang styles. If you have a more classic or conservative style, you'll want to opt for more subtle, classic bang styles.

Analyzing Your Facial Proportions

Another important factor to consider when determining whether bangs will suit you is your facial proportions. If you have a smaller forehead, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can overwhelm your features. If you have a larger forehead, you may want to opt for heavier, blunt bangs that will help to balance out your features.

Trying On Different Bang Styles

The best way to determine whether bangs will suit you is to try on different styles. You can do this by using a virtual makeover tool or by trying on wigs with different bang styles. This will give you a better idea of what styles work best with your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you've decided to get bangs, it's important to know how to maintain them. Here are some tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blot your bangs with a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.
  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay.
  • Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your bangs to help them stay in place.

FAQs

Q: Will bangs suit me if I have a round face shape?

A: Yes, bangs can suit you if you have a round face shape. However, you'll want to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs and opt for wispy, side-swept bangs instead.

Q: Can I still pull off bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still pull off bangs if you have curly hair. However, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs and opt for soft, wispy bangs that will complement your natural texture.

Q: How do I know which bang style is right for me?

A: The best way to determine which bang style is right for you is to try on different styles. You can do this by using a virtual makeover tool or by trying on wigs with different bang styles.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to determine whether or not bangs are right for you. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
