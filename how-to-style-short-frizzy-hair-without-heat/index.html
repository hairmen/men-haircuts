How To Style Short Frizzy Hair Without Heat

Written by Petter Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Frizzy hair can be a struggle, especially when you have short hair. Heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage your hair, making it even more difficult to manage. But don't worry, there are plenty of ways to style short frizzy hair without heat. In this article, we'll show you how to do just that!

Description

Short frizzy hair can be a challenge to style, but it doesn't have to be. With the right techniques, you can create beautiful hairstyles without using any heat. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style your short frizzy hair, as well as tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

How to Style Short Frizzy Hair Without Heat

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to styling short frizzy hair without heat is to wash and condition your hair. Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for frizzy hair. These products will help to smooth your hair and prevent frizz. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz. Use a small amount of product and distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Be careful not to apply too much, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

Step 4: Try a Twist Out

A twist out is a great hairstyle for short frizzy hair. Start by dividing your hair into sections and then twist each section tightly. Leave the twists in overnight or for several hours, and then unravel them to reveal beautiful, defined curls.

Step 5: Experiment with Braids

Braids are another great way to style short frizzy hair without heat. You can create a variety of braided styles, from simple two-strand braids to more complex styles like French braids. Experiment to find the styles that work best for you.

Step 6: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and clips can help to add some flair to your hairstyle. Experiment with different accessories to find the ones that work best for you and your hair.

Tips

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I use heat on my short frizzy hair?

A: While it's best to avoid using heat styling tools on your hair as much as possible, you can use them occasionally. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short frizzy hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with frizzy hair find that washing their hair every other day works best, while others prefer to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find the washing schedule that works best for you.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to managing frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid over-washing your hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Be gentle when detangling your hair and avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
