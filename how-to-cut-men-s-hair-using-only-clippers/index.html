How To Cut Men s Hair Using Only Clippers

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to give yourself or someone else a haircut but don't have access to scissors or other cutting tools, using clippers can be a great alternative. Not only are they relatively easy to use, but they can also provide an even and clean-cut look. Here's how to cut men's hair using only clippers.

Description

Clippers are electric hair-cutting tools that can be used to create a variety of hairstyles. They come with different guards that attach to the clipper blades to achieve different hair lengths. Clippers can be used to cut hair on the sides and back of the head, as well as to blend hair from different lengths.

How to

Before starting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. It's also a good idea to have a mirror nearby so you can see the back of your head. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Guard Length

Start by choosing the guard length you want to use. The guard will determine the length of the hair that's left after cutting. If you're not sure what length to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 2: Begin Cutting

Start cutting at the bottom of the head, using the clipper to create a straight line. Move the clipper up the head, cutting in a straight line. Once you reach the crown of the head, move to the sides and back, repeating the process to achieve an even cut.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

After the hair has been cut to the desired length, it's time to blend the different lengths. Remove the guard from the clippers and use the bare blades to blend the hair from different lengths. Use a sweeping motion to create a natural, even blend.

Step 4: Clean up the Edges

Finally, use the clippers to clean up the edges around the ears and at the nape of the neck. You can also use scissors or a razor to achieve a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut using only clippers:

  • Use a comb to separate sections of hair and make sure they're all the same length.
  • Go slowly and take your time to ensure an even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Always start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair using only clippers?

Yes, you can. Just make sure you use a mirror to see the back of your head and go slowly to ensure an even cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length and style of your hair. Generally, men's hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use clippers to create different hairstyles?

Yes, you can. Clippers can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, including buzz cuts, fades, and crew cuts.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.
