What Hairstyle Makes Your Face Look Skinnier

Written by Aprilia Jun 27, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about the shape of your face? A great way to give the illusion of a slimmer face is to choose the right hairstyle. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle makes your face look skinnier, as well as tips and tricks to make sure you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The right hairstyle can make all the difference in how your face looks. If you have a round face, you want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, you want to opt for hairstyles that create height and length to your face. This will give the illusion of a slimmer face.

If you have a square face, you want to soften the angles of your face. Soft, wavy hairstyles work well for this face shape. You also want to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the crown of your head.

If you have an oval face, you are lucky! This face shape can pull off just about any hairstyle. However, if you want to make your face look slimmer, you can try a side-swept bang or a pixie cut.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle that makes your face look skinnier, you want to consider the length and volume of your hair. Short hairstyles work well for round faces, while longer hairstyles work well for square faces. The key is to create dimension and height to your hair.

You also want to consider the texture of your hair. Soft waves and curls can help soften the angles of a square face. On the other hand, straight hair can make a round face look even rounder.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a hairstyle that makes your face look skinnier:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.
  3. Add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. Create height and length to your hair.

Tips

  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine what hairstyle works best for your face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair to create dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that works for you.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is a short, layered haircut. This style adds height to your hair and creates the illusion of a slimmer face.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

The best hairstyle for a square face is a soft, wavy hairstyle. This style helps to soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

An oval face can pull off just about any hairstyle. However, if you want to make your face look slimmer, a side-swept bang or a pixie cut can do the trick.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

You can also use hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

By taking care of your hair and choosing the right hairstyle, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face and feel more confident in your appearance.
