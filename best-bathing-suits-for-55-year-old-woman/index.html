Best Bathing Suits For 55 Year Old Woman A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 15, 2023 · 4 min read

As a 55 year old woman, finding the perfect bathing suit can be a challenge. You want to feel confident and comfortable while also staying on-trend. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide on the best one for you. In this blog post, we'll explore the best bathing suits for 55 year old woman, how to choose the right one for your body type, and some tips for maintaining healthy hair while swimming.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Bathing Suit
  • Best Types of Bathing Suits for 55 Year Old Woman
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair While Swimming
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Right Bathing Suit

When choosing a bathing suit, it's important to consider your body type and what areas you want to accentuate or hide. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Determine Your Body Type

There are four main body types: hourglass, pear, apple, and rectangle. Determine which one you are to find the most flattering bathing suit for your shape.

2. Consider Your Problem Areas

If you're self-conscious about certain areas, such as your stomach or thighs, look for bathing suits that provide coverage in those areas. For example, a one-piece bathing suit with ruching can help hide a tummy bulge.

3. Choose the Right Size

Make sure to choose a bathing suit that fits properly. If it's too small, it can create unflattering bulges, while a suit that's too large can look baggy and unflattering. Don't be afraid to try on multiple sizes to find the best fit.

Best Types of Bathing Suits for 55 Year Old Woman

Now that you know how to choose the right bathing suit, let's explore the best types for 55 year old women:

1. One-Piece Bathing Suits

One-piece bathing suits are a classic option that can be both flattering and comfortable. Look for suits with ruching or shirring to help hide any problem areas. You can also find one-piece suits with built-in support to help lift and shape your bust.

2. Tankinis

Tankinis are a great option if you want the coverage of a one-piece but the convenience of a two-piece. Look for tankinis with high-waisted bottoms to help smooth out your tummy area.

3. High-Waisted Bikinis

High-waisted bikinis are a trendy and flattering option for 55 year old women. They provide coverage for your stomach while accentuating your waistline. Look for bikinis with built-in support to help lift and shape your bust.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair While Swimming

Swimming can be harsh on your hair, but there are ways to maintain healthy locks while enjoying the water:

1. Wet Your Hair Before Swimming

Wet your hair with fresh water before getting into the pool. This will help prevent your hair from absorbing as much chlorine or saltwater.

2. Wear a Swim Cap

Wearing a swim cap can help protect your hair from the harsh chemicals in the water. It can also help keep your hair out of your face while swimming.

3. Rinse Your Hair After Swimming

Rinse your hair with fresh water immediately after swimming to help remove any chlorine or saltwater. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help prevent damage and keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

1. What color bathing suit is most flattering?

Dark colors, such as black or navy, are the most flattering for bathing suits. They help create a slimming effect and can hide any problem areas.

2. What type of bathing suit is best for a pear-shaped body?

A bathing suit with a high-waisted bottom and a halter top can help balance out a pear-shaped body. Look for bottoms with ruching or shirring to help hide any problem areas.

3. How do I know what size bathing suit to get?

Try on multiple sizes to find the best fit. Make sure the suit is snug but not too tight, and doesn't create any unflattering bulges.

With these tips and recommendations, you're sure to find the best bathing suit for your body type and age. Remember to take care of your hair while swimming to keep it healthy and hydrated. Enjoy your time in the water!
