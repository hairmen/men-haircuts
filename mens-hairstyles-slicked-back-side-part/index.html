Mens Hairstyles Slicked Back Side Part A Timeless Look In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 03, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a classic and stylish hairstyle, the slicked back side part is a great choice. This look has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we'll explore how to achieve the perfect slicked back side part, as well as tips for maintaining and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Slicked Back Side Part
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Style
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The slicked back side part is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a timeless look that is both stylish and sophisticated. This hairstyle is perfect for formal events, such as weddings or business meetings, but can also be worn casually. It's a versatile style that can be adapted to suit any occasion.

How to Achieve the Slicked Back Side Part

The slicked back side part is achieved by combing the hair back from the forehead and creating a part on one side of the head. This style can be achieved with any hair length, but works best with medium to long hair. To achieve the perfect slicked back side part, you'll need a few key products:

  • Hair gel or pomade
  • A comb
  • A hair dryer (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect slicked back side part:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to create a deep side part on one side of your head.
  4. Comb the hair on the opposite side of the part back and away from your face.
  5. Use a hair dryer on a low setting to dry your hair and set the style in place.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Style

The slicked back side part is a relatively low-maintenance hairstyle, but there are a few tips to keep in mind to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade for the best hold and shine.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid buildup of product.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Can I achieve the slicked back side part with short hair?

While the slicked back side part works best with medium to long hair, it can be achieved with short hair as well. You may need to use a stronger hold hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

What type of hair gel or pomade should I use?

It's important to use a high-quality hair gel or pomade for the best hold and shine. Look for products that are specifically designed for the slicked back side part style.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid buildup of product. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and personal preference.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to maintaining your slicked back side part, it's important to take care of your hair overall. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

By following these tips and maintaining your slicked back side part, you can achieve a stylish and timeless look that will never go out of fashion.
