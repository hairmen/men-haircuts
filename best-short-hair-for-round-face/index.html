Best Short Hair for Round Face: Tips, Styles, and Care

If you have a round face, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a bit challenging. You want a hairstyle that will complement your face shape and make it look slimmer and more elongated. In this post, we'll be discussing the best short hair for round face shapes, including tips on how to style and care for your hair.

  • Best Short Hair Styles for Round Faces
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short hair styles for round faces, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and highlight your best assets. Here are the steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a measuring tape and measure the distance between your hairline and the bottom of your chin.
  3. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Compare your measurements to the following face shape guide:

Oval: Forehead is wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.

Round: Cheeks are the widest part of the face, and the length and width are roughly the same.

Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all the same width.

Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheeks and chin, and the chin is pointed.

Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and chin are narrower.

Best Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's take a look at some of the best short hair styles for round faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for round faces. It's short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top that can be styled in different ways. You can add volume to the crown to elongate your face, or sweep the hair to the side for a more asymmetrical look.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for round faces. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be short or long, and can be styled in different ways depending on your preference. For round faces, a chin-length bob with side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add texture and volume to your hair, which can help elongate a round face. You can opt for a short layered cut, or a longer layered cut that adds movement and dimension to your hair.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen a short hair style that works for your round face, it's important to know how to style it to highlight your best features. Here are some tips for styling short hair for round faces:

  • Add volume to the crown to elongate your face.
  • Sweep your hair to the side to create an asymmetrical look.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and texture.
  • Opt for loose waves or curls to add movement and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What short hair styles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid short hair styles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at the widest part of your face. Instead, opt for styles that elongate your face, such as a pixie cut or a layered cut.

Can I still wear a ponytail if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail if you have short hair. Use bobby pins to secure any shorter layers or flyaways, and add volume to the crown for a more flattering look.

How can I add volume to my short hair?

You can add volume to your short hair by using a round brush when blow-drying, applying a volumizing mousse or spray, or using a texturizing powder at the roots.

Choosing the best short hair for round face shapes can be a fun and rewarding process. With the right style and care, you can highlight your best features and feel confident in your new look.
