How To Start Locks With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair and want to start locks, you may be wondering where to begin. Starting locks with short hair can be a bit challenging, but it’s definitely possible. In this guide, we’ll give you step-by-step instructions on how to start locks with short hair, as well as tips for maintaining your locks once they’ve been started.

Table of Contents

  • How to Start Locks with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Starting and Maintaining Locks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Locks

How to Start Locks with Short Hair

Starting locks with short hair can be a bit challenging, but it’s definitely possible. Here are some tips to help you get started:

Tip #1: Let your hair grow out a bit
Before you can start locks, you’ll need to let your hair grow out a bit. Ideally, your hair should be at least 3-4 inches long before you start. This will give your hair enough length to twist and lock.

Tip #2: Choose a locking method
There are several methods you can use to start locks, including two-strand twists, comb coils, and braids. Choose the method that works best for your hair type and texture.

Tip #3: Be patient
Starting locks takes time, especially if you have short hair. Be patient and don’t get discouraged if your locks take longer to form than you expected.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to starting locks with short hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Before you start your locks, you should wash and condition your hair. This will help remove any product buildup or dirt that may be in your hair.

Step 2: Choose a locking method

Choose the locking method that works best for your hair type and texture. Two-strand twists, comb coils, and braids are all popular methods.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or rubber bands. This will make it easier to work with your hair.

Step 4: Start twisting, coiling, or braiding your hair

Starting from the root, twist, coil, or braid your hair. Continue twisting, coiling, or braiding until you reach the end of the section.

Step 5: Repeat the process

Repeat the twisting, coiling, or braiding process until you’ve covered your entire head.

Step 6: Allow your hair to dry

Allow your hair to dry completely. This may take several hours or overnight.

Step 7: Maintain your locks

Once your locks have been started, you’ll need to maintain them by washing and conditioning them regularly, as well as retwisting or recoiling them as needed.

Tips for Starting and Maintaining Locks

Here are some tips for starting and maintaining locks:

Tip #1: Use natural hair products
When starting and maintaining locks, it’s important to use natural hair products that won’t cause buildup or damage to your hair.

Tip #2: Be gentle with your hair
Be gentle when washing and styling your hair to avoid damaging your locks.

Tip #3: Get regular trims
Getting regular trims will help keep your locks looking neat and healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I start locks with short hair?
A: Yes, it’s possible to start locks with short hair.

Q: How long does it take to start locks?
A: It can take several months to a year or more to start locks, depending on your hair type and texture.

Q: How do I maintain my locks?
A: To maintain your locks, you’ll need to wash and condition them regularly, as well as retwist or recoil them as needed.

Hair Care for Locks

Here are some tips for caring for your locks:

Tip #1: Wash your locks regularly
Wash your locks at least once a week to keep them clean and healthy.

Tip #2: Use a leave-in conditioner
Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your locks moisturized and prevent breakage.

Tip #3: Protect your locks at night
Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet at night to protect your locks while you sleep.

Starting locks with short hair can be a bit challenging, but with patience and the right techniques, it’s definitely possible. Follow these tips and steps to start your locks, and don’t forget to maintain them properly for healthy, beautiful locks.
