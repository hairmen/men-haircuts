How To Spike Your Hair Forward A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re looking to add some edge to your hairstyle, spiking your hair forward is a great way to do it. This style has been popular for decades and is still going strong in 2023. In this post, we’ll show you how to spike your hair forward in a few simple steps. We’ll also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Your Hair Forward
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Your Hair Forward
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Spike Your Hair Forward

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let’s talk about the tools you’ll need to create this hairstyle:

  • Hair gel
  • Hair spray
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair dryer (optional)

Now that you have your tools ready, here’s how to spike your hair forward:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it first.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel to your fingertips and rub it between your hands.
  3. Run your hands through your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward. Make sure you apply the gel evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Using a comb or brush, comb your hair forward. Start at the back and work your way forward, making sure your hair is evenly distributed.
  5. Use your fingers to create spikes in your hair. Start at the front and work your way back. Pull small sections of hair upwards and twist them to create spikes.
  6. Once you’ve created the spikes, use hair spray to hold them in place. Hold the can about 8 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair.
  7. Use your fingers to adjust any spikes that need it.

That’s it! You now know how to spike your hair forward.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair Forward

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle:

  • Use a strong-hold hair gel for best results.
  • Don’t use too much gel – a little goes a long way.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting to help set the spikes.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about spiking your hair forward:

How long does it take to spike your hair forward?

The process can take anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the length of your hair and how intricate you want your spikes to be.

Can I spike my hair forward if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, you may need to blow-dry your hair straight first to make it easier to create the spikes.

How do I maintain my spiky hairstyle?

Use a strong-hold hair spray to keep your spikes in place throughout the day. You may also want to touch up your hair with a comb or your fingers throughout the day to keep the spikes looking sharp.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

By following these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to create a spiky, edgy hairstyle that’s sure to turn heads. Have fun experimenting with different styles and lengths, and don’t be afraid to get creative!
