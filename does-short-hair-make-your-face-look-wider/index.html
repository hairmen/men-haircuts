Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Wider

Written by Bardi Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting a short haircut but worried that it might make your face appear wider? This is a common concern for many people, and it can be difficult to know whether or not to take the plunge. In this article, we will explore the question "does short hair make your face look wider?" and provide you with some tips and advice on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can be a great way to change up your look and refresh your style. However, many people worry that it might not suit their face shape or make their face appear wider than it actually is. While it is true that certain hairstyles can accentuate the width of your face, there are also many short hairstyles that can balance out your features and create a more flattering look.

How to

The first step in determining whether or not short hair will make your face look wider is to identify your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Depending on your face shape, certain hairstyles may be more flattering than others. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a style that adds height to your crown and elongates your face.

Step by step

Once you have identified your face shape, you can start exploring different short hairstyles that will complement your features. Some popular short hairstyles for different face shapes include:

  • Round face: pixie cut, bob with side-swept bangs, asymmetrical bob
  • Oval face: blunt bob, lob (long bob), shag cut
  • Square face: layered bob, angled bob, long pixie cut
  • Heart face: side-swept bangs, chin-length bob, textured pixie cut
  • Diamond face: layered bob, textured pixie cut, angled bob

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to consider your hair texture and thickness as well. If you have thin or fine hair, a cropped pixie cut may not be the best option, as it can emphasize the shape of your head and make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a layered bob or a lob with some texture to add volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a shag cut can help to reduce the appearance of width in your face and create a more balanced look.

FAQ

Q: Does short hair make your face look wider?
A: It depends on your face shape and the specific hairstyle you choose. Some short hairstyles can make your face appear wider, while others can balance out your features and create a more flattering look.

Q: What face shape is best suited for short hair?
A: Short hair can look great on any face shape, but certain styles may be more flattering for different face shapes. It is important to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Be sure to schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair in shape and prevent split ends. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair. With the right care and attention, you can rock a stylish short haircut that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
