How To Cut Wavy Hair In Layers Yourself

Written by Daniel Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to get it looking just right. One of the best ways to add some shape and movement to your locks is by cutting your hair in layers. But heading to the salon every few months for a trim can get expensive. So why not learn how to cut wavy hair in layers yourself?

Description

Cutting your hair in layers is a great way to add some volume and texture to your wavy locks. Plus, it's a simple process that you can do at home with just a few tools.

How to Cut Wavy Hair in Layers Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Next, wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will ensure that you're working with clean hair. Once your hair is dry, comb it out so that it's tangle-free.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Now it's time to section off your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide it into two sections. Clip one section out of the way and work on the other section first.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting at the length you want and working your way up. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair in that section.

Step 5: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you've finished cutting one section, unclip the other side and repeat the process.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

After you've cut both sections, it's time to blend the layers. Take small sections of hair and hold them between your fingers. Cut any uneven pieces to create a more seamless look.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, take a step back and check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair looks even and balanced.

Tips

Cutting your hair in layers can be a bit tricky, especially if you're doing it for the first time. Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up. It's better to cut too little hair than too much.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't be afraid to take breaks and step away from the mirror. This will help you avoid getting too caught up in the details.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair in layers if I have curly hair?

Yes! Cutting your curly hair in layers can help add some bounce and volume to your locks.

How often should I cut my hair if I have wavy hair?

It's generally recommended that you get a trim every 6 to 8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my hair in layers if it's short?

Yes, but it may be more difficult to create layers if your hair is very short. In this case, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to cut wavy hair in layers yourself, you can save money and achieve the perfect hairstyle from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and have fun!
