Bangs Makes You Look Younger

Description

One of the easiest ways to look younger is to get bangs. Bangs not only cover your forehead wrinkles but also add a youthful look to your face. Bangs can be styled in different ways which make them versatile and perfect for any face shape. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, there is a bang style that can suit you.

How To

Decide on the Right Bang Style

The first step to getting bangs is to decide on the right style for your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist or use online resources to find the right bang style that suits your face. There are different types of bangs such as blunt, side-swept, wispy, and baby bangs. Each style creates a different look and can highlight your best facial features.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your bangs is also important in achieving a youthful look. Shorter bangs tend to make you look younger, while longer bangs can add sophistication to your overall look. Consider how much time you are willing to spend styling your bangs and how often you will need to trim them to maintain the length.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before getting your bangs cut, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to section your hair and cut your bangs precisely.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair and separate the bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face to avoid cutting them accidentally.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Cut your bangs according to the style and length you have chosen. Start cutting from the center and work your way outwards. Use a sharp pair of scissors and cut little by little to avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to give them volume and shape. You can also use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to add some waves. Apply some hair serum to keep your bangs in place and give them a glossy finish.

Tips

  • Choose a bang style that complements your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider the maintenance required for your bangs before committing to a style.
  • Use styling products to keep your bangs in shape and avoid frizz.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and length.
  • Experiment with different bang styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Will bangs suit me if I have a round face?

A: Yes, there are bang styles that can complement a round face such as side-swept or wispy bangs.

Q: Can I still wear bangs even if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, curly hair can look great with bangs. Choose a style that works with your natural curls and use a diffuser to enhance them.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It depends on the length of your bangs and how fast your hair grows. As a general rule, trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid oil buildup.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to avoid breakage.
