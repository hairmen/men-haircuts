Best Layers For Thick Straight Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to having thick straight hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will make your hair more manageable, while also enhancing your natural beauty. One of the best ways to achieve this is by getting the right layers in your hair.

Description

Thick straight hair can be difficult to manage, especially if it's long. The weight of the hair can make it hard to style and can even cause headaches. However, adding layers to your hair can make a big difference. Layers can help to remove some of the weight from your hair, making it easier to style and more manageable.

Layering your hair can also add dimension and texture, which can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. This is especially important for those with thick hair, as it can be challenging to create volume without the right cut.

How to Get the Best Layers for Thick Straight Hair

The key to getting the best layers for thick straight hair is to find a style that complements your face shape and hair type. The right layers can enhance your natural features and make your hair more manageable.

When it comes to layering thick straight hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that works with your hair's natural texture
  • Avoid going too short with your layers, as this can make your hair look too thin
  • Consider adding long layers, which can help to add movement and texture to your hair
  • Ask your stylist to remove some of the weight from your hair, which can make it easier to style

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to get the best layers for your thick straight hair, follow these steps:

  1. Find a style that you like and that complements your face shape.
  2. Bring a picture of the style to your stylist.
  3. Ask your stylist to remove some of the weight from your hair.
  4. Ask for long layers, which can add movement and texture to your hair.
  5. Discuss any concerns you have about the cut with your stylist.
  6. After your haircut, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut

Once you have the perfect layers for your thick straight hair, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep your layers looking fresh.

FAQs

What is the best length for layered hair?

The best length for layered hair depends on your face shape and hair type. However, in general, long layers work well for thick straight hair.

How often should I get my hair cut with layers?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every six to eight weeks to maintain your layers and keep them looking fresh.

Can I add layers to my curly hair?

Yes, you can add layers to curly hair. However, it's important to find a stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair, as they will know how to work with your hair's natural texture.

Hair Care for Thick Straight Hair

When you have thick straight hair, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and getting the best layers for your thick straight hair, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and manageable.
