How To Give Yourself A Men s Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and take matters into your own hands, giving yourself a men's haircut is easier than you might think. With a little bit of practice and the right tools, you can achieve a fresh look from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps to give yourself a men's haircut:

How to:

1. Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the tools you'll need. You'll need a pair of sharp hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

2. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

It's best to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your haircut looks even. If your hair is wet, it may be more difficult to see where you need to make cuts.

3. Choose Your Style

Decide what style you want before you begin cutting. You can choose from a variety of styles, such as a buzz cut, crew cut, or a longer style. Look up pictures of the style you want to achieve to give you an idea of what the final result should look like.

4. Begin with the Clippers

Start by using the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Use a longer guard for the top of your head and a shorter guard for the sides and back. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to guide the clippers through your hair.

5. Blend the Top and Sides

Use scissors to blend the top and sides of your hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut off any uneven ends. Take your time and make small cuts to avoid any mistakes.

6. Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them with scissors. Make small cuts and take your time to ensure that they are even.

7. Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up the edges around your ears and neckline. Take your time and make sure they are even.

8. Check Your Work

Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments. Take your time and make sure everything looks even.

9. Rinse and Style

Rinse your hair and style it as desired. Use a little bit of hair product to keep it looking fresh.

Tips:

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  • Watch online tutorials or ask a friend for help if you're unsure about anything.

FAQ:

Can I give myself a men's haircut if I have long hair?

It's possible to give yourself a men's haircut if you have long hair, but it may be more difficult. You may want to consider going to a professional if you're unsure about cutting your own hair.

What kind of scissors should I use?

Use hair cutting scissors, which are sharper and designed for cutting hair. Don't use regular household scissors, as they may damage your hair.

How often should I give myself a haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your haircut. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame for a trim.

Hair Care:

After giving yourself a men's haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know how to give yourself a men's haircut, you can save some money and try out different styles from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and practice, and you'll be a pro in no time!
