How To Wear A Scarf On Your Head With Braids A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Scarves are a great fashion accessory that can add a touch of color and personality to any outfit. But did you know that scarves can also be worn on your head with braids? It's a stylish and versatile way to protect your hair from the elements, and it's perfect for those days when you don't have time to style your hair. In this post, we'll show you how to wear a scarf on your head with braids in a few easy steps.

Description

Wearing a scarf on your head with braids is a trendy and practical way to keep your hair out of your face and protect it from the sun, wind, and rain. It's also a great way to add some color and texture to your hairstyle. You can wear a scarf with any type of braids, whether they're box braids, cornrows, or twists. The key is to choose a scarf that complements your braids and your personal style.

How to Wear a Scarf on Your Head with Braids

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a scarf on your head with braids:

Step 1: Choose Your Scarf

The first step is to choose a scarf that complements your braids and your personal style. You can choose a scarf that matches your outfit, or you can go for a contrasting color or pattern to add some interest. You can also choose a scarf that's made from a lightweight fabric like cotton or silk, so it won't weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Fold Your Scarf

Once you've chosen your scarf, fold it in half diagonally to form a triangle.

Step 3: Place the Scarf on Your Head

Place the scarf on your head with the point of the triangle at the back of your head and the two corners at the front.

Step 4: Tie the Scarf at the Back

Tie the two corners of the scarf at the back of your head, making sure to tuck in any loose ends. You can tie the scarf in a knot or a bow, depending on your personal style.

Step 5: Tuck in the Remaining Fabric

Tuck in any remaining fabric from the scarf under the knot or bow, making sure that it's secure and won't come undone.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you wear a scarf on your head with braids:

  • Choose a scarf that complements your braids and your personal style.
  • Choose a lightweight fabric like cotton or silk, so it won't weigh down your hair.
  • Make sure the scarf is tied securely at the back of your head, so it won't come undone.
  • Experiment with different scarf styles and tying techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Can I wear a scarf on my head with any type of braids?

Yes, you can wear a scarf on your head with any type of braids, whether they're box braids, cornrows, or twists.

Can I wear a scarf on my head with natural hair?

Yes, you can wear a scarf on your head with natural hair. It's a great way to protect your hair from the elements and add some personality to your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Wearing a scarf on your head with braids can help protect your hair from the sun, wind, and rain, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Make sure to moisturize your hair regularly and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. You should also make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and avoid wearing your braids for too long to prevent damage to your hair.
