Male Haircuts Short Back Sides Long Top

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a trendy and low maintenance haircut, then the male haircuts short back sides long top is the perfect style for you. This haircut is a modern take on the classic crew cut and has become popular in recent years. In this article, we will discuss the different types of male haircuts short back sides long top, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair after the cut.

Description

The male haircuts short back sides long top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to fit your personal style. This haircut features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This creates a contrast in length that makes the hair on top stand out. The hair on top can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. This style is popular among men of all ages and can be worn in both casual and formal settings.

How to

The male haircuts short back sides long top is a low maintenance haircut that can be easily styled at home. Here are the steps to achieving this look:

Step 1: Choose your length

Before getting the haircut, decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. The length of the hair on top can range from 2 to 6 inches, depending on your preference. The shorter the hair on the sides and back, the more contrast there will be between the top and the sides.

Step 2: Get the haircut

Take a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your barber or stylist. The barber will then cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short, leaving the length on top. You can choose to have a fade or a taper on the sides and back for a more polished look.

Step 3: Style your hair

After getting the haircut, you can style your hair on top in many different ways. Apply a styling product to damp hair and use your fingers or a comb to create the desired look. For a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel. For a messy look, use a texturizing spray or wax. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a male haircuts short back sides long top:

- Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your barber or stylist. - Communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the length and style you want. - Use a good quality styling product to keep your hair looking fresh. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut. - Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about male haircuts short back sides long top:

What face shape is best for this haircut?

This haircut works well on most face shapes, but it looks especially good on square and oval faces.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can style your hair differently depending on the occasion. For a formal event, you can slick it back for a polished look. For a casual event, you can leave it messy or textured for a more relaxed look.

What hair products should I use?

Use a styling product that works best for your hair type and desired look. A pomade or gel is great for a slicked-back look, while a texturizing spray or wax is good for a messy or textured look.

Hair Care

After getting a male haircuts short back sides long top, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. - Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair. - Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. - Use a styling product that doesn't contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Overall, the male haircuts short back sides long top is a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut that looks great on most men. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain the look.
