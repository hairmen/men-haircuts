Bun Hairstyles For Short Hair Step By Step A Guide To Chic And Easy Styles

Written by Luffy Jun 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that you're limited in the hairstyles you can try. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! With a little creativity and some simple techniques, you can create beautiful and chic bun hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps for some of our favorite bun hairstyles for short hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Create a Bun Hairstyle for Short Hair

Creating a bun hairstyle for short hair is easier than you think. All you need is a few basic styling tools and some styling products to help hold your hair in place. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair ties
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray or other styling product

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first to make it easier to work with. Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair and create a smooth base.

Step 2: Create a Ponytail

Gather your hair into a high or low ponytail, depending on where you want your bun to sit. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 3: Twist and Pin

Take the length of your ponytail and twist it tightly. Wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail, tucking the ends under to create a neat and tidy bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your bun in place all day, spritz it with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Bun Hairstyles for Short Hair

Now that you know the basics of creating a bun hairstyle for short hair, let's walk through some specific styles you can try.

1. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a casual and effortless style that's perfect for a day out running errands or hanging out with friends. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by creating a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base, securing with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few face-framing pieces for a relaxed look.
  4. Mist with hairspray to hold your style in place.

2. The Low Bun

The low bun is a classic and elegant look that's perfect for a wedding or formal event. Here's how to create it:

  1. Create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base, securing with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few pieces around your face for a soft and romantic look.
  4. Mist with hairspray to hold your style in place.

3. The Braided Bun

The braided bun is a fun and playful style that's perfect for a summer day. Here's how to create it:

  1. Create a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Braid your ponytail and secure with a hair tie.
  3. Wrap your braid around the base of your ponytail, tucking the ends under.
  4. Secure with bobby pins and mist with hairspray.

Tips for Perfecting Your Bun Hairstyle

Whether you're creating a messy bun or a braided bun, there are a few tips you can follow to ensure your hairstyle looks its best:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to use bobby pins to secure your bun in place.
  • Experiment with different textures and finishes, such as braids or twists, to add interest to your style.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing product to add body and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I create a bun hairstyle for short hair if I have layers?

A: Yes! While layers can make it more challenging to create a bun, it's still possible. Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces, and don't worry if your bun isn't perfect. A messy bun can look just as chic.

Q: Can I create a bun hairstyle for short hair if I have thick hair?

A: Absolutely! In fact, thick hair can make it easier to create a full and voluminous bun. Just make sure to secure your hair tightly with a hair tie and bobby pins.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires its own unique hair care routine to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every few days to keep it clean and fresh.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create beautiful and chic bun hairstyles for short hair in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these styles are sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
