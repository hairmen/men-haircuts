Fade Haircut With Long Hair On Top A Guide For Men

Written by Frank Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish haircut that combines short sides with long hair on top, the fade haircut with long hair on top might be just the look you need. This trendy hairstyle has become increasingly popular among men of all ages, and for good reason. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and looks great on just about anyone. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about getting a fade haircut with long hair on top.

Description

The fade haircut with long hair on top is a combination of two classic haircuts: the fade and the undercut. The fade is a short haircut that gradually fades from the sides to the top, while the undercut involves shaving the sides and back of the head and leaving the hair on top long. The result is a stylish and modern look that's perfect for any occasion.

How To

If you're interested in getting a fade haircut with long hair on top, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Decide on the Length of Your Hair on Top

The first step in getting a fade haircut with long hair on top is deciding on the length of your hair. Typically, the hair on top should be at least 3-4 inches long to achieve the desired look.

Step 2: Choose the Type of Fade You Want

There are several types of fades to choose from, including low fade, mid fade, and high fade. A low fade starts near the ears and gradually fades into the hair, while a high fade starts near the temples and fades into the hair. A mid fade, as the name suggests, is somewhere in between. Choose the type of fade that suits your personal style.

Step 3: Visit Your Barber

Once you've decided on the length of your hair and the type of fade you want, it's time to visit your barber. Make sure to bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your barber. They'll be able to give you advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

Now that you have your fade haircut with long hair on top, it's important to maintain it properly. This means washing your hair regularly, using the right styling products, and getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a fade haircut with long hair on top:

- Choose a fade that suits your face shape. - Use hair products that work well with your hair type. - Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. - Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

What products should I use to style my hair?

Use hair products that work well with your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. If you have straight hair, use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

Can I style my hair differently every day?

Absolutely! The great thing about the fade haircut with long hair on top is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining your fade haircut with long hair on top. Here are a few tips:

- Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized. - Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can damage it. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Overall, the fade haircut with long hair on top is a trendy and stylish look that's easy to maintain. With a little care and attention, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy. So go ahead and give it a try!
