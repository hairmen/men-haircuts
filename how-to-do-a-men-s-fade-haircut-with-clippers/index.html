How To Do A Men s Fade Haircut With Clippers

Written by Daniel Sep 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a clean and stylish haircut, a men's fade haircut with clippers is the way to go. This popular haircut is characterized by the gradual tapering of hair from the back and sides to the top of the head. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking fade haircut at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to achieve a perfect fade haircut with clippers.

How to

Before we begin, you'll need to gather the following tools:

  • Clippers with different guard sizes
  • Barber scissors
  • Comb
  • Brush
  • Hair clippers oil

Once you have all the necessary tools, follow these steps:

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and give you a better idea of how much hair you need to cut.
  2. Using the longest guard, trim your hair around the sides and back of your head. Make sure to work in even strokes and go against the grain of your hair for a cleaner cut.
  3. Gradually switch to shorter guards and blend the hair from the sides and back with the hair on top of your head. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut with the clippers in a sweeping motion.
  4. When you reach the point where the hair on top of your head meets the hair on the sides and back, switch to a shorter guard size and blend the two sections together. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  5. Use barber scissors to trim any excess hair and create a clean line around the ears and neckline.
  6. Finally, brush and style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a men's fade haircut with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut in a sweeping motion for a smoother blend.
  • Don't be afraid to use multiple guard sizes to achieve the perfect blend.
  • Take your time and check your progress frequently to avoid any mistakes.
  • Clean your clippers and oil them regularly to maintain their performance.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's fade haircuts with clippers:

How long does a fade haircut last?

A fade haircut typically lasts 2-3 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can I do a fade haircut with just one guard size?

You can, but using multiple guard sizes will give you a smoother and more natural-looking blend.

Can I do a fade haircut on my own?

Yes, you can do a fade haircut on your own, but it may take some practice to achieve a professional-looking result.

Hair Care

To maintain your fade haircut, you'll need to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit a barber regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a perfect men's fade haircut with clippers. Practice makes perfect, so don't be discouraged if it doesn't turn out perfectly the first time. Keep at it, and soon you'll be a pro at giving yourself the perfect fade haircut.
