Do Curtain Bangs Work With Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally wavy hair and are considering getting curtain bangs, you might wonder if this trendy hairstyle will work for you. The answer is yes, curtain bangs can look great on wavy hair! In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting curtain bangs with wavy hair, including how to style them and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the center, framing your face on either side. They are longer than traditional bangs and can be styled in a variety of ways, including straight and sleek or tousled and textured. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years and is often seen on celebrities and influencers alike.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs, here are the steps you'll need to follow:

Step 1: Choose a Hairstylist

Choosing the right hairstylist is crucial to achieving the perfect look. Look for someone who has experience working with wavy hair and can create customized bangs that suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Consultation

During your consultation, you'll discuss the look you want to achieve and your hairstylist will give you advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape. They may also ask you questions about your hair care routine and lifestyle to ensure that the style you choose is low maintenance and easy for you to maintain.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you've decided on the style you want, your hairstylist will cut your bangs and style them to perfection. They may use a razor or scissors to create a textured look or use a straightener to achieve a sleek finish. You'll also learn how to style your bangs at home so you can maintain your look between salon visits.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

Once you have your new curtain bangs, you'll want to make sure you know how to style them properly to get the best results. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Blow dry your bangs while they're still damp to achieve a sleeker look, or let them air dry for a more natural, textured finish.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and lift to your bangs while blow-drying.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair, whether that's a texturizing spray or a smoothing serum.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Can I get curtain bangs if my hair is very curly?

Curtain bangs can be challenging to style with very curly hair, but it's not impossible. Talk to your hairstylist about your hair type and ask for their advice on how to style your bangs. You may need to use a straightener or blow dryer to get the look you want.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

It's important to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy. Regular trims will also help prevent split ends and breakage.

What's the best way to style curtain bangs with wavy hair?

The best way to style curtain bangs with wavy hair is to let them air dry for a natural, textured look. Use a texturizing spray to add definition and hold, and use a round brush to add volume while blow-drying.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair with Curtain Bangs

Wavy hair requires extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful, especially when you have bangs. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock curtain bangs with wavy hair and look fabulous every day. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at styling your new fringe and keeping your hair healthy and beautiful.
