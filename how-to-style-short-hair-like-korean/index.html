How To Style Short Hair Like Korean A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a fan of K-pop or Korean dramas, you've probably noticed how effortlessly chic and stylish Korean celebrities look, especially when it comes to their hair. Short hair is a popular trend in Korea, and it's not hard to see why. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and perfect for the hot and humid weather. In this article, we'll show you how to style short hair like Korean in a relaxed English language. Let's get started!

How to Style Short Hair Like Korean

Before we dive into the styling tips, it's important to know that there are different types of short hairstyles that are popular in Korea. Some of the most common ones include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered cut
  • Bangs

Now, let's move on to the styling tips that apply to all types of short hair.

1. Use Hair Wax or Pomade

Hair wax or pomade is essential in creating that effortless Korean look. It adds texture and volume to the hair, making it look fuller and more natural. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. Be careful not to use too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help add volume and texture, making it easier to style. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to go. For a more natural look, use your fingers to tousle your hair while blow drying it.

3. Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add some curls or waves to your hair, use a curling iron. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions and sizes.

4. Add Accessories

Accessories can add a pop of color or texture to your hair. Some popular accessories in Korea include hair clips, hair ties, and headbands. You can also use a scarf to tie your hair up or add a ribbon for a more feminine look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair like Korean:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Hair Wax

Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to go.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron to add some curls or waves to your hair. You can also use accessories like hair clips or headbands to add some color or texture.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair is easy to maintain, but there are still some tips that can help you keep it looking healthy and stylish:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to avoid tangling and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can short hair suit all face shapes?

A: Yes, short hair can suit all face shapes. It's all about finding the right style that complements your features.

Q: Is it easy to maintain short hair?

A: Yes, short hair is easy to maintain. It requires less time and effort than long hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal hair care, but it's still important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Now that you know how to style short hair like Korean, it's time to give it a try. Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the look that suits you best. Remember, the key to a great hairstyle is confidence!
