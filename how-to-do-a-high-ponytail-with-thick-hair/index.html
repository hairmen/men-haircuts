How To Do A High Ponytail With Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of struggling to put your thick hair into a high ponytail? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with thick hair find it challenging to create this classic hairstyle. But with the right technique and a little patience, you can achieve a sleek and stylish high ponytail that will last all day. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create a high ponytail with thick hair, along with some helpful tips and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and strong.

How to Do a High Ponytail with Thick Hair

Before we get started, let's go over some of the tools you'll need to create a high ponytail with thick hair:

  • Brush or comb
  • Elastic hairband
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create a high ponytail with thick hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to gather your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Using your brush or comb, gather your hair up towards the crown of your head. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps as you go.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic hairband. If you have thick hair, you may need to use two or three hairbands to keep your ponytail in place.
  4. If you want a sleeker look, spray some hairspray onto your brush and gently brush over the top of your ponytail to smooth any flyaways.
  5. If you want to add some volume to your ponytail, gently tug at the hair on the top of your head. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look.
  6. You can also add some texture to your ponytail by curling or straightening your hair before putting it up. This will give your ponytail a more polished look.

Tips for Creating a High Ponytail with Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you create the perfect high ponytail with thick hair:

  • Use a hair tie that is strong enough to hold your thick hair in place. Avoid using flimsy or thin hairbands that can break easily.
  • Consider using a hair clip to hold your ponytail in place while you gather the rest of your hair. This will prevent your ponytail from slipping down or becoming uneven.
  • If you have trouble keeping your ponytail in place, try using bobby pins to secure it to your head. This will help prevent your hair from slipping out of the elastic.
  • Experiment with different ponytail heights to find the one that looks best on you. Some people prefer a high ponytail that sits at the crown of the head, while others prefer a lower ponytail that sits at the nape of the neck.
  • If you have shorter layers around your face, consider leaving them out of your ponytail for a more relaxed, casual look.

FAQs About High Ponytails and Thick Hair

Here are some common questions and answers about creating a high ponytail with thick hair:

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled when I put it up in a ponytail?

A: Brush your hair thoroughly before putting it up in a ponytail. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to make your hair more manageable.

Q: Can I wear a high ponytail if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a hair tie that is gentle on your scalp, such as a scrunchie or a soft elastic band.

Q: How can I create a high ponytail that lasts all day?

A: Use a strong hair tie and consider adding some hairspray or hair gel to keep your ponytail in place. You can also try using bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you maintain your luscious locks:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this is less damaging than using a brush.

Now that you know how to create a high ponytail with thick hair, you can rock this classic hairstyle with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair by following our hair care tips, and experiment with different ponytail styles to find the one that suits you best. Happy styling!
