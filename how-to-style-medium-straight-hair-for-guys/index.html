How To Style Medium Straight Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Medium straight hair is a versatile hair type that can be styled in many different ways. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a more effortless and messy style, there are countless options for guys with medium length straight hair. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style medium straight hair for guys, along with some helpful tips and answers to frequently asked questions.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Medium Straight Hair for Guys

Before we get into the styling process, it's important to note that the best way to achieve a great hairstyle is to start with healthy hair. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type. Additionally, consider using a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Now, let's get into the styling process. Here are some popular hairstyles for guys with medium length straight hair:

Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, start by applying some hair wax or pomade to your hair. Use a comb to create a deep side part, and then smooth down the hair on either side. Finish with a light hairspray to keep the style in place.

Slicked Back

For a sleek and polished look, try the slicked back hairstyle. Start by applying a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, and then use a comb to slick your hair back. Finish with a light hairspray to keep the style in place.

Messy Textured

For a more relaxed and messy style, try the messy textured hairstyle. Start by applying some texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair, and then use your hands to tousle your hair and create texture. Finish with a light hairspray to keep the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style medium straight hair for guys:

  1. Start with clean, freshly washed hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as hair wax, pomade, or gel, to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to create your desired hairstyle, whether it's a side part, slicked back, or messy textured style.
  4. Use your hands to tousle your hair and create texture if desired.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Styling Medium Straight Hair

Here are some helpful tips for styling medium straight hair for guys:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and switch things up.
  • Consider getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener.
  • If you have trouble styling your hair, consider consulting a professional hairstylist for advice.

FAQs

Can guys with medium straight hair pull off a messy hairstyle?

Absolutely! Messy hairstyles can look great on guys with medium length straight hair, especially for a more casual or relaxed look. Just be sure to use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to create the right amount of texture and volume.

What hair products should I use for a side part hairstyle?

For a side part hairstyle, it's best to use a hair wax or pomade to create a sleek and polished look. Be sure to apply the product evenly and use a comb to create a deep side part.

How can I keep my hair looking healthy and shiny?

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type. Additionally, consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and hydration to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Straight Hair

Here are some helpful hair care tips for guys with medium straight hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with high-quality hair products.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add hydration and shine to your hair.
  • Get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style your medium straight hair for any occasion. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more relaxed and messy style, there are plenty of options to choose from. Experiment with different hairstyles and hair products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to switch things up and try something new.
