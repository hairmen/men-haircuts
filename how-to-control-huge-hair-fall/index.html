How To Control Huge Hair Fall

Written by Luffy Nov 01, 2023 · 3 min read
How to Control Huge Hair Fall: Tips, FAQ and Hair Care

Hair fall can be a huge problem for both men and women. Losing strands of hair every day is normal, but when this turns into huge hair fall, it can be alarming. There are many factors that can cause hair fall such as genetics, stress, poor diet, and even the use of hair products. If you’re experiencing huge hair fall, don’t worry! There are many ways to control it and promote hair growth.

Table of Content

  1. How to Control Huge Hair Fall
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Controlling Hair Fall
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care to Prevent Hair Fall

How to Control Huge Hair Fall

The first step in controlling hair fall is to identify the cause. If it’s due to genetics, it may be difficult to control, but there are still ways to slow it down. If it’s due to stress, try to manage it by practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga or meditation. If it’s due to poor diet, make sure to eat a balanced diet with protein, vitamins, and minerals that are essential for hair growth.

Another way to control hair fall is to avoid using harsh hair products that can damage your hair. Use natural products or those that are specifically designed for hair growth. You can also try using hair masks or oils that are rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair and scalp.

It’s important to remember that hair fall is a natural process and it’s normal to lose 50 to 100 strands of hair every day. However, if you’re experiencing more than that, it’s important to seek professional help from a dermatologist or a trichologist.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to control huge hair fall:

  1. Identify the cause of hair fall
  2. Manage stress
  3. Eat a balanced diet
  4. Avoid harsh hair products
  5. Use natural hair products or those specifically designed for hair growth
  6. Nourish your hair and scalp with hair masks or oils
  7. Seek professional help if necessary

Tips for Controlling Hair Fall

Here are some tips that can help you control hair fall:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair fall:

What causes hair fall?

Hair fall can be caused by genetics, stress, poor diet, hormonal changes, and the use of harsh hair products.

How much hair fall is normal?

It’s normal to lose 50 to 100 strands of hair every day.

Can hair fall be reversed?

Yes, hair fall can be reversed by identifying the cause and taking steps to address it.

Hair Care to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some hair care tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Condition your hair after every wash
  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Hair fall can be a huge problem, but with the right steps and hair care, it can be controlled. Remember to identify the cause of hair fall and take steps to address it. Seek professional help if necessary and practice good hair care habits to prevent hair fall. With patience and perseverance, you can have healthy and beautiful hair.
