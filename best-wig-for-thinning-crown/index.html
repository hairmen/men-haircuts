The Best Wig For Thinning Crown A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you struggling with a thinning crown and looking for a solution? Wigs can be a great option to cover up thinning hair and give you confidence. In this article, we’ll guide you through the best wig options for a thinning crown, how to choose the right one, and how to take care of it.

Description

Thinning crown is a common problem for both men and women. It can be caused by various factors such as genetics, stress, age, and medical conditions. Wigs are a great way to cover up thinning hair and give you a natural-looking full head of hair. However, not all wigs are created equal, and choosing the right one can make a big difference in how you look and feel.

How to Choose the Best Wig for Thinning Crown?

Choosing the best wig for a thinning crown can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the right wig:

Consider the Material

The material of the wig is an essential factor to consider. Synthetic wigs are affordable and easy to maintain, but they may not look as natural as human hair wigs. Human hair wigs are more expensive, but they look and feel more natural.

Choose the Right Style

The style of the wig can make a big difference in how you look. The best wig for a thinning crown is one that has volume and covers the crown area. A layered or bob style wig can help add volume and give you a natural-looking full head of hair.

Get the Right Fit

The fit of the wig is crucial for comfort and natural-looking appearance. Measure your head circumference and choose a wig that fits snugly but not too tight. You can also consider getting a custom-made wig for a perfect fit.

Step by Step Guide for Wearing a Wig

Wearing a wig may seem daunting, but it’s relatively easy once you get the hang of it. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Natural Hair

Start by tucking away your natural hair to ensure it doesn’t show under the wig. You can use a wig cap or braid your hair close to your scalp.

Step 2: Put on the Wig

Hold the wig by the ear tabs and place it at the front of your head. Tilt the wig back and slide it over your head. Adjust the wig to fit snugly on your head and align it with your natural hairline.

Step 3: Style the Wig

You can style the wig according to your preference, using a wig brush or comb. Be gentle and avoid using heat styling tools on synthetic wigs.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Wig

A well-maintained wig can last for a long time and look natural. Here are some tips for taking care of your wig:

Store Your Wig Properly

When you’re not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.

Wash Your Wig Regularly

Washing your wig regularly can help keep it clean and fresh. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner, and follow the manufacturer’s instructions.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools on synthetic wigs as they can damage the fibers. If you must style your wig, use a low heat setting or consult a professional stylist.

FAQs

Q: How long do wigs last?

A: The lifespan of a wig depends on several factors such as the material, quality, and how well it’s maintained. Synthetic wigs can last up to six months, while human hair wigs can last for years with proper care.

Q: Can I wear a wig while swimming?

A: It’s not recommended to wear a wig while swimming as chlorine and saltwater can damage the fibers. You can consider getting a swim cap or a waterproof wig if you want to swim while wearing a wig.

Q: Can I sleep with my wig on?

A: It’s not recommended to sleep with your wig on as it can damage the fibers and cause tangling. It’s best to remove your wig before going to bed.

Hair Care for Thinning Crown

In addition to wearing a wig, taking care of your natural hair can help prevent further thinning. Here are some tips for hair care:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage. Opt for loose and comfortable hairstyles instead.

Use Gentle Hair Products

Use gentle hair products that don’t contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Avoid using hot styling tools frequently.

Now that you know the best wig options for a thinning crown, how to choose the right one, and how to take care of it, you can confidently rock a full head of hair. Remember to take care of your natural hair as well to prevent further thinning.
