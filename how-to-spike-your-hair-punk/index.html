How To Spike Your Hair Punk

Are you looking to achieve that edgy, punk rock look? Spiked hair is a staple of the punk scene, but it can be intimidating to try to style. Don't worry, with a few easy steps and some tips, you can create the perfect punk hairdo.

How to Spike Your Hair

Before diving into the styling process, it's important to choose the right products for your hair type. Look for products with a strong hold, such as hair gel or pomade. You'll also need a comb and a blow dryer.

When it comes to styling, there are a few different methods you can try. One option is to use your fingers to create spikes, starting at the root and working your way down. Another option is to use a comb to create defined spikes.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Blow dry your hair with a hair dryer until it's completely dry.

2. Apply product.

Scoop a small amount of product onto your fingertips and rub your hands together. Work the product through your hair, using your fingers to create spikes.

3. Define the spikes.

If you want more defined spikes, use a comb to create them. Start at the root and comb upward, twisting the hair as you go. Hold the hair in place with your fingers and blow dry the hair for a few seconds to set the style.

4. Finish with hairspray.

Once you've achieved your desired style, finish with a strong hold hairspray to keep the spikes in place.

Tips for Spiked Hair

- Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

- Don't be afraid to use a blow dryer. Blow drying your hair can help to set the style and make it last longer.

FAQs

How long will spiked hair last?

With the right products and techniques, spiked hair can last for several hours to a full day.

Can I spike my hair if it's short?

Absolutely! Short hair can be spiked just as easily as longer hair.

How do I remove product from my hair?

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Finally, be sure to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect spiked punk hairdo. Remember to experiment with different products and methods to find the ones that work best for you.
