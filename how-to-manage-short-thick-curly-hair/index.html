How To Manage Short Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you someone with short, thick, curly hair? Do you struggle with managing your hair every day? You’re not alone! Many people with curly hair find it challenging to maintain their locks. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to manage short thick curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Manage Short Thick Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Short Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Manage Short Thick Curly Hair

Managing short thick curly hair can be a daunting task, but it’s not impossible. Here are some basic steps you can follow:

1. Start with a Good Haircut

The right haircut can make a world of difference when it comes to managing curly hair. Shorter haircuts tend to work better for curly hair, as they are easier to maintain. Make sure you find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair.

2. Find the Right Products

Curly hair requires different products than straight hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners. You may also want to invest in a good leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining cream.

3. Embrace Your Curls

The key to managing curly hair is to embrace your natural curls. Don’t try to straighten your hair or fight against your curls. Instead, work with your curls to create a style that works for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to manage short thick curly hair:

1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.

Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner.

A leave-in conditioner will help to hydrate and define your curls.

3. Apply a curl-defining cream.

A curl-defining cream will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

4. Use a diffuser to dry your hair.

A diffuser will help to enhance your curls while reducing frizz.

5. Style your hair.

Work with your curls to create a style that works for you.

Tips for Managing Short Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing short thick curly hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Don’t use a brush, as it can damage your curls.

2. Avoid heat styling tools.

Heat styling tools can damage your curls and cause frizz.

3. Sleep on a satin pillowcase.

Satin pillowcases can help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but most people with curly hair can go 2-3 days between washes.

Q: How do I prevent frizz?

A: Use a curl-defining cream and a diffuser when drying your hair. You can also sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Short Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for short thick curly hair:

1. Trim your hair regularly.

Regular trims will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

2. Deep condition your hair once a week.

Deep conditioning will help to hydrate and nourish your curls.

3. Protect your hair from the sun.

Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful UV rays.

Managing short thick curly hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can have beautiful, healthy curls. Remember to embrace your natural curls and work with them to create a style that works for you.
