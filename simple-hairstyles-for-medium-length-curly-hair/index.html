Simple Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It can look fabulous one day and frizzy the next. But with the right hairstyle, you can show off your stunning natural curls. Medium length hair is perfect for adding volume and bounce to your curls. Here are some simple hairstyles for medium length curly hair that will make you look fabulous every day.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Hairstyles
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Style Curly Hair

Before we get started on the hairstyles, let's talk about how to style curly hair. First, make sure you're using the right products. Curly hair needs moisture, so look for products that are hydrating and nourishing. Second, don't brush your hair. Brushing can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Third, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls.

Step-by-Step Hairstyles

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out. Start by parting your hair down the middle. Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a loose ponytail. Leave the rest of your hair down. You can add a bit of volume to your curls by teasing the hair at the crown of your head.

2. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well with medium length curly hair. Start by smoothing your hair down with a bit of gel or cream. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. You can leave some curls loose around your face to frame it.

3. Messy Bun

If you're in a rush, a messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle for curly hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins. Don't worry about making it perfect. The messier, the better.

4. Side Braid

A side braid is a romantic hairstyle that's perfect for a date night. Start by parting your hair to one side. Braid your hair loosely and secure it with a hair tie. You can loosen the braid and pull out a few strands to create a messy, boho look.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Curly hair requires some extra TLC. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Don't wash your hair every day
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Don't touch your hair too much throughout the day

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my curly hair?

You don't need to wash your curly hair every day. In fact, washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or less if your hair is very dry.

2. Can I brush my curly hair?

No! Brushing curly hair can cause frizz and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair needs a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and bouncy. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated

With these simple hairstyles and tips, you'll be able to show off your gorgeous curls every day. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid anything that can cause damage or frizz. Your curls will thank you for it.
