Do Curtain Bangs Suit Small Faces

Curtain bangs have been one of the most popular hair trends in recent years. They are versatile, easy to style, and can suit almost any face shape. But what about small faces? Do curtain bangs suit them? The answer is yes! Curtain bangs can be a great choice for small faces, as long as they are styled correctly.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

Before we dive into whether curtain bangs suit small faces, let's talk about how to get them. First, you'll need to decide on the length and thickness of the bangs you want. Then, you'll need to section off the front portion of your hair and cut it to the desired length.

Tip: It's always best to go to a professional hairstylist for a haircut, especially if you're not experienced in cutting hair. They can help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs for your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style curtain bangs:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Section off the front portion of your hair.
  3. Comb the section of hair forward.
  4. Cut the hair to the desired length.
  5. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to the bangs.
  6. Use a round brush to style the bangs to the side.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tip: If you have a small face, try cutting the bangs slightly shorter than usual to avoid overwhelming your features.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs on Small Faces

Now, let's talk about some tips for styling curtain bangs on small faces:

  • Opt for thinner bangs: Thick bangs can overpower small faces, so opt for thinner, wispy bangs instead.
  • Style your bangs to the side: This will help elongate your face and give the illusion of a longer forehead.
  • Keep your bangs textured: Adding texture to your bangs can help create volume and movement, making them appear less heavy on a small face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt bangs can make small faces appear even smaller, so try a more layered, textured cut instead.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs and small faces:

  • Can curtain bangs make my face look smaller? Yes, if they are not styled correctly. Opt for thinner bangs and style them to the side to avoid overwhelming your features.
  • How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs? It depends on how fast your hair grows, but generally every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.
  • Can I style curtain bangs with curly hair? Yes, but it may require more effort to straighten the bangs before styling them to the side.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Finally, here are some hair care tips for maintaining curtain bangs:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your bangs.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style.
  • Invest in a good quality round brush to help style your bangs to the side.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools on your bangs.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can definitely suit small faces as long as they are styled correctly. Opt for thinner bangs, style them to the side, and keep them textured for the best results. With the right haircut and styling techniques, curtain bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and frame your face in a flattering way.
