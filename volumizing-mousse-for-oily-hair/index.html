Volumizing Mousse For Oily Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have oily hair, you know the struggle of trying to add volume without making your hair look greasy. This is where volumizing mousse comes in handy. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about volumizing mousse for oily hair, including how to use it, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Volumizing Mousse for Oily Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Volumizing Mousse on Oily Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Oily Hair

Description

Volumizing mousse is a styling product that is specifically designed to add volume and body to your hair. It works by thickening each hair strand, which makes your hair look fuller and thicker. Volumizing mousse is ideal for people with fine or thin hair as it helps to create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

However, if you have oily hair, you need to be careful when using volumizing mousse as it can make your hair look greasy. The key is to choose a lightweight, non-greasy formula that won't weigh your hair down.

How to Use Volumizing Mousse for Oily Hair

When using volumizing mousse on oily hair, it's important to apply it correctly to avoid making your hair look greasy. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or oil. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Mousse

Shake the can of volumizing mousse well and dispense a small amount onto your hand. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly, and then apply it to your hair from roots to ends.

Step 3: Blow-dry Your Hair

Use a hair dryer to blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to lift your hair at the roots. Focus the dryer on the roots to create volume and lift.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use volumizing mousse for oily hair:

Step 1: Shampoo Your Hair

Start by shampooing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or oil. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Mousse

Shake the can of volumizing mousse well and dispense a small amount onto your hand. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly, and then apply it to your hair from roots to ends.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair. This will help to ensure that the product is evenly distributed and will prevent any clumps or buildup.

Step 4: Blow-dry Your Hair

Use a hair dryer to blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to lift your hair at the roots. Focus the dryer on the roots to create volume and lift. Once your hair is dry, use a round brush to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Using Volumizing Mousse on Oily Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using volumizing mousse on oily hair:

  • Choose a lightweight, non-greasy formula that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Apply the mousse to your roots and work it through your hair to the ends.
  • Blow-dry your hair with your head upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much after applying the mousse as this can make it look greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to help absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: Can volumizing mousse make my hair look greasy?

A: Yes, if you use too much or apply it incorrectly, volumizing mousse can make your hair look greasy. It's important to choose a lightweight, non-greasy formula and apply it correctly to avoid this.

Q: Can I use volumizing mousse on dry hair?

A: Yes, you can use volumizing mousse on dry hair. However, it's best to apply it to damp hair as this will help to distribute the product evenly and create better results.

Q: How often should I use volumizing mousse?

A: This depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have oily hair, you may want to use volumizing mousse every time you wash your hair to help keep it looking fresh and voluminous.

Hair Care Tips for Oily Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your oily hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your scalp of its natural oils.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup or oil.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as this can transfer oil from your hands onto your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to help absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

By following these tips and using volumizing mousse correctly, you can achieve beautiful, voluminous hair without making it look greasy.
