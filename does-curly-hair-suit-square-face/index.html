Does Curly Hair Suit Square Face

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post on the topic of "does curly hair suit square face"! If you have a square face and are wondering whether curly hair would suit you, then this article is for you. In this post, we will be discussing the suitability of curly hair for square faces, along with some tips on how to style your curly locks.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Firstly, let's understand what a square face shape is. A square face has a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a straight hairline. People with square faces often have angular features and a boxy appearance.

Curly hair can soften the sharp angles of a square face and add some dimension to the overall look. However, it is important to note that not all curly hairstyles will suit a square face. It's all about finding the right style that complements your face shape.

How to

The key to finding the right curly hairstyle for a square face is to opt for styles that add volume and softness to the face. Layers are a great way to add volume to curly hair and create a more textured look. Soft, side-swept bangs can also help to soften the angular features of a square face.

Another important factor to consider is the length of your hair. Mid-length to long curly hair can work well for square faces, as it adds length to the face and creates a more balanced look. Short curly hairstyles can also work for square faces, but it's important to avoid styles that are too boxy or angular.

Step by Step

If you have curly hair and a square face, here are some steps you can follow to find the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Visit a hair stylist who has experience with curly hair.
  2. Discuss your face shape and hair type with your stylist.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume and softness to your face.
  4. Consider adding layers and soft, side-swept bangs to your hairstyle.
  5. Experiment with different lengths to find the perfect balance for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling curly hair for a square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too boxy or angular.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to create more defined curls.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair and square faces:

Q: Can short curly hair work for square faces?

A: Yes, short curly hair can work for square faces, but it's important to avoid styles that are too boxy or angular.

Q: What is the best length for curly hair on a square face?

A: Mid-length to long curly hair can work well for square faces, as it adds length to the face and creates a more balanced look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curly locks. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

Overall, curly hair can definitely suit a square face, as long as the right style is chosen. By following the tips and steps outlined in this post, you can find the perfect curly hairstyle to complement your square face and enhance your natural beauty.
