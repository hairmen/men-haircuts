Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and have short hair, you may think that styling options are limited. However, there are many easy hairstyles that you can try to switch up your look and feel confident. In this article, we'll discuss some simple hairstyles that are perfect for short hair and won't require too much effort or time. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Hairstyles
  • Tips and Tricks for Short Hair
  • FAQs

How to Style Short Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about some general styling tips for short hair. First, it's important to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat or styling products that can damage your hair.

When styling your hair, start with clean, dry hair. You can use a light hold gel or mousse to add texture and hold. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair and create volume or texture. If you want to add some extra volume, you can use a volumizing spray or dry shampoo.

Hairstyling Tools

For short hair, you won't need many tools to style your hair. However, there are a few essentials that you should have on hand:

  • A comb or brush
  • A hair dryer
  • A curling iron or straightener (optional)
  • Hair clips or bobby pins

Step-by-Step Hairstyles

Now, let's explore some easy hairstyles that you can try on your short hair. These styles are perfect for everyday wear or special occasions.

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for short hair. To style, apply a small amount of gel or mousse to damp hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also use a comb to create a side part or add some texture to the top of your hair.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular hairstyle for short hair. To style, use a round brush and blow dry your hair, adding volume and texture. If you have a side part, you can use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

3. Messy Updo

If you're looking for a more elegant style, you can try a messy updo. Start by pulling your hair back into a loose bun or chignon. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place and create a messy, effortless look. You can also leave some hair loose around your face for a softer look.

4. Side Swept Bangs

If you have bangs, you can try a side swept look. Use a round brush and blow dry your hair, sweeping your bangs to one side. You can also use a straightener to add some texture or create a piecey look. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips and Tricks for Short Hair

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your short hair:

  • Experiment with different products and tools to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles or switch up your look.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands, scarves, or clips to add some extra flair to your hair.
  • If you're struggling to find the perfect hairstyle, consult with a hairstylist who specializes in short hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still color my hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, absolutely! Short hair can be colored just like any other length. However, be sure to use products that are gentle on your hair and won't cause damage or breakage.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days without washing. Experiment and find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your short hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat or styling products that can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.

With these easy hairstyles and tips, you'll be able to rock your short hair with confidence and style. Remember to have fun and experiment with different looks until you find what works best for you.
