Medium Length Haircuts For Round Faces And Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you searching for a new hairstyle that flatters your round face and wavy hair? Look no further than medium length haircuts! This versatile length can work with a variety of face shapes and hair textures. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about medium length haircuts for round faces and wavy hair.

Description

Medium length haircuts typically fall between the shoulders and collarbone. They can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose waves to sleek straight styles. This length is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still allows for versatility in styling.

How to

When choosing a medium length haircut for a round face and wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure the cut doesn't add too much volume to the sides of your face. This can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for layers that add volume to the top of your head.

Another important factor is the part. A side part can add asymmetry to your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face shape. Avoid center parts that can emphasize the roundness of your face.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best medium length haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Ask for layers that add volume to the top of your head, rather than the sides.

3. Consider a side part to create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

4. Style your hair with loose waves or a sleek straight style.

Tips

- Avoid haircuts that add too much volume to the sides of your face.

- Opt for layers that add volume to the top of your head.

- Consider a side part to create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

- Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair texture.

FAQ

What are the best medium length haircuts for round faces and wavy hair?

The best medium length haircuts for round faces and wavy hair are those that add volume to the top of your head, rather than the sides. Layers can help achieve this look, as well as a side part to create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

Can I still wear my hair straight with a medium length haircut?

Absolutely! Medium length haircuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including sleek straight styles.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length haircut looking its best, it's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes regular trims to prevent split ends and using products that are appropriate for your hair type.

For wavy hair, consider using a curl-enhancing product to define your waves. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing product to add body to your locks.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a medium length haircut that flatters your round face and wavy hair. Happy styling!
