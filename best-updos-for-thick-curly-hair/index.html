Best Updos For Thick Curly Hair

Are you struggling to find the perfect updo for your thick curly hair? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best updos for thick curly hair, providing step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to style, especially when it comes to updos. However, there are many options available that can make your hair look stunning. From braids to buns, there are plenty of updos that work well with thick curly hair.

How to

Before we dive into the best updos for thick curly hair, it is essential to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. After that, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and make it more manageable. Once your hair is dry, use a heat protectant spray and a curling iron or wand to define your curls.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best updos for thick curly hair:

Braided Updo

  1. Part your hair down the middle and create two French braids on each side of your head.
  2. Twist the braids together at the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.
  3. Use a curling iron to add some loose waves to the front of your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Bun Updo

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the elastic band to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to frame it.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when styling updos for thick curly hair:

  • Use hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and flowers to add extra flair to your updo.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Use a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What type of products should I use for my thick curly hair?

You should use products specifically designed for curly hair, such as a leave-in conditioner, curl defining cream, and anti-frizz serum.

How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with thick curly hair prefer to wash their hair once a week, while others prefer to wash it every few days.

Can I wear an updo with short thick curly hair?

Absolutely! There are plenty of updos that work well with short thick curly hair, such as a braided crown or a messy bun.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the best updos for thick curly hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or relaxers, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect updo for your thick curly hair. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new styles to find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
