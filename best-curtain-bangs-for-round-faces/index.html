Best Curtain Bangs For Round Faces

Written by William Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? If you have a round face, you might want to consider getting curtain bangs. Not only do they look stylish, but they can also complement your face shape. In this article, we'll go over the best curtain bangs for round faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep them looking fresh.

Best Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

When choosing the best curtain bangs for round faces, you want to consider the length and thickness. Bangs that are too short or too thick can make your face appear rounder. Here are some styles that can complement your face shape:

Long Curtain Bangs

Long curtain bangs that graze your cheekbones can help elongate your face and create a slimming effect. They also add a touch of sophistication to any hairstyle.

Side-Swept Curtain Bangs

Side-swept curtain bangs that are longer on one side can also help create an elongated look. They draw attention away from the roundness of your face and add a playful touch to your hairstyle.

Wispy Curtain Bangs

Wispy curtain bangs that are feathered and light can soften the roundness of your face. They also add texture and movement to your hair.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some ideas:

Blowout

A blowout can help add volume and bounce to your curtain bangs. Use a round brush and blow dryer to create a natural-looking wave.

Straight

Straightening your curtain bangs can create a sleek and polished look. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.

Updo

For a more formal occasion, you can style your curtain bangs in an updo. Pin them back and let the rest of your hair flow freely.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curtain Bangs

If you want to cut your own curtain bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Divide your hair into a triangle shape from the top of your head to your temples.
  2. Comb the hair forward and hold it in between your index and middle fingers.
  3. Cut the bangs at the desired length, starting from the middle and working your way outwards.
  4. Blend the edges by point-cutting with scissors.
  5. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking fresh, here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent oil buildup.
  • Consider using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.

FAQs About Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

Can curtain bangs work for all face shapes?

While curtain bangs can work for many face shapes, they may not be the best choice for those with a square face. This is because they can accentuate the sharp angles of the face.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Curtain bangs do require regular trims to maintain their shape, but they are relatively low-maintenance compared to other hairstyles.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

Now that you know the best curtain bangs for round faces, how to style them, and hair care tips, you're ready to try out this trendy hairstyle. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and fits your personal style. With the right care and maintenance, your curtain bangs will look great for months to come.
