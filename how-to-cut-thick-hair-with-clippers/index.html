How To Cut Thick Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. And when it comes to cutting your hair, it can be even more challenging. But with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this post, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting thick hair with clippers.

How to Cut Thick Hair with Clippers

Before we get started, it’s important to have the right tools. You’ll need a set of clippers with adjustable blades, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have the tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to choose the right length for your haircut. If you’re not sure what length to go for, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the right length. It’s always better to start with a longer length and work your way down, as you can always cut more if needed.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It’s important to start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry it until it’s completely dry.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Start by using the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Use the longer guard you chose in step 1 and start at the bottom, slowly working your way up. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut. Once you’ve finished the sides and back, switch to a shorter guard and trim around the ears and neckline.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Now it’s time to blend the top of your hair. Use the scissors to trim any longer hair on the top of your head, then use the clippers with a longer guard to blend the top with the sides and back. Again, use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Refine the Cut

Once you’ve finished the main cut, use the scissors to refine the cut. Look for any stray hairs or uneven spots and trim them as needed.

Step 6: Clean Up

Now that your haircut is finished, it’s time to clean up. Use a brush or vacuum to remove any loose hairs from your head and the floor.

Tips for Cutting Thick Hair with Clippers

Cutting thick hair with clippers can be challenging, but these tips can help make the process easier:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to find the right length.
  • Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the top with the sides and back for a natural look.
  • Take your time and don’t rush the process.
  • Use scissors to refine the cut and remove any stray hairs.
  • Have someone else check your work to ensure an even cut.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It’s important to have the right tools and technique, but with practice, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.

What’s the best length guard for thick hair?

The best length guard for thick hair depends on your personal preference and the style you’re going for. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the right length.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you should cut your hair depends on the length and style you’re going for. As a general rule, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Cutting your hair is just one part of maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking and feeling great:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein and vitamins to support hair growth.

By following these tips and learning how to cut your hair with clippers, you can achieve a great haircut and maintain healthy, thick hair.
