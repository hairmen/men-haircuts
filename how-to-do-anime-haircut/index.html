How To Do Anime Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a fan of anime and want to have your own anime haircut? If so, you're in the right place! In this post, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of achieving that cool anime look. From choosing the right hairstyle to maintaining it, we've got you covered.

What is an Anime Haircut?

An anime haircut is a hairstyle inspired by characters in Japanese animation. These hairstyles are known for their unique and often exaggerated styles, such as spiky hair, brightly colored hair, or asymmetrical cuts. Anime haircuts are popular among both men and women, and they're a great way to express your individuality.

How to Choose the Right Anime Haircut

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to choose the right anime haircut for you. Here are some tips:

1. Consider your face shape

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, go for a style that adds height to your hair to elongate your face.

2. Look for inspiration

Check out anime characters with hairstyles you like and use them as inspiration for your own haircut. You can find plenty of pictures online or in anime magazines.

3. Think about your lifestyle

Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into maintaining your hairstyle. Some anime haircuts require more maintenance than others.

How to Do an Anime Haircut

Now that you've chosen your anime haircut, it's time to get cutting! Here's how:

1. Gather your tools

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers (optional).

2. Wash your hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting it. Wet hair is harder to cut and can lead to uneven results.

3. Start with the sides

Use your clippers or scissors to trim the sides of your hair to your desired length. If you're going for a spiky look, leave the top longer.

4. Cut the top

Using your scissors, cut the top of your hair to the desired length. If you're going for a spiky look, cut the hair into short, choppy layers.

5. Style your hair

Once you've cut your hair, style it using gel, wax, or hairspray to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Anime Haircut

Now that you've got your anime haircut, you'll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Trim your hair regularly

To maintain your style, you'll need to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks.

2. Use the right products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and style.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it and cause split ends.

FAQs About Anime Haircuts

1. Can I dye my hair for an anime haircut?

Yes, you can dye your hair to achieve an anime-inspired look. Just make sure you choose a color that complements your skin tone and goes well with your chosen hairstyle.

2. Do I need to cut my hair short for an anime haircut?

Not necessarily. Anime haircuts come in all shapes and lengths, so choose a style that works for you and your hair type.

3. How do I know if an anime haircut will suit me?

The best way to know if an anime haircut will suit you is to try it out. If you're not sure, ask your stylist for their opinion.

Hair Care for Your Anime Haircut

Lastly, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining your anime haircut. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it and cause split ends.

3. Protect your hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather by wearing a hat or using a scarf.

With these tips and steps, you're now ready to achieve your own anime haircut. Remember to choose a style that works for you and your lifestyle, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
