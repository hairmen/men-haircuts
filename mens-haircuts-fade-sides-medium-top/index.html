Mens Haircuts Fade Sides Medium Top A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a man looking for a new haircut, you may have come across the term "fade sides medium top". This popular hairstyle has been around for a few years now and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. In this article, we will guide you through everything you need to know about mens haircuts fade sides medium top.

What is a Fade Sides Medium Top Haircut?

A fade sides medium top haircut is a hairstyle that features a gradual transition from short to longer hair. The sides and back are usually shaved or cut short, while the top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the short sides and the medium-length top, hence the name "fade sides medium top".

How to Get a Fade Sides Medium Top Haircut

To get a fade sides medium top haircut, you will need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. Here are the steps involved in getting a fade sides medium top:

  1. Start by choosing the length you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face.
  2. Ask your barber to use clippers to fade the sides and back of your head. The length of the fade should gradually increase as it moves up towards the top of your head.
  3. Once the sides and back have been faded, your barber will use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length.
  4. Finally, your barber will blend the top of your hair with the faded sides and back to create a seamless transition.

Tips for Maintaining a Fade Sides Medium Top Haircut

To keep your fade sides medium top haircut looking sharp, you will need to maintain it regularly. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your barber every 2-4 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to keep it in place throughout the day.
  • Consider getting a touch-up between haircuts to keep the fade looking sharp.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts fade sides medium top:

Q: What face shape is best for a fade sides medium top haircut?

A: A fade sides medium top haircut can work well with most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for men with round or square faces.

Q: Can I style my fade sides medium top haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your fade sides medium top haircut in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, textured, or messy.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to regular maintenance, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance fade sides medium top haircut. So, go ahead and book an appointment with your barber today!
