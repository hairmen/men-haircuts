Medium Length Hair With Bangs And Layers

Written by Luffy Jul 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will be discussing the popular hairstyle of medium length hair with bangs and layers. This versatile hairstyle has been a go-to for many women in recent years, and for good reason. With just the right amount of layers and bangs, this hairstyle can suit any face shape and hair type. Let's dive into how to achieve this look, some tips, and how to care for it.

Description

Medium length hair with bangs and layers is a hairstyle that falls between shoulder-length and just above the chest. The layers are cut throughout the hair to add dimension and texture, while the bangs can be side-swept or blunt. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance but stylish look.

How to

To achieve medium length hair with bangs and layers, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with your hair stylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's important to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can help determine if this hairstyle will suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Decide on the length and type of layers

Medium length hair with bangs and layers can have different types of layers, such as long layers or choppy layers. Decide on the type of layers you want and the length you want them to be.

Step 3: Decide on the type of bangs

There are different types of bangs that can complement medium length hair with layers, such as side-swept bangs or blunt bangs. Decide on the type of bangs you want and the length you want them to be.

Step 4: Cut and style

Once you have consulted with your hair stylist and decided on the length and type of layers and bangs, it's time to cut and style your hair. Your hair stylist will cut the layers and bangs to your desired length and style them accordingly.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting medium length hair with bangs and layers:

  • Regular trims are important to maintain the layers and bangs.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hair with bangs and layers:

What face shape does this hairstyle suit?

This hairstyle can suit any face shape depending on how the layers and bangs are cut.

Will this hairstyle work for thick hair?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for thick hair. However, it's important to consult with a hair stylist to determine the type of layers that will work best for your hair type.

Can I still wear my hair up with this hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this hairstyle. It may require some creativity with bobby pins and hair ties, but it's definitely possible.

Hair Care

To keep medium length hair with bangs and layers looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Medium length hair with bangs and layers is a popular hairstyle that can suit any face shape and hair type. With proper care and maintenance, this hairstyle can be a low-maintenance but stylish look.
