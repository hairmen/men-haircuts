How To Cut Curtain Bangs Step By Step A Relaxed English Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle for several years now, and it's not hard to see why. They're versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit almost any face shape. If you're looking to switch up your hair game, why not try cutting your own curtain bangs? In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to get the look you want without leaving your home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs

How to Cut Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs, also known as Bardot bangs, are a type of fringe that are parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They're usually longer at the sides and gradually get shorter towards the middle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own curtain bangs:

  1. Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it. This will make it easier to work with.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and comb it straight.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front and use your fingers to measure how long you want your bangs to be. Remember that you can always cut more hair, but you can't glue it back on!
  4. Using hair scissors, cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to cut at an angle towards the middle.
  5. Repeat the same process on the other side, making sure the lengths are even.
  6. Comb your bangs and check if they're even. If they're not, trim as needed.
  7. Style your new curtain bangs by using a round brush to blow-dry them inwards or outwards, depending on your preference.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your own curtain bangs:

  • Use sharp hair scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut a little at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • If you're not sure about the length, start with longer bangs and gradually cut more hair until you get the desired length.
  • Make sure your hair is dry and styled the way you usually wear it before cutting your bangs. This will help you get a better idea of how they'll look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Can I wear curtain bangs with any hair length?

Yes, curtain bangs can be worn with any hair length, from short to long.

What face shapes suit curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can suit almost any face shape, but they're particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You'll need to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care After Cutting Curtain Bangs

After cutting your curtain bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut your own curtain bangs, it's time to grab your scissors and give it a try. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a stylish new look that's perfect for any occasion. Happy cutting!
