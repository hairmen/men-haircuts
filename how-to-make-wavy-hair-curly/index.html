How To Make Wavy Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

In this article, we will guide you through the steps of making wavy hair curly. We will also give you tips on how to take care of your curly hair. So, let's get started.

How to Make Wavy Hair Curly

Before we start, let's understand the difference between wavy and curly hair. Wavy hair has loose S-shaped waves, whereas curly hair has tight, spiral curls. Now, let's see how you can make wavy hair curly.

There are different methods to make wavy hair curly, such as using heat styling tools, curling creams, and mousse. We recommend using a curling cream or mousse, as they are less damaging to your hair than heat styling tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make wavy hair curly:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. A conditioner will add moisture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously, as it can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Curling Cream or Mousse

Apply a curling cream or mousse to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair using your hands to create curls. Hold each section for a few seconds to set the curls.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. A diffuser will distribute the heat evenly, preventing frizz and enhancing the curls.

Step 6: Apply Hair Spray

Once your hair is dry, apply a hair spray to hold the curls in place. Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can disrupt the curls.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I make wavy hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can make wavy hair curly without using heat. You can use a curling cream or mousse to enhance your natural curls.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week. Washing your hair too often can strip it of natural oils, making it dry and frizzy.

3. How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

You can prevent frizz in your curly hair by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, avoiding rubbing your hair with a towel, and using a hair spray to hold the curls in place.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to make wavy hair curly, it's time to try it out. Follow these steps and tips to get beautiful, bouncy curls. Don't forget to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
