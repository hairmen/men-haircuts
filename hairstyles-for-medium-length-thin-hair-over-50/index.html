Hairstyles For Medium Length Thin Hair Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As women age, their hair tends to thin out, making it harder to find hairstyles that flatter their face. However, with the right cut and styling, medium length thin hair over 50 can still look fabulous. In this article, we will be sharing some hairstyles that are perfect for women with medium length thin hair over 50, as well as some tips and tricks to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for medium length thin hair over 50, it is important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

The right haircut can accentuate your best features and flatter your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a layered cut that frames your face can help to elongate it. If you have a square face, a side-swept bang can soften your features.

2. Choose the Right Length

Medium length hair is perfect for women over 50 as it is versatile and easy to style. However, it is important to choose the right length for your face shape and hair texture. If you have thin hair, a shorter cut can make your hair appear fuller.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

If you lead an active lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, a layered cut can offer more styling options.

Step by Step Guide to Styling

Styling medium length thin hair over 50 can be a challenge, but with the right products and tools, you can achieve a gorgeous look. Here is a step by step guide:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and thickness to your hair.

2. Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product like mousse or root lifting spray to your hair to add volume and texture.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots. Focus on the crown of your head for added volume.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a curling iron or straightener for added texture and volume. You can also add a side-swept bang or layers to frame your face.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Thin hair can be fragile, so it is important to take extra care to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

1. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling can damage your hair, making it more prone to breakage and thinning. Try to limit the use of heat styling tools and opt for low-maintenance hairstyles instead.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for thin hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners, to add body and thickness to your hair. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy by removing split ends and preventing breakage. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my thin hair?

Yes, you can dye your thin hair, but it is important to choose a gentle dye that will not damage your hair. Opt for a shade that is close to your natural hair color to avoid harsh contrasts.

2. Can I get a short haircut if I have thin hair?

Yes, a shorter haircut can make your hair appear thicker and fuller. However, it is important to choose the right length for your face shape and hair texture.

3. How can I make my thin hair look fuller?

You can make your thin hair look fuller by using volumizing products, blow drying your hair, and opting for layered haircuts that add volume and texture.


With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your medium length thin hair over 50 with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and getting regular trims to keep it healthy and beautiful.
