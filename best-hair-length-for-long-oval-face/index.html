Description

Having a long oval face means that you have a narrow face shape with a rounded jawline and forehead. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off different hair lengths and styles. However, there are still some hair lengths that can enhance the natural beauty of a long oval face.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair length for long oval face, first, you need to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Stand in front of a mirror and tie your hair back.

Step 2: Use a measuring tape and measure the following:

- Forehead: from hairline to hairline

- Cheekbones: widest part of your face

- Jawline: from the tip of your chin to the end of your jawline

- Face length: from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin

Step 3: Write down the measurements and use the following guide to determine your face shape:

- Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement with a smaller forehead and rounded jawline.

- Square: All measurements are similar with a strong jawline.

- Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline is pointed.

- Oval: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones and forehead is wider than the jawline.

- Diamond: Face length is the greatest measurement with narrow forehead and jawline and wide cheekbones.

Step by Step Guide in Choosing the Best Hair Length for Long Oval Face

Now that you know your face shape, here is a step-by-step guide in choosing the best hair length for long oval face:

Step 1: Determine your hair texture and type. This will help you in choosing the best hairstyle that will suit your face shape.

Step 2: Consider the length that you are comfortable with. It's important to choose a length that you are willing to maintain and style regularly.

Step 3: Choose a hair length that will balance your face shape. For long oval face shape, the best hair lengths are shoulder-length, long layers, and blunt cuts with long bangs.

Step 4: Consult with your hairstylist. They can help you in choosing the best hair length and style that will suit your face shape and hair type.

Tips in Maintaining the Best Hair Length for Long Oval Face

Here are some tips in maintaining the best hair length for long oval face:

- Regularly trim your hair to avoid split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

- Use hair products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture.

- Style your hair in different ways to avoid boredom and to enhance the natural beauty of your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear short hair with a long oval face?

A: Yes, you can. However, it's important to choose a short hairstyle that will balance your face shape.

Q: Can I wear bangs with a long oval face?

A: Yes, you can. Long bangs that sweep across your forehead can enhance the natural beauty of your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Face

Here are some hair care tips that you should follow if you have a long oval face:

- Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

- Regularly wash your hair to avoid product build-up and to maintain its cleanliness.
