How To Cut Two Front Pieces Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle without doing anything too drastic? Cutting two front pieces of hair can give you a fresh, new look without sacrificing too much length. In this guide, we’ll go over how to cut two front pieces of hair in a relaxed, easy-to-follow way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Two Front Pieces of Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Two Front Pieces of Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s go over the tools you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section off hair

Now, let’s get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by sectioning off the hair. Create a center part from the forehead to the crown of the head.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from one side of the part. This section should be about the width of your forehead.

Step 3: Comb the section of hair forward and hold it between your index and middle fingers. The amount of hair you hold will determine how short the bangs will be.

Step 4: Cut the hair straight across using sharp scissors. Be sure to cut in small sections and check often to make sure both sides are even.

Step 5: Repeat steps 2-4 on the other side of the part.

Tips for a Successful Cut

Now that you know the steps, here are some tips to ensure your cut turns out great:

  • Start with small sections of hair. You can always cut more, but you can’t put hair back once it’s cut.
  • Take your time. Rushing through the process can lead to uneven or crooked bangs.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can create split ends and make your hair look frizzy.
  • Check your work often. Hold a mirror up to see the back of your head and make sure both sides are even.

FAQs

Q: How short should I cut my bangs?

A: It’s up to you! The amount of hair you hold between your fingers will determine how short the bangs will be. Start with a small amount and cut gradually if you want them to be shorter.

Q: Can I cut my bangs when they’re wet?

A: It’s best to cut hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your front pieces, be sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant if you plan on using hot tools.
  • Trim your bangs every few weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to cut two front pieces of hair with ease. Have fun experimenting with your new look!
