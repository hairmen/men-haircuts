How To Cut Your Own Hair In Layers With Side Bangs

Written by Oliver Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money at the hair salon? Do you want to try something new and cut your own hair in layers with side bangs? Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be easy and fun! In this article, we will show you how to cut your own hair in layers with side bangs step by step.

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair in Layers with Side Bangs

Description

Cutting your own hair in layers with side bangs can save you money and give you a fresh new look. Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, while side bangs can frame your face and give you a youthful appearance. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve salon-quality results at home.

How to Cut Your Own Hair in Layers with Side Bangs

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need the following tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Mirror

Once you have these tools, you can start cutting your hair in layers with side bangs. Follow these simple steps:

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with dry, clean hair. Comb your hair and divide it into four sections: top, sides, and back.

Step 2: Clip the top section of your hair away from the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the side of your head and hold it between your fingers. Cut the ends of the hair with the scissors at an angle to create a soft, layered look. Repeat this step with the other side of your head.

Step 4: Release the top section of your hair and clip the sides away from the back.

Step 5: Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the ends of the hair at an angle to create a layered look. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have cut all of the hair in the back section.

Step 6: Release the sides of your hair and clip the back away from the sides.

Step 7: Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and hold it between your fingers. Cut the ends of the hair at an angle to create a layered look. Repeat this step with the other side of your head.

Step 8: Release the top section of your hair and clip the sides and back away from it.

Step 9: Starting at the top of your head, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the ends of the hair at an angle to create a layered look. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have cut all of the hair in the top section.

Step 10: Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down. Cut the ends of the hair at an angle to create side bangs. Repeat this step with the other side of your head.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little bit at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once you've cut it.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Comb your hair before cutting to avoid tangles.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.

FAQ

Q: Is it easy to cut your own hair in layers with side bangs?

A: With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be easy and fun.

Q: How long does it take to cut your own hair in layers with side bangs?

A: It depends on the length and thickness of your hair, but it usually takes about 30 minutes to an hour.

Q: Can I cut my hair in layers with side bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. Just be sure to cut your hair when it is dry and in its natural state to avoid any surprises.

Hair Care

After you have cut your hair in layers with side bangs, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your new look.

Cutting your own hair in layers with side bangs can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve salon-quality results at home. So go ahead and give it a try!
