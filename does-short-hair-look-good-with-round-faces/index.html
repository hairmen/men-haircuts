Does Short Hair Look Good With Round Faces

Written by Frank Aug 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and looking for a change? Are you considering chopping your locks into a short hairstyle, but worried about how it will look with your round face? You're not alone. Many women with round faces wonder if they can pull off a short hairstyle. The good news is that short hair can look amazing on round faces. In this article, we'll explore the question "does short hair look good with round faces?" and provide tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are widest at the cheekbones. The length and width of a round face are almost equal, and the jawline is typically round and not very defined. If you have a round face, you want to choose a short hairstyle that will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. The right short hairstyle can enhance your features and bring out your best qualities.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Round Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Length

The length of your hair is important when it comes to enhancing your facial features. Short hair can look great on round faces, but you want to avoid cuts that are too short. A pixie cut, for example, can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for a short bob or a lob that falls just below your chin or at your shoulders.

2. Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, but you want to be careful not to add too much volume around your cheeks. Soft, subtle layers can help elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

3. Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. However, you want to avoid blunt bangs that end at your eyebrows. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Short Hairstyle for a Round Face

1. Determine your face shape. If you're not sure if you have a round face, use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If they're all about the same width, you have a round face.

2. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines and online galleries to find short hairstyles that you like. Look for hairstyles that are worn by women with round faces.

3. Consult with a stylist. Bring pictures of the hairstyles you like to your stylist and ask for their opinion. They can help you choose a hairstyle that will enhance your features and flatter your face shape.

4. Consider your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain. Short hairstyles require more frequent trims and styling, so make sure you're willing to put in the time and effort.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on a Round Face

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair.

2. Use a round brush to style your hair and create volume at the crown.

3. Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face.

4. Use a hairspray or styling product to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

FAQ

1. Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face?

A pixie cut can be a great way to show off your features, but it's not the best choice for a round face. Pixie cuts can make your face appear even rounder, so it's best to opt for a short bob or a lob instead.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a round face. Side-swept bangs can be a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.

3. What if I don't like my short hairstyle?

If you don't like your short hairstyle, don't panic. It's just hair, and it will grow back. Talk to your stylist about making changes or growing your hair out.

Hair Care for Short Hair on a Round Face

Short hair requires more frequent trims and styling, so it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by using a leave-in conditioner or protective spray.

In conclusion, short hair can look amazing on round faces. With the right length, layers, and bangs, you can enhance your features and bring out your best qualities. Use these tips and tricks to choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
