Bun Hairstyles For Long Hair For Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day? If you have long hair, a bun hairstyle is the perfect choice. Not only is it elegant and timeless, but it is also practical and will keep your hair out of your face all day long. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about bun hairstyles for long hair for wedding, including how to create them, tips for making them last all day, and how to care for your hair before and after the big day.

Description

A bun hairstyle is a classic and elegant updo that is perfect for weddings. It involves pulling your hair up into a ball or knot shape at the back of your head. There are many different types of bun hairstyles, including low buns, high buns, messy buns, sleek buns, and braided buns. The type of bun you choose will depend on your personal style and the overall look you want to achieve on your wedding day.

How to Create a Bun Hairstyle for Long Hair for Wedding

Creating a bun hairstyle for long hair for a wedding is easier than you might think. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step is to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair a day before the wedding. This will give your hair enough time to air dry and will also make it easier to style. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage when using hot tools.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. You can also use a detangling spray to make your hair easier to work with.

Step 3: Create a Ponytail

Next, create a ponytail at the back of your head. You can make it as high or as low as you like, depending on the type of bun you want to create. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail.

Step 4: Twist and Pin

Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. You can also use a hair net to keep the bun in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a spritz of hairspray to keep the bun in place all day long.

Tips for Making Your Bun Hairstyle Last All Day

Here are some tips for making your bun hairstyle last all day:

  • Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your hair in place
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands
  • Consider using a hair net to keep your bun in place
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and overall look

FAQ

Can I create a bun hairstyle if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, creating a bun hairstyle may be more challenging. You can try using hair extensions or opting for a different hairstyle, such as a braided updo.

How long will my bun hairstyle last?

Your bun hairstyle should last all day if you use the right products and techniques to keep it in place.

Can I create a bun hairstyle myself or should I hire a professional?

You can create a bun hairstyle yourself, but if you want a more intricate or complex hairstyle, it may be best to hire a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care Before and After the Wedding Day

It is essential to take care of your hair before and after the wedding day to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too many hot tools on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals

In conclusion, bun hairstyles for long hair for weddings are a classic and elegant choice that will never go out of style. With the right products and techniques, you can create a beautiful and long-lasting hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful on your big day.
