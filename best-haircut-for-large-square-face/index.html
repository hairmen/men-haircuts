The Best Haircut For Large Square Face Tips Tricks And Styles

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you one of the many women with a large square face who has struggled to find the perfect haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best haircuts for your face shape, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your new style.

Description

A large square face is characterized by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and angular features. The goal of a great haircut for this face shape is to soften the angles and add some length to the face. The right style can help balance out your features and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For large square faces, you'll want to avoid styles that accentuate the squareness of your face, such as blunt bangs or straight cuts. Instead, opt for softer, layered cuts that add movement and volume.

Some great styles to consider include:

Long Layers

Long, layered hair is a great option for large square faces. This style adds movement and volume to your hair, while also softening the angles of your face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin and blend into the rest of your hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add some softness to your face. These bangs are cut at an angle and sweep across your forehead, drawing attention away from your angular features. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle and blend them into the rest of your hair.

Textured Bob

A textured bob is a great option for those with thick, straight hair. This style adds some movement and volume to your hair, while also framing your face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a bob that's cut at an angle and textured with layers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving the perfect haircut for your large square face:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in soft layers that add movement and volume to your hair.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs that accentuate the squareness of your face.
  4. Add some texture to your hair with layers or a texturizing spray.
  5. Style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and movement.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your new style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools that can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling on your hair and causing breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a large square face?

The best haircut for a large square face is one that adds some softness and movement to your hair. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and textured bobs are all great options.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a large square face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a large square face. Just be sure to add some movement and volume with layers or a texturizing spray.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

What products should I use to maintain my haircut?

Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add some movement and volume to your hair. Use a light hairspray to hold your style in place without weighing down your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect haircut for your large square face. Remember to consult with your stylist and take care of your hair to keep it looking great!
