Can I Change My Hairstyle In Pokemon Go

Written by Petter Aug 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go! This article will cover everything you need to know about how to change your hairstyle, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, this guide will help you achieve the look you want. Let's get started!

Description

Yes, you can change your hairstyle in Pokemon Go! In fact, it's a fun way to personalize your character and stand out from other players. You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, including short, long, curly, and straight. Additionally, you can change your hair color and add accessories like hats and glasses. Changing your hairstyle is easy and can be done at any time, so don't be afraid to experiment!

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go

Changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is a simple process. Here's how:

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Open the Pokemon Go app on your phone or tablet.
  2. Tap on the "Customize" button on the main menu.
  3. Select the "Avatar" option.
  4. Tap on the "Hair" option.
  5. Choose the hairstyle you want.
  6. Customize your hair color and add accessories if desired.
  7. Tap on "Save" to confirm your new hairstyle.

That's it! Your new hairstyle will now be visible to other players in the game.

Tips for Changing Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider adding accessories like hats and glasses to complete your look.
  • Don't be afraid to change your hairstyle frequently to keep things interesting.
  • Take screenshots of your avatar to share with friends and on social media.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

Can I change my hairstyle for free?

Yes, changing your hairstyle is completely free in Pokemon Go.

Can I change my hairstyle as often as I want?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as often as you like.

Can I change my hairstyle after I create my avatar?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle at any time after creating your avatar.

Hair Care Advice

While your virtual hairstyle doesn't require any maintenance, it's important to take care of your real hair. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you'll have healthy, shiny hair in real life and a stylish virtual hairstyle in Pokemon Go!

That's everything you need to know about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go. We hope this guide has been helpful and that you're now ready to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories. Happy playing!
