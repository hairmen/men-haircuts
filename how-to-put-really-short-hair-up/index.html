How To Put Really Short Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Dec 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have really short hair, you might think that putting it up is impossible. But fear not, there are actually quite a few ways to style your short locks. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to put really short hair up in a variety of styles. From sleek buns to messy updos, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Put Really Short Hair Up
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Short Hair
  4. FAQs

How to Put Really Short Hair Up

Before we get into the specific styles, let’s talk about some general tips for putting really short hair up:

  • Use bobby pins and hair elastics that match your hair color, so they blend in.
  • Start with clean, dry hair, and use a bit of hairspray or texturizing spray to add grip.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment! Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

Now, let’s get started with some specific styles.

Sleek Low Bun

This is a classic and elegant style that works well for both formal and casual occasions.

Step 1: Brush your hair back and gather it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail tightly and wrap it around the base to create a bun.

Step 3: Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide them as much as possible.

Step 4: Smooth down any flyaways with hairspray or a bit of gel.

Messy Updo

If you’re going for a more relaxed look, a messy updo is a great option.

Step 1: Start by creating a deep side part.

Step 2: Take small sections of hair and twist them back, securing them with bobby pins as you go. Continue until all of your hair is twisted and pinned up.

Step 3: Use your fingers to gently pull out some pieces of hair around your face and at the crown of your head to create a messy, undone look.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Half-Up, Half-Down

This style is perfect for those days when you want to get your hair out of your face, but still show off your length.

Step 1: Create a deep side part.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair on the side with less hair and twist it back, securing it with a bobby pin behind your ear.

Step 3: Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Use a bit of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a brief summary of the steps for putting really short hair up:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use hairspray or texturizing spray to add grip.
  3. Choose your style and gather your hair accordingly.
  4. Secure with bobby pins or hair elastics.
  5. Smooth down flyaways with hairspray or gel.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are a few additional tips for styling really short hair:

  • Invest in some good quality bobby pins and hair elastics.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help or advice from a hair stylist.

FAQs

Q: Can I put really short hair up in a ponytail?

A: It depends on how short your hair is. If it’s long enough to gather into a ponytail, then yes! However, if it’s too short, you may have to opt for a different style.

Q: How do I keep bobby pins from slipping out of my hair?

A: Try spraying your bobby pins with hairspray before using them to add a bit of grip.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Even though short hair is low-maintenance, it still requires some care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to put your really short hair up in a variety of styles. Whether you’re going for a sleek look or a messy updo, you can rock any style with confidence!
