How to Pick Your Hairstyle Male - Tips and Tricks

Choosing the right hairstyle can drastically change your appearance, boost your confidence and give you a different outlook on life. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to pick the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we will provide you with some useful tips and tricks on how to pick your hairstyle male.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick Your Hairstyle Male
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Pick Your Hairstyle Male

Before you decide on a hairstyle, it is essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining which hairstyle will suit you the best. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume on top, such as a quiff or a messy fringe.
  • If you have a square face, go for hairstyles that soften your sharp features, such as a side-swept fringe or a textured crop.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! Most hairstyles will suit you, so experiment with different styles and see what works best.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, choose hairstyles that balance out your features, such as a textured crop or a messy fringe.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, here is a step by step guide on how to choose the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Research different hairstyles online or in magazines that appeal to you.
  2. Consider your hair type and how much time you are willing to spend styling it.
  3. Take your face shape and personal style into account.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist and ask for their opinion on which hairstyle will suit you the best.
  5. Try out the hairstyle and see how you feel about it. Don't be afraid to make changes if it doesn't feel right.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing your hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and lifestyle.
  • Invest in good quality styling products to keep your hair looking great.
  • Be open to trying new things and experimenting with different styles.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice or recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the perfect hairstyle:

  • What is the best hairstyle for men with thinning hair?

    • A textured crop or a messy fringe can work well for men with thinning hair as it adds volume and texture.

  • Can I pull off a buzz cut?

    • It depends on your face shape and personal style. If you have a round face, a buzz cut may not be the best option as it can make your face appear even rounder.

  • How often should I get my hair cut?

    • It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.


Hair Care Tips

Lastly, taking care of your hair is just as important as choosing the right hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, choosing the perfect hairstyle for you will be a breeze. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with different styles until you find the one that suits you the best.
