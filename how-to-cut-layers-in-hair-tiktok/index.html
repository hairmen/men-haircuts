How To Cut Layers In Hair Tiktok A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a fresh, new hairstyle, why not try cutting layers into your hair? Layers can add volume, texture, and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and more dynamic. And thanks to TikTok, it’s easier than ever to learn how to cut layers in your hair at home. In this post, we’ll walk you through the steps of cutting layers in your hair using TikTok tutorials, as well as provide some tips and tricks for maintaining your new ‘do.

How to Cut Layers in Hair TikTok: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you’ll need to gather the necessary tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Spray bottle with water

Once you have all of your tools, you’re ready to get started!

Step 2: Section Your Hair

The first step in cutting layers into your hair is to section it. Use your comb to divide your hair into three sections: one on top of your head, one on each side, and one at the back. Secure each section with a hair tie.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Starting with the bottom layer, remove the hair tie and comb out any tangles. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut straight across with your scissors. This will create the base layer of your haircut.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Next, remove the hair tie from the middle section and comb out any tangles. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up. This will create the middle layer of your haircut.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, remove the hair tie from the top section and comb out any tangles. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it at a steeper angle than the middle layer, starting from the bottom and working your way up. This will create the top layer of your haircut.

Step 6: Blend Your Layers

Once you’ve cut all three layers, use your scissors to blend them together. Hold your hair between your fingers and chop into the ends to create a more natural, layered look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you’ve cut your layers, it’s time to style your hair! You can blow dry it straight, curl it with a curling iron, or let it air dry for a more natural look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips for Cutting Layers in Hair TikTok

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when cutting layers in your hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Watch multiple TikTok tutorials before starting
  • Don’t cut too much off at once

FAQ About Cutting Layers in Hair TikTok

Can I cut layers in my hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can cut layers in curly hair! Just make sure to cut each layer separately and use a diffuser when blow drying to avoid frizz.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don’t panic! You can always go to a professional hair stylist to fix it or try again later.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy, vibrant locks.

Hair Care After Cutting Layers in Hair TikTok

After cutting your layers, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layers
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

Cutting layers in your hair can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. By following these steps and tips, you can create a beautiful, layered hairstyle right from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
