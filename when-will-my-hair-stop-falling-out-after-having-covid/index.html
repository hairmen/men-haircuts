When Will My Hair Stop Falling Out After Having Covid

Written by William Oct 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

COVID-19 has affected millions of people worldwide, and it has brought many health issues, including hair loss. Hair loss is one of the lesser-known side effects of COVID-19, but it can be a cause of concern for many people. In this article, we will discuss when your hair will stop falling out after having COVID-19, how to prevent it, and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step

Description

After having COVID-19, it is normal to experience hair loss. Hair loss can happen due to many reasons, including stress, fever, and other medical conditions. However, hair loss is not permanent in most cases, and it can be reversed with proper care and treatment.

The hair loss caused by COVID-19 usually starts two to three months after the infection, and it can last up to six months. The hair loss can be mild or severe, depending on the severity of the infection and the individual's health condition.

How to

The best way to prevent hair loss after having COVID-19 is to take care of your overall health. Here are some ways to take care of your health:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Get enough sleep and rest.
  • Exercise regularly and stay active.
  • Manage stress and anxiety.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.

If you are experiencing hair loss after having COVID-19, here are some things you can do:

  • Consult a dermatologist or a hair specialist.
  • Use gentle shampoos and conditioners.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and curling irons.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails.
  • Take supplements like biotin, vitamins, and minerals.

Tips

Here are some tips to prevent hair loss after having COVID-19:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals like hair dyes and bleach.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow.
  • Avoid overwashing your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

FAQ

Q: How long does hair loss last after having COVID-19?

A: Hair loss can last up to six months after having COVID-19, but it is not permanent in most cases.

Q: Can hair loss be prevented after having COVID-19?

A: Yes, hair loss can be prevented by taking care of your overall health, using gentle hair products, and consulting a hair specialist.

Q: Can supplements prevent hair loss after having COVID-19?

A: Supplements like biotin, vitamins, and minerals can help prevent hair loss after having COVID-19, but they should be taken under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to prevent hair loss after having COVID-19:

  • Use gentle shampoos and conditioners.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and curling irons.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails.
  • Take supplements like biotin, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow.

Remember, hair loss after having COVID-19 is temporary in most cases, and it can be reversed with proper care and treatment. Consult a dermatologist or a hair specialist if you are experiencing severe hair loss or if you have any concerns about your hair.
