Short Hairstyles For 65 Year Old Women A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Oct 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As women age, they often look for a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and suits their lifestyle. Short hairstyles are a great option for women in their 60s who are looking for a chic and low-maintenance look. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for 65 year-old women along with tips on how to style and care for them.

Why Choose Short Hairstyles?

Short hairstyles are a popular choice among 65 year-old women because they require less maintenance, can be styled easily, and look chic and trendy. They also draw attention to the face and can highlight your best features. Short haircuts can also make you look younger and more energetic.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle?

Choosing the right short hairstyle can be tricky, but there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider your face shape and hair texture. Secondly, think about your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair. Lastly, consider your personal style and the image you want to project.

Face Shape:

If you have a round face, go for a short hairstyle with layers that add volume and height to the crown. If you have a square face, opt for a short bob with side-swept bangs that soften the jawline. For an oval face, any short hairstyle will suit you.

Hair Texture:

If you have thin hair, go for a short pixie cut with layers that add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, opt for a short bob or a layered cut that thins out the hair. For curly hair, go for a short layered cut that enhances your natural curls.

Lifestyle:

If you are always on the go, choose a short hairstyle that requires minimal styling. A pixie cut or a short bob with side-swept bangs are good options. If you have more time to style your hair, go for a layered cut that can be styled in different ways.

Personal Style:

Your personal style and the image you want to project also play a role in choosing the right short hairstyle. If you want to look edgy and trendy, go for a short pixie cut with bold highlights. If you prefer a classic look, opt for a short bob with subtle highlights.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair may seem daunting at first, but it can be easy once you get the hang of it. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

Step 1:

Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to add volume and texture.

Step 2:

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use your fingers to lift the roots for added volume.

Step 3:

If you have a pixie cut, use a texturizing product such as wax or pomade to define the layers and add texture. For a short bob, use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair.

Step 4:

To finish, apply a hairspray to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs some care. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

Tip 1:

Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your short hairstyle. Aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Tip 2:

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

Tip 3:

Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 4:

Avoid tying your hair up in tight hairstyles, as it can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: No, short hair can actually make you look younger and more energetic. It draws attention to the face and highlights your best features.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

A: Yes, short hair can be styled in many different ways. A layered cut can be styled in different ways, and you can use accessories such as headbands and clips to change up your look.

Q: Can I still dye my hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair if you have short hair. In fact, short hair is a great canvas for experimenting with different hair colors.

Conclusion

Short hairstyles are a great option for 65 year-old women who are looking for a chic and low-maintenance look. When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Styling short hair is easy once you get the hang of it, and maintaining it requires less effort than long hair. Follow our tips for maintaining short hair and you'll have a stylish and easy-to-manage hairstyle that suits your age and lifestyle.
