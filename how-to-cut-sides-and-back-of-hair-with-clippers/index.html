How To Cut Sides And Back Of Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking to achieve a fresh, clean-cut look, learning how to cut the sides and back of your hair with clippers can be an easy and affordable option. With a few simple steps and some basic equipment, you can give yourself a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll go through everything you need to know to achieve the perfect cut, including how to:

How to:

  1. Prepare Your Clippers and Equipment
  2. Wash and Dry Your Hair
  3. Create a Guide Line
  4. Start Cutting the Sides
  5. Blend the Top and Sides
  6. Cut the Back of Your Hair
  7. Blend the Back and Sides

Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers and Equipment

The first step in cutting your hair with clippers is to make sure you have all the necessary equipment. You'll need:

  • A set of clippers with various guard sizes
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • A towel to drape over your shoulders

Before you start cutting, make sure your clippers are clean and oiled. You should also choose a guard size that is appropriate for the length of hair you want to cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to wash and dry your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup that could interfere with the cutting process. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it and comb it out so that it's free of tangles.

Step 3: Create a Guide Line

The next step is to create a guide line. This will help you ensure that you're cutting your hair evenly and at the right length. To create a guide line, part your hair where you want the cut to start, and use your clippers to trim the hair along the part. This will create a clean, even line that you can use as a reference point as you continue to cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting the Sides

Now it's time to start cutting the sides of your hair. Begin by attaching the appropriate guard size to your clippers, and start cutting from the bottom of your hairline up to the guide line you created in step 3. Work your way around your head, making sure to keep the clippers level and using the same guard size for each section of hair.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

Once you've finished cutting the sides of your hair, it's time to blend the top and sides together. To do this, switch to a smaller guard size on your clippers and use them to fade the hair on the sides into the longer hair on top. Use a comb to help you blend the hair evenly, and be sure to check your progress in the mirror as you go.

Step 6: Cut the Back of Your Hair

Now it's time to move on to the back of your hair. This can be a bit trickier, as you'll need to use a mirror to see what you're doing. Begin by attaching the appropriate guard size to your clippers, and carefully start cutting from the bottom of your hairline up to the guide line you created in step 3. Work your way up the back of your head, using the same guard size for each section of hair.

Step 7: Blend the Back and Sides

Finally, it's time to blend the back and sides of your hair together. Use a smaller guard size on your clippers to fade the hair on the back of your head into the longer hair on the sides. Use a comb to help you blend the hair evenly, and be sure to check your progress in the mirror as you go.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter size if necessary.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and keep the hair even.
  • Take your time and be patient – rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Check your progress in the mirror frequently to ensure that you're cutting evenly.
  • Trim any stray hairs with a pair of scissors to achieve a more polished look.

FAQ

Can I Cut My Hair with Clippers If I Have Long Hair?

While clippers are typically used for shorter hairstyles, it is possible to use them on longer hair. Just be sure to choose a longer guard size and take your time to ensure that you're cutting evenly.

Can I Cut My Own Hair with Clippers?

Yes, it is possible to cut your own hair with clippers. However, it can be tricky to cut the back of your head, so you may want to enlist the help of a friend or family member to assist you.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

After you've finished cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking its best. Be sure to:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your cut and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a clean, professional-looking haircut right from the comfort of your own home.
