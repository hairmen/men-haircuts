How To Do A Low Messy Bun Updo

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Want something easy, yet chic? Look no further than the low messy bun updo. This versatile hairstyle can be dressed up or down and is perfect for any occasion. Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect low messy bun updo.

Description

The low messy bun updo is a hairstyle that is both elegant and effortless. It is a loose, casual hairstyle that can be created in minutes. The beauty of this hairstyle is that the messier it is, the better it looks. It is perfect for anyone who wants a low maintenance hairstyle that still looks put together.

How to

To achieve the perfect low messy bun updo, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your roots to add some texture to your hair. This will help your bun stay in place.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair into a Low Ponytail

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Use an elastic band to secure it in place. Make sure it is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it hurts.

Step 3: Create Your Bun

Twist your ponytail into a loose bun. Don't worry if it's messy, that's the whole point! Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. You can also use a scrunchie if you want a more casual look.

Step 4: Pull Out Some Strands

Gently pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a soft, romantic look. This will also help to frame your face.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your bun in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also help to tame any flyaways.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect low messy bun updo:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture to your hair
  • Don't worry if your bun isn't perfect, the messier the better!
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them
  • For a more polished look, try adding a headband or hair accessory

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the low messy bun updo:

What hair types work best with this hairstyle?

This hairstyle works best on medium to long hair that is straight or wavy. If you have curly hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the messy look.

Can I wear this hairstyle to a formal event?

Yes! The low messy bun updo is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. For a more formal look, try adding a hair accessory or pulling out fewer strands around your face.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. This includes washing it regularly, using a good conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling. Regular trims will also help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to create the perfect low messy bun updo, it's time to try it out for yourself. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
