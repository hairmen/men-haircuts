What Is 90S Hip Hop Style

Written by Luffy Oct 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on 90s hip hop style. This article will take you through everything you need to know about the fashion, music, and culture of the 90s hip hop era. We'll cover the history, the style, and how to achieve the iconic look. Let's get started!

Description

The 90s hip hop style is a fashion style that emerged during the golden age of hip hop music in the 1990s. It's a unique and iconic style that's still influencing fashion today. The style is characterized by oversized clothing, baggy jeans, bucket hats, sneakers, and gold chains. The style was popularized by hip hop artists such as Tupac, Notorious B.I.G., Wu-Tang Clan, and A Tribe Called Quest. The style was a reflection of the urban youth culture and the struggle of the black community at the time.

How to Achieve the 90s Hip Hop Style

Here's how to achieve the 90s hip hop style:

1. Oversized Clothing

90s hip hop style is all about oversized clothing. This means wearing clothes that are one or two sizes too big. Oversized shirts, jackets, and hoodies were a staple of the 90s hip hop style.

2. Baggy Jeans

Baggy jeans were a must-have for the 90s hip hop style. The jeans were loose and comfortable, with a wide leg and a low waist. The most popular brands were Tommy Hilfiger, FUBU, and Karl Kani.

3. Bucket Hats

Bucket hats were a key accessory in the 90s hip hop style. They were worn by both men and women and came in a range of colors and patterns. Bucket hats were often worn backwards or to the side.

4. Sneakers

Sneakers were a crucial part of the 90s hip hop style. The most popular brands were Nike, Adidas, and Reebok. High-top sneakers were particularly popular, and they were often worn with baggy jeans and oversized shirts.

5. Gold Chains

Gold chains were a sign of success and wealth in the 90s hip hop culture. The bigger and bolder the chain, the better. Gold chains were often worn over oversized shirts or tucked into baggy jeans.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the 90s hip hop style:

Step 1: Get Oversized Clothes

Start by getting oversized clothes. Look for shirts, jackets, and hoodies that are one or two sizes too big. You can find oversized clothes at thrift stores, vintage shops, and online retailers.

Step 2: Baggy Jeans

Next, get a pair of baggy jeans. Look for jeans with a wide leg and a low waist. Tommy Hilfiger, FUBU, and Karl Kani are great brands to look for.

Step 3: Bucket Hats

Get a bucket hat in your favorite color or pattern. Wear it backwards or to the side.

Step 4: Sneakers

Get a pair of high-top sneakers from Nike, Adidas, or Reebok. Wear them with your baggy jeans and oversized shirt.

Step 5: Gold Chains

Finally, get a gold chain. The bigger and bolder, the better. Wear it over your oversized shirt or tuck it into your baggy jeans.

Tips for Achieving the 90s Hip Hop Style

Here are some tips for achieving the 90s hip hop style:

1. Mix and Match

Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles and colors. The 90s hip hop style was all about individuality and self-expression.

2. Confidence is Key

The 90s hip hop style is all about confidence. Wear your clothes with pride and don't be afraid to stand out.

3. Accessorize

Accessorize with gold chains, earrings, and bracelets. These accessories were a key part of the 90s hip hop style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 90s hip hop style:

Q: What brands were popular in the 90s hip hop style?

A: Tommy Hilfiger, FUBU, and Karl Kani were popular brands in the 90s hip hop style.

Q: What kind of sneakers were popular in the 90s hip hop style?

A: High-top sneakers from Nike, Adidas, and Reebok were popular in the 90s hip hop style.

Q: What kind of jewelry was popular in the 90s hip hop style?

A: Gold chains, earrings, and bracelets were popular in the 90s hip hop style.

Hair Care

Hair was an important part of the 90s hip hop style. Men often wore their hair in a high-top fade, while women wore box braids or cornrows. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Moisturize

Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy. A good haircut can make all the difference.

That's it! You now know everything you need to know about the 90s hip hop style. Remember to mix and match, be confident, and accessorize. And don't forget to take care of your hair!
