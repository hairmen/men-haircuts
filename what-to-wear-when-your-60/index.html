What To Wear When You re 60 A Guide To Age Appropriate Fashion

Written by Robby Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our fashion sense may evolve, but that doesn't mean we can't still look stylish and trendy. If you're in your 60s and wondering what to wear, this guide is for you. From casual everyday wear to formal occasions, we've got you covered with tips and tricks to help you dress your best.

Description

When you're 60, you want to dress in a way that is age-appropriate but still shows off your style and personality. You don't have to sacrifice comfort or fashion for practicality. With a few simple tips and tricks, you can dress confidently and feel good about your appearance. This guide will help you navigate the world of fashion and find what works best for you.

How to

The key to dressing in your 60s is finding a balance between comfort and style. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Embrace Comfortable Fabrics

As we age, our bodies change, and we may become more sensitive to certain fabrics. Look for clothing made from natural, breathable fabrics like cotton, linen, and silk. These materials are comfortable and will keep you cool in warm weather.

2. Invest in Quality Pieces

When you're in your 60s, it's time to start investing in quality pieces that will last. Look for timeless, classic styles that can be dressed up or down. A well-made blazer, a pair of tailored pants, and a little black dress are all wardrobe staples that you can wear for years to come.

3. Accessorize

Accessories can take an outfit from drab to fab. Invest in a few statement pieces like a bold necklace or a pair of oversized sunglasses. These items can add a pop of color and personality to your outfit.

4. Dress for Your Body Type

As we age, our bodies may change, and it's essential to dress for our current shape. Look for clothing that flatters your figure and accentuates your best features. If you're unsure of what works best for you, seek the advice of a professional stylist.

Step by Step

Here are some simple steps to follow when choosing what to wear:

1. Determine the Occasion

The first step in choosing an outfit is to determine the occasion. Are you attending a casual lunch with friends, or is it a formal event?

2. Choose Your Colors

Choose colors that complement your skin tone and hair color. If you're unsure, stick to neutrals like black, white, and gray.

3. Find the Right Fit

Make sure your clothing fits properly. Ill-fitting clothing can be uncomfortable and unflattering.

4. Accessorize

Add accessories that complement your outfit, such as a scarf or a statement piece of jewelry.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Dress for Yourself

Dress in a way that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Don't worry about what others think.

2. Update Your Wardrobe

Update your wardrobe with a few key pieces each season to keep your look fresh.

3. Stay True to Your Style

Don't feel like you need to conform to a specific style just because of your age. Stay true to your personal style and wear what makes you feel good.

FAQ

What should I wear to a casual lunch with friends?

For a casual lunch, opt for comfortable yet stylish clothing like a pair of jeans and a blouse or a sundress.

What should I wear to a formal event?

For a formal event, choose a classic, timeless outfit like a little black dress or a tailored suit.

What should I wear to stay warm in the winter?

Invest in a warm, stylish coat and accessorize with a scarf and gloves.

Hair Care

Don't forget about your hair! As we age, our hair may become thinner or more brittle. Invest in quality hair care products that nourish and strengthen your hair. Consider a shorter haircut or a new hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently navigate the world of fashion and dress to impress at any age.
