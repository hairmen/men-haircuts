How To Cut A Blunt Textured Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Description

A blunt textured bob is a trendy and chic hairstyle that can suit any face shape and hair type. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in various ways, from sleek and straight to messy and tousled. In this article, we will teach you how to cut a blunt textured bob in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

How to Cut a Blunt Textured Bob

Cutting a blunt textured bob requires some skill and knowledge, but it's not impossible to do it yourself. Here's how to cut a blunt textured bob in simple steps:

Step 1: Prepare your tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, sectioning clips, and a mirror.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it completely. This will make it easier to cut your hair and ensure that you get the right length.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Clip each section separately to keep them organized.

Step 4: Cut the back section

Start with the back section, and cut it straight across. Make sure that the length is even and that you're cutting it at the desired length. Use the comb to guide you and keep your cuts straight.

Step 5: Cut the front section

Once you're done with the back section, move on to the front section. Cut it at an angle to create a textured look. Make sure that you're cutting it at the same length as the back section.

Step 6: Check for symmetry

After you've cut all four sections, check for symmetry. Make sure that both sides are even in length and that there are no uneven cuts.

Step 7: Create layers

To create a textured look, you can add some layers to your blunt bob. Start with the front section, and cut it at an angle to create long layers. Use your comb to guide you and create a seamless blend.

Step 8: Style your hair

Once you're done cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can straighten it, curl it, or leave it messy and tousled.

Tips

- Start with small cuts and trim as necessary to avoid cutting off too much hair. - Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure that it's even. - If you're not confident in cutting your hair, seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

FAQ

Q: How long does a blunt textured bob last? A: A blunt textured bob can last for several weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows. Q: Can I cut my hair blunt textured bob with regular scissors? A: No, it's important to use proper haircutting scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.

Hair Care

To maintain your blunt textured bob, it's essential to keep it healthy and nourished. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep it moisturized. Trim your hair every few weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Conclusion

Cutting a blunt textured bob is a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a trendy and chic look that suits your face shape and hair type. Remember to take your time and start with small cuts to avoid any mistakes. Good luck!
