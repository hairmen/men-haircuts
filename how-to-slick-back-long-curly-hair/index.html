How to Slick Back Long Curly Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

If you have long curly hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to slick it back for a more polished look. The answer is yes! With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a slicked-back style that will stay put all day. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps you need to take to slick back long curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Slick Back Long Curly Hair
  • Tips for Slicking Back Long Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Slicking Back Long Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Slick Back Long Curly Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You'll also need a few products:

  • Hair gel or pomade
  • A fine-tooth comb
  • Bobby pins (optional)

Here's what to do:

  1. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and rub them together.
  2. Starting at the crown of your head, run your hands through your hair, pulling it back as you go. Make sure to distribute the product evenly.
  3. Use a fine-tooth comb to smooth down any flyaways and create a clean part.
  4. If you have bangs, use the comb to slick them back as well.
  5. If your hair is long enough, you can twist it into a low bun or ponytail at the nape of your neck. Use bobby pins to secure it in place.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Congratulations, you've slicked back your long curly hair!

Tips for Slicking Back Long Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a hair gel or pomade that's specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Don't be afraid to use bobby pins to secure your hair in place. This can be especially helpful if your hair is very long or thick.
  • If your hair is prone to frizz, try using a smoothing serum or oil before applying the gel or pomade.
  • If you want a more natural look, you can skip the hairspray and let your curls do their thing.

FAQs About Slicking Back Long Curly Hair

Can I slick back my curly hair without using product?

It's possible, but it may not stay in place as well. If you want a more polished look, we recommend using a hair gel or pomade.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy when I slick it back?

Use a small amount of product, and make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair. You can also try using a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.

Can I slick back my hair if I have a lot of layers?

Yes, but it may be more difficult to get everything to stay in place. You may need to use more product or bobby pins to secure your hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Now that you know how to slick back your long curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls defined and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can have a sleek and polished look with your long curly hair. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
