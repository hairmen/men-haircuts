Will I Look Good With A Pixie Cut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting a pixie cut but unsure if it will suit you? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women have this question before taking the plunge. In this guide, we'll answer all your questions and provide you with tips and tricks to help you make the decision. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut short on the back and sides and slightly longer on top. It's a popular choice for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and stylish. Pixie cuts can be tailored to suit different face shapes, hair textures, and personal styles, making it a versatile option.

How to

The first step in deciding if a pixie cut is right for you is to consider your face shape. Pixie cuts look great on oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you might want to consider a longer pixie cut or a different hairstyle altogether.

Next, think about your hair texture. Pixie cuts work well on straight or wavy hair, but if you have curly hair, you might want to think twice before committing to a pixie cut. Curly hair can be harder to style in a pixie cut, and you may find that it requires more maintenance than you anticipated.

Finally, consider your personal style. Pixie cuts are a bold statement, so if you're someone who likes to play it safe with your hair, a pixie cut might not be for you. On the other hand, if you're someone who likes to take risks and try new things, a pixie cut could be the perfect hairstyle for you.

Step by Step

If you've decided that a pixie cut is right for you, here are the steps you can take to get the perfect cut:

  1. Find a stylist who has experience cutting pixie cuts.
  2. Bring pictures of pixie cuts that you like to your stylist.
  3. Discuss your face shape, hair texture, and personal style with your stylist.
  4. Trust your stylist's expertise and let them work their magic.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind before and after getting a pixie cut:

  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your pixie cut.
  • Invest in hair products that are designed for short hair to keep your pixie cut looking its best.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair texture.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your pixie cut with headbands, clips, or scarves.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

A: If you have a round face, a pixie cut can actually make your face look slimmer if it's cut correctly. Make sure to discuss your face shape with your stylist before getting a pixie cut.

Q: Will a pixie cut suit my hair texture?

A: Pixie cuts work well on straight or wavy hair, but if you have curly hair, you may find it harder to style. Consider your hair texture before committing to a pixie cut.

Q: Will a pixie cut suit my personal style?

A: Pixie cuts are a bold statement, so if you're someone who likes to play it safe with your hair, a pixie cut might not be for you. On the other hand, if you're someone who likes to take risks and try new things, a pixie cut could be the perfect hairstyle for you.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your pixie cut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for short hair.
  • Apply a styling product to damp hair to help with styling.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair texture.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your pixie cut.

So, there you have it. A complete guide to help you decide if a pixie cut is right for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before committing to this bold hairstyle. And don't forget to take care of your pixie cut with the right hair care products and regular trims. Good luck!
