How Does Head And Face Shape Affect A Men s Haircut

Written by Oliver Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, most men tend to focus on the style they want. However, one important factor that should not be overlooked is the shape of their head and face. The right haircut can enhance your features and bring out the best in your appearance while the wrong one can do the opposite. In this article, we will discuss how head and face shape affect a men's haircut, and give tips on how to choose the right style for your individual features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The shape of your head and face can greatly affect the way your haircut looks. Different face shapes require different haircut styles to complement their features. For example, a round face may benefit from a style that adds height and length, while a square face may benefit from a style that softens the angles of the jawline. The shape of your head can also impact how a haircut looks, as some styles may look better on a more oval-shaped head than a more angular one.

How to

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your head and face shape is to determine what your face shape is. This can be done by looking in a mirror and measuring the length and width of your face, as well as the shape of your jawline and hairline. Once you have determined your face shape, you can then research haircut styles that are recommended for your specific shape.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you may want to consider a style that adds height and length to your face. This can be achieved through a longer, layered cut, or a style that features a side-swept fringe. Avoid styles that are too short or blunt, as these can make your face appear even rounder.

Square Face

For those with a square face shape, a style that softens the angles of the jawline can be flattering. This can be achieved through a longer, layered cut or a style that features longer bangs. Avoid styles that are too short or blunt, as these can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Oval Face

Those with an oval face shape have the most versatility when it comes to haircut styles. Almost any style can be flattering, so it is important to consider your hair texture and personal style preferences when choosing a cut.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, a style that draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones can be flattering. This can be achieved through a shorter, layered cut or a style that features side-swept bangs. Avoid styles that are too heavy or blunt, as these can make your forehead appear even wider.

Step by step

When getting a haircut, it is important to communicate with your stylist about your head and face shape. They can use their expertise to recommend a style that will complement your features. Follow these steps to ensure you get the right cut:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research haircut styles for your specific shape
  3. Communicate with your stylist about your preferences and head/face shape
  4. Trust your stylist's expertise and advice
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and proper hair care

Tips

  • Bring a photo of the style you want to show your stylist
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a style
  • Ask for product recommendations to maintain your style at home
  • Be open to trying something new

FAQ

Can I still get a style that isn't recommended for my face shape?

Yes, of course! While certain styles may be more recommended for certain face shapes, ultimately it is up to personal preference. The important thing is to feel confident and comfortable with your new haircut.

How often should I get a haircut?

On average, most men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their style and keep their hair looking healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your new haircut looking fresh and stylish, it is important to take care of your hair. This can be done through regular washing and conditioning, using products recommended by your stylist, and avoiding over-styling or heat damage. When in doubt, consult with your stylist for personalized hair care tips.

Now that you know how head and face shape affect a men's haircut, you can confidently choose a style that will enhance your features and make you feel your best. Remember to communicate with your stylist and maintain your haircut with regular trims and proper hair care.
