How To Make 40S Waves In Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're looking for a classic, vintage hairstyle that never goes out of style, you might want to try the 40s waves. This hairstyle is characterized by soft, loose curls that frame your face and add a touch of glamour to your overall look. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look using some basic tools and techniques.

Description

The 40s waves hairstyle is a classic, vintage look that was popularized during the 1940s. It's characterized by soft, loose curls that are created using hot rollers or a curling iron. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or a casual night out with friends.

How to Make 40s Waves in Hair

Making 40s waves in your hair is easier than you might think. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving this classic look:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, you need to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair and then blow-drying it until it's completely dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the hot tools.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair in small sections and achieve a more defined wave.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a hot roller or a curling iron, start curling your hair in small sections. Make sure to curl away from your face for a more natural look. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds and then release.

Step 4: Pin the Curls

Once you've curled your hair, use bobby pins to pin the curls in place. This will help to set the curls and prevent them from falling out.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Cool

Allow your hair to cool for a few minutes before removing the bobby pins. This will help to set the curls and make them last longer.

Step 6: Brush Out Your Curls

Once your hair has cooled, remove the bobby pins and use a soft-bristled brush to gently brush out the curls. This will create a soft, loose wave that's perfect for the 40s look.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the hot tools. - Don't over-curl your hair. Use small sections and hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds only. - Pin the curls in place to set them and make them last longer. - Brush out the curls gently to avoid creating frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve this look without hot rollers or a curling iron? A: Yes, you can use a flat iron to create waves in your hair. Simply twist the flat iron as you glide it down your hair. Q: How long will this hairstyle last? A: This hairstyle can last up to a few days if you take care of it properly. Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and avoid washing your hair too often.

Hair Care

To maintain this hairstyle, you need to take good care of your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when necessary. Brush your hair gently and avoid pulling it too hard. With proper care, your 40s waves will look beautiful and last for days. In conclusion, making 40s waves in your hair is easy and fun. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this classic, vintage look in no time. Whether you're going to a formal event or a casual night out, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
