How To Have Your Hair Curly In The Morning Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Nov 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who loves to have curly hair, but can't seem to maintain it throughout the day, then you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to have your hair curly in the morning, and keep it that way all day long. From using the right products to adopting a proper hair care routine, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Use a curl-enhancing product

If you want to have curly hair in the morning, then you need to start with the right products. Look for a curl-enhancing product that works well with your hair type. This could be a mousse, gel, or cream. Apply the product to damp hair, scrunch it in, and let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.


Sleep in a pineapple or silk scarf

To prevent your curls from getting flattened while you sleep, try sleeping in a pineapple or silk scarf. A pineapple is a high ponytail that sits on the top of your head, which keeps your curls intact. Alternatively, you can wrap your hair in a silk scarf to protect your curls from friction and moisture loss.


Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer

If you're in a rush and need to dry your hair quickly, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and prevent your curls from getting frizzy. Make sure to use a low heat setting and avoid touching your hair too much while you dry it.


Try a twist-out or braid-out

If you prefer a more defined curl pattern, try doing a twist-out or braid-out. This involves twisting or braiding your hair while it's wet, and then unraveling it in the morning. This will give you more defined curls that will last throughout the day.


Don't over-wash your hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy. Instead, try washing your hair once or twice a week, and use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.


Avoid using too much heat

Using too much heat on your hair can cause it to become damaged and frizzy. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, and opt for heat-free styling methods instead.


Use a wide-tooth comb

When you're brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and keep your curls intact. This will also help to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.


Wrap your hair at night

Before you go to bed, wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your curls from friction and moisture loss. This will help to maintain your curls and prevent them from getting flattened while you sleep.


Don't touch your hair too much

Touching your hair too much can cause it to become frizzy and lose its shape. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day, and use a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.


Stay hydrated

Drinking plenty of water and staying hydrated can help to keep your curls moisturized and prevent them from becoming dry and brittle. Make sure to drink at least eight glasses of water a day, and eat a healthy, balanced diet to keep your hair and scalp healthy.


Hair Care FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair once or twice a week, using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy.

Q: How can I prevent my curls from getting frizzy?

A: To prevent your curls from getting frizzy, avoid using too much heat on your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to brush it, and apply a curl-enhancing product to keep your curls moisturized.

Q: How can I maintain my curls while I sleep?

A: To maintain your curls while you sleep, try sleeping in a pineapple or silk scarf, and wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet before you go to bed.

By following these tips and tricks, you can have your hair curly in the morning and keep it that way all day long. Remember to use the right products, adopt a proper hair care routine, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. With a little bit of effort and patience, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls that will turn heads wherever you go!
