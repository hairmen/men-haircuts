How To Hide Scalp Thinning Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Scalp thinning hair can be a frustrating and embarrassing problem for many people. It can affect both men and women and is often caused by genetics, age, or medical conditions. If you're struggling with scalp thinning hair, don't worry – there are plenty of ways to hide it and boost your confidence. Here are some tips and tricks to help you hide scalp thinning hair:

How to Hide Scalp Thinning Hair

1. Use Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a great way to cover up thinning hair and create the appearance of thicker, fuller hair. They are made of keratin proteins and come in a variety of colors to match your hair. Simply sprinkle the fibers onto your scalp and pat gently to blend them in. They will cling to your existing hair and create the illusion of more volume.

2. Try a Hairpiece or Wig

If you're dealing with more severe hair loss, a hairpiece or wig may be a good option. There are many types of wigs and hairpieces available, ranging from synthetic to real human hair. They can be customized to fit your head and styled to suit your preferences. Look for a quality wig or hairpiece that matches your hair color and texture for a natural look.

3. Use Hair Fillers

Hair fillers are similar to hair fibers but are applied in a different way. They typically come in a spray form and are designed to fill in thinning areas on the scalp. Simply spray the filler onto your scalp and blend it in with your fingers. It will create the appearance of thicker hair and cover up any bald spots.

4. Change Your Hairstyle

A simple change in hairstyle can make a big difference in hiding scalp thinning hair. Try a style that adds volume and texture, such as layers or waves. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate thinning areas. A side part can also help to conceal thinning hair.

5. Use Hair Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner

There are many hair thickening shampoos and conditioners on the market that can help to boost the appearance of your hair. Look for products that contain biotin, protein, and other ingredients that promote hair growth and thickness. Use these products regularly to see the best results.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hiding Scalp Thinning Hair

To effectively hide scalp thinning hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Products

Decide which product or combination of products will work best for your hair type and level of thinning. Consider using a combination of hair fibers, hair fillers, and thickening products for the best results.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair as usual, using your thickening shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Step 3: Apply the Products

Apply your chosen product(s) according to the instructions. Be sure to focus on the areas where your hair is thinnest.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, using a volumizing mousse or hairspray if needed. Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly or creating a slicked-back look, as this can reveal thinning areas.

Tips for Hiding Scalp Thinning Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you hide scalp thinning hair:

1. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Try to avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant spray.

2. Protect Your Scalp

Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scalp sunscreen. Sunburned or damaged skin can lead to hair loss or thinning.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote hair growth and thickness. Focus on eating plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats.

4. Talk to Your Doctor

If your scalp thinning hair is caused by a medical condition, talk to your doctor about treatment options. They may be able to prescribe medication or recommend other treatments to help.

FAQ About Hiding Scalp Thinning Hair

1. Can hair fibers damage my hair?

No, hair fibers are safe to use and won't damage your hair. They simply cling to your existing hair to create the illusion of thickness.

2. How long do hair fibers last?

Hair fibers can last all day and can be removed easily with shampoo and water.

3. Will a wig look natural?

A quality wig can look very natural if it is chosen and styled correctly. Look for a wig that matches your hair color and texture and have it customized to fit your head.

Hair Care Tips for Scalp Thinning Hair

To keep your hair healthy and prevent further thinning, follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water and harsh products that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Brush Your Hair Gently

Brush your hair gently using a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristled brush. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and thinning.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing your hair in tight hairstyles like braids or ponytails, as this can cause tension on your hair and lead to thinning.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently hide scalp thinning hair and feel great about your appearance. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
