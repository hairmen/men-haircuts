Should You Cut Hair Away From Dogs Eyes

Description

Dogs are adorable creatures, and they need proper grooming to stay healthy and happy. Grooming includes not only bathing but also trimming hair. One of the most common questions pet owners ask is, should you cut hair away from dogs' eyes?

The answer is not straightforward. It depends on the breed, the length of the hair, and the dog's personal preference. However, there are some general guidelines that you can follow to determine whether your dog needs a hair trim around the eyes.

How to

If you are unsure whether your dog needs a hair trim around the eyes or not, you can check the following:

  • Observe if the dog's hair is obstructing its vision.
  • Check if the dog is frequently rubbing its eyes or pawing at them.
  • Observe if there is any discharge or redness in the eyes.

Step by step

If you have decided to trim your dog's hair around the eyes, here are the steps you can follow:

  1. Choose a quiet and well-lit area to groom your dog.
  2. Make sure your dog is relaxed, and use treats to keep them calm and happy.
  3. Use a pair of scissors or clippers, depending on your preference and your dog's coat.
  4. Gently lift the hair away from the eyes and trim it carefully. Be careful not to cut too close to the eyes, as this can cause injury.
  5. Repeat the process on the other eye.
  6. Use a soft brush to remove any loose hair around the eyes.
  7. Give your dog a treat and cuddle to show them how much you appreciate their cooperation.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trimming your dog's hair around the eyes:

  • Use sharp scissors or clippers to avoid pulling or tugging on the hair.
  • Be patient and take breaks if your dog becomes restless or uncomfortable.
  • Trim the hair in small sections to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use treats and praise to reward your dog for good behavior.

FAQ

Can I trim my dog's hair around the eyes myself?

Yes, you can trim your dog's hair around the eyes yourself, but it is recommended to seek professional advice if you are unsure or if your dog has a complicated coat.

How often should I trim my dog's hair around the eyes?

It depends on the breed and the length of the hair. Some dogs need more frequent trimming than others. It is recommended to trim the hair when you notice it obstructing the dog's vision or causing discomfort.

Can I use human hair clippers on my dog?

No, it is not recommended to use human hair clippers on your dog as they are not designed for pet grooming and can cause injury.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for a healthy and happy dog. Here are some tips to keep your dog's hair healthy:

  • Brush your dog's hair regularly to remove tangles and mats.
  • Use a mild shampoo designed for dogs when bathing them.
  • Dry your dog's hair thoroughly after bathing to avoid skin infections.
  • Trim your dog's hair regularly to avoid matting and tangling.
  • Visit a professional groomer if you are unsure about how to groom your dog's hair.

In conclusion, should you cut hair away from dogs' eyes? It depends on your dog's breed, hair length, and personal preference. However, if you notice your dog's hair obstructing their vision or causing discomfort, it is recommended to trim it around the eyes. Remember to follow the proper grooming techniques and seek professional advice if you are unsure.
