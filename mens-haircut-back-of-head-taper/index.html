Mens Haircut Back Of Head Taper A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a back of head taper might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a popular choice among men because it looks great and doesn't require too much styling. In this guide, we'll show you how to get a back of head taper, step by step. We'll also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

A back of head taper is a short haircut that gradually gets shorter towards the back of the head. This haircut is characterized by its faded sides and back, which are blended into longer hair on top. The taper can be subtle or more dramatic, depending on your preference. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice among men of all ages.

How to Get a Back of Head Taper

Getting a back of head taper is easy, and you can do it yourself with some practice. Here's how:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

To get a back of head taper, you'll need a good pair of clippers. Look for clippers with adjustable guards so you can adjust the length of the cut easily. You'll also need a pair of scissors for trimming longer hair on top.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Next, decide on the length you want for your hair. For a classic back of head taper, start with a longer length on top and gradually fade the sides and back. You can also choose a more dramatic taper if you prefer.

Step 3: Start with the Sides and Back

Begin by using the clippers with a longer guard to trim the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, gradually decreasing the length as you go. Use a comb to ensure a smooth, even cut.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back, it's time to blend the top. Use scissors to trim any longer hair on top, and blend it into the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use your fingers to check for any uneven spots.

Step 5: Refine the Look

Finally, refine the look by checking for any stray hairs and trimming them with scissors. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any adjustments as needed.

Tips for a Perfect Back of Head Taper

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect back of head taper:

  • Use a comb to ensure an even cut
  • Start with a longer length and gradually fade the sides and back
  • Blend the top with scissors for a more natural look
  • Check for any stray hairs and trim them with scissors
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about back of head tapers:

How often should I get a back of head taper?

You should get a back of head taper every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Can I style a back of head taper?

Yes, a back of head taper can be styled in many different ways, depending on your preference. You can use gel, pomade, or wax to achieve different looks.

Is a back of head taper suitable for all hair types?

A back of head taper is suitable for most hair types, but it may not be suitable for very curly or thick hair. If you have very curly or thick hair, ask your stylist for advice on the best haircut for you.

Hair Care for a Back of Head Taper

Here are some tips to keep your back of head taper looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a hairdryer to style your hair, but don't use too much heat
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the look

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to achieve the perfect back of head taper. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles until you find the perfect look for you.
