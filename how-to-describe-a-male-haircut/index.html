How To Describe A Male Haircut In Relaxed English Language

Written by Frank Aug 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to describe your desired haircut to your barber? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men find it challenging to communicate their preferred hairstyle to their hairstylist or barber. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to describe a male haircut in relaxed English language, so you can confidently achieve the look you want.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

Before heading to the barber, it's essential to have a clear idea of what you want. You can research hairstyles online or on social media platforms like Instagram and Pinterest for inspiration. When describing your desired haircut, consider the length, texture, and shape of your hair. You can also mention specific details such as the type of fade, the length of the fringe, and the shape of the neckline.

How to

When describing your desired haircut, use simple and straightforward language. Avoid using technical terms that your barber might not understand. You can use visual aids such as pictures or videos to help you communicate your preferred hairstyle. Here are some phrases you can use:

For length:

  • I want my hair to be short/medium/long.
  • I want a buzz cut/crop cut/crew cut.
  • I want a high and tight.

For texture:

  • I want my hair to be straight/curly/wavy.
  • I want a textured/messy look.
  • I want a slicked-back style.

For shape:

  • I want a taper fade.
  • I want a skin fade.
  • I want a drop fade.
  • I want a hard part.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to describe your desired haircut to your barber:

  1. Research hairstyles online or on social media platforms for inspiration.
  2. Consider the length, texture, and shape of your hair.
  3. Use simple and straightforward language to describe your preferred hairstyle.
  4. Show visual aids such as pictures or videos to help you communicate your desired haircut.
  5. Be open to your barber's suggestions and recommendations.
  6. Confirm with your barber that you're both on the same page before starting the haircut.
  7. Communicate any changes or adjustments you want during the haircut.
  8. Ask your barber for styling tips and product recommendations.
  9. Thank your barber for their service and leave a generous tip.
  10. Enjoy your new haircut!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you describe your desired haircut:

  • Bring multiple pictures or videos to show your barber different angles and styles.
  • Be realistic about what will suit your face shape and hair type.
  • Ask your barber for their opinion on what will look best on you.
  • Be specific about the length and style of your fringe.
  • Consider the maintenance and styling requirements of your preferred hairstyle.

FAQ

What if I don't know what hairstyle I want?

If you're unsure about what hairstyle you want, ask your barber for their suggestions and recommendations. They're the experts and can provide you with advice based on your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

What if my barber doesn't understand my description?

If your barber doesn't understand your description, try using different words or phrases to explain your preferred hairstyle. You can also show them visual aids such as pictures or videos to help you communicate your desired haircut.

Hair Care

After getting your new haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a hair styling product that's appropriate for your desired hairstyle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, describing a male haircut in relaxed English language is all about using simple and straightforward language, being specific, and using visual aids to help you communicate your desired hairstyle. By following our step-by-step guide and additional tips, you can confidently achieve the look you want and maintain it with proper hair care.
