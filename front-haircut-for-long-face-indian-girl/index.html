Front Haircut for Long Face Indian Girl: How to, Tips, and Hair Care

Are you an Indian girl with a long face and wondering what haircut suits you best? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through everything you need to know about the front haircut for long face Indian girl, including how to get it, tips to maintain it, and hair care routines to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Front Haircut for Long Face Indian Girl
  • Tips to Maintain Front Haircut for Long Face Indian Girl
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Long Face Indian Girl

How to Get Front Haircut for Long Face Indian Girl

The front haircut for long face Indian girl is a style that frames the face and adds volume to the hair. Here's how to get it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers that start from your cheekbones and go down to the ends. This will create volume and movement in your hair.
  3. To frame your face, ask your hairstylist to add a few face-framing layers that start from your chin and go down to the ends. This will soften your features and make your face appear less elongated.
  4. Once the haircut is done, style your hair by blow-drying it with a round brush. This will add even more volume and shape to your hair.

Remember to communicate clearly with your hairstylist and show them pictures of the front haircut for long face Indian girl that you want. This will help them understand your vision better and give you the best results.

Tips to Maintain Front Haircut for Long Face Indian Girl

Now that you have got the perfect front haircut for long face Indian girl, here are some tips to maintain it:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking full and bouncy.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair and make it appear dull and lifeless.

FAQs

What other haircuts suit a long face Indian girl?

Other haircuts that suit a long face Indian girl include the long bob, side-swept bangs, and layered haircuts. These styles add volume, shape, and movement to the hair, and also soften the features.

Can I color my hair after getting a front haircut for long face Indian girl?

Yes, you can! Coloring your hair can add dimension and depth to your locks, but make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day, but if you have dry hair, you can wash it every two to three days. Also, if you exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair more often to remove sweat and dirt.

Hair Care Routine for Long Face Indian Girl

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care routines:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip away its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using tight hairstyles that can strain your hair and cause hair loss.

With these tips and routines, you can maintain your front haircut for long face Indian girl and keep your locks healthy and beautiful.
