How To Manage Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wavy frizzy thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look gorgeous when styled properly, it can also be a nightmare to manage on a daily basis. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on how to manage wavy frizzy thick hair. From how to style it to how to care for it, we've got you covered.

Description

Wavy frizzy thick hair is hair that has a natural wave pattern, is thick in texture, and tends to frizz easily. It can be difficult to manage because it requires a lot of moisture and can become unruly if not styled properly.

How to Manage Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

The key to managing wavy frizzy thick hair is to keep it moisturized and styled properly. Here are some tips on how to manage your hair:

Use the Right Products

When it comes to managing wavy frizzy thick hair, using the right products is key. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy or curly hair, as they will contain ingredients that help to define and tame your waves, while also providing moisture to combat frizz.

Embrace Your Natural Texture

Rather than fighting your natural texture, embrace it. Work with your waves and use products that enhance them, rather than trying to straighten or smooth them out.

Use a Diffuser

When blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your waves and reduce frizz.

Avoid Over-Washing

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than two to three times a week, and use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can be damaging to wavy frizzy thick hair, so try to avoid it as much as possible. If you do need to use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting on low.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to manage wavy frizzy thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancing cream to your hair while it's still damp.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose waves.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Managing Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing wavy frizzy thick hair:

  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help hydrate your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

How often should I wash my wavy frizzy thick hair?

It's best to wash your hair no more than two to three times a week, as over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and frizz.

Can I use a straightening iron on my wavy frizzy thick hair?

While you can use a straightening iron on your hair, it's best to avoid heat styling as much as possible, as it can be damaging to wavy frizzy thick hair. If you do need to use a straightening iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting on low.

Hair Care for Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

In addition to styling your hair properly, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips on how to care for your wavy frizzy thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can lead to dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can manage your wavy frizzy thick hair and keep it looking its best.
