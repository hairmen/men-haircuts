How to Style Curls for Guys

When it comes to styling, there are a few different options depending on the length and texture of your hair. Here are some popular styles for curly-haired guys:

Short Curls

If you have short hair, you can still embrace your curls by keeping them cropped close to your head. A textured crop or buzz cut with a fade can look great on curly hair. You can also use a small amount of pomade or wax to define your curls and add some texture.

Medium-Length Curls

For medium-length curls, you have a few more styling options. You can try a messy, tousled look by using a sea salt spray and scrunching your hair with your hands. You can also slick back your curls with a styling cream or gel for a more polished look.
