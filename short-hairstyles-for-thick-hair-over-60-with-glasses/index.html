Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 60 With Glasses

If you're over 60 and have thick hair, you might think that there are limited options when it comes to hairstyles. However, this couldn't be further from the truth. With the right cut and style, you can look and feel your best. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for thick hair over 60 with glasses, along with tips on hair care and maintenance.

Description

Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 60, as they can be easy to maintain and can help you look younger. If you have thick hair, there are plenty of styles that can work for you. The key is to find a cut that flatters your face shape and works with your glasses. Some of the best short hairstyles for thick hair over 60 with glasses include the pixie cut, the bob, and the layered cut.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair over 60 with glasses, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a layered cut can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a bob with side-swept bangs can soften your features. Once you've decided on a cut, it's important to communicate with your stylist and bring pictures of styles you like. This will help your stylist to understand your vision and create a look that works for you.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best short hairstyles for thick hair over 60 with glasses:

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and clip it away.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Unclip the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length, leaving some length on top for texture.
  5. Style with a texturizing spray or pomade.

Bob

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and clip it away.
  3. Using scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Unclip the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length, leaving some length on top for texture.
  5. Add side-swept bangs if desired.
  6. Style with a volumizing mousse or hairspray.

Layered Cut

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and clip it away.
  3. Using scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Unclip the top section of your hair and cut it into layers, starting at the crown and working your way down.
  5. Add side-swept bangs if desired.
  6. Style with a volumizing mousse or hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair over 60 with glasses:

  • Consider your face shape when choosing a cut.
  • Bring pictures of styles you like to your stylist.
  • Invest in a good pair of glasses that complement your hairstyle.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

FAQ

What should I consider when choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair over 60 with glasses?

When choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair over 60 with glasses, it's important to consider your face shape, bring pictures of styles you like to your stylist, and invest in a good pair of glasses that complement your hairstyle.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for short hair, it's important to keep it clean and well-maintained. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Invest in a good texturizing spray or pomade to help add volume and texture to your hair. Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance, and consider using heat protectant products when styling your hair with hot tools.

With these tips and styles in mind, you can rock a fabulous short hairstyle with confidence and style.
