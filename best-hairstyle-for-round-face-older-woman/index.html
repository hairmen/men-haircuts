Description

As we age, our face shape changes, and for women with round faces, choosing the right hairstyle can be quite challenging. A round face is characterized by having a circular shape, full cheeks, and a rounded jawline. The key to choosing the best hairstyle for round face older woman is to create the illusion of length and height to elongate the face. In this blog post, we will discuss the best hairstyles that flatter a round face shape and make you look younger and more stylish.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Face Older Woman

When choosing the best hairstyle for round face older woman, it is essential to consider the following factors:

Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, a round face has a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The goal is to elongate the face by creating the illusion of length and height. Choose hairstyles that have layers and angles to add height to the crown and length to the face.

Hair Texture

Choose a hairstyle that complements your hair texture. If you have thin hair, go for shorter hairstyles that add volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can opt for longer hairstyles that help to balance out the volume.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, go for low-maintenance hairstyles that require minimal styling. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you can opt for more elaborate hairstyles that require more attention.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the best hairstyle for round face older woman:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of the mirror and observe the shape of your face. If you have a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded jawline, you have a round face.

Step 2: Look for Inspiration

Look for hairstyle inspiration that flatters a round face shape. You can search for images online or look for hairstyle magazines. Take note of the hairstyle that catches your eye and bring it to your hairstylist for consultation.

Step 3: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle best suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Your hairstylist can recommend hairstyles that flatter your face shape and make you look younger and more stylish.

Step 4: Maintain Your Hairstyle

Maintain your hairstyle by following the tips provided by your hairstylist. Use the right hair care products and tools to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

Use the Right Hair Care Products

Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Choose products that are designed to add volume, texture, and shine to your hair.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause heat damage to your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style. Regular trims help to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face older woman?

The best hairstyle for a round face older woman is one that adds height to the crown and length to the face. Choose hairstyles that have layers and angles to create the illusion of length and height.

What hair texture is best for a round face older woman?

The best hair texture for a round face older woman depends on the individual. If you have thin hair, go for shorter hairstyles that add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you can opt for longer hairstyles that help to balance out the volume.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a deep conditioning treatment that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

Avoid Overstyling Your Hair

Avoid overstyling your hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Use heat styling tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

Choosing the best hairstyle for round face older woman can be challenging, but with the right guidance, you can find a style that flatters your face shape and makes you look younger and more stylish. Consult with your hairstylist and follow the tips provided to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
