Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs Heart Shaped Face The Perfect Look For 2023

Written by Luffy Sep 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your heart-shaped face? Look no further than shoulder length hair with bangs! This classic look is both stylish and practical, making it the perfect choice for women on the go. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve this look step-by-step, provide tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs
  2. Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle
  3. Frequently Asked Questions
  4. Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs

The first step in achieving this look is to visit your stylist. They will help you determine the right length for your face shape and assess the condition of your hair. Once you've decided on the length, your stylist will begin by cutting your hair to shoulder length. Next, they will add layers to give your hair volume and texture. Finally, they will create bangs that frame your face.

When it comes to bangs, there are several different styles to choose from. For a heart-shaped face, we recommend side-swept bangs that are longer on one side. This will help balance out your features and draw attention to your eyes.

Once your new hairstyle is complete, your stylist may suggest adding some color or highlights to enhance the look. This is a great way to add some dimension and depth to your hair.

Step-by-Step:

  1. Visit your stylist and determine the right length for your face shape.
  2. Cut your hair to shoulder length.
  3. Add layers for volume and texture.
  4. Create side-swept bangs that are longer on one side.
  5. Consider adding color or highlights to enhance the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Now that you have your new shoulder length hair with bangs, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you must use a hair dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Consider investing in some styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to enhance the look of your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will shoulder length hair with bangs work for all face shapes?

A: While this style works well for heart-shaped faces, it can also be flattering for other face shapes, such as oval or square. We recommend consulting with your stylist to determine the best length and bangs for your face shape.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

A: Absolutely! This style looks great both up and down. You can try a messy bun or a half-up half-down style to show off your bangs.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep your shoulder length hair with bangs looking great:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash it every 2-3 days instead.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and detangle your hair gently.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your new shoulder length hair with bangs all year long. Whether you're running errands or attending a fancy event, this look is both stylish and practical. So go ahead and book that appointment with your stylist – it's time for a new you!
