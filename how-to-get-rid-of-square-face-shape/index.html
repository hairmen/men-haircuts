How To Get Rid Of Square Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Are you struggling with a square-shaped face and trying to figure out how to soften your features? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people have square faces and want to learn how to make their faces look more oval or round. In this article, we'll discuss how to get rid of square face shape with tips and tricks that you can try at home.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Rid of Square Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs

How to Get Rid of Square Face Shape

The key to getting rid of a square face shape is to create the illusion of a more oval or round face. There are several ways to do this:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight certain areas. By contouring your face, you can create the illusion of a more oval or round face shape.

Start by applying a darker shade of foundation or powder to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline. Blend it out well to create a shadow effect. Then, apply a lighter shade to the center of your forehead, nose, and chin to highlight those areas. Blend it out well as well.

2. Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also make a big difference in how your face looks. If you have a square face shape, try to avoid hairstyles that are too angular or too short. Instead, go for hairstyles that are layered and longer.

Some hairstyle ideas for square faces include:

  • Long, layered waves
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Loose curls
  • A long bob

3. Accessories

Accessories can also help soften your features. Try wearing earrings that are round or oval-shaped instead of angular. You can also wear scarves or necklaces that create a V shape to draw attention away from your jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of square face shape:

  1. Start by contouring your face with makeup.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that is layered and longer.
  3. Avoid hairstyles that are too angular or too short.
  4. Wear accessories that are round or oval-shaped.
  5. Try wearing scarves or necklaces that create a V shape.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to get rid of square face shape:

  • Remember that everyone's face is unique, and there is no one-size-fits-all solution.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and makeup techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Be patient and don't expect immediate results.
  • Try to embrace your features instead of trying to change them completely.

FAQs

1. Can I get rid of a square face shape permanently?

No, you cannot permanently get rid of a square face shape. However, you can use makeup techniques and hairstyles to create the illusion of a more oval or round face shape.

2. Do I have to wear makeup to get rid of square face shape?

No, you don't have to wear makeup to get rid of square face shape. However, contouring can be a helpful technique to create the illusion of a more oval or round face shape.

3. Will changing my hairstyle really make a difference?

Yes, changing your hairstyle can make a big difference in how your face looks. Try to avoid hairstyles that are too angular or too short and go for hairstyles that are layered and longer.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces

If you have a square face shape, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too angular or too short.
  • Go for hairstyles that are layered and longer.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to see what works best for you.
  • Avoid wearing your hair too tight, as this can accentuate your jawline.

By following these tips and tricks, you can learn how to get rid of square face shape and create the illusion of a more oval or round face. Remember to embrace your unique features and have fun experimenting with different makeup techniques and hairstyles!
