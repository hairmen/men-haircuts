Which Is The Best Hair Fall Control Shampoo India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of seeing strands of hair on your pillow or in the shower drain? Hair fall can be a frustrating problem, but it's not uncommon. In fact, according to a survey conducted by the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery, around 60% of men and 50% of women experience hair loss at some point in their lives. But don't worry, there are plenty of hair fall control shampoos available in India. In this article, we'll help you choose the best one for your needs.

What Causes Hair Fall?

Before we dive into the best hair fall control shampoo India, let's first understand what causes hair fall. There are several factors that contribute to hair loss, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Poor nutrition
  • Environmental factors

Now that we know what causes hair fall, let's look at how hair fall control shampoos can help.

How Do Hair Fall Control Shampoos Work?

Hair fall control shampoos typically contain ingredients that nourish the scalp and hair, strengthen the hair follicles, and promote hair growth. Some common ingredients found in hair fall control shampoos include:

  • Biotin
  • Niacin
  • Caffeine
  • Keratin
  • Bhringraj
  • Amla
  • Reetha
  • Shikakai

These ingredients work together to improve blood circulation to the scalp, reduce inflammation, and nourish the hair follicles. As a result, they can help reduce hair fall and promote hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Choose the Best Hair Fall Control Shampoo India

Now that we know how hair fall control shampoos work, let's look at how to choose the best one for your needs.

Step 1: Identify Your Hair Type

The first step in choosing a hair fall control shampoo is to identify your hair type. Is your hair oily, dry, or normal? Do you have curly, straight, or wavy hair? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a shampoo that's tailored to your specific needs.

Step 2: Look for Specific Ingredients

Once you've identified your hair type, the next step is to look for specific ingredients that are beneficial for your hair. For example, if you have dry hair, look for a shampoo that contains moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter. If you have oily hair, look for a shampoo that contains ingredients like tea tree oil or salicylic acid that help control oil production.

Step 3: Check the pH Level

The pH level of a shampoo is important because it can affect the health of your scalp and hair. Ideally, you want to choose a shampoo with a pH level that's close to the natural pH level of your scalp, which is around 5.5. Shampoos with a pH level that's too high or too low can cause dryness, irritation, and damage to the hair.

Step 4: Read Reviews

Before purchasing a hair fall control shampoo, it's important to read reviews from other customers. This can give you an idea of how effective the shampoo is and whether it's worth the investment.

Tips for Using Hair Fall Control Shampoos

Now that you've chosen the best hair fall control shampoo India, here are some tips for using it effectively:

  • Use the shampoo as directed on the label
  • Massage the shampoo into your scalp for a few minutes to promote blood circulation
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water
  • Avoid using hot water, which can damage the hair
  • Use the shampoo regularly for best results

FAQs About Hair Fall Control Shampoos

Q: Can hair fall control shampoos cause hair growth?

A: Hair fall control shampoos can help promote hair growth by nourishing the hair follicles and improving blood circulation to the scalp. However, they cannot regrow hair that has already been lost.

Q: Can hair fall control shampoos be used on colored hair?

A: Yes, many hair fall control shampoos are safe to use on colored hair. However, it's always a good idea to check the label and make sure the shampoo is suitable for colored hair.

Q: Can hair fall control shampoos cause hair damage?

A: Hair fall control shampoos are generally safe to use and should not cause hair damage if used as directed. However, if you experience any irritation or discomfort, stop using the shampoo immediately and consult a dermatologist.

Hair Care Tips to Reduce Hair Fall

Using a hair fall control shampoo is just one step towards reducing hair fall. Here are some additional hair care tips that can help:

  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers and straighteners
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Conclusion

Hair fall can be a frustrating problem, but using a hair fall control shampoo can help reduce hair fall and promote hair growth. When choosing a hair fall control shampoo India, it's important to identify your hair type, look for specific ingredients, check the pH level, and read reviews. Remember to use the shampoo as directed and follow good hair care practices to reduce hair fall and keep your hair healthy.
