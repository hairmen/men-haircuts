How To Create Fake Curtain Bangs

Written by William Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh look? Well, you might want to try the trendy curtain bangs. Curtain bangs are face-framing fringe that looks flattering on any face shape. However, not everyone is willing to commit to a new hairstyle. If you're hesitant to cut your hair and want to try fake curtain bangs, then this guide is perfect for you. In this article, we'll show you how to create fake curtain bangs that look natural and stylish.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Create Fake Curtain Bangs
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Creating Fake Curtain Bangs

How to Create Fake Curtain Bangs

Fake curtain bangs can be achieved with the help of hair extensions or by using your own hair. This technique is perfect for those who want to try a new style without the commitment of a haircut. Here are some things you need to know before creating fake curtain bangs:

  • You need to have long hair that can be parted in the middle.
  • You need to have hair clips to hold your hair in place.
  • You need to have a comb and a hair straightener.
  • You need to have hair extensions (optional).

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create fake curtain bangs:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle using a comb. Make sure the parting is straight.
  2. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face using hair clips.
  3. If you have hair extensions, clip them under your hair, following the parting of your hair.
  4. Take a section of hair from the front, and straighten it using a hair straightener.
  5. Curve the straightened hair towards your face, and clip it using a hair clip.
  6. Repeat this step on the other side of your face.
  7. Release the rest of your hair from the clips.
  8. Curl the ends of your hair using a curling iron.
  9. Use a comb to blend the fake curtain bangs with the rest of your hair.
  10. Finish off with some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Creating Fake Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to make your fake curtain bangs look natural:

  • Choose hair extensions that match your hair color and texture.
  • Make sure the parting is straight and even.
  • Straighten your hair before creating the fake curtain bangs to make them look sleek.
  • Curl the ends of your hair to blend with the fake curtain bangs.
  • Use a comb to blend the fake curtain bangs with the rest of your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about fake curtain bangs:

  • Can I create fake curtain bangs without hair extensions?

    • Yes, you can create fake curtain bangs without hair extensions. Follow the same steps, but instead of using hair extensions, use your own hair.

  • Can I wear fake curtain bangs with any hairstyle?

    • Yes, you can wear fake curtain bangs with any hairstyle. They look great with a ponytail, bun, or any other updo.

  • How long do fake curtain bangs last?

    • Fake curtain bangs can last all day if you use hairspray to hold them in place.

Hair Care for Fake Curtain Bangs

To keep your fake curtain bangs looking fresh, here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid touching your fake curtain bangs too much to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and fresh.
  • Use a hairbrush to detangle your hair, but avoid brushing the fake curtain bangs too much to prevent them from losing their shape.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to create fake curtain bangs, you can easily change up your hairstyle without the commitment of a haircut. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a trendy and stylish look.
