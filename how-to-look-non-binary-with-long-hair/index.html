How To Look Non Binary With Long Hair

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 ¬∑ 3 min read

Long hair is often associated with femininity, but people of all genders can rock long locks. If you're non binary and want to embrace your long hair without feeling like you're conforming to gender norms, there are plenty of ways to style it to reflect your true self.

How to

Here are some steps you can take to look non binary with long hair:

Step 1: Consider Your Haircut

The first step to looking non binary with long hair is to make sure your haircut is gender-neutral. This means avoiding styles that are typically associated with one gender or the other. A layered cut, for example, can work well for people of all genders.

Step 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Once you have a gender-neutral haircut, you can start experimenting with different styles. A messy bun, a half-up half-down style, or a sleek ponytail can all be styled in a way that feels non binary. Try out different looks and see what makes you feel most comfortable.

Step 3: Play with Accessories

Accessories can be a great way to add some non binary flair to your long hair. Consider adding a hat, a headband, or a scarf to your look. You can also experiment with hair clips, pins, and barrettes to create unique styles.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you look non binary with long hair:

  • Experiment with color. Adding highlights or dyeing your hair can help you create a look that feels uniquely you.
  • Embrace texture. Curls, waves, and other textures can help you create a look that feels non binary.
  • Don't be afraid to add some edge. Shaved sides or an undercut can help you create a look that feels rebellious and non binary.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about looking non binary with long hair:

Can I still wear makeup if I want to look non binary with long hair?

Absolutely! Wearing makeup doesn't have to conform to gender norms. You can experiment with makeup in a way that feels authentic to you.

Do I have to cut my hair short to look non binary?

No. While some non binary people choose to cut their hair short, others prefer to keep it long. The key is to find a style that feels true to you.

What if my workplace has a dress code that doesn't allow for non binary styles?

It can be tough to navigate workplace dress codes, but there are still ways to express your non binary identity through your hair. You can experiment with different hairstyles or accessories that feel authentic to you while still adhering to the dress code.

Hair Care

Caring for long hair is important to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for keeping your long hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush designed for detangling to prevent breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can look non binary with long hair in a way that feels true to you. Remember to experiment with different styles and accessories to find a look that reflects your unique identity.
