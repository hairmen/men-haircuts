Does Fringe Make You Look Younger

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Content:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many women wonder if getting a fringe can make them look younger. The answer is yes! A fringe, also known as bangs, can be a great way to create a youthful and fresh look. Fringes can be cut in different lengths and styles to suit different face shapes and hair textures. They can also help to hide wrinkles and fine lines on the forehead.

How to

If you're considering getting a fringe to look younger, here's how to do it:

  1. Look for inspiration: Browse magazines and social media to find a fringe style that you like.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist: Your hairstylist can help you choose the right fringe style for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Maintain your fringe: Keep your fringe trimmed regularly to prevent it from growing too long and covering your eyes.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow when getting a fringe:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the hair that you want to cut into a fringe.
  3. Decide on the length of your fringe and mark it with a comb or your fingers.
  4. Cut the hair to the desired length, starting from the middle and working your way outwards.
  5. Check the length and trim if necessary.
  6. Style your fringe using a round brush and a blow dryer.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a fringe:

  • Choose a fringe style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe if you're not ready for a full fringe.
  • Ask your hairstylist to show you how to style your fringe at home.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying your fringe.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fringes:

Q: Will a fringe suit my face shape?

A: Yes, a fringe can suit any face shape. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right fringe style for your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a fringe?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a fringe. You may need to use bobby pins to keep your fringe in place.

Q: How often do I need to trim my fringe?

A: You should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your fringe looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your fringe too often.
  • Use a hair serum to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.
  • Avoid touching your fringe too much, as this can transfer oils from your fingers to your hair.

Overall, getting a fringe can be a great way to look younger and fresher. Just make sure to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture, and to maintain your fringe regularly.
