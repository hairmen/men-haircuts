What Is The Best Face Shape For Bangs

Written by Christine Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but unsure if they will suit your face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing what the best face shape is for bangs and providing some tips and tricks to make sure you get the perfect cut for your face.

Description

Bangs have been a popular hairstyle for many years and can instantly transform your look. However, not all face shapes suit bangs. It's important to consider your face shape before getting bangs to ensure that they enhance your features rather than detract from them.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what face shapes suit bangs, let's first determine how to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to pull your hair back and examine the outline of your face in the mirror. You can also measure the length and width of your face and compare it to the measurements of each face shape.

What is the Best Face Shape for Bangs?

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's discuss which shapes suit bangs best.

Oval Face Shape

The oval face shape is the most versatile and suits almost all types of bangs. You can experiment with different lengths and styles, such as side-swept or blunt bangs.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, opt for long, side-swept bangs that angle towards the corners of your eyes. This will create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, go for soft, wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. This will soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs that will accentuate the squareness of your face.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other. This will balance out the width of your forehead and create a more harmonious look. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that will make your forehead appear wider.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, go for choppy or textured bangs that are shorter in the center and longer on the sides. This will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones and create a more balanced look. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that will make your face appear wider.

Rectangle Face Shape

If you have a rectangle face shape, opt for soft, wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. This will soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs that will accentuate the length of your face.

Tips for Getting Bangs

Before getting bangs, there are some tips and tricks you should keep in mind to ensure you get the perfect cut.

  • Communicate clearly with your stylist about the type of bangs you want and your face shape.
  • Bring pictures of the type of bangs you like to show your stylist.
  • Consider the maintenance required for your chosen style.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Can anyone pull off bangs?

While there is no hard and fast rule, certain face shapes suit bangs better than others. It's important to consider your face shape before getting bangs to ensure they enhance your features.

Do bangs require a lot of maintenance?

It depends on the type of bangs you get. Blunt bangs will require more maintenance as they need to be trimmed regularly. Side-swept or wispy bangs require less maintenance but may need to be styled daily.

Hair Care for Bangs

Once you've got your bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best.

  • Wash your bangs every two to three days.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs in between washes.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs as this can weigh them down.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect bangs for your face shape. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look!
