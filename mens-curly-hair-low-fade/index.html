Mens Curly Hair Low Fade A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with curly hair, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. One popular option is the mens curly hair low fade, a style that combines the texture of curly hair with a sleek, modern fade. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve this look, as well as provide some tips for maintaining your curly hair.

Description

The mens curly hair low fade is a style that has become increasingly popular in recent years. It involves keeping the length of the hair on top while gradually tapering the sides and back down to the skin. This creates a clean, polished look that contrasts nicely with the texture of curly hair.

How to Achieve the Look

The key to achieving the mens curly hair low fade is finding a skilled barber who understands how to work with curly hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal style and the texture of your hair. If you have looser curls, you may want to go with a longer length, while tighter curls may benefit from a shorter cut.

Step 2: Gradually Taper the Sides and Back

The next step is to gradually taper the sides and back down to the skin. Your barber will use clippers or scissors to gradually reduce the length of your hair, creating a fade effect. The key is to blend the shorter hair on the sides and back with the longer hair on top.

Step 3: Define the Shape

Once the fade is complete, your barber will use scissors to define the shape of your hair on top. This may involve adding layers or texturizing the hair to enhance the natural curl pattern.

Step 4: Style as Desired

Finally, you can style your hair as desired. For a natural look, use a curl-enhancing product and let your hair air dry. For a more polished look, use a hair dryer and a round brush to smooth out the curls.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Curly Hair Low Fade

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens curly hair low fade:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Curly hair requires moisture to stay healthy, so use products that are designed for curly hair. Look for products that contain natural oils and avoid products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so try to limit washing to once or twice a week. On days when you don't wash your hair, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair at Night

Wear a silk or satin cap or use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the mens curly hair low fade with long hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the mens curly hair low fade with long hair. However, keep in mind that the longer your hair is, the more difficult it may be to maintain the shape of the fade.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the fade and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Maintaining mens curly hair low fade requires a bit of effort, but it's worth it for the polished, modern look it provides. By following these tips and working with a skilled barber, you can achieve the perfect look for your curly hair.
