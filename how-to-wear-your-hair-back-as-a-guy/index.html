How To Wear Your Hair Back As A Guy A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling in your face all the time? Do you want to try a new hairstyle that's both stylish and practical? Wearing your hair back as a guy is a great solution. It's easy to do and can work for any hair type or length. In this article, we'll show you how to wear your hair back as a guy step by step, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

How to Wear Your Hair Back as a Guy

There are several ways to wear your hair back as a guy, depending on your preference and hair type. Here are some popular options:

Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic and easy way to wear your hair back. Simply gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it with an elastic band. You can position the ponytail high or low, depending on your preference. This works well for medium to long hair.

Bun

A bun is a more polished version of a ponytail. Instead of tying your hair with an elastic band, twist it into a coil and secure it with bobby pins. You can position the bun at the top or back of your head. This works well for medium to long hair.

Half-Up

A half-up hairstyle is a good option if you want to keep some of your hair down. Simply gather the top half of your hair at the back of your head and tie it with an elastic band. You can leave the rest of your hair loose or style it as desired. This works well for medium to long hair.

Headband

A headband is a simple accessory that can help keep your hair back. Choose a headband that fits comfortably and doesn't slide off your head. You can wear it with your hair down or up in a ponytail or bun. This works well for short to medium hair.

Clip or Bobby Pin

A clip or bobby pin is a quick and easy way to secure your hair back. Simply twist or gather a section of your hair and clip it back. You can use one or multiple clips depending on your hair type and length. This works well for short to medium hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear your hair back as a guy:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  2. Decide on the hairstyle you want to try, whether it's a ponytail, bun, half-up, headband, or clip.
  3. Gather your hair at the back of your head or the crown of your head, depending on the hairstyle.
  4. Secure your hair with an elastic band if you're doing a ponytail or half-up hairstyle. Twist your hair into a coil if you're doing a bun.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  6. If you're using a headband, position it on your head and adjust it as needed.
  7. Style the rest of your hair as desired. You can leave it loose, slick it back with gel or pomade, or add texture with a salt spray.
  8. Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you wear your hair back as a guy:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair products like gel, pomade, or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Invest in good quality hair accessories like elastic bands, bobby pins, and headbands.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's easy to style and maintain.
  • Don't tie your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

FAQs

Can I wear my hair back if I have short hair?

Yes, you can. Use a clip or bobby pin to secure your hair back, or try a headband if you prefer.

How do I prevent my hair from falling out of a ponytail or bun?

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands, and tie your hair tightly with an elastic band. You can also use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Can I wear a headband with any hairstyle?

Yes, you can. A headband can be worn with your hair down, in a ponytail or bun, or in a half-up hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily wear your hair back as a guy and look stylish and practical at the same time. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
