How To Cut Male Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 27, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair with clippers? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll break down the steps to cutting male hair with clippers, provide tips for achieving your desired look, and answer frequently asked questions about hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Male Hair with Clippers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving Your Desired Look
  • FAQs About Hair Care

How to Cut Male Hair with Clippers

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to gather the necessary tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Clipper oil

Once you have these tools, you’re ready to get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for a successful at-home haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.
  2. Attach the desired clipper guard to your clippers. A shorter guard will leave less hair, while a longer guard will leave more.
  3. Begin by cutting the sides and back of the head. Use the clipper to cut against the direction of hair growth.
  4. Next, move to the top of the head. You can either use the same guard as before or switch to a longer/shorter one for a different length.
  5. Blend the sides and top together by using a higher guard to create a fade. Gradually lower the guard as you move towards the top of the head.
  6. Use scissors to trim any longer pieces that the clippers may have missed.
  7. Trim sideburns and the hairline around the ears with a smaller guard or no guard at all.
  8. Clean up the neckline with a trimmer or razor.
  9. Apply clipper oil to the blades to keep them running smoothly.
  10. Style hair as desired.

Congratulations! You’ve successfully cut your own hair with clippers.

Tips for Achieving Your Desired Look

Here are some tips to help you achieve your desired haircut:

  • Invest in high-quality clippers. Cheaper options may not cut as cleanly or last as long.
  • Be patient. Cutting hair takes time and practice.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. It’s easier to cut more hair off than to add it back on.
  • Use a comb to lift hair away from the scalp, making it easier to cut.
  • Use scissors to blend the top of the head with the sides and back for a more natural look.

FAQs About Hair Care

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair care:

How often should I wash my hair?

The frequency of hair washing depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people may need to wash their hair daily, while others can go several days without washing. Shampooing too often can strip hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage.

How can I prevent split ends?

To prevent split ends, avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant when you do. Additionally, avoid brushing wet hair, which can be more prone to breakage. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends from forming.

How can I promote hair growth?

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can promote hair growth. Additionally, minimizing stress and getting enough sleep can contribute to overall hair health. Using products specifically designed to promote hair growth, such as minoxidil, can also be helpful.

Cutting male hair with clippers is a cost-effective and convenient way to maintain your hairstyle. With these tips and a bit of practice, you’ll be able to achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
