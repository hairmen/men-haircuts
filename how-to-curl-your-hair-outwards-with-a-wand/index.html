How To Curl Your Hair Outwards With A Wand

Written by Eveline Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post where we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to curl your hair outwards with a wand. We know that styling your hair can be quite challenging, especially if you are not sure how to use a wand. So, we have created a comprehensive guide that will help you achieve gorgeous, bouncy, and voluminous curls.

Description

Curling your hair with a wand can be a great way to create beautiful, natural-looking curls. Curling outwards with a wand creates a bouncy and voluminous look that is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will show you how to achieve this style with ease.

How To

To start, you will need a few things:

  • A heat protectant spray
  • A wand
  • A comb or brush
  • A hairspray

Once you have gathered all the necessary items, you can begin the curling process.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start curling your hair, it is important to prepare it. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from the heat of the wand. After that, blow-dry your hair and brush it thoroughly to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section your hair into small pieces. This will make it easier to curl each strand and ensure that you do not miss any sections. You can use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the wand. Make sure that you wrap it away from your face to create an outward curl. Hold the hair on the wand for a few seconds and release it. Repeat this process until you have curled all of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Once you have finished curling your hair, apply a hairspray to hold the curls in place. You can also use your fingers to separate the curls to create a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect outward curl:

  • Use a wand that is the right size for your hair length. A smaller wand is perfect for short hair, while a larger wand is better for longer hair.
  • Do not hold the hair on the wand for too long, as this can damage your hair.
  • Brush your hair before you start curling to remove any tangles.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from the heat of the wand.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQ

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls can last for up to 24 hours if you use a good quality hairspray and do not touch your hair too much.

Q: Can I use a wand on wet hair?

A: No. It is important to dry your hair before using a wand to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Curling your hair can be damaging if you do not take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a wand.
  • Do not hold the hair on the wand for too long.
  • Use a good quality hairspray to hold the curls in place.
  • Do not wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Curling your hair outwards with a wand can be a great way to create a natural-looking, bouncy, and voluminous look. By following these step-by-step instructions and tips, you can achieve gorgeous curls that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated.
