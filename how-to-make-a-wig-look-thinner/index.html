How To Make A Wig Look Thinner

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find a way to make your wig look thinner? If so, you're in the right place! In this blog post, we're going to discuss some tips and tricks that can help you achieve a thinner-looking wig. We'll cover everything from how to style your wig to the right products to use. So, let's dive in!

How to

1. Use a Wig Cap

If you're not already using a wig cap, it's time to start. A wig cap can help keep your hair in place and prevent it from bulking up underneath your wig. It can also help to create a smoother surface for your wig to sit on, which can make it look thinner.

2. Trim Your Wig

If your wig is too thick, you can trim it to make it look thinner. You can either do this yourself or take it to a professional wig stylist. Just be sure to take it slow and trim a little at a time, so you don't accidentally cut off too much.

3. Style Your Wig

The way you style your wig can also make it look thinner. Try styling it in a way that creates less volume, like a sleek ponytail or straight and flat. You can also try using a straightening iron to flatten any bumps or lumps.

4. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can also help make your wig look thinner. Look for products that are designed to add volume or texture, as these can make your hair look thicker. Instead, opt for products that are designed to smooth and flatten your hair, like a smoothing serum or cream.

Step by Step

Step 1: Put on Your Wig Cap

Before you put on your wig, be sure to put on a wig cap. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent it from bulking up underneath your wig.

Step 2: Trim Your Wig

If your wig is too thick, you can trim it to make it look thinner. Take it slow and trim a little at a time, so you don't accidentally cut off too much.

Step 3: Style Your Wig

Style your wig in a way that creates less volume, like a sleek ponytail or straight and flat. Use a straightening iron to flatten any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed to smooth and flatten your hair, like a smoothing serum or cream.

Tips

Here are some additional tips that can help make your wig look thinner:

- Choose a wig with less volume - Opt for a shorter style - Use a wig with a lace front - Use a wig with a monofilament top

FAQ

Q: Can I thin out my wig on my own?

A: Yes, you can thin out your wig on your own, but it's best to do it slowly and carefully, so you don't accidentally cut off too much.

Q: What products can I use to make my wig look thinner?

A: Look for products that are designed to smooth and flatten your hair, like a smoothing serum or cream.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It's best to wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your wig looking its best. Here are some tips:

- Brush your wig regularly with a wig brush - Wash your wig with a wig shampoo and conditioner - Avoid using hot styling tools, as these can damage the fibers - Store your wig on a wig stand to help maintain its shape

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your wig look thinner and more natural. Remember to take it slow and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect look!
