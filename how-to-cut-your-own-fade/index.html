How To Cut Your Own Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your own hair at home, a fade is a great hairstyle to try. A fade is a popular haircut that features short hair on the sides and back that gradually gets longer towards the top. This style can be worn by men and women of all ages and hair types. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your own fade at home.

How to Cut Your Own Fade

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary supplies. You'll need a set of clippers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair and a towel to keep your clothing clean.

Step 2: Choose Your Fade Style

There are several different types of fades, including low, mid, and high fades, as well as skin fades. Decide on the type of fade you want before you start cutting.

Step 3: Start with the Sides and Back

Begin by using the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard for a gradual fade. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back, it's time to blend the top. Use the scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length, then blend it into the sides and back using the clippers.

Step 5: Refine the Fade

After you've blended the top, go back and refine the fade. Use the clippers without a guard to create a clean line around the edges, and use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, check your work in the mirror from all angles. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your fade looks even and polished.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Fade

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great fade:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard for a gradual fade.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.
  • Blend the top into the sides and back using the clippers.
  • Refine the fade by using clippers without a guard to create a clean line around the edges.
  • Check your work in the mirror from all angles.

FAQs

1. What is a fade?

A fade is a popular hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back that gradually gets longer towards the top.

2. Can I cut my own fade?

Yes, you can cut your own fade at home with the right tools and technique.

3. How long does a fade last?

A fade can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your fade looking fresh, be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Cutting your own fade can be a great way to save money and experiment with different hairstyles. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a polished and professional-looking fade at home.
