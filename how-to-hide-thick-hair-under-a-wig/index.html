How to Hide Thick Hair Under a Wig: Tips and Tricks

Are you looking to wear a wig but have thick hair? Look no further! In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to hide thick hair under a wig. Let's get started.

How to Hide Thick Hair Under a Wig

When it comes to hiding thick hair under a wig, there are a few different methods you can try. Here are some of the most effective ways:

Method 1: Braid Your Hair

One of the best ways to hide thick hair under a wig is to braid your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then braiding it into two braids. This will help to keep your hair flat and prevent any bumps or lumps from showing through the wig.

Method 2: Use a Wig Cap

Another way to hide thick hair under a wig is to use a wig cap. This is a thin cap that you wear underneath your wig to help smooth out any bumps or lumps. You can find wig caps at most beauty supply stores or online.

Method 3: Pin Your Hair Up

If you don't want to braid your hair or use a wig cap, you can also try pinning your hair up. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and then twisting it up into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place and then put on your wig.

Step by Step: How to Hide Thick Hair Under a Wig

Now that you know the different methods for hiding thick hair under a wig, let's go through the steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to use a blow dryer to help dry it faster. Once your hair is dry, brush it out to get rid of any tangles.

Step 2: Choose Your Method

Decide which method you want to use to hide your thick hair under your wig. Do you want to braid your hair, use a wig cap, or pin your hair up?

Step 3: Braid Your Hair

If you're going to braid your hair, part it down the middle and then braid it into two braids. Make sure the braids are tight and close to your scalp.

Step 4: Use a Wig Cap

If you're using a wig cap, put it on over your hair and make sure it's snug against your scalp. Tuck any loose hairs under the cap.

Step 5: Pin Your Hair Up

If you're pinning your hair up, pull it back into a ponytail and then twist it up into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 6: Put on Your Wig

Finally, put on your wig and adjust it as needed. Make sure it's sitting flat on your head and there are no bumps or lumps showing through.

Tips for Hiding Thick Hair Under a Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you hide your thick hair under a wig:

  • Use a wig cap that matches your skin tone to help it blend in better.
  • Try using a hair net over your braids or bun to help keep everything in place.
  • If you have really long hair, try tucking it up into a shorter style or cutting it to make it easier to hide under a wig.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding thick hair under a wig:

Can I wear a wig if I have thick hair?

Yes, you can still wear a wig if you have thick hair. You just need to make sure you use the right techniques to hide your hair underneath.

Do I need to use a wig cap?

No, you don't need to use a wig cap, but it can be helpful in smoothing out any bumps or lumps. It's up to personal preference.

Can I wear my hair in a bun under a wig?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a bun under a wig. Just make sure the bun is secure and tight against your scalp.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair even when wearing a wig. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy. Also, make sure to give your scalp a break from wearing a wig for long periods of time.

That's it! Now you know how to hide thick hair under a wig. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear a wig with confidence and ease.
