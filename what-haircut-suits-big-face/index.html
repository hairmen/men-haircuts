What Haircut Suits Big Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you have a big face. However, with the right style and cut, you can accentuate your best features and minimize the appearance of your face's size. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on what haircut suits a big face, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Big Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut and Style
  • FAQs on Haircuts for Big Faces
  • Hair Care Advice for Big Face Haircuts

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Big Face

Before choosing a haircut for a big face, it's essential to know your face shape. Generally, if you have a big face, you have a square, round, or heart-shaped face. The right haircut can help balance out these features and create a more proportionate look.

If you have a square face, choose a haircut that softens the angles and adds volume to the top. A layered or textured cut with side-swept bangs is ideal. For a round face, opt for a haircut with height at the crown and layers around the face. Long, layered cuts with side-swept bangs are flattering for heart-shaped faces.

Key Takeaways:

  • Know your face shape before choosing a haircut
  • Choose a haircut that softens angles and adds volume
  • Layered or textured cuts with side-swept bangs are ideal

Step-by-Step Guide on Getting the Perfect Haircut

Getting the perfect haircut starts with finding a skilled hairstylist who can work with your face shape and hair type. Make sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the look you want to achieve, and bring photos for reference.

During the haircut, your stylist will use techniques to create balance and proportion. For a big face, they may add layers or texture to create movement and volume. The stylist may also add side-swept bangs or fringe to soften the angles and create a more flattering look.

Key Takeaways:

  • Find a skilled hairstylist
  • Communicate clearly with your stylist
  • Ask for layers, texture, and side-swept bangs to create balance and proportion

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut and Style

Maintaining your haircut and style is essential to keep it looking fresh and flattering. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Use styling products that work with your hair type to create volume and hold. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Key Takeaways:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut
  • Use styling products that work with your hair type
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQs on Haircuts for Big Faces

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or one-length cuts. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look wider.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have a big face. Try a high ponytail or bun to create height and lengthen your face.

Q: Should I go for a bold or subtle haircut if I have a big face?

A: It depends on your personal style and preference. However, a subtle haircut with soft layers and side-swept bangs is generally more flattering for a big face.

Hair Care Advice for Big Face Haircuts

Proper hair care is vital for maintaining a healthy and flattering haircut. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type. Avoid heat styling as much as possible, and use a heat protectant when you do. Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or UV protectant spray.

Key Takeaways:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling and use a heat protectant when necessary
  • Protect your hair from the sun

Choosing the right haircut for a big face can be challenging, but with the right guidance and advice, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist, maintain your haircut and style, and take care of your hair for a healthy and beautiful look.
