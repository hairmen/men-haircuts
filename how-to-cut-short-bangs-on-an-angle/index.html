How To Cut Short Bangs On An Angle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle to freshen up your look, cutting your bangs on an angle may be just what you need. Not only does it add dimension to your hair, but it also frames your face in a unique way. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut short bangs on an angle.

Description

Short bangs on an angle are a great way to add a modern twist to your hairstyle. This cut involves cutting your bangs at an angle, which can help to soften your facial features and draw attention to your eyes.

How to Cut Short Bangs on an Angle

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning off your hair so that you only have your bangs to work with. Use a comb to create a clean, straight line across your forehead. This will help you to determine where you want to cut your bangs.

Step 2: Decide on the Angle

Once you have your hair sectioned off, it's time to decide on the angle you want to cut your bangs. You can choose to go for a subtle angle, or you can go for a more dramatic look. Use your comb to help you create the angle you want.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using your sharp scissors, start cutting your bangs on an angle. Take small sections at a time to ensure that you're cutting your hair evenly. Keep checking your progress in the mirror to make sure you're happy with the angle.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting your bangs, it's time to add some finishing touches. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs, and use your comb to make sure your bangs are even.

FAQ

Can I cut my bangs on an angle if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure you cut your bangs when your hair is dry so you can see how they'll look once they've settled.

What do I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always try to fix it by cutting more hair, or you can visit a hair salon to have a professional fix it for you.

How often do I need to cut my bangs?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look messy. Generally, you'll need to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your short bangs on an angle looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use a hair mask once a week to help keep your hair healthy and strong.

Now that you know how to cut short bangs on an angle, it's time to give it a try! Remember to take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect short bangs on an angle.
