Is It Rare To Have Natural Wavy Hair

Written by William Aug 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you one of those people who have always wondered if it's rare to have natural wavy hair? Well, you're not alone. Many people have this question in mind. In this article, we will be discussing everything related to natural wavy hair, including its rarity, how to achieve it, and hair care tips.

Table of Content

  • Rarity of Natural Wavy Hair
  • How to Achieve Natural Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Natural Wavy Hair
  • FAQs

Rarity of Natural Wavy Hair

Contrary to popular belief, natural wavy hair is not rare. In fact, it's quite common. Many people around the world have naturally wavy hair. It's just that some people have more defined waves than others, which can make it appear rare. However, if you look closely, you'll notice that many people have some degree of waviness in their hair.

It's also worth noting that hair texture and type play a significant role in the appearance of natural waves. For instance, people with fine hair tend to have more defined waves than those with thick hair. Similarly, people with curly hair may have looser waves, while those with straight hair may have tighter waves.

How to Achieve Natural Wavy Hair

If you don't have natural wavy hair and want to achieve it, there are several ways to do so. Here are some of the most common methods:

1. Using a Curling Wand

One of the easiest ways to achieve natural waves is by using a curling wand. Simply wrap sections of your hair around the wand, hold for a few seconds, and release. Repeat the process until you've curled all your hair. Once you're done, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create natural-looking waves.

2. Braiding Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another effective way to achieve natural waves. Simply divide your hair into sections and braid each section. Leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight for best results. Once you've taken out the braids, run your fingers through your hair to create natural-looking waves.

3. Using Sea Salt Spray

Sea salt spray is a popular product that helps create natural waves. Simply spray it on your damp hair, scrunch your hair with your fingers, and let it air dry. Once your hair is dry, you'll have natural-looking waves.

Hair Care Tips for Natural Wavy Hair

If you have natural wavy hair, it's essential to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Wavy hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain natural oils like coconut oil or argan oil.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it look frizzy. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Alternatively, you can air dry your hair or use the cool setting on your hair dryer.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Wavy hair is prone to tangles, so it's important to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to avoid breakage.

FAQs

Q: Is natural wavy hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, natural wavy hair is relatively easy to maintain. However, you should still take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Q: Can I achieve natural waves without heat styling?

A: Yes, you can achieve natural waves without heat styling. Braiding your hair or using sea salt spray are great alternatives to heat styling.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wavy hair?

A: It's best to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner on wavy hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.

In conclusion, natural wavy hair is not rare. Many people around the world have it. However, if you don't have natural waves, there are several ways to achieve them. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
