Hairstyles For Oval Faces Female Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with an oval face looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further. In this guide, we will provide you with a range of hairstyles that complement your face shape and age. From short bobs to long waves, we have got you covered. Read on to find out more about the best hairstyles for oval faces female over 50.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for an Oval Face

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, let's talk about how to choose the right hairstyle for an oval face. An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off most hairstyles with ease. However, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Think about the level of maintenance you are willing to commit to
  • Consider your lifestyle and personal style

Once you have considered these factors, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

Short Bob

The short bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For women over 50 with oval faces, the short bob can be a great option as it accentuates your cheekbones and jawline. This hairstyle is also low maintenance and easy to style. You can opt for a straight or textured bob, depending on your hair texture and personal style.

Long Layers

If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create movement and volume. Long layers that frame your face can be especially flattering for women over 50 with oval faces. This hairstyle works well for all hair textures and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great way to add dimension to your face and draw attention to your eyes. For women over 50 with oval faces, side-swept bangs can be a great option as they soften the face and create a youthful look. This hairstyle works well for all hair lengths and textures.

Messy Pixie

If you are looking for a low maintenance hairstyle, consider a messy pixie. This hairstyle is short and textured, which can be great for women over 50 with oval faces. The messy pixie can be styled in a variety of ways, including tousled or slicked back.

Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that can be great for women over 50 with oval faces. This hairstyle keeps your hair off your face and accentuates your cheekbones and jawline. You can opt for a sleek or textured ponytail, depending on your personal style.

Blunt Cut

A blunt cut can be a great way to add structure to your face and create a sophisticated look. This hairstyle works well for women over 50 with oval faces as it accentuates your cheekbones and jawline. You can opt for a straight or textured blunt cut, depending on your hair texture and personal style.

Highlights

If you want to add dimension to your hair, consider adding highlights. Highlights can be a great way to accentuate your face shape and create a youthful look. You can opt for subtle or bold highlights, depending on your personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking great, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type

FAQs

What is the best haircut for an oval face?

The best haircut for an oval face depends on your personal style and hair texture. However, some great options include a short bob, long layers, side-swept bangs, messy pixie, low ponytail, and blunt cut.

What hair color looks best on an oval face?

The best hair color for an oval face depends on your skin tone and personal style. However, some great options include warm tones like honey blonde, caramel, and copper.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for trims and touch-ups. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

There you have it, a comprehensive guide to hairstyles for oval faces female over 50. We hope this guide has helped you find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape and age. Remember to consider your hair texture, personal style, and maintenance level when choosing a hairstyle. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best every day.
