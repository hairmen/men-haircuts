How To Get Rid Of Thick Hair On Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick hair on your head can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can give you a full, voluminous look. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking to get rid of thick hair on your head, there are several ways to do so. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Rid of Thick Hair on Head

The first step to getting rid of thick hair on your head is to determine the cause. Thick hair can be caused by genetics, hormones, or even certain medications. If you're unsure of the cause, it's best to consult with a dermatologist or trichologist.

Once you've determined the cause, you can start exploring different methods to reduce the thickness of your hair. Some options include:

  • Thinning shears: This is a tool that your stylist can use to cut your hair in a way that reduces bulk and thickness.
  • Chemical straightening: This is a process that involves using chemicals to break down the protein bonds in your hair, making it easier to straighten and style.
  • Hair extensions: This is a temporary solution that involves adding hair to your existing hair to make it look thicker and fuller.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying one of the methods listed above, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist or trichologist to determine the best method for your hair type and texture.
  2. If you're considering chemical straightening, make sure to do your research and find a reputable salon that uses safe and effective products.
  3. If you're getting hair extensions, make sure to choose a type that matches your existing hair and won't cause damage.
  4. Follow the instructions of your stylist or trichologist carefully, and schedule follow-up appointments as needed to maintain your desired look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you manage and reduce the thickness of your hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help soften and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging too hard.
  • Consider using a hair serum or oil to help smooth and tame frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of thick hair on your head:

Is it safe to use thinning shears on my hair?

Yes, as long as they are used properly by a trained professional. Thinning shears can help reduce bulk and thickness without causing damage.

Will chemical straightening damage my hair?

It depends on the type of product used and how it's applied. Make sure to choose a reputable salon and follow the instructions carefully to minimize damage.

How long do hair extensions last?

It depends on the type of extensions and how well they are maintained. Some types can last up to six months, while others may need to be replaced every few weeks.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Whether you're trying to get rid of thick hair or simply manage it, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip away natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and repair your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the look you want and take good care of your hair at the same time. Whether you choose to use thinning shears, chemical straightening, or hair extensions, make sure to consult with a professional and follow their instructions carefully for the best results.
