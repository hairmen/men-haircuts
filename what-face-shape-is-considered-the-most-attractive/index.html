What Face Shape Is Considered The Most Attractive

Written by Oliver Jun 06, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to beauty standards, one of the most sought-after qualities is an attractive face shape. With so many different features and shapes out there, it can be difficult to determine which one is considered the most attractive. In this article, we’ll break down the most desirable face shapes and provide tips for accentuating your features.

Table of Contents

  • Description

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Accentuating Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Enhancing Your Features

Description

There are several different face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. While every face shape is unique and beautiful in its own way, studies have shown that the oval face shape is generally considered the most attractive.

Oval faces are characterized by a gently rounded hairline, a slightly wider forehead, and a narrow, tapered chin. This face shape is symmetrical and balanced, which creates a harmonious and pleasing appearance. Many people with oval faces are also able to pull off a wide range of hairstyles and makeup looks, which adds to their overall attractiveness.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is relatively simple. All you need is a mirror and a few minutes to examine your features. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face.
  2. Examine your face and note the following measurements:
    • Forehead width
    • Cheekbone width
    • Jawline width
    • Face length
  3. Compare your measurements to the following face shape descriptions:
    • Oval: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and forehead is wider than the jawline.
    • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement, with a rounded chin.
    • Square: All measurements are fairly similar, with a strong jawline.
    • Heart-shaped: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, with a pointed chin.
    • Diamond-shaped: Face length is greatest, with a pointed chin and high cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Accentuating Your Face Shape

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can use the following tips to accentuate your features:

Oval:

People with oval faces can pull off a wide range of hairstyles and makeup looks, making them some of the most versatile individuals when it comes to beauty. To accentuate an oval face shape, consider the following:

  • Hairstyles: Experiment with different lengths and styles, including long, short, straight, curly, and layered looks.
  • Makeup: Try out different eye and lip colors to see what works best for your skin tone and features.
  • Accessories: Consider wearing statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention to your face.

Round:

Individuals with round faces often have soft, feminine features. To accentuate a round face shape, consider the following:

  • Hairstyles: Try out long, layered looks that add volume and length to your face. Avoid short, blunt cuts that can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Makeup: Use contouring to create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline.
  • Accessories: Consider wearing statement necklaces or earrings that draw attention to your collarbone and neck.

Square:

People with square faces often have strong, angular features. To accentuate a square face shape, consider the following:

  • Hairstyles: Try out textured, layered looks that soften your features. Avoid blunt, straight cuts that can make your face appear even more angular.
  • Makeup: Use contouring to soften your jawline and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.
  • Accessories: Consider wearing round or curved earrings to contrast with your angular features.

Heart-Shaped:

Individuals with heart-shaped faces often have a pointed chin and wide forehead. To accentuate a heart-shaped face shape, consider the following:

  • Hairstyles: Try out long, layered looks that add volume to your chin and jawline. Avoid short, blunt cuts that can make your forehead appear even wider.
  • Makeup: Use contouring to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.
  • Accessories: Consider wearing statement earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your lower face.

Diamond-Shaped:

People with diamond-shaped faces often have high cheekbones and a pointed chin. To accentuate a diamond-shaped face shape, consider the following:

  • Hairstyles: Try out styles that add volume to your temples and jawline, such as long, layered looks or side-swept bangs.
  • Makeup: Use contouring to soften your cheekbones and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.
  • Accessories: Consider wearing statement earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your collarbone and neck.

Tips for Enhancing Your Features

No matter what your face shape is, there are a few tips you can use to enhance your features:

  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water can help keep your skin looking fresh and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet: Eating a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins can help keep your skin looking radiant.
  • Get enough sleep: Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep per night to help your skin rejuvenate and repair itself.
  • Wear sunscreen: Protect your skin from harmful UV rays by wearing sunscreen every day.
  • Practice good skincare: Develop a skincare routine that works for your skin type, including cleansing, toning, and moisturizing.

FAQs

Q: Is there really such a thing as an “attractive” face shape?

A: While beauty standards vary from culture to culture, research has shown that certain face shapes are generally considered more attractive than others.

Q: Can I change my face shape?

A: While you can’t change your bone structure, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and accessories to accentuate your features and create the illusion of a different face shape.

Q: How can I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, examine your features and note the measurements of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and face length. Compare your measurements to the descriptions of different face shapes.
