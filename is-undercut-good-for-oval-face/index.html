Is Undercut Good For Oval Face Find Out

Written by Oliver Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting an undercut but unsure if it will suit your oval face? Well, you've come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether an undercut is good for an oval face. Let's get started!

Description

An undercut is a trendy hairstyle that involves shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving the top long. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways, making it a popular choice for both men and women.

However, when it comes to oval faces, some people may wonder if it's a good idea to get an undercut. Let's explore this further.

How to

If you've decided to get an undercut, it's essential to find a skilled hairstylist who can give you the perfect cut. Here's how to get an undercut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, leaving the top section longer than the sides and back.
  3. Use clippers to shave the sides and back of your head, leaving a little bit of hair at the bottom.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top section of your hair to your desired length.
  5. Style your hair using your preferred product.

Remember to be clear with your hairstylist about the length and style you want for your undercut.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style an undercut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Apply your preferred hair product to your hair, focusing on the top section.
  3. Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair upwards and backwards to create volume.
  4. Style the sides and back of your hair using a comb or your fingers.
  5. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider before getting an undercut:

  • Consider your face shape and hair type before getting an undercut.
  • Choose a skilled hairstylist who can give you the perfect cut.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Maintain your undercut by getting regular trims to keep it looking sharp.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

1. Is an undercut suitable for an oval face?

Yes, an undercut can look great on an oval face. However, it's essential to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

2. How often do I need to get a trim for my undercut?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your undercut's shape and style.

3. Can I still tie my hair up with an undercut?

Yes, you can still tie your hair up with an undercut. However, it may require more styling products to keep it in place.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial to maintain a healthy and stylish undercut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying and straightening.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes and bleach, as they can damage your hair.

Conclusion

An undercut can look great on an oval face, as long as you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type. Remember to find a skilled hairstylist, experiment with different styling products and techniques, and maintain your undercut with regular trims. With these tips, you'll be rocking a stylish and trendy undercut in no time!
