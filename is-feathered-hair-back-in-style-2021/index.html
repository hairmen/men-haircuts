Description

Feathered hair was a popular hairstyle in the 1970s and 1980s, but is it back in style in 2021? The answer is yes! Feathered hair has made a comeback in recent years and continues to be a popular hairstyle choice for women of all ages. With its soft layers and feather-like texture, feathered hair is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down.

The key to achieving the perfect feathered hair look is to have your hair cut in layers, with the longest layers framing your face. This will give your hair volume and movement, which is essential for creating the feathered effect. Feathered hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose waves to sleek and straight.

How to Get Feathered Hair

If you're interested in getting feathered hair, the first step is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in creating this style. Look for someone who has worked with feathered hair before and can show you examples of their work.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to gather some inspiration photos of feathered hair styles that you like. This will give your stylist a clear idea of the look you're going for and ensure that you're both on the same page.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting feathered hair:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the length and layers that will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Once your hair is cut, your stylist will add layers to create the feathered effect.
  3. When styling your hair, use a round brush to create volume and movement.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Feathered Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your feathered hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your hair's natural texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about feathered hair:

Q: What face shapes look best with feathered hair?

A: Feathered hair looks great on a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can feathered hair be worn by women of all ages?

A: Yes! Feathered hair is a timeless style that can be worn by women of all ages.

Q: Is feathered hair high maintenance?

A: Not necessarily. While feathered hair does require regular trims to maintain its shape, it can be styled in a variety of ways and doesn't require a lot of daily maintenance.

Feathered Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for your feathered hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and brittle.
  • When blow-drying your hair, use a low heat setting to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking soft and healthy.

In conclusion, feathered hair is definitely back in style in 2021! This versatile hairstyle can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, and can be dressed up or down for any occasion. With the right care and maintenance, your feathered hair can look amazing for years to come.
