How To Figure Out If Bangs Look Good On You

Written by Luffy Jun 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're thinking of getting bangs but you're not sure if they'll suit you, you're not alone. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look, but they're not for everyone. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to figure out if bangs look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Figure Out If Bangs Look Good on You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting Bangs
  • FAQs

How to Figure Out If Bangs Look Good on You

There are a few things to consider when trying to figure out if bangs will suit you:

1. Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding whether to get bangs is your face shape. Not all bangs will work for all face shapes. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, go for longer, side-swept bangs.
  • If you have a square face, opt for soft, wispy bangs.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, try a choppy, textured fringe.
  • If you have an oval face, you're in luck – almost any type of bangs will suit you!

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in whether bangs will look good on you. If you have thick, curly hair, you may need to go for longer, heavier bangs to avoid them looking too puffy. If you have fine hair, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can look flat.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when deciding whether to get bangs. If you're a busy mom who doesn't have time to style your hair every day, blunt bangs may not be the best option for you. On the other hand, if you have a more flexible schedule and enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, you may be able to pull off more high-maintenance bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you figure out if bangs look good on you:

  1. Consider your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Look for inspiration photos of bangs that you like.
  3. Try on a wig with bangs to get an idea of what you'll look like.
  4. If you're not ready to commit to bangs, try clip-in bangs instead.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist – they can help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

Tips for Getting Bangs

If you've decided to get bangs, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the type of bangs you want.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron to style your bangs.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or bobby pins, to change up your look.

FAQs

Q: What if I don't like my bangs?

A: If you're not happy with your bangs, don't panic – they'll grow out eventually. In the meantime, you can try styling them differently or pinning them back with bobby pins.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with bangs. You may need to use some bobby pins to keep your bangs in place, but it's definitely doable.

Hair Care for Bangs

Bangs require a bit of extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to keep them clean.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to give them some volume.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much – the oils from your fingers can make them look greasy.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat.

Hopefully these tips have helped you figure out if bangs look good on you. Remember, there's no right or wrong answer – it's all about what makes you feel confident and beautiful!
