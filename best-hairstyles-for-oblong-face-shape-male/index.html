Description

The oblong face shape has a long, narrow shape with a high forehead and strong jawline. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to create balance and proportion. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the face and minimizing the length of the forehead. The best hairstyles for oblong face shape male include short and medium-length haircuts with layers, texture, and volume.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for oblong face shape male, consider the following:

  • Length: Short and medium-length haircuts work best for this face shape, as they help to balance the proportions of the face.
  • Layers: Layered haircuts add volume and texture to the sides of the face, creating a more balanced look.
  • Texture: Adding texture to the hair, such as through curls or waves, can help to create the illusion of a wider face.
  • Volume: Hairstyles with volume at the sides of the face can help to create a more proportionate look.

Step By Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles for oblong face shape male:

Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut is a low-maintenance, masculine hairstyle that works well for oblong face shape male. To style, simply use clippers to buzz the hair to a short length all over.

Side Parted Short Haircut

A side-parted short haircut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that works well for oblong face shape male. To style, use a comb to create a side part and then style the hair with a pomade or wax.

Textured Crop

A textured crop is a modern and stylish hairstyle that works well for oblong face shape male. To style, use a texturizing spray or pomade to create definition and texture in the hair.

Medium-Length Layers

Medium-length layers are a great option for oblong face shape male, as they help to add volume and balance to the face. To style, use a blow dryer and round brush to add volume and movement to the hair.

Tips

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as they can elongate the face even more.
  • Consider adding a beard or facial hair to balance the proportions of the face.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to the hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for oblong face shape male?

The best haircuts for oblong face shape male include short and medium-length haircuts with layers, texture, and volume.

Does facial hair help to balance an oblong face shape?

Yes, adding a beard or facial hair can help to balance the proportions of the face and create a more proportionate look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often.
  • Use styling products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oblong face shape male, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your style. Remember to consider the length, layers, texture, and volume of your hair when choosing a hairstyle, and use appropriate hair care techniques to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
