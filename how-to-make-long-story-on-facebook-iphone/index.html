How To Make Long Story On Facebook Iphone A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to fit everything into one short Facebook story? Do you want to share a longer story with your friends and followers? You're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make a long story on Facebook on your iPhone.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Open the Facebook App
  • Step 2: Start a New Story
  • Step 3: Add Text to Your Story
  • Step 4: Add Photos or Videos
  • Step 5: Add More Pages to Your Story
  • Step 6: Edit Your Story
  • Step 7: Post Your Story
  • Tips for Making a Great Story
  • FAQs
  • Bonus: How to Protect Your Hair While Using Your iPhone

Step 1: Open the Facebook App

First, make sure you have the Facebook app installed on your iPhone. If you don't have it, you can download it from the App Store. Once you have the app, open it and log in to your account.

Step 2: Start a New Story

To create a new story, click on the camera icon in the top left corner of the Facebook app. This will open up the camera screen. From there, you can swipe left to access the "Create Story" option.

Step 3: Add Text to Your Story

Once you're in the "Create Story" screen, you can add text to your story by tapping on the "Aa" icon in the top right corner. This will open up the text editor, where you can type out your story.

Step 4: Add Photos or Videos

If you want to add photos or videos to your story, you can do so by tapping on the camera icon in the bottom center of the screen. This will open up your camera roll, where you can select the photos or videos you want to add to your story.

Step 5: Add More Pages to Your Story

If you need more space to tell your story, you can add more pages to your story by tapping on the square icon in the bottom left corner of the screen. This will add a new page to your story, where you can continue typing or adding photos and videos.

Step 6: Edit Your Story

Once you've added all the content you want to your story, you can edit it by tapping on the pencil icon in the bottom left corner of the screen. This will open up the editing screen, where you can rearrange the pages of your story, add stickers or text overlays, and more.

Step 7: Post Your Story

When you're happy with your story, you can post it by tapping on the "Share" button in the bottom right corner of the screen. This will post your story to your Facebook page, where your friends and followers can view it.

Tips for Making a Great Story

  • Keep your story concise and focused on one topic.
  • Use high-quality photos and videos.
  • Add text and stickers to make your story more engaging.
  • Consider using a template or layout to make your story more visually appealing.
  • Use hashtags to make your story more discoverable.

FAQs

Can I make a long story on Facebook on my Android phone?

Yes, the process for making a long story on Facebook is the same on Android phones as it is on iPhones.

How long can my Facebook story be?

Your Facebook story can be up to 20 pages long, with each page containing text, photos, or videos.

Can I edit my Facebook story after I've posted it?

Yes, you can edit your Facebook story after you've posted it by tapping on the three-dot menu icon in the top right corner of the story and selecting "Edit Story."

Bonus: How to Protect Your Hair While Using Your iPhone

Using your iPhone for long periods of time can cause damage to your hair, especially if you're holding it against your head. To protect your hair, try using a phone case with a grip or a phone holder that clips onto your clothing. You can also try using a headset or speakerphone to reduce the amount of time your phone spends in contact with your hair.

Now that you know how to make a long story on Facebook on your iPhone, you can share all your long-form content with your friends and followers. Happy storytelling!
