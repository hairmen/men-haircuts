How To Cut Short Hair In Layers A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your short hair, cutting it in layers is a great option. However, it can be intimidating to attempt it at home. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of cutting short hair in layers, as well as some tips and hair care advice.

How to Cut Short Hair in Layers

Cutting short hair in layers involves creating different lengths of hair throughout your head. This will create texture and movement, giving your hair more volume and dimension. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry or use a hairdryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between two fingers. Use scissors to cut the hair at a slight angle, creating a longer layer. Repeat this step until you've cut all the hair in the back section.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between two fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, creating shorter layers towards the front of your face. Repeat this step until you've cut all the hair in both side sections.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Use a comb to blend the layers together, creating a seamless transition between the different lengths of hair. Make sure there are no visible lines or bumps.

Step 6: Trim the Ends

Use scissors to trim the ends of your hair, creating a clean and even look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to cutting short hair in layers:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, hair clips, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry or use a hairdryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 7: Trim the Ends

Use scissors to trim the ends of your hair, creating a clean and even look.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair in Layers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair in layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid any drastic mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • When cutting the sides, angle the scissors towards your face to create a more flattering look.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • If you're unsure, consider going to a professional stylist for help.

FAQs

Can I cut my short hair in layers myself?

Yes, you can cut your short hair in layers yourself. However, it can be difficult to achieve a professional-looking result, so it's best to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

What's the best way to blend the layers?

Use a comb to blend the layers together, and make sure there are no visible lines or bumps. You can also use thinning scissors to soften any harsh edges.

How often should I cut my short hair in layers?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, you should aim to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers and shape.

Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

Here are some tips to care for your layered short hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the layers.
  • Consider using a volumizing product to enhance the texture and movement of your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the layers and shape.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to cut your short hair in layers at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't hesitate to seek help from a professional if you need it.
