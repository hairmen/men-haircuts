Short Grey Hair Over 60 Uk A Guide To Looking Great

Written by Aprilia Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and considering going for a short grey hair look, then you're in the right place. Here we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to achieve the perfect short grey hair look, as well as some advice on how to take care of your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally loses its color and becomes grey. However, this doesn't mean that we can't still rock a short hairstyle that looks great. Short hairstyles can be a great option for older women who want to look youthful and fresh.

Short grey hair is becoming increasingly popular in the UK, and for good reason. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal effort to style, and it can also be very flattering for older women.

How to Achieve the Perfect Short Grey Hair Look

Here are some tips on how to achieve the perfect short grey hair look:

1. Choose the Right Cut

The first step in achieving the perfect short grey hair look is to choose the right cut. There are many different short hairstyles to choose from, so it's important to find a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

A pixie cut, for example, can be a great option for older women with thinning hair, as it can help to create the illusion of thickness and volume. A bob cut can also be a great option for women with thicker hair.

2. Embrace the Grey

Once you've chosen your cut, it's time to embrace the grey. Grey hair can be beautiful and can add a touch of sophistication to your look. If you're not ready to go completely grey, then you can always opt for highlights or lowlights to add some depth and dimension to your hair.

3. Add Some Texture

Adding some texture to your hair can help to create a more youthful and modern look. You can add texture by using a texturizing spray or by using a curling iron to create loose waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve the perfect short grey hair look:

Step 1: Choose Your Cut

Choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Embrace the Grey

Embrace your natural grey hair, or add some highlights or lowlights to add some depth and dimension to your hair.

Step 3: Add Some Texture

Add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or by using a curling iron to create loose waves.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a hair dryer and a styling brush to create volume and shape.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Grey Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining short grey hair:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Use a purple shampoo to keep your grey hair looking vibrant and to prevent it from turning yellow.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.

FAQs

Q: Is short grey hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, short grey hair is very easy to maintain. It requires minimal styling and can be washed and dried quickly.

Q: Will short grey hair make me look older?

A: No, short grey hair can actually make you look younger and more modern.

Q: Can I still color my hair if it's short and grey?

A: Yes, you can still color your hair if it's short and grey. Just make sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

Hair Care for Short Grey Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short grey hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't dry out your hair.

3. Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, short grey hair can be a great option for older women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that looks great. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve the perfect short grey hair look and feel confident and beautiful.
