How To Calm Down Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how frustrating it can be to keep it under control. Frizz, tangles, and dryness are just a few of the challenges that come with having curly hair. But fear not, with the right techniques and products, you can calm down your curls and have a gorgeous, healthy-looking mane. In this article, we will share with you a step-by-step guide on how to calm down curly hair.

How to Calm Down Curly Hair

Calm down curly hair by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain natural oils like argan, coconut, or jojoba oil. These oils will help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner will help to further moisturize and detangle your curls. Apply a small amount to your hair, focusing on the ends. Don't rinse it out.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

After applying the leave-in conditioner, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your curls. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Step 4: Apply a Curl Cream or Gel

A curl cream or gel will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount to your hair, working it through from the roots to the ends. Don't overdo it, as too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 5: Air Dry or Diffuse Your Hair

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls. Avoid using a regular blow dryer without a diffuser, as this can lead to frizz and damage your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to calm down your curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.
  4. Apply a curl cream or gel to define your curls.
  5. Air dry or diffuse your hair.

Tips for Calming Down Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for calming down curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Don't touch your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?

It's best to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Regular shampoos and conditioners can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and frizz.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and texture. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Should I use a diffuser attachment on my blow dryer?

Yes, a diffuser attachment can help to reduce frizz and enhance your curls. Make sure to use it on a low heat setting and move it around your head to dry your hair evenly.
