How To Cut Baby Boy Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a new parent wondering how to cut your baby boy's hair with scissors? It's a daunting task, but with the right techniques and tools, you can do it at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut baby boy hair with scissors, step-by-step, along with tips and FAQs to make the process easier.

How to Cut Baby Boy Hair with Scissors

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clip
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing your baby boy's hair and letting it dry completely. Wet hair is harder to cut and can lead to uneven results.

Step 2: Comb Hair

Using a comb, gently comb your baby boy's hair in the direction that it grows. This will help you see any tangles or knots that need to be removed.

Step 3: Clip Hair

Clip any hair that you don't want to cut, such as bangs or longer hair on top. This will help you focus on the areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 4: Wet Hair

Using the spray bottle filled with water, dampen the hair that you will be cutting. This will make it easier to cut and help you see any uneven areas.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Begin cutting small sections of hair at a time, starting from the bottom and moving upwards. Use your fingers to guide the hair and the scissors to trim any uneven ends.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

After cutting each section, check for evenness by combing through the hair. If you notice any areas that are longer than others, trim them with the scissors.

Step 7: Trim Bangs

If your baby boy has bangs, trim them by combing them straight down and cutting them in a straight line. Keep the scissors parallel to the hairline to ensure an even cut.

Step 8: Clean Up

Once you've finished cutting, clean up any loose hairs with a brush or towel. You can also rinse your baby boy's hair to remove any loose hairs.

Tips for Cutting Baby Boy Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take breaks if your baby boy becomes fussy
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid making mistakes
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQs

What age can I start cutting my baby boy's hair?

You can start cutting your baby boy's hair as early as a few months old. However, it's best to wait until he's older and able to sit still for longer periods of time.

Do I need special scissors to cut baby boy hair?

No, you don't need special scissors. However, it's important to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

What if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. You can always trim the hair a little shorter to fix the mistake. If it's a big mistake, you can always take your baby boy to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

After cutting your baby boy's hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a soft brush or comb to prevent pulling hair
  • Trim hair every few months to maintain a healthy length
  • Avoid using hair products on young babies

Now that you know how to cut baby boy hair with scissors, you can save money and time by doing it at home. Just remember to be patient, take breaks, and use sharp scissors for the best results.
