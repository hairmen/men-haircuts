How To Mix Hair Color At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hair color but don't want to spend a fortune at a salon, mixing your own hair color at home can be a great option. Not only is it more affordable, but it also allows you to customize your color to your liking.

Description

Mixing hair color at home can seem intimidating, but it's actually quite simple once you know what you're doing. This step-by-step guide will walk you through the process and provide you with tips for achieving the perfect color.

How to Mix Hair Color at Home

Step 1: Choose Your Color

The first step in mixing your own hair color is choosing the right shade. Look for a hair color kit that includes a developer and colorant in your desired shade. Make sure to read the instructions carefully and select the right shade for your hair type and color.

Step 2: Prepare Your Workspace

Before you start mixing your hair color, it's important to prepare your workspace. Lay down some old towels or newspapers to protect your surfaces from any spills or stains.

Step 3: Mix Your Color

Once you've selected your color and prepared your workspace, it's time to mix your hair color. Start by squeezing the colorant into the developer bottle, making sure to follow the instructions carefully. Shake the bottle well to ensure that the color is evenly mixed.

Step 4: Apply Your Color

Now that your color is mixed, it's time to apply it to your hair. Start by parting your hair into sections and applying the color to each section one at a time. Use a comb to ensure that the color is evenly distributed throughout your hair.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Once you've applied your hair color, let it sit for the recommended amount of time. Then, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that comes with your hair color kit to help keep your hair soft and shiny.

Tips for Mixing Hair Color at Home

  • Always wear gloves to protect your hands from the hair color.
  • Do a patch test before applying the color to your entire head to make sure you're not allergic to any of the ingredients.
  • Start with a lighter shade if you're unsure of what color to choose.
  • Follow the instructions carefully to avoid any mishaps.

FAQ

Can I mix two hair colors together?

Yes, you can mix two hair colors together to create a custom shade. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and test the color on a small section of hair before applying it all over.

How long should I leave the hair color on?

The amount of time you should leave the hair color on depends on the instructions that come with your kit. Typically, it's between 20-40 minutes.

How often should I color my hair?

It's best to wait at least 4-6 weeks between hair colorings to avoid damage to your hair.

Hair Care After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and vibrancy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading, and avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.

Now that you know how to mix hair color at home, you can experiment with different shades and create a look that's uniquely yours. Just remember to follow the instructions carefully, take your time, and have fun!
