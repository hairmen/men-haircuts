What Hairstyle Will Make My Face Look Thinner

Written by Frank Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Looking for a hairstyle that will make your face look thinner? You've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss different hairstyles that will help you achieve a slimmer look. Whether you have a round face or a square jawline, we have the perfect hairstyle for you. So, let's get started!

Description

Having a round or chubby face can be a challenge when it comes to hairstyling. But, there are several hairstyles that can make your face look slimmer and more defined. The key is to create an illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved through different haircuts and styles that will draw attention away from your cheeks and double chin.

How To

Firstly, consider getting a layered haircut. Layers create movement and texture that can soften round faces. The layers should start at the jawline or cheekbones and gradually get longer towards the ends. This will add length to your face and make it look less round.

Another hairstyle that will make your face look thinner is the long bob or 'lob'. This style is perfect for all face shapes and can be customized to suit your hair type and texture. The bob should end just below the jawline and be styled with a side part. This will create an asymmetrical look that will draw attention away from your cheeks.

If you have a square jawline, a side-swept fringe or bangs can help soften your features. The fringe should be longer on one side and gradually get shorter towards the other. This style will create a diagonal line that will make your face look less angular.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist about the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose a layered haircut or long bob to create an illusion of length and angles.

Step 3: Consider adding a side-swept fringe or bangs to soften a square jawline.

Step 4: Style your hair with a side part to create an asymmetrical look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle to make your face look thinner:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they will make your face look wider.
  • Use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair, as this will create movement and make your face look slimmer.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair, as this will draw attention away from your face.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to add some volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.

Q: Will a bob haircut make my face look wider?

A: No, a bob haircut can actually make your face look slimmer if it is cut to the right length and styled with a side part.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly. Use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools, and avoid using too much hairspray or hair gel, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, there are several hairstyles that will make your face look thinner. The key is to create an illusion of length and angles through different haircuts and styles. Consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different looks to find the perfect hairstyle for you. With the right hairstyle, you can achieve a slimmer and more defined look.
