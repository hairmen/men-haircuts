How To Make Straight Hair Curly For Guys Naturally A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and looking for a change? Do you want to try out some curly hairstyles but don't want to use heat or chemicals? You're in luck because in this article, we'll be sharing some natural ways to make your straight hair curly. From using hair products to DIY methods, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

How to Make Straight Hair Curly for Guys Naturally

Before we dive into the methods, it's essential to understand that these techniques won't give you permanent curls like a perm or a curling iron. However, they can help you achieve some bouncy waves and enhance your natural hair texture.

Here are some natural ways to make your straight hair curly:

1. Use Curl-Enhancing Products

Curl-enhancing products like mousse, gel, and cream can help you achieve curly hair without using heat. These products work by enhancing your hair's natural texture and adding definition to your curls. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls further.

2. Try the Twist-Out Method

The twist-out method is a popular technique used by many natural hair enthusiasts. It involves twisting small sections of your hair and leaving them to dry overnight. When you unravel the twists, you'll be left with defined curls. You can use a styling cream or butter to twist your hair, and don't forget to use bobby pins to secure the twists.

3. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another effective way to get curls without using heat. You can either do a traditional three-strand braid or try out a fishtail braid for a more intricate look. Leave the braids in overnight and unravel them in the morning for bouncy waves.

4. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are bendable foam rods that can help you achieve curls without heat. Simply wrap a small section of your hair around the rod and secure it in place. Leave the rods in overnight and remove them in the morning for voluminous curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different methods let's break down the steps you need to follow to achieve curly hair naturally:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Enhancing Products

If you're using curl-enhancing products, apply them to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands. If you're braiding or twisting your hair, use a styling cream or butter to help the twists hold better.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections to make it easier to work with. Use hair clips or bobby pins to secure the sections you're not working on.

Step 4: Twist, Braid, or Roll Your Hair

Depending on the method you're using, twist, braid, or roll your hair using flexi rods. Secure the ends with bobby pins or elastics.

Step 5: Leave Your Hair to Dry Overnight

Leave your hair to dry overnight or for a few hours. If you're using flexi rods, you can use a hairdryer on the cool setting to speed up the drying process.

Step 6: Undo the Braids or Twists

Undo the braids or twists, and use your fingers to separate the curls gently. Shake your head to loosen the curls and add volume.

Tips for Making Straight Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair once it's dry; it can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel.
  • Be gentle when separating the curls to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Can I use a hair straightener to curl my hair?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener to curl your hair, but it's not a natural method. We recommend using the techniques we shared in this article to avoid heat damage to your hair.

How long will the curls last?

The duration of your curls depends on your hair type, the method you used, and the products you applied. On average, the curls can last up to three days.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some hair care tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to restore moisture.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair regularly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve curly hair without using heat or chemicals. Experiment with different methods and products to find the ones that work best for your hair type. Remember to take good care of your curls to keep them healthy and bouncy!
