What Haircut Makes Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Frank Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your round face? Do you wish you could find a haircut that makes your face look thinner? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss what haircut can help you achieve your desired look. We'll cover everything from the best styles to how to care for your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

The right haircut can do wonders for your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to look for hairstyles that elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline. The key is to find a style that flatters your features while also complementing your personal style.

First, it's important to understand your face shape. To determine whether you have a round face, look in the mirror and note the following characteristics:

  • Your face is approximately the same width and length
  • Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face
  • Your jawline is rounded rather than angular

Once you've determined that you have a round face, you can start exploring hairstyles that will flatter your features. Keep reading for our top tips and recommendations!

How to

When it comes to choosing a haircut that will make your face look thinner, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Choose a style that adds height to the top of your head, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with volume at the crown.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, which can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for a longer length that grazes your collarbone or falls just below your shoulders.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut. Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face, while blunt bangs can make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different parting styles. A deep side part can help to elongate your face, while a center part can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step

Ready to take the plunge and get a new haircut? Here's what to expect during the process:

  • Consult with your stylist to discuss your goals and preferences.
  • Your stylist will evaluate your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best haircut for you.
  • Your stylist will cut your hair using scissors or a razor, depending on the desired style.
  • After your haircut, your stylist may use styling products to add volume or texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, which can cause damage and breakage.
  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look thinner?

A: It depends on the style. A short haircut with volume at the crown can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face, while a blunt bob that ends at the jawline may not be as flattering.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face, while blunt bangs can make your face appear wider. Talk to your stylist about the best bangs for your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

A: Absolutely! Just be mindful of the placement of your ponytail or bun. A high ponytail or top knot can help to elongate your face, while a low ponytail or messy bun may not be as flattering.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and flattering haircut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, which can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can achieve a slimmer, more flattering face shape. Happy styling!
