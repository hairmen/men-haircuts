How To Wear Frontal Wigs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new way to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a major cut or color change? Frontal wigs are a great option for those who want to experiment with new styles and looks without damaging their natural hair. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to wear frontal wigs and provide tips on hair care to ensure that your wig lasts for a long time.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Frontal Wigs
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Wear Frontal Wigs

Frontal wigs are similar to traditional wigs, but they have a lace frontal closure that mimics the look of a natural hairline. This makes them more versatile and realistic-looking than traditional wigs. Here are the basic steps to follow when wearing a frontal wig:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Prepare your natural hair by braiding it or slicking it back into a low bun or ponytail.
  2. Put on a wig cap to protect your natural hair and help the wig stay in place.
  3. Place the wig on your head and adjust it so that the hairline matches your natural hairline.
  4. Secure the wig using combs or clips around the perimeter of the wig.
  5. Trim the lace frontal to fit your forehead and create a natural-looking hairline.
  6. Style the wig as desired using hot tools, styling products, or accessories.

Tips for Wearing Frontal Wigs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when wearing a frontal wig:

  • Choose a wig cap that matches your skin tone to create a more natural-looking scalp.
  • Use a wig adhesive or tape to secure the wig for a longer-lasting hold.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and parting options to find the look that works best for you.
  • Be gentle when brushing or styling the wig to avoid damaging the hair fibers.

FAQs

Here are some common questions and answers about wearing frontal wigs:

Q: Can I wear a frontal wig if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a wig made from high-quality, hypoallergenic materials and to avoid applying too much pressure or tension to the scalp when securing the wig.

Q: How long can I wear a frontal wig?

A: Frontal wigs can be worn for several weeks at a time, but it's important to remove the wig periodically to care for your natural hair and scalp.

Q: Can I swim or exercise while wearing a frontal wig?

A: Yes, but you should be careful to avoid getting the wig wet or sweaty, as this can damage the hair fibers or cause the wig to slip out of place.

Hair Care for Frontal Wigs

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the quality and longevity of your frontal wig. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash the wig regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Hang the wig to dry or use a low heat setting on a hair dryer to avoid causing heat damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools or styling products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape and prevent tangling.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear your frontal wig and switch up your style as often as you like. Whether you're looking for a dramatic new look or just want to experiment with different hairstyles, a frontal wig is a great way to express your personal style and creativity.
