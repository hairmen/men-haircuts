How To Get Messy Waves For Medium Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to add some effortless, beachy vibes to your medium-length hair, messy waves are the perfect hairstyle for you. This low-maintenance look is easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques. In this guide, we'll show you how to get messy waves for medium hair in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents:

  1. What You'll Need
  2. How to Get Messy Waves for Medium Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Messy Waves
  4. FAQs About Messy Waves for Medium Hair
  5. Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Beautiful Waves

What You'll Need:

Before you get started, make sure you have the following items on hand:

  • A heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips or elastics to section your hair
  • A curling iron or wand
  • Texturizing spray or sea salt spray
  • Hairspray

How to Get Messy Waves for Medium Hair:

Follow these simple steps to achieve messy waves for medium hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Apply a heat protectant spray throughout your hair to protect it from damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Clip or tie up the top section of your hair, leaving the bottom layer loose.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl small sections of your hair away from your face. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 4: Repeat Steps 2 and 3

Release the top section of your hair and repeat steps 2 and 3 until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Add Texture

Spray texturizing or sea salt spray throughout your hair to create a messy, beachy look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Spray hairspray throughout your hair to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Waves:

Here are a few tips to keep your messy waves looking great all day:

  • Avoid brushing your hair after curling to maintain the messy texture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate your curls and create more volume.
  • Refresh your waves throughout the day with a texturizing or sea salt spray.

FAQs About Messy Waves for Medium Hair:

Here are some common questions and answers about messy waves:

Q: Can I get messy waves if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can! Use a curling iron or wand to create waves in your straight hair.

Q: How long will my messy waves last?

A: Your waves should last all day if you use the right products and techniques.

Q: Can I achieve messy waves without heat styling?

A: Yes, you can! Braid your damp hair before bed and release the braids in the morning for natural-looking waves.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Beautiful Waves:

Here are a few tips to keep your waves looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, messy waves for your medium-length hair. Whether you're headed to the beach or just want a low-maintenance hairstyle, messy waves are a great choice.
