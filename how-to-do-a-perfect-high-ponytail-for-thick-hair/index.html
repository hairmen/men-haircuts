How To Do A Perfect High Ponytail For Thick Hair

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick hair, a high ponytail can be a great way to keep it out of your face and look stylish at the same time. However, getting a perfect high ponytail can be a challenge. In this article, we'll show you how to do a perfect high ponytail for thick hair step by step.

Description

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. It's simple, yet elegant and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. This hairstyle is perfect for those with thick hair as it keeps the hair out of your face and off your neck.

How to

To get a perfect high ponytail for thick hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step is to start with clean, dry hair. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would, and then blow-dry it until it's fully dry.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Once your hair is dry, brush it thoroughly to remove any knots or tangles. Make sure you brush your hair from the root to the tip to ensure that it's smooth and free of any bumps.

Step 3: Gather your hair

Next, gather all of your hair at the crown of your head. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: Secure your hair

Once your hair is gathered, secure it with a hair tie. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort or headaches.

Step 5: Tease your ponytail

To give your ponytail more volume, use a comb to tease the hair at the base of your ponytail. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look.

Step 6: Wrap a section of hair around the hair tie

To hide the hair tie and give your ponytail a more polished look, take a small section of hair from the bottom of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie. Use a bobby pin to secure the end of the wrapped hair underneath the ponytail.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish your high ponytail with a light spritz of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail for thick hair:

  • Use a hair tie that's strong enough to hold your hair in place, but gentle enough not to cause damage.
  • Tease your hair at the base of your ponytail to create more volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Wrap a section of hair around the hair tie to hide it and give your ponytail a more polished look.

FAQ

Q: Can I do a high ponytail with wet hair?

A: It's best to start with clean, dry hair when doing a high ponytail. Wet hair is more prone to breakage, and it's harder to get a smooth, polished look with wet hair.

Q: How high should I do my ponytail?

A: The height of your ponytail is up to you. If you have a long face, a higher ponytail will help elongate your features. If you have a round face, a lower ponytail will help balance out your features.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure you're using a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or using hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends, and eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.
