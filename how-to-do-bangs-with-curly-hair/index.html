How To Do Bangs With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair always getting in the way of your bangs? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many curly-haired individuals struggle with achieving the perfect bangs. But fear not, because we're here to help! In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do bangs with curly hair. Let's get started!

How to:

Step 1: Determine the Type of Bangs You Want

The first step in achieving perfect bangs is to determine the type of bangs you want. There are many different types of bangs, including blunt, side-swept, and curtain bangs. Consider your face shape and the look you're going for when deciding on the type of bangs to go for.

Step 2: Cut Your Bangs Dry

When cutting your bangs, it's important to cut them dry. This is because curly hair shrinks when it dries, and cutting your bangs while wet can result in them being too short. Use sharp scissors and cut your bangs bit by bit to avoid cutting too much off at once.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, it's time to style them. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your bangs while scrunching them upward. This will help to enhance your curls and prevent them from falling flat.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add more definition to your bangs, you can use a curling iron to enhance your curls. Use a small curling iron and wrap each strand of hair around it, holding it in place for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 5: Use a Hair Spray

To keep your bangs in place all day, use a hair spray. Choose a hair spray that is specifically designed for curly hair to avoid weighing your curls down.

Tips:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different types of bangs to find the perfect style for you.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls before cutting your bangs.
  • Be patient and take your time when cutting your bangs.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to prevent your curls from falling flat.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause frizz.

FAQ:

Can I cut my bangs while my hair is wet?

We don't recommend cutting your bangs while your hair is wet, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries. This can result in your bangs being shorter than you intended.

How often should I trim my bangs?

We recommend trimming your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

What type of hair spray should I use?

Choose a hair spray that is specifically designed for curly hair to avoid weighing your curls down.

Hair Care:

When it comes to curly hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free. And don't forget to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

And that's it! With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve perfect bangs with your curly hair. Happy styling!
