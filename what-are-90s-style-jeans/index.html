What Are 90S Style Jeans

Written by Daniel Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fashion enthusiast who loves to keep up with the latest trends? If yes, then you might have heard about the comeback of the 90s style jeans. These jeans were popular during the 90s and are making a comeback in 2023. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about these jeans.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Wear 90s Style Jeans
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing 90s Style Jeans
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for the 90s Look

Description

The 90s style jeans are characterized by their high-waisted, loose-fitting design. They were popularized by celebrities like Drew Barrymore and Britney Spears. These jeans were often paired with crop tops, oversized t-shirts, and chunky sneakers. They were also commonly worn with a belt to accentuate the waist.

The 90s style jeans were a departure from the tight-fitting jeans of the 80s. They were more comfortable and allowed for a greater range of movement. These jeans were also versatile and could be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to Wear 90s Style Jeans

Wearing 90s style jeans can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're used to wearing skinny jeans. However, with the right styling, anyone can pull off this trend. Here are some tips on how to wear 90s style jeans:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by selecting a pair of high-waisted, loose-fitting jeans. Look for jeans that fit well around the waist and hips but have a relaxed fit around the legs.
  2. Pair your jeans with a crop top or oversized t-shirt. You can also wear a loose-fitting blouse or sweater.
  3. If you want to accentuate your waist, wear a belt. Opt for a chunky belt in a contrasting color to your jeans.
  4. For shoes, you can wear chunky sneakers, combat boots, or even heels. It all depends on the occasion and your personal style.
  5. Accessorize with statement jewelry, like hoop earrings or a chunky necklace.
  6. Finish off your look with a bold lipstick or eyeshadow. The 90s were all about experimenting with makeup, so don't be afraid to go bold.

Tips for Wearing 90s Style Jeans

Here are some additional tips for wearing 90s style jeans:

  • Experiment with different washes and colors. You don't have to stick to traditional blue denim.
  • Roll up the cuffs of your jeans for a more casual look.
  • Try layering a denim jacket over your outfit for a double denim look.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles. You can pair 90s style jeans with a modern crop top or an oversized blazer.

FAQs

Q: Are 90s style jeans only for women?

A: No, 90s style jeans are for everyone. Men can also rock this trend by pairing high-waisted jeans with a graphic t-shirt and sneakers.

Q: What body types look best in 90s style jeans?

A: 90s style jeans are flattering on all body types. The high-waisted design can accentuate the waist, while the loose fit around the legs can be more forgiving on the thighs and hips.

Q: Can I wear 90s style jeans to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace allows for casual attire, you can wear 90s style jeans with a blouse or blazer. However, if your workplace has a more formal dress code, it's best to stick to traditional trousers.

Hair Care Tips for the 90s Look

The 90s were known for their iconic hairstyles, like the Rachel cut and the high ponytail. If you want to complete your 90s look, here are some hair care tips:

  • Invest in a good hair straightener or curling iron to achieve sleek, straight hair or loose waves.
  • Experiment with hair accessories, like scrunchies and butterfly clips.
  • If you want to try the high ponytail, use a hair tie that won't damage your hair. Avoid using tight elastics or rubber bands.
  • For a more relaxed look, try a messy bun or half-up half-down style.

So there you have it, everything you need to know about 90s style jeans. Whether you're a fan of the trend or not, it's always fun to experiment with different styles and make them your own. Happy styling!
