Are you looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut? One popular style is the fade. Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Before starting the haircut, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can make it difficult to cut accurately.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Using the clippers with the longest guard, cut the hair to the desired length. This will be the starting point for the fade.

Step 3: Create a Guideline

Using the clippers without a guard, create a line around the head where you want the fade to end. This will be your guideline for the rest of the haircut.

Step 4: Start the Fade

Using the clippers with a shorter guard, start to fade the hair from the bottom up. Use a fluid motion and gradually decrease the length of the guard as you move upwards.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once youâve reached the guideline, use scissors or clippers to blend the hair between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair at the bottom.

Step 6: Refine the Fade

Use a comb and scissors to refine the fade and make sure there are no harsh lines or uneven spots.

Step 7: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the hair on top to the desired length.

Step 8: Style the Hair

Style the hair as desired using products such as wax, pomade, or gel.

Tips for Cutting Fade

  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Always start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down for a smooth transition.
  • Use a comb to make sure the hair is even and to blend the fade.
  • Remember to oil your clippers to keep them running smoothly.
