Why Does My Hair Lose Color So Fast

Written by Aprilia Sep 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Have you ever dyed your hair and noticed that the color fades away within a few washes? This can be frustrating, especially if you want your vibrant color to last longer. In this article, we will discuss why does my hair lose color so fast and what you can do to prevent it.

Description

Hair color can fade away for several reasons. It can be due to the use of harsh shampoos, excessive heat styling, exposure to the sun, and even genetics. However, the most common reason for hair color fading is the use of chemical treatments like relaxers and perms.

How to Prevent Hair Color from Fading

If you want your hair color to last longer, you need to take some precautions. Here are some tips that can help you prevent your hair color from fading:

1. Use Color-Safe Shampoos

When you wash your hair with regular shampoos, it can strip away the color from your hair. Therefore, it is essential to use color-safe shampoos that are gentle on your hair and help preserve the color.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make the color fade away quickly. Avoid using flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers as much as possible. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can also cause your hair color to fade away. Therefore, it is essential to protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

4. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip away the natural oils from your hair, making it dry and brittle. Try to limit your hair washing routine to 2-3 times a week to prevent the color from fading away too quickly.

Step by Step Guide to Prevent Hair Color from Fading

Here is a step-by-step guide that can help you prevent your hair color from fading:

1. Choose the Right Color

When selecting a hair color, choose a shade that suits your skin tone and complements your natural hair color. A professional colorist can help you choose the right shade for your hair.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and prevent the color from fading away.

3. Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can strip away the color from your hair, so avoid swimming in chlorinated pools. If you must swim, wear a swimming cap or use a protective spray that can help prevent the color from fading away.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Color Vibrant

Here are some additional tips that can help keep your hair color vibrant:

1. Use a Leave-in Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help protect your hair from damage and keep it hydrated. Use a leave-in conditioner after washing your hair to keep it soft and healthy.

2. Use a Color-Enhancing Shampoo

A color-enhancing shampoo can help boost the color of your hair and keep it vibrant. Use a color-enhancing shampoo once a week to keep your hair color looking fresh.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair color look dull and faded. Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent the color from fading.

FAQs

1. Can I prevent my hair color from fading?

Yes, you can prevent your hair color from fading by using color-safe shampoos, avoiding heat styling, protecting your hair from the sun, and limiting your hair washing routine.

2. How long does hair color last?

The duration of hair color depends on several factors like the type of hair color, the health of your hair, and your hair washing routine. On average, hair color can last between 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Color-Treated Hair

If you have color-treated hair, you need to take extra care of your hair to prevent the color from fading away. Here are some additional tips that can help:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfate-free shampoos are gentle on color-treated hair and help preserve the color. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and prevent the color from fading.

2. Avoid Hot Water

Hot water can open up the hair cuticles and cause the color to fade away quickly. Use lukewarm water when washing your hair to prevent the color from fading away too quickly.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help nourish your hair and prevent the color from fading away. Apply a hair mask to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

In conclusion, hair color can fade away due to several reasons, but you can prevent it from fading away quickly by taking some precautions. Use color-safe shampoos, avoid heat styling, protect your hair from the sun, and limit your hair washing routine to prevent the color from fading away too quickly.
