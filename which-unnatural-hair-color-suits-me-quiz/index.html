Which Unnatural Hair Color Suits Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Hair Color

Written by Petter Jun 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Have you ever thought about dyeing your hair an unnatural color but have no idea which color would look good on you? Well, you're in luck! We have created a quiz that will help you find the perfect hair color for you. In this article, we will guide you on how to take the quiz, how to prepare for the new hair color, and how to take care of your hair after dyeing it.

How To Take The Quiz

The quiz is super easy to take, and it won't take much of your time. You will be asked a series of questions regarding your skin tone, eye color, and current hair color. Based on your answers, the quiz will suggest the best unnatural hair color for you. You can take the quiz as many times as you want until you get your desired result.

Step By Step

1. Go to the quiz page.

2. Answer the questions truthfully.

3. Wait for the quiz to suggest the best unnatural hair color for you.

4. Repeat the quiz as many times as you want.

5. Choose your desired hair color and prepare for the dyeing process.

Tips

Before taking the quiz, make sure to have a clear idea of what you want. Search for inspiration pictures online, and have an idea of the hair color you want to achieve. Also, keep in mind that maintaining an unnatural hair color requires more upkeep than natural hair colors. You will need to invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

FAQ

Q: What if I don't like the hair color suggested by the quiz?

A: You can retake the quiz as many times as you want until you get your desired result. Don't worry; the quiz is designed to suggest the best hair color for you based on your answers.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's always best to consult a professional hairstylist before dyeing your hair. They can guide you on the best hair dye to use and how to take care of your hair after dyeing it.

Hair Care After Dyeing Your Hair

After dyeing your hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.

- Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can cause the hair color to fade faster.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished.

- Avoid using hot tools frequently, as they can damage the hair and cause the color to fade.

With these tips, you can maintain your new hair color and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

Now that you know which unnatural hair color suits you, it's time to get ready for the dyeing process. Remember to take care of your hair properly after dyeing it to keep it healthy and vibrant. With the right hair care products and a little bit of effort, you can achieve the hair color of your dreams.
