What Is The Best Head Shape

Written by Frank Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what is the best head shape? Look no further, because in this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about head shapes and what makes them the best.

Description

Head shape refers to the overall shape of your skull. It is determined by the size and proportions of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The best head shape is one that is balanced and symmetrical.

Having a balanced and symmetrical head shape can enhance your facial features and make you look more attractive. It can also make it easier to style your hair.

How to Determine Your Head Shape

The first step in determining your head shape is to look in a mirror and examine the proportions of your face. Take note of the size and shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

You can also use a measuring tape to measure the circumference of your head. Divide the measurement by the distance between your temples to determine your head shape.

Step by Step Guide to Determine Your Head Shape

  1. Stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Examine the proportions of your face.
  3. Take note of the size and shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Measure the circumference of your head.
  5. Divide the measurement by the distance between your temples.
  6. Compare your results to the head shape categories below.

Head Shape Categories

Head shapes are typically categorized into four main categories: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped.

Round Head Shape

A round head shape is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones with a rounded jawline. People with round head shapes have a face that is almost as wide as it is long.

Oval Head Shape

An oval head shape is characterized by a slightly wider forehead and tapering to a narrow chin. People with oval head shapes have a face that is longer than it is wide.

Square Head Shape

A square head shape is characterized by a prominent forehead and strong jawline. People with square head shapes have a face that is almost as wide as it is long.

Heart-Shaped Head Shape

A heart-shaped head shape is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones tapering down to a narrow chin. People with heart-shaped head shapes have a face that is wider at the temples and narrows at the jawline.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Your Head Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for your head shape can enhance your features and make you look more attractive. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Round head shape: Choose a style that adds height to the crown of your head, such as a pompadour or quiff.
  • Oval head shape: Almost any hairstyle works well with an oval head shape.
  • Square head shape: Choose a style that softens your features, such as a textured crop or side-swept fringe.
  • Heart-shaped head shape: Choose a style that adds volume to the lower half of your face, such as a layered bob or beachy waves.

FAQ

What is the most attractive head shape?

The most attractive head shape is one that is balanced and symmetrical. However, beauty is subjective, and what one person finds attractive may differ from another.

Can head shape be changed?

Head shape is determined by bone structure and cannot be changed without surgery. However, hairstyles and grooming techniques can enhance your head shape and make it appear more balanced.

Hair Care for Different Head Shapes

Regardless of your head shape, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care based on head shape:

  • Round head shape: Use volumizing products to add height to the crown of your head.
  • Oval head shape: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as it can make your face appear longer.
  • Square head shape: Keep your hair shorter on the sides and longer on top to balance out your features.
  • Heart-shaped head shape: Add volume to the lower half of your face with layers or waves.

In conclusion, while there may not be a one-size-fits-all answer to what is the best head shape, a balanced and symmetrical head shape is generally considered the most attractive. However, regardless of your head shape, there are hairstyles and grooming techniques that can enhance your features and make you look your best.
