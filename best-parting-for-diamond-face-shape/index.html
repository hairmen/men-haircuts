Are you tired of trying different hairstyles and still not able to find the perfect one for your diamond face shape? Well, worry no more as we are here to help you out. In this article, we will be discussing the best parting for diamond face shape and how to achieve it. So, letâs get started.

Description

Diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin with wide cheekbones. The aim of choosing a hairstyle for diamond face shape is to balance out the wide cheekbones and create an illusion of a more prominent forehead and chin. One of the easiest ways to achieve this is by choosing the right parting.

How to

The first step to finding the best parting for diamond face shape is to determine where your natural parting is. This can easily be done by wetting your hair and creating a middle parting with a comb. Once you have found your natural parting, you can experiment with different partings to see which one suits you the best.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wet your hair
Step 2: Create a middle parting with a comb
Step 3: Experiment with different partings
Step 4: Choose the one that suits you the best

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best parting for diamond face shape:

  • A side parting is the best option for diamond face shape as it creates an illusion of a more prominent forehead and chin.
  • Avoid center parting as it accentuates the wide cheekbones and makes the face look even wider.
  • You can experiment with deep side parting and zig-zag parting to add some dimension to your hair.
  • If you have short hair, go for a side parting that is closer to the crown of your head.

FAQ

What is the best parting for diamond face shape?

The best parting for diamond face shape is a side parting. It creates an illusion of a more prominent forehead and chin while balancing out the wide cheekbones.

Can I wear a center parting with diamond face shape?

Avoid center parting if you have a diamond face shape as it accentuates the wide cheekbones and makes the face look even wider.

Can I experiment with different partings?

Yes, you can experiment with different partings to see which one suits you the best. You can try deep side parting and zig-zag parting to add some dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Choosing the right parting is just the first step towards achieving the perfect hairstyle for diamond face shape. It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
