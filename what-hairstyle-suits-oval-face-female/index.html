What Hairstyle Suits Oval Face Female

Written by Daniel Nov 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are someone with an oval face, then you are lucky because you can try out a variety of hairstyles that will perfectly suit your face shape. An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and prominent cheekbones. However, choosing the right hairstyle can be tricky because you don't want to hide your beautiful face shape.

Description

This blog post will guide you through the different hairstyles that will enhance your oval face shape. You will learn about some key features that make a hairstyle suitable for an oval face shape, and you will also get some tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it healthy.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for an Oval Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for an oval face shape can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. However, there are a few key features that make a hairstyle suitable for an oval face shape:

1. Length

Almost any length of hair can suit an oval face shape, but medium to long hair tends to look the best. This is because longer hair can elongate your face and enhance your features.

2. Layers

Layers can add volume to your hair and help frame your face. However, it's important to avoid too many layers because it can make your face appear longer.

3. Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to balance out your face shape and draw attention to your eyes. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a tape measure to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are almost the same, then you have an oval face shape.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

You should also consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thick hair, then a layered haircut can help reduce the volume. If you have thin hair, then blunt cuts can make your hair appear fuller.

3. Choose the Right Length

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. If you have an oval face shape, then almost any length of hair can suit you. However, longer hair tends to look the best.

4. Add Layers

Adding layers can help frame your face and add volume to your hair. However, avoid too many layers because it can make your face appear longer.

5. Consider Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to balance out your face shape and draw attention to your eyes. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it healthy:

1. Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for an oval face shape?

The best hairstyles for an oval face shape are those that enhance your features and complement your face shape. Some examples include long layers, side-swept bangs, and a blunt bob.

Can I pull off short hair with an oval face shape?

Yes, you can pull off short hair with an oval face shape. However, it's important to choose the right length and style that complements your face shape.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, then you may need to wash it more often. However, if you have dry hair, then you can wash it less frequently.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and hydrated.

3. Avoid Using Hot Styling Tools Too Often

Avoid using hot styling tools too often because it can damage your hair.

4. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

5. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep can also help promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, an oval face shape is versatile and can suit a variety of hairstyles. The key is to choose a hairstyle that enhances your features and complements your face shape. Follow these tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape, and maintain your hair with proper care and maintenance.
