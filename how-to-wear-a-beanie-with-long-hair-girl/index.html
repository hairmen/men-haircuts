How To Wear A Beanie With Long Hair Girl

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long hair and want to wear a beanie, you may be wondering how to do it without looking messy or frumpy. The good news is that there are plenty of ways to wear a beanie with long hair that look stylish and put-together. In this blog post, we'll walk you through how to wear a beanie with long hair girl, step by step.

Description

Beanies are a great accessory for any season, but they can be particularly useful during the winter months when you want to keep your head and ears warm. If you have long hair, however, wearing a beanie can be a bit of a challenge. You don't want your hair to get tangled or flattened, and you don't want your beanie to look sloppy. With a few simple tips, though, you can wear a beanie with long hair girl and look great.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Beanie

The first step to wearing a beanie with long hair girl is to choose the right beanie. Look for a beanie that is slouchy or oversized, as this will give you more room for your hair. Try to avoid beanies that are too tight or have a lot of structure, as these can flatten your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before putting on your beanie, make sure your hair is prepped and ready to go. Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles, and use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some volume and texture. This will help your hair hold its shape under the beanie.

Step 3: Put on the Beanie

When putting on your beanie, make sure to pull it down over your forehead and ears. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from getting tangled. If your beanie is too tight, gently tug on it to loosen it up a bit.

Step 4: Adjust Your Hair

Once your beanie is in place, you can adjust your hair to create the look you want. You can leave your hair down and loose, or pull it back into a low ponytail or messy bun. You can also create a side part or a center part, depending on your preference.

Tips

  • Experiment with different beanie styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair from the rough texture of the beanie.
  • Try adding some braids or twists to your hair for added texture and interest.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair some extra lift before putting on the beanie.

FAQ

Can I wear a beanie with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, beanies can look great with curly hair. Just make sure to use a texturizing spray or mousse to help define your curls before putting on the beanie.

What if my hair gets flattened under the beanie?

If your hair gets flattened under the beanie, don't worry! Simply take off the beanie and fluff up your hair with your fingers. You can also use a volumizing spray or dry shampoo to add some lift and texture back into your hair.

Can I wear a beanie with bangs?

Yes, you can definitely wear a beanie with bangs! Simply pull your beanie down over your forehead, and let your bangs peek out. You can also sweep your bangs to the side for a more polished look.

Hair Care

When wearing a beanie with long hair girl, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Make sure to brush your hair gently and use a wide-toothed comb to remove any tangles. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, and avoid using heat tools too often. With the right care, your hair will look great both with and without a beanie!

Now that you know how to wear a beanie with long hair girl, you can rock this stylish accessory all winter long. Whether you're running errands or hitting the slopes, a beanie is the perfect way to keep your head and ears warm while looking chic and trendy.
