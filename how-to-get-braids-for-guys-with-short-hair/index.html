How To Get Braids For Guys With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Braids have long been a popular hairstyle for women, but in recent years, more and more men have been embracing the look. Braids can be a great option for guys with short hair who want to change up their style. If you're interested in getting braids, but you're not sure where to start, this guide is for you. Read on for step-by-step instructions, tips, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Braids for Guys with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Braids
  • FAQs

How to Get Braids for Guys with Short Hair

If you're considering getting braids, the first step is to decide what kind of braids you want. There are many different styles to choose from, including box braids, cornrows, and twists. Once you've decided on a style, you'll need to find a stylist who specializes in braiding. Look for someone with experience in braiding men's hair, and don't be afraid to ask for references.

Before you get your braids, you'll need to prepare your hair. This usually involves washing and conditioning your hair to remove any dirt or product buildup. It's important to make sure your hair is completely dry before you start braiding, as wet hair can lead to mold or mildew.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting braids for guys with short hair:

  1. Decide on the type of braids you want.
  2. Find a stylist who specializes in braiding men's hair.
  3. Wash and condition your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  5. Section your hair into small, even sections.
  6. Start braiding your hair, using a small amount of gel or pomade to help hold the braids in place.
  7. Continue braiding until all of your hair is braided.
  8. Trim any loose ends with scissors.
  9. Apply a small amount of oil to your scalp to keep it moisturized.
  10. Repeat the process every few weeks to maintain your braids.

Tips for Maintaining Braids

Once you have your braids, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Wrap your hair in a scarf or cap at night to prevent frizz.
  • Don't leave your braids in for too long, as this can lead to breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: How long do braids last?

A: It depends on how well you take care of them, but braids typically last anywhere from 2-8 weeks.

Q: Can I get braids if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: It's possible, but you may need to take extra precautions to avoid irritation. Talk to your stylist before getting braids if you have a sensitive scalp.

Q: Can I swim with braids?

A: Yes, but it's important to wear a swim cap to protect your braids from chlorine and salt water.

Hair Care for Braids

Even with the best care, braids can still cause some damage to your hair. To keep your hair healthy, it's important to take care of it before, during, and after you get your braids. Here are a few tips:

  • Deep condition your hair before getting braids to strengthen it.
  • Avoid tight braids, which can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Take a break between braids to give your hair a chance to recover.

With these tips and instructions, you should be well on your way to getting the braids you've always wanted. Just remember to take care of your hair, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
