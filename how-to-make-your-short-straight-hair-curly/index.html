How To Make Your Short Straight Hair Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short and straight hair, you might want to change up your look from time to time. One of the most popular ways to do this is by making your hair curly. However, it can be challenging to know how to do this, especially if you're not used to styling your hair. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make your short straight hair curly. We'll also give you some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect curly hairstyle.

How to Make Your Short Straight Hair Curly

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's essential to understand how you can make your short straight hair curly. There are several ways to do this, including:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using hot rollers
  • Using a hair straightener
  • Using hair rollers
  • Using a perm

In this article, we'll focus on using a curling iron, as it's the most common method and the easiest to do at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your short straight hair curly:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to prepare your hair for styling and prevent it from drying out.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent heat damage. You can use a spray or a serum, depending on your preference. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a hairdryer to dry your hair until it's completely dry. Make sure to brush your hair while blow-drying it to create volume and prevent tangles.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds and release it. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

Once you've curled your hair, apply a hairspray to hold the curls in place. You can also use a texture spray to add volume and definition to your curls.

Tips for Making Your Short Straight Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curly hairstyle:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel to create tight curls.
  • Use a curling iron with a large barrel to create loose waves.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can damage your hair and make your curls look unnatural.
  • Don't use too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

How long will my curls last?

It depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, your curls will last for a few hours to a day. You can use hairspray or other styling products to make your curls last longer.

Can I make my hair curly without heat?

Yes, you can use hair rollers or a perm to make your hair curly without heat. However, these methods may take longer and require more maintenance than using a curling iron.

How often should I curl my hair?

You should avoid curling your hair every day, as this can damage your hair. Instead, try to curl your hair once or twice a week and use heat protectant to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's essential to take care of it to prevent damage and maintain your curls. Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hair elastics or tight hair accessories, as these can damage your hair and create frizz.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and maintain your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your short straight hair curly and achieve the perfect curly hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling methods to find the one that works best for you.
