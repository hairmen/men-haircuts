Mens Short Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair A Guide For Men In 2023

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will suit your thick wavy hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be discussing the best mens short hairstyles for thick wavy hair. Whether you have a formal event or just want to change up your look, we have got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can give you a unique look, it can also be difficult to manage. Mens short hairstyles for thick wavy hair can help you embrace your hair's natural texture and make styling a breeze. These hairstyles are low maintenance and require minimal effort, making them perfect for men who don't have time to spend on their hair.

How to Choose the Right Mens Short Hairstyle for Thick Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the different mens short hairstyles for thick wavy hair, it is essential to understand how to choose the right hairstyle for you. The first thing you need to consider is your face shape. Not all hairstyles will suit every face shape. Secondly, consider your hair type and texture. Thick wavy hair requires a different approach than thin straight hair. Lastly, think about your lifestyle. Are you a busy man who doesn't have time to style his hair every day? Or, do you have the time to experiment with different styles?

Step by Step Guide to Mens Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

1. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic mens short hairstyle that is perfect for thick wavy hair. It is effortless to maintain and looks great on most face shapes. To achieve the buzz cut, ask your barber to use clippers with a number 2 or 3 guard. This will leave about Â¼ inch of hair on your head. You can also choose to go shorter or longer depending on your preference.

2. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a versatile mens short hairstyle that works well with thick wavy hair. It is a classic military-inspired cut that is short on the sides and longer on top. To achieve the crew cut, ask your barber to use clippers with a number 3 or 4 guard on the sides and back. The top should be left longer and trimmed with scissors to add texture.

3. The French Crop

The French crop is a trendy mens short hairstyle that is perfect for thick wavy hair. It is a textured cut that is short on the sides and longer on top. To achieve the French crop, ask your barber to use scissors to cut the top to about 2-3 inches in length. The sides should be trimmed with clippers with a number 1 or 2 guard.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Mens short hairstyles for thick wavy hair are low maintenance, but they still require some upkeep. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQs

What is the best mens short hairstyle for thick wavy hair?

The best mens short hairstyle for thick wavy hair depends on your personal preference and face shape. Some popular options include the buzz cut, crew cut, and French crop.

Can I style my mens short hairstyle for thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can style your mens short hairstyle for thick wavy hair. Use a lightweight styling product like a pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

Hair Care for Mens Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential to maintain your mens short hairstyle for thick wavy hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

In conclusion, mens short hairstyles for thick wavy hair are a great way to embrace your hair's natural texture and make styling a breeze. Whether you choose the buzz cut, crew cut, or French crop, make sure to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. With proper hair care, your mens short hairstyle for thick wavy hair will look great and require minimal effort.
