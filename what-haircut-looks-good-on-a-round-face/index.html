What Haircut Looks Good On A Round Face

Are you tired of experimenting with different haircuts and styles that don't seem to fit your round face? Look no further! This article will guide you through the best haircuts that complement your facial shape.

  • Top Haircuts for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before diving into the perfect haircuts for round faces, it's crucial to determine your facial shape. You can do this by following these simple steps:

  1. Take a picture of yourself facing the camera with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face with a pen or marker. Be sure to include your hairline and jawline.
  3. Compare your traced outline to the following facial shapes: oval, square, heart, diamond, and round.

If your traced outline resembles a circle with equal length and width, then you have a round face.

Top Haircuts for Round Faces

The key to finding the perfect haircut for a round face is to add angles and volume in all the right places. Here are some haircuts that will complement your facial shape:

Long Layers

Long layers that start at the collarbone or below add length to the face and create a slimming effect. Be sure to avoid blunt cuts as they can make the face appear rounder.

Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs add angles to the face and draw attention to your eyes. They also create the illusion of a longer face.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back creates angles and volume. This cut also draws attention to the jawline and cheekbones.

Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides creates height and volume. This cut also emphasizes the cheekbones and jawline.

Beach Waves

Beach waves create volume and texture, which can make the face appear slimmer. Use a curling iron or sea salt spray to achieve this look.

Tips for Styling Round Faces

Now that you know the best haircuts for round faces, here are some tips for styling:

Avoid Middle Parts

Middle parts can make the face appear wider, so opt for a side part instead.

Use Volume

Adding volume to the crown of the head can create the illusion of a longer face. Use a volumizing spray or mousse to achieve this look.

Accessorize

Accessories like headbands and hair clips can draw attention away from the roundness of the face. Be sure to use them strategically.

FAQs

Can I still have bangs with a round face?

Absolutely! Just be sure to opt for side-swept bangs instead of blunt bangs.

Should I avoid short haircuts with a round face?

No, not necessarily. Just be sure to choose a short haircut with angles and volume, like a layered pixie cut.

Can I still wear my hair straight with a round face?

Yes, you can! Just be sure to add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Regardless of your facial shape, hair care is crucial for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage hair and make it appear lifeless. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat tools daily.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on hair than a brush and can prevent breakage.

Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help restore moisture and strengthen hair. Use one once a week for best results.

In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut for a round face is all about creating angles and volume in the right places. Use these tips and tricks to find a style that complements your facial shape and accentuates your best features. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
