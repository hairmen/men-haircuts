How To Air Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of blow-drying and straightening your hair every day? Do you have wavy, frizzy hair that seems impossible to tame? If so, you're not alone! Air drying your hair is a great way to achieve natural, effortless waves without damaging your hair with heat tools. In this guide, we'll show you how to air dry wavy frizzy hair in a way that will leave you with gorgeous, healthy-looking locks.

How to Air Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair

Air drying your hair may seem like a simple task, but there are a few things you can do to ensure that your waves look their best. Here are the basic steps to air dry wavy frizzy hair:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Before you start air drying your hair, make sure it's clean and towel-dried. Gently blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water, but avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

2. Apply a styling product

To help define your natural waves and reduce frizz, apply a styling product to your damp hair. Look for products specifically designed for wavy or curly hair, such as mousse, gel, or curl cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

3. Use a diffuser attachment

If you're using a hair dryer, attach a diffuser to help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use the lowest heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser attachment as you dry your hair.

4. Let your hair air dry the rest of the way

Once your hair is partially dry, let the rest air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz. You can also gently scrunch your hair from time to time to help define your waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to air drying wavy frizzy hair:

1. Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Choose products that are specifically formulated for wavy or curly hair, as they will help define your natural waves and reduce frizz.

2. Gently Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently blot it dry with a soft towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

3. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair to help define your natural waves and reduce frizz. Choose a product that is specifically formulated for wavy or curly hair, such as mousse, gel, or curl cream.

4. Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you're using a hair dryer, attach a diffuser to help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use the lowest heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser attachment as you dry your hair.

5. Let Your Hair Air Dry the Rest of the Way

Once your hair is partially dry, let the rest air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz. You can also gently scrunch your hair from time to time to help define your waves.

Tips for Air Drying Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you air dry wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair, as it's gentler on your hair than a regular towel.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before applying styling products to help reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I air dry my hair overnight?

A: Yes, you can air dry your hair overnight. However, you should avoid going to bed with wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Q: How long does it take to air dry wavy frizzy hair?

A: The length of time it takes to air dry wavy frizzy hair can vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours.

Q: Can I use a hair oil on my wavy frizzy hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair oil on your wavy frizzy hair to help reduce frizz and add shine. Just be sure to use a small amount, as too much oil can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Wavy Frizzy Hair

If you have wavy frizzy hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for wavy or curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can air dry your wavy frizzy hair and achieve beautiful, natural waves that last all day. With a little practice and patience, you can say goodbye to heat tools and hello to healthy, gorgeous locks!
