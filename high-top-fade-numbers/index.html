High Top Fade Numbers A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In recent years, the high top fade has made a comeback in the world of men's hairstyles. This classic cut, made famous in the 80s and 90s, is characterised by its long top and short sides. But did you know that there are different high top fade numbers that determine the length of the hair on the top of the head? In this article, we'll take a closer look at these numbers and what they mean.

What are High Top Fade Numbers?

High top fade numbers refer to the length of hair on the top of the head. The numbers range from 1 to 8, with 1 being the shortest and 8 being the longest. The number you choose will determine how much hair is left on the top of your head.

How to Get a High Top Fade

If you're interested in getting a high top fade, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. Here's what you can expect:

Step 1: Consultation

Your hairstylist will start by discussing your desired look and the length you want your hair to be. They may also ask about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides

The stylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head. The length will depend on the high top fade number you choose.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

The stylist will then cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. This is where the high top fade number comes into play.

Step 4: Styling

Once the cut is complete, your hairstylist will style your hair using pomade or gel to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining a High Top Fade

Once you have your high top fade, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a brush or comb to keep hair on the top of your head neat and tidy
  • Use a moisturising shampoo and conditioner to keep hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down hair and make it look greasy

FAQs

What is a high top fade?

A high top fade is a men's hairstyle characterised by a long top and short sides.

What are high top fade numbers?

High top fade numbers refer to the length of hair on the top of the head. The numbers range from 1 to 8.

Can anyone get a high top fade?

While a high top fade can work for many different hair types, it's best suited to those with thick, curly hair.

Hair Care for High Top Fades

To keep your high top fade looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturising shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using heavy products, as these can weigh down hair and make it look greasy

With these tips and a good hairstylist, you'll be able to rock a high top fade with confidence!
