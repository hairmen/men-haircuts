Best Short Hair For Square Faces In 2023

If you have a square face, you may have found it challenging to find the right short hairdo that flatters your features. Luckily, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can accentuate your square jawline and bring out your best features. In this article, we will discuss the best short hair for square faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Pick the Right Short Hair for Square Faces

When it comes to short hair for square faces, the goal is to soften the sharp angles of the face while highlighting your best features. Here are some hairstyles that work well for square faces:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is one of the best short haircuts for square faces. It's short, layered, and textured, which adds softness to your face. The layers should be long and wispy to add movement and volume to your hair.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for square faces. The length of the bob should be just below the chin or shoulder-length, and the layers should be choppy and textured. This will add volume and movement to your hair, which will soften the sharp angles of your face.

3. Shag Cut

A shag cut is a versatile short hairstyle that can work well for square faces. The layers should be long and choppy, which will add volume and movement to your hair. The shag cut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and stylish.

Step-by-step Guide on Styling Short Hair for Square Faces

Now that you have picked the right short hairstyle for your square face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair for square faces:

1. Shampoo and Condition your Hair

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair with a product that is suitable for your hair type. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, leaving it clean and fresh.

2. Blow Dry your Hair

Next, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Make sure to blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of your natural part to add more volume to your hair.

3. Style your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling product that is suitable for your hair type to add texture and hold. Use your fingers to style your hair, focusing on creating soft waves or messy texture.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Square Faces

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair for square faces:

1. Trim your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are essential for maintaining short hair. It helps keep your hair healthy and prevents split ends. Plan to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

2. Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type to avoid damaging your hair. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Protect your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's essential to use a heat protectant spray before using them. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can dry out your hair and make it look dull.

FAQs about Short Hair for Square Faces

1. Can I still wear bangs with short hair for square faces?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with short hair for square faces. However, it's essential to choose the right type of bangs that flatter your features. Long, side-swept bangs are a great option for square faces.

2. What hair color works well with short hair for square faces?

Any hair color can work well with short hair for square faces, as long as it complements your skin tone. However, highlights or balayage can add dimension and texture to your hair, which can be especially flattering for square faces.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Square Faces

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Short hair may require more frequent washing, which can dry out your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Protect your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so it's essential to protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Avoid Over-washing your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look dry and dull. Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean without over-drying it.

In conclusion, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter square faces. By picking the right short hairstyle for your face shape and following the styling and hair care tips above, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that brings out your best features.
