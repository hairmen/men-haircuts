How To Make Your Face Look Rounder Hairstyles A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long or square-shaped face, you might want to make your face look rounder. This can be achieved through various hairstyles that add volume and width to your face. In this guide, we will show you how to make your face look rounder with hairstyles that are easy to maintain and style.

Description

Having a round face is considered to be one of the most attractive facial shapes. However, not everyone is blessed with a round face. If you have a long or square-shaped face, you might want to make your face look rounder. This can be achieved through various hairstyles that add volume and width to your face. In this guide, we will show you how to make your face look rounder with hairstyles that are easy to maintain and style.

How to Make Your Face Look Rounder Hairstyles: Step by Step

Step 1: Opt for a Short Hairstyle

Short hairstyles can make your face look rounder by adding volume to the sides of your face. You can go for a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob. Make sure that the length of your hair does not go beyond your chin. This will help to create an illusion of roundness.

Step 2: Add Layers to Your Hair

Adding layers to your hair can help to create volume and make your face look rounder. You can opt for long layers or short layers depending on the length of your hair. Make sure that the layers are not too thick as this can make your face look wider.

Step 3: Go for a Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to make your face look rounder by adding width to your forehead and temples. This can be achieved by sweeping your bangs to one side of your face. You can also go for a wispy bang that is longer on the sides.

Step 4: Try a Curly Hairstyle

Curly hairstyles can help to create volume and add width to your face. You can go for loose curls or tight curls depending on your preference. Make sure that the curls are not too tight as this can make your face look wider.

Tips

  • Use volumizing products such as mousse or hairspray to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back as this can make your face look longer.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my face look rounder with makeup?

A: Yes, you can make your face look rounder with makeup. You can use contouring techniques to create the illusion of a rounder face. However, this requires some skill and practice.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It is recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can cause damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools such as a curling iron or straightener. Finally, get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
