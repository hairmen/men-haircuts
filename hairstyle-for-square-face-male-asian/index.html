Hairstyle For Square Face Male Asian A Guide To Finding The Perfect Look

Written by Frank Nov 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a square-faced male Asian looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! This guide will provide you with everything you need to know to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. From how to determine your face shape to tips on hair care, this guide has got you covered.

  • Hairstyles for Square Face Male Asian

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. To do this, you need to look at the proportions of your face. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with the width and length of the face being roughly equal. To confirm that you have a square face, take a selfie and draw imaginary lines from your hairline to the bottom of your chin and from ear to ear. If the lines are equal, you have a square face.

Hairstyles for Square Face Male Asian

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to look at some hairstyles that will complement your features. Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for square-faced male Asians:

Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. It works well with a square face as it draws attention to the jawline and cheekbones.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to soften the strong features of a square face. They add a touch of femininity to the face and draw attention away from the jawline.

Textured Fringe

A textured fringe is a great way to add dimension to a square face. It softens the strong jawline and draws attention to the eyes.

Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle for square-faced male Asians. It adds volume and texture to the hair, drawing attention away from the strong jawline.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is important if you want to look your best. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, a low-maintenance hairstyle may be the best option for you.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thick hair, a short hairstyle may be more manageable. If you have thin hair, a longer hairstyle may be more flattering.

Consult a Professional

If you’re unsure about which hairstyle is best for you, consult a professional hairstylist. They can provide you with expert advice and help you choose a hairstyle that complements your features.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for square-faced male Asians:

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

Some of the best hairstyles for a square face include short buzz cuts, side swept bangs, textured fringes, and undercuts.

Can I wear long hair with a square face?

Yes, you can wear long hair with a square face. However, it’s important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair to balance out the strong features of your face.

Hair Care for Square Face Male Asian

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for square-faced male Asians:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Choose a hair mask that is suitable for your hair type.

Now that you know everything you need to know about finding the perfect hairstyle for your square face, it’s time to head to the salon and get a new look!
