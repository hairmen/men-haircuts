How To Style Short Layered Hair With Side Bangs

Written by Luffy Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short layered hair with side bangs is a stylish and trendy look that can be versatile for any occasion. Whether you have naturally curly, wavy, or straight hair, this style can work for you. In this post, we will go over how to style short layered hair with side bangs, including tips and tricks for a flawless look.

Description

Short layered hair with side bangs is a popular hairstyle that can be customized to fit any face shape or hair type. The layers add texture and volume, while the side bangs frame the face and give a youthful appearance. This style can be worn straight, wavy or curly, making it a versatile and low-maintenance option for those who want a stylish look without spending hours on their hair.

How to

There are a few steps you can take to style short layered hair with side bangs:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair thoroughly. Then, towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair until it's completely dry. Focus on your bangs, using the brush to create volume and shape. For added volume, lift your hair at the roots and blow dry in an upward motion. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Add Finishing Touches

Finish off your look by adding some hairspray or styling cream to hold your hair in place. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a headband or hair clip, to add some extra flair to your style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short layered hair with side bangs:

  • Use a round brush to create volume and shape when blow drying your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls for a more textured look.
  • Use a styling cream or hairspray to hold your hair in place and prevent frizz.

FAQ

Can short layered hair with side bangs work for any face shape?

Yes, short layered hair with side bangs can be customized to fit any face shape. The layers and side bangs can be adjusted to highlight your best features and flatter your face shape.

How often should I wash short layered hair with side bangs?

It's best to wash short layered hair with side bangs every other day to prevent excessive oil buildup. However, if your hair tends to be on the oily side, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Can I style short layered hair with side bangs without heat styling tools?

Yes, you can style short layered hair with side bangs without heat styling tools. Simply apply some styling cream to your hair and use your fingers to create a textured, tousled look.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered hair with side bangs looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent excessive oil buildup.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, which can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and free of split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your short layered hair with side bangs to perfection. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve a flawless, low-maintenance look that's perfect for any occasion.
