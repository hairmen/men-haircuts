Mens Shoulder Length Hair 2020 How To Style It And Keep It Healthy

Written by Petter Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a man with shoulder-length hair, you're in luck! Shoulder-length hair has been a popular trend among men in 2020, and it shows no signs of slowing down in 2023. In this blog post, we'll cover how to style your shoulder-length hair, tips for keeping it healthy, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder-Length Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Shoulder-Length Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Shoulder-Length Hair

When it comes to styling shoulder-length hair, there are many options to choose from. Here are a few popular styles:

1. Messy Waves

To achieve messy waves, apply a styling product to your damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting, using your fingers to shape your hair as it dries. Once dry, use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

2. Sleek and Straight

If you prefer a more polished look, try straightening your hair with a flat iron. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using the flat iron, and use a comb to straighten each section of hair. Finish with a shine spray for a sleek, polished look.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you want to keep your hair out of your face, try a half-up, half-down style. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair loose, and add some waves or curls for texture.

Remember, the key to styling shoulder-length hair is to experiment and find a style that works for you. Don't be afraid to try new products and techniques!

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder-Length Hair

Now that you know how to style your shoulder-length hair, let's talk about how to keep it healthy. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Since shoulder-length hair is longer than shorter haircuts, it can be more prone to dryness and damage. To keep your hair healthy, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Even if you're growing your hair out, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Additionally, try to limit your use of heat styling tools as much as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can shoulder-length hair be styled for formal occasions?

Absolutely! Shoulder-length hair can be styled in a variety of ways for formal occasions, from sleek updos to loose curls. Just make sure to use products that provide hold and texture.

2. How often should I wash my shoulder-length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. Experiment to find what works best for you.

3. Is it okay to use hair products every day?

It's generally okay to use hair products every day, as long as you're using products that are designed for your hair type and aren't causing any irritation or damage. However, try to avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

4. How can I prevent my shoulder-length hair from tangling?

To prevent tangling, try using a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently comb through your hair after washing it. Additionally, consider using a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to make your hair easier to comb through.

5. Can I dye my shoulder-length hair?

Yes, you can dye your shoulder-length hair. However, keep in mind that dyeing your hair can be damaging, so it's important to take steps to keep your hair healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment after dyeing your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your shoulder-length hair with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!
