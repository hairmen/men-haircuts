How To Make Your Hair Curl Outwards Guys

Written by Christine Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some volume and dimension to your hair, then curling it outwards is a great option. This style is especially popular among guys who want to add some flair to their hair without going overboard. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair curl outwards guys with some simple steps and tips. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Curl Outwards Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curling Your Hair Outwards
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you get started, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or larger, a heat protectant spray, and a comb. Here's how to make your hair curl outwards guys:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the bottom. Clip up the top sections of your hair so they don't get in the way. Use a comb to part your hair into sections that are about 1 inch wide.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, starting at the roots. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair, curling away from your face.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, use a comb to gently brush out your curls. This will give you a more natural look and help the curls to blend together.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To help your curls last longer, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair.

Tips for Curling Your Hair Outwards

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair curl outwards guys:

  • Choose the right curling iron size for your hair length and thickness.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  • Curl your hair away from your face to create an outward curl.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or it will look unnatural.
  • Use a comb to gently brush out your curls for a more natural look.

FAQs

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls should last for several hours, but you may need to touch them up throughout the day to keep them looking fresh.

Q: Can I curl my hair if it's short?

A: Yes, you can curl your hair no matter how short it is. Just make sure you choose a curling iron with a smaller barrel size.

Q: Will curling my hair damage it?

A: Curling your hair can cause damage if you don't use a heat protectant spray and if you use too much heat. Be sure to follow the instructions and use a lower heat setting if you have fine or thin hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know how to make your hair curl outwards guys, you can try this fun and easy style for yourself. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a unique and stylish look that's all your own.
