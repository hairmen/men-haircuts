Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to fade your own hair:

  1. Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair using a number 2 clipper guard. Make sure to go against the grain for a smoother and closer cut.
  2. Next, switch to a number 1 clipper guard and cut the hair just above the line you created with the previous guard.
  3. Blend the hair by using a number 1.5 clipper guard and cutting the hair in between the previous two lengths.
  4. Use a comb to check the length and adjust as needed.
  5. Move on to the top of your hair and comb it forward. Cut the hair to your desired length using scissors.
  6. Use the clippers to blend the top of your hair with the sides and back.
  7. Use a comb and scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a clean and sharp look.
  8. Finish off by cleaning up your neckline and around your ears.
  9. Check your hair in the mirror and make any final adjustments.
  10. Wash your hair to remove any loose hairs and style as desired.
