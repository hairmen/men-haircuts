Description

Are you currently in the awkward stage of growing out your short hair? Are you wondering how to style it without looking like a mess? Fear not, because we have got you covered! In this article, we will guide you on how to style short hair while you're growing it out. We will provide you with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking chic and stylish as it grows. So, let's get started!

How to Style Short Hair While You're Growing It Out

When growing out short hair, it is essential to keep your hair healthy and nourished. This will help promote hair growth and prevent hair breakage. Here are some ways to style your short hair while you're growing it out:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

One of the best ways to style short hair while you're growing it out is to embrace your natural texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, work with it, and don't fight it. Use products that enhance your natural texture, such as sea salt spray for beachy waves or curl-enhancing cream for defined curls.

2. Add Some Layers

Adding layers to your hair can help create movement and dimension, making it easier to style. Layers also help to blend in the shorter and longer hair as it grows out. Ask your hairstylist to add long layers or a shaggy cut to your hair.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Accessories are a great way to add style and personality to your hair. Try wearing headbands, clips, or scrunchies to keep your hair out of your face or add some flair to your hairstyle.

4. Try a New Parting

Changing your parting can instantly transform your hairstyle. Experiment with a middle parting or a deep side parting to create a different look. A deep side parting can also help to disguise any awkward lengths as your hair grows.

5. Opt for Updos

As your hair grows out, try different updos to keep your hair looking polished and put together. Low buns, messy buns, and braids are effortless and chic hairstyles that work well with short hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair while you're growing it out:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish and protect your hair.

Step 3: Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair gently.

Step 4: Apply styling products that enhance your natural texture.

Step 5: Use a blow dryer or air dry your hair, depending on your preference.

Step 6: Experiment with different hairstyles, such as updos, braids, or accessories.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair while you're growing it out:

1. Be patient. Growing out short hair takes time, so don't expect instant results.

2. Keep your hair healthy and nourished by using products that are suitable for your hair type.

3. Visit your hairstylist regularly to trim the ends and maintain the shape of your hair.

4. Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to keep your hair looking stylish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair while growing it out:

1. Will cutting my hair help it grow faster?

No, cutting your hair does not make it grow faster. However, regular trims can help to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Can I still use heat styling tools on my short hair?

Yes, you can still use heat styling tools on your short hair. However, always use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. How long does it take to grow out short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short your hair is. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take several months to a year to grow out short hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair while you're growing it out:

1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish and protect your hair.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause heat damage and breakage.

4. Visit your hairstylist regularly to trim the ends and maintain the shape of your hair.

Styling short hair while you're growing it out can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking chic and stylish. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair, and before you know it, you'll have long, luscious locks!
