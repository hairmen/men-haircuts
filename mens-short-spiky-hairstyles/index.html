Mens Short Spiky Hairstyles A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Jun 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both modern and edgy, mens short spiky hairstyles might be the right choice for you. This style has been popular for years and shows no signs of slowing down. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips, and hair care.

Description

Mens short spiky hairstyles are characterized by short hair on the sides and back with longer hair on top styled into spikes. This style is versatile and can be customized to fit any face shape and hair type. It's perfect for men who want a low maintenance yet stylish look.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the mens short spiky hairstyle, start by getting a short haircut on the sides and back. The length should be around 1-2 inches. Then, leave the hair on top longer, around 3-4 inches. Use a strong hold hair product like gel or pomade to style the hair into spikes. Use your fingers to create the spikes and finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step Guide

1. Get a short haircut on the sides and back
2. Leave the hair on top longer
3. Apply a strong hold hair product
4. Use your fingers to create the spikes
5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

1. Use a high-quality hair product to ensure the spikes stay in place
2. Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you
3. Regularly trim the sides and back to maintain the style
4. Use a hairdryer to help set the spikes in place
5. Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on achieving the look

FAQ

Q: Can anyone pull off the mens short spiky hairstyle?
A: Yes, this style can be customized to fit any face shape and hair type. It's all about finding the right length and style that works for you.

Q: What hair products should I use for this style?
A: Use a strong hold hair product like gel or pomade to style the hair into spikes. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Q: Is this hairstyle low maintenance?
A: Yes, this hairstyle is low maintenance and easy to style. Simply apply hair product and use your fingers to create the spikes.

Hair Care

To maintain the mens short spiky hairstyle, regularly trim the sides and back to keep the length short. Use a high-quality hair product to ensure the spikes stay in place and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles. Additionally, use a hairdryer to help set the spikes in place and ask your hairstylist for advice on achieving the look.

Mens short spiky hairstyles are a modern and edgy look that is perfect for men who want a low maintenance yet stylish hairstyle. By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can achieve the perfect look for your face shape and hair type.
