Is The Undercut Out Of Style

Written by Aprilia Aug 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The undercut is a popular hairstyle for men that has been around for decades. It involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head very short, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

But is the undercut still in style in 2023?

How To

If you want to try an undercut, here’s how:

  1. Decide on the length of the hair on top.
  2. Buzz or cut the hair on the sides and back short, leaving the hair on top long.
  3. Style the hair on top as desired.

Step By Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do an undercut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Decide on the length of the hair on top. Use scissors or clippers to cut the hair on top to the desired length.
  3. Section off the hair on top with clips or hair ties.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back short. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if desired.
  5. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top.
  6. Style the hair on top as desired using a comb or brush.

Tips

  • Make sure to use guards when using clippers to avoid cutting the hair too short.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head while cutting.
  • Experiment with different lengths on top to find the right look for you.

FAQ

Is the undercut still in style in 2023?

Yes, the undercut is still in style in 2023. It’s a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades and shows no signs of going out of style anytime soon.

Can anyone pull off an undercut?

While an undercut can look great on many people, it may not be the best fit for everyone. It’s best to consult with a hairstylist to determine if an undercut will work for your hair type and face shape.

How often do I need to get an undercut trimmed?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want to maintain the look. Generally, you should get an undercut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking sharp.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking its best, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much styling products, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the undercut.
  • If you’re going to be in the sun for an extended period of time, wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection to prevent damage.

In conclusion, the undercut is still a popular and stylish hairstyle in 2023. With the right cut and care, it can be a great look for many people.
