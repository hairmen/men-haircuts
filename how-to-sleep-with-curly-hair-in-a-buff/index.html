How To Sleep With Curly Hair In A Buff The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how important it is to protect your curls while you sleep. The friction between your hair and your pillowcase can cause frizz, breakage, and tangles. That's why many curly girls opt to sleep in a buff, a stretchy headband that covers your hair and keeps it in place while you sleep. In this guide, we'll show you how to sleep with curly hair in a buff and share some tips on how to care for your curls while you sleep.

What is a Buff?

A buff is a versatile piece of fabric that can be worn in many different ways, including as a headband. It's made of a stretchy, moisture-wicking material that's perfect for protecting curly hair while you sleep. Buffs are available in many different colors, patterns, and styles, so you can choose one that matches your personal style.

How to Sleep with Curly Hair in a Buff

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Before you put on your buff, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help prevent tangles and knots while you sleep. You can wash your hair as usual, or you can opt for a co-wash or dry shampoo if you don't have time for a full wash.

Step 2: Apply your favorite curly hair products

After washing your hair, apply your favorite curly hair products to help define your curls and keep them moisturized. This could include a leave-in conditioner, curl cream, or gel. Make sure to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Put on your buff

Now it's time to put on your buff. Start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck and pulling it up towards the top of your head. Then, place the buff over your head and tuck your hair inside. Make sure the buff covers all of your hair, including your edges and the back of your neck.

Step 4: Adjust the fit

Once your buff is in place, make sure it's snug but not too tight. You don't want it to be uncomfortable or to leave indentations on your forehead. If you have a lot of hair, you may need to adjust the fit by pulling the buff down slightly and tucking your hair in again.

Tips for Sleeping with a Buff

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of sleeping with a buff:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Try sleeping on your back to minimize tangles and friction.
  • Avoid using hair ties or clips, as they can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a diffuser on low heat to dry your hair before bed.

FAQ

Can I wear a buff if I have short hair?

Yes, buffs can be worn by people of all hair lengths. If you have short hair, you may need to adjust the fit of the buff to ensure it covers all of your hair.

How often should I wash my buff?

You should wash your buff after each use to keep it clean and hygienic. Simply toss it in the washing machine with your other clothes and let it air dry.

Can I wear a buff if I have straight hair?

Yes, buffs can be worn by people with all hair types. However, if you have straight hair, you may find that your hair gets flattened or creased while you sleep in a buff. You can try using a silk or satin scarf instead to protect your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and sleeping with a buff, you can keep your curly hair looking its best. Sweet dreams!
