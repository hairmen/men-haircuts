How To Get Rid Of Red Hair Color Without Bleaching

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are tired of your red hair color and want to switch it up without damaging your hair with bleach, you are in luck. There are several ways to get rid of red hair color without bleaching. In this blog post, we will discuss how to do just that.

Description

Red hair color can be a fun and bold choice, but it can also be a hassle if you decide it's not the right look for you. While bleach is a common method for removing hair color, it can be damaging to your hair and scalp. Luckily, there are other options available that are less harsh on your hair.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Without Bleaching

Here are some methods you can use to remove red hair color without bleach:

1. Use a clarifying shampoo

Clarifying shampoos are designed to remove buildup from your hair, including hair dye. Look for a clarifying shampoo that contains sulfates, as these are more effective at removing color. Use the shampoo as you normally would, focusing on the areas with the most color.

2. Try a color remover

Color removers are specifically formulated to remove hair dye without bleach. They work by breaking down the dye molecules so they can be washed out of your hair. Follow the instructions on the product carefully, as some color removers can be harsh on your hair.

3. Use vitamin C

Vitamin C is a natural hair lightener and can help fade hair color. Crush up vitamin C tablets and mix them with shampoo to create a paste. Apply the paste to your hair and let it sit for an hour before rinsing it out.

Step by Step

1. Clarifying shampoo method

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply the clarifying shampoo to your hair, focusing on the areas with the most color.
  3. Massage the shampoo into your hair and scalp for several minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly.
  5. Repeat the process if necessary.

2. Color remover method

  1. Follow the instructions on the color remover product carefully.
  2. Apply the product to your hair, focusing on the areas with the most color.
  3. Let the product sit for the recommended amount of time.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly.
  5. Condition your hair to restore moisture.

3. Vitamin C method

  1. Crush up several vitamin C tablets into a fine powder.
  2. Mix the powder with shampoo to create a paste.
  3. Apply the paste to your hair, focusing on the areas with the most color.
  4. Let the paste sit for an hour.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Always follow the instructions on the product carefully.
  • Be patient – it may take several treatments to completely remove the color.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after removing the color to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair while you are removing the color, as this can cause further damage.

FAQ

Can I remove red hair color without bleach?

Yes, there are several methods you can use to remove red hair color without bleach, including clarifying shampoo, color remover, and vitamin C.

Will removing hair color damage my hair?

While most methods for removing hair color are less damaging than bleach, they can still be harsh on your hair. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully and use a deep conditioning treatment afterwards to restore moisture to your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair before, during, and after removing hair color. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of moisture.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful, even after removing hair color.
