How To Fix A Skirt That s Too Long A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of tripping over your too-long skirt? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll show you how to fix a skirt that's too long in just a few easy steps. Whether you're a seasoned seamstress or a beginner, you'll be able to follow these instructions and have your skirt looking perfect in no time.

Description

Skirts are a staple in any wardrobe, but they can be a pain if they're too long. Not only can they be a tripping hazard, but they can also make you look shorter than you actually are. Luckily, fixing a skirt that's too long is a simple process that can be done at home with just a few tools.

How to Fix a Skirt That's Too Long

Before you start, you'll need a few things:

  • A measuring tape
  • A pair of scissors
  • A sewing machine or needle and thread

Step 1: Measure the Skirt

First, put on the skirt and measure how much you want to take off. Use the measuring tape to measure from the waistband to where you want the skirt to end. Make sure to measure all the way around the skirt to ensure that it's even.

Step 2: Mark the Skirt

Once you've measured the skirt, use a fabric marker or pins to mark where you want to cut. Make sure to mark all the way around the skirt to ensure that it's even.

Step 3: Cut the Skirt

Using a pair of scissors, carefully cut along the marked line. Make sure to cut straight and even to avoid any jagged edges.

Step 4: Hem the Skirt

Now that you've cut the skirt to the desired length, it's time to hem it. Fold the bottom of the skirt up about 1/4 inch and iron it flat. Then, fold it up another 1/4 inch and iron it again. This will create a clean edge for you to sew.

Step 5: Sew the Hem

Using a sewing machine or needle and thread, sew along the folded edge to secure the hem. Make sure to sew in a straight line and to backstitch at the beginning and end of the seam to secure it.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when fixing a skirt that's too long:

  • Always measure twice before cutting.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid jagged edges.
  • If you don't have a sewing machine, you can hem the skirt by hand using a whip stitch or running stitch.
  • Try on the skirt before you hem it to make sure it's the right length.

FAQ

Can I fix a skirt that's too long without cutting it?

If your skirt is only slightly too long, you may be able to fold it under and secure it with hem tape or safety pins. However, this method may not be as secure as cutting and hemming the skirt.

Can I hem the skirt without folding it?

You can hem the skirt without folding it by using a serger or overlocker to create a clean edge. However, this method may not work as well if the skirt is made of a delicate fabric.

Hair Care

While you're fixing your skirt, why not take some time to give your hair some TLC? Use a deep conditioning treatment to hydrate and nourish your hair, and try using a leave-in conditioner to keep it smooth and frizz-free. Don't forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools.

Fixing a skirt that's too long is a simple process that can be done at home with just a few tools. Follow these steps and you'll have your skirt looking perfect in no time!
