Men s Haircuts 2021 Short Length A Guide To The Latest Styles

Written by Frank Jun 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look this year, you might want to consider a short haircut. Short haircuts for men have been popular for years, and they continue to be a go-to option in 2021. In this guide, we'll walk you through the latest men's haircuts for short hair, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice

Description

Short haircuts can be worn in a variety of styles, from classic to edgy. In 2021, we're seeing a lot of textured crops, buzz cuts, and fades. These styles are easy to maintain, versatile, and perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks great.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting a short haircut in 2021, the first step is to find a style that suits your face shape and hair type. You can browse online or consult with a hairstylist to find inspiration. Once you've found a style you like, it's time to book an appointment with a professional stylist. They will be able to cut your hair to the right length and shape, creating a look that complements your features.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut:

  1. Choose a style that you like and that suits your face shape and hair type.
  2. Book an appointment with a professional stylist.
  3. Bring a photo of the style you want to show the stylist.
  4. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with the stylist.
  5. Let the stylist cut your hair to the desired length and shape.
  6. Ask the stylist for styling tips and product recommendations.

Styling Tips

Once you've got your new short haircut, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling short hair:

  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade, wax, or gel.
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often do I need to get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It's recommended that you get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, there are many short haircut styles that work well with a receding hairline, such as a buzz cut or a textured crop. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for you.

Hair Care Advice

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo every other day.
  • Condition your hair once a week to keep it soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Short haircuts are a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance, stylish look in 2021. With the right cut, styling products, and hair care, you can rock a short haircut with confidence.
