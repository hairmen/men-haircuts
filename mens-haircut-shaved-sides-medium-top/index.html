Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Medium Top A Trending Style For Men In 2023

Written by Robby Aug 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new and trendy haircut for men, the shaved sides medium top style might be the perfect choice for you. This style has been popular for several years now, and it doesn't seem to be going out of fashion anytime soon. This article will give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The shaved sides medium top style is characterized by having shaved or closely cropped sides with a longer, medium-length top. The sides are usually cut with a clipper, while the top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back or messy. This style is versatile and can suit different face shapes and hair types. It's also low-maintenance and easy to style.

How to Achieve the Shaved Sides Medium Top Style

Here are the steps to achieve this style:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Use a clipper to shave the sides of your hair. You can choose the length you prefer, but usually, a #3 or #4 guard is used.
  3. Blend the sides with the top using scissors or a clipper. Make sure there are no harsh lines between the two lengths.
  4. Style the top of your hair as desired. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to achieve different looks.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Shaved Sides Medium Top Style

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Visit your barber every 3-4 weeks for a trim and touch-up of the sides.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have a receding hairline, consider adjusting the length of the top to balance out your features.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the shaved sides medium top style:

Can I still style my hair if I have a widows peak?

Yes, you can still style your hair with a widows peak. Just make sure to adjust the length of the top to complement your hairline.

How long does it take to style this haircut?

It depends on the look you're going for and how comfortable you are with styling your hair. On average, it takes around 10-15 minutes to style this haircut.

Can I wear a hat with this style?

Yes, you can wear a hat with this style. Just make sure to adjust the top of your hair accordingly to avoid flattening it too much.

Hair Care

Here are some general tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, too frequently, as they can damage your hair and scalp.

Overall, the shaved sides medium top style is a versatile and stylish haircut for men. Whether you prefer a slicked-back or messy look, this style can suit your preferences. With proper maintenance and care, your hair can look its best and stay healthy.
