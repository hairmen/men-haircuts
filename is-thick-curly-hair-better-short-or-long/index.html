Is Thick Curly Hair Better Short Or Long

Description

Thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can be voluminous and beautiful, it can also be difficult to manage and style. One question that many people with thick curly hair ask is whether it is better to keep it short or long. The answer to this question depends on a variety of factors, including personal preference, face shape, and lifestyle.

How to

When deciding whether to keep your thick curly hair short or long, it can be helpful to consider your face shape. A short haircut can help to balance out a round face, while a longer style can elongate a square face. Your lifestyle is also an important factor to consider. If you have an active lifestyle and don't want to spend a lot of time styling your hair, a shorter cut may be a better option. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different styles and have the time and patience to take care of longer hair, a longer style may be a better fit.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consider your face shape

Step 2: Think about your lifestyle

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length for your hair

Tips

If you decide to keep your thick curly hair short, consider a layered bob or pixie cut. These styles can add volume and texture to your hair while also making it easier to manage.

If you decide to keep your thick curly hair long, consider investing in a quality hairbrush and detangling spray. These products can help to prevent tangles and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my thick curly hair look thinner?

A: Not necessarily. A layered short haircut can actually add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Q: Will a long haircut make my thick curly hair more difficult to manage?

A: It can, but with the right products and styling techniques, long curly hair can be just as manageable as shorter hair.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you decide to keep your thick curly hair short or long, it is important to take good care of it. This can include using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

When styling your hair, consider using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help define your curls. You can also experiment with different products, such as mousse or curl cream, to help enhance your curls and keep them looking their best.

Ultimately, whether you decide to keep your thick curly hair short or long is a personal choice. Consider your face shape, lifestyle, and personal preferences when making your decision, and don't be afraid to consult with a hairstylist for advice.
