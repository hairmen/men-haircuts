How Do You Know What Haircut Suits Your Face Tips And Tricks In 2023

Are you planning to get a new haircut but unsure which one suits your face shape? Choosing the right haircut that flatters your features is crucial to enhance your overall appearance. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to determine the best haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Haircut Suggestions for Different Face Shapes
  3. Tips and Tricks to Consider

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a haircut, you need to determine your face shape first. Here are the different face shapes and their corresponding characteristics:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than wide and has a softly rounded hairline and jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape has a similar width and length, with a rounded hairline and chin. The cheeks are the widest part of the face.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline, forehead, and cheekbones. The width and length of the face are almost the same, with a boxy shape.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow hairline and jawline, with wide cheekbones.

Pear Face Shape

A pear face shape has a narrow forehead and widens at the cheeks and jawline.

Now that you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that will flatter your features.

Haircut Suggestions for Different Face Shapes

Here are some haircut suggestions based on different face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape suits almost any haircut. You can try a bob, pixie cut, or layers.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape looks great with a long layered haircut, side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut with volume on top.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape can try a layered bob, long layers, or a side-swept bangs. Avoid blunt bobs or straight bangs.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape can try a bob with side-swept bangs, long layers, or a pixie cut with volume on top. Avoid blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape suits a textured pixie cut, layered bob, or side-swept bangs. Avoid blunt bobs or straight bangs.

Pear Face Shape

A pear face shape can try a layered bob, pixie cut, or side-swept bangs. Avoid chin-length bobs or blunt bangs.

Tips and Tricks to Consider

Aside from considering your face shape, here are some tips and tricks to help you choose the best haircut:

  • Consider your hair type and texture.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you can spend styling your hair.
  • Consult with your hairstylist for their professional opinion.
  • Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or celebrities with a similar face shape.
  • Try a virtual hairstyle app to see how different haircuts will look on you.

Hair Care to Maintain Your Haircut

After getting your new haircut, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

FAQs

1. Can I still get a haircut that doesn't suit my face shape?

Yes, you can still get a haircut that doesn't suit your face shape. However, it may not flatter your features as much as a haircut that complements your face shape.

2. How do I know if a haircut is suitable for my hair type and texture?

You can consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your hair type and texture. They can recommend products and techniques to enhance your hair's natural texture.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Choosing the right haircut for your face shape can do wonders for your overall appearance. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can find a haircut that complements your features and suits your personality. Don't be afraid to try new styles and consult with your hairstylist for their professional opinion.
