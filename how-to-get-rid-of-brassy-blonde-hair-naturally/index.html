How To Get Rid Of Brassy Blonde Hair Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are a blonde and have noticed that your hair has turned brassy, then you are not alone. Brassy blonde hair can be caused by a variety of factors such as hard water, sun exposure, chlorine or even hair products. Fortunately, there are natural ways to get rid of brassy blonde hair without damaging your hair. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to help you get rid of brassy blonde hair naturally.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Blonde Hair Naturally

1. Use Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a great way to neutralize brassy tones in blonde hair. Purple is opposite to yellow on the color wheel, which means that it can counteract the yellow tones in your hair. Use a purple shampoo once or twice a week to keep your blonde hair looking fresh.

2. Rinse Your Hair with Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is another natural remedy to get rid of brassy blonde hair. Mix one part apple cider vinegar with two parts water and rinse your hair with the mixture after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off with water. Apple cider vinegar helps to restore the pH balance of your hair and can also remove buildup caused by hair products.

3. Use Chamomile Tea

Chamomile tea is a natural brightener that can help to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair. Brew a cup of chamomile tea and let it cool. Use it to rinse your hair after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off with water. Chamomile tea can also add shine to your hair.

4. Apply a Hair Mask

A hair mask can help to nourish and hydrate your hair while also getting rid of brassy tones. Mix together equal parts of honey and plain yogurt and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with water. Honey is a natural humectant that can help to retain moisture in your hair, while yogurt contains lactic acid that can help to exfoliate your scalp.

5. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair and make it more prone to brassy tones. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible and opt for air-drying your hair instead. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Step 1: Use Purple Shampoo

Tips for Getting Rid of Brassy Blonde Hair Naturally

Here are some tips to help you get rid of brassy blonde hair naturally:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can lemon juice help to get rid of brassy blonde hair?

Lemon juice can help to lighten your hair, but it can also make it more prone to brassy tones. It is best to avoid using lemon juice on your hair if you are trying to get rid of brassy tones.

2. How often should I use a hair mask?

You can use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

3. Can I use purple shampoo every day?

No, you should only use purple shampoo once or twice a week to avoid drying out your hair.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

If you have blonde hair, it is important to take good care of it to prevent brassy tones. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can make it more prone to damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it can cause breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of brassy blonde hair naturally and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
