Description

Mens hairstyles long top short sides have been trending for a while now, and it's not hard to see why. This hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's perfect for guys who want to keep their hair short and easy to manage on the sides while still having enough length on top to style and experiment with.

This style is characterized by a short back and sides with longer hair on the top that can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, textured, or messy. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to Get Mens Hairstyles Long Top Short Sides

If you're interested in getting mens hairstyles long top short sides, the first step is to find a good barber who is experienced in this style. You can show them pictures of the style you want or describe it to them in detail.

The barber will then use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length, usually between a number 1 and 3 guard. The top of your hair will be left longer, and the barber will use scissors to trim it to the desired length and shape.

Once the haircut is complete, the barber will style your hair according to your preferences. This may involve using styling products such as pomade, wax, or gel to achieve the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

If you're interested in styling your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving mens hairstyles long top short sides:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax
  3. Using a comb, create a side parting on one side of your head
  4. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting, using your fingers to style it in the desired direction
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a styling product to add texture and hold
  6. Style the hair on top of your head by using your fingers to create volume or texture
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect mens hairstyles long top short sides:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair
  • Use a comb to create a sleek, polished look or use your fingers to create a more relaxed, messy style
  • Consider getting a regular trim to keep your hair in good condition and maintain the shape of the style
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice on how to style your hair at home

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles long top short sides:

Q: Can I still get mens hairstyles long top short sides if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! Mens hairstyles long top short sides can look great on curly hair, and the length on top can help to weigh down the curls and make them more manageable.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the style and keep your hair in good condition.

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's thinning or receding?

A: Yes, mens hairstyles long top short sides can be adapted to suit different hair types and can help to create the illusion of thickness and volume.

Hair Care for Mens Hairstyles Long Top Short Sides

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for mens hairstyles long top short sides. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock mens hairstyles long top short sides with confidence and style!
