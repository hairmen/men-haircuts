Description

The mens haircut short sides long on top has been a popular hairstyle for men in recent years. This style features shorter sides and longer hair on top, which can be styled in a variety of ways. The short sides can be faded or tapered, and the longer hair on top can be styled with a comb, brush, or fingers. This hairstyle can be suitable for both formal and casual events, depending on how it is styled.

How to

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut short sides long on top, it is recommended to go to a professional barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. They can help you determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape. It is important to communicate your preferences and style goals to your barber or stylist.

Step by step

Here are the steps for getting a mens haircut short sides long on top:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair on top of the head.
  3. Cut the hair on the sides to the desired length using clippers or scissors.
  4. Fade or taper the hair on the sides, if desired.
  5. Blend the hair on the sides with the longer hair on top.
  6. Cut and style the hair on top to the desired length and shape.
  7. Use product, such as pomade or wax, to style the hair on top.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining a mens haircut short sides long on top:

  • Regularly trim the sides to maintain the length and shape.
  • Use product, such as pomade or wax, to style the hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked back look or a messy, textured look.
  • Consider using a hair dryer to add volume and texture to the hair on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircut short sides long on top:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?
A: This hairstyle can be suitable for many hair types, but it is important to consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for your hair type. Q: How often should I get this haircut?
A: It is recommended to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape. Q: Can I style the hair on top in different ways?
A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as a slicked back look or a messy, textured look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Regularly shampoo and condition your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

Overall, the mens haircut short sides long on top is a versatile hairstyle that can be suitable for many men. It is important to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape, and to regularly maintain the length and shape of the sides. With proper care and styling, this hairstyle can be a great choice for both formal and casual events.
