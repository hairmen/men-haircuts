How To Cut Hair Clippers Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Want to learn how to cut your hair with clippers? This guide will teach you how to cut hair clippers like a pro. From choosing the right clippers to cleaning up after the cut, we have got you covered. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in cutting hair with clippers is to choose the right clippers. You need to consider the following factors:

  • Blade size
  • Motor power
  • Corded or cordless
  • Price

Make sure to choose a clipper that suits your hair type and budget. A good quality clipper can last for years and save you money in the long run.

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair with clippers, you need to prepare your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair and dry it completely
  • Use a comb to detangle your hair
  • Decide on the length you want to cut

Once you have prepared your hair, you are ready to start cutting.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Hair with Clippers

Follow these steps to cut hair with clippers:

  1. Attach the desired blade size to the clipper
  2. Turn on the clipper and start at the nape of your neck
  3. Move the clipper up towards the crown of your head
  4. Repeat until you have cut all the hair on the back of your head
  5. Move to the sides and cut the hair in an upward motion
  6. Blend the sides with the back using a comb and scissors
  7. Trim the hair on top using scissors or clippers
  8. Blend the top with the sides and back using a comb and scissors
  9. Trim the hairline and sideburns using clippers or scissors
  10. Clean up any loose hairs with a brush or towel

Make sure to take your time and go slowly. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you cut hair with clippers:

  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and keep the hair even
  • Start with a longer blade size and work your way down
  • Use scissors to blend the hair and create a natural look
  • Check your work in a mirror from different angles
  • Practice on a friend or family member before cutting your own hair

FAQs

1. Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. Just make sure to choose the right clippers and follow the steps in this guide.

2. What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always cut more hair or go to a professional for help.

3. How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on your hair type and style. Generally, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair with clippers, you need to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and shiny
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

Congratulations, you now know how to cut hair with clippers. With practice, you can become a pro and save money on haircuts. Happy cutting!
