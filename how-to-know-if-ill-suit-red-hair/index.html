How To Know If You ll Suit Red Hair

Written by William Nov 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering dying your hair red but not sure if it will suit you? Red hair can be a bold and beautiful choice, but it's important to consider if it will complement your skin tone and features. In this article, we'll discuss how to know if you'll suit red hair and offer tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Consider Your Eye Color

  • Consider Your Face Shape

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when deciding if red hair will suit you. There are two main categories of skin tone: warm and cool. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

Warm skin tones look best with red hair that has orange or gold undertones. Cool skin tones look best with red hair that has blue or purple undertones. If you're unsure of your undertones, try holding up clothing in different shades of red to your face to see which complements your skin best.

Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also impact how well red hair will suit you. Blue and green eyes tend to look stunning with red hair, while brown eyes may clash if they have warm undertones. However, don't let this discourage you from trying red hair if you have brown eyes – it's all about finding the right shade.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

The closer your natural hair color is to the shade of red you want, the more likely it is that you'll suit red hair. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach it before dyeing it red, which can be damaging to your hair. It's important to consider the upkeep and maintenance of red hair, as it can require more frequent touch-ups.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can also impact how flattering red hair will be on you. If you have a round face, lighter shades of red can help elongate your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, consider a darker shade of red to balance out your features. If you have a square face, a warm, coppery red can soften your features.

Consider Your Personality

Red hair is a bold and confident choice, so it's important to consider if it matches your personality. If you're naturally outgoing and love to stand out, red hair may be the perfect choice for you. If you prefer a more subtle look, consider trying a darker shade of red or adding highlights instead of dyeing your entire head.

Tips for Maintaining Red Hair

Once you've decided to dye your hair red, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent color from fading.
  • Avoid hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause color to fade.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can cause damage and fading.
  • Use a color-depositing conditioner or hair mask to refresh color between touch-ups.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as they can cause color to fade.

FAQ

Q: Will red hair suit me if I have a cool skin tone?

A: Yes, as long as you choose a shade of red with blue or purple undertones.

Q: How often will I need to touch up my red hair?

A: This depends on your natural hair color and the shade of red you choose. On average, touch-ups may be needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Red Hair

Red hair can be higher maintenance than other hair colors, but with proper care, it can stay vibrant and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent color from fading.
  • Limit exposure to sunlight, as it can cause color to fade.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can cause damage and fading.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach and hair dyes, which can damage hair and cause color to fade.

Now that you know how to determine if red hair will suit you, it's time to make the bold choice and try it out! Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and keep your new look fresh.
