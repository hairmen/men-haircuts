How To Fix Barbie Hair Easy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have children who love playing with Barbie dolls, you might have encountered a common problem: tangled hair. Barbie’s hair can easily become a mess, but don’t worry, there’s a simple solution to fix it. This guide will show you how to fix Barbie hair easy and get your child’s dolls looking brand new again.

Description

Barbie dolls come with long and beautiful hair that can be styled in many ways. However, with frequent play, Barbie’s hair can become tangled, matted, and damaged. This can be frustrating for children and parents alike, but there’s no need to throw away the doll or buy a new one. With a few simple steps, you can easily fix Barbie’s hair and restore it to its original beauty.

How to Fix Barbie Hair Easy: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather the Materials

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary materials. You will need a comb or a brush, a spray bottle filled with water, a small amount of fabric softener or conditioner, and a towel.

Step 2: Wet the Hair

Start by wetting Barbie’s hair with the spray bottle. Be careful not to soak the doll’s body or clothing. Use your fingers to distribute the water throughout the hair, ensuring that it is evenly damp.

Step 3: Apply Fabric Softener or Conditioner

Apply a small amount of fabric softener or conditioner to Barbie’s hair. This will help to loosen any tangles and make it easier to brush through. Be careful not to use too much, as this can leave the hair greasy or sticky.

Step 4: Comb the Hair

Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up, combing through the tangles gently. Be patient and take your time, as rushing can cause more damage to the hair. If you encounter any stubborn knots, use your fingers to gently pull them apart before using the comb.

Step 5: Rinse the Hair

Rinse the hair with clean water to remove any excess fabric softener or conditioner. Be sure to rinse thoroughly, as leaving any product in the hair can cause it to become sticky or greasy over time.

Step 6: Dry the Hair

Use a towel to gently pat the hair dry. Avoid rubbing, as this can cause the hair to tangle again. Once the hair is mostly dry, use a comb or brush to style it as desired.

Tips for Fixing Barbie Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you fix Barbie hair easy:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Be gentle and patient when combing through tangles, as pulling too hard can cause the hair to break.
  • Use a small amount of product to avoid leaving the hair greasy or sticky.
  • Encourage your child to take care of their Barbie’s hair by combing it regularly and storing it in a safe place.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use other products besides fabric softener or conditioner?

Yes, there are other products you can use to fix Barbie hair, such as detangling spray or silicone spray. However, these products can be more expensive and may not be as gentle on the hair as fabric softener or conditioner.

What should I do if the hair is still tangled after following these steps?

If the hair is still tangled, try repeating the steps or using a different product. If the hair is severely damaged or knotted, you may need to trim it or seek professional help.

Hair Care for Barbie Dolls

To keep Barbie’s hair looking its best, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Store Barbie in a safe place where her hair won’t get tangled or damaged.
  • Regularly comb or brush Barbie’s hair to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Avoid using heat or styling tools on Barbie’s hair, as this can damage it.
  • Clean Barbie’s hair with gentle shampoo and conditioner if it becomes dirty or greasy.

With these tips and steps, you can easily fix Barbie hair easy and keep your child’s dolls looking beautiful for years to come. Happy fixing!
