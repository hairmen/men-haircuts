How To Access Pop Hairstyles On Animal Crossing

Written by Bardi Oct 20, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're a fan of the popular game Animal Crossing, you may be wondering how to access pop hairstyles for your character. These fun and trendy hairstyles can help your character stand out and express their personality. In this article, we'll go over how to access pop hairstyles on Animal Crossing, step by step.

How to Access Pop Hairstyles

First, you'll need to visit the Able Sisters tailor shop. This is where you'll find the pop hairstyles available for purchase. Once you're inside the shop, head to the back and look for the mirror. This is where you can try on different hairstyles and purchase them.

When you're in front of the mirror, press the "A" button to interact with it. This will bring up a menu of hairstyles to choose from. You'll see a variety of options, including pop hairstyles. Scroll through the options until you find the one you want to purchase.

Once you've found the pop hairstyle you want, select it and confirm your purchase. The hairstyle will be added to your character's inventory, and you can wear it whenever you like.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to accessing pop hairstyles on Animal Crossing:

  1. Visit the Able Sisters tailor shop.
  2. Head to the back of the shop and find the mirror.
  3. Press "A" to interact with the mirror.
  4. Select the pop hairstyle you want to purchase.
  5. Confirm your purchase.
  6. The pop hairstyle will be added to your inventory.

Tips for Choosing a Pop Hairstyle

When choosing a pop hairstyle for your character, consider their personality and style. Some hairstyles may look better on certain characters, depending on their features and clothing. You can also experiment with different colors and accessories to create a unique look.

Another tip is to check back at the Able Sisters shop frequently, as they may add new pop hairstyles over time. You can also visit other player's islands to see what hairstyles they have and get inspiration for your own character.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about accessing pop hairstyles on Animal Crossing:

Can I change my character's hairstyle after purchasing it?

Yes, you can change your character's hairstyle at any time by visiting the Able Sisters shop and interacting with the mirror.

Do I need any special items to access pop hairstyles?

No, you don't need any special items to access pop hairstyles. You just need to visit the Able Sisters tailor shop and interact with the mirror.

How much do pop hairstyles cost?

The cost of pop hairstyles varies, but they typically range from 1,800 to 2,400 bells.

Hair Care on Animal Crossing

Just like in real life, it's important to take care of your character's hair on Animal Crossing. Here are some tips for keeping your character's hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Visit the salon regularly to try out new hairstyles and keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Use hair care products, such as shampoo and conditioner, to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Accessorize your hair with hats, headbands, and other items to add some extra flair.

By following these tips, you can keep your character's hair looking great and show off your pop hairstyles to the rest of the Animal Crossing community!
