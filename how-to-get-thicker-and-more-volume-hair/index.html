How To Get Thicker And More Volume Hair

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having thin and flat hair? Do you envy those with thick and voluminous locks? Well, you're not alone. Many people struggle with achieving fuller and more voluminous hair. But don't worry, there are several ways to get thicker and more volume hair. In this article, we'll discuss some tips and tricks to help you achieve the hair of your dreams.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Thicker and More Volume Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thicker and More Volume Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thicker and More Volume Hair

How to Get Thicker and More Volume Hair

There are several ways to get thicker and more volume hair. Some of these include:

  • Using volumizing products
  • Blow drying your hair upside down
  • Getting regular trims
  • Eating a healthy diet
  • Using hair extensions

Now, let's take a closer look at each of these methods.

Using Volumizing Products

Volumizing products are designed to make your hair look fuller and thicker. These products work by adding volume to your hair shaft, making it appear thicker. Some popular volumizing products include:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Hair mousse
  • Hair spray
  • Dry shampoo

When using volumizing products, it's important to follow the instructions carefully. Overusing these products can cause buildup and weigh down your hair, making it look flat and lifeless.

Blow Drying Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is another way to add volume to your hair. This technique works by lifting your hair at the roots, making it appear fuller. To blow dry your hair upside down, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and towel dry your hair
  2. Flip your head upside down
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  4. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style

Blow drying your hair upside down can be damaging if done too often. It's important to use a heat protectant spray and limit heat styling to once or twice a week.

Getting Regular Trims

Getting regular trims is important for maintaining healthy hair. When your hair is damaged and split, it can look thin and lifeless. Getting regular trims can prevent split ends and breakage, making your hair look fuller and thicker.

Eating a Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on your hair health. Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and thickness. Some foods that are good for your hair include:

  • Salmon
  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Avocado
  • Nuts and seeds

Using Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a quick fix, hair extensions can help add volume and thickness to your hair. There are several types of hair extensions available, including clip-ins, tape-ins, and sew-ins. It's important to choose a reputable brand and have them installed by a professional to avoid damage to your natural hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get thicker and more volume hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a volumizing product to your roots
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots
  4. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style
  5. If desired, use hair extensions to add extra volume and thickness

Tips for Thicker and More Volume Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve thicker and more volume hair:

  • Avoid overwashing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often

FAQs

Can I use hair extensions if I have thin hair?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and thickness to thin hair. Just be sure to choose a reputable brand and have them installed by a professional to avoid damage to your natural hair.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Hair Care for Thicker and More Volume Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving thicker and more volume hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the thick and voluminous hair you've always wanted. Remember to take care of your hair and be patient, as it may take some time to see results.
