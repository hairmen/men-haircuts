Mens Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides And Back A Relaxed Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on mens haircut long on top short on sides and back! This popular hairstyle has been around for years and is still going strong in 2023. If you're looking for a versatile, trendy cut that is low maintenance, then this might be the perfect style for you. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the long top short sides and back haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

The mens haircut long on top short on sides and back is a popular style that is both trendy and classic. The cut features longer hair on the top of the head, which is usually styled in a messy, textured way. The sides and back are cut short, usually with clippers, to create a sharp contrast. This style is perfect for men who want a low maintenance cut that still looks great.

How To

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back, you'll want to find a skilled barber or stylist who has experience with this style. When you go in for your appointment, be sure to bring a photo of the style you want so that your stylist can get a clear idea of what you're looking for.

Choosing the Right Length

One of the most important things to consider when getting a long top short sides and back haircut is the length. The length of the top of the hair can vary depending on personal preference, but most men opt for a length of 3-6 inches. The sides and back are usually cut to a shorter length, anywhere from a number 1 to a number 3 clipper guard.

Styling the Hair

Styling the long top of the haircut is relatively simple. You can use a matte pomade or a texturizing spray to create a messy, textured look. Simply apply a small amount of product to your hair and use your fingers to style it in the direction you want. For the sides and back, you can either leave them natural or use clippers to create a faded look.

Step by Step

Here's a step by step guide to getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back:

  1. Choose a skilled barber or stylist who has experience with this style.
  2. Bring a photo of the style you want to your appointment.
  3. Discuss the length and style you want with your stylist.
  4. Sit back and let your stylist work their magic!
  5. Style your hair at home with a texturizing product.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back:

  • Choose a length that works for your hair type and face shape.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality texturizing spray or pomade to make styling easier.
  • Find a barber or stylist who has experience with this style to ensure a great result.

FAQ

Here are some common questions and answers about the mens haircut long on top short on sides and back:

Is this style suitable for all face shapes?

While this style can work for many face shapes, it may not be the best choice for those with a round face. In general, this style works well for those with oval or square face shapes.

How often do I need to get a haircut?

This style is relatively low maintenance, but you'll still need to get regular trims to keep it looking sharp. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a long top short sides and back haircut:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying it out.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, use a curl defining product to enhance your natural texture.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

That's it for our guide on mens haircut long on top short on sides and back! We hope you found this information helpful and that you're now ready to rock this trendy style. Remember, finding a skilled barber or stylist is key to getting a great result, so don't be afraid to do your research and ask for recommendations. Happy styling!
