Cutting Men s Hair On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to men's haircuts, cutting the hair on top is one of the most important aspects. Whether you want a short, clean cut or a longer, textured style, the top of the hair is where all the action happens. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about cutting men's hair on top, including how to do it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Cutting men's hair on top is a crucial part of any haircut. This is where the hair is longest and where the most styling happens. Whether you're going for a classic undercut, a modern textured crop, or something in between, getting the top right is essential.

How To

Before you start cutting men's hair on top, there are a few things you'll need to do. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be harder to work with, and you may not get an accurate cut. Comb the hair out, so it's free of tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair so that you can focus on one area at a time. If you're cutting a classic undercut, section the hair from temple to temple, leaving the crown and back of the head free. If you're going for a textured crop, section the hair from the front to the back, leaving the sides free.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Using scissors or clippers, cut the hair to the desired length. Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. If you're using clippers, start with a higher guard and work your way down. Be sure to blend the hair so that there are no harsh lines or unevenness.

Step 4: Texturize the Hair

If you're going for a textured look, use scissors or texturizing shears to add texture to the hair. This will create a more natural, effortless look.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once you've cut the hair on top, style it using your preferred product. This could be a pomade, wax, gel, or any other styling product. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair on top:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter
  • Blend the hair so that there are no harsh lines or unevenness
  • Use texturizing shears to add texture to the hair
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair on top:

What's the best way to cut men's hair on top?

The best way to cut men's hair on top is to start with a longer length and work your way shorter. Be sure to blend the hair so that there are no harsh lines or unevenness.

What tools do I need to cut men's hair on top?

You'll need scissors or clippers, a comb, and texturizing shears (if you're going for a textured look).

How often should I cut my hair on top?

This depends on the style you're going for and how fast your hair grows. Generally, a haircut every 4-6 weeks is ideal.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy head of hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair after shampooing
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish

Conclusion

Cutting men's hair on top is an essential part of any haircut. Whether you're going for a classic undercut or a modern textured crop, getting the top right is crucial. Follow these tips and techniques to achieve the perfect cut every time.
