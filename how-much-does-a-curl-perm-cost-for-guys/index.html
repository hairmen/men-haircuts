How Much Does A Curl Perm Cost For Guys

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a guy looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, you may be wondering how much a curl perm will cost you. The answer depends on a few different factors, such as the salon you go to, the length and thickness of your hair, and the type of curl perm you choose.

Description

A curl perm, also known as a spiral perm, is a chemical treatment that adds curls or waves to your hair. It works by using a solution to break down the bonds in your hair, then wrapping your hair around rods to create the desired curl pattern. The solution is then rinsed out and a neutralizer is applied to set the curls.

How To

If you’re interested in getting a curl perm, the first step is to find a salon that offers the service. You can search online or ask for recommendations from friends or family members. Once you’ve found a salon, you’ll need to schedule a consultation with a stylist to discuss your hair type, desired curl pattern, and budget.

During the consultation, the stylist will examine your hair and determine if it’s healthy enough for a perm. If your hair is damaged or over-processed, they may recommend waiting until it’s in better condition before getting a perm.

If your hair is healthy enough, the stylist will recommend a curl pattern based on your hair type and desired look. They may also recommend a specific type of perm solution and rod size to achieve the desired result.

Once you’ve agreed on the curl pattern and solution, the stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair and wrap it around rods. They will then apply the neutralizer to set the curls and rinse out the solution.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a curl perm:

  1. Find a salon that offers curl perms and schedule a consultation.
  2. Discuss your hair type, desired curl pattern, and budget with the stylist.
  3. If your hair is healthy enough, the stylist will recommend a curl pattern, solution, and rod size.
  4. The stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair and wrap it around rods.
  5. The stylist will apply the neutralizer to set the curls and rinse out the solution.
  6. You’ll need to let your hair air dry or sit under a hood dryer.
  7. Once your hair is dry, the stylist will remove the rods and style your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind if you’re thinking about getting a curl perm:

  • Make sure your hair is healthy enough for a perm.
  • Be prepared for some maintenance, such as regular trims and conditioning treatments.
  • Choose a curl pattern and solution that works with your hair type.
  • Be willing to invest in quality styling products to maintain your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curl perms:

How much does a curl perm cost?

The cost of a curl perm for guys can vary depending on the salon you go to and the length and thickness of your hair. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $100 to $300 for a curl perm.

How long does a curl perm last?

A curl perm can last anywhere from 2 to 6 months, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you take care of your curls.

Is a curl perm damaging to your hair?

A curl perm can be damaging to your hair if it’s not done correctly or if your hair is already damaged or over-processed. It’s important to choose a reputable salon and stylist and to make sure your hair is healthy enough for a perm.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can break up your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair while it’s still damp to help define your curls.

Overall, a curl perm can be a great way to add texture and volume to your hair. By choosing a reputable salon and stylist and taking good care of your curls, you can enjoy your new look for months to come.
