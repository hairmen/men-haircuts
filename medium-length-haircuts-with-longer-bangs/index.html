Medium Length Haircuts With Longer Bangs A Guide To Rocking The Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great, consider medium length haircuts with longer bangs. This style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair at a manageable length while still adding some flair to their look. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect medium length haircut with longer bangs, offer some tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular hairstyle.

What are Medium Length Haircuts with Longer Bangs?

Medium length haircuts with longer bangs are hairstyles where the hair is cut to shoulder length or just below, with longer bangs that frame the face. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and chic. It's also a great option for those who want to experiment with different hair colors or highlights.

How to Get the Perfect Medium Length Haircut with Longer Bangs

To get the perfect medium length haircut with longer bangs, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it dry naturally or blow-drying it.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.
  3. Section your hair into two parts, with one section in the front for your bangs and the other in the back for the rest of your hair.
  4. Cut your hair to the desired length, keeping in mind that it's easier to take off more hair later than to add more back.
  5. Trim your bangs to the desired length, making sure they're longer on the sides to frame your face.
  6. Style your hair as desired using a curling iron, straightener, or other hair styling tools.

Remember to always use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and to cut your hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises when it dries naturally.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Haircuts with Longer Bangs

Now that you've got the perfect medium length haircut with longer bangs, it's time to style it. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new look:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and bounce.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair, depending on your mood.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them from drying out.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling your hair, so don't overload it with too many products.

FAQs about Medium Length Haircuts with Longer Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts with longer bangs:

Q: Will medium length haircuts with longer bangs suit my face shape?

A: Medium length haircuts with longer bangs are versatile and can work well with most face shapes. However, if you have a round face, it's best to avoid blunt bangs and opt for longer, side-swept bangs instead.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: You should get your hair cut every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and to avoid split ends.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with medium length haircuts with longer bangs. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style for a chic look.

How to Take Care of Your Medium Length Haircut with Longer Bangs

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length haircut with longer bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your medium length haircut with longer bangs with confidence and style. So go ahead and show off your new look!
