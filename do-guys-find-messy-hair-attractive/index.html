Do Guys Find Messy Hair Attractive

Written by William Sep 21, 2023 · 3 min read

When it comes to hair, every girl wants to look her best. But, sometimes, the best look can be achieved with minimal effort. In recent years, messy hair has become a popular trend among women. But, the question still remains, do guys find messy hair attractive? Let’s find out.

Description

Messy hair is a hairstyle that looks like you just got out of bed. It’s a relaxed, effortless look that can be achieved by simply tousling your hair with your fingers. Messy hair can be achieved with any hair length, from short pixie cuts to long and wavy hair.

How to Achieve Messy Hair

There are a few ways to achieve the perfect messy hair look. Here are some steps you can follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it with a diffuser. This will give your hair the volume it needs to achieve the messy look.

Step 2: Use Texturizing Spray

Use texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture to your hair. Spray it on your roots and scrunch your hair to create volume.

Step 3: Tousle Your Hair

Once your hair is dry and texturized, use your fingers to tousle your hair. Scrunch it, twist it, and play with it until you achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair

Once you have achieved the perfect messy hair look, here are some tips to maintain it:

Tip 1: Use Dry Shampoo

Use dry shampoo to absorb excess oils in your hair. This will keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.

Tip 2: Use Hair Oil

Use hair oil to add shine and nourishment to your hair. This will prevent your hair from looking dull and lifeless.

Tip 3: Don’t Overdo It

Remember, messy hair is all about looking effortless. Don’t overdo it with too much product or styling. Keep it simple and natural.

FAQ

Q: Do guys find messy hair attractive?

A: Yes, many guys find messy hair attractive. It gives off a carefree and relaxed vibe.

Q: Can messy hair be achieved with any hair type?

A: Yes, messy hair can be achieved with any hair type, from straight to curly.

Q: Is messy hair appropriate for all occasions?

A: No, messy hair may not be appropriate for all occasions. It’s best to stick to a more polished look for formal events.

Hair Care

Messy hair may be a low-maintenance look, but that doesn’t mean you should neglect your hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools. This will prevent damage and breakage.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet rich in protein and vitamins to promote hair growth and health.

In conclusion, messy hair can be a fun and attractive look for many women. It’s a low-maintenance style that can be achieved with minimal effort. So, go ahead and embrace your messy hair!
