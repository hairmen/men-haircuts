Is Hair Loss Common With Gastric Sleeve

Written by Frank Sep 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering undergoing gastric sleeve surgery but worried about hair loss? If so, you're not alone. Many people wonder whether hair loss is a common side effect of the procedure. In this article, we'll discuss the facts about hair loss with gastric sleeve and what you can do to prevent it.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Gastric sleeve surgery is a weight loss procedure that involves removing a large portion of the stomach, leaving a smaller sleeve-shaped stomach. This limits the amount of food you can eat, resulting in weight loss. While the procedure has been shown to be effective for weight loss, some people experience hair loss after the surgery.

Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is common, affecting around 50% of patients. It usually occurs 3-6 months after the surgery and can last for up to a year. The hair loss is usually temporary and will grow back once your body adjusts to the weight loss.

How to

Preventing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is possible with a few simple steps:

  • Take a daily multivitamin that contains biotin, zinc, and iron.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein.
  • Avoid crash diets or rapid weight loss.
  • Use gentle hair care products and avoid harsh styling techniques.

Step by step

If you're experiencing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, there are steps you can take to promote hair growth:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to increase blood flow to the hair follicles.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for thinning hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools or harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement that contains biotin, zinc, and iron.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.
  • Get enough sleep each night to reduce stress.
  • Avoid smoking, which can damage hair follicles.

FAQ

Q: Is hair loss permanent after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: No, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is usually temporary and will grow back once your body adjusts to the weight loss.

Q: How long does hair loss last after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Hair loss usually occurs 3-6 months after the surgery and can last for up to a year.

Q: Can taking a multivitamin prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Yes, taking a daily multivitamin that contains biotin, zinc, and iron can help prevent hair loss.

Hair Care

If you're experiencing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, it's important to take care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for thinning hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools or harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to increase blood flow to the hair follicles.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein.

Overall, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is common but usually temporary. By taking care of your hair and following a healthy diet and lifestyle, you can promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss.
