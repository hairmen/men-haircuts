How To Cut A Stacked Bob Haircut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are looking for a fresh and trendy hairstyle that is easy to maintain, a stacked bob haircut might be the perfect option for you. This short, layered haircut features stacked layers in the back and longer layers in the front, creating a dramatic and edgy look. While it might seem intimidating to cut your own hair, with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking stacked bob haircut at home. In this guide, we will walk you through the process of cutting a stacked bob haircut yourself.

Description

A stacked bob haircut is a short haircut that is cut in layers, with the back layers being shorter and stacked on top of each other. The front layers are longer and frame the face. This haircut is popular because it is versatile, low-maintenance, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How To

Tools You Will Need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair clips
  • Wide-tooth comb

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Separate the hair into four sections: top, back, and two sides. Clip each section to keep it separate.
  3. Begin with the back section. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold it between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across. Continue cutting small sections of hair, combing them straight down, and cutting them straight across until you have cut all of the back section.
  4. Release the top section and comb it straight down. Cut it to the desired length, using the back section as a guide.
  5. Release one side section and comb it straight down. Cut it to the desired length, using the back section as a guide. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Blend the layers by combing them straight down and cutting any uneven pieces.
  7. Check the length and symmetry of the haircut, making any necessary adjustments.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure precision.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can be difficult to fix mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Take breaks if your arms or hands become tired.

FAQ

Can I cut a stacked bob haircut on curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a stacked bob haircut on curly hair. However, keep in mind that the curls will affect the length and shape of the haircut. It may be best to cut curly hair when it is dry, so you can see how the curls fall.

How often should I cut my stacked bob haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of a stacked bob haircut. However, you can also trim your own hair in between salon visits to keep it looking fresh.

What styling products should I use for a stacked bob haircut?

Styling products will depend on your hair type and desired look. For a sleek and polished look, try a smoothing serum or cream. For a more textured and voluminous look, try a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care

To keep your stacked bob haircut looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With this step-by-step guide, you can confidently cut your own stacked bob haircut at home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and consult a professional if you have any concerns. With practice, you'll be able to maintain your trendy and fashionable look all on your own.
