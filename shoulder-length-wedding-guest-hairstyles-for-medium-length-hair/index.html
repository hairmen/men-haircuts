Shoulder Length Wedding Guest Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Eveline Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you attending a wedding soon and searching for the perfect hairstyle for your medium length hair? Look no further! In this article, we will be sharing some stunning shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair.

Description

Wedding season is upon us, and as a guest, you want to look your best. One of the most important aspects of your look is your hairstyle. The right hairstyle can complete your outfit and make you feel confident and beautiful. If you have medium length hair, you may be wondering what hairstyle options are available to you. Luckily, there are plenty of gorgeous shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair that you can choose from.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles you can choose from, let's go over some hair care tips to ensure your hair is healthy and ready to be styled. Make sure to wash and condition your hair a day or two before the wedding. This will give your hair time to settle and make it easier to style. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, and finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Step by Step

Now, onto the fun part - the hairstyles! Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of our favorite shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles:

1. Half-Up Half-Down

To achieve this look, start by curling your hair with a curling iron. Then, take the top section of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a clip or hair tie. You can leave some pieces of hair out to frame your face. Finish off with some hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Braided Updo

For a more elegant look, try a braided updo. Begin by creating a side part in your hair. Then, braid the front section of your hair on both sides and secure them at the back of your head. Gather the rest of your hair into a low bun, and secure with bobby pins. Finish off with some hairspray.

3. Loose Waves

If you want a more relaxed and effortless look, try loose waves. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron. Then, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look. Finish off with some hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing your shoulder length wedding guest hairstyle:

- Consider the dress code and theme of the wedding when choosing your hairstyle. - Don't be afraid to add accessories, such as hair clips, headbands, or flowers, to enhance your hairstyle. - Practice your chosen hairstyle before the wedding day to ensure you're comfortable with it and know how to style it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair:

Q: Can I wear my hair straight to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight to a wedding. However, consider adding some volume with a volumizing mousse or adding some waves with a flat iron for a more polished look.

Q: Can I wear a ponytail to a wedding?

A: While a ponytail may seem too casual for a wedding, you can dress it up by adding some volume at the crown of your head and wrapping a piece of hair around the hair tie to conceal it.

Q: How can I make my shoulder length hair look longer?

A: You can make your hair look longer by adding hair extensions or by creating a faux bob. To create a faux bob, tuck the ends of your hair under and secure them with bobby pins at the nape of your neck.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and ready for styling, make sure to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant sprays before using hot tools, and avoiding tight hairstyles that can cause breakage.

With these shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair and hair care tips, you'll be ready to attend any wedding with confidence and style.
