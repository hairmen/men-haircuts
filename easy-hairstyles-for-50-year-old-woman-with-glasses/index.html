Easy Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Glasses

Written by Aprilia Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a 50 year old woman with glasses, it can be challenging to find easy and stylish hairstyles that suit your face shape and eyewear. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, this article will provide you with some easy hairstyles that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Easy Hairstyles
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging, especially if you wear glasses. Here are some tips to help you pick the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape: Different hairstyles suit different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, try a layered haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair.
  • Think about your glasses: Your glasses can affect the shape of your face, so make sure to choose a hairstyle that complements your eyewear.
  • Consider your lifestyle: If you're a busy person who doesn't have much time to style your hair, opt for an easy and low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Step-by-Step Easy Hairstyles

Short Hair

If you have short hair, there are plenty of easy and stylish hairstyles to choose from. Here are some step-by-step instructions:

  • Pixie Cut: Apply some styling gel to your hair and comb it back. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add some texture. Finish with a light hairspray.
  • Bob: Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair and add volume. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add some texture. Finish with a light hairspray.

Medium Hair

If you have medium-length hair, you can try these easy and stylish hairstyles:

  • Half-Up Half-Down: Divide your hair into two sections, and tie the top section with an elastic band. Tuck the ends of the top section under the elastic band to create a messy bun. Curl the remaining bottom section of your hair with a curling iron. Finish with a light hairspray.
  • Low Ponytail: Comb your hair back and tie it into a low ponytail. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add some texture. Finish with a light hairspray.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, you can try these easy and stylish hairstyles:

  • Messy Bun: Pull your hair back into a messy bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out some strands of hair around your face to create a relaxed look. Finish with a light hairspray.
  • Side Braid: Part your hair to the side and braid it over your shoulder. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add some texture. Finish with a light hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bangs with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with glasses. Just make sure to choose a style that complements your eyewear and face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair up with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up with glasses. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your eyewear and face shape.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With these easy hairstyles and hair care tips, you can look and feel your best as a 50 year old woman with glasses. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best, and don't be afraid to try something new!
