Men s Hairstyles Short Sides And Back

Written by Christine Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short haircuts for men have always been in style, and there are many variations of it. One of the most popular styles for men is the short sides and back cut. This haircut is characterized by shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, while the top is left longer for styling. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintenance, and frequently asked questions about this haircut.

Description

The short sides and back haircut is a classic, versatile style that can be worn by men of all ages. This style is also known as the "fade" or "taper" haircut, as the hair gradually gets shorter from the top to the sides and back. The length on top can vary, from a few inches to a short buzz cut. This style can be worn with any hair type or texture, and can be easily styled for a variety of occasions.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the short sides and back cut, you will need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. The stylist will use clippers to gradually fade the hair from the top to the sides and back. The length on the sides and back can vary, from a skin fade to a longer taper. The stylist will also blend the longer hair on top into the shorter sides and back, creating a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Once you have the cut, you can style the hair on top in a variety of ways. Some popular styles include a messy fringe, a slicked-back look, or a textured crop. To achieve these styles, you will need to use a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel. These products will help to hold the hair in place and give it a defined shape.

Step by Step Guide

1. Visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in the short sides and back cut.

2. Communicate your desired length on the sides and back, and the length on top.

3. The stylist will use clippers to gradually fade the hair from the top to the sides and back.

4. The stylist will blend the longer hair on top into the shorter sides and back, creating a seamless transition.

5. Style the hair on top using a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips for Maintenance

To maintain the short sides and back cut, you will need to visit the barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. This will help to keep the length on the sides and back consistent, and prevent the hair from growing out of shape. You can also use a trimmer at home to touch up the sides and back in between visits.

To style the hair on top, use a small amount of styling product and work it evenly through the hair. Avoid using too much product, as this can make the hair look greasy or weighed down. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape, and finish with a hairspray or matte finish product to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Q: Can the short sides and back haircut be worn with any face shape?

A: Yes, this style can be worn with any face shape. The length on top can be adjusted to suit the individual's features.

Q: Can this style be worn with any hair type or texture?

A: Yes, this style can be worn with any hair type or texture. However, the length on top may need to be adjusted to suit the individual's hair type.

Q: Is this style easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this style is relatively easy to maintain. Regular trims and styling with a small amount of product are all that is needed to keep the hair looking neat and styled.

Hair Care

To keep the hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to nourish the hair and prevent damage from styling products and environmental factors. It is also important to avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage the hair and cause breakage. Regular trims and styling with a small amount of product are all that is needed to keep the hair looking neat and styled.

In conclusion, the short sides and back haircut is a classic, versatile style that can be worn by men of all ages. With regular trims and styling with a small amount of product, this style is easy to maintain and can be adjusted to suit any face shape or hair type. Talk to your barber or stylist about this style, and see if it is right for you.
