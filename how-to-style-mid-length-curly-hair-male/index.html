How To Style Mid Length Curly Hair For Men In 2023

Written by Bardi Oct 06, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have mid length curly hair, you may be wondering how to style it to make it look its best. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for styling mid length curly hair for men. Whether you’re looking for a casual, everyday look or a more formal style, we’ve got you covered. Read on for our step-by-step guide.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Mid Length Curly Hair for Men
  • Tips for Styling Mid Length Curly Hair for Men
  • FAQs

Hair Care

Before we get into styling, it’s important to take care of your curls. Curly hair tends to be dryer than straight hair, so you’ll want to make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, use lukewarm water and gently massage the shampoo into your scalp.

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause breakage and frizz. If you have particularly stubborn tangles, you can use a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to make the process easier.

How to Style Mid Length Curly Hair for Men

Now that your hair is clean and moisturized, it’s time to start styling. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Apply Product

Start with damp hair and apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, paying special attention to the ends.

Step 2: Define Your Curls

Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to define your curls. If you have particularly tight curls, you can use a curling iron to loosen them up a bit.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair by gently squeezing sections of hair from the ends up to the roots. This will help to enhance your curls and give them more definition.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can cause frizz. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Curly Hair for Men

Here are some additional tips for styling mid length curly hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my mid length curly hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to use a heat protectant and avoid using a straightener too frequently, as this can damage your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my mid length curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but generally it’s best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.

Q: Can I wear my mid length curly hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, but make sure to use a soft hair tie and avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your mid length curly hair to perfection. Remember to take care of your curls by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
