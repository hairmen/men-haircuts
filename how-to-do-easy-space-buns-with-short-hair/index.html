How To Do Easy Space Buns With Short Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our blog about how to do easy space buns with short hair. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of creating this trendy hairstyle. We'll cover everything from what products you need to how to style your hair to look like a pro.

Description

Space buns have become a popular hairstyle over the years, and they look great on any hair length. However, people with short hair might think that they can't rock this look. But that's not true! You can easily create space buns with short hair, and we're here to show you how.

How to

Before we dive into the steps, here are the products you'll need to create this hairstyle:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair ties
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair spray

Step by Step

Follow these steps to create easy space buns with short hair:

  1. Start by brushing or combing your hair to detangle it.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections, one on each side.
  3. Take one section and twist it tightly.
  4. Wrap the twisted section around itself to create a small bun.
  5. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pin.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side.
  7. Adjust the buns to make sure they're even and secure.
  8. Spray some hair spray to keep the buns in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your space buns look even better:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.
  • Add some hair accessories like clips or headbands to make your space buns look more stylish.
  • Don't worry if your buns aren't perfectly symmetrical. A little messiness can add to the charm of this hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about space buns with short hair:

Can I do space buns with really short hair?

Yes, you can! Just make smaller buns and use more bobby pins to secure them.

How long does it take to create space buns with short hair?

It should only take about 5-10 minutes to create this hairstyle.

Can I wear space buns to work or formal events?

It depends on the dress code at your workplace or the event you're attending. However, you can make your space buns look more elegant by adding some hair accessories or smoothing down any flyaways.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

We hope this article has been helpful in showing you how to do easy space buns with short hair. Now go out there and rock this trend with confidence!
