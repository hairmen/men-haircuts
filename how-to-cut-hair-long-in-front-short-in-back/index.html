How To Cut Hair Long In Front Short In Back A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a bold and edgy new hairstyle, cutting your hair long in front and short in the back might be just what you need. This trendy haircut is perfect for those who want to stand out and make a statement. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair long in front short in back, provide tips, answer frequently asked questions, and share some hair care advice.

How to Cut Hair Long in Front Short in Back

The first step in cutting hair long in front short in back is to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep the hair damp while cutting.

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 2: Cut the Back

Starting at the nape of the neck, use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line across. Gradually work your way up the back of the head, cutting the hair shorter as you go. Remember to use your comb to keep the hair in place and to create a clean line.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of the head. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the back and working towards the front. Keep the hair longer in the front and shorter in the back.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Finally, it's time to cut the front. Start by combing the hair straight down and cutting it to the desired length. Remember to keep the hair longer in the front and shorter in the back. Once you've cut the hair to the desired length, use your scissors to add some texture and layers to the front.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Gather your tools: scissors, comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water.
  2. Section the hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Cut the back in a straight line across, gradually working your way up the head.
  4. Cut the sides at an angle, keeping the hair longer in the front and shorter in the back.
  5. Cut the front to the desired length, keeping the hair longer in the front and shorter in the back.
  6. Add texture and layers to the front using your scissors.
  7. Style as desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair Long in Front Short in Back

  • Start with damp hair to make it easier to cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to create clean lines.
  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid making mistakes.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with the back of your head.
  • Experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Is it easy to cut hair long in front short in back?

While it may seem daunting at first, cutting hair long in front short in back is relatively easy to do with the right tools and techniques.

What face shapes work best with this haircut?

This haircut works well with oval, heart-shaped, and square face shapes.

How often should I get this haircut?

Depending on how quickly your hair grows, you may need to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care for Long in Front Short in Back Haircut

To keep your long in front short in back haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid washing your hair too often. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting hair long in front short in back is a great way to switch up your look and add some edge to your style. With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect haircut and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
