How To Make Long Hair Look Short Easy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you ever get tired of your long hair and want to change things up without going for the chop? Fortunately, there are ways to make your long hair look short without sacrificing length. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make long hair look short easy.

How to Make Long Hair Look Short Easy

The following steps will show you how to make long hair look short easy:

Step 1: Use a Headband

Using a headband is a quick and easy way to create the illusion of short hair. Simply tuck your hair under the headband and you're good to go!

Step 2: Create a Faux Bob

A faux bob is a great way to make long hair look short without actually cutting it. Start by curling your hair, then tucking it under and securing with bobby pins.

Step 3: Try a Half-Up, Half-Down Style

A half-up, half-down style is a classic look that can make your hair appear shorter. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure with a hair tie or clip.

Step 4: Use Hair Extensions

Hair extensions can be a great way to experiment with short hairstyles without actually cutting your hair. Opt for clip-in extensions that are the same length as your hair and style as desired.

Step 5: Experiment with Braids

Braids can be a great way to create the illusion of shorter hair. Try a braided crown or a messy braided updo to switch up your look.

Tips for Making Long Hair Look Short Easy

Here are some additional tips for making long hair look short easy:

Tip 1: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, clips, and barrettes can be a great way to create the illusion of short hair. Experiment with different styles and find what works for you.

Tip 2: Change Your Part

Changing your part can make a big difference in how your hair looks. Try a deep side part or a middle part to switch up your style.

Tip 3: Use Heat Tools

Heat tools like curling irons and straighteners can help you achieve a variety of short hairstyles. Experiment with different styles and find what works for you.

FAQ: Making Long Hair Look Short Easy

Q: Will using hair extensions damage my hair?

A: Hair extensions can be damaging if not properly cared for. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully and use high-quality extensions to avoid damage.

Q: Can I make my hair look shorter without using any products or tools?

A: Yes, you can create the illusion of shorter hair by simply tucking your hair under or using hair accessories.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

When using heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant to avoid damage.

Tip 2: Avoid Overwashing

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Aim to wash your hair every other day or as needed.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week can help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily make your long hair look short without sacrificing length. Experiment with different styles and find what works for you.
