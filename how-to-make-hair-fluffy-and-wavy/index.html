How To Make Hair Fluffy And Wavy

Description

If you're someone who loves voluminous and wavy hair, then you've come to the right place! Fluffy and wavy hair is not only a trend but also a timeless classic that never goes out of fashion. Achieving the perfect fluffy and wavy hair can seem like a daunting task, but it's not as challenging as it may seem. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps on how to make hair fluffy and wavy. We'll also provide you with some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new hairstyle.

How to

There are numerous ways to make hair fluffy and wavy. You can use a curling iron, a flat iron, braids, or even rollers. However, we'll focus on using a curling iron to achieve the look you desire. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Clips

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to get that perfect fluffy and wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  4. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, leaving the ends out.
  5. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds and release the hair.
  6. Repeat the process for all of the hair sections.
  7. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to comb through the curls to create a natural wave.
  8. Spray some hairspray to set the curls and add volume to your hair.
  9. You can also use a teasing comb to tease the hair at the crown to add more volume.
  10. Finally, flip your hair over and spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve that perfect fluffy and wavy hair:

  • Invest in a high-quality curling iron that has different temperature settings.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly as it can make your curls look unnatural.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair before blow-drying it to add extra volume.

FAQ

Q: How long will this hairstyle last?

A: The hairstyle can last for up to 2-3 days if you use the right hair products and take good care of your hair.

Q: Can I achieve this hairstyle on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve this hairstyle on short hair by using a smaller curling iron and focusing on the ends.

Hair Care

To maintain your fluffy and wavy hair, you need to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote hair growth.

By following these tips, you'll not only achieve the perfect fluffy and wavy hair, but you'll also keep your hair healthy and strong. Remember to have fun with your new hairstyle and experiment with different styles and looks!
