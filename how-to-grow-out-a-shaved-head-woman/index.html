How To Grow Out A Shaved Head Woman Tips And Tricks

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Growing out a shaved head can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance and patience, it can be a rewarding experience. Whether you’re growing out a buzz cut or a full-on bald head, there are a few tips and tricks to help you along the way. In this article, we’ll cover everything from how to care for your hair to styling tips to keep you looking your best.

How to

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Scalp

Before you begin growing out your hair, it’s important to start with a healthy scalp. This means regularly washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent any buildup of oil or dirt. You can also use a scalp scrub to exfoliate your scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

Step 2: Be Patient

One of the most important things to remember when growing out a shaved head is to be patient. Hair grows on average about half an inch per month, so it can take several months to a year to grow out a buzz cut or bald head. Resist the urge to cut your hair too short in the meantime and focus on keeping it healthy.

Step 3: Trim Regularly

Although it may seem counterintuitive, regular trims can actually help your hair grow out faster. Trimming your hair every 8-12 weeks can help to remove split ends and prevent breakage, which can slow down the growth process.

Tips

Tip 1: Experiment with Different Styles

As your hair grows out, it can be tempting to stick to one style to avoid awkward in-between stages. However, experimenting with different styles can help you find a look that works for you at every stage of the growth process. Try out different lengths and styles, from pixie cuts to messy buns, to find a look that makes you feel confident.

Tip 2: Use Hair Accessories

When you’re growing out your hair, hair accessories can be your best friend. Headbands, hair clips, and scarves can help to keep your hair out of your face and add some personality to your look. Plus, they’re a great way to hide any awkward phases as your hair grows out.

Tip 3: Invest in Quality Hair Products

As your hair grows out, it’s important to invest in quality hair products to keep it healthy and strong. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much heat or harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to grow out a shaved head?

A: On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. So, it can take several months to a year to grow out a buzz cut or bald head.

Q: How often should I trim my hair while growing it out?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to remove split ends and prevent breakage.

Q: What should I do if I have an awkward in-between stage?

A: Experiment with different styles and hair accessories to find a look that works for you. Don’t be afraid to try out shorter or longer styles if you’re feeling stuck in an awkward phase.

Hair Care

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair healthy and strong while it’s growing out. Look for treatments that are designed for your hair type and texture, and use them once a week to nourish your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Try to avoid using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners, and embrace your natural texture instead.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant hair product when you’re spending time outside.

Growing out a shaved head can be a challenging but rewarding experience. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and strong while finding a look that works for you at every stage of the growth process. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles, and invest in quality hair products to keep your hair looking its best.
