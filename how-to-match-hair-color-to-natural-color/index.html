How To Match Hair Color To Natural Color Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Matching your hair color to your natural color can be a tricky task, but with the right tips and tricks, it can be done. Whether you're looking to go lighter, darker, or just want to enhance your natural color, there are a few things you should keep in mind.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Match Hair Color to Natural Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Matching Hair Color to Natural Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Matching your hair color to your natural color means finding the right shade that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. It's important to consider these factors when choosing a hair color, as it can make a big difference in how your hair looks and how you feel about it.

Matching your hair color to your natural color can also be done through different techniques, such as highlights, lowlights, balayage, or ombre. These techniques can add dimension and depth to your hair, making it look more natural and effortless.

How to Match Hair Color to Natural Color

The first step in matching your hair color to your natural color is to determine your skin tone. This can be done by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

Once you know your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. For cool undertones, choose a hair color with cool undertones, such as ash blonde or cool brown. For warm undertones, choose a hair color with warm undertones, such as honey blonde or golden brown.

It's also important to consider your natural hair color when choosing a hair color. If you have dark hair, you may need to lighten it first before adding color. If you have light hair, you may only need to add color to enhance it.

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist.

2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.

3. Consider your natural hair color when choosing a hair color.

4. If you have dark hair, you may need to lighten it first before adding color.

5. If you have light hair, you may only need to add color to enhance it.

Tips for Matching Hair Color to Natural Color

1. Don't go too far from your natural color. A drastic change can be jarring and difficult to maintain.

2. Consider adding highlights or lowlights for a more natural look.

3. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color.

4. Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure about what color to choose.

5. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair to a completely different color?

A: Yes, but it's important to consider the upkeep and maintenance of a drastically different hair color. It may also require multiple sessions to achieve the desired color.

Q: Should I use box dye or go to a salon?

A: It's recommended to go to a professional stylist to ensure the best results. Box dye can be unpredictable and may cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.

2. Limit heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

4. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

5. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Matching your hair color to your natural color can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a natural-looking hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.
