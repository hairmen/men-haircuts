Best Haircut For Square Face Girl How To Choose The Perfect Cut

Are you a girl with a square face and struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features? Look no further! In this article, we'll guide you through the best haircut options for square face girls, and how to choose the perfect cut that complements your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we delve into the best haircuts for square face girls, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here's how to determine your face shape:

  • Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  • Trace the outline of your face on the mirror with a lipstick or eyeliner pencil.
  • Step back and examine the shape you've drawn. Is it round, square, oval, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped?

If your face shape is square, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately the same width, giving your face a strong and angular appearance.

Best Haircuts for Square Face Girls

The best haircuts for square face girls are those that soften the angles of the face and create a more oval-shaped appearance. Here are some haircut options to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers that fall below the jawline and around the face are ideal for square face girls. The layers add movement and softness to the face, creating a more feminine look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs that graze the cheekbones and blend into the rest of the hair are perfect for square face girls. The bangs draw attention to the eyes and soften the angles of the face.

3. Bob with Soft Layers

A chin-length bob with soft layers is another great option for square face girls. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, while the bob length accentuates the jawline and creates a more oval-shaped appearance.

4. Shoulder-Length Waves

Shoulder-length waves that fall around the face are perfect for square face girls. The waves add softness and movement to the hair, while the length draws attention away from the strong angles of the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know which haircuts work best for square face girls, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect cut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut will work best for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Bring in pictures of haircuts you like to show your hairstylist.
  3. During the haircut, ask your hairstylist to add layers around the face or side-swept bangs to soften the angles of your face.
  4. After the haircut, style your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement around the face.

Tips for Maintaining Your Square Face Girl Haircut

Now that you have the perfect haircut, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Style your hair using a round brush to add movement and softness around the face.

FAQs

1. Can square face girls wear short haircuts?

Yes, square face girls can wear short haircuts, but it's important to choose a cut that adds volume and softness around the face. Pixie cuts and blunt bobs may not be the best option for square face girls.

2. What hairstyles should square face girls avoid?

Square face girls should avoid hairstyles that emphasize the strong angles of the face. This includes blunt bangs, sharp bobs, and center parts that create a symmetrical appearance.

Hair Care for Square Face Girls

Now that you have the perfect haircut for your square face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and the perfect haircut, you'll be sure to turn heads and feel confident with your beautiful square face.
