How To Cut Short Hair In Layers Step By Step A 2023 Guide

Written by William Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our step-by-step guide on how to cut short hair in layers. If you're looking for a trendy, fresh look, layered short hair might be the answer. This guide will walk you through the process of cutting layers into your short hair. We'll cover everything from preparation to finishing touches, so you can achieve the perfect layered cut in no time.

How to Cut Short Hair in Layers: Step by Step

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to note that cutting hair can be tricky, especially if you're doing it for the first time. It's always a good idea to start with a small section and work your way up to bigger areas. If possible, have a friend or family member help you. And remember, practice makes perfect.

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips or ties to section off your hair. It's also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while you cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a clean cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips or ties to keep each section separate.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Starting with the top section, use your comb to lift a small section of hair straight up. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut the ends with your scissors. Repeat this process for the entire top section, cutting the hair to your desired length.

Step 5: Cut the Side Layers

Next, move on to the side sections. Use your comb to lift a small section of hair straight out from the head. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut the ends with your scissors. Continue this process for each side section, cutting the hair to your desired length.

Step 6: Cut the Back Layers

Finally, move on to the back section. Use your comb to lift a small section of hair straight up from the head. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut the ends with your scissors. Continue this process for the entire back section, cutting the hair to your desired length.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the layers, use your scissors to blend them together. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut diagonally into the ends of the hair. This will create a more natural, blended look.

Step 8: Check for Evenness

Check your hair for evenness by running your fingers through it. Make any necessary adjustments by cutting small sections that are longer than the rest.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once you're happy with the length and layers, style your hair as desired. You can use a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create different looks.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair in Layers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when you're cutting short hair in layers:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Keep hair damp while cutting to make it easier to work with
  • Blend the layers for a more natural look
  • Check for evenness by running your fingers through your hair

FAQ about Cutting Short Hair in Layers

Can I cut my own hair in layers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair in layers. However, it's always a good idea to have a friend or family member help you, especially if you're doing it for the first time.

How often should I cut my hair in layers?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care after Cutting Short Hair in Layers

After cutting your hair in layers, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you want to style your hair with heat, use a heat protectant spray first.

Cutting short hair in layers can be a fun and easy way to switch up your look. With this step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve the perfect layered cut in no time. Remember to take your time, start with small sections, and have fun!
